# Braised Chicken with Shiitake Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐓 Free-range chicken, half (washed and cut into large pieces)
🍄 Dried shiitake mushrooms, about 10 pieces (pre-soaked and cut into small pieces; fresh shiitake mushrooms can be used as a substitute)
🫚 Ginger, a small piece (about the size of a thumb, cut into chunks)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (about 4–5 cloves, peeled)
🧅 Scallions, a few (about 2 stalks, cut into sections)
🧂 Salt, as needed (for marinating)
🦪 Oyster sauce, a small amount (for marinating and enhancing flavor during braising)
🥢 Soy sauce, a small amount (for marinating color and stir-frying aroma)
🌶️ White pepper powder, as needed (for marinating)
🌾 Cornstarch, as needed (for coating)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for pan-frying chicken pieces)
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed (for enhancing aroma and removing gamey taste)
💧 Water, as needed (for braising)
🌊 Cornstarch slurry, as needed (for thickening the sauce)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🪵 Round cutting board
🔪 Chef's knife
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍳 Non-stick pan
🍲 Wok (honeycomb texture)
🥄 Spatula
🔥 Gas stove
🫗 Glass lid

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the chicken pieces in a bowl. Add salt, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and white pepper powder, and mix well. Then sprinkle in cornstarch and toss until the chicken pieces are evenly coated with a thin layer. Set aside to marinate for a short while.
2. Pour cooking oil into a non-stick pan and heat over medium heat. Arrange the marinated chicken pieces in a single layer in the pan and pan-fry until both sides are golden brown with charred spots. Remove and set aside.
3. Pour the chicken oil rendered from pan-frying into a wok. Add the ginger chunks and garlic cloves, and stir-fry until fragrant. Then add the shiitake mushrooms and stir-fry until the aroma is released.
4. Splash in the cooking wine along the edge of the wok. Add soy sauce and stir-fry evenly. Pour in enough water and add a little oyster sauce. Cover with a lid, reduce the heat to low, and braise for 3–5 minutes until the mushrooms are fully tender and flavorful.
   **Note**: Dried shiitake mushrooms are firmer than chicken, so they need to be pre-braised until soft.
5. Add the pan-fried chicken pieces to the wok and stir-fry evenly with the mushrooms. Cover with a lid, turn the heat to high, and braise for 1–2 minutes.
   **Optional**: If you prefer spiciness, add bird's eye chili or dried chili at this step.
6. Remove the lid, pour in the cornstarch slurry, and stir quickly until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients.
7. Sprinkle in the remaining scallion sections, stir-fry evenly, then remove from heat and plate.

## Health Tips
This braised chicken with shiitake mushrooms is rich in protein and low in fat. Free-range chicken paired with shiitake mushrooms helps boost immunity, making it suitable for those needing to replenish energy or regulate the spleen and stomach. Note that dried shiitake mushrooms should be thoroughly pre-soaked, and the amount of cornstarch used for marinating the chicken should not be excessive to avoid increasing calories.
