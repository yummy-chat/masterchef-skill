# Stir-Fried Beef with Yellow Beef Tenderloin
Cuisine: Hunan Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef tenderloin 400g
🥬 Celery 200g
🌶️ Red chili peppers 30g
🌶️ Pickled wild peppers 30g
🌿 Cilantro 30g
🧂 Cooking oil 15ml
🧂 Soy sauce 6ml

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪵 Cutting board
🍳 Wok

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the beef tenderloin into slices no wider than 3cm and about 3mm thick. Add 6ml of soy sauce and mix evenly with your hands. Marinate and set aside.
**Key point**: Ensure slices are uniform in thickness and well-marinated for flavor.

2. Cut the celery into segments no longer than 5cm and set aside.
3. Cut the red chili peppers into thin shreds and set aside.
4. Chop the pickled wild peppers into small pieces and set aside.
5. Cut the cilantro into segments no longer than 3cm and set aside.
6. Heat the wok over high heat until hot, then add 15ml of cooking oil. Heat for about 30 seconds until the oil temperature rises.
7. Add the shredded red chili peppers and chopped pickled wild peppers, and stir-fry until fragrant.
8. Add the marinated beef tenderloin slices and celery segments. Stir-fry quickly over high heat for 1 minute.
**Key point**: Maintain high heat throughout and stir-fry quickly to keep the beef tender and smooth.

9. Turn off the heat, sprinkle with cilantro segments, and immediately transfer to a serving plate.

## Health Suggestions
Stir-fried beef with yellow beef tenderloin is rich in high-quality protein and iron, making it suitable for individuals with anemia or those looking to build muscle. However, it contains a high amount of chili peppers, so people with sensitive gastrointestinal systems or those prone to heatiness should consume it in moderation. Quick stir-frying over high heat locks in the tenderness of the meat, and be mindful of controlling oil usage to avoid excessive fat intake.
