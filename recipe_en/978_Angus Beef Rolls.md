# Angus Beef Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧅 1 medium-sized onion (about 300-400g), peeled and sliced
🍄 80-100g enoki mushrooms, roots removed, washed, and separated into small clusters
🍝 100-150g vermicelli noodles, softened in advance in water
🥩 300g beef rolls
🧄 3 cloves garlic, minced
🌿 White sesame seeds, as needed (for garnish when serving)

**Sauce (Recommended: Sukiyaki Soy Sauce Version)**
🥫 4 tablespoons sukiyaki soy sauce
🧂 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
🧂 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
🍶 3 tablespoons mirin (can substitute with rice wine plus 1.5 tablespoons sugar or red wine; do not use cooking wine)
🧂 1/2 tablespoon beef powder (can substitute with a pinch of chicken bouillon)
🍬 1 tablespoon sugar
🧂 1 pinch white pepper powder
💧 About 300ml water (half a bowl)

**Alternative Sauce (If no sukiyaki soy sauce, use regular soy sauce version)**
🍶 4 tablespoons premium light soy sauce or light soy sauce
🧂 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
🧂 1.5 tablespoons oyster sauce
🍬 1.5 tablespoons sugar
🍶 3 tablespoons mirin
🧂 1/2 tablespoon beef powder
🧂 1 pinch white pepper powder
💧 About 300ml water

**Cooking Oil and Seasoning**
🫒 Sesame oil, as needed, for sautéing the base
🧂 Salt, as needed, to adjust saltiness before serving

## Tools and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, chef’s knife
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🔥 Gas stove or induction cooker
🥣 Small bowl and spoon (for mixing the sauce)
🥢 Chopsticks or spatula
🍽️ Glass lid

## Cooking Steps
### Preparation
1.  Slice the onion and mince the garlic. When cutting the onion, keep water in your mouth to reduce eye irritation.
2.  Remove the roots of the enoki mushrooms, wash them, and separate them into small clusters for better flavor absorption.
3.  Soak the vermicelli noodles in advance until soft; set aside the beef rolls.
4.  Choose one of the sauce recipes, combine all sauce ingredients in a small bowl, add 300ml water, and stir until well mixed. Set aside.

### Cooking
1.  Heat the wok over high heat, add an appropriate amount of sesame oil, and test the oil temperature with a small piece of garlic. When bubbles form, reduce to medium heat, add all the onion and minced garlic, and stir-fry for about 1 minute until the onion softens slightly.
2.  Pour in the prepared sauce, then layer the vermicelli noodles and enoki mushrooms on top. Add additional water along the edge of the wok until it reaches about half the height of the ingredients.
3.  Cover with the lid, reduce to low heat, and braise for about 4 minutes. Ensure there is enough liquid left for cooking the beef rolls.
4.  Increase to high heat, place the beef rolls into the boiling liquid, and cook over high heat for about 1 minute until the beef rolls are fully变色.
5.  Taste the broth and adjust saltiness as needed with a little salt. Turn off the heat, sprinkle with white sesame seeds, and serve.
   - Safety tip: Be sure to turn off the gas valve.

## Health Advice
This dish is high in protein but also high in sodium. It is recommended to pair it with light vegetables or mixed grain rice to balance the meal. The beef rolls are rich in iron and B vitamins, making them suitable for those needing to boost blood or build muscle. However, be mindful of portion sizes to avoid excessive calorie intake.
