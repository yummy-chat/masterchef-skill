# Vegetable Pie 🥟

Cuisine: Common home cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 Zucchini – appropriate amount (finely shredded)  
🥕 Carrot – appropriate amount (finely shredded, paired with zucchini)  
🍄 Wood ear mushrooms – appropriate amount (chopped)  
🥚 Eggs – appropriate amount (scrambled and chopped)  
🧅 Scallions – appropriate amount (chopped)  
🥟 Ready-made dumpling wrappers – as needed (replaces hand-kneaded dough)  
🧂 Salt – appropriate amount (for pickling vegetables)  
🧂 Soy sauce – 2 tablespoons  
🧂 Chicken bouillon – 1 teaspoon  
🌿 Thirteen spices – 1 teaspoon  
🫒 Sesame oil – 1 tablespoon

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥖 Rolling pin  
🍳 Flat-bottomed pan  
🥢 Chopsticks

## Preparation Steps
1. Shred the zucchini and carrot into thin strips, place them in a bowl, add an appropriate amount of salt, mix well, and let sit for 10 minutes.  
   **Key point**: The salt extracts excess moisture from the vegetables, preventing the filling from becoming watery later.
2. Squeeze the pickled vegetable strips firmly to remove all moisture, then transfer to a clean bowl.  
   **Key point**: Be sure to squeeze thoroughly to keep the filling dry and improve the texture.
3. Add the chopped wood ear mushrooms, scrambled eggs, and scallions.
4. Add 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 teaspoon of chicken bouillon, 1 teaspoon of thirteen spices, and 1 tablespoon of sesame oil. Mix all ingredients well.  
   **Key point**: Stir thoroughly to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed.
5. Take a ready-made dumpling wrapper and use a rolling pin to roll it out thinner.
6. Place an appropriate amount of filling on the rolled wrapper, seal the edges tightly using a baozi-wrapping technique, then flatten the pie into a disc shape.  
   **Key point**: The seal must be tight to prevent the filling from leaking out during frying.
7. Pour oil into a flat-bottomed pan and heat it. Place the pies in the pan, reduce the heat to the lowest setting, and fry each side for 2 minutes until both sides are evenly golden brown.  
   **Key point**: Fry over the lowest heat throughout to avoid burning the outside while leaving the inside undercooked.

## Health Tips
This vegetable pie features zucchini and carrots as main ingredients. It is low in fat and high in fiber, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or seeking light meals. Be sure to thoroughly squeeze out the vegetable moisture during preparation to prevent excess liquid from seeping out and affecting the texture while frying.
