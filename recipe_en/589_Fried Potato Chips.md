# Fried Potato Chips
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes, as needed (choose yellow-fleshed, high-starch varieties for best results)
🧂 Salt, 1 tbsp (for blanching) + to taste (for final seasoning)
🍬 Sugar, 1 tbsp (for blanching)
🌾 Cornstarch, as needed (for coating, to make chips crispier)
🌿 Cumin powder, to taste (for seasoning, adjust according to preference)
🌶️ Chili powder, optional (for seasoning, omit if you don’t like spicy food)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife and cutting board, or a slicer
🍳 Pot (for blanching and frying)
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Glass bowl
🧻 Paper towels
🍽️ Plate
👝 Airtight sealed bag
🥢 Chopsticks

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the potatoes into slices as thin and uniform as possible.
   **Key point**: If you lack knife skills, use a slicer or similar tool to ensure even thickness.
2. Rinse the potato slices repeatedly in water to wash away excess starch, preventing oxidation and sticking during frying.
3. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add 1 tbsp of salt and 1 tbsp of sugar, add the potato slices, and blanch until just cooked through.
   **Doneness check**: Blanch until the water boils again and the potato slices become slightly translucent.
4. Quickly remove the potato slices and immediately plunge them into ice water or cold water to cool.
   **Key point**: Quick cooling effectively prevents the potato slices from turning black and keeps them crisp.
5. Remove the cooled potato slices and thoroughly pat dry all surface moisture with paper towels.
   **Key point**: Moisture must be completely dried, or it will cause severe oil splattering when added to the frying oil.
6. Spread a layer of cornstarch on a plate, lay the dried potato slices flat on top, then sprinkle a thin layer of cornstarch over the surface, ensuring each slice is evenly coated with an extremely thin layer of dry powder.
7. Pour enough oil into a pot. When the oil reaches a temperature of about 140°C (four-tenths heat), add the potato slices and fry over high heat throughout.
   **Key point**: After adding to the pot, use chopsticks to quickly and gently stir the potato slices, ensuring even heating and preventing sticking. Fry continuously until all slices float to the surface and turn into a uniform golden brown, then remove and drain the oil.
8. While still hot, place the fried chips into an airtight sealed bag, then add cumin powder, chili powder, and salt to taste.
   **Key point**: Seal the bag tightly and gently shake it to evenly coat each chip with the seasoning. Then serve on a plate immediately.

## Health Advice
Fried potato chips are crispy and delicious, but high-temperature frying destroys the vitamin C in potatoes. It is recommended to pair them with a fresh vegetable salad to balance nutrition. People with high blood lipids and those controlling their weight should consume in moderation to avoid excessive fat intake.
