# Hand-Torn Pan-Seared King Oyster Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍄 King Oyster Mushrooms, as needed  
🧈 Butter, 1 small piece  
🌿 Black Pepper, to taste (freshly ground)  
🧂 BBQ Seasoning, a pinch (optional, to sprinkle when serving)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick Pan  
🥢 Tongs or Chopsticks  
🧂 Pepper Grinder  

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the king oyster mushrooms, then tear them into thin strips along their grain.
   **Note**: Be sure to tear by hand, not cut with a knife, to preserve the fibrous texture.
2. Place the mushroom strips directly into a non-stick pan without oil, and dry-fry over medium-low heat.
   **Key point**: Do not add oil; cook using the mushrooms' own moisture; fry until softened, volume reduced, and edges turn a golden brown.
3. Once the mushrooms are cooked, add a small piece of butter and quickly stir-fry evenly until the butter melts and coats the surface of the mushrooms.
   **Note**: Add the butter when the mushrooms are nearly done for the best flavor enhancement.
4. Transfer the cooked mushrooms to a plate and sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper to taste.
   **Optional**: After serving, you can also sprinkle a little BBQ seasoning for extra flavor.

## Health Advice
This hand-torn pan-seared king oyster mushroom dish uses butter for flavor and black pepper as a finishing touch. It's low in fat and high in protein, making it the "fake meat" of the vegetarian world—perfect for satisfying cravings during weight loss. However, be mindful of the butter amount to avoid excessive fat intake.
