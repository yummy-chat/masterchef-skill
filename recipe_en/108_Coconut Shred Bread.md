# Coconut Shred Bread
Cuisine Type: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 High-gluten flour, as needed
🧂 Dry yeast, as needed
🍬 White sugar, as needed
🧂 Salt, a pinch
🥛 Milk or water, as needed (ensure sufficient dough hydration)
🧈 Butter, a small amount (optional, to increase softness)
🖤 Black sesame seeds, a small amount (for surface decoration)
🥥 Shredded coconut, as needed (for dipping when serving)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍚 Rice cooker (with "cake" or "cook" function) or bread machine
🥣 Mixing bowl
🥄 Mixing spoon
🍽️ Plate (for inverting to cool)
🔩 Heavy object (such as a soy sauce bottle, to press down on the top of the dough during baking)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Kneading and Fermentation**
   Mix high-gluten flour, dry yeast, white sugar, and salt together, then add milk or water. Knead into a smooth dough with high hydration, and perform the first fermentation until the volume significantly expands.
   **Key Points**: Adequate hydration is crucial for the bread's soft and stringy texture; the dough should be quite moist. Ensure fermentation is complete.

2. **Shaping and Decorating**
   Remove the fermented dough, gently deflate it, and shape as desired. Place it into the rice cooker inner pot, lightly spray water mist on the surface, and sprinkle with black sesame seeds.
   **Note**: A second fermentation can be performed until the dough volume increases; adjust the time flexibly according to room temperature.

3. **Baking**
   Select the "cake" or "cook" program on the rice cooker to start baking. To prevent the dough from expanding and pushing open the lid, place a clean heavy object on top of the dough to press it down.
   **Doneness Check**: Bake until the bread surface is deep brown, and insert a toothpick into the center—if it comes out clean without wet batter, it is ready.

4. **Removing and Unmolding**
   After baking, remove the inner pot and invert the bread onto a plate to cool. A star-shaped indent on top due to the pressure is normal.

5. **Serving with Shredded Coconut**
   When serving, slice the bread or dip it directly into shredded coconut to enjoy the soft, stringy texture and coconut flavor.

## Health Advice
Coconut shred bread has moderate oil and sugar content. Using a rice cooker simplifies the process compared to an oven, making it suitable for home baking beginners. High-gluten flour and milk provide quality carbohydrates and calcium, while shredded coconut adds dietary fiber. However, control the amount of shredded coconut used to avoid excessive calorie intake.
