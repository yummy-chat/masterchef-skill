# Air Fryer BBQ Eggs  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings

## Instructions  
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the eggs, and cook for exactly 9 minutes and 12 seconds until fully hard-boiled. Remove and immediately immerse in cold water to cool, then peel off the shells.  
2. Use a small knife to make dense vertical cuts on the surface of the eggs, creating a lantern-like pattern to allow better flavor absorption.  
3. Place the scored eggs in a bowl, drizzle with an appropriate amount of dark soy sauce and light soy sauce, and mix thoroughly by hand to ensure the eggs are evenly colored and seasoned.  
4. Spray a layer of oil on the bottom of the foil baking dish for the air fryer, place the eggs inside, and then spray another layer of oil on the surface of the eggs.  
5. Place the baking dish into the air fryer and bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.  
6. At the 15-minute mark of baking, pull out the basket, evenly sprinkle the BBQ seasoning over the eggs, and return to continue baking for the remaining 5 minutes until the skin is slightly charred and the aroma is rich. Remove and serve.  

## Health Tips  
Eggs are a high-quality protein source, and when paired with BBQ seasoning, they are low in fat and high in protein, making them a great choice for satisfying cravings during weight loss. Be careful not to cut too deep when scoring the eggs to prevent the whites from bursting during baking; the cooking time can be adjusted according to the power of your air fryer.
