# Sugar-Free Yogurt Mousse Cake
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍮 Gelatin sheets 2 pieces (about 10g)
🥛 Heavy cream 180g
🍶 Sugar-free yogurt 300g
🍓 Strawberries 8 pieces (for decoration and filling)
🧂 Seasonings: None (relying on the natural flavor of the ingredients)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl (for soaking, whipping, and mixing)
🔌 Electric hand mixer
🍳 Saucepan (large and small, for double boiling)
🍴 Silicone spatula
🔪 Knife and cutting board
🍰 Square transparent mold (or 6-inch springform cake pan)

## Instructions
1. Soak the gelatin sheets in cold water until completely softened.
2. Whip the heavy cream with an electric hand mixer until soft peaks form; set aside.
3. Wash the strawberries. Dice some into small cubes and halve the rest. Press the cut side of the halved strawberries against the inner walls of the mold.
4. Place the softened gelatin sheets in a small bowl or small saucepan. Heat over a water bath (double boiler) and stir until fully melted into a light yellow liquid.
   **Key point**: Using a water bath prevents the gelatin from overheating, which can cause it to spoil or burn.
5. Pour the melted gelatin liquid into the sugar-free yogurt. Quickly mix with a silicone spatula until the color is uniform.
   **Key point**: Mix the gelatin liquid into the yogurt immediately to prevent localized solidification and lumps.
6. Pour the yogurt mixture into the whipped heavy cream. Gently fold until a smooth and silky mousse mixture forms.
   **Key point**: Fold gently to avoid overmixing, which can deflate the cream and affect the mousse texture.
7. Pour the mousse mixture into the mold lined with strawberries. Gently tap the mold to release air bubbles. Sprinkle the strawberry cubes on top.
8. Refrigerate the mold for at least 3 hours, until fully set. Unmold and serve.

## Health Advice
This sugar-free yogurt mousse cake replaces part of the cream with yogurt, reducing sugar and calorie content. It is suitable for those on a weight loss diet or controlling sugar intake when consumed in moderation. Ensure the gelatin is fully melted and avoid overmixing the mousse mixture to maintain a smooth texture.
