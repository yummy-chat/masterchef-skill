# Egg-wrapped Rice Roll
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 Leftover rice (about one bowl)  
🌽 Corn kernels (from 1 small corn cob)  
🍖 Ham sausage (1-2 sticks, diced)  
🥬 A few leafy greens (chopped)  
🥚 Eggs (3-4, beaten)  
🧂 Salt (to taste)  
🍘 Seaweed and dried meat floss (or crushed seaweed, to taste)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Chef’s knife & cutting board  
🛍️ Transparent plastic bag  
🍳 Square non-stick pan (tamagoyaki pan)  
🥄 Silicone spatula  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Steps  
1. Remove corn kernels from the cob, dice the ham sausage, and chop the leafy greens. All ingredients should be cut into small, fine pieces.  
2. Place the leftover rice, corn kernels, diced ham sausage, and chopped greens into a transparent plastic bag. Seal the bag tightly and shake vigorously to mix evenly.  
3. Pour a suitable amount of oil into the square non-stick pan. Add the mixed fried rice, sprinkle with salt and seaweed meat floss, and stir-fry over medium-low heat until evenly combined.  
   **Key point**: Use the shaking method to mix ingredients—efficient and effortless.  
4. Using a silicone spatula, shape the fried rice into a tight rectangular block inside the pan, and push it to one side.  
5. Pour about half of the beaten egg mixture into the empty area of the pan. Tilt the pan to spread the egg evenly across the bottom. Heat over medium-low until the bottom has set but the surface remains slightly runny.  
   **Key point**: Keep the egg surface moist to help subsequent egg layers stick together.  
6. Roll the rice block from one end to the other to form an egg roll, then push it to one side of the pan. Pour the remaining egg mixture and repeat the rolling process.  
   **Key point**: Pouring the egg mixture continuously increases the thickness of the egg layer—work without interruption.  
7. Transfer the completed egg roll to a cutting board. Let it cool slightly to set, then cut into pieces about 2 cm thick. Arrange on a plate and serve.  
   **Key point**: Cutting after slight cooling prevents the roll from falling apart and helps maintain its shape.  

## Health Tips  
The rice is hidden inside the egg skin. This combination of carbs and protein is suitable for those needing a quick energy boost. However, ham sausage and meat floss are high in sodium, so people with high blood pressure should consume sparingly.
