# Mashed Potatoes with Lettuce
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥔 Potatoes, appropriate amount (cut into pieces)  
🥕 Carrots, appropriate amount (cut into pieces)  
🥬 Lettuce, appropriate amount (torn into pieces)  
🥚 Eggs, 2 (steamed with shells on, then peeled)  
🧂 Light soy sauce, 2 tablespoons  
🧂 Black vinegar, 2 tablespoons  
🫒 Sesame oil, 1 tablespoon  
🌶️ Black pepper, 1 tablespoon  
🌰 Sesame seeds, 1 tablespoon  

## Cookware and Equipment  
♨️ Steamer  
🥣 Large bowl  
🥄 Masher  
🥄 Spoon  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Peel the potatoes and carrots, and cut them into pieces; tear the lettuce into small pieces; wash the eggs and set aside.  
2. Bring water to a boil in the steamer. Place the potato pieces, carrot pieces, and eggs (with shells on) into the steamer. Steam on high heat for 15 minutes, until the ingredients are tender.  
   **Key Point**: Eggs must be steamed with the shells on to make peeling easier and ensure whole cooked eggs.  
3. Place the steamed potatoes, carrots, and peeled eggs into a large bowl. Use a masher to mash them into a paste.  
   **Note**: Do not mash too finely; leaving some chunks provides a better texture.  
4. Add the torn lettuce and mix evenly.  
5. In a small bowl, combine the light soy sauce, black vinegar, sesame oil, black pepper, and sesame seeds to make a sauce. Pour the sauce over the mixed mashed potatoes and lettuce. Serve immediately.  

## Health Tips  
This mashed potato and lettuce dish is rich in dietary fiber and low in fat, making it suitable for those controlling oil intake, reducing fat, or with weaker digestive function. It is recommended to steam the potatoes with the skin on (washed thoroughly) to increase dietary fiber. If you find the texture too plain, you can add a small amount of shredded cheese for extra flavor.
