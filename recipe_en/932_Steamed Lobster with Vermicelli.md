# Steamed Lobster with Vermicelli
Cuisine: Fujian Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-year-round Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🦞 1 Boston lobster (about 600g)
🍝 100g vermicelli (soaked in warm water until soft in advance)
🧅 1 spring onion (white part finely shredded)
🌶️ 2 red chili peppers (seeded and finely shredded)
🫚 1 small piece ginger (peeled, half sliced for bottom lining, half finely shredded)
🫒 Peanut oil (for final drizzling)

**Vermicelli Seasoning Mix**
🫘 Sesame oil, to taste
🧴 Dark soy sauce, to taste (for coloring)
🐟 Steamed fish soy sauce, to taste (for base flavor)

**Finishing Seasoning**
🐟 Steamed fish soy sauce, to taste (for drizzling on top)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥣 Bowls (for soaking vermicelli and soaking shredded garnishes)
♨️ Steamer
🍳 Wok or frying pan (for heating oil)

## Steps
1. Soak vermicelli in warm water for about 5 minutes until transparent and soft. Drain well, then mix with sesame oil, dark soy sauce, and steamed fish soy sauce until evenly coated in color and flavor.
2. Crack the lobster's large claws with the back of a knife. Separate the head from the body. Cut the body into pieces. Trim the claws and tail for later use.
3. Take a steaming plate. Spread the seasoned vermicelli evenly over the bottom. Arrange the lobster pieces on top, placing claws and tail on both sides.
   **Optional**: Place a few slices of ginger and spring onion segments on the bottom of the plate to remove fishiness and enhance aroma.
4. Bring the steamer water to a boil. Place the lobster plate inside, cover tightly, and steam over high heat for 8 minutes.
   **Key point**: Time must be strictly controlled. 8 minutes ensures it is cooked through and tender; longer cooking will toughen the meat.
5. After steaming, remove the plate. If there is excess liquid in the plate, pour off a little. Sprinkle the pre-soaked (until curled) shredded garnishes (the three shreds: ginger, spring onion, chili) over the lobster surface.
6. Drizzle an appropriate amount of steamed fish soy sauce along the edge of the plate.
7. In a separate pan, heat peanut oil until smoking. Pour the hot oil evenly over the shredded garnishes to release their aroma, then serve immediately.
   **Key point**: Splashing hot oil on the three shreds is crucial for aroma burst; the oil temperature must be sufficiently high.

## Health Advice
This dish is a low-fat, high-protein choice, suitable for those controlling calorie intake. However, vermicelli easily absorbs oil, so control the final amount of oil drizzled to avoid increasing calories.
