# Oil-Exploded Tiny River Shrimp
Cuisine: Jiangsu Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 Live tiny river shrimp: 400g  
🧅 Scallion white: 1 stalk (cut into segments)  
🫚 Ginger: 1 small piece (sliced)  
🫒 Vegetable oil: As needed (for deep-frying and stir-frying)  
🍶 Cooking wine: 50g  
🧉 Dark soy sauce: About 3g  
🧂 Light soy sauce: 5g  
🍶 White vinegar: About 2g  
🧂 Table salt: About 1.5g  
🍬 Granulated sugar: About 8g (two full spoons)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, knife  
🥣 Seasoning bowl  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🍳 Wok (frying pan)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Cut the scallion white into segments and slice the ginger, set aside; in a bowl, mix the cooking wine, dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, white vinegar, salt, and sugar to form a seasoning sauce.  
2. Heat the wok over high heat, add enough oil and heat to 70% hot, add the drained river shrimp, deep-fry for about 40 seconds, then reduce to medium heat and continue frying for 1 minute until cooked through, remove with a slotted spoon and drain oil.  
3. Heat the oil in the wok to remove moisture, raise the temperature to 220°C, add the shrimp for a second fry for 40 seconds until the shells are crispy and the meat is firm and separated, drain the oil.  
4. Clean the wok and heat it over high heat, add a small amount of base oil, when the oil temperature reaches 50% hot, add the ginger slices and scallion segments to stir-fry until fragrant, add the fried shrimp, pour the seasoning sauce along the edge of the wok, quickly reduce to medium-low heat and stir-fry for 1 minute to allow the shells to absorb the sauce.  
   **Note**: The sauce contains sugar and is prone to burning, ensure to stir-fry quickly over medium-low heat to avoid sticking and bitterness.  
5. Remove from heat and plate, can be garnished with a sprinkle of chopped scallions.  

## Health Advice  
Oil-Exploded Tiny River Shrimp is rich in high-quality protein and calcium, suitable for children needing calcium supplementation and elderly people with bone loss. However, it is high in calories after deep-frying, so it is recommended to pair with light vegetables to balance nutrition.
