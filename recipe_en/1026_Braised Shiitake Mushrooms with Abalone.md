# Braised Shiitake Mushrooms with Abalone
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms (dried mushrooms) – appropriate amount (about one bowl), pre-soaked and cleaned, choose medium-sized ones  
🌀 Sea cucumbers – several, pre-soaked and frozen, thaw before use  
🐚 Abalone – several, pre-prepared and frozen, thaw before use  
🥬 Lettuce – 1 head, as a side dish, washed  
🫚 Ginger slices – several slices  
🦪 Abalone sauce (or oyster sauce) – appropriate amount  
🧈 Cooking oil – appropriate amount  
💧 Water or broth – appropriate amount  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok (with lid)  
🥢 Wooden spatula/chopsticks  
🥄 Soup spoon  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Strainer  
🍽️ Plate  

## Steps
1. Pre-soak the shiitake mushrooms until softened, then clean them. Pre-prepare the sea cucumbers and abalone, then freeze them. Thaw before use. Wash the lettuce and drain well.
2. Add an appropriate amount of cooking oil to the wok and heat over medium heat. Sauté the ginger slices until fragrant, then add the drained shiitake mushrooms and stir-fry until the surface turns glossy and the aroma is released.
3. Add an appropriate amount of abalone sauce and stir-fry evenly, then pour in enough water or broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to medium-low heat.
4. Add the thawed sea cucumbers and abalone, along with any residual liquid in the bowl, into the wok with the shiitake mushrooms. Braise together for about 5 to 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
**Note**: Sea cucumbers should not be cooked for too long, as they may dissolve. Stop when the sauce thickens and the ingredients are well-flavored.

5. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Blanch the lettuce quickly, then drain immediately. Arrange the lettuce on a plate as a base, or place it separately.
6. Reduce the sauce in the wok until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon, then turn off the heat. Transfer the shiitake mushrooms, abalone, and sea cucumbers onto the lettuce, and drizzle the thick sauce over the top.

## Health Tips
Braised shiitake mushrooms with abalone is a high-protein, low-fat nourishing dish. Shiitake mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber, while abalone and sea cucumbers provide high-quality protein and minerals. It is suitable for individuals recovering from illness or post-surgery. However, abalone sauce and oyster sauce are high in sodium, so those with high blood pressure should control their intake.
