# Wang Po Big Prawns
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 Large prawns 750g (butterflied and deveined)  
🌶️ Erjingtiao chili peppers 60g (for making Ciba chili sauce)  
🌶️ Bird's eye chili peppers 50g (for making Ciba chili sauce)  
🦐 Prawn heads 30 pieces (for making prawn oil)  
🌿 Cilantro 1 small bunch (for garnish)  
🔹 White sesame seeds, as needed (for garnish)  
🥬 Side vegetables, as needed (optional)  

### Ciba Chili Sauce  
🌶️ Rehydrated chili peppers (Erjingtiao + Bird's eye) 350g  
💧 Boiling water 1000g (for rehydrating chili peppers)  

### Secret Spice Powder  
🌿 20 types of spices (Grass fruit 3g, Amomum villosum 4g, seeds removed, other spices total approx. 78g) Grind into powder and sift, use 25g  

### Core Mixed Sauce (approx. 370g)  
🍯 Tianche Spicy Sauce 80g  
🍯 Axiangpo Beef Chili Sauce 40g  
🍯 Axiangpo Vegetarian Beef-Flavored Sauce 40g  
🍯 Qiaotou Hotpot Base (crushed) 80g  
🍯 Lee Kum Kee Spare Rib Sauce 25g  
🍯 Fanzaoyang Spicy Sauce 50g  
🍯 Lee Kum Kee Satay Sauce 20g  
🍯 Satay Sauce 45g  
🍯 Dingdian Brand Dry Pot Sauce 50g  
🍯 Chu Hou Paste 25g  
🍯 Pangzi Spicy Fish Sauce 100g  
🍯 Lee Kum Kee Hoisin Sauce 25g  

### Oils for Cooking  
🫒 Rapeseed oil 400g  
🐔 Chicken fat 700g  
🐮 Beef fat 150g  
🫘 Soybean oil 800g (for making prawn oil)  
🐷 Lard 200g (for making prawn oil)  

### Aromatics and Seasonings for Stir-frying  
🧅 Scallions 100g (used in two batches)  
🧄 Ginger 130g (used in two batches)  
🧅 Onion, sliced 70g  
🧄 Garlic 80g  
🧂 Broth 750g  
🧂 White pepper powder 3g  
🧂 White sugar 10g  
🧂 MSG 10g  
🧂 Chicken bouillon powder 10g  
🧂 Salt 15g  
🧂 Sesame oil 8g  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment  
🔪 Scissors  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Ladle  
🔁 Immersion blender  
🌀 Grinder  
🔍 Fine mesh strainer  
🍳 Wok  
🔥 Deep fryer/deep pot  
🥢 Slotted spoon  

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare Ciba Chili Sauce**
   Soak Erjingtiao and bird's eye chili peppers in freshly boiled water for 2 hours. Once softened, drain, add a small amount of the soaking liquid, and blend into a coarse, wet paste using a blender.
   **Key Point**: Must use boiling water to ensure the chili peppers soften completely.

2. **Prepare Secret Spice Powder**
   Crack grass fruit and amomum villosum, remove seeds. Grind together with the remaining 18 spices in a grinder for 30 seconds. Sift through a fine mesh strainer to obtain a fine powder.
   **Key Point**: Ensure seeds are removed from grass fruit and amomum villosum; the powder must be fine.

3. **Prepare Core Mixed Sauce**
   Weigh out the 12 types of store-bought sauces according to proportions. Mix thoroughly until fully combined.

4. **Make Prawn Oil**
   Add soybean oil and lard to a pot. Add 30g sliced ginger into the cold oil. As the oil heats and the ginger floats, add prawn heads. Fry over medium-low heat for about 15 minutes. During frying, press the prawn heads with a slotted spoon to extract the roe. Turn off the heat, let it cool while soaking. Strain to obtain the bright red prawn oil.
   **Key Point**: Start ginger in cold oil, fry over medium-low heat, squeeze prawn heads thoroughly.

5. **Prepare Prawns**
   Using scissors, cut from the bottom of the prawn head down to the tail, cutting about 2/3 deep into the body. Remove the vein. Wash and drain thoroughly.
   **Key Point**: The cut must be deep enough for flavor absorption and easy deveining.

6. **Make Secret Prawn Sauce**
   - Add rapeseed oil, chicken fat, and beef fat to a wok. Heat over high heat until melted. When oil temperature reaches 130℃, add 50g scallions, 50g ginger, and sliced onion. Fry over medium-low heat until caramelized, then remove.
   - Reduce to medium-low heat. Add 350g Ciba chili sauce. Stir constantly for about 10 minutes until chili skins slightly curl and change color.
   - Add approx. 370g Core Mixed Sauce. Continue stirring for 5–6 minutes until fragrant.
   - Add 25g Secret Spice Powder. Stir for 1 minute, then turn off the heat.
   - Continue stirring for an additional 5 minutes using residual heat to prevent burning. Let cool before using.
   **Key Point**: Maintain medium-low heat and stir continuously. The step of stirring with residual heat is crucial to prevent scorching.

7. **Deep Fry Prawns**
   Heat ample oil to 220℃. Add drained prawns. Fry quickly until the shells are crispy and bright red. Remove immediately.
   **Key Point**: Use high heat and fry briefly to avoid overcooking the prawn meat.

8. **Final Stir-fry**
   - Add 150g prawn oil to a wok. Add 50g scallions, 50g ginger, and 80g garlic. Stir until fragrant.
   - Add 250g Secret Prawn Sauce. Stir-fry over low heat until well combined and aromatic.
   - Pour in 750g broth. Bring to a boil. Season with white pepper powder, sugar, MSG, chicken bouillon powder, salt, and sesame oil.
   - If using side vegetables, blanch them first and place at the bottom of a serving dish.
   - Add the fried prawns. Simmer over high heat for about 3 minutes until flavorful.
   - Pour the prawns and sauce over the vegetables in the dish.
   **Key Point**: Ensure the sauce is stir-fried until fragrant. Do not overcook the prawns to maintain their springy texture.

9. **Finish and Garnish**
   Sprinkle toasted white sesame seeds on top. Arrange cilantro sprigs for decoration and serve.

## Health Advice  
Prawns are high in protein and low in fat. However, side vegetables and a heavy oil-and-sauce preparation can increase calorie content. It is recommended to pair this dish with light vegetables to balance nutrition. This dish is suitable for spicy food lovers, but those with sensitive stomachs should consume in moderation to avoid excessive irritation.
