# Homemade Dual-Flavor Hot Pot
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Beef Neck Bone 1 piece (with meat, about 23 yuan)
🥓 Spicy Beef Strips About half a pound
🦆 Duck Tongues 18 (about 1 pound)
🐄 Tripe 1 piece (about half a pound)
🦐 Black Tiger Shrimp About half a pound
🦆 Duck Intestines 1 pack
🍢 Hot Pot Fish Balls 1 portion
🐚 Abalone 6 pieces
🥬 Lettuce As needed
🌿 Pea Shoots 1 portion
🍄 Beech Mushrooms 1 small handful
🩸 Fresh Duck Blood 1 box
🫘 Tofu Skin As needed
🧅 Ginger 1 piece

🧂 Salt As needed
🌾 Flour As needed (for scrubbing duck intestines)
🧪 Baking Soda As needed (for soaking duck intestines)
🌽 Cornstarch As needed (for processing shrimp)
🍲 Hot Pot Base 1 block (for spicy broth)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Cutting Board
♨️ Pressure Cooker
✂️ Scissors
🥄 Spoon
⚖️ Kitchen Scale
🍲 Stockpot
🥅 Strainer
🍾 Empty Glass Bottle

## Instructions
### Preparation & Pre-processing
1. Place beef bones in cold water and bring to a boil over high heat. Skim off the foam thoroughly, then remove the bones and drain well.
   **Key Point**: Starting with cold water helps remove blood. Make sure to skim off all foam for a clear broth.
2. Transfer the blanched bones to a pressure cooker, add a piece of ginger, and pour in enough water. Pressure cook for 50 minutes after it starts steaming. Season with salt after cooking to get a milky white beef bone broth.
3. Peel off the thin membrane from duck tongues, use scissors to remove the hard grains near the trachea, and trim any excess parts.
4. Rub duck intestines with salt and flour repeatedly until the water runs clear. Rinse, then soak in a small amount of baking soda for at least 1 hour. Rinse thoroughly again until the intestines are translucent and crispy.
5. Peel and devein black tiger shrimp, butterfly the backs. Toss with cornstarch, cover with plastic wrap, and slowly pound flat into thin slices using an empty glass bottle.
6. Scrub abalone clean, scoop out the meat with a spoon, remove the teeth, and slice thinly.
7. Unmold the fresh duck blood and cut into pieces. Slice the spicy beef strips thinly. Wash pea shoots, beech mushrooms, lettuce, and tofu skin. Set aside.

### Preparing the Hot Pot Broths
8. Pour half of the cooked beef bone broth into a separate container to use as the clear broth.
9. Add some water and 1 block of hot pot base to the remaining beef bone broth. Cook until the base is fully melted to make the spicy broth.
10. Pour the clear broth and spicy broth into the two sides of a divided hot pot. Bring to a boil and serve.

### Cooking Tips for Dipping
11. Arrange all ingredients on plates and cook as you eat: beef slices are done when they change color; duck tongues, duck intestines, shrimp slices, and abalone slices should be cooked just until done to maintain a crunchy texture; duck blood must be fully cooked through.
    **Note**: Different ingredients require different cooking times. Avoid overcooking to preserve texture. The clear beef bone broth is rich and savory, while the spicy broth is bold and satisfying—mix and match as you like.

## Health Advice
This dish is extremely high in protein and purines. It's suitable for occasional indulgence, but people with gout or high blood lipids should skip it. When cooking, prioritize vegetables and shrimp slices, and drink less of the beef bone broth to help reduce calorie and fat intake.
