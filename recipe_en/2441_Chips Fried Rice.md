# Chips Fried Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Rice, appropriate amount (day-old rice or rice cooked with less water, loosened and cooled)  
🥚 Eggs, 2 (to be mixed into the rice)  
🧅 Scallions, appropriate amount (to be used in three batches)  
🍟 Chips, 1 bag (any flavor, crushed)  
🫒 Cooking oil, appropriate amount (slightly more, for sautéing and frying the rice)  
🧂 Light soy sauce, 1 drizzle  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon  
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Salt, 1 small pinch  
🍬 Sugar, 1 small pinch  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spatula  
🎒 Original chip packaging bag (for crushing the chips)  

## Preparation Steps
1.  Pre-loosen the rice until each grain is separate. Crack in the two eggs and mix thoroughly, ensuring every grain of rice is evenly coated with the egg mixture.
2.  Take out the chips, squeeze out the air from the bag, and crush them into small pieces inside the bag. Set aside.
3.  Place the wok over high heat and heat it up. Pour in a sufficient amount of cooking oil. When the oil temperature rises until it just starts to smoke, add the first batch of scallions and sauté until a rich scallion fragrance is released.
4.  Immediately pour in the rice coated with the egg mixture. Gently press it flat with the spatula and let it sit without stirring for 30 seconds, allowing the scallion fragrance to infuse the rice.
5.  Begin stir-frying, breaking up the rice. Continue to stir-fry for about 1 minute to evaporate excess moisture from the rice.
6.  Add the second batch of scallions and continue stir-frying for another 30 seconds to release the scallion aroma again.
7.  Drizzle a circle of light soy sauce along the edge of the wok. Add one tablespoon of dark soy sauce, one tablespoon of oyster sauce, a small pinch of salt, and a small pinch of sugar. Stir-fry quickly to evenly coat the rice grains with the seasonings.
8.  Continue stir-frying until the rice grains are evenly colored and release a dry, fragrant aroma. Add the last batch of scallions, stir-fry briefly to combine, then turn off the heat and remove from the wok.
9.  Sprinkle the crushed chips over the surface of the fried rice. Gently mix together and serve immediately for a combination of crispy and soft textures.

## Health Tips  
Chips Fried Rice offers a delightful blend of crispy and soft textures, making it a quick and convenient staple. Be mindful to control the amount of crushed chips to avoid excessive sodium intake.
