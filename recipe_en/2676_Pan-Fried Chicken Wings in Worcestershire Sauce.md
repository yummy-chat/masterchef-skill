# Pan-Fried Chicken Wings in Worcestershire Sauce

Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-year-round meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken mid-joint wings, about 500g  
🫚 Ginger slices, to taste  
🧅 Onion, to taste  
🍾 Light soy sauce, to taste  
🌶️ Ground pepper, to taste  
🥃 Worcestershire sauce, about 15ml (for 500g chicken wings)  
🍯 Honey, to taste (add after turning off the heat)  
🥬 Scallion whites, to taste  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board and chef's knife  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🍳 Pan  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Wash the chicken wings. Make two cuts on the surface of each wing to help them absorb flavor and cook faster.  
2. Prepare the marinade: In a bowl, combine ginger slices, onion, light soy sauce, and ground pepper. Mix well, then add the Worcestershire sauce and stir to combine.  
3. Place the chicken wings into the marinade, rub thoroughly to coat, and let marinate for 15 minutes.  
4. Heat the pan over high heat, add oil, and arrange the marinated chicken wings. First sear over high heat, then reduce to low heat and pan-fry patiently.  
**Note**: Keep the heat on low throughout to avoid burning the outside while the inside remains raw.  
5. Once the surface of the chicken wings is set, add a small amount of water to the pan to help them cook through more quickly.  
6. Continue cooking over low heat for about 8 minutes, until most of the liquid has evaporated.  
**Note**: Do not use excessive heat to prevent burning.  
7. When the liquid has evaporated, immediately turn off the heat. Drizzle in honey and toss using residual heat, ensuring the honey evenly coats the wings and gives them a glossy finish.  
**Key point**: Always add honey after turning off the heat to avoid high heat damaging its flavor.  
8. Add scallion whites, gently toss a few times to wilt and release aroma using residual heat, then serve.

## Health Advice  
Chicken wings are rich in protein and suitable for muscle building, but frying increases their calorie content. It is recommended to pair them with vegetables for a balanced diet. Adding honey after turning off the heat preserves its flavor and prevents nutrient loss from high heat.
