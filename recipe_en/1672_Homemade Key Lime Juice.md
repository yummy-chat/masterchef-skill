# Homemade Key Lime Juice  
Cuisine: Others  
Difficulty Level: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🟢 Key limes 125g  
💧 Drinking water 700ml  
🧊 Ice cubes approximately 400g  
🍬 White sugar 80g (can increase to about 120g for commercial-level sweetness)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
⚙️ Blender/juicer  
🧴 Strainer/filter  
🥛 Large measuring cup/pitcher  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🪥 Toothpick  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Wash the key limes under running water, cut them in half, and use a toothpick to remove most of the seeds.  
   **Key point**: Removing the seeds significantly reduces bitterness.  
2. Add the seeded key limes and 700ml of water to the blender, select the juice mode, and blend for **5 seconds**.  
   **Note**: Must be strictly controlled within 5 seconds, otherwise the bitterness from the peel will be released excessively.  
3. Strain the blended liquid through a strainer into a measuring cup, thoroughly removing the pulp for a smooth texture.  
4. Add about 400g of ice cubes and 80g of white sugar to the strained juice, and stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.  
   **Adjustable**: If you prefer a sweeter taste, add more sugar up to about 120g, closer to commercial sweetness.  
5. Pour into a glass and serve. It tastes refreshing, with the characteristic slight sourness and aroma of key limes.  

## Health Recommendations  
Key limes are rich in vitamin C. Reducing sugar intake when making it at home helps control calories, and consider using sugar substitutes to replace part of the white sugar. This drink is suitable for summer relief from heat, but those with excessive stomach acid should consume it in moderation.
