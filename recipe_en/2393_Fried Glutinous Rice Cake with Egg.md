# Fried Glutinous Rice Cake with Egg  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Glutinous rice cake  
🥚 Egg  
🧂 White sugar or brown sugar  
🧂 Salt  
🧂 Cooking oil  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks or spoon  
🍳 Pan  

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the glutinous rice cake into small rectangular pieces to ensure even heating during frying.
2. Crack an egg into a bowl, whisk until well blended, then add 2 grams of salt and mix well.
3. Place the cut glutinous rice cake pieces one by one into the egg mixture, turning them to coat both sides evenly.
4. Pour 10 ml of cooking oil into the pan, put the egg-coated glutinous rice cake pieces into the pan, and fry over low heat until they become soft.
5. Evenly pour the remaining egg mixture over the surface of the glutinous rice cakes.
6. Use chopsticks or a spoon to flip the glutinous rice cakes, fry repeatedly until both sides turn golden brown, then remove from the pan.

**Notes**
- Leave space between the glutinous rice cakes when placing them in the pan to prevent sticking.
- Adjust the amount of egg according to the quantity of glutinous rice cakes.
- The amount of sugar can be increased or decreased according to personal taste.

## Health Tips
This dish is rich in carbohydrates and protein, making it suitable for quickly replenishing energy after exercise. However, those with high blood sugar should use less sugar when dipping. Use low heat when frying to avoid a burnt exterior and hard interior. Pairing it with soy milk or vegetables can make the nutrition more balanced.
