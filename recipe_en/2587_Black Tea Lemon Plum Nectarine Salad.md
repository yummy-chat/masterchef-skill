# Black Tea Lemon Plum Nectarine Salad  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍑 3 nectarines (cut into strips or cubes)  
🫐 10 plums (halved and pitted)  
🍋 Lemon (zest of yellow peel and juice as needed)  
🧀 1 burrata cheese  
🍯 Honey to taste  
🍂 Black tea powder to taste  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🥄 Grater/zester  
🧤 Gloves  
🧊 Refrigerator  
📦 Plastic wrap  

## Instructions  
1. Wash the nectarines and cut them into strips or cubes of moderate size. Halve the plums and remove the pits.  
2. Use a grater to zest the yellow lemon peel, then squeeze the lemon to extract the juice and set aside.  
3. Place the cut nectarines and plums into a glass bowl.  
4. Add the lemon zest, honey, black tea powder, and an appropriate amount of lemon juice.  
5. Wearing gloves, thoroughly mix all ingredients by hand.  
6. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate to marinate.  
   **Key point**: Marinate for at least half an hour; overnight marination yields better flavor.  
7. When serving, remove from the refrigerator, plate the marinated fruit salad, and place a whole burrata cheese on top.  
   **Key point**: Add the cheese last to maintain its fresh texture and intact shape.  

## Health Tips  
This salad is rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber. The antioxidants in black tea powder contribute to health, making it ideal for refreshing summer meals. However, note that burrata cheese is relatively high in calories and should not be consumed in excess.
