# Braised Lamb Ribs with Noodles  
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🧂 Seasonings  
- White sugar  
- Salt  
- Dark soy sauce  
- Sichuan peppercorns  
- Dried chili peppers  
- Cooking oil  

🥬 Ingredients  
- Lamb ribs with skin  
- Green bell pepper  
- Red bell pepper  
- Scallion  
- Ginger  
- All-purpose flour  
- Water  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Wok, pot for blanching, bowls, rolling pin, cutting board  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place the lamb in cold water and bring to a boil over high heat. Skim off any foam, then remove the lamb and drain.  
2. Slice the ginger and set aside in a bowl with the dried chili peppers and Sichuan peppercorns.  
3. Pour enough cooking oil into a wok. Once hot, add the white sugar and stir until it caramelizes and bubbles. Add the lamb and stir-fry until evenly coated.  
4. Once the lamb's moisture has mostly evaporated, add salt, dark soy sauce, and the seasonings from step 2. Stir-fry until well combined.  
5. Add enough water to submerge the lamb. Bring to a boil over high heat, cook for 10 minutes, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 30 minutes.  
6. While the lamb simmers, prepare the dough: Mix all-purpose flour, salt, and water, gradually adding water while stirring. Knead into a smooth dough and rest for 10 minutes. Knead again until smooth, then rest for another 5 minutes. Roll the dough into a long log, cut into 6–8 small pieces, brush with oil, let rest for 5 minutes, then roll each piece into a thin sheet.  
7. Add the green bell pepper, red bell pepper, and scallion to the pot with the lamb. Then add the dough sheets one by one, cover, and simmer for about 4 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure even cooking.  
8. Stir everything together well, then remove from heat and serve.  

## Health Tips  
Braised lamb ribs with noodles are rich in high-quality protein and carbohydrates, making them suitable for those with high physical energy expenditure or muscle gain. However, they are relatively high in calories, so portion control is recommended during weight loss. Lamb is warming in nature, and pairing it with bell peppers helps balance the richness. Simmering thoroughly also aids digestion.
