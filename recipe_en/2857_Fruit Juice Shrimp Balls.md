# Fruit Juice Shrimp Balls
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍤 Shrimp, appropriate amount (prepared shrimp, need to be rechecked and cleaned)
🍎 Apple, 1 piece (choose a crisp apple, peel and core, cut into chunks)
🍊 Oranges, 3-4 pieces (for plating, peel and remove segments)
🍅 Ketchup, appropriate amount
🌽 Cornstarch, appropriate amount (for coating)
🧂 Salt, a small amount
🌿 White pepper powder, a small amount
🍶 Yellow wine, a little
🍬 Granulated sugar, about 60 grams
🍹 Concentrated orange juice, appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍳 Wok
🥘 Saucepan
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife
🥄 Wok spatula/soup spoon
🥢 Slotted spoon
⚖️ Digital scale

## Preparation Steps
1. Check and clean each shrimp one by one, remove any remaining vein, and blot dry excess surface moisture with a clean cloth.
2. Add a small amount of salt, white pepper powder, and yellow wine to the shrimp, mix well, and marinate briefly to enhance the base flavor and remove any fishy taste.
3. Peel and core the apple, cut into evenly sized chunks.
4. Place the marinated shrimp and apple chunks separately into cornstarch, coating evenly with a dry layer of starch.
5. Heat oil in a wok to about 50% hot, add the coated shrimp and apple chunks, and fry over medium-low heat until set and cooked through, the shrimp turning red and the apple surface hardening. Remove and drain oil.
6. In another clean wok, add a small amount of water, stir in ketchup until dissolved, add sugar and concentrated orange juice, then add some water to dilute. Cook over medium-low heat until boiling, add a pinch of salt to balance the flavor. Optionally, thicken with a cornstarch slurry to a suitable consistency.
7. Place the initially fried ingredients into hot oil heated to about 180°C (356°F), quickly fry again for about 30 seconds until the surface is golden and crispy. Immediately remove and drain oil thoroughly.
8. Immediately add the double-fried ingredients into the sauce wok, stir-fry quickly over high heat to coat evenly, ensuring the sauce tightly wraps each piece of shrimp and apple. Transfer to a serving plate.
**Note**: The process of tossing and coating with sauce must be quick to maintain the crispy shell.

## Health Advice
This fruit juice shrimp ball dish incorporates fruits, rich in Vitamin C and protein, suitable for those who crave a tasty meal while avoiding greasiness. Control the oil temperature during frying to prevent excessive oil absorption; diabetics are advised to reduce the amount of sugar.
