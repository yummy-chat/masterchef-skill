# Stir-fried Onion and Egg
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🧅 Ingredients
* Eggs
* Onion
* Scallions

🧂 Seasonings
* Cooking oil
* Salt
* Cooking wine

🍳 Utensils
* Large bowl
* Wok
* Spatula

👨‍🍳 Steps
1. Crack the eggs into a large bowl, add the sliced onion and salt, and beat thoroughly with chopsticks for about 60 seconds until the egg mixture and onion slices are well combined.
2. Heat the wok over high heat, add an appropriate amount of cooking oil, and heat until it reaches about 70% hot. Pour in the egg mixture and cook over medium heat until the bottom is set (about 30-45 seconds). Use a spatula to flip, then continue to stir-fry and break into large pieces. Stir-fry repeatedly for about 2-3 minutes until the eggs are fully cooked. Drizzle cooking wine along the edge of the wok, stir-fry evenly, and then remove from heat.
3. Transfer the stir-fried onion and eggs to a plate, and sprinkle with chopped scallions.

## Health Advice
Onions are rich in antioxidants, and eggs provide high-quality protein. This dish is low in fat and high in nutrients, making it suitable for those looking to lose weight or maintain heart health. Note that the oil temperature should not be too high when stir-frying the eggs to avoid generating excessive AGEs (advanced glycation end-products).
