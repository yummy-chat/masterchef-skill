# Raw Egg Ice Cream
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 4 pasteurized eggs (separated into yolks and whites)
🍬 35g white sugar (10g for yolks, 25g for egg whites)
🥛 210g heavy cream
🍯 140g sweetened condensed milk

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 2 glass bowls (for egg whites and yolks separately)
🥄 Manual whisk
🔌 Electric mixer
🍽️ Spatula
❄️ Freezer-safe container
🍧 Ice cream scoop

## Preparation Steps
1. Separate the yolks and whites of 4 pasteurized eggs into two clean, grease-free bowls.  
   **Note**: The bowl for egg whites must be completely free of water and grease, or they will not whip properly.
2. Make the egg yolk mixture: Add 10g sugar to the egg yolks and stir with a manual whisk until the sugar is fully dissolved and the mixture is smooth and creamy. Set aside.
3. Whip the egg whites: Add 25g sugar to the egg whites and whip with an electric mixer on high speed for about 3 minutes, until stiff peaks form when the whisk is lifted. Set aside.
4. Whip the heavy cream: Whip 210g heavy cream with an electric mixer on high speed for about 2 minutes, until it reaches 90% of the way to stiff peaks.
5. Combine condensed milk and cream: Gradually add 140g sweetened condensed milk to the whipped cream, gently folding each addition with a spatula to avoid deflating.
6. Mix in the egg yolk mixture: Pour the egg yolk mixture into the cream and condensed milk mixture, folding with a spatula until the color is uniform.
7. Incorporate the egg white mixture: Add the whipped egg whites to the batter in 3 additions, gently folding from the bottom up with a spatula each time. Work quickly to avoid deflating, until fully combined and smooth.
8. Freeze: Pour the ice cream base into a freezer-safe container, smooth the surface with a spatula, and freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight until completely firm.
9. Scoop: Remove the frozen ice cream and scoop into balls using an ice cream scoop. Serve and enjoy.

## Health Advice
This ice cream uses raw egg yolks directly mixed in. It is recommended to use pasteurized eggs suitable for raw consumption and ensure all steps are carried out at low temperatures. Those with sensitive stomachs should consume with caution. High in sugar and fat, be mindful of portion sizes.
