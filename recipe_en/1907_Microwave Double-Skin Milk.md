# Microwave Double-Skin Milk

Cuisine: Cantonese  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 1 egg white  
🥛 Milk, as needed (hot milk)  
🫘 Sweetened red beans, as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
📡 Microwave  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Spoon  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place the egg white in a bowl and beat thoroughly with chopsticks until frothy, ensuring no yolk is mixed in.  
2. Pour the hot milk into the egg white and stir until well combined.  
**Note**: The milk should be preheated; you can use boiled pure milk.  
3. Place the mixture in the microwave and heat for about 1 minute and 10 seconds, until the liquid is fully set into a smooth, solid consistency.  
4. Remove and let it cool. Once cooled, top the set double-skin milk with sweetened red beans, and it is ready to serve.  

## Health Tips  
This microwave double-skin milk is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable as a dessert for those focusing on fitness or controlling sugar intake. Note that the milk must be preheated, and the egg white should be beaten thoroughly to ensure a smooth texture.
