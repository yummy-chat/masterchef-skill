# Century Egg Tofu Salad  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🧈 Silken tofu 1 box  
🥚 Century eggs (preserved eggs) about 3  
🥬 Pickled mustard greens (zha cai) to taste (about half a bag, rinsed and finely chopped)  
🧄 Garlic to taste (minced)  
🌶️ Red chili peppers (xiaomi la) to taste (sliced into rings)  
🌿 Cilantro to taste (chopped)  
🧅 Scallions to taste (chopped)  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🍬 Sugar to taste  
🧂 MSG to taste  
🥄 Light soy sauce 2 tablespoons  
🍶 Mature vinegar (or chinkiang vinegar) 2 tablespoons  
🫒 Sesame oil 1 tablespoon  
🌶️ Stone-ground chili sauce 1 tablespoon  
🌶️ Spicy chili oil 1 tablespoon (optional)  

## Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Steamer  
🍽️ Serving plate  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🥣 Small bowl  
🥄 Spoon  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Tear open the back packaging of the silken tofu box, widen the opening, and gently shake the box to loosen the tofu. Invert the box onto a plate, cut off the four bottom corners, tap the bottom of the box to release the whole tofu, and slice it thinly on the plate.  
   **Note**：Cutting off the four corners allows air to enter and helps unmold the tofu intact; slicing is for presentation and can be omitted.  
2. Place the century eggs in a steamer and steam over medium heat for 10 minutes until the runny yolks set. Remove, let cool, peel, and cut into small pieces. Arrange on top of the tofu.  
   **Tip**：If you prefer a runny yolk texture, you can skip steaming and cut them directly.  
3. Rinse the pickled mustard greens, finely chop them, and set aside with minced garlic, chili rings, chopped cilantro, and scallions.  
4. In a bowl with the prepared toppings, add salt, sugar, MSG, light soy sauce, mature vinegar, sesame oil, stone-ground chili sauce, and spicy chili oil. Stir thoroughly to combine.  
   **Key point**：Chili sauce and chili oil are key for enhancing flavor; the chili oil can be omitted if desired.  
5. Evenly pour the mixed sauce over the arranged century eggs and tofu. Serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
Century egg tofu salad is high in protein and low in fat, making it ideal for stimulating the appetite in summer. However, century eggs are high in sodium, so people with high blood pressure should limit their intake. It is recommended to pair with mixed grain rice and avoid consuming on an empty stomach.
