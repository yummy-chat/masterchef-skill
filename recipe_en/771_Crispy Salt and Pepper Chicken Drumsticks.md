# Crispy Salt and Pepper Chicken Drumsticks
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken drumsticks, as needed (washed clean)  
🫚 Ginger, a few slices (thinly sliced)  
🥚 Eggs, about 2 (beaten into egg wash)  
🧂 Light soy sauce, 2 tbsp  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tbsp  
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tbsp  
🍶 Cooking wine, 1 tbsp  
🌶️ Black pepper, 1 tsp  
🧂 Salt, 1 tsp  
🌾 Starch, 1 tbsp  
🌾 Flour, 1 tbsp  
🧂 Salt and pepper powder, 1 tsp  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for spraying or deep-frying)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for marinating the chicken drumsticks)  
📋 Metal tray (for mixing the coating powder)  
🍽️ Plate (for holding the egg wash)  
🍳 Pot (for deep-frying)  
🥢 Chopsticks/slotted spoon (for turning and removing the chicken drumsticks)  
♨️ Air fryer (optional baking method)  

## Steps
1.  Wash the chicken drumsticks clean and thinly slice the ginger.

2.  Place the chicken drumsticks and ginger slices in a bowl. Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, cooking wine, black pepper, and salt. Wearing gloves, mix thoroughly by hand, ensuring each drumstick is evenly coated with the marinade.
    **Note**: Make sure the marinade is evenly distributed. Marinate for 1 hour to allow the flavors to penetrate.

3.  Mix the starch, flour, and salt and pepper powder evenly in the tray to create the coating powder. Beat the eggs in a plate to form the egg wash.

4.  Dip the marinated chicken drumsticks evenly into the egg wash, then place them in the coating powder. Press firmly and roll them around to coat the surface completely with the dry powder, pressing it in firmly.

5.  Choose one of the following cooking methods:
   - **Deep-frying**: Pour enough cooking oil into the pot and heat to about 180°C. Add the chicken drumsticks and fry over medium heat for about 12 minutes, until the surface is golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain the oil.
   - **Air fryer**: Arrange the chicken drumsticks in the air fryer basket. Spray a thin layer of cooking oil on the surface. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes, turning once halfway through, until both sides are golden brown and crispy.

## Health Advice  
This crispy salt and pepper chicken dish is rich in protein, making it suitable for those building muscle or with higher physical energy expenditure. The deep-fried version is higher in calories; it is recommended to use an air fryer instead, which can reduce fat intake by nearly half.
