# Seafood Assorted Stir-Fried Instant Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 Shrimp, as needed  
🍡 Assorted fish balls (white, pink, brown, etc.), as needed  
🦀 Crab sticks, crab fillet, as needed  
🌽 Corn, as needed (cut into sections)  
🥔 Potato, as needed (cut into chunks)  
🍜 Instant noodles, 1-2 blocks  
🥚 Eggs, 4 pieces  
🌭 Ham sausage, as needed (sliced)  
🥬 Greens (such as bok choy), as needed  
🦐 Peeled shrimp, as needed  
🧂 Seafood barbecue seasoning, about 1 packet  
🌿 Five-spice powder, as needed  
🥄 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🥄 Dark soy sauce, as needed  
🧂 Salt, as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer  
🍲 Pot for boiling  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place the shrimp, assorted fish balls, crab sticks, crab fillet, corn sections, and potato chunks into the air fryer basket. Set the appropriate temperature and time, and cook until fully done and the surface is slightly charred.  
   **Note**: Adjust the specific time and temperature according to your appliance.  
2. Once roasting is complete, immediately sprinkle with the seafood barbecue seasoning and five-spice powder. Cover the basket lid and shake vigorously to ensure the seasoning coats the ingredients evenly.  
3. Bring water to a boil in a pot. Add the instant noodle block and cook until the noodles disperse and become slightly soft. Quickly remove, rinse under cold water, drain well, and set aside.  
   **Note**: Do not overcook the noodles; keep them firm and chewy.  
4. Mix the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and salt together in advance to make the sauce. Set aside.  
5. Heat oil in a wok over medium heat. Add the eggs and quickly scramble them with chopsticks until formed into curds.  
6. Add the sliced ham sausage and stir-fry briefly.  
7. Add the greens, peeled shrimp, and all ingredients roasted in the air fryer.  
   **Note**: The peeled shrimp and greens cook quickly, so add them last.  
8. Pour in the prepared sauce and the drained noodles. Stir-fry quickly with chopsticks to mix all ingredients thoroughly, ensuring the noodles are evenly colored and absorb the flavors.  
   **Note**: Stir-fry quickly throughout to prevent sticking and burning.  

## Health Suggestions  
This dish is rich in protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. However, ingredients like shrimp and fish balls are high in sodium; people with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of barbecue seasoning. Rinsing the instant noodles in cold water helps reduce oil content and results in a firmer, chewier texture.
