# Creamy Mushroom Soup
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍄 Fresh white mushrooms 200-300g (sliced thickly)  
🧅 Onion half (finely minced)  
🌾 Cake flour about half a small bowl  
🫛 Peas half a small bowl (blanched in advance)  
🧈 Butter about 2 large pieces (used in two portions)  
🫒 Vegetable oil about 1 tablespoon  
🥛 Milk适量 (heated in advance)  
💧 Hot water about 1 bowl  
🧂 Salt 2 teaspoons (1 teaspoon for first blanching mushrooms, 1 teaspoon for final seasoning)  
🧂 MSG 1 teaspoon  
🍬 White sugar 2 teaspoons  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Large bowl/plate  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥄 Soup ladle/cooking spoon  

## Instructions
1. Wash fresh mushrooms, gently twist off the stems, and slice the caps thickly.
   **Key point**: After blanching, **do not rinse with cold water**; directly drain and set aside.
2. Finely mince the onion; portion the butter; prepare the cake flour; heat the milk in advance; blanch the peas in advance.
3. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add 1 teaspoon of salt, add the mushroom slices, cook for 1–2 minutes, then drain.
4. In another pot, melt one piece of butter over low heat, add the mushroom slices and stir-fry until fragrant, then add half of the minced onion. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until the aroma is rich, then remove and set aside.
   **Note**: Add the mushrooms while the butter is not fully melted.
5. In a separate pot, melt the other piece of butter and the vegetable oil over low heat, then add all the flour, stirring and frying continuously.
   **Key point**: Keep the heat low throughout to prevent burning; adding vegetable oil raises the smoke point, making scorching less likely.
   **Doneness test**: When the flour paste becomes smooth, fine, and small bubbles appear on the surface, it indicates the flour is cooked and fragrant.
6. Keep the heat low, and add the hot milk in about 4 small portions. After each addition, stir quickly and continuously until fully absorbed and the mixture is thick, then add the next portion.
   **Key point**: The milk must be hot and added in portions; constant stirring is essential to achieve a smooth, lump-free batter.
7. Add 1 bowl of hot water to the batter, stir well, bring to a boil until the soup is thick and smooth, then reduce to low heat. Season with 2 teaspoons of sugar, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 1 teaspoon of MSG.
8. After the seasoned soup comes to a boil, add the blanched peas and the cooked mushrooms and onions, stir well, and bring to a boil again. Serve immediately.
   **Key point**: All ingredients are already cooked; once combined and brought to a boil, the soup is ready.

## Health Advice
This creamy mushroom soup is rich in dietary fiber from the mushrooms and vitamins from the peas, making it suitable for those needing gentle brain nourishment and stomach care. Note the relatively high butter and flour content; it is recommended to pair with whole-wheat bread to balance calories. Those managing their weight should consume creamy soups in moderation.
