# Turkish Doner Kebab Rice
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Bone-in chicken thighs 15 lbs (boned net weight, each about 150-180g)
🥬 Lettuce to taste (shredded)
🧅 Onion to taste (shredded)
🌿 Semi-crushed cumin powder to taste (for final sprinkling)
🌾 Sesame seeds to taste (for final sprinkling)
🍯 Sweet black pepper sauce to taste (for drizzling on plate)
🥗 Salad dressing to taste (for drizzling on plate)

🧂 **Dry marinade** (total about 462g after mixing, use 375g for marinating):
 - 🍬 White sugar 100g
 - 🧂 Salt 50g
 - 🌶️ Sweet paprika powder 50g
 - 🌿 Cumin powder 30g
 - 🧂 Flavor powder 20g
 - 🐔 Chicken powder 30g
 - 🌿 Fennel powder 5g
 - 🧅 Onion powder 20g
 - 🧄 Garlic powder 15g
 - 🌿 Five spice powder 4g
 - 🫚 Ginger powder 10g
 - 🧪 Disodium ribonucleotide 2g
 - 🧪 Caramelized ethyl maltol powder 3g
 - 🌶️ Paprika oleoresin 1g (optional)

💧 Water 375g (added with dry marinade for marinating)

## Cookware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl (for mixing dry ingredients)
🪣 Large basin (for marinating chicken)
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife (for boning and cutting meat)
🧤 Disposable gloves
🍢 Commercial Turkish doner kebab machine (includes spit and drip tray)
🖌️ Small brush (for oiling)
🍱 Rectangular takeaway container (for serving)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare dry marinade**: Weigh all powdered ingredients, place in a glass bowl, and mix thoroughly to obtain the composite dry marinade. Set aside.
   **Key Point**: Ensure all powders are evenly mixed to maintain consistent flavor.
2. **Prepare chicken thighs**: Using a knife, cut along the thigh bone, sever the membrane connecting meat and bone, then push apart with your hands to remove the bone. Retain the chicken skin and fat. Try to keep the shape intact.
3. **Marinate**: Place the boned chicken thighs into a large basin. Add 375g dry marinade and 375g water. Wearing gloves, vigorously knead and toss for about 10 minutes until all moisture and marinade are fully absorbed into the meat. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 6 hours.
   **Note**: Thorough kneading is key to flavor infusion; insufficient marinating time will affect the taste.
4. **Thread meat**: Thread the marinated chicken thigh pieces one by one onto the spit. Fold the bottommost few pieces before threading, then follow the principle of "thread one piece, press tightly" for each subsequent layer, building the meat stack as high and compact as possible.
   **Key Point**: Each piece must be pressed firmly to ensure the meat stack is dense, preventing it from loosening during rotation and cutting.
5. **Roast**:
   - Initial shaping: Place the meat stack into the doner kebab machine and start automatic rotation. Turn on all three heating elements, set to medium heat, and roast for 25 minutes. Keep the oven door open during this stage to allow steam to dissipate.
   - Slow-roast to doneness: After 25 minutes, turn off 1–2 heating elements, leaving only one on low heat to continue slow roasting. The oven door can be closed.
   **Tip**: During slow roasting, use a small brush to dip into the hot oil in the drip tray and brush it back onto the meat surface, giving it a glossy, moist appearance.
6. **Slice and mix**: Using a long knife, cut vertically downward close to the meat surface to shave off the outer roasted layers. After slicing, reduce the heat to minimum and continue roasting the inner layers. Place the sliced meat into a metal tray, sprinkle with semi-crushed cumin powder and sesame seeds, add shredded lettuce and onion, and toss evenly with a spatula.
7. **Serve**: Place the mixed roasted meat and vegetables directly over a bed of rice. Optionally serve with a small amount of pickled vegetables. Finally, drizzle with sweet black pepper sauce and salad dressing.

## Health Advice
This Turkish doner kebab rice is rich in protein, but the dry marinade contains relatively high sugar and salt. It is recommended to pair it with a generous amount of shredded lettuce to balance the flavor. People with high blood pressure should use less sauce.
