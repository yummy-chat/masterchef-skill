# Scallion Oil Noodles
Cuisine: Shanghai Local Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧅 Essential Ingredients  
- Dried noodles  
- Scallions  

🧂 Seasonings  
- Light soy sauce  
- Dark soy sauce  
- White sugar  
- Cooking oil  

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Cookware  
- Wok  
- Pot for boiling  
- Chopsticks  
- Bowl  
- Container (for storing the sauce)  

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the scallions and cut them into long sections, separating the white parts from the green parts.
2. Pour 100 ml of cooking oil into a wok. Heat over medium heat until warm, then add the white scallion segments first. Stir-fry until they turn slightly yellow.
3. Add the green scallion segments, reduce to low heat, and continue stir-frying for about 15-20 minutes until the scallion pieces are crispy and golden brown. Remove them, keeping the scallion oil in the wok.  
   **Key Point**: Maintain low heat throughout to slow-fry and avoid burning, allowing the scallion fragrance to fully release.
4. Add 60 ml of light soy sauce, 20 ml of dark soy sauce, and 15 g of white sugar to the scallion oil. Heat over low heat and stir for about 1 minute until the sugar is completely dissolved and the sauce is well combined. Turn off the heat, pour into a container, let it cool, and seal for storage.
5. Pour 1000 ml of drinking water into a boiling pot. Bring to a boil over high heat, then add 80 g of dried noodles. Cook for 3-8 minutes according to the package instructions until fully cooked.  
   **Key Point**: Remove the noodles once they are cooked through without a white core. Avoid overcooking to maintain a chewy texture.
6. Drain the noodles and place them in a bowl. Add 15 ml of the scallion oil sauce, and quickly mix well with chopsticks.

## Health Tips
Scallion oil is rich in flavor but high in calories. It is recommended to control the amount of sauce used for each serving, making it a quick and satisfying dinner option for workdays. For the elderly and children, reduce the amount of dark soy sauce to lower the saltiness.
