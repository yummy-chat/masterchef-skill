# Tuscan Salmon
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐟 Salmon fillets 2 pieces
🥬 Spinach a handful (washed and set aside, no need to blanch)
🍅 Cherry tomatoes several (halved)
🧅 Onion half (chopped)
🧄 Garlic a few cloves (minced)
🍅 Oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes optional (chopped)

🫒 Olive oil as needed (for pan-frying the fish)
🧈 Butter about 15g
🍷 White wine half a cup (about 120ml)
🥛 Heavy cream 150ml
🥛 Milk 150ml
🧂 Salt to taste
🌶️ Black pepper to taste

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Cast iron skillet
🧻 Kitchen paper towels
🔪 Knife and cutting board
🥢 Tongs

## Instructions
1. Use kitchen paper towels to thoroughly pat both sides of the salmon fillets dry. No need to marinate.
2. Halve the cherry tomatoes, chop the onion, mince the garlic, and wash the spinach.
3. Preheat the pan over high heat. Reduce to medium-high heat and add olive oil. Once the oil is hot, place the salmon fillets in the pan. Sear patiently until one side is golden brown before flipping. Develop a flavorful crust on the surface, then remove and set aside.
4. Do not wash the pan. Using the remaining oil, add the butter. Once melted, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté over medium heat until fragrant and lightly golden.
5. Add the oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes or the fresh cherry tomatoes. Stir-fry quickly for a few seconds.
6. Pour in the white wine. Bring to a boil to let the alcohol evaporate. Then add the heavy cream and milk. Stir to combine, reduce to low heat, and season with salt and black pepper.
7. Add the spinach to the sauce and cook until wilted. Return the salmon fillets to the pan. Repeatedly spoon the sauce over the fillets. Simmer over low heat until the sauce thickens enough to coat a spoon.
   **Key**: After adding the cream, do not boil over high heat to prevent the cream from separating and releasing oil.

## Health Tips
Salmon is rich in Omega-3 fatty acids. Combined with spinach and tomatoes, this is a high-quality meal for heart and brain health. The creamy sauce is relatively high in calories, so consume in moderation. Suitable as an energy-boosting meal for fitness enthusiasts. Those with digestive issues should be mindful of the fat content.
