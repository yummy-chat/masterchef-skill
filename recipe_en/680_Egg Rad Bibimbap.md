# Egg Rad Bibimbap
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Rice appropriate amount (base)  
🐟 Silverfish appropriate amount (raw)  
🌭 Sausage appropriate amount (bear-shaped)  
🌱 Bean sprouts appropriate amount (need to be hand-torn)  
🥚 Quail egg 1 (raw)  
🥒 Sea cucumber appropriate amount (raw or cooked)  
🥬 Romaine lettuce appropriate amount (added later)  
🫒 Beauty oil appropriate amount (bottled liquid oil, drizzled before steaming)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Bowl (container for serving and steaming)  
♨️ Stainless steel steamer  
🥄 Spoon (for stirring and feeding)  

## Preparation Steps
1. Spread the rice in the bowl, then arrange the silverfish, sausage, bean sprouts, quail egg, sea cucumber, romaine lettuce, and other ingredients in order. Finally, drizzle evenly with beauty oil.
   **Note**: All ingredients should be pre-processed into a size and texture suitable for the baby's swallowing.
2. Place the bowl into the steamer, and once steam rises, steam for about 15 minutes until all ingredients are fully cooked.
3. Remove the steamed bowl, and use a spoon to thoroughly stir, chop, and mix the steamed egg, sausage, vegetables, and rice together.
   **Key**: Ensure the ingredients are mixed into a fine, soft, moist texture that is easy to chew and digest, making it convenient for the baby to eat.

## Health Tips  
This Egg Rad Bibimbap is rich in nutrients. The silverfish and quail egg provide high-quality protein, and the sea cucumber supplements collagen. It is suitable for babies who need easily digestible complementary foods. Note that adding romaine lettuce later preserves vitamins, and all ingredients must be chopped finely to prevent choking.
