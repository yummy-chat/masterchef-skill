# Lemon Shredded Chicken Salad
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐔 Chicken breast 2 pieces  
🥒 Cucumber 1 (cut into thin shreds)  
🍋 Lemon to taste (cut into wedges)  
🧄 Garlic plenty (minced)  
🧅 Scallions plenty (minced)  
🤍 White sesame seeds to taste  
🫚 Ginger a few slices (for boiling chicken breast)  
🌶️ Chili flakes to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil to taste (heated for drizzling)  
🦪 Oyster sauce to taste  
🍶 Zhenjiang vinegar to taste  
🧂 Light soy sauce to taste (can substitute with steamed fish soy sauce)  
🫒 Sesame oil a small amount  
🍶 Cooking wine to taste  
🧂 Salt a little (to adjust saltiness, the sauce should be slightly salty)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Cutting board and chef's knife  
🍲 Cooking pot  
🥣 Large bowl or glass container (for mixing and tossing)  
🥃 Small bowl (for preparing the sauce)

## Steps
1. Mince the garlic and scallions separately. Slice the cucumber into thin shreds. Cut the lemon into wedges and set aside.
2. In a small bowl, add minced garlic, minced scallions, chili flakes, and white sesame seeds. Heat the cooking oil in a pot until it smokes, then immediately pour it over the mixture in the bowl to release the aroma. Add oyster sauce, Zhenjiang vinegar, light soy sauce, and a small amount of sesame oil. Stir well. Adjust saltiness with a little salt if needed; the sauce should be slightly saltier to taste than directly eating, as it will mellow when mixed with the ingredients.
3. Fill a pot with enough water, add ginger slices, scallion segments, and cooking wine. Heat until small, fine bubbles appear at the bottom. Add the chicken breast and cook until fully white and done. Avoid adding the chicken when the water is at a rolling boil, as this may make the meat tough.
4. Remove the cooked chicken breast and immediately plunge it into ice water to cool, or let it cool naturally. Then, shred the chicken by hand along the grain. The shredded chicken should not be too thin; retaining some thickness gives a chewier texture.
5. Place the shredded chicken and cucumber shreds in a large bowl. Pour in the prepared sauce and add the lemon wedges. Use both hands to thoroughly mix and toss. While tossing, gently squeeze the lemon wedges to release the lemon juice and zest oils, giving a fresher and more layered flavor.
6. Transfer the mixed lemon shredded chicken salad to a serving plate. No additional garnishing is needed. It is refreshing and appetizing.

## Health Tips
Lemon shredded chicken salad is high in protein and low in fat, an ideal summer dish for weight loss. It is recommended to pair it with mixed grain rice as a light lunch. Boiling the chicken breast starting from cold water yields a more tender texture, but when shredding, do not make the pieces too fine to preserve the mouthfeel.
