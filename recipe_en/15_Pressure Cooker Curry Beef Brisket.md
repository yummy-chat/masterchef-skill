# Pressure Cooker Curry Beef Brisket  
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🫑 Bell peppers (red and green) -适量- (cut into chunks)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
♨️ Pressure cooker  
🔥 Gas stove  
🥄 Spoon  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  

## Instructions  
1. Place the pre-blanched and cut beef brisket into the pressure cooker. Add the curry paste and peanut butter, and mix well.  
2. Close the pressure cooker lid. Once steam is released, cook under pressure for 20 minutes.  
3. After opening the lid, adjust the tenderness of the beef brisket to your preference. Add the onion chunks, bell pepper chunks, sugar, coconut milk, and milk. Stir well.  
**Note**: The side vegetables cook quickly and should be added only after the beef brisket has been braised until tender, to avoid losing their texture with prolonged cooking.  
4. Continue heating over the fire, cooking until the side vegetables are just done and the sauce is well combined. Once a rich aroma is released, it is ready to serve.  

## Health Tips  
Beef brisket is rich in protein and iron, making it suitable for those needing to boost blood and muscle. Curry and coconut milk are relatively high in calories; it is recommended to pair with brown rice or a vegetable salad to balance. The pressure cooker saves time, and the braised beef brisket turns out tender and flavorful, particularly friendly for those with poor teeth.
