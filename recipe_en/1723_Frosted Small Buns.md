# Frosted Small Buns
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 All-purpose flour 500g
🧂 Baking powder 5g
🥚 Egg 1
🥛 Whole milk 240ml
🍬 White sugar (as needed for kneading and for making the frosting)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🪠 Rolling pin
🔪 Knife
🍳 Flat-bottomed pan
🥄 Spatula

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the flour, baking powder, egg, and an appropriate amount of white sugar in a bowl. Add the whole milk in batches, stirring as you pour, and knead until you have a smooth, soft dough.
2. Roll the dough into an even thin sheet. First cut it into long strips, then cut crosswise into small, uniform dough pieces.
3. Sprinkle a layer of dry flour over the small dough pieces. Gently shake so each piece is evenly coated with flour and naturally separated to prevent sticking.
4. Heat a flat-bottomed pan over low heat. Do not add oil or water. Place the small dough pieces directly into the pan and dry-roast over low heat, turning them occasionally.
   **Check**: The buns are done when they have puffed up in size, surface shows light yellow or golden brown spots, and the buns spring back when pressed lightly with a finger. Remove and set aside.
5. In a separate pan, add an appropriate amount of water and white sugar. Cook over low heat, stirring continuously, until the sugar is completely dissolved and the syrup turns a light amber color with dense, small bubbles.
6. Quickly add the roasted small buns into the pan. Stir-fry rapidly and continuously until each piece is evenly coated with a layer of white frosting. Remove from the pan and serve.

## Health Advice
This dry-roasted small bun with frosting has moderate calories but a high sugar content. It is suitable as an occasional snack but not recommended for people with diabetes or those controlling sugar intake. The milk and egg provide high-quality protein, and pairing with whole grains or vegetables can make the nutrition more balanced.
