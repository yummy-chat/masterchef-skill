# Light Cream Cheesecake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧁 Cake flour, about 60g
🥛 Light cream, about 150g
🥚 Eggs, 3 (separate egg whites and yolks)
🍬 Sugar, about 35g (for whipping egg whites)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
📏 Homemade plastic bottle measuring cup (or regular kitchen scale)
🥢 Chopsticks
⚡ Electric hand mixer
🍯 Silicone spatula
🍰 6-inch cake pan
📄 Parchment paper
🔥 Oven
🥘 Baking tray
🛡️ Aluminum foil

## Instructions
1. Mix the cake flour and light cream in proportion, stirring until smooth and lump-free.
2. Separate the egg whites and yolks. Add the yolks to the batter and mix well to form a smooth, flowing yolk batter that leaves a slow-fading trail when dropped. Place the egg whites in a clean, grease-free and water-free bowl and set aside.
   **Note**: The bowl for egg whites must be free of oil and water, or they will not whip properly.
3. Add all the sugar to the egg whites. Using an electric hand mixer, whip to soft peaks, where the meringue forms a soft, drooping hook when the beaters are lifted.
   **Key**: Do not over-whip to stiff peaks with upright tips, or the finished cake may crack.
4. Take about 1/3 of the meringue and add it to the yolk batter. Quickly fold together using a silicone spatula.
5. Pour the mixed batter back into the remaining meringue. Gently fold from the bottom upward until the mixture is uniform, thick, and smooth, being careful not to deflate.
6. Line a 6-inch cake pan with parchment paper. Pour in the batter and gently tap the pan a few times to release large air bubbles.
7. Preheat the oven. Place a baking tray on the lower rack and pour in cold water. Set the oven to 190°C top heat and 130°C bottom heat. Bake the cake for about 15 minutes to allow the surface to set and color.
8. Once the surface is colored, quickly cover the pan with a sheet of aluminum foil to prevent burning. Then adjust the oven temperature to 150°C and bake for 20 minutes, then reduce to 130°C and bake for 40-50 minutes until fully cooked.
   **Note**: Maintain a water bath throughout. Oven temperatures vary; adjust time and temperature as needed.
9. After baking, do not remove immediately. Let the cake rest in the oven for 5-10 minutes to reduce shrinkage from temperature differences.
10. Remove from the oven and unmold while still hot. Place on a wire rack to cool completely. The cake will be very soft and jiggly when hot; once cooled, the structure becomes more stable. The texture is moist and delicate, melting in the mouth.

## Health Advice
This light cream cheesecake has a moist and delicate texture, making it suitable as a mild dessert. Be mindful of sugar intake; those with diabetes or on a weight-loss diet should reduce the sugar amount or use a sugar substitute. Whipping the egg whites to soft peaks is key; avoid over-whipping to prevent cracking.
