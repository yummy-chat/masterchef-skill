# Classic Pancakes
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 150g cake or all-purpose flour
🥛 250g milk
🧂 1g salt
🍬 12g sugar
🧈 20g butter
🥚 1 egg
🫧 1 tsp baking powder
🫒 A little cooking oil

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥣 Large glass bowl
🍳 Iron skillet
🥄 Whisk
🥛 Measuring cup
🍦 Ice cream scoop
🔪 Spatula
🧻 Paper towel

## Instructions
1. Add the flour to the milk and whisk until roughly combined. Small lumps in the batter are fine.
   **Key Point**: Mixing the flour with the milk first, before adding the egg, allows for smoother blending.
2. Add the sugar, salt, melted butter, and egg to the batter. Whisk thoroughly until fully incorporated.
3. Finally, add the baking powder and stir until evenly mixed. The batter should be filled with tiny bubbles and light in texture.
   **Key Point**: Add the baking powder last to preserve its dual leavening action when exposed to liquid and heat.
4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Once the pan is hot and just starting to smoke, use a paper towel dipped in a little cooking oil to thinly coat the entire bottom of the pan.
   **Note**: Only oil the pan once; no additional oil is needed for subsequent pancakes.
5. Using a spoon or ice cream scoop, pour the batter into the pan from a fixed height, allowing it to naturally form a round shape. Reduce the heat to medium-low.
6. When the surface of the pancake is covered with small bubbles and still looks slightly moist (not completely dry), immediately flip it with a spatula.
   **Key Point**: This step is crucial for ensuring the pancake is cooked through while remaining soft and fluffy.
7. Continue cooking over medium-low heat for about 30 seconds after flipping, until both sides are evenly golden brown and the pancake puffs up. Remove from the pan.

## Health Advice
This pancake is high in carbohydrates and easy to digest, making it suitable for breakfast or as an energy boost after exercise. It is recommended to pair with fruit or yogurt to increase fiber and protein intake. Be mindful of the sugar and butter amounts used.
