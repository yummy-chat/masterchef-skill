# Basque-Style Chicken Stew
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken pieces (chicken wings, drumsticks), about 6-8 pieces  
🧅 White onion, half (chopped)  
🧄 Garlic, a few cloves (chopped)  
🫑 Red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, to taste (sliced)  
🥫 Tomato paste, to taste  
🫒 Olive oil, to taste  
🧂 Salt, to taste (used in two batches)  
🌶 Smoked sweet paprika, to taste  
🍇 White wine, about 50ml  
🍲 Chicken stock or water, to taste  
🌿 Bay leaves, two  
🌿 Thyme, a pinch  
⚫ Black pepper, to taste  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Cast iron pot (enameled cast iron)  
🎚 Paper towels  
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife  
🥢 Tongs  
🥄 Wooden spoon or spatula  
🔥 Oven (preheated to 170°C)  
🥣 Measuring cup  

## Steps  
1. Use paper towels to pat the chicken pieces dry, and evenly sprinkle salt over the chicken skin.  
2. Chop the white onion and garlic; remove the seeds from the red and yellow bell peppers and slice them into strips.  
3. Pour an appropriate amount of olive oil into the cast iron pot. Place the chicken pieces skin-side down and pan-fry over medium heat.  
   **Key point**: Do not turn the chicken until the skin is golden brown to prevent sticking.  
4. Once both sides of the chicken are golden brown, remove and set aside.  
5. Keep the remaining oil in the pot. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté over low heat until soft and fragrant.  
6. Add the bell pepper strips, and stir-fry until soft and fragrant; then add the smoked sweet paprika and stir to combine.  
7. Add the tomato paste and stir well. Pour in the white wine to enhance the aroma.  
8. Add chicken stock or water, enough to just cover the vegetables. Add the bay leaves and thyme, and season with salt and black pepper.  
   **Note**: Chili powder can be added according to personal preference.  
9. Return the pan-fried chicken pieces to the pot, ensuring the chicken is submerged in the liquid. Cover with the lid.  
10. Place the pot in the preheated oven at 170°C and roast for about 40 minutes, until the sauce thickens and the chicken can be easily pierced with chopsticks.  
    **Alternative**: This can also be directly simmered over low heat on the stove until tender.  
11. After removing from the heat, sprinkle with chopped parsley for flavor and color. Serve with rice or bread.

## Health Advice  
This dish stews chicken with bell peppers and tomato paste, making it rich in vitamin C and high-quality protein, suitable for those looking to build muscle or lose weight. Be mindful of salt intake, and serving with rice makes for a more balanced meal.
