# Stir-Fried Cauliflower
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥦 Cauliflower, about 300 g
🧄 Garlic, 2-3 cloves
🧂 Salt, 3 g
🫒 Cooking oil, 15 ml
💧 Drinking water, 50 ml

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted spoon
🧺 Colander
🔪 Knife
🪓 Cutting board

## Cooking Steps
1. Wash the cauliflower and break or cut it into small florets by hand or knife. Slice the thicker stems and set aside.
2. Peel the garlic and slice it into pieces. Set aside.
3. Pour 1000 ml of water into a wok and bring to a boil over high heat.
4. Add the cauliflower florets and blanch for 2-3 minutes until the color lightens and the texture softens slightly.
   **Key point:** Do not blanch for too long, or the cauliflower will become too soft and lose its crunch.
5. Remove the cauliflower, drain well, and set aside.
6. Heat another wok, add 15 ml of cooking oil, and heat over high heat until it reaches about 60% hot.
7. Add the garlic slices and stir-fry until fragrant.
8. Add the blanched cauliflower, turn to medium-high heat, and quickly stir-fry for about 2 minutes to ensure even heating.
   **Key point:** Adjust the stir-fry time according to personal preference: shorten for a crunchier texture, extend for a softer one.
9. Add 3 g of salt and stir well to combine.
10. Pour 50 ml of drinking water along the edge of the wok, cover, and simmer for 1 minute to fully cook and flavor the cauliflower.
11. Remove the lid, quickly stir-fry evenly, then transfer to a serving plate.

## Health Tips
Cauliflower is rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber, low in calories, and high in satiety, making it suitable for weight loss or blood sugar control. Blanching before stir-frying reduces oil absorption, but be careful not to boil it for too long to avoid nutrient loss.
