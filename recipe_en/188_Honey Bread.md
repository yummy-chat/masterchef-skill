# Honey Bread
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Bread flour 400g
🥛 Milk 200g
🧫 Yeast 4g
🥚 Egg 1
🧈 Butter 30g
🍯 Honey 20g
💧 Water 20g
🌰 Sesame seeds, as needed
🍬 Granulated sugar 70g
🧂 Salt 2g
🫒 Peanut oil, as needed

## Cookware & Equipment
🥘 Rectangular baking pan
🥘 Square baking pan
📄 Parchment paper
🥖 Rolling pin
🧻 Plastic wrap
🔥 Oven

## Instructions
1. Combine bread flour, milk, yeast, egg, granulated sugar, and salt, and knead into a smooth dough.
2. Add the butter and continue kneading until the dough can stretch into a thin film.
3. Cover with plastic wrap and let it ferment at room temperature for 1-2 hours, until the dough expands to 2-2.5 times its original size.
4. Divide the fermented dough into small pieces, each about 60g.
5. Take one piece and roll it into an oval shape, roll it up, and let it rest for 10 minutes.
6. Roll it into an oval shape again and roll it up, then cut it in half from the middle.
7. Repeat the rolling and cutting process once more to obtain four portions.
8. Line the baking pan with parchment paper and pour in the peanut oil. Dip the bottom of each rolled dough piece in water, then in flour, and place it into the pan.
9. Cover with plastic wrap for a second proofing, or refrigerate overnight and bake the next day.
10. Brush the surface of the proofed dough with egg wash.
11. Preheat the oven to 180℃, place the baking pan inside, and bake for 18-20 minutes.
12. Immediately brush the baked bread with honey water and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

## Health Advice
Honey bread is rich in carbohydrates and sugar, making it suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. However, those with high blood sugar should consume it in moderation. During baking, pay attention to controlling the oven temperature to avoid a burnt exterior and undercooked interior that may affect the texture.
