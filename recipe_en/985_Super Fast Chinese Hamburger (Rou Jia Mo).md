# Super Fast Chinese Hamburger (Rou Jia Mo)
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍖 Pork knuckle (appropriate amount, pre-braised until tender and falling off the bone)  
🫓 Frozen hand-grabbed dough sheet (1 piece)  
🌿 Cilantro (appropriate amount, chopped)  
🌶️ Red chili peppers (appropriate amount, chopped)  
🤷‍♂️ Liquid seasoning (cooking wine or soy sauce) (small amount, used to season the meat filling, adjust amount according to taste)  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment  
🪵 Wooden cutting board  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🎞️ Plastic wrap  
🍳 Flat-bottomed pan  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Braise the pork knuckle in advance until tender and falling off the bone, to the point where it can be easily shredded by hand.  
2. Take the frozen hand-grabbed dough sheet and peel off the top and bottom plastic protective films.  
**Note**: When removing the film, use plastic wrap to prevent sticking.  
3. Chop the cilantro and red chili peppers separately and set aside.  
4. Shred the braised pork knuckle into small pieces and place them together with the chopped cilantro and red chili peppers on the cutting board.  
5. Use the knife to repeatedly chop and mix all the ingredients until a fine, well-blended filling forms.  
**Key point**: During the chopping process, add a small amount of liquid seasoning such as cooking wine or soy sauce to balance the flavors.  
6. Heat a flat-bottomed pan over medium-low heat, place the hand-grabbed dough sheet in the pan, fry until one side turns golden, then flip. Continue frying until both sides are golden and the dough sheet is puffy and crispy.  
7. Cut the fried dough sheet open from the side, being careful not to cut all the way through.  
8. While hot, stuff the chopped meat filling into the dough sheet, filling it as much as desired.  
9. Once filled, you can wrap the bottom with a plastic bag or greaseproof paper for easy handheld eating.  

## Health Advice  
Pork knuckle is rich in collagen, and pairing it with the hand-grabbed dough sheet provides carbohydrates, making it suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. Red chili peppers can boost metabolism, but be mindful of the sodium content in cooking wine and soy sauce.
