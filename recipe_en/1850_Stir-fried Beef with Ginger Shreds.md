# Stir-fried Beef with Ginger Shreds
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 500g beef tenderloin (sliced thinly against the grain)
🫚 Ginger,适量 (cut into fine shreds, use more than usual for stir-frying)
🥬 Celery,适量 (cut into sections)
🌶 Spiral chili peppers, a few (cut into sections)
🫙 1-2 tablespoons of satay sauce
🧂 Soy sauce, 1 tablespoon

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔥 Gas stove
🍳 Wok (iron/stainless steel)
🥄 Wok spatula or ladle
🥣 Bowl (for mixing sauce)
🍽 Plate (for holding various ingredients)
🥢 Chopsticks (for separating beef)

## Cooking Steps
1. **Preparation**: Finely shred ginger, cut celery and spiral chili peppers into sections; slice beef thinly against the grain; in a small bowl, mix 1-2 tablespoons of satay sauce and 1 tablespoon of soy sauce into a sauce.
   - Note: Use more ginger shreds than usual stir-frying to release sufficient ginger aroma.

2. **Fry ginger shreds until fragrant**: Heat the wok, add an appropriate amount of cooking oil, add all ginger shreds, and stir-fry over medium heat until they turn golden and shiny with slightly burnt edges, becoming crispy, fully releasing ginger oil.
   - Key point: This step aims to extract ginger oil, enhancing the flavor and texture of the beef later.

3. **Add vegetables and beef**: After the ginger shreds are fragrant, first add the spiral chili pepper sections and stir-fry briefly, then immediately add all beef slices.
   - Key point: At this point, ginger oil has separated at the bottom of the wok, so the beef is less likely to stick.

4. **Stir-fry quickly over high heat**: Immediately use a spatula or chopsticks to quickly separate the beef pieces, drizzle a little oil along the edge of the wok to ignite wok hei (breath of the wok), and stir-fry rapidly over maximum heat throughout.
   - Key point: Maintain high heat throughout, cooking the beef in the shortest time to seal in juices.

5. **Add celery and sauce**: When most of the beef has changed color, add celery sections, then immediately pour in the prepared satay sauce and soy sauce mixture, and stir-fry quickly to combine evenly.
   - Key point: Add celery last to maintain its crisp texture.

6. **Serve**: Once all ingredients are evenly coated with the sauce, immediately turn off the heat and serve. The total time from adding beef to serving should be controlled within 25 seconds, keeping the beef tender and about 80-90% cooked, with slight pinkness if desired.
   - Key point: Timing is the soul of this dish; exceeding the time will make the beef tough and dry.

## Health Advice
Stir-fried beef with ginger shreds is rich in high-quality protein and gingerol, with the effects of warming the stomach, dispelling cold, and restoring energy. Slicing beef against the grain and stir-frying quickly for 25 seconds helps lock in juices; ginger shreds are most aromatic when fried until golden and shiny. However, those with a hot stomach should consume in moderation.
