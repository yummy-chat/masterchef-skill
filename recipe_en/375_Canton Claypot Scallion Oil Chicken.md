# Canton Claypot Scallion Oil Chicken
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients
🐔 Young chicken 1 (about 2.5 jin or less, preferably Qingyuan chicken)
🫚 Yellow ginger 适量 (cut into chunks)
🧅 Red shallots 适量 (halved)
🌿 Scallions  large amount (divided into two parts)
⭐ Star anise 2 pieces
🍃 Bay leaf 1 piece
🫚 Ginger slices  a little
🌿 Scallion segments  a little

## Seasonings
🧂 Salt  a little
🍬 Sugar  a little
🧴 Light soy sauce  a little
🍶 Rice wine 适量 (use 1 minute before cooking)
🧈 Chicken fat 适量 (for greasing the pan)
🧴 Soy sauce 适量 (for making sauce)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🍲 Claypot
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Strainer
🔪 Cutting board, knife

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash and drain the young chicken, evenly coat with salt, sugar, and light soy sauce, rub for 2–3 minutes; stuff the chicken cavity with ginger slices, scallion segments, star anise, and bay leaf.
**Key point**: Rice wine must be added 1 minute before cooking, and marinating time should not exceed 5 minutes, otherwise it will affect the meat texture.

2. Heat the wok, add chicken fat, and slowly pan-fry the whole chicken over medium-low heat until each side is evenly golden brown and fragrant, then remove and set aside.
3. Heat the claypot, add a little oil, stir-fry the yellow ginger chunks and red shallots until fragrant, spread a large amount of scallion segments on top, place the pan-fried chicken on the scallions, cover, and cook over medium-low heat without adding a single drop of water.
**Doneness check**: Insert chopsticks into the thickest part of the chicken thigh; if no blood appears and clear juices flow out, it is done.

4. Pour the bottom ingredients and oil from the claypot into the wok, add soy sauce and mix well, strain out the solids with a strainer, leaving only the pure sauce.
5. Briefly dip fresh scallion segments into the hot sauce to release their aroma.
6. Chop the cooked chicken into even pieces, arrange on a plate, pour the hot scallion oil sauce over the top, and serve.

## Health Advice
This scallion oil chicken uses a waterless cooking method, maximizing the retention of the chicken’s tenderness and nutrition. It is an excellent source of high-quality protein and low fat, especially suitable for fitness muscle building or low-fat diets. Note that while red shallots and turmeric can promote blood circulation, the intake of salt and soy sauce should be appropriately controlled.
