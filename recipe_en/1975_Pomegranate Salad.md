# Pomegranate Salad
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn (September-October)

## Ingredients
🍂 1-2 Tunisian soft-seed pomegranates (peeled into pomegranate arils)
🌿 Cilantro, to taste
🌶️ Bird's eye chili peppers, to taste (sliced into rings)

## Seasonings
🌶️ Chili flakes, to taste
🧂 Salt, to taste
🍬 Sugar, to taste
🦪 Oyster sauce, to taste
🍇 Fragrant vinegar, to taste
🤍 White sesame seeds, to taste

## Utensils and Equipment
🔪 Knife
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Spoon
✂️ Scissors

## Steps
1. Use a knife to cut a quadrilateral opening at the top of the pomegranate and lift off the cap.
2. Along the internal white membrane positions, use a knife to make several vertical cuts on the pomegranate skin from top to bottom. Cut following the direction of the membrane, with moderate force to avoid damaging the arils.
3. Hold the pomegranate with both hands and press outward along the cuts to break it into several segments, exposing the arils. Peel the arils off by hand into a bowl.
4. In the bowl with the pomegranate arils, add in order bird's eye chili rings, chili flakes, salt, sugar, oyster sauce, fragrant vinegar, and white sesame seeds.
5. Use a spoon to initially mix the pomegranate arils with the seasonings.
6. Use scissors to cut fresh cilantro into small pieces, sprinkle it over the mixed salad, and finally stir thoroughly to combine.
   **Key point**: Add the cilantro last to preserve its aroma.

## Health Advice
This pomegranate salad is rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, suitable for moistening dryness in autumn. However, control the amount of sugar and oyster sauce; diabetic patients should reduce consumption. Pomegranate seeds are high in dietary fiber, combined with chili and cilantro for a sweet, sour, and spicy appetizing effect.
