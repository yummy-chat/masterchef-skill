# Seaweed Meat Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Pork (lean and fatty mix) appropriate amount  
🍘 Instant seaweed (nori) appropriate amount (if lacking flexibility, stack two sheets)  
🦀 Crab sticks appropriate amount (1 per roll)  
🧂 Light soy sauce appropriate amount  
🧂 Oyster sauce appropriate amount  
🌿 Ground white pepper appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt appropriate amount  
🧊 Ice water or ice cubes appropriate amount (add in 2-3 batches)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Blender  
♨️ Steamer  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
📄 Plastic wrap  
🥢 Tongs  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Place the pork in the freezer for about 2 hours, until semi-frozen and pinchable. Divide into two portions and set aside.  
   **Key Point**: Semi-frozen state is easier to blend to a sticky texture, helping to enhance the tender and bouncy texture of the filling.  
2. Put one portion of pork into the blender, add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, ground white pepper, and salt. Add ice water or ice cubes in 2-3 batches, blending until the filling becomes sticky. Repeat with the other portion.  
   **Notes**: If the amount of meat is large, blend in batches to avoid overloading the machine; adding ice water in batches helps the filling fully reach a sticky consistency.  
3. Lay seaweed on a piece of plastic wrap, spread an appropriate amount of filling evenly over the seaweed, and place one crab stick in the middle.  
4. Use the plastic wrap to help roll the seaweed tightly, forming a meat roll.  
   **Key Point**: Using plastic wrap helps make the roll tighter and less likely to come loose.  
5. Place the rolled meat rolls on the steamer tray, and steam over boiling water for 10-15 minutes.  
6. After steaming, open the lid and use tongs to move each roll individually, then steam for an additional 1 minute with the lid open. This prevents the rolls from sticking to the bottom of the steamer.  
7. Remove the rolls. They can be eaten hot or refrigerated and sliced. Refrigeration makes slicing easier and enhances the firm, bouncy texture.  

## Health Advice  
Seaweed meat rolls are rich in high-quality protein and iodine, suitable for those needing to supplement iron or build muscle. However, crab sticks are high in sodium, so people with high blood pressure should limit their intake. The texture is more Q-bouncy when sliced after refrigeration, but excessive cold consumption should be avoided to prevent gastrointestinal discomfort.
