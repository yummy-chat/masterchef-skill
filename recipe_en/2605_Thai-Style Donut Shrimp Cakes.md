# Thai-Style Donut Shrimp Cakes
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Frozen shrimp 1 bag (peeled and deveined, part minced, part cut into small pieces)
🌿 Cilantro to taste
🌶️ Bird's eye chili to taste
🌱 Lemongrass to taste
🥔 Tapioca starch to taste
🍞 Breadcrumbs to taste
🥚 Egg to taste (use egg white for shrimp paste)
🫒 Vegetable oil to taste (for frying)
🐟 Fish sauce to taste
🤍 White pepper powder to taste
🧂 Salt to taste
🍬 Sugar to taste

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍴 Food processor
✂️ Scissors
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Spoon
🔗 Tongs
🍳 Frying pan

## Preparation Steps
1. After removing frozen shrimp from the bag, peel and devein them. Place most of the shrimp into the food processor, add fish sauce, white pepper powder, salt, sugar, and egg white, then blend into a fine shrimp paste.
   **Note**: Keeping the shrimp cold will result in a bouncier shrimp paste texture.
2. Cut the remaining shrimp into small pieces with scissors, then add them along with chopped cilantro, chopped bird's eye chili, and chopped lemongrass to the shrimp paste. Add tapioca starch and stir vigorously in one direction with chopsticks until the mixture becomes sticky and firm.
   **Key**: Adding shrimp pieces enhances the chewiness of the shrimp cakes; tapioca starch helps bind the shrimp paste.
3. Oil your palms to prevent sticking, take an appropriate amount of shrimp paste, shape it into a round, then hollow out the center to form a donut shape. Evenly coat with a layer of breadcrumbs.
4. Heat the oil in a pan to a moderate temperature, place the shrimp cakes in to fry. Flip them with tongs during frying. When the shrimp cakes float and turn a golden, crispy color, remove and drain the oil.
   **Doneness Check**: The shrimp cakes are cooked through when they float and the color deepens.
5. Arrange on a plate and serve with a side of tomato sauce dip. Eat while hot for a crispy exterior and tender interior.

## Health Advice
Shrimp is high in protein and low in fat, and pairing it with cilantro and lemongrass helps cut the richness and add fragrance, making it suitable for fitness and weight loss periods. Frying results in higher calories, so it is recommended to pair with a refreshing salad for a balanced meal. Be careful to control the oil temperature to prevent the breadcrumbs from absorbing too much oil.
