# Pork Rib and Bitter Melon Soup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍖 Pork ribs  
🥒 Bitter melon  
🦐 Dried shrimp skin  
🧅 Ginger  
🧂 Salt  
🌶️ White pepper  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥘 Soup pot  
🏺 Clay pot  
⚡ Electric pressure cooker  

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the pork ribs and cut them into pieces about 4 cm square. If you don't have a bone chopping tool, ask the vendor to handle it.
2. Pour 700 ml of cold water into a wok, add the ribs, heat to a boil, then turn off the heat. Remove the ribs and drain.
3. Cut the bitter melon in half lengthwise, scoop out the seeds and pith, slice into strips about 0.5 cm thick, wash, and drain.
4. 【Soup Pot Method】
   1. Pour 1100 ml of water into a soup pot, add the blanched ribs, and bring to a boil over high heat.
   2. After the water boils, add the bitter melon strips and dried shrimp skin. Reduce to low heat and simmer for 120 to 240 minutes.
   3. After 120 minutes, taste the ribs. If the meat easily separates from the bone, turn off the heat.
   4. Season with salt and white pepper when serving. Reference ratio: 0.3 to 0.6 grams of salt and 0.1 to 0.5 grams of white pepper per 250 ml of soup.
5. 【Electric Pressure Cooker Method】
   1. Pour 1000 ml of water into the electric pressure cooker, add the ribs, dried shrimp skin, and bitter melon.
   2. Select the soup function and start cooking.
   3. After cooking, season with salt and white pepper according to the above ratio.

## Health Tips  
This pork rib and bitter melon soup combines the high-quality protein of pork ribs with the heat-clearing and detoxifying effects of bitter melon, making it suitable for summer relief from heat or when experiencing internal heat. Dried shrimp skin is rich in calcium. Those with high blood pressure should reduce salt and use white pepper for flavor instead.
