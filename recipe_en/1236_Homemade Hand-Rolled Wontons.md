# Homemade Hand-Rolled Wontons
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Flour 500g
💧 Cold Water 210g
🧂 Salt 3g
🧂 Alkaline Water (Lye Water) as needed
🥚 Egg (optional)
🌾 Wheat Starch as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🪣 Stainless Steel Mixing Bowl
🥖 Rolling Pin
🔪 Knife
🪚 Cutting Board
🍲 Pot
🥄 Slotted Spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. **Knead the Dough**: Add salt, alkaline water, and optional egg to the flour. Gradually add cold water, stirring into floss-like pieces, then press into a ball. The dough should be firm; just bring it together initially.
2. **First Rest**: Place the dough in a bowl and seal it by inverting the bowl over the dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
3. **Smooth and Second Rest**: Remove the dough and knead until the surface is smooth. Form into a ball again, seal by inverting the bowl, and rest for another 30 minutes. Adequate resting relaxes the gluten, making it easier to roll thinly later.
4. **Pre-Rolling Preparation**: Dust the cutting board with wheat starch to prevent sticking.
5. **Initial Pressing**: Use the rolling pin to press evenly around the dough to flatten it initially, making the structure more compact.
6. **Repeat Rolling**:
   - Dust the dough sheet with starch, roll it onto the rolling pin, and press and roll firmly.
   - Unroll, dust with starch again, change direction, and roll again.
   - While rolling, you can gently slap the rolled dough to make it firmer.
   - Repeat until the dough is extremely thin and translucent, allowing you to see objects underneath clearly.
7. **Cutting**: Fold the large dough sheet repeatedly like a fan into a wide strip. Cut into even long strips. Stack these strips together and cut into small square pieces, which are the wonton wrappers.
8. **Wrapping**: Take one wrapper, place filling in the center. Gather the edges of the wrapper toward the middle, then press together in your palm to seal.
9. **Cooking**: Bring water to a boil and add the wontons. Once all wontons float to the surface, they are cooked. Remove with a slotted spoon.

## Health Tips
Homemade wonton wrappers are translucent and chewy, suitable for those needing an energy boost or who enjoy a chewy texture. However, be mindful not to consume too much anti-stick starch. Thorough kneading and resting are key to achieving perfect elasticity. Those with a weaker stomach can pair the wontons with a light broth.
