# Clear Lamb Soup
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦴 Lamb bones (for making the soup base)
🧈 Lamb fat (to enhance aroma and richness)
🍖 Lamb (add in large pieces)
🥕 White radish (sliced, to absorb oil)
🍜 Vermicelli (as needed, placed at the bottom of the bowl)
🧅 Small scallions (chopped, sprinkled to enhance aroma)
🌿 Cilantro (for garnish)
🧂 Salt (added when serving in a bowl)
🫙 Spice pouch (white pepper, cardamom, angelica, dried ginger, cumin, licorice root, placed in a gauze bag)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍲 Large soup pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🔪 Chef's knife
🪵 Cutting board
🍜 Large bowls
🥣 Soup ladle
🧤 Gauze bag (for holding the spices)

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the lamb bones and lamb with clean water, then soak them in clean water for 2-3 hours to fully draw out the blood.
**Key point**: This step effectively removes the gamey smell, making subsequent blanching unnecessary.
2. Bring a generous amount of water in a pot to a boil, then first add the soaked lamb bones and lamb fat. Keep the heat low throughout, and use a slotted spoon to skim off any foam.
**Note**: It is essential to maintain a low heat; high heat will turn the soup milky, while low heat ensures the soup stays clear.
3. After simmering the lamb bone soup on low heat for about 1 hour, add the whole pieces of lamb and continue cooking on low heat.
4. Place the spice pouch into the pot.
**Key point**: Do not cover the pot throughout the entire process, so that the gamey odors can escape with the steam.
5. Keep simmering on low heat for about 3 hours, until the lamb becomes tender.
6. Just before the lamb is done, add the white radish slices and cook until the radish becomes transparent.
**Effect**: The radish absorbs excess oil, making the soup less greasy.
7. Remove the lamb and slice it. Place a layer of vermicelli in the bottom of a bowl, top with lamb slices, and add salt to taste. Ladle in the piping hot lamb and radish soup along with the radish slices. Finally, sprinkle chopped small scallions, pour a ladle of hot soup to release their fragrance, and garnish with cilantro.

## Health Tips
Clear lamb soup is rich in high-quality protein and calcium, making it suitable for winter nourishment. However, lamb fat and lamb meat themselves are not low in calories; it is recommended to pair them with white radish and vermicelli to balance oil intake. People with high blood lipids can reduce the amount of lamb fat and skim off the floating oil when drinking the soup.
