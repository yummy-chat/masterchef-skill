# Spaghetti with Meat Sauce and Shrimp
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍝 Spaghetti, appropriate amount  
🥩 Ground pork, appropriate amount  
🍅 Tomato, 1  
🧅 Onion, appropriate amount  
🥕 Carrot, appropriate amount  
🥬 Celery, appropriate amount  
🧄 Garlic, appropriate amount  
🍤 Shrimp, appropriate amount  
🌭 Sausages, about 4-5  
🧀 Cheese slices, 4 slices  
🥬 Baby bok choy, a handful  

🫒 Olive oil, appropriate amount  
🥫 Tomato paste, 1 packet  
🧂 Light soy sauce, a little  
🧂 Salt, a little  
🧈 Butter, 1 small piece  
🌿 Dried basil, appropriate amount  
🧂 Black pepper, appropriate amount (for final sprinkling)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍲 Stockpot  
🍳 Pan (with lid)  
🍳 Frying pan  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🔪 Cutting board and kitchen knife

## Preparation Steps
1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add spaghetti, and cook for about 10 minutes. Drain well.
2. Secure the tomato with a skewer or chopsticks, roast it over an open flame until the skin is charred and wrinkled. Remove the skin and cut into small pieces.
3. Finely dice the onion, carrot, celery, and garlic.
4. Make a few cuts on the surface of the sausages. Use the boiling water from the pasta to quickly blanch the baby bok choy, then remove and set aside.
5. Heat a pan, add olive oil, and stir-fry the ground pork until it changes color. Add the tomato pieces and all the diced vegetables, and stir-fry until fragrant.  
   **Note**: The vegetables must be fully softened to release their sweetness.
6. Squeeze in one packet of tomato paste and stir well. Add an appropriate amount of water, season with a little light soy sauce and salt, stir, then reduce to medium-low heat and simmer until the sauce thickens.
7. In another frying pan, add oil and cook the shrimp and scored sausages until fully cooked and golden brown on the surface. Set aside.
8. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the meat sauce and toss well. Place a small piece of butter in the center.
9. Arrange the cooked shrimp and sausages on top of the spaghetti, then evenly place the four cheese slices.
10. Cover the pan and simmer over low heat or use residual heat until the cheese is completely melted.
11. Turn off the heat, remove the lid, sprinkle with dried basil and a little black pepper, and serve with the blanched baby bok choy.

## Health Advice
This dish is rich in protein and lycopene, making it suitable for muscle building or post-workout energy replenishment. Pay attention to the amount of sausages and cheese to avoid excess calories. For those with gout, reduce the amount of shrimp and drink plenty of water.
