# Mantou Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 2 Mantou (medium-sized, round)
🥚 4 Eggs
🧅 Scallions,适量 (chopped into green onion pieces)
🦐 Krill meat,适量
🍶 A few drops of white vinegar
🌽 Two small spoonfuls of cornstarch water
🧂 3 grams of salt
🌿 A pinch of thirteen spices

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok/cooking pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Large stainless steel bowl
🥣 Small stainless steel bowl
🔥 Gas stove
🍴 Spatula
🧤 Disposable gloves
🍽️ Plate

## Steps
1. **Blanch Mantou**: Place the mantou in a pot of boiling water. Once the water returns to a boil, continue cooking for 2 minutes, then remove.
   **Key point**: Control the blanching time to 2 minutes to avoid the mantou becoming overly soft.
2. **Cool and Squeeze Out Water**: Let the blanched mantou cool naturally, then use your hands to squeeze out excess moisture.
3. **Make Mantou Crumbs**: Place the squeezed mantou into a large bowl, and use your hands to break it into fine crumbs.
   **Key point**: After blanching, the mantou becomes soft and easier to crumble; absorbing excess moisture makes the final product fluffy without being dry or hard.
4. **Prepare Egg Mixture**: In a small bowl, crack the eggs, add white vinegar and cornstarch water, and beat well with chopsticks until fully combined.
   **Key point**: White vinegar removes the eggy smell, while the cornstarch water makes the texture smoother and more tender.
5. **Mix All Ingredients**: Pour the egg mixture into the mantou crumbs, add the scallions, krill meat, salt, and thirteen spices, and stir evenly to form a batter.
6. **Pan-fry**: Pour oil into the pan. Once the oil is hot, use a spoon to scoop the batter into the pan and shape it into a round pancake. Keep the heat on the lowest setting throughout.
   **Key point**: Must use low heat; high heat can cause the outside to burn while the inside remains raw.
7. **Flip and Cook Through**: Once one side is golden brown and set, use a spatula to flip the pancake, and continue cooking the other side over low heat.
   **Judgment standard**: Both sides should be golden brown, and the center of the pancake should puff up, indicating it is fully cooked inside.
8. **Remove and Plate**: Take the cooked mantou pancake out of the pan and place it on a plate to serve.

## Health Tips
This mantou pancake uses leftover mantou as the breakfast star, rich in carbohydrates and protein, making it suitable for a quick energy boost. Krill meat provides high-quality protein and minerals, but control the amount of oil when pan-frying to avoid excessive calories. It is recommended to pair with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. For those with poor digestion, the mantou crumbs can be crushed more finely.
