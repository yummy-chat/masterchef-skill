# Sprite Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍜 Non-fried instant noodle cake, as needed  
🥓 Ham sausage, as needed (shredded)  
🥒 Cucumber, as needed (shredded)  
🌿 Small cilantro, as needed (cut into sections)  
🧄 Minced garlic, as needed  
🌶️ Small chili peppers, as needed (cut into rings)  
🧅 Small scallions, as needed (chopped)  
🥢 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🍶 Rice vinegar, as needed  
🦪 Oyster sauce, as needed  
🪔 Sesame oil, as needed  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, as needed  
🫙 Lao Gan Ma Fermented Bean Curd with Chili Oil, as needed  
🥤 Sprite, about half a can (**key seasoning**)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl (for mixing the sauce)  
🥢 Chopsticks (for stirring and picking up noodles)  
🥄 Slotted spoon (for scooping noodles and draining water)  
🍽️ Plate (for serving the finished dish)  

## Cooking Steps
1.  Prepare the sauce: In a glass bowl, add minced garlic, small chili pepper rings, and chopped small scallions. Add light soy sauce, rice vinegar, oyster sauce, sesame oil, dark soy sauce, and Lao Gan Ma. Stir initially to combine. Finally, pour in about half a can of Sprite and gently mix to create the flavored sauce.
2.  Cook the noodles: Bring water to a boil in a pot. Add the instant noodle cake and cook until tender.
3.  Cool in cold water: Remove the cooked noodles and immediately place them in cold water to cool. Once the noodles are fully cool and have a smooth, springy texture, drain them.  
    **Key Point**: Cooling the noodles in cold water makes them chewier; do not skip this step.
4.  Plate: Place the drained noodles on a plate. Layer with shredded ham sausage, shredded cucumber, and cilantro sections in order.
5.  Pour sauce and mix: Evenly drizzle the prepared sauce over the ingredients. Mix thoroughly just before serving.

## Health Suggestions  
Sprite Noodles have moderate calories and are suitable as a quick cold noodle dish in summer. Cucumber and cilantro provide vitamins, but it is recommended to reduce the amount of ham sausage. Note that Sprite contains sugar, so intake should be monitored.
