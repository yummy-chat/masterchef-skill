# Shredded King Oyster Mushroom with Shredded Pork
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍄 4-5 king oyster mushrooms (medium-sized, cut into thin shreds)
🥩 1 bowl of shredded pork (pre-cut)
🥕 Half a carrot (shredded)
🌿 1 small bunch of chives (cut into sections)
🧄 2-3 cloves of minced garlic
🧂 Salt, to taste (used in 3 separate times)
🧴 Light soy sauce, to taste (used in 2 separate times)
🌿 A pinch of white pepper
🥚 Half an egg white
🌽 Cornstarch, to taste
🫒 Soybean oil, to taste (used in 3 separate times)

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife
🪵 Cutting board
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted spoon
🍽 Bowls and plates

## Cooking Steps
1. **Prepare the ingredients**
   Slice the king oyster mushrooms, then cut into thin shreds. Shred the carrot. Cut the chives into sections. Mince the garlic and set aside.
   **Key point**: All ingredients should be shredded to a uniform thickness to ensure even cooking.

2. **Marinate the shredded pork**
   Add salt to the shredded pork, and mix with your hands until the surface becomes sticky and tacky.
   Add light soy sauce and white pepper, and mix to incorporate the flavors.
   Add half an egg white and mix well, which will make the pork more tender and give it a whiter color.
   Add cornstarch and mix to coat, sealing in moisture.
   Finally, drizzle in soybean oil and mix well to prevent the shredded pork from sticking together when cooking.
   **Key point**: The marinating order is salt → light soy sauce and white pepper → egg white → cornstarch → oil. Mix thoroughly at each step. The secret to tender shredded pork lies in the coating of egg white and cornstarch.

3. **Blanch the vegetables**
   Bring a pot of water to a boil, add a small amount of cooking oil and salt.
   Once boiling, first blanch the shredded king oyster mushrooms for about 1 minute, then add the shredded carrot and blanch briefly, and finally add the chive sections for a quick blanch. Drain all three vegetables together.
   **Key point**: Blanching removes any off-flavor from the mushrooms and slightly cooks the carrot and chives, preventing them from being overcooked during the final stir-fry.

4. **Quick-fry the shredded pork**
   Heat the wok, add a small amount of oil, and when it reaches about 30% heat, add the marinated shredded pork.
   Gently separate the shreds with a spatula, and stir-fry until the surface of the pork is completely white. Immediately remove and drain the oil.
   **Key point**: Quick-frying at low heat ensures the pork stays tender. High oil temperature will make the meat tough.

5. **Stir-fry the vegetables**
   Leave a little oil in the wok, add the minced garlic and stir until fragrant.
   Add all the blanched vegetable shreds and stir-fry quickly over high heat.
   Add a suitable amount of salt and light soy sauce for seasoning, and continue stir-frying over high heat until the seasoning is evenly distributed.

6. **Combine and serve**
   Return the quick-fried shredded pork to the wok, and quickly stir-fry everything together until well combined. Turn off the heat and serve immediately.
   **Key point**: Add the shredded pork last and mix it in quickly to avoid overcooking and making the meat tough.

## Health Tips
This dish is high in protein and low in fat. King oyster mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber, making it suitable for weight loss or fitness diets. Note that a fair amount of salt is already used in marinating the pork, so adjust the final seasoning accordingly.
