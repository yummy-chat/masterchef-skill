# Mashed Potato and Beef Hot Pot Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes, as needed (peeled and cut into large chunks)
🥩 Beef hot pot rolls, as needed
🥕 Carrot, as needed (finely diced)
🧅 Onion, as needed (finely diced)
🫚 Ginger, as needed (minced)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (minced)
🍚 Rice, as needed (pre-cooked)
🌿 Scallions, as needed (chopped)
🫘 White sesame seeds, as needed (for garnish)
🍶 Shaoxing wine, as needed
🧂 Light soy sauce, as needed
🦪 Oyster sauce, as needed
🧂 Matsutake seasoning, as needed
🌶️ Freshly ground black pepper, as needed
💧 Water, as needed
🧂 Dark soy sauce, as needed (for coloring)
🧂 Salt, as needed

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Pot
🍽️ Plate
🔪 Cutting board, knife
🥢 Chopsticks, spoon
🍳 Lid

## Preparation Steps
1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into large chunks. Steam them until completely soft and tender, until they can be easily mashed with your fingers.
2. Bring water to a gentle boil in a pot. Quickly blanch the beef hot pot rolls to remove blood foam and any gamey smell. Drain and set aside.
3. Heat a small amount of oil in a pot. Add the diced carrots and onions, and stir-fry quickly until just cooked.
4. Add the minced ginger and garlic, and stir-fry until fragrant.
5. Pour the steamed mashed potatoes into the pot. At this point, the mashed potatoes should be in a sandy and powdery consistency.
6. Pour the Shaoxing wine along the edge of the pot, then add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, matsutake seasoning, freshly ground black pepper, some water, dark soy sauce, and salt. Stir until well combined.
   **Note**: Shaoxing wine must be poured along the edge of the pot to release its aroma; black pepper should be freshly ground to release its flavor.
7. Lay the blanched beef slices over the mashed potato mixture. Gently press them down with a spoon so they absorb the sauce.
8. Spread a layer of white rice evenly in the pot. Sprinkle with chopped scallions and white sesame seeds.
9. Cover the pot with a lid, turn to medium heat, and let it steam for 1 minute and 30 seconds.
10. Remove the lid, mix everything well while hot, and serve.

## Health Tips
This mashed potato and beef hot pot rice bowl is a perfect combination of carbohydrates and protein, ideal for quick energy replenishment after exercise. However, mashed potatoes have a relatively high glycemic index, so people with diabetes or those managing blood sugar levels are advised to pair it with ample vegetables. The beef should not be cooked for too long after blanching, or it will become tough.
