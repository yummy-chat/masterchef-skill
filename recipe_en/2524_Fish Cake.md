# Fish Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 Grass carp meat, about 430g (net meat after removing head, bones, and skin from a whole fish weighing about 2.5 jin)
🥚 Eggs, 2 (about 80g)
🌾 Tapioca starch, 100g (can substitute with sweet potato starch)
🫚 Ginger, 8g
🧅 Scallion segments, to taste
🧂 Salt, a little over half a spoon
🍬 White sugar, half a spoon
🌿 White pepper powder, a little under half a spoon
💧 Water, no more than 20ml (add depending on the dryness of the fish paste)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥣 Glass bowl
🌀 Blender
🥢 Chopsticks
🍶 Glass mold
🛡️ Plastic wrap
🦷 Toothpicks
♨️ Steamer

## Preparation Steps
1. Remove the head of the grass carp, discard the backbone and large intermuscular bones, fillet the net fish meat, and remove the fish skin.
   **Note**: The fish skin must be removed, otherwise it will affect the color and texture of the finished product.
2. Place the fish meat in a bowl, add ginger slices, salt, white sugar, eggs, and white pepper powder, and mix well.
3. Pour the mixed ingredients into a blender and blend into a fine fish paste. If the texture is too dry, add a small amount of water; if no blender is available, chop repeatedly by hand into a paste.
4. Return the fish paste to the bowl, add tapioca starch in batches, and stir in the same direction consistently.
   **Key**: Stir for at least 3 minutes to fully work the fish paste, making the finished product more elastic.
5. Brush a thin layer of oil on the inner wall of the glass mold, fill the mold with about 2/3 of the fish paste, press firmly, and smooth the surface.
   **Key**: Pressing firmly makes the finished product dense, giving it better resilience when sliced.
6. Cover the mold surface with plastic wrap and poke several small holes with a toothpick for ventilation.
7. Bring water to a boil in a steamer, place the mold in, and steam over medium heat for 30 minutes until fully cooked, then remove.
8. After the fish cake has completely cooled, unmold it. It can then be sliced for consumption or further processed. The finished product is springy and firm, with a rich fish aroma.

## Health Advice
Fish cake is high in protein and low in fat, rich in DHA and EPA, making it suitable for children, the elderly, and those on a weight-loss diet. Tapioca starch gives it a springy texture, but be mindful to control the amount of salt used; hypertensive patients can reduce it by half.
