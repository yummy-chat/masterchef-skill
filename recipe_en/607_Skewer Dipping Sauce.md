# Skewer Dipping Sauce
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

---

🍗 **Ingredients**  
- Dried chili flakes 60g  
- Cumin powder 20g  
- White pepper powder 10g  
- Five-spice powder 15g  
- Salt 20g  
- Sichuan pepper powder 15g  
- Chicken bouillon powder 8g  
- Thirteen-spice blend 5g  
- Spicy seasoning mix 5g  
- White sesame seeds 30g  

🧂 **Seasonings**  
- Sesame oil 10ml  
- Light soy sauce 10ml  
- Sichuan pepper oil 10ml  
- Oyster sauce 10ml  
- Hot oil, as needed (enough to submerge all dry ingredients)  

🔧 **Utensils**  
- Heat-resistant container  
- Pot  
- Stirring utensil  

---

📝 **Preparation Steps**  
1. Take a heat-resistant container. Add all the dried chili flakes, cumin powder, white pepper powder, five-spice powder, salt, Sichuan pepper powder, chicken bouillon powder, thirteen-spice blend, spicy seasoning mix, and white sesame seeds. Mix them thoroughly.  
2. Pour cooking oil into a pot and heat until it reaches about 70% of its smoking point (approx. 210°C / 410°F). The amount of oil should be enough to submerge all the dry ingredients in the container.  
3. Slowly pour the hot oil into the dry ingredients in three separate additions, each about one-third of the total. Stir vigorously and continuously while pouring to ensure the seasonings are heated evenly and prevent local scorching.  
4. After adding all the oil and mixing well, sequentially add the sesame oil, light soy sauce, Sichuan pepper oil, and oyster sauce. Stir thoroughly again until fully combined.  

⚠️ **Note**  
- The hot oil must be added in batches with continuous stirring, otherwise, the seasonings may burn or mix unevenly.  
- The amount of seasonings in the final step can be adjusted according to personal taste.  
- Ingredients can be scaled down proportionally, keeping the ratios consistent.

## Health Advice
This sauce is relatively high in sodium. Patients with high blood pressure should carefully control their intake. It is recommended to serve with a large amount of vegetable skewers to balance the meal.
