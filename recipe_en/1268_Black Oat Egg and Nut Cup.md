# Black Oat Egg and Nut Cup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥣 Black oats, to taste
🥚 1 egg
🥜 Mixed nuts, to taste (cashews, almonds, peanuts, etc.; plain nuts recommended)
🥛 Milk, to taste (chilled milk recommended)
(No additional seasonings required)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥤 Microwave-safe cup (with a vented lid)
🥄 Spoon
♨️ Microwave oven
🫙 Sealed jar (for storing oats and nuts)

## Preparation Steps
1. Place an appropriate amount of black oats into the microwave-safe cup, then crack one egg into it.
2. Slowly add water, just enough to cover some of the oats, not too much to prevent splattering during heating.
   **Note**: For the first attempt, do not replace water with milk, as it may affect the heating effect.
3. Close the lid tightly, open the vent, place it in the microwave, and heat on medium-high heat for about 2 minutes, until the egg white is set and the yolk is runny or semi-solid.
   **Key**: Do not heat for too long, to avoid the egg bursting or the oats becoming too dry.
4. Remove and immediately tighten the lid, letting it steam with residual heat until the egg reaches the desired doneness.
   **Key**: This is a crucial anti-burst step, using steam to cook the egg through.
5. Open the lid, add the mixed nuts, then pour in the chilled milk.
6. Stir all ingredients evenly with a spoon and serve.

## Health Tips
This Black Oat Egg and Nut Cup is rich in protein, dietary fiber, and healthy fats, making it suitable as a quick breakfast for fitness enthusiasts or those managing blood sugar. Nuts are high in calories, so it is recommended to keep the intake within 30 grams.
