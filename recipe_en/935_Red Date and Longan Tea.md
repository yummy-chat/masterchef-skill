# Red Date and Longan Tea
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫒 Red dates 50g (pitted)
🟤 Longan 70g (shelled)
🔴 Goji berries 10g (washed)
💧 Drinking water 2kg
🍬 Rock sugar 50g
🟫 Brown sugar 30g

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🪵 Wooden cutting board
🔪 Small knife
🔪 Large knife
🫖 Clay pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🍶 Glass teapot
🥛 Glass cup
🔥 Stove

## Cooking Steps
1. Shell the dried longan and remove the flesh; wash the red dates, cut them in half with a small knife, and remove the pits; wash the goji berries and set aside.
   **Key point**: Pitted red dates and shelled longan meat can be used directly to simplify the preparation steps.
2. Place the prepared red dates, longan meat, and goji berries into the clay pot, pour in water, and bring to a boil over high heat.
   **Key point**: After the tea comes to a boil, skim off any foam from the surface to ensure a clearer broth.
3. After skimming the foam, cover the pot, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for about 20 minutes until the tea color deepens and the flavors of the ingredients are fully released.
   **Note**: Maintain a low simmer to prevent rapid water evaporation.
4. Add rock sugar and brown sugar to the simmered tea, stir until well combined, and continue cooking for 5 minutes until the sugar is fully dissolved.
   **Key point**: The amount of sugar can be adjusted according to personal taste.
5. Pour the cooked red date and longan tea into the glass teapot. It can be paired with cookies or pineapple cakes. The flavor is best when consumed at a suitable temperature.
   **Note**: Be careful of burns when removing from the pot; it is most mellow when warm.

## Health Advice
Red date and longan tea warms the body and replenishes blood, making it suitable for people who feel cold in winter or have insufficient blood and qi. However, diabetic patients should control the amount of rock sugar and brown sugar. It is recommended to drink it warm and avoid consuming large amounts on an empty stomach to prevent上火 (internal heat).
