# Steamed Rice with Seasonal Vegetables and Shrimp
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Cooked rice, as needed  
🥓 Marinated meat slices, 3-6 slices  
🍖 Ham slice, 1 slice  
🥒 Sea cucumber, 3-4 stalks  
🐾 Pork bone soup jelly, 1 piece  
🦐 Shrimp paste, 1 log  
🌿 Okra, 2 segments  
🥚 Egg, 1 piece  
🫕 Yellow seasoning sauce, as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🍽️ White porcelain bowl  
🔪 Vegetable peeler  
🍲 Steamer with steaming rack

## Preparation Steps
1. Take a suitably sized white porcelain bowl and fill it with pre-steamed rice.  
   **Note**: Adjust the amount of rice based on appetite; it should be completely cooled or slightly warm before use to avoid premature mixing with the raw egg.

2. Lay the marinated meat slices over the rice. For a child’s portion, you may add an extra slice of ham for enhanced flavor.

3. Squeeze an appropriate amount of yellow seasoning sauce into the bowl and gently mix it with the rice.

4. Peel the sea cucumbers, cut them into strips, and arrange them over the rice.  
   **Key Point**: Peeling makes the sea cucumber softer and easier for children to chew.

5. Place the cat-paw-shaped pork bone soup jelly in the center of the ingredients; do not break it apart.

6. Squeeze the shrimp paste into a log shape and add it to the bowl. Cut the okra into segments and evenly distribute them.

7. Crack a raw egg directly into the bowl without stirring. This will form a soft egg layer after steaming.

8. Carefully place the bowl on the steaming rack of the steamer, ensuring the base is stable.

9. Cover with a lid, heat, and continue steaming once steam rises until all ingredients are fully cooked.  
   **Judgment Criteria**: The shrimp paste turns from translucent to pinkish-white, the egg white is fully set, the okra becomes soft, and the soup jelly completely melts into broth.  
   **Note**: Steaming time is flexible, based on color change and setting of ingredients, typically about 10-15 minutes. For children’s portions, let it cool slightly before serving.

## Health Advice
This steamed rice dish is rich in ingredients and suitable for toddlers over 1 year old transitioning to family meals. The pork bone soup jelly and marinated meat provide calcium and iron, while the peeled sea cucumber strips are soft and easy to chew. Ensure that the okra and shrimp paste are fully cooked. Steaming a whole egg retains a tender texture, but it is recommended to pierce the yolk to prevent choking.
