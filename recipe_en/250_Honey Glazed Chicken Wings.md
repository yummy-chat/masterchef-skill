# Honey Glazed Chicken Wings  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 9 chicken wings  
🍅 Cherry tomatoes (for garnish)  
🥬 Lettuce (for lining the plate)  
🫚 Ginger (some sliced for marinade, reserve one piece for brushing honey)  
🧄 Garlic (sliced)  
🌶️ Ground pepper (to taste)  
🥢 Light soy sauce (to taste)  
🍶 Cooking wine (to taste)  
🧂 Salt (to taste)  
🥄 Dark soy sauce (to taste)  
🍯 Honey (to taste)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef’s knife  
🪚 Cutting board  
🪥 Toothpicks  
🥘 Baking tray  
🧻 Aluminum foil  
🔥 Oven  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Instructions  
1. Wash the chicken wings thoroughly. Use a toothpick to poke several small holes on both sides, then gently tap with the back of a knife to tenderize the meat and allow it to absorb flavors more easily.  
2. Place the chicken wings in a container. Add sliced ginger, sliced garlic, ground pepper, light soy sauce, cooking wine, salt, and dark soy sauce. Rub the mixture evenly into each wing with your hands. Marinate for 3 hours.  
3. Preheat the oven for 5 minutes. Line a baking tray with aluminum foil, arrange the marinated chicken wings on the tray, and bake in the oven at 190°C (375°F) for 10 minutes.  
4. Remove the tray. Take one piece of ginger, use its cut side to dip into honey, and brush it evenly over each chicken wing.  
5. Return the chicken wings to the oven and continue baking at 190°C (375°F) for another 10 minutes, until the surface turns glossy red-brown with slightly charred edges.  
6. Cut the cherry tomatoes at a 45-degree angle, slice the lower half lengthwise to form heart shapes, and secure with toothpicks. Line a plate with lettuce leaves, arrange the baked chicken wings, and garnish with the heart-shaped cherry tomatoes.  

## Health Tips  
Poking, tapping, and marinating the chicken wings helps them absorb flavor while reducing odor, and is a good way to control salt and sugar. Use honey in moderation to avoid excessive sweetness. This dish is suitable for satisfying cravings while staying fit or for gathering with friends, but be careful not to set the oven temperature too high to prevent burning the outside while leaving the inside raw.
