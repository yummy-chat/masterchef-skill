# Baby Original Beef Ribs  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Bone-in beef ribs, about 3 pieces  
🍎 Apple, 1 piece  
🫒 Dried hawthorn, 4–5 pieces  
🥜 Walnuts, 3–4 pieces  
🫚 Ginger slices, 2–3 slices  
🍄 Dried shiitake mushrooms, 2 pieces (pre-soaked)  
🧅 Scallion, 1 piece  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Frying pan  
♨️ Electric pressure cooker  
🌀 Handheld immersion blender  
🧸 Silicone cartoon-shaped molds  
🛍️ Sealed storage bag  
🍽️ Serving plate  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Thaw the bone-in beef ribs in advance; wash the apple; pre-soak the dried shiitake mushrooms; slice the ginger; wash the scallion.  
2. Place the beef ribs in a pot with cold water, add ginger slices and scallion, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat. Skim off the foam thoroughly and blanch for about 3–5 minutes until the surface changes color and no blood seeps out. Remove and rinse with warm water.  
   **Note**: Starting with cold water helps remove blood more thoroughly; rinsing with warm water prevents the meat from contracting and becoming tough due to sudden temperature change.  
3. Place the blanched beef ribs, apple, walnuts, hawthorn, soaked shiitake mushrooms, and ginger slices into the electric pressure cooker. Add a small amount of water, select the "Beef/Lamb Stew" setting, and cook until the program ends and pressure is fully released. The beef ribs should be tender and falling off the bone.  
4. Take part of the cooked beef rib meat, some of the accompanying ingredients, and an appropriate amount of broth. Place them in a blending cup and use a handheld immersion blender to puree into a smooth, fine paste with no lumps.  
5. Transfer the meat puree into a piping bag, squeeze it into silicone cartoon-shaped molds, tap lightly to release air bubbles, then freeze until fully set. Transfer to a sealed storage bag for frozen storage, using as needed.  
6. Serve the whole cooked beef ribs directly; the meat is tender and suitable for children to hold and gnaw on.  

## Health Tips  
Stewing beef ribs with apple and hawthorn creates a tangy-sweet, appetizing flavor that aids digestion. Walnuts and shiitake mushrooms increase nutritional density, making this especially suitable for babies starting solids to practice chewing. Divide the frozen puree into small portions for more hygienic use each time; older babies can directly gnaw on the stewed beef ribs to supplement protein.
