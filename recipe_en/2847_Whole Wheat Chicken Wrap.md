# Whole Wheat Chicken Wrap
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Year-round Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 3 chicken thighs (boned and skinned)
🌾 200g whole wheat flour
🥚 2 eggs (1 for batter, 1 hard-boiled)
💧 400g water
🥒 1 cucumber (part diced, part sliced)
🥕 Half a carrot (peeled and cut into strips)
🥬 Lettuce, as needed
🍎 1 apple (post-meal fruit, cut into chunks)
🌿 Endive, as needed (garnish)
🫙 Sourdough starter (optional)
🧂 2g salt (for batter) + salt to taste (for marinating chicken)
🍾 Cooking wine, as needed
🌶️ Chili powder, as needed
🧂 Cumin powder, as needed
🌸 Sichuan peppercorn powder, as needed
🫒 Olive oil, as needed (for greasing the pan)
🥛 Yogurt, as needed (for sauce)
🍋 Lemon juice, as needed
🍷 Black vinegar, as needed
🌱 Mint leaves, as needed (optional)
🧄 Minced garlic, as needed (optional)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
✂️ Kitchen shears
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥣 Whisk/hand mixer
♨️ Air fryer
🍲 Saucepan
🔪 Cutting board and knife
📦 Aluminum foil/baking tray
🥄 Ladle or silicone spatula
🧻 Paper towels

## Instructions
1.  Use kitchen shears to remove the bones and skin from the chicken thighs to obtain clean chicken meat.
2.  Place the chicken meat in a dish. Add cooking wine, cumin powder, Sichuan peppercorn powder, chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well with your hands and marinate until flavorful.
3.  In a bowl, combine whole wheat flour, water, 1 egg, 2g salt, and sourdough starter (if using). Whisk until smooth and free of lumps.
    *Note*: Adding egg and sourdough starter improves the elasticity of the wrap, but will impart a slightly tangy flavor. The batter must be thoroughly mixed.
4.  Dice part of the cucumber, slice the remaining part. Peel the carrot and cut into strips. Wash the lettuce and drain well.
5.  Mix the diced cucumber with yogurt. Squeeze in some lemon juice. Add mint leaves and minced garlic to taste, if desired, and stir to combine. Set aside.
    *Key point*: This sauce is refreshing and juicy, balancing the overall texture.
6.  Place the egg in a saucepan with cold water, bring to a boil, then continue boiling until hard-boiled. Just before the egg is done, blanch the carrot strips in the boiling water briefly, then remove.
    *Note*: Do not blanch the carrots for too long to maintain a crisp-tender texture.
7.  Preheat a non-stick pan over low heat. Use a paper towel to spread a thin layer of olive oil on the pan. Ladle in an appropriate amount of batter and quickly rotate the pan to spread it into a round shape.
    *Heat control*: Keep the heat low and cook slowly. Once the surface of the wrap is set and the edges lift, flip it over. Cook until both sides are done. Using high heat will prevent the wrap from bubbling but will shorten the cooking time.
8.  Wrap the marinated chicken thighs in aluminum foil. Place in an air fryer and cook at 180°C until fully cooked.
9.  Take one wrap. Layer lettuce, the cooked chicken thigh, and the blanched carrot strips in the center. Drizzle with the cucumber yogurt sauce. Fold the two sides of the wrap inward, roll it up tightly, and secure the bottom with aluminum foil.
    *Note*: Do not overfill the wrap, or it will be difficult to roll. Wrapping the bottom with foil makes the wrap easier to hold and prevents it from falling apart.
10. Place the wrap on a plate. Arrange the halved hard-boiled egg, apple chunks, and a small handful of endive on the side. Drizzle a little black vinegar over the endive.

## Health Advice
The whole wheat wrap paired with grilled chicken thighs and vegetables is low in fat and high in protein, suitable for weight loss and muscle gain. The cucumber yogurt sauce can replace high-calorie salad dressings, offering a refreshing and healthy alternative.
