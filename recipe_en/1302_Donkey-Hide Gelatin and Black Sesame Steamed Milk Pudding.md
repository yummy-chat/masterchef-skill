# Donkey-Hide Gelatin and Black Sesame Steamed Milk Pudding
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
💧 Water 220g
🍬 Rock sugar 20g
🖤 Black sesame seeds 30g
🍚 Rice 20g
🥚 Egg whites 2
🥛 Milk 135g
🥫 Sweetened condensed milk, as needed
🍫 Instant donkey-hide gelatin powder 1 packet (used in two portions)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥛 Soy milk maker
🍲 Steamer
🥣 Measuring cup
🧺 Strainer
🥣 Bowl
📦 Plastic wrap
⚖️ Kitchen scale

## Steps
1. Accurately weigh all ingredients according to the recipe, reserving a portion of the instant donkey-hide gelatin powder for final use.
2. Pour the black sesame seeds, rock sugar, rice, and water into the soy milk maker, start the rice paste mode, and blend until a smooth and fine black sesame paste forms.
3. In a measuring cup, dissolve about half a packet of donkey-hide gelatin powder in the milk, stir until well combined, then add the egg whites and sweetened condensed milk, continuing to stir until fully incorporated.
4. Strain the mixed egg-milk liquid through a strainer into another container to remove impurities such as eggshell bits, ensuring a smooth texture for the steamed milk pudding.
5. Pour the prepared black sesame paste into a bowl and let it sit for about 10 minutes, until a layer of oil forms on the surface.
6. Slowly pour the strained egg-milk liquid along the side of the bowl onto the surface of the black sesame paste, creating clear layers.
   **Note**: Be sure to keep the oil layer intact to avoid mixing the two liquids.
7. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, place it in the steamer, and steam over high heat for 15 minutes.
8. After steaming, turn off the heat and let it rest for another 5 minutes before removing, allowing the steamed milk pudding to fully set.
9. Remove and sprinkle the reserved instant donkey-hide gelatin powder on top, forming a crispy, fragrant layer on the surface.

## Health Advice
This donkey-hide gelatin and black sesame steamed milk pudding combines blood-nourishing, hair-improving, calming, and beauty-enhancing benefits, making it especially suitable for those with cold hands and feet in winter or those hoping to improve hair quality. However, due to the combination of donkey-hide gelatin and sugar, individuals with diabetes or damp-heat constitution should consume it in moderation.
