# Electric Rice Cooker Braised Chicken
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 1 three-yellow chicken (or young chicken)
🧅 About half an onion (cut into chunks)
🌿 Appropriate amount of scallions (some cut into sections for marinating, some shredded for garnish)
🫚 About 5-6 slices of ginger
🧄 Appropriate amount of garlic (for lining the bottom)
🌶️ About 5-6 dried chili peppers
🫘 About 1 small handful of Sichuan peppercorns
🍬 About 1 small handful of rock sugar
🫙 1 piece of fermented bean curd
🫙 About 1 tablespoon of soybean paste
🦪 About 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
🌿 About 1 teaspoon of ground white pepper
🧂 About 2 tablespoons of light soy sauce
🧂 About 1 tablespoon of dark soy sauce
🌿 About 1 teaspoon of five-spice powder
🧂 5 grams of salt
🍶 About 1 tablespoon of Sichuan peppercorn wine (or Hua Diao wine)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
♨️ Electric rice cooker
🧤 Disposable gloves
🦷 Toothpicks
🥣 Stainless steel bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the three-yellow chicken into the inner pot of the electric rice cooker. Add one piece of fermented bean curd and rub it evenly over the chicken with your hands to coat.
2. Add soybean paste, oyster sauce, white pepper, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, five-spice powder, salt, onion chunks, scallion sections, ginger slices, dried chili peppers, Sichuan peppercorns, and rock sugar, then drizzle with Sichuan peppercorn wine.
3. Use your hands to thoroughly rub and mix all the seasonings with the chicken, squeezing the scallions and ginger to release their juices, ensuring the chicken is evenly coated with the marinade. Cover the pot and marinate for 30 minutes.
4. After marinating, stuff all the solid spices from the pot into the chicken's cavity.
5. Line the bottom of the rice cooker's inner pot with scallion sections, ginger slices, and garlic cloves to prevent burning during cooking.
6. Tuck the chicken feet into the cavity, seal the opening with toothpicks, and then secure the chicken wings behind the chicken's head.
7. Place the chicken breast-side up in the pot, close the lid, and select the "Wood Fire Rice" or "Cook Rice" function. Do not add water throughout the process.
8. After the program finishes, insert a chopstick into the thickest part of the chicken thigh. If no blood seeps out, it is fully cooked. For more flavor, unplug the pot and let it continue steaming for 30 minutes.
9. Remove the whole chicken and place it on a serving plate. Pour the remaining juices from the pot over the chicken, sprinkle with shredded scallions, and serve.

## Health Tips
This electric rice cooker braised chicken is marinated with fermented bean curd and various sauces, offering a savory and rich flavor. It is suitable as a low-fat, high-protein meal. Pairing it with whole grains or vegetables makes for a more balanced diet. Note that fermented bean curd and soy sauce have a high salt content, so people with high blood pressure should reduce the amount used.
