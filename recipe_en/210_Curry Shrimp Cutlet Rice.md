# Curry Shrimp Cutlet Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 Frozen shrimp cutlets, as needed (pre-packaged convenience item)  
🌾 Cornstarch, as needed  
🥚 Egg wash, as needed  
🍞 Panko breadcrumbs (yellow), as needed  
🍚 White rice, as needed  
🧅 Purple onion, about half (diced)  
🥕 Carrot, about 1 (cut into rolling chunks)  
🥔 Potato, about 1 (cut into rolling chunks)  
🖤 Black sesame seeds, a pinch (for garnish)  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for spraying)  
🍛 Curry roux, 2 large cubes  
💧 Water, as needed  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer  
🍚 Rice cooker (with stir-fry function)  
🍽️ Shallow bowl/plate (for coating)  
🫗 Oil spray bottle  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🥄 Wooden spatula  
🍛 Plate (for serving)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Remove the frozen shrimp cutlets. Dice the onion; cut the carrot and potato into rolling chunks, keeping the pieces roughly the same size.  
2. Coat each shrimp cutlet with cornstarch, dip thoroughly in the egg wash, and evenly press the panko breadcrumbs onto both sides. Gently shake off excess powder after each layer.  
3. Place the coated shrimp cutlets into the air fryer. Evenly spray the surface with cooking oil.  
   **Note**: Spraying oil helps achieve a golden color after air frying, mimicking the effect of deep frying.  
4. Set the air fryer to 180°C (356°F) and cook for 20 minutes.  
5. Lightly spray a small amount of oil into the rice cooker inner pot. Add the diced onion and stir-fry with a wooden spatula until softened, translucent, and fragrant.  
6. Add the carrot and potato chunks. Slightly stir-fry to release the aroma of the vegetables.  
7. Place two large cubes of curry roux into the pot. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients.  
8. Start the cooking function of the rice cooker. Heat until it comes to a boil, the curry roux fully melts, the sauce thickens, and the potato and carrot become tender.  
9. Open the rice cooker. Pour in the white rice and quickly stir to combine evenly, ensuring each grain of rice is coated with the curry sauce.  
10. Plate the curry rice. Remove the shrimp cutlets from the air fryer, cut them into pieces, and arrange them beside the rice. Sprinkle with a pinch of black sesame seeds for garnish.  

## Health Tips  
The shrimp cutlets are cooked using an air fryer, reducing oil intake compared to traditional deep frying. Paired with the curry vegetables and rice, this meal is nutritionally balanced and suitable for the whole family. Note that the shrimp cutlets are a pre-packaged item and may contain a relatively high amount of salt; individuals with high blood pressure should consume in moderation.
