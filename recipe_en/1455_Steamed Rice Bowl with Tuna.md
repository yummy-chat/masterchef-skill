# Steamed Rice Bowl with Tuna
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 White rice, about 1 bowl
🥩 Canned luncheon meat, 2-3 slices
🐟 Canned tuna, as needed
🍅 Cherry tomatoes, 2 pieces
🌿 Okra, 3-4 pieces (cut into sections)
🌽🥩 Corn and pork rib soup block, 1 piece (with corn and ribs)
🥕 Carrot, 1 small piece (cut into chunks)
🥬 Chinese cabbage, 2-3 leaves (torn into small pieces)
🫛 Snow peas, 1 piece
🎃 Pumpkin seed sauce, about 1 tablespoon

## Cookware and Equipment
🍲 Stainless steel bowl
♨️ Square steamer
🥄 Spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Take a stainless steel bowl, add the white rice, and arrange the luncheon meat slices, tuna, cherry tomatoes, okra sections, corn and pork rib soup block, carrot chunks, and snow peas on top in order.
2. Tear the Chinese cabbage into small pieces and spread them over all the ingredients.
   **Key point**: Tearing the cabbage eliminates the need for cutting, making the process convenient; side ingredients can be flexibly adjusted based on available items.
3. Pour water into the steamer, place the stainless steel bowl inside, cover, and start steaming. After the water comes to a boil, continue steaming for about 20 minutes, until all ingredients are fully cooked.
   **Key point**: Steaming time can be adjusted as needed based on the amount of ingredients, ensuring all side ingredients are thoroughly cooked.
4. Remove the steaming bowl, add the pumpkin seed sauce, and use a spoon to thoroughly mix the ingredients with the rice.
   **Key point**: The sauce should be added after steaming to preserve its flavor; stirring must be thorough and even, so every bite combines the sauce with the ingredients.

## Health Tips
This is a nutritionally balanced steamed rice dish, rich in protein and various vegetables. However, luncheon meat and canned tuna are high in sodium. For those sensitive to blood pressure, it is recommended to reduce the amount of canned ingredients by half. Pumpkin seed sauce is rich in zinc and magnesium, making it suitable for office workers with high mental demands.
