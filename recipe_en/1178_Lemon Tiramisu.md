# Lemon Tiramisu
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍋 Lemon 3-4 pieces (for zest and juice)
🥚 Eggs 5-6 pieces (2 whole eggs for lemon curd, 3 egg yolks for cheese mixture)
🧀 Mascarpone cheese 230g
🍪 Ladyfingers as needed
🧈 Butter 40g
🥛 Heavy cream about 150g (plus a small amount for top decoration and adjusting consistency)
🍬 White sugar about 110g (60g for curd, 30g for sugar syrup, 20g for egg and milk mixture)
🥃 Rum 30g (20g for sugar syrup, 10g for cheese mixture)
🌿 Vanilla paste as needed (vanilla extract or omit possible)
💧 Water 150g (for sugar syrup, plus a small amount for salvaging cookies)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🧈 Zester (grater)
🍋 Lemon juicer
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🥄 Whisk (handheld/electric)
🍳 Non-stick pan or milk pot
🥣 Glass measuring cup
🥄 Spatula
📦 Square pan or baking dish (for assembly)
🌀 Piping bag
🔪 Spatula

## Steps
### 1. Making Lemon Curd
1. Mix lemon zest, about 70g lemon juice, 60g white sugar, and 2 whole eggs in a bowl until combined.
2. Pour the mixture into a pot and heat over low heat, stirring continuously.
3. Stir until the liquid thickens noticeably, then remove from heat.
4. While hot, add the 40g butter cut into pieces. Stir using residual heat until fully melted and smooth.
5. Pour into a container, cover with plastic wrap directly on the surface, and refrigerate for later use.

### 2. Making Lemon Sugar Syrup
1. In a pot, combine the zest of 1 lemon, 40g lemon juice, 150g water, and 30g white sugar.
2. Add 20g rum, bring to a boil over low heat, then turn off the heat and let cool for later use.

### 3. Making Lemon Cheese Mixture
1. If eggs are not pasteurized, sanitize them first: mix egg yolks, a small amount of heavy cream, and 20g white sugar. Heat to 80°C and maintain for 15 seconds, then rapidly cool over an ice bath.
2. Beat the cooled egg mixture with an electric whisk until noticeably expanded and pale in color.
3. Beat the mascarpone cheese until smooth. Add the beaten egg mixture and fold together with a spatula until combined.
4. Add about 50g of the chilled lemon curd, 10g rum, and an appropriate amount of vanilla paste. Mix well.
5. Gradually fold the pre-whipped heavy cream (about 150g, whipped to a slightly runny state) into the cheese mixture, stirring until a smooth and creamy cheese mixture forms. If the cream is overwhipped, add a small amount of unwhipped liquid cream to adjust.

### 4. Assembly
1. Quickly dip ladyfingers into the lemon sugar syrup and arrange them tightly to cover the bottom of the container.
2. Spread a thin layer of lemon curd over the cookies.
3. Spread a layer of lemon cheese mixture and smooth it out with a spatula.
4. Repeat steps 1–3 to add a second layer of cookies, curd, and cheese mixture. If sugar syrup runs low, mix a small amount of lemon juice with water as a substitute.
5. If the cheese mixture is insufficient, pipe a layer of heavy cream on top and smooth it out. Then pipe reserved lemon curd in lines over the cream and gently spread with a spatula to create a pattern.
6. Grate fresh lemon zest over the surface.
7. Cover and refrigerate for at least one night to allow the flavors to meld before serving.

## Health Advice
Lemon zest is rich in antioxidants, and the vitamin C in the curd can help with iron absorption, making this suitable as an afternoon treat. Diabetic individuals should control their intake; those with stable blood sugar levels may occasionally indulge in small amounts.
