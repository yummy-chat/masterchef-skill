# Star Iced Rice Dumplings
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Clear jelly powder 100g
💧 Water 350g
🥭 Mango 3 pieces (pulp about 350-450g)
🌾 Rice flour 70g
✨ This recipe relies solely on mango for sweetness; no additional seasonings needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🥢 Whisk or chopsticks
♨️ Steamer
🔪 Knife
🥄 Spoon
🌀 Meat grinder or food processor
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🔧 Silicone spatula
🎨 Piping bag
⚖️ Kitchen scale

## Instructions
1.  Place 100g clear jelly powder and 350g water in a glass bowl, whisk thoroughly with a whisk or chopsticks until no lumps remain.
2.  Place the clear jelly batter in a steamer, steam over high heat for 20 minutes, until it becomes semi-transparent and blocky, and springs back when pressed. Remove and let cool naturally.
3.  Cut off the tail of the mango at an angle, scoop out the pulp with a spoon, and place it in a food processor. Blend into a smooth mango puree.
4.  Add 70g rice flour to the mango puree and mix well. Pour into a non-stick frying pan. Use a silicone spatula to stir-fry continuously over medium-low heat. Cook until the filling becomes sticky and reaches a paste-like consistency that is "flowable but not completely runny." Remove and set aside to cool.
    **Note**: Stir-fry the filling over medium-low heat at all times, stirring constantly to prevent sticking. The thickness is key to determining the texture and ease of wrapping.
5.  Transfer the cooled mango filling into a piping bag. Divide the steamed clear jelly wrapper into small portions, each weighing 30-40g.
6.  Take one wrapper portion and flatten it into a thin triangular piece. Pipe about 15g of mango filling into the center. Gather the edges to seal and shape into a triangle.
    **Key Point**: The wrapper-to-filling ratio should be about 2:1. This ensures the filling is enclosed while maintaining a translucent, stretchy texture.
7.  Place the wrapped dumplings in an airtight container, cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Serve chilled for a chewy, stretchy outer layer and a smooth, refreshing filling.

## Health Tips
This Star Iced Rice Dumpling replaces sugar with the natural sweetness of mango, making it suitable for those reducing sugar intake. It tastes best when chilled in summer. The clear jelly wrapper is high in carbohydrates but low in protein; pairing with protein and vegetables is recommended for balanced nutrition.
