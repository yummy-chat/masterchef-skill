# Everything in Spiced Brine
Cuisine: Jiangsu Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦆 Duck wings, duck tongues, duck gizzards (appropriate amounts each)  
🥬 Edamame (appropriate amount, trimmed ends)  
🥜 Peanuts (appropriate amount)  
🧄 Ginger slices (appropriate amount)  
🍶 Yellow wine (appropriate amount)  
🧂 Salt (appropriate amount)  
⭐ Star anise (appropriate amount)  
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorns (appropriate amount)  
🍲 Spiced brine (appropriate amount, enough to submerge ingredients)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🔪 Cutting board, knife  
🍱 Airtight container (or vacuum sealer)  
❄️ Freezer  

## Cooking Steps  
1.  Trim the ends of the edamame, wash the peanuts, and clean the duck wings, duck tongues, and duck gizzards separately.  
2.  Add water to the wok, put in ginger slices and yellow wine, add the duck parts and blanch. Once blood foam rises, remove and drain.  
3.  In a separate pot of clean water, add star anise, Sichuan peppercorns, and ginger slices. First, add the duck gizzards and cook for 20 minutes, then add the duck wings and cook for 15 minutes, finally add the duck tongues and cook for 10 minutes. Remove all and drain.  
**Note**: Each part has different cooking times. Strictly follow the order to avoid overcooking or undercooking.  

4.  Change the water in the pot, add a little salt, put in the edamame and cook for 5 minutes, then remove. Cook the peanuts separately for 30 minutes, then remove.  
5.  Let all cooked ingredients cool completely. Place them in an airtight container, pour in enough spiced brine to submerge everything, seal, and refrigerate to marinate.  
6.  For long-term meal prep, portion and vacuum-seal the cooked, drained ingredients, then freeze. Thaw before eating, then soak in spiced brine to marinate.  

## Health Tips  
Spiced brine is rich in B vitamins and is great for stimulating the appetite and cutting through grease in summer. However, its sodium content is high, so people with high blood pressure should consume it in moderation. Duck parts and edamame are rich in protein and dietary fiber, offering balanced nutrition.
