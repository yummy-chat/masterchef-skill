# Oil Pastry  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 Flour  
🫒 Oil  
🧂 Salt  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🥣 Small bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Instructions  
1. Take a small bowl, add flour and an appropriate amount of salt, and gently mix with chopsticks.  
2. Heat the cooking oil to 200°C, then pour it into the bowl while hot.  
   **Key point**: The oil temperature must reach at least 200°C to release the flour’s aroma and create a crispy texture.  
3. Stir quickly with chopsticks until the flour and oil are fully combined, forming a smooth, lump-free paste. Let it cool, and it becomes oil pastry.  
   **Key point**: Stir quickly to avoid clumps and ensure a fine, smooth result.  

## Health Tips  
Although oil pastry is fragrant, it has a high calorie density. It is recommended to use it as a dipping sauce for barbecue or as a flavor enhancer for pastries, limiting each serving to one tablespoon or less. Pair it with vegetables to reduce oil intake.
