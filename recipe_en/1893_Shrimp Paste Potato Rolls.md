# Shrimp Paste Potato Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Fresh shrimp, appropriate amount (heads, shells, and vein removed, made into shrimp paste)
🥔 Potato, 5-6 slices (cut into slices about 0.5 cm thick)
🥕 Carrot, appropriate amount (cut into small dice)
🌽 Sweet corn kernels, appropriate amount
🥚 Egg, 1
🥡 Cornstarch, appropriate amount
🫒 Cooking oil, appropriate amount
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount
🌶️ White pepper powder, appropriate amount
🍅 Ketchup, appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🪥 Toothpicks
♨️ Air fryer
🧺 Baking tray/fryer basket
🖌️ Brush

## Cooking Steps
1. Remove the heads, shells, and vein from the fresh shrimp, rinse with clean water, and drain.
   **Key point**: The shrimp vein must be thoroughly removed to avoid affecting the texture.

2. Chop the shrimp into a paste and place it in a bowl. Add the egg, cornstarch, salt, and white pepper powder. Using chopsticks, stir continuously in one direction until the shrimp paste becomes sticky, elastic, and develops a firm texture.
   **Key point**: Stirring clockwise until the mixture is firm is the key to forming the shrimp paste.

3. Cut the carrot into small dice and add it along with the sweet corn kernels to the shrimp paste. Mix well.

4. Wash and peel the potato, then cut into slices about 0.5 cm thick.

5. Place an appropriate amount of the mixed shrimp paste filling on top of a potato slice, shaping it into a mound. Use a toothpick to pierce through the shrimp paste and the potato slice to secure it.
   **Key point**: The toothpick must go through both the potato slice and the shrimp paste to ensure they do not come apart during baking.

6. Line the bottom of the air fryer basket with parchment paper or brush it with a thin layer of oil. Place the assembled shrimp paste potato rolls inside, and brush the surface evenly with cooking oil.

7. Set the air fryer to 180°C and bake for 10 minutes.

8. Pull out the basket, brush ketchup onto the surface of each roll, then flip them over one by one.

9. Return the basket to the air fryer and continue baking at 180°C for another 10 minutes.

10. Once baking is complete, remove the toothpicks used to secure the rolls, plate, and enjoy.

## Health Tips
These shrimp paste potato rolls are low in carbohydrates and high in protein. Made with an air fryer, they are low in oil and healthy, suitable for consumption during a weight loss phase. Note that the potato slices should not be cut too thick, otherwise they may burn on the outside while remaining raw inside. The shrimp paste needs to be stirred thoroughly until it firms up to ensure it holds together well.
