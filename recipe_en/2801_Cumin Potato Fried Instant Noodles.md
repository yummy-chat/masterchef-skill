# Cumin Potato Fried Instant Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potato 1 (diced and blanched in advance)
🍜 Instant Noodles Dry noodle cake, as needed
🥩 Ham Sausage About 3-4 slices (sliced)
🥕 Carrot As needed (diced and blanched in advance)
🧅 Onion As needed (diced)
🌿 Scallions A small bunch (cut into sections)
🫒 Cooking Oil 1 tablespoon (for frying)
🧂 Light Soy Sauce 1/2 tablespoon + 1/2 tablespoon (used in two batches)
🧂 Dark Soy Sauce 2-3 drops
🍬 White Sugar A small amount
🦪 Oyster Sauce As needed
🌿 Cumin (powder or seeds) As needed
🌱 Sesame Seeds As needed

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted Spoon
🥣 Bowl (or basin)
🥘 Pot (for boiling noodles and blanching vegetables)

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the potato and carrot into small dices. Blanch in boiling water until fully cooked, then remove and drain. Slice the ham sausage, dice the onion, and cut the scallions into sections.
2. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add the instant noodles and cook until the desired texture is achieved. Remove and drain thoroughly.
3. Place the noodles in a bowl. Add 1/2 tablespoon of light soy sauce, 2-3 drops of dark soy sauce, a small amount of white sugar, and an appropriate amount of oyster sauce. Gently mix evenly with your hands, ensuring the noodles are uniformly colored and flavored. Set aside.
4. Pour 1 tablespoon of cooking oil into the wok. Heat over medium heat until the oil reaches about 40% hot. Add the potato dices and shake the wok to coat them evenly with oil. Fry until the surface is golden brown.
   - **Note**: Adding the potatoes when the oil is 40% hot prevents sticking. Too high oil temperature can burn them, while too low oil temperature will cause them to absorb excess oil.
5. Add the ham sausage slices. Continue frying until the edges are slightly charred and the surface is golden brown. Use a slotted spoon to remove the potatoes and ham sausage together and set aside.
6. Leave the base oil in the wok. Add the fried potatoes, ham sausage, and blanched carrot dices. Pour the remaining 1/2 tablespoon of light soy sauce along the edge of the wok, quickly stir-frying to release the sauce's aroma.
7. Immediately sprinkle in the cumin, sesame seeds, and diced onion. Stir-fry vigorously over high heat until the fragrance is released.
8. Pour the pre-seasoned instant noodles into the wok. Sprinkle in the scallion sections. Using chopsticks, quickly toss and mix everything evenly, ensuring all ingredients and seasonings are well combined. Serve immediately on a plate.
   - **Key Points**: Maintain medium-high heat throughout. Toss quickly to prevent the noodles from clumping or burning. Adding the cumin at the end preserves its strong aroma.

## Health Suggestions
Potatoes and carrots are rich in dietary fiber and vitamins, but instant noodles and ham sausage have a high sodium content. It is recommended to pair with a light vegetable salad or fruit for balance. This dish is suitable for quickly replenishing carbohydrates after exercise. Those with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of light soy sauce and oyster sauce.
