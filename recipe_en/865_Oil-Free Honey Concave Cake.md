# Oil-Free Honey Concave Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## 🥚 Ingredients  
🥚 5 eggs (2 whole eggs, 3 egg yolks only)  
🌾 20 grams cake flour  
🌽 5 grams cornstarch  

## 🧂 Seasonings  
🍬 7 grams white sugar  
🍯 30 grams honey  
🧂 1 gram salt  
🌿 1 drop vanilla paste (optional)  

## 🍳 Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Large bowl  
🔌 Electric hand mixer  
🪄 Sieve  
🥧 6-inch cake pan or foil pan  
📜 Parchment paper  
🔥 Oven or air fryer  

## 🧑‍🍳 Instructions  
1. Place the 5 eggs in warm water to warm them to about 30°C, which helps with whipping.  
2. Wet the parchment paper, wring it out, and line the pan so it fits the shape of the pan, ensuring the edges of the finished cake are neat.  
3. Beat 2 whole eggs and 3 egg yolks into a large bowl. Add white sugar, honey, salt, and vanilla paste (if using).  
4. Whip on high speed with an electric hand mixer until the egg mixture lightens in color, increases in volume, and when the beaters are lifted, the batter can draw clear lines that do not disappear easily.  
5. Reduce the mixer to low speed and beat steadily for about 1 minute to refine the bubbles, resulting in a finer texture.  
6. Combine the cake flour and cornstarch, sift them together, and add to the egg batter.  
7. Using the electric hand mixer on low speed or a spatula, fold until no dry flour remains (about 10 seconds). Be careful not to overmix, which could deflate the batter.  
8. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.  
9. Bake in a preheated oven at 170°C (top and bottom heat) for about 13 minutes; if using an air fryer, bake at 150°C for about 25 minutes, until the surface is evenly golden brown.  
10. Let cool naturally after removing from the oven. The cake will form a concave shape as it shrinks. After cooling, seal and refrigerate for better flavor; it can be stored for about 3 days.  

## Health Tips  
This oil-free honey concave cake uses egg yolks and honey to provide natural sweetness and aroma, making it suitable as a low-fat dessert during weight loss. However, people with diabetes or those controlling sugar intake should be mindful of the sugar content in honey. The texture is even better when chilled, fluffy like clouds.
