# Steak Brunch
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Steak 1 piece  
🥑 Avocado 1  
🍞 Sourdough bread 2 slices  
🥚 Eggs 2  
🥬 Mixed microgreens as needed (sunflower shoots, purple cabbage shoots, broccoli shoots, Swiss chard shoots)  
🍑 White peach 1  
🫙 Yogurt as needed (for spreading)  
🍋 Lemon juice as needed  
🧂 Black pepper as needed  
🧂 Salt as needed  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Frying pan  
🔗 Tongs  
♨️ Air fryer  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🥣 Small bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍴 Fork  
🔥 Oven (for toasting bread)  

## Steps  
1. Remove the steak from the refrigerator in advance and let it come to room temperature. Pat the surface dry with paper towels.  
2. Heat a frying pan over medium heat. Place the steak in the pan and sear both sides until browned and caramelized.  
   **Note**: No additional oil is needed; use the steak’s own fat for searing.  
3. Wrap the seared steak in aluminum foil, place it in the air fryer, and cook at a suitable temperature until the desired doneness is reached. Remove and let it rest briefly, then slice and plate.  
4. Bring a pot of water to a boil, then reduce to low heat, maintaining a gentle simmer. Crack an egg into a small bowl, gently slide it into the water near the surface, and use chopsticks to gently shape the egg white. Cook until the white is set and the yolk is runny, then remove and drain.  
   **Key point**: No slotted spoon is needed; shaping with chopsticks alone is sufficient.  
5. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, scoop the flesh into a bowl, add lemon juice, black pepper, and salt, and mash with a fork into a paste, leaving some chunks.  
   **Key point**: Retaining some texture improves the mouthfeel.  
6. Place the sourdough bread slices in the oven and toast until slightly crispy.  
7. Assemble: Take the toasted bread slices, first spread a layer of yogurt, then the avocado mash, and top with the poached egg. On the plate, arrange the mixed microgreens, sliced steak, and halved peach.  
8. Serve and enjoy, ideally with a refreshing drink.  

## Health Tips  
Steak provides high-quality protein and iron, while avocados are rich in healthy fatty acids, making this suitable for muscle building or blood sugar management. Using the steak’s own fat during searing reduces the need for extra oil, but the total calorie count is relatively high, so pairing with microgreens helps balance nutrition.
