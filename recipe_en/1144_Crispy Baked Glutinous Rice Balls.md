# Crispy Baked Glutinous Rice Balls
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍡 Frozen glutinous rice balls (tangyuan), to taste (black sesame or peanut filling)
🥐 Frozen egg tart shells, to taste (1:1 ratio with glutinous rice balls)
🥚 1 egg (only the egg yolk for brushing)
⚫ Black sesame seeds, to taste (for surface decoration)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
♨️ Air fryer
🥄 Brush (for applying egg yolk)
🧻 Foil liners (to prevent sticking)

## Cooking Steps
1. Remove the frozen egg tart shells and let them soften at room temperature until pliable.
2. Place one frozen glutinous rice ball in the center of an egg tart shell and wrap it completely, pinching the edges tightly to seal, then roll into a round ball.
   **Note**: The wrapper must be fully sealed and pinched tight to prevent the filling from leaking during baking.
3. Arrange the wrapped glutinous rice balls evenly in the air fryer basket lined with foil liners.
4. Brush the surface evenly with beaten egg yolk, then sprinkle with an appropriate amount of black sesame seeds.
5. Place in the air fryer, set the temperature to 180°C, and bake for about 12 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Monitor the browning during baking and adjust the time as needed, until the surface is evenly golden and the pastry layers are distinct.
6. Remove and enjoy while hot. The outer tart shell is crispy and golden, the inner glutinous rice ball is soft and chewy, with a flowing filling, creating a rich layering of textures.

## Health Advice
This crispy baked glutinous rice ball dish is relatively high in calories, so it is recommended as an occasional dessert rather than a meal replacement. It is suitable for those who enjoy trying new flavors, but people with weak digestive systems should control their intake.
