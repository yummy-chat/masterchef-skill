# Braised Yellow Croaker with Rice Cakes
Cuisine: Zhejiang Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 1 Yellow croaker (scaled, fins removed, skin peeled from head, body scored)
🍚 Rice cakes, as needed (Taizhou hand-made rice cakes, cut into thick slices)
🥩 Pork belly, as needed (sliced)
🌿 Garlic chives, as needed (cut into sections, added before serving)
🧅 Shallots, as needed (for aromatics)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (for aromatics)
🫚 Ginger, as needed (for aromatics)
🌶 Bird’s eye chili, as needed (for aromatics)
🧅 Scallion segments, as needed (added during cooking)
🧂 Salt, as needed (for marinating the fish and preparing the sauce; mixed with sugar in a 1:1 ratio in the sauce)
🍬 Sugar, as needed (for preparing the sauce; mixed with salt in a 1:1 ratio)
🧂 Chicken powder, as needed (for preparing the sauce)
🍾 Light soy sauce, as needed (for preparing the sauce)
🍾 Oyster sauce, as needed (for preparing the sauce)
🌰 White pepper powder, as needed (for preparing the sauce)
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed (to remove fishy smell and enhance aroma)
🐟 Fish broth, as needed (prepared in advance to add freshness)
🧈 Lard, as needed (for sautéing the base)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Iron wok or frying pan
🥄 Wok spatula
🔪 Cutting board
🔪 Chef’s knife
🍴 Spatula
🥣 Ladle
🥄 Skimmer
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍲 Lid

## Steps
### Preparation Stage
1. Cut the rice cakes into thick slices. Use Taizhou hand-made rice cakes for a sticky but non-sticky texture.
2. Prepare the yellow croaker: Cut off the fins, scrape off the scales, remove the skin on the fish head, score both sides of the body, and rinse thoroughly under running water.
3. Rub salt evenly inside and outside the fish and marinate for 15 minutes.
4. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, mix one spoon of sugar, one spoon of salt, an appropriate amount of chicken powder, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and white pepper powder. Stir until well combined. The sugar-to-salt ratio is 1:1.
### Cooking Stage
1. Heat the wok, add cooking oil and lard. Stir-fry the pork belly slices until the fat renders. Then add shallots, garlic, ginger, and bird’s eye chili in order, sautéing over medium heat until fragrant.
2. Place the marinated yellow croaker flat in the wok, skin side down, and fry until fragrant.
3. Add scallion segments, drizzle in cooking wine, pour in the pre-prepared fish broth and the sauce mixture, then arrange the rice cake slices around the fish.
4. Cover with a lid, reduce heat to low, and braise for 4 and a half minutes. Occasionally shake the wok to prevent sticking and burning.
5. Remove the lid, sprinkle with garlic chive sections. Carefully plate the fish and rice cakes, then drizzle the thick sauce over the top.

## Health Advice
This dish features tender fish and rice cakes soaked in rich broth, making it a high-protein, low-fat choice suitable for those needing nutrition without excess grease. It is recommended to reduce the amount of pork belly and lard to lower saturated fat intake. Those with gout should consume the thick broth with caution.
