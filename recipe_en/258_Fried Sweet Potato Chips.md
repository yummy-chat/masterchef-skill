# Fried Sweet Potato Chips
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍠 Sweet potatoes (honey varieties), about 10  
💧 Water, as needed (for washing and soaking)  
🧂 Seasoning powders (chili powder, tomato powder, etc.), as needed (optional, sprinkle after frying)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Vegetable peeler  
🔪 Slicer (or a scraper as a substitute)  
🪣 Large basin (for soaking sweet potato slices)  
🧺 Colander or bamboo basket  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
📄 Paper towels (optional, for drying surface moisture)  
🛍️ Sealed bag (for storing finished chips)  

## Steps
1. Wash the sweet potatoes and peel them with a vegetable peeler.  
2. Submerge the peeled sweet potatoes in clean water, and use a slicer to cut them into even thin slices, allowing the slices to fall directly into the water.  
   **Key point**: The slices must be thin enough. If you do not have a slicer, use a scraper or knife to slice them by hand.  
3. Gently rinse the sweet potato slices in clean water 2–3 times to remove excess starch, being careful not to break the thin slices.  
4. Remove the sweet potato slices and drain off surface moisture. Let them air dry naturally or use paper towels to absorb moisture until no water droplets remain.  
5. Pour enough cooking oil into the wok and heat to about 50–60% hot. Add the sweet potato slices.  
   **Note**: Do not blanch the sweet potatoes; fry them directly.  
6. Reduce to low heat and continuously stir the slices with a slotted spoon to ensure even heating.  
   **Key point**: Maintain low heat throughout the entire frying process. Sweet potatoes are high in sugar, and high heat will easily burn them.  
7. Observe the state of the slices: Once most of the moisture has evaporated and dense small bubbles begin to form in the center of the slices, continue frying for about 1 more minute.  
   **State assessment**: The formation of small bubbles in the center indicates the moisture is gone, which determines the final crispness.  
8. When the slices turn a darker color and are completely crispy, remove them with a slotted spoon to drain the oil.  
9. After slight cooling, you can sprinkle with chili powder, tomato powder, or other seasonings according to preference, or enjoy the original flavor. Any leftover chips must be fully cooled before storing in a sealed bag to maintain crispness.  

## Health Advice
Sweet potatoes are high in sugar, so it is recommended to control the portion size per serving (about a small bowl). Pairing with green tea helps to cut the grease. Suitable for those needing an energy boost but avoiding greasy foods. Ensure low heat and slow frying throughout to minimize the formation of high-temperature byproducts such as acrylamide.
