# Cicadas on the Tree
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦗 Live cicada nymphs (适量) (If not cooking immediately, freeze in water to prevent molting)
🧂 BBQ seasoning (适量) (Use generously; sprinkle while hot)
🧂 Table salt (适量) (Can be used alone or mixed with BBQ seasoning)

## Cookware and Equipment
🫕 Wok / Deep fryer
🥄 Slotted spoon / Strainer (For removing and draining oil)
🥣 Bowl (For washing or freezing cicada nymphs, and for final seasoning)

## Preparation Steps
1. Clean the cicada nymphs thoroughly. If not cooking immediately, freeze them in water.
2. Pour sufficient oil into the wok and heat to 50% hot; maintain a relatively high oil temperature.
3. Add the cicada nymphs, immediately cover the wok, and keep the heat high throughout to prevent oil splatter.
4. Continue deep frying until the steam dissipates and the shells become crispy, then remove.
5. Use a slotted spoon to remove the cicada nymphs and drain excess oil thoroughly.
6. While hot, transfer the cicada nymphs to a bowl, sprinkle generously with BBQ seasoning and salt, and toss quickly until evenly coated. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice
Cicada nymphs are rich in high-quality protein and trace elements, making them suitable for replenishing energy in summer. However, frying increases the calorie content, so consumption should be moderate. Pair with cold cucumber salad or leafy greens to balance the greasiness. Individuals with high blood lipids should only taste a small amount.
