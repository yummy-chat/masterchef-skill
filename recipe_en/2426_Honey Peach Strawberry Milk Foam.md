# Honey Peach Strawberry Milk Foam
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍑 Peach flesh 180g (peeled and diced)  
🍓 Strawberry jam 2 tablespoons  
🧊 Ice cubes 250g  
🍵 Biluochun tea leaves 2 tablespoons  
🌸 Fresh jasmine flowers 2 tablespoons  
💧 Cold water (as needed, for cold brewing, enough to cover the tea leaves)  
🧀 Cream cheese 100g (softened at room temperature)  
🥛 Milk 80g  
🥛 Heavy cream 200g (chilled)  
🧂 Rock salt 1g  
🥫 Sweetened condensed milk 30g  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🫗 Glass pitcher  
♨️ Microwave  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Whisk or hand mixer  
💉 Piping bag  
🥤 Blender  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🫙 Glass  
🪤 Plastic wrap  

## Instructions
1. **Prepare cold-brewed jasmine tea base**
   Place the Biluochun tea leaves and fresh jasmine flowers into a tea bag, put it in a glass pitcher, and add cold water and ice cubes. Cover and refrigerate overnight to fully extract the tea aroma.

2. **Prepare the cream cheese milk foam**
   - Microwave the milk for about 40 seconds until warm, then pour it into the softened cream cheese. Stir until smooth and no lumps remain.
   - Add rock salt and sweetened condensed milk, and mix well.
   - In another bowl, whip the chilled heavy cream with a whisk until it reaches a 60–70% stiffness.
   - Combine the whipped cream with the cream cheese mixture, gently folding until well mixed. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until needed.

3. **Prepare the honey peach strawberry milk foam drink**
   - Take out the chilled cold-brewed jasmine tea and measure about 100 ml.
   - Add the peeled and diced peach flesh, cold-brewed jasmine tea, ice cubes, and strawberry jam into a blender. Blend until smooth to form a pink fruit slushie.
   - Pour the fruit slushie into a glass.
   - Remove the chilled cream cheese milk foam, transfer it to a piping bag, and pipe evenly on top of the slushie.

4. Serve immediately. The milk foam is salty and sweet with a rich, smooth texture, while the slushie is refreshing with a fruity aroma.

## Health Advice
This beverage is primarily made with a cold-brewed tea base and fresh fruit. The sugar content mainly comes from sweetened condensed milk and strawberry jam, so it is recommended to control intake to reduce calories. Suitable for cooling off in summer, but due to the high content of cream cheese and heavy cream, those with lactose intolerance or on a weight loss plan may reduce the amount of milk foam.
