# Seafood Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

🌾 Whole wheat flour (5 tablespoons)  
🥚 Eggs (3)

🧂 Yellow wine (or cooking wine, 适量, for marinating to remove fishy smell)  
🌶️ White pepper powder (适量, for marinating to remove fishy smell)  
🧂 Salt (approximately 4 grams)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Bowl/basin (for marinating, mixing batter, and combining)  
🥄 Spoon (for measuring flour, stirring, and scooping batter)  
🔪 Knife and cutting board (for chopping scallions)  
✂️ Scissors (for preparing shrimp)  
🥢 Slotted spoon/strainer (for draining seafood)  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🔥 Gas stove  
🪄 Spatula (for flipping and removing)  

## Steps  
1. Remove the gills and stomach sac from the scallops and tear into small pieces; peel the shrimp and cut into segments; tear the crab sticks into strips. Wash all seafood thoroughly and drain well with a slotted spoon. Add yellow wine and white pepper powder, mix well, and marinate.  
2. Wash the scallions and chop them into small pieces.  
3. Place the whole wheat flour in a basin, gradually add cold water while stirring, and mix until a thin batter forms.  
4. In another bowl, crack the eggs and whisk until the egg mixture is even and frothy.  
5. Combine the marinated seafood, prepared batter, and beaten eggs in a large basin. Add the chopped scallions and salt, and stir evenly with chopsticks.  
6. Pour an appropriate amount of oil into a non-stick frying pan and heat until both the pan and oil are hot, then immediately turn off the heat. Use a spoon to scoop an appropriate amount of the mixed batter and spread it into a thin pancake. Do not cook too many at once; prepare them in batches. Utilize the residual heat to slowly cook the pancake. Because the batter is thin and contains a lot of liquid, the pancake will set slowly. Turn the heat on and off as needed to control the temperature and prevent the bottom from burning while the inside remains undercooked. Once one side is set and golden brown, carefully flip the pancake with a spatula and continue cooking until both sides are golden brown and fully cooked through.  

## Health Tips  
This seafood pancake uses whole wheat flour and a variety of seafood, providing rich protein and dietary fiber. It is suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or needing to control blood sugar. When pan-frying, make good use of the residual heat to cook slowly, avoiding a burnt exterior and undercooked interior.
