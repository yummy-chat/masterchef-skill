# Lard Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Cooked white rice (short-grain recommended) 200 g  
🧈 Lard 15 g (reduce to 10 g if adding oyster sauce)  
🌿 Chopped scallions 0–12 g (start with 5 g)  
🥓 Pork cracklings 5 g  
🧂 Light soy sauce 5 ml (reduce to 3 ml if adding oyster sauce)  
🫘 Dark soy sauce 2 ml  
🦪 Oyster sauce 8 g (optional)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍜 Bowl  
🥄 Spoon  

## Steps
1. Evenly spread the lard at the bottom of the bowl.  
2. Scoop freshly cooked hot rice into the bowl, covering the lard.  
   **Key point**: Using hot rice helps the lard melt and evenly coat each grain.  
3. Drizzle in light soy sauce and dark soy sauce; add oyster sauce if using.  
   **Note**: When adding oyster sauce, reduce the amount of lard and light soy sauce proportionally to avoid greasiness or excessive saltiness.  
4. Sprinkle chopped scallions and pork cracklings on top.  
5. Use a spoon to mix all the ingredients thoroughly until well combined.  

## Health Tips
Lard rice is rich in carbohydrates and fats, making it suitable for individuals with high energy expenditure or those needing a quick energy boost. Due to its high cholesterol content, it should be consumed in moderation by people with high blood lipids. Pair it with lean meat and vegetables to balance nutrition.
