# Beef Noodle Cutlet
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 Toast bread 4-5 slices (ground into breadcrumbs)
🥩 Beef rolls about 1 box
🥚 Egg white 1
🌾 Flour as needed
🫒 Cooking oil (for filling) as needed
🧂 Salt as needed
🍬 Sugar as needed
🦪 Oyster sauce as needed
🍢 BBQ sauce as needed
🌿 BBQ seasoning as needed
🫒 Cooking oil (for frying) as needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥄 Food processor
👨‍🍳 Rolling pin
🍳 Flat-bottom frying pan
🍢 Bamboo skewers

## Steps
1. **Make breadcrumbs**: Tear the toast bread into small pieces, put them into a food processor and grind into fine breadcrumbs. Remove and spread on a chopping board for later use.
2. **Mix the filling**: Put the beef rolls, salt, sugar, oyster sauce, cooking oil, egg white, and flour together into the food processor. Blend until the filling becomes firm and sticky, forming a thick paste.
3. **Shape and coat with breadcrumbs**: Take an appropriate amount of filling and roll it into a ball. Place it on the breadcrumbs and press flat. Use a rolling pin to repeatedly roll it out into a very thin, large piece, ensuring both sides are evenly coated with breadcrumbs.
   **Note**: The thinner you roll it, the crispier the texture will be after frying.
4. **Deep-fry**: Pour enough oil into the pan and heat until dense small bubbles form around the cutlet when added. Turn to medium-low heat and fry the beef noodle cutlet until it completely floats, turns a deep golden brown, and the outer shell is crispy.
   **Note**: The oil temperature should not be too high to avoid the outside burning while the inside remains raw; make sure to fry thoroughly.
5. **Season**: Remove from the oil and drain. Thread onto bamboo skewers. Immediately brush with BBQ sauce and sprinkle with BBQ seasoning. Serve.

## Health Advice
This dish is made by wrapping in toast breadcrumbs and deep-frying, resulting in a crispy texture. However, deep-frying is high in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. Suitable for young people who need a quick energy boost. Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid greasiness.
