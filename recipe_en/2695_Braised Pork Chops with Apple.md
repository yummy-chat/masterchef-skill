# Braised Pork Chops with Apple
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork Shoulder 550g (chilled, sliced about 1cm thick)
🧂 Salt 5.5g
🌿 Black Pepper 1g
🫒 Cooking Oil (cold-pressed rapeseed oil) About 8g
🧅 Onion适量 (for marinating)
🧅 Spring Onions 4 large (can be substituted with onion)
🍎 Shandong Fuji Apple 1 (unpeeled, use 1.5 if small)
🫚 Ginger 8–10 slices
🧄 Garlic 6–8 cloves (lightly crushed)
🧈 Butter 20g
🌿 Black Pepper Crystals 0.5g
🍂 Cinnamon Powder 0.5g
🍬 Brown Sugar 11g
🍶 Donggu Yipin Fresh Soy Sauce 22g
🦪 Oyster Sauce 9g
🥃 Brandy 14g (can be substituted with yellow wine, white liquor, or rice wine)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's Knife
🪵 Round Wooden Cutting Board
🥘 Stainless Steel Rectangular Tray
🥣 Stainless Steel Bowl
🍳 Iron Pan (thicker)
🥄 Wooden Spatula
🥛 Glass Measuring Cup
⚖️ Electronic Scale
♨️ Lid

## Steps
1. Cut the pork shoulder into slices about 1cm thick, and use the heel of the knife to cut through the white sinews. Add 5.5g salt, 1g black pepper, cooking oil, and onion strips. Mix well, seal, and refrigerate to marinate for at least 30 minutes.

2. Peel the spring onions and cut them in half. Core the apple and cut into 4–5 wedges. Slice the ginger, peel the garlic and lightly crush it. Prepare the sauce: mix the black pepper crystals, cinnamon powder, brown sugar, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and brandy together.

3. Heat the empty pan until a drop of water sizzles and evaporates instantly. Turn off the heat, pour a small amount of oil to coat the pan. Place the pork chops in, turn the heat to high, and use the wooden spatula to press the meat flat against the pan bottom. Fry until one side is charred brown, then flip.

4. After flipping, add the ginger slices and garlic cloves in the gaps around the pan. Once both sides of the pork chops are colored, reduce heat to low. Add the butter and sauce, then turn to medium heat to aromatize.

5. Add the spring onions and apple wedges. Pour in enough boiling water to just cover the pork chops. Cover with a lid, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium-low heat and braise for 10–15 minutes. Watch the liquid level; add more boiling water if necessary.

6. Turn off the heat. First, arrange the apple and spring onions at the bottom of a serving plate. Then cut the pork chops into pieces and place on top. Remove the ginger, garlic, and other aromatics from the pan and discard. Reheat the remaining sauce over high heat until thickened. Pour the sauce over the pork chops and garnish with dried parsley or chopped cilantro.

## Health Tips
This dish uses pork shoulder and apples, providing protein and pectin, suitable for fitness and sugar-conscious individuals. However, butter and brown sugar are high in calories, so consume in moderation to avoid overindulging with staple foods.
