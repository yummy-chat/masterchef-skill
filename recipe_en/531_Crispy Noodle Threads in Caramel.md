# Crispy Noodle Threads in Caramel
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍝 Raw noodles – 1 plate (cut into inch-long sections)  
🍬 White sugar – 1 tablespoon  
💧 Water – 1 tablespoon  
🫒 Cooking oil – a small amount (used when making caramel to prevent sticking)  
🤍 White sesame seeds – as needed  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board  
🍳 Wok (iron wok)  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥄 Ladle  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Instructions
1. Place the noodles on a cutting board and cut into inch-long sections with a chef’s knife. Put the cut pieces into a plate for later use.  
   **Note**: Keep the sections uniform in length.  
2. Pour enough oil into the wok and heat to 170°C (340°F). Scatter the cut noodles and add them to the oil. Do not stir them immediately after adding; allow them to set.  
   **Key**: Oil temperature should be 170°C (340°F); the noodles must be scattered to prevent sticking.  
3. Deep-fry the noodles over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for about 2 minutes until golden and crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain excess oil.  
   **Note**: Do not use low heat, or the noodles will absorb too much oil and affect the texture.  
4. In another wok, add 1 tablespoon of water, 1 tablespoon of white sugar, and a small amount of cooking oil. Heat over medium heat, stirring constantly with a ladle. Watch the caramel as it changes: first, large “fish-eye” bubbles will appear; continue heating for 20–30 seconds until they become small “fish-eye” bubbles; then heat for about another half minute until the caramel turns light yellow, then immediately turn off the heat.  
   **Key**: Be patient while making the caramel; accurately identify the three stages: large bubbles → small bubbles → light yellow. Turn off the heat as soon as it turns yellow to avoid burning the caramel.  
5. After turning off the heat, quickly pour the fried noodles into the wok with the caramel. Stir-fry rapidly to coat each noodle evenly with caramel.  
   **Note**: Work quickly, using the residual heat to mix.  
6. Once evenly mixed, sprinkle in an appropriate amount of white sesame seeds, stir again, and then transfer to a serving plate.

## Health Advice
This dish, Crispy Noodle Threads in Caramel, is high in calories and suitable only for occasional indulgence; it should not be eaten frequently. Control the oil temperature during deep-frying to prevent the noodles from absorbing too much oil. People with diabetes or those managing blood sugar levels should consume with caution.
