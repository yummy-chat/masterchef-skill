# Three Delicacy Chive Rolls
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Spring (January-April)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌮 Dumpling wrappers 10 pieces (stacked)  
🥬 Chives appropriate amount (chopped)  
🍜 Sweet potato starch noodles appropriate amount (cooked and chopped)  
🥚 Eggs appropriate amount (scrambled and chopped)  
🦐 Dried shrimp appropriate amount  
🫒 Vegetable oil appropriate amount (for soaking dumpling wrappers and frying)  
🧂 Dark soy sauce appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt appropriate amount  
🧂 Chicken bouillon appropriate amount  
🦪 Oyster sauce appropriate amount  
🫒 Sesame oil appropriate amount  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥘 Steamer  
🍳 Frying pan  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Spoon  
🪯 Rolling pin (for thinning dumpling wrappers)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Dip each dumpling wrapper one by one into vegetable oil, remove, and stack ten sheets together. Use a rolling pin to evenly roll and thin the stack, ensuring each sheet is coated with oil and the dough is stretched thin.  
2. Bring water to a boil in a steamer, place the rolled dumpling wrappers inside, and steam over high heat for 10 minutes. Remove and immediately peel each sheet apart while hot to prevent sticking.  
3. Cook the sweet potato starch noodles until tender, then chop them. Mix with an appropriate amount of dark soy sauce for coloring.  
4. Scramble the eggs and chop them; wash and chop the chives; set aside the dried shrimp.  
5. Combine the chopped noodles, chives, scrambled eggs, and dried shrimp in a bowl. Add salt, chicken bouillon, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, and mix well to form the filling.  
   **Note**: Adjust the amounts of seasonings to taste as desired.  
6. Take one steamed dumpling wrapper, place an appropriate amount of filling on it, and roll it tightly like a spring roll, ensuring the filling does not leak out.  
7. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat, add an adequate amount of oil, and once the oil is hot, place the chive rolls in the pan. Fry over medium-low heat until both sides are golden and crispy.  
   **Key point**: Turn the rolls occasionally during frying to prevent burning, and remove from the pan once the outer skin turns a charred-golden color.  

## Health Advice  
This dish of three delicacy chive rolls is rich in dietary fiber and high-quality protein, suitable for nourishing the liver and aiding digestion in spring. However, frying increases the calorie count, so it is recommended to pair it with a light soup. Note that chives are warming in nature; those with a hot constitution or excessive stomach acid should avoid overconsumption.
