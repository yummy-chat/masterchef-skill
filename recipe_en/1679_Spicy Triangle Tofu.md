# Spicy Triangle Tofu
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧈 Firm Tofu, as needed (firm texture, suitable for deep-frying)
🌽 Corn Starch, as needed (for coating)
🧂 Chicken Bouillon, a pinch
🧂 Salt, to taste
🍬 White Sugar, 2 tablespoons
🍶 Fragrant Vinegar, 2 tablespoons
🫗 Light Soy Sauce, as needed
🌶️ Chili Oil, sufficient amount (provides spiciness and red oil color)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🛍️ Plastic Bag (for draining tofu in the refrigerator)
🥣 Stainless Steel Bowl (for soaking tofu and mixing sauce)
🥄 Slotted Spoon (for removing tofu and draining oil)
🍳 Wok (for deep-frying with ample oil)
🍳 Deep Fryer (for deep-frying with ample oil)
🥢 Wooden Spatula (for testing oil temperature and turning tofu)
🥢 Chopsticks (for testing oil temperature and turning tofu)
🔪 Cutting Board (for cutting tofu)
🔪 Knife (for cutting tofu)

## Preparation Steps
1.  Drain Tofu: Place the whole block of firm tofu in a plastic bag and refrigerate for 1-2 hours to naturally release excess water.
   **Note**: This step makes the tofu texture firmer and less likely to break during deep-frying.
2.  Soak Tofu in Salt Water: Soak the drained tofu in warm salted water for 20 minutes. Remove and let the surface air dry slightly.
   **Note**: Soaking in salt water adds a base flavor and further firms the tofu.
3.  Cut Tofu: Cut the tofu into triangular pieces of even thickness.
   **Key Point**: Ensure uniform size for even cooking.
4.  Coat Tofu: Place the tofu pieces in corn starch, coating both sides evenly with a thin layer. Let them rest until the surface becomes slightly moist.
5.  Prepare Sauce: In a bowl, mix chicken bouillon, salt, white sugar, fragrant vinegar, light soy sauce, and chili oil. Stir well and set aside.
6.  First Deep-Fry: Heat oil to 180°C. Slide the tofu pieces in along the edge of the wok, maintaining medium-high heat.
   **Doneness Check**: Deep-fry until all pieces float and turn light yellow, then remove and drain oil.
   **Note**: Do not add too many tofu pieces at once to avoid a sudden drop in oil temperature.
7.  Second Deep-Fry: Reheat the oil to 180°C. Add all the tofu pieces and deep-fry over high heat for about 1-2 minutes.
   **Doneness Check**: The color should be golden. When stirred, there should be a crispy, hard texture and a rustling sound.
   **Note**: The second deep-fry expels excess oil and makes the outer shell crispy. Remove quickly and drain oil thoroughly after deep-frying.
8.  Mix: Place the deep-fried tofu in a large bowl. Pour in the prepared sauce and gently toss until each piece is evenly coated.
   **Note**: You can sprinkle toasted sesame seeds or chopped green onions for extra aroma and garnish.

## Health Advice
Firm tofu is high in protein and low in fat. The calorie content increases after deep-frying, so it’s recommended to pair it with leafy green vegetables for balanced nutrition. It’s suitable for those who enjoy spicy flavors and need calcium supplements, but people with weak digestion or who are sensitive to greasy food should consume it in moderation.
