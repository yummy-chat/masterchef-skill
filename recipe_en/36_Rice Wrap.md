# Rice Wrap
Cuisine: Northeastern Chinese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Rice
🦐 Fresh shrimp
🥔 Potato
🍆 Eggplant
🥚 Eggs
🌭 Ham sausage
🥜 Peanuts
🌿 Cilantro
🥬 Lettuce
🫘 Salted egg yolk
🫒 Cooking oil
🧂 Soy sauce
🫙 Bean paste
🌿 Thirteen-spice powder
🦪 Oyster sauce

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍚 Rice cooker
🍳 Frying pan
🧤 Disposable gloves
🥡 Plastic wrap
🫗 Large bowl
🥄 Spoon
🍽️ Serving plate

## Steps
1. Wash the rice thoroughly, place it in the rice cooker inner pot, and add water according to the inner pot's刻度 markings.
**Key Point**: Water amount should follow the rice cooker inner pot's markings.

2. Place the peeled and deveined fresh shrimp, peeled and roughly cut potato, and whole eggplant on top of the rice. Start the standard or deluxe rice cooking mode and cook until done.
**Key Point**: All ingredients are steamed together with the rice; the eggplant should remain whole or only have its stem removed.

3. Heat oil in a frying pan, pour in the beaten eggs, and quickly stir-fry until set into tender yellow pieces. Remove and set aside.
**Note**: Do not overcook the eggs.

4. Add the cooked eggs back into the pan, drizzle with soy sauce and bean paste, sprinkle with a little thirteen-spice powder, and stir-fry evenly. Then add diced ham sausage and some chopped cilantro, stir-frying until fragrant to make the egg sauce.
**Key Point**: The egg sauce is the core seasoning of the rice wrap; the sauce should evenly coat the eggs.

5. Once the rice is cooked, remove the eggplant while hot. Wrap it in plastic wrap and gently knead it, then peel off the skin.
**Note**: Use the heat to separate the skin from the flesh; the peeled eggplant will mix more easily.

6. Place the peeled eggplant, steamed shrimp, potato, cooked egg sauce, peanuts, and remaining chopped cilantro into a large bowl.
7. Drizzle an appropriate amount of oyster sauce into the bowl. Wearing disposable gloves, thoroughly mix all ingredients and the rice by hand until well combined.
**Key Point**: Mixing by hand is more flavorful than using a spoon; ingredients must be evenly combined.

8. Spread a lettuce leaf on a piece of plastic wrap. Place an appropriate amount of the mixed rice filling on top. Optionally add a small piece of salted egg yolk. Use the plastic wrap to roll the lettuce leaf tightly into a rice wrap.
**Key Point**: The plastic wrap helps shape it; rolling it tightly makes it easier to hold and eat.

9. Remove the plastic wrap, plate, and serve.

## Health Advice
This rice wrap combines a staple food with vegetables and high-quality protein for balanced nutrition. Potatoes and eggplant provide dietary fiber, while shrimp is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for weight loss or work meals. Note that the sauce and salted egg yolk are high in salt; those with high blood pressure can reduce the amount.
