# Lemon Pickled Chicken Feet
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 Chicken feet 2 lbs
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (sliced)
🧄 Garlic, appropriate amount (sliced)
🌶️ Bird's eye chili, appropriate amount (cut into rings)
🍋 Lemon, 3-4 slices
🧅 Scallion, appropriate amount (for cooking chicken feet)
💧 Water, one bowl (for making spice water)
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns, appropriate amount
⭐ Star anise, appropriate amount
🍃 Bay leaves, appropriate amount
🍶 Rice vinegar, 2 cups
🍬 White sugar, half cup
🧂 Light soy sauce, 1 cup
🧂 Salt, 2 tablespoons
🧂 MSG, 1 tablespoon
🫙 Pickled chili water, 100g
🍾 Cooking wine, appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🍳 Pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🫙 Plastic wrap

## Instructions
1. Clean the chicken feet thoroughly and trim the nails. Slice the ginger and garlic, cut the bird's eye chili into rings, and slice the lemon.
2. Place the chicken feet in a pot of cold water, add scallion, ginger, and cooking wine. Bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, then reduce to low heat and cook for about 15 minutes until the chicken feet are fully cooked.
**Key point**: Immediately transfer the cooked chicken feet to ice water to cool, then rinse under running water to remove surface grease, resulting in a firmer texture.
3. Cut the cooled chicken feet in half lengthwise to enhance flavor absorption.
4. Prepare the spice water: Add Sichuan peppercorns, star anise, and bay leaves to a bowl of water. Boil over high heat for 10 minutes until the water turns yellow, then strain. **Ensure it is completely cooled before use**.
5. Prepare the vinegar water: Mix rice vinegar, white sugar, light soy sauce, salt, and MSG, stirring until well combined.
**Key point**: Taste after mixing and adjust according to personal preference.
6. Place the chicken feet, sliced ginger, sliced garlic, bird's eye chili rings, lemon slices, and pickled chili water into a large bowl. Pour in the completely cooled vinegar water and spice water. Wearing gloves, mix everything evenly.
7. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight to marinate. Stir a few times during marination to ensure even color and flavor absorption.

## Health Tips
Pickled chicken feet are rich in collagen and have a chewy texture, but they are high in sodium. People with high blood pressure or edema should consume them in moderation.
