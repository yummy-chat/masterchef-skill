# Braised Small Yellow Croaker in Clay Pot
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🐟 Small yellow croaker (pomfret) about 3 pieces  
🧅 Scallions 1 bunch (cut green part into diagonal sections, green part into flowers)  
🧄 Ginger 1 piece (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic several cloves (crushed or whole)  
🌶️ Red bell pepper/chili pepper a little (minced for garnish)  
🧂 Salt a little  
🫒 Sesame oil as needed  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍲 Clay pot  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the small yellow croaker, score both sides of the fish with a knife, sprinkle a little salt and rub evenly, let it marinate for a while.
2. Pour sesame oil into the bottom of the clay pot, spread scallion sections, ginger slices, and crushed garlic cloves.
3. Arrange the marinated small yellow croaker neatly on top of the scallions, ginger, and garlic.
4. Cover the pot and braise over medium-low heat for about 10 minutes.
**Doneness Check**: When the fish eyes turn white and bulge, the fish is cooked through and fresh.

5. Before serving, sprinkle with scallion flowers and minced red bell pepper for garnish.

## Health Tips  
Small yellow croaker is rich in high-quality protein and Omega-3. The clay pot braising method uses little oil and retains the natural juices, making it suitable for those who need to control oil and reduce fat. Note that the fish is tender and cooks quickly; 10 minutes of braising is sufficient, and do not overcook to avoid a dry texture.
