# Korean Mayak Eggs
Cuisine: Other
Cooking difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Ingredients
- Eggs 5-8
- Green onions 15g
- Chili peppers (Thai bird's eye chili or green/red chili) 15g

🧂 Seasonings
- Korean soy sauce (or light soy sauce) 4 tablespoons
- Japanese mirin 2 tablespoons
- Sugar 2 tablespoons
- Sesame oil 5ml

🍳 Kitchenware
- Pot
- Small pot
- Ice water basin

## Instructions
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the eggs, and cook for 7 minutes.

2. While the eggs are cooking, prepare the sauce: Pour 4 tablespoons of soy sauce, 4 tablespoons of water, 2 tablespoons of mirin, and 2 tablespoons of sugar into a small pot.

3. Heat the sauce until it boils to evaporate the alcohol in the mirin, then turn off the heat and let it cool.

4. Add the sliced chili pepper rings to the cooled sauce.

5. Remove the cooked eggs and immediately place them in ice water to cool, then peel the shells.

6. Place the peeled eggs into the sauce, drizzle with sesame oil, and sprinkle with chopped green onions.

7. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours to allow the eggs to fully absorb the flavor.

## Health Advice
This soft-boiled egg dish has a rich sauce with high sodium content. It is recommended to serve with rice or vegetables to balance the saltiness. People with high blood pressure should consume in small amounts. The addition of chili peppers and green onions can aid digestion, making it suitable as an appetizer.
