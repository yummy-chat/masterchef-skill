# Shrimp Paste Steamed Dumplings
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥦 Broccoli, appropriate amount (blanched and finely chopped)  
🥕 Carrot, appropriate amount (blanched and finely chopped)  
🦐 Shrimp paste, appropriate amount (as the main filling)  
🥟 Wonton wrappers, appropriate amount (used as dumpling wrappers)  
🧂 Black pepper, appropriate amount (added when seasoning the filling)  
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount (added when seasoning the filling)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍲 Soup pot (for blanching vegetables)  
🥄 Slotted spoon (for removing blanched vegetables)  
🔪 Cutting board and knife (for chopping blanched vegetables)  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for mixing the filling)  
♨️ Steamer (for steaming the dumplings)  
🍽️ Plate and dipping sauce dish (for serving the finished dumplings and sauce)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the broccoli and carrot pieces, and blanch for 5 minutes.  
2. Remove the blanched broccoli and carrots, place them on a cutting board, and chop finely.  
3. Mix the chopped vegetables with the shrimp paste, add black pepper and salt, and stir thoroughly until well combined.  
4. Take one wonton wrapper, place an appropriate amount of filling in the center, fold it in half, and pinch the edges tightly to ensure a complete seal, preventing the filling from leaking during steaming.  
5. Bring water to a boil in a steamer, line it with a steaming cloth, place the wrapped dumplings inside, cover with a lid, and steam for 10 minutes, until the wrappers become translucent and the filling is fully cooked.  

## Health Tips  
The filling of shrimp paste steamed dumplings combines vegetables with shrimp paste, offering low fat and high protein, making it suitable for those on a weight loss diet or managing blood sugar. Note that wonton wrappers are thin, so ensure the edges are tightly sealed during steaming to avoid leakage. It is recommended to serve with vinegar or soy sauce for enhanced flavor.
