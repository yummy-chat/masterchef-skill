# Braised Prawns  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦐 Prawns: about 10-20 pieces (adjust according to the number of people)  
🧅 Scallion: appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🫚 Ginger: appropriate amount (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic: appropriate amount (sliced)  
🛢️ Cooking oil: appropriate amount  
🥫 Ketchup: appropriate amount (main seasoning)  
🍬 White sugar: a pinch (to balance acidity)  
🧂 Salt: a pinch (to balance flavors)  

## Kitchen Utensils & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan / wok  
✂️ Scissors  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🥄 Spatula  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Use scissors to trim off the sharp spines on the prawn heads and the long antennae.  
2. Curve the prawn body, press and secure it with your fingers, and use a chef's knife to slice along the back from the head to the tail to fully butterfly the prawn.  
   **Key point**: This step prevents the shell from splitting during cooking and ensures a clean cut.  
3. Remove the black vein exposed after butterflying.  
   **Key point**: The vein must be thoroughly removed, or it will affect the texture and create a gritty mouthfeel.  
4. Pour an appropriate amount of cooking oil into the pan and heat until hot.  
5. Place the prepared prawns into the pan and fry over medium heat.  
6. Fry one side until it turns red and firms up, then flip. Fry each side for about 2 minutes.  
7. After both sides are fried, add scallion sections, ginger slices, and garlic slices, and stir-fry together until fragrant.  
8. Add ketchup, a pinch of white sugar, and a pinch of salt, and stir-fry quickly until well combined.  
   **Key point**: Ketchup is the main seasoning; sugar and salt are used to balance acidity and adjust the flavor.  
9. When the sauce evenly coats the prawns and the aroma is rich, remove from heat and plate.  

## Health Tips  
Braised prawns are rich in high-quality protein and Omega-3, making them suitable for nutritional supplementation. Be sure to thoroughly remove the vein to avoid a gritty texture. People with high cholesterol or seafood allergies should consume with caution.
