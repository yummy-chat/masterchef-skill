# Braised Beef Brisket and Tomato Noodle Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Beef brisket/beef – appropriate amount (cut into chunks)
🍅 Tomato – appropriate amount (cut into chunks)
🥚 Eggs – 3 (with shells)
🫚 Ginger – appropriate amount (sliced)
🧅 Scallion – appropriate amount (cut into sections)
🥬 Leafy greens (e.g., baby bok choy) – appropriate amount (for serving)
🍜 Instant noodle cake – 1 serving (for the base)
🧂 Light soy sauce – appropriate amount
🧂 Dark soy sauce – appropriate amount
🦪 Oyster sauce – appropriate amount
🍅 Ketchup – appropriate amount
🌶️ Black pepper – appropriate amount
🧂 Salt – appropriate amount
🍬 Rock sugar – appropriate amount
🌿 Bay leaves – appropriate amount
🪵 Cinnamon stick – appropriate amount
🍋 Lime – 1 (juiced)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍚 Rice cooker
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Large bowl
🥢 Chopsticks

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut beef brisket into chunks, tomatoes into large chunks, slice ginger, and cut scallion into sections. Rinse the egg shells with water and set aside.
2. Place the beef brisket chunks and whole eggs in the rice cooker inner pot with cold water. Add ginger slices and scallion sections. Pour in enough water to submerge the ingredients.
   **Note**: This step is for blanching to remove the gamey smell. The water will need to be discarded and the inner pot rinsed later.
3. Start the rice cooker’s blanching program. Once done, use a slotted spoon to remove the beef brisket chunks and eggs. Peel the eggs and set aside. Drain the beef brisket chunks.
4. Place the blanched beef brisket chunks, peeled hard-boiled eggs, tomato chunks, ginger slices, scallion sections, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, and rock sugar back into the rice cooker inner pot.
5. Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, and ketchup in sequence. Sprinkle in black pepper and salt.
6. Squeeze in the lime juice, then use your hands to mix all ingredients and seasonings evenly, ensuring the sauce coats everything well.
7. Cover the rice cooker lid, select the stewing mode, and cook until the beef brisket is tender and flavorful, and the sauce has thickened.
8. In a separate pot, cook the instant noodles. Drain and place into a bowl, topping with blanched leafy greens.
9. Ladle the stewed tomato beef brisket and sauce over the noodles and greens. Add the braised egg on top.

## Health Advice
This braised beef brisket and tomato noodle soup is rich in high-quality protein and lycopene, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost or recovery. However, the noodles and rock sugar contribute to calorie content, so those managing their weight should be mindful of portion sizes.
