# Mushroom Three-Fresh Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍄 King oyster mushroom, appropriate amount (cut into rolling knife pieces)  
🍄 Pine mushroom, appropriate amount (cleaned)  
🧈 Old tofu, appropriate amount (cut into pieces)  
🥚 Eggs, 6 pieces  
🦐 Shrimp, appropriate amount  
🧅 Scallion, appropriate amount (chopped into scallion flowers)  
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount  
🌶️ White pepper powder, a pinch  
🫒 Vegetable oil, appropriate amount  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Knife, cutting board  
🍳 Frying pan  
🫕 Clay pot  
🥄 Soup ladle  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Cut the king oyster mushroom into rolling knife pieces, clean the pine mushrooms, cut the old tofu into pieces, and chop the scallion into scallion flowers.  
2. Pour vegetable oil into a frying pan and heat until warm. Fry the eggs over high heat until both sides are golden to form an egg pancake. Remove and cut into large pieces.  
   **Key point**: Cut the egg pieces slightly larger for better texture.  
3. Place the fried egg pieces into a clay pot, add an appropriate amount of water, and simmer over high heat until the soup turns a rich white color.  
4. Reduce the heat to low, add all the mushrooms, replenish with a small amount of water, cover the pot, and simmer for 15 minutes.  
5. Add the shrimp and tofu, continue to cover the pot, and simmer for another 10 minutes.  
6. Remove the lid, sprinkle with scallion flowers and a pinch of white pepper powder, add salt to adjust to your preferred saltiness, and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This mushroom three-fresh soup is rich in high-quality protein and dietary fiber, making it suitable for fitness and weight loss individuals. However, the egg pancake made from 6 eggs will result in higher cholesterol content. Gout patients should consume mushrooms in moderation to avoid triggering discomfort.
