# Yellow Catfish with Tofu
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 Yellow catfish 2 pieces (about 1 pound, cut into large chunks)
🧈 Tofu 1 block (cut into bite-sized pieces)
🧅 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced)
🧅 Scallions appropriate amount (cut into sections)
🌶️ Red chili pepper appropriate amount (cut into rings)
🌶️ Green chili pepper appropriate amount (cut into rings)
🧂 Salt appropriate amount
🧂 MSG a pinch
🍶 Cooking wine appropriate amount
🧂 White pepper powder appropriate amount
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🔪 Chef's knife
🪓 Cutting board

## Cooking Steps
1. Heat the wok, pour in cooking oil. Once the oil temperature rises, add the yellow catfish pieces. Reduce to medium-low heat and slowly fry until both sides are golden brown, allowing the fish pieces to set and remove any fishy smell while enhancing sweetness.
   **Key Point**: Slow-fry over medium-low heat to ensure the fish pieces remain intact and avoid being burnt on the outside while raw on the inside.
2. Once the fish pieces are fried, add ginger slices and scallion sections to the side of the wok, stir-frying until fragrant.
3. Pour in enough boiling water, then turn to high heat and bring to a boil.
4. Add the tofu pieces, salt, a pinch of MSG, and cooking wine, stirring well.
5. Cover the wok with a lid, turn to medium-high heat, and simmer for about 6 minutes until the soup turns a milky white color and the ingredients are well-flavored.
   **Doneness Check**: The soup turns rich and white, and the fish pieces are fully cooked.
6. Before serving, sprinkle in the red and green chili pepper rings and white pepper powder. Mix well and plate.

## Health Tips
Yellow catfish and tofu soup is rich in high-quality protein and calcium, making it suitable for those needing calcium supplementation or recovery of physical strength; the tofu absorbs the fish soup and becomes more tender. However, individuals with gout should consume it in moderation.
