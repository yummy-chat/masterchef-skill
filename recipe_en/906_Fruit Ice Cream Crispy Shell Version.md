# Fruit Ice Cream Crispy Shell Version
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍓 Strawberries, as needed (cut into small pieces)
🫐 Blueberries, 1 box
🥭 Mango, as needed
🍍 Pineapple, as needed
🥛 Milk, 1 box (250ml)
🥛 Milk powder, 2 tablespoons
🌾 Glutinous rice flour, 1 tablespoon
🧈 Whipping cream, 2 boxes (500ml)
🥫 Condensed milk, 1 large tablespoon
🍬 White sugar, for jam (1/5 of fruit weight) and 1 large tablespoon for whipping cream
🍋 Lemon juice, 1/10 of fruit weight
🍫 Dark chocolate, about 150g (mix 70% and 54%)
🧈 Cocoa butter, 50g
🌰 Crushed hazelnuts, 40g
🍓 Strawberry powder, as needed
🍠 Purple sweet potato powder, as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥣 Stainless steel bowls (multiple)
🥄 Whisk
🥄 Silicone spatula or spoon
🔲 Ice cream molds
🧁 Paper cups (can be used as simple molds)
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🛁 Hot water basin (for melting chocolate over water)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Make jam**: Process strawberries, blueberries, mango, and pineapple separately. Take strawberry jam as an example: combine strawberries, white sugar, and lemon juice in a ratio of about 5:1:0.5 in a non-stick pan. Simmer over low heat until soft and thick. All jams must be completely cooled before use.
2. **Make milk glutinous rice paste**: Pour milk, milk powder, and glutinous rice flour into a pot. Stir well. Heat over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until thick and smooth, and the paste hangs on the spatula without easily dripping. Pour out and cool completely.
   **Key point**: Glutinous rice flour is key to making the ice cream creamy and free of ice crystals.
3. **Whip the cream**: In a clean, grease-free bowl, add whipping cream, condensed milk, and 1 large tablespoon of white sugar. Whip until thick and yogurt-like, with visible lines. Do not over-whip until stiff.
4. **Mix the ice cream base**: Pour the completely cooled milk glutinous rice paste into the whipped cream. Stir until smooth and free of lumps.
5. **Fill molds and freeze**: Spread a layer of ice cream base in the bottom of the mold. Add a spoonful of jam. Cover with more ice cream base. Gently tap the mold to release air bubbles. Alternatively, use paper cups with popsicle sticks inserted. Freeze until completely firm.
6. **Prepare the crispy shell liquid**: Melt dark chocolate and cocoa butter in a ratio of about 3:1 over hot water. Stir well, then add crushed hazelnuts. For coloring, melt white chocolate with cocoa butter separately, then mix in strawberry powder or purple sweet potato powder.
7. **Dip into the crispy shell**: Remove the frozen ice cream from the mold. Quickly dip it into the crispy shell liquid, then lift it out. Let the shell solidify on the surface.
   **Key point**: Work quickly. In hot weather, you can place the container of crispy shell liquid over ice water to help it solidify faster.

## Health Advice
This recipe is rich in fresh fruit, and the homemade jam contains no additives. However, the whipping cream and crispy chocolate shell are relatively high in calories. It is recommended to enjoy as an occasional summer dessert and pair it with appropriate exercise. Suitable for the whole family to share. Diabetic patients should control the amount of jam used.
