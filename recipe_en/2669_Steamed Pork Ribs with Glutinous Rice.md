# Steamed Pork Ribs with Glutinous Rice
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦴 Pork spare ribs, about 650g (cut into 6-7cm pieces, choose tender meat with marbling and thin bones)
🍚 Glutinous rice, 150g (soak for at least 3 hours in advance, then drain)
🍠 Sweet potato, 1 (peel and slice; can substitute with potato, pumpkin, or yam)
🧅 Green onion, as needed (cut into sections for marinating, chop for garnish)
🧄 Ginger, as needed (crush for marinating)
🧂 Salt, 2 tablespoons
🍬 Sugar, 1 tablespoon
🌫️ White pepper powder, 1 tablespoon
🥄 Light soy sauce, as needed
🦪 Oyster sauce, as needed
🫘 Fermented soybean paste (yellow bean sauce), 1 tablespoon (can substitute with rib sauce or hoisin sauce)
🥢 Dark soy sauce, a small amount (for color)
🌾 Starch, 1 tablespoon (for cleaning)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🪣 Stainless steel bowl
🥄 Slotted spoon
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board
♨️ Pressure cooker (recommended) or regular steamer
🎋 Bamboo steamer
🍃 Lotus leaves or bamboo leaves

## Cooking Steps
1. **Prepare the ribs**: Cut the ribs into large pieces, place in a bowl, add 1 tablespoon of starch and a little water, rub to remove blood, then rinse thoroughly with water and drain until the surface is dry.
2. **Marinate the ribs**: Add crushed green onion and ginger, salt, sugar, white pepper powder, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, fermented soybean paste, and a small amount of dark soy sauce to the ribs. Rub and mix repeatedly with your hands until the mixture feels sticky and the meat tightens.
   - **Key point**: Thoroughly working until sticky is crucial for tender, fall-off-the-bone ribs.
3. **Prepare ingredients and let rest**: After 10 minutes of marinating, remove and discard the green onion and ginger. Peel and slice the sweet potato. Drain the glutinous rice.
4. **Line the steamer**: Line the bottom of the bamboo steamer with lotus leaves or bamboo leaves, then arrange the sweet potato slices in a single layer on top.
5. **Coat the ribs with rice and arrange**: Add the drained glutinous rice to the ribs and mix well, ensuring each rib is evenly coated. Arrange them in a single layer on top of the sweet potato slices. **Do not overlap** to ensure even steam penetration.
6. **Steam**: Add enough water to the pressure cooker, place a steaming rack inside, and set the steamer on top. Once steam builds up and pressure is reached, reduce to low heat and cook for 30 minutes.
   - **Regular steamer**: Once water is boiling, steam for about 1.5 hours.
7. **Serve**: After turning off the heat, allow the pressure to release naturally. Open the lid, remove the steamer, and sprinkle with chopped green onion as garnish.

## Health Advice
Glutinous rice has a high glycemic index. It is recommended to eat with plenty of vegetables. Diabetic patients should consume sparingly. This dish is suitable for those needing to replenish energy or gain weight, but the amount of ribs should be controlled to avoid excessive fat intake.
