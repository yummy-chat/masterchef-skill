# Sweet and Sour Potato & Fragrant Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥔 2 Potatoes (1 large, 1 small, cut into pieces and rinsed to remove starch)
🥚 4 Eggs (beat with 2 tablespoons of water or milk)
🌭 4 Sausages (cut into pieces)
🧄 5 Cloves Garlic (minced, added in two batches)
🧅 1 Scallion (minced)
🥄 2 tbsp Light Soy Sauce
🥄 2 tbsp Vinegar
🥫 1 tbsp Tomato Paste (if using thin ketchup, use 2 tbsp)
🍬 2 tbsp White Sugar
🥄 1/2 tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
🌽 1 tbsp Corn Starch
💧 4 tbsp Water
🦪 1 tbsp Oyster Sauce
🧂 Salt to taste (add after turning off the heat)
🫒 Cooking Oil (slightly more than usual for stir-frying, used in two batches)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Chef’s Knife, Cutting Board
🗑️ Strainer (Slotted Spoon)
🥣 Bowl, Chopsticks, Spoon
🍳 Non-Stick Pan
🥄 Spatula
🔥 Gas Stove

## Preparation Steps
1.  Peel the potatoes. First cut into thick slices, then into strips, and finally into small cubes about 1-2 cm. Place in clean water to rinse off surface starch. Drain with a strainer, then pat dry with paper towels to prevent oil splatter when frying.
2.  Cut the sausages into pieces similar in size to the potato cubes. Crush and mince the garlic, dividing into two portions. Mince the scallion.
3.  Crack the eggs into a bowl, add 2 tablespoons of water or milk, and whisk thoroughly until well combined.
4.  In a bowl, combine light soy sauce, vinegar, tomato paste, white sugar, dark soy sauce, corn starch, water, and oyster sauce. Stir well to create the sauce mixture.
5.  Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, add an appropriate amount of oil. When the oil is hot, pour in the beaten eggs. Let sit for about 5 seconds until the bottom layer sets, then push the eggs from the outside in with chopsticks. When about 70% set, flip and cook through. Transfer to a plate, keeping the eggs in large, tender curds.
6.  Without washing the pan, add slightly more oil than usual for stir-frying. When hot, add the drained potato cubes and spread them in a single layer. Pan-fry over low heat for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent burning, until the surface is mostly golden brown and the inside is cooked through.
7.  Add the sausage pieces and stir-fry over medium heat for about 2 minutes.
8.  Add half of the minced garlic and scallion, stir-fry until fragrant. Then add the cooked eggs, gently breaking them into bite-sized pieces with a spatula.
9.  Pour in the prepared sauce mixture and stir-fry evenly to coat all ingredients. For a softer, creamier potato texture, you may add about 100 ml of water and simmer for 2 minutes over low heat.
10. Before turning off the heat, sprinkle in the remaining minced garlic and scallion, stir-fry quickly to release fragrance, then turn off the heat.
11. Taste and add salt as desired. This dish pairs well when mixed with steamed rice.

## Health Suggestions
Potatoes are rich in dietary fiber. Combined with eggs and sausages, they provide high-quality protein, making this dish suitable for those needing an energy boost or muscle gain. Note that the sweet and sour sauce contains a moderate amount of sugar; those managing sugar intake or on a weight loss plan may wish to reduce the amount of white sugar.
