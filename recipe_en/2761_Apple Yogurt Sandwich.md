# Apple Yogurt Sandwich
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍎 1 apple  
🥛 Plain yogurt, as needed  
🌸 A pinch of dried butterfly pea flowers  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🧊 Yogurt press container  
🔪 Knife  
🍴 Spatula  
🪓 Cutting board  
🍽️ Plate  

## Steps
1. **Make cold-brew yogurt**: Pour plain yogurt into a press container lined with filter cloth, place the press lid on top, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours until the whey is strained out and the yogurt is thick like cheese.  
2. **Prepare the apple**: Cut the apple into six wedges, remove the core, then make two horizontal cuts in the middle of each wedge and scoop out some flesh to create a groove for filling with yogurt.  
3. **Fill with yogurt**: Use a spatula to fill the apple grooves with the cold-brew yogurt, and smooth the surface.  
4. **Assemble**: Piece the yogurt-filled apple wedges back together to form the original apple shape.  
5. **Garnish and serve**: Sprinkle a small amount of dried butterfly pea flowers on the yogurt surface for decoration, then plate and enjoy.  
   **Key point**: Ensure the cold-brew yogurt is strained long enough to achieve a thick consistency for shaping; choose a crisp and sweet apple variety. The cold-brew yogurt is unsweetened, giving the dish a fresh and natural flavor.

## Health Tips
This dish replaces sugar with cold-brew yogurt, and the natural sweetness of the apple serves as the perfect seasoning. It is refreshing and low-calorie, suitable for weight loss periods or as a light afternoon snack. Note that the cold-brew yogurt must be pressed for at least 4 hours; otherwise, it will be difficult to shape when filling the apple grooves.
