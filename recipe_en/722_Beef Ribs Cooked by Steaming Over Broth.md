# Beef Ribs Cooked by Steaming Over Broth
Cuisine: Common Home-style
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef ribs 2kg (whole piece)
🦴 Beef spine, oxtail, beef bones as needed
🧅 Onion about half (1/2 for broth base, 1/2 for steaming tray base)
🥬 White radish as needed
🥔 Potatoes as needed
🥕 Carrots as needed
🍅 Fresh tomato 1 (cut into chunks)
🍝 Vermicelli as needed
🌶️ Small chili peppers as needed (for dipping sauce)
🧄 Garlic as needed (minced)
🧅 Green garlic or scallions as needed (for garnish)
🫘 Sesame seeds as needed (white)
🌿 Bay leaf 1
🍛 Curry cubes as needed
🧂 Salt as needed
🧂 White pepper as needed
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns as needed
🥫 Doubanjiang (broad bean paste) as needed
🥫 Tomato paste as needed
🍅 Xinjiang tomato canned as needed (for richer flavor)
🫒 Vegetable oil as needed (for frying bones, oil splashing, mixing grilled meat)
🌶️ Chili flakes as needed (for grilled meat)
🌱 Cumin as needed (for grilled meat)
🧂 Light soy sauce as needed (for dipping sauce)
🧂 Oyster sauce as needed (for dipping sauce)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass mixing bowl

♨️ Deep pot/stockpot with steamer insert
🧽 Damp cloth (for covering the lid)
✂️ Kitchen scissors
🫙 Vacuum sealer and food storage bags
🧊 Ice cube tray
🥄 Strainer
🥘 Saucepan/stockpot (for subsequent dishes)

## Preparation Steps
### Pre-cooking Beef Rib Broth Base
1. Place beef ribs in a large bowl, add salt, white pepper, and Sichuan peppercorns. Mix well to marinate.
2. Place beef spine, oxtail, and beef bones in a frying pan, fry until golden brown on the surface.
3. Fill a stockpot with enough water, add the fried bones, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for about 1 hour to make the base broth.
4. Take a deep pot with a steamer insert, fill the lower layer with about half a pot of broth, add half the onion, and keep heating over low heat.
5. Spread the remaining half of the onion on the steamer tray, then lay the marinated beef ribs flat on top of the onion.
   **Key point**: The meat should not touch the water to lock in the meat's aroma and umami, preventing flavor loss into the broth.
6. Cover the pot, **place a damp cloth on top of the lid**, keep heating over low heat for 80 minutes, until the beef ribs can be easily pierced with chopsticks, achieving a tender state.

### Portioning and Storage
7. Remove the steamed beef ribs, cut into bite-sized pieces with kitchen scissors.
8. Divide into portions according to each meal, place into food storage bags, and vacuum seal. Refrigerate or freeze as versatile pre-cooked beef.
9. Continue simmering the broth in the lower pot, strain out impurities, cool, and skim off most of the surface fat.
10. Pour the broth into an ice cube tray, freeze into concentrated beef broth cubes. Can be frozen for at least a month.

### Five Ways to Enjoy
**Monday · White Cut Beef Ribs**
- Reheat one portion of pre-cooked beef.
- Dipping sauce: Place minced garlic, small chili peppers, scallions, and sesame seeds in a bowl. Pour hot oil to release aroma, then add light soy sauce and oyster sauce, and mix well.

**Tuesday · Beef Ribs Vermicelli Soup**
- Put one portion of pre-cooked beef and one concentrated broth cube into a pot, add water, and bring to a boil.
- Add sliced radish and vermicelli, cook until soft. Season with salt and white pepper, garnish with green garlic before serving.

**Wednesday · Tomato-Stewed Beef**
- Sauté minced onion and garlic. Add doubanjiang and tomato paste, stir-fry until the oil turns red. Add Xinjiang canned tomatoes, a bay leaf, one portion of pre-cooked beef, water, and a few broth cubes. Simmer, then cut into smaller pieces. Finally, add fresh tomato chunks, season with salt and black pepper, and reduce the sauce.

**Thursday · Curry Beef Ribs**
- Sauté onion, carrots, and potatoes. Add one portion of pre-cooked beef, water, and broth cubes. Cook until soft, then cut into smaller pieces.
- Add curry cubes, cook until thickened.

**Friday · Air Fryer Grilled Beef Ribs**
- Mix pre-cooked beef pieces with vegetable oil, cumin, and chili flakes. Place into a foil tray.
- Air fry at 180°C for 8 minutes, remove and sprinkle with white sesame seeds.

## Health Tips
These beef ribs are based on a rich broth, offering multiple ways to enjoy the meat. They are rich in protein and collagen, suitable for muscle building and those needing an energy boost. Pay attention to the salt content in the dipping sauce and curry cubes. Those with high uric acid or gout should limit broth consumption.
