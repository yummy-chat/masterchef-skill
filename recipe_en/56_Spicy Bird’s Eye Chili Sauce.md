# Spicy Bird’s Eye Chili Sauce
Cuisine type: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌶️ Bird’s eye chili (Thai chili) 600g (high spiciness, wear gloves when handling)  
🧄 Garlic 60g (peeled and minced)  
🍎 Apple 1 (peeled and finely chopped)  
🍅 Tomato 1 (score a cross on the skin, blanch in boiling water to remove the skin, then finely chop)  
🧅 Onion 1 (minced)  
🍬 White sugar 60g  
🧂 Salt 60g  
🍶 Brewed white vinegar 60g  
🫒 Cooking oil 60g  
🥃 High-proof liquor 15g (add after turning off the heat and cooling, to extend shelf life)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl (for washing and holding chilies)  
🔪 Chef’s knife and cutting board (for chopping ingredients)  
🍳 Wok or frying pan (for simmering the sauce)  
🥄 Wooden spatula (for constant stirring to prevent sticking)  
🫙 Glass jars (pre-sterilized with boiling water and dried, for storing the sauce)  

## Instructions
1. Wash the bird’s eye chilies, drain thoroughly, and remove the stems.  
   **Note**: Wear gloves throughout the process to avoid skin irritation from chili juice.
2. Finely chop the chilies, leaving a few small chunks for better texture.
3. Score the tomato surface with a cross, blanch briefly in boiling water to remove the skin, then finely chop; peel and finely chop the apple; mince the onion; peel and mince the garlic. Set aside.
4. Place the chopped chilies, tomato, and apple into a cold pan. Add the white sugar, salt, brewed white vinegar, and cooking oil.
5. Cook over high heat, stirring constantly. After about 2 minutes, the sugar and salt will dissolve; after 3 minutes, the mixture will come to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and continue simmering, stirring continuously to prevent sticking.
6. Continue cooking and stirring. By 6 minutes, the liquid will have visibly reduced; by 8 minutes, most of the moisture will have evaporated. Turn off the heat.
7. Once the sauce has cooled completely, add the prepared minced garlic, minced onion, and 15g of liquor. Mix thoroughly.
8. Transfer into glass jars that have been pre-sterilized with boiling water and dried. Seal and store in the refrigerator.  
   **Tip**: It can be eaten immediately if needed, but the flavor improves after a few days. Great for mixing with rice or stir-frying.

## Health Advice  
This spicy sauce is made with apple and tomato, whose sweetness and tartness help balance the heat, making it suitable for those who want to try spicy flavors but are sensitive to intense stimulation. Please wear gloves throughout the preparation to avoid chili skin irritation. Consume in moderation to avoid stomach upset.
