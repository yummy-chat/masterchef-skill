# Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes and Shrimp
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍅 Tomato, about 2 pieces (cut into small pieces)
🧄 Garlic, to taste (minced)
🧅 Scallions, a small handful (cut into segments or chopped)
🥚 Eggs, 3-4 pieces
🍤 Shrimp, about 8-10 pieces
🧂 Salt, a small amount (add a small amount when beating the eggs, add a small amount when stir-frying the tomatoes)
🥛 Water starch, about half a small bowl (add to the egg mixture and whisk together)
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste (more oil is needed when scrambling the eggs, and the oil temperature should be high)
🍶 Pure tomato juice (unsweetened), to taste (add after the tomatoes release their juice during stir-frying)
🌿 White pepper powder, to taste (recommended to add more, add during the stir-frying stage of the tomatoes and shrimp)
🍬 Sugar, to taste (add according to personal preference before serving)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪑 Cutting board
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks or whisk
🍳 Wok
🥄 Spatula or silicone spatula

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces; crush the garlic with the flat side of a knife and then mince; cut the scallions into segments or chop them, and set aside.
2. Prepare the egg mixture: Crack 3-4 eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and whisk until well combined, then pour in half a small bowl of water starch and mix thoroughly.
   **Key Point**: Water starch makes the eggs more tender and silky.
3. Scramble the eggs: Heat the wok over high heat, add a generous amount of cooking oil, and heat until it starts to smoke slightly. Pour in the egg mixture, and once the bottom sets quickly, continuously push and stir with a spatula to form large curds. Cook until about 80-90% done, then remove and set aside.
   **Key Point**: Use high heat when pouring in the egg mixture to help it set quickly and prevent sticking. Don't worry about the eggs clumping; they will be broken up later when returned to the wok. It's fine if they are not fully cooked through, as they will be cooked again later.
4. Stir-fry the tomatoes and shrimp: Leave some oil in the wok and heat over medium heat. First, add the tomato pieces and minced garlic, stir-fry, and add a pinch of salt to help soften them. Once the tomatoes have softened and released their juice, pour in the pure tomato juice, then add the shrimp, sprinkle in a generous amount of white pepper powder, and season with a little salt.
   **Key Point**: Add the shrimp only after the tomatoes have softened and released their juice to prevent them from becoming overcooked.
5. Combine and serve: Return the scrambled eggs to the wok and stir-fry together with the tomatoes and shrimp until evenly mixed.
   - If you prefer a sweeter taste, you can add sugar at this point. Do not reduce the sauce too much; it's better to keep a little sauce. You can add a small amount of water to adjust the consistency if needed.
6. Plate and garnish: Serve the stir-fried dish directly over rice, and sprinkle with chopped scallions.

## Health Advice
This dish is rich in high-quality protein and lycopene, making it suitable for supplementing nutrition without easily causing weight gain. It is recommended to use less oil and sugar, and cook the shrimp until just tender to preserve the texture.
