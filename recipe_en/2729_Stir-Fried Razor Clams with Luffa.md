# Stir-Fried Razor Clams with Luffa
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April to August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦪 Razor clams 400g (soak in salted water in advance to purge sand, change water midway)
🥒 Luffa 2-3 (retain a layer of green skin when peeling, cut into rolling knife pieces)
🧅 Scallion, as needed (white parts for aromatics, green parts for final garnish)
🫚 Ginger, as needed (sliced, for blanching and stir-frying)
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed (for blanching razor clams to remove fishiness)
🫒 Rapeseed oil, as needed (for stir-frying)
🧂 Salt, as needed
🍬 Sugar, a pinch
🍗 Chicken broth, a pinch
🌾 Cornstarch 1-2 tbsp (mixed with water for thickening)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥣 Small saucepan
🥄 Strainer/slotted spoon
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🥣 Bowls (for soaking clams, mixing cornstarch slurry, serving)

## Preparation Steps
1. Soak razor clams in salted water for at least 2-3 hours in advance, changing the water 1-2 times to ensure they fully expel sand.
2. Bring water to a boil in a small saucepan, add cooking wine and ginger slices. Once boiling, add razor clams and blanch for about 15 seconds, then quickly remove and transfer to cold water to cool.
   **Note**: Do not blanch for too long to keep the meat tender.
3. Remove the clam meat from the shells, and remove the black line from each piece of meat.
4. When peeling the luffa, retain a layer of green inner skin, then cut into rolling knife pieces.
5. Heat the non-stick pan, pour in rapeseed oil, add ginger slices and scallion white parts, and stir-fry over low heat until fragrant.
6. Add the luffa pieces, turn to medium-high heat, and stir-fry for about 1 minute until the luffa softens and releases a sweet aroma.
7. Add the prepared razor clam meat, stir-fry evenly, then season with salt, a pinch of sugar, and chicken broth.
8. Pour in a small amount of water along the edge of the pan, cover with a lid, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 2 minutes.
9. Remove the lid, drizzle in the prepared cornstarch slurry, gently toss to coat the ingredients in a lightly thickened glossy sauce, and finally sprinkle with scallion greens, toss evenly, and serve.
   **Key**: Use a light slurry to brighten the sauce and coat the ingredients; add scallions last to preserve their aroma.

## Health Suggestions
Razor clams are rich in high-quality protein and zinc, while luffa helps cool and hydrate the body. This dish is refreshing and nutritious for summer consumption. It is suitable for those needing to supplement protein, but individuals with high uric acid should control their intake.
