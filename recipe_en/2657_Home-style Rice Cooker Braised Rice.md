# Home-style Rice Cooker Braised Rice
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Cooked rice, as needed
🥩 Pork ribs, 1-2 pieces (washed clean)
🥔 Chinese yam, as needed (peeled and cut into small sections)
🍄 Dried shiitake mushrooms, 2 pieces (soaked in advance and washed clean)
🥬 Green vegetables, as needed (chopped)
🌭 Ham sausage, as needed (diced)
🥕 Carrot, 1 small piece (shredded)
🥬 Cabbage leaves, as needed (torn into small pieces by hand)
🥚 Egg, 1 (beaten into egg liquid)
🥄 Lard, about 1 tablespoon (can be substituted with other cooking oil)
🧂 Black pepper, crushed, as needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍚 Rice cooker
🔪 Kitchen knife
🪵 Cutting board
🥣 Small bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash all ingredients one by one: peel the Chinese yam and cut into small sections, chop the green vegetables and ham sausage into dices, shred the carrot, tear the cabbage leaves into small pieces by hand, and soak the dried shiitake mushrooms in advance then wash clean.
2. Spread the cooked rice evenly on the bottom of the rice cooker inner pot. Place the chopped green vegetables, diced ham, Chinese yam sections, pork ribs, shiitake mushrooms, shredded carrot, and cabbage leaves on top in order.
3. Add 1 tablespoon of lard, pour in the beaten egg liquid, then gently stir with chopsticks or a rice paddle to mix the egg liquid thoroughly with the other ingredients and rice.
   **Key point**: Lard can significantly enhance the oily fragrance of the rice; the egg liquid helps bind the ingredients to the rice, preventing them from coming loose after steaming.
4. Cover the rice cooker lid, press the "Cook Rice" button, and braise for about 2 hours and 40 minutes until the program ends automatically.
5. After opening the lid, sprinkle in an appropriate amount of crushed black pepper, and stir well with a rice paddle before serving.
   **Key point**: Adding black pepper just before serving makes its aroma more pronounced; no additional salt is added in this recipe, but you can add salt or other seasonings to taste.

## Health Advice
Lard and egg liquid add aroma and nutrition to the rice. The combination of meat and vegetables is rich and suitable for the whole family. Note that pork ribs and ham sausage are high in sodium. People with high blood pressure can reduce the amount of ham sausage or blanch the ribs in advance to lower the salt content.
