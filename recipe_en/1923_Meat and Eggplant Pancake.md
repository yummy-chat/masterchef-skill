# Meat and Eggplant Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧂 Salt 1-2g
🥬 Rice flour (ground from rice) 250g
🥬 Wheat flour 50g
🥬 Egg 1
🥬 Cooked cured meat 100g
🥬 Eggplant 1 (about 10-15cm long, choose a long, round shape)
🥬 Cooking oil 10-15ml
🥬 Water (to adjust consistency)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥢 Chopsticks or spoon
🥣 Bowl

## Steps
1. Peel the eggplant and slice it; slice the cooked cured meat and set aside.

2. In a bowl, add 250g rice flour, 50g wheat flour, and 1 egg.

3. Gradually add water while stirring to form a batter. The batter should evenly coat the eggplant and meat slices without dripping. Place the eggplant and meat slices into the batter, ensuring they are evenly coated.

4. Pour 10-30ml of cooking oil into a non-stick pan and heat over low heat.

5. Use chopsticks or a spoon to place the batter-coated eggplant and meat slices into the pan. Fry until both sides are golden brown, then continue cooking for 3-6 minutes until fully cooked. If the oil level decreases during cooking, add more oil as needed. Keep the heat low throughout.

6. Sprinkle in the salt, stir-fry evenly, then remove from the pan and plate.

## Health Advice
Eggplant and meat pancake provides high-quality protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable for energy replenishment. However, cured meat is high in sodium, so those with high blood pressure should reduce salt usage. Pair with vegetables to balance nutrition.
