# Pistachio Sea Salt Rolls
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Bread flour, appropriate amount  
🥜 Pistachio paste, appropriate amount (kneaded into dough and for filling)  
🧈 Butter, appropriate amount (kneaded into dough)  
🧂 Salted butter, appropriate amount (cut into strips for filling)  
🌽 Corn oil, appropriate amount (for brushing edges of dough)  
🥛 Milk or water, appropriate amount  
🍚 Yeast, appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt, a pinch  
🍬 Sugar, a pinch  
⚪ White sesame seeds, appropriate amount (for coating the bottom)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔥 Oven  
🥣 Baking tray  
🧽 Kneading mat  
🪄 Rolling pin  
🖌️ Brush  
🪣 A bowl of warm water (for proofing)  
💨 Spray bottle  
🪚 Serrated knife  
🍽️ Plate for white sesame seeds

## Steps
1. Mix bread flour, pistachio paste, yeast, salt, sugar, milk, and butter, and knead into a smooth green dough.  
   **Note**: Ensure the dough develops a windowpane; video does not show kneading details.
2. Perform the first proofing of the dough. Once it has expanded in volume, divide and pre-shape into teardrop shapes, then refrigerate to relax.  
   **Key**: Sufficient relaxation time is essential; otherwise, the dough will shrink when rolled out later.
3. Cut salted butter into thin strips. Set aside pistachio paste and white sesame seeds.
4. Take one relaxed dough piece and roll it out into a long, thin triangle with the rolling pin. Roll it as long as possible to increase layers.
5. Place a strip of salted butter at the base of the triangle, and spread a small amount of pistachio paste. Brush corn oil on the two side edges of the dough to help create distinct layers after baking.
6. Roll up from the base toward the tip, place the seam side down, lightly dip the bottom into white sesame seeds, and place on the baking tray.
7. Arrange all shaped dough pieces on the baking tray, place inside the oven with a bowl of warm water underneath, and let them proof for about 1 hour until they have significantly expanded in volume and feel light and springy to the touch.
8. Preheat the oven. Evenly spray water mist onto the surface of the proofed dough pieces to create steam for a crispy crust.
9. Place the baking tray into the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes. Watch the coloring closely to avoid over-baking.
10. Once out of the oven, immediately transfer to a cooling rack to cool. Enjoy after cooling.

## Health Advice
These pistachio sea salt rolls feature a dual filling of nut paste and salted butter. It is recommended to enjoy them with unsweetened beverages. Suitable for those needing an energy boost, such as fitness enthusiasts or students. Be mindful of portion size to avoid excessive calorie intake, and adjust the temperature flexibly according to your oven’s characteristics to prevent over-browning.
