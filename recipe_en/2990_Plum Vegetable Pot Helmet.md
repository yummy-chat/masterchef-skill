# Plum Vegetable Pot Helmet  
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 All-purpose flour 1080g  
🐖 Pork (hind leg meat) 850g (ground into mince)  
🥬 Preserved plum vegetables 480g (soaked in advance and chopped)  
🧂 Spring onions 1 bunch (chopped into green onion pieces)  
🧂 Salt 10g for dough, 18g for filling, 4g for oil pastry  
🫒 Cooking oil 35g (for kneading the dough)  
🌿 Five-spice powder 5g (for filling), 4g (for oil pastry)  
🌶️ Sichuan pepper powder 5g (for filling), 4g (for oil pastry)  
🥄 Light soy sauce 35g  
🥄 Dark soy sauce 20g  
⚪ White sesame seeds appropriate amount (sprinkled on the surface of the pie)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Large bowl/mixing bowl  
🔄 Rolling pin  
⚖️ Electronic scale  
🔥 Oven  
🎨 Silicone brush  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🌯 Plastic wrap/sealable bag  

## Preparation Steps  
1. **Make the dough**: Mix 1080g flour and 10g salt with 680g warm water, add 35g cooking oil, and knead repeatedly until the surface is smooth. Evenly brush a layer of oil on the dough surface, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rest.  
**Key point**: The dough must be kneaded until smooth; applying oil prevents drying and helps relax the gluten.  

2. **Prepare the filling**: Place the soaked and chopped preserved plum vegetables (480g), ground hind leg meat (850g), salt (18g), Sichuan pepper powder (5g), five-spice powder (5g), light soy sauce (35g), dark soy sauce (20g), and a bunch of chopped spring onions in a bowl. Mix thoroughly until evenly combined and well-blended.  

3. **Make the oil pastry**: Take a small amount of flour, add salt (4g), five-spice powder (4g), and Sichuan pepper powder (4g). Heat cooking oil until very hot, pour it in, and stir into a smooth paste.  

4. **Prepare the dough piece and wrap the filling**: Divide the rested dough into about 30 small pieces. Take one piece and roll it out, spread a thin layer of oil pastry evenly, roll it up from one end, pinch the ends tightly, then press down into a flat disc. Let it rest briefly again, then roll it out into a wrapper.  
Wrap in one spoonful of the prepared plum vegetable filling, and seal the seam tightly. Gently roll the filled dough piece again, trying to make it as thin as possible.  
**Key point**: Both rolling steps should aim for thinness, which is crucial for a crispy texture after baking.  

5. **Bake**: Evenly sprinkle white sesame seeds on the surface of the rolled-out dough piece. Place it in an oven preheated to 220°C and bake for about 12 minutes.  
**Judgment**: Bake until the surface is golden brown, the edges are slightly charred, and a rich, fragrant aroma is released.  

## Health Advice  
This pot helmet has a relatively high fat content, so it is recommended to pair it with a light vegetable salad or clear soup to balance the meal. Preserved plum vegetables are rich in dietary fiber but have a high sodium content; individuals with high blood pressure should pay attention to controlling salt intake.
