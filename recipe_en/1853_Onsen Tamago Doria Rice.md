# Onsen Tamago Doria Rice
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients
🧅 White onion 200g (finely diced)
🥕 Carrot 120g (finely diced)
🥬 Celery 50g (finely diced)
🥩 Ground beef 450g
🐷 Ground pork 200g
🍅 Canned tomatoes 2 (medium-sized)
🥫 Tomato paste to taste
🧈 Butter 15g
🌾 Flour 15g
🥛 Cold milk 500ml
🧀 Cheddar cheese several slices
🍚 Rice as needed
🥚 Egg 1 per serving

## Seasonings
🌿 Black pepper to taste
🧂 Salt to taste
🧀 Grated cheese to taste

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Cast iron pot/saucepan
🥄 Kitchen spatula/spoon
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🥘 Saucepan
🥢 Whisk/stirrer
🔥 Oven
🫖 Thermos/hot water kettle
🥣 Bowl

## Instructions
1. Dice the onion, carrot, and celery into uniform small cubes.
   **Note**: White onion is recommended for its milder flavor.
2. Over medium heat, stir-fry the ground beef and ground pork, seasoning with black pepper and salt, until the meat turns golden brown.
   **Key**: The browned bits at the bottom of the pot are full of flavor; set aside the meat without washing the pot.
3. Using the remaining oil in the pot, sauté the vegetable cubes over medium heat, using their released juices to deglaze the browned bits from the pot bottom.
4. Add tomato paste, canned tomatoes, and the cooked meat. Pour in a little water, cover, and simmer over medium-low heat for 90 minutes, until the meat and vegetables are completely tender.
5. Prepare the cheese sauce: In a saucepan over low heat, melt the butter, add flour, and cook until bubbling. Gradually pour in the cold milk while stirring continuously until the sauce thickens. Add the cheddar cheese slices and stir until fully melted and smooth.
6. Make the onsen tamago: Place a room-temperature egg into a thermos, pour in approximately 72°C hot water, seal, and let steep for 1 hour. The yolk should be semi-liquid.
7. Assemble: Layer rice in a bowl, drizzle with cheese sauce, spread the meat sauce on top, smooth the surface, and sprinkle with grated cheese.
8. Place the assembled bowl into the oven and bake at the highest temperature for 3–5 minutes, until the surface is slightly charred.
9. Remove from the oven, place an onsen tamago in the center, and serve.

## Health Tips
This doria rice is rich in high-quality protein and lycopene, suitable for muscle building and weight loss; however, the cheese sauce is high in calories, so it is recommended to control the rice portion. Cutting open the onsen tamago and mixing it into the rice enhances the texture; enjoy while hot for the best flavor.
