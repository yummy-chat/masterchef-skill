# Big Lazy Dragon / Meat Dragon
Cuisine: Beijing Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Preparation Steps  
1. Prepare the dough in advance and let it rise until leavened; set aside.  
2. Prepare the raw filling: Place about one-third of the pork filling in a bowl, add minced ginger, two parts light soy sauce, and one part soybean soy sauce. Stir vigorously in one direction until the mixture is firm and elastic.  
**Key Point**: Soy sauce and soybean paste already contain salt, so no additional salt is needed.  

3. Once the raw filling is firm and elastic, drizzle in the Sichuan pepper oil and mix well again.  
**Key Point**: Always stir in the same direction; add the oil only after the filling has become firm and elastic.  

4. Cook the cooked filling: Heat oil in a wok, add the remaining two-thirds of the pork filling, and stir-fry over low heat to render out excess fat.  
- Add yellow rice wine to remove any strong smell and replenish moisture, then add minced ginger and soy sauce to stir-fry.  
   - Stir-fry until the filling is completely loose and no longer watery. Use a strainer to drain off excess oil.  
**Heat and State**: Cook over low heat throughout until the filling is loose and dry on the surface with no moisture.  

5. Combine the fillings: Pour the drained cooked filling into the bowl with the raw filling and mix well. Finally, add the finely chopped scallion and gently mix.  
**Key Point**: Avoid over-mixing after adding the scallion to prevent a pungent onion smell.  

6. Shape the meat dragon: Roll out the leavened dough into a large sheet, spread the combined filling evenly over it, and roll it up from one end into a long log. Pinch the ends tightly to seal.  
7. Steam: Add water to the steamer and heat until it is about to boil but not yet boiling. Place the meat dragon on the steaming tray, cover with the lid, and steam for 20 minutes.  
**Key Point**: Place it in the steamer when the water is about to boil but not yet boiling, which helps with a second proofing and setting.  

8. Remove and cut: After steaming, turn off the heat, take out the meat dragon, and cut it into sections with a knife. The cross-section will show a spiral layered pattern.  

## Health Advice  
This meat dragon combines raw and cooked fillings, offering a rich, savory flavor with plenty of oil, making it suitable for those with high physical exertion or needing a protein boost. However, its total calorie content is relatively high, so it is advisable to pair it with light vegetables and control the portion size per serving.
