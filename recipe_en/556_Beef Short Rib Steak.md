# Beef Short Rib Steak
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 1 piece of beef short rib steak (thick cut)  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🌶️ Black pepper to taste  
🥬 A handful of endive (for side salad)  
🌻 Vegetable oil as needed (for pan-frying)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🔥 Oven with convection mode  
🔪 Knife  
🪵 Wooden cutting board  
⚡ Electric grinder  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Remove the beef short rib steak from the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature.  
2. Heat the non-stick pan over medium-high heat until smoking, then place the steak in the pan.  
   **Note**: The pan must be hot enough before adding the steak, as high heat quickly seals in the juices.  
3. Sear each side until a crispy, caramelized crust forms on the surface.  
   **Key point**: Achieving a crispy crust is essential for a tender interior with a rich flavor.  
4. Once both sides are crusted, immediately season evenly with salt and black pepper using the grinder.  
5. Transfer the seared steak to a preheated oven and bake using convection mode.  
   **Note**: Thick cuts of short rib require oven cooking to ensure even doneness inside; pan-searing alone is insufficient. Baking time depends on steak thickness and preferred doneness.  
6. Remove the baked steak from the oven and let it rest for a moment.  
   **Key point**: Resting allows juices to redistribute evenly inside the steak, preventing excessive loss when cut.  
7. Slice the rested steak into bite-sized thick pieces and serve with washed endive salad on the side.  

## Health Tips  
Beef short rib is rich in protein and iron, making it suitable for muscle building and blood replenishment, but it is high in fat. Pairing with endive salad helps balance the richness. Searing to lock in juices and resting afterward effectively preserves the savory flavors.
