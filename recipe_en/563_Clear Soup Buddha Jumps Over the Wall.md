# Clear Soup Buddha Jumps Over the Wall
Cuisine: Fujian Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 Fish Maw 22-head fish maw tubes (pre-soaked for 6 days)
🐚 Dried Abalone Around 20-head South African dried abalone (pre-soaked for 7 days)
🦑 Sea Cucumber To taste (pre-soaked for 6 days)
🦈 Golden Hook Shark Fin To taste
🐚 Conpoy To taste
🥩 Ham To taste
🐢 Softshell Turtle About 1
🐔 Old Hen 1
🥩 Pork Ribs To taste
🐷 Pork Trotter To taste
🥩 Pork Shoulder Meat To taste
🐔 Chicken Feet To taste
🦐 Dried Shrimp To taste
🍊 Dried Tangerine Peel A little
🌶️ White Pepper A little
🍶 Yellow Wine To taste
🧂 Salt A little
🫚 Ginger To taste
🧅 Spring Onion To taste

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥘 Clay Pot or Large Soup Pot
🥄 Fine Mesh Strainer
🪣 Stainless Steel Basin
🍳 Wok
🍲 Special Buddha Jumps Over the Wall Pot (Red Ceramic Stewing Pot)
📜 Sandpaper or Jade Paper
♨️ Steamer Cabinet
⚖️ Kitchen Scale
🔪 Chef’s Knife and Cutting Board

## Cooking Steps
1.  **Prepare the Superior Broth**: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add blanched pork trotters, pork ribs, pork shoulder meat, old hen, chicken feet, ham, dried tangerine peel, and white pepper. Bring back to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 6 hours. Do not skim off the surface fat during the entire process to preserve the broth's flavor.
2.  **Refine the Broth Base**: Strain the cooked superior broth through a fine mesh strainer to remove solids. Skim off the surface fat. Mix a portion of the pure superior broth with "second broth" to create a more balanced flavor profile.
3.  **Soak the Dried Goods**:
    *   **Fish Maw**: Dry-steam for 30 minutes → Soak in ice-cold purified water, changing water every 8 hours → The next day, soak in boiling water until naturally cooled → Continue soaking in ice-cold purified water for 6 days, changing the water multiple times during this period.
    *   **Dried Abalone**: Dry-steam for 30 minutes → Soak in ice-cold purified water, change water the next day → On the 3rd day, soak in boiling water until naturally cooled → Continue soaking in ice water, changing water, and clean thoroughly. The entire process takes about 7 days.
    *   **Sea Cucumber**: Dry-steam for 30 minutes → Soak in ice-cold purified water, change water the next day → On the 3rd day, cut open and remove the internal organs, wash clean → Continue soaking in ice water and changing water, rehydrating for 6 days.
4.  **Pre-treat the Softshell Turtle**: Kill and clean the softshell turtle. Remove all fat. Scald with hot water and scrape off the outer membrane. Cut into pieces. Fat and outer membrane must be removed to ensure the soup is clear and has no off-flavor.
5.  **Pre-treat Other Ingredients**: Cut the rehydrated sea cucumber and fish maw into pieces. Cut off the hard bottom part of the dried abalone.
6.  **Blanch and Stir-fry the Softshell Turtle**: Blanch the softshell turtle pieces, then drain. In a separate wok, sauté ginger and spring onion until fragrant. Add the softshell turtle and stir-fry vigorously. Splash in some yellow wine. Stir-fry until the turtle skin tightens and aroma releases. Remove and rinse clean.
7.  **Braise the Seafood Ingredients**: In a wok, sauté ginger, spring onion ends, garlic, and dried shrimp until fragrant. Pour in the "second broth" and a little salt, bring to a boil. Add golden hook shark fin, fish maw, dried abalone, and sea cucumber in sequence, braising each until flavorful and achieving the desired tenderness.
8.  **Assemble in the Pot**: Place all the prepared ingredients into the Buddha Jumps Over the Wall pot. Pour in the mixed clear broth until it almost reaches the rim of the pot. Traditionally, a suitable amount of yellow wine can be drizzled along the rim of the pot.
9.  **Seal the Stewing Pot**: Cover with the lid. Completely seal the rim of the pot with wet sandpaper or jade paper to lock in the aroma and temperature.
10. **Steam to Finish**: Place the sealed pot into a steamer cabinet. Steam over medium heat for 3 hours.
11. **Serve**: Remove the pot from the steamer. Cut open the sealed paper and serve. The soup is golden and clear, all ingredients are soft and infused with flavor, the aroma is rich.

## Health Advice
This Clear Soup Buddha Jumps Over the Wall combines land and sea delicacies, rich in collagen and high-quality protein. Suitable for those needing nourishment or recovering energy. Note its high purine and fat content; those with gout or high blood lipids should enjoy it in small amounts.
