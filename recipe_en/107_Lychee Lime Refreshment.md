# Lychee Lime Refreshment  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍈 Lychee – appropriate amount (peeled, pitted, whole flesh intact)  
🟢 Lime – 5–6 (juice half, slice the other half)  
🌿 Reed root – a small handful (cut into sections)  
🎋 Sugarcane – 3–4 medium-length pieces (peeled and cut into sections)  
🟤 Water chestnuts – appropriate amount (peeled and sliced)  
🍃 Mint leaves – 2–3  
🌿 Rosemary – 1 small sprig  
🟡 Rock sugar – appropriate amount  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥘 Stainless steel stockpot  
🧺 Juicer  
🫙 Glass jar with lid  
🪚 Wooden cutting board, knife  
🥄 Strainer  

## Instructions  
1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the reed root and sugarcane sections. Cook over medium heat until the ingredients release their flavor.  
2. Add rock sugar and the sliced water chestnuts. Continue to boil briefly.  
   **Note**: Do not boil water chestnuts for too long, to keep them crisp and tender.  
3. Turn off the heat. Let the liquid and ingredients cool naturally to room temperature.  
   **Key**: Ensure the liquid is completely cooled, to avoid damaging subsequent ingredients and to keep the drink refreshing.  
4. Line the bottom of the glass jar with lime slices. Pour in the freshly squeezed lime juice.  
5. Using a strainer, scoop the cooled liquid along with the reed root, sugarcane, and water chestnuts into the jar. Then pour in the remaining liquid.  
6. Add the lychee flesh and mint leaves. Place a sprig of rosemary on top.  
7. Seal the jar. Refrigerate until the flavors meld before serving, for better taste.  

## Health Tips  
This is a summer beverage that helps clear heat and replenishes vitamin C. It is suitable for those prone to internal heat or looking to cut through richness. Adjust the amount of sugar based on the lychee's sweetness to avoid unnecessary extra intake.
