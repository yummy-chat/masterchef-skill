# Sweet and Spicy Roasted Whole Chicken Wings
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

🍗 **Ingredients**  
- 4 whole chicken wings  

🧂 **Seasonings**  
- 45ml light soy sauce  
- 15ml dark soy sauce  
- 10g garlic powder  
- 5g pepper  
- 10g sugar  
- 10g sweet paprika  
- 5g chili powder  
- 15ml oyster sauce  
- 20ml water  
- 10ml oil  

🔪 **Kitchen Tools**  
- Air fryer  
- Aluminum foil tray  
- Plastic wrap  
- Spoon  
- Tongs or heat-resistant gloves  

📝 **Steps**  
1. Take 4 whole chicken wings. Slit the skin between the two bones of the middle wing section, making one cut on each side to allow flavor to penetrate.  
2. Place the chicken wings in a bowl. Add all seasonings: light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, garlic powder, pepper, sugar, sweet paprika, chili powder, oyster sauce, water, and oil.  
3. Use a spoon to evenly coat the chicken wings with the sauce, focusing on the cuts in the middle wing section. Continue for about 3 minutes to ensure thorough coverage.  
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate to marinate for 120 minutes.  
5. Remove the chicken wings and arrange them in an aluminum foil tray. Pour any remaining sauce from the bowl evenly over the wings.  
6. Place the foil tray in the air fryer basket. Roast at 200°C for 25 minutes.  
   **Note**: This yields a mild spiciness. To increase heat, add more chili powder, but do not exceed 30g. If you prefer a less charred brown color, skip the step of pouring leftover sauce and roast each side for 20 minutes. Roasting time may need adjustment based on the power of your air fryer.  
7. Open the air fryer, carefully remove the foil tray, and flip the chicken wings.  
8. Continue roasting at 200°C for another 25 minutes.  
9. Remove and plate for serving.  
   **Note**: The foil tray will be extremely hot right out of the air fryer. Be sure to use tongs or heat-resistant gloves when handling.  

## Health Advice
This roasted chicken wing dish is rich in protein, but the combination of sugar and soy sauce in the sweet and spicy marinade results in a high calorie count. It is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance the meal. Suitable for moderate consumption during muscle-building phases. For weight loss, it's best to remove the skin and limit intake to no more than one pair.
