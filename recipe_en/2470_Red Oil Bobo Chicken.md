# Red Oil Bobo Chicken
Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 Whole free-range chicken (for broth and meat)
🦴 Pork leg bones, appropriate amount (for broth base)
🐾 Chicken feet, appropriate amount (for broth and soaking)
🍗 Chicken wings, appropriate amount (for broth and soaking)
🌶️ Guizhou dried chilies, 100g (for red oil)
🌶️ Small chili dried chilies, 100g (for red oil)
✨ Sichuan peppercorns, 10g (for red oil)
🌱 White sesame seeds, appropriate amount (added to broth base)
🥒 Lotus root, appropriate amount (sliced, for skewers)
🥔 Potatoes, appropriate amount (sliced, for skewers)
🥦 Broccoli, appropriate amount (for skewers)
🍖 Other meat, appropriate amount (such as quail eggs, tripe, optional)
🍶 Cooking wine, appropriate amount (for deodorizing broth)
🧅 Scallions, appropriate amount (for deodorizing broth)
🧄 Ginger, appropriate amount (for deodorizing broth)
🧂 Salt, 2 tbsp (for seasoning broth) + appropriate amount (for blanching)
🧂 Chicken bouillon, 1 tbsp (for seasoning broth)
🧂 MSG, 1 tbsp (for seasoning broth)
🌿 Five-spice powder, 10g (for red oil) + 1 tbsp (for seasoning broth)
🍬 Sugar, 1 tbsp (for seasoning broth)
🫒 Sichuan pepper oil, 1 tbsp (for seasoning broth)
🫒 Flower pepper oil, 1 tbsp (for seasoning broth)
🌶️ Homemade red oil, 2-3 tbsp (for seasoning broth, homemade)
🫒 Cooking oil, appropriate amount (for frying chilies and pouring hot oil)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍲 Large soup pot (for boiling chicken and bone broth)
🥘 Small pot/frying pan (for frying chili segments)
🥄 Slotted spoon (for removing ingredients and skimming foam)
🌀 Food processor (for grinding chilies)
🥣 Large bowl (for mixing red oil broth base)
🫙 Glass jar (for storing red oil)
📌 Bamboo skewers (for skewering ingredients)

## Steps
1.  **Prepare the broth base**: Place the whole chicken, pork leg bones, chicken feet, and chicken wings in a pot with cold water. Add cooking wine, scallions, and ginger to remove odors. Bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, then reduce to low heat and simmer slowly.
    **Note**: Start with cold water and skim the foam thoroughly to ensure a clear broth.

2.  **Remove ingredients**: Remove chicken wings and chicken feet after 15 minutes of cooking; remove the whole chicken after 25 minutes, and immediately immerse it in ice water to firm up the meat.
    **Key point**: The cold water bath helps seal in the meat juices and improves texture.

3.  **Handle the chicken**: Debone the whole chicken and tear the meat into small pieces; return the bones to the pot and continue simmering for 30 minutes to enrich the broth.

4.  **Make the red oil**:
    - Soak chilies and Sichuan peppercorns in hot water for 5 minutes, drain, and cut into segments.
    - Heat oil in a wok, add the chili segments. Stir-fry over high heat to dry out the moisture, then reduce to low heat and stir-fry until the color turns red and fragrant.
    - Transfer the fried chilies to a food processor and grind into a coarse powder. Mix in 10g five-spice powder.
    - Pour hot oil over the chili powder in three stages: the first pour with the highest oil temperature, wait 2 minutes for the second pour, then another 2 minutes for the third. Stir well after each pour.
    - Cover and let sit for 5 minutes. Once cool, transfer to a glass jar and seal for later use.

5.  **Prepare the skewers**: Cook meats in advance; thread vegetables onto bamboo skewers, blanch in boiling salted water for a few seconds, then quickly remove to maintain a crisp and tender texture.
    **Note**: Blanch vegetables briefly to avoid them becoming mushy.

6.  **Season the broth**: In a large bowl, combine 2 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp chicken bouillon, 1 tbsp MSG, 1 tbsp five-spice powder, an appropriate amount of white sesame seeds, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp Sichuan pepper oil, 1 tbsp flower pepper oil, and 2-3 tbsp homemade red oil. Pour in hot chicken broth and stir well.
    **Tip**: Taste first and adjust seasoning quantities based on saltiness.

7.  **Soak to infuse flavor**: Submerge all skewers in the seasoned red oil broth, ensuring the ingredients are completely covered. Let sit for 20-30 minutes until fully infused with flavor.
    **Note**: A sufficient soaking time is essential for the ingredients to fully absorb the flavors.

## Health Advice
Red oil bobo chicken offers balanced nutrition, with chicken providing high-quality protein and vegetables supplying vitamins. It is suitable for gatherings, but be mindful of the meat-to-vegetable ratio. Caution is advised for those with high oil and salt intake; people with conditions like high blood pressure, high blood sugar, or high cholesterol should limit consumption of the broth and focus on vegetable skewers.
