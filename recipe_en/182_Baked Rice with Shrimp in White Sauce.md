# Baked Rice with Shrimp in White Sauce
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Shrimp 8-10 pieces (peeled, deveined, and split along the back)
🍄 White mushrooms to taste (sliced)
🧅 Onion to taste (chopped)
🥓 Bacon or ham 2-3 slices (chopped)
🥦 Broccoli to taste (cut into small florets, blanched)
🍚 Rice to taste (cooked)
🧀 Cheese to taste (grated)
🥚 Egg 1 piece (optional, for making batter water)
🌾 Flour to taste (mixed with water and egg to make batter water)
🥛 Milk to taste
🧈 Butter to taste
🧂 Salt to taste
🧂 Freshly ground black pepper to taste
🫒 Vegetable oil a little (for frying shrimp)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🥄 Slotted spoon
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
♨️ Air fryer or oven
🍚 Rice cooker
🥣 Ceramic baking dish
🥚 Whisk or wooden spoon
🧀 Grater

## Steps
1. **Prepare Shrimp**
   Freeze fresh shrimp for at least 2 hours in advance. Remove, peel off the heads and shells, split along the back to check and remove any remaining veins, rinse, and drain.
   **Key Point**: Freezing makes the shells firmer and easier to peel; splitting the back ensures no grit remains.

2. **Prep Ingredients and Pre-treatment**
   - Slice mushrooms, chop onion, chop bacon, cut broccoli into small florets, blanch, and drain.
   - Mix flour with enough water to make a smooth batter water, set aside.

3. **Fry Shrimp**
   Add a little vegetable oil to a non-stick pan, heat, then add shrimp. Fry until both sides turn red and curl, then remove and set aside.

4. **Sauté Aromatics**
   Wipe the pan clean, add butter, melt, then add chopped onion and bacon. Sauté until fragrant.
   **Note**: Wiping the pan clean prevents burning and flavor contamination.

5. **Make White Sauce**
   Pour the batter water and milk into the pan together. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and coats a spoon. Season with salt and black pepper.
   **Note**: Stir continuously to prevent lumps.

6. **Combine Main Ingredients**
   - If mushrooms were not fried earlier, sauté them in a separate pan with a little butter until cooked, then add to the white sauce.
   - Return the fried shrimp to the white sauce pan, mix well, and turn off the heat.

7. **Assemble Baked Rice**
   Place rice at the bottom of a baking dish, arrange blanched broccoli on top, pour the white sauce with mushrooms and shrimp over it, and evenly sprinkle grated cheese on the surface.

8. **Bake**
   Place in a preheated air fryer or oven at 200°C (392°F) until the cheese on top is fully melted and has golden spots, then remove.
   **Time Note**: Depends on the device; monitor the browning.

## Health Tips
This baked rice in white sauce is rich in protein and calcium, suitable for those needing calcium or muscle gain. However, cheese and butter are high in calories, so pair with a portion of stir-fried vegetables for balanced nutrition. Shrimp and broccoli provide high-quality protein and dietary fiber; control the rice portion to avoid excess carbs.
