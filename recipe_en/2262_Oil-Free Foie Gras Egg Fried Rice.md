# Oil-Free Foie Gras Egg Fried Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Rice, about 1 small bowl (cooked)  
🦢 Foie gras, about 1 piece (diced)  
🥔 Chinese yam, about 1 piece (peeled and sliced)  
🥚 Egg, 1 (raw)  
🧂 Black pepper, to taste  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥣 Bowl  
📡 Microwave oven  
🍽️ Plate  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps
1. Peel the Chinese yam and slice it thinly; dice the foie gras into small cubes.
2. Place the rice into a bowl, then layer the yam slices and foie gras cubes on top in order.
3. Sprinkle with an appropriate amount of black pepper, and crack a raw egg into the bowl.
4. Cover the bowl with a plate turned upside down, place it in the microwave, and heat on medium-high power for about 2-3 minutes.
   **Note**: Ensure the bowl is tightly covered to prevent ingredients from splattering during heating.
5. After heating, remove the bowl and stir thoroughly with chopsticks, mixing the egg liquid, rendered fat from the foie gras, mashed yam, and rice evenly until the rice takes on a uniform golden-yellow color.

## Health Advice  
This oil-free foie gras egg fried rice is made using a microwave, eliminating the need to clean a wok. However, the natural fats from the foie gras are sufficient to make the rice rich and fragrant, making it a good occasional indulgence. The Chinese yam adds dietary fiber, helping to balance the burden of saturated fats from the foie gras, but it is recommended for those with high cholesterol to consume in moderation.
