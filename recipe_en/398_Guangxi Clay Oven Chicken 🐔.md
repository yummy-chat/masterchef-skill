# Guangxi Clay Oven Chicken 🐔
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 1 three-yellow chicken (about 800g)
🧅 Half an onion
🟢 A bunch of spring onions
🥄 7g sand ginger powder
🥄 5g thirteen-spice powder
🧂 Half a spoon of chicken bouillon
🥄 2 spoons of light soy sauce
🥄 1 spoon of dark soy sauce
🍶 1 spoon of cooking wine
🦪 1 spoon of oyster sauce
🍬 1 teaspoon of sugar

## Utensils and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🔩 Aluminum foil
♨️ Air fryer

## Cooking Steps
1. Soak the whole chicken in warm water to remove any gamey smell and improve the skin color.
**Note**: Use warm water for soaking.

2. Shape the chicken: Thread one chicken wing through the chicken's mouth, wrap the other wing tightly around the neck, then tuck the chicken feet into the chicken's belly to prevent it from falling apart during cooking and to achieve a nicer appearance.
3. Prepare the marinade: In a bowl, combine 7g sand ginger powder, 5g thirteen-spice powder, half a spoon of chicken bouillon, 2 spoons of light soy sauce, 1 spoon of dark soy sauce, 1 spoon of cooking wine, 1 spoon of oyster sauce, and 1 teaspoon of sugar. Mix well.
**Key**: Sand ginger powder is the core seasoning; if unavailable, you can substitute it with salt-baked chicken powder.
4. Evenly coat the chicken inside and out with the marinade, ensuring every part is covered.
5. Marinate for 1 to 2 hours; the longer it marinates, the more flavorful it becomes. It is recommended to marinate overnight.
6. Stuff half an onion and a bunch of spring onions into the chicken's belly to help shape it, remove any gamey smell, and enhance the aroma.
7. Wrap the whole chicken tightly with aluminum foil to seal in the moisture.
8. Place in the air fryer and cook at 200°C for 20 minutes.
9. Take it out, flip it over, and cook again at 200°C for another 20 minutes, until the skin is a deep golden red and the meat easily separates from the bones.
**Judgment**: The chicken skin should be crispy and wrinkled, and the meat should come off the bone with a gentle pull.

## Health Advice
This clay oven chicken is replicated using an air fryer, resulting in less oil and tender meat, making it suitable for satisfying cravings during weight loss. However, the marinade contains a considerable amount of chicken bouillon, sugar, and salt, so it is recommended to pair it with plenty of leafy green vegetables for balance.
