# Date and Walnut Cake
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫒 Red dates 100g (pitted)
🌾 Cake flour 100g (sifted)
🥚 Eggs about 100g (about two, whole egg liquid)
🌽 Corn oil 80g (neutral-flavored vegetable oil)
🌰 Cooked walnuts 50g (chopped)
🧂 Salt 1g
🍞 Baking powder 2g

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
✂️ Scissors
♨️ Steamer
🔌 Electric hand mixer
🪥 Sieve
🥄 Spatula
🍞 Loaf pan (or rectangular mold)
📜 Parchment paper
🔥 Oven
🌬️ Cooling rack
🫙 Plastic wrap

## Instructions
1. Use scissors to cut the red dates open and remove the pits. Place them in a steamer. Once the water boils, reduce the heat to low and steam for about 20 minutes, until the dates are soft and tender.
   **Key point**: The dates must be steamed rather than boiled, which helps the cake have a drier texture.
2. Remove the steamed dates and mash them into a paste. Add salt, whole egg liquid, and corn oil to the date paste. Mix all wet ingredients until well combined.
3. Use an electric hand mixer on low speed to beat the date mixture until the color lightens, the volume expands slightly, and the texture becomes thick.
4. Sift the cake flour, add the baking powder, and stir with a hand whisk or chopsticks until evenly mixed.
5. Pour the dry ingredients into the beaten date mixture. Use a spatula to fold until no dry flour remains and the batter is smooth and lump-free.
6. Add the chopped walnuts and fold until evenly distributed.
7. Pour the batter into a loaf pan lined with parchment paper. Smooth the surface with a spoon. Gently tap the pan twice on the counter to release large air bubbles. Use a spoon to create a deep trench in the center of the batter, which helps form a natural crack during baking.
8. Preheat the oven. Place the pan on the lower-middle rack and bake at an actual temperature of 160°C for about 35 minutes.
9. Once the cake is out of the oven, immediately drop the pan onto the counter from a height of about 10 cm. Quickly unmold and place the cake on a cooling rack to cool completely.
10. When the cake is completely cool, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
    **Key point**: Refrigerating to allow the oils to redistribute is a crucial step for enhancing flavor and texture and must not be skipped.

## Health Tips
Red dates provide iron, and walnuts are good for the brain. Although this cake is not low in calories, it has high nutritional density and is suitable as a breakfast or afternoon tea treat. Corn oil and the natural sweetness of the date paste eliminate the need for additional sugar. It can be consumed in small amounts by diabetic patients.
