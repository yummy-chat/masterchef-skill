# Oil-Blanched Cabbage
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥬 Oval cabbage (flat cabbage) to taste (torn into pieces by hand)
🫚 Ginger 1 small piece (smashed and minced)
🌶️ Dried chili peppers to taste
🫒 Cooking oil generous amount (for frying) + a little base oil
🧂 Light soy sauce 8 grams
🦪 Oyster sauce 5 grams
🧂 Chicken bouillon a pinch

## Kitchenware and Equipment
♨️ Wok
🥄 Iron spatula
🥢 Slotted spoon
🔪 Cutting board and knife
🔥 Induction cooker (or stove)

## Preparation Steps
1. Pour generous oil into the wok and heat to 50% hot. Add the hand-torn cabbage pieces and fry over medium heat until just cooked.
   **Note**: Generous oil ensures even heating for the cabbage, quickly cooking it through and removing raw flavor.
2. Remove the fried cabbage with a slotted spoon, making sure to drain the oil thoroughly.
   **Key Point**: The oil must be drained completely, or the dish will be greasy and affect the texture.
3. Leave a little base oil in the wok, reduce to low heat, add the minced ginger and dried chili peppers, and stir-fry until fragrant. Then add oyster sauce and light soy sauce, stirring well to release the aroma.
   **Note**: The aromatics need to be stir-fried slowly over low heat to avoid burning.
4. Add the drained cabbage, sprinkle in a pinch of chicken bouillon, turn to very low heat, and quickly toss to combine evenly. Serve immediately.
   **Key Point**: Only mix until the seasonings are evenly coated; no need to stir-fry for long, to keep the cabbage tender and flavorful.

## Health Advice
Cabbage quickly fried in generous oil becomes fragrant and tender, but it is essential to drain the oil thoroughly to reduce greasiness. Suitable for those seeking flavor; those on a weight-loss diet should consume in moderation.
