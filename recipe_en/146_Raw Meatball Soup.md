# Raw Meatball Soup
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Season Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork front leg meat (fat-to-lean ratio 3:7)
🥚 Egg white (from one egg)
🥔 Potato starch (40g)
🍄 Wood ear mushrooms (适量)
🌼 Daylily buds (适量)
🌿 Small scallions (适量)
🍜 Soaked vermicelli (适量)
🌱 Cilantro (适量)
🧂 Salt (适量)
🌶️ White pepper powder (适量)
🐔 Chicken powder (适量)
🫒 Sesame oil (3-5 drops)
🫘 Cooked soybean oil (适量)
💬 Ginger-scallion-Sichuan peppercorn water (80ml per 500g minced meat)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Knife
🍳 Pot
🥣 Bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the pork front leg meat, maintain a fat-to-lean ratio of 3:7. Tenderize the meat by pounding with the back of a knife, then repeatedly chop and pound into minced meat.
2. For every 500g of minced meat, add 6g of salt and 1g of white pepper powder, mix evenly by hand.
3. Gradually add the ginger-scallion-Sichuan peppercorn water, stirring in the same direction continuously to allow the meat filling to fully absorb the liquid.
**Key Point**: Add the Sichuan peppercorn water in batches and stir thoroughly to ensure the meat filling absorbs enough moisture.

4. Add the egg white, continue stirring in the same direction until well combined.
5. Add 40g of potato starch, stir well.
6. Add cooked soybean oil, mix evenly to maintain tenderness and elasticity of the meat filling.
7. Bring water to a boil in a pot, then reduce to low heat and maintain a gentle simmer. Use your hand to form and add meatballs.
**Note**: When poaching meatballs, keep the water temperature low and maintain a gentle simmer.

8. After all meatballs float to the surface, cook on low heat for 1 minute.
9. Place soaked vermicelli, wood ear mushrooms, daylily buds, and small scallions at the bottom of a bowl. Then season with salt, white pepper powder, and chicken powder as a base.
10. Pour the cooked meatballs and broth into the bowl, drizzle 3-5 drops of sesame oil, and top with a small piece of cilantro.

## Health Advice
This meatball soup is high in protein and low in fat, suitable for those who need nutritional supplementation without gaining weight. Hand-chopped meatballs are more tender and bouncy, but watch the salt content; those with high blood pressure can reduce salt appropriately. The wood ear mushrooms and daylily buds in the soup are rich in dietary fiber, aiding digestion.
