# Thick Omelette Sandwich
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Eggs, about 3 (fully beaten)
🍞 Sliced white bread, 2 slices
🥬 Lettuce, 1-2 leaves (washed and drained)
🍖 Ham, 1 slice
🫕 Mayonnaise, as needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🥢 Wooden spatula
🔪 Knife
🪵 Cutting board
🫙 Plastic wrap

## Steps
1. Beat the eggs thoroughly until smooth to form the egg mixture.
2. Preheat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat, then reduce to medium-low and pour in the egg mixture.
3. When the bottom is set and the surface is still runny, use a wooden spatula to gently push the uncooked egg mixture from the edges toward the center.
4. Once most of the egg mixture has set into a round shape, roll the egg omelette from one side and push it to the edge of the pan.
5. Cover the pan with a lid and let it steam over residual heat for 30 seconds to 1 minute until fully cooked through, forming a thick omelette.
6. Take one slice of bread, spread an appropriate amount of mayonnaise, then layer with lettuce, ham slices, and the thick omelette. Top with the other slice of bread.
7. Tightly wrap the entire sandwich in plastic wrap and press down gently to set the shape.
8. Cut the wrapped sandwich in half with a knife and serve.

## Health Tips
This thick omelette sandwich is rich in high-quality protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable for breakfast or post-workout energy replenishment. Mayonnaise and ham are high in sodium, so it is recommended to pair with unsweetened tea or choose low-sodium versions of the ingredients.
