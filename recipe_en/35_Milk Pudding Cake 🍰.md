# Milk Pudding Cake 🍰
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥛 Milk 500ml
🍬 White sugar 30g
🌽 Cornstarch 50g
🥚 Egg yolks 2
🧀 Cheese slices 2

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥄 Whisk
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (used as a mold)
🔪 Knife

## Steps
1. Add milk, white sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks to a non-stick pan. Whisk thoroughly until smooth and light yellow, with no lumps.
   **Note**: Ensure all base ingredients are fully mixed before heating to avoid lumps.
2. Heat over medium heat, continuously stirring with a whisk.
   **Key**: Do not stop stirring throughout the process to prevent burning or clumping. Cook until the mixture thickens significantly, and clear lines remain visible when the whisk is drawn through, then remove from heat.
3. While hot, add cheese slices. Use residual heat to stir until fully melted and the milk batter is smooth.
   **Note**: If residual heat is insufficient, reheat briefly over very low heat while stirring, but avoid high heat.
4. Pour the cooked milk batter into a clean stainless steel bowl, smoothing the surface with a spoon.
   **Tip**: For easier unmolding, you can lightly brush the bowl’s inner surface with a thin layer of flavorless vegetable oil, though this is optional.
5. Place the bowl in the refrigerator and chill until the milk batter is fully set.
   **Doneness check**: The surface is smooth, non-sticky when lightly pressed with a finger, and the cake is springy. Setting time varies; typically takes several hours.
6. Remove the set milk pudding cake from the bowl. Invert the bowl onto a plate to unmold, then cut into triangular pieces with a knife. Serve immediately.
   **Note**: Unmold gently and steadily; the cake should slide out intact without additional handling.

## Health Notes
Milk is rich in calcium, making it suitable for children and the elderly needing calcium supplementation. However, egg yolks and cheese slices add fat, so consume in moderation. The taste is best when chilled; those with weak spleen or stomach should avoid eating too much.
