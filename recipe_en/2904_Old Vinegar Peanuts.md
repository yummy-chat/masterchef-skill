# Old Vinegar Peanuts
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥜 Peanuts appropriate amount (washed, peeling recommended)  
🧅 Purple onion half (cut into small cubes)  
🌶️ Hot pepper/green pepper appropriate amount (remove veins, cut into small cubes)  
🌿 Cilantro 1 small bunch (chopped) + extra bunch (for boiling the sauce)  
🧄 Garlic 1 clove (whole) + appropriate amount (minced)  
🍬 White sugar 40g  
🥄 Wei Ji Xian soy sauce 16g  
🦪 Oyster sauce 18g  
🥃 Hengshun 4.5-degree aromatic vinegar 40g  
💧 Water small amount (for sauce preparation)  
🛢️ Cooking oil appropriate amount (for frying peanuts)  
🧂 Cooked white sesame seeds appropriate amount (for garnish)  
⭐ Star anise 1 piece (for boiling the sauce)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🪣 Stainless steel bowl  
🍳 Snowflake pot/non-stick pan  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🔥 Gas stove  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥢 Wooden spatula/chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare peanuts**: Wash the peanuts clean; it is recommended to remove the peanut skin to prevent them from becoming soft when mixed. If troublesome, the skin can be left on.
2. **Prepare sweet and sour sauce**: Take a bowl, add white sugar, Wei Ji Xian soy sauce, oyster sauce, aromatic vinegar, and a small amount of water. Stir until the white sugar is completely dissolved.
3. **Cut ingredients**: Dice the purple onion into small cubes; remove the veins from the hot pepper and cut into small cubes; chop the cilantro and garlic into mince respectively.
4. **Fry peanuts**: Pour cooking oil into a cold pan, add peanuts and 1 whole garlic clove. Fry over low heat throughout. When the garlic clove turns deep golden brown, the peanuts should be crispy. Remove and let cool completely.
5. **Boil sweet and sour sauce**: Pour the prepared sweet and sour sauce into the pan, add star anise and one bunch of cilantro. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce to low-medium heat and simmer. After the aroma is released, remove the spices. Continue to simmer until the sauce thickens and can be pulled into thin strands. Turn off the heat and let cool completely.
6. **Mix**: After the peanuts and sweet and sour sauce have completely cooled, place the peanuts, onion cubes, hot pepper cubes, minced cilantro, and minced garlic into a bowl. Pour in the sweet and sour sauce and mix quickly.
7. **Plate**: Transfer the mixed dish to a serving plate. Sprinkle with cooked white sesame seeds and enjoy.

## Health Advice  
This old vinegar peanut dish is sweet and sour, appetizing. Peanuts are rich in unsaturated fatty acids, making them suitable as a side dish to cut through grease. However, the frying process increases the calorie content, so consumption should be moderated. Diabetic patients are advised to reduce the sugar amount and replace it with a sugar substitute.
