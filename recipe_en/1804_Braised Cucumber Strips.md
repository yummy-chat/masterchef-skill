# Braised Cucumber Strips
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥒 1 cucumber (choose a straight one)  
🧂 About 1 spoonful of salt (for marinating and drawing out water)  
🌶️适量 Sichuan chili peppers (sliced into rings)  
🧄适量 garlic (sliced into pieces)  
🫗适量 soy sauce (not too much)  
🥃适量 white vinegar (ratio with sugar 1:1.5)  
🍬 White sugar (1.5 times the amount of white vinegar)  
🧂适量 Sichuan peppercorns (start in cold or warm oil)  
🌶️适量 dried chili peppers (cut into segments)  
🫒适量 cooking oil (heated for braising)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl (for marinating and seasoning)  
🥄 Chopsticks or mixing spoon  
🍳 Small pot or wok (for heating oil)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Cut off both ends of the cucumber, split it lengthwise down the middle, and use a knife to remove the core.  
   **Note**: The core is soft and releases water easily; removing it makes the final dish crisper.  
2. Cut the cored cucumber into strips about finger-length, and place them in a bowl.  
3. Add one spoonful of salt, mix well, and let rest for 10 minutes to draw out water.  
4. After marinating, add clean water to the bowl, gently rinse to remove excess salt, then remove and thoroughly drain.  
   **Note**: Rinsing prevents the dish from being too salty; draining is the basis for later flavor absorption.  
5. To the drained cucumber strips, add soy sauce, white vinegar, white sugar, Sichuan chili pepper rings, and garlic slices, mixing well with chopsticks.  
   **Note**: Control the amount of soy sauce to avoid dark color; add sugar proportionally to balance sweet and sour.  
6. Pour cooking oil into the pot, add Sichuan peppercorns, and fry over low heat until the fragrance is fully released and the color darkens.  
7. Immediately after the peppercorns are fragrant, add the dried chili pepper segments, fry for a few seconds, then quickly pour the hot Sichuan peppercorn and chili oil, along with the oil, over the mixed cucumber strips.  
   **Key Point**: This is the critical step of "braising"; the oil must be hot, and the action must be fast, using high heat to release the aroma of garlic, chili, and all other ingredients.  
8. Mix well immediately, then serve.  

## Health Tips  
This braised cucumber strip dish is low in fat and crispy, suitable for weight loss or summer appetite stimulation. However, since salt is used during marinating, soy sauce should not be added excessively to avoid high sodium intake.
