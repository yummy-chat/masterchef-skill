# Cumin Sausage Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍜 Instant noodles 1 block (single serving)
🌭 Sausage about 1 piece (15–20 cm long)
🥔 Potato about 1 piece (medium, cut into 1.5 cm cubes)
🧅 Scallion as needed (chopped)
🧄 Garlic about 3–5 cloves (minced)

## Seasonings
🌶️ Chili flakes as needed (for stir-frying and sauce)
🌿 Ground cumin as needed (core flavor)
🧂 Salt a pinch (only for seasoning stir-fried ingredients)
🤍 White sesame seeds as needed (added to sauce for aroma)
🧄 Minced garlic as needed (placed on top of dry sauce ingredients, then drizzled with hot oil)
🫒 Hot oil as needed (to bloom the spices)
🍅 Ketchup as needed (for sauce)
🥫 Light soy sauce as needed (for sauce)
🦪 Oyster sauce as needed (for sauce)
🍬 White sugar as needed (for sauce)
🍶 Rice vinegar as needed (for sauce)
🫗 Sesame oil as needed (for sauce)

## Utensils & Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, chef’s knife (for prepping ingredients)
🍳 Frying pan (for frying potatoes and sausage)
🥢 Chopsticks (for turning ingredients)
🥣 Bowl, spoon (for mixing sauce)
🥣 Large bowl (for tossing noodles)
🧤 Disposable gloves (for mixing noodles by hand)

## Instructions
1.  Remove the casing from the sausage, then score the surface in a crosshatch pattern, cutting about halfway through without slicing all the way.
2.  Peel the potato and cut it into cubes approximately 1.5 cm on each side.
3.  Chop the scallion into small pieces and mince the garlic.
4.  Pour cooking oil into a frying pan, enough to cover about one-third of the potato cubes. Once the oil is hot, add the potato cubes. Fry over medium-low heat until the surface is golden and the inside is fully cooked.
   **Key point**: Keep the heat at medium-low throughout the entire frying process.
5.  In the same pan, add the scored sausage pieces. Fry over medium-low heat until the cuts fully open and the surface turns a bright red.
6.  Add chili flakes, ground cumin, and a pinch of salt to the pan. Stir-fry until evenly combined. Finally, sprinkle with chopped scallion, give it a quick toss, then remove and set aside.
7.  In a bowl, place chili flakes, ground cumin, white sesame seeds, and minced garlic. Heat a small amount of oil until smoking, then pour it over the seasonings to bloom the aromas. Add ketchup, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, white sugar, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. Stir well to make the sauce.
8.  Cook the instant noodles according to package instructions. Drain well and transfer to a large bowl.
9.  Pour the prepared sauce over the noodles, then add the stir-fried cumin sausage and potatoes. Put on disposable gloves and toss everything together until evenly coated. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice
This noodle dish is relatively high in fat and carbohydrates, making it suitable as an occasional treat rather than a daily staple. Consider serving it with a side of stir-fried greens or cucumber salad to balance the nutrition. Both sausage and instant noodles are high in sodium, so individuals with high blood pressure should adjust salt intake accordingly.
