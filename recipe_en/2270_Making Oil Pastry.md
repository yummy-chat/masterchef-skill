# Making Oil Pastry
Cuisine: Lu Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 Flour 100g  
🌿 Five-spice powder 3g (homemade recipe: star anise 15g, cinnamon 10g, fennel seeds 10g, Sichuan pepper 8g, bay leaves 2g, mixed and ground into powder)  
🧂 Salt 10g  
🫒 Rapeseed oil 120g  
🧅 Scallion,适量 (sliced)  
🫚 Ginger,适量 (sliced)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Ladle  
🥣 Bowl  
🕸️ Strainer  
🥄 Spoon  

## Steps  
1. Pour rapeseed oil into the wok, heat over high heat until the oil is fully cooked and emits blue smoke to remove the raw oil flavor.  
2. Turn off the heat, let the oil temperature drop slightly, then add sliced scallion and ginger. Use the residual heat to fry them until dry and yellow, infusing the oil with fragrance.  
3. Remove the fried scallion and ginger with a strainer and discard them.  
4. Place the wok back on the stove without turning on the heat. Allow the oil temperature to naturally drop to about 30-40% hot. Gradually sprinkle in the flour while continuously stirring with a ladle until fully combined without any dry flour or lumps.  
**Key point**: The oil temperature must not be too high, or the flour will burn upon contact; stir continuously.  

5. Turn on low heat, add five-spice powder and salt, and continue stirring non-stop until the flour is fully cooked, turns slightly yellow, and the aroma is fully released.  
**Key point**: Maintain low heat throughout, and keep stirring the bottom to prevent burning; once the flour is cooked and the aroma is released, remove from heat immediately.  

6. Pour the cooked oil pastry into a bowl. The resulting oil pastry has a bright yellow color and rich aroma, suitable for making oil pastry cakes, baked flatbreads, and other pastries.  

## Health Advice  
This oil pastry is high in calories, suitable for those needing quick energy replenishment, but it has a high fat content, so it is recommended to pair it with vegetables for a balanced diet. People with high blood pressure should watch their salt intake and may reduce the amount of salt appropriately.
