# Fried Enoki Mushrooms
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Cookware and Equipment  
🥣 Bowl (for preparing the egg wash)  
🥘 Metal tray (for holding enoki mushrooms and cornstarch)  
🥢 Chopsticks (for testing oil temperature and turning ingredients)  
🍳 Dark-colored wok (for deep frying)  

## Steps  
1. **Prepare the egg wash**: Crack the eggs into a bowl, add light soy sauce, salt, and chicken bouillon, and whisk until well combined.  
2. **Prepare the enoki mushrooms**: Wash the enoki mushrooms, trim off the roots, and slice into thin pieces.  
3. **Coat the mushrooms**: Evenly dip the sliced enoki mushrooms into the egg wash, then coat them with a layer of cornstarch. Place them on the tray for later use.  
4. **Test oil temperature**: Pour enough oil into the wok and heat over high heat. Insert chopsticks into the oil; when fine bubbles rapidly form around the chopsticks, the oil has reached the right temperature.  
5. **First fry**: Place the coated enoki mushroom pieces one by one into the oil. Fry over high heat for about 3 minutes until the surface turns golden and set, then remove and drain the oil.  
6. **Second fry**: Raise the oil temperature again, add the enoki mushrooms, and fry for about 20 seconds until the outer layer becomes crispy and fragrant. Immediately remove from the oil.  
7. **Plate and season**: Arrange the fried enoki mushrooms on a plate and evenly sprinkle BBQ seasoning over them. Serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
Enoki mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber and B vitamins. Frying increases the calorie content, so it is recommended to pair them with a vegetable salad to balance the diet. This dish is suitable for young people who enjoy a crispy texture. Those managing sugar intake should consume in moderation.
