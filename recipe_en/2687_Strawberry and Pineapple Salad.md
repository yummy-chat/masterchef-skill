# Strawberry and Pineapple Salad
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Dish  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍓 Strawberries, as needed (hulled, cut into pieces)  
🍍 Pineapple, as needed (cut into pieces)  
🍈 Passion fruit, 2 (extract the pulp)  
🍯 Honey or syrup, 2 tablespoons  
🧂 Salt, a pinch  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Bowl (glass or ceramic)  
🥄 Spoon  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
❄️ Refrigerator  

## Preparation Steps
1. Hull the strawberries and cut them into evenly sized pieces; cut the pineapple into pieces and set aside.
2. Place the strawberry pieces in a bowl and add the passion fruit pulp, honey or syrup, and a very small pinch of salt.  
   **Key point**: Use an extremely small amount of salt, only to enhance the flavor and balance the sweetness and sourness; do not overdo it.
3. Stir the strawberry mixture continuously with a spoon for about 2 minutes, until the surface of the strawberries becomes glossy and shiny, and the juice is evenly coated.  
   **Doneness check**: The strawberry surface should appear glossy, with the juice evenly coating them.
4. Add the pineapple pieces to the bowl and gently toss to combine.  
   **Key point**: Add the pineapple later to prevent it from releasing too much water too early and affecting the texture.
5. Cover or seal the bowl, and refrigerate for several hours or more.  
   **Key point**: Chilling allows the flavors of the strawberries and pineapple to penetrate and meld, resulting in a better taste.

## Health Tips
Strawberries and pineapple are rich in vitamin C; passion fruit adds flavor. This dish is suitable for cooling down in summer. Be mindful of the syrup amount; diabetic patients should reduce the added sweetener.
