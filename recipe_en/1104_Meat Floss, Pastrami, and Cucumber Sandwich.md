# Meat Floss, Pastrami, and Cucumber Sandwich
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Eggs, as needed (beaten into egg wash)
🍞 Baguette bread, 2 slices (cut into long strips)
🥩 Pastrami, a few slices
🥒 Cucumber, 1 (partially shredded, partially sliced for plating)
🥦 Broccoli, as needed (washed, for plating)
🍖 Meat floss (pork sung), as needed
🟡 Yellow mustard, as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🔪 Knife
🪓 Cutting board
🥒 Vegetable peeler
🥄 Spatula
🍽️ Plate

## Instructions
1. Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Pour in the beaten egg wash, tilt the pan to spread it evenly across the bottom. Fry until both sides are golden and fully set, then remove and set aside.
   **Key point**: Keep the egg wash thickness as even as possible to avoid overly thick areas.
2. Lay the cooked egg crepe flat on a cutting board. Sprinkle meat floss evenly over the surface.
   **Key point**: Distribute the meat floss evenly to ensure every bite has a layered texture.
3. Place two strips of baguette bread on top of the meat floss-covered egg crepe. Wrap the bread strips with the egg crepe, pressing gently to ensure the egg crepe adheres tightly to the bread.
4. Layer several slices of pastrami in the center on top of the egg-wrapped bread, spreading them flat.
5. Using a vegetable peeler, directly shave cucumber strips over the pastrami, covering the surface evenly.
6. Squeeze an appropriate amount of yellow mustard over the cucumber strips, adjusting the quantity to taste.
7. Using a sharp knife, cut the assembled sandwich in half. Place the halves in a plate with the cut side facing up.
8. Cut the remaining cucumber into strips and arrange them along with the washed broccoli on the edge of the plate as garnish.
   **Key point**: The plating should be simple and neat; the broccoli is purely decorative.

## Health Advice
This meat floss, pastrami, and cucumber sandwich is rich in protein, making it suitable for post-workout energy replenishment or a quick lunch. Note that cucumber shreds should be prepared and consumed immediately to avoid releasing excess water and affecting the texture.
