# Refined Lard
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐖 Pork leaf fat (caul fat) about 2500 grams
🧅 Scallion 250 grams (cut into small sections)
🫚 Ginger 150 grams (sliced)
🧂 Edible salt about 3 grams (placed at the bottom of the earthenware jar)
🫘 Green Sichuan pepper to taste (placed at the bottom of the earthenware jar)
🍶 Cooking wine to taste (added during the second blanching)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪓 Cutting board
🍳 Large iron wok
🥄 Wok spatula / hand ladle
🥘 Strainer / slotted spoon
🏺 Earthenware jar
🔥 Gas stove
🥣 Stainless steel bowl

## Steps
1. **Prepare the leaf fat**: Trim any lean meat and lymph nodes from the leaf fat, then cut into evenly sized mahjong tile pieces.
2. **Prepare aromatics**: Cut scallion into small sections, and slice ginger.
3. **Prepare the storage container**: Place about 3 grams of salt and an appropriate amount of green Sichuan pepper at the bottom of the earthenware jar to help extend the shelf life.
4. **First blanching**: Place the leaf fat in a pot of cold water. Once the water boils, blanch for 1 minute, then remove. This aims to remove blood and impurities.
5. **Second blanching**: Bring fresh water to a boil, add the leaf fat and an appropriate amount of cooking wine, and blanch again for 1 minute. This aims to thoroughly remove any off-odors from the fat.
6. **Render using the water method**: Clean the wok, add one bowl of purified water, put in the blanched leaf fat, and render over medium-low heat for about 10 minutes. In the first stage, the water evaporates, white steam appears from the wok, and the oil appears milky white; in the second stage, the steam decreases, and the oil gradually becomes clear; in the third stage, the oil output and fragrance increase.
7. **Add aromatics**: At the end of the second stage, add the sliced ginger and scallion sections to enhance fragrance and remove any remaining odor.
8. **Finish and filter**: Continue rendering until the cracklings, ginger, and scallions turn a golden yellow, then immediately turn off the heat. Use the slotted spoon to strain the hot oil into the pre-prepared earthenware jar with salt and Sichuan pepper at the bottom.
9. **Handle the cracklings**: The leftover cracklings can be rendered for an additional 1 minute over low heat until crispy, then removed and drained. The cracklings can be sprinkled with sugar and eaten directly, or used in stir-fried vegetables or stews.

## Health Advice
Lard is rich in saturated fat. It is recommended to use only a small spoonful to enhance flavor. Individuals with high blood pressure, high cholesterol, or those on a diet should consciously control their intake. It is suitable for pairing with light vegetables to enhance the flavor of vegetarian dishes.
