# Dry-Fried Lamb Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Lamb rolls, about 100g (blanched and drained in advance)  
🌿 Cilantro, as needed (to enhance flavor and aroma)  
🧄 Minced garlic, as needed  
🌶️ Dried chili pepper segments, as needed  
🧅 Onion shreds, as needed  
🫒 Oil, as needed  
🧂 Light soy sauce, 3 tbsp  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tbsp  
🧂 BBQ seasoning, 2 tbsp  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spatula  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Blanch the lamb rolls in boiling water, then remove and drain.  
   **Note**: Blanching helps remove blood and impurities.  
2. Heat an appropriate amount of oil in a wok until warm. Add the blanched lamb rolls and pan-fry over medium heat until the edges are slightly crispy. Remove and set aside.  
3. In another wok, heat oil and add minced garlic, dried chili pepper segments, and onion shreds. Stir-fry until fragrant.  
4. Return the pan-fried lamb rolls to the wok. Drizzle with the mixed light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and BBQ seasoning. Stir-fry quickly over high heat to combine evenly.  
5. Finally, add the cilantro segments and quickly stir-fry for a few seconds. Remove from heat and serve.  
   **Key Point**: Add cilantro at the very end to prevent over-heating and loss of freshness.  

## Health Suggestions  
This dry-fried lamb roll dish is rich in protein, suitable for muscle building or iron supplementation. Note that blanching and pan-frying the lamb rolls until slightly crispy can reduce oil intake. However, BBQ seasoning and light soy sauce are high in sodium, so those with high blood pressure should consume with caution.
