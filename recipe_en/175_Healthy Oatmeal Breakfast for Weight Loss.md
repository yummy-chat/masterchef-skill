# Healthy Oatmeal Breakfast for Weight Loss  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥣 Mixed Oats about 1 scoop (high-fiber oats)  
🥚 Egg 1  
💧 Water as needed (enough to cover the oats)  
🥜 Nuts (almonds) a small handful  
🥛 Plain milk 1 carton (about 200-250ml)  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment  
☕ Microwave-safe plastic cup with lid (with ventilation holes)  
🥄 Large spoon  
🪵 Wooden spoon  
📡 Microwave oven  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Take a microwave-safe plastic cup with lid, add 1 scoop of mixed oats, and pour in enough water to cover the oats.  
2. Crack 1 egg into the cup, close the lid, and ensure the ventilation hole is open.  
   **Note**: When heating food containing eggs, a ventilated lid must be used to prevent the cup from bursting due to excessive pressure.  
3. Place the cup in the microwave and heat on medium-high power for 1.5 to 2 minutes, until the egg white is set and the yolk is either runny or fully cooked.  
   **Key Point**: The heating time can be adjusted based on the microwave power; it is recommended to heat for 1.5 minutes first and check the status.  
4. Remove the cup, add a small handful of nuts and 1 carton of plain milk.  
   **Key Point**: Adding nuts and milk after heating keeps the nuts crispy and the milk smooth in texture.  
5. Use a wooden spoon to stir the oats, egg, nuts, and milk until well combined and it reaches a moist, porridge-like consistency. Serve immediately.  

## Health Tips  
This oatmeal breakfast is rich in dietary fiber and high-quality protein, making it suitable as a low-fat, high-satiety breakfast during weight loss. Pay attention to controlling the amount of nuts—about 10 grams—to avoid excess calories. The egg must be fully cooked to ensure food safety.
