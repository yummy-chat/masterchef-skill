# Avocado and Poached Egg Toast
Cuisine Type: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥑 1 avocado (ripe, pitted and mashed with a fork)
🍞 2 slices thick-cut toast
🥚 1 egg
🥓 2-3 slices bacon
🥬 About 30g lettuce
🍊 Half a grapefruit (cut into pieces or slices)
🥜 About 15g crushed nuts
🫒 3-5 drops lemon juice (added to raw egg for poaching to help coagulation)
🧂 Black pepper to taste (ground and sprinkled over poached egg and avocado toast)
🧂 Sea salt (optional, add according to personal taste)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🥄 Small saucepan
🥄 Slotted spoon
🔪 Serrated knife (for cutting toast)
🧂 Grinder (for black pepper and salt)
🍴 Fork (for mashing avocado)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare ingredients**: Cut the avocado in half, remove pit, scoop out flesh, and mash with a fork. Cut toast into thick slices. Wash and drain lettuce. Cut grapefruit into pieces or slices. Crush nuts and set aside.
2. **Prepare poached egg**: Crack the egg into a small bowl, add a few drops of lemon juice, and gently stir to combine.
3. **Toast the bread**: Place the toast slices in a frying pan without oil. Cook over medium-low heat until golden and crispy on both sides, about 1-2 minutes per side. Adjust based on browning.
   - **Key point**: Pan-fried toast is crispier than toasted, but watch the heat carefully to avoid burning.
4. **Cook bacon**: Use the same pan to cook the bacon until the fat renders and edges are slightly crispy. Remove and set aside.
5. **Poach the egg**: Fill a small saucepan with water and bring to a gentle simmer. Gently pour in the egg. Use a slotted spoon to gently nudge the egg to prevent sticking. Remove once the white is set and the yolk remains runny.
   - **Checking doneness**: Lift the egg with a slotted spoon and gently press. It should feel soft and springy inside.
   - **Key point**: The water should not be boiling vigorously, or the egg will break apart. Cooking time varies, so rely on touch to judge doneness.
6. **Assemble the toast**: Spread the mashed avocado onto the toasted bread slices. Sprinkle with crushed nuts. Place the poached egg on top. Grind black pepper over it.
7. **Plate**: Arrange the assembled toast on a plate. Add cooked bacon, lettuce, and grapefruit pieces on the side. Serve immediately.

## Health Tips
The combination of avocado and runny egg offers high-quality fats and protein, making it suitable for muscle-building or low-carb diets. However, note that bacon and toast are high in both carbs and fat, so manage total intake. Pairing with grapefruit and lettuce helps cut richness and provides vitamin C. Recommended for breakfast to avoid excessive calorie intake in the evening.
