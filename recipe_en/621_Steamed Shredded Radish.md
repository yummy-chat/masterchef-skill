# Steamed Shredded Radish  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥒 Green radish – about 900g (peeled and shredded into thin strips)  
🌾 All-purpose flour – one tablespoon  
🌽 Cornmeal – one tablespoon  
🧄 Garlic – to taste (minced)  
🌿 Cilantro – to taste (cut into sections)  
🌶️ Small chili peppers – to taste (sliced into rings)  
🧂 Salt – 5g (for pickling)  
🧂 Pepper salt powder – 5g  
🫒 Cooking oil – 5g  
🥄 Light soy sauce – two tablespoons  
🥄 Fragrant vinegar – one tablespoon  
🫗 Sesame oil – a small amount  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Vegetable peeler  
🧇 Grater  
🍴 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Ladle  
♨️ Steamer (with steaming rack and cloth)  
🥃 Small bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🧤 Disposable gloves  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Peel the green radish and grate it into thin shreds using the grater.  
   **Note**: The radish skin has a strong pungent taste and must be removed.  
2. Add 5g of salt to the shredded radish, mix well, and let it sit for 5 minutes to draw out moisture.  
   **Key point**: Pickling removes the pungent taste and makes the texture crisper.  
3. Use your hands to squeeze the pickled shredded radish dry, removing as much water as possible.  
   **Note**: Squeeze out as much moisture as possible; otherwise, the mixture will be too wet when adding flour.  
4. In a small bowl, combine minced garlic, cilantro sections, chili pepper rings, two tablespoons of light soy sauce, one tablespoon of fragrant vinegar, and a small amount of sesame oil. Stir well to make the sauce. Set aside.  
5. Add one tablespoon of all-purpose flour, one tablespoon of cornmeal, 5g of pepper salt powder, and 5g of cooking oil to the squeezed shredded radish. Wearing gloves, thoroughly mix everything together.  
   **Desired state**: Each shred of radish should be evenly coated with flour, distinct, and not sticking together.  
   **Key point**: Adjust the amount of flour based on the radish's moisture content; the goal is for each shred to remain separate. Pepper salt powder is the main source of flavor.  
6. Loosen the coated shredded radish and spread it evenly on the steaming cloth in the steaming rack.  
7. Bring water in the steamer to a boil, place the steaming rack in, and steam over high heat for 3 minutes, then reduce to medium heat for another 2 minutes. Turn off the heat and let it sit for 2 minutes.  
   **Note**: The total steaming time should not be too long, otherwise, the texture will deteriorate.  
8. After steaming, let it cool slightly, then remove and plate in a mound shape. Drizzle the prepared sauce over the top and serve.  

## Health Advice  
After drawing out the moisture, the shredded radish is then steamed, which removes the pungent taste and maintains a crisp and tender texture. This dish is suitable for those who need to increase vegetable intake and have a weak spleen and stomach. It is low in fat and high in fiber, and when paired with a garlic and vinegar sauce, it is appetizing without being greasy. Those with high blood pressure, high blood sugar, and high blood lipids can also consume it in moderation.
