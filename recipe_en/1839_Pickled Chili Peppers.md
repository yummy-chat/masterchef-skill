# Pickled Chili Peppers  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🫑 Thin-skinned chili peppers (spiral peppers) 1000g (if you can't handle too much heat, substitute with long line peppers or a mix)  
🌶️ Bird's eye chili peppers 100g (optional, or substitute with non-spicy varieties, mainly for color)  
🫚 Ginger 20g (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic 30g (sliced)  
🌿 Cilantro 25g (cut into sections)  
🍬 White sugar 280g (used instead of salt to draw out water, resulting in a crisper texture)  
🍾 Aged vinegar 230g  
🥄 Donggu Yipinxian soy sauce 100g  
🧂 Seafood soy sauce or Maggi seasoning 120g  
🥢 Light soy sauce 260g  
🥄 Dark soy sauce 55g (for color and to add caramel flavor)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board  
🫙 Oil-free and water-free stainless steel bowl or container  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
🧊 Refrigerator  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Wash all ingredients thoroughly and drain completely to prevent spoilage.  
2. Remove the stems from the thin-skinned chili peppers, keep the seeds, and cut into large rolling chunks; slice the bird's eye chili peppers diagonally or cut in half; slice the ginger and garlic; cut the cilantro into sections.  
3. Place the cut green and red chili peppers into a clean, oil-free and water-free container, and add 280g of white sugar.  
4. Put on gloves and thoroughly mix the sugar with the chili peppers by hand until the peppers release a little moisture and the sugar is partially dissolved.  
   - Key point: Using sugar instead of salt to draw out water gives the peppers a crisper texture.  
5. Add the sliced ginger, garlic, and cilantro, then pour in 230g aged vinegar, 100g Donggu Yipinxian soy sauce, 120g seafood soy sauce, 260g light soy sauce, and 55g dark soy sauce in order.  
   - Key point: Mixing multiple types of soy sauce enriches the flavor profile; dark soy sauce mainly adds color.  
6. Mix everything evenly again, cover the container, and refrigerate overnight. Turn the mixture 1-2 times during this period to ensure even pickling.  
7. When serving, use oil-free and water-free chopsticks to take out the desired amount. The remaining pickling liquid can be reused; adjust the seasoning by adding more soy sauce, sugar, and vinegar to taste.  

## Health Advice  
This dish uses sugar instead of salt to draw out water, resulting in a crisper texture but a higher sugar content. It is recommended to consume only a small amount as an appetizer and not as a main dish in large quantities. Chili peppers are rich in vitamin C, but those with sensitive stomachs should consume with caution.
