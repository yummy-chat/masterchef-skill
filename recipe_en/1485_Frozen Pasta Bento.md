# Frozen Pasta Bento
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍝 Short pasta (e.g., penne) about 125g per serving
🧂 Salt to taste (for boiling pasta)
🫒 Olive oil a small amount (to prevent sticking, about 1 tsp)

**French Vinaigrette (Optional Sauce)**
🟡 Dijon mustard 100g
🌿 Dried parsley 5g
🧄 Garlic cloves 5 cloves (minced)
🍬 Sugar 15g
🧂 Sea salt 8g
⚫ Black pepper 5g
🍋 Lemon juice to taste
🫒 Olive oil 360g

## Cookware & Equipment
🍲 Stainless steel soup pot
🥄 Slotted spoon/strainer
🥣 Stainless steel large bowl
🥄 Silicone spatula
🫙 Reusable zipper bags
📦 Stainless steel square tray (freezer-safe)
🥣 Glass bowl
🥢 Hand whisk
🫙 Glass jar with lid (for storing sauce)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Cook the pasta**: Fill a pot with water and bring to a boil. Add salt, then add the short pasta. Cook for 1 minute less than the package instructions, so it remains firm and chewy.
   - **Key point**: Do not cook until fully done; otherwise, the texture will become mushy after reheating from frozen.
2. **Coat with oil to prevent sticking**: Drain the pasta thoroughly and transfer to a large bowl. Drizzle with a little olive oil and quickly toss with a silicone spatula until each piece is evenly coated with a thin layer of oil.
3. **Portion and freeze**: Divide the pasta into individual meal portions and place in reusable zipper bags. Flatten to remove air and lay flat on a stainless steel square tray. Transfer to the freezer. Can be stored for about a week.
4. **Make the French vinaigrette**: Place Dijon mustard, dried parsley, minced garlic, sugar, sea salt, black pepper, and lemon juice in a glass bowl. Whisk together with a hand whisk. While slowly pouring in the olive oil, continue whisking rapidly until the dressing thickens and emulsifies. Transfer to a sealed jar and refrigerate until needed.
5. **Assemble the bento**: Remove the frozen pasta and thaw at room temperature until the noodles regain their elasticity. Add your choice of ready-to-eat protein, blanched and drained vegetables, and fruit. Drizzle with the vinaigrette, toss, and serve.

## Health Advice
For frozen pasta bento, it is recommended to use short pasta with a higher resistant starch content, making it suitable for blood sugar management. Although the homemade French vinaigrette is relatively high in fat, the Dijon mustard and lemon juice help balance the flavor. When cooking, control the amount of olive oil used.
