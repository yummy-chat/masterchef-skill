# Lemon Herb Chicken Thighs
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍗 Chicken thighs (drumstick-thigh pieces) about 2, deboned and cut into pieces
🧅 Purple onion about half, sliced
🍋 Lemon half, thinly sliced and deseeded
🌿 Cilantro to taste, cut into sections
🌶️ Bird's eye chili 1-2, sliced into rings or small sections
🧄 Garlic 2-3 cloves, sliced
🧂 White pepper to taste
🧂 Sea salt to taste
🥄 Light soy sauce 1 tbsp
🍶 Cooking wine 1 tbsp (for marinating) + a little (to add before serving)
🥣 Oyster sauce a little
🍯 Honey 1 tbsp
🧂 Chicken powder 1 tsp (optional)
🌾 Cornstarch 2 tbsp
🫒 Camellia oil as needed (for sealing and pan-frying)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, knife
✂️ Kitchen scissors
🥣 Glass bowl
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Spatula
🔥 Stove

## Steps
1. Slice the lemon into thin rounds, using the tip of a knife to remove all seeds.
   **Key**: Seeds must be completely removed, otherwise they will cause bitterness after cooking.
2. Debone the chicken thighs and cut them into bite-sized pieces.
3. Place the chicken pieces in a bowl. Add white pepper, sea salt, light soy sauce, cooking wine, oyster sauce, honey, chicken powder, and cornstarch. Mix well to coat. Finally, drizzle in a small amount of camellia oil and mix again to seal in moisture.
   **Key**: Cornstarch and oil sealing are crucial for tender chicken.
4. Heat the pan, add an appropriate amount of camellia oil, and arrange the marinated chicken pieces in a single layer. Pan-fry quickly over high heat.
   **Key**: Must use high heat, otherwise it will easily burn or release water; fry until the surface is browned and the inside is cooked through.
5. Once the chicken is cooked and has browned edges, add the sliced onion, garlic slices, and bird's eye chili. Stir-fry until the onion softens and releases fragrance.
6. Add the lemon slices, and stir-fry quickly for about 15-20 seconds.
   **Key**: Do not cook the lemon for too long, otherwise high heat will cause bitterness and destroy vitamins.
7. Just before serving, drizzle a little cooking wine along the edge of the pan, add cilantro sections, and stir-fry until evenly mixed. Plate immediately.

## Health Tips
This dish balances the calories of chicken thighs with lemon and honey. For a lower-fat version, remove the skin before cooking. Onion and cilantro provide dietary fiber and vitamins, making it suitable to serve with mixed grain rice. Note that lemon must be added at the end to avoid bitterness.
