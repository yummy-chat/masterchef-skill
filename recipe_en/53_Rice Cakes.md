# Rice Cakes
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 Glutinous rice: 500g  
🌾 Rice: 500g  
💧 Water: 550ml (added to rice flour in batches)  
🫘 Red beans: To taste (must be soaked in advance)  
🍶 Sweet rice wine (fermented glutinous rice): To taste (for sweet rice wine rice cake soup)  
🥚 Eggs: To taste (for sweet rice wine rice cake soup)  
🥬 Chinese cabbage: To taste (for spicy stir-fried rice cakes; can substitute with onion)  
🧅 Onion: To taste (optional, for spicy stir-fried rice cakes)  
🌶️ Korean chili paste: 2 tablespoons  
🧴 Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons  
🥤 Sprite: 2 tablespoons  
🍬 White or brown sugar: To taste (for seasoning)  
🤍 White sesame seeds: To taste (for garnish)  
🫒 Cooking oil: To taste (for anti-stick and deep-frying)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Food processor (to grind rice into flour)  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (to mix rice flour and water)  
♨️ Steamer (to steam the wet rice flour)  
🍲 Pot (for boiling red beans, cooking rice cakes, and boiling sauce)  
🍳 Wok (for deep-frying rice cakes and stir-frying spicy rice cakes)  
🔲 Cutting board (for kneading and cutting rice cakes)  
🔪 Knife (for cutting rice cakes)  
🧤 Gloves (to prevent burns and sticking when kneading hot dough)  

## Steps  
1. Make rice flour: Mix 500g glutinous rice and 500g rice, then grind into fine flour in a food processor.  
   **Tip**: Alternatively, directly mix store-bought rice flour and glutinous rice flour in a 1:1 ratio.  
2. Mix wet flour: Gradually add 550ml water to the rice flour, and break apart large clumps by hand.  
   **Moisture standard**: The mixture should form a lump when squeezed in hand but crumble easily when touched.  
3. Steam flour: Spread the wet flour mixture evenly on a damp cloth-lined steamer basket. Steam over boiling water on high heat for 25–30 minutes.  
   **Key**: Spread the flour layer thinly to allow steam to circulate and ensure it is fully cooked.  
4. Boil red beans: Cook the pre-soaked red beans in a pot for about 20 minutes until tender.  
   **Key**: Do not overcook the red beans, or they will break apart when kneaded into the dough.  
5. Knead base dough: Transfer the steamed rice flour onto an oiled cutting board while hot. Wearing gloves, knead repeatedly until it forms a smooth, non-sticky, and moderately firm dough.  
   **Key**: Knead while hot; the dough becomes difficult to work with once cooled. Wear thick gloves if necessary to avoid burns.  
6. Make red bean rice cakes: Take half of the kneaded dough and gently incorporate the cooked red beans until evenly distributed.  
   **Key**: Knead gently to avoid crushing the red beans.  
7. Make plain rice cakes: Roll the remaining base dough directly into a long log.  
8. Shape: Cut the red bean and plain rice cake logs into evenly sized pieces, shaping them into small cylinders. Roll any remaining dough into thinner, longer logs and cut into smaller pieces to make hot pot-style rice cakes.  
   **Key**: Shape all rice cakes while the dough is still hot.  
9. Serving suggestions:  
   - **Deep-fried rice cakes**: Slice red bean or plain rice cakes thinly. Fry in oil heated to 160–170°C (medium-low heat) until they float. For a soft texture, remove immediately; for a crispy texture, fry until lightly golden and firm. Drain and sprinkle with sugar or barbecue seasoning.  
   - **Spicy stir-fried rice cakes**: Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry 2 tablespoons Korean chili paste, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, and 2 tablespoons Sprite. Add enough boiling water and bring to a boil. Add rice cake pieces and cook until the sauce thickens. Add shredded Chinese cabbage or onion slices and stir-fry until well combined. Garnish with white sesame seeds before serving.  
   - **Sweet rice wine rice cake soup**: Once water boils, add rice cake pieces and cook until they float. Add sweet rice wine, drizzle in beaten egg, and sweeten with sugar to taste.  

## Health Advice  
Rice cakes are high in glutinous rice content, which digests slowly. Consume in small portions as a staple. Diabetics or those on a weight-loss diet should control portions. The deep-fried and spicy Korean chili paste versions are higher in calories; it is recommended to choose the sweet rice wine rice cake soup, adding eggs to balance nutrition.
