# Sour and Spicy Boneless Chicken Feet
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 Ingredients  
- Chicken feet 1kg  
- Ginger 4 slices  
- Scallion 3 sections (5cm each)  
- Garlic 10 cloves  
- Xiaomi chili peppers (4 for mild heat/6 for medium heat/12 for high heat, high heat recommended)  
- Onion half  
- Cilantro 3 sprigs  
- Lemon 2 (adjust sourness by quantity)  

🧂 Seasonings  
- Cooking wine 65g  
- Light soy sauce 75g  
- Oyster sauce 30g  
- Black vinegar (aged vinegar recommended) 50g  
- Sugar 10g  
- Salt 3g  
- Sichuan pepper oil 10ml  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Scissors or knife  
🍳 Large pot  
🥣 Container (bowl/basin)  
❄️ Refrigerator  

## Preparation Steps
1. **Chicken Feet Pre-treatment**
   - Using scissors or a knife, thoroughly remove the nails of the chicken feet, including the meat and bone under the nail, ensuring no residue remains.
   - Rinse the chicken feet with clean water, drain, and set aside.
   - Place the chicken feet in a large pot, add scallion sections, cooking wine, and ginger slices.
   - Pour in enough clean water to cover the chicken feet, bring to a boil over high heat, skimming off any foam during the process.
   - After the water boils, continue cooking for 10 minutes, then turn off the heat, remove the chicken feet, drain, rinse again, and place them in a basin.

2. **Deboning**
   - Freeze the chicken feet in the freezer for 20 minutes, then transfer them to the refrigerator. Remove them in batches for deboning.
   - Starting from the tips of the chicken feet, use a knife to cut along each toe up to the back of the foot, then from the back down to the base.
   - Gently bend each joint one by one, pushing the skin from the tip toward the palm area, then continue pushing to the base to completely extract the bone. Place the boneless meat in a bowl and set aside.

3. **Preparing Marinade and Marinating**
   - Cut Xiaomi chili peppers into small pieces, mince garlic, onion, and cilantro. Cut lemons in half and squeeze the juice into the container holding the chicken feet.
   - Add all the seasonings as well as the chopped Xiaomi chili peppers, garlic, onion, and cilantro to the chicken feet container.
   - Mix thoroughly by hand, cover, and refrigerate for 6 hours to marinate before serving.

## Health Advice  
Chicken feet are rich in collagen but are relatively high in calories, making them suitable for occasional consumption. People with high blood lipids should monitor their intake.
