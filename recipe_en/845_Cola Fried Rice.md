# Cola Fried Rice  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Leftover rice (as needed, preferably overnight rice with less moisture for a drier texture)  
🥚 Eggs 2 (1 for a sunny-side-up egg with runny yolk, 1 raw for steaming)  
🫒 Cooking oil (as needed)  
🧂 Salt (a pinch)  
🥤 Cola (as needed)  
🧂 Light soy sauce (as needed)  
🧂 Dark soy sauce (as needed)  
🦪 Oyster sauce (as needed)  
🌶️ White pepper powder (a pinch)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spatula / wooden spatula  
✂️ Kitchen scissors  
🔥 Stove  
🫖 Lid  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Heat the wok over high heat, add oil, and sprinkle in a pinch of salt. Fry 1 egg into a sunny-side-up egg until the egg white is crispy and the yolk remains runny. Remove and cut into small pieces using kitchen scissors.  
   **Key point**: The runny yolk egg is one of the flavor highlights of this dish.  
2. Return the cut fried egg pieces to the wok, pour in cola, then immediately add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, and white pepper powder. Keep the heat high and bring to a boil to meld the flavors.  
3. Add the leftover rice, stir-fry continuously until the liquid is fully absorbed by the rice, the grains are distinct and evenly colored, and a caramel aroma emerges.  
4. Spread the fried rice evenly in the wok, make a well in the center with the spatula, crack in the other raw egg, cover immediately, reduce heat to low, and steam for about 2 minutes 30 seconds.  
   **Key point**: The steaming time determines the doneness of the runny yolk; the egg white should be set while the yolk remains runny for the best result.  
5. Uncover, break the runny yolk egg with the spatula, and quickly toss it with the rice to mix evenly. Use the residual heat to coat the rice grains with the egg liquid, then serve.  

## Health Tips  
Cola fried rice replaces traditional caramel coloring with the caramel flavor of cola and is suitable for occasional consumption. Cola has a high sugar content, so control the amount used. It is recommended to pair with vegetables for a balanced diet. The runny yolk egg adds texture and is suitable for healthy individuals, but those with weaker digestive systems should ensure the yolk is fully cooked.
