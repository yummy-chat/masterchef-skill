# Beef Hot Pot
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 One whole piece of beef tenderloin (separate fat and lean; slice the fatty part for hot pot, slice the lean part for stir-fried beef with cilantro)
🌿 A large bunch of cilantro (wash and cut into long sections)
🍢 A dozen beef meatballs
🧈 Tofu, appropriate amount (cut into pieces)
🥒 Cucumber, appropriate amount
🥬 Various vegetables, appropriate amount (wash and set aside)
🧅 Scallion, appropriate amount (cut into sections for stir-frying)
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (slice for stir-frying, mince for aroma)
🧄 Garlic, appropriate amount (mince)
🌶️ Bird's eye chili, appropriate amount (cut into rings)
🫘 A large piece of hot pot base
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount
🍶 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount
🌽 Cornstarch, appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🍳 Wok
🥄 Soup ladle / spatula
🍲 Electric hot pot
🍽️ Bowls, plates

## Preparation Steps
1.  Separate the fatty and lean beef tenderloin. Cut the fatty part against the grain into thin slices for hot pot; also slice the lean part into thin slices, then marinate with salt, light soy sauce, and cornstarch.
**Key Point**: Process fat and lean separately; slicing fatty meat against the grain into thin slices makes it easier to cook in hot pot; marinating the lean meat increases tenderness.

2.  Cut the tofu into pieces, wash and drain the vegetables, and take out the beef meatballs for later use.
3.  Place a wok over heat, pour in an appropriate amount of oil and heat until hot. Add scallion sections and ginger slices, stir-fry until fragrant. Add the hot pot base, reduce to medium-low heat, and stir-fry until melted and red oil is released. Pour in enough water and bring to a boil, then transfer to an electric hot pot.
**Note**: Avoid high heat when stir-frying the hot pot base to prevent burning; releasing the red oil results in a better color.

4.  In another wok, heat oil over high heat. Add minced ginger, minced garlic, and bird's eye chili rings, stir-fry until fragrant. Add the marinated beef slices, quickly stir-fry over high heat until they change color. Immediately add the cilantro sections, stir-fry quickly and evenly until the cilantro is slightly wilted, then remove from heat.
**Key Point**: The thinner the beef slices, the more tender they are; the cilantro beef must be stir-fried over high heat throughout to prevent the beef from becoming tough and the cilantro from releasing water and turning yellow.

5.  Bring the broth to the table, add beef meatballs, tofu, vegetables, and other ingredients, and cook until done. The cooked beef slices can also be dipped in your preferred dipping sauce.

## Health Tips
Beef hot pot is high in fat and protein, suitable for those with high physical energy expenditure or those needing to warm up. It is recommended that people with high blood pressure, high blood sugar, high blood lipids, or those on a weight loss diet eat more vegetables and drink less of the red oil broth.
