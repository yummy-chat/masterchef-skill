# Steamed Crab with Vermicelli
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn (September-October)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦀 2 swimming crabs  
🍜 1 bundle of vermicelli (need to be pre-soaked in warm water until soft)  
🧅 2-3 stalks of scallions (used in two parts: scallion segments before steaming, and scallion blossoms after steaming)  
🪵 3-4 slices of ginger  
🧄 5-6 cloves of garlic (crushed)  
🍾 Steamed fish soy sauce, to taste  
🫗 Sesame oil, to taste  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (used twice: brushed on the crab surface before steaming, and drizzled hot oil after steaming)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
✂️ Scissors (for bleeding and cleaning the crab)  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for soaking vermicelli)  
🍽️ Deep plate (for arranging and steaming)  
♨️ Wok or steamer  
⏱️ Timer  
🖌️ Brush (for brushing oil)  
🥄 Soup spoon (for drizzling hot oil)  

## Steps  
1. **Prepare the crab**: Insert scissors under the mouth of the live crab to bleed it out. Once it stops struggling, place it in water and scrub it clean with a brush.
   **Key point**: After bleeding, the crab will not struggle easily, making it easier to clean, and the legs are less likely to fall off when cutting into pieces later.
2. **Clean the crab body**: Lift the carapace, use scissors to remove the sand sac, teeth, gills on both sides, the back cover, and the tips of the crab legs.
   **Key point**: These five parts are inedible and must be thoroughly removed.
3. **Cut the crab body**: Cut the cleaned crab body diagonally into pieces, ensuring each piece of crab meat is attached to a pair of crab legs.
   **Key point**: Cutting diagonally forward ensures a neat and attractive final presentation.
4. **Prepare the crab claws**: Separate the large crab claws at the joint, and crack the shell with the back of a knife to allow for flavor absorption and easy eating.
5. **Soak the vermicelli**: Soak the vermicelli in warm water for about 5 minutes until it turns white and becomes completely soft.
6. **Season the vermicelli**: Drain the soaked vermicelli, then mix in steamed fish soy sauce and sesame oil to prevent sticking and add a base flavor.
7. **Arrange the dish**: Spread the seasoned vermicelli on the bottom of the plate, neatly arrange the cut crab pieces and cracked crab claws on top, and finally cover with the carapace.
8. **Pre-steaming treatment**: Evenly brush a layer of cooking oil onto the crab surface, and place ginger slices and scallion segments on the crab pieces.
9. **Steam**: Bring water to a boil in a steamer. Once steam rises, place the plate in and steam on high heat for 8 minutes.
   **Note**: Strictly control the time to 8 minutes to keep the crab meat moist and not dry.
10. **Post-steaming seasoning**: After steaming, remove the plate, discard the scallion segments and ginger slices, sprinkle fresh scallion blossoms on top, then heat a spoonful of cooking oil until smoking hot and quickly drizzle it over the scallions and crab to release the aroma.

## Health Advice  
This dish is rich in high-quality protein and minerals. Crab meat is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for consumption during weight loss. However, vermicelli absorbs oil and sauce, so the total amount should be controlled. Note that crab is cold in nature; those with a weak spleen and stomach are advised to consume it with ginger and vinegar.
