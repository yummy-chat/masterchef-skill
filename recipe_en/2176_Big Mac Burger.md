# Big Mac Burger  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Beef, about 500g  
🌽 Corn kernels, as needed  
🧀 Cheese slice, 1 per burger  
🍍 Pineapple, 1 slice per burger  
🥬 Lettuce, 1 leaf per burger  
🍅 Tomato, 1 slice per burger  
🍞 Burger bun, 1 per burger  
🧅 Onion, as needed (to blend with beef)  
🧂 Soy sauce, as needed  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed  
🥫 Ketchup, as needed  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Meat grinder  
🥣 Bowl  
🔪 Knife, cutting board  
📄 Plastic wrap/parchment paper  
🍳 Frying pan  
♨️ Air fryer  
🥄 Spoon  

## Steps  
1. Cut the beef into chunks and blend with onion chunks in a meat grinder to make the filling.  
2. Add corn kernels, soy sauce, and salt to the filling, mix well, then drizzle in a little cooking oil and stir evenly.  
   **Note**: There are no strict ratios for the seasonings; adjust to taste as needed.  
3. Cut a ring from a paper cup to use as a mold, line the bottom with parchment paper, fill the ring with the meat mixture, press firmly into a round patty, then remove the mold.  
   **Key point**: Place parchment paper underneath to prevent sticking and make it easier to transfer the patty.  
4. Prepare the burger bun, cheese slice, lettuce leaf, tomato slice, and pineapple slice in advance.  
5. Preheat the frying pan, add the patty, and cook over medium-low heat for about 3–4 minutes. Once the bottom is colored and set, remove the parchment paper, flip, and continue cooking until fully cooked through.  
   **Doneness check**: The bottom should be golden brown, the patty should be fully colored with no oozing blood, and it should spring back when pressed.  
6. Spread ketchup on the bottom half of the burger bun, then layer with lettuce, the cooked patty, cheese slice, pineapple slice, and tomato slice. Finally, place the top half of the bun on top.  
7. Place the assembled burger in the air fryer and heat for 2–3 minutes until the cheese is slightly melted and the bun surface is lightly crispy.  
   **Note**: This step is optional but enhances the overall flavor.  

## Health Advice  
It is recommended to choose beef filling with a higher proportion of lean meat. The lettuce and pineapple add dietary fiber and vitamin C, making this suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. Be mindful of the salt and ketchup amounts; those with high blood pressure can reduce the seasoning.
