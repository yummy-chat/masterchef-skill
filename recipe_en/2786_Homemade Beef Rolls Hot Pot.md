# Homemade Beef Rolls Hot Pot
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 A large piece of beef with alternating fat and lean (bone-in whole piece, to be thinly sliced)
🍅 Fresh tomatoes, appropriate amount (juiced for broth base, a few slices reserved for garnish)
🧅 Purple onion, appropriate amount (chopped and sautéed)
🥬 Celery, appropriate amount (chopped and sautéed)
🧄 Minced garlic, appropriate amount
🍄 Fresh shiitake mushrooms, appropriate amount (sliced for the pot)
🌱 Scallions, appropriate amount (for final garnish)
🌽 Sweet corn, appropriate amount (cut into sections for boiling)
🥬 Lettuce, appropriate amount
🥬 Seaweed knots, appropriate amount
🧈 Frozen tofu, appropriate amount
🧈 Dried bean curd sticks, appropriate amount
🦐 Shrimp paste, appropriate amount
🍜 Fenpi dough sheets, appropriate amount (for wrapping shrimp paste)
🦪 Oyster sauce, appropriate amount
🍬 White sugar, appropriate amount
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Blender/food processor
🍲 Soup pot
🌀 Strainer
🔪 Manual slicer
🎞️ Plastic wrap
🪥 Toothpicks
🔥🍲 Hot pot stove (induction cooker)

## Preparation Steps
### Preparation
1. Wash the beef and pat dry the surface moisture. Lay it flat on plastic wrap, roll it tightly into a firm cylindrical shape, seal the ends, poke several small holes on the surface with toothpicks for venting, and freeze until firm.
2. Braid the fenpi dough sheets, squeeze on shrimp paste, roll them up, blanch in boiling water until cooked, then remove and set aside.
3. Blend the tomatoes in a blender into a smooth tomato juice; chop the purple onion and celery.
4. Cut the corn into sections, wash lettuce, seaweed knots, and other side dishes; soak frozen tofu and dried bean curd sticks in advance; slice the shiitake mushrooms; cut scallions into sections; set aside a few tomato slices.
### Tomato Broth Base
1. Heat oil in a pot, add the chopped purple onion, celery, and minced garlic, and sauté over medium heat until fragrant.
2. Pour in the tomato juice, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium-low heat and simmer slowly until the broth turns bright red and rich.
3. Season simply with oyster sauce, white sugar, and salt. Use a strainer to remove all vegetable residue, then pour the broth back into the pot.
4. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms, tomato slices, and scallion sections. Bring to a boil, then serve at the table.
### Slicing and Boiling
1. Remove the frozen beef roll, and use a manual slicer to cut it quickly and evenly into thin, uniform beef slices.
2. Arrange the beef rolls, shrimp paste fenpi rolls, corn, lettuce, frozen tofu, dried bean curd sticks, seaweed knots, etc., on plates. Once the broth base boils, cook according to preference.
- **Note**: Boil the beef rolls for a short time; remove as soon as they change color. The shrimp paste fenpi rolls are already blanched; just heat them through.

## Health Advice
Beef rolls hot pot is high in fat. It is recommended to pair it with more vegetables and corn to balance nutrition, and enjoy it occasionally for gatherings. The tomato broth base is low in oil and salt, making it more suitable for those managing blood pressure and weight, but attention should still be paid to controlling meat intake.
