# Baguette Sandwich
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Bread flour, as needed
🍞 Yeast, as needed
🐟 Salmon, 1 piece
🥑 Avocado, 1
🫒 Vegetable oil, as needed
🧂 Salt, as needed
🌶️ Black pepper, as needed
🥛 Yogurt, as needed
🍯 Mustard, as needed (recommended in reduced amount)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Measuring cup
🥄 Mixing bowl
🧺 Proofing cloth
🛷 Transfer board
🔪 Scoring knife
♨️ Baking tray
🔥 Oven
🍳 Dutch oven
🍳 Frying pan
🥣 Bowl
🍴 Fork

## Steps
1. **Prepare dough in advance**: Mix bread flour, yeast, and water into a dough, and let it ferment overnight.
   **Note**: Be sure to add salt to the dough; otherwise, the flavor will be insufficient.
2. **Marinate the salmon**: Coat the salmon evenly with oil, salt, and black pepper, and marinate. This should be done during the dough fermentation period.
3. **Shape the dough**: Transfer the fermented dough to a floured proofing cloth, gently pat to release air, and shape.
4. **Transfer and score**: Use a transfer board to move the dough onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Use a scoring knife to make several diagonal cuts on the surface at a moderate depth to help the bread expand during baking.
5. **Preheat and create steam**: Preheat the oven, and place the Dutch oven on the bottom rack to preheat as well. Once the temperature is reached, put the bread in, quickly pour a cup of hot water into the Dutch oven, close the door immediately, and use the steam to make the baguette crust crispy. Bake until golden brown.
6. **Pan-fry the salmon**: Heat oil in a frying pan, add the marinated salmon, and fry until both sides are golden brown and cooked through. Optionally, fry a sausage as well. It is normal for the fish skin to stick to the pan; it will be mashed later.
7. **Make the filling**: In a bowl, combine avocado flesh, yogurt, mustard, and black pepper. Mash into a paste with a fork. Be careful to use only a small amount of mustard to avoid an overpowering taste.
8. **Mix in the salmon**: Add the pan-fried salmon to the sauce bowl, roughly crush with a fork, and mix well, leaving some larger pieces of fish for better texture.
9. **Assemble the sandwich**: Once the baked baguette has cooled, cut open one side, and fill it with the salmon and avocado filling.
10. **Serve**: Enjoy with a fried egg, green leafy salad, etc., drizzle with balsamic vinegar, and pair with a refreshing drink.

## Health Advice
The baguette sandwich is rich in high-quality protein and healthy fats, making it suitable for those who work out or need an energy boost. However, the baguette is high in carbohydrates, so be sure to balance your intake with vegetables.
