# Blueberry Tiger Skin Cheese Ball
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August), All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 8 egg yolks
🍬 45g granulated sugar (for tiger skin)
🌽 15g cornstarch
🧀 150g cream cheese (filling)
🍬 35g granulated sugar (filling)
🫐 40g berry jam
🥛 300g heavy cream
🫐🔴 Mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries), as needed (for cooking jam)
🧂 A pinch of salt (improvement suggestion for final product, can enhance flavor layers)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
👨‍🍳 Stand mixer
🔥 Oven
🔪 Spatula
📦 Square baking pan
🏗️ Disposable cooling rack/drying rack
🥣 Hand whisk
🍳 Saucepan
⭕ Round mousse ring/mold
🫙 Plastic wrap
💼 Piping bag

## Preparation Steps
1. Preheat the oven to 210°C.
2. Place 8 egg yolks and 45g granulated sugar into the stand mixer bowl. Beat on high speed for about 8 minutes.
   - **Key point**: Beat until the egg yolk mixture turns noticeably pale and increases in volume, and the drips hold their shape without disappearing easily. This is crucial for forming the tiger skin pattern.
3. Sift 15g cornstarch into the beaten egg yolk mixture. Mix on low speed for about half a minute until evenly combined.
   - **Note**: Do not mix for too long; just combine evenly.
4. Pour the batter into a square baking pan lined with parchment paper. Use a spatula to smooth the surface.
   - **Note**: The batter has poor fluidity, so it requires patience to smooth it manually.
5. Place the pan in the preheated oven on the middle rack. Bake at 210°C for 7-8 minutes, until a distinct bumpy tiger skin pattern forms on the surface and it is browned.
6. While baking, make the berry jam: Heat the mixed berries in a saucepan and cook until thickened.
   - **Key point**: Cook until it reaches a certain thickness, suitable for filling.
7. Remove the baked cake sheet and let it cool completely on a cooling rack.
8. Make the berry cream cheese filling: Combine 150g cream cheese, 35g granulated sugar, 40g berry jam, and 300g heavy cream. Use a hand whisk to beat until the mixture shows clear lines and is firm in texture.
9. Take one piece of tiger skin cake and place it on plastic wrap, supporting the bottom with a round mold.
10. Pipe in a layer of berry cream cheese filling, add a little berry jam, then cover with another layer of cream.
    - **Key point**: Use plastic wrap and the mold to help shape it.
11. Lift the four corners of the plastic wrap, gather the tiger skin cake, and twist it tightly to form a ball shape.
    - **Key point**: Twist the plastic wrap tightly to set the shape of the cake ball.
12. It is ready to eat after setting. It resembles a rice ball in shape, with tiger skin patterns on the outside and a berry cream cheese filling inside.

## Health Advice
This blueberry tiger skin cheese ball is rich in protein and calcium. The egg yolks and cream cheese provide high-quality nutrition, but it is also high in calories, making it suitable as a post-workout snack or an occasional treat. Be mindful of sugar intake; diabetic patients should reduce the sugar amount.
