# Braised Beef in Chili Bean Sauce
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐

## Preparation Steps
1. Select whole beef short ribs, do not cut, and blanch directly in cold water.
**Key point**: Must be placed in cold water to allow blood foam to release slowly, effectively removing odor.

2. After water boils, skim off foam, cook until beef is set, then remove. Let cool slightly, then cut into evenly sized pieces.
**Key point**: Cutting after blanching keeps meat pieces more intact and prevents shrinkage.

3. Blanch broccoli, then remove and set aside; dice carrot, and prepare peas and minced garlic.

4. Heat wok and add oil. When oil is hot, add beef pieces and stir-fry over medium heat until surface is slightly charred and tightened.
**Key point**: This step uses the Maillard reaction to lock in moisture inside the beef, making the texture more flavorful.

5. Add Pixian chili bean paste and stir-fry until red oil releases. Then add scallion segments and ginger slices, stir-fry evenly. Pour a generous amount of Huadiao wine along the edge of the wok, then add light soy sauce, sugar, and white pepper powder.
   **Key point**: Both the wine and subsequent water additions should be hot liquids to prevent the meat from becoming tough due to temperature shock.

6. Pour enough boiling water into the wok to submerge the beef. Transfer all ingredients to a pressure cooker.

7. Cover with lid, cook over high heat until pressure is reached, then reduce to low heat and pressure cook for 20 minutes.
**Key point**: If using a regular pot, simmer over low heat; water should cover the meat by about 2 cm.

8. After cooking, remove the beef and arrange on a plate. Strain the cooking liquid through a strainer to remove solids, yielding a clean red soup.
9. In another pot, sauté minced garlic in a little oil until fragrant. Add diced carrot and peas, stir-fry, then pour in the strained original soup and bring to a boil. Add water starch to thicken the soup to a smooth, glossy consistency.
10. Evenly pour the thickened sauce over the plated beef and broccoli. Serve.
