# Beef Rib Pasta  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All-season meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Beef ribs: appropriate amount (sliced thinly)  
🍝 Pasta: appropriate amount  
🌶️ Dried chilies: appropriate amount (broken open and seeded)  
🧅 Scallions: appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🧄 Garlic: appropriate amount (sliced)  
🧀 Cheese: appropriate amount (grated, for garnish)  
🌿 Dried parsley: a pinch (for garnish)  

🧂 Salt: appropriate amount (for seasoning pasta water and marinating meat)  
🫒 Olive oil: appropriate amount (to prevent pasta from sticking and as cooking oil)  
🧂 Light soy sauce: appropriate amount (for the sauce)  
🦪 Oyster sauce: appropriate amount (for the sauce)  
🍬 Sugar: appropriate amount (for the sauce)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
♨️ Stove  
🥘 Cooking pot  
🍳 Pan  
🥢 Tongs  
🧀 Grater  

## Steps  
1. Slice the beef ribs thinly, add a little olive oil and salt, mix well, and marinate briefly to make the meat tender and flavorful.  
2. Bring water to a boil in a cooking pot, add salt and olive oil, cook the pasta until al dente, drain, and reserve a bowl of pasta water for later use.  
   **Note**: Refer to the package instructions for cooking time, usually about 10 minutes. Retaining the pasta water is key to a rich sauce later.  
3. Pour olive oil into a pan, add scallion sections, garlic slices, and dried chili pieces, and sauté over low heat until fragrant.  
4. Remove all the sautéed scallions, garlic, and chilies, leaving only the infused oil in the pan.  
   **Key Point**: Removing the aromatics prevents biting into burnt pieces later, while leaving a clean base flavor.  
5. Add the marinated beef rib slices to the infused oil and stir-fry quickly until just cooked.  
6. Add the cooked pasta, pour in the prepared sauce, and toss quickly to emulsify the sauce and coat each strand thoroughly.  
   **Key Point**: The starch in the pasta water helps create a thick, clingy sauce texture.  
7. Cook until the sauce thickens and the beef is fully cooked, then plate. Garnish with grated cheese and a sprinkle of dried parsley.  

## Health Advice  
Beef rib pasta combines high-quality protein and carbs, making it suitable for muscle building or fitness recovery. However, cheese and oyster sauce are high in sodium, so people with hypertension should reduce the amount. Combining beef with chilies may help boost metabolism. The reserved pasta water can be used as a natural thickener to help the sauce adhere more easily.
