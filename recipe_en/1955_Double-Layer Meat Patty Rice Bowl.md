# Double-Layer Meat Patty Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🧅 Onion, half (finely chopped)  
🍞 Breadcrumbs, 50g  
🥛 Milk, 100g  
🥩 Ground beef, 200g  
🐖 Ground pork, 300g  
🥚 Egg, 1  
🍚 Rice, as needed  
🧂 Sea salt, generous amount  
🌶 Black pepper, generous amount  
🍅 Ketchup, 2 tbsp  
🥫 Teriyaki sauce, 2 tbsp  
🍯 Honey, 1 tbsp  
🍷 White wine, 3 tbsp  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🔪 Knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🍳 Frying pan  
🥣 Bowl  
🍴 Fork  
🫗 Lid  

## Steps  
1. Finely chop the onion, sauté in a little oil until translucent, then remove and let cool.  
2. Add 50g breadcrumbs to 100g milk, allowing them to absorb fully.  
3. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork, cooled onion, egg, and the soaked breadcrumbs. Season with sea salt and black pepper. Mix in one direction until the mixture becomes sticky and firm.  
4. Oil your hands, then repeatedly toss the mixture to remove air. Divide into 4 equal portions and shape into thick patties.  
5. Heat enough oil in a frying pan. Add the patties and cook over medium-high heat until the bottom is set and browned. Flip and cook the other side until the Maillard reaction occurs.  
6. Pour out excess oil, add some hot water, cover with a lid, and simmer over low heat for 5 minutes.  
7. Uncover, add ketchup, teriyaki sauce, honey, and white wine. Continue heating and stirring until the sauce thickens and becomes glossy.  
8. Serve rice in a bowl, place the patties on top, and drizzle with the cooked sauce.

## Health Advice  
This double-layer meat patty rice bowl is rich in protein and iron, suitable for muscle building, but is high in calories. Pair with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. Control the amount of sea salt; those with high blood pressure should reduce it by half.
