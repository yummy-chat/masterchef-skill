# Cantonese-Style Homestyle Curry Fish Balls
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍢 Fish balls, about half a bowl  
🧈 Fish tofu/fried tofu, about half a bowl  
🧂 Curry powder, about 2 tablespoons  
🥛 Milk/coconut milk, as needed (coconut milk works better)  
🍬 Sugar, about 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Salt, as needed  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍲 Deep non-stick milk pot  
🔘 Pot lid  

## Steps
1. Place the fish balls and fish tofu in a small milk pot, then add enough water to submerge the ingredients.  
2. Add 2 tablespoons of curry powder, 1 tablespoon of sugar, and an appropriate amount of salt.  
3. Pour in an appropriate amount of milk or coconut milk, stirring well to create a creamy, aromatic broth.  
   **Note**: Using coconut milk yields a richer and smoother broth.  
4. Cover with a lid and simmer for about 10 minutes until the broth thickens and the fish balls are fully infused with flavor.  
   **Key point**: Simmer until the broth thickens so the curry flavor clings well to the fish balls.  

## Health Tips
Fish balls paired with curry and coconut milk are rich in calcium and protein, making them suitable for those needing an energy boost. However, fish balls are high in sodium, so individuals with high blood pressure should control their intake.
