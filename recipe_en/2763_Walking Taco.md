# Walking Taco
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Season: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Ground beef 200g  
🧄 Minced garlic, to taste  
🌽 Corn chips 1 bag  
🧀 Shredded mozzarella cheese, to taste  
🍅 Diced tomatoes, to taste  
🧅 Diced onions, to taste  
🥬 Diced celery, to taste  
🥒 Diced pickles, to taste  
🌿 Cilantro, to taste  
🍝 Pasta sauce, to taste  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
⚫ Black pepper, to taste  
🥛 Sour cream, to taste  
🟡 Yellow mustard, to taste  
🌶️ Hot sauce, to taste  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
✂️ Scissors  
🍳 Pan  
🥄 Wooden spatula  
🥣 Bowl  

## Preparation Steps
1. Take the corn chip bag and cut a large opening horizontally through the middle with scissors, creating a bowl-shaped container.
   **Note**: Keep the bag intact while cutting so it can be held securely or placed down.
2. Place the pan over medium heat, add the ground beef and minced garlic, and stir to break up the meat.
3. Continue sautéing until the moisture from the ground beef evaporates and clear oil separates at the bottom of the pan.
4. Add the pasta sauce, salt, and black pepper. Stir well and continue cooking until the meat sauce thickens and clumps together.
5. Dice the tomatoes, onions, celery, and pickles respectively, and set aside.
6. Spread a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese at the bottom of the cut corn chip bag.
7. Place the slightly cooled meat sauce on top of the cheese.
   **Note**: The meat sauce should be slightly cooled to prevent high heat from damaging the bag or melting the cheese too quickly.
8. Layer the diced tomatoes, onions, celery, and pickles on top of the meat sauce in order.
9. Squeeze sour cream, yellow mustard, and hot sauce on top according to personal preference.
10. Finally, sprinkle cilantro on top and serve.

## Health Advice  
Walking Taco uses the corn chip bag as a container, cleverly eliminating the need to wash dishes, but be cautious of burns. Ground beef paired with cheese and sour cream provides protein and calcium, making it suitable for energy replenishment or party snacks. It is recommended to increase the vegetable amount and reduce sauces to improve nutritional balance.
