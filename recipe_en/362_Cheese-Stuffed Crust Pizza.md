# Cheese-Stuffed Crust Pizza
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 Toast slices 3-4 slices
🥚 Egg 1
🧀 Cheese sticks 3 sticks
🧀 Shredded mozzarella cheese About 50g (divided into two layers)
🫑 Mixed vegetable diced (bell pepper, onion, etc.) About 20g (pre-cut and set aside)
🥩 Diced ham About 20g (pre-cut and set aside)
🦐 Shrimp 2 pieces
🍅 Ketchup As needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🧤 Disposable gloves
📜 Parchment paper pizza pan
🥄 Stainless steel spoon
🔥 Oven
🪨 Baking tray

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the toast slices into a stainless steel bowl, crack in the egg, and wearing disposable gloves, crush the toast and mix thoroughly with the egg to form a uniform yellow dough.
   **Key Point**: Knead thoroughly so the egg fully integrates into the toast; the dough should have no large toast remnants, be evenly textured, and not sticky.
2. Place the dough on a parchment paper pizza pan, press it with your palm into a thin crust base, covering the pan, and make the edges slightly thicker.
   **Key Point**: Apply even pressure while pressing to ensure uniform crust thickness.
3. Arrange cheese sticks along the edge of the crust base, fold the edge inward to wrap the cheese sticks, forming a stuffed crust, and press tightly to seal.
   **Key Point**: The stuffed crust must be sealed tightly to prevent the cheese from leaking during baking.
4. Place the stuffed crust base into a preheated oven at 180°C, and bake for 5 minutes until the crust is set.
   **Status Check**: The edges of the crust are slightly golden and the shape is fully fixed.
5. Remove the set crust, squeeze ketchup into the center and spread evenly with a spoon; first sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese, then top with mixed vegetable diced, diced ham, arrange the shrimp, and finally sprinkle another layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
   **Key Point**: Evenly distribute the toppings; arrange the shrimp decoratively to enhance appearance.
6. Place the topped pizza back into the oven, bake at 180°C for 10-12 minutes, until the cheese is fully melted and the surface is slightly golden.
   **Status Check**: The cheese is golden yellow with slight browning spots, and the crust is fully baked.
   **Key Point**: Let the pizza rest for 1 minute before cutting to prevent excessive cheese flow.

## Health Advice
This toast-based cheese-stuffed crust pizza replaces traditional dough with egg-moistened bread, offering lower carbohydrate content and easier preparation, making it perfect for a quick indulgence. Calorie control is appropriate, with shrimp and vegetables adding protein and dietary fiber, but cheese remains a high-fat ingredient, so pairing with a salad is recommended to balance the meal.
