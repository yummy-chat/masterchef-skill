# Soul-Stirring Beef Rice Bowl

Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Cooking Steps  
1. In a bowl, add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, white pepper powder, sesame oil, some sesame seeds, and water. Stir until well combined, then add some of the sliced onions to soak.  
   *Note: The sauce is the core of the flavor, so ensure it is thoroughly mixed.*  

2. Finely slice the remaining onion, and slice the green and red bell peppers into thin strips. Set aside.  

3. In the frying pan, layer the mung bean sprouts on the bottom, then a layer of sliced onion, and finally place the raw beef rolls evenly on top.  
   *Key point: Layer strictly in the order of "bean sprouts - onion - beef rolls."*  

4. Pour the prepared sauce along with the soaked onions evenly over the beef rolls. Cover with a lid and braise over medium heat for 5 minutes, until the beef rolls are completely cooked and have changed color.  

5. Remove the lid, turn up the heat to high, and reduce the sauce for about 1 minute and 30 seconds, until the sauce thickens and coats the ingredients. Sprinkle the remaining sliced onion, green and red bell pepper strips, and sesame seeds on top.  

6. Serve the beef rolls and sauce over the cooked rice.  
   *Note: Ensure there is enough sauce to soak into the rice.*  

## Health Advice  
This rice bowl is rich in protein but high in fat, so it is recommended to pair it with light vegetables to balance the calories. Onions and mung bean sprouts provide dietary fiber, and be mindful of the sugar content in the sauce. Since the beef rolls themselves are high in fat, no additional cooking oil is needed.
