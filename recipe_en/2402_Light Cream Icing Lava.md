# Light Cream Icing Lava  
Cuisine: Everyday Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🫕 Light Cream 100g  
🍫 Dark Chocolate 80-90g  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Spoon  
♨️ Microwave or double boiler  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Place the dark chocolate and light cream in a bowl in a 1:1 ratio.  
2. Heat using a double boiler or microwave on high for about 1 minute. Remove and stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.  
   **Key Point**: Maintain a 1:1 ratio of chocolate to light cream to ensure a rich, soft center in the final product. Avoid overheating to prevent oil-water separation.  
3. Let the chocolate mixture cool to hand temperature. It can be used directly as a cake glaze. Alternatively, pour it into a mold and refrigerate until set to make an "Icing Lava" dessert.  
   **Key Point**: Refrigerate for about 1-2 hours. Once set, the texture is smooth, rich, and melts in the mouth.

## Health Advice  
Although this dessert is simple, dark chocolate is rich in antioxidants and beneficial for cardiovascular health in moderation. However, light cream is high in calories, so it is recommended to limit each serving to a small portion and pair it with fruit to balance the sweetness. It is suitable as an occasional afternoon treat.
