# Cheese-Stuffed Shrimp Balls with Potato Chips
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Season: All-Year  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥔 Potato chips (original flavor or leftover chips)  
🦐 Shrimp paste (pre-seasoned seafood product)  
🧀 Cheese slices (cut into small pieces or torn into bits, used as filling)  
🥚 Egg (beaten into egg wash)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔐 Sealed bag (for crushing chips and shaking coating)  
♨️ Air fryer  
📜 Baking paper (to line the fryer basket)  
🥣 Bowl (for egg wash)  

## Instructions
1. **Process the chips**: Place the potato chips into a sealed bag and crush them repeatedly with your fist into a fine powder.
   - **Key point**: The finer the crumbs, the more even the coating, and the crispier the outer texture.

2. **Shape and fill**: Take an appropriate amount of shrimp paste, flatten it into a thin disc in your palm, place a small piece of cheese in the center, then gather the shrimp paste around it and roll it into a ball.
   - **Note**: Flatten the shrimp paste before filling to make it easier to wrap the filling and round the ball, preventing the cheese from leaking out.

3. **Coat with egg wash and crumbs**: First, evenly coat each shrimp ball with egg wash, then place it into the sealed bag with the crushed chips. Close the bag and shake to thoroughly coat the surface with the chip crumbs.
   - **Key point**: The shaking method saves effort and ensures full coverage. Wearing gloves can prevent sticking.

4. **Cook**: Place the coated shrimp balls on a baking paper-lined air fryer basket and cook at 180°C for 8 minutes.
   - **Doneness check**: Remove once the exterior is golden and crispy, and the cheese inside has melted.
   - **Note**: Adjust the cooking time as needed based on the size of the shrimp balls and the performance of your air fryer to avoid burning.

5. Serve immediately while hot. The outer layer is crispy and fragrant, the shrimp meat is tender and bouncy, and the cheese pulls into rich, gooey strands.

## Health Tips  
This dish substitutes breadcrumbs with potato chips, which is creative but high in calories. Pair it with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition, or enjoy it as an occasional indulgence. Shrimp paste and cheese already contain salt, so no additional seasoning is needed. It is ideal for those craving a crispy texture without deep frying.
