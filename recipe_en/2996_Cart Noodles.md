# Cart Noodles
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍜 1 pack of udon noodles
🍥 3-4 beef balls
🍥 2 fish balls
🧀 Half slice of cheese
🥒 1 stalk of celery (served raw as a side)
🥒 1 cucumber (served raw as a side)
🥫 About 2 tablespoons of XO sauce

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍲 Cooking pot
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Tongs
🥄 Spoon
🥢 Chopsticks

## Cooking Steps
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot, then add the udon noodles, beef balls, and fish balls. Cook together until fully done.
2. Use tongs to remove the cooked noodles and balls, and place them into a bowl.
3. Add about 2 tablespoons of XO sauce and mix thoroughly with chopsticks. **Key point**: XO sauce is the soul seasoning; make sure to mix it well.
4. Place half a slice of cheese on top of the mixed noodles, allowing it to partially melt using residual heat.
5. Serve with washed and cut raw celery and cucumber on the side to cut the richness.

## Health Tips
XO sauce is a seafood essence, but it is high in sodium. Pairing it with raw celery and cucumber helps cut the greasiness and adds dietary fiber, making it suitable for those needing a quick energy boost. The melted cheese adds calories; during a weight loss phase, it is recommended to substitute with low-fat cheese.
