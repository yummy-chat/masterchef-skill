# Razor Clams with Eggs
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦪 Razor clams, appropriate amount (cleaned)  
🥚 Eggs, appropriate amount  
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (finely minced)  
🌶️ Small chili peppers, appropriate amount (sliced into rings)  
🌿 Chinese chives, appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🍶 Cooking wine, appropriate amount (for blanching)  
🍾 Yellow wine, appropriate amount  
🥡 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount  
🦪 Oyster sauce, appropriate amount  
🍬 White sugar, appropriate amount (to enhance freshness)  
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount  
🧴 Dark soy sauce, appropriate amount (for coloring)  
🍶 Vinegar, appropriate amount (drizzled before removing from heat)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥣 Bowl  
🔪 Wok spatula  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Bring water to a boil in a wok, add the razor clams, and blanch with a little cooking wine for about ten seconds. Remove immediately once the clams open.  
2. Break open the blanched razor clams, remove the internal organs and black threads one by one, and clean thoroughly.  
3. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, combine yellow wine, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, white sugar, salt, and dark soy sauce. Stir until well mixed.  
4. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk thoroughly. Add the prepared razor clam meat and mix well.  
5. Heat the wok over high heat, pour in cooking oil, and once the oil is hot, add the egg mixture with clams.  
   **Key point**: Gently push the egg mixture with a wok spatula to allow the eggs to set into large pieces while remaining tender and smooth.  
6. When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly moist, add the minced ginger, sliced small chili peppers, and Chinese chive sections. Stir-fry quickly and evenly.  
7. Pour the prepared sauce along the edge of the wok, and stir-fry quickly until all ingredients are evenly coated with the sauce.  
8. Just before removing from heat, drizzle a little vinegar along the edge of the wok, then immediately turn off the heat and plate.  

## Health Advice  
Razor clams are rich in high-quality protein and zinc, while eggs provide lecithin, making this dish suitable for those needing to boost brain function and immunity. Note that the clams should be blanched briefly to avoid a tough texture. People with gout should consume in moderation.
