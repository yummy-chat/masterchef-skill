# Standing Fish Undefeated  
Cuisine: Cantonese Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Preparation Steps  
1.  Score the fish: Make a deep cut into the thick part of the fish's back without cutting through the belly; cut one side of each belly to allow the fish to bend into a U-shape for standing.  
2.  Rinse the fish with clean water, then soak it in ice-cold water for about 15 minutes to make the flesh whiter.  
3.  Remove the fish, pat it dry both outside and inside, and sprinkle a thin layer of salt both inside and out. Lay ginger slices and scallion segments on the bottom of a plate, stand the fish upright on the plate, and insert toothpicks horizontally through both sides of the belly to secure the shape. Drizzle a little cooking oil over the fish.  
4.  After the steamer water boils, place the fish in and steam over high heat for 8 minutes.  
**Note**: The fish must be added after the water boils, and maintain high heat throughout.  
5.  While steaming the fish, mix the steamed fish soy sauce with a little dark soy sauce, bring it to a boil, and set aside.  
6.  Soak shredded scallions and bell pepper shreds in ice-cold water to make them curl and become crisp.  
7.  After the fish is steamed, discard the liquid from the plate, and remove the ginger, scallion segments, and toothpicks.  
**Note**: The steamer liquid must be completely discarded to avoid residual fishy taste.  
8.  Lay the curled scallion shreds and bell pepper shreds on top of the fish.  
9.  Heat a spoonful of oil until it is about 70% hot, then quickly pour it over the scallion shreds to release the aroma.  
10. Slowly pour the hot sauce around the edge of the plate, and it is ready to serve.  

## Health Advice  
This steamed standing fish recipe uses minimal oil and salt, is high in protein and low in fat, making it suitable for those managing weight or needing high-quality protein. However, note that the soy sauce is relatively high in salt, so consume in moderation. Ensure the steamer liquid is completely discarded, as it can affect the flavor.
