# Braised Noodles with Green Beans
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 Green beans 300g, washed, strings removed, ends trimmed
🥩 Pork belly 250g, sliced
🧅 Scallion 20g, chopped
🧄 Garlic 20g, minced
⭐ Star anise 4 small petals (about half a whole), do not use more
🌶️ Dried chili pepper segments, to taste
🍜 Fresh noodles 500g, must use fresh medium-thin noodles

🧂 Cooking oil, normal stir-fry amount (used in two batches)
🧂 Thirteen-spice powder 1/3 tsp (about 1g)
🧂 Reduced-salt light soy sauce 4 tbsp (about 40g), or 3 tbsp if using regular light soy sauce
🧂 Dark soy sauce 1.5 tbsp (about 15g)
🧂 Oyster sauce 1.5 tbsp (about 15g)
🧂 White sugar 1 tsp (about 4g), for blending and enhancing freshness
🧂 Water three bowls (about 780g)
🧂 MSG, to taste

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Iron wok (stir-fry pan)
🪵 Wooden lid
🥢 Two pairs of chopsticks
🥄 Wok spatula

## Steps
### Preparation
1. Wash green beans, remove strings along the growing direction, and trim both ends.
2. Slice pork belly; chop scallion; mince garlic; prepare star anise and dried chili segments.
3. Mix chopped scallion and minced garlic together and set aside.

### Cooking
1. **Pan-fry the noodles**
   - Add a normal stir-fry amount of oil to the wok. Over high heat, swirl to coat the sides, heat until just smoking, then reduce to medium-low heat. Spread the fresh noodles flat in the wok.
   - **Note**: During frying, you may briefly remove the wok from heat and shake it to prevent sticking. After about 2 minutes, when one side is golden and set, flip the entire noodle cake.
   - Drizzle another circle of oil along the edge of the wok, and fry the other side to golden brown using the same method. Adjust the wok's position for even heating.
   - Once both sides are golden, remove and set aside.

2. **Stir-fry pork belly and aromatics**
   - Add another normal stir-fry amount of oil to the wok. Add sliced pork belly and stir-fry over high heat until the fat renders and edges are slightly crispy.
   - Add thirteen-spice powder and fry briefly in the hot oil to release fragrance.
   - Reduce to medium heat, add half of the scallion-garlic mixture, star anise, and dried chili segments. Stir-fry until fragrant.

3. **Braise the green beans**
   - Add green beans, return to high heat, and stir-fry for about 1 minute until all beans turn bright green.
   - Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, and white sugar. Stir-fry evenly until the sauce aroma develops.
   - Add three bowls of water. Bring to a boil over high heat, cover with lid, reduce to medium heat, and braise for 10 minutes.

4. **Braise the noodles**
   - Remove the lid. Scoop out half of the broth and vegetables from the wok and set aside. Place the pan-fried noodle cake into the wok on top of the remaining vegetables. Then evenly pour the reserved broth and vegetables over the noodle cake.
   - Cover with lid and braise over medium heat for 2 minutes.
   - Remove lid. Use chopsticks to gently separate the noodles, being careful not to break them.
   - Cover again and braise over the lowest heat for 2 minutes.
   - **Key step**: Turn off the heat. Let the noodles continue cooking in residual heat for another 3 minutes.

5. **Final sauce reduction**
   - Remove lid. Add the remaining scallion-garlic mixture and MSG. Over the lowest heat, use two pairs of chopsticks to lift the noodles from the bottom and toss repeatedly.
   - **Note**: Do not stir randomly to avoid breaking the noodles. Toss until the liquid at the bottom is fully absorbed, leaving only oil with no visible liquid. The noodles should be glossy and dry. Then turn off the heat.

## Health Advice
Braised noodles with green beans provide quality protein and fat from pork belly, combined with dietary fiber from green beans, offering strong satiety, suitable for those with high physical exertion. Be mindful of controlling noodle and oil portions. Reduced-salt light soy sauce helps lower sodium intake; those who are obese or have high blood pressure should consume in moderation.
