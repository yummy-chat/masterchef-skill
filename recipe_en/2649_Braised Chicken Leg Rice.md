# Braised Chicken Leg Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken leg meat, appropriate amount (cut into pieces)
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms, appropriate amount (sliced)
🍚 Cooked rice, appropriate amount
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (sliced)
🧅 Scallions, appropriate amount (chopped, for garnish)

**Marinade**
🧂 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount
🧂 Dark soy sauce, appropriate amount
🍶 Cooking wine, appropriate amount
🌾 Cornstarch, appropriate amount

**Sauce**
🧂 Light soy sauce, 2 tablespoons
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon
🍬 White sugar, half a tablespoon

🫒 Cooking oil, appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok (with glass lid)
🥄 Stainless steel spoon
🥢 Slotted spoon
🍚 Rice paddle

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the chicken leg meat into pieces, place in a bowl, add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, cooking wine, and cornstarch. Use your hands to mix thoroughly, then marinate for 10 minutes.
   **Note**: Mixing thoroughly allows the meat pieces to fully absorb the seasonings.
2. Slice the shiitake mushrooms, slice the ginger, and chop the scallions. Set aside.
3. Prepare the sauce in advance: Mix 2 tablespoons of light soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce, and half a tablespoon of white sugar until well combined.
4. Pour cooking oil into the wok and heat until hot. Add the ginger slices and stir-fry over medium-high heat until fragrant.
5. Add the marinated chicken leg meat and continue stir-frying over medium-high heat until the meat changes color and the surface is slightly charred.
   **Key point**: Searing until slightly charred can release a richer aroma.
6. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms and stir-fry until they soften and are cooked through.
7. Pour in the prepared sauce and stir-fry quickly to combine, ensuring all ingredients are evenly coated with the sauce.
8. Directly spread a layer of cooked rice over the ingredients in the wok, **do not stir**, so the rice rests on top of the dish.
9. Cover with the lid, reduce the heat to low, and braise for 5 minutes.
   **Key point**: Braising over low heat allows the rice to fully absorb the broth.
10. Before serving, sprinkle with chopped scallions and use the residual heat to release their fragrance. Serve immediately.

## Healthy Tips
Chicken leg meat is rich in protein and iron, paired with the dietary fiber from shiitake mushrooms, offering a balanced nutritional profile suitable for a busy workday meal. For those watching their sugar intake, reduce the amount of white sugar; dark soy sauce already contains salt, so no additional salt is needed for those with a lighter palate.
