# Stir-Fried Eggs with Green Peppers  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 Eggs, about 4 pieces  
🌶️ Green peppers, appropriate amount (cut into pieces)  
🧂 Salt, a pinch  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Steps  
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Using chopsticks, beat them rapidly in one direction until the mixture is uniform and foamy, and becomes fluffy.  
2. Wash the green peppers and cut them into bite-sized pieces.  
3. Place the wok over high heat to heat up, then pour in enough cooking oil. Heat until the oil surface just starts to smoke slightly, and a drop of egg mixture quickly expands and floats upon contact.  
4. Maintain high heat, pour the egg mixture into the wok. Gently stir with chopsticks to let the uncooked egg flow to the bottom, ensuring even heating and a fluffy, puffed texture.  
5. When most of the egg is set and one side is fried to a golden brown, sprinkle in a pinch of salt for seasoning.  
6. Add the green pepper pieces. Stir-fry quickly over high heat until the peppers turn bright green and become tender with a fragrant aroma. Immediately remove from heat and serve.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is rich in high-quality protein and vitamin C, making it suitable for those in need of a quick energy boost. Use less oil and salt, and beat the eggs thoroughly before frying for a more tender and smooth texture.
