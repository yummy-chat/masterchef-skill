# Microwave Black Oat Porridge
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 Black oats, about half a cup  
🥚 Egg, 1  
💧 Water, a small amount  
🥛 Milk, as needed  
🟡 Turmeric powder, a pinch  
🟤 Cinnamon powder, a pinch  
⚫ Freshly ground black pepper, a pinch  
🫐 Blueberries, a few (for serving)  
🥜 Mixed nuts, about 1 tablespoon  
🌰 Pecans, about 1 tablespoon  
🧀 Cheese, about 2-3 pieces (small balls)  
🥩 Braised beef, 2-3 slices (pre-braised)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🔬 Microwave  
🥛 Glass measuring cup (with spout, microwave-safe)  
🥄 Spoon  
🌡️ Grinder (for black pepper)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Pour the black oats into a microwave-safe glass measuring cup, crack in a raw egg, and add a small amount of water. Stir until well combined.  
   **Key Point**: The amount of water determines the final thickness of the porridge; adjust according to personal preference.  
2. Place the mixture in the microwave and heat on high for 1.5 minutes.  
   **Key Point**: The heating time may need adjustment based on microwave power; watch carefully to prevent overflow.  
3. Remove the heated porridge. At this point, the egg should be set and the oats softened.  
4. Add a pinch each of turmeric powder, cinnamon powder, and freshly ground black pepper in turn. Then stir in the mixed nuts and pecans.  
   **Key Point**: Pecans are high in oil, so control the amount used.  
5. Pour in a small amount of milk and stir well. The porridge base is now ready.  
6. Serve the porridge with blueberries, cheese, and braised beef slices.  
   **Note**: Cheese can replace salad dressing for flavor, while the beef provides additional protein.  

## Health Advice  
This porridge offers a balanced mix of carbohydrates, protein, and fat. The braised beef and egg provide a strong sense of satiety, making it suitable for those who exercise or need a high-energy breakfast. Note that nuts and cheese are calorie-dense; reduce the amount by half during weight loss periods.
