# Stir-Fried Chicken with Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken breast, one piece (about 8.5 yuan)
🍄 Mushrooms, appropriate amount (about 10 yuan, remove the root before weighing)
🌶️ Green chili pepper, appropriate amount (for color)
🌶️ Red chili pepper, appropriate amount (for color)
🧅 Scallion, appropriate amount (part crushed for scallion-ginger water, part cut into sections for aromatics)
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (part crushed for scallion-ginger water, part sliced for aromatics)
🧄 Garlic, appropriate amount (sliced for aromatics)
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount (for marinating and sauce)
🌿 White pepper powder, appropriate amount (for marinating and sauce)
💧 Scallion-ginger water, appropriate amount (500g meat with 100g water, made by squeezing crushed scallion and ginger)
🥚 Egg white, a little
🌽 Cornstarch, appropriate amount (for marinating and sauce)
🫒 Cooking oil, a little (to lock in moisture)
🍬 Sugar, appropriate amount
🧂 MSG, appropriate amount
💧 Water, appropriate amount (to dilute the sauce)
🫗 Sesame oil, a little
🐖 Lard, appropriate amount (for aromatics)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board and chef's knife
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for marinating chicken)
🍶 Bowl and spoon (for preparing sauce)
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted spoon
⚖️ Kitchen scale

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare the chicken breast**: Rinse the chicken breast, then cut it into four small pieces along the grain. Slice diagonally into uniform thin pieces.
   *Note*: Do not wash after cutting to avoid excess water affecting the coating.
2. **Marinate the chicken**: Add salt, white pepper powder, and scallion-ginger water to the chicken pieces. Rub and mix vigorously until sticky and gelatinous, allowing the chicken to fully absorb the water. Then add a little egg white and cornstarch, mix well again. Finally, drizzle in a little cooking oil and mix to lock in moisture.
3. **Prepare the mushrooms**: Slice the mushrooms into slightly thick pieces, as they will shrink during cooking.
4. **Prepare the sauce**: In a bowl, combine salt, sugar, MSG, white pepper powder, and cornstarch. Dilute with water and stir to dissolve. Drizzle in sesame oil and mix well.
5. **Prepare the aromatics**: Cut the scallion into sections, slice the ginger and garlic, and cut the green and red chili peppers into chunks.
6. **Cook the chicken**: Bring water in the wok to a boil, then reduce to low heat to maintain a gentle simmer. Add the coated chicken pieces one by one and cook over low heat for about half a minute until the chicken turns white and is cooked through. Remove and drain.
*Key*: Maintain low heat and a gentle simmer throughout to prevent the coating from being washed off, ensuring tender chicken.
7. **Fry the aromatics**: Heat a separate wok, add lard, and stir-fry the scallion, ginger, and garlic over low heat until slightly yellow. Once the fragrance has infused the oil, remove the aromatics and place them together with the cooked chicken. Keep the oil in the wok.
8. **Stir-fry the mushrooms**: Using the remaining oil in the wok, add the mushroom slices and stir-fry vigorously over high heat until the mushrooms release their own moisture and it partially evaporates, leaving some savory juice.
9. **Combine and season**: Add the green and red chili pepper slices and stir-fry briefly. Then return the chicken and the fried aromatics to the wok. Stir the sauce again, drizzle it in, and quickly stir-fry until evenly coated. Turn off the heat and serve.
*Note*: Do not stir-fry for too long after seasoning to maintain the tenderness of the chicken and the crispness of the vegetables.

## Health Tips
Mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber and vitamin D, while chicken breast is low in fat and high in protein, making this dish an excellent choice for fitness enthusiasts and those watching their calorie intake. Ensure the chicken is thoroughly coated and cooked over a gentle simmer to keep it tender and not dry.
