# Tomato Beef Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

🍅 Tomato, 1-2 pieces (cut into chunks)

🍜 Vermicelli, 1 small handful (optional, not pre-soaked)
🧄 Minced garlic,适量
🫒 Cooking oil,适量

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🍳 Wok
🥢 Long chopsticks or slotted spoon
🥣 Bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. Spread the tofu skin flat, evenly lay a layer of beef slices on top, then spread shrimp paste over the beef, place washed cabbage leaves on top, and roll tightly from one end to form beef and cabbage rolls.
   **Note**: Compact the roll firmly during rolling to prevent it from falling apart while cooking.

2. Cut the tomatoes into chunks, mince the garlic, and slice the ham sausage. Set aside.

3. Heat the wok over high heat, pour in an appropriate amount of cooking oil, and once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

4. Add the tomato chunks and stir-fry over medium heat until they soften, release juice, and turn into a paste.

5. Add enough water to cover, bring to a boil over high heat to create a tomato broth base.

6. Place the beef and cabbage rolls, ham sausage slices, and vermicelli into the wok, and cook until the beef rolls float and change color and the vermicelli is fully cooked, then remove from heat.
   **Key point**: Do not overcook the beef rolls; cook only until just done to maintain a tender texture.

## Health Tips
This dish is rich in protein, and the sweet and sour tomato broth is appetizing, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts and children with poor appetite. Vermicelli and beef slices are relatively high in calories, so portion control is recommended. Remove the beef rolls as soon as they are just cooked to avoid a tough texture.
