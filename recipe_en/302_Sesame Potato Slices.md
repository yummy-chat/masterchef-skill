# Sesame Potato Slices
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Yellow-fleshed potatoes, about 500g (peeled, sliced 2-3mm thick)
🥜 Peanut butter, 1 tablespoon
🌰 Sesame paste, 1 tablespoon
🍬 White sugar, 3 tablespoons
🧂 Light soy sauce, 3 tablespoons
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon
🍶 Fragrant vinegar, 3 tablespoons
🌿 Five-spice powder, 1 tablespoon
🧄 Garlic water, to taste (optional, add according to preference)
🫒 Sesame oil, 1 tablespoon
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for thinning the sauce)
🌶️ Chili oil, to taste (optional, for adding heat and aroma)
🫘 Toasted sesame seeds, to taste

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board
🥣 Large glass bowl (for washing potato slices and final mixing)
🍲 Cooking pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Medium bowl (for cold water to cool)
🍵 Small bowl (for mixing sauce)
🥢 Chopsticks
🧤 Food gloves

## Cooking Steps
1. Peel the potatoes and slice them 2-3mm thick, starting from the largest side.
2. Place the sliced potatoes in clean water and wash them thoroughly by hand to remove surface starch, changing the water until it runs clear to prevent oxidation and darkening.
3. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the potato slices, and blanch for about 1 minute until they turn translucent but still firm, then immediately remove.
4. Quickly transfer the blanched slices into cold water to cool, which keeps them crisp. Then drain well and set aside.
5. Prepare the sauce: Place peanut butter, sesame paste, sugar, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, fragrant vinegar, five-spice powder, sesame oil, and garlic water in a small bowl. Stir with chopsticks first. If the sauce is difficult to dissolve, do not add water; instead, gradually add cooking oil while stirring until smooth and pourable.
6. Combine: Put the drained potato slices in a large bowl, pour in the prepared sauce, and add chili oil and toasted sesame seeds as desired. Wear gloves and mix thoroughly by hand, ensuring each slice is evenly coated with the sauce.
7. Plate and serve immediately. The texture is crisp, and the sauce is rich and aromatic.

## Health Tips
This sesame potato slice dish uses potatoes as a substitute for staple foods, paired with sesame paste and vinegar, making it low-fat and refreshing. It is suitable for weight loss periods or as an appetizer in summer. Blanching the potato slices and then cooling them in cold water keeps them crispy, and using oil to thin the sauce prevents bitterness. Note that the sugar and oil content in the sauce is relatively high, so control the amount used.
