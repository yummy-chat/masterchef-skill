# Leftover Medley  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Rice, as needed  
💧 Water, as needed  
🥚 Egg, 1  
🥦 Leftovers (such as broccoli, etc.), as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Bowl  
🍽️ Plate  
♨️ Microwave  
🥄 Spoon or Fork  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Take a heat-resistant bowl, add an appropriate amount of rice, and pour in water.  
2. Add the leftover dishes into the bowl, then crack in a raw egg.  
3. Invert a plate to cover the bowl's opening to prevent splattering during heating.  
4. Place the bowl in the microwave and heat on high for 8 minutes.  
5. After heating, remove the bowl, carefully take off the covering plate, and enjoy.  

## Health Advice  
Before reheating leftovers, ensure they have not spoiled. Adding a raw egg boosts protein content and flavor, but make sure the egg is thoroughly cooked. This dish is suitable for busy office workers and students, but repeated heating may lead to vitamin loss.
