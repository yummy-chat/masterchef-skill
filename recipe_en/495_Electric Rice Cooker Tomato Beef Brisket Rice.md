# Electric Rice Cooker Tomato Beef Brisket Rice  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Beef brisket (flank cut, with tendon and fat) about 600g  
🍅 Tomatoes moderate amount (cut into chunks, no need to peel)  
🧅 Shallots moderate amount (peeled, whole)  
🫚 Ginger moderate amount (smashed with skin on or cut into chunks)  
🧄 Garlic moderate amount (peeled, whole)  
⭐ Star anise a pinch  
🌿 Bay leaves a pinch  
🫒 Cooking oil moderate amount  
🧂 Light soy sauce about 5 tablespoons  
🍬 Rock sugar 50g  
🥫 Tomato paste 100g  
💧 Water moderate amount (enough to submerge ingredients)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🍚 Electric rice cooker  
🔪 Chef’s knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥄 Spoon  

## Instructions  
1. Cut the beef brisket into bite-sized cubes; remove the stem ends of the tomatoes and cut them into six wedges; peel the shallots and garlic, and smash the ginger with the skin on.  
   **Note**: Do not cut the beef brisket too large, as it will shrink during cooking.  
2. Pour cooking oil into the electric rice cooker’s inner pot, and place shallots, ginger, garlic, star anise, and bay leaves at the bottom.  
   **Key point**: Use the rice cooker’s heating function to briefly sauté the aromatics and release their fragrance.  
3. Add the raw beef brisket cubes, light soy sauce, rock sugar, followed by the tomato chunks and tomato paste.  
4. Add enough water to fully submerge all ingredients. Close the lid, select the “quick cook” mode, and simmer for 1 hour 20 minutes.  
   **Key**: Add all the water at once; after opening the lid, the beef brisket should be tender and the sauce thick.  
5. Once done, ladle the tomato beef brisket and sauce over steamed rice and serve.  

## Health Tips  
Beef brisket is rich in iron and protein, while tomatoes provide vitamin C and lycopene, making this dish suitable for replenishing iron and blood. Be mindful of the rice portion, as the fat from the beef brisket and rock sugar add considerable calories.
