# Rice Cold Cake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍚 Rice 300g (Chao Gui rice is preferred; for regular rice, add an appropriate amount of pea starch)
💧 Water Approximately 1500ml (used in batches: 300ml for rice paste, 900ml for alkaline water, 300ml for thinning the rice batter)
🧂 Edible Alkali 3g
🍯 Brown Sugar 100g
🌰 White Sesame Seeds Appropriate amount
🥣 Pea Starch Appropriate amount (add when using regular rice)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥛 Glass Bowl
🌀 Blender
🕸️ Strainer
🍳 Non-Stick Pan
🥄 Silicone Spatula
🥣 Small Bowls (as molds)
🥄 Spoon
🍯 Small Milk Pan

## Cooking Steps
1. Wash 300g of rice thoroughly, soak in enough water until the grains can be easily crushed by hand.
2. Drain the soaked rice, pour into the blender, add 300ml of water, blend at high speed for about 20 minutes until the rice paste is smooth. Strain through a strainer to remove coarse particles.
3. Add 3g of edible alkali to 900ml of water, stir until fully dissolved to make alkaline water.
4. Mix the strained rice paste with the alkaline water evenly, pour into a non-stick pan, turn on medium heat, and stir continuously in one direction with a silicone spatula.
5. Cook until the rice batter becomes thick, smooth, and large bubbles appear on the surface. When lifted with a spatula, it should drip slowly in a triangular shape. At this point, add 300ml of water, continue stirring and cooking for 5-6 minutes until the batter is smooth again, bubbles rise, and it still drips slowly in a triangular shape when lifted.
6. Turn off the heat, quickly divide the batter into small bowls, let it cool naturally until fully set.
7. Place 100g of brown sugar and 200ml of water in a small milk pan, cook over medium-low heat until the sugar is fully dissolved, the mixture boils with large bubbles, the liquid reduces visibly, and the syrup thickens. Remove from heat and let cool for later use.
8. After the cake is set, pour a little cold boiled water around the edge of the bowl, gently shake, then invert to release the cake. Place it in cold boiled water and refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
9. Remove the chilled rice cake, plate it, drizzle with the cooled brown sugar syrup, and sprinkle with white sesame seeds.

## Health Advice
Rice Cold Cake is an excellent summer refreshment. Rice provides carbohydrates, making it suitable for replenishing energy in hot weather. However, it contains alkali and sugar, so diabetic patients should control their intake. It is recommended to pair it with a small amount of nuts or fruit to increase dietary fiber and vitamins.
