# Durian Pizza
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 Cake flour 500g  
🌾 Bread flour 500g  
💧 Water about 500g  
🍥 Yeast 3g (activated with warm water)  
🧂 Salt a pinch  
🫒 Olive oil 20g (for kneading dough)  
🍈 Fully ripe durian flesh 80g (pitted and mashed into paste)  
🧀 Shredded mozzarella cheese about 80g  

## Utensils & Equipment  
🥣 Mixing bowl  
🍽️ Work surface  
🔪 Dough scraper  
📦 Plastic wrap  
🥘 Pizza pan  
🥄 Dough docker  
♨️ Oven  

## Steps
1. Place the yeast in warm water and let it sit to activate. Mix cake flour and bread flour together, add a pinch of salt, gradually add about 500g of water and stir into a flaky texture. Pour in the yeast water and knead into a dough until it starts to develop gluten.
   **Key**: Mixing cake flour and bread flour makes the crust “tender yet chewy.”
2. Add 20g of olive oil in batches, continue kneading thoroughly until the dough is smooth.
3. Brush a little oil on the surface of the dough, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for about 10 minutes to ferment.
4. Take out the dough, divide into small portions with a dough scraper. For each piece, use the “gather inward” technique with your palm to knead until the surface is smooth, then gently press into a round disc to expel excess air.
   **Key**: Pressing out the air prevents large bubbles from forming on the crust during baking.
5. Place the pressed dough pieces in an oiled pan, brush another layer of oil on top, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for about 4 hours to ferment.
6. Take 80g of fully ripe durian flesh, remove the pits, and mash into a paste. Set aside.
7. Take one 80g piece of proofed dough, evenly coat it with cake flour to prevent sticking. On the work surface, flatten and spread it into a round crust, leaving a slightly thicker edge.
8. Brush a thin layer of oil inside the pizza pan, lay the crust in, and use a docker to prick holes evenly.
9. Evenly spread the durian paste over the crust, then sprinkle about 80g of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
10. Preheat the oven to 180°C top heat / 220°C bottom heat. Place the pizza on the middle rack and bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until the cheese melts and the surface turns golden brown. Remove and slice.

## Health Advice  
Durian pizza is not low in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance the meal. Durian is rich in dietary fiber and potassium, making it suitable for moderate consumption after exercise. Be mindful of the amount of cheese to avoid excess fat intake.
