# Stir-Fried Pork Belly
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Recommended Season: All-year-round  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Pork belly 500g  
🌶️ Bird’s eye chili 4 pieces  
🌶️ Red chili pepper 4 pieces  
🌿 Scallion 0.5-1 stalk  
🧄 Garlic 2 cloves  
🧂 Black fermented beans 10g (adjust ±5g according to personal taste)  
🍯 Bean paste 10g (adjust ±5g according to personal taste)  
🧂 Dark soy sauce 10ml  
🌾 Cornstarch 10g  
🧂 Salt 1-2g  
🫒 Cooking oil 15ml  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥣 Bowl (for marinating)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Slice the pork belly into thin pieces.  
2. Place the pork slices in a bowl, add cornstarch, dark soy sauce, and salt, mix well, and marinate for 30 minutes.  
3. Cut the scallion into sections and set aside.  
4. Slice the red chili pepper and bird’s eye chili diagonally into sections and set aside.  
5. Heat the wok over high heat, then pour in the cooking oil.  
6. Once the oil is hot, add the pork belly and stir-fry until it changes color, then remove and set aside.  
7. In the same wok, leave a little oil, add the garlic cloves and stir-fry until fragrant, then add the black fermented beans and stir-fry evenly.  
8. Add the bean paste and stir-fry evenly.  
9. Return the pork belly to the wok and quickly stir-fry until well combined.  
10. Add the red chili pepper, bird’s eye chili, and scallion sections, and stir-fry for about 40 seconds until just cooked.  
11. Turn off the heat, remove from the wok, and serve on a plate.  

## Health Advice  
Stir-fried pork belly is rich in protein and fat, making it suitable for people with high physical energy expenditure. However, its high oil and sodium content means it should be eaten in moderation by those managing weight or blood pressure. Pairing it with a light vegetable salad or brown rice is recommended to balance nutrition.
