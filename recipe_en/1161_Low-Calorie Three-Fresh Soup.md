# Low-Calorie Three-Fresh Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐖 Lard 1 tablespoon
🥚 Eggs 4
🥒 White radish About 1 (shredded into thin strips)
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms 5-6 (sliced)
🥩 Lean meat Appropriate amount (minced into paste)
🥬 Chinese cabbage Appropriate amount (use the leaf parts)
🫘 Tofu Appropriate amount (cut into small pieces)
🧅 Scallions A pinch (chopped into green onion pieces)
🧂 Salt To taste

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Iron wok
🥄 Soup ladle
🔪 Cutting board
🥒 Shredder
🍽️ Non-stick pan plate

## Preparation Steps
1. Shred the white radish into thin strips, slice the shiitake mushrooms, mince the lean meat into paste, cut the tofu into small pieces, wash the Chinese cabbage, and chop the scallions into green onion pieces.
2. Prepare the meat paste: Add seasonings to the minced meat, stir until firm, and set aside in a zip-top bag.
3. Heat the wok over medium heat, add lard, and fry the eggs until both sides are golden and set.
4. Pour enough boiling water into the wok, add the shredded radish, and bring to a boil over high heat.
   **Note**: Boiling water must be added; this is key to achieving a milky white soup.
5. Break the fried eggs into pieces with a spatula, and cook over medium heat for a moment to release the umami flavor into the soup.
6. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms, snip open the zip-top bag and squeeze in the meat paste, then add the Chinese cabbage leaves. Keep over medium heat with a gentle boil.
   **Doneness Check**: The meat paste has changed color and the cabbage leaves have softened.
7. Taste the soup and adjust seasoning with salt as needed.
8. Add the tofu pieces, and cook over low heat for about 1 minute until the tofu is heated through.
   **Note**: Add the tofu last to avoid it breaking apart.
9. Sprinkle with chopped scallions for fragrance and garnish, then serve.

## Health Suggestions
This low-calorie three-fresh soup uses lard-fried eggs infused with boiling water to create a savory broth, but the overall calorie count is manageable, making it suitable for consumption during weight loss. The combination of white radish and tofu aids digestion and provides calcium; just be mindful of controlling the salt amount.
