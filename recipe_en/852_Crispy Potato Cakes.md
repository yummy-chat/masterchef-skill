# Crispy Potato Cakes
Cuisine Type: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes (peeled, as needed)
🧀 Cheese (cut into cubes, for filling, as needed)
🧄 Garlic (added when boiling potatoes, as needed)
🌶️ Chili peppers (dried chili peppers, for flavoring when boiling potatoes, as needed)
🌟 Star anise (for boiling potatoes, as needed)
🌸 Sichuan peppercorns (for boiling potatoes, as needed)
🍃 Bay leaves (for boiling potatoes, as needed)
🧂 Salt (for seasoning the mashed potatoes, as needed)
🧄 Garlic powder (to enhance garlic flavor, as needed)
🌿 Dried parsley (use generously, add in batches, for aroma and color)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍲 Stockpot
🥣 Wooden bowl
🍴 Potato masher or fork
🪓 Wooden cutting board
🍳 Deep fryer or pot for oil

## Steps
1. Peel the potatoes, ensuring no skin remains on the surface.
   **Note**: Must be peeled thoroughly.
2. Place the peeled potatoes in a stockpot along with garlic, dried chili peppers, star anise, Sichuan peppercorns, and bay leaves. Add water and cook over low heat for 12 minutes.
3. Remove the cooked potatoes. While still hot, mash them with a potato masher or fork into a completely smooth, lump-free mashed potato.
4. Add salt, garlic powder, and dried parsley to the mashed potatoes. Mix thoroughly until well combined.
5. Divide the seasoned mashed potatoes into equal portions. Roll each portion into a ball, then flatten it with your palm into a thick disc.
6. Place a piece of cheese in the center of each disc. Wrap the potato around the cheese to form a sealed ball, then gently press it into a patty shape. Use a fork to press a grid pattern onto the surface.
   **Key**: Apply gentle pressure to avoid the cheese leaking out.
7. Pour enough cooking oil into a pot and heat to about 70% hot (approximately 350°F/175°C). Gently slide the potato cake bases into the oil along the side of the pot. Fry over medium-low heat until the surface is golden brown and crispy, and the cakes float to the top. Ensure the cheese inside is fully melted. Remove and drain the oil.

## Health Advice
Mashed potatoes wrapped around cheese and deep-fried are rich in carbohydrates and calcium, but are high in calories. Be mindful of consumption. Suitable for occasional indulgence, and it is recommended to eat no more than three at a time.
