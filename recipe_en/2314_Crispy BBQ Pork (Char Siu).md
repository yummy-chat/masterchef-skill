# Crispy BBQ Pork (Char Siu)
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Skinless pork belly strips (with bones) – appropriate amount  
🧄 Minced garlic – appropriate amount (for first marinade)  
🧅 Chopped scallions – appropriate amount (for first marinade)  
🍶 Cooking wine – appropriate amount (for first marinade)  
🫘 Fermented red bean curd (nam yu) – appropriate amount (for first marinade)  
🧂 Salt – appropriate amount (for second seasoning)  
🍬 Granulated sugar – appropriate amount (for second seasoning)  
🍚 Crispy coating powder (mixture of sugar and flour) – appropriate amount (for coating)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 Square deep bowl (for mixing marinade)  
📥 Stainless steel tray (for coating)  
♨️ Commercial oven (with adjustable temperature and timer)  
🪝 S-shaped roasting hook (for hanging meat strips)  
🧻 Kitchen paper (for wiping meat strips)  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board (for portioning)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare the first marinade**: Place minced garlic, chopped scallions, cooking wine, and fermented red bean curd in a bowl. Mix well by hand until the aroma of the cooking wine is released.
2. **First marination**: Place the skinless pork belly strips into the marinade. Rub thoroughly to ensure each piece is evenly coated. Set aside to marinate.
3. **Wipe off marinade solids**: Remove the marinated meat strips. Carefully wipe off any remaining chopped scallions, minced garlic, and fermented red bean curd fragments from the surface.
   - Must be wiped completely clean, otherwise the solids will easily burn and turn black during roasting.
4. **Second seasoning**: Evenly rub salt and granulated sugar onto the cleaned meat strips. Gently massage to allow the seasoning to penetrate. Set aside to marinate for about 1 hour.
5. **Dry again**: After the second marination is complete, use kitchen paper to gently wipe off or absorb any excess sugar and salt from the surface of the meat strips.
6. **Apply crispy coating powder**: Place the prepared meat strips into a tray containing the crispy coating powder. Gently roll and press to ensure the surface is evenly coated with a thin layer of powder.
   - Do not press too hard; just roll gently for even coverage.
7. **Roasting**: Preheat the oven to 190°C. Use an S-shaped roasting hook to hang the meat strips. Suspend them inside the oven and roast for 60 minutes, until the skin is golden red and produces a crisp "clang" sound when tapped.
8. **Rest and slice**: After removing from the oven, let the meat rest for a short while. Once slightly cooled, slice into bite-sized pieces with a knife and serve.
   - The freshly roasted pork is very hot; be careful to avoid burns during handling.

## Health Advice
The key to this crispy BBQ pork's crispy exterior and tender interior lies in the double marination and the crispy coating powder method, but the sugar content is not low. It is recommended to pair it with plenty of vegetables or brown rice to help balance blood sugar levels. Those with high blood lipids should consume in moderation, as pork belly is high in fat.
