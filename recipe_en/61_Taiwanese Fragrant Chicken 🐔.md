# Taiwanese Fragrant Chicken 🐔
Cuisine: Taiwanese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 500g chicken breast (cut into pieces)
🧄 Garlic, to taste (mashed into garlic puree)
🥚 1 egg (use the egg white)
🌾 Cake flour, as needed (for coating)
🌾 Tapioca starch, as needed (for both marinating and coating)

🧂 Five-spice powder, to taste
🧂 Chives powder, to taste
🧂 1 tsp white pepper powder
🧂 1 tsp baking soda (key for tenderizing meat)
🍶 Cooking wine, to taste
🍶 Light soy sauce, to taste
🍶 Dark soy sauce, to taste (for color)
🧂 2g baking powder (key for crispiness)
🍬 Plum powder, to taste (sprinkle after cooking)
🌶️ Chili powder, to taste (sprinkle after cooking)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for spraying)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
⚖️ Electronic scale
🥣 Glass bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
♨️ Air fryer
💨 Oil spray bottle

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the chicken breast into evenly sized pieces.
2. In a bowl, combine garlic puree, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, cooking wine, egg white, baking soda, white pepper powder, five-spice powder, and chives powder. Mix well.
3. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, toss thoroughly to coat, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate overnight.
**Key point**: Baking soda is essential; a long marination ensures the meat is fully flavored and stays tender.
4. In another bowl, mix cake flour, tapioca starch, and baking powder evenly.
**Key point**: Baking powder is crucial for achieving a crispy outer coating.
5. Place the marinated chicken pieces into the coating mixture and coat evenly with dry powder.
6. Arrange the coated chicken pieces in a single layer in the air fryer basket, and spray a layer of cooking oil evenly over the surface using an oil spray bottle.
**Key point**: Spraying oil is necessary for forming a crispy crust.
7. Set the air fryer to 200°C and bake for 20 minutes.
**Note**: After 10 minutes, remove and flip the chicken pieces, then continue baking until the remaining time is up, until the surface is golden and crispy.
8. Remove from the air fryer, sprinkle with plum powder and chili powder while hot, and serve.

## Health Advice
This Taiwanese Fragrant Chicken is made with chicken breast and an air fryer, reducing oil intake while being high in protein, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts to supplement nutrition. Baking soda and the extended marination ensure the meat is tender; be mindful of seasoning amounts to reduce sodium intake. Although lower in calories than deep-frying, plum powder contains sugar, so sprinkle sparingly.
