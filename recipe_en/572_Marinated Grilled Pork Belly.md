# Marinated Grilled Pork Belly
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork belly, appropriate amount (sliced)
🧅 Onion, appropriate amount (finely chopped, in a 1:1 ratio with garlic)
🧄 Garlic, appropriate amount (minced, in a 1:1 ratio with chopped onion)
🌶️ Coarse chili flakes, 2 parts
🌶️ Fine chili powder, 1 part (mainly for coloring)
🌿 Cumin seeds, appropriate amount (toasted in advance)
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon
🧂 Light soy sauce, 1 tablespoon
🫘 White sesame seeds, appropriate amount
🥤 Sprite, appropriate amount (key ingredient)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Spoon
🧤 Disposable gloves
🥢 Tongs
🍳 Frying pan
🔥 Stove

## Preparation Steps
1. Make the marinade: In a glass bowl, combine the chopped onion and minced garlic in a 1:1 ratio. Add the coarse chili flakes and fine chili powder, then add the cumin seeds that have been toasted in a frying pan over low heat. Add 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce, and an appropriate amount of white sesame seeds. Stir well, then gradually add Sprite to form a flowing sauce.
   **Key points**: Fine chili powder is mainly for coloring; cumin seeds must be toasted to release flavor; Sprite helps tenderize the meat.
2. Marinate the pork belly: Place the sliced pork belly into the sauce. Wear disposable gloves and thoroughly mix and massage the meat, ensuring each slice is evenly coated with the sauce.
3. Grill the pork belly: Heat a frying pan over high heat and add a little oil to coat the pan. Lay the marinated meat slices flat in the pan one by one. Cook each side for about 10 seconds, flipping twice. Cook until both sides are slightly charred and the meat slices naturally release oil.
   **Note**: Strictly control the cooking time to avoid the meat becoming tough.
4. Serve: It is recommended to wrap the meat in lettuce leaves, and you can add soybean paste for extra flavor.

## Health Advice
This pork belly dish is rich in protein and fat, suitable for those who expend a lot of energy, but be mindful of its high calorie content and control your intake. It is recommended to eat it with lettuce to increase dietary fiber, aiding digestion.
