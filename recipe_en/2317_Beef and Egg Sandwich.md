# Beef and Egg Sandwich
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef, approximately 100g (cut into strips)
🥚 Egg, 1 piece
🍞 Whole wheat bread slices, 2 pieces
🥬 Lettuce, as needed
🟡 Yellow mustard, as needed
🧈 Butter, a small piece (for spreading on bread)
🧂 Ground seasonings, as needed (such as black pepper, etc.)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for greasing the pan)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🥢 Tongs
🧂 Grinding bottle
✂️ Scissors
🍽️ Plate

## Preparation Steps
1. Heat the frying pan over medium heat, brush with a thin layer of oil, add the beef strips, and stir-fry until they change color and are cooked through.
   **Note**: The beef can be lightly marinated with a little salt in advance for better flavor.
2. Crack the egg into the other side of the pan and fry until the egg white is set. The yolk can be kept runny or fully cooked according to personal preference.
   **Key Point**: The beef and egg can be fried at the same time in the same pan to save time.
3. Place the bread slices in the pan and gently heat using the residual heat or low heat until the surface is slightly crispy.
4. Remove the bread, cut it in half with scissors, and evenly spread butter on the inner sides of both slices.
5. Layer on the washed and dried lettuce, fried beef strips, and fried egg in order.
6. Squeeze an appropriate amount of yellow mustard onto the beef and sprinkle with ground seasonings.
7. Close the bread, gently press down, and cut into pieces to serve.

## Health Advice
Beef and eggs provide high-quality protein, while whole wheat bread adds dietary fiber, making this suitable for fitness or a quick breakfast. Note that yellow mustard is high in sodium; hypertensive patients should use it in moderation. If making a runny yolk, it is recommended to use pasteurized eggs.
