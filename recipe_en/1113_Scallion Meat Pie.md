# Scallion Meat Pie
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 500g all-purpose flour
💧 320g warm water (about 30℃)
🫒 20g cooked soybean oil (for dough) + appropriate amount for frying
🥩 Pork filling, as needed
🧅 Scallions, as needed
🍬 3g sugar
🫙 5g active dry yeast
🧂 Meat filling seasoning, as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 Glass large bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🖌️ Silicone brush
🛡️ Plastic wrap
🪵 Wooden cutting board (pre-oiled)
🍳 Non-stick flat pan or electric griddle
🔪 Spatula
夾 Tongs

## Preparation Steps
1. Add the yeast and sugar to the warm water, stir well, and let it sit to activate.
2. Place the all-purpose flour in a large bowl, create a well in the center, pour in the yeast water and cooked soybean oil, then mix with your hands into a soft, moist dough.
3. Repeatedly press and fold the dough with your fist until it is initially combined, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rest at room temperature for 10 minutes.
4. Knead the dough again until it is smooth and elastic, shape it into a ball, cover with plastic wrap, and let it ferment in an environment of about 30℃ until it has noticeably increased in volume.
   **Note**: The plastic wrap must be tightly sealed to maintain humidity; 30℃ is the optimal temperature for yeast activity.
5. Oil the cutting board, take out the fermented dough, roll it into a long strip, and divide it into pieces of about 100g each.
6. Flatten a dough piece, spread the pork filling, add a generous amount of scallions, then seal it by pinching the edges together like a baozi, and pinch off any excess dough.
7. Place the sealed side down and gently press the piece into a round flatbread.
8. Pour enough oil into the flat pan and heat it up. Place the flatbread in the pan and fry over medium-low heat until the bottom is golden brown, then flip.
   **Key**: Use enough oil; a method of half-frying, half-deep-frying will make the texture crispier.
9. When both sides are golden brown, push the flatbread to the edge of the pan and let it bake with the residual heat for a moment to ensure the inside is fully cooked and the edges are crispy. Remove and drain excess oil.

## Health Suggestions
This scallion meat pie is relatively high in fat, so it is recommended to serve with a light vegetable soup or salad. Diabetic patients should control the seasonings used in the dough and meat filling. As a high-calorie staple, it can quickly replenish energy when consumed in moderation after exercise.
