# Zhengxin Big Chicken Chop
Cuisine: Taiwanese
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: All Year Round

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 Chicken breast about 4 pieces (need to be tenderized in advance)
🌿 Small spring onions 1-2 stalks
🧅 Onion half (sliced)
🫚 Ginger 3-4 slices
🧄 Garlic 5-6 cloves (crushed)
🧂 Salt 2 tablespoons
🍶 Huadiao wine (or cooking wine) appropriate amount
🌫️ White pepper powder appropriate amount
🍄 Matsutake seasoning appropriate amount
🌾 Corn starch appropriate amount
🧄 Garlic powder appropriate amount
🍗 Orleans marinade powder appropriate amount
💧 Water appropriate amount
🍠 Sweet potato starch appropriate amount (for coating)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Large bowl
🥄 Whisk
🔪 Cutting board
🔗 Tongs
🍳 Deep pot (for frying)
🕸️ Wire rack/oil draining rack
🫗 Oil jug

## Steps
1. Gently tenderize the chicken breast with the back of a knife or a meat mallet to loosen the fibers, making it easier to absorb flavors.
2. In a large bowl, combine sliced onion, small spring onions, ginger slices, crushed garlic, salt, Huadiao wine, white pepper powder, matsutake seasoning, corn starch, garlic powder, Orleans marinade powder, and an appropriate amount of water. Stir to form a marinade.
**Key Point**: Orleans marinade powder is the core flavor and cannot be omitted.
3. Submerge the chicken breast in the marinade, mix thoroughly by hand, and let it marinate for sufficient time to absorb the flavors.
4. Remove the marinated chicken breast and place it into sweet potato starch. Press with your hands to evenly coat each side with dry powder.
**Key Point**: Sweet potato starch creates a golden, crispy crust that does not easily soften.
5. Pour oil into the pot and heat to about 70% hot. Carefully place the coated chicken chop into the oil.
**Note**: Gently lower it into the oil to prevent splashing; the oil temperature should not be too low, or the crust will absorb oil and become soft.
6. Fry for about 60 seconds until the surface is golden and crispy and the chop floats to the top. Remove it.
7. Place the fried chicken chop on a wire rack or oil draining rack to drain excess oil.
8. Sprinkle with chili powder, cumin powder, or other seasonings according to personal preference, and enjoy.

## Health Advice
Chicken breast is a low-fat, high-protein ingredient, but frying significantly increases the calorie content. It is recommended to serve with a vegetable salad or reduce the amount of oil used. Orleans marinade powder is high in sodium, so individuals with high blood pressure should control their intake.
