# Tomato and Egg Drop Soup
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍅 Tomato 1
🥚 Egg 1-2
🧅 Scallion appropriate amount (minced)
🧄 Ginger appropriate amount (minced)
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (minced)
🧂 Salt 15 grams
🧂 Sesame oil 2 drops
🧂 MSG 5 grams (optional)
🫒 Cooking oil 15 milliliters

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Whisk

## Cooking Steps
1. Wash the tomato and cut into pieces. Mince the scallion, ginger, and garlic respectively. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat evenly with chopsticks or a whisk.
2. Place the wok over heat, pour in the cooking oil and heat until it begins to lightly smoke. Add the minced scallion, ginger, and garlic, and stir-fry until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
3. Add the tomato pieces and stir-fry for 1 minute until the juice releases.
4. Pour in water, with a volume approximately 1.2 times the height of the ingredients in the wok. Add salt and bring to a boil over high heat.
5. Once the soup is boiling, slowly pour the beaten egg mixture along the edge of the wok, and quickly stir with chopsticks to form egg flowers.
6. Add MSG and sesame oil, cook slightly for 30 seconds, then turn off the heat and remove from the wok.

## Health Tips
Tomatoes are rich in lycopene and vitamin C, eggs provide high-quality protein, making this dish suitable for the whole family in all seasons. It is recommended to use less oil and salt, especially halving the amount of salt, to avoid excessive sodium intake.
