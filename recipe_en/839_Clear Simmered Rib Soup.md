# Clear Simmered Rib Soup
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Pork ribs (about 500g)  
🌽 Sweet corn (sliced into sections)  
🥒 Winter melon (peeled and sliced)  
🥕 Carrot (cut into strips)  
🧅 Scallion (chopped)  
🧈 Cooking oil (a small amount, for greasing the pan)  
🧂 Salt (about 1 teaspoon, for seasoning before serving)  
🧅 Scallion and ginger (a small amount each)  
🌿 Star anise, cinnamon stick, bay leaf, Sichuan peppercorn (very small amounts each, for stir-frying to remove gaminess, not added during simmering)  
💧 Boiling water (enough)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🍲 Soup pot or clay pot  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🍵 Bowl  

## Steps  
1. Soak the pork ribs in clean water for 30 minutes, changing the water 2-3 times during the process to fully remove the blood. Remove and drain, then trim off any excess surface fat.  
2. Heat a wok, add a thin layer of cooking oil to grease the pan, and stir-fry the ribs over medium heat until the surface shrinks and turns slightly golden. Add scallion, ginger, star anise, cinnamon stick, bay leaf, and Sichuan peppercorn, and continue to stir-fry until fragrant.  
   **Note**: Use only a small amount of spices to remove gaminess and enhance aroma without overpowering the natural flavor of the ingredients; do not add the spices to the soup during simmering.  
3. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add the stir-fried ribs to the boiling water, bring to a boil over high heat, and use a slotted spoon to skim off any foam and oil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 1 hour.  
   **Key**: Keep the pot uncovered throughout to maintain a clear, non-greasy broth.  
4. Once the ribs are tender and cooked, add the corn sections, winter melon slices, and carrot strips. Continue simmering over low heat until the vegetables are soft and cooked through.  
5. Season only with salt before serving, stir gently to combine, then turn off the heat.  
   **Note**: Add only salt to preserve the natural sweetness of the ribs and vegetables.  
6. Place chopped scallions at the bottom of a bowl, scoop in the ribs and vegetables, then pour in the boiling hot broth to release the scallion's aroma before enjoying.  

## Health Advice  
Clear simmered rib soup is rich in high-quality protein and minerals, suitable for those needing nutritional supplementation or recovery. Corn and winter melon are high in dietary fiber, which aids digestion and promotes diuresis. However, be sure to skim off floating oil to manage calorie intake.
