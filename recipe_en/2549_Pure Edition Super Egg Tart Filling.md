# Pure Edition Super Egg Tart Filling  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥚 2 free-range eggs  
🥛 160g whole milk  
🍇 Raisins (adjust to taste, can substitute with dried blueberries or dried cranberries)  
🍬 20g white sugar  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Bowl (for mixing and holding)  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🧺 Strainer  
♨️ Air fryer  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Take a bowl, crack in two free-range eggs, add white sugar, and roughly stir with chopsticks.  
   - Sweetness can be adjusted according to personal preference.  
2. Pour in the whole milk and stir thoroughly until the egg mixture is a uniform pale yellow and the sugar is completely dissolved.  
3. Strain the egg-milk mixture through a filter into another container to remove egg filaments and foam; this step is crucial for a smooth texture.  
4. Place the bowl with the strained egg mixture into the air fryer, set to 160°C, and bake for 20 minutes.  
5. After about 15–18 minutes of baking, when the top layer of the egg mixture has initially set and can support toppings, pull out the fryer basket.  
   - Wait until the top layer is set before adding toppings; otherwise, the dried fruit will sink to the bottom.  
6. Evenly sprinkle raisins on top, push the basket back into the air fryer, and continue baking for the remaining time.  
7. Once done, remove from the fryer. The surface should be golden yellow, and the texture tender and smooth.  

## Health Tips  
This egg tart filling is rich in protein and calcium, and low in fat, making it suitable as a dessert during weight loss or for those with chewing difficulties. It is recommended to reduce the sugar amount and rely on the natural sweetness of the dried fruit for a healthier option.
