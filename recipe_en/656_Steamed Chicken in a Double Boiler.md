# Steamed Chicken in a Double Boiler
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🐔 1 whole free-range chicken (about 3 lbs / 1.5 kg, must be fresh)  
🧂 Salt, about 5g (used in two portions: for marinating + for rubbing on the skin)  
🍶 Soy sauce, about 10g  
🥜 Peanut oil, about 10g  

## Utensils & Equipment  
🪣 Large basin  
🔪 Cutting board & chef's knife  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🍲 Ceramic stewing pot with lid  
🫙 Non-toxic plastic wrap  
♨️ Large stainless steel steamer & steaming rack  
🧻 Kitchen towel  

## Steps  
1. **Wash the chicken**: Rinse thoroughly under clean water, remove the yellow membrane from the skin, pull off the lymph nodes and excess fat from the neck, and remove any remaining lungs, kidneys, and other organs from the cavity. Rinse until the water runs clear.  
2. **Prepare the chicken**: Chop off the chicken feet. Make one cut down the center of the chicken breast, and one cut on each side.  
3. **Marinate**: Mix the salt, soy sauce, and peanut oil together. Pour the mixture over the cuts and rub evenly all over the inside and outside of the chicken. Tuck the chicken feet into the cavity, then rub a thin layer of salt on the surface of the skin. Place the chicken into the stewing pot and pour in any remaining marinade from the basin.  
4. **Seal**: Tightly cover the mouth of the stewing pot with plastic wrap, wrapping it around several times to ensure a complete seal. Press the wrap firmly against the pot.  
**Key**: Sealing is essential for locking in the juices and flavor—must be tight and secure.  

5. **Steam**: Fill the steamer with water to about one-third full. Place the steaming rack inside. Bring the water to a full boil over high heat. Once steam rises, place the sealed stewing pot into the steamer. Keep the heat on high and steam for about 50 minutes.  
6. **Remove**: Use a towel to lift the stewing pot out of the steamer. Tear off the plastic wrap and serve.  

## Health Tips  
This double-boiled steamed chicken is low in fat and light, preserving the original flavor. It is suitable for fitness enthusiasts and those with sensitive stomachs, but be mindful of salt intake to avoid excessive sodium. It is recommended to serve with a side of mixed vegetable salad for balanced nutrition.
