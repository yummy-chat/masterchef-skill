# Chinese Flower Pastry
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 235g all-purpose flour
🐷 80g lard (for water-oil dough)
💧 95g water
🍬 35g granulated sugar
🌾 180g low-gluten flour (for oil dough)
🐷 90g lard (for oil dough)
🍑 25g filling per piece (peach oolong, green plum, red bean paste, etc.)
🥚 Egg yolk wash, as needed
🌰 Black or white sesame seeds, as needed
🎨 Food coloring or fruit/vegetable powder, as needed (for coloring)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Large bowl or basin
🌯 Plastic wrap
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🔪 Bench scraper
🥖 Rolling pin
🍳 Baking sheet
🔥 Oven or air fryer
🖌️ Brush
✂️ Small knife or blade

## Preparation Steps
**Preparation Stage**
1. Make the water-oil dough: Combine all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, lard, and water. Knead until the dough is smooth and elastic. Shape into a ball, seal, and refrigerate for 1 hour to rest.
2. Make the oil dough: Thoroughly mix low-gluten flour with lard until no dry flour remains. Seal and refrigerate for 20 minutes to rest.
3. Color and portion the oil dough: Remove the oil dough and divide it into several portions. Add food coloring or fruit/vegetable powder to each portion and mix well. Roll the water-oil dough into a long strip and divide it into 20 small pieces. Also divide the oil dough into 20 small pieces. Immediately cover all pieces with plastic wrap to prevent drying.

**Wrapping and Rolling**
4. Wrap the oil dough: Take a piece of water-oil dough, flatten it with the smooth side facing out, and wrap in a piece of oil dough. Gently push upward with your thumb and forefinger to seal the opening, then roll it into a ball. Place seam side up. After wrapping all pieces, cover with plastic wrap and rest for 10 minutes.
5. First rolling: Take a wrapped dough piece, seam side up. Roll it from the center upward and downward into a tongue shape. Roll it up from top to bottom into a cylinder. Place seam side up, cover, and rest for 15 minutes.
6. Second rolling: Take a rested dough roll, seam side up. Roll it again from the center upward and downward into a longer, thinner shape. Roll it up tightly from top to bottom. Place seam side down and rest for 15 minutes.

**Filling and Shaping**
7. Wrap the filling: Take a pastry roll, press it flat from the center, fold the two ends toward the center, and flatten into a round disc. Flip it over, wrap in 25g of filling, use your thumb and forefinger to gather the edges and seal, then roll it into a ball.
8. Shaping:
   - Peach blossom pastry: Press the ball flat into a disc. Use a knife to cut 5 equal sections. Make a deep cut in the center of each petal, and two shallow cuts on each side. Pinch the edges of each petal to form pointed tips.
   - Lucky clover pastry: Press the ball flat. Cut into 8 equal sections. Pair two sections together, gently rotate inward and flip over to form a four-leaf clover shape.
   - Bow pastry: Press the ball flat. First, make a cut down the center. Then make one cut on each side symmetrically. Fold the top and bottom parts toward the center. Use a spare strip of dough to wrap around the center and pinch the bottom firmly to secure.
   - **Key point**: Handle all shaping gently to avoid damaging the layers.

**Baking**
9. Decoration: Brush the center of each shaped pastry with egg yolk wash and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
10. Baking:
    - Preheat the oven to 160°C. Place the baking sheet inside and bake for about 18 minutes; or use an air fryer at 160°C for about 12 minutes.
    - Bake until the pastries are fully puffed, the patterns are set, and the surface is dry with slight browning. Remove from the oven. If the color becomes too dark, cover with aluminum foil midway through.

## Health Advice
This Chinese flower pastry has distinct layers and contains a considerable amount of lard and sugar. It is best enjoyed occasionally as a festive dessert, paired with unsweetened tea to cut through the richness. It is high in calories and fat. Those with high blood pressure, high blood sugar, high cholesterol, or those trying to lose weight should only have a small taste.
