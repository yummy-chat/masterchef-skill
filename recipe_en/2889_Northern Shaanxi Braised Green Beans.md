# Northern Shaanxi Braised Green Beans
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍖 Green beans 600g  
🥔 Potatoes 2  
🍅 Tomatoes 2  
🌶️ Spiral chili peppers 4 (optional)  
🧂 Salt 12g  
🥄 Light soy sauce 12ml  
🌿 Five-spice powder 6g  
🫙 Oyster sauce 12ml  
🧅 Scallion 3 rings  
🧄 Ginger 2g  
🧄 Garlic 2 cloves  
🌿 Cilantro, chopped (optional)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Wok or deep pot  
🥄 Spatula  
🔪 Knife  
📋 Cutting board  
🥢 Chopsticks (to check potato tenderness)

## Cooking Steps
1. Slice the scallion into rings, mince the garlic, and julienne the ginger. Set aside.
2. Remove the strings from the green beans and cut into 2–10 cm long segments. Set aside.
3. Peel the potatoes and cut into 1 cm cubes. Set aside.
4. Blanch the tomatoes in boiling water, peel, and cut into 1 cm cubes. Set aside.
5. Remove the seeds from the spiral chili peppers and cut into 0.15 cm wide strips. Set aside.
6. Heat the wok, pour in 10–15 ml cooking oil. Once the oil begins to smoke, add the scallion, ginger, and garlic. Stir-fry until fragrant.
7. Add the green bean segments and stir-fry until their color changes from bright green to a deeper green.
8. Add the potato cubes and continue stir-frying for 30 seconds.
9. Pour in hot water until it just covers the ingredients. Cover with a lid and braise until the potatoes are tender and can be easily pierced with chopsticks.
10. Add the tomato cubes, salt, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, five-spice powder, and spiral chili pepper strips. Continue braising until the tomatoes have broken down and released their juice. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking to the bottom.
**Note**: Stir occasionally after adding the tomatoes to prevent scorching.

11. Sprinkle in the chopped cilantro, mix well, and remove from heat. Serve.

## Health Suggestions
This Northern Shaanxi braised green bean dish is rich in dietary fiber and vitamin C. The potatoes provide a feeling of fullness, making it suitable for those managing blood sugar or losing weight. However, be mindful of the starch content in potatoes. It is recommended to pair with a small amount of lean meat or tofu to enhance protein absorption.
