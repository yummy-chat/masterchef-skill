# Braised Chicken with Chestnuts
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🐔 Free-range chicken ½ (choose a slightly older chicken, cut into large pieces)  
🌰 Chestnuts 150g (net weight after shelling)  
🍄 Shiitake mushroom 1 (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic 2 cloves (peeled)  
🫚 Ginger a small piece (sliced)  
🧂 Salt to taste (for marinating the chicken)  
🍬 Sugar to taste (for marinating the chicken)  
🧴 Soy sauce a little (for marinating the chicken)  
🍶 Cooking wine a little (for marinating the chicken) + an appropriate amount (for deglazing during cooking)  
🌾 Cornstarch an appropriate amount (for marinating the chicken)  
🦪 Oyster sauce a little (added during braising)  
🧴 Dark soy sauce a little (for color during braising)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife  
🍳 Wok  
🥘 Clay pot  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Ladle or spatula  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Add salt, sugar, soy sauce, cooking wine, and cornstarch to the chicken pieces. Mix well by hand and marinate until flavored. The cornstarch helps coat the seasonings and lock in moisture.  
2. Heat oil in a wok, add the marinated chicken pieces, and pan-fry over medium heat until both sides are golden brown and slightly crispy. Remove or push the chicken to the side of the wok.  
   **Key point**: Pan-frying is essential for the rich flavor of braised dishes.  
3. Using the rendered chicken fat in the wok, sauté the ginger slices and garlic cloves until fragrant. Then add the shiitake mushroom slices and stir-fry.  
4. Drizzle cooking wine along the edge of the wok and stir-fry until aromatic.  
5. Add about three times the volume of water relative to the chicken. Add the chestnuts, then stir in oyster sauce for umami and dark soy sauce for color. Mix evenly. Cover with a lid and bring to a boil over high heat.  
6. After boiling, skim off any surface oil to keep the dish from being greasy.  
7. Reduce to medium-low heat and continue braising for about 10 minutes.  
8. Remove the lid. Gently press a chestnut with a spoon—if it breaks easily, it is about 80% cooked.  
9. Turn the heat to high and reduce the sauce until thickened and the chestnuts are fully cooked.  
10. Transfer the dish to a preheated clay pot to keep warm before serving.  

## Health Suggestions  
Braised chicken with chestnuts is a nourishing dish perfect for autumn. Chestnuts help tonify qi and strengthen the spleen, while chicken warms the middle and boosts energy, making it suitable for those with weak constitutions or sensitivity to cold. It is recommended to use less dark soy sauce to control sodium intake.
