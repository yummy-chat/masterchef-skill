# Shaanxi Hand-Pulled Noodles
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Flour 1000g  
💧 Room temperature water 450g  
🌽 Corn starch, as needed  
🧂 Salt 10g  
🧂 Edible alkali 2g  
🌶️ Chili powder, as needed  
🧄 Minced garlic, as needed  
🧅 Chopped green onions, as needed  
🫒 Hot oil, as needed  
🥬 Greens, as needed  

## Cookware and Equipment
🪣 Stainless steel bowl  
🔪 Cutting board  
🪠 Rolling pin  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🍲 Cooking pot  
🥄 Strainer  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍜 Bowl  

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the flour in a bowl, add salt and edible alkali, and mix evenly. Gradually add room temperature water, stirring while adding to form flocculent pieces, until no dry flour remains at the bottom of the bowl. Knead vigorously into a dough, and continue kneading until the dough surface is smooth, the bowl is clean, and your hands are clean.
2. Shape the dough into a round ball and flatten it. Cover with the bowl, seal, and let it rest for 20 minutes to allow the gluten to fully relax.
3. Sprinkle a generous amount of corn starch on the cutting board and place the dough on it. Use a rolling pin to press and turn the dough, rolling it into a round sheet. Sprinkle starch on the dough sheet, roll it up with the rolling pin, and push and roll from the center toward both ends with both hands. Repeat the process of opening, sprinkling starch, changing direction, rolling up, and pushing and rolling, focusing on rolling the edges to the same thickness as the center, until you have a large, thin, and round dough sheet.
4. Evenly sprinkle corn starch over the dough sheet and spread it out. Use the rolling pin to help fold the dough sheet like a fan in a wave-like pattern, sprinkling enough starch between each layer to prevent sticking. Cut into evenly wide noodles with a knife.
5. Lift the middle of the noodles and gently shake them to remove excess starch. Be careful to handle gently to avoid breaking.
6. Fill a pot with enough water, bring it to a full boil over high heat. Add the hand-pulled noodles, gently stir the bottom with chopsticks to prevent sticking, and cook until the noodles are thoroughly done.
7. Remove the noodles into a bowl, top with blanched greens, and sprinkle with chopped green onions, minced garlic, and chili powder. Pour hot oil that has been heated until smoking over the seasonings to release the aroma, mix well, and serve.

## Health Advice
Hand-pulled noodles are primarily composed of carbohydrates, paired with vegetables and oil-splashed chili, making them suitable for replenishing energy for manual laborers or after exercise. It is recommended to control the amount of minced garlic and chili; those with sensitive stomachs should reduce the intake of hot oil.
