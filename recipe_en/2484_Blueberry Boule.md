# Blueberry Boule
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍞 1 boule dough

## Kitchenware & Equipment
♨️ Oven  
🍳 Dutch oven  
🔪 Dough scraper

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 260°C, and place the Dutch oven inside to fully preheat as well.  
   **Key Point**: The Dutch oven must be preheated together with the oven, using the sealed high-temperature steam inside to help the bread expand.

2. Dust the surface of the proofed dough generously with bread flour, and use a dough scraper to decisively score cuts of moderate depth.  
   **Note**: Score the dough with a clean, sharp motion, with cuts about 0.6–1 cm deep, avoiding any hesitation.

3. Wearing heat-resistant gloves, remove the scorching hot Dutch oven, carefully place the dough inside, immediately cover with the lid, and return to the oven to bake for 20 minutes.

4. After 20 minutes, remove the Dutch oven, take off the lid, and continue baking for about 20 more minutes until the crust is dark brown.  
   **Judgment Standard**: Tapping the bottom of the bread should produce a hollow, crisp sound.

5. Once baked, unmold the bread and let it cool completely on a wire rack before slicing to prevent the internal crumb from becoming soggy due to residual heat.

## Health Advice
Blueberry boule is rich in antioxidants from the blueberries, and lemon zest aids digestion, making it suitable as a healthy breakfast staple. However, whole blueberries may cause relatively rapid blood sugar fluctuations; it is recommended to pair with protein or healthy fats (such as nut butter) when consuming, and avoid eating it on an empty stomach.
