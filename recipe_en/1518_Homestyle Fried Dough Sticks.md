# Homestyle Fried Dough Sticks
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 All-purpose flour 350 g
🥛 Cold milk 250 g
🧪 Baking powder 10 g
🧂 Baking soda 3 g
🧂 Salt 6 g
🫒 Oil 12 g (for mixing dough)
🛢️ Frying oil as needed (for deep frying)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🧪 Measuring cup
🥣 Large bowl
🎞️ Plastic wrap
🪄 Rolling pin
🔪 Knife
🥢 Chopsticks
🥘 Large pot

## Instructions
1. Pour cold milk into a measuring cup, add baking powder and baking soda, and stir until fully dissolved.
   **Key**: Must use cold milk to prevent the leavening agents from activating prematurely.
2. In a large bowl, place the all-purpose flour and salt. Pour in the liquid mixture from step 1 and 12 g of oil. First, mix into a flaky texture with chopsticks, then knead by hand into a smooth, non-sticky dough.
3. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
4. Remove the dough, knead it into a long strip, evenly coat the surface with a layer of oil, wrap tightly with plastic wrap, and refrigerate overnight.
   **Key**: Chilling allows the dough to fully relax, enhancing flavor; if making on the same day, let it rest at room temperature for at least 2 hours.
5. The next day, take out the dough, gently shape it, and roll it into a rectangular sheet about 0.5 cm thick. Cut into strips about 2 cm wide.
6. Stack two strips together, press down firmly in the center with a chopstick to create a dent, then press once at each end.
   **Key**: Pressing the ends ensures even expansion and a nice shape for the fried dough sticks.
7. Pour enough frying oil into a large pot and heat to a high temperature: insert a dry chopstick; if dense small bubbles immediately rise around it, the oil is ready.
8. Slightly stretch each piece of dough, carefully place it into the oil, maintain medium-high heat, and keep turning continuously with chopsticks.
   **Key**: Continuous turning ensures even heating and prevents burning.
9. Fry until the entire piece turns an even golden brown or deep golden color. Remove and drain oil, placing on paper towels or a wire rack.

## Health Advice
Although fried dough sticks are crispy and delicious, high-temperature frying produces trans fatty acids. It is recommended to pair them with soy milk or oatmeal porridge for balanced nutrition. Refrigerating the dough overnight can reduce the amount of leavening agents needed. Those with sensitive stomachs should consume in moderation to avoid bloating.
