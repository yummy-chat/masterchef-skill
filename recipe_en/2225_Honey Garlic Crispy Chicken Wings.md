# Honey Garlic Crispy Chicken Wings  
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken mid-joint wings, about a dozen  
🧄 Garlic, a large amount (divided into two parts: some crushed for marinating, some finely minced for fried garlic)  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🧇 Fermented red bean curd, 1 piece  
🍶 Rice wine, to taste  
🌶️ Ground white pepper, to taste  
🍯 Honey, to taste  
🌾 Cornstarch, to taste (mixed with flour in a 1:1 ratio)  
🌾 Flour, to taste (mixed with cornstarch in a 1:1 ratio)  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for frying garlic and pan-frying chicken wings)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board  
🥣 Large glass bowl  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🍲 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon/strainer  
🥣 Strainer/sieve  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Wash the chicken wings and make a few cuts on the **fleshy side** to allow the flavors to penetrate.  
2. Add salt, crushed garlic, and fermented red bean curd, mix well, and marinate for half an hour.  
3. Just before cooking, add **rice wine** and **ground white pepper**, and mix evenly.  
**Key point**: The rice wine must be added last to preserve its deodorizing and flavor-enhancing effect.  
4. Mix cornstarch and flour in a 1:1 ratio to create the coating powder.  
5. Finely mince a large amount of garlic, rinse once with water to remove surface mucus, and drain thoroughly.  
6. Heat oil in a wok, **add the drained minced garlic to cold oil**, and fry over medium-low heat; once the garlic starts to turn yellow, immediately turn the heat to high, quickly remove and drain, letting residual heat finish cooking until golden and crispy.  
7. Add a small amount of oil to a non-stick frying pan, arrange the coated chicken wings in a single layer once the oil is hot, and fry over medium-low heat. After frying one side for about 1.5 minutes, flip and fry until both sides are golden brown.  
8. Once the chicken wings are fully cooked on both sides, increase the heat and quickly fry for about 30 seconds to make the skin crispier, then remove.  
9. Heat the honey and pour it over the fried chicken wings, quickly tossing to coat each wing evenly with the honey glaze.  
10. Plate and sprinkle with the fried golden garlic.  

## Health Tips  
Honey Garlic Crispy Chicken Wings are richly flavored with honey and fermented red bean curd, but they are relatively high in calories. It is recommended to serve with a vegetable salad or clear soup to balance the oil intake. When frying the garlic, control the heat carefully to avoid burning, which can produce harmful substances.
