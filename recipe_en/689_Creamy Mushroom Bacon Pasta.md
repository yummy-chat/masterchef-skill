# Creamy Mushroom Bacon Pasta
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍝 Pasta 1 pack (about 500g)
🧅 Onion 1/2 (diced)
🍄 Mushrooms 6-8 (sliced)
🥓 Bacon 4-5 slices (diced)
🧈 Butter about 15-20g (for sautéing)
🥛 Milk as needed
🥛 Heavy cream as needed
🧀 Cheese slices 1-2 pieces
🧂 Salt as needed (for pasta water and final seasoning)
🌿 Black pepper as needed
🌿 Parsley flakes a little (for garnish)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
♨️ Induction cooker
🍳 Frying pan (non-stick)
🍲 Soup pot
🥄 Strainer/colander
🔪 Cutting board, knife

## Preparation Steps
1. Bring water to a boil in a soup pot, add a little salt, then add the pasta and cook until al dente. Drain and set aside. Optionally toss with a little olive oil to prevent sticking.
2. Over low heat, melt butter in a frying pan. Add diced onion and sauté until translucent and fragrant. Add diced bacon and cook until the edges are slightly crispy and fat renders. Then add sliced mushrooms and sauté until softened.
3. Pour milk and heavy cream into the pan, bring to a gentle simmer. Add cheese slices and stir continuously until fully melted, forming a smooth sauce. Stir to prevent the bottom from burning.
4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce pan and toss quickly to coat each strand evenly. Season with salt and black pepper, then remove from heat.
5. Plate and garnish with parsley flakes before serving.

## Health Advice
This dish is rich in protein and calcium, but also high in fat and calories. It is recommended to pair it with a serving of lightly sautéed vegetables to balance nutrition, and it is suitable for those looking to build muscle. Those with sensitive stomachs should be mindful of the greasiness from bacon and cream; the amount of butter can be reduced.
