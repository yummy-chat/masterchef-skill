# Crispy Fried Pork
Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🍳 Frying pan (non-stick, also used as a deep fryer)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Make Sichuan peppercorn salt**: Heat a frying pan over low heat without oil. Dry-fry the Sichuan peppercorns until they crumble easily when pinched. Remove and grind them. Mix in 1 tablespoon of salt.
- **Note**: Keep the heat low to prevent burning; the spices need to be toasted until crisp to grind easily.
2. **Marinate the pork**: Place the pork strips in a bowl. Add half of the Sichuan peppercorn salt,适量 baijiu and cooking wine, 1 tablespoon of sugar, a little light soy sauce, and two shakes of ground white pepper. Mix well and marinate for 25 minutes.
- **Note**: The pork should have some fat and some lean meat; marinate for the full time for thorough flavor.
3. **Prepare the frying batter**: In another bowl, combine sweet potato starch and all-purpose flour in a 1:2 ratio. Add a pinch of baking powder and the remaining Sichuan peppercorn salt. Crack in the two eggs and mix first. Then, gradually add water a little at a time, mixing until it reaches a very thick yogurt-like consistency. Finally, drizzle in a little cooking oil and stir to combine.
- **Note**: You must use sweet potato starch; the batter should be thick enough; adding oil makes the crust crispier.
4. **Coat with batter**: Pour the batter over the marinated pork strips. Mix well until each strip is evenly coated.
5. **First deep-fry**: Add enough oil to the pan. Heat over high heat until small bubbles form around a pair of chopsticks. Add the pork strips one by one. Fry until golden brown and the crust is crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
- **Note**: Add the strips one at a time, spacing them out, to prevent sticking.
6. **Second fry for extra crispiness**: Continue heating the oil over high heat until it just starts to smoke. Add all the fried pork at once. Re-fry for about 30 seconds, until the color deepens slightly and the crust makes a rustling sound. Remove and drain.
- **Note**: The second fry is very short; watch carefully to prevent burning. Listen for the sound to judge crispiness.

## Health Tips
Crispy fried pork is delicious, but deep-frying is high in calories. It is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance the meal. It is suitable for an occasional treat. People with high blood pressure, high blood lipids, or high blood sugar should consume with caution. Be careful not to exceed 30 seconds for the second fry to avoid scorching.
