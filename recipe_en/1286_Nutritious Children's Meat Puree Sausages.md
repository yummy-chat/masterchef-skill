# Nutritious Children's Meat Puree Sausages
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Pork spare ribs, about 500g (2 ribs, cut into sections)
🌽 Corn, 1 ear (cut into sections)
🥕 Carrot, 1 (cut into chunks)
🥔 Chinese yam, 1 section (cut into sections)
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms, 2-3 pieces
🍯 Candied date, 1
🌾 Dried Chinese yam, appropriate amount
🫘 White lotus seeds, appropriate amount
🫚 Ginger, 1-2 slices
🍶 Cooking wine, appropriate amount (for blanching)
💧 Water, appropriate amount (for stewing)
🍲 Stewing broth, 1-2 tablespoons (for adjusting puree consistency)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Wok
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
♨️ Electric pressure cooker
🔄 Immersion blender
🧴 Blending cup
👝 Piping bag
🐻 Silicone molds (animal shapes)
❄️ Freezer compartment

## Steps
1. Place spare rib sections in a pot with cold water. Add ginger slices and cooking wine. Bring to a boil over high heat, skim off any foam, then remove and rinse clean.
   **Key Point**: Starting with cold water helps remove blood and fishy odors more thoroughly.
2. Cut corn into sections. Peel and cut carrot and Chinese yam into chunks. Clean shiitake mushrooms, candied date, dried Chinese yam, and white lotus seeds.
   **Key Point**: Wear gloves when peeling Chinese yam to prevent skin irritation.
3. Place the blanched spare ribs, corn, carrot, Chinese yam, shiitake mushrooms, candied date, dried Chinese yam, and white lotus seeds together into the inner pot of the electric pressure cooker.
   **Key Point**: Do not cut ingredients too small, making it easier to later pick out the rib meat and some vegetables for pureeing.
4. Add water, enough to mostly submerge the ingredients. Close the lid and select the "Soup" or "Stew" mode. Cook until the spare ribs are tender and fall apart.
5. Open the lid. Pick out the rib meat and some carrot chunks and Chinese yam chunks. Place them into a blending cup. Add 1-2 tablespoons of the broth from the pot.
   **Key Point**: Add just enough broth to allow the immersion blender to work smoothly, without being too dry or too thin; do not add too much.
6. Use an immersion blender to blend until smooth and silky, with a uniform texture and no visible chunks.
7. Transfer the meat puree into a piping bag. Snip a small opening at the tip. Evenly pipe the puree into clean, dry silicone molds. Fill completely, then gently tap the mold to release any air bubbles.
8. Place the mold flat in the freezer compartment. Freeze until completely solid and hard.
   **Key Point**: Freeze for a sufficient amount of time, or the sausages will not unmold cleanly.
9. Remove the meat puree sausages from the mold. They can be eaten directly or reheated before serving. For storage, place them in a sealed bag and continue to store in the freezer.

## Health Tips
These meat puree sausages are made by stewing spare ribs, corn, Chinese yam, and other ingredients, then pureeing them into a balanced, nutritious meal. They are especially suitable for picky eaters or babies during teething, helping them get vegetables and calcium. Remember to wear gloves when peeling Chinese yam, and freeze until completely solid for clean unmolding.
