# Steamed Flower Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍖 Ingredients  
- 5 frozen flower rolls  

🧂 Seasonings  
- None  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Kitchenware  
- Round plate  
- Steaming rack  
- Pot with lid  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Remove 5 frozen flower rolls from the packaging.  
2. Arrange the flower rolls flat on the plate, avoiding any overlap.  
3. Pour 400ml of water into the pot, place the steaming rack inside, set the plate with the flower rolls on the rack, and cover with the lid.  
4. Heat over high heat until the water comes to a boil.  
5. Reduce the heat to medium and steam for 15 minutes.  
6. Open the lid and feel the surface of the flower rolls with your hand to check if they are fully heated; if not, continue steaming with the lid on; if fully heated, turn off the heat and remove.  
7. Take out the plate, let it cool to about 50°C, and then serve.  

⚠️ Notes  
- During steaming, monitor the water level in the pot to ensure it remains at least 50ml to prevent dry burning.  

## Health Advice  
Steamed flower rolls are a simple source of carbohydrates, suitable for breakfast or a quick snack. Note that frozen food must be fully steamed and eaten while hot. Those with poor digestion should not overconsume. It is recommended to pair with protein foods for balanced nutrition.
