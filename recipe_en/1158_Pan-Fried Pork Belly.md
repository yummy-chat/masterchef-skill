# Pan-Fried Pork Belly
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🧄 Ingredients  
- 🥩 Pork belly strip (recommended dimensions: 20cm*6cm*5cm)  
- 🥬 Lettuce  
- 🧄 Ginger  
- 🧄 Garlic  
- 🫘 Broad bean paste  

🧂 Seasonings  
- 🥫 Soy sauce  
- 🧂 Salt  
- 🧂 MSG  
- 🍶 Cooking wine  
- 🛢️ Cooking oil  

🍳 Utensils  
- 🔪 Knife  
- 🥣 Bowl  
- 🍳 Frying pan  
- 🍽️ Plate  

📝 Steps  
1. Slice the pork belly strip thinly along the long side, each slice about 1 to 1.5 mm thick. Set aside.  
2. Place the sliced pork belly in a bowl, then add 8g soy sauce, 1g salt, 1g MSG, 10g cooking wine, two slices of ginger, and two cloves of garlic crushed flat. Marinate for 10 minutes.  
3. Tear lettuce leaves by hand, wash and drain. Set aside.  
4. Heat the frying pan, pour in 5ml cooking oil. When the oil begins to smoke slightly, add the pork belly slices. Be careful to avoid oil splatters and burns. Fry one side until golden brown, then flip and fry the other side to the same color.  
5. Once the pork belly is done, remove from the pan and plate.  
6. Spread broad bean paste on a lettuce leaf, roll it up with the pan-fried pork belly slices, and enjoy.  

## Health Advice
Although pork belly is delicious, it is recommended to eat it with lettuce to reduce greasiness and increase dietary fiber. For those with high blood lipids, reduce the amount of broad bean paste or substitute it with minced garlic.
