# Toast Mooncake
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍞 Toast bread, as needed  
🥛 Milk, as needed  
🥚 Salted egg yolks (or other fillings such as red bean paste, chocolate filling, or coconut filling), as needed  
🍬 Jelly or soft candy (for transparent-shaped mooncakes), as needed  
🍫 Chocolate sauce or dark batter (for coloring and drawing outlines), as needed  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Food processor  
🥮 Mooncake mold  
🔥 Oven  
🧻 Baking tray  
📄 Parchment paper  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Cut off the hard crust from the toast bread and tear it into small pieces.  
2. Place the toast pieces into a food processor, add an appropriate amount of milk, and blend until the crumbs can be formed into a ball with some plasticity.  
   - If the dough is too dry, add a small amount of milk; if too wet, add more toast crumbs.  
3. Optionally, wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for a short while to help it blend better and make subsequent handling easier.  
4. Take an appropriate amount of dough, flatten it, wrap in your preferred filling, seal the edges, and roll into a ball.  
5. Place it into the mooncake mold and press to form the pattern.  
   - To create a dark crust, mix chocolate sauce or cocoa powder into the dough for coloring.  
6. For transparent-shaped mooncakes, fill a piping bag with dark batter and pipe outlines of shapes like moons or hearts onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper.  
7. Fill the outlined shapes with chopped jelly or soft candy.  
8. Place the shaped mooncakes into a preheated oven and bake at 140°C for 10 minutes.  
9. Remove and let cool before serving.  

## Health Advice  
Toast mooncakes use toast as the main ingredient, making them lower in calories than traditional mooncakes and suitable for those pursuing a healthy diet. Be mindful of controlling the sweetness of the filling; using salted egg yolks or low-sugar jam is healthier. It is recommended to consume in moderation to avoid excessive carbohydrate intake.
