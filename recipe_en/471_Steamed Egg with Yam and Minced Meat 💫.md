# Steamed Egg with Yam and Minced Meat 💫
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌽 Corn kernels, as needed  
🥔 Yam, as needed (peeled, cut into pieces, and mashed)  
🐷 Minced pork, as needed (fat ratio no more than 20%)  
🐂 Minced beef, as needed (optional, to replace minced pork)  
🫚 Ginger, as needed (minced or shredded)  
🥚 Egg, 1  
🧅 Scallion, as needed (chopped)  

🦪 Oyster sauce, as needed  
🧂 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🫒 Sesame oil, a dash (optional)  
🍶 Cooking wine, a dash (optional)  
🌶️ Pepper, a dash (optional)  
🧂 Salt, as needed  

## Kitchen Utensils & Equipment  
🔪 Peeler, chef's knife, cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Mashing tool  
🍴 Fork  
🍲 Deep bowl (e.g., earthenware bowl)  
♨️ Steamer or steaming pot  

## Instructions  
1. Prepare the corn kernels. Peel and cut the yam into pieces, then mash them in a bowl. Mince the ginger. Chop the scallion.  
2. In a large bowl, combine the mashed yam, minced pork, minced ginger, and corn kernels. Add oyster sauce, light soy sauce, and, if desired, sesame oil, cooking wine, pepper, and salt.  
   **Key**: It is recommended to mix all ingredients and seasonings together by hand for better flavor absorption.  
3. Transfer the seasoned meat mixture into a deep bowl. Use a fork or spoon to press a hollow in the center, forming a "volcanic crater" shape.  
4. Crack a whole egg into the hollow.  
5. Steaming:  
   - **Steamer**: Set to 100°C (212°F) and steam for 15–20 minutes.  
   - **Steaming pot**: After the water comes to a boil and steam rises, steam over high heat for 10–15 minutes.  
   **Key**: Ensure the meat mixture is fully cooked and the egg white has set. Steam for at least 15 minutes to guarantee thorough cooking.  
6. Once done, drizzle a dash of sesame oil if desired, and garnish with chopped scallions.  

## Health Tips 🥗  
The combination of yam and minced meat adds dietary fiber while reducing oil intake, making it suitable for those aiming to control fat and build muscle. The steaming method preserves nutrients, and the dish is easy to digest for the elderly and children. Be mindful of the fat-to-lean ratio in the minced meat to avoid a dry texture.
