# Microwave Steamed Egg
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

🧺 **Ingredients**  
🥚 2 eggs  
💧 100-120ml warm water or broth

🧂 **Seasonings**  
🧂 1g salt  
🥄 2ml light soy sauce (optional)  
🫒 a few drops of sesame oil

🍽️ **Utensils**  
🥣 heat-resistant bowl  
🫙 plastic wrap or microwave-safe lid  
🪥 sieve (optional)  
🪥 toothpick

📝 **Steps**  
1. Beat the eggs until smooth, add warm water or broth, salt, and light soy sauce. Gently stir until well combined, minimizing bubbles as much as possible.  
2. Strain the egg mixture through a sieve into a heat-resistant bowl. If any bubbles remain on the surface, gently prick them with a toothpick.  
3. Cover with plastic wrap and poke 8–10 small holes in it, or use a microwave-safe lid (leaving a gap).  
4. Heat in the microwave:  
   - 700W: 1 minute 30 seconds → then add 20–30 seconds as needed until the surface is just set  
   - 600W: approximately 1 minute 40 seconds to 2 minutes 10 seconds  
   - 800W: approximately 1 minute 10 seconds to 1 minute 40 seconds  
5. After heating, remove and let rest for 1 minute to allow residual heat to fully cook the center.  
6. Drizzle with sesame oil and sprinkle with chopped green onions before serving.

## Health Advice
This microwave steamed egg is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for weight loss periods or for those with dental issues. Be careful to keep the heating time shorter rather than longer to avoid a honeycomb texture.
