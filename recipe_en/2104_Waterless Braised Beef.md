# Waterless Braised Beef
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Beef brisket or beef short ribs 1500g (at least 750g, must cover the bottom of the pot)
🥔 Potato 1 (cut into rolling chunks)
🥕 Carrot 1 (cut into rolling chunks)
🧅 Scallion to taste (chopped, for finishing)
🌿 Cilantro to taste (for finishing)
🧂 Light soy sauce 50g
🧂 Dark soy sauce 25g
🧂 Oyster sauce about 1 tablespoon
🧂 White pepper powder 2g
🧂 Sugar 3g
🧂 Star anise 2 pieces
🧂 Bay leaf 1 piece
🧂 Ginger 40g (sliced)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
♨️ Pressure cooker (electric or stovetop)
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥄 Spoon
🥢 Chopsticks
🍳 Wok (optional, for reducing the sauce)

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the beef into large chunks about three fingers wide. Soak in cold water for at least 2 hours, then drain.
   **Note**: Soaking removes blood, reduces odor, and allows the meat to absorb water. This is key to producing the broth in waterless braising.
2. Arrange the beef chunks tightly on the bottom of the pressure cooker. Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper powder, sugar, star anise, bay leaf, and ginger slices. Mix well to coat the meat evenly with the seasonings.
3. Cover the pressure cooker lid and start the first pressure cooking cycle:
   - For electric pressure cooker: select the "Beef/Mutton" function;
   - For stovetop pressure cooker: after steam comes up, reduce to low heat and cook for 25 minutes.
4. Open the lid. Use a spoon to skim off the floating oil from the soup surface. Remove the bay leaf, ginger slices, and other solid seasonings.
   **Note**: Skimming the oil makes the dish less greasy. The skimmed oil can be refrigerated and used for stir-frying, but never pour it directly down the drain.
5. Add the potato chunks and carrot chunks to the pot. Mix well with the beef.
6. Cover the lid and start the second pressure cooking cycle:
   - For electric pressure cooker: select the "Congee" function;
   - For stovetop pressure cooker: after steam comes up, reduce to medium-low heat and cook for another 5 minutes.
7. Open the lid. Depending on preference, either serve directly or transfer to a wok to reduce the sauce. Sprinkle scallions and cilantro on top before serving. The beef and vegetables are ready when easily pierced with chopsticks.

## Health Tips
This dish uses a pressure cooker for waterless braising, preserving nutrients well. Beef is rich in iron, while potatoes and carrots provide vitamin C, making it ideal for warming up in autumn and winter. Skimming off the floating oil makes it healthier, but those with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of dark soy sauce and oyster sauce.
