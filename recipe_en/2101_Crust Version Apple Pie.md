# Crust Version Apple Pie
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍎 1 apple (diced, for filling)
🥟 3 frozen egg tart shells (keep frozen until use)
🍬 Brown sugar, to taste
🌿 Ground cinnamon, to taste
🥚 Egg yolk wash, as needed (for brushing surface)
🌰 Sesame seeds or almond slices, a pinch (for garnish, optional)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
♨️ Air fryer
📡 Microwave
🥣 Aluminum foil bowl or heat-resistant container
🥢 Rolling pin
🖌️ Brush
🥄 Spoon
🧤 Disposable gloves (optional)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare Filling**
   Dice the apple and mix evenly with brown sugar and ground cinnamon. Microwave on high for about 10 minutes, or cook in a pot until soft. Remove and let cool, then thoroughly drain excess liquid.
   **Key Point**: The filling must be drained well to prevent moisture from soaking the crust during baking, ensuring a crispy texture.

2. **Prepare Crust**
   While frozen, peel off the bottom foil from 3 egg tart shells. Stack them together and let them thaw naturally. Use a rolling pin to flatten into a dough sheet about the thickness of a coin.
   **Key Point**: Keep the tart shells frozen when removing foil to avoid sticking; the crust softens after thawing, making it easier to roll out.

3. **Assemble Apple Pie**
   Place the rolled-out crust into an aluminum foil bowl or heat-resistant mold. Fill with the drained apple filling. Cut the remaining crust into strips and weave a lattice pattern on top of the filling.
   **Key Point**: The lattice weave should not be too tight, leaving some gaps to allow steam to escape.

4. **Decorate and Bake**
   Brush the surface evenly with egg yolk wash. Sprinkle with sesame seeds or almond slices according to personal preference. Place in the air fryer and bake at 180°C for about 20 minutes.
   **Doneness Check**: The crust should have distinct layers, the surface should be golden brown, and it should emit an aroma of caramel and cinnamon when ready.
   **Key Point**: Egg yolk wash is key for browning, but do not apply too thickly; baking time may be adjusted based on the actual power of your air fryer.

## Health Advice
This apple pie uses egg tart shells to simplify preparation, making it suitable for those seeking a convenient baking experience. Be sure to drain the filling thoroughly to prevent the crust from becoming soggy and affecting the texture.
