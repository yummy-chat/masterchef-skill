# Perfect Boiled Egg  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

🧂 Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥚 Fresh egg (AA grade recommended) 1 (approx. 60g)  
💧 100°C boiling water 1500ml  
💧 30°C warm water 1500ml  
🧊 Ice water (0°C) as needed  

🍳 Kitchen Utensils  
🫕 Pot for 100°C boiling water (diameter ≥ 15cm)  
🫕 Pot for 30°C warm water (diameter ≥ 15cm)  
⏱️ Timer  
🥄 Slotted spoon  

📝 Steps  
1. Prepare two pots of water: Pot A maintains 100°C boiling water, Pot B maintains 30°C warm water.  
2. Place the egg in a slotted spoon and lower it into Pot A boiling water, starting the timer at the same time.  
3. Transfer the egg to the other pot every 2 minutes.  
4. Repeat the transfer operation a total of 16 times (total duration 32 minutes).  
5. After the final transfer to Pot B, let it sit for 30 seconds.  
6. Immediately place the egg into ice water and soak for 30 seconds to stop the cooking process.  
7. When peeling, start from the air cell at the blunt end of the egg and peel the egg membrane along the longitudinal axis.  

## Health Tips  
Boiled eggs are nutritious, but prolonged heating may affect texture and nutrition. It is recommended to cook room-temperature eggs directly for 8 minutes to ensure good results and save energy. A greenish yolk is caused by an iron-sulfur reaction, which is normal and safe to eat.
