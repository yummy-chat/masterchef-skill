# Spicy Mixed Noodles (Mala Ban)
Cuisine: Other (Shanxi style)
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients
🥬 Leafy green vegetables (three portions, any variety such as spinach, bok choy, etc.)
🥔 Potato slices, as needed
🍄 Mushrooms, as needed
🍡 Hot pot meatballs, as needed
🍜 Wide vermicelli noodles, as needed
🍜 Instant noodles, about ten packets
🥩 Beef (brisket or shank), as needed (cut into chunks)
🥩 Beef hot pot rolls, as needed
🐟 Fish fillets, as needed
🦐 Shrimp, about several pieces (divided into large, medium, and small sizes)

## Seasonings
🫙 Sesame paste, one jar
🥜 Peanut butter, about half a jar
🥤 Sprite, as needed (to thin the paste)
🌶️ Fine chili flakes, generous amount (for color)
🌶️ Coarse chili flakes, small amount (for heat and texture)
🫒 Sesame oil, one tablespoon
🫒 Hot oil, as needed (to pour over chili flakes)
🌿 Sichuan peppercorn powder, two tablespoons
🍶 Baijiu (Chinese liquor), a small amount
🦪 Oyster sauce, three tablespoons
🍶 Zhenjiang vinegar, one and a half tablespoons
🧂 Soy sauce, three tablespoons
🧂 Chicken powder, as needed
🍬 Sugar, as needed
🧂 Salt, as needed
🥜 Crushed peanuts, as needed
⚪ Sesame seeds, as needed
🌿 Sichuan peppercorn oil, as needed
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed (for cooking shrimp to remove odor)
🍬 White sugar, as needed (for final umami boost)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🪣 Large stainless steel basin (for mixing sauce, final serving, and stirring)
🥣 Small stainless steel bowl (for preparing chili oil)
🥄 Soup ladle (for mixing sauce, stirring ingredients, and drizzling oil)
🍲 Large iron wok (for blanching all ingredients)
🥄 Slotted spoon (for retrieving cooked ingredients)

## Preparation Steps
### Prep Phase
1. Prepare "Spicy Chili Oil": In a small bowl, add a generous amount of fine chili flakes, a small amount of coarse chili flakes, and one tablespoon of sesame oil. Heat oil until it just begins to smoke, then pour it over the chili flakes in three batches, stirring with a spoon each time. Finally, add two tablespoons of Sichuan peppercorn powder and a small amount of baijiu, and stir well.
**Key Point**: The oil temperature must be high enough; pour in batches to fully release the chili's color and aroma.

2. Prepare "Sesame Paste Base": In a large basin, pour in one jar of sesame paste and about half a jar of peanut butter. Use Sprite to thin it out and stir until smooth.
**Key Point**: Stir until no lumps remain and the mixture is smooth; the bubbles and sweetness of Sprite help thin the paste and enhance the flavor.

### Cooking Phase
1. Blanch vegetables and hardy ingredients: Bring a large pot of water to a boil, add a little cooking oil. Add the three types of leafy greens, potato slices, mushrooms, and hot pot meatballs in sequence. Blanch until cooked, then remove with a slotted spoon and place into the large basin.
**Doneness Check**: Vegetables are soft, ingredients change color or float when done.

2. Cook wide vermicelli noodles: Add the wide vermicelli to the boiling water and cook for about 10 minutes, until they become soft and transparent, then remove.
**Key Point**: Cook the vermicelli until transparent and tender, but not overly soft.

3. Blanch meats: Add the beef chunks, beef hot pot rolls, and fish fillets in sequence. Blanch until they change color and are fully cooked, then remove.
**Doneness Check**: Beef changes color, hot pot rolls curl, fish fillets turn white.

4. Cook instant noodles and shrimp: Add multiple packets of instant noodles to the boiling water, cook until they loosen, then remove. In a separate pot of water, add cooking wine to remove odor, then add the small, medium, and large shrimp in sequence. Cook until they turn red and curl, then remove.
**Key Point**: Cook shrimp until they turn red and curl; overcooking will make the meat tough.

5. Combine all ingredients: Place all cooked vegetables, vermicelli, meats, instant noodles, and shrimp into the large stainless steel basin.
6. Add sauce and mix: Pour all of the prepared "Spicy Chili Oil" into the basin. Use a large soup ladle to stir vigorously, initially mixing the sauce. Then drizzle in the prepared "Sesame Paste Base," sprinkle with some white sugar, and stir thoroughly again to ensure all ingredients are evenly coated in the sauce.
**Key Point**: With a large quantity of ingredients, stir from the bottom upward, applying even force to ensure the ingredients at the bottom are also fully coated.

## Health Advice
This spicy mixed noodles dish is rich in ingredients. It is recommended to control the amount of instant noodles and sesame paste to avoid excessive calories. It is suitable for sharing with friends at a gathering. During a weight loss phase, increase the amount of vegetables and fish/shrimp.
