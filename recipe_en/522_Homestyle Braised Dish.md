# Homestyle Braised Dish
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
💧 Water 4 jin (about 2 liters, used as base broth)
🍖 Meat (pork trotters, chicken wings, chicken feet, etc.) about 3 jin (blanched beforehand)
🥬 Vegetables (lotus root slices, peanuts, seaweed, bean sticks, tofu skin, etc.) appropriate amount (washed and drained)
🧅 Scallion appropriate amount
🧄 Ginger appropriate amount
🌿 Braising spice packet 1 packet (pre-packaged)
🌶️ Chili packet 1 packet (optional)
🍾 Cooking wine appropriate amount (used when blanching meat)
🧂 Salt appropriate amount (add when reusing)

## Cookware and Equipment
🫕 Stainless steel stockpot
🔥 Gas stove
🥄 Slotted spoon, soup ladle
📦 Plastic wrap, storage container
⚖️ Electronic scale, mineral water bottle

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare the braising base broth**
   Pour 4 jin of clean water into the pot, add the braising spice packet and chili packet.
   **Key point**: The spice packet must be added to cold water.

2. **Blanch the meat**
   Place the meat in cold water, add scallion segments, ginger slices, and cooking wine. Bring to a boil and cook for about 3 minutes, skimming off any foam during the process.
   **Key point**: Start with cold water and use the "deodorizing trio."

3. **Rinse the meat**
   Remove the blanched meat and rinse thoroughly with warm water to remove surface foam and impurities.
   **Key point**: Rinsing thoroughly ensures a clearer finished braising broth.

4. **Simmer the braising liquid**
   Bring the spiced water to a boil, then reduce to medium heat and maintain a rolling boil for 2 minutes.
   **Key point**: A brief simmer allows the spices to release their initial flavors.

5. **Braise the meat**
   Place the prepared meat into the boiling braising liquid. Start timing once it returns to a boil, and cook according to the specific type of meat.

6. **Braise the vegetables**
   Add the vegetables to the boiling braising liquid. Once it returns to a boil, cook uniformly for 20 minutes.
   **Key point**: All braising times are counted from when the liquid returns to a boil, to avoid overcooking.

7. **Steep for flavor**
   Immediately turn off the heat once the braising time is up, cover the pot, and let it steep for 10 minutes.
   **Key point**: Use residual heat to infuse more flavor into the ingredients.

8. **Remove from pot**
   Use a slotted spoon to remove the braised items and arrange them on a plate. They taste better after cooling.

9. **Store and reuse the braising liquid**
   Allow the braising liquid to cool completely, then seal it tightly with plastic wrap and store in the freezer. The liquid can be reused 2–3 times. For the first use, it is recommended to braise only meat to enrich the liquid. When reusing, add salt and water as needed to adjust the flavor.

## Health Advice
This braised dish offers a balanced mix of meat and vegetables, but the braising liquid is high in salt and purines. People with high blood pressure or gout are advised to consume the broth sparingly and opt for lean meat and vegetables. Suitable for the whole family, the braising liquid can be reused 2–3 times to save costs.
