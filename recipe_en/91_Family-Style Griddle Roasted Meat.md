# Family-Style Griddle Roasted Meat
Cuisine: Beijing Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 500g beef (top blade, tenderloin, ribeye, or other cuts; can also use beef rolls)  
🧅 Scallions, as needed (use only the white part, cut into eyebrow-shaped slivers)  
🥓 2 small pieces of pork fat (for rendering oil to grease the pan)  
🐚 2 dried scallops (fresh scallops) (ground into powder for sprinkling)  
🌿 Cilantro, as needed (optional for marinating the meat)  
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns, as needed (for making the two-flavor water)  
⭐ Star anise, as needed (for making the two-flavor water)  
🧂 Chicken bouillon, as needed  
🧂 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🧂 White pepper powder, as needed  
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed  
🍶 Vinegar, as needed  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🧂 Five-spice powder, as needed  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, as needed  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for sealing the meat)  
🧂 Cumin powder, as needed (about half the amount of the scallop powder)  
🌶️ Chili flakes, as needed  
🌱 White sesame seeds, as needed  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥣 Bowl  
🍳 Frying pan (or griddle, cast iron pan)  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍽️ Plate  
🥖 Rolling pin (for grinding the dried scallops)  
🔥 Small stove  

## Preparation Steps  
1. In a bowl, combine Sichuan peppercorns and star anise. Pour in boiling water and let steep until cooled completely. Set aside.  
2. Split the white part of the scallions lengthwise, then slice diagonally into thin slivers. Arrange on a plate. If you prefer, you can substitute with onions.  
3. Slice off any fatty parts from the beef and set aside. Cut the lean meat into bite-sized slices or strips.  
4. Marinate the beef:  
   - Pour the cooled two-flavor water into the beef. Use your hands to knead thoroughly, ensuring the meat absorbs the liquid.  
   - Add chicken bouillon, light soy sauce, white pepper powder, cooking wine, vinegar, salt, five-spice powder, and dark soy sauce. Mix well.  
   - Add a handful of the cut scallion slivers to the meat and mix evenly.  
   - Finally, drizzle in cooking oil to seal in the moisture. Refrigerate and marinate for at least 30 minutes.  
5. Prepare the sprinkling powder: Grind the dried scallops into a fine powder using a rolling pin. Mix with cumin powder, chili flakes, and white sesame seeds.  
6. Place the frying pan or griddle over a small flame to heat up. Add the reserved pork fat pieces and render the lard, coating the entire bottom of the pan.  
7. Quickly pour all the marinated beef into the hot pan. Use chopsticks to swiftly separate the pieces. Keep the heat on medium-high and stir continuously until the beef is fully cooked and the moisture has evaporated. Listen for a sizzling sound and look for slightly charred edges on the meat.  
   **Key Point**: All moisture must be thoroughly evaporated, otherwise, the dish will be braised meat instead of roasted meat.  
8. Once the moisture is gone, immediately add a handful of fresh raw scallion slivers. Stir-fry quickly to release the aroma.  
9. Evenly sprinkle the mixed powder over the meat. Toss a few times, then turn off the heat and plate.  

## Health Tips  
Beef is rich in high-quality protein. Paired with scallion slivers to remove the gamey smell and enhance flavor, this dish is suitable for fitness muscle building or warming up in winter. Note that the two-flavor water helps tenderize and deodorize the meat, but consumption of high-fat meat cuts should be controlled.
