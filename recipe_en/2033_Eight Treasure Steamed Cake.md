# Eight Treasure Steamed Cake
Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Flour about one bowl (main ingredient)  
🥜 Walnuts appropriate amount (chopped)  
🥜 Peanuts appropriate amount (chopped)  
🫐 Red dates appropriate amount (pitted and chopped)  
🍇 Raisins appropriate amount  
🍑 Dried apricots (or dried sweet potatoes, etc.) appropriate amount (chopped)  
💧 Boiling water appropriate amount (for making batter)  
🧈 Lard ratio to flour approximately 1:1.5  
🍬 White sugar appropriate amount (generous amount)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Large spoon (spatula)  
🥣 Small bowl (for shaping)  
🍽️ Plate  
🥢 Chopsticks or whisk (for beating egg whites)  

## Preparation Steps
1. Chop or crush the walnuts, peanuts, red dates, raisins, dried apricots, and other dried fruits separately and set aside.
2. Heat the wok over heat, add the lard, and heat over low heat until melted.  
**Note**: The ratio of lard to flour is approximately 1:1.5. The lard should be sufficient for fragrance.

3. Add the flour and stir-fry continuously over low heat for about 8 minutes.  
**Key point**: Stir constantly throughout to prevent burning at the bottom. Cook until the flour turns light yellow and reaches a fluffy, "sandy" state. Be careful not to burn.

4. Once the flour reaches the sandy state, slowly pour in a generous amount of boiling water and stir quickly to combine with the oil and flour into a thick paste.  
**Key point**: Use boiling water to quickly form a thick paste similar to red bean paste using high heat. The ratio of water, oil, and flour must be mastered by experience; the paste should not be too thick or too thin.

5. Add a generous amount of white sugar and stir-fry evenly.  
**Note**: This dish is purely sweet, so the sugar should be sufficient.

6. Add all the chopped dried fruits into the wok and stir-fry evenly with the paste. This is the main body of the Eight Treasure Steamed Cake.
7. Spoon the fried cake into a small bowl, press down and smooth the surface, then invert onto a plate to form a dome shape.
8. Beat the egg whites until fluffy and snow-white, gently spread on top of the cake. Garnish with a few goji berries and cilantro leaves.  
**Traditional technique**: Beat the egg whites thoroughly and patiently to create the "snow cap" decoration.

## Health Advice
This Eight Treasure Steamed Cake is high in oil and sugar, suitable for occasional enjoyment rather than daily consumption. It is recommended to pair with plain tea to cut the grease. Those with poor digestion should eat less, and individuals managing blood sugar levels should taste sparingly.
