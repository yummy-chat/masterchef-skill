# Pan-fried Water Spinach Omelette
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 1 bunch water spinach (about 200-300g)  
🥚 4 eggs  
🍤 Dried small shrimp/krill, to taste  
🌶️ A few small chili peppers (sliced, optional)  
🍶 A few drops of white vinegar  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🍬 Sugar, to taste  
🌿 A pinch of ground pepper  
🥣 2 teaspoons of starch water  
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
🥄 Slotted spoon and soup ladle  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl/mixing bowl  
🍽️ Plate  

## Cooking Steps
1.  **Prepare the water spinach:**
    - Bring a pot of water to a boil, add a little cooking oil. First, blanch the water spinach stems for about 5 seconds, then add the leaves. Once the water returns to a boil, blanch for another 3 seconds. Quickly remove.
    - Immediately rinse under cold water, then firmly squeeze out all moisture. Finely chop and place in a large bowl.
    - **Note:** Blanching stems and leaves separately ensures even doneness; must rinse with cold water and squeeze dry after blanching to prevent excess moisture during pan-frying.

2.  **Prepare the egg mixture:**
    - In a separate container, crack the 4 eggs, add a few drops of white vinegar, and beat until well combined.
    - Pour the beaten eggs over the chopped water spinach. Add the chili pepper slices, salt, sugar, pepper, and 2 teaspoons of starch water. Mix thoroughly.
    - **Key point:** White vinegar and starch water help create a tender, smooth texture for the eggs.

3.  **Sauté for fragrance:**
    - Heat cooking oil in the pan over low heat. Add the dried small shrimp and slowly stir-fry until fragrant.
    - **Key point:** Slow stir-fry over low heat to fully release the shrimp's umami flavor.

4.  **Pan-fry the omelette:**
    - Pour the water spinach and egg mixture into the pan, spreading it evenly over the sautéed shrimp.
    - Gently push the edges of the uncooked egg liquid inward using the back of a soup ladle to help shape it into a round disc.
    - Cook over medium-low heat. Once the bottom is golden and set, carefully flip using a plate. Cook the other side until golden.
    - **Key point:** Medium-low heat prevents burning; using a plate to flip helps keep the omelette intact.

5.  **Cut and serve:**
    - Once both sides are golden, use a spatula to cut the omelette into bite-sized pieces. Serve immediately from the pan.

## Health Advice  
Blanching and squeezing the water spinach dry before pan-frying helps reduce oxalic acid and lock in moisture. This dish is high in protein and low in calories, suitable for weight management. Adding dried shrimp provides calcium and boosts flavor, while eggs offer high-quality protein, making it suitable for all ages.
