# Old-Style Lean Beef
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef knuckle (shin meat) 500g
🍐 Pear 80g
🫚 Ginger 30g
🍍 Pineapple 4g
🧅 Onion 100g
💧 Water 30g
🥚 Whole egg liquid 25g
🧅 Onion slices (as needed)
🫒 Soybean oil (as needed)
🧂 Korean BBQ steak sauce 20g
🧂 Beef seasoning powder 5g
🧂 Black pepper powder 0.5g
🧂 White pepper powder 0.5g
🧂 Salt 5g
🧂 Flavor powder (MSG) 5g
🍬 White sugar 25g

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🫗 Household blender or high-speed blender
🧽 Gauze bag or strainer
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🧤 Food-grade gloves
🔪 Knife and cutting board

## Preparation Steps
### Making the Old-Style Marinade
1. Place the pear, ginger, pineapple, onion, and water into the blender. Blend until a fine puree forms.
   **Key point**: The fruit and vegetable juice is the soul of the marinade, providing natural fruit aroma and tenderizing effect.
2. Use a gauze bag or strainer to squeeze out the fruit and vegetable juice, discarding the solid residue.
3. In a clean container, add Korean BBQ steak sauce, beef seasoning powder, black pepper powder, white pepper powder, salt, flavor powder, and white sugar. Mix well.
4. Measure 100g of the fruit and vegetable juice and pour it into the seasoning mixture. Stir thoroughly until the sugar and salt are completely dissolved. The old-style marinade is now ready.
   ———
### Marinating the Beef
5. Cut the beef knuckle into even slices about 2mm thick and place them in a stainless steel bowl.
6. Divide the prepared old-style marinade into two equal portions. Pour one portion over the beef.
7. Using your hands, repeatedly knead and toss the beef, ensuring all the liquid marinade is fully absorbed by the meat and no excess liquid remains at the bottom of the bowl.
   **Key point**: The liquid must be forcefully worked into the meat fibers; this directly affects the tenderness and flavor of the final product.
8. Add the whole egg liquid at a ratio of 25g per 500g of beef. Continue kneading and tossing until the egg liquid is completely absorbed by the beef.
   **Key point**: The egg liquid must be added only after the marinade is fully absorbed. Do not reverse the order.
9. Cover the marinated beef with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours to allow the flavors to fully penetrate.
   ———
### Serving Preparation
10. Before serving, remove the chilled beef and mix in an appropriate amount of onion slices and soybean oil. Gently toss to combine, then arrange on a plate for grilling.
    **Key point**: Add oil only at serving time to enhance the meat slices' sheen and aroma. The onion slices add an extra layer of flavor.
