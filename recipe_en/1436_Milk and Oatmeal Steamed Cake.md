# Milk and Oatmeal Steamed Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥛 Fresh milk 500 ml
🍬 White sugar 50 g
🍄 High-sugar tolerant yeast 5 g
🌾 All-purpose flour 300 g
🥣 Instant oatmeal 80 g (part mixed into batter, part sprinkled on top)
🍚 Tapioca starch 100 g (to add a soft and chewy texture)
🧈 Cooking oil about 10 g
🫧 Baking powder 3-5 g
🌸 Dried osmanthus flowers as needed (for garnish and aroma)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥛 Measuring cup
🥣 Glass bowl
🥢 Whisk
🥢 Chopsticks
🎥 Plastic wrap
📦 Air fryer aluminum foil pan (6-inch mold)
🍽️ Steamer
🔥 Steaming pot
🔪 Knife and cutting board

## Preparation Steps
### Preparation Stage
1. Pour 500 ml of room temperature fresh milk into a large bowl, add 50 g of white sugar and 5 g of high-sugar tolerant yeast, and stir until the sugar and yeast are initially dissolved.
   **Key point**: Ensure the yeast and sugar are evenly dissolved in the milk to facilitate subsequent fermentation.
2. Add 300 g of all-purpose flour, 80 g of instant oatmeal, and 100 g of tapioca starch to the milk mixture.
3. Use a whisk to thoroughly mix all ingredients until there are no dry lumps or clumps, forming a smooth, thick batter.
   **Key point**: Stir evenly to avoid lumps.
4. Seal the bowl with plastic wrap and place it in a warm spot to ferment until the batter volume expands to about double.

### Production Stage
1. Add about 10 g of cooking oil and 3-5 g of baking powder to the fermented batter, and stir thoroughly with chopsticks to completely release the gas from the batter.
2. Divide the degassed batter evenly into three 6-inch aluminum foil pans.
3. Sprinkle a small amount of instant oatmeal and dried osmanthus flowers on the surface of the batter in each mold.
4. Place the filled molds on a countertop to rest without covering. Observe the batter slowly expand until it rises to about 90% full at the edge of the mold.
5. Bring water to a boil in a steamer. Place the molds of the second-fermented batter into the steamer, cover with a lid, and steam over high heat for 28 minutes.
6. Remove the lid immediately after steaming. The cake has a rich aroma and an appealing appearance.

## Health Advice
Milk and oatmeal steamed cake is rich in dietary fiber and high-quality protein, making it suitable as breakfast or a snack. Diabetic patients can use sugar substitutes and should control the amount of oatmeal to avoid blood sugar fluctuations.
