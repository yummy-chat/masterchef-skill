# Potato Mash Sandwich
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥔 Potato, about 1 piece (pre-cooked, peeled)  
🥚 Hard-boiled egg, 1 piece (pre-cooked, peeled)  
🍖 Sliced ham, about 3-4 slices  
🌽 Corn kernels, a small handful  
🥬 Lettuce, a few leaves (washed and chopped)  
🍞 Sliced bread, 2-3 slices  
🥛 Milk, a little (to adjust moisture)  
🌿 Black pepper, to taste  
🥄 Salad dressing, to taste (can be replaced with yogurt for fat reduction)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔨 Potato masher  
🥣 Stainless steel mixing bowl  
🔪 Knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥄 Spoon/fork  
🍽️ Plate  

## Steps  
1. Place the pre-cooked peeled potato and hard-boiled egg (with shell removed) into a bowl. Use a potato masher to crush them into a coarse mash, retaining a few small lumps for texture.  
2. Add the sliced ham, corn kernels, and chopped lettuce to the bowl.  
3. Grind in an appropriate amount of black pepper, pour in a little milk, and squeeze in the salad dressing. Mix all the ingredients evenly.  
   **Key point**: Milk can adjust the dryness or moisture of the filling. For weight loss, replace salad dressing with unsweetened yogurt.  
4. Take one slice of bread and spread the prepared filling evenly in the center, making it slightly thicker in the middle and leaving a gap along the edges.  
5. Place another slice of bread on top. Use your palm to gently press down to make the sandwich more compact.  
6. Cut the sandwich in half with a knife and serve.  

## Health Tips  
This potato mash sandwich is nutritionally balanced. Potatoes provide carbohydrates, eggs and ham supply protein, while lettuce and corn add dietary fiber. For weight loss, it is recommended to use yogurt instead of salad dressing. Those with high blood pressure should be aware that sliced ham contains a relatively high amount of salt.
