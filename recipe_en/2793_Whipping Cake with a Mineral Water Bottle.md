# Whipping Cake with a Mineral Water Bottle  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 3 eggs (separate egg whites and yolks)  
🥛 1 tablespoon milk (add to yolks)  
🌾 2 level tablespoons flour (add to yolks)  
🥛 Some whipping cream (for making matcha cream)  
🍵 Some matcha powder (to mix with whipping cream)  
🍓 Some strawberries (for decoration)  
🍘 1 piece Want Want Snow Rice Crackers (crush for snow top)  
🧂 About 1 tablespoon sugar (add to egg whites)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🫙 Plastic mineral water bottle (cut into "dragon whisker" shape as a whisk)  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🔥 Gas stove  
🥣 Bowls and basins (for mixing ingredients)  
🥄 Spoon (for stirring and decorating)  
✂️ Scissors (for cutting the bottle and the bag)  
🛍️ Plastic bag (cut a corner as a simple piping bag)  

## Steps  
1. Use scissors to cut the bottom of an empty mineral water bottle into thin, elongated strips, forming a dragon whisker shape, to use as a manual whisk.  
2. Separate the egg whites and yolks of 3 eggs into clean, oil-free containers.  
3. Add 1 tablespoon milk and 2 level tablespoons flour to the yolks, and stir until smooth, with the drizzled lines disappearing quickly.  
4. Whipping the meringue:  
   - Use the homemade bottle whisk to manually beat the egg whites until lines appear, then add about 1 tablespoon sugar.  
   - Continue whipping until stiff peaks form: the meringue does not flow when the container is inverted, and the whisk leaves short, upright tips.  
5. Add the meringue to the yolk mixture in two batches, gently folding until evenly combined.  
6. Frying the cake:  
   - Heat a non-stick frying pan over low heat; when the pan is hot, scoop in an appropriate amount of batter.  
   - Cover with a lid and steam; when the lid feels hot to the touch, the batter is mostly set.  
   - You can add another layer of batter and drizzle a little water along the edge of the pan to create steam, aiding in thorough cooking.  
   - Once the batter is completely set, carefully flip and fry until both sides are golden brown.  
7. Add an appropriate amount of matcha powder to the whipping cream, and continue whipping with the homemade bottle whisk until slightly thick and able to hold its shape.  
8. Decorating the cake:  
   - Trim the fried cake bases into desired shapes and stack them.  
   - Cut a small corner of a plastic bag, fill it with matcha cream, pipe it onto the cake surface, and roughly spread with a spoon.  
   - Use a spoon to press patterns into the cream, arrange strawberries on top, and finally crush the Want Want Snow Rice Cracker over the top to create a "snow top" effect.  

## Health Advice  
Use a mineral water bottle to whip the meringue, and use crushed Want Want Snow Rice Crackers for the snow top. Note that the meringue must be whipped to stiff peaks, with no flow when inverted; otherwise, the cake base may collapse.
