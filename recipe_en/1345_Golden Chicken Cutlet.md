# Golden Chicken Cutlet

Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Leftover rice, about half a bowl  
🥚 Eggs, as needed (beaten into egg wash)  
🍞 Breadcrumbs, as needed  
🧂 Soy sauce, as needed  
🍅 Ketchup, as needed  
🥗 Mayonnaise, as needed  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🧊 Refrigerator  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
♨️ Air fryer  
📄 Air fryer parchment paper  
🫗 Oil spray bottle  
🔪 Knife  
🪓 Cutting board  

## Steps  
1. Place the leftover rice in a stainless steel bowl, add soy sauce, and mix with your hands until the rice becomes sticky and can form clumps.  
2. Shape the seasoned rice into patties.  
3. Coat each rice patty with egg wash and then breadcrumbs.  
4. Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper, place the coated rice patties inside, and spray the surface evenly with oil.  
5. Put the basket in the air fryer and bake until the surface is golden and crispy. Note: Spraying with oil helps achieve a crispier texture. The exact temperature and time can be adjusted based on your device.  
6. Remove, cut into strips, and drizzle with ketchup and mayonnaise.  

## Health Advice  
Golden Chicken Cutlet made from leftover rice has a high carbohydrate content and relatively low protein. It is suitable for occasional consumption to use up leftover rice, but it is not recommended as a regular meal. Pair it with vegetables and protein-rich foods, such as fried eggs or chicken breast, to balance the nutrition.
