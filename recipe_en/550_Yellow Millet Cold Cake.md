# Yellow Millet Cold Cake
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Round glutinous rice 200g
🌾 Yellow millet 100g
🎃 Pumpkin about 200g (steamed and mashed into puree)
🫐 Dried cranberries 30g (soaked in warm water until soft, then chopped)
🍇 Raisins 30g (soaked in warm water until soft, then chopped)
🍶 Rice wine liquid 300g (filtered from rice wine)
💧 Warm water 300g (for soaking)
🍬 White sugar 24g (16g for glutinous rice layer, 8g for yellow millet layer)
🍯 Honey 50g
💧 Warm boiled water 50g (mixed with honey to make honey water)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🧽 Cheesecloth or fine mesh strainer
🌡️ Thermometer
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍳 Steamer
🥄 Spoon or rolling pin (for mashing pumpkin puree)
🗃️ Square baking pan or mold
🔪 Spatula or scraper
🖌️ Oil brush
📦 Plastic wrap

## Instructions
1.  Filter the rice wine through cheesecloth, pressing to extract the liquid; set aside. Place the glutinous rice and yellow millet in separate bowls, add equal amounts of rice wine liquid and warm water at 30-40°C to each, stir well, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate to soak overnight.
2.  Soak the dried fruits in warm water at about 70°C for 20 minutes, drain and chop. Cut the pumpkin into pieces, steam until cooked, and mash into a smooth puree. Mix honey and warm boiled water in a 1:1 ratio to make honey water; set aside.
3.  Rinse the soaked rice separately, drain, place in a steamer, and steam over high heat for 30 minutes until fully cooked.
4.  While the steamed glutinous rice is still hot, add 16g white sugar and all the chopped dried fruits. Stir vigorously in one direction with chopsticks until the grains become sticky and form a stretchy paste.
5.  While the steamed yellow millet is still hot, add 8g white sugar and the pumpkin puree. Stir in the same direction until evenly sticky and combined.
6.  Brush the bottom of the mold evenly with honey water. Also apply a little honey water to your hands to prevent sticking. First, spread the glutinous rice layer, pressing firmly to make it compact and even. Then, spread the yellow millet and pumpkin layer on top, smoothing it out with a spatula or your hands. Brush the surface with another layer of honey water.
7.  Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours. Once fully set, unmold and cut into pieces. Serve with honey water or osmanthus syrup for best flavor.
**Key Point**: Stir clockwise to develop a gelatinous texture; otherwise, the texture will be loose and not chewy enough. Each layer must be pressed firmly after spreading. The honey water prevents sticking and keeps the cake moist.

## Health Advice
Yellow Millet Cold Cake is made primarily from glutinous rice and yellow millet, combined with pumpkin puree and dried fruits. It has a soft, sticky, and sweet taste, making it suitable for cooling off in summer. However, glutinous rice is difficult to digest. Those with weak digestive systems should consume it in moderation, and individuals managing blood sugar levels should also be cautious.
