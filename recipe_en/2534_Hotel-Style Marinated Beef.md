# Hotel-Style Marinated Beef
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef, appropriate amount (first cut into large pieces along the grain, then slice thinly against the grain)
🧂 Salt, a pinch
🧂 Tenderizer, a pinch (within the permissible range by food safety authorities)
💧 Water, appropriate amount (add in batches)
🧂 MSG, a pinch
🍬 Sugar, a pinch
🧂 Beef bouillon, a pinch (to enhance flavor)
🧂 Dark soy sauce, a pinch (for coloring)
🌾 Cornstarch, a pinch
🫒 Cooking oil, a pinch (for sealing)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🥄 Colander
🥄 Soup ladle
🍳 Wok
🔥 Gas stove

## Preparation Steps
1. First, cut the beef into large pieces along the grain, then slice thinly against the grain. Rinse with clean water twice to remove blood and any gamey smell, then place in a colander and squeeze out excess water thoroughly.
2. Place the squeezed beef in a bowl. Add salt and a pinch of tenderizer and pat continuously with your palm for about 20 minutes, allowing the seasonings to fully penetrate the meat.
3. Gradually add water in batches. After each addition, pat the beef until the water is completely absorbed before adding the next batch. This helps the beef become firm and tender.
4. Once the beef has absorbed the water, refrigerate to rest for 1 hour. After taking it out, if there is some water on the surface, pat again until absorbed.
5. Add MSG, sugar, beef bouillon, and dark soy sauce to the beef, then continue patting evenly to enhance flavor and add color.
6. Add a pinch of cornstarch and mix thoroughly to lock in the seasonings and moisture. Finally, drizzle in cooking oil and mix again to seal the surface. The marination is complete.
7. For stir-frying, heat the wok until hot, swirl in oil to coat the wok and then pour it out. Add cold oil, then stir-fry the marinated beef quickly for about 15 seconds until 80% done. Remove and drain the oil for later use.

## Health Suggestion
This beef marination technique is professional, resulting in tender and juicy meat. It is suitable for those needing a high-protein, low-fat diet. It is recommended to control the amount of tenderizer and MSG. Hypertensive patients can reduce the salt content.
