# Clear-Broth Beef Brisket Noodles
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍖 Beef brisket/short ribs: appropriate amount (cut into chunks)  
🍎 Apple: 1 (whole, with core, for simmering the broth)  
🧅 Onion: about 1/4 (cut into chunks)  
🫑 Scallion: 1 section  
🍜 Dried noodles: 1 serving (thin round noodles)  
🥬 Baby bok choy/Chinese cabbage: 2-3 leaves  
🌿 Cilantro: appropriate amount (chopped)  
🧅 Scallion: appropriate amount (chopped)  
🧂 Salt: appropriate amount  
🫒 Cooking oil: a few drops  
🍶 Soy sauce: appropriate amount (added before eating)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
♨️ Pressure cooker  
🥘 Soup pot  
🥣 Large bowl  
🥄 Soup ladle  
🥢 Chopsticks  
✂️ Scissors  
🫗 Oil dispenser  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place the beef brisket, onion, scallion, and whole apple into the pressure cooker inner pot. Add enough water to submerge the ingredients, and add an appropriate amount of salt.  
**Key point**: No need to peel or core the apple; add it whole to the broth for a sweet and fresh flavor.  

2. Close the pressure cooker lid tightly. Heat over high heat until steam pressure builds, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 30 minutes to ensure the beef is tender.  
**Key point**: Reduce to low heat after pressure builds to avoid excessive pressure while ensuring the meat becomes tender.  

3. While the pressure cooker is simmering, bring water to a boil in another soup pot. Add the dried noodles and a few drops of cooking oil. Cook until the noodles have no white center.  
**Key point**: Adding cooking oil prevents the noodles from sticking and keeps them smooth.  

4. 1-2 minutes before the noodles are done, add the baby bok choy leaves to blanch until cooked. Remove together with the noodles and place into a large bowl.  

5. Use a soup ladle to scoop the cooked beef brisket into the bowl, then cut into bite-sized pieces with scissors.  

6. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and scallions.  

7. Ladle the hot beef brisket broth from the pressure cooker into the bowl until the broth level is roughly even with the ingredients.  
**Key point**: The broth must be piping hot to release the aroma of the cilantro and scallions.  

8. Before eating, add an appropriate amount of soy sauce to taste.  

## Health Advice  
Beef brisket is rich in protein and iron. Simmering it with an apple adds sweetness and reduces greasiness, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost or with anemia. Note that people with high blood pressure should use less salt. Thin round noodles are easy to digest, but they are not low in calories, so portion control is advised.
