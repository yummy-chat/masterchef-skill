# Pan-Fried Tofu with Scallions
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧈 White Tofu  
🧅 Scallions  
🌶️ Green Chili Peppers  
🧂 Salt  
🧂 Chicken Bouillon  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick Pan  

## Cooking Steps
1. Wash the white tofu and cut it into slices about 5mm thick, arranging them on a plate for later use.  
2. Wash the scallions, trim off the roots, and cut into chopped scallions for later use.  
3. Wash the green chili peppers, cut them open and remove the seeds, then cut into 1cm cubes for later use.  
4. Place the pan over heat and add an appropriate amount of cooking oil, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom.  
5. Lay the tofu slices one by one in the pan, and pan-fry over low heat until the bottom is golden brown. Flip and continue frying until both sides are golden brown, then remove and set aside.  
6. Add more oil to the pan to cover the bottom, pour in the green chili pepper cubes, and stir-fry over high heat while crushing them with a spatula for about 3 minutes.  
7. Return the fried tofu to the pan, add salt and chicken bouillon, and stir-fry over medium heat for 1 minute. Then drizzle in 10ml of water, and turn the heat to high to reduce the liquid.  
8. Before serving, sprinkle in the chopped scallions and stir-fry quickly until well combined, then plate and serve.  

## Health Tips
Tofu is a great source of high-quality plant protein and calcium, low in fat and satiating, making it suitable for those needing calorie control or protein supplementation. Using less oil and low heat during pan-frying prevents the tofu from absorbing too much oil. Adding scallions at the end enhances the flavor, but as chicken bouillon is high in sodium, people with high blood pressure should use it sparingly.
