# Stir-fried Celery with Bamboo Shoots
Cuisine: Jiangsu Cuisine
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Spring (January-April)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌿 Celery, appropriate amount (cut into inch-long segments)
🎋 Fresh or rehydrated bamboo shoots, appropriate amount (sliced)
🧄 Garlic, 3-4 cloves (sliced)
🫚 Ginger, a small amount (sliced)
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount
🫒 Vegetable oil, appropriate amount
🧴 Soy sauce, a little (for color)
🍶 Yellow wine, a little
🫗 Sesame oil, a few drops

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Bowl (for holding cold water)
🍽️ Plate

## Cooking Steps
1. Bring water to a boil in the wok, add one spoonful of salt and a little vegetable oil.  
**Key Point**: Adding salt and oil helps keep vegetables vibrant green and adds a subtle base flavor.

2. First, blanch the bamboo shoot slices. When the water boils again, add the celery segments and blanch until just cooked and the color turns bright green.  
**Note**: Add bamboo shoots first, then celery, as they have different cooking times. Keep the heat high throughout; do not blanch celery for too long.

3. Quickly remove the ingredients and transfer them to cold water to cool, then drain well.  
**Key Point**: Cooling in cold water is key to maintaining crisp texture and bright green color.

4. Heat another wok, add an appropriate amount of vegetable oil, then add the sliced garlic and ginger. Sauté over medium-low heat until fragrant.  
**Note**: Prevent the garlic from burning.

5. Turn to high heat, add the drained celery and bamboo shoots, and stir-fry quickly to combine evenly.
6. Season with salt and stir-fry evenly.

7. Splash a little yellow wine along the side of the wok, using the high heat to release the wine's aroma.
8. Add a little soy sauce for color and stir-fry evenly.
**Note**: Use a very small amount of soy sauce, only to enhance the visual appeal.

9. Just before removing from heat, drizzle in a few drops of sesame oil, stir-fry evenly, then immediately turn off the heat and serve.

## Health Tips
Celery and bamboo shoots are both high-fiber, low-calorie ingredients, ideal for spring weight loss and gut health. Blanching and then cooling in cold water helps maintain a crisp texture. It is recommended to use minimal oil and salt, and can be paired with lean meat strips for a balanced nutritional profile.
