# Rice Porridge (Congee)
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍚 Rice, as needed
💧 Water, as needed
🫒 Vegetable oil, as needed (optional)

🧂 Seasonings
(None)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Pot
🥄 Measuring cup (optional)
🧊 Refrigerator (optional)

## Instructions
1. Take rice according to the number of servings, about 60–110 ml per person, and rinse thoroughly. Use 9–12 times the volume of rice for water.
2. Mix the rinsed rice with vegetable oil, using about one-tenth of the raw rice volume, ensuring each grain is evenly coated.
3. Place the oil-coated rice in the refrigerator, chilling for about one-tenth of the raw rice volume in minutes.
4. Put the rice and water together in the pot, and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce to low heat just before the water starts to boil to prevent overflow.
5. Continue cooking over low heat, with a total heating time of about 10 minutes per 500 ml of water, until the rice grains bloom and the porridge thickens.
6. Turn off the heat and serve.

## Health Tips
Rice porridge is gentle on the stomach and easy to digest, suitable for those with weak digestion or during recovery from illness. Avoid making it too watery to prevent low nutritional density.
