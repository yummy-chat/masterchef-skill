# Salmon Egg Cake
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍄 Button mushrooms: appropriate amount (stems removed, sliced)
🍅 Cherry tomatoes: appropriate amount (halved)
🌿 Cilantro: a little (chopped, for garnish)
🐟 Salmon: appropriate amount (cut into small pieces)
🧅 Onion: appropriate amount (chopped)
🧄 Garlic: 2-3 cloves (minced)
🥚 Eggs: 5 (suitable for a frying pan about 20 cm in diameter)
🧂 Salt: appropriate amount
⚫ Black pepper: appropriate amount
🌶️ Chili sauce or ketchup: appropriate amount (optional)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Frying pan (about 20 cm)
🔪 Cutting board and knife
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Spatula
🔗 Lid
🍾 Grinder (for black pepper)

## Preparation Steps
1. Prepare ingredients: Remove mushroom stems and slice, halve cherry tomatoes, chop cilantro, cut salmon into bite-sized pieces, chop onion and garlic separately.
2. Prepare egg mixture: Crack eggs into a bowl, add appropriate salt and black pepper, and whisk until well combined.
3. Sauté aromatics: Pour oil into the frying pan over cold pan and cold oil, heat over medium-high heat, add minced garlic and chopped onion, and stir-fry until the onion is translucent but not browned.
   **Note**: Avoid browning; only release the aroma.
4. Cook mushrooms: Add sliced mushrooms, slightly increase heat, and stir-fry slowly until the mushrooms soften.
   **Key point**: At this stage, sprinkle a little salt to speed up mushroom softening.
5. Cook salmon: Add salmon pieces and cook until the surface is just done.
   **Note**: If using frozen salmon, it is recommended to cook until fully done.
6. Add cherry tomatoes: Add cherry tomatoes in batches, retaining some texture.
7. Pour in egg mixture and stir: Evenly pour the egg mixture into the pan, then immediately use a spatula to slowly and continuously stir from the outside to the inside until the egg mixture is mostly set.
8. Arrange and simmer: Quickly adjust the distribution of mushrooms, salmon, and cherry tomatoes on the surface for a neat appearance, then cover with the lid and simmer for about 3-5 minutes.
   **Tip**: Doneness can be adjusted according to personal preference; keeping the surface slightly moist provides a better texture.
9. Plate and garnish: Slide the egg cake onto a plate, sprinkle with chopped cilantro, and optionally add more black pepper or salt. Serve with chili sauce or ketchup as desired.

## Health Advice
Salmon and eggs are rich in high-quality protein and Omega-3 fatty acids, which help build muscle and support brain health; button mushrooms and cherry tomatoes provide dietary fiber and vitamins. Ensure salmon is fully cooked to avoid parasite risks, and control oil usage to prevent excess calories.
