# Crispy Starch Sausage 🍢
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Year Round

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥜 Roasted Peanuts 100g
🌰 Cashew Nuts 15g
🫘 Perilla Seeds 20g
⬜ Roasted Sesame 40g (25g for grinding, 15g whole seeds added at the end)
🍪 Walnut Cookie 20g
🌿 Semi-Coarse Cumin Powder 20g
🌶️ Fine Chili Powder 10g (for spicy seasoning)
🌶️ Chili Flakes 30g (for oil-infused seasoning)
🌿 Cumin Powder 30g (for oil-infused seasoning)
⬜ Raw White Sesame 20g
🌭 Starch Sausages Several (100g each)
🧂 Spicy & Fresh Seasoning 1.5g
🧂 Nande Premium Seasoning 1g
🧂 MSG (Flavor Powder) 2g
🧂 Powdered Sugar 1g
🧂 Salt 2g
🫒 Hot Oil 100g (approx. 180°C)
🍶 Sweet Bean Paste 60g
🦪 Oyster Sauce 15g
🌶️ Limin Garlic Chili Sauce 5g
🍯 Haitian Seafood Sauce 20g
🍬 White Sugar 5g
🧂 Totole Fresh Flavor Powder 1g
💧 Hot Water 150g (approx. 80°C)
🫒 Cooking Oil As needed

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Commercial Sausage Roaster
⚖️ Kitchen Scale
🔪 Chef’s Knife
🥢 Bamboo Skewers (approx. 30 cm)
🥣 Glass Bowl
🥄 Silicone Brush
🥘 Metal Baking Tray
🍵 Household Food Processor (Blender)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Make Original Seasoning**: Place roasted peanuts, cashew nuts, perilla seeds, 25g roasted sesame, and walnut cookie into a food processor. Grind until broken. Pour out and add semi-coarse cumin powder, spicy & fresh seasoning, Nande seasoning, MSG, powdered sugar, and salt. Mix well. Finally, add 15g of whole roasted sesame seeds and combine evenly.
   **Note**: Adding whole sesame seeds at the end significantly enhances the texture.

2. **Make Spicy Seasoning**: Add 10g fine chili powder to the original seasoning base. Mix thoroughly.

3. **Prepare Special Sausage Sauce**:
   - Mix 30g chili flakes, 30g cumin powder, and 20g raw white sesame seeds. Pour 100g of hot oil at about 180°C over the mixture. Stir to infuse and release aroma. This is the oil-infused seasoning.
   - Combine sweet bean paste, oyster sauce, garlic chili sauce, seafood sauce, white sugar, and fresh flavor powder. Pour 150g of hot water at about 80°C into the mixture. Stir until completely dissolved. This is the secret sauce.
   - Pour the secret sauce into the oil-infused seasoning. Stir until well combined.
   **Key**: The secret sauce must be made with hot water for complete integration.

4. **Skewering**: Take a starch sausage without peeling off the casing. Insert a bamboo skewer straight through the bottom.
   **Note**: Skewering with the casing on prevents the sausage from splitting and makes it easier to prepare and store in advance.

5. **Preheat & Score**: Brush each roasting slot of the sausage roaster generously with cooking oil. Preheat to a constant 160°C. Remove the casing from the sausage. Score the sausage body in a spiral pattern.

6. **Roast**: Place the scored sausages into the roasting slots. Continuously rotate the skewers during frying to ensure even heating on all sides. Fry until the skin is golden and crispy and the scored cuts have fully opened.

7. **Seasoning**:
   - **Dry method**: Remove the crispy sausages from the roaster. While hot, roll them in a metal baking tray containing the dry seasoning to coat evenly.
   - **Sauce method**: Brush the sausages with the special sausage sauce.
   - **Combination method**: Brush with sauce first, then dip in dry seasoning.
   **Tip**: Use a generous amount of dry seasoning for a richer aroma and flavor.

## Health Advice
Crispy starch sausages are relatively high in calories. It is recommended to consume them occasionally and pair with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. They are suitable for sharing at gatherings. Individuals with high blood pressure should be mindful of the salt content in the sauce.
