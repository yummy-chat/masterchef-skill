# Fresh Rice Cake  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Fresh rice 500g  
🍚 Glutinous rice 150g  
🍬 White sugar 100g (ground into powdered sugar)  
🌸 Osmanthus sauce/strawberry sauce/blueberry sauce, as needed (for serving)  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl (for soaking)  
🧺 Bamboo basket/sieve (for draining)  
⚙️ High-speed blender/stone mill/grinder (for grinding wet rice flour)  
🔍 Metal sieve (for sifting rice flour)  
♨️ Steamer  
🧻 Dry cloth (wetted and laid on the steamer base)  
🍰 Scraper (for shaping and pre-cutting)  

## Instructions  
1. Soak the rice and glutinous rice separately in clean water overnight, allowing the grains to fully absorb water.  
2. The next day, rinse the soaked rice separately, then drain for 5-10 minutes until no more water drips from the bottom of the sieve.  
3. Place the drained rice and glutinous rice separately into a high-speed blender and grind into fine wet rice flour. Set aside separately.  
4. Grind the white sugar into a fine powder using a grinder.  
5. Pour the powdered sugar, wet rice flour, and wet glutinous rice flour into a large bowl, and mix evenly by hand.  
   **Key point**: The mixed rice flour should be such that "it holds together when squeezed in the hand but falls apart with a light touch."  
6. Sift the mixed rice flour through a metal sieve once to remove coarse particles, resulting in a finer texture.  
7. Lay a wetted and wrung-out cloth on the bottom of the steamer.  
8. Sift the rice flour again into the steamer through the sieve to let it settle naturally and fluffy.  
9. Gently level the surface of the rice flour with a scraper, **do not press down**.  
   **Note**: This recipe quantity is just enough to steam two layers.  
10. Lightly press cross-shaped dividing lines on the surface with the scraper to pre-divide the flour into four portions, making it easier to cut clean squares after steaming.  
11. Bring water in a pot to a boil, place the steamer on top, and steam over medium heat for 25 minutes.  
12. After taking off the steamer, gently press the rice cake with a fingertip; if it quickly springs back, it is fully cooked.  
13. Serve with osmanthus sauce, strawberry sauce, or blueberry sauce according to personal preference.  

## Health Advice  
Fresh rice cake is made from rice and glutinous rice, rich in carbohydrates, making it suitable for people needing a quick energy boost. However, diabetics should control their consumption. Pairing it with osmanthus sauce or fruit sauce can enhance flavor, but be mindful of additional sugar intake.
