# Electric Rice Cooker Ham and Cheese Pie
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Flour: Two bowls (about 500g, all-purpose or bread flour)
💧 Water: One and a half bowls (room temperature water)
🍞 Yeast: 3-4g (summer amount)
🧅 Onion: To taste (sliced)
🥩 Ham sausage: To taste (sliced)
🫑🌶 Green and red bell peppers: To taste (sliced)
🌽 Sweet corn kernels: To taste (cooked)
🫘 Sweet peas: To taste (cooked)
🧀 Shredded cheese: To taste
🧂 Salt: A pinch (for kneading dough, optional)
🍬 Sugar: A pinch (for kneading dough, optional)
🛢 Oil: A little (for kneading dough, optional)
🫗 Light soy sauce: To taste (for stir-frying filling)
🧂 Ground pepper: To taste (for stir-frying filling)
🍅 Ketchup: To taste (for stir-frying filling)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍚 Electric rice cooker (for kneading, proofing, and baking the pie)
🍳 Non-stick frying pan (for stir-frying the filling)
🥣 Bowl (for measuring flour and water)
🥢 Chopsticks (for stirring the batter)
🥄 Silicone spatula (for stirring, folding, and flipping)

## Preparation Steps
1. Pour flour, water, yeast, salt, sugar, and oil into the electric rice cooker inner pot. Stir with chopsticks until a thick, sticky batter forms with no dry flour.
   **Note**: Salt, sugar, and oil are only for flavor; they can be omitted. Operate directly in the rice cooker inner pot; no need to transfer.
2. Let the batter rest for a moment, then stir with a silicone spatula until smooth. Allow it to undergo initial fermentation at room temperature.
   **Key Point**: The batter becomes easier to stir to a smooth consistency after resting.
3. Prepare the filling: Heat oil in a pan. Stir-fry the sliced onion, ham slices, and bell pepper strips until fragrant. Add the cooked sweet corn kernels and sweet peas, and stir-fry. Then season with light soy sauce, ground pepper, and ketchup. Stir well, remove from heat, and let cool.
   **Note**: The filling must be cooked through and aromatic to enhance the final flavor.
4. Pour the cooled cooked filling into the fermented batter. Using a silicone spatula, fold to mix, ensuring the batter evenly coats the filling and covers the surface.
   **Key Point**: You can spread a layer of shredded cheese in the middle before covering with the remaining batter. Have the filling ready in advance to avoid omissions. After assembly, let it proof for another 20 minutes to make the pie fluffier.
5. No need to oil the rice cooker inner pot. Place the assembled ingredients inside and press the "Quick Cook" button.
6. After the first program cycle ends, open the lid. Carefully flip the pie using a silicone spatula, then press the "Quick Cook" button again to cook the other side.
   **Note**: Flip gently to ensure both sides heat evenly, develop color, and cook through.
7. Once the second program cycle is complete, remove the pie, cut into pieces, and enjoy.

## Health Advice
This dish offers a rich combination of vegetables and cheese, making it nutritious and well-balanced. It is suitable as a quick family breakfast or for lunch boxes. Be careful not to let the batter ferment for too long, as it may develop a sour taste. Since ham and cheese are high in sodium, those with high blood pressure can reduce the amounts used.
