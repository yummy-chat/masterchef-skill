# Bingzhou Pan-Fried Crabs  
Cuisine: Fujian Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn (September-October)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦀 3 female mud crabs (choose plump, firm ones)  
🥚 Eggs as needed (use yolks for sealing)  
🍠 Sweet potato starch as needed (for sealing, can substitute with flour)  
🫚 Old ginger as needed (shredded, to line the pan and remove fishy odor)  
🫒 Sesame oil as needed (for frying the crabs)  
🍶 Yellow wine as needed (to remove fishy odor)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Flat-bottomed pan  
✂️ Scissors (for handling crabs)  
🪥 Brush (for cleaning crab shells)  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥣 Small bowls (for starch and egg yolk mixture)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Use scissors to cut off the tips of the crab legs to reduce mobility, scrub clean, and cut in half.
2. Dip the cut side of the crab halves sequentially in dry starch, egg yolk, and then dry starch again to seal them.  
**Key Point**: Sealing with starch and egg yolk locks in the crab roe and juices, preventing loss of freshness during frying.

3. Heat the flat-bottomed pan, pour in sesame oil, add shredded ginger, and stir-fry over medium heat until the ginger turns slightly yellow and releases fragrance.
4. Place the crabs cut-side down neatly on the ginger shreds, pour yellow wine along the edge of the pan, and immediately cover with a lid.  
**Note**: The ginger shreds at the bottom prevent sticking and remove the fishy odor; pouring yellow wine along the pan edge enhances aroma.

5. Maintain medium heat and pan-fry with the lid on for about 10 minutes. No need to add water or salt, as the crabs will release their own juices.
6. When the crabs have turned completely red, the ginger shreds are caramelized, and the liquid in the pan has evaporated, remove and plate.

## Health Advice  
The crab roe and meat are wrapped in starch and egg yolk, locking in freshness, making this dish suitable for autumn nourishment. People with high cholesterol should consume in moderation, while pregnant women and those with weak health can safely enjoy it.
