# Chaoshan Beef Hotpot
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Three-flower tendon about 1 jin (front leg tendon, tender and springy)
🥩 Hanging dragon companion about 2.25 jin (back ridge meat, with fascia and fat, milky aroma)
🥩 Chest oil about 1.7 jin (chest fat, crispy and not greasy)
🦴 Beef bones appropriate amount (for broth)
🌽 Corn appropriate amount (cut into segments)
🥔 Water chestnuts appropriate amount (peeled)
🧅 Scallions appropriate amount (cut into segments)
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced)
🥜 Shacha sauce about 1 tablespoon
🥜 Sesame paste about 1 tablespoon
🍲 Hotpot base 1 small piece (optional)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
♨️ Pressure cooker
🍲 Soup pot
🔥 Induction cooker
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🥄 Strainer
🍽️ Bowls and plates

## Preparation Steps
1. **Select the beef**: Choose fresh three-flower tendon, hanging dragon companion, and chest oil by cut. The hanging dragon companion and hanging dragon heart can be sliced separately as requested.
2. **Prepare the beef bone broth**:
   - Place beef bones in cold water, bring to a boil and blanch thoroughly. Skim off the foam, then remove.
   - Put the blanched bones, scallion segments, and ginger slices into a pressure cooker. Add enough water, bring to pressure, then reduce to low heat and cook for 30 minutes.
   - Open the lid, add corn segments and peeled water chestnuts, then cover and cook for another 5 minutes until the broth turns milky white and aromatic with a sweet flavor.
3. **Make the dipping sauces**:
   - Basic dipping sauce: Mix 1 tablespoon of shacha sauce and 1 tablespoon of sesame paste in a bowl. Stir until well combined.
   - Spicy dipping sauce: Place a small piece of hotpot base, chopped scallions, cilantro, and chili flakes in a small bowl. Pour in boiling beef bone broth to dissolve.
4. **Slice the beef**:
   - Slice the three-flower tendon thinly against the grain. The knife must be sharp to ensure the tendons are cut but the meat remains intact.
   - The hanging dragon companion can be sliced slightly larger and thicker, preserving its pattern.
   - Slice the chest oil into thin, even pieces to ensure a crispy texture after cooking.
5. **Cook and enjoy**:
   - Pour the prepared broth into a hotpot pot and heat on an induction cooker until boiling.
   - Cook the beef slices: Pick up a slice of meat, dip it into the broth, and quickly stir for 8-10 seconds. Remove as soon as it changes color to highlight tenderness.
   - Cook the chest oil: Boil for about 2 minutes until the slices curl slightly and become crispy. Remove.
   - Dip in the sauces and enjoy. The broth can be consumed directly as soup.

## Health Advice
Chaoshan beef hotpot is high in protein and low in fat, making it suitable for those on a fitness or calorie-controlled diet. Chest oil has a higher calorie content and should be consumed in moderation. It is recommended to prioritize lean cuts of meat, paired with the sweet and refreshing corn and water chestnut broth to cut the richness.
