# Tender Chicken Steak
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍗 Chicken Breast 1200g (about 5 pieces)
🧂 Baking Soda 7g
🧅 Onion Powder 2g (can substitute with minced garlic and shredded onion)
🧄 Garlic Powder 2g (can substitute with minced garlic and shredded onion)
🌶️ White Pepper Powder about 0.5g
🧂 MSG 5g (can reduce according to preference)
🧂 Salt 3g (if without Orleans marinade, increase appropriately)
🍗 Orleans Marinade 20g
💧 Water 250g
🌽 Cornstarch 90g

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
📄 Kitchen Paper
🔪 Chef's Knife
🥣 Large Bowl
⚖️ Kitchen Scale
🍶 Measuring Cup/Pitcher
🥢 Chopsticks
🧤 Disposable Gloves
🫙 Plastic Wrap
🎒 Ziplock Bag
🍳 Non-stick Frying Pan
🥄 Spatula/Food Tongs

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the chicken breasts, pat dry with kitchen paper, remove excess fat and connective tissue, then slice each breast horizontally into 3–4 thin pieces.
2. In a large bowl, add baking soda, onion powder, garlic powder, white pepper powder, MSG, salt, and Orleans marinade. Stir to combine.
3. Add water and stir until completely dissolved, then add cornstarch and stir into a smooth, lump-free batter.
4. Place the chicken slices into the batter. Wearing gloves, toss and mix for about 1 minute, ensuring each slice is evenly coated.
5. Seal the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate in the fresh food compartment to marinate overnight.
6. After marinating, take out enough for one or two meals. Lay the remaining portions flat in separate ziplock bags, squeeze out the air, seal, and store in the freezer. Before cooking, transfer from the freezer to the refrigerator the night before to thaw slowly and naturally, avoiding quick thawing at room temperature.
7. Choose one of the following cooking methods:
   - **Pan-frying Method**: Spray a thin layer of oil in a non-stick pan, heat for about 2 minutes, then place the chicken slices in. Pan-fry over high heat for about 1 minute, flip, and fry for another 90 seconds or until both sides are golden brown.
   - **Steam-frying Method**: Place the chicken directly into a pan without oil. Once the bottom is set, flip, add a small amount of water, cover, and cook over high heat. When the water is almost gone, flip, add a splash of water again, cover, and cook until the water evaporates. This method yields a more chewy texture.

## Health Advice
This tender chicken steak uses low-fat chicken breast, tenderized with baking soda to control calories, making it a suitable protein source for weight loss. Marinating overnight thoroughly removes any gamey taste and locks in moisture. Pan-frying over high heat throughout quickly sets the exterior and seals in the juices.
