# Pistachio Salt Bread
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 Bread flour, as needed (for the dough)  
🥄 Pistachio paste, as needed (store-bought, with about 35% pistachio content)  
🧈 Butter, about 5g (for the filling inside each bread)  
🥛 Milk, as needed (to be added to the dough or crispy batter)  
🦠 Yeast, as needed (for both the dough and crispy batter)  
🍬 Sugar, as needed  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🟢 Green food coloring, a small amount (to tint the dough green)  
⚪️ White sesame seeds, as needed (for decorating the bottom)  
🍚 Rice flour, as needed (for the crispy batter; requires more water)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥖 Rolling pin  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥛 Measuring cup  
📜 Baking tray and parchment paper  
♨️ Oven (preheat in advance)  
🧴 Piping bag (for piping the crispy batter)  

## Instructions  
1. Mix the bread flour, milk, yeast, sugar, salt, and green food coloring together to form a green dough. Allow it to undergo its first fermentation for about 40 minutes.  
   **Key point**: A softer dough enhances the texture but makes shaping more difficult.  
2. In a separate container, prepare the crispy batter: Mix the rice flour, water, yeast, sugar, and salt until well combined. Let it rest until the surface becomes active with bubbles.  
   **Note**: Rice flour has strong water absorbency; you may add double the amount of water.  
3. Take out the fermented green dough, divide it into small portions, and roll each piece into a long, thin triangular sheet using a rolling pin.  
4. Spread a layer of pistachio paste over the wide end of the sheet, and place a piece of butter (about 5g) on top.  
5. Roll from the wide end toward the tip, tucking the seam underneath, and shape it into a croissant form.  
6. Dip the bottom of the dough in white sesame seeds, then place it on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.  
7. Decorate based on the shaping result: For well-shaped breads, sprinkle crushed pistachios on top; for less tidy ones, pipe a layer of the fermented crispy batter over them.  
8. Preheat the oven to the appropriate temperature, place the baking tray inside, and bake for 22 minutes.  
   **Key point**: If the bread browns too quickly during the second half of baking, cover the tray with a sheet of aluminum foil to prevent excessive coloration.  
9. Once out of the oven, serve immediately. The finished bread has a crispy exterior and a soft interior, rich with the aroma and savory flavor of pistachios.  

## Health Suggestion  
The combination of pistachio paste and butter filling provides healthy fats, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost. However, the total calorie content is relatively high, so it is recommended to enjoy this bread with black coffee to balance the sweetness. The green color of this bread comes from food coloring; if a natural alternative is preferred, matcha powder can be used as a substitute.
