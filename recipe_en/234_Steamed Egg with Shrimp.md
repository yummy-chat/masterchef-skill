# Steamed Egg with Shrimp
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 Eggs – as needed  
🦐 Shrimp – as needed  
💧 Warm water (about 1.5 times the volume of the egg mixture)  
🧅 Chopped green onion (for garnish, optional)  
🧂 Salt – a pinch  
🍶 Cooking wine – a dash  
🫗 Sesame oil – a drizzle (for finishing)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Steamer or steaming pot  
🥛 Measuring cup  
🥢 Strainer  
🥣 Steaming bowl  
🍽️ Plate or plastic wrap  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Crack the eggs into a measuring cup and whisk thoroughly with chopsticks until the mixture is even.  
2. Add warm water and continue to stir. Then season with a pinch of salt and a dash of cooking wine.  
3. Strain the egg mixture through a strainer into a steaming bowl to remove foam and lumps.  
   **Key point**: Straining ensures a smooth surface and a tender, fine texture.  
4. Cover the steaming bowl with a plate or plastic wrap, then place it in a preheated steamer with steaming vapor.  
   **Note**: Covering prevents water droplets from falling onto the egg custard, which could cause holes.  
5. Steam over high heat for about 8–10 minutes. When the egg mixture is mostly set, gently lift the lid and arrange the shrimp evenly on top of the custard.  
6. Continue steaming until the egg custard is fully set and the shrimp turn red and are cooked through. Remove from heat.  
   **Key point**: Adding the shrimp later keeps them tender and prevents overcooking.  
7. Remove the bowl, drizzle with sesame oil, and sprinkle with chopped green onion for garnish. Serve immediately.  

## Health Tips  
Steamed egg with shrimp is a high-quality protein combination, suitable for the elderly and children to supplement nutrition. Using strained egg mixture and warm water helps achieve a smoother and more tender texture. Covering with plastic wrap during steaming prevents condensation from ruining the surface.
