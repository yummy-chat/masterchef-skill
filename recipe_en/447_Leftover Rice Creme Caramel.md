# Leftover Rice Creme Caramel
Cuisine: Others  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Leftover rice 1 large bowl  
🥛 Whole milk as needed  
🍦 Whipped cream as needed (aerosol can)  
🍬 White sugar as needed  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥤 Blender  
🫗 Rice cooker inner pot  
🥣 Small bowls  
❄️ Refrigerator  
🥄 Metal spoon  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Instructions  
1. Pour the leftover rice and whole milk into the blender together.  
   **Note**: The rice should be refrigerated or at room temperature; avoid using hot rice to prevent affecting the blending process.  
2. Start the blender's "thick soup" program and blend until the rice paste is smooth and fine, with no visible grains.  
3. Pour the rice paste into a large container such as the rice cooker inner pot, squeeze in an appropriate amount of whipped cream, and stir well.  
4. Portion some of the rice paste into small bowls as the pudding base, then place in the refrigerator to freeze until semi-solid.  
   **Key point**: The semi-solid state allows the caramel to quickly set into a crisp shell when poured on top.  
5. Make the caramel: Take a metal spoon, add about one spoonful of white sugar, and heat over medium-low heat on the gas stove. Continuously shake the spoon to melt the sugar evenly.  
   **Note**: This step carries a risk of burns, so be extremely careful; heat until the sugar is completely melted into a light amber color and begins to smoke.  
6. Remove the semi-solid pudding, and quickly and evenly pour the hot caramel over the surface to form a crisp caramel shell.  
7. It can be served immediately, or sprinkled with a little powdered sugar for decoration.  

## Health Advice  
This leftover rice creme caramel cleverly repurposes carbohydrates for a delicious dessert, making it ideal for those who want to use up leftover rice and satisfy a sweet craving. However, the sugar content in the caramel is not low, so it is recommended to control portion size. Those with blood sugar sensitivity should consume with caution.
