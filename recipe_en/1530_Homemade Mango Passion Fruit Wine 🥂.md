# Homemade Mango Passion Fruit Wine 🥂
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥭 Mango, about 10g (cut into small cubes)  
💛 Passion fruit, 1 piece (scoop out the pulp)  
🍬 Yellow rock sugar, about 8g  
🍶 Pure grain rice-flavored 38% soaking wine (such as Jiujiang Shuangzheng), about 45ml  

## Tools & Equipment  
🫙 Glass sealing jar (with clamp)  
🥄 Spoon  
🔪 Knife  

## Steps
1. Cut the mango into small cubes, cut the passion fruit in half, and scoop out the pulp.
2. Place the mango cubes and passion fruit pulp into a clean, oil-free, and water-free glass sealing jar.
3. Add about 8g of yellow rock sugar.
4. Slowly pour in the soaking wine until the liquid completely covers all ingredients and reaches near the rim of the jar.  
   **Key point**: Use pure grain rice-flavored 38% soaking wine as the base; avoid using high-proof wine or wine with an incompatible flavor profile.
5. Seal the jar and gently shake it to initially distribute the yellow rock sugar evenly.
6. Store in a cool place or refrigerate, let it sit for 2-3 days. Once the yellow rock sugar has completely dissolved, the wine is ready to open and drink.  
   **Note**: No need for long-term soaking; it is ready to drink after 2-3 days. During this period, you can add ice cubes, jasmine tea, or other ingredients for customization.

## Health Advice  
Mango and passion fruit are rich in vitamin C. Paired with a small amount of yellow rock sugar, this homemade fruit wine is better for sugar control than store-bought beverages. It is suitable for those who want a small drink but are wary of alcohol. However, do not overindulge. Remember to have some nuts afterward to balance blood sugar levels.
