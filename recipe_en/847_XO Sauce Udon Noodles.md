# XO Sauce Udon Noodles
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍜 Udon noodles (packaged, as needed)  
🥄 XO sauce (adjust to taste, can add more if desired)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Wok (for boiling water to cook noodles)  
🥢 Strainer (or soup ladle)  
🥢 Chopsticks (for mixing noodles)  
🍽️ Serving bowl  

## Preparation Steps
1. Bring water to a boil in a wok, add the udon noodles, and cook until the noodles loosen, following the time specified on the package instructions.  
   **Note**: After cooking, remove the noodles with a strainer and drain thoroughly to avoid residual broth affecting the sauce's adherence.
2. Place the drained udon noodles in a bowl and add an appropriate amount of XO sauce.
3. Mix thoroughly with chopsticks until each strand of noodle is evenly coated with the sauce, then serve.

## Health Advice  
This dish is enhanced by the savory XO sauce, and the udon noodles provide a strong sense of satiety, making it ideal for a quick energy boost during busy times. However, the sauce has a relatively high salt content, so individuals with hypertension should consider reducing the amount used.
