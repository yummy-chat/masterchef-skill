# 0 Oil 0 Sugar Steamed Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 3 eggs (medium-sized, separated into yolks and whites)
🥛 About 80g unsweetened yogurt (or formula milk, coconut milk, fruit/vegetable juice, or about 60g water)
🌾 About 70g cake flour (or other flour)
🌽 About 3-5g cornstarch
🍋 Lemon juice, as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 Mixing bowl
⚡ Electric hand mixer
🪄 Flour sifter
🥄 Spatula
🪣 Stainless steel bowl (about 14cm in diameter, used as a mold)
♨️ Steamer
🫙 Plastic wrap

## Instructions
1. Separate the egg yolks from the egg whites, ensuring no yolk gets into the whites. Then place the egg whites in the freezer for a short while.
   **Key point**: Cold egg whites are easier to whip, and separation must be done without any yolk residue.
2. Add the unsweetened yogurt to the egg yolks, sift in the cake flour, and stir until thick. The mixture should slowly lose surface lines when dropped, forming the egg yolk batter.
3. Add enough water to the steamer and bring it to a boil in advance.
4. Take the egg whites out of the freezer. Beat them with an electric mixer on the highest speed until distinct lines appear. Add lemon juice and cornstarch, then switch to low speed to refine the meringue until the whisk attachment forms short, upright stiff peaks when lifted.
   **Key point**: Beating on low speed at the end makes the meringue finer and more stable.
5. Take about two spoonfuls of the meringue and add it to the egg yolk batter. Quickly mix in a straight-line motion using a whisk. Then pour the mixture back into the remaining meringue and gently fold it together quickly and lightly with a spatula. The final batter should be thick and able to hold its shape when piled.
   **Note**: Fold gently and quickly to avoid deflating the batter.
6. Lightly grease the bottom of the stainless steel bowl with a thin layer of cooking oil. Pour in the batter, gently tap the bowl twice to release large air bubbles, and seal the top tightly with plastic wrap.
   **Note**: Do not fill the bowl too full; leave room for the cake to expand. Sealing with plastic wrap prevents steam from dripping onto the cake.
7. Place the mold into the preheated steamer. Steam over medium heat for 45-50 minutes.
   **Note**: Ensure there is enough water in the steamer; do not add water during the process.
8. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 1-3 minutes. First, open the lid a crack to release the hot steam, then fully uncover and remove the mold.
9. After unmolding, gently tap the cake on the counter twice to release steam. Invert it onto a cooling rack and let it cool completely. Gently press around the edges with your hand to remove the cake from the mold.

## Health Advice
This 0 oil, 0 sugar steamed cake uses yogurt for moisture and flavor, with no added sugar. It is suitable for people managing sugar intake or looking for a satisfying treat during weight loss. Properly whipped egg whites and slow steaming over low heat result in a soft, fluffy texture that is not dry. It tastes even better after being chilled.
