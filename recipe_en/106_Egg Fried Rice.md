# Egg Fried Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Cold rice  
🥚 Eggs  
🍖 Ham  
🥒 Cucumber (optional)  
🥕 Carrot (optional)  
🥩 Cooked meat (optional)  
🫒 Oil  
🧂 Salt  
🌶️ Pepper  
🧂 Light soy sauce  
🌿 Scallions  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment  
🥄 Spatula  
🥣 Large bowl  
🍳 Wok  

## Instructions  
1. Use a spatula to pre-loosen the cold rice into small pieces; dice the ham, carrot, and cucumber as needed; set aside the cooked meat.  
2. Separate the egg whites and yolks into two large bowls, whisk each until smooth, without adding salt.  
3. Heat the wok over high heat until smoking, add oil, pour in the egg whites, and once the egg whites are mostly set, remove and set aside.  
4. If the oil in the wok is insufficient, add more and reheat over high heat; add the egg yolks, and once the yolks are mostly set, reduce to medium-low heat.  
5. Add the ham, cooked meat, carrot, cucumber, and other mix-ins, stir-fry quickly for about 10 seconds until fragrant.  
6. Return the egg whites to the wok, stir-fry for 5 seconds, then promptly add the rice; turn the heat to high and stir-fry while breaking up any clumps of rice, continuing until the rice and mix-ins are evenly combined.  
7. Reduce the heat to low, add salt, pepper, and light soy sauce, stir-fry evenly until the rice grains appear to "bounce" in the wok.  
8. Add the scallions, stir-fry for 10 seconds, turn off the heat, and serve in a bowl.  

## Health Tips  
Egg fried rice offers a balanced mix of protein and carbohydrates, making it a suitable quick meal. Be mindful of the amount of ham, oil, and salt used; individuals with high blood pressure may reduce the light soy sauce.
