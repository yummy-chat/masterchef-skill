# Braised Eggplant
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥬 Green eggplant  
🧄 Garlic  
🧅 Scallion  
🌶️ Green chili pepper  
🧅 Onion  
🍅 Tomato  
🥚 Egg  
🌾 Flour  
🌾 Cornstarch  
🧂 Salt  
🫗 Soy sauce  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍳 Wok  
🔪 Knife  
🪵 Cutting board  

## Steps
1. Wash the green eggplant, green chili pepper, tomato, onion, and scallion with clean water.
2. Cut the scallion into 5 mm wide rings; peel and crush the garlic; cut the tomato into pieces about 6 cm on each side; cut the green chili pepper and onion into pieces weighing about 5 grams each.
3. First cut the eggplant into 2 cm thick slices, then into 2 cm wide strips, and finally cut diagonally into diamond-shaped pieces.
4. Pour flour into a bowl, add water a little at a time, and stir into a thick, sticky batter.
5. Add cornstarch and 30 g of water, and continue stirring until evenly combined.
6. Crack the egg into the batter, add an appropriate amount of salt, and stir thoroughly until well mixed.
7. Add the cut eggplant pieces into the batter and toss until each piece is evenly coated.
8. Heat the wok over high heat, pour in 500 ml of cooking oil. When the oil surface starts to emit a faint wisp of smoke, reduce to low heat. Use chopsticks to add the eggplant pieces one by one into the oil. After all pieces are in the wok, turn to medium heat and fry until golden brown. Remove and drain the oil from the wok.
9. Leave a small amount of oil in the wok. Add the garlic and scallion and stir-fry for 15 seconds. Then add the green chili pepper pieces and stir-fry for 30 seconds. Next, add the tomato pieces and stir-fry for another 30 seconds.
10. Add the fried eggplant pieces, then pour in water until it reaches 0.8 times the height of the ingredients in the wok.
11. Season with soy sauce and an appropriate amount of salt.
12. Cook without covering the wok until the sauce thickens and the liquid level drops to 0.2–0.3 times the height of the ingredients. Turn off the heat and serve.

## Key Notes
- Steps 4 through 6 must be performed in order, otherwise the batter may fail.
- When frying the eggplant, first lower them into the oil over low heat, then turn to medium heat to ensure a crispy exterior and fully cooked interior.
- During simmering, closely monitor the sauce to prevent it from reducing too much and becoming too thick.

## Health Advice
Braised eggplant is relatively high in oil. It is recommended to pair it with a light cold dish or whole-grain staple to balance calories and dietary fiber. Suitable for those who enjoy a soft and tender texture, but those with high blood lipids or during a weight loss period should control their intake.
