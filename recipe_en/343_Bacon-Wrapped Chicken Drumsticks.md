# Bacon-Wrapped Chicken Drumsticks
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍗 Chicken drumsticks about 3 lbs (12 pieces, use drumettes)
🥓 Bacon 1 pack plus 1 slice (for wrapping chicken)
🧅 Onion appropriate amount (sliced, for stir-frying the secret seasoning)
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced, for stir-frying the secret seasoning)
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (sliced, for stir-frying the secret seasoning)
🧂 Salt appropriate amount (for stir-frying and marinating)
🌶️ Dried Sichuan peppercorns 15 g (toasted and crushed)
🧂 Salt 3 g (for marinating)
🧂 Chicken bouillon 5 g
🧂 Ground white pepper 3 g
🧂 Thirteen-spice powder 2 g
🦪 Oyster sauce 20 g
🧂 Light soy sauce 15 g
🧂 Homemade soul seasoning powder 12 g (made from toasted aromatics and Sichuan peppercorns, ground)
🌶️ Chili flakes 12 g
🍶 High-proof baijiu (Chinese liquor) appropriate amount (for removing gamey smell and enhancing aroma)
🧂 Barbecue seasoning appropriate amount (store-bought complex seasoning, sprinkled before the second bake)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Wok (for stir-frying the secret seasoning)
🥄 Strainer/skimmer (for filtering out salt)
🫗 Mortar and pestle (for grinding aromatics and Sichuan peppercorns)
🥣 Mixing bowl/container (for marinating chicken)
🥘 Baking tray (for holding drumsticks during baking)
🔥 Oven (for baking drumsticks)
🖌️ Silicone brush (for brushing oil and juices)
🔪 Knife, cutting board (for scoring drumsticks and slicing ingredients)
🪥 Toothpicks (for securing bacon)
📦 Aluminum foil (for wrapping the exposed bone ends to prevent burning)

## Steps
1. **Thaw chicken drumsticks**: Place frozen drumsticks in a bowl and cover with water. Soak for 2 hours to thaw completely, allowing blood to fully leach out.  
   **Key point**: Fully thaw and remove all blood to ensure tender meat.

2. **Make the "soul seasoning"**: Heat salt in a wok. Add sliced onion, ginger, and garlic. Stir-fry over medium-high heat until the aromatics change color and moisture is evaporated. Remove the aromatics; discard the salt. In the same wok (no oil), add dried Sichuan peppercorns and toast over low heat for about 2 minutes until fragrant. Place the dried aromatics and toasted peppercorns into a mortar and pestle. Pound into a coarse powder. Set aside.  
   **Key point**: Aromatics must be dried out; Sichuan peppercorns must be toasted until fragrant; grind just enough to release aroma.

3. **Prepare chicken drumsticks**: After thawing, make a cut along the inner side of each drumstick lengthwise, without cutting through the bottom. Use your hands to push the meat downward, exposing the bone and forming a compact ball of meat at the top, creating a "lollipop" shape.  
   **Key point**: Proper scoring and flipping is the key to shaping; ensure uniform and neat appearance.

4. **Marinate drumsticks**: Place the shaped drumsticks into a bowl. Add 3 g salt, 5 g chicken bouillon, 3 g white pepper, 2 g thirteen-spice powder, 20 g oyster sauce, 15 g light soy sauce, 12 g homemade soul seasoning powder, 12 g chili flakes, and an appropriate amount of high-proof baijiu. Mix thoroughly by hand until well combined. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.  
   **Key point**: Mix thoroughly to ensure the drumsticks are fully seasoned; refrigerating improves flavor.

5. **Wrap with bacon and secure**: Remove the marinated drumsticks from the refrigerator. Use one slice of bacon to wrap around the meat portion of each drumstick. Secure the bacon with a toothpick. Wrap the exposed bone end with a small piece of aluminum foil to prevent burning.  
   **Key point**: Use toothpicks to prevent the bacon from unraveling; foil protects the bone end.

6. **Preheat the oven**: Preheat the oven to 250°C (482°F) using both top and bottom heating elements. Maintain for 10 minutes.

7. **First bake**: Arrange the wrapped drumsticks neatly on a baking tray. Place the tray on the middle rack of the preheated oven. Bake at 250°C (482°F) with both top and bottom heating elements for 30 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Ensure the oven is fully preheated for optimal baking results.

8. **Brush with juices and sprinkle seasoning**: Remove the baking tray from the oven. At this point, the drumsticks should be mostly cooked, with the bacon browned and fat rendered. Use a silicone brush to dip into the rendered oil and juices at the bottom of the tray, and brush evenly over the surface of each drumstick. Then sprinkle a layer of barbecue seasoning over the drumsticks.  
   **Key point**: Brushing with juices gives the finished dish a glossy sheen; sprinkling seasoning adds layers of flavor.

9. **Second bake**: Return the seasoned drumsticks to the oven. Continue baking at 250°C (482°F) with both top and bottom heating elements for another 5 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Do not bake for too long; the main purpose is to release the aroma of the barbecue seasoning and help it adhere.

10. **Serve**: Once baking is complete, remove the drumsticks from the oven and transfer to a serving plate.

## Health Advice
Wrapping chicken drumsticks in bacon significantly increases the calorie and sodium content. It is recommended to serve with a large vegetable salad and control the portion size (1–2 drumsticks per serving). This dish is rich in protein, making it suitable for those in a muscle-building phase or with high physical energy expenditure. However, individuals with high blood pressure should consume it sparingly.
