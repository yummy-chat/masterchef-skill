# Pique Volcano Mashed Potatoes
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥔 Potatoes – appropriate amount (peeled and sliced)  
🧄 Garlic – appropriate amount (crushed and minced)  
🫑 Green bell pepper – appropriate amount (finely diced)  
🌶️ Red bell pepper – appropriate amount (finely diced)  
🥩 Ground pork – appropriate amount  
🫒 Cooking oil – appropriate amount  
🫘 Broad bean paste – two tablespoons  
🦪 Oyster sauce – one tablespoon  
🧂 Light soy sauce – one drizzle  
🍬 Sugar – a small pinch  
🧂 Salt – a pinch  
⚫ Black pepper – a pinch  
💧 Water – a small cup  

## Cookware and Equipment  
♨️ Steamer  
🍳 Non-stick pan or wok  
🔪 Cutting board and chef’s knife  
🥄 Spoon  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly thick slices. Place them in a bowl.  
2. Put the bowl into the steamer and steam over high heat for 20 minutes, until the potato slices are fully tender and soft.  
3. Remove the steamed potato slices, add a pinch of salt and black pepper, and gently mash them into a puree with a spoon.  
   **Key point**: Mash just enough to form a puree; avoid over-mixing.  
4. Transfer the mashed potatoes to another bowl. Using a spoon or chopsticks, shape them into a volcano-like mound with a raised center.  
5. Heat oil in a pan over low heat. Add two tablespoons of broad bean paste and stir-fry until red oil is released.  
6. Add the minced garlic and continue stir-frying over low heat until fragrant.  
7. Add the ground pork and stir-fry until the color turns white.  
8. Add the diced green and red bell peppers and stir-fry evenly.  
9. Season with oyster sauce, light soy sauce, and sugar, stirring well.  
10. Pour in the water, stir to combine, then turn to medium heat and cook for about 1 minute, until the sauce is slightly bubbling and has thickened a little.  
11. Turn off the heat. Pour the meat sauce evenly over the mashed potato volcano while it is still hot.  
    **Key point**: The sauce should cover the volcano shape to create a lava-like effect.  

## Health Tips  
Mashed potatoes provide high-quality carbohydrates, while the ground pork and bell peppers add protein and vitamins. This dish is suitable for moderate consumption during fitness or weight loss periods. Pay attention to controlling the amount of broad bean paste and oil to avoid excessive sodium and calorie intake.
