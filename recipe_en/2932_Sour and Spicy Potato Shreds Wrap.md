# Sour and Spicy Potato Shreds Wrap
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥔 Potato: About 2 (medium-sized, can be hand-cut or shredded)  
🫑 Green Pepper: 1 (slightly spicier variety preferred, seeded and julienned)  
🧄 Garlic: 4 cloves (smash and mince)  
🫓 Ready-made Wraps: As needed (for wrapping)  
🌶️ Dried Chilies: As needed (to be fried together with minced garlic)  
🫒 Cooking Oil: As needed (use more if potatoes are not rinsed)  
🍶 Light Soy Sauce: 1 tbsp  
🍬 Sugar: 1 pinch (enough to hold between four fingers)  
🧂 Chicken Bouillon: 1 pinch  
🧂 Salt: As needed  
🍶 Vinegar: 2 tbsp (poured along the edge of the wok)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Chef's Knife  
🪵 Bamboo Cutting Board  
🔥 Gas Stove / Induction Cooktop  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spatula / Turner  
🧤 Julienne Peeler (optional)  
🍴 Fork (to secure the potato while shredding)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Peel the potato, first slice into thin pieces, then stack neatly and cut into thin shreds; or use a julienne peeler to shred, using a fork to secure the potato piece to prevent injury. Cut the green pepper in half, remove the seeds and inner ribs, flatten, and cut into thin shreds. Smash the garlic cloves and mince.  
2. Pour cooking oil into the wok, heat over medium heat, add the minced garlic and dried chilies, and stir-fry until fragrant.  
3. Keeping the heat on medium, add the potato shreds and stir-fry quickly. As soon as they begin to soften slightly, immediately reduce the heat to low.  
   **Key**: Potato shreds cook very quickly and must not be stir-fried for too long; season only after reducing to low heat to avoid burning.  
4. Over low heat, add light soy sauce, sugar, chicken bouillon, and salt. Stir well to ensure the potato shreds are evenly coated with the seasonings.  
5. Add the green pepper shreds and stir-fry until just cooked but still crisp.  
6. Pour the vinegar along the edge of the wok, avoiding direct contact with the potato shreds. Use the high heat of the wok edge to release the vinegar's fragrance, making the sourness milder.  
7. Stir to combine, then turn off the heat and transfer to a plate. Take a ready-made wrap, place the cooked sour and spicy potato shreds on it, optionally add chili oil to taste, roll it up tightly, and serve.  

## Health Advice  
Potato shreds are rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber, but excessive oil during stir-frying significantly increases calorie content. It is recommended to control the amount of oil used, and using a non-stick pan is preferable. Suitable for busy individuals needing quick meal preparation, but those requiring strict salt control should consume with caution.
