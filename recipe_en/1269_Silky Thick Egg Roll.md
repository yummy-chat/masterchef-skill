# Silky Thick Egg Roll
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 3 Eggs
🍖 Meat Floss, as needed
🌿 1 Mint Leaf
🧂 Seasoned Salt, as needed
🌶 Black Pepper, as needed
🥗 Mayonnaise, as needed
🍅 Ketchup, as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 Glass Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🍲 Glass Pot
🔥 Gas Stove
🎋 Bamboo Mat
🍽 Flat Plate
🥄 Skimmer
🥄 Spoon

## Instructions
1.  Crack 3 eggs into a glass bowl. Add seasoned salt and black pepper to taste. Whisk thoroughly with chopsticks until the egg mixture is evenly blended.
   **Note**: The egg mixture must be whisked thoroughly to ensure the seasonings are fully dissolved, resulting in a consistent texture for the final egg roll.
2.  Add enough water to a glass pot and bring to a full boil over high heat.
3.  Slowly pour the egg mixture into the boiling water. The egg mixture will quickly solidify into fluffy egg ribbons upon contact with the hot water. Gently stir with chopsticks to prevent sticking.
4.  Once the egg ribbons are fully set and no liquid egg remains, remove them all with a skimmer and drain off excess water.
5.  Spread the drained egg ribbons evenly on a bamboo mat. Starting from one end, roll them into a tight cylindrical shape. Press firmly with your hands to set the shape, then cut into evenly thick slices.
   **Key Point**: When rolling, ensure it is rolled tightly. Press to set the shape before cutting to keep the slices neat and intact.
6.  Arrange the thick egg roll slices in a circular pattern on a flat plate. Drizzle mayonnaise and ketchup over the top, sprinkle with meat floss as desired, and finally garnish with a mint leaf.

## Health Advice
This thick egg roll uses egg ribbons instead of the traditional pan-fried method, resulting in lower fat content and a more tender texture. It is suitable for people on a weight-loss diet or those with weaker digestive functions. Mayonnaise and ketchup are relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to use them sparingly as a garnish.
