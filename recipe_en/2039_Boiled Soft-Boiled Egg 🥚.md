# Boiled Soft-Boiled Egg 🥚
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Eggs: About 10 (regular cooking eggs)
🧂 No seasonings

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔥 Gas stove
🍲 Deep pot
🫖 Pot lid
🥘 Glass dish (for holding cooked eggs)
🔪 Knife (for cutting to check doneness)

## Steps
1. Place the eggs in a deep pot, add cold water, ensuring the water completely covers all eggs.
   **Note**: Starting with cold water prevents the eggs from cracking due to sudden temperature changes and ensures even heating.
2. Turn the heat to high and bring the water to a boil.
3. Once the water is boiling, immediately reduce the heat to low, cover with a lid, and let the eggs simmer.
   **Key**: The simmering time is crucial for controlling the yolk's doneness; too short will result in uncooked egg whites, too long will fully cook the yolk.
4. Simmer until the egg whites are fully set, then remove from heat when the yolk is runny when cut.
5. Transfer to a glass dish or briefly cool in cold water before serving. Season with preferred condiments as desired.

## Health Tips
It is recommended to serve with vegetables or whole wheat bread for a more balanced nutrition. Suitable for those needing high-quality protein and healthy fats, such as fitness enthusiasts. Ensure eggs are fresh, and note that soft-boiled eggs are not suitable for pregnant women, the elderly, or those with weakened immune systems due to the risk of salmonella.
