# Zhou Hei Ya Braised Beef in Spiced Marinade
Cuisine: Hubei Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef shank, as needed  
🍖 Beef tendon, as needed  
🌶️ Dried chilies, about 40g  
🌸 Red Sichuan peppercorns, about 30g  
🟢 Green Sichuan peppercorns, about 30g  
🧅 Onion, about 100g  
🫒 Scallion, about 50g  
🫚 Ginger, about 30g  
🧄 Garlic, about 50g  
🍬 Rock sugar, about 80g  
🫘 Broad bean paste, 50g  
🥡 Light soy sauce, 100g  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 40g  
🍶 Yellow wine, 150g  
🫗 Sweet bean sauce, 50g  
🦪 Oyster sauce, 30g  
🌿 Thirteen-spice powder, 10g  
⚪ White pepper powder, 5g  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (liberal amount)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Cast iron pot or deep stew pot  
🥄 Measuring cup  
👐 Tongs  
🧤 Heat-resistant gloves  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
📦 Airtight container  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Soak dried chilies, red Sichuan peppercorns, and green Sichuan peppercorns in water for 10 minutes, then drain. Cut the onion into chunks, the scallion into sections, slice the ginger, and smash the garlic cloves.  
2. In a measuring cup, sequentially pour in light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, yellow wine, sweet bean sauce, oyster sauce, rock sugar, thirteen-spice powder, and white pepper powder. Stir to combine into a mixed sauce.  
3. Pour enough cooking oil into the pot and heat to 180°C. Add the drained spices and vegetables. Stir-fry over medium-low heat until the vegetables turn slightly yellow and the aroma is released.  
**Note**: Do not stir-fry for too long to avoid the peppercorns becoming bitter.  

4. Add enough water to the pot so that it can fully submerge the beef and beef tendon when added later. Bring to a boil over high heat.  
5. Add the broad bean paste and the mixed sauce, stir to combine, and continue boiling over high heat. The marinade should now be a deep black color.  
6. Add the cleaned beef shank and beef tendon to the boiling marinade, ensuring they are completely submerged. Bring to a boil again, then reduce to low heat, cover with a lid, and braise for 2 hours.  
**Key point**: After 2 hours, test the beef tendon with chopsticks. If it is not tender enough, extend the braising time by 1 hour or until it reaches the desired texture.  

7. Do not remove the meat immediately after turning off the heat. Allow it to cool naturally in the marinade, then place the entire pot in the refrigerator and soak overnight to allow the flavors to penetrate thoroughly.  
8. Remove the soaked beef and beef tendon. Slice the beef and cut the beef tendon into pieces for serving. The remaining marinade can be strained and frozen for later use to braise duck feet, lotus root slices, etc.  

## Health Advice  
Braised beef is high in protein and low in fat, making it suitable for fitness muscle building or weight loss diets. However, the sauce and rock sugar content are relatively high, so individuals with high blood pressure should control their intake. Soaking overnight allows the flavors to deepen further.
