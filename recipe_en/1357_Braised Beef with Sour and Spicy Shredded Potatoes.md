## Cooking Steps
1. Slice the beef into thin pieces, shred the potato into thin strips, shred or slice the green and red peppers, and mince the garlic. Set aside.
2. Marinate the beef: In a bowl, combine the beef slices with light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper, and cornstarch. Mix well. Finally, drizzle in cooking oil and mix again. Let it marinate for 10 minutes.
   **Key Point**: The oil seals in the beef's moisture, ensuring a tender texture.
3. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, mix 3 tablespoons of light soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of aged vinegar, 1 tablespoon of sugar, and half a bowl of water. Stir until well combined.
4. In the pan, first spread a layer of shredded potato evenly, then add the shredded green and red peppers on top. Finally, place the marinated beef slices in a single layer on top.
   **Key Point**: Layering the ingredients ensures even cooking and full absorption of the sauce.
5. Evenly pour the prepared sauce over the ingredients. The sauce will naturally seep downward to coat everything.
6. Cover the pan with the lid and cook over the lowest heat for 10 minutes. It's done when steam continuously escapes from the edge of the lid and the sauce is gently bubbling.
   **Doneness Check**: The beef has changed color and is cooked through, the shredded potatoes are tender yet still retain some texture, and the sauce has thickened.
7. Turn off the heat, remove the lid, and sprinkle the minced garlic on top. Let the residual heat release the garlic aroma. Serve immediately.
