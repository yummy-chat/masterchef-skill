# Internet-famous Lava Omurice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧅 1/2 onion (finely minced)
🥕 1 carrot (finely minced)
🍗 Boneless, skinless chicken thigh (cut into 1 cm cubes)
🍚 Cooked rice (preferably day-old)
🥚 3-4 eggs (approx. 60 g each)
🧂 Salt to taste
🧂 Black pepper to taste
🍛 Curry sauce, about 3 tablespoons
🥢 A splash of soy sauce (for adjusting curry sauce)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed
🧈 Butter as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board
🥣 Bowls (for beating eggs and shaping)
🥢 Strainer/sieve
🍱 Plastic wrap
🥄 Spatula
🍽️ Serving plate

## Cooking Instructions
1. Remove bones and skin from chicken thigh, cut into approx. 1 cm cubes; finely mince onion and carrot.
2. Beat eggs, pass through a fine-mesh sieve to remove membranes, season with salt, and set aside as egg mixture.
3. Heat oil in a frying pan, add chicken cubes, season with salt and black pepper, stir-fry until lightly golden, then remove and set aside.
4. Leave residual oil in the pan, sauté minced onion and carrot until softened, add day-old rice, and stir-fry until well separated and heated through.
5. Add the cooked chicken cubes, pour in curry sauce mixed with a splash of soy sauce, and stir-fry until the rice is evenly coated, dry, and fragrant.
6. Transfer the fried rice into a small bowl, press firmly, invert onto a serving plate, wrap with plastic wrap, and manually shape into an oval rice ball.
7. In another frying pan, melt butter over high heat, reduce to medium heat, pour in the egg mixture, and quickly stir with chopsticks to ensure even heating.
8. When the bottom is set but the surface is still runny, stop stirring, reduce to medium-low heat, and roll up about one-third of the omelet from one side.
9. Gently tilt the pan to let the uncooked egg flow toward the edge, use chopsticks to shape the omelet into an oval, set, then flip briefly and cook the other side.
10. Slide the omelet onto the rice ball, make a lengthwise cut down the center with a knife to let the runny egg naturally cover the rice, pour warm curry sauce on top, and sprinkle with chopped scallions to serve.

## Health Tips
This lava omurice uses chicken and vegetables to enhance satiety, but curry sauce and butter are relatively high in calories. It is recommended to pair with a light salad or green tea for balance. Suitable for those needing a quick energy boost, but portion control of rice is advised during weight loss periods.
