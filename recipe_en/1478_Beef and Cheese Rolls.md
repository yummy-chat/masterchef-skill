# Beef and Cheese Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 High-gluten flour 450g  
🐄 Beef 1000g (thinly sliced)  
🧀 Shredded cheese about 450g (for filling)  
🫑 Green bell pepper appropriate amount (julienned)  
🌶️ Red bell pepper appropriate amount (julienned)  
🧅 Onion appropriate amount (julienned)  
🫘 White sesame seeds appropriate amount (for garnish)  
🧂 Salt 5g (for dough) + 10g (for marinating meat)  
🍬 Sugar 5g (for dough) + 15g (for marinating meat)  
💧 Warm water 250g (60–70°C)  
🫒 Cooking oil 20g (for dough) + appropriate amount (for brushing surface)  
🍶 Cooking wine 15g  
🥄 Light soy sauce 30g  
🌑 Black pepper powder 8g  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Large glass bowl  
🪓 Rolling pin  
🔲 Cutting board  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Silicone spatula  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
🪣 Baking tray  
🔥 Oven (convection/conventional)  
🦷 Toothpicks  

## Instructions  
1. **Make the dough**: Mix 5g salt and 5g sugar with 250g warm water. Use chopsticks to stir while scalding the flour. Add 20g cooking oil and knead into a smooth dough. If the dough is difficult to knead evenly, let it rest briefly before kneading again.
2. **Proof the dough**: Brush the dough surface with a layer of cooking oil, cover, and let proof for 40 minutes.
3. **Cook the filling**: Slice the beef thinly, then marinate with 10g salt, 15g sugar, 15g cooking wine, 30g light soy sauce, and 8g black pepper powder. Heat oil in a wok, stir-fry the beef until it changes color, then add julienned green and red bell peppers and onion. Stir-fry until softened. Remove and let cool completely. Finally, mix in the shredded cheese.
   **Note**: The filling must be completely cooled before adding cheese to prevent premature melting that could affect rolling.
4. **Divide and roll out the dough**: Divide the proofed dough into 6 portions. Roll each into a ball, brush with oil, and let rest for 10 minutes. Take one portion and roll it into a thin, large circle.
5. **Roll up**: Place a generous amount of filling in the center of the dough circle and press it firmly. Lift one side of the dough and roll it forward, stretching slightly as you roll for a tighter roll. Then fold both sides inward and continue rolling until sealed. Place seam-side down on a baking tray.
   **Key point**: Stretch slightly while rolling to ensure a tight roll. Placing seam-side down prevents unraveling during baking.
6. **Pre-baking preparation**: Brush the surface with a layer of cooking oil, sprinkle with white sesame seeds, and poke several small holes with a toothpick for venting.
7. **Baking**:
   - Convection oven: Bake at 210°C for 10 minutes
   - Conventional oven: Bake at 230°C for 10–15 minutes
   Bake until the crust is golden and crispy.
8. **Cooling**: Remove and let cool until warm enough to handle, then serve.

## Health Suggestions  
This beef and cheese roll is rich in protein and calcium, making it suitable for post-workout energy replenishment or as a main dish for family gatherings. However, cheese and oil are high in calories, so pairing with a vegetable salad is recommended for balanced nutrition.
