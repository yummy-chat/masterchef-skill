# Basque Cheesecake
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Four Seasons Meal

---

🧀 Essential Ingredients  
- 212g Cream Cheese  
- 60g White Sugar  
- 2 Eggs  
- 1 Egg Yolk  
- 120g Heavy Cream  
- 10g Cake Flour  

🍫 Optional Ingredients (Chocolate Flavor)  
- 38g Chocolate (melt regular chocolate, add during the step of adding heavy cream and mix well)  

---

🍽️ Kitchenware  
- 7-inch Round Mold (original recipe uses 6-inch)  
- Whisk  
- Microwave  
- Oven  

---

👩‍🍳 Preparation Steps  
1. Place cream cheese in the microwave, heat in 10-second intervals until softened.  
2. Add white sugar to the softened cream cheese, whisk until smooth and free of lumps.  
3. Add 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition.  
4. Add heavy cream, continue mixing until fully combined.  
5. Sift in cake flour, stir until the batter is smooth with no dry spots.  
6. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F), pour batter into the mold, and bake for 20–25 minutes (20 or 22 minutes recommended).  
7. Remove the baked cake, let it cool naturally, then transfer to the refrigerator. It is recommended to chill overnight for the best flavor.  

---

⚠️ Key Tips  
- It is normal for the center of the cake to jiggle after baking; the texture will develop as it cools. Be sure to refrigerate before serving.  
- If the surface does not turn deep brown, it is because the oven lacks a hot air or top heating element function. If available, use hot air to help with browning.

## Health Advice
Cheesecake is rich in calcium and protein but high in sugar and fat, making it suitable for occasional indulgence. It is recommended to pair with unsweetened tea or black coffee to balance the sweetness. The texture becomes creamier after being refrigerated overnight.
