# Cumin Spicy Baby Potatoes
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥔 Baby potatoes, about 1 tray (washed)  
🌿 Cilantro, to taste (minced)  
🌶️ Red chili peppers, to taste (sliced into rings)  
🧅 Onion, to taste (diced)  
🫑 Green chili peppers, to taste (diced)  
🧄 Garlic, to taste (minced)  
🧅 Scallions, to taste (chopped)  
🧂 Ground Sichuan pepper, 1 tbsp  
🌶️ Fine chili powder, 2 tbsp  
🧂 Cumin powder, 1 tbsp  
🤍 Sesame seeds, 2 tbsp  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🧂 Sugar, to taste  
🧂 MSG, to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
♨️ Steamer (with steaming rack)  
🔪 Cutting board, Chef's knife  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
🥄 Spatula, Chopsticks  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🍽️ Plate  

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the washed baby potatoes on a steaming rack. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and steam for 40 minutes, until a chopstick can easily pierce through.  
   **Key point**: Once boiling, promptly reduce to low heat to avoid excessive water evaporation.  
2. Remove the potatoes and let them cool naturally before peeling off the skins.  
   **Note**: Wait until cooled to avoid burning your hands.  
3. Prepare the cilantro, red chili peppers, onion, green chili peppers, garlic, and scallions by mincing, slicing into rings, dicing, etc., and set aside.  
4. In a stainless steel bowl, combine the ground Sichuan pepper, fine chili powder, cumin powder, and sesame seeds to form the seasoning mix. Set aside.  
5. Heat oil in a wok or frying pan over medium heat, then add the peeled baby potatoes. Fry over the lowest heat, turning occasionally, until each side is golden brown, about 2–3 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Use a low flame to prevent burning.  
6. Pour in the prepared seasoning mix and stir-fry together with the potatoes until fragrant.  
7. Add salt, sugar, and MSG to taste, and stir-fry quickly to melt the seasonings and evenly coat the potatoes.  
8. Add all the chopped ingredients, turn the heat to high, and stir-fry quickly until fragrant. Once the aroma is fully released, remove from heat and serve on a plate.  

## Health Advice  
This dish uses steaming instead of deep-frying, reducing trans fatty acid content, making it suitable for those managing weight or blood lipid levels. It is recommended to pair with light vegetables or high-protein foods to avoid excessive carbohydrate intake from a single dish.
