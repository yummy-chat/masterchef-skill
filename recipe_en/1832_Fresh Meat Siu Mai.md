# Fresh Meat Siu Mai
Cuisine: Jiangsu Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Available all year round

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍖 Siu Mai wrappers  
🥩 Ground pork (fat-to-lean ratio 3:7)  
🧅 Minced ginger  
🌿 Minced scallions (green parts)  
🍄 Winter bamboo shoots  
🍮 Pork skin gelatin  
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms  
🦐 Shrimp  
🧈 Lard or chicken fat  
🧂 Light soy sauce  
🍶 Cooking wine  
🧂 Salt  
🍬 Sugar  
🌶️ White pepper  
🫒 Sesame oil  
🥣 Broth  
🧪 Compound food thickener  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Meat grinder  
♨️ Steamer (with steamer liner)  
🧊 Refrigerator  

## Preparation Steps
1. If making a low-fat version: Take 10 mL of cold water and mix with compound food thickener to form a paste; pour in 25 mL of boiling water and stir until a semi-transparent gel forms; let cool, then mix into 280 g of lean ground meat and 15 g of melted lard.
2. Combine the ground pork with minced ginger and scallions. Add light soy sauce, cooking wine, salt, sugar, and white pepper. Stir in one direction until the mixture becomes sticky.
3. If using bouillon cubes, dissolve one cube in 15 mL of hot water and stir until the emulsion is fully dispersed, then add 15 mL of room-temperature water.
4. Gradually add the broth while continuing to stir until the filling absorbs the liquid and thickens. If too dry, add 5 mL of water and continue stirring.
5. Add optional ingredients and finally drizzle with sesame oil, mixing well. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to let the flavors meld.
6. Take one siu mai wrapper and place about 20–25 g of filling in the center. Be careful not to overfill to prevent the wrapper from closing.
7. Gently gather the edges of the wrapper using the tiger’s mouth shape to form a "vase" shape, leaving the top open. Lightly press the bottom to ensure it stands steadily.
8. If adding shrimp, place one shrimp on top.
9. Repeat the wrapping process until all are done. Arrange the siu mai in the steamer, leaving space between each to prevent sticking.
10. Once the water is boiling, place the steamer over high heat and steam for 8–10 minutes.

## Health Advice
The filling contains pork skin gelatin and shiitake mushrooms, making it juicy, but the fat and sodium content may be high. It is recommended to pair with a vegetable salad or a light soup to balance the meal. Enjoy while hot for the best texture, as the gelatin solidifies and affects the flavor when cooled.
