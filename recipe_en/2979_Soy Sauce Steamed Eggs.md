# Soy Sauce Steamed Eggs
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥚 3 eggs  
🧂 Light soy sauce (as needed, for the base flavor)  
🫒 Cooking oil (as needed, to drizzle after cooking)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🍳 Flat-bottomed pan or steamer  
🔲 Steaming rack  
🍽️ Oval steaming dish  
🫖 Lid  
🔥 Gas stove  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Steps  
1. Take the steaming dish and pour an appropriate amount of light soy sauce into the bottom to form a base flavor.  
   **Note**: The amount of light soy sauce can be adjusted to personal taste; it is the main source of savory flavor.  
2. Crack 3 eggs directly into the dish, keeping the yolks and whites intact without stirring.  
3. Place the steaming rack in the pot and add water. The water level should be enough to generate sufficient steam without submerging the dish. Place the dish steadily on the rack and cover with the lid.  
4. Steam over high heat for about 10 minutes, until the egg whites are fully set and turn white.  
5. After turning off the heat, carefully remove the lid to avoid steam burns.  
6. Drizzle a little cooking oil over the top for added sheen and aroma, then serve.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is rich in protein and low in carbohydrates, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or in need of quick nutritional replenishment. Note that light soy sauce has a high sodium content, so it is recommended to pair with light vegetables to balance the salt intake.
