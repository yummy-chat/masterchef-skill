# Oil-Free Water-Fried Egg Avocado Bagel
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Eggs 2
🥯 Bagel 1 (frozen, remove in advance)
🥑 Avocado ½ (mashed into a paste)
🫑 Yellow bell pepper about ¼ (cut into large chunks, raw side dish)
🧂 Black pepper sea salt to taste (grind for seasoning)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🫗 Glass lid
♨️ Oven (or air fryer function)
🔪 Cutting board and knife
🥄 Spoon
🧂 Black pepper sea salt grinder
🥢 Tongs
🍽️ Flat plate

## Preparation Steps
1. Remove the frozen bagel, rinse it briefly under running water until the surface is moist and softened, no need to fully thaw.
2. Wash the yellow bell pepper, cut about ¼ into large chunks, and set aside.
3. Place the non-stick frying pan on the stove, preheat until hot enough, crack in 2 eggs, and reduce to low heat.
   **Note**: The pan must be fully preheated to prevent sticking.
4. When the egg whites begin to set at the edges while the centers are still runny, gently use tongs to merge the whites of the two eggs together in the center of the pan, creating a cohesive shape.
5. Add a small amount of water along the edge of the pan, immediately cover with the glass lid, and continue to steam-fry over low heat for about 1-2 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Adding water creates steam to quickly cook the surface of the yolks while preventing the bottom from burning.
6. Once the egg whites are fully set and white, and the yolks are covered with a thin white film, appearing half-cooked and runny, turn off the heat and remove the fried eggs.
7. Meanwhile, place the rinsed bagel into the preheated oven and bake for about 5-6 minutes until the outside is crispy and the inside is soft.
8. Mash the avocado into a paste with a spoon, and spread it evenly on the cut surfaces of the bagel.
9. Take one slice of bagel with avocado spread, place a fried egg on top, and evenly sprinkle with an appropriate amount of black pepper sea salt using the grinder.
   **Note**: Avocado itself is bland, so the saltiness of the fried egg is needed to enhance the flavor.
10. Arrange the assembled bagel sandwich, the remaining fried egg, and the yellow bell pepper chunks on a plate, and serve.

## Health Advice
Avocado paired with runny egg is a golden combination of high-quality fats and protein, suitable for those on a weight loss or muscle-building diet. However, bagels are high in carbohydrates, so portion control is recommended.
