# Lazy Stir-fried Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍜 Instant noodles 1 pack
🥚 Egg 1
🌭 Ham sausage适量 (shredded)
🥕 Carrot适量 (shredded)
🧅 Onion适量 (shredded, used in two batches)
🌱 Scallions适量 (cut into sections, used in two batches)
🧂 Light soy sauce适量
🌶️ Chili oil about 2 spoons
🍶 Rice vinegar适量

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Strainer

## Steps
1. Shred the ham sausage and carrot, shred the onion, cut the scallions into sections, and set the instant noodle block aside.
2. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add the instant noodles, cook briefly until just tender, then drain.
   **Note**: Do not add salt to the boiling water. After cooking, drain off excess oil to prevent the noodles from becoming too soft.
3. While the noodles are still hot, crack a raw egg into them. Gently stir with chopsticks until fully mixed.
   **Key step**: This ensures the noodles separate clearly and develop a crispy, charred aroma when fried.
4. Heat oil in the wok, add the egg-coated noodles, spread them flat, and fry until both sides are golden and crispy. Remove to a plate or push to the side of the wok.
   **Note**: Control the heat to avoid burning.
5. Add the shredded ham sausage, shredded carrot, scallion sections, and a small amount of shredded onion to the fried noodles. Drizzle with light soy sauce and stir-fry until fragrant.
6. Add about two spoons of chili oil, drizzle rice vinegar along the edge of the wok, then add the remaining shredded onion and scallion sections.
   **Key step**: Adding rice vinegar along the edge of the wok releases a rich aroma.
7. Stir-fry over high heat until all ingredients are evenly mixed and colored. Serve immediately.

## Health Tip
Mixing raw egg into the boiled noodles before frying reduces oil absorption and adds protein, making this a quick energy boost for busy days. It is recommended to serve with a portion of leafy greens to balance dietary fiber.
