# Crayon Shin-chan Special Croquette  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Four Seasons Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef belly 500g (for Japanese curry)  
🥩 Steak meat approx. 200g (cut into 2cm cubes, for filling)  
🥕 Carrot 1  
🥔 Potato 1  
🧅 Onion 1 (half sautéed until fragrant, half simmered)  
🍠 Sweet potato approx. 3 small (instead of potato for mash)  
🥚 Eggs appropriate amount (for egg wash coating)  
🌾 Flour appropriate amount (first coating layer)  
🍞 Breadcrumbs appropriate amount (outer coating)  
🦪 Sea urchin approx. 8 slices (top garnish)  
🧂 Salt a pinch (for seasoning beef while pan-frying)  
🫒 Oil appropriate amount (for pan-frying beef and deep-frying)  
🍛 Curry blocks appropriate amount  
🥛 Milk 50–100g (added to curry to harmonize flavor)  
🍲 Stock (or water) appropriate amount (for simmering)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🍳 Cast iron pot (or stew pot)  
🥄 Spatula  
🥢 Chopsticks  
📡 Microwave (or pot)  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🍳 Frying pan  
🍲 Deep fryer (or deep pot)  
🔗 Tongs  
📦 Wooden box (for holding sea urchin)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Cut beef belly into large pieces, cut steak meat into 2cm cubes; chop carrot, potato, and onion into chunks; peel sweet potatoes.  
2. Microwave sweet potatoes on high heat for 8-10 minutes until fully cooked, mash into a paste, add a little milk and mix well, set aside.  
3. Heat oil in a cast iron pot, add beef belly and sear until browned, push to the side of the pot, add half the onion and sauté until soft. Pour in stock or water, bring to a boil, then reduce to medium-low heat, cover and simmer for about 40 minutes, until the beef can be easily pierced.  
4. Add the remaining potato and carrot chunks, continue cooking until the potato crushes easily when pressed. Turn off the heat, add curry blocks and stir until melted, then add milk and stir well, this is the curry sauce.  
5. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat, pan-fry the steak meat cubes until golden brown on the surface, remove and set aside.  
6. Take an appropriate amount of sweet potato mash, roll into a ball, make a hollow in the center, fill with one spoonful of curry sauce and a few pieces of pan-fried beef cubes, seal the opening and roll into a ball, repair any breaks with sweet potato mash. Coat evenly with flour, egg wash, and breadcrumbs in order.  
7. Heat the oil to 170-180°C, place the croquettes in and deep-fry for about 3 minutes, until the surface is golden and crispy, then remove.  
8. Arrange on a plate and place sea urchin on top, serve and enjoy.  

## Health Advice  
This dish combines wagyu, curry, sea urchin, and deep-fried carbohydrates, resulting in high protein and energy density, suitable for muscle building or celebratory occasions. Be mindful to control portion size to avoid excessive intake of saturated fat and calories.
