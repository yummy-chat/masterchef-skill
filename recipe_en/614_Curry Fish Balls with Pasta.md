# Curry Fish Balls with Pasta
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Preparation Steps
1. Bring water to a boil in a stockpot, add fish balls, fish balls (fish balls), and fish sausage. Add curry powder, sugar, and salt. Stir well and keep boiling until the fish balls float and the broth turns a rich curry color.  
**Note**: Use salt sparingly, as fish products are naturally salty; stir the curry powder thoroughly to prevent clumps.

2. In a separate frying pan, bring water to a boil, add the pasta, immediately cover with a lid, and turn off the heat. Let it steep for about 10 minutes, until the noodles are soft and no longer have a hard core.  
**Note**: The steeping time can be adjusted based on the thickness of the noodles.

3. Before removing the curry fish balls from the heat, add the milk and coconut milk, stir well, and heat briefly before turning off the heat. Avoid prolonged boiling.

4. Divide the steeped pasta into bowls, ladle the curry fish balls and broth over it, and serve.

## Health Advice
Curry fish balls with pasta combines seafood protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts to replenish energy. However, fish products tend to be high in salt, so people with high blood pressure should watch their salt intake. The addition of coconut milk and milk enhances the flavor but also increases calories, so it is recommended to pair with a vegetable salad for balanced nutrition.
