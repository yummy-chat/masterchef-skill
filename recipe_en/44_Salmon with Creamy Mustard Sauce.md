# Salmon with Creamy Mustard Sauce
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐟 Salmon fillet 1 piece (skin-on or skinless)
🧅 Onion, to taste (finely chopped)
🥛 Milk or heavy cream, to taste
🟡 Mustard (e.g., Dijon mustard), to taste
🍯 Honey, to taste
🍋 Lemon juice, to taste
🍋 Lemon zest, a pinch
🫒 Olive oil, to taste
🧈 Butter, to taste
🧂 Salt, to taste
⚫ Black pepper, to taste
🌿 Fresh herbs, chopped (parsley, dill, or thyme), a pinch (for garnish)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🥄 Spatula
🔪 Chef's knife
🟫 Cutting board
🥄 Small spoon
🧀 Grater (for lemon zest)
🍴 Fork
🍽️ Plate
⚙️ Pepper grinder

## Steps
1. Finely chop the onion and set aside.
2. Add olive oil and butter to a frying pan. Once hot, place the salmon fillet in the pan and sear until both sides are golden and crispy.
   - **Note**: Keep the inside of the fish tender, cook to desired doneness, then remove and let it rest for roughly the same amount of time as the cooking time to allow the juices to redistribute.
3. Using the remaining oil in the pan, add the chopped onion and sauté until golden and fragrant.
4. Pour in the milk or heavy cream, add the mustard, stir to combine, and cook until the sauce thickens slightly.
5. Remove from heat, then add honey, lemon juice, lemon zest, and freshly ground black pepper. Stir until well combined.
   - **Key point**: Be sure to remove from heat before adding these ingredients to preserve the fresh aroma of the lemon and prevent the honey from losing flavor due to excessive heat.
6. Adjust salt to taste.
7. Pour the sauce over the rested salmon, garnish with fresh herbs and a pinch of lemon zest.

## Health Notes
Salmon is rich in omega-3 fatty acids. Pairing it with a mustard sauce enhances the flavor while keeping calories manageable, making it suitable for a weight-loss diet. Use cream sauce in moderation and consider pairing with light vegetables to balance the taste.
