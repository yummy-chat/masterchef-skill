# Mini Rolled Crepe Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 4 eggs (for plain batter)
🌾 120g cake flour
🥛 500ml milk (about 2 cartons)
🧈 40g butter (melted into liquid, can substitute with vegetable oil)
🍓 15g strawberry powder (for strawberry-flavored batter)
🍵 3g matcha powder (for matcha-flavored batter)
🍫 5g cocoa powder (for chocolate-flavored batter)
🍪 Crushed Oreo cookies, as needed (for chocolate flavor garnish)
🫘 Sweetened red beans, as needed (for matcha flavor garnish)
🥭 Diced mango, as needed (for plain flavor garnish)
🍍 Diced pineapple, as needed (for strawberry flavor garnish)

🧂 55g granulated sugar (added to egg mixture for batter)
🧂 28g granulated sugar (added to whipping cream for filling)

## Cookware & Equipment
🥣 2 or more large bowls (for mixing ingredients, making batter and cream)
🥄 Whisk, manual or electric (for stirring batter, whipping cream)
🍳 Non-stick frying pan (for cooking crepes)
🫗 Strainer (for straining batter, making it smoother)
🔪 Spatula (to assist stirring, remove crepes)
💧 Piping bag (for piping cream garnish)
🍽️ Plate (for holding cooked crepes)
🫙 Small bowl (for mixing flavored batters)
🧊 Basin + ice cubes (to cool workspace, prevent cream from melting)

## Instructions

### Preparation Phase
1.  Prepare plain crepe batter: Crack eggs into a large bowl, add granulated sugar, and stir until combined. Add cake flour and mix until smooth with no lumps. Gradually add milk in portions, stirring to combine. Add the melted butter and mix thoroughly. Strain the batter through a sieve 1-2 times to obtain a smooth plain crepe batter.
   **Key Point**: Stir until smooth with no lumps; straining is crucial for delicate crepes.

2.  Prepare other flavored batters: For strawberry flavor, take a portion of plain batter and stir in strawberry powder until combined. For matcha flavor, dissolve matcha powder in hot water until no lumps remain, then mix with a portion of plain batter. For chocolate flavor, dissolve cocoa powder in hot oil until smooth, then mix with a portion of plain batter.
   **Key Point**: Flavor powders must first be dissolved in a small amount of liquid to avoid clumping.

3.  Prepare the cream filling: Add granulated sugar to whipping cream and whip with an electric mixer until firm with clear lines. For matcha cream, mix a small amount of whipped cream with matcha powder until combined, then combine with the remaining plain cream. For chocolate cream, similarly mix a small amount of whipped cream with cocoa powder. For fruit cream, directly mix fruit jam with the whipped cream.
   **Key Point**: Cream must be chilled before whipping; place the bowl over an ice water bath to keep it cool and prevent melting.

### Cooking Phase
1.  Cook the crepes: Preheat a non-stick frying pan until warm when you hold your hand above it. Reduce the heat to the lowest setting. Stir the batter well before cooking. Pour a ladleful of batter into the pan, quickly swirl to coat the bottom evenly, then pour any excess batter back into the bowl, leaving a thin, even layer. Cook over the lowest heat until the batter is fully set, the surface is covered with small bubbles, and the edges slightly lift. Gently lift an edge with a spatula to confirm it is cooked, then slide the crepe onto a plate to cool.
   **Key Point**: Use low heat and work quickly to create thin, even crepes. Repeat this step for each flavor.

2.  Assemble the mini rolled crepe cakes: Place one completely cooled crepe flat. Fold the two sides toward the center. Spread a layer of cream in the middle section. Starting from one end, tightly roll up the crepe to form a cylinder.
   **Key Point**: The crepe must be completely cooled, otherwise the cream will melt. Roll tightly.

3.  Garnish and finish: Pipe decorative cream on top of the rolled crepe cake. According to the flavor, sprinkle with crushed Oreo cookies, sweetened red beans, or place diced mango or pineapple on top. Done.

## Health Advice
This mini rolled crepe cake is rich in carbohydrates and fats, suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. However, it is recommended to consume only one roll at a time to avoid excess cream and sugar intake. Diabetic individuals and those on a weight-loss diet should taste sparingly, pairing it with unsweetened tea for a better option.
