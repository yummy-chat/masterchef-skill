# Seafood Borscht
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥬 Pre-made vegetable pack 1 pack (contains cabbage, carrots, onions, etc.)
🍅 Tomato paste 1 pack
🐚 Frozen scallops appropriate amount (thawed in advance, drained)
🧂 Black pepper salt appropriate amount (for seasoning before serving)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔥 Gas stove
🍲 Soup pot
🧂 Grinder bottle
🥄 Soup ladle

## Cooking Steps
1. Pour water into the pot and bring to a boil. Add the pre-made vegetable pack, and squeeze in the entire pack of tomato paste at the same time.
   **Key point**: The tomato paste should be added early in the soup cooking process to allow its flavor to fully infuse the soup.
2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat. Cook until the vegetables soften and the soup turns an orange-red color.
3. Add the thawed and drained scallops. Cook until they turn white and slightly shrink in size.
   **Key point**: Scallops cook very quickly and should not be boiled for too long.
4. Before serving, grind an appropriate amount of black pepper salt into the soup using a grinder bottle. Stir well and turn off the heat.
   **Key point**: Add the black pepper salt at the end to preserve its aroma.

## Health Tips
This seafood borscht uses a pre-made vegetable pack and frozen scallops, making it convenient to prepare and rich in nutrition. The lycopene in the tomato paste is more easily absorbed after cooking, making it suitable for busy office workers. However, scallops can easily become tough, so they should be added last and cooked only until they turn white, then immediately turned off the heat to maintain a tender texture.
