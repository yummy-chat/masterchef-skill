# Strange-Flavored Chicken Shreds
Cuisine: Sichuan  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken breast 500g  
🥒 Cucumber appropriate amount (shredded for base)  
🧅 Scallion 1 (part cut into sections for boiling, part shredded for garnish)  
🫚 Ginger 3-4 slices  
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns a small pinch (about a dozen, for boiling)  
⭐ Star anise 1 (for boiling)  
🫙 Sesame paste 30g  
🫒 Sesame oil 20g (added in batches)  
🧂 Salt 3g  
🍾 Light soy sauce 10g  
🫗 Vinegar 10g  
🍬 White sugar 5g  
🌶️ Ground Sichuan pepper 5g  
🌶️🫙 Chili oil 30g  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Pot  
🥣 Transparent glass bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks, spoon  
🥖 Rolling pin  
🛍️ Resealable bag  
🔪 Cutting board  
🍽️ Plate  

## Steps  
1. Prepare the strange flavor sauce: Place sesame paste in a bowl, first add 20g sesame oil and stir until smooth and free of lumps, then successively add salt, light soy sauce, and vinegar, stirring clockwise until thick. Add white sugar, ground Sichuan pepper, and 5g sesame oil, mix well. Finally, drizzle in chili oil and stir to combine. Set aside.  
2. Boil the chicken breast: Add about 500g water to a pot, add scallion sections, ginger slices, Sichuan peppercorns, and star anise, and bring to a boil over high heat. Add the chicken breast. Once the water returns to a boil, cook for 8 minutes. Turn off the heat, immediately cover the pot, and let it steep for 10 minutes.  
**Key point**: The combination of boiling and steaming locks in freshness and keeps the chicken breast tender and moist.  

3. Process the chicken breast: Remove the slightly cooled chicken breast and place it in a resealable bag. Gently pound it with a rolling pin until the meat is loose. Remove, tear into fine shreds along the grain, creating a rough "shredded surface" to better absorb the sauce.  
4. Assemble: Lay cucumber shreds at the bottom of a plate, top with the chicken shreds, and evenly drizzle with the prepared strange flavor sauce. Garnish the top with shredded scallion.  

## Health Tips  
This strange-flavored chicken shreds uses a combination of boiling and steaming to lock in the tenderness of the chicken breast. Paired with sesame paste and chili oil, it is low in fat and high in protein, suitable for those who are fitness-conscious or managing their weight. The cucumber shreds at the base add a crisp texture, but the sauce is relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to consume in moderation.
