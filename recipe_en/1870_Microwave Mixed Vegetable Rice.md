# Microwave Mixed Vegetable Rice

Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Rice about 1 bowl  
🥒 Cucumber about half (sliced into thin pieces)  
🥬 Purple cabbage appropriate amount (finely shredded)  
🍄 Black fungus (dried) appropriate amount  
🐟 Fish floss appropriate amount  
🦐 Dried shrimp appropriate amount  
🧊 Pork bone broth jelly cube 1 piece  
💧 Water appropriate amount (just enough to cover the ingredients)  
(No additional salt, soy sauce, or other seasonings added; flavor comes from the broth jelly and fish floss/dried shrimp)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Heat-resistant bowl  
🔪 Vegetable peeler  
♨️ Microwave oven  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Spoon  
🍽️ Flat plate (for covering and sealing)  

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the rice in a bowl and rinse it clean with water.
   **Note**: Gently rub the rice while rinsing to avoid breaking the grains.
2. Use a vegetable peeler to slice the cucumber into thin pieces, and add them along with the finely shredded purple cabbage into the bowl.
3. Add the black fungus, fish floss or dried shrimp in sequence, then place the pork bone broth jelly cube.
4. Slowly pour in water, with the amount just enough to cover all ingredients.
   **Note**: Too much water will make the rice mushy and lose its grainy texture.
5. Take a flat plate of suitable size and invert it over the bowl to seal it.
6. Place the bowl in the microwave, select the "Steam" mode, and heat for approximately 8 minutes and 14 seconds.
   **Note**: Ensure both the bowl and plate are microwave-safe; be careful of steam burns when removing.
7. After removing, use chopsticks to thoroughly mix the rice with all the ingredients, and it is ready to serve.
   **Key point**: Stir thoroughly so that the broth jelly and flavors from the ingredients are evenly absorbed into the rice.

## Health Advice  
This microwave mixed vegetable rice has diverse ingredients with no added salt, and the broth jelly and fish floss provide umami flavor, making it suitable for a fast-paced lifestyle or for those on a low-sodium diet. It is recommended to pair it with a portion of leafy greens or protein for a more balanced nutrition.
