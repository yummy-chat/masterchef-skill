# Potato Cake
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes, appropriate amount (steamed and mashed)
🧅 Onion, appropriate amount (diced)
🫛 Green peas, appropriate amount
🍖 Ham, appropriate amount (diced)
🧀 Cheese, appropriate amount (for filling to achieve stretchiness)
🌾 Flour, appropriate amount (for mixing into mashed potatoes and coating)
🌾 Starch, appropriate amount (for mixing into mashed potatoes)
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount
🥛 Milk, appropriate amount
🥚 Eggs, appropriate amount (beaten into egg wash)
🍞 Breadcrumbs, appropriate amount (for final coating)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
♨️ Steamer/Steam oven
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Rolling pin (for mashing)
♨️ Air fryer
🍽️ Containers (for egg wash, flour, breadcrumbs)

## Cooking Steps
1. Steam the potatoes until fully cooked, remove and place in a bowl, then use a rolling pin to mash into a fine potato puree.
   **Key point**: Potatoes must be fully steamed to be easily mashed into a puree.
2. Add an appropriate amount of salt, milk, and starch to the mashed potatoes, mix evenly by hand, and knead into a non-sticky, pliable dough.
3. Prepare the filling: dice the onion, green peas, ham, and cheese separately, and mix them evenly. The filling can be adjusted according to personal preference.
4. Prepare the "three-step coating" ingredients: place the flour, beaten egg wash, and breadcrumbs into separate containers.
5. Take an appropriate amount of potato dough, roll it into a ball, flatten into a round disc, wrap in an appropriate amount of filling, seal the opening like wrapping a bun, and gently flatten into a cake shape.
   **Key point**: Do not overfill to prevent leakage; ensure the seal is tight.
6. Coat the wrapped potato cakes one by one with a thin layer of flour, dip in the egg wash, and finally coat evenly with breadcrumbs.
   **Key point**: Ensure each layer is coated evenly; breadcrumbs enhance crispiness.
7. Place the coated potato cakes into the air fryer basket, set to 150°C, and bake for 10 minutes.
   **Note**: This recipe uses a specific model of air fryer. If using other equipment, it may need to be flipped halfway through, and the temperature and time may need to be adjusted accordingly.
8. Bake until the outside is golden and crispy, then remove and enjoy. The melted cheese will be stretchy, with a crispy exterior and soft interior.

## Health Tips
This potato cake has a crispy outside and soft inside with stretchy cheese, rich in carbohydrates and protein. However, whether fried or air-fried, its calorie content is not low, making it suitable as an energy boost before or after workouts or as a fun staple for children. Be mindful of controlling the salt content in the filling; onions and green peas can increase dietary fiber.
