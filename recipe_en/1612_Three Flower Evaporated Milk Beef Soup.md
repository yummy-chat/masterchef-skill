# Three Flower Evaporated Milk Beef Soup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
💧 Water, about half a pot
🥬 Cilantro, to taste (chopped)
🧅 Scallions, to taste (chopped)
🧴 Beef bone extract (bone broth paste), one spoonful (provides beef flavor base)
🫒 Refined cooking oil, a small amount (to create an oily sheen effect)
🥛 Three Flower Evaporated Milk, one spoonful (about 30-50ml)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥄 Soup ladle
🔥 Gas stove

## Cooking Steps
1. Chop the cilantro and scallions separately, set aside.
2. Pour about half a pot of water into the wok, bring to a boil over high heat.
3. Once boiling, add one spoonful of beef bone extract, stir continuously until fully dissolved, the soup turns light yellow.
   **Note**: The beef bone extract is thick and sticky, needs thorough stirring to dissolve and avoid clumps.
4. Add a small amount of refined cooking oil to the soup, gently stir to distribute, creating an oily sheen on the surface.
   **Key Point**: This step simulates the oily effect of long-simmered meat broth.
5. Add one spoonful of Three Flower Evaporated Milk, gently stir a few times, the soup quickly turns milky white.
   **Note**: Evaporated milk should not be boiled for long; its lecithin acts as an emulsifier, prolonged boiling can cause oil-water separation and loss of surface oil sheen.
6. Sprinkle in the chopped cilantro and scallions, stir well, then remove from heat.

## Health Advice
This is a quick soup rich in protein and calcium, but potentially high in sodium. Suitable for busy office workers needing a fast energy boost, but those with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of bone broth paste.
