# Stir-fried Sponge Gourd with Eggs  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥒 Sponge gourd 2 (peeled and cut into rolling chunks)  
🥚 Eggs 4  
🌶️ Red chili pepper to taste (cut into sections)  
🧅 Scallion to taste (cut into sections)  
💧 Water as needed (for soaking sponge gourd)  
🧂 Salt a pinch (for egg mixture), to taste (for stir-frying)  
🫒 Cooking oil as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🪵 Round wooden cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🪣 Stainless steel basin  
🥄 Metal slotted spoon  
🔪 Stainless steel wok spatula  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Peel the sponge gourd and cut it into rolling chunks, then immediately immerse them in water to prevent oxidation and darkening.  
2. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt, and beat with chopsticks until the egg mixture is even.  
3. Heat the wok over high heat, add an appropriate amount of cooking oil to coat the wok, then pour out the oil, leaving a thin layer at the bottom. This warms the wok to prevent sticking.  
4. Pour in the egg mixture and stir-fry over medium heat until fully set, then remove and set aside.  
5. Heat the remaining oil in the wok, add the scallion sections and stir until fragrant, then add the drained sponge gourd. Stir-fry over high heat until it turns bright green.  
6. Add salt to taste and stir-fry until the salt dissolves. Add the cooked eggs, red chili pepper sections, and the remaining scallion sections. Stir-fry quickly until evenly mixed, then transfer to a serving dish.  
   **Key point**: Do not add any water throughout the process to preserve the sponge gourd’s crisp, tender original flavor.

## Health Tips  
Sponge gourd is rich in water and dietary fiber, helping to clear heat, relieve summer heat, and promote digestion. Eggs provide high-quality protein, making this dish suitable for supplementing nutrition when appetite is low in summer. Note that sponge gourd is cool in nature, so those with a weak spleen and stomach should not eat too much. Also, avoid adding water during stir-frying to retain the crisp texture.
