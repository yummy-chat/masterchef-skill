# Old-fashioned Ice Cream with Glutinous Rice and Red Dates
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍎 Dried red dates 80g (pitted and cut into small pieces)
🍚 Glutinous rice 150g (soaked for more than 3 hours in advance)
💧 Water 1600ml (for cooking porridge)
🍬 White sugar 80g

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Knife, cutting board
🥣 Glass bowl (for soaking and rinsing glutinous rice)
🍲 Soup pot
🥄 Silicone spatula or wooden spatula (for stirring to prevent sticking)
🧊 Ice cream molds and ice cream sticks
❄️ Refrigerator

## Steps
1. Remove pits from dried red dates and cut into small pieces; soak glutinous rice for more than 3 hours in advance, then rinse clean.
2. Put the soaked glutinous rice and chopped red dates into the soup pot, add 1600ml of water, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium-low heat and simmer.
   **Note**: Stir continuously during cooking to prevent sticking and burning at the bottom; cook until the glutinous rice and red dates are soft and the liquid is thick.
3. Add 80g of white sugar, stir until completely dissolved, then remove from heat.
4. Continue stirring after removing from heat to help the porridge cool down quickly. When it is no longer hot to the touch, pour into the ice cream molds, filling tightly to avoid air pockets.
5. Cover the molds with lids and place in the refrigerator to freeze until completely solid.
6. Remove from molds and serve. Ensure the ice cream sticks are correctly inserted into the molds before unmolding.

## Health Advice
This glutinous rice and red date ice cream is rich in carbohydrates, making it suitable for satisfying cravings and replenishing energy in summer. However, it has a high sugar content, so those monitoring their sugar intake should consume it in moderation.
