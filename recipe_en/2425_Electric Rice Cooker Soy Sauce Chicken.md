# Electric Rice Cooker Soy Sauce Chicken
Cuisine: Cantonese
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 1 whole chicken
🧅 Scallions, as needed (cut into sections)
🫚 Ginger, as needed (sliced)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (sliced)
🍶 Light soy sauce, a generous amount
🍯 Dark soy sauce, as needed
🦪 Oyster sauce, as needed
🍯 Honey, as needed
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed

## Tools and Equipment
🍚 Electric rice cooker
🧤 Disposable gloves
🥣 Mixing bowl
🥄 Spoon
🥢 Chopsticks

## Cooking Steps
1. Clean the whole chicken, removing internal organs and impurities.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, and cooking wine. Add scallion sections, ginger slices, and garlic slices. Stir to create the marinade.
3. Wearing disposable gloves, evenly spread the marinade over the entire chicken, inside and out. Rub and massage repeatedly. Marinate for about 1 hour.
**Note**: Massaging allows the seasonings to fully penetrate; ensure enough marinating time.

4. Place the marinated whole chicken into the inner pot of the electric rice cooker. Do not add extra water.
**Note**: The rice cooker seals well; the chicken’s own moisture and the steam from the seasonings will cook it through. Adding water will dilute the flavor.

5. Close the rice cooker lid and start the rice cooking mode to braise. After it switches to keep warm, if there is still a lot of liquid in the pot, continue heating until the sauce thickens and the chicken skin turns glossy red.
**Note**: Adjust the time based on your rice cooker’s power. The goal is a thick sauce and well-colored skin.

6. Insert chopsticks into the thickest part of the chicken thigh and pull them out. If no blood oozes out, the chicken is fully cooked.
**Note**: The chicken thigh is the hardest part to cook through; checking here is the most reliable.

7. Remove the cooked chicken, drain excess sauce, chop into pieces, and serve.
**Note**: If you don’t have a professional cleaver, use a regular kitchen knife to chop as best you can—no need to worry about perfect knife skills.

## Health Advice
This electric rice cooker soy sauce chicken is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts. However, dark soy sauce and honey contain high sugar levels; those needing to control blood sugar should consume in moderation.
