# Meatball Soup Noodles with Soft-Boiled Egg
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🐙 Squid balls: 3-4 pieces  
🐮 Beef balls: 2-3 pieces  
🐷 Pork balls: 2-3 pieces  
🥚 Eggs: 2  
🥬 Green vegetables: appropriate amount (any variety, about a small handful)  
🍜 Thin noodles: appropriate amount (vermicelli or similar variety)  
🧂 Soup base powder: about 1 teaspoon (for seasoning the broth)  
🧂 Salt: appropriate amount  
🍛 Noodle sauce (powdered): about 1 teaspoon  
🌶️ Garlic chili sauce: about 1 teaspoon  
🌿 Black pepper: appropriate amount  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍲 Cooking pot (with lid)  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for beating eggs and adding water)  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
✂️ Kitchen scissors  
🥄 Large soup ladle  
🍽️ Bowl  
🥄 Seasoning spoon  
🥢 Chopsticks or spoon  

## Steps
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add soup base powder and salt to create a basic flavored broth.
2. Add squid balls, beef balls, and pork balls, cook until the balls float to the surface.
3. Crack the eggs into a small bowl, add a small amount of water, and gently slide them into the pot.
**Key point**: Do not stir the eggs once they are in the water. Immediately cover the pot with a lid and let simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing the egg whites to set and the yolks to remain runny or semi-runny.

4. Remove the cooked soft-boiled eggs and set aside.
5. Cut the green vegetables into sections using kitchen scissors, and add them along with the thin noodles into the soup to cook until done.
6. Place the cooked noodles in a serving bowl, add garlic chili sauce and black pepper.
7. Arrange the soft-boiled eggs, meatballs, and green vegetables on top.
8. Ladle the hot soup from the pot into the bowl, enough to submerge the noodles.
9. Before eating, sprinkle additional black pepper to taste.

## Health Advice  
This meatball soup noodle dish with soft-boiled egg offers a balanced mix of meat and vegetables and is rich in protein sources, making it suitable for a quick energy-boosting everyday meal. Soft-boiled eggs should be consumed with food safety in mind; those with sensitive stomachs are advised to cook the eggs until fully set. The meatballs contain relatively high sodium levels, so individuals with high blood pressure may reduce the amount of soup base powder.
