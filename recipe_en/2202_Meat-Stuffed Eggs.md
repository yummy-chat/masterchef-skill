# Meat-Stuffed Eggs
Cuisine: Fujian Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🐖 Pork (pork butt meat) appropriate amount  
🥚 Duck eggs several pieces  
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms appropriate amount (finely minced)  
🧅 Scallion and ginger water appropriate amount  
🍶 Huadiao wine (or cooking wine) appropriate amount  
🧂 Soy sauce appropriate amount  
🦪 Oyster sauce appropriate amount  
🌿 White pepper powder a pinch  
🍄 Matsutake seasoning a pinch  
🦐 Dried shrimp paste a pinch  
🧅 Chopped scallions appropriate amount (for cooking eggs)  
🌿 Seaweed a small amount (for cooking eggs)  
🧂 Salt appropriate amount (for cooking eggs)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍲 Soup pot  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Cooking Steps  
1. **Prepare the Pork**: Finely chop the pork using the "fine chop, coarse mince" technique, leaving one-tenth of the meat pieces not fully minced, so the filling is evenly sized with a grainy texture.  
2. **Season the Filling**: Place the minced pork and minced shiitake mushrooms in a bowl. Add scallion and ginger water, Huadiao wine, soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper powder, matsutake seasoning, and dried shrimp paste. Stir clockwise until the mixture is firm and elastic.  
3. **Stuff the Eggs**: Crack a fresh duck egg into a bowl. Poke a small hole in the white spot on the yolk’s surface. Use chopsticks to slowly insert the prepared filling into the yolk’s center through the hole.  
- **Key**: Make sure to aim precisely at the egg spot; if the yolk breaks, the stuffing fails. This step requires patience, and repeated practice can improve success rates.  
4. **Cook the Eggs**: Heat water in a soup pot until it is just about to boil. Gently slide the stuffed duck eggs into the pot, maintaining a gentle simmer until fully cooked.  
- **Doneness Check**: The egg white is fully set, and the filling is cooked through, then remove.  
5. **Season and Serve**: Place the cooked stuffed eggs into a bowl. Sprinkle with chopped scallions and seaweed, add a little salt, and pour in the original broth. Serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
This dish of meat-stuffed eggs is rich in high-quality protein. The combination of pork and duck eggs is suitable for those needing energy replenishment. The alcohol in the Huadiao wine evaporates during cooking, so there is no need for concern. Stuffing the eggs requires patience to aim precisely at the egg spot; multiple attempts can improve success rates.
