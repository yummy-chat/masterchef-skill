# Baked Steamed Bun Pizza
Cuisine Type: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍞 2 plain steamed buns (torn into small pieces)  
🥚 1 egg  
🫑 1/2 red bell pepper (thinly sliced)  
🟢 Green peas, as needed  
🌽 Sweet corn kernels, as needed  
🧀 Mozzarella cheese, as needed (added in two portions; shredded or cut into small pieces)  
🌭 2-3 crispy sausages (cut into thin rounds, about 0.5 cm thick)  
🌿 Dried oregano, as needed  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🫗 Stainless steel bowl (for mixing and as a baking pan)  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
🔪 Peeler  
✂️ Scissors  
🔪 Small knife  
🍴 Fork  
🔥 Oven (preheated in advance)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Tear the steamed buns into small pieces and place them in a stainless steel bowl.  
   **Note**: The bun pieces should be as fine and uniform as possible for easier mixing and shaping.  
2. Thinly slice the red bell pepper and prepare the green peas and corn kernels. Use scissors to cut the crispy sausages into small rounds about 0.5 cm thick. Set aside.  
3. Crack the egg into the bowl of torn buns. Put on disposable gloves and mix thoroughly until the egg is evenly absorbed, and the bun pieces are moist and sticky.  
   **Texture check**: The mixture should be able to form a ball when pressed together, not dry and crumbly.  
4. Press the mixed bun pieces flat and even at the bottom of the bowl to form a uniform crust.  
5. Layer the red bell pepper strips, green peas, and corn kernels evenly on top, then sprinkle the first portion of mozzarella cheese.  
6. Arrange the sausage slices neatly over the vegetable layer.  
7. Sprinkle a thicker layer of mozzarella cheese on top, and add dried oregano to taste.  
   **Key point**: The top layer of cheese should be thick enough to create a stretchy, lightly browned crust.  
8. Place the stainless steel bowl in the preheated oven’s middle rack. Bake at 180–200°C for 10–15 minutes, until the cheese on top is fully melted and displays a golden or slightly browned color.  
   **Note**: Observe the browning during baking to avoid burning; adjust the exact temperature and time based on your oven’s performance.  
9. Remove from the oven, cut into slices, and serve. The finished dish has a firm yet soft texture, with stretchy cheese, similar to a thick-crust pizza.  

## Health Tips  
Using steamed buns as the crust gives leftover buns a new texture, but be mindful of the amount of cheese and sausages to avoid excess calories. Suitable for those who enjoy creative textures.
