# Braised Pork Ribs in Clay Pot
Cuisine: Cantonese
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork spare ribs about 1 lb (cut into small pieces, wash with cornstarch to remove blood, pat dry)
🫘 Fried tofu puffs 6 pieces
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (diced)
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (diced)
🌶️ Red chili peppers appropriate amount (sliced into rings)
🧅 Scallions appropriate amount (chopped)
🧂 Salt appropriate amount
🍬 Sugar appropriate amount
🥫 Light soy sauce appropriate amount
🥫 Oyster sauce appropriate amount
🥫 Dark soy sauce appropriate amount
🌾 Cornstarch appropriate amount (for marinating)
🫒 Peanut oil appropriate amount (for sealing moisture in marinade and frying aromatics)
🍶 Rice wine appropriate amount (sprinkle before serving)

## Cookware and Equipment
🫕 High-temperature resistant clay pot
🥢 Mixing tools
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board

## Steps
1. Cut the pork ribs into small pieces, wash with cornstarch, rinse clean, and pat dry.
2. Add salt, sugar, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, and cornstarch to the ribs. Mix well. Finally, drizzle in a little peanut oil and mix to seal in moisture. Marinate to infuse flavor.
3. Heat the clay pot, add oil, and stir-fry the ginger and garlic until golden and slightly charred on the surface.
4. Spread the marinated ribs evenly over the ginger and garlic, then layer the fried tofu puffs on top of the ribs.
5. Cover the lid. Keep the heat on medium-low and braise for about 9 minutes.
**Note**: Maintain medium-low heat throughout. Use the steam circulation inside the clay pot to cook through. Avoid high heat to prevent burning the bottom.
6. Remove the lid. Drizzle rice wine along the edge of the pot to instantly release the aroma. Sprinkle with chopped scallions and red chili pepper rings. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice
Pork ribs provide high-quality protein, but fried tofu puffs absorb oil. Pair with leafy greens to balance nutrition. The medium-low heat braising keeps the meat tender and moist, suitable for those needing an energy boost.
