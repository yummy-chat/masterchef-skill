# Key Lime Cheesecake Mousse
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍪 Biscuits (or rolled oats) 60g  
🧈 Cream cheese 100g  
🥛 Thick yogurt 200g  
🍋 Key lime 1 (use green zest and juice)  
🍮 Gelatin sheets 7.5g (1.5 sheets)  
🍒 Lychee appropriate amount (for filling)  
🍬 Plum jelly (lychee sea salt key lime flavor) appropriate amount  
🧈 Melted butter 24g (about 3 bottle caps)  
🍬 White sugar 24g (about 3 bottle caps, can be substituted with sweetener)  

## Tools & Equipment  
🥤 Disposable paper cups/paper molds  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Spatula  
🧀 Grater  
🔪 Knife  
❄️ Refrigerator  

## Steps  
1. Soak gelatin sheets in ice water until fully softened and springy, then remove and squeeze out excess water.  
   **Key point**: Must use ice water to prevent the gelatin from melting prematurely and losing effectiveness.  
2. Place biscuits in a sealed bag and crush them into fine powder using a rolling pin. Add melted butter and mix until the mixture can clump together. Divide the mixture into paper cups and press firmly with a bottle cap or similar tool. Chill in the refrigerator to set.  
   **Note**: If no butter is available, substitute with milk, ensuring the mixture can clump together; the biscuit base must be pressed firmly to prevent it from falling apart after unmolding.  
3. Heat cream cheese over a water bath until smooth and free of lumps, then stir in white sugar.  
4. Add thick yogurt and mix well. Grate key lime zest into the mixture, squeeze in lime juice, and stir until evenly combined.  
5. Melt the soaked gelatin over a water bath. Remove from heat immediately and pour it into the mousse mixture, stirring quickly to combine evenly.  
6. Take out the chilled biscuit base cups. First, pour in a suitable amount of mousse batter. Add plum jelly and lychee flesh as a filling, then pour in the remaining mousse batter. Gently tap the cups to level the surface.  
7. Refrigerate overnight or freeze for about 4 hours until fully set. Tear off the paper cups and decorate the surface with grated lime zest.  

## Health Advice  
This mousse replaces some cream cheese with yogurt, reducing fat and calories. However, the gelatin and sugar content remain relatively high, so portion control is advisable. Suitable for cooling down in summer. Those with lactose intolerance should consume with caution. Diabetic patients are advised to use a sugar substitute.
