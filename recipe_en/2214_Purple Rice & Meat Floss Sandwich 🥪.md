# Purple Rice & Meat Floss Sandwich 🥪
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍞 2 slices of bread
🍚 Purple rice (to taste, pre-steamed and mixed with sugar while hot)
🍙 2 sheets of seaweed
🍡 Rice cake (to taste, pre-heated until soft and chewy)
🍖 Meat floss (to taste, for filling the edges)
🥚 Salted egg yolk (to taste, crushed or used whole)
🍶 Mayonnaise (to taste, squeezed onto the filling)
🍬 White sugar (to taste, mixed with purple rice)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥪 Sandwich press
🔪 Chef’s knife
🥄 Spoon or spreading knife (for spreading purple rice)
Tongs (for assisting transfer)

## Steps
1. **Prepare the purple rice**: Steam the purple rice in advance, mix with white sugar while hot, and let cool.
2. **Prepare the rice cake**: Heat the rice cake in advance until the center is softened.
3. **Assemble the sandwich**:
   - Take one slice of bread and spread a thick layer of purple rice over it, pressing it flat.
   - Cover with one sheet of seaweed.
   - Place the heated rice cake on top.
   - Fill the gaps around the rice cake with meat floss to create an even surface.
   - Add the salted egg yolk and squeeze mayonnaise on top.
   - Cover with another sheet of seaweed to seal the filling.
   - Spread another thick layer of purple rice over the seaweed.
   - Top with the other slice of bread, lightly press the edges together, and assemble into a thick sandwich.
4. **Press and toast**: Place the sandwich in the sandwich press, press the lid down firmly, and lock it.
   **Doneness check**: Toast until the bread surface shows golden grill marks with slight charring, allowing the fillings to meld fully from the heat.
5. **Remove**: Open the machine and use tongs to take out the sandwich.
   **Note**: The fillings will be very hot right after removal; be careful not to burn your mouth when eating.
6. **Cut and serve**: Use a chef’s knife to cut the sandwich in half, revealing distinct layers of purple rice, rice cake, meat floss, and molten salted egg yolk.

## Health Tips
Purple rice is rich in anthocyanins and dietary fiber, making it suitable for those needing antioxidant support and improved digestion. However, rice cake and mayonnaise are relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to pair with a light beverage for balance.
