# Henan Steamed Noodles
Cuisine: Henan Cuisine
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Ingredients
- Dried noodles (round ones recommended)
- Pork belly
- Garlic scapes
- Scallion
- Ginger
- Garlic

🧂 Seasonings
- Cooking oil
- Light soy sauce
- Dark soy sauce
- Oyster sauce
- Salt
- Chicken bouillon
- Thirteen-spice powder
- Cooking wine
- Sesame oil

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Kitchen Tools
- Pot
- Steaming rack
- Chef's knife
- Spatula
- Extra bowl

## Steps
1. First steaming: Fill the pot with water to about 70% of its capacity. Bring to a boil, then place the steaming rack inside.
2. Spread the dried noodles evenly, drizzle 5ml of cooking oil over them and rub to coat evenly. Steam for 15 minutes.
3. Remove the noodles and steaming rack, set aside. Discard the water in the pot.
4. Prepare the vegetables: Cut the pork belly into 2mm thick slices; cut garlic scapes into 3cm sections; slice scallion into 0.2cm thin rounds; shred ginger into fine strips; crush garlic and chop into 1mm pieces.
5. Stir-frying: Dry the pot over heat, then add 3ml of cooking oil. Swirl the pot to coat two-thirds of the inner surface with oil.
6. Over medium heat, add the pork belly slices and stir-fry for 1 minute.
7. Add scallion, ginger, garlic, and cooking wine. Continue stir-frying for 1 minute.
8. Add the garlic scape sections and stir-fry for 1 minute.
9. Seasoning: Add dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, chicken bouillon, and thirteen-spice powder. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
10. Add 500ml of water until the vegetables are submerged. Simmer for 1 minute.
11. Add the steamed noodles and stir continuously for 3 minutes until the noodles are evenly colored. Turn off the heat.
12. Transfer all the noodles and vegetables into an extra bowl.
13. Second steaming: Fill the pot with cold water to about 70% of its capacity. Place the steaming rack, and spread the noodles and vegetables evenly on top.
14. Once the water boils, steam over high heat for 15 minutes. Remove from the pot.
15. Drizzle with 10ml of sesame oil and serve.

## Health Advice
This Henan steamed noodle dish balances carbohydrates and fiber with pork belly and garlic scapes, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost or fighting hunger. Be mindful of the amount of dark soy sauce and salt used to avoid excessive sodium intake. The double steaming process makes the noodles easier to digest.
