# Sweet Potato Steamed Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍠 Sweet potato puree 300g
🌾 Flour 200g
🌽 Corn starch 40g
🍬 White sugar 20g
🥚 Egg 1
🫗 Yeast 3g
💧 Warm water as needed
🍇 Raisins as needed
🫐 Red dates as needed
🫘 Sesame seeds as needed
🫒 Cooking oil as needed

## Cookware & Equipment
🍚 Rice cooker
🥣 Stainless steel bowl/ basin
🥄 Silicone spatula
🥄 Spoon
🔪 Knife
🪵 Cutting board

## Steps
1.  Place sweet potato puree, flour, corn starch, white sugar, and yeast into a container, and initially stir into a granular mixture.
2.  Crack in the egg, and gradually add warm water at about 35℃ in small amounts, stirring continuously, to form a batter that is slightly thick and flows slowly.
    **Note**: Water that is too hot will kill the yeast, causing the fermentation to fail.
3.  Leave the batter in the rice cooker inner pot, cover with the lid, and perform the first fermentation until it expands to twice its original volume, with a dense honeycomb structure inside.
4.  Add raisins and a little cooking oil to the fermented batter, and use a spatula to stir thoroughly to release gas, eliminate large air bubbles, and evenly incorporate the oil and dried fruit.
    **Key point**: Adding oil helps prevent sticking and keeps the finished product from drying out and hardening after cooling.
5.  Dip the spatula in a little water and smooth the surface of the batter.
6.  Arrange red dates on the surface, then sprinkle with raisins and sesame seeds for decoration.
7.  Cover with the lid and perform a second proofing for about 15 minutes, until the batter visibly expands again and the gaps between the decorations become smaller.
8.  Secure the lid tightly, activate the "Wood Fire Rice" or standard rice cooking program on the rice cooker, and steam.
    **Key point**: Avoid opening the lid frequently throughout the entire steaming process.
9.  After the program ends, let it rest covered for 3 minutes before opening the lid to prevent the cake from shrinking and hardening due to the temperature drop.
10. Run a spatula along the inner wall of the pot to loosen the cake, invert to release it, let it cool slightly, then cut into pieces.

## Health Advice
Sweet potato steamed cake replaces part of the flour with sweet potato puree, increasing dietary fiber and vitamin A content. It is suitable for those with weaker digestive function or those who need to control refined carbohydrate intake. Pay attention to controlling the amount of white sugar used; thorough fermentation can make the texture softer.
