# Fat-Reducing Cabbage and Tofu Soup
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 Cabbage 2-3 leaves (cut into pieces)  
🧈 Tofu 1 block (either firm or silken tofu)  
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms 1-2 (can be scored with a cross pattern)  
🍄‍🟫 Enoki mushrooms appropriate amount (roots removed)  
🥩 Sliced pork belly appropriate amount (can be substituted with other meats)  
🍊 Kumquat 1 (for dipping sauce, can be substituted with other citrus juice)  
🥃 Light soy sauce 1 teaspoon  
🍶 Sake (or cooking wine) appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt appropriate amount (for final seasoning)  
🥄 Low-sodium light soy sauce appropriate amount (for dipping sauce)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, knife  
🍲 Small clay pot  
🔥 Stove  
🥣 Small bowl (for mixing dipping sauce)  

## Instructions  
1. Cut the tofu into pieces, score the shiitake mushrooms with a cross pattern, remove the roots from the enoki mushrooms, cut the cabbage into pieces, and prepare the sliced pork belly.  
2. Arrange all ingredients one by one into the small clay pot. Add water to seven-tenths full, then add one teaspoon of light soy sauce.  
   **Note**: Use only a small amount of soy sauce to enhance flavor; do not add too much.  
3. Heat over medium heat until boiling, then continue cooking over medium heat for 5 minutes.  
4. After removing the lid, drizzle in a small amount of sake or cooking wine, and add salt to adjust the saltiness to taste.  
   **Key point**: The amounts of sake and salt can be flexibly adjusted according to personal taste.  
5. Serve with citrus dipping sauce for enhanced flavor.  

## Health Suggestions  
This cabbage and tofu soup is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for weight loss periods or light diets. The pork belly can be replaced with lean meat to reduce saturated fat intake. Tofu and mushrooms provide plant-based protein and dietary fiber, cabbage supplies vitamin C, and the overall calorie content is low, making it easy to control energy intake.
