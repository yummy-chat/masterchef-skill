# Air Soufflé  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥚 4 room-temperature eggs (separate egg whites and yolks; bowl must be grease-free and water-free)  
🍬 50g white sugar (add to egg whites)  
🧈 Butter, as needed (grease the pan to prevent sticking)  
🍯 Honey, as needed (drizzle as final sauce)  
🧂 3g salt (add to egg yolks)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🥣 Large glass bowl (for whipping egg whites and mixing batter)  
🥣 Small glass bowl (for stirring egg yolks)  
🌀 Electric hand mixer  
🥄 Silicone spatula  
🖌️ Silicone brush  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🫙 Transparent lid  
🍴 Fork/spoon  
🔥 Induction cooktop  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Mix the egg yolks with salt and set aside; add white sugar to the egg whites.  
2. Use an electric hand mixer to whip the egg whites until clear lines form and the mixer head can pull up stiff or slightly curved peaks.  
   **Key point**: Whipping must be thorough—this is essential for the soufflé’s fluffiness.  
3. Pour the egg yolk mixture into the meringue and gently fold from the bottom upward using a silicone spatula until evenly combined.  
   **Key point**: Do not stir in circles or shake vigorously, to avoid deflating and causing the final product to collapse.  
4. Heat a non-stick frying pan over the lowest heat; set the induction cooktop to 100 watts. Evenly brush a layer of melted butter using a silicone brush.  
5. Pour the mixed batter into the center of the pan, forming a round shape. Immediately cover with a lid and cook over the lowest heat for 15 minutes.  
   **Texture check**: The bottom should be set and golden brown.  
6. Turn off the heat. Carefully fold the soufflé in half into a crescent shape using a silicone spatula. Transfer to a plate.  
7. Drizzle honey on the surface and enjoy.  
   **Texture description**: The outer layer is slightly crisp, while the inside is soft and airy like a cloud.  

## Health Advice  
This air soufflé’s calories mainly come from eggs and sugar. It is suitable as a light afternoon treat, but those managing blood sugar or losing weight should consume in moderation. Pairing with fresh fruit is recommended to balance the sweetness. Ensure thorough whipping to avoid deflating and resulting in a less fluffy texture.
