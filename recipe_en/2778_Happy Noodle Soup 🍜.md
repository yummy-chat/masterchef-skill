# Happy Noodle Soup 🍜
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings

🫘 Tofu 1 block (cut into pieces)

🍳 Non-stick pan
🔥 Gas stove
🥄 Ladle
🥣 Heatproof bowl (for making chili oil)
🍜 Noodle bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Strainer

## Instructions
1. **Make chili oil**: Place chili flakes in a heatproof bowl and moisten with a small amount of sesame oil; heat oil used for frying eggs until smoking hot, pour it into the bowl to release aroma, and set aside.
   - Note: Moisten chili flakes with sesame oil first to prevent burning when pouring hot oil.
2. **Fry eggs**: Heat oil in a non-stick pan, crack in eggs, fry until set, then remove and drain oil. Set aside.
3. **Fry tofu**: Using the remaining oil in the pan, add tofu pieces and fry until the bottom is golden and slightly crispy. Flip and continue frying until both sides are set.
4. **Fry shrimp**: Add prepared shrimp, drizzle in cooking wine to remove any fishy smell, and stir-fry together with the tofu until the shrimp shells turn red and the oil takes on a reddish hue.
   - Key point: Frying shrimp to release red oil helps achieve a rich, milky soup base.
5. **Simmer the soup**: Pour enough boiling water into the pan, add the fried eggs, bring to a boil over high heat, and continue simmering for 5 minutes until the soup turns thick and milky white.
   - Key point: Boiling water must be used; this is essential for a milky white broth.
6. **Prepare bowl base**: In a noodle bowl, add chicken bouillon, salt, sugar, white pepper, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, and light soy sauce. Add a small piece of lard.
   - Substitute: If lard is unavailable, butter can be used to add a creamy flavor.
7. **Pour soup**: Ladle the thick, milky white soup from the pan into the bowl, stirring to fully dissolve the seasonings and fats.
8. **Cook noodles**: Add the hanging noodles to the pan and cook until they reach the desired tenderness.
   - Tip: Adjust the amount of noodles according to personal appetite.
9. **Assemble bowl**: Lift the cooked noodles into the soup bowl and arrange the fried eggs, tofu, and shrimp on top.
10. **Garnish**: Sprinkle scallions mixed with sesame oil over the surface and drizzle with chili oil.
   - Finished dish characteristics: Soup is thick and milky white, scallion and chili aromas blend, noodles are smooth, and toppings are abundant.

## Health Advice
This dish provides high-quality protein from eggs, tofu, and shrimp, paired with a milky white broth made with lard. It is nutritionally balanced but relatively high in calories, making it suitable for those with high physical exertion or needing muscle gain. Pay attention to controlling the amount of chili oil and salt; those with high blood pressure or sensitive stomachs should consume with caution.
