# Margaret Biscuits
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧈 Cooked egg yolk  
🧈 Butter  
🌾 Cake flour  
🌾 Cornstarch  
🧂 White sugar  
🧂 Salt

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔥 Oven  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Mixing tool

## Cooking Steps
1. Melt the butter by heating it over water, and grind the cooked egg yolk into a fine powder for later use.
2. Add white sugar, salt, and the crushed cooked egg yolk to the melted butter, and stir thoroughly until well combined.
3. Sift in the cake flour and cornstarch, and knead into a uniform dough.
4. Evenly divide the dough into small pieces weighing about 8 grams each, and roll them into balls.
5. Gently press the center of each small dough ball with your thumb, allowing natural cracks to form.
6. Preheat the oven to 150°C, place the small dough balls on the middle rack, and bake for 20 minutes.
7. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly to room temperature before serving.

## Health Advice
Margaret biscuits feature a unique sandy and crumbly texture thanks to the cooked egg yolk, making them an ideal afternoon tea snack. They are relatively high in butter and sugar; it is recommended to enjoy 3-5 pieces per serving and pair with sugar-free tea.
