# Soufflé Omelette
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 2 eggs (separate egg whites and yolks)
🧂 1-2 grams salt
🍬 3 bottle caps of white sugar (about 16 grams)
🍬 Powdered sugar as needed (for decoration)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🥣 Small stainless steel bowl (for holding and mixing egg yolks)
🥣 Enamel bowl or large bowl (for holding and whipping egg whites)
🥢 Chopsticks (for initially stirring egg yolks)
🥄 Manual whisk (for whipping egg whites)
🥄 Spatula (for folding batter and scooping meringue)
🍳 Non-stick pan (a larger pan is recommended)
♨️ Electric cooking pot or griddle (a griddle ensures more even heat distribution on the pan bottom)
🍳 Lid (for steaming the inside until cooked)

## Cooking Steps
1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks, ensuring the container for the egg whites is free of oil, water, and any yolk residue.
2. Add 1-2 grams of salt to the egg yolks and stir evenly with chopsticks.
3. Add 3 bottle caps of white sugar to the egg whites, and whisk at high speed with a whisk until stiff peaks form. Aim to finish whipping within 10 minutes to prevent deflating.
4. Take about 1/3 of the whipped meringue and add it to the egg yolk mixture. Quickly fold together, then pour the mixture back into the remaining meringue. Using a scooping-from-the-bottom folding method, quickly and gently mix from the bottom upwards. The technique must be light and fast to prevent the meringue from deflating; if any lumps appear, quickly break them apart by hand.
5. Preheat the pan, then pour in the batter. Gently spread it flat with a spatula, cover with the lid, and cook over medium-low heat for about 3 minutes and 30 seconds.
6. Reduce the heat to low and continue cooking for another 3 minutes and 30 seconds. Lift the lid to check if the surface has set.
7. Once the surface is set, fold the egg pancake in half. Flip it over, cover again, and cook for about 1-2 minutes, until the bottom is golden brown and the inside is fully cooked. To check for doneness: if it smells like cake when you bring it close, it is ready.
8. Remove from the pan, sift an appropriate amount of powdered sugar over the top for decoration, and serve.

## Health Tips
This soufflé omelette is high in protein and low in fat, but it contains about 16 grams of added sugar. It is recommended to reduce the amount as needed or substitute with zero-calorie sweetener, making it suitable for occasional consumption during a weight loss period. When whipping the egg whites, ensure the container is free of water and oil, and the folding motion must be light and quick; otherwise, the meringue may deflate, affecting the final fluffiness of the dish.
