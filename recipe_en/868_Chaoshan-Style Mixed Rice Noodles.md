# Chaoshan-Style Mixed Rice Noodles
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef, to taste (raw beef slices)  
🍢 Beef balls, to taste  
🥓 Pork cake, to taste  
🍡 Pork balls, to taste  
🥟 Fish skin dumplings, to taste  
🍜 Rice noodles, to taste  
🥬 Lettuce, to taste  
🐟 Fried fish skin, a few pieces  
🌿 Cilantro, to taste  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🐟 Dried fish powder, about one small spoonful  
🧄 Fried garlic bits, to taste  
🌶️ Chili sauce, to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍲 Stove and pot  
🥢 Tongs  
✂️ Scissors  
🥄 Spoon  
🕸️ Strainer  
🍜 Bowl  
📡 Microwave (optional, for quickly heating rice noodles)  

## Cooking Steps
1. Quickly heat the rice noodles in the microwave, or blanch them in boiling water until soft, then drain and set aside.
2. Cut the cilantro into small pieces; cut the various balls, pork cake, and fish skin dumplings as needed; prepare the fried fish skin and set aside.
3. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add salt and dried fish powder to make the broth.  
**Key point**: Dried fish powder is the soul for enhancing umami. After boiling, use a strainer to remove any residue, keeping the broth clear.
4. Lay the rice noodles in a bowl, then arrange the cilantro, raw beef slices, balls, pork cake, and fish skin dumplings on top.
5. Pour the boiling broth directly into the bowl, using the heat of the broth to cook the beef until it turns a light pink.  
**Note**: The broth must be hot enough to ensure all raw ingredients are fully cooked.
6. Sprinkle fried garlic bits on top for aroma; add chili sauce to taste before eating.
7. Finally, place two pieces of fried fish skin on top for a crispy texture, and enjoy while hot.

## Health Advice
This Chaoshan-style mixed rice noodle dish is rich in high-quality protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable for those needing a quick energy boost. The beef slices must be thoroughly cooked by the boiling broth to ensure food safety. While fried garlic bits and fried fish skin are the finishing touches, they are high in calories, so consume in moderation during weight loss periods.
