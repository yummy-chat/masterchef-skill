# Black Pepper Prawns  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦐 Large prawns (tiger prawns) – about 250g  
🧅 Onion – a small amount (finely chopped)  
🧄 Garlic – a small amount (finely chopped)  
🌿 Black peppercorns – to taste  
🧈 Butter – a small amount  
🍶 Cooking wine – to taste  
🥄 Soy sauce – to taste  
💧 Water – to taste  
🧂 Salt – to taste  
🍬 Sugar – to taste  
🦪 Oyster sauce – a small amount  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Wok/frying pan  
🥄 Large spoon/ladle  
🥣 Slotted spoon/strainer  
🫙 Bowl  
🪣 Basin  
🔪 Chef’s knife & cutting board  

## Cooking Steps  
1. **Prepare the prawns**: Snip off the sharp head spines, slit open the back, remove the vein, rinse clean, and drain well.  
   **Key point**: Butterflying allows the black pepper flavor to fully penetrate the prawn meat.  
2. **Prepare the aromatics**: Finely chop the onion and garlic; set aside the black peppercorns.  
3. **Blanch**: Bring water to a boil in a wok, add a splash of cooking wine, then add the prawns. Once they turn from blue-green to red and the surface just changes color, remove them and quickly rinse under cold water.  
   **Note**: This step only removes surface impurities—no need to fully cook through.  
4. **Flash-fry**: Heat the wok and add oil. When the oil reaches about 160°C, add the blanched prawns and fry quickly until the shells are crispy, then remove.  
   **Key point**: High heat and short frying time prevent the meat from becoming tough, maintaining a springy texture.  
5. **Prepare the sauce**:  
   - Leave a little oil in the wok, add the crushed black peppercorns, and stir-fry over low heat until fragrant.  
   - Add the butter, chopped onion, and minced garlic; stir-fry until aromatic.  
   - Splash in cooking wine and soy sauce along the edge of the wok, add some water, then season with salt, sugar, and a little oyster sauce. Bring to a boil over high heat.  
6. **Reduce and coat**: After the sauce is fragrant, add the fried prawns and stir-fry quickly over high heat. When the liquid is mostly reduced and evenly coats and seeps into the prawn meat, remove from heat.  
   **Key point**: Reducing over high heat makes the black pepper flavor adhere more intensely to the prawns.  

## Health Advice  
This black pepper prawn dish is high in protein and low in fat, suitable for weight loss or muscle building. However, the frying and butter increase the calorie content, so it’s recommended to control the amounts and pair with vegetables for a more balanced meal. Be sure to remove the vein thoroughly to ensure safe eating.
