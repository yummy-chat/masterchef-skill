# Homemade Sour Plum Drink
Cuisine: Beijing Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April to August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫐 Smoked Plum 40g
🍒 Hawthorn 35g
🌸 Roselle (Hibiscus Sabdariffa) 10g
🍊 Dried Tangerine Peel 15g
🌹 Rose Petals 10g
🫐 Dried Mulberries 8g
🌿 Licorice Root 2g (Note: Do not use too much, or it will have a strong medicinal flavor)
🌱 Dried Mint Leaves 5g
🍬 Old Rock Sugar 200g (adjust to taste)
🌼 Dried Osmanthus Flowers, to taste
💧 Purified Water, about 2.5-3 liters

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🫗 Large Glass Bowl (for washing and soaking)
🥘 Deep Glass Pot with Lid
🥄 Soup Ladle
🥅 Strainer/Slotted Spoon
🍾 Glass Sealable Bottle (for portioned storage)

## Preparation Steps
1. Weigh all dried ingredients according to the proportions and mix them evenly.
2. Rinse the mixed dried ingredients clean under running water.
3. Place the washed ingredients in the pot, add 2.5 to 3 liters of purified water, and soak for 20 to 30 minutes to allow the flavors to release initially.
4. Bring to a boil over high heat, immediately cover with the lid, then reduce to the lowest heat and simmer covered for 28 minutes, until the liquid is deep red and rich.
5. Turn off the heat, add the old rock sugar and dried osmanthus flowers, then cover and let steep for a moment until the sugar is dissolved and the osmanthus aroma is infused.
6. Thoroughly remove all the solid residue with a slotted spoon to ensure a clear liquid.
7. Let cool slightly, then pour into glass bottles, seal, and refrigerate. It tastes better when served chilled.

## Health Advice
This sour plum drink is rich in natural ingredients like smoked plum and hawthorn, which can help produce body fluids, relieve summer heat, and promote digestion. It is especially suitable for drinking when you have no appetite during summer. Note that licorice root should not be used excessively; diabetics can reduce the amount of rock sugar.
