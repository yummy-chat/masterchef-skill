# Stir-Fried Okra with Eggs
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🍳 Wok

🔪 Knife and cutting board
🥣 Stainless steel bowl

## Cooking Steps
1. Blanch the okra: Add water to the wok, add a pinch of salt, bring to a boil over high heat. Add the okra and an appropriate amount of lard, and blanch until just cooked.
2. Cool and slice: Remove the okra and immediately immerse it in cold water to cool quickly. Drain thoroughly and slice evenly into thin pieces.
3. Prepare the eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt, and whisk vigorously with chopsticks until the egg mixture is uniform.
4. Coat the wok and scramble the eggs: Heat the wok, coat it with oil, then pour out the excess. Add a base layer of oil and heat it. Pour in the egg mixture and stir-fry quickly over high heat until fully set. Break the eggs into small pieces, then remove and set aside, draining any excess oil.
5. Sauté aromatics and combine: Leave a small amount of oil in the wok. Add the onion strips and sauté until fragrant. Then add the okra slices and stir-fry quickly over medium-high heat. Drizzle the light soy sauce along the edge of the wok.
6. Season: Add a pinch of salt and ground white pepper, and stir-fry quickly to combine.
   **Note**: The okra already has some base flavor from blanching, so use seasonings sparingly at this stage.
7. Mix and serve: Return the scrambled eggs to the wok. Stir-fry everything together quickly with the okra and onion until well combined. Remove from heat and plate immediately.
   **Key Point**: Avoid prolonged stir-frying after adding the okra to maintain its crisp and tender texture.

## Health Tips
Okra is rich in dietary fiber and mucilaginous protein, which aid intestinal motility and blood sugar control. Eggs provide high-quality protein. This dish is suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or managing the "three highs" (high blood pressure, high blood sugar, high cholesterol). However, since lard is added during blanching, be mindful of controlling overall oil intake.
