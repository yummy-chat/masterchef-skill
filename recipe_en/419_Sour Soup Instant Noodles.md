# Sour Soup Instant Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍜 Instant noodle cake 1 piece
🍅 Tomato about 1 (peeled)
🧄 Minced garlic as needed
🧂 Instant noodle seasoning packet 1 piece
🧂 Light soy sauce as needed
🧂 Vinegar as needed
🦪 Oyster sauce as needed
🍬 Sugar as needed
🌶️ Chili oil as needed
🥫 Ketchup as needed
💧 Water as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🥖 Rolling pin
🥣 Glass bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🥛 Measuring cup/bowl

## Instructions
1. Place the peeled tomato in a frying pan. Add the instant noodle seasoning packet and minced garlic. Use a rolling pin to directly crush and mix all ingredients in the pan into a thick sauce.
   **Key point**: Peeling the tomato in advance improves texture; using a rolling pin to crush helps the tomato release juice quickly and mix more evenly with the seasonings.
2. In another bowl, mix light soy sauce, vinegar, oyster sauce, sugar, chili oil, ketchup, and an appropriate amount of water. Stir well to create the sour soup sauce.
   **Key point**: This sauce is the flavor core of the entire noodle dish; adjust the ratio of seasonings according to personal taste.
3. Pour the prepared sour soup sauce into the pan, combine with the tomato base, and add enough water so that it just covers the noodle cake to be added later. Bring to a boil over high heat.
   **Key point**: The water should be level with the noodle cake to ensure the cake fully absorbs the soup.
4. Once the water is boiling, add the instant noodle cake. Use chopsticks to loosen the noodle cake. Cook over high heat until the noodles become soft, fully cooked, turn red in color, and have absorbed the soup.
   **Key point**: Cook quickly over high heat to avoid the noodles becoming too soft; the noodles are done when they are plump and flavorful.

## Health Tips
Tomatoes and vinegar add vitamin C and an appetite-stimulating effect to this noodle dish, making it suitable when you have poor appetite. However, it is high in sodium, so people with high blood pressure should drink less of the soup.
