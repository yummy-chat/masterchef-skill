# Sichuan Pepper Black Fish Fillets
Cuisine: Sichuan  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All seasons

## Instructions  
1. **Prepare the fish fillets**: Remove the slime from the black fish, debone, and cut diagonally into evenly thin slices. Add salt and water, rub repeatedly until the water runs clear, then drain and squeeze dry.  
2. **Marinate the fish fillets**: Add salt to the fillets, gradually add scallion-ginger water, and stir in one direction until the water is fully absorbed and the fillets become sticky. Finally, add cornstarch and mix well to coat. Toss the fish bones with the remaining scallion-ginger water and set aside.  
3. **Prepare the aromatics**: Mince scallions, ginger, and garlic; cut pickled chili peppers into sections; chop green and red chili peppers finely. Soak some Sichuan green peppercorns in water and drain.  
4. **Pan-fry the fish bones**: Heat cooking oil in a wok, add the fish bones, and fry over medium heat until both sides are golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.  
5. **Prepare the broth**: In another wok, heat lard, add scallions, ginger, garlic, pickled chili peppers, and some Sichuan green peppercorns, stir until fragrant. Add the fried fish bones and stir-fry, then pour in boiling water. Bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, reduce to medium heat, and cook until the broth turns rich and white.  
6. **Season**: Add salt, MSG, white pepper powder, rice vinegar, oyster sauce, and Sichuan pepper oil to the broth. Stir well.  
7. **Cook the base**: Add the potato noodles and cook until done. Use a slotted spoon to transfer them to a large bowl, spreading them evenly on the bottom.  
8. **Cook the fish fillets**: Reduce the broth to low heat to keep it barely simmering. Add the fish fillets one by one. Once all are added, turn the heat to high and cook until all fillets turn white. Immediately remove from heat and pour everything (broth and fillets) into the bowl.  
9. **Sizzle with hot oil**: Heat cooking oil in another wok until smoking. Add the chopped green and red chili peppers and the remaining Sichuan green peppercorns, fry until fragrant, then quickly pour the hot oil over the fish fillets.  

## Health Advice  
This dish is high in protein and low in fat. The black fish fillets, paired with a spicy and numbing seasoning, are suitable for fitness muscle-building and satisfying cravings. It is recommended to control the amount of oil and salt. People with high blood pressure should consume less of the broth. Pairing with vegetables makes for a more balanced meal.
