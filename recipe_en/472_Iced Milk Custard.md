# Iced Milk Custard
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥛 Milk 1 cup (measured with a regular household cup, protein content must be ≥3.2%)
🍚 Fermented rice wine (jiuniang) Take about 1/3 cup of juice (strain and use only the liquid, discard the rice grains)
🍬 White sugar To taste (optional, add according to personal preference)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥛 Household glass cup (as a measuring tool)
🍳 Milk pot
🔍 Fine-mesh strainer
🥄 Spoon
🍲 Steamer
🧻 Plastic wrap
🦷 Toothpick (or sharp object)
🥣 Heat-resistant steaming bowl

## Instructions
1. Pour the fermented rice wine into a fine-mesh strainer to extract the clear juice. Retain only the rice wine juice and discard the rice grains.
2. Pour the milk into a milk pot and heat over medium heat, stirring constantly to prevent sticking. Heat until it is just about to boil and foam up, then immediately remove from heat. Let it cool until a milk skin forms on the surface.
3. Use the same cup to measure 1/3 cup of rice wine juice. Remove the milk skin from the milk's surface, then pour the rice wine juice into the milk pot and stir well.
4. Pour the mixture into a heat-resistant steaming bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and use a toothpick to poke several small holes in the plastic wrap.
5. Fill a steamer with cold water, place the bowl inside, and cover with the lid. Heat over high flame. Once steam is visibly rising from the lid's edge, reduce to medium-low heat and steam for 25 to 30 minutes, until the liquid is fully set and has a smooth, tender texture.
6. Once steamed, remove from the steamer and refrigerate until chilled. Before serving, drizzle with syrup or sprinkle with dried osmanthus flowers as garnish.

## Health Advice
This dessert is high in protein and low in fat, making it suitable for summer refreshment or as a healthy sweet treat. Fermented rice wine contains a small amount of alcohol, so pregnant women and children should consume it in moderation. It is recommended to use high-protein milk to ensure proper setting.
