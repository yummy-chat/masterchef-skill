# No-Top Instant Noodles  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Season: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍅 1 tomato (peeled and diced)  
🥛 1 bag of milk  
🍖 Canned luncheon meat, as needed (pre-sliced and pan-fried)  
🥚 Eggs, as needed (pre-fried)  
🥩 Beef slices, as needed  
🍜 1 pack of instant noodles  
🥬 Lettuce, as needed  
🧄 Garlic, as needed (crushed and minced)  
🌶️ Chili flakes, as needed  
🍅 1 tbsp ketchup  
🧂 1 swirl of dark soy sauce  
🧂 1 swirl of light soy sauce  
🧂 A pinch of salt  
🍬 1 tbsp sugar  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Frying pan  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🫙 Glass bowl  
🫕 Pot lid  
🍽️ Bowl  

## Instructions  
1. Score a cross on the top of the tomato, blanch with boiling water to peel, then dice and set aside. Submerge the bagged milk in hot water to warm. Mince the garlic. Slice and pan-fry the luncheon meat, and fry the eggs, then set aside.  
2. Using the same pan from the luncheon meat, add a little oil. Sauté the minced garlic until fragrant, then add the diced tomato and cook until soft, mushy, and juice is released.  
3. Reduce to low heat. Add chili flakes, ketchup, dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, salt, and sugar. Stir-fry evenly until the mixture becomes a thick sauce.  
4. Add a small amount of boiling water and bring to a boil over high heat. First, add the beef slices, then place the instant noodle cake. Add a little more water if needed and cook for about 2 minutes, then turn off the heat.  
5. After turning off the heat, pour in the warmed milk, cover with a lid, and let it sit for 2 minutes to allow the noodles to fully absorb the broth.  
6. Remove the lid, add the lettuce, and let the residual heat wilt it. Ladle the noodles and broth into a bowl, then top with the prepared luncheon meat and fried eggs.  
   **Key Points**: Warm the milk beforehand to prevent the broth temperature from dropping suddenly when added. Add the lettuce last to keep it crisp. Cook the base sauce over low heat to avoid burning.  

## Health Advice  
The combination of tomato and milk makes the instant noodle broth richer while providing calcium and vitamin C, perfect for those who want a quick yet tasty meal. However, luncheon meat and sauces are high in sodium, so those with high blood pressure should avoid drinking too much of the broth.
