# Golden Corn Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌽 Sweet corn 1 ear
🥄 Cornstarch (potato starch) appropriate amount
🥛 Milk appropriate amount
🍬 White granulated sugar appropriate amount (for sprinkling after cooking)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted spoon
✂️ Kitchen scissors
🥄 Spoon
🛍️ Plastic bag

## Cooking Steps
1. Use kitchen scissors to cut lengthwise along the corn cob and tear it open, then remove the corn kernels completely.
2. Place the corn kernels into a plastic bag, add an appropriate amount of cornstarch and milk, seal the bag, and shake thoroughly so that each kernel is evenly coated with the starch mixture.
3. Pour cooking oil into the wok and heat to 60–70% hot. Add the coated corn kernels and gently stir with a slotted spoon. Fry until the kernels float and the surface turns slightly golden, then remove and drain excess oil.
4. Leave a small amount of oil in the wok. Add the prepared corn kernels and quickly spread them flat with the back of a spoon, shaping them into an even, round pancake.
   **Note**: Spread quickly and evenly to ensure consistent thickness.
5. Fry over medium-low heat. Once the bottom is set and golden brown, carefully flip the pancake and fry the other side until golden and crispy.
6. Slide the cooked corn pancake onto a plate and sprinkle white granulated sugar evenly over it while still hot.

## Health Advice
Corn is rich in dietary fiber and B vitamins, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost. However, due to the frying process and sugar content, it is recommended to control portion size and pair with vegetables for a balanced diet.
