# All-Purpose Garlic Butter
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🧈 200g unsalted butter (high-quality, softened at room temperature until very easy to press)  
🧄 4 cloves garlic  
🌿 Fresh parsley (curly parsley), to taste (leaves only)  
🧂 Sea salt, to taste  
🍋 Lemon juice, a small amount  
🍋 Zest of 1 lemon (yellow peel only)  

## Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Fine grater  
🥣 Small food processor (or knife)  
🥣 Glass mixing bowl  
🥄 2 metal spoons  
🎞️ Plastic wrap  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🍳 Frying pan (for demonstration of sautéing)  

## Instructions  
1. Cut the butter into small cubes and let it soften at room temperature until a spatula can press it easily.  
2. Using a fine grater, grate the garlic into a fine paste; chop the parsley leaves in a food processor or finely mince them with a knife.  
3. Add the garlic paste, chopped parsley, sea salt, a small amount of lemon juice, and the grated lemon zest to the softened butter.  
4. Stir thoroughly with a spatula until the color is evenly distributed.  
5. **For immediate use**: Heat two metal spoons in water at about 50℃, dry them, then use a push-pull motion to scoop the butter, shaping it into olive-shaped balls. Place directly on bread or hot dishes.  
6. **For long-term storage**: Spread the mixed butter onto plastic wrap, roll it tightly like a candy into a firm cylinder, twist both ends, and refrigerate. Slice as needed when using.  

## Health Tip  
Using garlic and lemon zest with butter enhances flavor while providing vitamin C and antioxidants, making it suitable for a daily diet balancing taste and health. Pair with whole wheat bread or steamed vegetables to reduce additional fat intake.
