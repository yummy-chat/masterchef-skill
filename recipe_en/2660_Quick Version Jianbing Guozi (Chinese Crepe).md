# Quick Version Jianbing Guozi (Chinese Crepe)
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥟 Wonton wrappers, as needed (about 10 pieces, to replace traditional crepe skin and crispy cracker)
🥚 Egg, 1 piece (to spread on the crepe)
🍖 Ham slices, about 3 slices
🥩 Pork floss, about 10g
🥒 Cucumber, about 1 piece (shredded into thin strips)
🌰 Black sesame seeds, as needed (to sprinkle on the egg skin)
🫘 Sweet bean paste-style red prepared sauce, as needed (to brush on the egg skin)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for frying the crepe skin and crispy cracker)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🥣 Aluminum foil container (for frying the crispy cracker and heating corn with cheese)
🔪 Kitchen knife
🥒 Vegetable peeler
🥢 Chopsticks

## Preparation Steps
1. Heat a non-stick frying pan over low heat. Place a wonton wrapper in the pan and fry until the bottom turns slightly yellow.
   **Note**: Fry slowly over low heat to avoid burning the wrapper.
2. Crack an egg onto the wrapper. Use chopsticks to quickly spread the egg liquid evenly, ensuring the wrapper is evenly coated. Meanwhile, place the aluminum foil container with cooking oil next to the frying pan. Add a small piece of wonton wrapper to fry into a crispy cracker. Heat over low heat for about 30 seconds until the egg liquid is half-set; fry the crispy cracker for about 1 minute until the surface is golden and bubbly, and the texture is crisp.
   **Note**: The oil temperature should not be too high when frying the crispy cracker, to prevent burning.
3. Once the egg skin is fully set, flip it over. Evenly brush the red prepared sauce onto the egg skin. Layer with ham slices, sprinkle with pork floss, and add the fried crispy cracker and cucumber shreds.
   **Note**: The fillings can be adjusted according to personal preference.
4. Tightly roll up the crepe, then cut it in half and plate.
   **Note**: Roll the crepe as tightly as possible to prevent the fillings from falling apart.

## Health Suggestions
Using wonton wrappers instead of the crispy cracker can reduce calories, making it suitable for those managing their weight. Eggs and pork floss provide protein, while cucumber shreds add a refreshing texture. Sweet bean paste has a high sodium content, so use it in moderation.
