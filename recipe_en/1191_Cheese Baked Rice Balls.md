# Cheese Baked Rice Balls
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥦 Broccoli, about 1 small floret  
🥕 Carrot, about 1 small section  
🍚 Cold cooked rice, 1 bowl  
🐟 Canned tuna, 1-2 tbsp (drained)  
🌽 Frozen corn kernels, to taste  
🧀 Shredded cheese, to taste  
🥗 Mayonnaise, to taste  
🍘 Seaweed sheets, 3 small pieces  
🤍 White sesame seeds, to taste  
🍶 Soy sauce, to taste (for brushing the surface)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
✂️ Kitchen scissors  
🔪 Food processor  
🍚 Rice cooker inner pot  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
♨️ Air fryer  
📄 Baking paper  
🥄 Spoon

## Instructions
1. Cut broccoli and carrot into small pieces, then place them in a food processor and pulse until finely minced.  
   **Key point**: The vegetables must be processed until very fine to avoid affecting the texture and shape of the rice balls.
2. In the rice cooker inner pot, combine the cold rice, drained tuna, corn kernels, and minced vegetables.
3. Add an appropriate amount of shredded cheese and mayonnaise. Wearing disposable gloves, mix everything thoroughly until the mixture is evenly colored and sticky, forming a uniform rice ball base.  
   **Key point**: Ensure even mixing so that every bite tastes consistent.
4. Take an appropriate amount of the rice mixture and shape it into a tight triangular rice ball using your hands. Brush the surface evenly with soy sauce, wrap it with a piece of seaweed, and sprinkle a little white sesame seeds on top.  
   **Key point**: The tighter the rice ball is shaped, the less likely it will fall apart during baking. Apply the soy sauce evenly for a beautiful color.
5. Line the air fryer basket with baking paper and place the rice balls spaced apart.
6. Insert the basket, set the temperature to 180°C, and bake for about 10 minutes, until the surface is golden and slightly crispy and the cheese inside is fully melted.  
   **Key point**: Adjust the temperature and time according to the size of the rice balls and the power of your air fryer.

## Health Advice
This rice ball combines vegetables, protein, and carbohydrates, making it suitable for a quick meal. Note that cheese and mayonnaise are relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to serve with a vegetable salad to balance the nutrition.
