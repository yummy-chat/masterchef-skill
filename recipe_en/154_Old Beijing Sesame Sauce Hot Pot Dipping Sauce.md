# Old Beijing Sesame Sauce Hot Pot Dipping Sauce
Cuisine: Beijing Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥜 Sesame paste (sesame sauce) Approximately 2 parts
🥜 Peanut butter Approximately 1 part
💧 Warm water As needed
🧂 Soy sauce As needed
🍬 White sugar As needed
🦐 Shrimp paste oil As needed
🧀 Fermented bean curd As needed (to taste)
🌿 Chinese chive flower sauce As needed (to taste)
🌶️ Freshly fried chili oil As needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Transparent glass bowl
🥄 Wooden spoon
🍲 Hot pot pot

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the sesame paste and peanut butter in a glass bowl in a 2:1 ratio, and stir evenly with a wooden spoon.
**Key point**: Peanut butter is used to neutralize the slight bitterness of sesame paste and enhance the sweet and fragrant taste; both pastes must be mixed until no lumps remain.
2. Add warm water gradually in small amounts, stirring immediately after each addition.
**Note**: Avoid adding too much warm water at once, as this can cause the sesame paste to clump and ruin the smooth texture.
3. Continue stirring until the paste reaches a smooth, yogurt-like consistency.
4. Add an appropriate amount of soy sauce and white sugar, stirring until fully incorporated.
5. Add shrimp paste oil, fermented bean curd, and Chinese chive flower sauce to taste, mixing well.
**Note**: The amounts of these ingredients are flexible and can be increased, decreased, or omitted based on preference.
6. Finally, add the freshly fried chili oil and stir evenly.
**Key point**: Freshly fried chili oil is the finishing touch, significantly enhancing the aroma and depth of the dipping sauce.
7. The dipping sauce is ready; serve with hot pot-cooked meat.

## Health Advice
Sesame paste is rich in calcium and healthy fatty acids, making it suitable for pairing with vegetables and lean meats to improve the nutritional balance of hot pot. Note that shrimp paste oil and fermented bean curd have high salt content, so individuals with high blood pressure should reduce the amount used accordingly.
