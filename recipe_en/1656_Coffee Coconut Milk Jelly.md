# Coffee Coconut Milk Jelly
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥛 Heavy cream 125ml  
🥥 Coconut brand coconut milk 250ml  
☕ Espresso coffee 35ml  
🥥 Coconut water 50ml  
🧊 Gelatin 10g  
🍬 Sugar (optional)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Strainer (optional)  
🍮 Molds  
❄️ Refrigerator

## Preparation Steps
1. Pour heavy cream, coconut milk, espresso coffee, and coconut water into a container, and mix thoroughly.
2. Heat the mixture to 50-60°C for about 1 minute, controlling the heat carefully to avoid boiling.  
   **Key Point**: Do not heat too high to prevent bubbling, which affects the final smoothness.
3. If you prefer sweetness, add sugar to taste and stir until dissolved.
4. Add gelatin and stir continuously until fully melted, then continue heating for about 1 minute.
5. For a smoother texture, strain the liquid through a fine mesh sieve to remove undissolved particles.
6. Pour the strained liquid into molds.
7. If there are surface bubbles, gently skim them off with a spoon to improve the final appearance and texture.
8. Place the molds in the refrigerator and let set for 3 hours until fully solidified.  
   **Note**: Avoid moving the molds during refrigeration to prevent uneven setting.

## Health Advice
This coffee coconut milk jelly combines coconut aroma with coffee flavor, making it suitable as a low-sugar dessert. It is recommended to control the amount of added sugar and ensure the gelatin is fully dissolved to avoid clumping. Those sensitive to caffeine should be mindful of consumption timing.
