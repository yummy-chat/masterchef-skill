# Eggplant Egg Pancake 🥞
Cuisine Type: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Instructions 📝
1. Place the eggplant slices in a bowl, add 1 spoon of salt, mix well, and let sit for 5 minutes to draw out moisture.
   **Note**: Salt removes bitterness and softens the eggplant, making it easier to coat later.
2. Squeeze the eggplant slices firmly with your hands to remove as much water as possible.
   **Key**: If the water is not fully squeezed out, the batter will be too thin, and the pancake won't hold its shape.
3. Add eggs, flour, black pepper, and chopped green onions to the squeezed eggplant. Stir into a thick batter, ensuring each slice is evenly coated.
4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Use a spoon to scoop in the eggplant mixture and flatten it into a pancake shape with the back of the spoon.
   **Heat**: Keep the heat at medium-low after the oil is hot to avoid burning the outside while leaving the inside raw.
5. Fry until the bottom is golden and set, then flip. Continue frying until both sides are golden brown and the inside is fully cooked. Remove from the pan.

## Health Tips 🌿
Eggplant is rich in dietary fiber but absorbs oil easily. It is recommended to use less oil and fry slowly. This pancake is suitable for those looking for a vegetarian-friendly option. Note calorie control, and those with high blood lipids should consume in moderation.
