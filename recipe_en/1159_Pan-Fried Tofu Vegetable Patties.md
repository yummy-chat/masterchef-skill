# Pan-Fried Tofu Vegetable Patties
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🧈 Firm/old tofu – 1 block  
🌾 All-purpose flour – about 1 tablespoon  
🥕 Carrot – to taste (about half a carrot, finely diced)  
🧅 Scallion – to taste (chopped)  
🦐 Dried shrimp – to taste (about a small handful)  
🥚 Eggs – 2  
🧂 Fine salt – about 5 grams  
🧂 MSG – to taste  
🌿 Thirteen-spice blend – to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel mixing bowl  
🍳 Frying pan  
🥄 Large spoon  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Step by Step Directions
1. Place the firm tofu into a bowl and use your hands to crumble it until there are no large chunks.  
   **Key point**: The tofu must be thoroughly broken down to ensure the mixture forms evenly later.

2. Add the flour, and mix well with your hands. Adjust the amount of flour based on the dry or wet consistency of the tofu; the mixture should be able to hold together when formed.

3. Add the chopped scallions, diced carrot, dried shrimp, eggs, fine salt, MSG, and thirteen-spice blend. Use your hands to thoroughly mix everything together, so the filling becomes sticky and can be squeezed into a ball without falling apart.

4. Place the frying pan over high heat until hot, then pour in an appropriate amount of cooking oil. Form the mixture into evenly sized balls and place them in the pan. Gently press each ball with your hand into a flat, round, small patty shape.

5. Fry over the lowest heat possible throughout the entire cooking process. Shake the pan occasionally so the patties heat evenly and prevent them from sticking to the bottom.

6. After frying for about 2 minutes, once the bottom is set and golden brown, flip the patties. Continue frying the other side over the lowest heat.

7. Fry until both sides are golden brown. Gently press the center of a patty; if it feels springy, it is cooked through. Remove from the pan and serve on a plate.

## Health Suggestions  
Firm tofu provides calcium, dried shrimp add umami, and carrots are good for eyesight – these patties offer balanced nutrition. The pan-frying method increases the calorie content, so limit consumption if you are on a weight loss plan.
