# Homemade Pickles  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥬 Cabbage (torn into small pieces)  
🥕 White radish (cut into strips)  
🥕 Carrot (cut into strips)  
🥒 Cucumber (cut into strips)  
🫚 Ginger (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic (sliced)  
🌶️ Small chili peppers  
🌶️ Pickled chili peppers (along with one bowl of pickled chili pepper liquid)  
🧂 Salt (one tablespoon)  
🍬 White sugar (two tablespoons)  
🍶 White vinegar (two tablespoons)  
🥤 Sprite (enough to cover the vegetables)  

## Kitchen Utensils & Equipment  
🏺 Pickle jar (with water-seal groove)  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Soup spoon  
🥢 Strainer/draining basket  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Wash the cabbage, white radish, carrot, cucumber, ginger, garlic, and small chili peppers, and drain off surface moisture. Then, cut the white radish, carrot, and cucumber into strips, tear the cabbage into small pieces, and slice the ginger and garlic.  
   **Note**: Ensure all ingredients are thoroughly drained, otherwise it will affect the storage time.  
2. Place all the prepared vegetables into the pickle jar.  
3. Pour in one bowl of pickled chili peppers, then add one tablespoon of salt, two tablespoons of white sugar, and two tablespoons of white vinegar.  
4. Pour in Sprite so that the liquid covers all the vegetables.  
   **Key point**: Using Sprite instead of water increases sweetness and crispness and is more conducive to preservation.  
5. Cover the jar with its lid and pour water into the groove on the rim to create a water seal, ensuring it is airtight.  
6. Place the sealed pickle jar in the refrigerator and let it ferment for one day, then it can be taken out and eaten.  

## Health Advice  
This pickle uses Sprite instead of water, making it sweet, crispy, and appetizing, ideal for relieving greasiness in summer side dishes. However, diabetics should be mindful of the sugar content, and those with sensitive stomachs are advised to consume in small amounts.
