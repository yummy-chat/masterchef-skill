# Electric Rice Cooker Braised Rice  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Cooked rice (leftover rice) as needed  
🎃 Pumpkin, diced, as needed  
🥕 Carrot, diced, as needed  
🧅 Onion, diced, as needed  
🍖 1 large piece of cooked bone-in meat (e.g., spare ribs or braised meat)  
🥚 1 egg  
🥜 1 handful of raw peanuts  
🌿 1 scallion (placed whole)  

🥢 Dark liquid seasoning (soy sauce / dark soy sauce), as needed (spray before serving)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🍚 Electric rice cooker  
🧴 Oil spray bottle (or seasoning spray bottle)  

## Steps  
1. Spread cooked rice on the bottom of the rice cooker inner pot. Add diced pumpkin, diced carrot, diced onion, the whole scallion, and the large piece of cooked bone-in meat in order. Crack a raw egg on top and sprinkle with raw peanuts.  
   **Note**: No additional water is needed throughout, and do not stir the ingredients.  
2. Place the inner pot into the rice cooker, select the rice cooking program, cover the lid, and start braising.  
3. After the program ends, open the lid and spray dark liquid seasoning evenly over the rice surface using a spray bottle.  
4. Stir to your preference and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This braised rice is rich in beta-carotene from pumpkin and carrots, paired with peanuts and meat for quality protein, making it a convenient one-pot meal for busy office workers. However, note that sodium content may be high; those with high blood pressure can reduce the amount of soy sauce used.
