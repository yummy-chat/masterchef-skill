# Fat Cow Shrimp Paste Pot
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Shrimp paste appropriate amount (packaged finished product)
🥩 Fat cow rolls appropriate amount (frozen rolled)
🥬 Baby cabbage 1 small piece (for lining the bottom)
🍄 Enoki mushrooms 1 bunch (remove roots and separate)
🍜 Vermicelli appropriate amount (pre-soaked until no hard core)
🧄 Minced garlic appropriate amount
🌶️ Small chili peppers appropriate amount (chopped)
🧅 Chopped green onions appropriate amount (sprinkle when serving)
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount
❓ Sauce appropriate amount (specific ingredients not specified in the video)
🫙 Garlic chili sauce appropriate amount (pre-cooked minced garlic and chili sauce)
💧 Water about 1 bowl

## Cookware and Equipment
🥘 Boiling pot (for blanching)
🍳 Stir-fry pan (for cooking sauce)
🍲 Clay pot or casserole (final cooking vessel)
🥄 Slotted spoon (for removing blanched ingredients)
🥄 Spatula (for stirring sauce)

## Steps
1. Wash the baby cabbage and cut into sections or keep whole; wash the enoki mushrooms, remove roots, and separate; pre-soak vermicelli in cold or warm water until no hard core; mince garlic and chop small chili peppers for later use.
2. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add shrimp paste, cook until it floats and changes color, then remove.
   **Note**: Remove shrimp paste as soon as it is cooked to avoid overcooking.
3. In the same pot of water, add fat cow rolls and quickly stir to separate, cook until completely changed color, then remove immediately.
   **Note**: Blanch fat cow rolls for a short time; remove as soon as they change color to prevent the meat from becoming tough.
4. Heat a stir-fry pan, add oil, and when the oil is hot, add minced garlic and chopped small chili peppers. Stir-fry over low heat until fragrant.
5. Pour in the sauce, stir-fry evenly, and bring to a boil.
   **Note**: The ingredients of the sauce are not specified; it must fully combine with the garlic and chili and boil.
6. In the clay pot, layer the bottom with baby cabbage, enoki mushrooms, and pre-soaked vermicelli in order.
7. Place the blanched fat cow rolls and shrimp paste on top of the vermicelli, and evenly pour the stir-fried garlic chili sauce over them.
8. Add about one bowl of water to the pot, with the amount sufficient to partially submerge the ingredients.
9. Cover the clay pot and simmer over medium-low heat for 10 minutes, until the ingredients are cooked through, the broth is boiling, and the vermicelli has absorbed the broth.
10. Remove the lid, sprinkle with chopped green onions, and serve.

## Health Tips
This dish provides high-quality protein from shrimp paste and fat cow rolls, paired with baby cabbage and enoki mushrooms to increase dietary fiber, making it suitable for weight loss or nutritional supplementation. Note that vermicelli and garlic chili sauce have high sodium content, so those controlling salt intake should avoid drinking too much broth.
