# State Banquet Clear Noodle Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌿 Spinach, appropriate amount (roots removed)  
🍤 Shrimp, a small amount (pre-marinated)  
🍅 Tomato, 1 piece (blanched, peeled, and diced)  
🍜 Noodles, appropriate amount (finest cut noodles recommended)  
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount (used separately for marinating shrimp and seasoning the soup)  
🌶 White pepper powder, appropriate amount (used separately for marinating shrimp and seasoning the soup)  
🍶 Yellow wine, appropriate amount (for marinating shrimp to remove odor and enhance aroma)  
🌽 Cornstarch, a small amount (for marinating shrimp to lock in moisture)  
🍲 Clear chicken broth, appropriate amount (strained before use to ensure a clear soup base)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🔪 Cutting board and kitchen knife  
🥣 Bowl (for marinating shrimp)  
🥄 Soup ladle  
🥢 Chopsticks (to separate shrimp)  
🕸 Strainer (to strain chicken broth)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Remove the roots from the spinach, keep the tender leaves, wash, and drain.  
2. Add a pinch of salt, white pepper powder, and yellow wine to the shrimp, mix well. Then add a small amount of cornstarch and continue mixing until well-coated.  
3. Blanch the whole tomato in boiling water until the skin wrinkles and splits, then remove and peel off the skin. Cut into evenly sized dices.  
4. Using the water from blanching the tomato, blanch the spinach until just cooked, then remove and drain.  
   **Key Point**: Blanching removes oxalic acid from the spinach.  
5. Heat oil in a wok to 40% hot, add the marinated shrimp, and quickly separate them with chopsticks. Remove and drain as soon as the shrimp change color.  
   **Key Point**: Keep the oil-frying time short to maintain the shrimp's tenderness.  
6. Pour the strained clear chicken broth into the wok, bring to a boil, and season with salt and white pepper powder. Keep the soup clear.  
7. Once the soup boils, add the tomato dices and cook briefly to release flavor.  
8. In a separate pot, cook the noodles until done. Transfer to a bowl, pour in the seasoned clear soup, and top with the stir-fried shrimp, blanched spinach segments, and tomato dices. Serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
This clear noodle soup is made with a chicken broth base and enhanced with tomato. It is nutritious, easy to digest, and especially suitable for those with poor appetite or sensitive stomachs. The shrimp, marinated and quickly stir-fried, become as tender as tofu, while the blanched spinach has its oxalic acid removed, making this a low-fat, high-quality protein meal.
