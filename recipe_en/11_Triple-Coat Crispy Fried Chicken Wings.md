# Triple-Coat Crispy Fried Chicken Wings
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken wings 6-10 pieces  
🌾 Wheat flour (all-purpose flour) as needed  
🥚 Egg 1 piece (beaten well)  
🍞 Golden breadcrumbs as needed  
🧂 Salt as needed  
🌶️ Pepper as needed  
🌶️ Sichuan pepper powder as needed  
🌶️ Paprika as needed  
🫗 Light soy sauce as needed  
🌿 Other spices as needed (for seasoning the flour or marinade)  

## Utensils and Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowls multiple (for flour, egg wash, breadcrumbs, and marinating)  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🔲 Wire rack  
🌡️ Kitchen thermometer  
🍳 Deep fryer/deep pot  
♨️ Air fryer  

## Steps  
1. **Marinate the chicken wings**: Clean the chicken wings, add salt, pepper, Sichuan pepper powder, paprika, light soy sauce, and other spices. Mix thoroughly and marinate for at least 20 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate.  
2. **First coat**: Place the marinated chicken wings into the flour and press firmly to ensure even coverage with dry flour, then shake off any excess.  
   - Mix a little salt and spices into the flour in advance to prevent the coating from being bland.  
3. **Second coat**: Dip the flour-coated chicken wings into the beaten egg mixture, rolling to coat evenly, then lift and let any excess egg drip off.  
   - Beat the egg thoroughly to avoid large egg whites affecting adhesion.  
4. **Third coat**: Using dry hands, grab breadcrumbs and sprinkle them over the chicken wings, pressing gently to ensure even adhesion.  
   - Keep hands dry to prevent egg from contaminating the remaining breadcrumbs.  
5. **Rest**: Place the coated chicken wings on a wire rack and let them rest for about 5 minutes to allow the egg, flour, and breadcrumbs to bond, strengthening the coating’s adhesion.  
6. **Cooking**:  
   - **Traditional deep frying**: Heat oil to 160–180°C, add the chicken wings and fry until the outer shell is golden and crispy, then remove.  
     - Note: If the oil temperature is too high, the outside may burn while the inside remains undercooked; if too low, the coating will absorb oil and become soggy.  
   - **Air frying**: Place the chicken wings in the air fryer, spray a little oil on the surface, and cook at 180°C for about 15 minutes until the shell is crispy.  

## Health Tips  
These fried chicken wings are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, rich in protein, making them suitable for muscle building or as an occasional treat. However, deep frying is relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to pair them with a vegetable salad for balanced nutrition, or use an air fryer to reduce oil intake.
