# Cucumber Pancakes
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 Cucumber, about 1 piece (shredded)
🥕 Carrot, about 1/4 piece (shredded)
🧅 Scallions, to taste (chopped)
🥚 Eggs, 3 pieces
🌾 All-purpose flour, to taste
🧂 Salt, about 2 grams
🌿 Five-spice powder, to taste
🍶 Soy sauce, to taste
🍶 White vinegar, about 2 drops (added to the egg mixture)
🫒 Cooking oil, about 10 grams (mixed into the batter)

## Cookware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Spoon
🍳 Frying pan
🍽️ Plate
🔪 Knife
🪵 Cutting board

## Instructions
1.  Shred the cucumber and carrot into even strips. Chop the scallions into small pieces.
2.  Pour the flour into the shredded cucumber. Stir with chopsticks so that each piece of cucumber is evenly coated with flour to lock in moisture.
3.  Add the shredded carrot, scallions, salt, five-spice powder, soy sauce, and cooking oil. Mix well again.
    **Note**: Cooking oil makes the pancakes more fragrant and prevents sticking to the pan.
4.  Crack the eggs into a bowl, add the white vinegar, and whisk thoroughly with chopsticks.
    **Key Point**: White vinegar removes the eggy smell and makes the pancakes fluffier.
5.  Pour the egg mixture into the seasoned cucumber batter. Stir until it reaches a thick, sticky consistency. If the batter is too dry, adjust by adding another egg instead of water.
6.  Heat oil in a frying pan. Pour in the batter and spread it into a flat pancake shape with a spoon. Cook over the lowest heat throughout. Cook each side for about 2 minutes, until set and golden brown, then flip.
    **Key Point**: The lowest heat prevents the outside from burning while the inside remains undercooked or prevents burning entirely.
7.  Use a plate to help flip the pancake — place the plate over the pancake, quickly invert the pan to drop the pancake onto the plate, then slide it back into the pan to cook the other side. This prevents the pancake from breaking.
8.  Once both sides are golden brown, remove from the pan, cut into pieces, and serve.

## Health Tips
Cucumber pancakes incorporate vegetables into the batter, making them suitable for those who do not like eating vegetables. The combination of eggs and vegetables provides balanced nutrition and is low in fat, making it ideal as a breakfast option during weight loss periods.
