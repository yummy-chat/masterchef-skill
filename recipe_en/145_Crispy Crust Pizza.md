# Crispy Crust Pizza
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 Bread flour 100g  
🌾 Cake flour 100g  
🍬 Sugar 6g  
🧂 Salt 2g  
🍞 Dry yeast 2g  
💧 Room temperature water 120g  
🫒 Olive oil 10g (for dough)  
🧀 Mozzarella cheese appropriate amount (generous, used in two separate portions)  
🌭 Sausage appropriate amount  
🫑 Bell peppers appropriate amount  
🌽 Cornmeal appropriate amount (for dusting to prevent sticking)  
🍅 Pizza sauce appropriate amount  
🫒 Olive oil appropriate amount (for brushing crust edge)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
⚖️ Measuring cup  
🥚 Whisk  
🪣 Large mixing bowl  
🔪 Spatula  
🛡️ Plastic wrap/damp cloth  
🔲 Bench scraper  
🍽️ Metal pan  
📏 Cutting board  
🔥 Non-stick baking sheet  
📜 Parchment paper/silicone mat  
🖌️ Brush  
♨️ Oven  

## Instructions  
1. Pour dry yeast into room temperature water and stir until fully dissolved.  
2. In a large bowl, combine bread flour, cake flour, sugar, and salt, and whisk thoroughly to mix.  
3. Add the yeast water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Use a spatula to stir into a dough, scraping the sides of the bowl to incorporate all the dry ingredients into the dough.  
4. Grease your hands lightly with oil and knead the dough until the surface is smooth and tight. Return the dough to the bowl, cover with plastic wrap or a damp cloth, and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.  
5. Divide the proofed dough into two portions and roll each into a ball. This recipe makes two 6-inch pizzas.  
6. Sprinkle cornmeal on the bottom of a container, place the dough balls inside, and refrigerate for up to two days for later use. If making immediately, sprinkle a generous amount of cornmeal on the cutting board, take the dough, press it to release air, and shape it into a round pizza base, rolling the edges to form a crust.  
7. Place the pizza base on a baking sheet lined with a silicone mat or parchment paper. Prick the base evenly all over with a fork to prevent air bubbles during baking.  
8. Brush the crust edge with olive oil. Place the baking sheet in an unheated oven with a cup of water next to it, and let it proof for 30 minutes.  
9. Just before proofing is complete, preheat the oven to 230°C top and bottom heat.  
10. On the proofed pizza base, spread pizza sauce evenly, sprinkle a layer of mozzarella cheese, top with sausage and bell peppers, then sprinkle another layer of mozzarella cheese.  
    **Key point**: Use a generous amount of cheese to ensure the toppings are moist and juicy.  
11. Place the baking sheet on the lowest rack of the preheated oven and bake at 230°C top and bottom heat for about 15 minutes, until the crust bottom is golden brown.  
    **Key point**: Baking on the lowest rack helps the bottom brown and become crispy. Use the crust color to determine when it is done.  
12. Remove the pizza immediately, slice, and serve.  

## Health Tips  
This crispy crust pizza is high in carbohydrates and cheese, making it suitable for post-workout energy replenishment or occasional consumption. Pairing it with bell peppers adds dietary fiber. Be mindful of the amount of sausage and cheese used to avoid excessive sodium and saturated fat intake.
