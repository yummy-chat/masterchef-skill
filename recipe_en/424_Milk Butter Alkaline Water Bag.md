# Milk Butter Alkaline Water Bag
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥖 High-gluten flour 300g
🥛 Milk powder 9g (dough) + 50g (milk butter filling)
🧈 Unsalted butter 9g (dough) + 34g (milk butter filling)
🍬 Granulated sugar 20g
🥚 Whole egg liquid 17g (room temperature)
🍵 Matcha powder approx. 0.7g
🍫 Cocoa powder approx. 1g
🫙 Low-sugar tolerant dry yeast 2g (needs to be dissolved in an equal amount of water)
🧂 Salt 4g (dough) + 20g (alkaline water) + 1g (milk butter filling)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
⚖️ Electronic scale
🔪 Dough scraper
🛍️ Plastic storage bag
🥖 Rolling pin
🧽 Baking oil cloth (silicone mat)
🔥 Oven
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Silicone spatula

## Steps
1. **Make the original milk butter filling**: Mix softened butter with granulated sugar and 1g salt until well combined. Add room-temperature whole egg liquid in two batches, stirring until uniform. Finally, add 50g milk powder and mix well. You can divide part of the original milk butter filling and mix in matcha powder or cocoa powder to create different flavors.
   **Key point**: The egg liquid must be at room temperature to prevent oil-water separation.
2. **Mix the dough**: Mix high-gluten flour, 9g milk powder, and 4g salt evenly. Form a well on the work surface. Add all the water except the reserved portion. Gradually mix the flour from the center with the water into a flocculent state. Knead into a rough dough with no dry flour.
3. **Cold rest**: Wrap the rough dough in a plastic bag, flatten it, and refrigerate for 30 minutes to rest.
4. **Add yeast and butter**: Take out the rested dough, tear it open and spread it flat. Spread the yeast liquid (dissolved in water) over the dough. Place 9g butter on top. Use a dough scraper to repeatedly cut and mix until the butter is fully incorporated into the dough. Then knead until the dough is smooth, firm, and can be stretched into a thin but resilient membrane.
   **Key point**: Dry yeast must be dissolved in an equal amount of water in advance before use.
5. **Divide and pre-shape**: Divide the dough evenly into 6 portions. Round each portion until smooth on the surface. Pinch the bottom tightly to seal. Let them rest for 10 minutes.
6. **Shape**: Take one small dough portion and roll it into a cone shape. Roll it out into a long tongue shape that is narrower at the top and wider at the bottom. Flip it over. Spread the milk butter filling evenly. Roll it up from the wide end, slightly stretching the dough while rolling and pushing the filling towards the center. Shape it into an olive shape with a bulging middle and tapered ends.
   **Note**: The shaping must create an arched bread shape to prevent filling leakage.
7. **Freeze**: Place the shaped bread dough on a baking tray, cover, and freeze for about 30 minutes until set.
8. **Prepare the alkaline water**: Mix warm water, 20g salt, and baking soda until fully dissolved.
   **Warning**: Baking soda is corrosive. Be sure to wear gloves and use a glass or plastic container.
9. **Soak in alkaline water**: Wearing gloves, fully submerge the frozen bread dough in the alkaline water for about 30 seconds. Remove and place on a baking tray lined with oil cloth.
   **Note**: The baking tray must be lined with a silicone oil cloth to prevent sticking.
10. **Score the bread**: Use a sharp knife to make two vertical deep cuts on the bread surface, about 1cm deep or more. Complete each cut in one motion, avoiding repeated cutting.
11. **Bake**: Place in a preheated oven and bake at 200°C top heat and 180°C bottom heat for about 14 minutes, until the surface is evenly browned.
    **Key point**: Insufficient browning will cause the crust to become sticky after cooling.

## Health Advice
This milk butter alkaline water bag uses high-gluten flour and butter, providing complex carbohydrates and high-quality fats, making it suitable as an energy supplement for breakfast or post-exercise. The alkaline water treatment gives it a unique chewy texture, but baking soda is corrosive, so be sure to wear gloves during operation. Additionally, it is high in salt, so individuals with high blood pressure should consume it in moderation.
