# Steamed Egg with Clams
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🐚 Clams - appropriate amount (about 15-20, pre-washed)  
🥚 Eggs - 2  
🫚 Ginger - 2-3 slices  
🧅 Scallion - 1 small section  
💧 Water - appropriate amount (for boiling clams, to be reserved as broth later)  
🧂 Soy sauce / sesame oil - appropriate amount (drizzled after cooking)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
♨️ Microwave  
🥣 Bowl  
🍽️ Plate (for covering the bowl)  
🥄 Spoon (for skimming foam and stirring)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Thoroughly wash the clams and place them in a bowl. Add ginger slices, scallion sections, and enough water to submerge the clams.  
   **Key point**: Do not discard the water used to boil the clams; reserve it as broth, as it is the key to the steamed egg's umami flavor.  
2. Place the bowl in the microwave and heat on high for 3 minutes, until the clams are fully open.  
   **Key point**: Do not discard the water used to boil the clams; reserve it as "broth," as it is the key to the steamed egg's umami flavor.  
3. Beat the eggs thoroughly, then add the warm reserved clam broth and mix well.  
   **Key point**: Skim off the foam to ensure the steamed egg has a smooth surface and tender texture.  
4. Use a spoon to skim off the foam from the egg mixture. Then pick out the cooked clam meat and add it to the egg mixture.  
   **Key point**: Skimming off the foam ensures the steamed egg has a smooth surface and tender texture.  
5. Cover the bowl with a plate and place it in the microwave. Heat on high for 2 minutes and 30 seconds.  
   **Note**: Covering prevents water vapor from dripping in, avoiding a honeycomb-like surface on the steamed egg.  
6. After removing from the microwave, drizzle with an appropriate amount of soy sauce or sesame oil according to personal taste.  

## Health Tips  
Steamed egg with clams is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for weight loss diets, the elderly, and children. Note that the clam broth itself contains some saltiness, so soy sauce should be used sparingly for health benefits. The microwave method saves time and effort, but the step of skimming foam should not be omitted, as it affects the smoothness of the steamed egg's surface.
