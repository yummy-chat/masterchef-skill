# Carrot and Lamb Dumplings 🥟  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Lamb leg meat, appropriate amount (preferred cut, chopped into small granular filling)  
🥕 Carrots, appropriate amount (shredded into thin strips)  
🧅 Scallions, appropriate amount  
💧 Sichuan peppercorn water, appropriate amount (added in batches to the meat filling)  
🧂 Salt, 3 grams  
🥢 Light soy sauce, 20 milliliters  
🥢 Dark soy sauce, 5 milliliters  
🌶️ White pepper, a pinch (one shake)  
🍶 Huangjiu (Chinese rice wine), 10 milliliters  
🫒 Sesame oil, a few drops (added last to lock in moisture and enhance aroma)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef’s knife (for chopping lamb)  
🥒 Grater or shredder (for shredding carrots)  
🍳 Wok or frying pan (for stir-frying carrots)  
🥣 Glass bowl (for holding and mixing filling)  
🥢 Chopsticks (for stirring filling)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. First chop the lamb leg meat into small pieces, then hand-chop into small granular filling.  
   **Key Point**: Hand-chopped meat filling offers a better texture than machine-ground.  
2. Prepare the lamb filling: Gradually add Sichuan peppercorn water to the meat, stirring in one direction until fully absorbed. Then add salt, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, white pepper, and Huangjiu. Finally, drizzle in sesame oil and mix thoroughly.  
   **Note**: Follow the principle of "no Sichuan peppercorn in pork, no star anise in lamb." Avoid adding star anise or other strong spices to lamb filling to preserve its natural flavor.  
3. Use a grater or shredder to shred carrots into thin strips.  
4. Stir-fry the carrots: Heat a wok or frying pan with a little oil, add scallions and stir until fragrant. Then add the shredded carrots and stir-fry until they soften and excess moisture evaporates.  
   **Key Point**: Stir-frying precooks the carrots for better texture and aids in the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins.  
5. Add the fully cooled stir-fried carrots to the lamb filling and mix well with chopsticks.  
6. Take a dumpling wrapper, fill with an appropriate amount of filling, fold in half, and pinch the edges tightly to seal into a dumpling shape.  
   **Note**: The wrapping method can be tailored to personal preference; ensure edges are tightly sealed to prevent leakage during cooking.  

## Health Advice 🏃  
The β-carotene in carrots works synergistically with the zinc and iron in lamb to boost winter immunity. This combination is especially beneficial for those with cold hands and feet. Control the amount of Sichuan peppercorn water to avoid making the filling overly wet and compromising texture.
