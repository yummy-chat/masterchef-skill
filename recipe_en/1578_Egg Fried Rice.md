# Egg Fried Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Rice  
🥚 Eggs  
🍖 Ham Sausage (diced)  
🧅 Scallions (chopped)  
🫒 Cooking Oil  
🧂 Salt  

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Metal Spatula  
🔥 Gas Stove  
🍚 Rice Cooker  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps

### Pre-preparation
1. **Steam the Rice**: Soak the rice for at least 1 hour in advance, then steam it with a rice-to-water ratio of approximately 1:1 to achieve distinct, separate grains.
2. **Prepare the Garnishes**: Dice the ham sausage and chop the scallions. When cracking the eggs, tilt the bowl and whisk vigorously in an elliptical motion with chopsticks to make the egg mixture light and fluffy.
3. **Season the Wok to Prevent Sticking**:
   - Heat the empty wok over high flame, then reduce to low flame and pour in cooking oil to coat the wok, allowing the oil to fully penetrate the pores of the wok's surface.
   - Pour out the hot oil, using thermal expansion and contraction to cool the wok and lock in an oil film, leaving only about half a tablespoon of oil at the bottom.
   - Control the temperature throughout. This process can be repeated 2–3 times to ensure the rice won't stick during frying.

### Hot Frying Method
1. Heat the wok over high flame until the oil is quite hot, then pour in the egg mixture. The eggs will puff up instantly—do not stir them immediately.
2. Use a metal spatula to lift layers of the fluffy egg skin, allowing the edges to undergo the Maillard reaction for a slightly charred, aromatic flavor.
3. Place the rice on top of the half-cooked eggs, and stir-fry over medium heat until the eggs and rice are well combined.
4. Season with salt. You may first stir-fry the diced ham sausage until fragrant, then add it together with the rice. Stir-fry evenly, sprinkle in the chopped scallions, and remove from heat as soon as the scallion aroma is released.
   - **Note**: Do not stir-fry the rice for too long when using the hot method, as it may affect the texture.

### Cold Frying Method
1. Crack 2 eggs directly into the cooled rice and stir thoroughly, ensuring each grain is coated with egg mixture.
2. After seasoning the wok, pour in the egg-coated rice. Initially, do not stir too quickly; wait for the egg mixture to gradually set, creating a half-fried, half-stirred state to prevent sticking.
3. As moisture evaporates, the rice will gradually loosen. Continue stir-frying over medium heat for about 2 minutes, until the grains are dry and distinct.
4. Sprinkle in salt, add the diced ham sausage and stir-fry until fragrant, then finally add the chopped scallions, stir evenly, and serve.

## Health Tips
This egg fried rice offers a balanced ratio of carbohydrates and protein, making it suitable as a quick lunch or post-workout energy boost. Be mindful to control the amount of ham sausage and oil to reduce sodium and saturated fat intake; those with high blood pressure may reduce the salt content appropriately.
