# Braised Sweet Potato Noodles in Spiced Broth
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍠 Dried sweet potato noodles (vermicelli) 适量
🧆 Beef balls 6-8 pieces
🥚 Egg 1 piece
🌿 Dried seaweed 1 large sheet
🌱 Celery  a little (use the tenderest center)
🥬 Iceberg lettuce about 2 leaves
🧂 Pre-mixed braising spice mix (provides savory broth base)
🧄 Fried garlic 1 tablespoon

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
♨️ Single-handled saucepan
♨️ Snow pot (for additional water)
🔥 Gas stove
🥢 Long chopsticks or tongs
✂️ Scissors
🥄 Ladle
🥢 Eating chopsticks
🍜 Bowl
🥣 Small bowl

## Instructions
1. **Toast the seaweed**: Use long chopsticks to hold the dried seaweed sheet. Toast it quickly over an open gas flame, flipping frequently. Once the seaweed turns bright green and becomes crisp, remove it from the heat immediately. Use scissors to cut it into thin strips or small pieces. Set aside.
   - **Note**: Do not leave the seaweed over the flame for too long to prevent burning.

2. **Prepare celery bits**: Take the tenderest center part of the celery and cut it into fine small pieces. Set aside.

3. **Simmer the braising broth**: Place the pre-mixed braising spice mix, beef balls, and enough water into a saucepan. Bring to a boil over high heat.

4. **Add the sweet potato noodles**: Once the water is boiling, add the sweet potato noodles. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the noodles soften.
   - **Judgment standard**: The noodles turn from white to translucent and can be easily snapped apart with chopsticks.

5. **Poach the egg**: Reduce the heat to low. Pour a little water into a small bowl, then crack the egg into it. Bring the bowl close to the soup surface and gently slide the egg into the pot. Immediately cover the pot with a lid, turn off the heat or keep it on the lowest setting, and let the egg poach in residual heat for about 5 minutes.
   - **Desired doneness**: The egg white should be set, and the yolk should be runny or semi-set. Adjust the steaming time according to personal preference.

6. **Blanch the iceberg lettuce**: First, remove the poached egg from the pot and place it at the bottom of a serving bowl. Then, add the iceberg lettuce to the pot. Blanch for a few seconds until just wilted, then remove it, or pour it into the bowl along with the broth.
   - **Note**: Iceberg lettuce cooks very quickly and should not be simmered for long, or it will lose its crisp texture.

7. **Assemble and season**: Into the serving bowl, add the toasted seaweed pieces and the celery bits in order. Finally, sprinkle one tablespoon of fried garlic on top. The braising broth is already sufficiently salty and savory, so extra salt or soy sauce is usually not needed.

## Health Advice
Sweet potato noodles paired with beef balls offer a balanced combination of carbohydrates and protein. The braising broth provides a rich, savory base, and adding a soft-boiled egg increases the nutritional value. This dish is suitable for people who need a quick energy boost. However, as the noodles can cause a rapid rise in blood sugar, it is recommended to consume with plenty of vegetables to avoid sharp fluctuations.
