Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐

## Preparation Steps  
1. After slaughtering the grass carp, remove the black membrane inside the belly, scrape off the slime on the skin with a knife, and pull out the pharyngeal bone behind the gills.  
   **Key point**: These three parts are the main sources of fishy odor; be sure to clean them thoroughly.  
2. Cut the cleaned grass carp into sections about 1 cm thick, place in a bowl, add salt and water, wash to remove blood, then drain.  
3. To the drained fish pieces, add salt, thirteen spices, white pepper powder, MSG, sugar, soy sauce, yellow wine, ginger slices, scallion segments, and cornstarch. Mix well with your hands and marinate for 10 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Mix thoroughly to ensure the fish is evenly seasoned.  
4. Place the clay pot on the stove and heat it up. Pour in a little oil, add ginger slices, and stir-fry over medium heat until fragrant.  
5. Spread the sliced onion on the bottom of the clay pot and stir-fry until the onion releases its aroma.  
6. Layer enoki mushrooms over the onion, then a layer of marinated fish pieces. Repeat alternating layers, placing the fish head on the very top.  
   **Key point**: Layer the ingredients evenly.  
7. Pour any remaining marinade from the fish into the pot, then add an appropriate amount of beer—just enough to partially submerge the ingredients.  
   **Key point**: Don’t waste the fish marinade; use a moderate amount of beer.  
8. Cover the clay pot with its lid and simmer for 10 minutes.  
   **Doneness check**: After 10 minutes, uncover—the broth should be boiling and the aroma rich.  
9. Turn off the heat, sprinkle with chopped scallions, and serve.  

## Health Suggestions  
Grass carp is rich in high-quality protein and unsaturated fatty acids, making it suitable for adults needing nutritional supplementation. Be mindful of the amounts of soy sauce and salt; those with high blood pressure can reduce them appropriately. Clay pot simmering uses less oil and is healthier, but although beer helps remove fishiness and adds flavor, it is not low in calories, so consume in moderation.
