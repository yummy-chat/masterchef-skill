# Beef Tenderloin Bagel Sandwich
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Beef tenderloin about 200g (sliced thinly)  
🥯 Bagels 2 (halved)  
🥬 Lettuce 1 leaf  
🧀 Cheese slice 1 slice  
🧈 Butter about 5g (a small piece)  
🥚 White mayonnaise as needed  
🧂 Black pepper (ground) as needed  
🧂 Sea salt (ground) as needed  
🍊 Orange-yellow mayonnaise as needed  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🪵 Wooden cutting board  
🔪 Knife  
🔧 Grinder bottle  

## Preparation Steps
1. Slice the beef tenderloin into thin pieces, and halve the bagels.
   **Note**: Cut the meat slices as thin as possible for quick cooking and tenderness.
2. In a pan without oil, dry toast the bagels cut-side down over medium-low heat until lightly golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.
3. Place the beef tenderloin slices in the pan and pan-fry over medium heat until one side changes color, then flip.
4. When the meat slices are nearly fully cooked, add butter, letting the melted butter enhance the beef's aroma.
5. Before removing from heat, grind black pepper and sea salt onto both sides of the meat slices, turn to coat evenly, then remove from heat.
6. Assemble the sandwich: Take the bottom half of the bagel, spread white mayonnaise on the cut side, layer with cheese slice, lettuce leaf, and the pan-fried beef tenderloin slices. Squeeze on the orange-yellow mayonnaise, and top with the bagel top half.

## Health Advice  
Beef tenderloin is high in protein and low in fat, paired with bagels for carbohydrates, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts or quick energy replenishment. However, the combination of two sauces is higher in calories, so it is recommended to control the amount of mayonnaise used. Pan-frying quickly over medium heat maintains the tenderness of the beef, avoiding overcooking that would toughen the texture.
