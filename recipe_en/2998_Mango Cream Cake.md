# Mango Cream Cake
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥭 4-5 small Tainong mangoes (60g for decoration, two portions of 120g each for filling)
🎂 1 chiffon cake base, 6 inches (baked in advance, horizontally sliced into 3 layers)
🥛 400g whipping cream (Blue Windmill recommended)
🍬 24g powdered sugar

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔌 Electric hand mixer
🔪 Spatula
🪚 Serrated knife
🥢 Tongs
🕸️ Sieve
🌀 Turntable
🧊 Ice water (for whipping cream over ice)

## Instructions
1. Use a serrated knife to horizontally cut the chiffon cake base into 3 even layers of equal thickness.
2. Peel the mangoes and remove the flesh. Select 60g of neatly shaped mango cubes and set aside for decoration. Dice the remaining flesh and divide into two portions, each about 120g, for the filling.
3. Add powdered sugar to the whipping cream. Place the bowl over ice water and whip on high speed with an electric hand mixer.
   - **Key point**: Once the cream shows clear lines and has some firmness, first remove a portion of the firmer cream for the filling. Continue whipping the remaining cream until slightly softer and flowable for spreading, to make later adjustments easier.
4. Place one cake layer on the turntable. Spread about 40g of cream, evenly arrange 120g of mango cubes, then cover with about 40g of cream and smooth it out.
5. Place the second cake layer on top, gently press to adhere, and repeat step 4.
6. Place the final cake layer on top and gently press. Add about 100g of cream to the top, first smooth the top, allowing some cream to naturally flow to the sides, then roughly coat the sides.
7. Check the consistency of the remaining cream. If it has softened, briefly rewhip until smooth and slightly firm. Use the spatula to evenly spread the cream on the sides, then finalize the top and sides until smooth and even.
8. Use tongs to arrange the reserved 60g of mango cubes in a ring on top of the cake.
9. If mirror glaze is unavailable, lightly dust a thin layer of anti-humidity powdered sugar through a sieve for decoration. Use sparingly to avoid buildup.

## Health Advice
Mangoes are rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber. The cake's calories primarily come from the cream and sugar, making it suitable for summer party sharing. Diabetics or those monitoring sugar intake should consume in small amounts, no more than one small slice per serving.
