# Spicy Mixed Potato Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potato, about 5 pieces (peeled and sliced)
🌾 Potato starch, as needed
🥛 Pure milk, as needed
🧂 Salt, as needed
🧂 Light soy sauce, as needed
🌶️ Chili powder, as needed
🧄 Minced garlic, as needed
🌸 Sichuan peppercorn powder, as needed
🫘 Sesame seeds, as needed
🧅 Chopped scallions, as needed
🌿 Cilantro, as needed
🫒 Hot oil, as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🚰 Vegetable washing basin
🍳 Wok
🥄 Strainer
🍲 Cooking pot
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks, spoon
🔪 Knife, cutting board

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash and peel the potatoes, then cut into uniform slices about 0.5 cm thick.
2. Heat oil in a wok, add the potato slices, sprinkle with an appropriate amount of salt, and fry until both sides are golden brown, then turn off the heat.
   **Note**: Frying fully releases the aroma of the potatoes.
3. While still hot, pour in the pure milk, and use a tool or your hands to press the potato slices into a smooth mashed potato.
   **Key point**: Use the residual heat to fully incorporate the milk, achieving a dense and moist mashed potato consistency.
4. Gradually add potato starch to the mashed potatoes, kneading as you go, until a soft, non-sticky, and pliable dough forms.
   **Note**: The amount of starch should be flexibly adjusted based on the dough's moisture; adding it in batches makes it easier to control.
5. Take the dough and roll it into evenly thick long strips. They can be coiled into rings or dropped directly into the pot.
6. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add the potato noodle strips, and cook until they all float to the surface and are noticeably expanded, with a transparent and soft texture, then remove.
7. Place the cooked potato noodles into a bowl, and sequentially add light soy sauce, chili powder, Sichuan peppercorn powder, minced garlic, sesame seeds, chopped scallions, and cilantro.
8. Heat oil in a wok until smoking, pour it over the seasonings to release their aroma, mix well, and serve.

## Health Advice
These potato noodles are freshly made from potatoes, containing fewer additives than instant versions, making them suitable for carb lovers who enjoy cooking. Be mindful of the amount of hot oil used in the dressing to prevent the calorie and spiciness levels from rising together.
