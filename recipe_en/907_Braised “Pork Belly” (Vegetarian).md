# Braised “Pork Belly” (Vegetarian)
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥒 Skin-on winter melon, to taste (cut into cubes, score the skin side in a crosshatch pattern)  
🫘 Firm tofu, to taste (cut into cubes the same size as the winter melon pieces)  
🧅 Scallion, ginger, garlic, to taste (for layering at the bottom to infuse aroma)  
🥬 Drought-tolerant radish seedlings (or similar edible plant stems and leaves), to taste (blanched until soft, used for tying)  
🫐 Jujubes, 3-4 pieces  
🧂 Salt, to taste (for marinating the winter melon skin)  
🧴 Dark soy sauce, to taste (for marinating the winter melon skin)  
🍶 Red yeast rice broth, to taste (core seasoning for color and flavor)  
⭐ Star anise, about 2 pieces  
🌿 Cinnamon stick, 1 small piece  
🍃 Bay leaves, 2 pieces  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board  
🍳 Wok/frying pan  
🫕 Clay pot  
🥄 Spoon  

## Cooking Steps  
1. **Prepare the winter melon skin**: Score the skin side of the winter melon in a crosshatch pattern. Evenly apply salt and a little dark soy sauce, then let it marinate briefly to release moisture, toughen, and develop color, creating a texture similar to pork skin.  
2. **Cut the tofu**: Cut the firm tofu into cubes the same size as the winter melon pieces.  
3. **Fry the tofu**: Heat a generous amount of oil in a wok to about 70% hot. Add the tofu cubes and fry until the surface turns golden and forms a hard crust. Remove and drain the oil.  
   - **Note**: The oil must be hot enough to quickly set the tofu.  
4. **Prepare the tying material**: Wash the drought-tolerant radish seedlings, blanch them in boiling water until soft, and set aside.  
5. **Assemble the vegetarian “meat” pieces**: Stack in the order of “winter melon piece (skin side up) – tofu cube – fried tofu cube.” Tie the stack firmly with the blanched radish seedlings in a cross pattern, ensuring all three layers are the same size and securely bound.  
6. **Arrange the pot**: Layer the bottom of the clay pot with scallion segments, ginger slices, and garlic cloves. Place the tied vegetarian pieces skin side down neatly in the pot.  
7. **First braising with seasoning**: Add star anise, cinnamon stick, bay leaves, and jujubes. Evenly pour in the red yeast rice broth. Cover and simmer over low heat for 15 minutes.  
   - **Key point**: Do not add water initially; rely on the moisture from the ingredients and a small amount of broth for braising.  
8. **Flip and reduce the sauce**: Carefully open the lid and flip the pieces so the winter melon skin faces upward. Continue simmering over low heat until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients, achieving the state described as “slowly braised to an amber luster.”  
   - **Key point**: Flip to ensure both sides are evenly flavored.  

## Health Tips  
This dish replaces meat with winter melon and tofu, making it low in fat and calories while rich in dietary fiber and plant protein. It is suitable for weight loss and vegetarian diets. The red yeast rice broth has a high salt content, so those with high blood pressure should moderate their intake.
