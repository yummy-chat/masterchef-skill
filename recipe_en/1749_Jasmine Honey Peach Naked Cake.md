# Jasmine Honey Peach Naked Cake
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥛 Whipping cream 200g  
🍵 Jasmine tea powder 3 packets (about 1g each, used respectively for cream, tea jelly, and milk jelly)  
🥛 Milk 250g  
💧 Water 250g  
🍮 Gelatin sheets 2 pieces (10g total, for tea jelly) + 1 piece (10g, for milk jelly, both softened in ice water in advance)  
🍑 Fresh honey peach appropriate amount (cut into small dices)  
🍃 Mint leaves appropriate amount (for decoration)  
🍰 Cake slices 2 slices (plain and pandan flavor, pre-baked)  
🍬 White sugar 12g (for whipping cream) + 10g (for tea jelly) + 10g (for milk jelly)  
🍋 Lemon juice appropriate amount (to marinate peach and prevent oxidation)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for ice water bath)  
🌀 Electric hand mixer  
🍳 Saucepan  
🔪 Knife  
🥄 Spoon  
🎂 Mousse collar (secured with tape)  
🫗 Piping bag  
✂️ Scissors  
🥣 Bowl (for cutting cake slices)  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  

## Instructions

### Prepare Jasmine Cream
1. Place whipping cream, white sugar, and jasmine tea powder in a stainless steel bowl. Set the bowl over an ice water bath and whip with an electric hand mixer until thick and pipeable.
   **Note**: The ice water bath maintains low temperature for stability; jasmine tea powder is unsweetened, so adjust sugar to taste.

### Make Jasmine Tea Jelly
1. In a saucepan, combine water, white sugar, and jasmine tea powder. Heat and stir until the sugar is fully dissolved.
2. Remove from heat, add the gelatin sheets previously softened in ice water, and stir until completely dissolved.
3. Pour into a flat container and refrigerate for about half an hour until set. Remove and cut into small pieces for later use.

### Make Jasmine Milk Jelly
1. Substitute water with milk, add white sugar and jasmine tea powder, and repeat the steps for tea jelly.
2. After refrigerating until set, cut into small pieces for later use.

### Prepare Fruit
1. Dice the fresh honey peach into small cubes. Mix with lemon juice and a little sugar. Marinate for 5–10 minutes.
   **Note**: Lemon juice and sugar prevent oxidation and add flavor.

### Prepare Cake Slices
1. Take the pre-baked plain and pandan round cake slices. Use a bowl of suitable size to invert and cut out the desired dimensions.
   **Note**: Store-bought cake slices can also be used.

### Make Simple Piping Tip
1. At the tip of the piping bag, first make a straight cut, then a diagonal cut to form a star-shaped opening.

### Assemble the Cake
1. Place one cake slice on a cake board. Wrap it with a mousse collar and secure with tape.
2. Evenly pipe a layer of jasmine cream onto the cake slice. Spread the marinated peach dices on top.
3. Cover with the second cake slice. Pipe cream evenly and distribute the jasmine tea jelly and milk jelly pieces on top.
4. Cover with the third cake slice. Use the homemade simple piping tip to pipe a ring of cream border around the top edge.
5. Place the remaining peach dices in the center of the top. Garnish with mint leaves.

## Health Advice  
This cake combines the fresh aroma of jasmine with the sweetness of honey peach. The dual texture of tea jelly and milk jelly enriches the layers. Suitable for those who prefer lightly sweet cakes, but the amount of cream and sugar is relatively high, so controlling the single serving size is recommended. Pair with unsweetened green tea to balance the sweetness.
