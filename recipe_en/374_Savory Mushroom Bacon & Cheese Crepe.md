# Savory Mushroom Bacon & Cheese Crepe  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥓 Bacon – to taste (sliced or cut into small pieces)  
🍄 Mushrooms – to taste (sliced)  
🌾 Whole wheat flour – 100 g  
💧 Water – 200 g (more can be added to thin the batter)  
🥚 Eggs – about 2 (1 beaten into the batter, 1 whole egg for the center of the filling, remaining egg liquid for the filling)  
🧈 Butter – a little (melted, for brushing the pan and the batter)  
🧀 Cheese – to taste (grated, part for the filling, part for garnish)  
🌿 Fresh parsley – to taste (chopped, for garnish)  
🧂 Salt – a pinch  
🌶️ Black pepper – a pinch  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Kitchen knife, cutting board  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🥣 Glass bowl, measuring cup, whisk  
🔌 Electric mixer  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥞 Crepe maker (or frying pan)  
🪞 Spatula  
🧇 Grater  
🍲 Large lid  
🍽️ Serving plate  

## Instructions  
1. Slice the bacon and mushrooms.  
2. Heat a non-stick frying pan, add the bacon slices and stir-fry until the fat renders. Add the mushroom slices and cook until tender and the liquid has evaporated.  
3. Season with salt and black pepper, stir well, then remove and set aside.  
4. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, combine 100 g whole wheat flour. Gradually add 200 g water while stirring. If the batter is too thick, add more water to make it easier to spread into thin crepes.  
5. Add half an egg’s worth of beaten egg and a little melted butter. Beat with an electric mixer until smooth and lump-free.  
6. Preheat the crepe maker and brush its surface with melted butter.  
7. Pour the batter onto the center of the machine, quickly spread it with a spatula into a thin, even round crepe. Cook over medium heat until the edges lift and the surface is set.  
8. Place the cooked bacon and mushroom filling in the center of the crepe. Sprinkle with some grated cheese, drizzle with the remaining beaten egg, and crack a whole egg in the center.  
9. Use a spatula to fold the four edges of the crepe toward the center, covering the filling to form a square.  
10. Cover with a large lid, reduce the heat to low, and let steam until the surface egg is fully cooked.  
11. Slide the crepe onto a plate, garnish with fresh chopped parsley and a little grated cheese.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is rich in protein and dietary fiber. The mushrooms and whole wheat crepe base are the highlights, making it suitable for those who exercise or need an energy boost. Note that bacon and cheese are high in sodium; people with high blood pressure should reduce the amount or choose low-sodium versions.
