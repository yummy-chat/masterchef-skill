# Baked Sweet Potatoes  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November–December)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍠 Sweet potatoes (red or yellow honey yam, amount as desired)  
💧 Water, half a bowl (for pouring into the bottom of the fryer basket)  
💧 Cold water, one basin (for soaking sweet potatoes)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer  
🥣 Bowls (for holding water and cold water)  

## Steps  
1. Thoroughly scrub the skin of the sweet potatoes clean. No need to peel.  
2. Pour about half a bowl of water into the bottom of the air fryer basket.  
   **Note**: Adding water to the pan bottom prevents the sweet potatoes from drying out; dry baking directly can make the flesh dry and tough.  
3. Place the washed sweet potatoes in the basket, set the temperature to 200°C, and bake for 20 minutes.  
4. Once the first baking is done, immediately remove the sweet potatoes and quickly soak them in the prepared cold water for a short while.  
   **Key point**: Use the thermal expansion and contraction principle of alternating hot and cold to encourage the sweet potatoes to release syrup.  
5. Return the water-soaked sweet potatoes to the basket, set to 200°C, and continue baking for another 20 minutes.  
6. After the second baking, when the skin is dry and the inside is fully cooked, remove and enjoy.  
   **Finished state**: The inside is orange-yellow, sweet, soft, and sticky, not dry or tough, with abundant syrup.  

## Health Advice  
Sweet potatoes are rich in dietary fiber and vitamin A. Baking makes them easier to digest and absorb, making them suitable for warming up in winter. Diabetics should control their intake, as the natural sugars concentrate under high heat.
