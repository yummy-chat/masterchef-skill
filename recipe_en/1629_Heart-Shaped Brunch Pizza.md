# Heart-Shaped Brunch Pizza  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍞 Bread flour/pizza flour, as needed  
🫙 Natural starter, as needed (unactivated, only as part of the recipe)  
🫙 Dry yeast, a small amount (to rescue dough fermentation)  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🫒 Olive oil, 20-40g  
🍕 Pizza sauce, as needed  
🧀 Fresh mozzarella cheese, one large piece (partially sliced)  
🥓 Turkey bacon, as needed (cut into small pieces)  
🌿 Fresh basil leaves, a few  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔧 Dough scraper  
🧑‍🍳 Pastry mat  
🥖 Rolling pin  
🍳 Baking tray  
📄 Parchment paper  
🔥 Oven  
✂️ Kitchen scissors  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🧊 Cooling rack  

## Instructions  
1. Dough rescue and kneading: Turn the pre-mixed dough onto a lightly floured surface. Since the natural starter is unactivated, add a small amount of dry yeast. Fold and knead repeatedly until the dough is smooth and even.  
2. Shaping: Use a rolling pin to roll the dough into an even thickness. Trim it into a heart shape with kitchen scissors. Transfer to a baking tray lined with parchment paper, and let it rest for a while.  
3. Prepare toppings: Slice the fresh mozzarella cheese into thick pieces. Dice the turkey bacon into small cubes. Any leftover cheese should be wrapped in foil and stored in the freezer.  
4. Assemble the pizza: Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle the diced bacon on top, then arrange the cheese slices in a staggered pattern.  
5. Bake: Place the tray into the preheated oven, positioning it as close to the top heating element as possible. Bake until the pizza edges are golden and the cheese is fully melted with some browned spots.  
6. Decorate: Remove the pizza from the oven and place it on a cooling rack. Scatter fresh basil leaves on top. Cut with kitchen scissors and serve while hot.  

## Health Advice  
This pizza replaces traditional pork bacon with turkey bacon, reducing fat intake by about 30%. Paired with mozzarella cheese, it also provides calcium. Suitable for those who want to enjoy pizza while managing their weight.
