# Low-Calorie Low-Fat Luosifen Fried Egg  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 Eggs 2  
🫒 Cooking oil as needed (spray type)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer  
🥣 Round foil baking pan  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🧴 Oil spray bottle  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk thoroughly with chopsticks until well combined to form egg liquid.  
2. Evenly spray a thin layer of oil on the inner surface of the foil baking pan, then pour in the egg liquid.  
3. Evenly spray another thin layer of oil on the surface of the egg liquid.  
4. Place the baking pan into the air fryer, set the temperature to 200℃, and bake for 5 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Bake until the egg liquid is fully set, puffy and expanded, with a golden brown surface.  
5. Remove immediately once done, resulting in a golden, fluffy low-fat fried egg. Great for pairing with Luosifen soup or other broths, offering excellent juice absorption.  

## Health Tips  
Eggs are a high-quality protein source. The air fryer method with reduced oil is suitable for those on a weight loss diet. However, egg yolks are high in cholesterol, so it is recommended not to exceed one whole egg per day. When pairing with Luosifen, be mindful of sodium intake and add more vegetables to balance the meal.
