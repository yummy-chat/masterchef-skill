# Glutinous Rice Bean Buns
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫘 Red kidney beans 400g
🍚 Water-milled glutinous rice flour 500g
💧 Hot water 350ml (70-80℃)
🍬 White sugar 2 tablespoons (adjustable to taste)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for greasing the steaming mat)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl (for soaking and kneading dough)
♨️ Electric pressure cooker (for cooking beans)
🍽️ Large bowl (for mashing beans)
🥄 Chopsticks (for stirring)
🤲 Plastic wrap (for covering the dough)
🥟 Steamer
📜 Steaming mat (or parchment paper)
🖌️ Silicone brush (for brushing oil)

## Steps
1. Soak the red kidney beans in plenty of water overnight until fully expanded.
2. Pour the water-milled glutinous rice flour into a bowl. Gradually add hot water at 70-80℃ while stirring with chopsticks until it forms flocculent pieces. Let it cool slightly, then knead into a smooth dough. Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest overnight.
3. Pour the soaked kidney beans along with the soaking water into an electric pressure cooker. Select the "Beans/Tendons" program and cook until soft and tender. Drain the cooked beans, transfer them to a large bowl, add white sugar while still hot, and mash into a slightly chunky paste. Let the bean paste cool, then roll into evenly sized balls and refrigerate until set.
4. Take a piece of the rested glutinous rice dough, roll it into a ball, and press it into a bowl shape in your palm. Place a chilled bean filling ball in the center. Use the tiger's mouth of your hand to gather the dough upward, pinching the opening to seal tightly. Gently roll it smooth.
5. Lightly brush the steaming mat with cooking oil. Place the assembled buns close together on the mat. Once the steamer water is boiling, place the buns in, cover, and steam over medium heat for 15 minutes.
   **Doneness check**: The buns are ready when the lid is covered with water droplets, the buns have visibly expanded, and the surface appears translucent and glossy.
6. Serve hot. The outer skin is soft and stretchy, the filling is moderately sweet with a natural chunky texture from the red beans.

## Health Tips
Glutinous rice bean buns are rich in carbohydrates and dietary fiber, making them suitable for post-workout energy replenishment or as a breakfast option. However, individuals with diabetes should control sugar intake. The red kidney bean filling is rich in iron and protein, but overconsumption should be avoided to prevent gastrointestinal discomfort.
