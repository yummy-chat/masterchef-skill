🥣 Large transparent glass bowl (for mixing the final product)  

## Steps
1. Cut the mango in half and scrape the flesh using the rim of a small glass. Mash most of the flesh into a puree in a glass jar, and set aside a small amount cut into small pieces.
2. Cut the passion fruit open and scoop out the seeds with a spoon.
3. Slice the orange into thick pieces about the thickness of two coins.
4. Slice the lemon and remove the seeds; halve the small green citrus or cut into thick slices.
5. Peel and deseed the watermelon, then cut the flesh into bite-sized cubes.
6. In the large glass bowl, layer the watermelon chunks, mango puree, and passion fruit seeds at the bottom.
7. Layer the lemon slices and orange slices on top, then squeeze the juice from the small green citrus into the bowl, and add the fruit flesh as well.
8. Add a large amount of ice cubes, then pour in an appropriate amount of fruit syrup.
9. Slowly pour in the cooled jasmine tea and gently mix everything together.

## Health Advice
This fruit tea is rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, making it an ideal summer drink to beat the heat and quench thirst. However, be mindful of the sugar content from the fruit syrup and the fruit itself; those with diabetes should drink with caution. It is recommended to replace the ice cubes with chilled tea to reduce dilution of flavor.
