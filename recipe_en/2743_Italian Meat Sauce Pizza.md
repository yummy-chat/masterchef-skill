# Italian Meat Sauce Pizza
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 300g bread flour (protein content above 12g)
💧 210g water
🍬 20g sugar
🫙 3g yeast
🧂 3g salt
🧈 10-20g butter (softened at room temperature)
🍝 Italian meat sauce (as needed, spread in a thick layer)
🧀 Mozzarella cheese (as needed, generous amount)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔥 Oven
♨️ Air fryer
🥣 Mixing bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🍞 Cutting board
🧊 Silicone mat
🍴 Fork
📜 Parchment paper
🥄 Spoon

## Steps
1. Place bread flour, water, sugar, yeast, salt, and softened butter into a mixing bowl. First, stir with chopsticks until flaky, then knead into a dough by hand.
   **Note**: The dough has high hydration and will be very sticky, which is normal. If it's too difficult to handle, use a small amount of dry flour, but try not to add extra flour.
2. Cover and let the dough rest for 15 minutes. Then perform the first fold: pull the dough from the edges toward the center, folding in a circular motion for 1-2 turns.
3. Cover again and rest for 15 minutes. Perform the second fold using the same method.
4. Cover and rest for another 15 minutes. Perform the third fold. The dough should have noticeably improved extensibility at this point. After folding, cover and let it ferment in a warm place until the interior has a honeycomb structure.
   **Key point**: The fermented dough can be refrigerated overnight or longer for better flavor.
5. Sprinkle a small amount of dry flour on a cutting board. Turn out the fermented dough, gently press to release air, and divide into two or four portions.
6. Take one portion of dough. Use your hands to flatten and spread it into a round base with a thin center and slightly thicker edges, leaving a "tire rim" around the edge.
   **Note**: Do not use a rolling pin for shaping. Simply pat and spread the dough with your hands.
7. Transfer the dough base to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Use a fork to prick small holes evenly across the crust, leaving the edges unpricked.
8. Spread a thick layer of Italian meat sauce over the crust, then sprinkle generously with mozzarella cheese.
   **Key point**: Use ample amounts of both sauce and cheese. The hallmark of homemade pizza is generous toppings.
9. Bake in a preheated oven or air fryer:
   - Oven: Upper heat 220°C, lower heat 210°C, about 15 minutes.
   - Air fryer: 190°C, about 20 minutes.
   Bake until the cheese is melted and golden brown, and the crust edges are a deep brown.
   **Key point**: Baking parameters may need slight adjustments based on your specific equipment.

## Health Advice
This pizza provides a balanced mix of carbohydrates, protein, and fat, making it suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. However, it is relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition.
