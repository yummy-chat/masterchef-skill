# Braised Lamb Ribs with Radish
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Lamb ribs 400g
🥕 One white radish
🧅 One large scallion
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorns 10 pieces
🌸 Angelica dahurica (optional)
🧄 Ginger 10g (usually buy one piece of ginger, cut about 4 slices)
🍶 Cooking wine or rice wine 30ml-40ml
🧂 Table salt 10g
🍬 Rock sugar 2-4 pieces

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Knife
🥄 Spoon
🍲 Pressure cooker
🍳 Regular pot

## Preparation Steps
1. Peel the white radish, cut into large chunks of 3-5 cm using a rolling cut, set aside.
2. Chop the lamb ribs into pieces, set aside.
3. Place the lamb ribs in a pot with cold water, add half of the cooking wine and half of the scallion and ginger. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and blanch for 10 minutes. Skim off any foam during this time, then drain the ribs.
4. In another pot, add water and bring to a boil. Add the radish chunks and half of the rock sugar. Boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and cook for 5 minutes. Drain to remove the spiciness of the radish.
5. Place the blanched lamb ribs into the pressure cooker. Add enough water to cover the ingredients, then add about 300 ml more.
6. Add the remaining scallion segments, ginger slices, cooking wine, Sichuan peppercorns, rock sugar, angelica dahurica, and salt into the pot. Cover the lid. Once the pressure cooker reaches pressure, reduce to medium heat and cook for 15 minutes. If you do not have a pressure cooker, use a regular pot instead. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour.
7. Turn off the heat. Allow the pressure cooker to release pressure naturally, then open the lid. Add the blanched radish chunks. Taste the broth and adjust the saltiness by adding 3-10 g more salt or a small amount of water as needed.
8. Cover the lid again. Heat over medium heat until the cooker reaches pressure, then continue to cook for 10 minutes.
9. Turn off the heat. Release the pressure from the cooker, then open the lid. Serve the dish in a bowl.

⚠️ Key Points
During the braising process, monitor the liquid level. If the broth falls below 2/3 of the ingredients' height, add an appropriate amount of hot water to keep the ingredients fully submerged.

## Health Suggestions
Braised lamb ribs with radish is an excellent winter warming dish. Lamb is rich in high-quality protein and iron, while white radish aids digestion and cuts through the grease. It is suitable for people with a cold body constitution or those needing an energy boost. Ensure thorough blanching to remove the gamey flavor and cook the radish long enough to eliminate its spiciness. Braise for sufficient time to ensure the meat is tender and soft. Patients with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of salt appropriately.
