# Tuna Salad Sandwich
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥫 Canned tuna in water 65g
🍞 Square sliced bread 2 slices
🧀 Cheese slice (optional)
🍖 Ham slice (optional)
🥚 Mayonnaise 50mL
🥒 Russian pickled cucumber juice 10-15mL

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Spoon
🔧 Sandwich press

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the canned tuna in water, mayonnaise, and Russian pickled cucumber juice in a bowl. Stir with a spoon until the tuna chunks are fully broken apart and blended with the sauce, forming a smooth paste. Set aside.
   **Key point**: The tuna salad can be prepared the night before and stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for no more than one week.

2. Take one slice of square bread and place it flat on the bottom of the sandwich press.

3. Evenly spread the tuna salad prepared in step 1 onto the surface of the bread. A recommended spreading amount is about 10–15mL, but can be adjusted according to personal taste.

4. Place the other slice of square bread on top of the spread surface. Press down the top lid of the sandwich press to ensure a tight fit. Confirm the power is disconnected before pressing down and locking, then plug in and heat.
   **Note**: Make sure the power is not connected before pressing down to ensure operational safety.

5. Once the sandwich press automatically stops heating, remove the sandwich and place it on a plate for serving.

## Health Advice
This sandwich is rich in high-quality protein and Omega-3, making it suitable for fast-paced office workers or as a post-workout snack. However, mayonnaise is high in calories; it is recommended to substitute half with Greek yogurt for a refreshing taste without compromising flavor.
