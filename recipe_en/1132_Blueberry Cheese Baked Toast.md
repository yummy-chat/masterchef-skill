# Blueberry Cheese Baked Toast  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍞 Bread slices, 2 slices  
🫐 Frozen blueberries, about 10 pieces  
🧀 Shredded cheese, to taste  

## Cookware & Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer oven  
📄 Baking tray and parchment paper  

## Steps  
1. Line the baking tray with parchment paper, place the two bread slices on the tray, evenly sprinkle shredded cheese on the surface of the bread, then place several frozen blueberries on top.  
   **Note**: Use the blueberries directly from frozen; no need to thaw in advance.  
2. Place the baking tray on the middle rack of the air fryer oven, and bake at 180°C for 10 minutes.  
   **Doneness check**: Bake until the cheese is completely melted, the surface turns golden brown, and a rich aroma is released.  
3. Remove and enjoy while hot. The finished product is crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, with the savory flavor of cheese blending with the sweet and sour taste of blueberries.  

## Health Tips  
Blueberries are rich in antioxidants, cheese provides calcium, and pairing with whole wheat bread can increase dietary fiber. Suitable as breakfast or afternoon tea, but be mindful of cheese portions to avoid excessive calorie intake.
