# Cold Eggplant Salad
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍆 Eggplant 600g (about 3, cut into strips, steamed, then torn into pieces)
🌶️ Green chili pepper 30g (about 2, finely diced)
🌶️ Bird's eye chili 10-20g (about 5, finely diced)
🌿 Cilantro 20g (about 2 stalks, minced)
🧄 Garlic 20g (about 5 cloves, minced)
🥜 White sesame seeds 10g (about 1 spoon, can substitute with crushed peanuts)
🫒 Sesame oil 10g (1 spoon)
🫗 Cooking oil appropriate amount (2-3 spoons, for drizzling hot oil)
💧 Light soy sauce 30g (3 spoons)
🦪 Oyster sauce 10g (half spoon)
🍶 Aged vinegar 7g (2/3 spoon, can increase to taste)
🍬 Sugar 3g (1 teaspoon)
🌼 Sichuan pepper oil 3g (1/3 spoon)
🧂 Salt appropriate amount (add last according to taste)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Knife, cutting board
🍲 Steamer (with steamer rack/steamer cloth)
🍳 Small pot (for heating oil)
🥣 Metal bowl, glass bowl
🥄 Spoon, 🥢 Chopsticks
🧤 Disposable gloves (for mixing)

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the eggplants, cut off the stems, split lengthwise into quarters, then cut into strips. Finely dice the green chili peppers and bird's eye chilies, crush and mince the garlic, and mince the cilantro.
2. Place the diced green chili peppers, bird's eye chilies, minced garlic, and minced cilantro into a bowl. Add white sesame seeds and sesame oil, stir well, and set aside.
   **Note**: Mixing in sesame oil beforehand prevents the ingredients from being scorched by the hot oil when drizzled later, and enhances aroma.
3. Bring water in the steamer to a boil over high heat. Place a steamer cloth on the steamer rack, spread the eggplant strips on top. Steam for 10 minutes once steam rises, until a chopstick can easily pierce through.
   **Key point**: Start timing only after the water has boiled and steam is rising.
4. Remove the steamed eggplants, let them cool slightly until not too hot to handle. Tear them into strips by hand, gently press with a spoon to remove excess water, to avoid a watery final dish.
5. Pour cooking oil into a small pot, heat over high heat until the oil smokes. While stirring the ingredient bowl, quickly drizzle the hot oil over the ingredients in batches until all are evenly coated.
6. Add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, aged vinegar, sugar, and Sichuan pepper oil into the oil-drizzled ingredient bowl, stir to form the seasoning sauce.
   **Note**: Do not add too much Sichuan pepper oil, or it may become bitter; aged vinegar can be slightly increased to personal taste.
7. Pour the seasoning sauce entirely into the bowl with the eggplant strips. Wearing gloves, gently mix and toss until each eggplant strip is evenly coated with the sauce.
8. Taste and add salt as needed, mix well, then plate. Since light soy sauce and oyster sauce already contain salt, add salt last to avoid excessive saltiness.

## Health Suggestions
This steamed eggplant dish is low in oil and high in fiber, suitable as a main dish during weight loss. Pay attention to reducing salt intake and avoid excessively high-calorie sauce. Those with sensitive stomachs should be cautious with the stimulation from chili and vinegar. Avoid lying down immediately after eating.
