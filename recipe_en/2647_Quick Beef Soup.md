# Quick Beef Soup
Cuisine: Common home cooking
Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Lean beef, about 50g (pre-cooked and thawed)
🍲 Beef broth/stock, about 300–400 ml
🥚 Egg, 1 (raw)
🥬 Celery stalks, as needed (frozen)
🍃 Dried seaweed, a small pinch
🧂 Salt, to taste
🧄 Fried garlic (or fried shallots), 1 tablespoon

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🛡️ Ziplock bags (for portioning and freezing)
🥣 Heat-resistant bowl with lid (with handles)
✂️ Kitchen scissors
📡 Microwave
🥄 Soup spoon

## Cooking Steps
1. Freeze pre-cooked beef pieces and broth in separate portions in advance. Thaw before use.
2. Pour the thawed beef and broth into a heat-resistant bowl. Add the raw egg and dried seaweed.
3. Use kitchen scissors to cut the frozen celery stalks into small pieces directly into the bowl. Season with salt and stir gently.
   **Note**: Frozen vegetables do not need to be thawed and can be used directly.
4. Cover with a microwave-safe vented lid and microwave on medium-high heat for about 2 minutes, until the soup is piping hot and the egg is fully set.
   **Note**: Use a heat-resistant, vented lid to prevent the soup from boiling over.
5. Carefully remove from the microwave and stir in one tablespoon of fried garlic. Mix well and serve.
   **Key Point**: Add the fried garlic at the very end to maintain its crunchy texture and rich aroma.

## Health Advice
This beef soup is rich in protein and ideal for a quick energy boost. Frozen vegetables and broth help retain nutrients. Be mindful of salt intake, as the broth base already contains saltiness; those with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of dried seaweed used.
