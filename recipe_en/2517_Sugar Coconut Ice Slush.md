# Sugar Coconut Ice Slush
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍹 Bottled coconut juice 500ml (choose one with a wide mouth)  
🌰 Crushed nuts, as needed (optional)  
🧂 Coffee sugar 10g (approximately two packets of Taigu coffee sugar)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
❄️ Freezer compartment of refrigerator  
💪 Hard surface (such as a corner, chair back, or table edge, ensuring it will not be damaged)  

## Preparation Steps
1. Pour out about 200ml of the 500ml bottled coconut juice, then tighten the cap so that approximately 300ml of coconut juice remains in the bottle.  
2. Place the prepared coconut juice bottle in the freezer and freeze for at least 10 hours, until the coconut juice is completely frozen solid.  
3. Remove the frozen coconut juice bottle, confirm that the contents are fully solidified, and firmly hit it against a hard surface until the frozen coconut juice inside turns into a slushy state.  
4. Open the cap and pour the slush into a bowl.  
5. Evenly sprinkle coffee sugar or crushed nuts over the surface of the slush, then serve.  

## Health Advice
This sugar coconut ice slush is low in calories, and coconut water is rich in electrolytes, making it suitable for post-exercise refreshment or summer relief. However, using coffee sugar adds empty calories; it is recommended to reduce the amount or use a substitute sweetener. Crushed nuts can add healthy fats.
