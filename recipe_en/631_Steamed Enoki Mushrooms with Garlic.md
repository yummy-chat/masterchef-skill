# Steamed Enoki Mushrooms with Garlic
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍄 Enoki mushrooms (1 bunch, roots trimmed)  
🧄 Garlic (2 heads, minced and divided into three equal portions)  
🦪 Oyster sauce (1 tbsp)  
🍬 Sugar (1 tbsp)  
🧂 Salt (a pinch)  
🥄 Light soy sauce (2 tbsp)  
🫒 Sesame oil (to taste, for drizzling after cooking)  
🌿 Scallions (to taste, for sprinkling after cooking)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife  
🍳 Wok  
🍲 Steamer  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Instructions  
1. Trim the roots off the enoki mushrooms and arrange them in a radial pattern on a plate.
2. Place the wok over heat. Once hot, add oil and the first portion of minced garlic. Fry over low heat until golden and fragrant.
   **Key point**: This step creates the rich, crispy garlic flavor.
3. Turn off the heat and immediately add the second portion of minced garlic. Stir for about 15 seconds, using the residual heat to release its aroma.
4. Pour the hot oil and mixed garlic from the wok into a bowl containing the third portion of raw minced garlic. This creates a composite garlic sauce that combines fried, semi-cooked, and raw garlic flavors.
5. Add oyster sauce, sugar, salt, and light soy sauce to the bowl. Stir well to combine into a garlic sauce.
6. Evenly pour the garlic sauce over the enoki mushrooms.
7. Once the steamer is steaming, place the plate inside and steam for 8 minutes.
8. After removing from the steamer, drizzle with sesame oil and sprinkle with scallions to serve.

## Health Tips  
This dish is low in fat and high in fiber, making it ideal for satisfying cravings during weight loss. However, oyster sauce and light soy sauce are high in sodium—those with high blood pressure should avoid drinking the broth.
