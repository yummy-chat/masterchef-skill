## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare the pork trotters**  
   Scrape and clean the pork trotters thoroughly, then cut into evenly sized bite-sized pieces. Note the different parts: the front part is crunchy, while the rear part is rich in collagen.
2. **Blanch and whiten**  
   Bring water to a boil in a pot, add适量 rice wine and white vinegar. Add the pork trotter pieces and cook over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes, until the color turns white and the skin tightens. This step removes odor and softens the collagen, giving the trotters a pale, clean appearance.
3. **Cook the pork trotters**  
   Heat another wok over high heat, add cold oil, and sauté the crushed ginger and whole scallions until fragrant. Splash in rice wine along the side of the wok, then add enough water and bring to a boil over high heat. Add the blanched trotter pieces. Once boiling again, add the angelica slices, star anise, bay leaves, and适量 salt. Reduce to medium heat, cover, and cook for about 40 minutes, until the trotters are tender and a chopstick can easily pierce through. This cooking process allows the trotters to absorb the aromatic flavors and removes any off-flavors.
4. **Prepare the sweet and sour sauce**  
   In a clear glass pot, mix water, white vinegar, and rock sugar in a 1:1:1 ratio, then add the mashed preserved plums. Bring to a boil over high heat until the rock sugar is completely dissolved. Reduce to low heat, add a small spoonful of salt, and stir. Once boiling, add the white radish cubes and sliced chili peppers. Finally, turn off the heat and add a few slices of fresh lemon. **Key point**: The sweet and sour sauce must include salt for a base flavor and must be completely cooled before use.
5. **Marinate for flavor**  
   Place the fully cooled cooked trotters into the completely cooled sweet and sour sauce. Seal with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight. **Key point**: The trotters and sweet and sour sauce must both be cooled before mixing; refrigerated marination allows the trotters to fully absorb the flavors, resulting in a crunchy and refreshing texture.

## Health Tips
This White Cloud Pork Trotter dish is rich in collagen, making it ideal for cooling and stimulating the appetite in summer. However, the sweet and sour sauce is relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with vegetables for a balanced diet. Ensure the trotters are fully cooled before marinating, and refrigerating overnight will yield a crunchier and more elastic texture.
