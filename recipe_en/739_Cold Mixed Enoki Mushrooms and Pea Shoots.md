# Cold Mixed Enoki Mushrooms and Pea Shoots
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 Pea shoots, one handful  
🍄 Enoki mushrooms, one handful  
🧂 Salt, to taste (for blanching)  
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste (for blanching)  
🥢 Soy sauce, to taste  
🫒 Sesame oil, to taste  
🌱 White sesame seeds, to taste  
🌶️ Chili oil, to taste (optional)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Pot  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🍽️ Plate  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot, then add salt and cooking oil.  
2. Add the enoki mushrooms and cook for 3 to 5 minutes until fully tender and soft.  
3. Add the pea shoots and continue cooking for about 30 seconds, until they turn bright green, then promptly remove to avoid overcooking.  
4. Transfer the blanched ingredients into a large bowl, add soy sauce, sesame oil, and white sesame seeds. Add chili oil to taste, then mix well with chopsticks.  
5. Plate the dish; arrange in a stacked shape for a more appealing presentation.  

## Health Advice  
This cold dish is low in fat and high in fiber. Enoki mushrooms and pea shoots are rich in vitamins and dietary fiber, making it suitable for weight loss or for those needing intestinal cleansing. Note that enoki mushrooms must be thoroughly cooked to avoid gastrointestinal discomfort; the blanching time for pea shoots should be short to retain their crisp texture and more nutrients.
