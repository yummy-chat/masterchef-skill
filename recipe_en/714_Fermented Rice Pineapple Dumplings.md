# Fermented Rice Pineapple Dumplings
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Spring (January-April)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Glutinous rice flour 250g  
💧 Water approx. 150g (for kneading)  
🍍 Pineapple half a bowl (chopped)  
🍶 Fermented rice 250g  
🔴 Goji berries适量  
🫚 Ginger a few slices  
🥚 Egg 1  
🍬 White sugar 3 tablespoons  
🍯 Brown sugar适量 (for coloring)  
🌽 Cornstarch a small amount (for slurry)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl (for kneading dough)  
🥄 Measuring cup  
🔪 Knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🍲 Deep pot (for cooking dumplings)  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥄 Soup ladle  
🍳 Shallow pot (for making the soup base)  
🥣 Small bowl  

## Steps
1. Place the glutinous rice flour in a bowl, gradually add a small amount of water, and knead until the bowl, hands, and dough are all clean.
   **Key point**: Add water slowly to avoid adding too much at once.
2. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest for 15 minutes to enhance elasticity.
3. Roll the rested dough into a long strip, dust the cutting board with a little glutinous rice flour to prevent sticking, cut into small pieces, and roll into balls. Set aside.
4. Bring water in a deep pot to a boil, add the dough balls, keep at a rolling boil until they all float to the surface, then remove and drain.
   **Note**: Add the dough balls after the water boils to prevent sticking; floating indicates they are fully cooked.
5. In a separate shallow pot, add about half a pot of water and bring to a boil. Add fermented rice, chopped pineapple, goji berries, white sugar, ginger slices, and an appropriate amount of brown sugar water.
   **Key point**: Sweetness can be adjusted to personal taste; ginger slices are essential for flavor.
6. Add a small amount of cornstarch slurry to thicken the soup base slightly.
7. Beat the egg. When the soup base is just about to boil, pour the egg liquid in a circular motion along the edge of the pot. Let it sit briefly to naturally form egg flowers, then gently stir to combine.
   **Note**: Do not stir immediately after adding the egg liquid.
8. Scoop the cooked dough balls into the soup base and gently stir to combine, then serve.

## Health Advice  
This dessert is rich in carbohydrates and B vitamins, making it suitable for warming the stomach in spring. However, it is high in sugar. Diabetic patients should reduce the sugar or consume in small amounts; glutinous rice is difficult to digest, so it is recommended to eat in moderation.
