# Cherry Jam Tiramisu
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 Egg yolks 2
🧀 Mascarpone cheese 250g
🥛 Whipping cream approx. 70g (2 tablespoons)
🍪 Ladyfingers 6 pieces
🍒 Cherries appropriate amount (for making jam and decoration)
🍬 Granulated sugar 20g (for egg yolk mixture)
🍬 Granulated sugar appropriate amount (for jam)
💧 Water 10g
🍋 Lemon juice appropriate amount
🍒 Cherry liqueur appropriate amount

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
♨️ Microwave
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Spoon
🎨 Piping bag
🥢 Chopsticks

## Steps
1. Make cherry jam: Wash the cherries and place them in a bowl. Add an appropriate amount of granulated sugar and lemon juice, mix well, then microwave on high power for 3 minutes until the cherries soften and release juice. Remove and squeeze to remove the pits. Microwave the pulp and juice again on high power for 2 minutes. Remove, add a little cherry liqueur for fragrance, and stir until a thick jam forms. Set aside.
   **Key point**: Heating time can be adjusted according to the microwave power to achieve the desired thickness of the jam.

2. Make the cheese mixture: Add 20g granulated sugar and 10g water to the egg yolks, stir well, then microwave on high power for 40 seconds. Remove and immediately stir until smooth. Add the mascarpone cheese and mix well. Then pour in the whipping cream and gently fold until the cheese mixture is smooth, thick, and glossy.
   **Key point**: The egg yolk mixture must be stirred immediately after heating, otherwise it may clump; if clumping occurs, pass through a sieve to restore smoothness.

3. Prepare the soaking liquid: Use a portion of the cherry jam as the dipping sauce for the ladyfingers.

4. Assemble: Quickly dip a ladyfinger into the cherry juice/jam, gently squeeze to remove excess liquid, then place it at the bottom of a dish. Fill a piping bag with the cheese mixture, pipe evenly over the ladyfinger layer, then spread a layer of cherry jam. Repeat the "dip ladyfinger—pipe cheese—spread jam" steps to create 2–3 layers.
   **Key point**: Ladyfingers absorb liquid very quickly; only a light dip is needed to avoid them becoming too soggy and affecting the texture.

5. Top decoration: After piping the final cheese layer on top, add cherry jam and garnish with a fresh cherry.

6. Refrigerate: Place the assembled tiramisu in the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld and the texture to set. Remove and serve.
   **Key point**: The longer the refrigeration time, the better the flavors integrate; overnight chilling yields even better flavor.

## Health Tips
This tiramisu replaces coffee with cherry jam, reducing caffeine stimulation, making it suitable as an afternoon tea or dessert. The cheese and egg yolks are rich in protein and calcium, but the calorie content is high. It is recommended to pair with black coffee to cut through the richness, or share with friends to control portion sizes.
