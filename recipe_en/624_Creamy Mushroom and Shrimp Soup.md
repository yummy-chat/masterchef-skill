# Creamy Mushroom and Shrimp Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍄 1 pack of Seven Treasures Mushroom Soup Pack (includes morel mushrooms, cordyceps flowers, king oyster mushrooms, etc., rinse with warm water and trim off the roots)
🥚 3 eggs (beaten)
🍤 Shrimp, as needed (can be added directly to the pot if water-retaining agents are present)
🧅 Scallions, as needed, chopped
🧂 Salt, as needed
🫒 Peanut oil, as needed (recommended for frying eggs, for richer flavor)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Soup ladle
✂️ Scissors (for trimming mushroom roots)

## Cooking Steps
1. Briefly rinse the dried mushrooms from the Seven Treasures Mushroom Soup Pack with warm water, inspect each one, and trim off the roots with scissors.
   **Note**: Improper root removal will affect the soup's flavor, so they must be thoroughly removed.
2. Heat the wok over high heat and add an appropriate amount of peanut oil. When the oil reaches about 70% hot, pour in the beaten eggs.
   **Key**: The oil must be hot, and the amount should not be too little, so the eggs puff up and brown, making it easier to absorb the broth later.
3. Fry the eggs until both sides are golden brown, then add the shrimp and the prepared mushrooms.
4. Immediately pour enough boiling water into the wok. At this point, the soup will be clear.
   **Key**: Boiling water must be used, and salt should not be added yet, otherwise, the soup will not turn white.
5. Keep the heat high and let it boil vigorously for about 5–10 minutes. The soup will automatically change from clear to a milky white color.
   **Principle**: The fat is broken into tiny droplets by the vigorous boiling, and the lecithin in the eggs acts as a natural emulsifier, coating the oil droplets to form a stable emulsion, giving the soup its milky white color.
6. Once the soup has reached the desired milky white color, add salt to taste.
7. Turn off the heat, sprinkle with chopped scallions, and serve.

## Health Advice
This mushroom and shrimp soup is rich in high-quality protein and mushroom polysaccharides, making it suitable for those who need to boost their immunity or manage their weight. Note that if the shrimp contain water-retaining agents, use less salt in the soup to avoid excessive sodium intake.
