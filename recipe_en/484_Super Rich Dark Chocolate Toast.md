# Super Rich Dark Chocolate Toast
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🧊 Water 234g (for sponge dough)  
🍞 High-sugar-tolerant fresh yeast 12g (for sponge dough) + 3g (for main dough)  
🍬 Caster sugar 30g (for sponge dough) + 30g (for main dough)  
🌾 Bread flour 360g (for sponge dough) + 140g (for main dough)  
🥚 Whole egg liquid 60g  
💧 Water 40g  
🥛 Whipping cream 25g  
🍯 Condensed milk 25g  
🍫 Cocoa powder 20g  
🧂 Salt 6g  
🍫 Pure cocoa butter dark chocolate 100g (melted before use)  
🧈 Unsalted butter 40g (softened before use)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍞 Stand mixer  
🥘 Dough scraper  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🌡️ Thermometer  
📦 Plastic wrap or plastic bag  
🥖 Rolling pin  
📥 Loaf pan  
🔥 Oven (convection or conventional)  
🧊 Baking tray  

## Instructions  
### Prepare the Sponge Dough
1. Place all the water, high-sugar-tolerant fresh yeast, caster sugar, and bread flour required for the sponge dough into the stand mixer. Mix on speed 3 for about 5 minutes, until a rough dough forms and a thick, uneven membrane can be pulled. It does not need to be smooth.
2. Ferment at room temperature or in a 30°C environment for about 1 hour, until the dough has tripled in volume and appears honeycombed inside.

### Pre-treatment of Main Dough Liquids
3. Mix the whole egg liquid, water, whipping cream, and condensed milk from the main dough ingredients together evenly. Freeze until slushy.
   **Purpose**: To effectively control kneading temperature and prevent the dough from overheating.

### Prepare the Chocolate-Butter Mixture
4. Melt the pure cocoa butter dark chocolate over a water bath, then cool to room temperature.
5. Mix the softened unsalted butter with the cooled melted chocolate until smooth. Set aside.

### Make the Main Dough
6. Tear the fermented sponge dough into small pieces and place them into the stand mixer along with the frozen liquid mixture, the caster sugar, the high-sugar-tolerant fresh yeast, bread flour, cocoa powder, and salt from the main dough. Mix on low speed until no dry flour remains, using the dough scraper to assist in scraping the bowl as needed. Ensure the dough temperature does not exceed 26°C.
7. Increase to medium speed and knead until the dough surface is smooth, then increase to high speed and knead until the early stage of gluten development. The dough should be able to pull a thick, easily torn membrane with a jagged edge on the tear.
8. Add the prepared chocolate-butter mixture. Mix on low speed for 3 minutes until the fat is fully incorporated, then switch to high speed and mix for 2 minutes.
9. The dough should now reach full gluten development. It can be stretched into a very thin, translucent, non-tearing windowpane membrane with a smooth edge.

### Shape and Proof the Toast
10. Weigh out approximately 550g of the total dough. Round it into a ball and perform the first proof. Proof at 28°C for about 50 minutes.
11. After proofing, divide the dough into two equal portions. Round each piece and let them rest for 25 minutes.
12. Roll out each rested piece of dough, roll it up, and place it seam-side down into the loaf pan.

### Final Proof and Baking
13. Perform the final proof until the dough rises to 80-90% of the pan's height.
14. Baking reference parameters:
    - **Convection Oven**: Bake at 170°C for approximately 21 minutes.
    - **Conventional Oven**: Bake at 160°C top heat / 200°C bottom heat for approximately 23 minutes.
    **Note**: Actual temperature and time should be adjusted according to your home oven's characteristics.

### Finished Product
15. Immediately unmold after removing from the oven and cool on a wire rack. The finished loaf is a deep brown color with a fine crumb structure and intense chocolate flavor.

## Health Advice  
This chocolate toast is rich in antioxidants and is suitable for breakfast for those with high energy expenditure. Each slice contains approximately 250 kilocalories, so portion control is advised. Due to its high sugar and fat content, those with diabetes or on a weight loss plan should consume it in moderation.
