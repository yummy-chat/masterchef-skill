# Dumpling Wrappers
Cuisine: Shandong Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 High-gluten or all-purpose flour 1000g (2 pounds)  
🥔 Potato starch 40g  
🥚 Egg whites 2  
💧 Cold water 480g  
🌽 Corn starch, as needed (for dusting to prevent sticking)  
🧂 Salt 6g  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🫗 Stainless steel bowl  
🔪 Cutting board  
🥢 Rolling pin  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🍲 Pot  

## Steps
1. Pour the flour, salt, and potato starch into a bowl. Mix evenly by hand, ensuring the dry ingredients are thoroughly combined.
2. Add the egg whites, then gradually pour in the cold water in small amounts while stirring by hand until the flour forms a flaky texture. Do not add all the water at once; add it gradually while stirring to control the firmness of the dough.
3. Press the flaky dough into a ball, transfer it to a cutting board, and knead forcefully until the surface is smooth. Kneading until the dough is smooth is key to achieving a chewy dumpling wrapper.
4. Return the kneaded dough to the bowl, cover it, and let it rest for about 20 minutes to relax the gluten, making it easier to roll out later.
5. Dust the cutting board with corn starch to prevent sticking. Take out the rested dough, roll it into a long log, and tear off evenly sized small pieces by hand. A crisp sound when tearing indicates the dough has the right chewiness.
6. Flatten each small piece with your palm, then roll it out with a rolling pin into a round dumpling wrapper with a thicker center and thinner edges. A slightly softer dough makes it easier to seal when wrapping.
7. Take a dumpling wrapper, place filling in the center, fold it in half, and pinch the edges tightly to seal.
8. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the dumplings, and immediately gently stir the bottom with the back of a slotted spoon to prevent sticking. Cook over high heat until it boils, then reduce to medium heat, keeping the water bubbling without a vigorous boil. The dumplings are done when they float to the surface and the wrappers become translucent.

## Health Advice  
Adding egg whites and starch to the dumpling wrappers results in a more chewy texture, making them suitable for those needing blood sugar control in small amounts. Both flour and starch are high-carb ingredients, so consumption should be monitored. Although the salt content is low, the filling often contains significant salt, so overall sodium intake should be controlled.
