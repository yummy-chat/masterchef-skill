# Tiger Skin Soft-Boiled Eggs
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 3 eggs  
🧅 About 3 stalks of scallions (minced)  
🧄适量 ginger (minced)  
🧄 2 cloves garlic (minced)  
🫘适量 white sesame seeds (for garnish)  
🫒适量 cooking oil (for frying eggs and sautéing)  
🧂 Four bottle caps of light soy sauce  
🧂 One bottle cap of dark soy sauce  
🍬 One bottle cap of sugar  
🦪 One bottle cap of oyster sauce

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board  
🥣 Small bowl  
🍽️ Plate

## Cooking Steps
1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add the eggs, and cook for exactly 5 minutes and 20 seconds. Remove immediately and immerse in ice water to cool completely.  
   **Note**: Ice water helps with peeling and keeps the egg white tender; the cooking time must be precise to ensure a runny yolk.
2. Carefully peel the eggs, keeping the egg white as intact as possible to avoid damage.
3. In a small bowl, combine four bottle caps of light soy sauce, one bottle cap of dark soy sauce, one bottle cap of sugar, and one bottle cap of oyster sauce. Stir well to make the sauce.
4. Pour enough cooking oil into the wok and heat until small bubbles appear on the surface. Add the peeled eggs and fry over medium heat for about 1 minute, until the surface is wrinkled like tiger skin.  
   **Key point**: Monitor the heat closely to prevent burning; turn off the heat and remove the eggs after frying.
5. Leave a small amount of oil in the wok, heat it, then sauté the minced scallions, ginger, and garlic until fragrant.
6. Pour in the prepared sauce, stir well, and bring to a boil over high heat until the sauce thickens.
7. Add the fried tiger skin eggs, gently shake the wok to coat them evenly with the sauce, and cook for no more than 1 minute to allow the sauce to absorb while keeping the yolk runny.  
   **Note**: Avoid vigorous stirring to prevent damaging the egg white.
8. Plate the eggs and sprinkle with white sesame seeds for garnish.

## Health Advice
This tiger skin soft-boiled egg dish is rich in high-quality protein, and the runny yolk provides lecithin, making it suitable for muscle building or mental work. However, frying increases the calorie content, so it is recommended to pair with vegetables for balanced nutrition. Diabetic patients should reduce the amount of sugar used.
