# Sesame Oil Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍜 Instant noodles 1 block (air-dried instant noodles or any brand, seasoning packet not needed)
🫒 Sesame oil 15ml
🫗 Dark soy sauce 10g
🌶️ White pepper 10g
🧂 Salt 30g (optional, used for boiling noodles, not fully consumed)
🫗 Light soy sauce 5g (optional)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🍳 Pot
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🕸️ Strainer
💧 Draining tool

## Preparation Steps
1. Fill the pot with water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat. If you prefer a chewy texture, add salt to the water to enhance the noodles' elasticity.
2. Once the water is boiling, add the instant noodles and cook for about 3 minutes.
3. When the noodles begin to loosen, gently stir them with chopsticks to ensure even heating.
4. After cooking, drain the water and transfer the noodles to a bowl.
5. Add sesame oil, dark soy sauce, white pepper, and light soy sauce in sequence.
6. Mix thoroughly with chopsticks until well combined, then serve.

## Health Advice
Sesame oil noodles are rich in carbohydrates and beneficial fatty acids from sesame oil, making them suitable for a quick energy boost. However, the salt content is relatively high, so individuals with high blood pressure should be mindful of the amount used. It is recommended to pair with vegetables or protein for a balanced nutritional profile.
