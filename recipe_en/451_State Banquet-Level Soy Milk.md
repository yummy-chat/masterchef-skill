# State Banquet-Level Soy Milk
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫘 Soybeans, as needed  
🍚 Rice, as needed  
🥜 Peanuts, as needed  
💧 Water, as needed (add according to the blender's water level mark)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥛 Blender  
⚖️ Measuring cup with刻度  
🥣 Mixing cup  

## Steps
1. Place the soybeans, rice, and peanuts together into the blender's mixing cup.  
   **Note**: For a smoother texture, you can soak the soybeans for 2–4 hours in advance; using dry beans directly is also fine.  
2. Add water up to the blender's recommended water level, ensuring the liquid fully covers the ingredients.  
3. Secure the lid tightly, select the "Soy Milk" or "Multigrain Milk" mode, and start the machine.  
4. Once the program finishes and the soy milk is smooth and uniform, pour it out and serve.  
   **Key Point**: No additional filtering is needed, as the blender has thoroughly ground the ingredients.  

## Health Advice
This soy milk is rich in high-quality plant protein and dietary fiber, making it an excellent breakfast pairing with whole wheat bread. It helps stabilize blood sugar and provides sustained energy. It is recommended to drink it without added sugar, as the peanuts contribute natural oils and aroma. For those who need to control calorie intake, reduce the amount of peanuts.
