# Toothpick Spicy Beef
Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef tenderloin 250g (slice thinly against the grain)
🪥 Toothpicks适量 (soak in warm water in advance)
🦪 Oyster sauce适量
🧂 Light soy sauce适量
🧂 Salt a pinch
🌽 Corn starch a small handful
🧄 Minced garlic适量
🌿 Cumin powder one tablespoon
🌶️ Chili powder one tablespoon
🌸 Sichuan pepper powder one tablespoon
🌱 White sesame seeds one tablespoon
🫒 Cooking oil enough for frying (reserve a little for base oil and hot oil for aroma)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Deep fryer/deep pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🪣 Strainer
🍲 Wok
🥣 Bowl

## Steps
1. Soak the toothpicks thoroughly in warm water to prevent them from burning and turning black during frying.
2. Slice the beef tenderloin thinly against the grain, add oyster sauce, light soy sauce, a pinch of salt, and corn starch. Mix well and marinate for 20 minutes.
3. Thread the marinated beef slices onto the soaked toothpicks.
4. Pour enough cooking oil into the pot and heat until a toothpick inserted rapidly bubbles and floats.
**Key point**: Fry in batches to prevent sticking; medium heat ensures a crispy exterior and tender interior.
5. Fry the beef skewers in batches over medium heat until the edges are slightly charred, then remove.
6. Pour out the frying oil and filter it, leaving two tablespoons of base oil in the wok. Sauté the minced garlic until fragrant, then turn off the heat.
**Note**: No need to wash the wok.
7. With the heat off, add cumin powder, chili powder, Sichuan pepper powder, and white sesame seeds in order. Then drizzle in one tablespoon of clean hot oil to release the aroma.
8. Return the fried beef skewers to the wok, stir-fry over low heat until evenly coated with the seasonings. Turn off the heat and plate.

## Health Advice
This toothpick spicy beef is rich in protein, making it suitable for muscle building and satisfying cravings. However, as it is deep-fried, it is high in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with refreshing vegetables for balance. For those with high blood lipids, consume in moderation. Remove the toothpicks before eating.
