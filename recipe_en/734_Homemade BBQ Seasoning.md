# Homemade BBQ Seasoning
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌶️ Chili powder 3 tbsp (adjust spiciness to taste)  
🧂 Salt 1/2 tbsp  
🧂 MSG 1 tbsp  
🍬 White sugar 2 tbsp  
🧂 Spicy and numbing seasoning 3 tbsp  
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns 1/2 tbsp  
🌰 Toasted white sesame seeds 3 tbsp (pre-toasted until fragrant)  
🥜 Toasted crushed peanuts 5 tbsp (pre-toasted until fragrant)  
🌿 Cumin seeds 3 tbsp  
🌱 Fennel seeds 2 tbsp  
🌱 Toasted soybean powder 3 tbsp (not soy milk powder)  
🍃 Perilla powder 3 tbsp (do not skip)  
🥜 Cashews 2 tbsp (can substitute with 2 tbsp sunflower seeds)  
🍪 Tao Su (shortbread cookie) about 1 piece

## Cookware & Equipment
🥣 Large mixing bowl  
🥄 Grinding cup (or food processor)  
🍳 Flat-bottom pan (for serving suggestion)

## Preparation Steps
1. Toast peanuts and white sesame seeds separately in a pan over low heat until lightly golden and fragrant. Set aside.
2. Place Sichuan peppercorns, toasted white sesame seeds, crushed peanuts, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, chili powder, toasted soybean powder, perilla powder, cashews, salt, MSG, white sugar, and spicy and numbing seasoning into a large bowl. Mix thoroughly.
3. Break the Tao Su shortbread cookie into small pieces and add to the dry mixture.  
   **Note**: Tao Su must be broken into pieces, otherwise it will be difficult to grind evenly.
4. Pour all ingredients into the grinding cup and grind until a powder forms.  
   **Key point**: Grind in two to three batches; do not grind too finely—retaining some granular texture enhances the mouthfeel.
5. Mix the ground powder again until well combined. It will be golden yellow with a grainy texture, ready as BBQ dipping seasoning.
6. Recommended serving: In a flat-bottom pan without oil, pan-fry sliced pork belly over medium heat until both sides are golden and sizzling with fat. Dip the cooked slices generously into the seasoning, place on a lettuce leaf, add two cloves of garlic, and roll up to eat.

## Health Advice
Homemade BBQ seasoning allows you to control the amounts of salt and sugar, making it suitable for family gatherings when paired with vegetables and lean meats. Peanuts and cashews provide healthy fats, but total calorie intake should be monitored. Light dipping is recommended, and individuals with high blood lipids should avoid excessive intake of oils from the Tao Su cookie.
