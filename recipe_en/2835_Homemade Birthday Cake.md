# Homemade Birthday Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 5 large eggs (for baking the cake base, prepare in advance)
🍰 3-4 layers chiffon cake base (baked in advance)
🧈 Whipping cream, as needed (for frosting and piping)
🍓 Strawberries, as needed (sliced for filling)
🔴 Red food coloring, as needed (for pink cream and writing)
🟢 Green food coloring, as needed (for green cream; add a tiny bit of red for a more natural color)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔥 Oven (for baking the cake base in advance)
🪜 Pot lid + steamer basket (homemade piping turntable, secured with a screw)
🥣 Metal mixing bowl (for whipping and coloring cream)
🪄 Piping bag (for piping roses, leaves, and borders)
✂️ Scissors (for cutting piping bags to make tips and picking up roses)
🔪 Offset spatula (for smoothing cream)
🧤 Disposable gloves (for hygienic handling)
🧷 Piping nail (or similar fixing tool, for assisting piping)

## Steps
1. Place the pre-baked cake base on the homemade turntable. Pipe an appropriate amount of whipping cream onto it. Rotate the turntable and use an offset spatula to spread the cream evenly and smoothly.
   **Note**: Rotating the homemade turntable helps achieve a more even frosting surface without the need for professional equipment.

2. Arrange a layer of strawberry slices evenly on the frosted cake base. Pipe another layer of cream over the strawberries, then place the next cake layer on top. Repeat this process until all cake layers are stacked.
   **Key Point**: The strawberry slices should be spread evenly, and the cream layer should be of moderate thickness to enhance the texture and flavor layers.

3. Take a portion of whipping cream. Add red food coloring a little at a time, stirring until a uniform pink color is achieved.
   **Key Point**: The color of whipping cream tends to be darker after coloring, so add the coloring sparingly each time to avoid an overly dark hue.

4. Make pink roses:
   - Center: Pipe a small amount of cream onto the piping nail to secure it. Rotate the piping nail and pipe a small center piece.
   - Petals: For each petal, use a "down—up—down" curved motion to pipe it. Each layer consists of 3 petals, for a total of 3 layers. The starting point of each petal should be halfway over the previous petal.
   **Note**: Whipping cream has a soft texture, so the petals may droop easily. Control the pressure and speed during piping to maintain the three-dimensional shape of the petals.

5. Gently pick up the finished rose with scissors and place it in a suitable position on top of the cake.
   **Key Point**: Handle the scissors gently to avoid damaging the shape of the petals.

6. Make green leaves:
   - Coloring: Take a portion of cream and add green food coloring. Then add a tiny drop of red food coloring and mix well to create a more natural, soft green tone.
   - Cut the piping bag: Fold the tip of the piping bag in half twice along the centerline. Then make a symmetrical cut on each side of the fold to create a leaf-shaped tip.
   - Pipe leaves symmetrically around the roses.

7. Make the bottom border:
   - Use a new piping bag. Similarly, fold the tip in half twice, then make a symmetrical triangular cut on each side to create a border tip.
   - Pipe a continuous white border evenly around the bottom and the side edge of the cake.

8. Use the red cream to pipe a greeting message on the side of the cake.
   **Note**: Control the pressure on the piping bag to keep the lettering clear and uniform in size.

9. Assembly complete. Insert candles and celebrate.

## Health Advice
This homemade cake uses whipping cream and strawberries, making it more natural and nutritious than store-bought cakes, suitable for sharing with family. Be mindful of the amount of food coloring used. Diabetic individuals or those on a weight-loss diet can reduce sugar and cream intake.
