# Shrimp and Pork Wontons
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Shrimp 500g (need to soak and rinse)
🐔 Chicken skin 30g
🍗 Chicken thigh meat 50g
🥟 Wonton wrappers as needed
🥚 Egg white 1
🌿 Cilantro as needed (for broth)
🧅 Scallions as needed (for broth)
🍘 Seaweed as needed (for broth)
🦐 Dried shrimp as needed (for broth)
🧂 Baking soda 1 tsp (for soaking shrimp)
🍶 White vinegar 1 tbsp (optional, to remove alkaline taste)
🧂 Salt 1/3 tsp (for filling) + 1/4 tsp (for broth)
🌶️ White pepper 1/3 tsp (for filling) + 1/4 tsp (for broth)
🧂 MSG 1/3 tsp (for filling) + 1/4 tsp (for broth)
🌿 Thirteen spices 1/8 tsp
🍶 Light soy sauce 2 tsp (for filling) + 1 tsp (for broth)
🦪 Oyster sauce 1 tbsp
🫒 Sesame oil 1 tbsp (for filling) + 1 tsp (for broth)
🌽 Cornstarch 1 tsp

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Food processor
🍲 Soup pot
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥄 Measuring spoons

## Preparation Steps
1. Add baking soda and water to the shrimp, soak for 30 minutes, then rinse thoroughly. If sensitive to the alkaline taste, you can mix in white vinegar and rinse again to make the shrimp more translucent and bouncy.
2. First, put the chicken skin into the food processor and chop it finely. Then add the chicken thigh meat, about a quarter of the shrimp, egg white, and all the seasoning ingredients for the filling. Blend until a smooth paste forms.
3. Add the remaining shrimp and pulse a few times to retain some texture. The wonton filling is now ready.
4. Take a wonton wrapper. Use the back of a spoon to press the filling onto the wrapper, gather the edges with your fingers, and pinch the opening tightly with your thumb and index finger to seal.
5. Once the water is boiling, add the wontons and gently stir to prevent sticking. Cook for about 3 minutes until the wontons float and the wrappers become translucent. Remove and drain.
6. In the bottom of a bowl, place the cilantro, scallions, seaweed, dried shrimp, and seasoning ingredients for the broth. Ladle the hot wonton soup over them to dissolve. Finally, add the cooked wontons.

## Health Advice
Shrimp and pork wontons are rich in high-quality protein. The shrimp are bouncy, and the chicken thigh meat is low in fat, making them suitable for fitness or weight control individuals. After soaking with baking soda, rinse thoroughly to reduce sodium intake. Adding seaweed and dried shrimp to the broth provides calcium, but people with high blood pressure should use less salt and MSG.
