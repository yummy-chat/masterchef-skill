# Hot Dry Noodles (Re Gan Mian)
Cuisine: Hubei Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
* 🍜 Alkaline noodles specific to hot dry noodles (250g)
* 🧅 Spring onion (10g)
* 🫘 Pickled long beans (20g)
* 🥩 Ground meat (30g)
* 🥕 Dried radish (50g)
* 🧄 Garlic water (30ml)
* 🍖 Meat broth (30ml)
* 🥜 Sesame paste (40ml)
* 🌶️ Chili oil (0-10ml, optional)
* 🧂 Salt (3g)
* 🍗 Chicken bouillon (0-3g, optional)
* 🥄 Soy sauce (5ml)
* 🌿 White pepper (0-10g, optional)

## Utensils & Equipment
* Cooking pot
* Strainer
* Mixing bowl
* Measuring spoon or cup

## Preparation Steps
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot, blanch the alkaline noodles for 25 seconds, then drain thoroughly.
2. Sprinkle salt, chicken bouillon, and white pepper over the noodles.
3. Dilute the sesame paste with 90ml of water, stir until smooth, and pour over the noodles.
4. Add 5ml of soy sauce, then sequentially add 30ml each of meat broth and garlic water.
5. Add dried radish, ground meat, pickled long beans, and chopped spring onion.
6. Mix all ingredients thoroughly until well combined, then serve on a plate.

## Health Tips
The sesame paste in hot dry noodles is rich in calcium and protein, but the alkaline noodles and ground meat are relatively high in calories, making it suitable as a quick lunch. It is recommended to pair it with a light vegetable dish to balance nutrition. Individuals with high blood lipids should reduce the amount of chili oil and meat broth.
