# White Jade Rolls & Snow Lady

Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍚 Glutinous rice flour 100g (for the skin) + about 50g (for dusting, pre-toasted)  
🌾 Wheat starch 25g  
🥛 Milk 150g  
🫒 Vegetable oil 10g  
🍬 Sugar 40g (for the skin)  
🎨 Food coloring (pink, blue, green, yellow, white) about half a drop to one drop each  
🥛 Heavy cream 400ml  
🍬 Sugar 20g (for whipping cream, optional)  
🍓 Strawberries, as needed (cut into small pieces)  
🥭 Mango, as needed (cut into small pieces)  
🍰 Cloud cake 2 slices (about 40% egg content, used as filling layer)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl (for mixing batter and whipping cream)  
🥄 Whisk (for stirring batter and whipping cream)  
🍳 Frying pan (for toasting dusting flour)  
♨️ Microwave  
📦 Baking tray (for holding bowls in the microwave)  
🫙 Plastic wrap  
🪓 Rolling pin  
🥣 Small bowls, multiple (for coloring and assisting shaping)  
🔪 Knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🗡️ Spatula (for smoothing cream)  
✂️ Scissors (for trimming closures)

## Instructions

### Prepare the Colored Skins
1. In a glass bowl, mix 100g glutinous rice flour, 25g wheat starch, and 40g sugar. Gradually add 150g milk while stirring with a whisk until smooth and lump-free. Then add 10g vegetable oil and mix well.
2. Divide the batter evenly into 5 portions and place in small bowls. Add half a drop to one drop of pink, blue, green, yellow, and white food coloring to each bowl, stir well, and cover with plastic wrap.
3. Place 50g glutinous rice flour in a frying pan and stir over low heat until slightly yellow and fragrant. Set aside.
4. Heat each bowl of colored batter in the microwave on high for 1 minute 30 seconds to 2 minutes. Remove and stir, flipping the uncooked batter at the bottom. Re-cover with plastic wrap and continue heating on high for about 1 minute until no liquid batter remains.
5. Sprinkle a generous amount of toasted dusting flour on the work surface. Take out each colored dough and gently knead until the surface is smooth. Cut each colored dough into small pieces, arbitrarily combine and pinch them into a large multicolored dough. Roll it out into a sheet with a rolling pin, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate until needed.

### Prepare the Filling
6. Add 20g sugar to 400ml heavy cream and whip with a whisk until clear lines form and the texture is stable.
7. Remove the stems from strawberries and cut into small pieces. Peel the mango and cut into small pieces. Cut the cloud cake into small squares or strips matching the size of the shaping container.

### Shape the Spherical Snow Lady
8. Take out the refrigerated dough sheet, continue rolling it thin, cut off a piece, and lay it into a small bowl.
9. Layer as follows: one spoonful of whipped cream, place a few pieces of fruit and gently press into the cream, then cover with another spoonful of cream, place a piece of cake and press lightly, and finally add cream on top, ensuring the cake is surrounded by cream.
10. Gather the edges of the dough sheet at the rim of the bowl toward the center and pinch tightly. Trim off excess dough, dust the closure with a little toasted flour to prevent sticking, and invert the bowl to release, forming a round ball.

### Shape the White Jade Rolls
11. Take a piece of dough sheet and roll it into a rectangle. Evenly spread a thin layer of whipped cream over it.
12. Arrange strips of cake close to your edge. Roll up the dough sheet to enclose the cake and cream, like rolling a cake roll.
13. Wrap in plastic wrap to set the shape.

### Set and Enjoy
14. Refrigerate the wrapped Snow Lady or White Jade Rolls for 30 minutes to set. Remove and cut with a knife to serve. After chilling, they will be slightly firm, resulting in cleaner slices.

## Notes
- Sift the dusting flour before use for more even anti-stick effect.
- Adjust microwave heating time based on your microwave's power; the dough should be fully set with no liquid.
- Ensure the dough closure is tightly pinched to prevent filling from leaking.
- Do not skip the refrigeration step, or the slices may collapse and deform.

## Health Advice
This White Jade Rolls & Snow Lady dish primarily uses glutinous rice flour and cream, suitable as an occasional dessert. Diabetic patients and those on a weight-loss diet should consume in small amounts. Remember to refrigerate until set before cutting to maintain clean slices.
