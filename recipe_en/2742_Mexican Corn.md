# Mexican Corn
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌽 3 ears yellow waxy corn (individually vacuum-sealed)
🥛 50g Greek yogurt (low-fat, unsweetened)
🍋 1/4 lemon (juice and zest)
🧀 30g grated cheese or cheese powder (for coating)
🧄 1 clove garlic (minced)
🌿 Cilantro, to taste (chopped, for garnish)
🧂 Salt, to taste
🌶️ Paprika, to taste

## Cookware and Equipment
🥣 Small pot (for boiling corn)
🔥 Kitchen torch (for torching the surface)
🥄 Mixing bowl (for preparing the sauce)
🖌️ Silicone brush (for applying the sauce)
🧽 Zester (for lemon zest)
📡 Microwave (optional heating method)

## Instructions
1. Make the seasoned yogurt sauce: Place 50g Greek yogurt, minced garlic from 1 clove, salt, and black pepper to taste into a mixing bowl. Squeeze in the juice of about 1/4 lemon, add a small amount of lemon zest, and stir thoroughly until well combined.
   **Key point**: This sauce replaces traditional high-calorie mayonnaise and is essential for the low-calorie version. The lemon zest adds a fresh flavor.
2. Heat the corn: Tear a small opening in the vacuum-sealed corn package, then boil in water for 15 minutes. Alternatively, microwave for 3 minutes.
   **Key point**: Keeping the package on helps lock in moisture, resulting in more fragrant and chewy corn. The cooked corn should be golden yellow and springy; pressing with a fork should release some liquid.
3. Add a smoky flavor: Insert bamboo skewers into the heated corn, then use a kitchen torch to evenly toast the surface until it is slightly charred in spots.
   **Key point**: The goal is to create a smoky, charred aroma to enhance the flavor profile.
4. Apply the sauce: Use a silicone brush to generously and evenly coat the entire ear of corn with the prepared yogurt sauce.
5. Coat and garnish: Roll the sauce-coated corn in a plate of cheese powder to ensure it is evenly covered, then sprinkle with paprika and chopped cilantro to taste.
   **Key point**: Cheese powder adheres more evenly. The amounts of paprika and cilantro can be adjusted according to personal preference.
6. The finished dish can be enjoyed immediately. The outer layer is savory and fresh, while the corn is soft, chewy, and satisfying.

## Health Tips
This low-calorie version of Mexican corn uses Greek yogurt instead of mayonnaise, reducing fat without sacrificing flavor, making it suitable for those who are fitness-conscious or watching their calorie intake. Lemon zest and paprika add flavor, but although cheese powder is lower in fat, it should still be consumed in moderation.
