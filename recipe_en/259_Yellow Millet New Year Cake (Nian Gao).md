# Yellow Millet New Year Cake (Nian Gao)
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 2000g large yellow millet (ground into about 2000g yellow millet flour, or use ready-made yellow millet flour directly)
🫘 400g red beans (soaked in advance)
🫒适量 red dates (for surface decoration)
🍬 100g white sugar
🧂 180g brown sugar
💧适量 warm water at 50-60°C (about 480 ml for the white sugar dough, slightly less for the brown sugar dough)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
⚙️ Grain mill (or use ready-made yellow millet flour)
🧺 Flour sieve
🥣 Mixing bowl or glass bowl
🥘 Steamer basket
🧻 Damp cloth for steaming
🔪 Kitchen knife (dip in water when cutting the cake)

## Preparation Steps
1. Grind the large yellow millet into powder using a grain mill, then sift to obtain fine yellow millet flour. If using ready-made yellow millet flour, skip this step.
   **Note**: Sifting results in a finer texture for the New Year cake.
2. Prepare the white sugar red bean dough: Take 1000g yellow millet flour, add 100g white sugar and 200g soaked red beans. Mix well, then gradually add about 480 ml of warm water at 50-60°C. Knead until the dough is smooth and not sticky.
   **Key point**: Adjust the amount of water as needed based on the dough's consistency; it should just come together into a ball.
3. Prepare the brown sugar red bean dough: Take another 1000g yellow millet flour, add 180g brown sugar and 200g red beans. Knead using the same method. The dough should be slightly firmer than the white sugar dough.
   **Key point**: The brown sugar dough should not be too soft, or it may collapse and deform during steaming.
4. Line the steamer basket with a damp cloth. Alternately layer the white sugar dough and brown sugar dough in the steamer—one layer of white sugar dough, one layer of brown sugar dough.
   **Key point**: You can layer them all at once, or steam each layer until cooked before adding the next, to ensure clear, distinct layers.
5. Evenly spread red dates on the surface of the cake.
6. Place in the steamer and steam over high heat for about 2 hours, until completely cooked through.
   **Note**: Ensure sufficient steaming time to prevent the inside from being undercooked.
7. Once done, let it cool slightly. Use a knife dipped in clean water to cut into small pieces.
   **Key point**: Dipping the knife in water effectively prevents sticking, resulting in clean, smooth cut surfaces.

## Health Advice
Yellow millet New Year cake is rich in complex carbohydrates and the dietary fiber from red beans, providing lasting satiety. It is suitable for those needing an energy boost. However, due to the high content of brown sugar and white sugar, those with diabetes or controlling sugar intake should only have a small taste.
