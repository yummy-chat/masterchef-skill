## Cooking Steps
1. Fill the soup pot with water and bring to a boil. Add ginger slices and an appropriate amount of pickling salt to create a basic flavor.
2. Put all the prepared fish pieces into the boiling soup.
**Note**: If you have a clay pot at home, you can immediately cover the pot and turn off the heat after adding the fish pieces, allowing the residual heat to gently cook the fish, resulting in more tender meat.
3. Once the soup comes to a boil again, add the sliced lean pork and cook until the meat changes color.
4. Finally, sprinkle in the celery sections and cook briefly until the aroma is released, then remove from heat.
**Note**: Adjust the heat throughout based on the doneness of the fish and the clarity of the soup. Avoid prolonged cooking to maintain tender fish and a clear broth.

## Health Advice
Fish soup is rich in high-quality protein and Omega-3, making it suitable for those recovering or engaged in mental work. Lean pork enhances the flavor, but attention should be paid to controlling salt intake. Do not simmer the soup for too long to preserve the tender texture of the fish.
