# Green Dragon Lying on Snow Mountain
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-year-round meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦞 1 live lobster
🍜 Appropriate amount of vermicelli (pre-soaked)
🥬 Appropriate amount of spring onion (cut into scallion rings)
🌶️ Appropriate amount of small chili peppers (cut into small rings)
🧂 Appropriate amount of salt
🫒 Appropriate amount of cooking oil (used once to prevent vermicelli from sticking, once to brush the lobster before steaming)
🥢 Appropriate amount of steamed fish soy sauce (added after steaming)
🔥 Appropriate amount of hot oil (added at the end to release aroma)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
✂️ Scissors
🔪 Chef's knife
🪓 Cutting board
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍽️ Porcelain plate
♨️ Steamer
🥄 Spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the live lobster on its side, insert scissors from the side to drain the blood, then cut through the two joints connecting the head and body, and pry the head apart. Remove the gills, teeth, spikes, and tips of all legs.
**Note**: Draining blood from the side prevents the legs from breaking; the connecting joints must be cut through; remove all inedible or dangerous parts.

2. Use a chef's knife to split the lobster body longitudinally along the midline of the back, then cut into large pieces along the joints. Carefully pick out the intestinal vein from each piece of meat with the knife tip.
**Note**: Cut along the joints, do not chop randomly; the intestinal vein must be carefully removed.

3. Add salt and an appropriate amount of cooking oil to the pre-soaked vermicelli, and mix evenly with chopsticks.
**Note**: Adding cooking oil prevents the vermicelli from sticking together in clumps after steaming.

4. Spread the seasoned vermicelli as a base on the plate, then neatly arrange the prepared lobster pieces, head, and tail on top of the vermicelli, restoring the lobster's original shape.
**Note**: The plating should restore the lobster's original shape.

5. After the steamer water comes to a boil, evenly brush a thin layer of oil over the surface of the plated lobster.
**Note**: Brushing oil seals in the moisture of the lobster meat, resulting in white and tender flesh after steaming.

6. Place the plate into the steamer with steam already rising, cover the lid, and steam over high heat for 6 minutes.
**Note**: Start timing after the water boils.

7. After steaming, remove the plate, sprinkle with scallion rings and small chili rings, and drizzle with an appropriate amount of steamed fish soy sauce. Use a spoon to scoop a spoonful of hot oil heated until sizzling, and quickly pour it over the scallion rings and lobster surface to release the aroma.
**Note**: The hot oil added at the end must be sufficiently hot to release the aroma.

## Health Tips
Lobster is rich in high-quality protein and low in fat, making it suitable for fitness muscle building and post-surgery recovery, but gout patients should consume it in moderation. The vermicelli base absorbs the umami of the lobster and soy sauce, and precise steaming time locks in a tender and springy texture.
