# Braised Fish Head
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 1 fish head
🧅 200g scallion
🫚 80g ginger
🧄 3-4 garlic cloves
🌶️ 1/4 red chili pepper
🌿 4 sprigs of cilantro
⭐ 2 star anise
🌶️ 5 dried chilies

🧂 Salt
🧂 Chicken bouillon
🍶 Light soy sauce
🍶 Dark soy sauce
🍶 Aged vinegar
🌑 Black pepper powder
🍶 Cooking wine

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🍳 Wok
🥣 Basin

## Steps
1. Prepare ingredients: Clean the scallions, cut the white part into 4cm sections, cut the green part into sections and split into four pieces lengthwise. Slice the ginger into 3mm thick pieces. Crush the garlic cloves. Remove the roots from the cilantro, cut two sprigs into 1.5cm pieces, keep the other two sprigs whole. Slice the red chili pepper into 3mm thick rings. Cut the dried chilies into four sections. Briefly rinse the star anise.
2. Prepare the fish head: Scale the fish head, remove the internal organs, cut off the fins, and remove the gills. Cut the connection between the lower jaw and the body of the fish head. Cut the body into pieces, and chop the fish head into four or six pieces. Place in a basin and rinse repeatedly with clean water to remove as much blood as possible.
   **Note**: Be extremely careful with the knife during this process to prevent cuts; thorough rinsing effectively removes the fishy smell.
3. Marinate the fish head: Drain the fish pieces, add 5g salt, 10g light soy sauce, 10g cooking wine, add the chopped scallion greens and 1/3 of the ginger slices. Mix well and let marinate for 1-2 hours.
4. Sauté aromatics: Pour 30ml cooking oil into the wok, heat over low heat until slightly warm. Add the remaining ginger slices, slowly sauté until the edges are slightly charred and the slices are golden and dry. Add the white scallion sections, stir-fry until they appear slightly pale. Then add the crushed garlic, star anise, and dried chilies, stir-fry for 5 seconds until fragrant.
5. Cook the main ingredient: Add the marinated fish head pieces to the wok, increase to medium-high heat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the surface of the fish pieces changes color. Add 500ml water, season with 2g salt, 3g chicken bouillon, 5g light soy sauce, 3g dark soy sauce, 5g cooking wine, 2g black pepper powder, 3g aged vinegar. Add the two whole sprigs of cilantro. Cover the wok, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and simmer until the liquid is reduced by half, then uncover.
6. Reduce sauce and plate: Increase to high heat to reduce the sauce. When the sauce is thick and reduced to about 1/3, turn off the heat. Place the softened cooked cilantro from the wok at the bottom of a serving dish. Arrange the fish head on top and pour the remaining sauce over it. Garnish with the remaining cilantro pieces and red chili pepper rings, and serve.
   **Key Point**: Watch closely during the sauce reduction to prevent the sauce from drying out and burning or sticking to the wok.

## Health Suggestions
Braised fish head is rich in high-quality protein and DHA, making it suitable for office workers who use their brains extensively and for the elderly to nourish the brain. However, the sauce is rich and contains a relatively high amount of salt, so those with high blood pressure should consume it in moderation.
