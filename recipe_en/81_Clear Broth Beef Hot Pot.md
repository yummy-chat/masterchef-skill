# Clear Broth Beef Hot Pot
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
### Broth Base and Main Hot Pot Ingredients  
🥩 Guizhou Yellow-Biao Beef (tenderloin, foreleg, etc.) Approximately 2.2 kg (sliced for quick cooking)  
🍄 Enoki Mushrooms 1 small handful  
🍄 Shiitake Mushrooms 2-3 pieces  
🌰 Red Dates 3-4 pieces  
🍅 Tomato 1 (sliced)  
🥕 White Radish Half (sliced)  
🫚 Ginger 1 small piece (sliced)  
🧅 Scallions 2-3 stalks (cut into sections)  
🧄 Garlic Sprout Roots 3-4 stalks  
🥬 Pea Shoots 1 plate (for cooking)  
🧈 Frozen Tofu 1-2 pieces (for cooking)  

### Dipping Sauce Ingredients  
🧄 Minced Garlic To taste  
🧅 Chopped Scallions To taste  
🌿 Cilantro To taste  
🌶️ Bird’s Eye Chili To taste  

### Seasonings  
🐔🐖 Chicken Fat and Lard Mixture 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Salt About 2 teaspoons  
🧂 Chicken Bouillon 1-2 teaspoons  
🧂 MSG 1-2 teaspoons  
🥢 Light Soy Sauce To taste (for dipping sauce)  
🫒 Sesame Oil To taste (for dipping sauce)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍲 Stainless Steel Stockpot  
🥄 Stirring Ladle (long-handled)  
🥢 Strainer Skimmer  
🔪 Chef’s Knife  
🪓 Cutting Board  
🥣 Bowl  

## Preparation Steps  
### Prep Phase  
1. Slice the beef against the grain into thin pieces to ensure a tender texture.  
2. Wash all vegetables; slice the tomato and white radish; cut scallions into sections; take the roots of the garlic sprouts; slice the ginger and shiitake mushrooms.  
3. Prepare the dipping sauce: in a bowl, add minced garlic, chopped scallions, cilantro, and bird’s eye chili, then mix in light soy sauce and sesame oil. Adjust spiciness to personal taste.  

### Cooking Phase  
1. Fill the pot with enough water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.  
2. Add enoki mushrooms, shiitake mushrooms, red dates, tomato slices, white radish slices, ginger slices, scallion sections, and garlic sprout roots as the broth base.  
3. Add 1 tablespoon of the chicken fat and lard mixture, stir to combine until the broth turns golden; then add about 2 teaspoons of salt, and 1-2 teaspoons each of chicken bouillon and MSG, stirring evenly. Adjust seasoning amounts as needed.  
4. Keep the broth boiling, then add beef slices, pea shoots, frozen tofu, and other ingredients to cook. Beef slices are done as soon as they change color—remove quickly to avoid overcooking; cook other ingredients until just done.  
5. Dip the cooked ingredients into the prepared dipping sauce before eating. The clear broth is savory, and spiciness can be controlled through the dipping sauce.  

## Health Advice  
This clear broth hot pot offers controllable calories, with beef providing high-quality protein and the white radish and pea shoots adding dietary fiber, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or preferring light eating. Be mindful of salt and oil amounts in the dipping sauce; those with high blood pressure should especially reduce the use of light soy sauce.
