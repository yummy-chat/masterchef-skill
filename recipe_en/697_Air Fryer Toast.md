# Air Fryer Toast
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 Bread slices (whole grain bread recommended)  
🧂 None

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔧 Air fryer

## Instructions
1. Take two slices of bread, preferably whole grain bread.
2. Place the bread slices vertically in the air fryer basket, avoiding stacking.  
   **Key point**: Vertical placement allows hot air to circulate evenly, ensuring consistent toasting results.
3. Set the temperature to 200°C and toast for 5 minutes. Adjust the time based on your air fryer’s actual power to prevent burning.
4. Once done, remove the toast and serve on a plate.

## Health Tips
Air fryer toast is low in fat and high in fiber, making it suitable for weight loss. Whole grain bread is rich in dietary fiber, which helps increase satiety. However, be mindful of the toasting time to avoid excessive charring. It is ideal for those who want to control calories while satisfying cravings.
