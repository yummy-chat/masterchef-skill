# Low-Calorie Chickpea M&M Tiramisu
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Egg yolk 120g
🍬 White sugar 15g (for egg yolk mixture)
🍯 Honey 28g
🌾 Cake flour 70g
🧈 Butter 30g
🥛 Milk 35g
🥚 Egg whites 160g
🍬 White sugar 65g (for whipping egg whites)
🥣 Yogurt about half a bowl
🧀 Mascarpone cheese about half a bowl
🌰 Chickpea M&Ms appropriate amount
🍓 Fresh strawberries appropriate amount
🍓 Freeze-dried strawberry crumbles appropriate amount
🍫 Cocoa nibs appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Hand whisk
🌀 Electric mixer
🔪 Spatula
📜 Parchment paper
🍳 Flat baking sheet
🔥 Oven
📐 Mold (for cutting cake slices)
💼 Piping bag
🍱 Transparent assembly container

## Instructions
1. Make the egg yolk mixture: Combine egg yolks with 15g white sugar and honey in a bowl and mix well. Sift in the cake flour and stir until no dry flour remains.
2. Add the mixture of milk and melted butter, continue stirring until the batter is smooth, and set aside.
3. Whip the egg whites: Using an electric mixer on medium-low speed, whip the egg whites, adding 65g white sugar in three additions, until stiff peaks form with a slight droop when lifting the whisk.
   **Key point**: The soft peak stage is crucial for a successful chiffon cake; avoid over-whipping.
4. Combine the batters: Add about one-third of the meringue to the egg yolk mixture and fold until combined. Then pour back into the remaining meringue and use a spatula to cut and fold gently until evenly mixed, being careful not to deflate the batter.
5. Bake the cake: Pour the batter into a flat baking sheet lined with parchment paper, spread the surface evenly, and bake in a preheated oven until cooked through.
   **Note**: Baking temperature and time should be adjusted based on your oven and the thickness of the cake sheet.
6. Make the yogurt mascarpone cream: Mix yogurt and mascarpone cheese in a roughly 1:1 ratio and stir until smooth. If using plain unsweetened yogurt, add sugar to taste.
7. Prepare the cake slices: Once the cake is baked and cooled, cut it into pieces that match the size of your assembly container using a mold.
8. Assemble the base layer: Evenly spread a layer of chickpea M&Ms at the bottom of the transparent assembly container.
9. Pipe the first cream layer: Pipe a layer of yogurt mascarpone cream over the M&Ms.
10. Add the cake layer: Place one cake slice on top.
11. Pipe another cream layer: Pipe another layer of yogurt mascarpone cream over the cake slice.
12. Decorate: Sprinkle the surface with cocoa nibs or freeze-dried strawberry crumbles, arrange fresh strawberry chunks, and optionally garnish with a few chickpea M&Ms.

## Health Tips
This low-calorie tiramisu uses chickpea M&Ms and yogurt to replace traditional high-sugar ingredients, offering higher protein and dietary fiber content, making it suitable as a guilt-free dessert during weight loss. Note that egg yolks themselves are relatively high in fat, so it is advisable to control the overall portion size.
