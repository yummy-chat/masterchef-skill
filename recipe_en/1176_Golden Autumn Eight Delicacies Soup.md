# Golden Autumn Eight Delicacies Soup  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦐 Giant freshwater prawns (heads and bodies separated)  
🥚 Salted duck eggs 2 (yolks only, crushed into fine powder)  
🥩 Beef brisket fat (or fatty beef) to taste  
🧈 Tofu to taste (soaked in salt water to remove beany flavor)  
🥬 Napa cabbage to taste (washed and cut into sections)  
🍄 Cordyceps flowers to taste (soaked in advance)  
🌾 A little flour (for thickening)  
🍶 Wuliangye liquor (or high-proof distilled spirit) to taste  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🍬 White sugar about 2 tablespoons  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🍳 Frying pan  
🥘 Soup pot  
🔥 Gas stove  
🥄 Soup ladle  
🥢 Slotted spoon  
✂️ Kitchen scissors  
🥣 Bowls/containers  

## Instructions  
1. Cut off the prawns' legs and antennae, squeeze out the stomachs, remove the veins with a toothpick, split open the backs, and separate the heads from the bodies for later use.  
2. Soak the tofu in salt water. Crush the salted egg yolks into fine powder. Cut the napa cabbage into sections. Soak the cordyceps flowers in advance.  
3. Heat a frying pan over low heat, add the beef brisket fat and stir-fry until the fat is fully rendered, then remove the cracklings.  
4. Add the prawn heads and stir-fry over low heat until the prawn oil is released.  
5. Add the crushed salted egg yolks and continue stir-frying over low heat until bubbling and fragrant.  
6. Pour in the Wuliangye liquor along the edge of the pan. After the alcohol evaporates to remove the fishy smell, add a little flour and stir-fry evenly.  
   **Note**: Only a small amount of flour is needed, just enough to form a thin layer with the oil, to help create a milky white soup base later.  
7. Add a sufficient amount of boiling water all at once. Bring to a boil over high heat and maintain a rolling boil for 10 minutes.  
   **Key**: Must use boiling water for the soup to turn white quickly.  
8. Skim off any foam from the surface with a slotted spoon. The soup should now be milky white.  
9. Add the napa cabbage sections, tofu pieces, prawn meat, and soaked cordyceps flowers. Reduce the heat to low and cook for 5 minutes.  
   **Optional**: The prawn heads can be removed or kept.  
10. Skim off any new foam again, season with salt and sugar, then turn up the heat briefly to meld the flavors. Turn off the heat.  
11. Transfer to a soup pot and garnish with chopped green onions if desired.  

## Health Tips  
This soup is rich in astaxanthin, egg yolk lecithin, and high-quality protein, making it suitable for autumn nourishment. However, beef brisket fat and salted egg yolks contain high saturated fat, so people with high blood lipids should consume in moderation. Using Wuliangye liquor to remove fishy odors and enhance flavor—the alcohol mostly evaporates during cooking, so no need to worry.
