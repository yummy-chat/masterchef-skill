# Breakfast Oat Bowl
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 Rolled oats 50g (traditional thick oats)  
🌱 Chia seeds 1 tsp  
🥛 Milk (or oat milk) 180-200ml  
🥝 Kiwi fruit appropriate amount (diced)  
🥑 Avocado appropriate amount (diced)  
🍯 Honey appropriate amount  
🥬 Kale powder 1 pack  
🥣 Baked oats appropriate amount (for garnish)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🫙 Wide-mouth glass jar (Mason jar) or bowl  
🔪 Knife  
🥄 Spoon  
❄️ Refrigerator  

## Steps  
1. Take a wide-mouth glass jar or bowl, add 50g rolled oats and 1 tsp chia seeds.  
2. Pour in 180-200ml milk or oat milk, stir thoroughly until well combined.  
   **Note**: If using thick plain yogurt instead of milk, it should not be used directly as the soaking liquid, as refrigerating overnight will make the texture excessively dry and hard, affecting the final consistency.  
3. Cover with a lid and refrigerate overnight to form the oat base.  
4. The next day, remove the chilled oat base from the refrigerator.  
5. Add honey, diced kiwi fruit, and diced avocado in turn, then sprinkle in 1 pack of kale powder.  
6. Finally, top with a layer of baked oats. Serve immediately, or cover and take it with you.  

## Health Tips  
A breakfast full of green vitality, oats, chia seeds, and avocado provide quality fiber and healthy fats, suitable for those who need to control blood sugar fluctuations or seek satiety without gaining weight. Note that while kale powder is beneficial, it should not be consumed in excess.
