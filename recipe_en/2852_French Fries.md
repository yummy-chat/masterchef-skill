# French Fries
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥔 Pre-cut Frozen French Fries (Recommended Brand: McCain)  
🫒 Spray Oil (Optional)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
♨️ Air Fryer  

## Steps
1. **Open the French Fries Package**
   Remove the pre-cut frozen French fries from the package. Open the package with a small opening to reduce frost buildup on the fries during long-term storage.

2. **Preheat the Air Fryer**
   Plug in the power, set to 200°C, and preheat for 5 minutes to ensure the temperature inside the air fryer has reached the target before adding the ingredients.
   **Key Point**: Do not remove the fries from the freezer until the preheating is complete, to avoid them softening at room temperature before frying.

3. **Fry the French Fries**
   Place an appropriate amount of French fries into the air fryer basket. Set to 200°C and fry for 20 minutes.
   **Note**: The surface of the pre-cut frozen fries already contains oil. Use tongs or a utensil to handle them, avoiding direct hand contact.
   After 5–10 minutes of frying, pull out the basket and shake it to ensure even heating and prevent sticking.

4. **Crisping Treatment**
   After 10–15 minutes of frying, pull out the basket and spray a layer of oil onto the already dried surface of the fries, covering about 2/3 of the surface area. Then return the basket to the air fryer and continue frying.

5. **Remove and Plate**
   After frying is complete, if you prefer crispy fries, remove them and shake the basket to allow air to flow over the fries’ surface before plating. If you prefer soft fries, plate them directly.
   Serve with dipping sauce or pour sauce over the fries for enhanced flavor.

## Health Advice
Cooking French fries in an air fryer can reduce oil intake, but pre-cut frozen fries themselves contain oil. It is recommended to pair them with ketchup and control portion sizes to avoid hidden excess calories. This dish is suitable for occasional indulgence and is not recommended for frequent consumption by those on a weight-loss or blood pressure management diet.
