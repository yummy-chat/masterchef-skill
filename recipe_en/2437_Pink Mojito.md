# Pink Mojito
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌿 Purple perilla, to taste  
🍋 Lemon, 2, cut into lemon wedges  
🍋‍🟩 Lime, 1, halved  
🧊 Ice cubes, to taste  
🫧 Sparkling water, to taste  
🍯 Honey, 1 tablespoon  
🍎 Apple cider vinegar, 5 ml  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥤 Shaker  
🥄 Muddler  
🫗 Strainer/ice strainer  
🥄 Spoon  
🥃 Glass  
🔪 Knife  

## Steps  
1. Place the purple perilla, lemon wedges, and lime pieces into the shaker.  
2. Use the muddler to thoroughly press and smash the ingredients in the shaker until they release juice and aroma.  
3. Add an appropriate amount of ice cubes to the shaker, then add the honey and apple cider vinegar in sequence.  
4. Tightly close the lid and shake vigorously until all ingredients are evenly mixed and well chilled.  
5. Fill a clean glass with fresh ice cubes.  
6. Pour the shaken liquid through the strainer into the glass, discarding the solid residue.  
7. Add an appropriate amount of sparkling water according to personal preference, allowing a natural layered effect to form.  

## Health Tips  
Purple perilla paired with lemon and apple cider vinegar makes this non-alcoholic mojito rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, ideal for relieving summer heat, stimulating appetite, and aiding digestion. Note that honey provides hidden sugars; during weight loss, reduce the amount or substitute with a sugar alternative.
