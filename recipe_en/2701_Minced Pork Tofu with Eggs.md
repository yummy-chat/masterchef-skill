# Minced Pork Tofu with Eggs
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Minced pork, about 150g  
🫘 Silken tofu, 1 box (about 350g, cut into pieces)  
🥚 Eggs, 4  
🧅 Onion, half (finely chopped)  
🌶️ Chili peppers, 3 (sliced)  
🧂 Salt, to taste (for seasoning egg mixture and minced pork separately)  
🍶 Cooking wine,适量 (added to egg mixture)  
🌿 Ground pepper, to taste (added to minced pork)  
🌶️ Chili powder, to taste (added to minced pork)  
🥄 Light soy sauce, 1 tablespoon  
🥄 Dark soy sauce, a small amount  
💧 Starch water, 4-5 tablespoons  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan (or frying pan)  
🥄 Spatula  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks or whisk  
🥄 Spoon  
🫙 Transparent lid  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Finely chop the onion, slice the chili peppers, and cut the silken tofu into bite-sized pieces. Set aside.  
2. In a bowl, crack 4 eggs, add a suitable amount of salt and cooking wine, and whisk until well combined to form an egg mixture.  
3. Heat a suitable amount of cooking oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and stir-fry until translucent and fragrant.  
4. Add the minced pork and quickly break it apart. Season with salt, ground pepper, and chili powder. Stir-fry until the surface of the pork turns slightly golden.  
5. Gently slide the silken tofu pieces into the pan. Add 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce and a small amount of dark soy sauce. Gently shake the pan to evenly coat the ingredients with the seasonings.  
6. Pour in 4-5 tablespoons of starch water, add the sliced chili peppers, and stir gently. Cover with a lid and simmer over medium heat for about 1 minute to infuse the tofu with flavor.  
7. Reduce the heat to low. Evenly pour the egg mixture over all the ingredients in the pan. Use a spatula to gently lift the tofu and minced pork slightly, allowing some ingredients to emerge above the egg mixture.  
8. Cover with the lid and simmer over low heat until the eggs reach the desired doneness. Then turn off the heat.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is rich in protein. The combination of tofu, eggs, and meat makes it suitable for those looking to build muscle or control carbohydrate intake. The minced pork naturally contains some fat, so use less oil during cooking. Silken tofu is delicate and prone to breaking, so handle it gently when stirring.
