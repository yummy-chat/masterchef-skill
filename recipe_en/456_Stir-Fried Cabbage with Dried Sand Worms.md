# Stir-Fried Cabbage with Dried Sand Worms
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 Dried sand worms, to taste  
🥬 Cabbage, to taste (shredded)  
🧄 Garlic, to taste (minced)  
🧂 Salt, a small amount  
🫒 Vegetable oil, a little  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Stainless steel wok  
🥄 Wooden spoon  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place the wok over medium heat, add a little oil, then add the dried sand worms. Stir-fry until fragrant.  
**Note**: Dried sand worms have a natural sea flavor. Keep the heat at medium throughout to avoid burning.  
2. Add the minced garlic and continue stir-frying until the garlic aroma is released.  
3. Add the shredded cabbage and stir-fry until it is just cooked through.  
4. Add a small amount of salt to season, stir-fry evenly, then remove from heat.  
**Key Point**: Dried sand worms are naturally salty and savory, so use less salt than usual when cooking. Taste before adding more if needed.  

## Health Tips  
Dried sand worms are rich in protein and minerals, while cabbage provides high fiber, making this dish suitable for those needing nourishment and strength. Due to the natural saltiness of dried sand worms, be mindful of salt usage. The dry-frying method helps retain nutrients, making it especially suitable for consumption during summer when appetite is low.
