# Stir-Fried Beef with Lotus Root Tips
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Steps  
1. **Marinate the beef**: Slice the beef into thin pieces, place in a bowl, add 1 tablespoon light soy sauce,适量 oyster sauce, white pepper powder, and cornstarch. Mix well and marinate for 10 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Cornstarch locks in moisture, ensuring the beef is tender and not dry.  
2. **Prepare lotus root tips and red pepper**: Pour the bagged lotus root tips into a colander, rinse with water, and drain; dice or slice the red bell pepper, mince the garlic, cut the scallions into sections, and set aside.  
3. **Quick-fry the beef**: Heat the wok, add cooking oil, and when the oil reaches about 70% heat, turn to high heat. Add the marinated beef slices and quickly stir-fry until they change color, then immediately remove.  
   **Note**: Do not overcook the beef; remove it as soon as it changes color to avoid toughness.  
4. **Sauté the vegetables**: Leave a little oil in the wok, add the minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant, then add the diced red pepper and drained lotus root tips. Stir-fry quickly over high heat. Add a pinch of salt at this stage if desired to lightly season the vegetables.  
5. **Combine and season**: Return the cooked beef to the wok, drizzle with a little light soy sauce, sprinkle in the scallion sections, and quickly stir-fry everything evenly, then remove from heat.  
   **Key point**: Adding scallions at the end uses residual heat to enhance aroma and keep them vibrant green.  
6. Serve on a plate. This dish features tender beef and crunchy lotus root tips, with a tangy, spicy pickled chili flavor that is appetizing.  

## Health Advice  
Beef is high in protein and low in fat, paired with the crisp texture of lotus root tips and the mild spiciness of pickled chili, making it suitable for summer to stimulate appetite and replenish energy. Keep the stir-fry time for beef short to prevent toughness; rinsing and draining the lotus root tips removes excess salt.
