## Preparation Steps  
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt, and whisk until the egg mixture is evenly combined.  
2. Use a serrated knife to split the baguette lengthwise, and spread an even layer of butter on the inside of the bread.  
3. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, scoop out the flesh with a spoon, and slice it thinly.  
4. Place the non-stick pan over heat, pour in an appropriate amount of cooking oil and heat it up, then add the egg mixture and quickly stir and scramble with a wooden spatula.  
   **Key Point**: Use medium heat and stir-fry quickly. Remove from heat immediately when most of the egg is set but still soft and tender to avoid overcooking; use a non-stick pan and wooden spatula to prevent sticking.  
5. On the buttered bread, layer the avocado slices, scrambled eggs, and finally spread a layer of pesto, ensuring even layers.  
6. Close the bread, cut it into bite-sized pieces with a serrated knife, and serve.  

## Health Tips  
Avocado provides healthy fats, scrambled eggs supply protein, paired with the whole wheat fiber of the baguette, it is suitable as a breakfast for fitness or weight loss phases. Note that pesto has a high oil content, so it is recommended to make a low-fat version at home to control calories.
