# Steamed Beef with Enoki Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Beef shank, as needed (sliced thinly against the grain)  
🍄 Enoki mushrooms, as needed (roots removed, cut into three sections)  
🌶️ Pickled chili peppers, as needed (minced)  
🫚 Ginger, as needed (julienned)  
🧅 Scallions, as needed (finely chopped)  
🥚 Egg white, half  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🍬 Sugar, as needed  
🦪 Oyster sauce, a small amount  
🥢 Soy sauce, a small amount  
🌽 Cornstarch, as needed  
🫒 Peanut oil, a small amount (for sealing during marination) + as needed (for drizzling at the end)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, chef’s knife  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Strainer  
🍽️ Plate  
🔥 Steamer  

## Steps  
1. Slice the beef shank thinly against the grain; remove the roots of the enoki mushrooms and cut into three sections. Wash them and thoroughly squeeze out excess water using a strainer. Mince the pickled chili peppers, julienne the ginger, and finely chop the scallions.  
   **Key**: Enoki mushrooms must be squeezed as dry as possible, otherwise, they will release water during steaming and affect the flavor absorption.  
2. Add salt, sugar, oyster sauce, soy sauce, cornstarch, and egg white to the beef slices. Mix repeatedly until the meat becomes sticky and cohesive, ensuring the egg mixture coats evenly.  
3. Add the minced pickled chili peppers and julienned ginger, and mix well. Finally, drizzle in a small amount of peanut oil and mix again to lock in moisture.  
4. Once the steamer is producing steam, spread the squeezed-dry enoki mushrooms on the bottom of a plate. Cover with the lid and steam over high heat for about 2 minutes.  
5. Open the lid and evenly spread the marinated beef slices over the enoki mushrooms. Continue steaming over high heat for another 5 minutes.  
   **Note**: About 3 minutes into steaming, open the lid and use chopsticks to separate any overlapping beef slices to ensure even cooking. Then, cover the steamer again and continue until the time is up.  
6. After a total steaming time of about 7 minutes, remove from the steamer. Sprinkle the chopped scallions on top and drizzle with hot peanut oil to release the aroma.

## Health Advice  
This dish is high in protein and low in fat. Enoki mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber. The steaming method reduces oily fumes, making it suitable for weight loss. Slicing beef thinly against the grain and steaming for 7 minutes yields the most tender texture; enoki mushrooms must be squeezed dry to prevent water from diluting the flavor.
