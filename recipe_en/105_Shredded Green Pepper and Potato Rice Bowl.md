# Shredded Green Pepper and Potato Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥔 Potato (appropriate amount, cut into thin shreds)  
🫑 Green pepper (appropriate amount, cut into thin shreds)  
🍚 White rice (appropriate amount, pre-cooked)  
🧅 Scallion (appropriate amount, minced)  
🧄 Garlic (appropriate amount, minced)  
🧂 Light soy sauce (2 tbsp)  
🧂 Dark soy sauce (1 tbsp)  
🧂 Salt (1 tsp)  
🌿 Thirteen-spice powder (1 tsp)  
💧 Water (1 bowl)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spatula  
🥣 Bowls (for serving rice and water)  
🥢 Spoon (for eating)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Cook the rice in advance. Cut the potato and green pepper into thin shreds. Mince the scallion and garlic and set aside.  
2. Place the wok over heat, pour in an appropriate amount of oil and heat until hot. Add the minced scallion and garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.  
3. Add the potato shreds and stir-fry continuously until they are cooked through and slightly tender, with a slight change in color and texture.  
4. Add the green pepper shreds, then immediately add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, salt, and thirteen-spice powder. Turn the heat to high and stir-fry quickly, ensuring the ingredients are evenly coated with the sauce.  
5. Pour in one bowl of water, cover the wok with a lid, and reduce the heat to medium-low. Simmer for 5 minutes until the potato shreds are well-flavored and soft.  
6. Remove the lid, turn the heat to high, and stir-fry continuously to reduce the sauce until it thickens and evenly coats the ingredients.  
7. Serve the cooked shredded green pepper and potato over the rice. Enjoy.  

## Health Advice  
The shredded green pepper and potato paired with rice offers a balanced ratio of vegetables to staple food. Since potato shreds are rich in starch, it is recommended to control the amount of rice to avoid excessive carbohydrate intake. This dish is suitable for those who need a quick energy boost.
