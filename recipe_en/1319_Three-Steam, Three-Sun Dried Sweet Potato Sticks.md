# Three-Steam, Three-Sun Dried Sweet Potato Sticks  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍠 Sweet Potatoes 3750g  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Peeler  
🔪 Chef’s Knife, Cutting Board  
🍳 Steamer (with steaming rack)  
🕸️ Drying Rack  

## Instructions  
1. Peel the sweet potatoes and cut them into evenly sized long strips.  
2. Place the sweet potato strips into the steamer and steam over high heat for 25 minutes, until a chopstick can easily pierce through.  
3. Remove the steamed sweet potato strips, spread them out on a drying rack, and dry them under the sun or in a dehydrator until they feel firm and springy when pressed.  
   **Note**: This is the first steam and dry cycle.  
4. Place the semi-dried sweet potato strips back into the steamer and steam over high heat for another 25 minutes.  
5. Remove and continue drying under the sun or in a dehydrator.  
   **Note**: After this drying, the chewiness will increase further.  
6. Repeat the third steaming for 25 minutes and drying step.  
   **Key Point**: The entire process requires completing “three steams and three dries.” No sugar is added; the sweetness comes from the natural sugar concentration of the sweet potatoes themselves. The finished product should feel firm and chewy when squeezed, and when torn apart, resemble preserved fruit.

## Health Tips  
The three-steam, three-dry process makes these sweet potato sticks sweet without being cloying. They are rich in dietary fiber and low in fat, making them a satisfying snack. However, portion control is important due to their natural sugar content; those with diabetes should consume only in small amounts.
