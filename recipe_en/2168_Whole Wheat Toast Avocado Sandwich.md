# Whole Wheat Toast Avocado Sandwich
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 Whole wheat toast 4-6 slices (homemade, very soft)
🥑 Avocado 1 (ripe)
🥩 Thick-cut steak 1 large piece (cut into 4 small pieces)
🥬 Leafy greens 1 handful
🥚 Eggs 2
🧈 Butter as needed (melted and brushed on both sides of toast)
🧂 Black vinegar as needed (for dressing salad)
🧂 Mayonnaise as needed (squeezed on avocado)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Serrated knife
🔪 Knife, cutting board
♨️ Air fryer
🎞️ Aluminum foil
🥘 Frying pan
🍳 Snow pot (small saucepan)
🥄 Slotted spoon
🧊 Measuring cup (for ice water)
📦 Sealed bag
🫗 Cooling rack

## Preparation Steps
1. Slice the homemade whole wheat toast with a serrated knife. As it is very soft, handle with extra care.
2. Place eggs in cold water, bring to a boil, then reduce to medium-low heat and cook until hard-boiled. Immediately transfer to ice water to cool for easy peeling.
3. Cut the thick-cut steak crosswise and lengthwise into 4 equal pieces to ensure even cooking.
4. Peel the ripe avocado by hand, remove the pit, and use the tip of a knife to slice the flesh to prevent sticking.
5. Evenly brush both sides of the toast slices with melted butter, then place them in an air fryer lined with aluminum foil.
6. Bake at 180°C for 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy.
7. Heat a frying pan, add the steak pieces, and cook until both sides are browned and the interior reaches desired doneness. Remove from pan.
8. Arrange the washed leafy greens on a plate and drizzle with black vinegar as the salad base.
9. Assemble: Place the crispy toast, then layer salad greens, seared steak, and avocado slices. Squeeze mayonnaise on top, and serve with the peeled and sliced eggs on the side.

## Health Tips
Whole wheat toast provides quality carbohydrates, while avocado and eggs are rich in healthy fats and protein, making this suitable for those aiming to lose fat or build muscle. Be mindful of the portions of mayonnaise and steak to avoid excessive calorie intake.
