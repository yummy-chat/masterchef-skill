## Cooking Steps
1. Slice the pig liver and place it in a bowl. Add a large amount of salt, rub and mix for about 1 minute, then let it sit for 3 minutes to allow blood water to seep out.
   **Note**: Use a generous amount of salt to remove the odor; it must be thoroughly rinsed later.
2. Rinse the pig liver repeatedly with clean water until clean, then drain well.
3. Add oyster sauce, a little salt, white pepper powder, and cornstarch to the pig liver. Mix well to coat evenly.
   **Key**: Cornstarch locks in moisture for a tender and smooth texture.
4. Add half a tablespoon of cooking oil and mix well to seal the coating and prevent it from coming off.
5. Cut the tomato into chunks, slice the ginger, and cut the garlic sprouts into sections. Set aside.
6. Pour enough oil into the wok and heat to about 120°C. Add the pig liver and quickly separate the slices. Once it changes color, immediately remove and drain the oil.
   **Key**: The oil temperature should not be too high. Use medium-low heat to cook until the surface turns completely grayish-brown, ensuring it remains tender and not tough.
7. Leave a base layer of oil in the wok. Add the ginger slices and sauté over medium-low heat until fragrant.
8. Add the tomato chunks and stir-fry over medium heat until they soften and release juice. If the wok becomes too dry, add a little water to help extract the juice.
9. Once the tomato juice is released, season with适量 salt. Add the blanched pig liver and turn the heat to high.
10. Add the garlic sprout sections, drizzle cooking wine along the edge of the wok, and stir-fry quickly for about 10-15 seconds until evenly coated with the sauce. Remove from heat immediately.
    **Note**: The pig liver is already cooked; stir-frying over high heat is only to combine the flavors. Overcooking will make it tough.

## Health Advice
Stir-fried pig liver with tomatoes is rich in iron and vitamin A, making it suitable for those with anemia or in need of eye care. However, it is high in cholesterol, so those with high blood lipids should consume it in moderation.
