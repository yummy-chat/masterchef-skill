# White Sesame Seed Cake (Baiji Bing)  
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 1000g all-purpose flour  
🧈 60g lard  
🫧 3g yeast  
🫧 5g aluminum-free baking powder  
💧 420g water  
*(Plain cake preparation, no salt or seasonings used; flavor comes from the braised meat added later)*  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🫗 Stainless steel mixing bowl  
🔪 Cutting board/work surface  
🥢 Fish-belly rolling pin (date pit-shaped rolling pin)  
🍳 Flat iron griddle or electric pancake pan  
🔥 Drawer-style oven/roaster  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Knead the dough: Place flour, yeast, baking powder, and lard in a bowl. Gradually add water while stirring to form flocculent pieces, then knead into a dough.  
**Key point**: Use aluminum-free baking powder; knead until the bowl and hands are clean.  

2. First resting: Cover the dough with the bowl and let it rest for 20 minutes to relax the gluten.  

3. Second kneading: Remove the dough onto the cutting board, knead it thoroughly and evenly, applying force from left to right in a continuous pressing motion.  

4. Second resting: Fold the smooth dough, cover it with the bowl again, and let it rest for 30 minutes.  
**Note**: Resting time can be slightly shorter in summer and longer in winter.  

5. Pre-shaping and shaping:  
   - Take a piece of dough, knead it smooth, and roll it into a long strip with one end thicker than the other.  
   - Use the fish-belly rolling pin to lift the strip, while stretching and rolling it into a tongue-shaped thin sheet.  
   - Starting from one end, roll it into a tight coil and pinch off any excess dough.  
   - Place the coil upright, press it flat with your palm, and dip it lightly in flour.  
   - Use the fish-belly rolling pin to roll it into a round, thin cake, using a “push and turn” technique.  
   - Lift the cake blank, press the center with your thumb while rotating it with the other hand to form a “bowl-shaped” cake base.  

6. Marking with grill marks: Place the bowl-shaped cake blank on a preheated flat iron griddle. Grill both sides until golden-brown patterns appear.  
**Key point**: Griddle temperature should be around 240°C; both sides must be marked.  

7. Baking: Transfer the marked cake blanks to the preheated lower rack of the drawer-style oven.  
**Key point**: The marked side must face down; lower rack temperature should be around 220°C; bake until the cake puffs up and forms a hollow interior.  

8. Remove from oven: Take out the finished cakes, split them open while hot, and fill with braised meat for serving.  

## Health Advice  
Baiji Bing is primarily made of fat and carbohydrates. It is recommended to pair it with plenty of vegetables and lean meat to balance the meal. It is suitable for replenishing energy before or after exercise. Be mindful of the amount of filling to avoid excessive intake of saturated fat and sodium.
