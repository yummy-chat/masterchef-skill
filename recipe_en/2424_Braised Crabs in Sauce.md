# Braised Crabs in Sauce
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn (September to October)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦀 **Ingredients**  
- Crabs (preferably river crabs, or swimming crabs as an alternative) 500 g (about 3-4 medium-sized crabs)  
- Ground pork 50 g  
- Egg 1 (about 50 g)  

🧂 **Seasonings**  
- Broad bean paste 30-50 g  
- Dark soy sauce 15 ml  
- Ketchup 15 ml  
- Cooking wine 5 ml  
- Rock sugar 0-15 g  
- Vegetable oil 20 ml (preferably unrefined rapeseed oil, or peanut oil as an alternative)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board  
🔪 Knife  
🍳 Pot  
🥘 Plate (heat-resistant, suitable for steaming)  
🔥 Steamer  

## Preparation Steps
1. Scrub the crabs clean, split them in half on a cutting board, and set aside.
   **Key point**: Must be scrubbed clean in advance to avoid sandiness affecting the texture.

2. Pour vegetable oil into a pot, add minced ginger and broad bean paste, stir-fry over heat until fragrant, then add rock sugar and stir until melted. When small bubbles emerge from the syrup, remove the stir-fried sauce and set aside.
   **Key point**: Control the heat to prevent the rock sugar from burning and turning bitter.

3. Spread an even layer of the stir-fried sauce on the bottom of a plate. Then place the crabs with the cut side facing down, arranging them neatly on top of the sauce.
   **Key point**: The cut side facing down helps the crab meat fully absorb the sauce.

4. Top with scallion sections and ginger slices. Optionally, spread ground pork on the bottom of the plate or crack an egg beside the crabs to add layers of flavor.
   **Key point**: Ground pork or egg are optional ingredients, not mandatory.

5. Transfer the plate to the steamer. Steam for 10-12 minutes after the steam rises.
   **Key point**: Do not steam for too long to prevent the crab meat from becoming tough; keep it sweet, tender, and springy.

6. After removing from the steamer, sprinkle with a little chopped scallion as desired to enhance aroma and color.

**Taste Adjustment Tips**: For a saltier flavor, increase the amount of broad bean paste; for a sweeter flavor, reduce the broad bean paste and add more rock sugar and ketchup accordingly.

## Health Advice  
This dish, paired with ground pork and egg, can neutralize the cold nature of crabs, making it suitable for nourishment in autumn. However, crab meat is high in protein and purines, so those with gout should consume it sparingly. Keeping the steaming time within 12 minutes preserves the sweetness and tenderness of the crab meat.
