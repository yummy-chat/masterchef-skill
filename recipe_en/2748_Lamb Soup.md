# Lamb Soup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)

## Ingredients
🥩 Lamb or lamb offal 300g
🧅 Scallion 50g
🌿 Cilantro 20g (optional)

## Seasonings
🧂 Cooking oil 10ml
🍶 Cooking wine 20ml
🧂 White pepper powder 1g
🧂 Table salt 5g
🌶️ Cumin powder 1g (optional)

## Cookware
🍳 Wok
🍲 Soup pot
🥄 Strainer
🔪 Chef's knife
🪓 Cutting board

## Steps
1. Cut the lamb into pieces 5 cm long and 0.5 cm wide.
2. Cut the scallion into sections and set aside.
3. Place the lamb in the soup pot, add 1000ml of room temperature water, pour in the cooking wine, and add the scallion sections.
4. Bring to a boil over high heat for 2 minutes, then remove the lamb, rinse thoroughly with room temperature water, and drain well.
5. In a separate wok, heat the cooking oil over high heat, add the lamb and stir-fry for 2 minutes until the surface is lightly browned.
6. Add enough boiling water, then turn to high heat.
7. Keep boiling over high heat for 5 minutes, then add white pepper powder and salt, and continue boiling for another 5 minutes.
8. Ladle into bowls, sprinkle with cilantro and cumin powder, stir well, and serve.

## Notes
- After adding boiling water, maintain high heat to help the fat and broth fully blend.

## Health Tips
Lamb soup is rich in high-quality protein and iron, making it ideal for winter nourishment, especially for those with cold hands and feet. Those with high blood pressure should reduce the salt, and gout patients should consume the concentrated broth with caution.
