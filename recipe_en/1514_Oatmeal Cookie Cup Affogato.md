# Oatmeal Cookie Cup Affogato
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🧈 Softened butter 90g  
🍬 Brown sugar 45g  
🍚 Powdered sugar 35g  
🥚 Egg 1  
🌾 Instant oatmeal 40g  
🌾 Cake flour 250g  
🥜 Crushed nuts as needed (for decorating the rim)  
🍦 Ice cream as needed  
☕ Coffee beans as needed (for extracting espresso)  
🍫 Dark chocolate as needed (melted over water, for brushing the inner wall)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥣 Glass mixing bowl  
🍳 Whisk  
🔪 Spatula  
🧂 Fine-mesh sieve  
🍮 Soufflé ramekin  
🖌️ Small brush  
🧹 Rolling pin  
🔥 Oven  
🍳 Baking tray  
🧤 Oven mitts  
🗡️ Small spatula  
☕ Coffee machine  

## Steps
1. Evenly brush a thin layer of cooking oil inside the soufflé ramekin to prevent sticking. Melt the dark chocolate over a water bath; chop the nuts and set aside.
2. Place softened butter, brown sugar, and powdered sugar in a mixing bowl. Whisk until smooth and creamy, with no visible granules.
3. Add the egg and continue whisking until fully incorporated. Then gradually add the sifted cake flour and instant oatmeal in batches. Mix initially with a spatula, then knead by hand into a smooth dough.
   **Note**: Cake flour must be sifted to avoid lumps in the dough; adding dry ingredients in batches makes mixing easier.
4. Divide the dough into 4 equal portions. Place each portion into the oiled ramekins. Press the dough with your fingers to fit the inner wall, while shaping the rim to ensure even thickness and a neat edge.
5. Preheat the oven to 190°C. Place the ramekins on a baking tray and bake in the middle rack for about 20 minutes, until the surface is light brown, the edges are slightly charred, and the texture feels firm.
6. Remove the cookie cups. Let them cool, then unmold. Evenly brush a layer of melted dark chocolate on the inner wall. Then dip the rim into the crushed nuts for decoration.
   **Key point**: The chocolate coating must completely cover the inner wall to act as a moisture barrier, preventing liquid from seeping in and softening the cookie.
7. Scoop an appropriate amount of ice cream into the decorated cookie cup. Place the cup under the coffee machine and extract espresso directly over the ice cream. Serve immediately.
   **Note**: Do not extract the coffee for too long, to avoid melting the ice cream excessively and affecting the balanced flavor of sweetness and bitterness.

## Health Advice  
This cookie cup affogato balances carbohydrates, fats, and caffeine. It is recommended as an occasional indulgence, perfect for an afternoon pick-me-up. Limit the amount of crushed nuts and ice cream to avoid excessive calorie intake.
