# Chocolate Lava Iceberg
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍫 Dark chocolate, as needed (broken into small pieces)  
💧 Hot water, as needed (volume ratio to chocolate approximately 1:2)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🫙 Glass jar with airtight lid  
⚡ Electric kettle  
🥣 Baking dish or pudding cup  
🥄 Small spoon  

## Instructions  
1. Break the dark chocolate into evenly sized small pieces and place them in a clean glass jar with a good seal.  
2. Pour in hot water, filling to about half the height of the chocolate.  
   **Note**: Water quantity is crucial; do not add too much, or it will not set.  
3. Quickly tighten the lid, hold the jar with both hands, and shake vigorously about 400 times until the mixture becomes a smooth, silky dark brown liquid with no obvious lumps.  
   **Note**: The jar will be hot; handle with care.  
4. Immediately pour the chocolate liquid into a baking dish or pudding cup; the liquid should flow smoothly.  
5. Refrigerate until completely set.  
6. Remove and scoop with a spoon to enjoy; the texture is cold and silky. If there is any residue in the jar, add a small amount of milk, shake, and drink.  

## Health Advice  
This chocolate lava iceberg is suitable for occasional indulgence but is high in calories. It is recommended to enjoy it in small portions as an afternoon treat. Diabetics should consume with caution.
