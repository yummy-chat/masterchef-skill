# Steamed Egg Custard  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 2 fresh eggs  
🧂 2g salt  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🥣 Bowl  
🥘 Aluminum foil (or plastic wrap)  
🍽️ Plate (for covering the bowl)  
🍳 Pot with lid  

## Steps  
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk thoroughly with chopsticks until the egg mixture is even.  
2. In a separate container, pour in 260ml of warm water, add 2g of salt, and stir until fully dissolved.  
3. Pour the salted water into the egg mixture, stirring in one direction until well combined. Use a spoon to skim off any bubbles on the surface.  
4. Cover the bowl with aluminum foil and place it in a pot pre-filled with about 3cm of water. Put the lid on the pot.  
5. Heat over medium heat until the water boils, then reduce to the lowest heat and steam for 4 minutes before removing from heat.  
   **Key points**: The ratio of egg mixture to water is 1:1.5; stirring must always be in one direction, never back and forth; after cooking, you can drizzle with steamed fish soy sauce, sprinkle with chopped green onions, and add a drop of sesame oil for flavor; let it cool slightly before eating to preserve the natural aroma of the eggs.  

## Health Advice  
Steamed egg custard is rich in high-quality protein, making it especially suitable for the elderly, children, and those who need a light diet. It is recommended to use warm water (20–30°C) instead of boiling water to prevent the egg mixture from clumping; using less salt is healthier.
