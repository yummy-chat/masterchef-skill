# Garlic Mushroom Chicken Breast
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken breast 2 pieces (cut into strips, then dice)
🍄 White mushrooms 5 pieces (cut into quarters)
🧄 Garlic 2 heads (peeled, cut in half)
🫒 Cooking oil as needed (for sealing, frying garlic, and stir-frying base oil)
🧂 Salt to taste
🌿 Black pepper to taste
🥄 Light soy sauce 2 tbsp (1 tbsp for marinating, 1 tbsp for seasoning)
🥄 Oyster sauce 1 tbsp
🌾 Cornstarch as needed (for coating)
🥄 Dark soy sauce as needed (for color)
💧 Water starch about 2 tbsp (for thickening, mix in advance)
🌱 Chopped green onions a little (for garnish)

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥣 Bowl (for marinating chicken)
🍳 Wok
🥄 Spatula or wooden spoon
🥄 Spoon
🥢 Slotted spoon (for removing fried garlic)
🍽️ Plate

## Steps
1. Cut the chicken breast into strips, then dice; cut the mushrooms into quarters; peel the garlic and cut in half.
2. Add salt, black pepper, 1 tbsp light soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, and a small amount of cornstarch to the diced chicken. Mix thoroughly. Then drizzle in some cooking oil to seal in moisture. Let marinate for 15 minutes.
   **Note**: Sealing with oil locks in moisture and prevents sticking when cooking.
3. Pour a generous amount of cooking oil into the wok. Add all the garlic cloves. Fry over low heat until the garlic turns golden. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside. Keep the garlic oil in the wok.
   **Key Point**: Maintain low heat throughout to avoid burning.
4. With the remaining oil in the wok, increase to medium-high heat. Quickly stir-fry the marinated chicken until the surface is fully cooked and no longer pink.
5. Add the mushrooms. Continue stir-frying for about 1–2 minutes until the mushrooms are cooked and slightly golden on the surface.
6. Add 1 tbsp light soy sauce, a suitable amount of dark soy sauce, and about 2 tbsp of pre-mixed water starch. Return the fried garlic to the wok. Switch to medium-high heat and stir-fry quickly until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients.
7. Turn off the heat and transfer to a plate. Sprinkle with a little chopped green onion and serve.

## Health Tips
Garlic oil stir-fried chicken breast and mushrooms offer a low-fat, high-protein option suitable for fitness or calorie-controlled diets. Marinating the chicken in advance and sealing with oil locks in moisture and prevents dryness. Frying the garlic until golden and returning it to the dish releases rich garlic aroma while keeping calories manageable.
