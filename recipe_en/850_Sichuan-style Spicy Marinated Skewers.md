# Sichuan-style Spicy Marinated Skewers
Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌶️ Dried chili peppers 80g (for red oil base)
🧄 Garlic, to taste
🫚 Ginger, to taste
🥦 Broccoli, to taste
🥒 Lotus root slices, to taste
🫘 Seaweed knots, to taste
🍄 Mushrooms, to taste
🧈 Tofu skin/dried tofu, to taste
🥚 Quail eggs, to taste
🍲 Broth 2.5kg (for making the marinade)

🫘 Broad bean paste 100g
🫘 Fermented black beans 10g
🫒 Soybean oil 900g
🧈 Beef tallow 100g
🧂 Premium soy sauce, to taste
🧂 Salt, to taste
🧂 Chicken powder, to taste
🌶️ Dried chili peppers and Sichuan peppercorns, to taste (for enhancing the marinade flavor)

**Spice packet ratio (in grams)**:
- 🌿 Star anise 2g
- 🌿 Amomum tsao-ko 1g
- 🌿 White pepper 3g
- 🌿 Coriander seeds 5g
- 🌿 Cao guo 5g
- 🌿 Cassia cinnamon 3g
- 🌿 Lemongrass 1g
- 🌿 Bay leaves 2g
- 🌿 Angelica dahurica 2g
- 🌿 Cloves 1g
- 🌿 Sichuan peppercorns 6g

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🔪 Meat grinder
🥄 Strainer
🥄 Ladle
🖌️ Brush

## Preparation Steps
1. Boil 80g dried chili peppers in water until soft, easily pierced by fingers, then remove.
**Key point**: The peppers must be fully softened to remove harshness and release the red oil color.

2. Put the boiled peppers into a meat grinder, add 100g broad bean paste, 10g fermented black beans, and an appropriate amount of garlic and ginger. Grind into a coarse paste resembling sticky rice chili.
**Key point**: The peppers must be fully softened to remove harshness and release the red oil color.

3. Pour 900g soybean oil and 100g rendered beef tallow into a wok. Heat until melted, then add the ground chili paste. Simmer over low heat, stirring continuously to prevent sticking, until the chili pieces dry out, the oil turns bright red, and the aroma is released.
**Key point**: After frying, remove from heat and cover to let it rest, which helps reduce the harshness of the chili.

4. In another pot, add 2.5kg of broth and the prepared red oil.
**Key point**: After frying, remove from heat and cover to let it rest, which helps reduce the harshness of the chili.

5. Add an appropriate amount of green and red Sichuan peppercorns and dried chili peppers to the broth. Soak the spice packet in clean water for 10 minutes, then add it to the pot. Also add premium soy sauce, salt, and chicken powder.
**Key point**: Simmer over low heat for 30 minutes. The marinade should be quite strong in flavor overall for the vegetables to absorb it well.

6. Add the skewered ingredients to the simmering marinade, cook over low heat for 5-6 minutes until just cooked through, then turn off the heat and let the ingredients soak in the liquid for about 30 minutes.
**Key point**: Cooking is for doneness, **soaking** is the key step for true flavor infusion.

7. After soaking, remove the skewers and brush each skewer with the marinating oil from the pot. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice
This spicy marinated skewer dish is rich in dietary fiber and plant protein from vegetables and soy products. However, the marinade is high in oil and salt, and the chili is quite stimulating. It is recommended to pair it with a light staple food. Those with weak digestive function should control their serving size.
