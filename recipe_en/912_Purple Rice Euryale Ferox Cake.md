# Purple Rice Euryale Ferox Cake
Cuisine: Others  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Rice flour 140g (100g for purple rice layer, 40g for white layer)  
🍙 Glutinous rice flour 80g  
💜 Euryale ferox powder 30g  
🍇 Purple rice powder 30g  
💧 Water 150g (for mixing powder)  
🍬 Fine granulated sugar 50g (for purple rice layer)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🥘 Stainless steel steamer  
🌬️ Stainless steel sieve  
🥣 Enamel basin  
🥛 Measuring cup (made from a plastic bottle)  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🪵 Wooden cutting board  
🧵 Cheesecloth (for lining the steamer basket and covering the lid)  
📐 Square mousse ring  
🪀 Rubber band  
🍳 Pot lid  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place purple rice powder, euryale ferox powder, glutinous rice flour, and rice flour into a basin. Add water and mix well. Rub the mixture with your hands to break up any lumps. Check the moisture of the powder mixture: When you grab a handful and squeeze, it should form a clump; when gently tossed, it should not fall apart; when broken in half, the cross-section should be neat. If it is too dry, add a small amount of water to adjust.  
2. Sift the mixed powder through a stainless steel sieve until it is fluffy and fine.  
3. In another basin, combine the rice flour, glutinous rice flour, and an appropriate amount of water to achieve the same moisture level. Sift similarly and set aside.  
4. To prevent water droplets from falling during steaming, wrap the pot lid with cheesecloth and secure it with a rubber band.  
5. Line the steamer basket with cheesecloth and place the square mousse ring inside. First, evenly spread half of the purple rice powder mixture, then all of the white rice powder mixture, and finally the remaining purple rice powder mixture. All powder layers should be gently placed, not pressed down. Smooth the surface with the back of a knife.  
6. It is recommended to pre-cut division lines on the raw cake using a knife for easy removal after steaming.  
7. Once the water in the steamer is boiling, place the steamer basket with the raw cake inside. Cover with the prepared lid and steam over medium heat for 30 minutes.  
8. When the cake color darkens and changes from a powdery to a solid state, remove it from the steamer. Let it cool slightly, then lift the cheesecloth to release the whole cake. Cut along the pre-cut lines into small pieces.  

## Health Advice  
Purple rice euryale ferox cake is rich in dietary fiber and B vitamins, making it suitable for people with weak spleen and stomach as well as those needing gentle nourishment. Note that euryale ferox is difficult to digest, so consumption should be controlled. Pairing with hot tea is more beneficial for stomach health.
