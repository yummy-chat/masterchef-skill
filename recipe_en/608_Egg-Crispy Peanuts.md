# Egg-Crispy Peanuts
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty level: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥜 Red-skinned peanuts, appropriate amount (plump and full, rinsed and drained)
🥚 Eggs, 2
🍬 White sugar, 1 tablespoon
🧂 Salt, 1 small tablespoon
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorn powder, appropriate amount
🫒 Sesame oil, a small amount
🌽 Corn starch, appropriate amount (or wheat starch)
🌾 All-purpose flour, appropriate amount

## Cookware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🧺 Slotted spoon
🍳 Wok or small saucepan

## Preparation Steps
1.  Rinse the red-skinned peanuts with clean water and drain off excess moisture, keeping the surface slightly damp.
2.  Add white sugar, salt, and Sichuan peppercorn powder to the peanuts. Crack in the eggs and stir continuously in one direction until the sugar and salt are completely dissolved.
3.  Drizzle in a small amount of sesame oil and mix well. Then, add corn starch a little at a time, stirring as you go, so each peanut is evenly coated with starch paste.
4.  Sprinkle in the all-purpose flour and use your hands to toss and mix until the peanuts are individually coated and not sticking together.
5.  Pour the coated peanuts into a slotted spoon and gently shake to remove excess dry powder.
6.  Pour enough oil into the wok and heat over medium heat until dense small bubbles appear around a chopstick inserted in the oil.
7.  Add all the peanuts to the oil at once. Do not stir initially; wait until the surface batter sets and the peanuts float. Then, gently push them with the slotted spoon for even heating.
8.  Keep observing, and as soon as the coating turns slightly yellow, immediately turn off the heat.
   **Note**: The residual heat will continue to deepen the color.
9.  Quickly pour the peanuts along with the oil into a large slotted spoon to drain the oil. Then, spread them out to cool naturally. Once completely cool, the texture becomes extremely crispy.

## Health Advice
This egg-crispy peanut dish is rich in protein and healthy fats, making it suitable as a snack for energy replenishment. Be mindful of the portion size to avoid excessive calorie intake. Elderly people and children should chew carefully to prevent choking.
