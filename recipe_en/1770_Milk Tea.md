# Milk Tea
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍵 Black tea bags  
🥛 Whole milk powder  
🥛 Evaporated milk  
🍬 Granulated sugar  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥤 Cup  
⚖️ Measuring tool  

## Steps
1. Place 2 black tea bags in a cup, then pour in 180–200mL of boiling water.  
   **Key Point**: After pouring in the boiling water, maintain the temperature inside the cup by using a lid or a small-mouthed cup to prevent heat loss, ensuring proper extraction.  

2. Let steep for 20–30 minutes. During this time, lift the tea bags or stir gently to ensure an even tea concentration.  
   **Key Point**: Do not shorten the steeping time, or the tea flavor will be insufficient. You may extend steeping to 30 minutes for a stronger taste.  

3. Measure 11–12g of whole milk powder and 5–7g of granulated sugar, then add them to the brewed tea.  
   **Key Point**: The milk powder must be fully dissolved. If using evaporated milk, add it directly and stir to combine.  

4. Stir until the sugar and milk powder are completely dissolved. Serve immediately.  

## Health Advice
Although milk tea is delicious, it is high in sugar and fat. It is recommended to replace whole milk powder with low-fat milk and reduce the amount of granulated sugar for a healthier option. Suitable for occasional enjoyment. Those with high blood sugar or those on a weight-loss plan should consume with caution.
