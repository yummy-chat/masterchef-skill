# Plum Blossom Mini Cakes
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 Eggs 4 (about 160g)
🥛 Pure milk 200ml
🌾 Low-gluten flour 210g
🧈 Cooking oil or butter 20g
🧂 Salt 2g
🍬 White sugar 80g
🧂 Aluminum-free baking powder 6g

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl / mixing bowl
🥄 Hand whisk
🍶 Measuring cup
🍳 Non-stick plum blossom mini cake mold
🖌️ Silicone brush
🔥 Gas stove

## Preparation Steps
1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add salt and white sugar, whisk until the sugar is completely dissolved.
2. Add pure milk and cooking oil, whisk again until well combined.
3. Sift in low-gluten flour and aluminum-free baking powder, stir in a zigzag motion until a smooth, thick, and flowable batter forms.
4. Pour batter into a measuring cup and let rest for 20 minutes.
5. Place the mold on the gas stove over medium heat, and evenly brush a thin layer of oil into each cavity on both sides.
6. With the oiled side facing down, pour batter into the cavities until level with the top edge.
7. Immediately close with the other preheated and oiled side, quickly flip the mold so batter evenly flows into both halves, forming a full plum blossom shape.
8. Keep over medium heat and flip the mold evenly back and forth, heating for about 6 minutes in total. Midway, you may gently lift a corner to check; once the batter sets and the surface turns golden brown, it's cooked through.
9. Turn off heat, remove cakes, break them into individual mini plum blossom cakes, and enjoy.

## Health Advice
These plum blossom mini cakes are relatively high in calories but visually appealing. Recommended as a breakfast or snack, no more than 3 pieces per serving. Eggs and milk provide high-quality protein, making them suitable for children and office workers to replenish energy. Diabetic patients should reduce sugar intake.
