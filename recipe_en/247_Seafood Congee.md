# Seafood Congee
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🦀 1 Crab
🐚 3-4 Abalones (shelled, cut into small pieces, retain innards)
🐚 1 Small Bowl Clams
🦐 5-6 Shrimp
🍖 1 Large Pork Rib
🍚 1 Cup Rice (soaked in advance)
🌿 1 Celery Root (finely chopped)
🧅 A Few Scallions (finely chopped)
🌿 1 Small Bunch Cilantro (finely chopped)
🥫 Appropriate Amount Preserved Winter Vegetable (about 1 tbsp)
🥜 Appropriate Amount Satay Sauce (about 1 tbsp)
🥜 Appropriate Amount Peanut Butter (about 1 tbsp)
🫒 Appropriate Amount Sesame Oil
🌶️ Appropriate Amount Ground White Pepper

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍲 Clay Pot
🔪 Cutting Board, Knife
🥄 Ladle
🔗 Tongs

## Steps
1.  **Prepare the broth**: Place the pork rib in a clay pot with cold water. Cover and bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
    **Note**: Starting with cold water allows the flavors to fully release.
2.  **Prepare the garnishes**: Finely chop the celery root, scallions, and cilantro separately. Set aside.
    **Tip**: Prepare a slightly larger amount of garnishes to enhance the flavor layers.
3.  **Prepare the seafood**: Scrub the crab clean. Remove the shells from the abalones, trim the root ends, then cut into small pieces, retaining the innards. Wash the clams and shrimp thoroughly.
4.  **Cook the congee base**: After the pork rib broth is done, skim off any foam from the surface. Add the soaked rice and stir well.
    **Key**: When covering the pot, leave a gap to prevent the liquid from boiling over. Monitor the water level to avoid the pot drying out.
    **State**: Cook until the congee comes to a bubbling boil.
5.  **Add seafood in sequence**: First, add the crab and cook for about 2 minutes. Then add the abalone pieces, clams, and shrimp.
    **Judgment**: The seafood is perfectly cooked when all clams have opened.
    **Note**: Adding the crab first allows it to fully release its savory flavors.
6.  **Continue cooking for 5-10 minutes**, allowing the seafood broth to become richer.
7.  **Season**: Add the preserved winter vegetable, satay sauce, and peanut butter. Stir until well combined.
8.  **Just before serving**: Sprinkle in the chopped celery root, scallions, and cilantro, then immediately turn off the heat. Drizzle with sesame oil and sprinkle with ground white pepper.
9.  **Ladle into deep bowls and serve immediately.**

## Health Advice
This seafood congee is rich in high-quality protein and minerals, making it suitable for those who need nutritional supplementation. However, individuals with gout should limit their intake of crab and abalone. Although the pork rib broth is flavorful, it is relatively high in calories. For those on a weight loss plan, reduce the amount of pork ribs and consume more of the broth.
