# Steamed Assorted Cured Meats
Cuisine: Hunan Cuisine  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 1 piece cured pork belly (pre-boiled until soft, then sliced)  
🌭 1 Chinese sausage (pre-boiled until soft, then sliced)  
🍗 1 cured duck leg (pre-boiled until soft, then chopped)  
🦴 Some pork ribs (pre-boiled until soft, then cut into segments)  
🌶️ Some white chili peppers (or smoked tofu, used as a base)  
🧄 Some minced garlic  
🌶️ 1 tbsp chopped chili  
🫘 Some fermented black beans  
🥄 Some light soy sauce or low-salt light soy sauce  
🫒 Some cooking oil  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board  
♨️ Steamer  
🥣 Steaming bowl  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Boil the cured pork belly, Chinese sausage, cured duck leg, and pork ribs in boiling water until soft and cooked through, to remove some salt and make slicing easier.  
2. Cut the boiled cured meats separately: slice the cured pork belly, slice the Chinese sausage, chop the cured duck leg, and cut the pork ribs into segments.  
3. Take a steaming bowl and line the bottom with a layer of white chili peppers.  
4. Evenly arrange the sliced cured pork belly, Chinese sausage, cured duck leg, and pork ribs on top of the base ingredients.  
5. Sprinkle minced garlic, chopped chili, and fermented black beans on top in order.  
6. Drizzle with an appropriate amount of light soy sauce and cooking oil.  
**Key Point**: Add the cooking oil last to lock in moisture and give the dish a glossy, moist finish.  

7. Add water to the steamer, bring to a boil over high heat, then place the steaming bowl inside. Maintain high heat throughout and steam for about 25–35 minutes, until the cured meats are soft, cooked, and flavorful.  
**Heat and Timing**: Maintain high heat throughout, steaming for about 25–35 minutes until the cured meats are tender and infused with flavor.  

8. Remove the steaming bowl, and while hot, use chopsticks to stir the juices, seasonings, and ingredients from the bottom of the bowl evenly.  
**Key Point**: Mixing well enhances the fusion of flavors, resulting in a rich cured meat aroma.  

## Health Advice  
This steamed assorted cured meats dish is high in salt. It is recommended to pair it with plenty of vegetables or mixed grain rice to balance sodium intake. Suitable for those with high physical exertion or needing salt replenishment, but those with high blood pressure should only taste it lightly.
