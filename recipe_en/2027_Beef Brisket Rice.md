# Beef Brisket Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef brisket 1500g (choose cuts with skin and connective tissue, beef ribs can be used as a substitute)
🥔 Potatoes 500g (cut into chunks)
🧅 Scallions 20g (cut into three sections)
🫚 Ginger 100g (diced)
🧄 Garlic 50g (minced)
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorns 1g
🌿 Cloves 0.3g (3 pieces)
🌿 Licorice 2g (1 slice)
⭐ Star anise 2 pieces
🍃 Bay leaves 3 pieces (use 4 if the leaves are small)
🥬 Baby bok choy (as needed, to serve with rice)

### Seasonings
🫘 Pixian chili bean paste 15g
🧈 Red fermented bean curd 2 pieces (mashed)
🥫 Chu hou paste 45g
🥫 Char siu sauce 30g
🦪 Oyster sauce 30g
🧂 Chicken bouillon 4g
🍬 White sugar 10-15g
🧴 Dark soy sauce 10g
🍶 High-proof Chinese liquor 20g
🧴 Light soy sauce 20g

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, chef’s knife
🍳 Wok
♨️ Electric or stovetop pressure cooker
🫕 Clay pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Small bowl
🥄 Spoon

## Instructions
1. **Cut the beef brisket**: First cut the brisket into long strips, then into pieces about two fingers wide. Do not cut the pieces too small, as they will shrink during braising.
2. **Blanch**: Place the beef brisket in cold water and bring to a boil over high heat. Stir occasionally to help release impurities. Once fully blanched, remove and rinse thoroughly with warm water. Ensure there is enough water so no blood residue remains at the bottom.
3. **Prepare aromatics**: Cut the scallions into sections, dice the ginger, and mince the garlic.
4. **Prepare dry spices**: Set aside the Sichuan peppercorns, cloves, licorice, star anise, and bay leaves.
5. **Prepare the sauce**: In a small bowl, combine the Pixian chili bean paste, mashed red fermented bean curd, chu hou paste, char siu sauce, oyster sauce, chicken bouillon, sugar, and dark soy sauce. Mix well.
6. **Fry the aromatics**: Pour a little more oil than usual for stir-frying into a wok. When the oil is slightly warm, add the scallion sections, diced ginger, and minced garlic. Fry over high heat until lightly browned and fragrant, turning occasionally to prevent burning.
7. **Stir-fry the beef**: Add the drained brisket pieces to the wok and stir-fry for about 1 minute to combine well with the aromatics.
8. **Add liquor and light soy sauce**: Pour the high-proof Chinese liquor along the edge of the wok and stir-fry over high heat until the alcohol evaporates. Then pour the light soy sauce along the edge and stir-fry until fragrant. Do not use a sauce-style liquor.
9. **Stir-fry the sauce and spices**: Add the prepared sauce mix and dry spices to the wok. Continue to stir-fry over high heat for about 2 minutes until the sauce and spices are fully aromatic.
10. **Pressure cook**: Transfer the stir-fried beef and sauce to a pressure cooker. Add water. For an electric pressure cooker, add enough water to almost cover the beef. For a stovetop pressure cooker, add enough water to just cover the beef. Use the “Beef/Mutton” setting on an electric pressure cooker. For a stovetop pressure cooker, once pressure is reached, reduce to the lowest heat and cook for 35–40 minutes.
11. **Skim fat and strain**: After pressure cooking, open the lid and skim off the surface fat. Remove the beef and strain the liquid through a slotted spoon to remove any solid residue.
12. **Add potatoes and simmer**: Return the beef to the pot, add the potato chunks, and mix well. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat, cover with a clay pot lid, and simmer for 20 minutes until the potatoes are tender.
13. **Finish**: Turn off the heat. It is ready to serve. No additional salt is needed. The sauce does not need to be reduced too much.

## Health Tips
This beef brisket rice is rich in high-quality protein and collagen, making it suitable for those with high physical exertion or who need to warm up. Potatoes have a high starch content, so those on a weight-loss plan can reduce their rice portion. Pairing with baby bok choy balances the nutrition. The steps of skimming the fat and blanching help reduce calorie content.
