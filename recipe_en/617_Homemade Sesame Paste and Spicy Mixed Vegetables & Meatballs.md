# Homemade Sesame Paste and Spicy Mixed Vegetables & Meatballs
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Season Meals  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 White sesame seeds (about 6 yuan worth, core ingredient)  
🍡 Mixed hot pot meatballs (to taste, leftover ingredients)  
🥦 Cauliflower (to taste)  
🌭 Sliced sausages (to taste)  
🧅 Scallion, ginger, garlic (to taste, for seasoning sauce)  
🌶️ Chili powder (2 tablespoons, for the sauce; can also sprinkle extra on top later)  
🧂 Light soy sauce (3 tablespoons)  
🍾 Black vinegar (2 tablespoons)  
🧂 Chicken bouillon (1 teaspoon)  
🍬 White sugar (1 teaspoon)  
🫒 Cooking oil (optional, to accelerate oil release from sesame seeds)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl (for soaking sesame seeds)  
🧺 Strainer (for draining sesame seeds)  
🍳 Frying pan (for toasting sesame seeds)  
🥢 Chopsticks (for stirring and turning)  
🍽️ Plate (for cooling sesame seeds)  
⏲️ Food processor (for grinding sesame seeds)  
🥣 Seasoning bowl (for mixing sauce)  
🥄 Spoon (for scooping and stirring)  
🥄 Slotted spoon (for retrieving cooked ingredients)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Pour the white sesame seeds into a glass bowl and add enough fresh water to soak them, in order to remove the raw, astringent taste.  
   **Note**: Soaking is a necessary step to remove any unpleasant odor and must not be skipped.  
2. Drain the sesame seeds using a strainer and leave them in a ventilated area to air-dry naturally until no residual moisture remains on the surface.  
   **Key point**: Ensure they are completely dry; otherwise, oil splatter may occur during toasting.  
3. Pour the dried sesame seeds into a frying pan, turn the heat to low, and stir slowly and continuously with chopsticks.  
   **Status**: Keep the heat low throughout the process; toast until the seeds turn a light yellow.  
   **Note**: Stir constantly to prevent localized burning.  
4. Take a small amount of sesame seeds and test them by crushing between your fingers. If they crumble easily, they are done; remove from heat immediately.  
   **Criterion**: The seeds should be a light yellow and crumble easily between fingers—this signals they are ready to be removed from the pan.  
5. Transfer the toasted sesame seeds to a clean plate and spread them out to cool until no longer hot to the touch.  
   **Key point**: They must cool down before grinding, to avoid damaging the food processor with hot steam or affecting oil extraction efficiency.  
6. Pour all the cooled sesame seeds into a food processor, pulse intermittently, and blend repeatedly until they turn into a paste.  
   **Key point**: Continuous grinding uses mechanical friction to release the seeds' natural oils.  
7. Let the sesame paste sit for about 3 hours to allow the oil to naturally separate; if you need faster oil extraction, add a small amount of cooking oil, stir well, and let it rest for a short while longer.  
   **Alternative**: Adding oil can significantly shorten the waiting time.  
8. After resting, process the sesame paste again in the food processor for 1 minute, resulting in a thick, smooth, and glossy sesame paste.  
9. Prepare the spicy mixed sauce: Take a seasoning bowl, add scallion, ginger, garlic, 1 tablespoon of white sesame seeds, 3 tablespoons of light soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of black vinegar, 1 teaspoon of chicken bouillon, 1 teaspoon of white sugar, 2 tablespoons of chili powder, then add an appropriate amount of homemade sesame paste. Stir thoroughly to combine; finally, sprinkle a little extra chili powder on top according to preference.  
   **Tip**: The sauce ratio can be adjusted to personal taste.  
10. Use a slotted spoon to retrieve the cooked hot pot meatballs, cauliflower, sausage slices, and other ingredients, draining them well. Place them in a bowl, pour the prepared sauce over them, mix well, and serve.  
    **Key point**: Ensure all ingredients are evenly coated with the sauce for the best flavor.  

## Health Advice  
Homemade sesame paste is rich in calcium and healthy fatty acids. Combined with the vegetables and meatballs in the spicy mixed dish, it offers a more balanced nutrition. Pay attention to controlling the amount of chili and sugar, making it suitable for appetizing when you have lost your appetite.
