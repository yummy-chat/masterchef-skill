# Milk-Boiled Corn
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌽 Sweet corn, about 1 cob (cut into sections, insert a bamboo skewer into each)
🥛 Whole milk, about 250ml (enough to submerge the corn)
🧈 Butter, to taste
🍬 White sugar, 1 teaspoon
🥛 Whipping cream, to taste (optional, for extra richness)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍲 Saucepan
✂️ Scissors (to open milk packaging)
🥄 Spoon (for stirring and scooping sugar)
🥢 Tongs (to remove corn)
🍽️ Plate

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the sweet corn into sections and insert a bamboo skewer into each section for easy handling.
2. Place the corn sections into a saucepan and pour in the whole milk until the corn is submerged.
3. Add butter, white sugar, and whipping cream.
   **Key Point**: Adjust the amount of sugar based on the corn’s natural sweetness and personal taste; whipping cream is optional but enhances the creamy richness.
4. Heat over medium-low heat until the corn is cooked through, the milk slightly thickens, and the sweet aroma of corn is released.
5. Turn off the heat, use tongs to remove the corn, plate, and serve.

## Health Advice
Milk-boiled corn is rich in dietary fiber and calcium, making it suitable as a breakfast or snack. Be mindful of the lactose from the milk and the fat content from the butter, and consume in moderation. Pair with a cup of unsweetened tea to balance calories; for those with diabetes, reduce the amount of sugar.
