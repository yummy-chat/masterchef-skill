# Thai-Style Lemongrass Pork Neck  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥓 Pork neck 500g  
🧄 Garlic a few cloves  
🌿 Lemongrass 1 stalk  
🌿 Cilantro roots 2  
🌶️ Bird's eye chili (to taste, for dipping sauce)  
🌿 Cilantro (to taste, for dipping sauce and garnish)  
🧂 Light soy sauce 25g  
🧂 Dark soy sauce 5g  
🐟 Fish sauce 25g (for marinade) + to taste (for dipping sauce)  
🍬 Sugar 25g (for marinade) + to taste (for dipping sauce)  
🍯 Oyster sauce 20g  
🍋 Lemon juice (to taste, for dipping sauce)  

## Tools & Equipment  
🔨 Mortar and pestle  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
🪥 Toothpicks  
♨️ Air fryer  
🍶 Small dish  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Instructions  
1. Cut the lemongrass into small sections, peel the garlic, and wash the cilantro roots.  
   **Note**: Cilantro roots are the key flavor source and must be kept.  
2. Place the lemongrass sections, garlic, and cilantro roots into a mortar and pestle, and pound thoroughly into a fine spice paste.  
   **Note**: It must be pounded into a paste to fully release the flavor; simply chopping will not work as well.  
3. In a bowl with the pork neck, add the pounded spice paste, then add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, 25g fish sauce, 25g sugar, and oyster sauce in order.  
4. Put on gloves and massage the pork neck thoroughly so it is evenly coated with the sauce.  
   **Note**: Use toothpicks to poke holes in the meat to help with flavor absorption; massaging promotes sauce absorption.  
5. Refrigerate the marinated pork neck for at least 2 hours; marinating overnight is recommended for better flavor.  
6. Remove the marinated pork neck and scrape off some of the surface spice fibers to prevent burning.  
7. Preheat the air fryer to 200°C, place the pork neck inside, and cook for about 18 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the surface is browned and fragrant.  
8. While waiting, prepare the dipping sauce: In a small dish, mix to taste sugar, lemon juice, and fish sauce, then add chopped bird's eye chili and cilantro, and stir well.  
9. Let the cooked pork neck rest briefly, then slice it diagonally, plate it, and serve with the dipping sauce, garnished with fresh lemongrass.  
   **Note**: Slicing diagonally shows the meat's texture.  

## Health Advice  
Pork neck has a high fat content, but marinating it with lemongrass and cilantro roots helps promote digestion. It is suitable for those who enjoy rich flavors but need to control blood lipids, in moderate amounts. Using an air fryer is recommended to reduce extra oil, and pairing with a sour and spicy dipping sauce helps balance the taste.
