# Tiramisu
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍪 Ladyfingers (as needed)
🥛 Heavy cream (as needed)
🧀 Mascarpone cheese (as needed)
🥚 Egg yolks (several)
🍬 Granulated sugar (approx. 50g)
☕ Coffee liquid (as needed)
🍫 Cocoa powder (as needed)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥣 Mixing bowl
⚡ Electric hand mixer
🥄 Silicone spatula
🍳 Heatproof hot water
🍽️ Glass or ceramic container

## Steps
1. Make the egg yolk mixture: Place egg yolks and granulated sugar in a mixing bowl, and whisk continuously over a hot water bath until the mixture expands in volume, turns pale in color, and becomes thick.
   **Note**: Alternatively, hot sugar water can be poured in while whisking, but care must be taken to prevent the egg yolks from curdling due to high heat.

2. Incorporate the cheese: Gradually add mascarpone cheese, softened to room temperature, into the egg yolk mixture in batches. Fold with a spatula or mix on low speed with an electric mixer until smooth and lump-free.
   **Key point**: If using a substitute, whisk it until smooth in advance.

3. Whip the heavy cream: Whip the heavy cream until it shows lines but is still flowable (about 70% whipped), then gently fold it into the cheese mixture until well combined.

4. Assemble: Quickly dip the ladyfingers into the prepared coffee liquid, then layer them at the bottom of the container. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the cookies. Repeat the layering until reaching the desired number of layers.
   **Note**: The dipping time should be short to prevent the ladyfingers from absorbing too much liquid and becoming soggy.

5. Chill and set: Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the ladyfingers and cheese mixture to fully meld.

6. Before serving, use a sieve to evenly dust a layer of cocoa powder on top. Then scoop and enjoy.

## Health Advice
This tiramisu is made with egg yolks whisked over a hot water bath and combined with mascarpone cheese, resulting in a dense and creamy texture. However, it is high in sugar and cream. It is recommended to pair it with black coffee and control the portion size. It is suitable for enjoying in small amounts during afternoon tea time.
