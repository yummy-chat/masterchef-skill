# Spicy Shrimp Bagel Sandwich  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦐 Fresh shrimp about 18 (mince part into paste, keep some whole)  
🥔 Potato 1 (cut into chunks)  
🥒 Cucumber 1 (half sliced, half diced)  
🥬 Lettuce appropriate amount  
🍊 Orange 1 (sliced)  
🥚 Egg appropriate amount (egg white for shrimp paste, whole egg liquid for coating)  
🧈 Plain yogurt appropriate amount  
🥫 Ketchup appropriate amount (for dipping potato chunks)  
🌶️ Chili powder appropriate amount  
🌸 Sichuan peppercorn powder appropriate amount  
🫘 White sesame seeds appropriate amount  
🥜 Peanut powder appropriate amount  
🍬 Sugar appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt appropriate amount  
🧀 Cheese powder appropriate amount (optional)  
🫒 Olive oil appropriate amount  
⚫ Black pepper appropriate amount  
🍯 Dijon mustard seeds sauce appropriate amount  
🍞 Breadcrumbs appropriate amount  
🌾 Cornstarch appropriate amount  
🍏 Apple juice appropriate amount  
🫧 Sparkling water appropriate amount  
🥖 Plain bagel dough 1 portion (prepared in advance)

## Instructions  
1. Soak potato chunks in lightly salted water for 5 minutes, drain, blanch in boiling water for 2 minutes, drain thoroughly and pat dry with paper towels. Mix with olive oil, black pepper, chili powder, and salt.  
2. Spread seasoned potato chunks in a single layer on a baking tray. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 10 minutes, then increase temperature to 200-210°C and bake another 15 minutes until golden brown and edges are crispy.  
3. Peel and devein shrimp. Use the back of a knife to pound some shrimp into paste; keep others whole. Mix shrimp paste with cornstarch, salt, and egg white, stir in one direction or repeatedly slap until firm and cohesive.  
4. Lay out plastic wrap, spread a layer of shrimp paste, place several whole shrimp in the middle, then cover with another layer of shrimp paste. Wrap with plastic wrap and shape into a round patty. Freeze briefly to set.  
5. Coat the set shrimp patty with cornstarch, then whole egg liquid, then breadcrumbs. Heat enough oil in a pan until bubbles rapidly form around the patty when added. Fry until golden and crispy on both sides, drain on paper towels.  
6. Shape the bagel dough into a ring or petal shape. Bring water to a boil, drop in the bagels, boil for about 30 seconds per side, drain well.  
7. Mix chili powder, Sichuan peppercorn powder, white sesame seeds, peanut powder, sugar, salt, and cheese powder to make a coating. Dip the top of each boiled bagel into the coating. Place on a baking tray and bake in a preheated oven at 180-200°C (middle rack) for 15-20 minutes until nicely colored.  
8. Mix plain yogurt with Dijon mustard seeds sauce, black pepper, and diced cucumber to make a yogurt sauce.  
9. Slice the baked bagel horizontally. Spread yogurt sauce on the cut sides. Layer cucumber slices, lettuce, fried shrimp patty, drizzle more yogurt sauce, add another slice of cucumber, then top with the bagel top. Secure with a skewer if desired.  
10. Arrange the bagel sandwich, potato wedges, lettuce salad, and orange slices on a plate. Serve with a small dish of ketchup. Fill a glass with ice cubes, pour in apple juice, then top with sparkling water. Stir gently.  

## Health Advice  
Bagel paired with shrimp patty offers a classic combination of carbs and high-quality protein, but the fried shrimp patty is relatively high in calories. It is recommended to pair with low-fat yogurt sauce and plenty of lettuce to balance flavor and health. Suitable for those looking to build muscle or satisfy cravings, but watch the calories from dipping sauces.
