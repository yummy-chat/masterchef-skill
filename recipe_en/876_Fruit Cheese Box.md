## Instructions  
1. **Make fruit jam**: Boil peach peels in water until the water changes color, then remove the peels. Add diced peaches, rock sugar, and lemon juice; cook until soft and thick. Cook blueberries and mango separately with white sugar and lemon juice until thickened, making three jams. Cool and set aside.  
   **Note**: Fruits with high water content can be cooked without added water; adjust sweetness and acidity to taste.  
2. **Prepare cake slices**: Bake a square cake base in advance. Once completely cooled, press the bottom of the box onto the cake to cut out square cake slices the same size as the box bottom.  
   **Note**: If you don’t have an oven, substitute with store-bought cake or bread slices.  
3. **Make cheese mixture**:  
   - Press and stir the cream cheese until smooth. If too firm, soften over warm water.  
   - Add 25g of white sugar to the whipping cream and beat with a mixer until it reaches a thick yogurt-like consistency.  
   - Gradually add the whipped cream to the cheese in batches, folding gently with a spatula to combine, creating plain cheese mixture.  
   - Divide the plain cheese mixture evenly into three portions. Mix each with the cooled blueberry, mango, and peach jams respectively to make three flavored cheese mixtures.  
   **Note**: Fold gently to avoid deflating; adjust thickness by adding or reducing whipping cream or substituting some yogurt if needed.  
4. **Assemble the box**:  
   - Pipe a layer of any flavored cheese mixture onto the bottom of the box.  
   - Place a cake slice on top.  
   - Pipe a layer of plain cheese mixture, then a layer of fruit-flavored cheese mixture, and finally a layer of corresponding fruit jam.  
   - Repeat layering cake slices, cheese mixtures, and jams until the box is filled.  
   **Note**: Smooth each layer evenly to ensure distinct layers when cut.  
5. **Chill and set**: Cover with the lid and refrigerate for at least 3 hours to allow the cheese mixture to fully set and flavors to meld.  
   **Note**: Sealed and refrigerated, it keeps for 3 days; frozen, it keeps for 1 month.  

## Health Tips  
Fruit and cheese complement each other nutritionally, but cream cheese and whipping cream are high in calories. It’s recommended to enjoy one small box at a time and pair with green tea to cut through the richness. Suitable for those who prefer light desserts and enjoy DIY cooking; note that fruits contain sugar, so those with diabetes should consume sparingly.
