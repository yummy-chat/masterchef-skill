# Passion Fruit Chicken Soup Noodles
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐔 Whole chicken (chopped into even pieces)
🫚 Ginger (as needed, for making soup)
🌿 Codonopsis pilosula (as needed)
🫘 Red dates (as needed)
🍄 Morel mushrooms (as needed)
🫐 Goji berries (as needed)
🍹 Passion fruit (as needed, separate the peel and pulp)
🍋 Lemon (about 1 slice, with peel)
🎃 Pumpkin puree (as needed)
🍜 Dried noodles (as needed)
🧂 Salt (for cooking noodles)
🧄 Minced garlic (as needed, for sauce)
🌶️ Hainan yellow lantern chili sauce (as needed)
🍬 White sugar (as needed)
🧂 White pepper powder (as needed)
🫚 Ginger slices (as needed, for sauce)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥣 Steaming bowl (with a strainer bowl)
🍲 Cooking pots (2)
🥄 Strainer
🥣 Stainless steel container
🥢 Bowl, chopsticks, spoon

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the evenly chopped chicken pieces along with ginger, codonopsis pilosula, red dates, morel mushrooms, and goji berries into a steaming bowl. Add enough water to submerge all ingredients. Place the bowl in a steamer and steam to make a chicken soup base.
   **Key Point**: The chicken pieces should be uniform in size to ensure even flavor extraction. The water must fully submerge the ingredients.
2. In a separate pot, add minced garlic, ginger slices, Hainan yellow lantern chili sauce, white sugar, and white pepper powder. Simmer over low heat until the sauce thickens, then pour out and set aside.
   **Key Point**: The sauce needs to be cooked until thick, allowing the sweet, sour, and spicy flavors to fully meld.
3. Pour the steamed chicken soup into another pot. Add the passion fruit peels, lemon slices, and pumpkin puree. Bring to a boil over high heat and then continue boiling for 15 minutes to allow the flavors to fully combine.
   **Key Point**: Boiling over high heat allows the fruity aroma to fully infuse with the chicken soup's umami flavor.
4. Use a strainer to remove the solid residues from the soup. Then add the passion fruit pulp and stir well to create a smooth soup base with bits of fruit.
   **Key Point**: Straining results in a smoother soup texture, while the retained pulp adds layers of texture.
5. In a pot, bring water and an appropriate amount of salt to a boil. Add the dried noodles and cook until they reach a firm and chewy texture. Drain and place the noodles into a serving bowl.
   **Key Point**: Adding salt to the water enhances the noodle's base flavor. The cooking time must be controlled for the best chewy texture.
6. Pour the refined passion fruit chicken soup base over the noodles in the bowl. Drizzle the prepared flavor sauce over the top according to personal preference.
   **Key Point**: The soup base and sauce can be flexibly combined to enrich the flavor profile.

## Health Advice
This passion fruit chicken soup noodle dish is sweet, sour, and appetizing. The pumpkin puree and morel mushrooms make the soup base nutritious and balanced, making it suitable for those with weak digestion or those looking to try something new. Note that passion fruit and chili sauce have a stimulating effect, so those with sensitive stomachs should consume in moderation.
