# Spanish Seafood Paella
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦐 Black Tiger Shrimp - to taste (heads removed, deveined; heads reserved)  
🦑 Squid - to taste (cut into small pieces)  
🦪 Mussels - to taste (washed in advance)  
🌭 Spanish Chorizo - to taste (cut into small pieces; can substitute with Chinese spicy sausage)  
🍚 Short-grain rice - 250g  
🧅 Onion - half (finely chopped)  
🧄 Garlic - to taste (finely chopped)  
🫑 Red and yellow bell peppers - half each (diced)  
🍅 Tomato - 1 (peeled and cut into pieces)  
🍋 Lemon - to taste (cut into wedges)  
🌿 Parsley - to taste (for garnish)  
🫒 Olive oil - to taste  
🍂 Bay leaves - 2  
🌶️ Smoked paprika - 1 tablespoon  
🍷 White wine - half a cup  
🧂 Salt - to taste  
🧂 Black pepper - to taste  
🌸 Saffron - 1 small pinch  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍲 Stockpot (for making broth, blanching shellfish)  
🥘 Large frying pan or cast-iron pan (for stir-frying, simmering rice)  
🥄 Wooden spatula  
🔪 Knife, cutting board  
🥅 Strainer  
🥛 Measuring cup  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Heat a small amount of olive oil in a pot, add the shrimp heads and stir-fry until red oil is released. Add some of the chopped onion and garlic, stir until fragrant. Pour in enough water, add bay leaves, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 20 minutes to make seafood broth.  
2. Use some of the seafood broth to blanch the mussels until they open, then remove and set aside. Strain the broth, discarding the shrimp heads, bay leaves, and other solids; reserve the liquid.  
3. Cut the squid into small pieces. Heat a generous amount of olive oil in a large frying pan, add the squid, and quickly stir-fry until just cooked. Remove and set aside.  
4. Using the residual oil in the pan, stir-fry the Spanish chorizo pieces over low heat until red oil is released. Add the remaining chopped onion, garlic, and diced bell peppers, stir until fragrant. Then add the smoked paprika and stir to combine.  
5. Add the diced tomato and white wine, stir-fry until the tomato softens.  
6. Return the uncooked rice and the stir-fried squid to the pan, mixing well with the other ingredients so that the rice grains are coated with oil. Season with salt and black pepper.  
7. Pour in the hot broth, enough to slightly cover the rice. Sprinkle in the saffron and stir to combine. Cover with a lid and simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.  
8. Remove the lid. Arrange the black tiger shrimp and the opened mussels on top of the partially cooked rice. Cover with the lid again and simmer over low heat for 8 minutes.  
9. Turn off the heat but do not remove the lid. Let it rest for another 10 minutes using residual heat, allowing the rice to fully absorb the liquid.  
10. Once done, garnish with lemon wedges and sprinkle with parsley leaves before serving.  

## Health Advice  
This Spanish seafood paella is rich in high-quality protein, dietary fiber, and antioxidants, making it suitable for those seeking a balanced diet. However, be mindful of the rice portion to avoid excessive carbohydrate intake. Additionally, chorizo and saffron may affect blood pressure; individuals with hypertension should consume in moderation.
