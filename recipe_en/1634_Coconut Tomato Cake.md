# Coconut Tomato Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍅 Fresh tomatoes, about a dozen (farm-grown, approximately 5 yuan per pound)
🌾 Pea starch, 200 grams
💧 Water, as needed (for making tomato juice, plus an additional 150 ml for starch slurry)
🍬 White sugar, one bowl (about 100-150 grams, adjust to taste)
🥥 Coconut flakes, as needed (for coating the finished product)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Knife
🪣 Large basin for water
🥤 Blender/food processor
🍳 Pot
🥄 Wooden spatula/spatula
🥢 Chopsticks
🍮 Glass bowl (for setting)
📋 Cutting board

## Steps
1. **Prepare the tomatoes**: Wash the tomatoes, score a cross on the top, place them in a basin, and pour hot water over them until the skin curls. Remove, let cool slightly, and peel off the skin.
2. **Make tomato juice**: Cut the peeled tomatoes into large chunks, place them in a blender, add a suitable amount of water, and blend into a smooth tomato juice, yielding about 1500 ml in total.
3. **Prepare starch slurry**: Put pea starch in a bowl, add 150 ml of water, and stir thoroughly with chopsticks until no lumps remain.
4. **Cook the sweet tomato juice**: Pour the tomato juice into a pot, add white sugar, and cook over low heat until it boils, stirring gently with a spatula to help the sugar dissolve.
5. **Thicken and cook the cake**: Once the tomato juice in the pot is boiling, reduce to the lowest heat. Stir the starch slurry again, then slowly pour it into the pot while stirring quickly and continuously with chopsticks or a spatula to prevent clumping. Keep the heat low and stir constantly. Cook until the mixture becomes very thick, semi-transparent, and darker in color, then turn off the heat.
6. **Set in a mold**: While hot, pour the tomato paste into a clean glass bowl and smooth the surface with a scraper. Let it cool naturally at room temperature for 2-3 hours until fully set.
7. **Cut and coat with coconut flakes**: Once set, invert the bowl to release the cake. Cut into uniform cubes, then roll them in coconut flakes to coat evenly. Arrange on a plate. Optionally, serve with honey.

## Health Suggestions
Tomatoes are rich in lycopene and vitamin C. Combined with pea starch, this makes a low-fat dessert suitable for summer appetizer or as a healthy snack. However, it is high in sugar, so diabetics should reduce the amount. It is recommended to pair with unsweetened yogurt to balance the sweetness.
