# Sour Plum Drink
Cuisine: Common Home-style
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍑 Black Plum 50g
🍑 Smoked Black Plum 10g
🌸 Roselle Flower 5g
🍒 Hawthorn 40g
🌿 Licorice Root 5g
🍇 Mulberry 10g
🌿 Mountain Mint 5g
🌿 Red Cardamom 1g
🌿 Schisandra Berry 3g
🌸 Dried Osmanthus Flower To taste
🍬 Yellow Rock Sugar To taste

## Cookware & Equipment
🥣 Glass Bowl
🫙 Gauze Bag
🍲 Deep Pot
🔥 Gas Stove
🥄 Ladle
🧊 Measuring Cup
🧊 Refrigerator

## Steps
1. Weigh all dry ingredients according to the recipe. The amount of smoked black plum can be adjusted as needed. Put everything into a gauze bag, tighten the opening, and rinse quickly with clean water to remove surface dust.
2. Place the herb bag in the pot, pour in 2 liters of purified water, and soak for half an hour until the liquid turns red.
   **Key Point**: Use purified water; soaking is crucial for extracting color and flavor.
3. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Uncover, add an appropriate amount of yellow rock sugar, stirring until fully dissolved. Adjust the amount of sugar to taste.
5. Turn off the heat, strain the first batch of liquid into a bowl, and keep the herb bag in the pot.
6. Add another 2 liters of water to the pot, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
7. Again add yellow rock sugar and stir until dissolved.
8. Pour the first batch of liquid from the bowl back into the pot and mix with the second batch.
9. After mixing, reduce to low heat and simmer for another 10 minutes, then turn off the heat.
10. Do not uncover the pot after turning off the heat. Let it sit covered until the sour plum drink has completely cooled.
    **Key Point**: Ensure it cools completely; do not rush.
11. Once cooled, transfer to the refrigerator and let it chill overnight. It tastes best when iced.
12. When serving, place dried osmanthus flowers and plenty of ice cubes in a cup, pour in the iced sour plum drink, shake or stir to combine.
    **Key Point**: Add the dried osmanthus flowers right before serving to better preserve their aroma.

## Health Tips
This sour plum drink is rich in organic acids from black plum and hawthorn, which help stimulate saliva production, quench thirst, aid digestion, and stimulate appetite. It is ideal for those with poor appetite or heavy sweating in summer. Add yellow rock sugar in moderation. Diabetics should consume with caution. It tastes better when chilled, but avoid over-icing to prevent stomach discomfort.
