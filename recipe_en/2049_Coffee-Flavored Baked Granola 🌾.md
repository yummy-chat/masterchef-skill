# Coffee-Flavored Baked Granola 🌾
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Rolled oats 150g
🥜 Almond slices approx. 50g
🎃 Pumpkin seeds approx. 50g
🥥 Cashew nuts approx. 50g
🥚 Egg white 1
🥥 Coconut flakes appropriate amount (added at the end)
🍁 Maple syrup appropriate amount
☕ Instant coffee powder approx. 1 teaspoon
🧂 Sea salt appropriate amount (a pinch)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥣 Large glass bowl
🥄 Spatula
⚖️ Kitchen scale
📜 Parchment paper
🍳 Baking tray
🔥 Oven
🍴 Fork
🍶 Glass jar with lid

## Instructions
1.  Pour the oats, almond slices, pumpkin seeds, cashew nuts, instant coffee powder, and sea salt into a large glass bowl. Add the maple syrup and egg white. Mix thoroughly with a spatula until all dry ingredients are evenly coated. The mixture should darken in color and feel slightly moist, with the coffee powder fully dissolved and no dry powder remaining.

2.  Preheat the oven to 140°C. Spread the mixed oat mixture evenly on a baking tray lined with parchment paper, ensuring it is in a single layer without overlapping. Bake for 20 minutes.
    **Note**: Low-temperature slow baking is intended to dehydrate and crisp the granola; do not increase the temperature.

3.  Remove the baking tray from the oven. Use a fork to flip the granola from the bottom, turning it over to ensure even heating on both sides. Return to the oven and bake for another 10 minutes.

4.  Remove the tray again, flip the granola, and evenly sprinkle with coconut flakes. Return to the oven and bake for approximately another 5 minutes, until the granola is golden brown, fragrant, and dry and crispy.
    **Key point**: Coconut flakes burn easily; add them only at the final stage and closely monitor their color change.

5.  Remove the baking tray from the oven. Transfer the granola along with the parchment paper to a countertop to cool completely. The texture will become crispy only after it is fully cooled. Avoid sealing it immediately to prevent it from becoming soft due to moisture absorption.

6.  Once completely cooled, store the granola in a clean, dry airtight jar to prevent moisture. It can be enjoyed with yogurt, milk, or eaten directly at any time.

## Health Tips
This coffee-flavored granola uses egg white instead of oil, making it low-carb and high-protein. The combination with nuts provides high-quality fats, making it suitable for consumption before or after workouts or as a sugar-controlled breakfast. Note that maple syrup itself contains sugar; those with diabetes or on a weight-loss diet should control the portion size.
