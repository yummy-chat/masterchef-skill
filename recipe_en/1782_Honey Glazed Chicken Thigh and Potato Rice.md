# Honey Glazed Chicken Thigh and Potato Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken thigh meat, appropriate amount (cut into small pieces)
🥔 Potato, appropriate amount (cut into small pieces)
🍚 Rice, appropriate amount
🌱 White sesame seeds, appropriate amount (for garnish)
🧂 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount (for marinating), plus another 3 tablespoons (for the sauce)
🌿 White pepper powder, appropriate amount (for marinating)
🫗 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon
🍯 Honey, 1 tablespoon
🌰 Thirteen spices, 1 teaspoon
🥡 Cornstarch, 1 tablespoon
💧 Water, half a bowl

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥄 Wooden spoon
🔧 Spatula

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the chicken thigh meat into small pieces and place them in a bowl. Add light soy sauce and white pepper powder. Mix thoroughly by hand and marinate for 10 minutes. Cut the potato into evenly sized small pieces and set aside.
   **Note**: Mix thoroughly so the chicken is evenly coated with the seasonings. Ensure the marinating time is 10 minutes for proper flavor absorption. Cut the potato pieces as uniformly as possible to ensure even cooking when pan-frying.
2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Add the potato pieces and pan-fry over medium-low heat for about 5 minutes until the surface is golden brown. Remove from the pan, leaving the base oil in the pan.
3. Add the marinated chicken thigh pieces to the pan. Stir-fry until they are completely changed in color, flipping evenly during the process to prevent sticking to the bottom of the pan.
4. Pour in the pre-fried potato pieces. Add the pre-mixed sauce. Bring to a boil over high heat for 2 minutes, then stir-fry evenly until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients.
5. Remove the cooked honey glazed chicken thigh and potato from the pan. Mix with the rice. Sprinkle with white sesame seeds. Stir thoroughly until the rice absorbs the sauce, then serve.

## Health Advice
The combination of chicken thigh and potato provides protein and carbohydrates, but the sauce has a relatively high sugar content. It is recommended to control the amount of honey used or pair the dish with stir-fried vegetables to balance the nutrition. This dish is suitable for replenishing energy after exercise but is not recommended as a staple for weight loss periods.
