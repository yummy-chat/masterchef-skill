# Flowery Egg Breakfast Sandwich
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 English Muffins 2 pieces (cut in half)
🥚 Eggs 2 pieces
🧀 Cheese Slice 1 slice
🥫 Thousand Island Dressing appropriate amount
🥒 Pickle Relish appropriate amount
🫒 Cooking Oil appropriate amount (for frying eggs)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Frying Pan
📡 Microwave
🥄 Spatula
🔪 Small Knife

## Steps
1. Cut the English muffins in half, place them on a plate, and heat them in the microwave for a few tens of seconds until the muffins soften.
2. Pour an appropriate amount of cooking oil into a frying pan, heat over medium heat, and crack the eggs into the pan.
3. Once the bottom egg white solidifies and turns white, pour a small amount of water along the edge of the pan, immediately cover with a lid, and cook over medium heat for a few tens of seconds until the egg white is fully set and the yolk remains runny.
4. Carefully remove the runny fried eggs with a spatula.
5. Take out the heated muffins, place a slice of cheese on the bottom muffin, then top with the runny fried egg.
6. Spread Thousand Island dressing and pickle relish to taste, then cover with the top muffin.

## Health Advice
Runny eggs paired with cheese provide high protein, which can quickly replenish energy, suitable for those needing a rapid energy boost in the morning. Since the yolk is not fully set, pregnant women and those with weakened immune systems are advised to cook the eggs until fully done. Overall calorie count is controllable, but Thousand Island dressing and cheese slices are high in sodium, so the amount of seasoning can be reduced as needed.
