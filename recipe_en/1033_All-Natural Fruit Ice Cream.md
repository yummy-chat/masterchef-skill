# All-Natural Fruit Ice Cream
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍌 Banana: to taste (peeled, chopped, and frozen)  
🫐 Blueberry: to taste (washed and frozen)  
🥭 Mango: to taste (peeled, pitted, chopped, and frozen)  
🥝 Kiwi: to taste (peeled, chopped, and frozen)  
🍍 Pineapple: to taste (chopped and frozen)  
🍈 Durian: to taste (frozen)  
🍋 Lime: 1 (juice and zest)  
🍇 Passion Fruit: 1 (pulp)  
🍒 Lychee: to taste (pitted and frozen)  
🌹 Edible Rose Petals: to taste  
🌿 Chinese Yam: to taste (steamed and frozen)  
🥛 Yogurt: to taste (optional)  
🍫 Cocoa Powder: 1–2 tbsp

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Knife, cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel storage containers  
❄️ Refrigerator  
🥤 Small food processor  
🥄 Spoon  
🍴 Fork  
🧴 Zester

## Instructions

### Preparation Phase
1. Pre-process the chosen fruits: peel and chop bananas; peel, pit, and chop mango, kiwi, and pineapple; steam Chinese yam, let cool, then cut into segments; peel and pit lychee; wash blueberries; cut open passion fruit and scoop out pulp; zest and juice the lime and set aside.  
   **Key Point**: All fruits used for blending must be frozen. Banana and Chinese yam are the core ingredients for achieving a creamy texture.
2. Portion the processed fruits into separate stainless steel containers.  
   **Note**: Freeze separately to prevent flavor mixing.
3. Place all containers in the freezer and freeze overnight until completely solid.

### Production Phase
1. Remove the desired frozen fruits without thawing. Place the frozen base along with one type of frozen fruit or seasoning into the food processor blender cup. Recommended combinations include:  
   - Blueberry flavor: frozen banana + frozen blueberry  
   - Mango passion fruit flavor: frozen mango + frozen Chinese yam + passion fruit pulp  
   - Durian flavor: frozen durian + frozen banana  
   - Lime flavor: frozen banana + lime juice + lime zest  
   - Rose lychee flavor: frozen lychee + frozen Chinese yam + rose petals  
   - Kiwi flavor: frozen kiwi + frozen Chinese yam  
   - Chocolate flavor: frozen banana + cocoa powder  
   **Note**: Do not overfill the blender cup, as it may affect blending.
2. Cover with the lid and start blending. For a manual food processor, pull the cord firmly; for an electric one, use short pulses. Blend until all ingredients are fully combined, uniform in color, and smooth and thick in texture.
3. Scoop the ice cream into cups or bowls using a spoon. Use a fork to scrape vertical stripes on the surface for decoration, and garnish with fruit.
4. If the ice cream softens at room temperature, return it to the freezer briefly to firm up before serving.  
   **Key Point**: The finished product melts easily in hot weather; consume promptly or refreeze.

## Health Advice
Banana provides a creamy base, while Chinese yam can be used as a substitute to reduce sugar, making it suitable for those controlling sugar intake. Frozen fruits are rich in vitamins; it is recommended to portion them as needed to avoid flavor mixing.
