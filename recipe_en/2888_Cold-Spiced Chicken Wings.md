# Cold-Spiced Chicken Wings
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty Rating: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🧅 Scallions (适量, cut into sections)
🫚 Ginger (适量, sliced)
🧂 Light soy sauce (适量 for marinating, plus 2 tablespoons for sauce)
🧂 Dark soy sauce (适量 for marinating, plus half a tablespoon for sauce)
🦪 Oyster sauce (适量 for marinating, plus 1 tablespoon for sauce)
🍶 Cooking wine (适量)
🌶️ Chili powder (1 tablespoon)
🌿 Cumin (2 tablespoons)
🌶️ Dried chili shreds (适量)
🌸 Sichuan peppercorns (适量)
🫒 Cooking oil (适量)
⚪ White pepper powder (适量)
🍬 Sugar (1 tablespoon)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🫗 Stainless steel bowl
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥄 Slotted spoon
🧴 Strainer or oil-draining rack
🔗 Spatula

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the chicken wings in a bowl. Add scallion sections, ginger slices, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, cooking wine, and white pepper powder. Mix thoroughly by hand, ensuring each wing is evenly coated. Marinate for 15 minutes.
**Note**: Mix well to ensure every wing is evenly coated with seasonings for thorough flavor infusion.

2. Pour sufficient cooking oil into the pan and heat until the oil is hot enough. Add the marinated chicken wings, reduce to medium-low heat, and fry for about 7 minutes until both sides are golden brown and cooked through. Remove.
**Key point**: The oil must be sufficiently hot before adding the wings. Fry over medium-low heat to prevent burnt exteriors and undercooked interiors. Use a slotted spoon to remove and place on a strainer or oil-draining rack to remove excess oil.

3. Leave a small amount of oil in the pan. Add dried chili shreds and Sichuan peppercorns, stir-frying over medium-low heat until fragrant.

4. Mix light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, chili powder, and cumin into a sauce and set aside. Return the fried chicken wings to the pan. Increase the heat to high, pour in the sauce, and stir-fry quickly to evenly coat each wing.
**Note**: Stir-fry quickly after adding the sauce to prevent sticking.

## Health Advice
Chicken wings are rich in protein, but frying increases their calorie content. It is recommended to serve with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. Suitable for those who enjoy spicy flavors, but those with sensitive stomachs should consume in moderation.
