# Pan-fried Sea Bass
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🐟 1 sea bass (medium-sized)  
🧅 2 scallions (cut into sections)  
🫚 1 small piece of ginger (smashed or sliced)  
🍋 Half a lemon (sliced for marinating, squeeze juice before serving)  
🌿 A few mint leaves (for garnish and aroma)  
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed  
🧂 Salt, as needed (add a bit more for marinating)  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife  
🐟 Fish scaler  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
🥣 Bowl or plate (for mixing marinade and plating finished dish)  
🥄 Spatula  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Scrape off the fish scales, split the fish open from the backbone while keeping the belly connected, flatten the fish into a butterfly shape, carefully remove the innards and gills, and be careful not to break the gallbladder.  
2. Gently tap the main backbone of the fish with the back of the knife a few times to allow the flavors to penetrate better and to keep the fish stable during frying.  
3. In a bowl, combine ginger slices, scallion sections, lemon slices, cooking wine, a generous amount of salt, and a little water. Rub the mixture to release the juices from the scallion and ginger, then add the prepared fish and marinate for 20 minutes.  
4. Heat the wok over high heat, pour in the cooking oil, and swirl to coat the surface evenly. Pour out the excess hot oil.  
5. Place the marinated fish in the pan and fry over medium heat. Do not rush to flip it. Once the bottom skin has set and turned firm, carefully flip the fish with a spatula.  
6. Flip the fish 3–5 times, frying until both sides are golden brown and crispy. Remove from the pan and plate.  
7. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and squeeze lemon juice over the fish before serving.  

## Health Tips  
Sea bass is rich in high-quality protein and DHA, making it suitable for office workers needing brain nourishment and growing children. Use less oil when frying, and pair with lemon juice to cut the grease and enhance the freshness.
