# Hermès Hot Pot Milk Instant Noodles 🍜
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the hot pot base in a frying pan and heat over medium-low heat until the base is completely melted and fragrant.
2. Add equal amounts of milk and water, continue heating and stirring thoroughly to fully combine the base with the liquid, and bring to a boil until the soup base turns a reddish-orange color.
3. Use a fine-mesh strainer to remove the spice residue from the soup base, making the broth smoother.
4. Pour the strained soup base into a small pot, add ingredients that require longer cooking, such as hot pot meatballs, crab sticks, and sliced shiitake mushrooms, and cook over medium-low heat until softened.
5. Place the instant noodle cake in the center of the pot, gently press it down with chopsticks to submerge it in the soup, and cook over medium heat for 1 to 2 minutes until the noodle cake loosens.
   **Note**: Avoid excessive stirring to prevent the noodles from breaking.
6. Sprinkle in the washed leafy vegetables, continue cooking for 30 seconds to 1 minute, until the vegetables turn bright green and become tender.
7. Turn off the heat immediately once the vegetables soften to avoid overcooking, and serve.

## Health Tips
Replacing half the water with milk both neutralizes the spiciness of the hot pot base and makes the broth smoother, making it suitable for those who are sensitive to spice but want to try something new. While the combination of meatballs, crab sticks, vegetables, and instant noodles provides balanced nutrition, the sodium content is relatively high, so it is recommended to consume less soup and drink more water.
