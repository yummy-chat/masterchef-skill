# Baby Nutritious Mixed Rice
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍚 Rice (appropriate amount)
⭐ Star anise (4-5 pieces)
🪿 Foie gras (about 2 small pieces)
🎃 Pumpkin (about 2 slices)
🍲 Pheasant soup stock (about 1 frozen cube, for both broth and seasoning)
🍄 Mushroom (1 piece, stem removed, stuffed with shrimp paste)
🦐 Shrimp paste (appropriate amount)
🥚 Egg (1 piece)
🐟 Salmon (about 1-2 small pieces)
🥬 Celery (about 1 stalk)
🌿 Seaweed/seaweed flakes (appropriate amount)
🍅 Cherry tomatoes (appropriate amount)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Bowl (small bowl as container, large bowl as lid)
💧 Sink (for washing)
✂️ Kitchen scissors
♨️ Microwave
🥄 Spoon

## Cooking Steps
1.  Place rice and star anise together in a bowl, add water to rinse, then drain.
   **Key point**: The star anise can be rinsed and cooked together with the rice, allowing the rice to absorb its aroma.
2.  Remove the stem from the mushroom and fill the cap with shrimp paste; use kitchen scissors to cut the celery into small sections.
3.  Place the rinsed rice in the bowl.
4.  Layer all ingredients on top of the rice in order: foie gras pieces, pumpkin slices, frozen pheasant soup cube, mushroom stuffed with shrimp paste, cherry tomatoes, a whole raw egg cracked directly in, and salmon pieces.
5.  Sprinkle in the celery sections and seaweed/seaweed flakes.
6.  Place a large bowl upside down over the small bowl to create a sealed space.
   **Purpose**: To utilize steam to cook the ingredients and prevent moisture loss.
7.  Place the whole setup in the microwave and heat on high for 7 minutes.
   **Note**: Adjust the cooking time as needed based on your microwave's power and the total amount of ingredients.
8.  Remove and uncover the large bowl, use a spoon to thoroughly mix all ingredients with the rice until it reaches a moist, risotto-like consistency, then serve.
   **Judgment standard**: All ingredients are fully cooked, and the rice has fully absorbed the broth and egg liquid.

## Health Tips
This dish combines foie gras, salmon, and shrimp paste, making it nutrient-rich and suitable for supplementing picky-eating babies with brain power and calcium. It is recommended to control the amount of star anise to avoid an overly strong aroma in the rice; microwave on high for 7 minutes to prevent the foie gras from overcooking.
