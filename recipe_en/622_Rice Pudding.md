# Rice Pudding
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-seasons meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Rice 1.5 bowls (leftover rice is better)  
🥛 Milk 3 bowls (measured using a common household rice bowl)  
🥛 Whipping cream 50g (optional, adds richer flavor)  
🍬 White sugar 40g  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Rice bowl (for measuring)  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🥄 Spatula or silicone scraper  
🌀 Blender or food processor  
🍨 Glass cup or bowl (for serving)  
❄️ Refrigerator  

## Steps  
1. Pour milk, rice, white sugar, and optional whipping cream into a non-stick pan. Stir thoroughly to combine.  
2. Heat over medium-low heat, stirring continuously. Cook for about 10 minutes until the mixture thickens into a porridge-like consistency.  
   **Note**: Stir constantly to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom and burning.  
3. Pour half of the cooked rice pudding into a blender and blend until smooth with no lumps.  
4. In a serving container, first spread a layer of the unblended rice pudding with grain texture, then cover it with the smooth rice pudding to form a double-layered structure.  
5. Refrigerate for 2 hours until chilled and set. Remove from the fridge and serve.  

## Health Suggestions  
Rice pudding made with leftover rice effectively utilizes ingredients. The combination of dairy and carbohydrates provides a quick energy boost. However, it is relatively high in sugar, so it is recommended to reduce the amount by half or use a sugar substitute, making it more suitable for those on a weight-loss or sugar-control diet. It tastes best when chilled, but those with sensitive stomachs should avoid consuming it too cold.
