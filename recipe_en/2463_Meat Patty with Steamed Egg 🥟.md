# Meat Patty with Steamed Egg 🥟  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Ground pork  
🥚 Eggs  
🍶 Cooking wine  
🧂 Light soy sauce  
🌶️ White pepper powder  
🫒 Sesame oil  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Bowl, plate  
🍳 Pot  
🥄 Spoon  

## Steps  
1. Place ground pork, cooking wine, light soy sauce, white pepper powder, 1 egg, and sesame oil in a bowl. Stir in one direction until evenly mixed.  
2. Spread the seasoned ground meat on a plate. Use a spoon to press a dent in the center, and crack the remaining egg into the dent.  
3. Fill the pot with water to 1/4 of its height. Bring to a boil over high heat, then place the plate inside. Cover and steam over medium heat for 15 minutes.  
4. When removing, be careful to avoid burns and handle with caution.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is rich in high-quality protein, making it suitable for those needing muscle gain or an energy boost. However, ground pork has a higher fat content, so it's recommended to reduce the proportion of fatty meat. Avoid over-steaming to prevent the egg from becoming tough.
