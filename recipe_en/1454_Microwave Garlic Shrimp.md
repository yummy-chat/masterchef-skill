# Microwave Garlic Shrimp  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🍽️ Plate (for covering the bowl)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Place the garlic cloves with skins into the microwave and heat on high for about 20 seconds. Remove and easily peel off the skins.  
   **Note**: Do not heat for too long; the skins should be soft and easy to peel.  
2. Put the peeled garlic cloves into a food processor and blend into minced garlic. Then rinse the minced garlic under running water and drain.  
   **Key point**: Rinsing the minced garlic removes bitterness, resulting in a fresher garlic flavor.  
3. Place the fresh shrimp in a bowl, add the green onion sections, ginger slices, cooking wine, and white pepper powder. Mix well and marinate to remove any fishy smell.  
4. Combine the marinated shrimp with half of the minced garlic, place them in a microwave-safe bowl, and cover the bowl’s opening with a plate.  
   **Key point**: Covering with a plate mimics the effect of "steaming," locking in moisture for tender and succulent shrimp.  
5. Microwave on high for 6 minutes. Remove once the shrimp are fully red and curled, and the garlic aroma is rich.  

## Health Tips  
This shrimp dish is high in protein and low in fat. Quick microwave cooking reduces oil fumes, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or seeking convenient nutrition. Note that rinsing the minced garlic to remove bitterness is a key step and should not be omitted.
