# Sweet and Sour Pickled Radish
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥕 White radish 1 jin (about 500g, washed and sliced)
🌶️ Green chili pepper appropriate amount (cut into sections)
🌶️ Red chili pepper appropriate amount (cut into sections)
🧄 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced)
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (sliced)
🍬 White sugar 1 liang (about 50g, for drawing out water)
🍬 White sugar about half a small spoon (for seasoning)
🍶 Light soy sauce 1 bowl
🍎 Rice vinegar 1 bowl

## Tools and Equipment
🥣 Glass large bowl
🫙 Glass pickling jar
🧤 Disposable gloves

## Instructions
1. Wash the white radish and slice into pieces about 0.5 cm thick.
2. Place the radish slices into a glass large bowl. Add white sugar at a ratio of 50g sugar per 500g radish. Use your hands to mix thoroughly, then let sit for 30 minutes to draw out excess water.
   **Note**: This step is key to ensuring a crisp texture.
3. Squeeze the pickled radish slices firmly to remove water. **Do not rinse with water**, simply squeeze dry, as this extends the shelf life.
4. Place the squeezed radish slices into a clean glass pickling jar.
5. Add in order: about half a small spoon of white sugar, green and red chili pepper sections, ginger slices, and garlic slices.
6. Pour in one bowl of light soy sauce and one bowl of rice vinegar.
7. Cover the jar with its lid and fill the rim channel with water to create a water seal, blocking air and forming an anaerobic fermentation environment.
8. Place the sealed pickling jar in a cool, ventilated area. Pickle overnight before consuming.

## Health Advice
This sweet and sour pickled radish is low in calories and rich in dietary fiber, making it suitable for those who want to curb cravings while managing weight. Use sufficient white sugar when drawing out water to ensure a crisp texture.
