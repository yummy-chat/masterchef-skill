# Miaoxi Baked Milk
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 2 Egg yolks
🍬 3 tbsp White sugar (based on the wooden spoon in the video)
🌽 8 tbsp Corn starch (based on the same wooden spoon)
🥛 500ml Milk
🧀 2 slices Cheese
🥚 Appropriate amount Whole egg liquid (for brushing the surface before baking)

## Cookware & Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🥄 Wooden spoon
🥢 Chopsticks
🍳 Flat-bottomed pan
🔪 Knife
📦 Baking tray
📜 Parchment paper
🖌️ Brush
🧊 Mold or bowl (for chilling and setting)
🔥 Oven

## Steps
1. Place the egg yolks, white sugar, and corn starch in a bowl. First, add a small amount of milk and mix until smooth and lump-free, then pour in the remaining milk and stir thoroughly to form a smooth liquid.
   **Note**: Adding milk in batches and stirring thoroughly is key to preventing starch lumps.
2. Pour the mixture into a flat-bottomed pan, add the cheese slices, and heat over medium-low heat while stirring slowly and continuously. When the liquid thickens, clumps together, and forms a smooth dough-like mass, immediately turn off the heat and use residual heat to continue mixing evenly.
   **Note**: Continuous stirring throughout the process is essential to prevent burning on the bottom.
3. Pour the milk paste into a mold or bowl, smooth the surface with a spoon, gently tap to remove air bubbles, and refrigerate for about 3 hours until fully set into a solid milk cake.
4. Remove the set milk cake from the mold, place it on a baking tray lined with parchment paper, and cut into evenly sized triangular pieces with a knife.
   **Note**: Cut quickly or slightly heat the knife for cleaner edges.
5. Brush the surface of the milk cake pieces evenly with whole egg liquid. Preheat the oven to 190°C (top and bottom heat) for 10 minutes, then bake in the middle rack for 20 minutes until caramel-colored spots appear on the surface.
   **Note**: Brushing with whole egg liquid is key for browning; baking temperature and time should be adjusted based on your specific oven.

## Health Tips
This baked milk is made with egg yolks, cheese slices, and milk, with a texture similar to solid milk tea. It is suitable for dessert enthusiasts who enjoy novelty and are not averse to a bit of effort. Be mindful of sugar intake; for diabetics or those on a weight loss diet, it is recommended to halve the sugar amount.
