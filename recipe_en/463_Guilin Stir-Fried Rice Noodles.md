# Guilin Stir-Fried Rice Noodles
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍜 Rice noodles (flat rice noodles) – about 2 servings  
🥩 Lean pork – about 150g (partially ground into minced meat)  
🥚 Eggs – 2  
🥬 Lettuce – 2 handfuls (or other greens, washed and drained)  
🧅 Scallions – to taste (chopped)  
🥒 Pickled bamboo shoots – to taste (cut into small sections or minced)  
🫘 Pickled long beans – to taste (cut into small sections)  
🌶️ Pickled chili peppers – to taste (cut into small sections)  
🫒 Peanut oil – to taste  
🧂 Salt – a pinch (for seasoning the meat and stir-frying)  
🍾 Light soy sauce – to taste  
🍾 Dark soy sauce – to taste  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Meat grinder (to grind the lean pork into minced meat)  
🍳 Wok (or frying pan)  
🥄 Spatula  

## Instructions  
1. Grind the lean pork into minced meat using a meat grinder, add a pinch of salt, mix well, and marinate. Cut the pickled bamboo shoots, pickled long beans, and pickled chili peppers into small sections or mince them. Chop the scallions into small pieces. Wash the greens and drain excess water.  
2. Pour peanut oil into the wok and heat it up. Crack the eggs directly into the wok, quickly stir and scramble them with a spatula until set, then remove them or push them to the side of the wok for later use.  
3. Add a little more oil to the wok, add the minced meat and stir-fry until it breaks apart and changes color. Then add the pickled bamboo shoots, pickled long beans, and pickled chili peppers, stir-frying until aromatic. Next, add the greens and stir-fry until just cooked.  
4. Add the rice noodles to the wok, drizzle with light soy sauce and dark soy sauce, sprinkle with a little salt and the chopped scallions. Stir-fry quickly over high heat, ensuring each noodle is evenly coated with the sauce and color. Stir-fry until the noodles are dry and there is no excess liquid in the wok, then remove from heat.  
   - **Note**: Maintain high heat throughout the stir-frying process to prevent the noodles from sticking to the wok or releasing water. Dark soy sauce is mainly used for coloring, so do not use too much, or the color will be too dark.  

## Health Tips  
This Guilin stir-fried rice noodle dish balances meat and vegetables well, with a sour and spicy flavor that can stimulate the appetite, especially when you have little desire to eat. However, rice noodles and oil are relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to pair this dish with a light soup or fruit to control the total calorie intake per meal.
