# Egg Dumplings  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 18 eggs  
🌾 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour  
🌽 1 tablespoon cornstarch  
🥩 Ground meat filling (made from ground pork, grated carrots, minced mushrooms, etc., to taste)  
🧂 Salt, to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Electric griddle (or non-stick pan)  
🥄 Strainer  
🥄 Soup spoon  
🥣 Steamer  
🧊 Airtight container  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Crack the 18 eggs into a bowl, add 1 tablespoon of flour and 1 tablespoon of cornstarch, and whisk thoroughly with chopsticks until the egg mixture is smooth and free of lumps.  
   **Key point**: Adding flour and starch strengthens the egg wrapper and prevents it from tearing when spreading.  
2. Strain the egg mixture through a strainer into another bowl to remove bubbles and any unwhisked egg white clumps, ensuring a smooth and fine wrapper.  
3. Add salt to the ground meat filling and stir to season. To enhance flavor, you may add soy sauce, sesame oil, or other seasonings to taste; this is the basic seasoning.  
4. Preheat the electric griddle. Use a soup spoon to scoop half a spoonful of the egg mixture onto the center of the griddle. Immediately press gently with the back of the spoon and swirl in a circular motion to spread the mixture into a thin, round wrapper. Once the surface is set and the edges slightly lift, peel off the wrapper.  
   **Note**: Work quickly; remove the wrapper as soon as the egg mixture sets, or it will dry and harden.  
5. Take one egg wrapper, place an appropriate amount of meat filling in the center, fold the wrapper in half, and pinch the edges closed with your thumb and index finger to form an open-mouthed dumpling, leaving some filling exposed.  
6. Lightly brush the steamer rack with oil to prevent sticking. Arrange the wrapped dumplings neatly in the steamer. Once the water is boiling, steam over high heat for 20 minutes.  
7. After steaming, the dumplings are ready to eat. Cool any leftover dumplings, place them in an airtight container, and freeze. To serve, steam directly from frozen without thawing.  

## Health Tips  
Eggs are rich in high-quality protein. Paired with meat filling and vegetables, this dish offers balanced nutrition, suitable for those looking to build muscle or increase iron intake. Note that using an electric griddle without oil reduces calories, but since 18 eggs yield a large batch, it is advisable to eat a few at a time.
