# Homestyle Barbecue
Cuisine: Northeastern Chinese
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Season: All seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Beef (500g, sliced thinly)
🥓 Pork belly (1 strip, sliced thickly)
🍗 Chicken wings (4 pieces, halved)
🦐 Shrimp (5-6 pieces)
🦑 Squid (2 pieces, cleaned and cut into pieces)
🥬 Lettuce (as needed, for lining and wrapping)
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms (as needed, scored)
🥒 Zucchini (as needed, sliced)
🍄 Enoki mushrooms (as needed)
🍎 Apple (as needed, finely chopped)
🧅 Onion (as needed, finely chopped)
🧅 Scallion (as needed, chopped)
🧂 Light soy sauce (as needed, generous amount)
🦪 Oyster sauce (as needed)
🧂 Freshly ground black pepper (as needed)
🫒 Sesame oil (as needed)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Large chef's knife
🪵 Grid-pattern wooden cutting board
🐻 Bear-shaped small cutting board
🧺 Nine-grid wooden serving box
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Ceramic bowl with handle (for mixing sauce)
🥄 Soup spoon
🍳 Electric double-sided grill pan
🥢 Meat tongs
🥢 Chopsticks

## Instructions
1. Rinse the beef, pork belly, chicken wings, shrimp, squid, and all vegetables separately under running water, then drain and set aside.
**Note**: Pork belly and squid require thorough rinsing; devein the shrimp and clean thoroughly.

2. Slice the pork belly into thick pieces, cut the squid into chunks, score the shiitake mushrooms, and slice the zucchini.
**Key point**: Pork belly should be of moderate thickness; too thin and it will burn easily, too thick and it won't cook through.

3. Prepare the barbecue sauce: In a bowl, combine the chopped apple, chopped onion, and chopped scallion. Add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and freshly ground black pepper. Finally, drizzle in sesame oil and stir well to combine.
**Key point**: The finer the chopped ingredients, the better they will meld and release flavor.

4. In a nine-grid wooden serving box, line the bottom with washed lettuce leaves. Then arrange the beef, pork belly, chicken wings, shrimp, squid, shiitake mushrooms, zucchini, and enoki mushrooms neatly in separate compartments.
**Note**: Ensure the lettuce leaves line the bottom to prevent the ingredients from directly contacting the serving box.

5. Bring the nine-grid platter and the prepared sauce to the table. Preheat the electric double-sided grill pan.
**Key point**: The grill pan must be fully preheated to ensure the ingredients are immediately heated upon contact.

6. Use the meat tongs to place the ingredients one by one onto the grill pan. Flip them at appropriate intervals and grill until the surface changes color, shrinks, and develops a charred brown color.
**Doneness indicator**: A sizzling sound and slightly charred edges indicate the ideal state.

7. Dip the grilled meat into the sauce, wrap it in a lettuce leaf, and enjoy. The texture is tender and flavorful without being greasy.

## Health Tips
Beef and pork belly are rich in high-quality protein and iron. Wrapping them in lettuce leaves helps effectively reduce greasiness, making this suitable for those managing sugar intake and fitness enthusiasts. Note that shrimp and squid should not be grilled for too long to prevent the meat from becoming tough. Adding chopped apple to the sauce naturally enhances freshness, thereby reducing the need for salt.
