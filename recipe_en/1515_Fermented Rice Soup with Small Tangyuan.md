# Fermented Rice Soup with Small Tangyuan  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

---

🍚 **Ingredients**  
- Small tangyuan (glutinous rice balls)  
- Fermented rice (laozao)  
- White sugar  
- Goji berries (optional)  

🧂 **Seasonings**  
- White sugar  

🍳 **Cookware**  
- Pot  

---

📝 **Steps**  
1. Fill the pot with water and bring to a boil over high heat.  
2. Add the small tangyuan, and cook over medium heat for about 8 minutes, until the tangyuan float and are cooked through.  
3. Add the fermented rice and goji berries, and continue cooking for about 2 minutes to blend the flavors.  
4. Ladle the cooked tangyuan and soup into a bowl, adjust the sweetness with white sugar to taste, stir well, and serve.  

⚠️ **Notes**  
- Adjust the amount of white sugar according to personal taste.  
- For a thicker consistency, reduce the water volume by up to 100 ml.  
- If you prefer softer goji berries, add them together with the tangyuan at the start of cooking.

## Health Tips  
Fermented rice soup with small tangyuan is easy to digest and absorb, making it suitable for those with poor digestion or a cold constitution. Diabetic patients should reduce the amount of white sugar or use a sugar substitute. Be mindful of overall sugar intake and consume in moderation.
