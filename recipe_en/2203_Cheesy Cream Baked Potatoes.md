# Cheesy Cream Baked Potatoes
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potato 600g (peeled and sliced, about 2mm thick)
🧄 Garlic 2 cloves (core removed, one clove minced, one clove kept whole)
🥛 Milk 200ml
🥛 Heavy Cream 200ml
🧀 Mozzarella Cheese appropriate amount (adjust according to preference)
🧂 Black Pepper appropriate amount
🧂 Sea Salt appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Vegetable Peeler
🔪 Knife
🪓 Cutting Board
🥘 Pot
🥄 Slotted Spoon
🍲 Baking Dish
🔥 Oven
🥢 Chopsticks

## Cooking Steps
1. Peel the potatoes and slice them into pieces about 2mm thick.
   **Note**: The slices must be thin and even to cook through quickly during boiling; if too thick, they will not cook easily.
2. Remove the green inner core from the garlic. Mince one clove, and keep the other clove whole.
   **Note**: Removing the garlic core prevents a coarse texture.
3. In a pot over unlit heat, add the milk, heavy cream, the whole garlic clove, and the minced garlic.
   **Note**: Use small bottles of heavy cream to avoid waste; add all ingredients while the heat is off.
4. Add the potato slices, and season with an appropriate amount of black pepper and sea salt.
5. Turn on low heat and heat the mixture. Turn off the heat immediately once it comes to a boil.
   **Note**: Cook slowly over low heat to allow the potatoes to fully absorb the liquid; turn off the heat immediately after boiling to prevent overcooking.
6. Use a slotted spoon to remove the potato slices and place them into a baking dish. Pour all the liquid from the pot over the potatoes. Use chopsticks to arrange the slices so the center is slightly higher than the edges.
   **Note**: Pour all the liquid into the dish to avoid waste.
7. Place the baking dish into an oven preheated to 150°C (300°F) and bake for 40 minutes.
   **Doneness Check**: The potatoes are fully infused, and the milky flavor is well integrated. Baking at a low temperature helps the potatoes absorb the liquid.
8. Remove the baking dish from the oven. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top according to personal preference.
   **Note**: Add more cheese if you like it, or omit it if you prefer.
9. Return the dish to the oven and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and forms a golden, crispy crust.
   **Doneness Check**: A crispy, hard crust forms on the cheese layer. Baking temperature and time can be adjusted according to the actual performance of your oven.

## Health Advice
This baked potato dish is rich in milky flavor and concentrated in calories, suitable as an occasional indulgent main dish or side. It is recommended to pair it with a portion of stir-fried vegetables or a salad to balance carbohydrates and fiber. Pay attention to controlling the amount of cheese used to avoid excessive sodium and saturated fat intake.
