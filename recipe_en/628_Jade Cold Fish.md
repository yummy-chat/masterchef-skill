# Jade Cold Fish
Origin: Shaanxi Cuisine
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April to August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Pea starch 1 jin (can be substituted with wheat flour, rice flour, or mixed grain flour for a smooth and translucent texture)
💧 Water 1 jin (for soaking the starch)
🥬 Spinach juice 6 jin (strained to remove residue)
💧 Cool boiled water as needed (for cooling and setting)
🍵 Fermented vegetable broth about 1 spoonful (Flavor Option 1, already stir-fried)
🌶️ Chili rings as needed (Flavor Option 1)
🍯 Brown sugar water about 2 spoonfuls (Flavor Option 2)
🍒 Goji berries as needed (Flavor Option 2)
🧄 Minced garlic about 1 spoonful (Flavor Option 3)
🥣 Tangy seasoning sauce about 1 spoonful (Flavor Option 3)
🌶️ Chili oil about 1 spoonful (Flavor Option 3)

## Utensils & Equipment
🥣 Bowls (multiple)
🍲 Cooking pot
🥄 Wooden spoon
🕳️ Slotted spoon (medium holes)
🪣 Stainless steel basin

## Instructions

### Preparation Stage
1. Soak the starch: Mix 1 jin of pea starch with 1 jin of water, stir evenly, and let it sit to soak.
**Key Point**: The ratio must be accurate.

2. Prepare the spinach juice: Strain the spinach juice to remove any residue, and set aside 6 jin.
**Key Point**: Strain thoroughly to avoid impurities affecting the texture.

### Cooking Stage
1. Cook the spinach juice: Pour 6 jin of spinach juice into the pot and bring to a boil over high heat.
**Key Point**: Immediately reduce to the lowest heat once it boils, keeping it at a gentle simmer. Do not let it boil vigorously. Heat control is crucial throughout; low heat prevents the batter from clumping prematurely or becoming too thick due to excessive evaporation.

2. Mix in the starch water: Stir the soaked starch water again, then slowly pour it into the pot.
**Key Point**: While pouring the starch water, use the other hand to stir quickly and continuously with a wooden spoon along the bottom of the pot.

3. Stir until firm: Continue stirring quickly and vigorously until the batter becomes smooth, fine, and glossy, with a thick, flowing consistency.
**Key Point**: This is the core step to ensure evenness and smoothness of the cold fish. Make sure the starch and spinach juice are fully blended.

4. Steam with residual heat: Turn off the heat, immediately cover the pot with a lid, and let the residual heat steam the batter for a moment to ensure it is fully cooked through.
**Key Point**: Steam for about 2-3 minutes; do not exceed this time.

5. Form the fish and cool: Prepare cool boiled water in a stainless steel basin. Fill the slotted spoon with the hot batter and let it drip naturally into the cool water to set into "fish" shapes.
**Key Point**: Fill the slotted spoon completely with batter; gently tap the edge of the spoon to speed up dripping if needed.

6. Season and serve: Remove the set jade cold fish from the water and place in bowls. Choose one of the following flavor options:
   - Fermented vegetable broth flavor: Pour over the fermented vegetable broth and sprinkle with chili rings.
   - Sweet flavor: Pour over brown sugar water and garnish with goji berries.
   - Garlic flavor: Layer minced garlic as a base, pour over the tangy seasoning sauce, and finish with a drizzle of chili oil.

## Health Tips
Jade cold fish is rich in dietary fiber and vitamins from spinach, with low calories, making it suitable for cooling off in summer. Pay attention to heat control and stirring during preparation to ensure a smooth and tender texture. Choose from flavors like fermented vegetable broth, sweet, or garlic to suit diverse preferences.
