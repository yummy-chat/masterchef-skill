# Cinnamon Apple Pie
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🧈 80g softened unsalted butter  
🍬 25g granulated sugar  
🧂 1.5g salt  
🥛 60g room temperature milk  
🌾 200g cake flour  
🍎 Cinnamon apple filling, as needed (prepared in advance, main ingredients: apples and cinnamon)  
🥚 Egg wash, as needed (for brushing the surface)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Silicone spatula / whisk  
🕸️ Strainer  
📜 Parchment paper  
🪱 Rolling pin  
🥧 Fluted round pie dish  
📍 Pricker or fork  
🔪 Bench scraper  
🖌️ Brush  
🔥 Oven  
🥘 Baking sheet or rack  

## Steps
1. Place the softened unsalted butter, granulated sugar, and salt in a bowl. Mix and stir until well combined.
2. Gradually add the room temperature milk, whisking until fully incorporated.
   **Note**: Room temperature milk must be used; cold milk will re-harden the butter and affect the dough texture.
3. Sift in the cake flour. Use a spatula to fold until clumps form, then knead by hand into a smooth dough.
   **Key Point**: Fold with a spatula first before kneading by hand to reduce stickiness.
4. Wrap the dough and refrigerate to rest for about 1 hour.
5. Remove the rested dough. Take about 2/3 of the dough, place it on parchment paper, and roll it into a round sheet slightly larger than the pie dish.
6. Line the pie dish with the dough sheet. Gently press it with your fingers to make it fit the mold, and trim the edges neatly.
7. Use a fork or pricker to poke holes evenly across the bottom crust to prevent puffing during baking.
8. Evenly fill in the pre-prepared cinnamon apple filling, covering the bottom completely.
9. Roll out the remaining 1/3 of the dough and use a bench scraper to cut it into evenly wide strips.
10. Over the filling, weave the dough strips into a lattice pattern by alternating over and under placement. Trim any excess strips and press the edges to seal.
11. Brush a thin, even layer of egg wash over the lattice crust.
12. Preheat the oven. Bake the pie at 200°C for 15 minutes, then reduce the temperature to 185°C and bake for another 15 minutes, until the surface is a beautiful golden brown.

## Health Advice
This cinnamon apple pie is rich in dietary fiber and antioxidants, making it a suitable energy dessert for autumn and winter. Sugar intake should be controlled; for diabetics or those on a weight-loss diet, it is recommended to serve with unsweetened tea.
