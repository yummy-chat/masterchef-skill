# Char-Grilled Chicken Leg Burger
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Preparation Steps
1. Make the bun dough: Add the starter ingredients and main dough ingredients to the stand mixer in order, and knead until a smooth dough forms.
   **Note**: The char-grilled bun dough needs to be shaped into a long oval.

2. Shape and proof the bun: Shape the dough into a long oval, brush the surface with water, sprinkle with crushed corn grits, and make a shallow cut on the surface with a blade. Perform the final proofing.

3. Prepare the chicken leg: Debone the chicken leg, keeping the skin intact and covering the meat. Use a fork to evenly pierce holes in the skin surface.
   **Key point**: Piercing holes helps with flavor absorption and promotes fat drainage.

4. Initial marination: Place the chicken skin-side up, sprinkle with 20g of white sugar, and let sit for 15 minutes. Then rinse clean with water and pat dry.

5. Prepare the marinade and marinate: Mix all chicken marinade ingredients into a sauce. Place the chicken together with the sauce in a cooking bag, seal, and refrigerate overnight for marination.

6. Prepare the grill sauce: Mix together mayonnaise, mustard seed paste, white vinegar, cheese powder, pickled cucumber cubes, corn syrup, original seasoning, and coarsely ground black pepper. Set aside.

7. Bake the bun: Preheat the oven to 220°C top heat, 190°C bottom heat. Place the proofed bun in the oven and bake for 10 minutes. After removing, slice it horizontally. Place the cut side down in a hot frying pan and toast until golden brown.

8. Pan-fry the chicken leg without oil: Do not add oil to the frying pan. Place the chicken leg skin-side down, and cook over medium-high heat until the skin is golden brown and fat renders. Flip, reduce to medium-low heat, and continue cooking for 3 minutes. Pour in all the reserved marinade liquid, cover the pan, and simmer over medium heat for 2 minutes.
   **Indicator**: The skin is golden brown, and the chicken is fully cooked.

9. Oven-bake the chicken leg: Transfer the chicken leg to a baking tray lined with aluminum foil. Brush the surface with the pan juices. Place in a preheated oven at 210°C and bake for 1 minute. Remove, brush with more pan juices, and repeat for another minute.
   **Key point**: Repeatedly brushing with juices and baking gives a glossy red color.

10. Assemble the burger: Spread the grill sauce evenly on the cut surface of the bottom bun. Place the chicken leg on top, add lettuce, and cover with the top bun.

## Health Tips
This char-grilled chicken leg burger uses a homemade bun with reduced oil and sugar. The chicken leg is prepared by piercing holes to release fat and pan-frying without oil, lowering the calorie content. It is suitable for fitness cravings or parent-child cooking. Note that the marinade contains sugar and the sauce is slightly salty; individuals with high blood pressure can reduce the amount of marinade used.
