# Lamb Skewers on Red Willow Branches
Cuisine: Xinjiang Cuisine
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Lamb leg meat 800g (cut into cubes of about 3 cm)
🥩 Lamb tail fat 200g (cut into pieces)
🧅 Onion 80g
🥚 Whole egg liquid 50g
💧 Water 40g
🫒 Seasoned oil 20g
🧂 Compound seasoning powder (mixed salt, sugar, white pepper, yeast extract, curry powder, etc.) to taste
🌶️ Cumin powder to taste
🌶️ Chili powder to taste

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Knife and cutting board
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🧤 Disposable gloves
🍢 Red willow branches (35 cm long, about 6 mm thick)
🔥 Charcoal grill
🖌️ Brush
🧊 Plastic wrap

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the lamb leg meat and lamb tail fat into cubes of about 3 cm.
2. Prepare the compound seasoning powder: Mix salt, sugar, white pepper, yeast extract, curry powder, etc., in proportion until evenly combined.
3. In a stainless steel bowl, place the lamb cubes and lamb tail fat, then add the compound seasoning powder, whole egg liquid, water, seasoned oil, and onion.
4. Wearing gloves, mix everything thoroughly with your hands until the seasoning liquid is fully absorbed by the meat cubes and the surface becomes sticky.
5. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours to marinate.
6. Thread the marinated lamb and lamb tail fat alternately onto the red willow branches, ensuring a balanced mix of lean and fat.
7. Light the charcoal and grill over medium-low heat. During grilling, brush with seasoned oil to enhance flavor and form a protective layer, allowing the skewers to stick together for better taste.
8. Grill until small oil bubbles appear on the surface of the skewers, and a significant amount of fat from the lamb tail fat is released, forming an oil film. Sprinkle with cumin powder and chili powder, then remove from the grill.

## Health Tips
Lamb is rich in heme iron and high-quality protein, suitable for those needing iron supplementation and muscle building. However, lamb tail fat is high in calories, so it is recommended to pair with a vegetable salad for a balanced diet. Control the heat during grilling to avoid excessive production of polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons. Wrapping the skewers in aluminum foil can reduce carcinogen formation.
