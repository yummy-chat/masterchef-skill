# Blueberry Jam Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫐 Blueberries, about 150g (for making jam)
🥚 Eggs, 4 (separate egg whites and yolks)
🍬 White sugar, to taste (for egg whites, about 1 measure)
🌽 Corn oil, to taste (for egg yolks, about 2 measures)
🥛 Milk, to taste (for egg yolks, about 3 measures)
🌾 Cake flour, to taste (for egg yolks, about 5 measures)
🧊 Rock sugar, to taste (for making jam, adjust to taste)
🫐 Fresh blueberries, to taste (for decoration)
🍋 Lemon slices, to taste (for decoration)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍚 Rice cooker
🌀 Electric hand mixer
🥣 Mixing bowl
🥄 Soup spoon
🍴 Silicone spatula
🔪 Knife
🍽️ Plate

## Preparation Steps
1. **Make the blueberry jam**
   Place the blueberries and rock sugar into the rice cooker's inner pot. Select the "Cook Rice" program and heat until the blueberries are soft and the mixture thickens. Stir occasionally as needed. Remove and set aside once done.

2. **Prepare the cake batter**
   - Separate the egg whites and yolks. Place the egg whites in a clean, grease-free container, add 1 measure of white sugar, and chill in the freezer for about 10 minutes.
   - In a bowl with the egg yolks, add 2 measures of corn oil and 3 measures of milk. Whisk until well combined. Sift in 5 measures of cake flour and stir with a silicone spatula until smooth and no dry flour remains, forming the egg yolk batter.

3. **Whip the meringue**
   Remove the chilled egg whites. Use an electric hand mixer on high speed to beat until stiff peaks form—when the beaters are lifted, they should hold upright, sharp peaks.

4. **Combine the batters**
   Add about one-third of the meringue to the egg yolk batter. Fold gently with a spatula until combined. Then pour the mixture back into the remaining meringue. Fold and cut through quickly to mix evenly, avoiding deflation.

5. **Bake in the rice cooker**
   - Lightly grease the inner pot of the rice cooker with a thin layer of oil to prevent sticking.
   - Pour in half of the batter and spread it evenly. Spread a layer of blueberry jam in the middle, then cover with the remaining batter and smooth the surface.
   - Close the lid, select the "Cook Rice" program. After the program ends, let it rest for another 5 minutes. Then invert the cake to remove it and let it cool.

6. **Decorate**
   Slice the cake into rounds. Spread blueberry jam between the layers and over the top. Finally, decorate with fresh blueberries and lemon slices.

## Health Suggestions
Blueberries are rich in anthocyanins. Paired with eggs and milk to make this cake, it offers balanced nutrition and is suitable for breakfast or afternoon tea. Be mindful of sugar intake; diabetic patients and those on a weight-loss diet should consume in moderation.
