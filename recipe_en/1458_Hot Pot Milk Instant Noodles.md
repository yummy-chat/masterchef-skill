# Hot Pot Milk Instant Noodles  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍲 Hot pot base 1 piece (spicy flavor, used as a compound seasoning source)  
🥛 Milk 1 portion (mixed with water in a 1:1 ratio)  
💧 Water 1 portion (mixed with milk in a 1:1 ratio)  
🍡 Assorted hot pot meatballs to taste  
🦀 Crab sticks to taste  
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms 1-2 pieces (halved)  
🍜 Instant noodle cake 1 piece (use only the noodle cake, discard seasoning packets)  
🥬 Leafy greens (e.g., Shanghai bok choy) to taste (chopped)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🥘 Wok or milk pot  
🔥 Stove  
🥄 Strainer  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place the hot pot base in the pot and heat over low heat until fully melted.  
2. Add the milk and water, then bring to a boil over medium heat.  
   **Note**: The 1:1 ratio of milk to water is key to achieving the spicy and creamy flavor.  
3. Use a strainer to remove and discard the solid residue from the broth, keeping the smooth liquid.  
4. Add the hot pot meatballs, crab sticks, shiitake mushrooms, and other ingredients that require longer cooking. Cook over medium heat until fully done.  
5. Add the instant noodle cake and cook for 2-3 minutes according to the package instructions, until the noodles are separated and soft.  
6. Add the chopped leafy greens, cook until softened, then immediately turn off the heat.  
7. After turning off the heat, serve and enjoy.  

## Health Tips  
Pairing milk with hot pot base can neutralize spiciness and provide calcium. Suitable for those who are lactose tolerant as an occasional treat. It is recommended to limit meatball intake to avoid excessive sodium.
