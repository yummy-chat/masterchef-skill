# Microwave Caramel Pudding
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 1 egg
🥛 Milk, to taste
🍬 Sugar, to taste
🌿 Vanilla paste, optional

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🥣 Heat-resistant ceramic bowl
🧺 Strainer
♨️ Microwave oven

## Steps
1. Make the caramel: Heat sugar in a frying pan until it turns caramel color, then quickly pour it into the bottom of a heat-resistant bowl.
   **Key point**: The caramel must be fully cooled to a semi-set soft caramel state before pouring in the pudding mixture to prevent them from mixing.

2. Make the pudding mixture: Whisk the egg and milk together evenly. Add vanilla paste if desired.
   **Key point**: The pudding mixture must be strained to remove air bubbles and any unbroken egg whites, ensuring a smooth texture.

3. Gently pour the strained pudding mixture into the bowl over the cooled caramel.

4. Place in the microwave and heat on medium power.
   **Key point**: Watch the microwave closely during heating. Stop immediately when the surface of the pudding shows slight bubbling. The total time is about 3 minutes.
   **Note**: Do not use too high heat or heat for too long, as this can create air holes and affect the tender texture.

5. Remove and let the pudding cool completely.
   **Key point**: It must be fully cooled before unmolding, or the pudding will easily fall apart.

6. Run a small knife around the edge of the bowl, place a plate on top of the bowl, and quickly flip it over to unmold.

## Health Tips
Eggs and milk provide high-quality protein and calcium, but the caramel is high in sugar. Consider reducing the sugar or pairing with unsweetened yogurt for balance. Making it in the microwave saves time, but requires constant monitoring to avoid overheating and affecting the texture.
