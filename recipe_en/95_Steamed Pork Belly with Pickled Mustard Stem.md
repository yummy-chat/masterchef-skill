# Steamed Pork Belly with Pickled Mustard Stem
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Pork belly, appropriate amount (pre-frozen, sliced thinly)  
🥬 Mild pickled mustard stem, 1 small pack (about 80-100 grams)  
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (sliced or julienned)  
🧅 Scallion, appropriate amount (cut into sections or chopped)  
🦪 Oyster sauce, appropriate amount  
🍬 White sugar, a pinch  
🧂 White pepper, a pinch  
🍶 Cooking wine or rice wine, appropriate amount  
🥄 Light soy sauce, a few drops  
🥄 Dark soy sauce, a few drops  
🌾 Cornstarch, a little  
🥜 Peanut oil, a little  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🍽️ Small stainless steel dish  
🥘 Long plate  
♨️ Steamer  

## Instructions  
1. Freeze the pork belly overnight until firm, remove and slice thinly. Place slices in water to thaw. Once fully thawed, squeeze out excess water and transfer to a large bowl.  
2. Add the pickled mustard stem, ginger slices, and scallion sections. Then add oyster sauce, white sugar, white pepper, cooking wine, light soy sauce, and dark soy sauce in order.  
   **Note**: Both the pickled mustard stem and soy sauce are salty, so no extra salt is needed.  
3. Add a small amount of cornstarch and mix thoroughly by hand to evenly coat the meat slices. Then drizzle in a little peanut oil and mix again.  
4. Lay the marinated pork slices flat on a long plate, one by one, minimizing overlap to ensure even heating.  
5. Bring water in the steamer to a boil, place the plate inside, and steam over high heat for about 7 minutes.  
   **Key point**: If slices overlap, extend the steaming time as needed.  
6. Once steamed, remove from the steamer. Sprinkle with some fresh chopped scallion for garnish, and serve.  

## Health Tips  
Pork belly steamed with pickled mustard stem is high in fat and salt. It is recommended to pair with coarse grains or plenty of leafy greens. Those with high blood pressure should control their portion size.
