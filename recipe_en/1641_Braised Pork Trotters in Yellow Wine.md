## Cooking Steps  
1. **Prepare ingredients**: Slice the ginger, cut the scallions into sections, and soak the dried soybeans in boiling water for about 1 hour until rehydrated.  
2. **Blanch the trotters**: Wash the trotters, place them in a pot with cold water, and bring to a boil over high heat.  
   - Remove the foam that rises before the water boils promptly, otherwise it will stick to the trotters once cooled.  
   - No need to add scallions, ginger, or cooking wine; any odor will be dealt with in subsequent steps.  
3. **Caramelize the sugar**: In a cold wok, add half a tablespoon of water, half a tablespoon of oil, and a little over half a bowl of rock sugar. Heat over high heat, stirring clockwise in one direction until the sugar melts.  
   - When the caramel turns a date-red color and small mung-bean-sized bubbles appear on the surface, immediately turn off the heat and remove the wok from the burner.  
   - If the caramel drips off the spoon in a thread-like stream, it indicates the temperature has dropped. Then slowly pour in boiling water and stir to create caramelized sugar water.  
4. **Sauté and stir-fry**: In another wok, heat oil. First, stir-fry the ginger slices until fragrant, then add the scallion sections and stir-fry until aromatic. Next, add the blanched trotters and stir-fry over medium-high heat until the surface is slightly tightened.  
   - This step removes any gamey smell and gives the trotters a firmer, less greasy texture.  
5. **Season and braise**: Add the prepared caramelized sugar, half a catty of yellow wine, water, a small half bowl of light soy sauce, 3 tablespoons of salt,适量 oyster sauce, the cinnamon stick, and dried chili peppers. Stir well to combine.  
   - The caramelized sugar provides sufficient color, so no need to add dark soy sauce.  
6. **Add soybeans**: Pour in the rehydrated soybeans and mix everything evenly.  
7. **Pressure cook**: Transfer all ingredients to a pressure cooker, cover with the lid, and cook for 15 minutes after the steam builds up.  
8. **Serve**: Turn off the heat and allow the pressure cooker to cool naturally. Only open the lid after the red safety valve drops. Do not force the lid open. Transfer the dish to a clay pot and serve at the table.  

## Health Advice  
Pork trotters are rich in collagen, making them suitable for those looking to nourish the skin. However, they are high in fat, so it is recommended to pair them with light vegetables. Braising in yellow wine helps remove any odor and enhances flavor, but the salt and sugar content are relatively high; individuals with high blood pressure, high blood sugar, or high cholesterol should consume in moderation.
