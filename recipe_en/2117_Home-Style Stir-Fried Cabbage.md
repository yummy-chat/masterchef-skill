# Home-Style Stir-Fried Cabbage
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 Cabbage - to taste (shredded)  
🥕 Carrot - to taste (shredded)  
🧂 Salt - to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil - to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Frying pan  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  

## Cooking Steps
1. Shred the cabbage and carrot into even, thin strips.  
   **Key point**: The strips should be uniform in thickness to ensure even cooking.  
2. Place the frying pan over heat, pour in the cooking oil, and heat until it reaches 50-60% hot. Add the shredded cabbage and carrot, and stir-fry quickly.  
   **Note**: The oil temperature should not be too high to avoid burning.  
3. Continue stir-frying until the vegetables soften and are fully cooked. Add salt to taste according to personal preference, and stir-fry evenly.  
4. Remove from heat and serve on a plate.

## Health Tips  
This stir-fried cabbage is rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber. It is low in calories and high in satiety, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss or blood sugar control diet. Use minimal oil and stir-fry quickly to retain a crisp texture, and avoid prolonged heating to prevent nutrient loss.
