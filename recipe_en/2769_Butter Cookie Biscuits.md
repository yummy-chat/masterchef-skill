# Butter Cookie Biscuits  
**Cuisine:** Other  
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐  
**Seasonal Recommendation:** Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥛 Whipping cream – to taste  
🧈 Butter – to taste  
🍞 Cake flour – to taste  
🥚 Egg – 1 piece  
🍵 Matcha powder – to taste  
🍫 Cocoa powder – to taste  
🍒 Dried cranberries – to taste  
🌾 White sesame seeds – to taste  
🌿 Seaweed flakes – to taste  
🧂 Salt – to taste  
🍬 Granulated sugar – to taste  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔌 Electric hand mixer  
🥣 Stainless steel mixing bowl  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🍴 Cutting board  
🔥 Oven  

## Instructions  
1. Pour the whipping cream into a mixing bowl and beat with an electric hand mixer until thick and distinct lines form.  
2. Cut the softened butter into small pieces, combine with cake flour, and rub together with your hands until the mixture resembles coarse sand.  
3. Make a well in the center of the flour and butter mixture, crack in the egg, mix well, and knead into a dough.  
4. Divide the dough and knead in matcha powder, cocoa powder, dried cranberries, white sesame seeds, and seaweed flakes separately to create different flavored doughs. Shape each flavored dough into a rectangular or cylindrical log, refrigerate until firm, then slice into evenly thick rounds with a knife.  
5. Preheat the oven in advance. Arrange the sliced biscuits on a baking sheet and bake until the edges are set and the surface is lightly browned and toasted. Remove from the oven.  

## Health Advice  
These butter cookies are rich in high-quality fats and carbohydrates, making them suitable as energy-boosting snacks, but they are relatively high in calories. It is recommended to consume only 3 to 5 pieces per serving. Diabetic patients and those on a weight loss diet should control their portion size, and they can pair the cookies with unsweetened tea to balance the flavor.
