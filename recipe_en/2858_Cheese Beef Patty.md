# Cheese Beef Patty
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Ground beef, as needed  
🧀 Cheese slices, 1-2 slices  
🧅 Onion, a small amount (finely chopped)  
🫘 Sweet peas, as needed (side dish)  
🥬 Romaine lettuce, as needed (side dish)  
🍚 Rice, as needed (staple food pairing)  
🫒 Vegetable oil, as needed (for pan-frying)  
🧂 Black pepper sea salt, to taste  
🌾 Starch water, as needed  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🌀 Food processor  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🫓 Pan lid  
🥢 Tongs  
🪵 Wooden spatula  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🍲 Soup pot  
🍚 Rice cooker  
🥣 Bowl  

## Preparation Steps
1.  Place the ground beef, finely chopped onion, black pepper sea salt, and starch water into a food processor. Blend until the meat filling is smooth, sticky, and elastic.
   **Note**: Do not use too much onion; if concerned about the patty falling apart, you can manually slap the meat filling several times.
2.  Take an appropriate amount of meat filling, press it into a patty shape in your palm, place a cheese slice in the center, wrap it completely with the meat filling, and shape it into a round patty.
3.  Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, add an appropriate amount of vegetable oil, and place the patty in the pan to cook. Avoid moving it frequently after placing it; flip only after one side is set and browned.
4.  Cover the pan with a lid and continue to cook over medium heat. Covering prevents oil splatter and uses steam to help the thick patty cook evenly inside.
5.  Cook until both sides are golden brown, the inside is fully cooked, and the cheese is melted. You can use a wooden spatula to gently press the patty; it is done when it feels firm and springy.
6.  In a separate soup pot, bring water to a boil. Blanch the sweet peas and romaine lettuce until just tender, then remove with a slotted spoon and drain.
7.  Place rice in a bowl, arrange the cooked beef patty and blanched vegetables on top. Serve with your favorite sauce. Alternatively, the patty can be placed in a hamburger bun to make a burger.

## Health Advice  
Cheese beef patties are rich in high-quality protein and calcium, suitable for muscle building or calcium supplementation. Pair with vegetables to balance nutrition and use low-fat sauces sparingly. Control the heat during cooking to avoid high oil temperatures that could produce harmful substances.
