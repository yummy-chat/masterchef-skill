# Okonomiyaki  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥬 Cabbage 1/4 head (finely chopped)  
🧅 Green onion to taste (finely chopped)  
🍤 Large shrimp to taste (cut into small pieces)  
🥩 Pork belly slices 5 slices  
🧀 Cheese slices 2 slices  
🥚 Egg 1 whole  
🌾 Cake flour 100g  
💧 Water 100ml  
🥔 Yam 1 small piece (grated into paste)  

🧂 Sea salt to taste  
🧂 Black pepper to taste  
🍯 Japanese teriyaki sauce to taste  
🍶 Mayonnaise to taste  
🐟 Bonito flakes to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil a small amount (for frying)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Grater (for grating yam paste)  
🍳 Frying pan  
🍴 Silicone spatula  
🍽️ Plate (for assisting flipping)  
🖌️ Brush  

## Steps  
1. Finely chop the cabbage and green onion, cut the large shrimp into small pieces, peel the yam and grate it into paste using a grater.  
2. In a bowl, sift the cake flour, add water, and stir into a smooth batter.  
3. Add the yam paste, shrimp, chopped green onion, chopped cabbage, and the whole egg to the batter, and season with sea salt and black pepper.  
   **Key point**: Stir gently to avoid the vegetables releasing water, ensuring a fluffy result.  
4. Spray a small amount of cooking oil in a frying pan, pour in the mixed ingredients, do not press down, and gently shape into a round with a spatula.  
5. Evenly arrange the pork belly slices on top of the batter.  
   **Heat**: Fry over low heat for about 4 minutes, until the bottom is set and can be moved.  
6. Place a plate upside down on the pancake, quickly flip the pan so the pancake falls onto the plate, then slide the pancake back into the pan.  
7. Continue frying over low heat for about 4 minutes.  
8. Use the plate again to assist flipping, so the pork belly side faces up, and place the cheese slices on top.  
   **Heat**: Cover with a lid and simmer over low heat for about 3 minutes, until the cheese melts.  
9. Transfer to a plate, brush the surface with Japanese teriyaki sauce, squeeze mayonnaise on top, and finally sprinkle bonito flakes.  

## Health Advice  
This okonomiyaki uses yam paste and lightly cooked vegetables to maintain a fluffy texture; the vegetables and shrimp provide dietary fiber and high-quality protein, making it suitable as a staple for a balanced meal. Note that pork belly and cheese are relatively high in calories, so controlling portions is better for health.
