# Milk Curry Rice
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧅 Onion, to taste (minced)
🧄 Garlic, to taste (crushed and minced)
🍎 Apple, about 1/4 (peeled and cored, sliced thinly)
🥩 Ground meat, to taste (pork or beef均可)
🧂 Salt, 1 small spoon
🍛 Curry block, 1 piece
🥛 Milk, to taste (add until 80% full of ingredients)
🌶️ Chili powder or chili oil, to taste (for adding spiciness and color)
🌶️ Red curry, 1 small spoon (optional)
🌿 Cilantro, to taste (chopped, for final garnish)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Frying pan (non-stick)
🔪 Knife, cutting board
🥄 Spoon

## Cooking Steps
1. Mince the onion, crush and mince the garlic, peel and core the apple, then slice thinly.
2. Heat a small amount of oil in the pan, add the ground meat and stir-fry, add 1 small spoon of salt, cook until the meat changes color and develops a fragrant sear, then remove and set aside.
   **Note**: Adding salt promotes the Maillard reaction, giving the meat a more aromatic browned flavor.
3. Using the remaining oil in the pan, first stir-fry the minced onion and minced garlic until fragrant, then add one piece of curry block and stir-fry evenly with the base.
4. Return the cooked meat to the pan, add the apple slices, pour in milk until it covers about 80% of the ingredients, add chili powder and 1 small spoon of red curry to taste, stir well, and simmer for about ten minutes.
   **Note**: Oil separation may occur during simmering, which is normal.
5. After simmering, stir the curry to restore a thick and uniform consistency; if the sauce is too dry, add a little more milk as needed.
6. Pour the cooked curry over rice, sprinkle with chopped cilantro, and serve.

## Health Tips
The combination of milk and curry provides calcium and warms the stomach. The natural sweetness from apple slices reduces the need for added sugar, making it suitable for those managing sugar intake or building muscle. Be mindful to use less oil when stir-frying the ground meat to avoid excess calories.
