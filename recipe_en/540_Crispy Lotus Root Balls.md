# Crispy Lotus Root Balls
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥒 Lotus root 1000g (use 9-hole crispy lotus root)  
🧅 Chopped green onions 50g  
🫚 Minced ginger 20g  
🌾 Flour 100g  
🌽 Cornstarch 50g  
🧂 Table salt 9g  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Peeler  
🧇 Shredder  
🥣 Large bowl  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🍽️ Tray  

## Cooking Steps
1. Wash the lotus root thoroughly, peel off the skin, and shred it into fine strips using a shredder.
2. Add chopped green onions, minced ginger, table salt, flour, and cornstarch to the shredded lotus root. Wear gloves and mix thoroughly.
   **Key point**: Lotus root contains natural moisture and starch, so no additional water is needed. Mix until the mixture can be formed into balls without falling apart.
3. Pour cooking oil into the wok and heat to 50-60% hot. Take an appropriate amount of the shredded lotus root mixture, roll it into small balls, press firmly, and lower them into the oil.
4. Fry over medium-low heat. Once the balls are set, gently turn them with a slotted spoon. Continue frying for about 7-8 minutes.
   **Doneness check**: The surface turns golden brown, and the shell becomes firm and crispy.
5. Remove and drain excess oil, then plate and serve. The outside is crispy, the inside is soft and chewy, with a rich aroma of green onions.

## Health Tips
These crispy lotus root balls are rich in dietary fiber and carbohydrates, making them suitable for those who need an energy boost or wish to improve intestinal motility. However, since frying increases the calorie content, it is recommended to pair them with a vegetable salad to balance the meal.
