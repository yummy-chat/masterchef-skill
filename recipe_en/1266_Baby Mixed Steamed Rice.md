# Baby Mixed Steamed Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Rice approx. 1 bowl (for layering the base)
🐟 Salmon 2-3 slices
🥦 Broccoli approx. 5-6 small florets (used in two batches)
🟣 Purple rice balls approx. 2 small balls
🍖 Beef bone soup meatball 1 piece
🥕 Red-skinned radish approx. 1/4 piece (shredded)
🍄 Black fungus approx. 3-4 slices
🥚 Quail egg 1 piece
🥬 Baby bok choy 2-3 leaves
🍼 DHA supplement powder appropriate amount (added after steaming)
🥄 Dark sauce appropriate amount (e.g., meat sauce or soy sauce, added at the end)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 White ceramic steaming bowl
☁️ Steaming bowl lid with vents
♨️ Stainless steel steamer
🥢 Chopsticks

## Preparation Steps
1. Take a white ceramic steaming bowl and evenly spread a layer of white rice on the bottom of the bowl.
2. Arrange salmon slices, some broccoli florets, purple rice balls, and the beef bone soup meatball in separate sections on top of the rice.
   **Key Point**: Arranging in sections makes it easier to identify ingredients and ensures even heating.
3. Fill the gaps between ingredients with shredded red-skinned radish.
   **Key Point**: Adds texture and color.
4. Continue by adding black fungus slices and one whole quail egg.
5. Place the washed baby bok choy leaves along the rim of the bowl.
   **Key Point**: Leafy greens cook quickly; placing them by the bowl edge helps maintain their color and tenderness.
6. Cover with the vented steaming bowl lid, place it in the stainless steel steamer, and steam over high heat for 15–20 minutes until all ingredients are fully cooked.
   **Key Point**: Ensure raw ingredients like salmon and quail egg are thoroughly cooked; steam must be sufficient.
7. Remove the steaming bowl and be careful of steam burns when lifting the lid.
8. Sprinkle an appropriate amount of DHA supplement powder.
9. Drizzle with one spoonful of dark sauce; the amount and type can be adjusted according to taste.
10. Using chopsticks, thoroughly stir all ingredients and sauce in the bowl, ensuring the rice, side dishes, and sauce are evenly mixed and the color is uniform.
    **Key Point**: Stirring must be thorough to ensure every bite contains a variety of ingredients.

## Health Tips
Salmon, quail egg, and beef bone soup meatball provide high-quality protein and DHA, supporting baby's brain development; purple rice and black fungus increase dietary fiber. Note that steamed rice has a higher moisture content overall, so avoid feeding the baby overly watery food to prevent digestive issues.
