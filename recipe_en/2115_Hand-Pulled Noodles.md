# Hand-Pulled Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home-Cooked Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 All-purpose flour 500g
🥚 Egg 1
💧 Cold water approx. 220g (adjust according to flour absorbency)
🧂 Salt 5g (to strengthen the dough and prevent tearing)
⚪ Alkaline water (lye water) 1g (to increase elasticity and keep noodles from softening)
🌽 Cornstarch as needed (to use as dusting flour, prevent sticking, and make noodles more translucent)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🫙 Stainless steel bowl
🪹 Rolling pin
🔪 Kitchen knife
🪑 Work surface

## Preparation Steps
1. Pour the flour into a bowl, add the alkaline water and salt, and mix thoroughly by hand.
2. Crack in the egg and continue mixing evenly.
3. Add cold water in batches, first mixing into flocculent pieces, then kneading into a dough.
4. Knead repeatedly until the "Three Lights" (bowl, hands, and dough are all smooth), the dough is firm and smooth, and slightly firmer than bun dough.
**Note**: Knead thoroughly; otherwise, the dough structure will be loose and prone to tearing when rolled.
5. Cover the bowl tightly and let the dough rest for 15 minutes to relax the gluten, preventing it from shrinking during rolling.
6. Sprinkle cornstarch on the work surface, place the rested dough on it, and use the rolling pin to press it flat, flattening it into a round shape.
7. Sprinkle cornstarch on the dough sheet, roll out the edges first, turning the dough while rolling to stretch the edges out.
8. Roll the dough sheet onto the rolling pin, and apply even pressure from the center toward both ends with both hands.
**Key point**: Distribute the force evenly to ensure uniform thickness.
9. Unroll the dough sheet, adjust the rolling angle, sprinkle more cornstarch, and repeat the rolling process until the sheet is extremely thin and has expanded in area, lifting it to check for good elasticity without tearing.
10. Fold the large dough sheet like a fan into a strip about 10 cm wide, dusting cornstarch between each layer to prevent sticking.
11. Use a kitchen knife to cut the noodles to the desired width. After cutting, lift from the folded end and shake them apart.
**Quality check**: The noodles should be separate and distinct, not breaking easily when shaken or tapped.

## Health Advice
These hand-pulled noodles are high in carbohydrates and moderate in protein, making them suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. However, care should be taken to limit high-salt sauces to avoid excessive sodium intake. People with sensitive stomachs may reduce the amount of alkaline water and cook the noodles until softer for easier digestion.
