# Spicy Braised Duck Parts
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦆 Duck feet (as needed)
🦆 Duck tongues (as needed)
🦆 Duck wings (as needed)
🦆 Duck necks (as needed)
🦆 Duck intestines (as needed)
🌶️ Dried chili peppers (to taste, use a variety that is fragrant but not too spicy, adjust as needed)
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns (to taste)
🌿 Numbing peppercorns (to taste)
🌿 Braising spice mix (1 portion, includes star anise, cinnamon, bay leaves, angelica root, galangal, betel nut slices, black cardamom, white cardamom, amomum tsaoko, sand ginger, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, white peppercorns, etc., total cost about 12 yuan)
🧂 Salt (approx. 30g)
🍬 Rock sugar (approx. 40g)
🧂 MSG (approx. 10g)
🧂 White pepper powder (approx. 10g)
🥄 Light soy sauce (1 tbsp)
🥄 Rib sauce (2 tbsp, optional)
🎨 Red yeast rice powder (a pinch, for coloring, optional)
🍶 High-proof liquor (to taste)
🧅 Scallions (to taste, smashed)
🧄 Ginger (to taste, smashed)
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns (for blanching, to taste)
🫘 Pixian broad bean paste (2 tbsp)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🪣 Stainless steel basin
🍲 Large pot
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Soup ladle
🥄 Slotted spoon
✂️ Scissors
🔥 Gas stove

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare Duck Parts**: Trim the claws from the duck feet and scrape off the black membrane on the pads. For duck intestines, separately add flour, salt, and white vinegar, rub thoroughly, and rinse until the water runs clear. Place duck feet, duck tongues, duck wings, duck necks, and duck intestines in a basin, add clean water, and soak for 1 hour to draw out blood.
2. **Blanch Duck Parts**: Add duck feet, duck tongues, duck wings, and duck necks to a pot of cold water. Add smashed scallions and ginger, Sichuan peppercorns, and high-proof liquor. Bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, and continue boiling for 5 minutes. Remove and rinse clean.
   **Note**: Duck intestines must be blanched separately to avoid overcooking and becoming tough.
3. **Prepare Spices**: Soak all dry spices in clean water for half an hour, then drain and set aside.
4. **Cook the Base**: Heat oil in a wok over low heat. When the oil reaches about 30% hot (just warm), add the drained spices and fry for 5 minutes. Add 2 tablespoons of Pixian broad bean paste and stir-fry over low heat until the red oil is released. Then add Sichuan peppercorns, numbing peppercorns, and dried chili peppers. Continue stir-frying for about 5 minutes over low heat until fragrant and numbing.
5. **Prepare the Braising Liquid**: Transfer the cooked spice base to a large braising pot. Add enough clean water. Season with salt, rock sugar, MSG, white pepper powder, light soy sauce, rib sauce, and red yeast rice powder. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for half an hour to fully extract the spice flavors.
6. **Braise in Batches**: First, add duck feet, duck wings, and duck necks. Braise over low heat for half an hour. Then add duck tongues and continue braising for 15 minutes. Finally, add duck intestines and braise for 5 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Different duck parts are added in batches according to their texture to ensure even doneness.
7. **Soak for Flavor**: After turning off the heat, do not remove the duck parts. Let them soak in the braising liquid for another 2 hours to allow the flavors to fully penetrate.

## Health Suggestions
Duck parts are rich in collagen, but the braising liquid is high in salt. It is recommended to pair them with light vegetables. People with high blood pressure should consume in moderation. This dish is suitable for gatherings and as an accompaniment to alcohol.
