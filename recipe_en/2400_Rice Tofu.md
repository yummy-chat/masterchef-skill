# Rice Tofu
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Early season rice 500g (or aged rice, even cheap rice that is not good for cooking)
💧 Water 1000ml (for soaking)
💧 Water 2500ml (for boiling)
🧪 Edible alkali 10g
🧂 Salt about 10-15g (1 tablespoon)
🍶 Light soy sauce to taste
🍇 Fragrant vinegar to taste
🌶️ Chili oil to taste
🧅 Chopped scallions to taste

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🍶 Blender
🍳 Large pot
🥄 Silicone spatula or spoon
🧊 Square plastic food storage container
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife

## Steps
1. Place the rice in a basin, add water and wash repeatedly until the grains break, rinsing off excess surface starch, then drain the washing water. Add 1000ml of water and soak for at least 4 hours, until the grains are fully hydrated and can be easily crushed by hand.
   **Key point**: Thorough soaking softens the rice, aiding subsequent grinding.

2. Pour the soaked rice along with the soaking water into a blender, and blend at high speed into a fine rice paste, as fine as possible.

3. Add 10g of edible alkali to the rice paste, stirring thoroughly until fully dissolved.

4. Pour 2500ml of water into a large pot and heat until small bubbles appear on the surface. Slowly pour the rice paste with alkali into the pot, maintaining medium-low heat throughout.
   **Key point**: Immediately stir continuously after adding the rice paste to prevent sticking to the bottom.

5. Stir continuously for about 5 minutes; the rice paste will gradually thicken. Reduce to low heat and continue to cook for about 10 minutes, constantly scraping the bottom of the pot to avoid sticking.
   **Texture check**: When lifted with a spoon, the rice paste should fall in continuous large sheets, not in drops or threads.

6. Add 1 tablespoon of salt to the cooked rice paste, stir quickly to combine, then cook for another 5 minutes before removing from heat.

7. Quickly pour the rice paste into a clean square plastic food storage container, smooth the surface with a spoon, and let it cool naturally at room temperature until fully set.

8. Unmold the set rice tofu by inverting the container, cut into thick slices, then into small cubes. Place in a bowl, drizzle with light soy sauce, fragrant vinegar, and chili oil, and sprinkle with chopped scallions before serving.

## Health Advice
This rice tofu is made with aged rice, making it a low-fat, filling option. Rich in carbohydrates and low in protein, it is suitable for those looking to control oily diets. Be mindful of the amount of edible alkali; those with sensitive stomachs should consume in small quantities.
