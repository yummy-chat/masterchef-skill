# Multi-Grain Rustic Bread
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 Whole wheat flour approx. 300g  
🌾 Bread flour approx. 200g  
💧 Water approx. 350g  
🧂 Salt approx. 10g  
🍞 Natural yeast (or yeast) approx. 10g  
🟤 Flax seeds as needed  
🟡 Pumpkin seeds as needed  
⚫ Black sesame seeds as needed  
🍚 Cooked quinoa as needed  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Large mixing bowl  
🧺 Banneton proofing basket (lined with cotton cloth)  
📄 Baking paper  
🔪 Sharp scoring knife  
🏺 Dutch oven (with lid)  
🔥 Oven  
🧤 Oven mitts  

## Preparation Steps  
1. The night before, mix whole wheat flour, bread flour, water, salt, and yeast into a dough. Cover with plastic wrap and let it ferment at room temperature.  
   **Key Point**: Subsequently, perform fold-and-stretch every 30 minutes. No need for excessive kneading.  
2. Mix flax seeds, pumpkin seeds, black sesame seeds, and cooked quinoa into a multi-grain filling. Set aside.  
3. During the early fermentation stage, pick up one corner of the dough and fold it toward the center every 30 minutes. Repeat 4-6 times to strengthen the gluten.  
4. After folding, transfer the dough into a banneton lined with a cotton cloth. Refrigerate for overnight cold fermentation.  
5. The next day, take out the dough and invert it onto baking paper.  
   **Note**: At this point, spiral patterns have formed on the dough's surface.  
6. Quickly score a pattern on the surface using a scoring knife.  
7. Place the Dutch oven with its lid inside the oven and preheat to 230-250°C.  
   **Key Point**: The Dutch oven must be fully preheated.  
8. Carefully remove the hot Dutch oven. Transfer the dough along with the baking paper into the pot. Quickly cover with the lid and return to the oven.  
   **Baking Time**: Bake covered for 25 minutes.  
9. After 25 minutes, remove the lid and continue baking for 15-20 minutes, until the surface is deep brown and tapping the bottom produces a hollow sound.  
10. Once out of the oven, let it cool completely on a wire rack.  

## Health Advice  
This multi-grain rustic bread is rich in whole wheat and seeds, providing ample dietary fiber and high-quality fatty acids. It is suitable for those needing to control blood sugar or focus on gut health. It is recommended to pair with a protein source (e.g., eggs, cheese) to balance blood sugar fluctuations.
