# Overnight Oats
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥣 Old-fashioned rolled oats 2~3 tbsp  
🥛 Milk Approximately 1:1 volume ratio with oats  
🍦 Yogurt Approximately half the amount of oats  
🌱 Chia seeds To taste  
🥜 Mixed nuts To taste (pre-toasted)  
🍑 Plums To taste (diced)  
🍒 Cherries To taste  
🥜 Nut butter (peanut butter or almond butter) To taste  
🍁 Maple syrup To taste  
🧂 Salt A pinch (optional)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🫙 Glass storage container with airtight lid  
🔥 Oven and baking sheet  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Measuring spoons  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Spread mixed nuts evenly on a baking sheet and toast in the oven at low temperature until crispy. Remove and let cool, then chop into pieces and set aside.  
2. Place old-fashioned rolled oats in an airtight container. Add milk, yogurt, maple syrup, a pinch of salt, and chia seeds in order. Stir thoroughly until well combined.  
   **Note**: The volume ratio of milk to oats should be approximately 1:1, yogurt about half the amount of oats, and the total liquid should just cover the oats.  
3. Seal the container tightly and refrigerate overnight to allow the oats to fully absorb the liquid and become soft.  
4. The next day, remove the overnight oats and transfer to a bowl. Drizzle with nut butter, top with diced plums, cherries, and chopped nuts. Garnish with edible flower petals, if desired, and serve directly.  

## Health Tips  
This overnight oats dish is rich in dietary fiber and healthy fats, making it suitable for weight management or those needing gut health support. Be mindful of the amounts of nuts and maple syrup used to avoid excessive calorie intake.
