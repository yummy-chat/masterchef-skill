# Cold Mixed Enoki Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍄 Enoki mushrooms 150 g (about 1 small pack)
🌿 Scallions 5 g
🧄 Garlic 2 cloves
🧂 Light soy sauce 15 ml
🧂 Vinegar 10 ml
🧂 White sugar 3 g (optional)
🧂 Sesame oil 5 ml (optional)
🌶️ Chili oil 5 ml (optional)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Pot
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🔪 Knife
🔪 Cutting board

## Cooking Steps
1. Trim the roots of the enoki mushrooms, rinse thoroughly with clean water, and drain for later use.
2. Wash the scallions and chop them into green onion pieces for later use.
3. Peel the garlic and mince it for later use.
4. Pour about 1000 ml of drinking water into a pot and bring to a boil over high heat.
5. Add the enoki mushrooms and cook for 1–2 minutes until softened, then remove.
   **Key Point**: The blanching time for enoki mushrooms should not exceed 2 minutes, otherwise, it will affect their crisp texture.
6. Remove the cooked enoki mushrooms, drain well, and place them in a large bowl for later use.
7. In a separate small bowl, add light soy sauce, vinegar, white sugar, and sesame oil.
8. Add the minced garlic.
9. Stir well until the white sugar is completely dissolved and the sauce is evenly mixed.
   **Key Point**: The sauce ratio can be flexibly adjusted according to personal taste.
10. Evenly pour the prepared sauce over the enoki mushrooms.
11. Sprinkle the chopped scallions on top.
12. Drizzle with chili oil according to personal preference.
    **Key Point**: Chili oil is added optionally based on your tolerance for spiciness.
13. Gently toss with chopsticks until well combined, then plate and serve.

## Health Tips
Enoki mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber and B vitamins, with extremely low calories, making them suitable for those on a weight loss diet or those needing to regulate intestinal function. It is best to mix and serve immediately to avoid a prolonged wait affecting the crisp texture; those with sensitive stomachs may reduce the amount of chili oil as needed.
