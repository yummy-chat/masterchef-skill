# Brown Sugar and Red Date Blossom Steamed Buns
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍞 500g Bread Flour (protein content ≥ 12g/100g)  
🍬 100g Brown Sugar  
💧 260g Warm Water (approximately 40℃)  
🫘 3g Yeast  
🌿 3g Baking Powder  
⚪ 3g Baking Soda  
🧅 Dried Red Dates, as needed (pitted and finely chopped)  
🥃 10g Dark Soy Sauce (approximately one tablespoon)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥘 Steamer  
🥣 Stainless Steel Bowl  
🥄 Dough Scraper  
✂️ Scissors  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Spoon  
🍶 Mineral Water Bottle  
⚖️ Kitchen Scale  
🔲 Silicone Mat  
🔲 Cutting Board  
🤖 Stand Mixer  
🧻 Steamer Cloth  

## Steps  
1. In a bowl, combine the bread flour, yeast, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix well by hand.
2. Completely dissolve the brown sugar in the warm water. Add the dark soy sauce and stir to combine. Gradually pour this mixture into the flour, stirring with chopsticks until a shaggy dough forms.
3. Knead by hand until the dough is initially smooth and no dry flour remains in the bowl.  
   **Note**: It is normal for the dough to be relatively firm and dry at first. Cover and let it rest for 10 minutes before kneading again; this makes it easier to knead thoroughly.
4. Cover the dough and let it ferment at room temperature for about 30 minutes.  
   **Indicator**: The dough is ready when a finger dipped in flour poked into the dough leaves a hole that does not spring back, or when the inside of the dough has a honeycomb structure.
5. Remove the dough and press it to release the gas. Press it into a large flat piece with your hands. Sprinkle the chopped red dates evenly over the surface, then press them into the dough with your hands.
6. Tightly roll the dough piece up from one end into a long log. Pinch the seam and both ends to seal.  
   **Key**: The roll must be tight to prevent the bun from loosening after steaming.
7. Hold one end of the dough log and twist off individual portions (劑子) with your other hand. Place them with the cut side facing up, **do not press down on the cut surface**. This is the key for the buns to "blossom" after steaming.
8. Place the dough portions on a steamer basket lined with a steamer cloth, leaving enough space between each.
9. **Place the steamer over cold water**, cover with a lid, and bring to a full boil over high heat. Once steaming, **steam for a full 25 minutes**.  
   **Important**: You must start with cold water. After steaming, turn off the heat and let the buns rest covered for a few minutes before opening the lid to prevent them from collapsing.
10. Remove and serve. The buns are rich in brown sugar and red date flavor, with a dense, chewy texture.

## Health Advice  
The combination of brown sugar and red dates provides iron and dietary fiber, making this suitable for women and those with anemia. However, the bread flour and sugar content make these buns relatively high in calories. It is recommended to eat only one per serving and pair it with a cup of unsweetened soy milk to balance blood sugar.
