# Runny-yolk Fried Eggs
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 2 eggs
🧂 Salt, to taste
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🔥 Gas stove
🥄 Spatula

## Instructions
1. Pour an appropriate amount of cooking oil into a non-stick frying pan and heat over medium heat until the oil reaches about 70% hot.
2. Crack the 2 eggs into the pan one at a time. When the edges of the egg whites begin to set, the oil temperature is suitable. Be careful not to let the oil get too hot or too cool.
3. Turn the heat to high and quickly fry the bottom and edges of the eggs until set, about 20–30 seconds.
4. Reduce the heat to low and fry slowly until the egg whites are fully set and the yolks remain runny.
   **Key point**: The entire process takes about 3 minutes. Frying slowly over low heat is key to avoiding burning and keeping the yolks runny.
5. Before removing from the pan, sprinkle a little salt evenly over the surface of the yolks.
6. Carefully lift the eggs with a spatula and transfer them to a plate. Serve immediately.

## Health Tips
Eggs are a high-protein, low-calorie quality breakfast ingredient. Runny egg yolks are rich in lecithin, making them suitable for office workers and students who need to boost brain function. It is recommended to pair with whole wheat bread and vegetables for balanced nutrition. However, pregnant women, the elderly, and those with weakened immune systems should avoid eating raw egg yolks and must cook them until fully set.
