# Steamed Eggs with Minced Pork and Shrimp

Cuisine: Northeastern Chinese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 Eggs, 6  
🦐 Shrimp, appropriate amount (marinate in advance with cooking wine and salt)  
🥩 Minced pork, appropriate amount  
🌭 Minced Chinese sausage, appropriate amount (mix 3/4 into the meat filling, sprinkle 1/4 on top)  
🥕 Diced water chestnuts, appropriate amount  
🧅 Scallions, appropriate amount (finely chopped, for final sprinkle)  
🍶 Cooking wine, a little  
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount  
🍬 White sugar, appropriate amount  
🧂 Chicken powder, appropriate amount  
🧂 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount (for both the meat filling and the final drizzle)  
🧂 White pepper powder, appropriate amount  
🧅🫚 Scallion and ginger water, a little  
🥫 Soybean paste, 1 small spoon  
🫒 Sesame oil, appropriate amount (needs to be heated before drizzling)  
🧂 Steamed fish soy sauce, 1 spoon  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for mixing ingredients)  
🍽️ Plate (steaming dish)  
🍳 Pot (for steaming)  
🥢 Chopsticks (to elevate the steaming dish)  
🥄 Spoon (for drizzling sauce)  

## Cooking Steps
1. **Prepare Shrimp**: Add a little cooking wine and salt to the shrimp. Mix well with your hands until it becomes slightly sticky. Set aside.
2. **Prepare Meat Filling**: In the minced pork, add 3/4 of the minced Chinese sausage, diced water chestnuts, salt, white sugar, chicken powder, light soy sauce, white pepper powder, scallion and ginger water, and 1 small spoon of soybean paste. Stir clockwise until the mixture becomes sticky and firm.
> Stirring clockwise until the mixture is firm is the key to a bouncy and chewy meat filling; if the filling seems too dry, add a little more scallion and ginger water.

3. **Assemble the Steaming Dish**: Spread the meat filling evenly on the plate and press a well in the center. Crack the 6 eggs one by one into the well. Sprinkle the remaining minced Chinese sausage on top.
4. **Steam**: Add water to the pot and place chopsticks or a steaming rack inside. Gently place the steaming dish on top. Cover with the lid, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and steam for about 10 minutes.
> The steaming time should be slightly adjusted based on the quantity of ingredients and heat intensity; check for egg coagulation to avoid overcooking.

5. **Flavor with Drizzle**: Remove the steaming dish and sprinkle with chopped scallions. First, drizzle with heated sesame oil, then add 1 spoon of light soy sauce and 1 spoon of steamed fish soy sauce. Serve.

## Health Advice  
This steamed egg dish, combining shrimp, minced pork, and Chinese sausage, is rich in protein and energy, making it suitable for those needing to build muscle or replenish energy. However, Chinese sausage and soybean paste are high in salt; patients with high blood pressure should reduce the amount or pair it with light vegetable dishes.
