# Carrot Chain
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥕 1 carrot (straight, uniform in size, and regular in shape)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Small carving knife (or a sharp small knife)

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the carrot, trim one side flat, then shape it into a rectangular sheet of even thickness.  
   - Uniform thickness of the sheet is essential for successful subsequent carving.
2. On the sheet, use the carving knife to cut a crisscross grid pattern, forming a row of connected squares, keeping the connections between the squares intact.  
   - The cutting depth must be moderate: the edges of the squares should be clear, but the connecting points must not be cut through.
3. Insert the knife into each square, carefully cut the connection points between adjacent squares, turning the originally solid squares into movable, independent links.  
   - This step requires extreme patience and precision, directly determining whether the chain can move flexibly.
4. Gently stretch and arrange the separated structure to form a continuous chain.  
   - Be very gentle to avoid breaking the fragile carrot links.
5. Remove any excess carrot debris to keep the finished product neat.

## Health Tips
This carrot chain is a purely visual creation, containing almost no calories, making it suitable for plate decoration or entertaining children. Be careful with knife safety to avoid cutting your fingers.
