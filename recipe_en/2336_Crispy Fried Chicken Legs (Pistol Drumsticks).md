# Crispy Fried Chicken Legs (Pistol Drumsticks)
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍗 Whole chicken legs (pistol drumsticks) 4 pieces (about 350g each)  
🌾 All-purpose flour 300g (for coating)  
🌽 Cornstarch 100g (for coating)  
🫚 Ginger slices, to taste  
🧄 Minced garlic, to taste  
🧅 Onion strips, to taste  
🧂 Salt 15g (for marinade) + 3g (for coating)  
🍬 Sugar 40g  
🌿 Five-spice powder 3g  
🧂 White pepper powder 3g (for marinade) + 3g (for coating)  
🌶️ Chili powder 30g (optional, can omit if not spicy)  
🧪 Aluminum-free baking powder 3g  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥣 Mixing bowls (one large, one medium)  
🍳 Deep fryer pot  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
📦 Plastic wrap  
🧻 Kitchen paper towels  

## Instructions  
1. Make a few cuts into the thickest part of each chicken leg to allow the marinade to penetrate.
2. Place the chicken legs in a large bowl. Add ginger slices, minced garlic, onion strips, 15g salt, 40g sugar, 3g white pepper powder, 3g five-spice powder, and 30g chili powder. Wearing gloves, rub vigorously to ensure the seasonings are fully worked into the chicken. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
3. In a medium bowl, mix 300g all-purpose flour, 100g cornstarch, 3g salt, 3g white pepper powder, and 3g baking powder. Stir well to combine for the coating.
4. Coating steps:
   - Take one marinated chicken leg and coat it evenly in the dry flour mixture.
   - Quickly dip it into clean water, then lift it out immediately.
   - Return it to the dry flour mixture and use your hands to press firmly and repeatedly, ensuring the coating adheres tightly and forms a scaly, textured pattern.
   - Gently shake off any excess loose flour.
   **Key point**: The “pressing” during the second coating is the core technique for creating a crispy, fish-scale-like crust.
5. Frying steps:
   - Heat oil to medium-high heat (about 50-60% hot). Add the chicken legs.
   - Maintain medium-low heat and fry for about 13-15 minutes.
   - Fry until the crust is set and turns a deep golden brown. Insert a chopstick into the thickest part of the meat; if no blood juice seeps out, they are done. Remove and drain.
   **Key point**: The initial oil temperature must be high enough to quickly set the crust, then reduce to medium-low heat to ensure the inside is fully cooked.
6. Place the fried chicken legs on kitchen paper towels to absorb excess oil. Serve immediately.

## Health Suggestions  
This dish is rich in protein and calories. It is recommended to pair it with a large vegetable salad to balance nutrition. It is suitable as a "cheat meal" after a workout or on days needing an energy boost. Note that the marinating time should not be too short to ensure full flavor penetration.
