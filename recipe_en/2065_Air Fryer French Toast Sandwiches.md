# Air Fryer French Toast Sandwiches  
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍞 White bread 2 slices  
🥚 Egg 1  
🥛 Milk half a carton (about 60-80ml)  
🍌 Banana appropriate amount (sliced)  
🍫 Chocolate a few pieces  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks or fork  
📄 Parchment paper  

## Instructions  
1. Place the egg and milk in a bowl, and whisk until well combined to form an egg-milk mixture.  
2. On one slice of bread, layer the banana slices and chocolate pieces, then cover with the other slice of bread and gently press down.  
3. Immerse the filled bread completely in the egg-milk mixture, coating both sides evenly, allowing it to absorb the liquid.  
**Key point**: The bread needs to fully absorb the egg-milk mixture to become crispy on the outside and tender on the inside after baking.  
4. Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper and place the coated bread inside.  
5. Bake at 180°C, flipping once midway, until both sides are golden brown and crispy.  
**Doneness check**: The surface should be golden brown, and it should spring back when gently pressed with tongs.  

## Health Tips  
This French toast sandwich uses an air fryer to reduce oil, but bananas and chocolate still make it relatively high in calories. It is suitable as a breakfast or afternoon tea, and portion control is recommended. For those with high blood sugar, reduce the amount of chocolate and replace it with nuts for a healthier option.
