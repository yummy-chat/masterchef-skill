# Lychee Rose Jam
Cuisine: Common Home-style Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍈 Lychee pulp 250g
🌹 Dried rose petals 3g
🍋 Lemon half (juice only)
🍬 Rock sugar approx. 50g (can substitute with brown sugar, adjust to sweetness)
🧂 Salt as needed (for making saltwater)
💧 Water as needed (for making saltwater)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
✂️ Scissors
🥣 Bowl
🍳 Frying pan
🥄 Silicone spatula
🍴 Fork
🫙 Glass jar

## Steps
1. Take the lychee, use scissors to cut open the shell, peel out the whole pulp, and place it in a bowl.
2. Add enough saltwater, gently stir with a fork, soak for about 15 minutes, then drain the saltwater.
   **Key point**: Soaking in saltwater effectively removes astringency; do not shorten the time.
3. Take the dried roses, rub to separate the petals from the stems, and collect the petals for later use.
4. Add rock sugar to the drained lychee pulp and mix well.
   **Key point**: The amount of rock sugar can be adjusted according to the lychee's own sweetness; if the lychee is very sweet, you may reduce the sugar.
5. Pour the lychee pulp mixed with sugar into a frying pan, add the dried rose petals, and start cooking over heat.
   **Key point**: During cooking, continuously stir with a silicone spatula to prevent sticking.
6. When it thickens, squeeze in the juice of half a lemon and stir well.
   **Key point**: Lemon juice enhances the flavor and also acts as a preservative.
7. While hot, pack the jam into a clean glass jar and seal for storage.
   **Key point**: Without high-temperature sealing, the shelf life is shorter; it is recommended to make small batches and consume quickly.

## Health Advice
Lychee rose jam is rich in vitamin C and anthocyanins, suitable for relieving summer heat and greasiness. However, rock sugar and lychee have high sugar content; it is recommended to consume one spoonful per serving, preferably with unsweetened yogurt for a healthier option. Note that the time for soaking in saltwater to remove astringency should not be too short.
