# Animal Cream Piped Flower Cake  
Cuisine: Common Home-style  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍰 1 Japanese-style sponge cake layer (pre-made, no need to brush with syrup)  
🥛 Animal whipping cream (Millac/Blue Windmill) 200g for filling + 250g for dark decoration + 150g for light decoration  
🍓 Raspberry jam 75g for dark color (30% of cream) + approx. 22g for light color (approx. 15% of cream)  
🍬 Icing sugar 12g for filling + 15g for dark cream + 9g for light cream  
🥃 Rum 4g (add to filling cream)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Cake leveler  
🌀 Electric hand mixer  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
👛 Piping bag  
🌹 Rose piping tip  
🎠 Turntable  
🍴 Offset spatula  
🍰 Cake server  
🔥 Kitchen torch/gas stove (for heating knife)  
🧊 Frozen damp towel (to wrap piping bag and cool knife)  

## Step-by-step Instructions  
1. **Prepare frozen damp towels**: Take two clean towels, dampen them until they don't drip, roll them up separately, and place in the freezer for later use.  
   *Key point*: Used to wrap the piping bag to insulate from hand heat and prevent animal cream from melting.  
2. **Slice the cake**: Use a cake leveler to horizontally cut the sponge cake into 3 even layers. Discard the topmost layer.  
   *Key point*: Gently press the cake with one hand while using a sawing motion with the leveler in the other hand.  
3. **Whip filling cream**: Combine 200g whipping cream, 12g icing sugar, and 4g rum. Whip over an ice bath at high speed until it reaches 70-80% stiffness.  
   *Key point*: Sugar should be ground into icing sugar; switch to low speed later to avoid over-whipping.  
4. **Secure the cake base**: Spread a small amount of cream on the turntable and place the first cake layer on top to prevent shifting.  
5. **Apply filling cream**: Weigh 100g of filling cream onto the cake layer and spread evenly with an offset spatula.  
6. **Stack cake layers**: Place the second cake layer on top, gently press with your palm to adhere. Spread another 100g of filling cream evenly, then place the third layer on top and press gently.  
7. **Prepare base color cream**: Whip 250g whipping cream, 15g icing sugar, and 75g raspberry jam until firm. Thinly spread a layer over the cake's top and sides as a base coat; it doesn't need to be smooth, just enough to prevent the cake from showing through when piping.  
   *Key point*: Jam color may be unstable; adjust quantity flexibly based on the hue. Clean the turntable with a damp cloth after finishing.  
8. **Prepare dark-colored piping bag**: Insert the rose piping tip into the piping bag, fold the bag's opening over a cylindrical tool, fill with dark raspberry cream, push the cream down to the tip with a scraper, snip the bag's end, and wrap the outside with a frozen damp towel.  
   *Key point*: Avoid direct hand contact with the bag. The cold, damp towel is crucial for keeping the piped shapes firm.  
9. **Pipe dark flowers**: Holding the towel-wrapped piping bag, pipe an even ring of dark roses around the cake's side.  
10. **Prepare light-colored piping**: Use the remaining light-colored cream, fill a piping bag the same way, and wrap with a damp towel.  
11. **Pipe light flowers**: Fill the cake's top with light roses, creating an ombre effect.  
12. **Chill to set**: Place the decorated cake in the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours.  
13. **Slice the cake**: Remove the cake, heat a knife with a kitchen torch or gas stove, then cool it with a damp towel to the right temperature before slicing for clean cuts.  
    *Key point*: Heating then cooling prevents cream from sticking to the knife. Use a small knife to assist in lifting pieces from the cake.  

## Health Note  
This is a high-sugar, high-fat dessert. It's recommended to enjoy with unsweetened tea or black coffee and to control total daily calorie intake. Although animal cream is healthier than plant-based cream, it is still calorie-dense and suitable for occasional indulgence or festive sharing.
