# Steamed Golden Pomfret with Black Bean and Garlic Sauce
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 1 Golden pomfret (cleaned, with slits scored on the fish body)
🫚 Ginger, to taste (sliced into shreds, used for lining the plate and topping the fish)
🫘 Black beans, to taste (sprinkled over the fish body)
🧄 Garlic, to taste (minced into a paste)
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste (heated until hot, drizzled over the fish before serving)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok/Steamer
🥘 Steaming rack
🍽️ Fish steaming plate
🔪 Knife
⏲️ Mechanical timer (for precise 8-minute timing)

## Preparation Steps
1. Clean the golden pomfret and score several slits on both sides of the fish body. Line the plate with a layer of ginger shreds, place the fish on top, then spread more ginger shreds over the fish surface, and evenly sprinkle with black beans and minced garlic.
**Note**: Sufficient ginger shreds must be spread to achieve the effect of removing fishy odor.

2. Add enough water to the steamer, place the steaming rack inside, and bring the water to a boil over high heat until steam rises. Place the fish plate into the steamer.
3. Cover the lid, maintain high heat, and strictly steam for 8 minutes according to the timer. After the time is up, turn off the heat and let it rest with residual heat for a brief moment.
**Key**: 8 minutes is the essential cooking time for tender and non-dry fish meat. The fish must be placed into the steamer after the water has boiled and steam is rising, and the heat should be sufficiently high.

4. Remove the fish plate, pour off some of the steaming liquid from the plate, and drizzle a spoonful of oil heated until smoking over the fish body. Immediately release the aroma of black beans and garlic, and serve.
**Note**: Drizzling hot oil is the soul step of steamed fish, maximizing the release of the black bean and garlic fragrance.

## Health Advice
This dish is low in fat and high in protein, rich in Omega-3, suitable for weight control and for those seeking heart and brain health. Black beans and garlic help remove fishy odors and enhance flavor, but attention should be paid to controlling the amount of oil drizzled to avoid adding extra calories.
