# Abalone Seafood Fried Rice
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken thigh 350g (peeled and cut into small pieces)  
🦐 Shrimp 150g (peeled and deveined)  
🐚 Abalone 200g  
🦪 Dried scallops (conpoy) 30g (pre-soaked, remove tough stems and crumble, reserve soaking liquid)  
🥬 Chinese broccoli stems 100g (leaves removed, cut into small dice about the thickness of two coins)  
🍚 Cooked rice 400g  
🥚 Eggs 2  
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms 5 (sliced)  
🧅 Scallion whites, as needed (for marinating)  
🍬 Sugar 5g  
🧂 Salt 10g  
🦪 Oyster sauce 10g  
🧂 Light soy sauce 10g  
🌶 White pepper 5g  
🍶 Huadiao wine 10g (drizzle along the side of the wok)  
🦪 Abalone sauce, as needed (for seasoning)  
🌾 Cornstarch 10g (mixed with water for slurry)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spatula or ladle  
🥣 Bowls or basins  
🔪 Chef’s knife and cutting board  
🍚 Rice cooker  

## Steps  
1. **Marinate meat and seafood**: Place the chicken thigh pieces, shrimp, and abalone into separate bowls. Add scallion whites, sugar, salt, oyster sauce, light soy sauce, and white pepper. Mix well to marinate and allow flavors to penetrate.  
**Key point**: The same marinade formula is used for both chicken and seafood.  
2. **Prepare dried scallops**: Pre-soak the dried scallops. Remove the tough stems, crumble by hand, and reserve the soaking liquid.  
3. **Make golden egg fried rice**: Mix the beaten eggs into the rice until well combined. Stir-fry in a wok until the rice is golden, dry, and aromatic. Transfer to a rice cooker and keep warm.  
**Key point**: The egg fried rice serves as the base and should be stir-fried until each grain is separate.  
4. **Cook the topping**: Heat oil in a wok over high heat. First, add the marinated chicken thigh pieces and stir-fry until they change color. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms and stir-fry until fragrant. Then add the shrimp and abalone, stir-frying over high heat throughout.  
5. **Add wine for aroma**: Drizzle the Huadiao wine along the side of the wok to release its fragrance.  
6. **Add dried scallops**: Add the crumbled dried scallops and their reserved soaking liquid. Stir-fry until evenly combined.  
7. **Add Chinese broccoli stems**: Add the diced Chinese broccoli stems. Reduce heat to medium and cook for 1 minute.  
8. **Season**: First taste the sauce to check saltiness. The ingredients already have a base flavor, so adjust by adding abalone sauce as needed.  
9. **Thicken the sauce**: Pour the cornstarch slurry into the wok. Stir-fry until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients.  
10. **Combine and plate**: Pour the cooked seafood topping, including the sauce, over the warm golden egg fried rice. Serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
Abalone seafood fried rice is rich in protein. The shrimp and shellfish provide high-quality protein and zinc, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts and students needing mental energy. Note that dried scallops and abalone sauce are naturally salty, so those with high blood pressure can reduce additional salt as needed. It is recommended to serve with a side of lightly stir-fried vegetables for balanced nutrition.
