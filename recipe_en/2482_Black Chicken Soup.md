# Black Chicken Soup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 1 black chicken (about 3 lbs, a hybrid of meat chicken and Taihe black chicken)  
🍄 1 packet of mushroom soup mix (about 50g, containing morel, Brazilian mushroom, deer mushroom, cordyceps flower, and chanterelle; cordyceps flower is not suitable for children)  
🔴 2–3 red dates  
🌿 1 small handful of goji berries  
🫚 2–3 slices of ginger (smashed flat)  
🍾 Suitable amount of yellow wine (or cooking wine)  
🌶️ About a dozen white peppercorns  
🍊 1 small piece of dried tangerine peel  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🧅 Suitable amount of scallions (finely chopped)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife  
✂️ Scissors  
🍳 Wok or soup pot (for blanching)  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🫕 Clay pot  
🫖 Kettle  
🍵 Small bowl  

## Instructions
1. **Prepare the black chicken**: Cut the limbs along the bone joints, remove the head, use scissors to open the lower beak and clean the inside; remove the esophagus and trachea from the neck; split the body and remove the lungs and kidneys from the back; rinse all parts repeatedly until the water runs clear.  
   **Note**: Be sure to remove the esophagus, trachea, lungs, and kidneys, otherwise the broth will have an unpleasant odor.  
2. **Blanch the chicken**: Place the chicken pieces in a pot of cold water, ensuring they are fully submerged. Heat until the water reaches about 70°C (just before boiling), then immediately turn off the heat. Let it sit for 3–5 minutes using residual heat, stirring occasionally to allow the blood foam to rise completely. Remove the chicken.  
   **Key point**: Do not let the water come to a full boil, to prevent the chicken fat from being lost.  
3. **Rinse after blanching**: Rinse the blanched chicken pieces thoroughly under running water until the water runs clear again.  
4. **Prepare the ingredients**: Wash the dried mushrooms from the soup packet; smash the ginger flat; do not rinse the goji berries.  
5. **Start simmering**: Place the cleaned chicken pieces, mushrooms, and red dates into a clay pot. Add the ginger slices, yellow wine, white peppercorns, and dried tangerine peel. Pour in enough boiling water at once.  
6. **Skim and simmer**: Bring to a boil over high heat, skim off any foam from the surface, then reduce to the lowest heat and simmer for about 1 hour, until the chicken is tender. If using an older free-range chicken, simmer for over 2 hours.  
7. **Season and serve**: Once done, place scallions and salt into a bowl according to the serving amount, then ladle in the hot soup.  
   **Note**: Salt must be added only when serving; adding salt earlier will cause the protein to coagulate, affecting the soup’s umami and the meat’s texture.  

## Health Tips
Black chicken soup is rich in high-quality protein and mushroom polysaccharides, making it suitable for those with weak constitutions, post-surgery recovery, or autumn/winter nourishment. Cordyceps flower should not be given to children. Be sure to thoroughly remove the organs and blood foam, and always use boiling water when simmering to lock in the flavor.
