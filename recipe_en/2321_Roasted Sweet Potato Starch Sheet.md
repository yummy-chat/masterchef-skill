# Roasted Sweet Potato Starch Sheet

Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Sweet potato starch 400g  
🥔 Potato starch 50g  
💧 Water 900ml  
🧂 Salt 1g (for batter) + 5g (for sauce)  
🫒 Cooking oil as needed (for greasing pan, brush, and anti-stick)  
🧅 Pickled cowpeas, chopped, as needed  
🧅 Scallions, chopped, divided into two portions  
🥩 Luncheon meat, diced, as needed  
🧄 Minced garlic 20g  
🌶️ Chili powder 15g (reserve a little for final sprinkle)  
🌿 Cumin powder 10g (reserve a little for final sprinkle)  
🥢 Soy sauce 15g  
🍬 Sugar 3g  
🫗 Hot oil 120g (approximately 180°C)

## Cookware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥢 Whisk or chopsticks  
🖌️ Silicone brush  
📦 Square baking pan or mold  
🍲 Steamer  
🔧 Tongs  
🚿 Basin of cold water  
🍳 Non-stick skillet or electric griddle  
🍴 Spatula  
🍢 Bamboo skewers

## Preparation Steps
1. Mix sweet potato starch, potato starch, and 1g of salt. Gradually add water while stirring until no dry lumps remain, forming a smooth batter.
2. Chop pickled cowpeas, dice luncheon meat, and chop scallions. In a separate bowl, combine minced garlic, chili powder, cumin powder, soy sauce, 5g salt, and sugar. Pour in hot oil heated to approximately 180°C, and stir well to make the sauce.
3. Brush the inside of the square baking pan evenly with a layer of cooking oil to prevent sticking.
4. Bring water to a boil in the steamer. Ladle about 150ml of the starch batter into the pan, tilt to spread evenly to a thickness of about 2-3mm. Place in the steamer, cover, and steam over medium-high heat for about 2 minutes, until the surface sets and large bubbles appear. Remove.
5. Immediately submerge the pan in a basin of cold water to cool. Once cooled, carefully peel the whole starch sheet off from the edge. If stacking multiple sheets, brush a little oil between each to prevent sticking.
6. Brush oil on the skillet and preheat. Place one starch sheet in the pan and cook over medium-low heat until small bubbles appear on the bottom, then flip.
7. Brush a layer of sauce on the starch sheet. Evenly spread chopped pickled cowpeas, scallions, and diced luncheon meat in the center. Fold the left and right sides of the sheet toward the center. Insert two bamboo skewers parallel from the bottom to secure.
8. Brush another layer of sauce on the surface of the rolled sheet. Sprinkle with a little extra chili powder, cumin powder, and chopped scallions. Serve on a plate.

## Health Advice
This roasted sweet potato starch sheet is made from sweet potato starch, low in fat but rich in carbohydrates, making it suitable for post-exercise energy replenishment. Pickled cowpeas and luncheon meat add a savory flavor but are high in sodium, so people with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of sauce. Homemade versions allow better control of oil compared to street vendors, achieving a balance between health and taste.
