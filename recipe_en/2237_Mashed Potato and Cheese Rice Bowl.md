# Mashed Potato and Cheese Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Yellow-skinned potatoes (steamed and mashed)
🥓 Bacon (sliced into strips)
🍅 Tomato (peeled and crushed by hand)
🧅 Onion (chopped)
🧄 Garlic (minced)
🟢 Green peas (to taste)
🧀 Shredded cheese (generous amount for topping)
🌱 Chopped green onions (for garnish)
🧈 Animal butter (essential)
🥫 Ketchup
🧂 Salt
🍬 White sugar (to enhance flavor and balance acidity)
🍾 Light soy sauce (drizzled along the edge of the pan)
🌿 Dried rosemary
🌿 Dried parsley flakes
🌶️ Black pepper

## Cookware and Equipment
🎋 Bamboo steamer
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Potato masher
🥄 Spatula
🍲 Lid

## Steps
1. Peel the potatoes and steam them in a bamboo steamer until a chopstick can be easily inserted. Remove and mash with a potato masher, then knead lightly and set aside.
2. Peel the tomatoes and crush them by hand into natural chunks. Slice the bacon into strips. Chop the onion and mince the garlic.
3. Place the animal butter in a cold pan, let it melt naturally using residual heat, then turn on the heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and stir-fry over low heat until fragrant.
4. Add the bacon strips and stir-fry quickly. Add ketchup and cook until the oil turns red. Add the crushed tomatoes, and season with salt and white sugar.
   **Note**: When the tomatoes release juice and the sauce becomes rich, drizzle light soy sauce along the edge of the pan to release the aroma.
5. Push the mashed potatoes into the pan. Use a potato masher or spatula to stir and combine until the mashed potatoes are fully mixed with the sauce, resulting in a smooth and thick consistency.
6. Evenly sprinkle green peas, dried rosemary, dried parsley flakes, and black pepper over the surface. Then cover generously with shredded cheese.
7. Cover the pan with a lid and simmer over low heat for about 3 minutes, until the cheese is fully melted and stretchy, with a slightly browned surface.
   **Key point**: Keep the heat low throughout to prevent scorching at the bottom and ensure the cheese melts completely.
8. Remove from heat, garnish with chopped green onions, and serve.

## Health Advice
Mashed potato with cheese is a high-carb, high-fat dish, suitable for fitness muscle building or quick energy replenishment. Control the amount of bacon and butter, and reduce salt intake for those with high blood pressure.
