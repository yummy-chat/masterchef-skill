# Clear-Braised Beef Short Ribs
Cuisine: Northeastern Chinese
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef short ribs, as needed
🥬 Lettuce, as needed
🫚 Ginger, 1 piece (smashed)
🌿 Cilantro, as needed
🧅 Scallions, as needed
🧄 Garlic sprouts, as needed
⚫ Black peppercorns, as needed
🧂 Salt, as needed
🍶 Shaoxing wine, as needed
🌱 Chinese chive flower paste, as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🪣 Stainless steel basin
🥘 Deep pot
♨️ Electric pressure cooker
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🥄 Soup ladle
🕸️ Fine mesh strainer
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Large slotted spoon

## Instructions
1. Select beef short ribs, choosing thinner pieces with membrane on both sides and a dry surface.
2. Smash the ginger, finely chop scallions, cilantro, and garlic sprouts, and set aside.
3. Add enough cold water to a deep pot, place the whole beef short ribs in, and heat over high flame.
**Note**: It is essential to start with cold water and boil for a while to allow the blood to fully seep out.
4. After the water boils, skim off the foam, then remove the beef short ribs and some of the cooking liquid.
5. Transfer the beef short ribs to a pressure cooker, add enough boiling water to submerge the meat, then add the smashed ginger and black peppercorns; add Shaoxing wine if needed to reduce gamey smell.
**Key point**: You must use boiling water for braising, otherwise the broth will foam.
6. Cover, and cook under pressure for 30–40 minutes after the steam releases.
**Key point**: Do not add salt during the entire braising process; salt is only used in the dipping sauce.
7. Prepare the dipping sauce: In a bowl, place the chopped scallions, cilantro, and garlic sprouts, add a moderate amount of salt, then ladle in hot broth to bloom the mixture. Finally, mix in Chinese chive flower paste and stir well.
8. Remove the cooked beef short ribs, let them cool slightly, then slice thinly. Arrange on lettuce leaves and serve with the dipping sauce.

## Health Advice
This clear-braised beef short rib dish is light, high in protein, and low in salt. It replenishes energy without being greasy, making it especially suitable for fitness enthusiasts or those on a low-sodium diet. Be careful to use only a small amount of salt in the dipping sauce, as the chive flower paste is already salty.
