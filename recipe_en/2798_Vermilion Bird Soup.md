# Vermilion Bird Soup
Cuisine: Henan Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 Eggs  
🍬 White sugar  
💧 Water  
🫒 Sesame oil  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Whisk  
🔥 Pot for boiling water  

## Steps
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, and use chopsticks to whisk thoroughly until the egg mixture is even and frothy.
2. Add 2 ml of sesame oil to the egg mixture and stir gently to combine.
3. Fill the pot with water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
4. While the water is boiling, quickly pour the boiling water into the bowl with the egg mixture.
5. Add 20 grams of white sugar and stir until completely dissolved.

## Key Tips
- The eggs must be whisked thoroughly until the mixture is even and frothy, otherwise the soup's delicate texture will be affected.  
- The water poured into the egg mixture must be at a rolling boil; insufficient water temperature will result in a cloudy and thin soup.  
- This is a sweet version from eastern Henan; if you prefer a savory flavor, replace the white sugar with salt.  
- The amount of white sugar can be adjusted according to personal sweetness preference.  
- This soup has the dietary benefit of clearing heat and reducing internal fire.  

## Health Advice
This Vermilion Bird Soup is a low-fat, high-protein heartwarming dessert, suitable for a quick energy boost after breakfast or staying up late. Note that those with high blood sugar should reduce the sugar amount or use a sugar substitute.
