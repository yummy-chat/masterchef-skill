# Longan and Red Date Congee
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Glutinous rice (or regular rice) - appropriate amount
🫘 Red dates - appropriate amount
🫐 Longan - appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Bowl
🍚 Rice cooker

## Cooking Steps
1. Remove the shells from the longan to get the flesh, rinse with clean water twice, and soak in a bowl for 10 minutes.
2. Rinse the red dates with clean water twice, and soak in a bowl for 10 minutes.
3. Place the glutinous rice in the rice cooker, rinse with clean water twice, and add 2000 ml of clean water.
4. Add the soaked longan and red dates into the rice cooker.
5. Start the rice cooker on the porridge setting, and cook for about 1 hour until the congee is ready.  
   **Key point**: The thickness of the congee can be adjusted by varying the amount of water; sweetness can be controlled by increasing or decreasing the amount of longan.

## Health Suggestions
Longan and red date congee helps nourish qi and blood, making it suitable for people with insufficient qi and blood or cold hands and feet. Diabetic patients should control the amount of longan used. Glutinous rice is easy to digest, but excessive consumption should be avoided to prevent blood sugar spikes.
