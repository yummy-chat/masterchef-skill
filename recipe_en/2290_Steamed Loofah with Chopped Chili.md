# Steamed Loofah with Chopped Chili  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥒 Loofah 1 piece (choose tender young loofah)  
🌶️ Chopped chili sauce 50g  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🔪 Peeler  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🍽️ Plate  
♨️ Steamer  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Peel the loofah using a peeler, cut into even strips on the cutting board, and arrange neatly on a plate.  
2. Evenly drizzle the chopped chili sauce over the loofah strips.  
3. Once the steamer water is boiling, place the plate of loofah inside and steam over high heat for 8 minutes.  
   **Note**: The steamer water must be brought to a boil before adding the loofah.  
4. After steaming, remove and enjoy while hot. The loofah will be crisp and vibrant green, with the salty and fragrant taste of chopped chili.  

## Health Advice  
Loofah is high in water content and very low in calories. Chopped chili can stimulate appetite and aid digestion. This dish is especially suitable for those looking to control oil and reduce fat in summer. However, chopped chili is high in sodium; those sensitive to blood pressure should use less or pair it with mixed grain rice.
