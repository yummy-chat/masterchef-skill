# Mulled Wine with Fruit 🍷  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Season: Winter (November–December)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍎 Apple – 1 (cored, cut into thick slices)  
🍊 Orange – 1 (peel off some of the skin to get the orange zest, avoiding the white pith; cut the rest into thick slices)  
🍷 Red wine – 1 bottle  
🥫 Canned fruit in syrup – as needed  
🧊 Rock sugar – as needed  
🍃 Licorice – as needed  
🌿 Bay leaves – as needed  
🌰 Cinnamon stick – as needed  
⭐ Star anise – as needed  
🥜 Cardamom – as needed  
🍂 Dried tangerine peel – as needed  
🍊 Fresh orange zest – as needed  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🥄 Paring knife  
🍲 Stainless steel pot  
🥄 Stirring spoon  
🍷 Glass  

## Steps  
1. Place rock sugar, licorice, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, star anise, cardamom, and dried tangerine peel at the bottom of the pot to form the spice mix.  
   **Note**: Spices can be adjusted to taste; dried tangerine peel is recommended.  
2. Core the apple and cut into thick slices; use a paring knife to peel part of the orange for zest, then cut the remaining orange into thick slices.  
   **Optional**: Canned fruit in syrup can substitute for fresh fruit.  
3. Add the sliced apple, sliced orange, and fresh orange zest to the pot along with the spices.  
4. Pour in the entire bottle of red wine, ensuring the liquid mostly covers the ingredients.  
5. Simmer over low heat for 30–40 minutes, until the aroma is rich, the wine color deepens, and the flavors of fruit and spices have infused.  
   **Key point**: Keep the heat low throughout to avoid rapid alcohol evaporation; if the liquid becomes too low, replenish with more red wine or a small amount of water.  
6. Once done, pour into a glass. Optionally, garnish with an orange slice or a sprig of rosemary. Serve hot.  

## Health Tips  
Mulled wine with fruit blends antioxidants and vitamin C into the wine, making it a convenient way to warm up in winter. However, canned fruit in syrup and rock sugar can easily lead to excess calories, so it is advisable to control the sugar content. Suitable for those who feel cold or seek a light buzz for health, but avoid drinking on an empty stomach to prevent stomach irritation.
