# Egg White Cloud Cookies
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 2 eggs (use only the egg whites, ensure no egg yolk is mixed in)
🍼 30g milk powder
🥛 20g thick yogurt (about 1 tablespoon)
🌽 10g cornstarch
🍋 A few drops of lemon juice (to remove the eggy smell and stabilize the meringue)
🍬 5g sugar (optional; this recipe uses a sugar-free version in the video, but it is recommended to add sugar for babies who can eat it)

## Tools and Equipment
🔌 Electric hand mixer
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (must be clean, oil-free, and water-free)
🥄 Spatula
🧁 Piping bag (fitted with a piping tip)
📜 Parchment paper (baking paper)
🍳 Baking sheet
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🔥 Oven

## Instructions
1. Place the egg whites in a clean, oil-free, and water-free stainless steel bowl, and add a few drops of lemon juice.
   **Key Point**: The mixing bowl and beaters must be clean, oil-free, and water-free, or the egg whites will not whip properly.
2. Using an electric hand mixer on medium speed, beat the egg whites until lines appear and the whisk attachment forms a soft peak when lifted.
3. Add the cornstarch and continue mixing on low speed until well combined, avoiding powder flying out.
4. Whip until stiff peaks form, meaning when you lift the whisk, the meringue stands up in short, firm peaks.
5. Pour the pre-mixed yogurt mixture into the meringue all at once, and gently fold together quickly with a spatula to avoid deflating. The batter should hold its shape and not drip.
6. Transfer the mixed batter into a piping bag fitted with a piping tip.
7. Pipe three-dimensional flower shapes onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
   **Key Point**: The egg white cloud cookie batter is stable, and the piped shapes should not spread out easily. To make other flavors, replace the yogurt with an equal amount of fruit or vegetable juice in step 5, and adjust the amount of milk powder accordingly.
8. Optionally, dot the tops of the egg white cloud cookies with leftover egg yolk batter to create a "fried egg" shape.
9. Place the baking sheet in a preheated oven and bake at 90°C (195°F) for about 1 hour, until the cookies are firm and can be easily removed from the parchment paper.
   **Key Point**: The baking time depends on the cookies being completely dried out to ensure a crispy texture.

## Health Tips
These egg white cloud cookies are high in protein and low in fat, making them suitable for babies and those controlling sugar intake as a snack. They need to be baked at a low temperature until completely dehydrated to achieve a crispy texture. It is recommended to consume them immediately after making, and store them in an airtight container for no more than 3 days to prevent them from becoming soft due to moisture.
