# Braised Silkie Chicken Soup with Rehmannia Root
Cuisine: Cantonese Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐓 One whole silkie chicken (cleaned, with toenails trimmed off, and chicken feet and head tucked into the cavity)
🍄 3-4 dried shiitake mushrooms (soaked for 30 minutes in advance, scrubbed with cornstarch, then rinsed clean)
🫚 A few slices of ginger
🫘 A few red dates (soaked)
🌿 Two pieces of rehmannia root (core medicinal ingredient, soaked)
🫐 A small handful of goji berries (soaked)
🧂 One tablespoon of salt (for final seasoning)
🧂 Two tablespoons of chicken bouillon (or chicken powder) (for final umami boost)

## Cookware and Equipment
🫗 Large glass bowl (for soaking and cleaning mushrooms)
🥣 Small bowls (for separately soaking goji berries, rehmannia root, and red dates)
🫕 Dark brown clay pot (used as a double-boiling vessel to hold ingredients)
♨️ Multi-layer stainless steel steamer (with water at the bottom, used for double boiling)
🥄 Soup ladle (for stirring, seasoning, and serving)
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board (for slicing ginger and preparing the silkie chicken)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Pre-treat Dried Ingredients**
   - Place dried shiitake mushrooms in a large glass bowl, add water, and soak for 30 minutes. Remove, scrub with cornstarch, and rinse thoroughly with clean water.
   - Place goji berries, rehmannia root, and red dates in separate small bowls, add water, and set aside to soak.
2. **Prepare the Silkie Chicken**
   - Clean the silkie chicken thoroughly and trim off the toenails.
   - Tuck the chicken feet and head back into the cavity, shaping the chicken into a compact form for easy placement in the clay pot.
3. **Assemble in the Pot**
   - Place the prepared silkie chicken into the clay pot. Add the soaked shiitake mushrooms, ginger slices, red dates, rehmannia root, and goji berries in sequence.
   - Pour in enough hot water to fully submerge all ingredients, ensuring the pot has enough headspace to allow for boiling.
4. **Double Boil**
   - Cover the clay pot with its lid and place it into a steamer with boiling water. Then cover the steamer with its outer lid.
   - **Heat and Timing**: Bring to a boil over high heat until steam appears, then immediately reduce to low heat and simmer gently for 120 minutes.
   **Note**: Ensure the steamer base has sufficient water to avoid drying out during the 2-hour cooking process. If adding water midway, use hot water.
5. **Season and Serve**
   - After cooking, remove the lid. Add one tablespoon of salt and two tablespoons of chicken bouillon, and gently stir with a ladle until fully dissolved.
   - Serve immediately. The soup will be deep brown with a glossy layer of oil, and the chicken will be tender enough to fall off the bone.

## Health Tips
Silkie chicken soup with rehmannia root has the effect of nourishing yin and enriching the blood. It is especially suitable for those with cold hands and feet or insufficient qi and blood during autumn and winter. Although silkie chicken is low in fat, the soup still contains a considerable amount of oil. It is recommended to skim off the floating oil before drinking to reduce fat intake.
