# Grilled Cheese Toast
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍞 4 slices of bread
🥛 40g whipping cream
🧈 30g butter
🧀 3 slices of cheese (cheddar)
🥛 15g milk
🍬 10g sugar
🍯 15g honey or condensed milk

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Saucepan
🔥 Induction cooker (or stove)
🥄 Silicone spatula
♨️ Oven
🍽️ Baking tray

## Steps
1. Combine the whipping cream, butter, cheese slices, milk, sugar, and honey or condensed milk in the saucepan.
2. Heat over low heat, stirring continuously with a silicone spatula, until the butter and cheese are completely melted and form a smooth sauce.
   **Note**: Stir constantly to prevent scorching.
3. Arrange the bread slices flat on the baking tray. While the sauce is still hot, evenly spread the prepared grilled cheese sauce on the surface of the bread.
4. Preheat the oven to 200°C. Bake the bread for 7–8 minutes, until the surface turns caramelized.
5. Remove and serve.

## Health Advice
Grilled cheese toast is rich in calcium and energy, making it suitable as a breakfast or quick post-workout replenishment. However, it is high in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with fruits or a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. Be mindful of sugar intake; for diabetics or those on a weight loss diet, reduce the amount of honey or condensed milk used.
