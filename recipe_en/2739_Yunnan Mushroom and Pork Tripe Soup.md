# Yunnan Mushroom and Pork Tripe Soup

Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-year-round

## Steps
1.  Clean the tripe: Place the tripe in a bowl, add flour and white vinegar, and knead thoroughly until the surface slime and odor are completely removed. Rinse clean with water.
2.  Add water to a pot, put in the tripe, scallion segments, ginger slices, and cooking wine. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
3.  Remove the tripe, scrape off the white membrane inside with a knife, then slice diagonally into thin pieces.
4.  Soak the dried mushrooms from the Yunnan mushroom soup pack in warm water for 5 minutes until soft. Reserve the mushroom soaking water.
5.  Place the sliced tripe, soaked mushrooms, ginger slices, and the reserved mushroom soaking water into a clay pot. Add boiling water to 90% full, cover with lid, and simmer over low heat for 50 minutes until the soup turns golden and aromatic.
6.  After removing the lid, add goji berries and an appropriate amount of salt, stir well, and serve.

## Health Tips
Pork tripe is rich in collagen, and the Yunnan mushrooms provide various amino acids. This soup is suitable for those with weakened constitution and those needing to nourish the stomach and qi. However, individuals with high cholesterol should consume it in moderation. It is recommended to clean the tripe with flour to remove the smell. Do not discard the mushroom soaking water to retain the natural umami flavor.
