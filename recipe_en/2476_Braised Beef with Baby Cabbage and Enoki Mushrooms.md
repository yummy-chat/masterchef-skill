# Braised Beef with Baby Cabbage and Enoki Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home-Cooked Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef (lean cut, such as beef tenderloin) about 300g  
🥬 Baby cabbage 1 piece  
🍄 Enoki mushrooms 1 bunch  
🌶️ Green and red chili peppers to taste  
🧄 Minced garlic to taste  

🍶 Light soy sauce 3 tbsp (for the sauce) + to taste (for marinating)  
🍶 Dark soy sauce 1 tbsp (for the sauce) + to taste (for marinating)  
🦪 Oyster sauce 1 tbsp (for the sauce) + to taste (for marinating)  
🌫️ White pepper powder to taste (for marinating)  
🌾 Cornstarch to taste (for marinating)  
🫒 Cooking oil to taste (to seal the meat after marinating)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan or heavy-bottomed pot  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Spoon  

## Steps  
1. Cut the beef into thin slices and place in a bowl. Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper powder, and cornstarch. Mix thoroughly. Finally, drizzle in the cooking oil and mix well to seal the meat. Marinate for 10 minutes.  
   **Note**: Use less marinating seasoning compared to the sauce amount, adjusting based on the beef quantity. Sealing with oil at the end locks in moisture, making the meat more tender.  
2. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, combine minced garlic, 3 tbsp light soy sauce, 1 tbsp dark soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, and half a bowl of water. Stir until well mixed and set aside.  
3. Take a cold pan. Layer the bottom sequentially with baby cabbage, enoki mushrooms, and green and red chili pepper segments. Finally, spread the marinated beef slices evenly on the top layer.  
   **Key point**: Arrange the ingredients as flat as possible. Do not stack the beef too thickly to ensure even heating.  
4. Pour the prepared sauce evenly over the ingredients in the pan. Cover with a lid. Bring to a boil over high heat, then immediately reduce to the lowest heat. Braise for 10 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Maintain the lowest heat throughout to avoid scorching. Use the steam to cook the beef through without needing to stir.  
5. Once the braising time is up, open the lid and check that the beef is fully cooked (color changed) and the vegetables are soft with released juices. Remove from the heat and serve on a plate.  

## Health Advice  
This dish combines meat and vegetables, offering low fat and high protein. The beef provides high-quality iron and protein, while the baby cabbage and enoki mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss or fitness diet. Sealing the meat with oil during marinating helps retain tenderness, but note that the sauce has a relatively high sodium content. For those sensitive to blood pressure, consider reducing the amount of light soy sauce used.
