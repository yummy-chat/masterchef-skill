# Fruit Bowl Cake  
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer, Autumn  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍠 Water chestnut starch 300g  
💧 Water approx. 600ml (300ml for raw batter, 300ml for sugar water)  
🍬 White sugar 50g  
🥝 Passion fruit approx. 3 (pulp approx. 80g)  
🐉 Dragon fruit as needed  
🍒 Dried cranberries as needed  
🍇 Raisins as needed  
🍋 Lemon juice a little (to prevent discoloration of dragon fruit pulp)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥚 Whisk  
🥄 Strainer  
🍯 Saucepan  
🥄 Spoon  
♨️ Steamer  
🪜 Steaming rack  
🫗 Small glass bowls  
🍢 Bamboo skewers or toothpicks  

## Instructions  
1. Mix 300g water chestnut starch with 300ml water, whisk until no lumps remain, then strain through a strainer to obtain a smooth raw batter.  
**Key point**: Be sure to strain to remove lumps for a finer texture.  

2. In a saucepan, combine 50g white sugar and 300ml water, bring to a boil over high heat.  
**Key point**: The sugar water must be fully boiled.  

3. While the sugar water is boiling, quickly pour it into the raw batter, whisking rapidly to form a translucent raw-cooked batter.  
**Key point**: The sugar water must be boiling and poured in quickly while whisking rapidly; this is the key to success.  

4. Prepare the fruits: Cut open the passion fruit and scoop out about 80g of pulp; mash the dragon fruit pulp into a puree and mix with a little lemon juice to prevent oxidation and discoloration.  
**Key point**: Adding lemon juice to dragon fruit helps keep it fresh.  

5. Divide the prepared raw-cooked batter into two equal portions. Mix one portion with the passion fruit pulp and the other with the dragon fruit puree, stirring each well.  
**Key point**: The fruit ratio can be adjusted to personal preference.  

6. Molding:  
   - Plain flavor: Place dried cranberries and raisins at the bottom of a small bowl, then pour in the plain raw-cooked batter (without added fruit).  
   - Fruit flavor: Pour the passion fruit batter and dragon fruit batter into separate small bowls.  
   **Key point**: Do not fill the bowls too full.  

7. Bring water in the steamer to a boil. Place the small bowls on the steaming rack, cover with the lid, and steam over medium heat for about 10 minutes.  
**Key point**: Steam until the cake has no white center and appears translucent.  

8. Remove the steamed bowl cakes. Let them cool completely until the surface is no longer sticky. Use a bamboo skewer to run along the edge of the bowl, then lift out the bowl cake.  
**Key point**: Must allow to cool completely before unmolding.  

## Health Advice  
This dessert uses water chestnut starch instead of glutinous rice flour, making it easier to digest, low in fat, and refreshing, ideal for cooling down in summer. Dragon fruit and passion fruit are rich in vitamin C and anthocyanins, but they also contain a fair amount of sugar. It is recommended to consume in moderation and control your intake.
