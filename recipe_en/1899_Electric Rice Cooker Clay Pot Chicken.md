# Electric Rice Cooker Clay Pot Chicken
Cuisine: Guangdong Hakka Cuisine

Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Preparation Steps
1. Clean the three-yellow chicken thoroughly and place it in a large glass bowl.
2. Add scallion sections, shredded ginger, salt, white sugar, white pepper powder, sand ginger powder, light soy sauce, and cooking oil.
**Key Point**: Sand ginger powder is crucial for flavor, do not omit.
3. Wearing food gloves, evenly rub and massage all the seasonings over the chicken’s body and inside the cavity for 3-5 minutes.
4. Stuff the remaining scallion, ginger, and chicken feet into the chicken cavity.
5. Place the whole chicken and the remaining marinade from the bowl into a ziplock bag, seal the bag tightly.
**Key Point**: Refrigerate and marinate overnight. If the time is too short, the chicken will be tasteless and the final dish will lack flavor.
6. Pour the marinated chicken along with all the marinade from the bag into the inner pot of the electric rice cooker.
**Note**: Do not add water or extra oil; the chicken will release its own juices and oil.
7. Close the rice cooker lid and press the "Cook" function button.
8. After the first cooking cycle ends, open the lid and carefully flip the chicken with a spatula.
9. After flipping, close the lid again and press the "Cook" function button.
**Key Point**: Pressing the cook button twice is crucial to achieve tender, fall-off-the-bone chicken.
10. The dish is done when the chicken skin turns golden brown and the meat easily separates from the bone when pulled by hand.
11. Remove the cooked chicken, place it on a plate, and pour the remaining oil and juices from the bottom of the rice cooker over the chicken before serving.

## Health Tips
Electric rice cooker clay pot chicken uses sand ginger and low-temperature slow cooking to lock in the juices, making it suitable for those controlling salt intake. Marinating overnight allows the flavor to penetrate deeply into the bones. Note that two cook cycles are required to achieve tender, fall-off-the-bone results.
