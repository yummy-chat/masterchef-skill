# Fried Instant Noodles  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍜 Instant noodles  
🥚 Eggs  
🌭 Ham sausage (optional)  
🧂 Instant noodle seasoning packets (vegetable packet, sauce packet, powder packet)  
🛢️ Cooking oil  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🍳 Pot for boiling  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
🥣 Small bowl  
🥄 Slotted spoon or strainer  
🥄 Spatula  

## Cooking Steps  
1. **Cut ham sausage**: Remove the casing and cut the ham sausage into small cubes, about 1 cm per side.  

2. **Prepare the noodles**: Fill a pot with about 1200 ml of water and bring to a boil over high heat. Add the instant noodle block and cook for **45 seconds**, occasionally lifting and loosening the noodles with chopsticks. Immediately turn off the heat, then scoop out 80 ml of the noodle water and set aside. Drain the noodles and rinse under cold water, then drain thoroughly. **Note**: Do not fully cook the noodles; rinsing with cold water removes surface starch and stops residual cooking.  

3. **Make the sauce**: In a small bowl, squeeze in all the vegetable packet, sauce packet, and **50% to 80%** of the powder packet. Add the reserved 80 ml of hot noodle water and stir well to combine.  

4. **Scramble the eggs**: Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat. Heat the wok, add some cooking oil, pour in the eggs, and scramble for about **20 seconds** until set. Remove and set aside. No need to wash the wok.  

5. **Combine and stir-fry**: Reheat the same wok, add more cooking oil, and stir-fry the ham sausage cubes for **10 seconds**. Add the prepared noodles and stir-fry for **30 seconds**. Pour in the prepared sauce and stir-fry for another **30 seconds**. Finally, add the scrambled eggs and stir-fry for **30 seconds**. Turn off the heat and transfer to a plate.  

**Key Points**  
- Rinsing the noodles with cold water prevents sticking and keeps them chewy.  
- Strictly cook the noodles for **45 seconds** and stop immediately to avoid overcooking.  
- Reduce the amount of powder packet to prevent the dish from becoming too salty.  
- Do not salt the eggs; let the overall seasoning balance the flavor.  
- Optionally, add sesame paste after plating; if too thick, dilute it with water at a 1:1 ratio.  

## Health Tips  
Rinsing the fried instant noodles with cold water prevents sticking, but reserving 80 ml of noodle water for the sauce helps the seasoning blend better. This dish is ideal for busy students or singles; be mindful to reduce the powder packet to avoid excessive sodium intake.
