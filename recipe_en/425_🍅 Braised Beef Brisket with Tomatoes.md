# 🍅 Braised Beef Brisket with Tomatoes
Cuisine: 🏠 Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: 🍂 Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 3-4 tomatoes (about 200g each)
🥩 500g beef brisket (choose one with a good balance of lean and fat for better texture)
🧂 20-30ml cooking oil
🧂 Light soy sauce
🧂 Ground white pepper
🧂 White sugar
🧂 5-10ml cooking wine (Huangjiu)
🧂 Salt
🧂 3 small pieces of star anise
🧅 10g scallions
🧄 10g ginger
🌿 Chopped scallions (for garnish)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Gas stove
🍳 Pressure cooker
🍲 Clay pot
🥘 Regular aluminum pot
🪓 Iron wok
🥢 Chopsticks
🔪 Knife and fork
🥣 Cooking pot

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the beef brisket into strips, then dice into 2 cm cubes. Place in cold water, bring to a boil over high heat, and continue boiling for 2 minutes. Skim off any foam, remove, and rinse clean.
   **Key Point**: Starting with cold water helps fully draw out the blood, and boiling for 2 minutes ensures the gamey smell is removed.

2. In another pot, bring 2L of clean water to a boil. Add the scallion cut into two sections, two slices of ginger, 3 small pieces of star anise, and 5-10ml of cooking wine. Add the blanched beef, cover, and simmer. If using a clay pot, simmer over low heat for 1 hour. If using a pressure cooker, select the stew function for 45 minutes until the beef can be easily pierced with chopsticks.
   **Note**: When using a clay pot or aluminum pot, reduce to medium-low heat after boiling; be familiar with your pressure cooker's operation beforehand.

3. Peel the tomatoes: Cut a cross on the top of each tomato down to the midline. Insert a chopstick or fork into the stem end. Over low heat on the gas stove, rotate the tomato over the flame until the skin wrinkles, then peel it off. Peeled tomatoes have a smooth surface; be careful when cutting them into small pieces.
   **Safety Tip**: Watch out for burns when peeling tomatoes over an open flame, and be cautious of the knife slipping while cutting.

4. Heat oil in a pan until it reaches 70% hot. Add 10g of scallion segments and 10g of ginger slices, stir-fry until fragrant. Then add the peeled and cut tomatoes, stir-fry well until the red juice is released.

5. Pour in the stewed beef brisket and its original broth, ensuring the liquid just covers the meat. Season with salt, white sugar, and light soy sauce to taste, then cover.

6. After bringing to a boil, continue cooking over high heat for 3-5 minutes until the tomato sauce reaches a medium thickness. Turn off the heat, sprinkle with chopped scallions, and serve.

## Health Advice
This dish of braised beef brisket with tomatoes is rich in high-quality protein and lycopene, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts building muscle or for warming up in autumn and winter. However, beef has a relatively high fat content, so portion control is advised. Key to the cooking process is peeling the tomatoes and stir-frying them to release the red juice, which maximizes both nutrition and flavor.
