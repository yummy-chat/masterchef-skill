# Mudslide Drink  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April - August)  

## Ingredients & Condiments  
☕️ Oolong tea, 1 bottle  
🥛 Wangzai milk, 1 bottle  
🍪 Oreo crumbs, 1 pack  
🧊 Ice cubes, as needed  

## Condiments  
None  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥛 Glass cup  
🥄 Stirring spoon  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Take a glass cup, first add 1 pack of Oreo crumbs, then pour in 1 bottle of Wangzai milk.  
2. Next, pour in 1 bottle of oolong tea, and stir thoroughly with a stirring spoon.  
   **Note**: The Oreo crumbs need to be stirred well to ensure they are evenly distributed in the drink, enhancing the texture.  
3. Add an appropriate amount of ice cubes according to personal preference, stir well, and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This mudslide drink combines tea aroma, milk sweetness, and biscuit crumbs, and is high in sugar and calories. It is recommended to control the frequency of consumption, suitable for those who need a quick energy boost or have a sweet tooth.
