# Chaoshan Beef Balls
Cuisine: Cantonese cuisine
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef hind leg meat 1000g (about 2 pounds, thoroughly remove all sinew)
🌾 Tapioca starch 100g
💧 Ice water 400ml (add in 3 batches)
🧈 Beef tallow as needed (optional, for juicy texture)
🧂 Salt about 1 teaspoon (add in two batches)
🌶️ Black pepper to taste
🥜 Satay sauce 1 tablespoon
🐟 Fish sauce 3 tablespoons

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🪓 Cutting board
🔪 Chef's knife
🔨 Iron mallet (about 1.5 kg)
🥣 Large wooden bowl
🍲 Cooking pot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥄 Wooden spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the beef hind leg meat on a cutting board, carefully remove all white sinew with a knife, then cut into small pieces suitable for pounding.
**Key**: The cleaner the sinew removal, the finer the meatball texture.

2. Continuously pound the beef pieces with an iron mallet for about 30 minutes, until the meat is mostly pasty but still has a sinewy texture.
3. Pick out any obvious sinew from the meat paste, add the first teaspoon of salt, and continue pounding for about 30 minutes. During this time, you can pick out sinew while pounding.
**Key**: Adding salt makes it easier to develop gelatin. Total pounding time is about 60-70 minutes.

4. Transfer the pounded meat paste into a large bowl, add black pepper, satay sauce, fish sauce, and tapioca starch. Mix evenly.
5. Add 400ml ice water in 3 batches. Each time, vigorously stir or slap the mixture in one direction until the water is fully absorbed, and the meat paste becomes smooth and sticky. It should be able to be squeezed through the tiger's mouth into smooth balls.
**Key**: Keep the mixture cold throughout the process. Stir in one direction to develop the texture.

6. Boil water in a pot until small bubbles appear at the bottom, then reduce to medium-low heat to maintain the water temperature. Squeeze meatballs from the tiger's mouth and scrape them into the warm water with a wooden spoon.
7. After all meatballs are in the pot, cook over medium heat until they float, then continue cooking for another 3-5 minutes until they turn light gray and expand in size. Remove and serve.

## Health Suggestions
Beef is rich in high-quality protein and iron, low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for muscle building and weight loss. However, the pounding process is time-consuming; it is recommended to pair with vegetables to balance dietary fiber. Pay attention to salt intake; patients with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of fish sauce.
