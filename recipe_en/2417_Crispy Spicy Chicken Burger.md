# Crispy Spicy Chicken Burger
Cuisine Type: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken thighs 3 (about 600g, deboned with skin on)
🌾 Cake flour 200g
🌽 Corn starch 60g
🍞 Baking powder 2g
🥬 Celery appropriate amount (for marinating aroma)
🥕 Carrot appropriate amount (for marinating aroma)
🧅 Onion appropriate amount (for marinating aroma)
🌿 Cilantro roots appropriate amount (for marinating aroma)
🍔 Burger buns (sesame top and bottom buns)
🥬 Lettuce appropriate amount

🧂 Garlic powder (key flavor)
🧂 Salt
🧂 Black pepper powder
🧂 White pepper powder
🍄 Matsutake seasoning
🌶️ Paprika powder
🍶 Hua Diao wine
🥄 Soy sauce
🦪 Oyster sauce
🫒 Peanut oil (a small amount, for sealing)
🍅 Ketchup
🥚 Mayonnaise (egg-based)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🥣 Large bowls (multiple)
🍳 Deep fryer (or deep pot)
🔥 Stove
🥢 Chopsticks

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare the chicken**: Lay the deboned chicken thighs flat and lightly score the thicker parts to help marinate and prevent shrinking during frying.
2. **Marinate the chicken**: Mix the chicken with celery, carrot, onion, cilantro roots, garlic powder, salt, black pepper powder, white pepper powder, matsutake seasoning, paprika powder, Hua Diao wine, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Gently knead until well combined. Finally, drizzle a small amount of peanut oil to seal, and let marinate for 30 minutes.
   - Garlic powder is a core flavor and cannot be omitted; the peanut oil seal locks in moisture.
3. **Prepare the dry flour mix**: Mix 200g cake flour, 60g corn starch, and 2g baking powder evenly.
4. **Divide the flour mix**: Split the dry flour mix into two portions, about 1/3 for dry coating and about 2/3 for the batter.
5. **Prepare the batter**: Add water to the 2/3 flour mix and stir into a thick batter resembling thick yogurt, thick enough to cling to the chicken.
6. **Coating**:
   - Batter: Dip the marinated chicken into the batter and coat evenly.
   - Dry coating: Place into the dry flour mix, press and stack with hands to allow the flour to adhere and form initial scales.
   - Water dip: Quickly dip into clean water, then remove and shake off excess water.
   - Second dry coating: Place back into the dry flour mix and press thoroughly to fully coat, creating rich, flaky scales.
7. **Frying**: Heat oil to medium-low (around 170°C). Gently slide in the coated chicken thighs and fry over medium-low heat for about 6 minutes, until the coating is golden and crispy, with a clear tapping sound when tapped with chopsticks.
   - Do not let the oil get too hot to avoid burning the outside while leaving the inside raw; 6 minutes ensures thorough cooking.
8. **Assemble the burger**: Layer the bottom bun with lettuce, the fried chicken thigh, squeeze on ketchup and mayonnaise, and cover with the top bun.

## Health Note
This homemade crispy spicy chicken burger has relatively moderate calories thanks to the marinating vegetables and seasonings, but the frying and mayonnaise remain the main calorie sources. It is recommended to pair with a large vegetable salad to balance, or enjoy it as an occasional treat. Suitable for those craving a crispy texture without waiting in line, with attention to controlling oil temperature to avoid burning the outside while leaving the inside raw.
