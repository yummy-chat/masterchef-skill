# Braised Eggplant
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍆 Eggplant 1 (cut diagonally into diamond-shaped thick slices)  
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (minced, divided into two batches)  
🧅 Scallion appropriate amount (minced)  
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (minced)  
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount  
🧂 Light soy sauce about two circles  
🧂 Dark soy sauce one drop  
💧 Water a small amount

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife  
📋 Cutting board  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Strainer  
🥄 Spatula

## Instructions
1. Cut the eggplant diagonally into evenly thick diamond-shaped slices. Mince the scallion, ginger, and garlic separately and set aside.
2. Heat cooking oil in a wok over high heat, then reduce to medium-low heat. Add the eggplant slices and slowly pan-fry until both sides are slightly charred. Remove and set aside.
   **Key point**: Eggplant pan-fried until slightly charred has a richer aroma and better texture.
3. Without washing the wok, add a thin layer of oil. Stir-fry the minced scallion, ginger, and part of the minced garlic until fragrant.
4. Return the pan-fried eggplant to the wok. Add about two circles of light soy sauce, one drop of dark soy sauce, and a small amount of water. Stir-fry evenly.
   **Note**: Adding water helps the eggplant braise and absorb flavor. Do not add too much water.
5. Cover and reduce to low heat. Braise for about 120 seconds until the eggplant softens and absorbs the flavor.
6. Remove the lid and increase to high heat to reduce the sauce. When the sauce thickens, add the remaining large amount of minced garlic and stir-fry evenly. Then plate.
   **Key point**: Garlic is added in two batches. The garlic added at the end retains a strong aroma, enhancing the flavor.

## Health Tips
Eggplant is rich in dietary fiber and anthocyanins, but it absorbs oil easily. It is recommended to pre-treat with a small amount of oil over low heat or in a microwave to reduce fat content. Suitable for those who need to control oil intake. Those with a weak and cold spleen and stomach should consume in moderation.
