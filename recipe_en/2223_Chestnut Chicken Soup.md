# Chestnut Chicken Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn (September–October)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐔 Chicken ½ bird (choose fresh crispy chicken; no need to blanch)  
🌰 Chestnuts 1 bag (already shelled, rinsed clean)  
🫚 Ginger a few slices (for removing fishy smell)  
🫘 Red dates 2 pieces (rinsed to enhance aroma)  
🧂 Salt to taste (add in two batches, adjust by tasting)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍲 Clay pot  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Cutting board

## Steps
1. Place the rinsed chicken pieces directly into the clay pot, add chestnuts and ginger slices.  
   **Note**: Fresh chicken does not need to be blanched, preserving the original umami of the broth.
2. Pour in enough water to fully submerge all ingredients.
3. Add two rinsed red dates, cover the clay pot with its lid.
4. Bring to a boil and simmer until the soup is clear, the chicken tender, and the chestnuts soft and powdery.  
   **Key point**: The exact cooking time depends on the ingredient condition; ensure they are thoroughly cooked.
5. Before serving, add salt in two batches, stirring each time and tasting the soup, adjust to the desired saltiness and enjoy.

## Health Advice
Chestnut Chicken Soup is an excellent nourishing dish for autumn. Chestnuts are rich in starch and dietary fiber, which help strengthen the spleen and stomach, while chicken provides high-quality protein. It is suitable for those with weak constitution or who tend to feel cold. Note that chestnuts are relatively high in calories, so consume in moderation if you are on a weight loss plan.
