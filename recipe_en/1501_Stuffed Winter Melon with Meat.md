# Stuffed Winter Melon with Meat
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

---

🍖 **Ingredients**  
- Winter melon  
- Ground pork  
- Egg  
- Scallion  
- Minced scallion and ginger  

🧂 **Seasonings**  
- White pepper powder  
- Light soy sauce  
- Cornstarch  
- Salt  

🍳 **Cookware**  
- Regular iron wok  
- Bowl  
- Plate  

---

📝 **Steps**  
1. Peel the winter melon and cut it into slices approximately 25 cm long and 3 cm thick.  
   **Key point**: Ensure even thickness for easier rolling and uniform doneness during steaming.  

2. Place the cut winter melon slices in a bowl, add 15 g of salt, and rub evenly. Let sit for 10 minutes to soften.  
   **Note**: Do not marinate for too long, or the melon will become overly salty and fragile.  

3. In another bowl, combine ground pork, minced scallion and ginger, 5 g of salt, 5 g of cornstarch, white pepper powder, and light soy sauce. Stir clockwise for about 2 minutes until the filling is firm and the color is uniform.  
   **Key point**: Stirring clockwise increases the stickiness of the filling, making it easier to stuff.  

4. Rinse the softened winter melon slices under running water three times, then drain thoroughly.  
   **Note**: Rinsing removes excess surface salt to prevent the dish from being too salty.  

5. Take one slice of winter melon, roll it up, and stuff the prepared meat filling into the roll. Press firmly.  
   **Key point**: Do not overfill, or the filling may spill out during steaming.  

6. Arrange the stuffed winter melon rolls neatly on a plate, placed along the edge, leaving the center empty.  

7. Crack 1 egg into the empty center of the plate.  
   **Note**: The egg is optional; omit if you dislike the taste.  

8. Bring enough water to a boil in the iron wok, place the plate inside, cover, and steam over high heat for 15 minutes.  

9. Remove the lid and carefully take out the steamed stuffed winter melon (watch for heat). Pour the accumulated liquid from the plate into a small pot.  

10. Add water mixed with cornstarch and 50 ml of water to the liquid in the pot. Stir well and bring to a boil until the sauce thickens and becomes glossy.  
    **Key point**: Premix the cornstarch slurry thoroughly, and stir while pouring to prevent lumps.  

11. Evenly pour the prepared sauce over the stuffed winter melon rolls.  

## Health Tips
Stuffed winter melon with meat is low in fat and high in protein. Winter melon helps clear heat and promote urination, making it ideal for weight loss periods or summer consumption. It is recommended to use lean meat for the filling to reduce fat intake.
