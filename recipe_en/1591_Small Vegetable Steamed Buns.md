# Small Vegetable Steamed Buns  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥕 1 carrot (finely shredded)  
🥒 1 zucchini (finely shredded)  
🍄 Dried wood ear mushrooms (soaked in advance, shredded)  
🥚 2-3 eggs (scrambled and crumbled)  
🦐 Dried shrimp (for umami flavor)  
🥟 Wonton wrappers (as needed)  
🧂 Salt (for drawing water out of vegetables)  
🥄 Light soy sauce (to taste)  
🥄 Oyster sauce (to taste)  
🫒 Sesame oil (to taste)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Strainer  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
♨️ Steamer  
🍽️ Perforated steaming tray  
🖌️ Silicone brush  
🥄 Spoon  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps  
1. **Prepare Vegetables**: Finely shred the carrot and zucchini into even strips. Place them in a bowl, add salt, and mix well. Let them sit for 10 minutes to draw out moisture.  
   **Key Point**: Use salt to extract excess water from the vegetables, ensuring the filling stays dry.  

2. **Squeeze Out Water**: Using your hands or a strainer, thoroughly squeeze the salted vegetable shreds to remove excess liquid.  

3. **Prepare Other Ingredients**: Shred the soaked wood ear mushrooms. Scramble the eggs until tender and crumble them into small pieces.  
   **Key Point**: Scramble the eggs until soft and evenly crumbled.  

4. **Make the Filling**: In a bowl, combine the squeezed vegetable shreds, shredded wood ear mushrooms, crumbled eggs, and dried shrimp. Add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Mix thoroughly until well combined.  
   **Key Point**: All ingredients must be mixed evenly to allow the flavors to fully blend.  

5. **Assemble the Buns**: Take one wonton wrapper and place a suitable amount of filling in the center. Fold it in half, then pinch the two sides together toward the center to form a ingot shape. Ensure the seal is tight.  

6. **Arrange for Steaming**: Lightly brush the steaming tray with a thin layer of oil. Place the assembled buns on the tray, leaving some space between each to prevent sticking.  

7. **Steam**: Bring water in the steamer to a boil. Place the steaming tray inside, cover tightly, and steam over high heat for about 10 minutes, until the wrappers become translucent and slightly puffed up. Remove from heat and serve.  

## Health Tips  
This vegetable steamed bun is made with a rich vegetable filling, low in calories and high in fiber, making it a suitable staple for people on a weight-loss or weight-management diet. However, wonton wrappers are refined carbohydrates, so it is recommended to pair them with a bowl of soup or an appropriate amount of protein to avoid blood sugar spikes when eaten alone. Dried shrimp and oyster sauce are used to enhance umami, but since salt is already used to draw moisture from the vegetables, be cautious with the amount of light soy sauce to prevent excessive sodium intake.
