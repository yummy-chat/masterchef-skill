# Lemon Paste
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍋 4 lemons (about 500g)
🍬 500g (1 lb) yellow rock sugar

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔌 Electric rice cooker
🥣 Glass bowl
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife
🥄 Measuring cup
🥄 Slotted spoon (without holes, for draining lemons)
🥢 Chopsticks
🍶 Glass jar with airtight lid

## Instructions
1. Place the lemons in a glass bowl, add 3 tablespoons of salt and enough water. Rub the lemon skin with your hands, soak for 10 minutes, then discard the salted water and rinse thoroughly with clean water.
   **Note**: Rubbing with salt and soaking effectively removes wax and impurities.

2. Cut off both ends of the lemons, slice them thinly, and remove the seeds from each slice one by one.
   **Key**: Removing seeds is a crucial step to prevent the final product from becoming bitter.

3. In the inner pot of the electric rice cooker, first layer some lemon slices, then sprinkle a layer of yellow rock sugar. Continue alternating layers until all lemon slices and rock sugar are used.

4. Close the lid of the electric rice cooker, press the "Keep Warm" setting, and keep warm for about 2 hours until the yellow rock sugar is completely melted.
   **Note**: This stage allows the rock sugar to melt slowly and extract lemon juice.

5. Once the rock sugar has melted, close the lid again and continue using the "Keep Warm" setting for about 18 hours, until the color deepens and the paste gradually thickens.
   **Key**: The long, slow warming process is essential for forming a thick paste and developing the flavor.

6. Open the lid, press the "Cook" setting to reduce the liquid. Once the liquid starts boiling with many bubbles, close the lid and continue cooking until the rice cooker automatically switches back to the "Keep Warm" setting.
   **Tip**: The automatic switch indicates that the liquid has been reduced to the ideal consistency.

7. First, arrange the cooked lemon slices at the bottom of a clean, dry glass jar, then pour in the dark brown, viscous liquid. Seal the jar for storage.
   **Note**: Ensure the jar is free of water and oil for better preservation.

## Health Advice
Lemons are rich in vitamin C, and yellow rock sugar is known for moistening the lungs and relieving coughs, making this preparation suitable for soothing throat discomfort during seasonal transitions. For those with high blood sugar or those managing weight, consume in small amounts or substitute with an alternative sweetener.
