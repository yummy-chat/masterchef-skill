# Honey-Glazed Roasted Sweet Potato Slices
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍠 Sweet potatoes, to taste (choose long, slender varieties)
🥚 Eggs, 2
🌾 Cornstarch, 2 tablespoons
🧂 Baking powder, a pinch
🥛 Sweetened condensed milk, to taste (optional)
🍬 White sugar, to taste (optional, as a substitute for condensed milk)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board and chef's knife
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Mixing tool (chopsticks or whisk)
📜 Baking tray and parchment paper/aluminum foil
🔥 Oven

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the sweet potatoes thoroughly and peel off the skin.
   **Note**: The sweet potatoes must be peeled to ensure a smooth texture.

2. Cut the peeled sweet potatoes into slices 0.5-1 cm thick.
   **Note**: The slices must maintain a certain thickness; if too thin, they will become dry and hard inside after baking. Thicker slices achieve a crispy exterior and soft interior.

3. Prepare the egg batter: In a bowl, crack 2 eggs, add 2 tablespoons of cornstarch and a pinch of baking powder, and stir until it reaches a thick yogurt-like consistency. If using condensed milk or white sugar, add them at this step and stir again to combine.
   **Note**: The thickness of the batter is key; if too thin, it won't adhere evenly to the sweet potato slices.

4. Line the baking tray with parchment paper or aluminum foil to prevent sticking.

5. Add the cut sweet potato slices to the egg batter and gently toss to ensure each slice is evenly coated.
   **Note**: Make sure every sweet potato slice is thoroughly coated with the batter; none should be missed.

6. Arrange the coated sweet potato slices in a single layer on the baking tray, leaving proper space between each slice.

7. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F) for about 10 minutes.

8. Place the baking tray on the middle rack of the oven and bake at 190°C (375°F) with top and bottom heat for about 25 minutes, until the surface turns a golden caramel color.
   **Note**: Monitor closely during the final baking stage, as actual oven temperatures vary. Remove immediately when the surface turns golden brown and slightly caramelized to avoid burning.

## Health Advice
Sweet potatoes are rich in dietary fiber and beta-carotene. Combined with egg protein, they offer balanced nutrition, making this a healthy snack or light meal. Control the amount of condensed milk and white sugar added to avoid blood sugar fluctuations; keep slice thickness at 0.5-1 cm for a crispy exterior and soft interior.
