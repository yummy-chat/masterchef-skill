# Smoked Plum Juice (Wumei Zhi)
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍑 Smoked plum 50 grams (non-smoked optional)
🍒 Hawthorn 35 grams
🍊 Tangerine peel 15 grams
🌿 Licorice 6 grams
💧 Water 500 ml (for soaking)
💧 Water 3500 ml (for boiling)
🍬 Rock sugar 250 grams

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🍲 Stainless steel large pot
🥄 Wooden spoon
🥅 Strainer or slotted spoon
🫖 Glass water pitcher
🥃 Small glass cups

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the smoked plums, hawthorn, tangerine peel, and licorice into a bowl. Rinse several times with water, then drain.
2. Add 500 ml of water to the bowl and soak for 30 minutes to fully soften the ingredients.
3. Pour the soaked ingredients along with the soaking water into a stainless steel pot. Then add 3500 ml of water.
4. Bring to a boil over high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to the lowest setting, cover with a lid, and keep at a gentle simmer for 40 minutes.
5. Turn off the heat and immediately add the rock sugar. Stir with a wooden spoon, allowing the residual heat to fully dissolve the sugar.
6. Let the smoked plum juice cool to room temperature.
7. Use a strainer to remove all solid ingredients.
8. Place the strainer over a glass pitcher and pour the juice through to filter it into the pitcher.
9. Cover the pitcher and refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Serve chilled.
   **Note**: The flavor becomes more balanced and refreshing after chilling. It can also be enjoyed warm.

## Health Advice
Smoked plum juice is sour and sweet, helping to produce body fluids. It is suitable for summer to cool down and stimulate the appetite, especially for those with poor appetite or dry mouth. Control the amount of rock sugar used; diabetics should reduce or avoid the sugar.
