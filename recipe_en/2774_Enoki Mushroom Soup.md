# Enoki Mushroom Soup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍄 Enoki mushrooms 400-500 grams
🥚 Egg (optional)
🧂 Salt 15 grams
🧂 MSG 5 grams
🫒 Sesame oil or sesame oil 3-4 drops (added after removing from heat, optional)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife or fruit knife
🍳 Pot
🥄 Stirring tool (such as chopsticks or spoon)

## Cooking Steps
1. Gently separate the enoki mushrooms by hand, making them as loose as possible. Wash them thoroughly and drain well.
2. Cut the enoki mushrooms into segments with a knife, no longer than 5 cm. The length can be adjusted according to personal preference.
3. Place the cut enoki mushrooms into a pot and add water. The water level should be about 1.1 times the height of the ingredients. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and cook for 3 minutes.
4. Add MSG and salt, and stir until well combined.
5. Continue heating for about 30 seconds, then turn off the heat and transfer the soup to a bowl.
6. If adding an egg, after the water comes to a boil in step 3, slowly pour the beaten egg into the pot. When the egg flowers float to the surface, turn off the heat. After removing from the heat, add a few drops of sesame oil or sesame oil for fragrance.

## Health Tips
Enoki mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber and B vitamins, low in calories, making them suitable for people on a weight-loss diet or those needing to improve bowel movements. Be careful not to overcook them to avoid nutrient loss. Thoroughly separating the mushrooms before cutting into segments aids digestion.
