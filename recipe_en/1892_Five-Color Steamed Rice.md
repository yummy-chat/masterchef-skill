# Five-Color Steamed Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍚 Rice, appropriate amount (cooked white rice)
🥩 Ground meat, appropriate amount (pork or chicken)
🐟 Fish bone, about one piece (for flavor and calcium supplementation)
🥬 Vegetable puree, appropriate amount (dark green, such as spinach or broccoli puree)
🍄 Mushrooms / meatball products, appropriate amount (shaped to be cute)
🥔 Chinese yam, appropriate amount (peeled into thin slices)
🌭 Sausage, about one link
🥚 Egg, 1 piece
🍖 Spare ribs, appropriate amount
🧪 Probiotic dried products, appropriate amount

**Seasonings**: No additional seasonings added; relies on the natural flavors of the ingredients.

## Cookware & Equipment
🥣 Bowl
♨️ Steamer
🥄 Spoon
🥢 Chopsticks

## Preparation Steps
1. Take the cooked white rice and divide it into two bowls to serve as the base for the steamed rice.
2. Layer the ground meat, fish bone, vegetable puree, mushrooms / meatball products, the peeled thin slices of Chinese yam, probiotic dried products, and sausage on top of the rice in order, and crack a raw egg on the surface.
   **Notes**: The Chinese yam must be peeled into thin slices with a peeler to ensure it cooks through during steaming. The ingredients cover staples, protein, vegetables, and nutritional supplements, offering a balanced combination.
3. Place the bowls with the ingredients into the steamer and steam over high heat until all ingredients are fully cooked.
   **Doneness check**: The egg is set, the ground meat changes color and is cooked through internally, and the rice is heated thoroughly.
   **Key point**: Ensure all ingredients reach a safe eating temperature, especially the ground meat and Chinese yam, which must be fully steamed.
4. Once steamed, remove the bowls and use chopsticks to thoroughly mix all the ingredients in the bowl with the rice, allowing the flavors to blend together, resulting in a moist, soft, and easy-to-eat consistency.

## Health Tips
This five-color steamed rice features a variety of ingredients, making it suitable for children or elderly individuals who need balanced nutrition. Probiotics support gut health, and fish bone provides calcium supplementation. Note that the Chinese yam and ground meat must be fully steamed to prevent digestive issues.
