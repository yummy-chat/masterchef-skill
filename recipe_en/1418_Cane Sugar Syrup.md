# Cane Sugar Syrup
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧂 Granulated sugar 100g  
💧 Drinking water 100g  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
⚖️ Digital scale  
🫙 Sealable container  
🥄 Mixing tool  

## Preparation Steps
1. Place 100g granulated sugar and 100g drinking water in a sealable container, and stir until fully dissolved.  
   **Key point**: The sugar-to-water ratio is 1:1, making it easy to calculate sugar content by weight. If needed urgently, use hot water to dissolve, then cool into syrup.  
2. Seal tightly and store in the refrigerator.  
   **Key point**: It can generally be kept in the refrigerator for up to one week without spoiling.  

## Health Advice
Cane sugar syrup is essentially liquid sugar, suitable for precise measurement and flavoring in baking or beverages. However, it is high in calories and has a high glycemic index. Diabetic patients or those who need to control sugar intake should use it with caution. It is recommended to pair it with naturally acidic ingredients (such as lemon juice) to balance the sweetness and avoid consuming the syrup directly on its own.
