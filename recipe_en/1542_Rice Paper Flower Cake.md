# Rice Paper Flower Cake
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Rice paper sheets, about 300+ pieces  
🍰 1 chiffon cake base (6 inches)  
🥛 Whipping cream, as needed  
🍬 Sugar, as needed  
🍪 Oreo cookie crumbs, as needed  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🪵 Wooden board, small bowl, water, brush  
🥄 Electric hand mixer  
🪣 Stainless steel mixing bowl  
🍰 Round cake pan (6 inches)  
🔥 Oven  
🎂 Cake turntable  
🔪 Offset spatula  
📦 Cake box (with drawer)  

## Instructions
1. Make the rice paper flowers: Dip the edges of rice paper sheets in water to moisten them. Using the adhesive property of water, stack and roll the sheets one by one, shaping them into roses or other flower forms. Let them sit and dry completely after shaping, avoiding contact with moisture.  
   **Key point**: Flowers must be prepared in advance and fully dried and set before use.  
2. Bake the chiffon cake base: Pour the cake batter into the pan and bake in the oven. Once done, immediately invert the cake and let it cool completely before unmolding.  
   **Note**: Adjust baking temperature and time according to a standard chiffon cake recipe.  
3. Make the Oreo salted cream: Whip the whipping cream with sugar until stiff peaks form, then fold in the Oreo cookie crumbs.  
   **Key point**: The cream for the filling must be whipped sufficiently to provide enough support and prevent the cake from collapsing.  
4. Assemble the cake: Unmold the cooled chiffon cake and slice it into layers. Stack in the following order: a layer of cake, a layer of Oreo salted cream, another layer of cake, then a layer of cream, repeating as needed.  
5. Frost the cake: Use the whipped cream to evenly smooth the entire surface of the cake.  
6. Decorate: Attach the pre-made rice paper flowers closely around the sides and top of the cake to form a "bouquet" shape.  
   **Key point**: Assemble the flowers immediately after frosting to prevent the rice paper from absorbing moisture and losing its shape.  
7. Package: Carefully transfer the finished cake into the cake box.  

## Health Advice
Rice paper flower cake is best enjoyed fresh, and the rice paper decorations should be kept away from moisture. The chiffon cake paired with Oreo salted cream is relatively high in calories, so enjoy in moderation if you are on a diet.
