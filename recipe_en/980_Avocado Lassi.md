# Avocado Lassi
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥑 Ripe avocado  
🍶 Plain yogurt  
🥛 Cold milk  
🍯 Honey  
🌿 Cardamom powder (optional)  
🌱 Mint leaves or crushed nuts (optional)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥤 Blender (high-speed blender/juicer)  

## Instructions
1. Cut the ripe avocado in half, remove the pit, peel off the skin, and cut the flesh into small pieces. Set aside.  
2. Add 120ml plain yogurt, the chopped avocado, 1 tablespoon of honey, and 60ml cold milk to the blender.  
3. Optional: Add a pinch of cardamom powder to enhance the flavor.  
4. Start the blender and blend for about 1 minute until the liquid is completely smooth and thick.  
5. Pour the blended drink into a glass.  
6. Optional: Garnish the top with a few mint leaves or sprinkle 5g of chopped nuts for decoration.  

## Health Advice
Avocados are rich in healthy fats and dietary fiber. Paired with yogurt and milk, this beverage provides additional calcium and protein, making it suitable for those who need an energy boost or are managing blood sugar levels. Be mindful of the amount of honey used to avoid excess sugar intake affecting health.
