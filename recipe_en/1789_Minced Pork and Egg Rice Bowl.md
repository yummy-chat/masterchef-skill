# Minced Pork and Egg Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Rice (cooked white rice, prepared in advance)
🥩 Minced pork (can be substituted with minced beef)
🥚 Eggs 2-3 (beaten into egg liquid)
🧅 Onion about 1 bowl (diced)
🥦 Broccoli appropriate amount
🦐 Sweet shrimp meat appropriate amount (peeled shrimp in the video)
🍋 Lemon 2 (cut in half, for garnish)
🧄 Minced garlic appropriate amount
🧂 Vinegar appropriate amount (mixed with white sugar to make simple sushi vinegar)
🍬 White sugar appropriate amount
🍯 Oyster sauce appropriate amount
🧂 Salt appropriate amount
🈶 Salad dressing appropriate amount
🍘 Dried seaweed flakes appropriate amount
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🥄 Wooden spatula/wooden spoon
🍚 Rice paddle
🥣 Small milk pot
🥢 Egg beater/chopsticks
🫗 Glass lid
⚡️ Rice cooker inner pot (for mixing sushi rice)
🍲 Wok (for stir-frying broccoli)

## Instructions
**Preparation Stage**
1. Mix vinegar with white sugar, pour into freshly cooked hot rice, and mix well with a rice paddle to make simple sushi rice.
2. Mince the pork, dice the onion, wash and break broccoli into small florets, beat the eggs into egg liquid, and mince the garlic.
3. Prepare sweet shrimp meat, dried seaweed flakes, salad dressing, lemon, and other garnishes.

**Cooking Stage**
1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat, add cooking oil, then add minced pork and stir-fry until it changes color, loosens, and becomes fragrant.
2. Add diced onion and stir-fry until softened and translucent. Add oyster sauce and stir-fry evenly.
3. Reduce heat to low, evenly pour the egg liquid over the minced pork, immediately cover with a lid, and steam until the egg liquid is fully set, forming a minced pork and egg cake. Remove from heat and cut into pieces in the pan.
4. In another wok, heat oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Add broccoli and a small amount of water, cover with a lid, and steam until the broccoli is cooked through, bright green, and the liquid has evaporated. Season with salt, stir-fry, and remove from heat.
5. The sweet shrimp meat is ready-to-eat; place it directly into a bowl for later use.
6. Assemble and plate: Place the mixed seaweed sushi rice into a bowl, top with the cut minced pork and egg pieces, arrange the cooked broccoli and sweet shrimp meat on the side, add a lemon wedge, and finally drizzle with salad dressing.

## Health Tips
This dish combines protein, vegetables, and carbohydrates, offering balanced nutrition, ideal for a quick energy boost after a busy day. It is recommended to control the amount of salad dressing to avoid excess calories; the combination of shrimp and lemon can increase vitamin C intake.
