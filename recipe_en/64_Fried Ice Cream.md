# Fried Ice Cream
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍞 Bread slices (pumpkin flavor or other) 2 slices  
🍦 Ice cream (vanilla, chocolate, etc.) 1 scoop (must be frozen solid)  
🥚 Egg(s) appropriate amount (beaten into egg wash)  
🍞 Breadcrumbs appropriate amount  
🥫 Canned fruit (yellow peach, pineapple, etc.) appropriate amount  
🍪 Crushed cookies (Oreo crumbs, etc.) appropriate amount  
🍬 Colorful sprinkles appropriate amount  
🍓 Jam appropriate amount  
🧁 Whipped cream (aerosol can) appropriate amount  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥢 Rolling pin  
🍨 Ice cream scoop  
🥛 Round glass cup (or mold)  
🍳 Frying pan (or deep pot)  
🥄 Slotted spoon (strainer)  
🌡️ Thermometer  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🍽️ Serving plate  

## Instructions  
1. Use a rolling pin to flatten and press each of the two bread slices firmly, expelling air to form a dense insulating layer.  
2. Take out the pre-frozen ice cream and scoop it into a ball using an ice cream scoop; beat the egg(s) into egg wash, and prepare the breadcrumbs and decorative toppings.  
3. Place one flattened bread slice, center the ice cream ball on it, cover with the other bread slice, and press down vertically with a round glass cup or mold to cut off the excess edges while sealing the edges tightly so the top and bottom slices are completely closed.  
   **Note**: Ensure the edges are fully sealed to prevent leakage during frying.  
4. Evenly coat the sealed patty with egg wash, then roll it in breadcrumbs to coat thoroughly, gently pressing to ensure the coating adheres firmly.  
5. Pour cooking oil into the pot and heat to 150–160°C. Use a slotted spoon to lower the patty into the oil, gently flipping it to ensure even heating on the surface.  
   **Key**: Maintain medium-low heat. Observe the outer crust—once no large bubbles appear on the surface and it turns golden brown overall, remove it immediately to avoid the ice cream melting from over-frying.  
6. Place the fried ice cream on a plate, arrange canned fruit around it, drizzle with jam, pipe on whipped cream, sprinkle with crushed cookies and colorful sprinkles, and serve immediately to experience the contrast of crispy hot and icy cold.  

## Health Advice  
Enjoy while hot to experience the contrast of a crispy outer shell and a cool, icy interior. Suitable for those who enjoy creative desserts, but it is high in calories, so consume in moderation.
