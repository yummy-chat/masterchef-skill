# Tiramisu
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧀 Mascarpone cheese 250g
🥚 Eggs (regular) 2 (separated yolks)
🍪 Ladyfingers as needed
🥛 Heavy cream approx. 120g
☕ Coffee beans (medium-dark roast) as needed (freshly brewed espresso)
🍫 Dark chocolate 200g (for the crispy coating)
🧈 Cocoa butter 70g
🥜 Crushed nuts 50g
🍫 Cocoa powder as needed (for top decoration)
🍬 White sugar approx. 20g (for egg yolk mixture)
💧 Water approx. 10g

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Mixing bowl
🔌 Electric hand mixer
🥄 Whisk/spatula
🍳 Small saucepan
🔥 Stove
🫗 Strainer/sieve
☕ Moka pot
🥤 Disposable paper cups (for shaping)
🍽️ Shallow dish
🥢 Bamboo skewer (for dipping coating)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare coffee liquid and coating liquid**: Brew espresso with a moka pot and pour into a bowl to cool. Melt the dark chocolate and cocoa butter together, mix in the crushed nuts, and pour into a deeper container for later use.
2. **Prepare mascarpone**: Place mascarpone cheese in a large bowl and stir with a spatula until smooth and lump-free.
3. **Make egg yolk mixture**: Add egg yolks, white sugar, and water to a small saucepan and stir to combine.
4. **Heat egg yolk mixture**: Place over the stove on the lowest heat. Stir quickly and continuously until the mixture becomes very thick and can cling to the spatula without dripping. Remove from heat immediately.
   **Key point**: Stir quickly to prevent the yolks from curdling; heating to thicken is essential for sterilization and success.
5. **Strain and combine**: Strain the heated egg yolk mixture through a sieve into the smooth mascarpone cheese and mix well.
6. **Whip heavy cream**: Whip the heavy cream until it reaches a yogurt-like consistency.
7. **Combine batters**: Add the whipped cream to the cheese and egg yolk mixture. Fold gently until evenly combined to form the tiramisu batter.
8. **Assemble**: Place a layer of ladyfingers dipped in coffee liquid at the bottom of a disposable paper cup. Pour in a layer of batter. Add another layer of coffee-dipped ladyfingers, then pour in the remaining batter to fill the cup.
9. **Freeze to set**: Place the paper cups in the freezer until completely solid.
10. **Unmold and coat**: Remove the frozen tiramisu, tear off the paper cup, insert a bamboo skewer into the bottom, and dip it into the chocolate and nut coating liquid. Evenly coat with a layer of the crispy coating.
    **Key point**: Must be fully frozen and set to unmold and coat smoothly.
11. **Decorate**: Place the coated tiramisu on a plate and sift a layer of cocoa powder over the top. Let it sit in the refrigerator to soften before serving for a better texture.

## Health Tips
It is recommended to control sugar intake; the white sugar in the egg yolk mixture can be halved or replaced with a sugar substitute, suitable for those with high blood sugar. Eggs must be thoroughly heated for sterilization, making it safer for the elderly and children.
