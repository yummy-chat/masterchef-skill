# Spicy Beef Soup
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef brisket with tendons and skin 500g
🥩 Beef leg meat (lean) 250g
🍎 Apple 1/2 (about 100g, peeled, cored, and cut into pieces)
🧅 Large green onion 80g (20g cut into sections for stewing, 60g sliced diagonally for garnish)
🫚 Ginger 10g (sliced)
🧄 Garlic 15g (pressed and minced)
🥕 White radish 60g (peeled and sliced thinly)
🧅 Onion 50g (shredded)
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms 50g (stems removed and sliced)
🌿 Dried fernbrake 30g (cooked, squeezed dry, and cut into sections)
🌶️ Green chili pepper 30g (sliced diagonally)
🥚 Egg 1 (added just before serving)
🧂 Chili powder 15g (1.5 tablespoons)
🧂 Light soy sauce 20g (2 tablespoons)
🧂 White pepper powder 0.5g
🧂 Beef seasoning powder 10g (1 level tablespoon, Korean-style seasoning)
🧂 Chicken bouillon 3g (1 tablespoon)
🧂 Salt about 3g (added to taste at the end)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Knife, cutting board
🍳 Frying pan
♨️ Electric pressure cooker/pressure cooker
🥘 Clay pot
🥣 Large bowl
🥄 Slotted spoon

## Instructions
1. Soak the beef brisket and beef leg meat in cold water for 1 hour in advance to remove blood. Place in cold water, bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, remove, and rinse clean with warm water.
2. Put the blanched beef into a pressure cooker, add the green onion sections, ginger slices, and apple pieces. Add water to the maximum water level line, cover the lid, and select the "Beef and Mutton" function to stew until tender.
   **Key point**: Large pieces of meat can be cut in half to shorten the time; if using a regular pot, simmer over medium-low heat for about 90 minutes.
3. While the meat is stewing, prepare the dried fernbrake: rinse in clean water, then place in boiling water and cook over high heat for 10 minutes. Remove, rinse and rub repeatedly under running water, squeezing dry about 3 times until the water is clear and odorless. Squeeze out the water, cut into sections, and set aside.
4. Prepare the other vegetables: slice the shiitake mushrooms, slice the large green onion diagonally, shred the onion, slice the white radish thinly, slice the green chili pepper diagonally, and press and mince the garlic. Mix all the vegetables together.
5. Once the meat is stewed, remove it and strain out any residue from the soup. Pour about 1000 ml of the original broth into a clay pot and bring to a boil over high heat. Hand-tear the beef leg meat into shreds and cut the beef brisket into strips.
6. When the soup boils again, add all the vegetables and both types of shredded/strip-cut meat. Add the chili powder, reduce the heat to medium-low, cover with a lid leaving a gap, and cook for 5 minutes.
7. Turn the heat to high, add the light soy sauce, white pepper powder, beef seasoning powder, and chicken bouillon, and stir to combine. Taste and add salt as needed.
8. Crack an egg into the bubbling center of the soup without stirring. Once the egg white has set, turn off the heat. Garnish with green and red chili pepper rings. Before eating, the egg yolk can be broken and stirred into the soup.

## Health Advice
This spicy beef soup is high in protein and low in fat, suitable for warming up in winter or recovery after exercise. Be mindful to control the amount of chili powder and sodium; it should be consumed with caution by those with high blood pressure. The beef with tendons and skin is stewed until tender and rich in collagen; consume in moderation.
