# Summer Fruit Pickle
Cuisine: Taiwanese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍐 Guava, as needed (cut into thick slices with skin on)
🍋 Lemon, 3 slices
🫐 Sour plums, 5 pieces
🍋‍🟩 Small limes, 5 (halved, juice squeezed)
🍬 White sugar, 2 tbsp (for marinating)
🧂 Salt, 1 tbsp (for marinating)
🍭 Rock sugar, as needed (for making sugar water)
💧 Cool boiled water, 300g
🍑 Sour plum powder, 25g

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl/container
🍳 Small milk pot
⚖️ Measuring cup
🔪 Kitchen knife and cutting board
🫙 Plastic wrap

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the whole guava thoroughly, keep the skin on, and cut directly into thick slices.
**Key point**: Keeping the skin on makes the texture crisper and enhances the color.

2. Add 2 tbsp white sugar and 1 tbsp salt to the guava slices, mix well, and marinate for 20 minutes to draw out water.
**Key point**: Marinating firms up the guava texture and removes raw, astringent flavors.

3. Pour water into a small milk pot, add rock sugar, and heat over low heat until the rock sugar is completely dissolved to make sugar water. Set aside to cool.
4. In a glass bowl, pour 300g of cool boiled water. Add lemon slices, sour plums, squeezed small lime juice, cooled sugar water, and 25g sour plum powder. Stir well to make the seasoning liquid.
5. Lightly rinse the marinated guava slices with a small amount of purified water, then discard the excess salty-sweet liquid.
**Key point**: Rinsing removes excess saltiness and sweetness from the surface, preventing the final product from being too salty.

6. Pour the rinsed guava slices into the seasoning liquid. Seal the container with plastic wrap and refrigerate.
**Note**: Chilling overnight yields the best flavor, allowing the fruit to fully absorb the seasoning.

7. When serving, take out the guava slices, place them on a plate, and drizzle with an appropriate amount of the seasoning liquid.

## Health Advice
This summer fruit pickle is rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber, making it suitable for relieving heat and cutting grease. However, it has a high sugar content. It is recommended to control the portion size. Diabetic patients or those managing sugar intake should consume with caution.
