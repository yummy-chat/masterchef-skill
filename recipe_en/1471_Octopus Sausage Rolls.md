# Octopus Sausage Rolls
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌯 Hand-grasp flatbread skin, about 2-3 sheets (thawed and cut into thin strips)  
🌭 Sausages, about 5-6 pieces  
🥚 Egg, 1 piece (only the yolk, used as wash)  
🍬 White sugar pearls, 4 pieces (for eye decoration)  
🍶 Mayonnaise, as needed (squeezed on the head to attach sugar pearls)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
♨️ Air fryer  
📄 Baking paper  
🔪 Knife  
🖌️ Silicone brush

## Instructions
1. Thaw the hand-grasp flatbread skin and cut it into thin long strips. Take one sausage and tightly wrap it with a flatbread strip in a spiral around the middle, leaving one end as the head, and cut the other end into several thin strips to form tentacles.  
   **Note**: The flatbread strips must be wrapped tightly and the tentacles cut evenly.
2. Line the air fryer with baking paper and place the shaped octopus inside. Use a silicone brush to evenly coat each surface with egg yolk wash.
3. Set the air fryer to 160°C and bake for 15 minutes.  
   **Check**: The flatbread skin should be golden and crispy.
4. Remove the baked octopus, and while still hot, squeeze an appropriate amount of mayonnaise onto the head and attach white sugar pearls as eyes.
5. Finish and plate.

## Health Advice
This dish combines sausages with hand-grasp flatbread skin and is relatively high in calories. Suitable for occasional consumption, not for regular meals. The egg yolk wash enhances color, but the mayonnaise and sugar pearls increase sugar intake; use them in moderation.
