# Cheese Crispy Sticks 🧀
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧀 Mozzarella cheese 100g (finely chopped and set aside)
🥛 Milk 450g
🍬 Sugar 60g
🧂 Salt 10g
🌾 Bread flour 90g
🧈 Butter 110g
🥔 Potato starch 430g
🥚 Eggs 3
🥥 Cocoa powder (optional)
🍵 Matcha powder (optional)
🧀 Golden cheese powder (optional)

## Utensils & Equipment
🔪 Knife and cutting board
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥄 Silicone spatula
🥣 Bowl
🫖 Piping bag
🍞 Baking tray (lined with parchment paper)
🔥 Oven (recommended 38L or above, with independent upper and lower temperature control)

## Steps
1.  Finely chop the mozzarella cheese into as small pieces as possible and set aside.
    **Key point**: The finer the cheese pieces, the easier they blend into the batter and pass through the piping bag.

2.  In a non-stick pan, add milk, butter, sugar, salt, and bread flour. Heat over low heat, stirring continuously, until the mixture becomes a thick, smooth, lump-free paste, then remove from heat.
    **Key point**: This is the "flour scalding" step. Heating allows the flour to fully absorb liquid, creating a crisp, porous interior in the finished product.

3.  Gradually add the potato starch to the hot paste, mixing thoroughly with a silicone spatula until a smooth dough forms.
    **Key point**: Potato starch is essential for a crispy texture; add it in batches for easier mixing.

4.  Once the dough has cooled to warm (not hot to the touch), add the eggs one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition until fully absorbed and the batter becomes smooth and glossy.
    **Key point**: The dough must be cooled to prevent cooking the eggs; adding eggs gradually ensures proper emulsification. The batter should be slightly thinner than choux pastry batter.

5.  Add all the chopped mozzarella cheese to the batter and mix evenly to create the plain batter.
    **Key point**: Ensure the cheese pieces are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

6.  For other flavors, divide the plain batter into portions and mix in appropriate amounts of cocoa powder, matcha powder, or golden cheese powder until smooth.

7.  Transfer the batter to a piping bag. Snip a small opening and pipe uniform long strips onto the parchment-lined baking tray.
    **Key point**: If the batter is too soft due to room temperature, refrigerate briefly before piping for easier shaping. Each batch of batter can make about 2 trays.

8.  Preheat the oven. Place the tray in the middle rack and bake at 140°C for about 30 minutes, or until the sticks are dry, set, and lightly browned on the edges.
    **Key point**: A professional oven with a capacity of 38L or above and independent temperature control for upper and lower heating elements is recommended for even baking. If using an air fryer, bake at 150°C for about 18 minutes, then flip and bake for another 10 minutes.

## Health Advice
This cheese crispy stick is relatively high in calories and is best enjoyed as an occasional snack, especially suitable for those needing a quick energy boost. Consider controlling the amount of sugar and salt to reduce sodium and sugar intake.
