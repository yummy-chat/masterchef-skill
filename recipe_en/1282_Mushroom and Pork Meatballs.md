# Mushroom and Pork Meatballs
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Suggestion: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐖 Pork leg 500g (trimmed of fat and sinew, using only lean meat)
🍄 Dried shiitake mushrooms appropriate amount (pre-soaked, squeezed dry)
🧊 Ice cubes 250g
💧 Water appropriate amount
🧄 Fried garlic appropriate amount
🌱 Chopped scallions appropriate amount
🧂 Salt 1 spoon
🧂 Chicken bouillon 1 spoon
🥔 Tapioca starch 2 spoons

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
⚙️ Home meat grinder
🍲 Soup pot
🥄 Slotted spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Trim the fat and sinew from the pork leg, take the lean meat, and slice it thinly.
2. Place the pork slices into the meat grinder, add salt, chicken bouillon, tapioca starch, ice cubes, and an appropriate amount of water.
   **Key point**: The home meat grinder should work intermittently, pausing for a moment after each grinding session, repeat 3-5 times to prevent the motor from overheating.
   **State check**: The ground meat paste should be pink in color and have a noticeably sticky texture.
3. Put the soaked and squeezed-dried shiitake mushrooms into the meat grinder and chop them into fine pieces. Add them together with the fried garlic into the meat paste and mix evenly.
4. Grab an appropriate amount of meat paste in your hand, squeeze out a ball shape from the tiger's mouth (the space between thumb and index finger), scrape it off with a spoon, and gently place it into a pot with warm water.
   **Note**: The water temperature should be controlled at 60-70°C. Do not let it boil, otherwise the meatballs will fall apart.
5. Cook over medium-low heat. The meatballs will gradually float to the surface. This process takes about 3 minutes.
   **Doneness check**: When most of the meatballs float on the water surface and have slightly expanded in size, they are fully cooked.
6. Use a slotted spoon to remove the cooked mushroom and pork meatballs. They can be eaten directly, or added to a clear broth and sprinkled with chopped scallions.

## Health Suggestions
Pure lean pork provides high-quality protein, and the addition of shiitake mushrooms enhances umami flavor and dietary fiber, making it suitable for fitness muscle building or fat control. Using ice cubes and tapioca starch increases the springiness of the meatballs without adding many calories.
