# One-Person Steamed Meal Set
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Lean pork 200g (diced)
🐚 Abalone 1 piece (score a crosshatch pattern on the surface)
🍤 Shrimp 2 pieces (peel to get the shrimp meat, butterfly the back and devein)
🥚 Eggs 2 pieces
🥬 Bok choy 100g (tear apart the root, wash clean)
🧅 Scallion 10g (cut into sections, mince)
🫚 Ginger 15g (slice, mince)
🌶️ Red bell pepper 10g (mince, cut into rings)
🧄 Garlic 10g (slice)
🍚 Rice 50g
🧂 Salt appropriate amount (about 15g, use in multiple stages)
🐟 Steamed fish soy sauce 15g
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board · Chef's knife
🥣 Ceramic stewing cups 4 pieces
🍳 Wok
🍲 Steamer
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Spoon
🍶 Bowl (for holding warm water and for beating eggs)

## Steps
### Preparation Stage
1. Wash the rice clean, place it in a stewing cup, pour in clear water until it reaches about the first knuckle above the rice surface, add a little salt.
2. Crack the eggs into a stewing cup, add half a bowl of warm water and a little salt, use chopsticks to whisk thoroughly and evenly.
   **Key Point**: Adding warm water makes the egg custard more tender and smooth; the egg liquid must be whisked thoroughly.
3. Prepare the lean pork, abalone, and shrimp respectively. Prepare the scallion, ginger, red bell pepper, and garlic as directed and set aside.

### Filling and Steaming
4. Fill four stewing cups with the following contents respectively:
   - Stewing cup 1: Rice and water.
   - Stewing cup 2: Diced pork, abalone, ginger slices, add a little salt, pour in clear water until 80% full.
   - Stewing cup 3: Prepared egg liquid.
   - Stewing cup 4: Line the bottom with scallion sections and ginger slices, place the fish pieces inside, drizzle with steamed fish soy sauce.
5. Place the four stewing cups into the steamer. Bring the water to a boil over high heat. Once steam appears, reduce to medium heat and steam for 20 minutes.

### Stir-frying Vegetables and Serving
6. During the last 3 minutes of steaming, heat the wok over high heat and add oil. Add the garlic slices and stir-fry until fragrant. First add the bok choy stems and stir-fry quickly, then add the bok choy leaves and red bell pepper rings. Season with salt. Stir-fry vigorously over high heat until the leaves soften and turn bright green.
7. After steaming, remove all stewing cups. Sprinkle scallions and minced red bell pepper on the surface of the steamed egg custard. Serve together with the stir-fried bok choy.

## Health Advice
This steamed meal is low in fat and high in protein. Abalone and shrimp can supplement zinc and selenium. Adding warm water when steaming eggs makes the texture more tender and smooth, making it suitable for those on fitness or weight loss diets or those needing gentle digestive regulation. Note that steamed fish soy sauce contains salt. When used together with table salt, people with high blood pressure should reduce the amount accordingly.
