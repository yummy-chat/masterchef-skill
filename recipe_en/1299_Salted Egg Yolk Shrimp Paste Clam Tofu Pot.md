# Salted Egg Yolk Shrimp Paste Clam Tofu Pot  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November-December)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 Shrimp 200g (thawed before use)  
🧊 Frozen tofu 1 piece (cut into chunks)  
🐚 Clams Appropriate amount (cleaned thoroughly)  
🧅 Scallions A large amount (cut into chopped scallions, used in two batches)  
🧄 Garlic 3-4 cloves (crushed and minced)  

🧂 Salt Appropriate amount (added in two batches)  
🌶️ White pepper powder Appropriate amount (added in two batches)  
🥚 Salted egg yolk paste 2 tablespoons (can also be substituted with crushed fresh salted egg yolks)  
💧 Water starch Appropriate amount (for thickening)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍲 Clay pot  
🔪 Chef’s knife and cutting board  
🥣 Bowl  
🫙 Blender (optional)  
🥄 Silicone spatula  
🥄 Soup ladle  
🥛 Measuring cup  

## Cooking Steps  
1. **Prepare Ingredients**: Mince the garlic, chop the scallions into chopped scallions. Dice the shrimp, then use the back of the knife to pound it into a slightly chunky shrimp paste, mix in an appropriate amount of salt and white pepper powder and set aside.  
2. **Fry the Sauce**: Heat oil in the clay pot over medium-high heat, add minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add 2 tablespoons of salted egg yolk paste, keep the heat on low and stir-fry until the paste bubbles and its color lightens slightly.  
   **Note**: Keep the heat on low throughout to avoid burning and bitterness.  
3. **Simmer the Broth**: Add water to the pot and bring to a boil over high heat.  
4. **Add Ingredients**: Once the water boils, first add the frozen tofu chunks, then add the prepared shrimp paste one portion at a time.  
5. **Add Clams**: After the broth in the pot comes to a boil again, add the clams, cover the pot, and continue cooking for 3-5 minutes until all the clams have opened.  
6. **Thicken and Season**: Drizzle in water starch as desired, gently stir to evenly thicken the broth. Finally, sprinkle in the chopped scallions, taste and adjust the saltiness with a little extra salt and white pepper powder, then turn off the heat and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This dish is rich in high-quality protein and calcium, making it suitable for warming and nourishing the body in winter. However, the salted egg yolk paste has a high sodium content, so those with high blood pressure should control their intake. The shrimp paste and tofu are easy to digest, so both the elderly and children can enjoy it with confidence.
