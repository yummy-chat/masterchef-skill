# Steamed Meat Cake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐖 Pork (pork belly/half-fat half-lean) 1 kg
🥚 Eggs 5
🌾 Sweet potato starch about 250 g (half a catty)
🫚 Ginger 2 small pieces
🧅 Scallions a few stalks
🧂 Salt about 2 spoons
🌶️ Sichuan green peppercorn powder to taste
🧂 Ground white pepper a pinch
🫒 Cooking oil as needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🌀 Meat grinder (optional)
📦 Square stainless steel mold
🖌️ Brush
♨️ Steamer

## Steps
1. **Prepare the pork**：Remove the skin from the pork belly, wash with warm water, cut into thick slices, then chop or grind into a very fine meat paste.
   **Key point**: The skin must be completely removed; the finer the paste, the better the texture of the finished product.
2. **Make ginger-scallion water**：Place scallions and crushed ginger in warm water, rub and squeeze with your hands until the water turns green, then strain out the ginger and scallion solids, keeping only the liquid.
   **Key point**: The ginger-scallion juice is used to remove odor and enhance flavor; do not use the solids.
3. **Separate the eggs**：Separate the egg whites from the yolks. Set the egg whites aside. Beat the yolks until smooth and set aside.
4. **Prepare the starch slurry**：Add an equal amount of cold water to the sweet potato starch, mix by hand until smooth, forming a starch slurry.
5. **Season the meat filling**：Add the ginger-scallion water, all the egg whites, and half of the starch slurry to the meat paste. Mix in one direction until well combined. Gradually add the remaining starch slurry in batches, mixing until each addition is fully absorbed. Finally, add salt and stir until the mixture is firm and springs back. Sprinkle in the Sichuan green peppercorn powder and ground white pepper, and mix well.
   **Key point**: The starch slurry must be added in batches, with each batch fully absorbed before adding the next. Always stir in the same direction to build the texture.
6. **Fill the mold**：Brush the inside of the mold with cooking oil. Pour in the meat paste, press down firmly, and smooth the surface to avoid air pockets inside.
7. **First steaming**：Place the mold in a steamer. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and steam for about 35 minutes, until the meat cake is set and has changed color.
   **Key point**: Do not use too high a heat, to avoid overcooking.
8. **Brush with egg yolk for color**：Remove the mold, pour off any excess water that has risen to the surface, and brush an even layer of beaten egg yolk over the top. Return to the steamer and steam until the yolk is set and cooked through. Repeat the egg yolk brushing several times until a satisfying golden crust forms.
9. **Cool and slice**：Remove from the steamer. Let the meat cake cool completely before unmolding, then cut into thick slices.
   **Key point**: Must be completely cooled before cutting, otherwise the soft, tender cake will fall apart.

## Health Advice
This steamed meat cake is rich in high-quality protein and iron, suitable for people needing to replenish energy. Note that the amount of sweet potato starch is relatively high; during a weight-loss period, you may reduce it by about one-third.
