# Breakfast Burger
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 1 egg
🥯 1 English muffin
🍅 About 1 tomato (take 1-2 crosswise slices)
🧀 About half a slice of cheese
🫒 A small amount of cooking oil (for greasing the pan, optional)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
♨️ Air fryer
🥄 Silicone spatula
🔪 Knife and cutting board
🫖 Small saucepan (for adding water to the frying pan)

## Cooking Steps
1. Wash the tomato and cut 1-2 crosswise slices for later use. Take the English muffin and cheese slice out of the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature.
   **Key Point**: Cutting the tomato crosswise gives a more attractive cross-section.
2. Place the English muffin in the air fryer and bake at 180°C for 2 minutes, until the surface is slightly crisp and the inside remains soft.
3. Heat the non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat. Crack in 1 egg. Pour a small amount of water along the edge of the pan, then immediately cover with a lid.
   **Note**: Do not use too much water. Use the steam to cook the egg white until set, leaving a runny yolk. If the pan has a non-stick coating, you can skip adding extra oil.
4. Turn off the heat once the egg white is fully set and the yolk is still runny. Remove the fried egg with a silicone spatula.
5. Assemble the burger: Take the bottom of the toasted English muffin, layer with tomato slices, the steamed fried egg, and half a slice of cheese. Cover with the top of the English muffin.

## Health Advice
This breakfast burger is quick to prepare. The egg provides high-quality protein, the tomato supplements vitamin C, and the half slice of cheese should be consumed in moderation. It is suitable for a morning when a quick energy boost is needed. Be mindful of total caloric intake, and it is recommended to pair with a sugar-free beverage.
