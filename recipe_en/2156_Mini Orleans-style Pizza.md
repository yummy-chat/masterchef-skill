# Mini Orleans-style Pizza
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Recommended Season: All seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 All-purpose flour 500g
🍬 Granulated sugar 15g
🥄 Yeast 4g
🧂 Salt to taste (1g for dough, a pinch for sauce)
💧 Water 300g
🫒 Cooking oil 20g (for kneading) + a little (for marinating)
🍗 Chicken legs 6 pieces
🧀 Mozzarella cheese as needed
🍖 Ham as needed (sliced)
🌽 Corn kernels as needed
🫑 Green and red bell peppers as needed (cut into strips)
🧅 Onion as needed (cut into chunks)
🍗 Orleans seasoning powder two tablespoons
🥄 Ginger powder a pinch
🍶 Light soy sauce two circles
🍅 Ketchup as needed
🧂 Black pepper a pinch
🍬 Sugar (or salt) a pinch (for sauce seasoning)
🦪 Oyster sauce three tablespoons

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🥢 Chopsticks/spatula
🤲 Kneading mat
📏 Rolling pin/hands
🍴 Fork
♨️ Oven
🍳 Non-stick pan and spatula
🔪 Cutting board and knife
🖌️ Brush
♨️ Air fryer
📦 Plastic wrap

## Steps
1. **Knead the dough and shape the crust**
   Mix flour, sugar, yeast, and salt together. Add water and stir into flaky pieces. Then add 20g cooking oil and knead into a soft dough. Divide the dough into 8 equal portions, roll each into a ball, and flatten with your palm or a rolling pin into a round crust with a thicker edge and thinner center. Poke dense holes in the crust with a fork, cover with plastic wrap, and let rest for 20–30 minutes.

2. **Pre-bake to set**
   Preheat the oven to 180°C. Bake the crusts for 5 minutes, then remove and let cool.

3. **Prepare the chicken**
   Debone the chicken legs and cut into small pieces. Add two tablespoons of Orleans seasoning powder, a pinch of ginger powder, two circles of light soy sauce, and a little cooking oil. Mix well and marinate for 30 minutes.

4. **Cook the filling**
   Heat a non-stick pan over high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until fully cooked and the sauce thickens. Remove and set aside.

5. **Make the pizza sauce**
   In a bowl, add ketchup, grind in some black pepper, add a pinch of salt and three tablespoons of oyster sauce. Stir until well combined.

6. **Assemble the pizza**
   Evenly brush the cooled crusts with pizza sauce. Sprinkle a thin layer of mozzarella cheese. Then spread the cooked Orleans chicken on top, followed by ham slices, corn kernels, green and red bell pepper strips, and onion chunks. Finally, generously cover with mozzarella cheese.

7. **Freeze for storage**
   Wrap the assembled pizzas tightly in plastic wrap and place in the freezer. They can be stored for 10–15 days.

8. **Final bake**
   Take a frozen pizza crust out of the freezer without thawing. Place directly into an air fryer. Bake for about 10 minutes, until the cheese is fully melted and the edges are golden brown.

## Health Tips
This Orleans-style mini pizza replaces traditional Italian salami with chicken, offering higher protein and lower fat content. However, the cheese and oil quantities are not low, so it’s recommended to serve with a salad. It’s suitable for family gatherings or weekend enjoyment. Those with sensitive stomachs should watch their portion size.
