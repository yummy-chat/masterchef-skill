# Green Pepper and Potato Slices
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes, about 2 (peeled and sliced thinly)
🌶️ Green peppers, about 2 (seeded and cut into pieces)
🧅 Scallions, a small bunch (minced)
🧄 Garlic, about 5 cloves (minced)
🥄 Light soy sauce, 3 tablespoons
🥄 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon
🧂 Salt, 1 teaspoon
✨ Thirteen-spice powder, 1 teaspoon

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan or wok
🥄 Silicone spatula
🫗 Transparent lid
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for serving rice and dishes)

## Cooking Steps
1. Peel the potatoes and slice them into evenly thick pieces. Seed the green peppers and cut into pieces. Mince the scallions and garlic, set aside.
   **Note**: The potato slices must be uniform in thickness; too thin makes them prone to breaking, too thick makes them hard to cook through.
2. Pour an appropriate amount of cooking oil into the pan and heat until warm. Add the minced scallions and garlic, and stir-fry over medium-low heat until fragrant, about 30 seconds, when the aroma is released and they turn slightly golden.
   **Note**: Avoid high heat to prevent burning and bitterness.
3. Add the potato slices, turn the heat to high, and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until the edges of the slices become translucent and slightly browned.
   **Key point**: Stir frequently to ensure even heating.
4. Add the green pepper pieces, pour in the pre-mixed sauce, and stir-fry quickly over high heat for about 1 minute to coat the ingredients evenly with the sauce.
5. Add one bowl of water, cover with the lid, and turn the heat to medium. Braise for about 5 minutes to allow the potato slices to fully absorb the liquid, becoming soft and flavorful.
6. Uncover and turn the heat to high. Continue stir-frying to reduce the sauce for about 1 minute, until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients.
7. Remove from heat and plate. Serve the stir-fried green pepper and potato slices directly over hot rice, and enjoy while hot.

## Health Tips
Potatoes are rich in dietary fiber and potassium, while green peppers provide vitamin C. This dish is suitable for those on a weight loss diet or needing to control blood pressure. Note to use less oil and salt; pre-soaking the potato slices in water to remove starch can help lower the glycemic index.
