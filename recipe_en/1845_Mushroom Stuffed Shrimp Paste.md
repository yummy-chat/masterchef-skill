# Mushroom Stuffed Shrimp Paste
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍄 Button mushrooms – as needed (stems removed to form a hollow)  
🦐 Shrimp paste – as needed (to fill the mushrooms)  
🌿 Light soy sauce – 2 tbsp  
🌿 Dark soy sauce – 1 tbsp  
🌶️ White pepper – a pinch  
🌾 Cornstarch – 1 tbsp  
💧 Water – half a bowl  
🫒 Cooking oil – as needed  
🫘 Sesame seeds – as needed (for final garnish)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan or skillet  
🥄 Spatula or spoon  
🍽️ Plate  
🥢 Chopsticks (for assistance)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Remove the stems from the button mushrooms to form a hollow shape. Squeeze the shrimp paste into the hollow and smooth the surface.
   **Note**: Ensure the mushrooms remain intact and the shrimp paste is packed firmly to prevent it from falling off during frying.
2. Heat oil in the pan. Place the stuffed mushrooms shrimp-paste side down in the pan. Fry over medium heat until both sides are golden brown.
   **Key point**: Frying the shrimp-paste side first helps set the shape. Flip gently to prevent the shrimp paste from separating.
3. Mix light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, white pepper, cornstarch, and water together to form the sauce. Set aside.
4. Once the mushrooms are golden brown, pour the sauce along the edge of the pan. Reduce to medium-low heat, cover, and simmer for about 5 minutes.
5. Remove the lid and increase to high heat to reduce the sauce. Gently turn the mushrooms, and once the sauce thickens and evenly coats them, remove from the heat.
   **Note**: Stir frequently during reduction to prevent burning.
6. Transfer to a plate and sprinkle sesame seeds on top for garnish and aroma.

## Health Advice  
This dish is low in fat and high in protein. Button mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber, making it suitable for those on a weight loss or blood sugar control diet. It is recommended to use a non-stick pan with minimal oil. Since the shrimp paste is already salty, halving the sauce can help avoid excess sodium.
