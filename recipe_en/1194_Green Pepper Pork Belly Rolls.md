# Green Pepper Pork Belly Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Frozen pork belly rolls, 1 pack (about 200~300g)  
🧅 Onion, 1 (sliced)  
🫑 Green pepper, 1 (cut into strips)  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed  
🧂 Light soy sauce, 3 tablespoons  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon  
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Chicken bouillon, 1 teaspoon  
🍬 Sugar, a pinch  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
🥄 Spatula  
🥣 Bowl (for mixing sauce)  
🥢 Chopsticks or tongs (for handling meat rolls)  
🔥 Stove  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, mix 3 tablespoons light soy sauce, 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon oyster sauce, 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon, and a pinch of sugar. Stir until well combined. Set aside.  
2. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add the frozen pork belly rolls and stir-fry until they are fully cooked and have changed color.  
3. Add the sliced onion and green pepper strips. Continue stir-frying until the vegetables are tender-crisp.  
4. Turn the heat to high, pour in the prepared sauce, and quickly stir-fry until everything is evenly coated.  
5. Once well mixed, immediately remove from heat and plate.  

## Health Tips  
While pork belly is rich and flavorful, pairing it with green pepper and onion provides vitamins and dietary fiber, helping to balance the richness. This dish is suitable for occasional indulgence; those with high blood lipids or on a weight-loss plan should consume it in moderation.
