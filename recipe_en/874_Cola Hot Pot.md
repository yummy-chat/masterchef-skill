# Cola Hot Pot
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥤 Cola, about 1000ml
🥩 Sliced beef brisket (fatty beef rolls), as needed
🩸 Duck blood tofu, as needed
🧈 Tofu, as needed
🥬 Lettuce, as needed
🌭 Ham sausage / sausage, as needed
🫒 Cooking oil, a small amount (to grease the pot and prevent sticking)
🫙 Light soy sauce, one teaspoon
🌶️ Spicy hot pot base dry seasoning block, one piece (containing Sichuan green and red peppercorns, bullet-head chilies, etc.)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍲 Stockpot
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Spoon
🔥 Gas stove

## Cooking Steps
1. Grease the stockpot with a small amount of oil to prevent sticking.
2. Pour both small bottles of cola entirely into the pot.
3. Add one teaspoon of light soy sauce and stir well.
4. Add the spicy hot pot base dry seasoning block, bring the broth to a boil over high heat, and wait until red oil surfaces and the seasoning aroma is fully integrated.
   **Key point**: The cola provides sweetness and a caramel-colored broth; the dry seasoning block provides numbing and spicy flavors—no need to stir-fry spices separately.
5. Reduce to medium-high heat to maintain a rolling boil, then first add slow-to-cook ingredients such as duck blood tofu, tofu, and sausages.
6. After boiling for about 2–3 minutes, add the quick-cooking ingredients like sliced beef brisket and lettuce, blanch until the beef changes color, then remove.
   **Note**: Do not add too many ingredients at once, as this can lower the broth temperature and affect the boil.
7. No need to prepare a separate dipping sauce; simply remove the ingredients directly and enjoy them coated in the spicy, sweet, and numbing broth.

## Health Tips
The sweet and spicy combination of cola hot pot can stimulate the appetite, but it is high in sugar and oil. It is recommended to balance it with vegetables like lettuce and enjoy it occasionally as a treat. Duck blood tofu and tofu are rich in protein and are good choices to prioritize.
