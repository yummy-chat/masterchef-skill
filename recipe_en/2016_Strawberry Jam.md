# Strawberry Jam  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Spring (January–April)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍓 1200g strawberries  
🧂 400g white sugar (can be reduced to 200g if a low-sugar diet is needed)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🥣 Bowl  
🫙 Plastic wrap  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🥫 Sealed jar  

## Instructions  
1. Wash the strawberries thoroughly and remove the stems.  
2. Chop the strawberries and place them in a bowl of suitable size.  
3. Add the white sugar to the strawberries and mix well.  
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it sit for 1 hour.  
5. Pour the rested mixture into a non-stick pan and bring to a boil over high heat.  
6. Once boiling, reduce to low heat and stir continuously until the jam becomes thick, then turn off the heat.  
7. Allow the strawberry jam to cool, then transfer it to a pre-prepared sealed jar.  

## Health Tips  
Strawberries are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. Homemade jam allows you to control the sugar content, making it suitable for those on a sugar-reduced diet. It is recommended to pair with whole wheat bread or yogurt, and avoid consuming large amounts on an empty stomach to prevent blood sugar fluctuations.
