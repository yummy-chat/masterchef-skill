# Stir-Fried Shredded Pancake with Meat

Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Pork tenderloin 200g (cut into thicker shreds)  
🥬 Cabbage appropriate amount (finely shredded)  
🥕 Carrot appropriate amount (grated)  
🧅 Onion about 1/4 piece (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (divided into sliced garlic and a generous amount of minced garlic)  
🫓 Raw shredded pancake appropriate amount  
🍾 Huadiao wine / yellow wine a little (for marinating meat)  
🥡 Light soy sauce a little (for marinating meat) + appropriate amount (drizzled along the edge of the wok when braising the pancake)  
🌾 Cornstarch about 5g (for marinating meat)  
💧 Water a little (mixed with cornstarch for marinating meat)  
🧂 Salt 1-2 tsp (added when stir-frying vegetables)  
🍶 Vinegar appropriate amount (drizzled along the edge of the wok before removing from heat, for acidity and aroma)  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment  
🍳 Chinese wok  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Wooden cutting board  
🧽 Grater  
🥢 Chopsticks / spatula  
🍽️ Plate / bowl  

## Instructions  
1. Slice the pork tenderloin, then cut into thicker shreds. Add a little huadiao wine, light soy sauce, cornstarch, and a small amount of water. Mix well and marinate.  
   **Key point**: The meat shreds should not be too thin, to prevent them from breaking during stir-frying; marinating only aims to remove gaminess and add basic flavor; no need for excessive coating.  
2. Finely shred the cabbage, grate the carrot, and slice the onion. Divide the garlic into two portions: one part lightly crushed and sliced, the other part minced into a generous amount; set aside.  
3. Heat the wok over high heat, add oil, and stir-fry the meat shreds over medium heat until they are fully separated and completely white and cooked through.  
4. Add the sliced garlic and onion shreds, stir-fry until fragrant. Then add the shredded cabbage and grated carrot, stir-fry briefly.  
5. Add salt to season, stir-fry evenly. At this point, the saltiness should be based on the vegetables; do not over-salt.  
6. Reduce the heat to low. Spread the raw shredded pancake evenly over the surface of the vegetables and meat; do not stir. Drizzle light soy sauce along the edge of the wok, cover with a lid, and braise for about 1 minute to soften the pancake shreds with steam.  
7. Once the pancake shreds are soft, gently mix them with the vegetables and meat on the bottom using chopsticks or a spatula, stirring evenly.  
8. Sprinkle with a generous amount of minced garlic, drizzle vinegar along the edge of the wok, immediately turn the heat to high and stir-fry quickly and evenly. Once the garlic and vinegar aromas are released, turn off the heat and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This stir-fried shredded pancake with meat includes cabbage and carrots, which provide dietary fiber. It pairs well with coarse grains to balance refined carbohydrates. The pork tenderloin shreds are relatively low in fat, but the overall calorie content of the dish is not low. It is recommended to serve with a clear soup or a cold dish.
