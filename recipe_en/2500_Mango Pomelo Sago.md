# Mango Pomelo Sago

Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥭 1 mango  
🍊 1/2 grapefruit  
🌾 24g chia seeds  
🧊 2 small ice cubes  
🥛 50ml milk  
🥥 150ml coconut milk  
🥭 Dried mango strips (optional)  
🍊 Dried orange strips (optional)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥤 Cup  
🔪 Fruit knife  
⚙️ Blender/Juicer  

## Steps
1. Soak the chia seeds in milk and let them sit for 10 minutes to fully expand.
2. While soaking, peel half of the mango and the grapefruit, cut them into small dices, and place them into a cup for later use.
3. Cut the other half of the mango into small pieces, add them to the blender along with the ice cubes and coconut milk, then blend into a puree.
4. Pour the blended mango coconut milk puree into the cup containing the fruit dices.
5. Garnish with dried mango strips or dried orange strips as desired.

## Health Tips
This dessert replaces sago with high-fiber chia seeds, which helps control blood sugar and promote intestinal motility; however, coconut milk is relatively high in calories, so be mindful of the serving size. Paired with the vitamin C in mango and grapefruit, it offers both sun protection and a refreshing effect when enjoyed in summer.
