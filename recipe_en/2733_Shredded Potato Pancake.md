## Preparation Steps
1. Place the steamed potato in a wooden bowl and mash it into a smooth puree using a masher.
   **Note**: The potato must be fully steamed, otherwise the texture will be coarse.
2. Add diced ham sausage, diced carrot, diced yellow bell pepper, diced red bell pepper, and green peas. Mix all ingredients evenly with the potato puree.
   **Key point**: The diced vegetables must be cut finely and mixed thoroughly to facilitate subsequent shaping.
3. Wearing gloves, take an适量 amount of the mixed potato mixture. First form it into a large ball, then press it into a pancake shape with a slightly thicker center and thinner edges.
4. Place an适量 amount of mozzarella cheese shreds in the center of the potato pancake. Then fold the edges inward to seal the filling, re-form it into a tight ball, and gently press it into a sole-shaped patty.
   **Key point**: Ensure the cheese is completely wrapped to prevent leakage during frying.
5. Coat the shaped patty sequentially with dry starch, egg wash, and breadcrumbs. The order must not be reversed, and the breadcrumbs need to cover evenly.
6. Pour an适量 amount of oil into a wok and heat to 50% hot. Reduce to medium-low heat, and gently slide the potato patty into the oil.
   **Note**: The oil temperature should not be too high to avoid a burnt exterior and raw interior.
7. Fry slowly over low heat, gently turning with chopsticks occasionally. When the patty surface is evenly golden brown, remove it with a slotted spoon and place it on oil-absorbing paper to drain excess oil.
   **Doneness check**: Golden and crispy surface is the indicator.
8. Serve hot for a cheesy, crispy-on-the-outside, tender-on-the-inside experience.
