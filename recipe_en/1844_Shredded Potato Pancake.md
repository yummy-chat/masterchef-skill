# Shredded Potato Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes, appropriate amount (peeled and shredded into thin strips)
🥚 Egg, 1
🌭 Ham sausage, appropriate amount (shredded)
🌾 Flour, appropriate amount
🌾 Starch, appropriate amount
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount (to draw out moisture)
🍂 Five-spice powder, a pinch
🌿 Cumin, a pinch
🌶️ Red and yellow bell pepper dices, a pinch
🫒 Sesame oil, a pinch
🧂 Secret barbecue seasoning, appropriate amount (for sprinkling after cooking)
🧅 Scallions, chopped, appropriate amount (for sprinkling after cooking)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🧈 Grater/shredder
🥣 Glass bowl/large bowl
🍳 Iron pan (preferably pre-seasoned or a non-stick iron pan)
🥄 Spatula
🔪 Knife, cutting board

## Cooking Steps
1. Peel the potatoes and shred them into thin strips using a grater. Place in a large bowl.
   **Note**: Do not rinse the shredded potatoes; retain the starch for better binding.
2. Sprinkle with appropriate amount of salt, mix well, and let sit to allow the salt to draw out some moisture from the potato shreds.
3. Crack in one egg, add five-spice powder, cumin, red and yellow bell pepper dices, and shredded ham sausage. Stir to combine.
4. Gradually add flour and starch in small batches, mixing until the potato shreds are evenly coated with a thin batter.
5. Finally, drizzle in a little sesame oil and mix again.
6. Heat the iron pan over high heat, then add a small amount of oil. Pour in the potato mixture and spread it into a round pancake shape with a spatula.
   **Note**: The iron pan needs to be pre-seasoned beforehand, or use a pre-seasoned non-stick iron pan to prevent sticking.
7. Cook over medium heat for about 120 seconds. Once the bottom is set and golden brown, flip the pancake.
   **Key Point**: If the pan is non-stick, you can easily flip the pancake by tossing it. Continue cooking the other side until golden and crispy.
8. Once cooked, remove from the pan and cut into pieces for easy serving.
9. Sprinkle with secret barbecue seasoning and chopped scallions before serving.

## Health Suggestions
Potatoes are rich in dietary fiber and potassium. Combined with eggs and ham sausage, this dish offers balanced nutrition and is suitable for breakfast or a late-night snack. Control the amount of oil used during frying to avoid a greasy exterior and interior, and be mindful of calorie intake.
