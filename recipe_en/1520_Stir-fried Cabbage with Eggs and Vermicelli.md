# Stir-fried Cabbage with Eggs and Vermicelli  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 Cabbage  
🥚 Eggs  
🍝 Vermicelli  
🥕 Carrot  
🧅 Scallion  
🧄 Garlic  
🌶️ Dried chili pepper  
🫒 Rapeseed oil  
🧂 Salt  
🍾 Light soy sauce  
🍾 Dark soy sauce  
🍾 Oyster sauce  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🥣 Bowl  
🍲 Wok  
🥢 Spatula  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Wash the carrot and cabbage, then shred them separately and set aside.  
2. Soak the vermicelli in cold water for 1 hour, then blanch in boiling water until soft. Drain and set aside.  
3. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt, and beat for about 15 seconds until well mixed.  
4. Chop the scallion into葱花, mince the garlic, and slice the dried chili pepper into small rings. Set aside.  
5. Heat the wok over high heat, add rapeseed oil, pour in the egg mixture, and quickly stir-fry until set. Remove and set aside.  
6. Add more oil to the wok and heat. Add the chopped scallion, minced garlic, and dried chili rings, stir-frying for about 8 seconds until fragrant.  
7. Add the shredded carrot and cabbage. Stir-fry over high heat for about 30 seconds until just tender.  
8. Add the softened vermicelli and quickly toss to combine.  
9. Season with 15ml light soy sauce, 10ml dark soy sauce, 10ml oyster sauce, and 2 grams salt. Stir-fry until the seasonings are evenly distributed.  
10. Add the previously cooked eggs and continue stir-frying for about 15 seconds until all ingredients are well mixed.  
11. Transfer to a plate and serve.  

## Health Tips  
Cabbage is rich in dietary fiber, which aids digestion; eggs provide high-quality protein; vermicelli adds a sense of fullness. This dish is suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or seeking light meals. Ensure the vermicelli is fully soaked and softened to avoid a hard texture after stir-frying; adjust the amount of dried chili pepper according to personal preference.
