# Explosive Dry-Fried Chicken
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken thigh meat (boneless, cut into small pieces)
🫚 Ginger (sliced)
🧄 Garlic (sliced)
🌶️ Long hot green peppers (choose finger-thick, slender varieties, cut into small sections)
🫒 Cooking oil (slightly more than usual stir-frying amount)
🌶️ Dried chili peppers
🍶 High-proof liquor (a small amount)
🥢 Light soy sauce (about two swirls)
🍬 White sugar (a pinch)
🧂 Salt (a pinch)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪵 Cutting board
🍳 Wok
🥄 Wooden spatula

## Cooking Steps
1. Heat the wok over high heat, then add slightly more cold oil. Once the oil temperature rises, reduce to low heat, add the chicken thigh pieces, and patiently stir-fry until the surface shrinks and turns a dark brown color, achieving a state where "you see oil, not water." Remove and drain, setting aside.
**Note**: Must be stir-fried slowly over low heat to make the chicken dry and fragrant; slightly more oil prevents sticking.

2. Leave a small amount of oil in the wok. Keep the heat low, add the ginger slices, garlic slices, and dried chili peppers, and stir-fry until fragrant.
**Note**: Maintain low heat throughout to prevent the aromatics from burning.

3. Turn the heat to high, add the green pepper sections, and quickly stir-fry for about 30 seconds, keeping them vibrant green and crisp-tender.
4. Return the stir-fried chicken to the wok and toss evenly with the green peppers and other aromatics.
5. Drizzle a small amount of high-proof liquor along the edge of the wok. The liquor will instantly ignite upon contact with the heat, allowing its aroma to permeate the ingredients.
**Note**: Be careful during this step to avoid burns from the flame.

6. Drizzle about two swirls of light soy sauce, sprinkle in a pinch of white sugar to enhance freshness, and continue stir-frying for about 30 more seconds.
7. Just before removing from the heat, sprinkle with a pinch of salt and quickly stir to combine.
8. Turn off the heat and plate. The finished dish is dry, aromatic, rich, and melts in the mouth.

## Health Suggestion
After dry-frying, the protein in the chicken is concentrated, making it suitable for those building muscle. However, the oil content is high, so it is recommended to pair it with light vegetables to balance calories. Chili peppers, ginger, and garlic can boost metabolism, but those with sensitive stomachs should consume in moderation.
