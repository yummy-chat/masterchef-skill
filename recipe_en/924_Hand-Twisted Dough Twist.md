# Hand-Twisted Dough Twist  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients  
🥨 Dough, appropriate amount (finished product should be soft and elastic)  

## Seasonings  
🧂 None  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔧 Stainless steel work surface  

## Steps  
1. Take a piece of dough and roll both hands to form a long strip, making the middle thin and both ends thick.  
   **Key point**: The dough surface should be smooth, and the shape must meet the requirement; this is the foundation for developing tension.  

2. Fold the long strip in half, hook one end, and twist while rolling to create even spiral patterns in the dough.  
   **Key point**: The twisting direction must remain consistent to generate and maintain tension.  

3. Hold one end of the twisted dough twist and forcefully slap it upward several times in the tightening direction.  
   **Key point**: The slapping direction must match the tightening direction to prevent loosening; the twist will become longer and firmer after slapping.  

4. Fold the slapped long strip in half, press down on the folded part with your hand, thread the other end through the loop formed by the fold, and pinch the closure tightly.  
   **Key point**: The pressing and threading actions must be continuous to ensure a neat, compact shape without unraveling, forming a tight braid-like raw piece.  

## Health Advice  
The process of making hand-twisted dough twists involves repeated rolling, slapping, and threading, but the finished product is essentially fried carbohydrate food. It is recommended to consume occasionally, paired with vegetables and protein to balance calories. Pay attention to portion control.
