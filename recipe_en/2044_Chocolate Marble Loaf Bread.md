# Chocolate Marble Loaf Bread
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥖 Bread flour 250g
🍞 Cake flour 50g
🍬 Granulated sugar 48g
🥛 Milk powder 6g
🧂 Salt 4g
🥚 Whole egg liquid 45g (for dough)
🧊 Ice-cold milk 145g
🍥 Instant dry yeast (high sugar tolerance) 3g
🧈 Unsalted butter 30g (for dough, frozen state)
🍫 70% cocoa dark chocolate 75g (for filling)
🥛 Milk 55g (for filling)
🧈 Unsalted butter 20g (for filling)
🍫 Cocoa powder 7g
🥚 Egg wash, as needed (for brushing surface before baking)

## Tools & Equipment
🍞 Bread machine (for kneading, fermentation, and baking)
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🥣 Measuring cup/bowl
🥄 Whisk
🥖 Rolling pin
🍴 Silicone spatula
📜 Parchment paper
🍳 Baking tray
🔪 Knife

## Steps
1. **Make the chocolate filling**: Chop the dark chocolate into small pieces. Mix with 20g butter and 55g milk. Melt over warm water at 60°C, stirring until smooth. Add 7g cocoa powder and stir until thick. Pour into a container lined with parchment paper and refrigerate until firm. Remove, wrap in parchment paper, and roll into a sheet about 16cm × 22cm. Continue to refrigerate until needed.
2. **Knead the dough**: Add ice-cold milk, whole egg liquid, granulated sugar, salt, milk powder, bread flour, and cake flour in that order into the bread machine bucket. Start the kneading program and knead for about 10 minutes until the dough is initially formed.
3. Add instant dry yeast (high sugar tolerance) and 30g frozen unsalted butter. Continue kneading for about 20 minutes.
   **Key point**: Using frozen butter helps cool the dough; knead until the dough can be stretched into a thin, translucent membrane that shows fingerprints.
4. **Wrap and fold**: Remove the kneaded dough. Dust the surface and bottom with flour. Roll into a sheet about 22cm × 32cm. Place the chocolate sheet in the center of the dough sheet. Wrap the dough around the chocolate and seal the seams tightly.
5. Dust with flour and roll the dough out to about 45cm × 25cm. Perform a single four-fold.
6. Dust with flour again. Roll the dough wider and then longer, adjusting any uneven thickness, until about 32cm × 25cm. Perform a second four-fold. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate to relax for 20 minutes.
7. **Shape and braid**: Remove the dough and dust with flour. Roll into a long strip about 32cm × 12cm. Make 4 lengthwise cuts to reveal the chocolate pattern. Braid into a 4-strand braid.
8. After braiding, press the two ends flat and fold them toward the bottom, pinching to seal. Then flip the entire braid over. Fold the ends toward the center to form a shape suitable for the bread machine bucket.
9. **Ferment and bake**: Remove the bread machine's mixing paddle. Place the shaped dough into the bucket. Use the fermentation function to ferment for about one and a half hours until the dough rises to 90% of the bucket's height.
10. After fermentation, remove the bread bucket. Brush the surface of the dough evenly with egg wash. Return to the bread machine. Select the baking program and bake until the surface is golden brown and the inside is fully cooked.

## Health Tips
This loaf bread provides a balanced ratio of protein and carbohydrates. The chocolate filling is rich in antioxidants, making it suitable for replenishing energy before or after workouts. Be mindful of portion sizes due to the higher sugar and butter content. During weight loss periods, it is recommended to consume 1–2 slices per serving.
