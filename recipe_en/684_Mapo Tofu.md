# Mapo Tofu
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Silken tofu 1 box
🥩 Salted duck egg 1 (use the egg white)
🥩 Pork belly 20-30g
🥩 Garlic 2 cloves
🥩 Ginger 2 slices
🥩 Millet peppers 5
🧂 Salt 3g
🧂 Soy sauce 10g
🧂 Garlic chili sauce 5g
🧂 Sichuan peppercorns 20

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Paring knife
🔪 Chef's knife
🍳 Wok

## Steps
1.  Mince the garlic and ginger separately, and set aside.
2.  Cut the millet peppers into rings, and set aside.
3.  Mince the pork belly into a paste.
4.  Add half of the salt and soy sauce to the pork paste, mix well, and set aside.
5.  Cut the salted duck egg in half lengthwise with a chef's knife, remove the yolk, and crush the egg white into small pieces about 2mm in size (doesn't need to be too fine), and set aside.
6.  Open the tofu package, and use a paring knife to cut the tofu into pieces about 2.5cm x 3cm inside the box, and set aside.
7.  Heat the wok over high heat, pour in 10 to 15ml of cooking oil, and heat for about 10 seconds until the oil temperature rises.
8.  Reduce the heat to low, add the garlic, ginger, pepper rings, Sichuan peppercorns, salted duck egg white, and garlic chili sauce, and stir-fry for 20 seconds until fragrant.
9.  Increase the heat to medium, add the pork paste, and stir-fry for about 1 minute until the meat is cooked and changes color.
10. Reduce the heat to low, add the tofu, and evenly sprinkle the remaining salt and soy sauce over the surface of the tofu.
11. Slowly pour in boiling water along the edge of the wok, just enough to submerge the tofu.
12. Increase the heat to high, and once the water boils, immediately reduce the heat to medium and cook for about 10 minutes.
13. When the liquid in the wok has reduced to about one-fifth and the surface of the tofu has been colored and flavored, turn off the heat, and transfer to a plate.

## Health Advice
Adding salted duck egg white and pork belly to Mapo Tofu increases the protein content and umami flavor, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost. Be mindful to control the amount of salt used to avoid excessive sodium intake.
