# Salmon and Pumpkin Mixed Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Leftover white rice about 1 large bowl + 1 small bowl (loosened in advance)
🐟 Salmon sashimi about 2 slices
🦀 Crab sticks about 5 pieces
🎃 Golden pumpkin cubes about 2 portions (prepared in advance)
🍅 Tomato and beef brisket chunks about 2 portions (prepared in advance)
🍄 Shimeji mushrooms about 6 pieces
🌱 Chinese chives 1 small bunch
🥬 Baby bok choy 1 small piece
🍗 Braised chicken leg 1 piece
🌭 Sausage 1 piece
🥄 Light soy sauce to taste
🧂 Korean soybean paste about 1 tablespoon

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Large bowl, small bowl
🍲 Steamer (with steaming rack)
🌀 Fan
🥄 Spoon

## Steps
1. Place the leftover white rice into a large bowl and a small bowl respectively. Use a spoon to loosen it in advance, ensuring it is fluffy and not clumpy.
2. Evenly arrange all ingredients on the surface of the rice: salmon sashimi, crab sticks, golden pumpkin cubes, tomato and beef brisket chunks, shimeji mushrooms, Chinese chives, baby bok choy, braised chicken leg, and sausage.
   **Note**: Ingredients can be flexibly substituted based on leftovers; cartoon-shaped ingredients can help boost children's interest, try to distribute them evenly on the rice surface.
3. Place the bowl on the steaming rack in the steamer, add water, and steam until all ingredients are fully cooked and thoroughly heated.
   **Key point**: The steaming rack must be placed under the bowl to prevent the bottom from touching the water, which would make the rice soggy; use the doneness of the chicken leg meat, softening of vegetables, and thorough heating of the rice as indicators.
4. Once steamed, remove the bowl and evenly sprinkle a suitable amount of light soy sauce over the ingredients, then add about 1 tablespoon of Korean soybean paste.
   **Key point**: Sprinkle the light soy sauce evenly; the amount of soybean paste can be adjusted according to personal taste.
5. Use a spoon to thoroughly mix all the rice, ingredients, and seasonings until every grain of rice is coated with the sauce.
6. Use a fan to blow air over the mixed rice to quickly cool it to a temperature suitable for eating, especially taking care to prevent children from burning their mouths.

## Health Advice
This mixed rice is rich in ingredients, with pumpkin and salmon providing high-quality fats and vitamins, making it suitable for children or office workers needing balanced nutrition. Pay attention to controlling the sodium content from the braised chicken leg and sausage; individuals with high blood pressure are advised to reduce the amount of sauce used.
