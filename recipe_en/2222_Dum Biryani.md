# Dum Biryani
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧄 Chicken thighs 500g (boneless, cut into large pieces)
🍚 Basmati long-grain rice 300g
🥛 Yogurt 100ml
🧅 Onions 3 (about 300g, thinly sliced)
🍅 Tomato 1 (cut into pieces)
Ginger 15g (grated)
🧄 Garlic 6 cloves (about 18g, grated)
🌶️ Green chilies 3 (cut into sections)
🌿 Cilantro leaves 20g
🌿 Mint leaves 20g
🧈 Ghee 30g
🥛 Milk 30ml
🌺 Saffron 0.5g
🍋 Lemon juice 15ml
🌶️ Red chili powder 5g
🟡 Turmeric powder 3g
🧂 Garam masala 5g
🧂 Salt (5g for marinating meat + 10g for cooking rice)
🫒 Cooking oil 50ml
🍃 Bay leaves 2
🌿 Cinnamon stick 1
🟢 Green cardamom 3
⚫ Cloves 4
⭐ Star anise 1

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Heavy-bottomed pot (with lid, for sealing)
🥣 Large bowl
🫙 Plastic wrap
🧻 Paper towels
🥟 Dough (for sealing the pot lid, or aluminum foil as substitute)
🥄 Large spatula

## Cooking Instructions
### A. Marinate the Chicken
1. In a large bowl, mix together yogurt, grated ginger and garlic, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, 5g salt, and lemon juice.
2. Add the chicken thigh pieces, tossing well to ensure the marinade coats the surface evenly.
3. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour; overnight marination yields better flavor.

### B. Prepare the Toppings
4. Soak the saffron in warm milk for 15 minutes until the color is released. Set aside.
5. Pour cooking oil into a pot and heat over medium heat until it reaches about 50-60% hot.
6. Add the thinly sliced onions and stir-fry continuously for 12-15 minutes until deep golden brown and crispy.
7. Remove the fried onions, spread them on paper towels to absorb excess oil, and set aside.

### C. Parboil the Rice
8. Soak the Basmati rice for 30 minutes in advance, then drain.
9. Bring 2000ml of water to a boil in a large pot. Add the bay leaves, green cardamom, cloves, cinnamon stick, star anise, and 10g of salt.
10. Add the rice and cook over high heat for 5-6 minutes, until the grains are about 70% cooked.
11. Immediately drain the rice, remove and discard all whole spices, and set aside.

### D. Assemble and Dum Cook
12. Spread half of the ghee on the bottom of a heavy-bottomed pot.
13. First layer: Spread the marinated chicken evenly over the bottom of the pot.
14. Second layer: Spread half of the parboiled rice over the chicken, then top with half of the fried onions, mint leaves, cilantro leaves, and green chilies.
15. Third layer: Spread the remaining rice on top, then sprinkle with the remaining fried onions, mint leaves, and cilantro leaves.
16. Drizzle the saffron milk evenly over the top layer of rice, and place the remaining ghee on top.
17. Seal the edge of the pot lid with dough to close any gaps, or wrap the lid tightly with aluminum foil, ensuring a complete seal.
18. Heat over high heat for 3 minutes until steam begins to escape, then immediately reduce the heat to the lowest setting and cook for 25-30 minutes.
19. After turning off the heat, keep the pot sealed for an additional 5 minutes without opening the lid.
20. Open the lid, gently fluff the biryani from the bottom using a large spatula, and serve.

## Health Tips
This biryani is nutritionally well-rounded, with chicken providing high-quality protein, and saffron and spices aiding digestion. It is suitable for muscle building or can be paired with a salad to balance calories. Be mindful of the rice portion to avoid excess carbs. Those with a sensitive stomach should reduce the amount of chili.
