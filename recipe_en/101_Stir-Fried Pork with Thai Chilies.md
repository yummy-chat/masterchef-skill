# Stir-Fried Pork with Thai Chilies
Cuisine: Hunan Cuisine
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌶️ Thai chilies (to taste)
🥩 Pork belly (to taste)
🥩 Lean pork (to taste)
🧄 Garlic (to taste)
🧄 Ginger (to taste)
🫒 Peanut oil (to taste)
🧂 Salt (to taste)
🫗 Light soy sauce (to taste)
🦪 Oyster sauce (to taste)
🌶️ Doubanjiang (chili bean paste) (to taste)
🧂 Chicken bouillon (to taste)
🍬 Sugar (to taste)

## Utensils and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🔪 Chef's knife
🪵 Cutting board

## Instructions
1. Wash the Thai chilies and slice them diagonally into larger pieces.  
**Key Point**: Slicing diagonally increases the surface area for heat, making it easier to release the spiciness.

2. Shred or slice the pork belly or lean pork, then marinate with light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and salt for 5 minutes.  
**Key Point**: Do not marinate for too long to prevent the meat from becoming too salty.

3. Heat the wok over high heat, add an appropriate amount of peanut oil, and stir-fry the marinated pork until it just changes color. Remove and set aside.  
**Key Point**: Stir-fry quickly over high heat; remove as soon as the meat changes color to keep it tender.

4. Leave a base layer of oil in the wok, add ginger and garlic to release their fragrance, then add Doubanjiang and stir-fry until red oil is released.  
**Key Point**: Doubanjiang must be fully fried, with sufficient red oil, to give the dish a bright red color.

5. Add the sliced Thai chilies and stir-fry, then return the cooked pork to the wok. Add a small amount of light soy sauce, chicken bouillon, salt, and sugar. Stir-fry evenly and remove from heat.  
**Key Point**: Adding sugar at the end enhances freshness and balances the spiciness. Watch the heat to avoid burning.

## Health Advice
Thai chilies are rich in vitamin C, but their high spiciness may irritate the stomach. It is recommended to eat with rice to reduce irritation. Pork belly has a high fat content and is suitable for occasional consumption. People with high blood pressure should be mindful of the sodium content from salt and Doubanjiang.
