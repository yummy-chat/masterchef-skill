# Beer-Braised Grass Carp
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

🌶️ Red chili peppers (适量, minced)
🌿 Cilantro (适量, for garnish)

🔪 Cutting board
🔪 Chef's knife
🥣 Bowl
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Ladle or spoon
🍲 Lid

## Instructions
1.  Clean the grass carp and cut it straight into evenly sized pieces.
2.  In a bowl, combine light soy sauce, a small amount of dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, ground white pepper, and chili powder. Stir until evenly mixed to create the marinade.
3.  Pour the marinade over the fish pieces, gently mix until coated, and let marinate for 15 minutes.
4.  Slice the onion, wash the enoki mushrooms after removing the roots, mince the red chili peppers, and wash the cilantro. Set aside.
5.  Place the wok over heat, add a small amount of oil, then add Sichuan peppercorns, scallion sections, and ginger slices. Stir-fry over low heat until fragrant.  
    **Note**: Use low heat to release the aroma and prevent the aromatics from burning.
6.  Over the aromatics, first layer the sliced onion, then evenly distribute the enoki mushrooms, covering the entire bottom of the wok.
7.  Arrange the marinated fish pieces neatly on top of the enoki mushrooms, avoiding overlapping as much as possible.
8.  Pour in the beer until most of the ingredients are submerged. Cover with the lid and braise over medium heat for about 15 minutes, until the broth comes to a boil and the fish is cooked through.  
    **Judgment**: The fish is done when the broth is bubbling and a chopstick can easily pierce the thickest part of the fish.
9.  Remove the lid, sprinkle with minced red chili peppers, and garnish with cilantro before serving.

## Health Advice
This dish uses beer instead of water for braising, resulting in tender fish with reduced fishy odor. It suits those who enjoy a fresh, spicy flavor and need high-quality protein. Note that the enoki mushroom layer should not be too thick, or the fish will be cooked before the mushrooms absorb the flavor. Those in the acute phase of gout should limit consumption of the broth.
