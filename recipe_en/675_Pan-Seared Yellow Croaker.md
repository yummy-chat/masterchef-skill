# Pan-Seared Yellow Croaker
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 Yellow Croaker 2
🥩 Pork Belly about 5-6 slices
🫚 Ginger 3-4 slices
🧄 Garlic 4-5 cloves
🧅 Scallion 2 sections (white part)
🧅 Onion about 1/4 piece (cut into chunks)
🌿 Scallion Greens 1 small bunch
🧂 Salt about 2 teaspoons (for marinating and final seasoning)
🥓 Lard (or other cooking oil) as needed
🍶 Yellow Wine about 2 tablespoons
🥤 Light Soy Sauce about 1 tablespoon
🌶️ White Pepper Powder a pinch
🍬 White Sugar 1 tablespoon
💧 Boiling Water as needed (about enough to cover half the fish)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's Knife
🪓 Cutting Board
🍳 Wok (non-stick pan)
🥄 Spatula
🥢 Chopsticks (for removing fish innards)
🧂 Seasoning Bowl/Dish

## Cooking Steps
1. **Prepare the Yellow Croaker**: Make a horizontal cut above the fin, insert chopsticks from the gill into the belly and twist, pulling out the gills and innards through the mouth.
   **Key Point**: Keep the fish body intact; be sure to remove the hard skin above the eyes.
2. **Score the Fish**: Make several even cuts on both sides of the fish, with consistent depth.
3. **Marinate**: Rub salt evenly over the entire fish, inside and out, gently massaging for a moment to allow the salt to penetrate.
   **Key Point**: Salt is key for enhancing freshness; massage evenly.
4. **Render the Pork Belly**: Heat the wok and add lard. Once melted, add the pork belly slices and stir-fry over medium heat until the fat is fully rendered, the slices become translucent, and the edges are slightly crispy.
5. **Aromatics**: Add ginger slices, garlic cloves, scallion sections, and onion chunks. Stir-fry over medium heat until fragrant.
6. **Pan-Fry the Fish**: Place the marinated fish skin-side down on top of the aromatics. Pan-fry over high heat for 4-5 minutes until the skin is set and golden brown.
   **Key Point**: Do not move the fish immediately after adding it to prevent the skin from breaking.
7. **Deglaze and Add Water**: Pour yellow wine along the edge of the wok. After the wine aroma has dissipated, add boiling water.
   **Key Point**: Must use boiling water, to prevent the fish flesh from contracting and becoming tough when meeting cold liquid.
8. **Season and Simmer**: Add light soy sauce, white pepper powder, and white sugar. Gently stir the broth to combine. Cover the wok and simmer over low heat for 15 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Slow simmer over low heat allows the fish to absorb the flavors while remaining tender and juicy.
9. **Reduce and Adjust Seasoning**: Remove the lid and taste. Add a little more salt if needed. Turn the heat to high and reduce the sauce, repeatedly spooning the broth over the fish. Cook until the broth thickens and coats the fish.
10. **Serve**: Just before turning off the heat, sprinkle with scallion greens. Let the residual heat release their aroma, then plate.
    **Key Point**: Add scallion greens at the end to keep them bright green and fragrant.

## Health Advice
Yellow croaker is rich in high-quality protein and unsaturated fatty acids. Paired with pork belly for added flavor, it is suitable for adults and the elderly who need nutritional supplementation. Pay attention to controlling the salt amount. People with high blood pressure can reduce the salt used in marinating and final seasoning.
