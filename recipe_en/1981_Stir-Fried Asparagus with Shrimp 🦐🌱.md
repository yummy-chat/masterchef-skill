# Stir-Fried Asparagus with Shrimp 🦐🌱
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Spring (January to April)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌱 Asparagus 300g (choose thin ones, remove tough skin, cut diagonally into sections)
🍄 White mushrooms 200g (remove stems, cut into thick slices)
🦐 Shrimp approx. 250g (thaw, butterfly to remove vein, squeeze dry)
🖤 Soaked black fungus, as needed
🌶️ Red chili pepper, as needed (sliced)
🧄 Ginger, as needed (part sliced, part crushed)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (part sliced, part crushed)
🧅 Scallion, as needed (crushed, for marinating shrimp)
🧂 Salt, as needed (used in stages)
🌫️ White pepper powder, as needed (to remove fishy smell and enhance aroma)
🍬 White sugar, as needed (to enhance freshness)
🧂 MSG, as needed (to enhance flavor)
🌽 Cornstarch 1 tsp (for sauce) + a little (for coating shrimp)
💧 Water, a little (for the sauce)
🫒 Sesame oil, a little (for the sauce)
🥓 Lard, as needed (for stir-frying)
🫒 Cooking oil, a little (for blanching)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Peeler
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🍳 Wok or deep pan
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Basin for water
🥣 Bowls/containers
🥄 Spoons

## Cooking Steps
1. **Prepare ingredients**: Cut asparagus diagonally into sections, slice mushrooms thickly, slice red chili, soak black fungus in advance; slice part of the ginger and garlic, crush the other part; crush scallions and set aside.
2. **Marinate shrimp**: Add crushed scallion and ginger, salt, and white pepper powder to the shrimp. Toss until sticky. Press and crush the scallion and ginger to release juice, continue mixing well. Add a little cornstarch to coat the shrimp.
3. **Prepare sauce**: In a bowl, mix salt, white sugar, MSG, white pepper powder, 1 tsp cornstarch, and a little water. Stir well, then drizzle in sesame oil and set aside.
4. **Blanch**: Bring water to a boil, add a little cooking oil and salt. First add mushrooms and black fungus, blanch for about 1 minute. Then add asparagus and red chili, blanch for 30 seconds. Remove and immediately immerse in cold water to cool, then drain well. Bring the water to a boil again, add shrimp, blanch until they change color, then remove.
   - **Key points**: Adding oil and salt during blanching helps maintain the color of ingredients. Cooling in cold water makes vegetables crisper.
5. **Stir-fry**: Heat the wok, add lard and melt it. Add ginger slices and garlic slices, stir-fry over low heat until fragrant. Turn to high heat, add all the vegetables. Use a combination of pan-frying and stir-frying until the surface is slightly browned and charred. Then add the shrimp and stir-fry quickly.
   - **Note**: The pan-frying method helps release wok hei (breath of the wok) and avoids prolonged stir-frying, which could cause ingredients to release water.
6. **Thicken sauce and serve**: Stir the sauce again, then pour it into the wok. Stir-fry quickly until the sauce evenly coats all ingredients. Once the sauce reduces and there is no excess liquid left at the bottom, remove from heat and plate.

## Health Tips
This dish is high in protein and low in fat. Asparagus is rich in dietary fiber, making it suitable for weight loss or those seeking balanced nutrition. Marinating and blanching the shrimp results in a tender texture, but be mindful of salt usage to avoid excessive sodium intake.
