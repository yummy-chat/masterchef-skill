# Pink Crispy Noodles  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍜 Noodles (e.g., instant noodle cake from mushroom chicken flavor)  
🟥 Super iron block (for coloring)  
🧂 Calcium powder  
🧂 MSG  
🐟 Fish roe paste  
🧂 Zinc soybean paste  
🌿 Dried seaweed flakes  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🔥 Oven  
🫙 Ziplock bag  
🥖 Rolling pin  

## Instructions  
1. Bring water to a boil in a wok. Add the noodles and the super iron block dye, and cook until the noodles are tender and the water and noodles turn pink.  
   **Note**: The super iron block is only used for coloring. It can be substituted with food coloring.

2. Remove the cooked pink noodles and place them in a bowl. Add calcium powder, MSG, fish roe paste, zinc soybean paste, and dried seaweed flakes in order.

3. Use chopsticks to thoroughly mix all seasonings with the noodles, ensuring each strand is evenly coated with the sauce and flakes.

4. Transfer the seasoned noodles into a ziplock bag and use a rolling pin to flatten them into a thin sheet.  
   **Key point**: Rolling flat ensures even heat distribution during baking, resulting in a crispy texture.

5. Place the flattened noodle sheet in a preheated oven. Bake until completely dehydrated, the color deepens, and the texture becomes crispy.  
   **Note**: Specific temperature and time are not provided. It is recommended to bake at 150–160°C and monitor the progress.

6. Once removed and cooled, the pink crispy noodles are ready. Break into pieces for serving.

## Health Advice  
This pink crispy noodle recipe relies heavily on seasoning additives. It is recommended to consume only occasionally as a snack and not as a meal replacement. Although calcium powder and fish roe paste provide some minerals, the sodium content is high. People with high blood pressure should avoid excessive intake.
