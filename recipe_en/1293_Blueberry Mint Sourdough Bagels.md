# Blueberry Mint Sourdough Bagels
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Bread flour, as needed (for the dough)  
🧂 Salt, as needed (for the dough and avocado spread)  
🫐 Blueberry mint sourdough starter liquid, as needed (strained, fermented for 4 days)  
🥚 Eggs, 2 (for fried sunny-side-up eggs)  
🥓 Bacon, 3 strips (fried until crispy)  
🥑 Avocado, 1 (for making spread)  
🧀 Fresh mozzarella balls, as needed (about 3 per bagel)  
🍊 Oranges, as needed (cut into wedges for serving)  
🍋 Lemon juice, as needed (added to avocado spread)  
🧂 Black pepper, as needed (ground into avocado spread)  
🌶️ Chili flakes (or chili powder), as needed (sprinkled on top of finished bagels)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🫙 Glass jar (for fermenting the starter liquid)  
🫗 Strainer (for straining the starter liquid)  
🍞 Stand mixer (with dough hook, for kneading)  
🍳 Baking sheet, parchment paper (for proofing and baking)  
🍳 Skillet (for boiling bagels)  
🔥 Oven (for baking bagels)  
♨️ Air fryer (for reheating bagels and re-baking with toppings)  
🍳 Frying pan (for frying eggs and bacon)  
🥣 Glass bowl (for mixing avocado spread)  
🍴 Fork (for mashing avocado)  
🔪 Knife, cutting board (for cutting ingredients)  
💧 Spray bottle (for spraying water when reheating to keep moist)

## Instructions
1. **Check and Use the Starter Liquid**  
   Take out the blueberry mint starter liquid that has been fermented for 4 days. Open the lid and observe; if a large amount of bubbles rises, it indicates sufficient activity. Strain the starter liquid and set aside the pure liquid for use.  
   **Note**: No additional activity test is needed.

2. **Mix and Knead the Dough**  
   Combine bread flour, salt, and the strained starter liquid. Knead with a stand mixer until a dough forms.  
   **Key Point**: No commercial dry yeast is added throughout; it relies entirely on the natural starter liquid for fermentation.

3. **Dough Fermentation**  
   Let the kneaded dough ferment at room temperature until it doubles in volume, about 6 hours.

4. **Divide and Shape**  
   Divide the fermented dough, round it into balls, cover with plastic wrap and let it rest, then roll into long strips and join the ends to form bagel rings.  
   **Note**: Pinch the seams tightly to prevent them from opening during boiling and baking.

5. **Final Proofing**  
   Place the shaped bagel dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Let it proof again until it visibly expands.

6. **Boil the Bagels**  
   Heat water in a skillet. Place the proofed bagels, still on the parchment paper, into the water. Boil each side for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then flip. Remove and drain.  
   **Purpose**: This step creates a chewy crust, which is key to the bagel texture.

7. **Bake the Bagels**  
   Preheat the oven. Place the boiled bagels in and bake until the surface is evenly golden brown and the inside is fully cooked.

8. **Prepare the Brunch Toppings**  
   - Fried sunny-side-up eggs: Fry an egg in the pan with the yolk intact.  
   - Fried bacon: Use the remaining oil in the pan or start a new pan, and fry the bacon until crispy.  
   - **Note**: Bacon can splatter oil; clean carefully.  
   - Make avocado spread: Add lemon juice, black pepper, and a pinch of salt to the avocado flesh. Mash with a fork into a smooth paste.  
   - Cut oranges: Wash and cut oranges into wedges to serve as a side fruit.

9. **Reheat and Assemble the Bagels**  
   - Slice the overnight or cooled bagel horizontally and spray a little water on the cut sides.  
   - Place in an air fryer and reheat at 180°C for about 3 minutes.  
   **Key Point**: Spraying water prevents the bagels from becoming too dry.  
   - Spread avocado spread on the cut sides of the reheated bagels and arrange mozzarella balls on top.  
   - For a more integrated flavor, place the spread bagels back in the air fryer and re-bake at 180°C for 2 to 3 minutes, until the cheese is slightly melted.  
   - Remove, sprinkle with chili flakes, and serve with sunny-side-up eggs, bacon, and orange wedges.

## Health Tips
Blueberry mint sourdough bagels are rich in B vitamins and dietary fiber from natural yeast fermentation. Paired with the healthy fats in avocado spread, they are suitable for those on a low-sugar diet. Bacon and cheese are higher in calories, so it is recommended to control portions.
