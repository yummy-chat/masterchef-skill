# Microwave Mandarin Fish
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 1 mandarin fish (with slits cut on the back)
🫚 2-3 slices of ginger (inserted into the slits on the fish)
🥬 1-2 whole napa cabbage leaves (covering the fish)
🫗适量 steamed fish soy sauce (drizzled at the end)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
♨️ Microwave oven
🍽️ Microwave-safe deep dish or bowl

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the prepared mandarin fish in the deep dish. Insert the ginger slices into the slits cut on the fish.
2. Take the washed napa cabbage leaves and completely cover the fish, ensuring the fish is fully wrapped.
   **Key Point**: The cabbage leaves help lock in moisture, simulating the steaming effect and preventing the fish from drying out during microwave heating.
3. Place the dish in the microwave and heat on high power.
4. Once heating is complete, remove the dish and discard the covering cabbage leaves. While the fish is still hot, evenly drizzle steamed fish soy sauce over it and serve.

## Health Tips
Mandarin fish is low in fat and high in protein. The microwave cooking method uses minimal oil and is light, making it suitable for those watching their calorie intake. It is recommended to serve with a side of lightly stir-fried seasonal vegetables to balance the moisture and fiber from the cabbage leaves. Note that the heating time should be kept on the shorter side to avoid overcooking and shrinking the fish.
