# Luxurious Instant Noodles 🍜
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍜 Instant noodles – 1 packet
🥚 Eggs – 2
🥩 Ham sausage or luncheon meat – as desired
🍢 Hot pot balls (fish balls, shrimp balls, crab sticks, etc.) – as desired
🍡 Rice cakes – as desired
🥖 Hot pot youtiao (fried dough sticks) – as desired
🥓 Beef rolls – as desired
🥬 Baby bok choy – as desired
🧅 Onion – 1/2 (medium-sized)
🍅 Tomato – 1 (optional)
🧄 Scallion whites – 1 stalk
🌿 Scallions or cilantro – as desired
🫑 Green bell pepper – as desired (julienned)
🌶️ Bird’s eye chili – as desired (sliced into rounds)
🧄 Minced garlic – as desired

🫒 Cooking oil – as needed
🧂 Instant noodle seasoning packet – 1 packet
🧂 Light soy sauce – 1–2 tbsp
🥜 Sesame paste – 1 tbsp
🥜 Peanut butter – 1 tbsp
🦪 Oyster sauce – 1 tbsp
🫒 Sesame oil – 1 tbsp
🍅 Tomato paste – 1/2 tbsp
🍬 Granulated sugar – 2 pinches
🧂 Chicken bouillon – 1 pinch
🧂 Beef bouillon powder – 2 pinches
💧 Water – 2 tbsp
🌶️ Chili oil – as desired (optional)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🔥 Gas stove or induction cooktop
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Measuring spoon or small spoon
🥢 Spatula or chopsticks
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board

## Instructions
### Preparation Phase
1. **Prepare the ingredients**
   - Slice scallion whites, cut onion or tomato into small pieces, julienne green bell pepper, slice bird’s eye chili into rounds, and mince garlic, scallions, and cilantro.
   - Slice thicker hot pot balls into thin rounds.

2. **Prepare the mixed sauce**
   - In a bowl, add sesame paste, peanut butter, oyster sauce, sesame oil, light soy sauce, tomato paste, sugar, chicken bouillon, beef bouillon powder, and 2 tbsp water in that order.
   **Key point**: Stir in one direction until thick and smooth, the color lightens, and it reaches a yogurt-like consistency. Set aside.

### Cooking Phase
1. **Pan-fry the add-ins**
   - Pour oil into the frying pan, heat over medium heat, and crack in the eggs. You can flip and fry until golden on both sides, or fry only one side and then add water to make poached eggs.
   - Fry the ham sausage or luncheon meat until the surface is slightly charred.
   **Note**: Use minimal oil to avoid splattering.

2. **Sauté the base aromatics**
   - Remove the fried eggs and set aside. Leave about a tablespoon of oil in the pan. Over medium heat, sauté the scallion whites until fragrant, then add the onion or tomato pieces. Stir-fry until the onion softens and the tomato releases its juice.
   **Key point**: Thoroughly sautéing the aromatics enhances their fragrance and sweetness, which is key to elevating the overall flavor. For a quicker version, skip this step and add water directly.

3. **Build the broth and cook the ingredients**
   - Add the instant noodle seasoning powder or paste to the sautéed base and stir until well combined.
   - Add water – 400ml for a richer broth, or about 600ml if you prefer more soup.
   - Before the water boils, add the rice cakes, hot pot balls, and hot pot youtiao, which need longer cooking.
   - Keep the heat at medium. Once the broth comes to a boil, add the beef rolls and cook until they change color.
   **Doneness check**: Use chopsticks to poke a ball – if it goes through easily, it’s done.
   **Key point**: Ladle 2 spoonfuls of the boiling broth into the prepared sesame paste bowl, stir quickly until smooth and thin, and set aside.

4. **Cook the noodles and combine**
   - Once the ingredients in the pot are mostly cooked, turn the heat to medium-high. Add baby bok choy and the instant noodle block.
   - Cook for about 1 minute, until the bok choy is just wilted and the noodles have softened.
   - Return the fried eggs to the pot.
   - Pour the thinned sauce evenly over everything.
   - Sprinkle with julienned green bell pepper, bird’s eye chili, minced scallions and cilantro, and minced garlic.
   **Doneness check**: Cook the noodles to your preferred doneness, then turn off the heat.
   **Key point**: Adding the sauce at the end helps it coat the noodles and ingredients better, resulting in a richer flavor.

## Health Advice
This luxurious instant noodle dish has a good combination of protein and vegetables, but it is high in sodium and calories. It is recommended to limit soup consumption and control the frequency of eating it. Suitable for occasional indulgence; not recommended for regular consumption by those on a weight-loss diet or with high blood pressure.
