# Home-Style Braised Pan-Fried Tofu and Eggs
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧈 Firm tofu 1 block (cut into triangular pieces about 1 cm thick)  
🥚 Egg 1 (pan-fried and cut into triangular pieces)  
🥬 Garlic sprouts appropriate amount (cut into small sections)  
🌶️ Small chili peppers appropriate amount (sliced into rings)  
🧅 Scallions appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (minced)  
🧂 Dark soy sauce appropriate amount  
🧂 Light soy sauce appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt appropriate amount  
🦪 Oyster sauce appropriate amount  
💧 Water appropriate amount  
🌽 Cornstarch appropriate amount  
🫒 Sesame oil a little

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🥄 Cooking spatula  
🥣 Bowl

## Steps
1. Cut the firm tofu into triangular pieces about 1 cm thick, ensuring even shape and thickness.
2. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, combine dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, salt, oyster sauce, water, and cornstarch, stirring until well mixed. Set aside.  
   **Note**: Preparing the sauce in advance makes the cooking process smoother; cornstarch is key to thickening the sauce.
3. Heat oil in a pan. Once the oil is hot, add the tofu pieces and pan-fry over medium heat.  
   **Key point**: Do not flip the tofu immediately after adding it to the pan. Wait about 1 minute until the bottom is set and firm, then gently shake the pan. Avoid frequent flipping to prevent the tofu from breaking.
4. Pan-fry until both sides are golden brown, then remove and set aside.
5. For the egg, you can either scramble it or fry it whole over low to medium heat until both sides are golden brown, then remove and cut into triangular pieces similar to the tofu.
6. Leave a little oil in the pan, add the scallion sections and minced garlic, and stir-fry over low to medium heat until fragrant.
7. Pour in the prepared sauce and bring to a boil.
8. Add the pan-fried tofu and egg pieces, and cook over medium heat for about 3 minutes, allowing the ingredients to fully absorb the sauce.
9. Sprinkle in the garlic sprout sections and chili pepper rings to retain their crisp texture and vibrant color.
10. Drizzle in a little sesame oil, stir-fry evenly, then remove from heat and plate.  
    **Key point**: Adding sesame oil at the end maximizes its aroma.

## Health Tips
This dish pairs tofu and eggs, providing a rich source of high-quality protein and calcium, making it suitable for those needing calcium or muscle building. Be careful not to flip the firm tofu too often during pan-frying, and avoid overcooking during the braising process to prevent it from breaking apart.
