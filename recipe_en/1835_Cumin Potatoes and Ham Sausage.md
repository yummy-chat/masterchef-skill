# Cumin Potatoes and Ham Sausage
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes, about 3 (diced)  
🌭 Ham sausage, 1 large piece (diced)  
🧄 Garlic, to taste (minced)  
🧅 Scallions, to taste (chopped)  
🫘 Sesame seeds, to taste (dry-fried with cumin)  
🧂 White pepper, to taste  
🧂 Salt, a pinch  
🧂 Chicken powder, 1.5 tablespoons  
🍬 Sugar, a pinch  
🥄 Soy sauce, 2 tablespoons  
🌿 Cumin, to taste (dry-fried)  
🌶️ Chili powder, to taste  
🫒 Sesame oil, 1 tablespoon

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🍳 Pan  
🥄 Strainer  
🥄 Ladle

## Instructions
1. Dice the potatoes and ham sausage into uniform pieces. Mince the garlic and chop the scallions. In a pan without oil, dry-fry the cumin and sesame seeds over low heat until fragrant. Remove and set aside.

2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the diced potatoes and blanch for 2–3 minutes after the water returns to a boil, until the potatoes are cooked through. Drain thoroughly.  
   **Note**: Ensure the potatoes are well-drained, otherwise they will release water during subsequent frying.

3. Add an appropriate amount of oil to the pan and heat until 70% hot. Add the potatoes and fry over high heat for 5–6 minutes, until the surface is golden brown.  
   **Key Point**: The pan must be hot before adding the potatoes. If the pan is not hot enough, the potatoes will release water and affect the texture.

4. Add the diced ham sausage and continue frying until the sausage surface is crispy.

5. Pour off excess oil, leaving a thin layer. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Return the fried potatoes and ham sausage to the pan, and stir-fry over high heat to incorporate the garlic flavor.

6. Add white pepper, salt, chicken powder, sugar, and soy sauce in sequence. Stir-fry evenly to coat all ingredients thoroughly with the seasonings.

7. Add the dry-fried cumin and sesame seeds, chili powder, and sesame oil. Stir-fry quickly and evenly to distribute the spices onto every piece.

8. Plate and serve.

## Health Tips
Potatoes provide dietary fiber, and ham sausage offers protein. However, since this dish uses a fair amount of oil for frying, it is recommended to pair it with a serving of stir-fried vegetables for balanced nutrition. It works well as a satisfying weekend snack. Individuals with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of salt and soy sauce used.
