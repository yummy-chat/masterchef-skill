# Braised Rice with Morel Mushrooms and Sausages  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Rice About 1 serving (pre-washed and drained)  
🥩 Pork ribs As needed (about 3–4 small pieces)  
🌭 Small sausages About 4 pieces  
🍄 Morel mushroom 1 piece  
🥬 Cabbage About 1/4 piece (torn into small pieces by hand)  
🥕 Carrot About 1 small section (shaved into thin slices)  
🥗 Mixed vegetable bits As needed (finely chopped)  
🧂 Light soy sauce As needed  
🍘 Seaweed rice seasoning As needed  
🌫️ Browning spray (seasoning spray) As needed (add before serving)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍚 Rice cooker  
🍳 Inner pot of rice cooker  
🔪 Cutting board + kitchen knife  
🥒 Vegetable peeler  

## Cooking Steps  
1. **Rice pre-treatment**: Wash the rice thoroughly, drain the water, and place it in the inner pot of the rice cooker.  
   **Key point**: Make sure to drain well after washing to avoid excess moisture, which could make the rice too soft.  
2. **Prepare the side ingredients**: Finely chop the mixed vegetable bits, tear the cabbage into small pieces by hand, and shave the carrot into thin slices with a peeler. Prepare the pork ribs, sausages, and morel mushroom.  
   **Key point**: Shaved carrot slices cook more easily than chunks, and hand-torn cabbage absorbs the broth better.  
3. **Combine ingredients and basic seasoning**: Add the mixed vegetable bits, pork ribs, sausages, morel mushroom, cabbage, and carrot slices to the inner pot in order. Then add the seaweed rice seasoning and drizzle in an appropriate amount of light soy sauce. Mix well.  
   **Key point**: Complete the basic seasoning before braising so the ingredients fully absorb the flavors during cooking.  
4. **Braise**: Place the inner pot into the rice cooker, close the lid, select the regular rice cooking mode, start the program, and braise until it automatically switches off.  
   **Key point**: No manual heat control is needed throughout; rely on the rice cooker’s automatic program.  
5. **Finish seasoning and stir after cooking**: After the braising is done, open the lid, drizzle in an appropriate amount of browning spray, and thoroughly mix the rice with all the ingredients.  
   **Key point**: Adding the seasoning spray before serving enhances flavor distribution, ensuring every bite is well-seasoned.  

## Health Advice  
Morel mushrooms are rich in fungal polysaccharides and dietary fiber. Paired with pork ribs and sausages, they provide high-quality protein and iron, making this dish suitable for those needing iron supplementation or muscle building. However, sausages have a high sodium content; for those with high blood pressure, it is recommended to use half the amount and increase the cabbage and carrot in the braised rice to balance the sodium-to-potassium ratio.
