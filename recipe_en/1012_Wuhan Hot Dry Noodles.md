# Wuhan Hot Dry Noodles
Cuisine: Hubei Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍜 Alkaline noodles, as needed (dried noodles)  
🫘 Sesame paste, 7 parts (used in combination with peanut butter)  
🥜 Peanut butter, 3 parts  
🦴 Beef bones, as needed (pre-boiled into a milky white broth)  
🧅 Onion, as needed  
🥕 Carrot, as needed  
🧄 Spring onion, as needed  
🫚 Ginger, as needed  
🧄 Garlic, as needed  
🌿 Spices (star anise, bay leaves, cinnamon, angelica, Sichuan peppercorns, cardamom, cumin), a small amount of each  
🫘 Pickled long beans, as needed  
🌿 Cilantro, as needed  
🧅 Small scallions, as needed  
🛢️ Cooking oil, as needed (for infused oil)  
🧂 Soy sauce, 4 small spoons (for mixing sesame paste)  
🧂 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, as needed  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🧂 White pepper powder, as needed  
🫒 Rapeseed oil, as needed (to prevent noodles from sticking)  
🌶️ Chili oil, as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
♨️ Wok  
🥘 Clay pot  
🍲 Soup pot  
🥄 Strainer spoon  
🥣 Glass container  
🔍 Fine mesh sieve  
🧣 Cloth bag  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Place the wok over heat and pour in a sufficient amount of cooking oil. While the oil is still cold, add onion, carrot, spring onion, ginger, garlic, star anise, bay leaves, cinnamon, angelica, Sichuan peppercorns, cardamom, and cumin. Fry over medium-low heat slowly until the spices turn golden brown and the aroma is fully released.  
**Key point**: Ensure to fry slowly over medium-low heat to allow the fragrance to fully infuse into the oil. After frying, remove the spice solids, and strain the infused oil through a fine mesh sieve into a glass container for later use. Wrap the strained spice solids in a cloth bag and set aside.  

2. Mix the sesame paste and peanut butter in a 7:3 ratio until well combined. First, add 4 small spoons of soy sauce and stir until it becomes thick and sticky.  
**Key point**: Then apply the "oil-loosening" technique by adding the prepared infused oil little by little to the thick sesame paste while stirring continuously, until the paste becomes smooth, fine, and glossy.  

3. Pour the prepared beef bone broth into a clay pot. Add the cloth bag with the spice solids, along with light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, salt, and white pepper powder to season. Keep it at a gentle simmer to allow the broth to develop a richer depth of flavor.  
**Key point**: Maintaining a gentle simmer allows the spice flavors to fully incorporate into the broth.  

4. Bring water to a boil in a soup pot. Add the alkaline noodles and cook until the center of the noodles still retains a white core.  
**Key point**: This stage is essential for ensuring the noodles are chewy and springy. After removing, immediately drizzle with rapeseed oil and use chopsticks to quickly toss and separate the noodles, ensuring each strand is evenly coated with oil. This creates "brushed noodles" to enhance flavor and prevent sticking.  

5. When serving, take a suitable amount of the brushed noodles in a strainer spoon and quickly blanch them in boiling water or broth. Immediately transfer to a bowl.  
**Key point**: Keep the blanching time short to avoid the noodles becoming too soft and losing their elasticity.  

6. Pour the seasoned beef bone broth and fragrant sesame paste into the bowl. Then sprinkle pickled long beans, cilantro, and small scallions on top.  
**Key point**: The ratio of sesame paste to broth can be adjusted according to personal taste.  

7. Finally, drizzle with bright red chili oil. The eater should quickly mix the noodles thoroughly with the sauce and toppings before eating.  
**Key point**: Mixing quickly prevents the noodles from clumping and ensures the best texture.  

## Health Suggestions  
Hot Dry Noodles are rich in sesame paste and peanut butter, providing high-quality fatty acids, but they are also high in calories. It is recommended to consume in moderation as breakfast and pair with vegetables to balance nutrition. The beef bone broth is rich in calcium, making it suitable for individuals needing calcium supplementation.
