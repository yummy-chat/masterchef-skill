# Baby Silkie Chicken and Vegetable Microwave Rice Meal  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Raw rice, as needed  
🍗 Silkie chicken piece, 1 piece (pre-cooked)  
🍗 Small chicken drumstick, 1 piece  
🥬 Leafy greens, about 2 small stalks (choose varieties that soften easily)  
💊 Probiotic granules, as needed  
🫒 Hair oil (baby辅食 oil), as needed  
💧 Water or broth, as needed (to cover part of the rice grains)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🥣 Microwave-safe bowl  
🍽️ Ceramic plate  
📡 Microwave oven  
🥄 Small spoon  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Take a heat-resistant microwave-safe bowl and add an appropriate amount of raw rice.  
   **Note**: The bowl must be microwave-safe to avoid damage from high heat.  
2. Evenly sprinkle the probiotic granules over the surface of the raw rice.  
   **Note**: Probiotics require no pre-treatment; sprinkle directly onto the rice grains. They will be absorbed by the rice during the heating process.  
3. Place the small chicken drumstick, pre-cooked silkie chicken piece, and washed leafy greens in layers on top of the rice.  
   **Note**: Placing meats and vegetables on top of the rice ensures more even heating during microwave cooking.  
4. Drip in an appropriate amount of baby辅食 oil, then add enough water or broth. The liquid should cover part of the rice grains.  
   **Note**: The liquid amount should be sufficient to ensure the rice absorbs enough moisture to become soft and to prevent scorching during heating.  
5. Place a ceramic plate upside down over the bowl opening to seal it completely.  
   **Note**: Sealing prevents food from splattering during microwave heating and reduces cleaning effort.  
6. Place the sealed bowl in the microwave and heat on high for 5–8 minutes until all ingredients are thoroughly cooked.  
   **Note**: The specific heating time should be adjusted according to the power of your microwave oven.  
7. Carefully remove the heated bowl and lift off the sealing ceramic plate.  
8. Use a small spoon to thoroughly stir and mix all ingredients in the bowl, combining the rice, meat, and vegetables. Serve as baby辅食.  
   **Note**: Stirring slightly lowers the food temperature, and mixing makes it easier for the baby to chew and swallow.  

## Health Advice  
This baby meal cleverly combines silkie chicken, leafy greens, and probiotic granules. It is nutritionally complete and easy to digest, suitable for babies aged 6 months and above who are gradually starting complementary foods. Control the amount of meat and oil to avoid greasiness, and ensure the rice is cooked to a soft texture to protect the baby's delicate stomach.
