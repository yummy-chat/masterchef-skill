# Jiangxi Spicy Chicken Wings
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken mid-joint wings, as needed (blanched and set aside)
🧄 Minced garlic, as needed
🌶️ Small red chili peppers, as needed (sliced into rings)
🫑 Green peppers, as needed (cut into sections)
🌿 Cilantro, as needed (for final aroma)
🧂 Light soy sauce, 3 tablespoons
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon
🧂 Chicken bouillon, 1 teaspoon
🍬 White sugar, 1 teaspoon
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Spatula
🥢 Chopsticks

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the chicken wings in cold water and blanch. After boiling, skim off the foam, then drain and set aside.
   **Note**: Blanching removes gamey odors and pre-cooks the wings, making it easier to control the heat during frying.
2. Heat the wok, add cooking oil. Once the oil is hot, add the blanched chicken wings and fry over medium-low heat.
   **Key Point**: Fry until both sides are golden brown, ensuring the surface is set and the inside is fully cooked.
3. Add the minced garlic, small red chili pepper rings, and green pepper sections. Stir-fry until the aromas of garlic and chili peppers are released.
4. Premix the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, chicken bouillon, and white sugar into a sauce. Pour it along the edge of the wok.
   **Note**: Turn the heat to high at this point and stir-fry quickly to evenly coat each chicken wing with the sauce.
5. Sprinkle in the cilantro sections, stir-fry over high heat a few times until the cilantro softens, then remove from the wok and plate.
   **Key Point**: Add the cilantro last to preserve its fresh aroma; do not overcook.

## Health Advice
This dish is rich in protein, and pairing it with chili peppers can boost metabolism, making it suitable for stimulating appetite when lacking it. However, it uses a relatively high amount of high-sodium seasonings, so individuals with high blood pressure should consume it sparingly.
