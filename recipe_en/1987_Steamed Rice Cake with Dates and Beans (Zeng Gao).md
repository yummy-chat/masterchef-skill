# Steamed Rice Cake with Dates and Beans (Zeng Gao)
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Glutinous rice appropriate amount (soak for more than 6 hours in advance)
🫒 Dried red dates appropriate amount (pitted and cut into pieces)
🫘 Red beans appropriate amount (soak for 24 hours in advance)
🍇 Raisins appropriate amount (washed)
🫘 Candied dates appropriate amount (use more, to cover the top layer)
💧 Water appropriate amount (add in two batches)
🧂 Seasonings none (sweetness comes entirely from red dates, raisins, and candied dates)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, knife
🥣 Large bowl
🍚 Rice cooker
🪜 Steaming rack
🫗 Measuring cup or kettle
🥄 Spoon

## Preparation Steps
1.  Pit the dried red dates and cut them into pieces, ensuring the pits are completely removed.
2.  Soak the glutinous rice for more than 6 hours in advance until the grains turn white and absorb water; soak the red beans for 24 hours in advance until the beans are fully expanded.
3.  Spread a layer of red date pieces evenly on the bottom of the large bowl, then add a layer of soaked glutinous rice. Next, spread a layer of washed raisins, followed by another layer of glutinous rice. Then, spread a layer of soaked red beans, and add another layer of glutinous rice. Finally, cover the top layer completely with candied dates.
    **Note**: Each layer must be spread evenly. After spreading, press all ingredients firmly with your hands.
4.  Add water to the bowl, just enough to submerge the top layer of candied dates.
5.  Add an appropriate amount of water to the bottom of the rice cooker's inner pot and place the steaming rack inside.
6.  Place the large bowl steadily on the steaming rack, cover the lid, and start the steam function to steam for about 2 hours.
7.  After steaming is complete, let it steam (keep warm) in the rice cooker overnight.
    **Key point**: The long steaming (keep warm) process is the crucial step for achieving the soft, sticky, and chewy texture of Zeng Gao.
8.  Use the back of a spoon to press the top layer of candied dates into a paste and smooth the surface.
9.  Serve in small bowls.

## Health Advice
The sweetness of Zeng Gao comes from natural ingredients. It is rich in carbohydrates, iron, and dietary fiber, making it suitable for warming the body in winter or replenishing energy after exercise. However, glutinous rice and candied dates have a high glycemic index; people with diabetes or those controlling blood sugar should consume it in small amounts.
