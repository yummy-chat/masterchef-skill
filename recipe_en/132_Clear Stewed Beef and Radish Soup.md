# Clear Stewed Beef and Radish Soup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Warm and nourishing in autumn and winter, suitable for all seasons

## Steps
1. Place the pre-blanched beef short ribs into the pot and add enough hot water.
   **Key point**: The beef must be pre-blanched to remove blood foam; use hot water during stewing to keep the meat tender.
2. Add scallion sections and ginger slices, cover the lid, and simmer over medium-low heat for 1 hour.
   **Key point**: Scallion and ginger are used to remove odor and enhance flavor; slow simmering over medium-low heat makes the beef soft and tender.
3. Remove the lid, add thick slices of white radish, and continue stewing for half an hour.
   **Heat**: Maintain medium-low heat; add the radish only after the beef has become tender to ensure consistent texture.
4. Add salt, MSG, and chopped cilantro, stir well, then turn off the heat and serve.
   **Key point**: Season at the end to keep the soup clear and the flavor fresh.

## Health Tips
This is a high-quality soup with high protein and low fat. Beef is rich in heme iron, and white radish aids digestion. It is especially suitable for warming the body in autumn and winter and for post-surgery recovery. It is recommended to add the radish only after the beef has been stewed until tender to avoid overcooking the radish and losing its nutrients.
