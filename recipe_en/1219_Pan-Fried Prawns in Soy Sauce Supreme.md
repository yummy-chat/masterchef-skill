# Pan-Fried Prawns in Soy Sauce Supreme
Cuisine Category: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 9-jointed prawns (or giant river prawns, sand shrimp)  About 500g  
🧄 Minced garlic  To taste  
🫑 Red, yellow, and green bell peppers  To taste (cut into chunks)  
🧅 Scallion segments  To taste  
🧈 Butter  About 1 tablespoon  
🥫 Premium soy sauce (light soy sauce)  About 3 tablespoons  
🍯 Dark soy sauce  About 1 teaspoon  
🍬 Rock sugar water  About 1 teaspoon  
🌶️ White pepper  To taste  
🧂 Salt  A pinch  
🍶 Rice wine  A splash  
💧 Water  A small amount  

## Cookware and Utensils  
🍳 Chinese wok  
🥄 Spatula  
🥣 Slotted spoon  
🥣 Bowl  
🍽️ Plate  

## Preparation Steps  
1.  Trim the prawns by cutting off the whiskers and sharp rostrum. Make a slit along the back or belly and remove the digestive tract.  
**Key point**: Opening the back allows the prawns to absorb flavor more easily during cooking.  
2.  In a bowl, combine premium soy sauce, dark soy sauce, rock sugar water, and white pepper. Mix well to form the soy sauce supreme mixture and set aside.  
**Key point**: Using premium soy sauce and rock sugar water is the core of the flavor.  
3.  Heat the wok over high heat, add a small amount of cold oil, and lay the prawns flat in the wok. Fry over medium-low heat until both sides are golden and the prawns are dry. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt to enhance freshness, then remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.  
4.  Leave the base oil in the wok, add minced garlic, and stir-fry over low heat until lightly golden and fragrant.  
5.  Return the fried prawns to the wok, stir-fry evenly with the garlic, splash rice wine along the edge of the wok, and then add a small amount of water.  
**Key point**: Cooking wine removes fishy odors; adding water creates a sauce for braising.  
6.  Add the bell pepper chunks and pour in the prepared soy sauce supreme mixture. Stir-fry evenly to coat the prawns with the sauce.  
7.  Add the butter and scallion segments, quickly stir-fry until the butter melts and the sauce thickens and becomes glossy. Remove from heat and serve on a plate.  
**Key point**: Adding butter at the end preserves its aroma and enhances the shine and fragrance.  

## Health Advice  
This prawn dish is rich in high-quality protein and omega-3 fatty acids, making it suitable for nutritional supplementation. However, the soy sauce supreme mixture and butter are relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to pair this dish with light vegetables. Ensure the digestive tract of the prawns is thoroughly removed, and control the oil temperature during frying to avoid burning.
