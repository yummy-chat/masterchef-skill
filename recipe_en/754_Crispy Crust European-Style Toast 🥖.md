# Crispy Crust European-Style Toast 🥖
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Bread flour 1200g
💧 Water 860g
🍞 Dry yeast powder 8g
🧂 Salt 21g
🫒 Olive oil 60g
🌾 Dusting flour (bread flour) as needed (for anti-stick)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
⚖️ Digital scale
🔲 Kneading mat or large work surface
🥄 Dough scraper
📏 Rolling pin
💦 Spray bottle
🔥 Oven (convection or conventional, steam function recommended)
🍞 Toast loaf pans (450g size, 4 pieces)
🧺 Cooling rack
🧻 Damp cloth

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the bread flour on the work surface, add salt, and roughly mix. Dig a large well in the center of the flour to form a flour wall.
   **Key Point**: The well should be wide; work gently to avoid collapsing the flour wall and causing liquid to spill.
2. Gradually pour the water into the flour well, add the dry yeast powder. Use the dough scraper to stir the yeast and water, and gradually mix in the flour from the inner walls until the mixture forms a shaggy dough with no dry flour.
3. Add the olive oil to the shaggy dough. Using the scraper, gather and press the dough towards the center with a "folding and pressing" motion until the dough is evenly mixed and no dry flour remains.
4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
   **Condition Check**: At room temperature around 25°C, 30 minutes is needed; the dough's extensibility will noticeably improve.
5. After resting, stretch and fold the dough twice: Stretch the dough as thin and wide as possible, fold it towards the center; flatten it, then stretch and fold once more.
   **Key Point**: Strive to stretch it thin each time; perform two folds.
6. Shape the folded dough into a ball, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rest at room temperature for another 20–30 minutes.
7. Stretch the dough again, fold it twice, then round it. Slap the dough on the work surface to tighten and smooth the surface, and knock out large air bubbles.
8. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and perform the bulk fermentation for 60 minutes.
   **Condition Check**: Dust the dough surface with flour and poke a hole. The dough is ready if the hole does not spring back or collapse.
9. Degas the fermented dough, sprinkle a little dusting flour on the surface to prevent sticking, and divide it into 8 equal portions. Round each small piece of dough, place seam side down, cover with a damp cloth, and let rest for 25 minutes.
10. Take one rested small dough piece, lightly dust the surface with some flour, and roll it out into a tongue shape with a rolling pin. Flip it over so the smooth side faces down. Perform a tri-fold, flip it again, and gently roll it out to about 25cm long. Roll it up from top to bottom, pinch the seam to seal, and place it seam-side down at the bottom. Repeat for all dough pieces.
    **Key Point**: Roll gently, knock out edge bubbles; the center should be slightly fuller, with the ends a bit lower when rolling up.
11. Place the rolled dough pieces into the toast pans in pairs, positioning them to facilitate hot air circulation.
12. Baking: When placing into the oven, spray 2–3 bursts of water onto the inner oven walls with a spray bottle to create steam.
    **Temperature Guide**:
    - Convection oven: 190–200°C, bake for about 30 minutes.
    - Conventional oven: Top heat 190°C, bottom heat 230°C, bake for about 40 minutes.
    **Condition Check**: Sugar-free dough browns slowly; remove from oven once the surface reaches a satisfactory golden brown color.
13. Immediately unmold after baking and place the toast on a cooling rack to cool completely.
    **Key Point**: Timely unmolding results in a crispier crust; a "diamond crack" pattern may appear on the surface upon cooling.

## Health Advice
This crispy crust European-style toast is rich in carbohydrates and dietary fiber, making it suitable for post-exercise energy replenishment. However, its salt content is relatively high, so individuals with high blood pressure should control their intake. It is recommended to pair it with protein and vegetables for balanced nutrition.
