# Spicy Stir-Fried Beef Rolls
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Beef rolls (适量, need to be blanched)  
🧄 Minced garlic (适量)  
🌶️ Small red chili peppers (适量, sliced into rings)  
🌶️ Chili flakes (适量)  
🌿 Cilantro (about one large handful, cut into sections)  
🧂 Light soy sauce (2 tbsp)  
🧂 Dark soy sauce (1 tbsp)  
🦪 Oyster sauce (1 tbsp)  
🧂 Chicken bouillon (1 tsp)  
🍬 White sugar (1 tsp)  
🍳 Cooking oil (适量)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spoon  
🍽️ Plate/bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Blanch the beef rolls in boiling water to remove scum and any unpleasant smell. Drain and set aside.  
2. Mix light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, chicken bouillon, and white sugar together to make the sauce.  
3. Heat cooking oil in the wok over medium heat until warm. Add minced garlic, small red chili pepper rings, and chili flakes. Stir-fry until fragrant and red oil is released.  
4. Add the blanched beef rolls and the prepared sauce. Immediately turn to high heat and quickly stir-fry until everything is evenly coated with the sauce.  
   **Note**: The beef rolls are already cooked through; stir-fry quickly to avoid overcooking, which can make the meat tough.  
5. Add the cilantro sections and quickly stir-fry a few more times until the cilantro is just wilted. Remove from heat and plate.  
   **Key**: Add the cilantro just before finishing to preserve its fresh aroma.  

## Health Advice  
Beef rolls are rich in protein and iron, making them suitable for those needing to replenish energy and iron. However, this dish is high in chili and oil, so it is recommended to pair it with light vegetables. Pay attention to the heat; prolonged stir-frying will make the beef rolls tough and affect the texture.
