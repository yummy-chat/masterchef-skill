# Braised Pork Rib Rice
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐖 Pork Ribs about 500g (cut into 3cm small sections)
🍚 Rice 1 measuring cup (washed and set aside)
🥔 Potato 1 (cut into 1cm small cubes)
🥕 Carrot 1 (cut into 1cm small cubes)
🌽 Corn kernels as needed
🟢 Green peas as needed
🍖 Chinese sausage 1 (sliced)
🥚 Egg 1
🧅 Scallion 2 stalks (cut into sections)
🫚 Ginger 3 slices
🍬 Rock sugar 5 pieces
🧂 Light soy sauce as needed
🧂 Dark soy sauce as needed
🧂 Songxianxian Organic Matsutake Soy Sauce Spray spray 1-2 times

## Kitchenware & Equipment
♨️ Rice cooker
🥄 Stirring spoon or ladle

## Preparation Steps
1.  Place the rice cooker inner pot on the stove over low heat. Add scallion sections, ginger slices, and rock sugar, and stir-fry until the sugar melts and turns amber in color.
   **Note**: When caramelizing the sugar, keep the heat on low the entire time to avoid burning and bitterness.
2.  Add the pork rib sections and stir-fry until the surface changes color and turns slightly golden.
3.  Add an appropriate amount of light soy sauce and dark soy sauce. Continue stir-frying until the ribs are evenly coated with color, and the sauce thickens and wraps around the ribs.
   **Note**: When reducing the sauce, keep a small amount of liquid as the base for braised rice. Do not dry it out completely.
4.  Pour the washed rice into the rice cooker inner pot and mix thoroughly with the ribs and sauce.
   **Key Point**: The amount of liquid should be about 1 cm above the rice to ensure the rice is fully cooked and flavorful.
5.  On top of the rice, layer the potato cubes, carrot cubes, corn kernels, green peas, and Chinese sausage slices in order.
6.  Crack one egg in the center of the ingredients, and spray 1-2 times with the Songxianxian soy sauce spray.
   **Key Point**: Spraying allows for more even seasoning and prevents some areas from being too salty.
7.  Close the rice cooker lid and press the cook button to braise normally.
8.  After the cooking program finishes, let it sit for another 5 minutes before opening the lid.
   **Key Point**: Using residual heat to steam makes the rice texture softer and fluffier.
9.  Open the lid and use a spoon to mix the ribs, rice, and vegetables evenly. Then serve and enjoy.

## Health Advice
This braised pork rib rice combines carbohydrates and protein in one pot, making it ideal for busy office workers. Note that the Chinese sausage and ribs are already salty on their own, so control the amount of soy sauce used. It is recommended to pair with a portion of quick-stir-fried vegetables to balance dietary fiber.
