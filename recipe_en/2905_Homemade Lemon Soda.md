# Homemade Lemon Soda
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April - August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍋 Fresh lemon, as needed
🧊 Ice cubes, as needed
🌿 Fresh mint leaves, a few
🥤 Soda water, as needed
🍯 Honey, as needed
🧂 Salt, a pinch (for scrubbing the lemon peel)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🪵 Wooden cutting board
🔪 Knife
🧄 Mortar and pestle
🥛 Glass

## Instructions
1. Sprinkle a pinch of salt on the lemon peel, rub it vigorously with your hands until a fragrant scent is released, then rinse thoroughly with clean water.
   **Note**: Rubbing with salt cleans the peel and releases the lemon peel's aroma.
2. Slice the lemon into thin slices and remove all seeds using a tool.
   **Note**: Lemon seeds are the main source of bitterness; be sure to remove them completely.
3. Place the lemon slices into a mortar and crush them, then add honey and stir well to blend the flavors.
4. Put the blended lemon and honey into a glass, and add a generous amount of ice cubes.
5. Pour soda water into the glass until it is full.
6. Garnish the top of the drink with a few fresh mint leaves and serve immediately.

## Health Tips
Homemade lemon soda is a great choice for summer hydration and vitamin C intake. It is recommended to use less honey to control sugar content, making it suitable for those who need to cool down and refresh. Be sure to remove all lemon seeds, otherwise, the bitterness will ruin the taste of the entire drink.
