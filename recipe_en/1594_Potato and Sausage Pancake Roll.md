# Potato and Sausage Pancake Roll
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥔 1 potato  
🥚 1 egg  
🌭 2 sausages (cooked meat sausages, such as pork or chicken sausages)  
🥬 2 lettuce leaves  
🫗 Cooking oil, as needed  
🫙 Pancake sauce, as needed (commonly sweet bean sauce or fermented bean paste)  
⚫ Black sesame seeds, as needed  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🔪 Peeler  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍽️ Plate  

## Steps  
1. Use a peeler to slice the potato into thin, uniform pieces, ensuring consistent thickness for even cooking and crispy texture when fried.  
2. Beat the egg into a liquid and set aside.  
3. Heat the frying pan over low heat, add a small amount of cooking oil, and arrange the potato slices in an overlapping pattern in the pan, slightly overlapping but not scattered, forming a pancake shape.  
4. Evenly brush a layer of egg liquid over the potato slices, sprinkle some black sesame seeds, and continue frying over low heat.  
   **Key point**: The egg liquid helps bind the loose potato slices into a solid pancake base, making it easier to roll later.  
5. Fry for about 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden brown, then carefully flip and continue frying until the other side is golden, the potato slices are crispy, and the egg liquid is fully set.  
6. Remove the cooked potato pancake and place it on a plate, then brush with an appropriate amount of pancake sauce.  
7. Place the washed lettuce leaves on the pancake, then add the sausages.  
8. Using chopsticks for assistance, tightly roll the pancake from one end. Once rolled, it can be eaten directly or sliced for serving.  
   **Note**: When rolling, reduce the heat to the lowest setting to keep the pancake warm without burning, and roll tightly to prevent it from coming apart.  

## Health Advice  
The potato and sausage pancake roll is rich in carbohydrates, protein, and fats, making it suitable as a breakfast or quick lunch. However, attention should be paid to controlling the sodium content in the sausages and sauce. The crispy potato slices cause a rapid rise in blood sugar, so it is recommended to pair this dish with a side of cold mixed vegetables for balanced nutrition during weight loss periods.
