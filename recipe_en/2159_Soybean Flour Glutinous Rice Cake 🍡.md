# Soybean Flour Glutinous Rice Cake 🍡
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🫘 Soybeans 300g
💧 Water 250g
🍚 Glutinous rice flour 130g
🌽 Cornstarch 30g
🍬 White sugar 40g (for making soybean flour)
🍬 White sugar 40g (for making batter)
🫒 Cooking oil 20g

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥄 Wooden spatula
🌀 Blender/food processor
🕸️ Sieve
🥣 Glass bowl/mixing bowl
♨️ Steamer
👝 Piping bag
✂️ Scissors

## Instructions
1. Place the soybeans in a wok and stir-fry over low heat for about 20 minutes, stirring continuously, until the beans turn yellow-spotted and emit a rich aroma. Turn off the heat, remove, and let cool. **Note**: Keep the heat low and stir constantly to prevent burning.
2. Put the cooled roasted soybeans and 40g white sugar into a blender. Blend into a fine powder, then sift through a sieve to obtain a smooth soybean flour. Set aside.
3. In a mixing bowl, combine 250g water, 20g cooking oil, and 40g white sugar. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. Then add 130g glutinous rice flour and 30g cornstarch. Mix thoroughly to form a thin batter.
4. Place the batter in a steamer. Steam over cold water, then after the water boils, reduce to medium heat and steam for 30 minutes, until the batter becomes translucent and solidifies. Turn off the heat.
5. Remove the steamed batter. Stir evenly while hot. Let it cool slightly until it is no longer scalding, then transfer to a piping bag. Tighten the bag opening.
6. Cut a large opening at the tip of the piping bag. Squeeze out small segments of the batter, snipping them off with scissors at the bottom so the segments fall directly into a bowl of soybean flour.
7. Gently toss the glutinous rice cake pieces in the bowl to coat them evenly with soybean flour. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice
This glutinous rice cake is rich in carbohydrates. The combination of glutinous rice flour, sugar, and soybean flour has a high glycemic index, making it suitable for those needing a quick energy boost and sweet-tooth lovers. However, individuals with diabetes or those on a weight-loss diet should consume it with caution. Control the portion size to avoid blood sugar fluctuations.
