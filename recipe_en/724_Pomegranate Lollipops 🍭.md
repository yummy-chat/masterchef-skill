# Pomegranate Lollipops 🍭
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🫐 Pomegranate – appropriate amount (about half, for juicing)  
🍬 Rock sugar – 150g  
💧 Water – 100ml  
🧃 Pomegranate juice – 30g (extracted by squeezing and filtering pomegranate seeds)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
👝 Piping bag  
🍳 Small pot/saucepan  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥣 Small bowl  
🍭 Lollipop silicone mold  
🥄 Spoon  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🧊 Refrigerator  

## Steps  
1. Cut the pomegranate in half, remove the seeds, place them in a piping bag, and squeeze to extract the juice. Filter to obtain 30g of pomegranate juice and set aside.  
2. Add 100ml of water and 150g of rock sugar to a pot. Heat over low to medium heat.  
   **Key point**: Do not stir throughout the process to prevent the sugar syrup from crystallizing.  
3. Once the rock sugar is fully melted and the syrup becomes thick with dense small bubbles, add 30g of pomegranate juice.  
   **Note**: Gently swirl the pot to mix the color. Do not stir.  
4. Continue cooking over low heat until the syrup becomes thick again and filled with dense small bubbles.  
5. Test the consistency: Dip a chopstick into the syrup, then drop it into cold water. If the syrup solidifies immediately and feels hard and brittle when pinched, remove the pot from heat.  
6. Spoon the cooked syrup into silicone lollipop molds that have been prepped with lollipop sticks.  
7. Place the mold in the freezer for about 5 minutes to quickly set the lollipops.  
8. Unmold the lollipops and wrap each one individually in plastic wrap to prevent moisture absorption.  

## Health Advice  
This pomegranate lollipop is rich in vitamin C, but the 150g of rock sugar contains nearly three times the World Health Organization’s recommended daily sugar intake. It is best enjoyed as an occasional treat. Children should brush their teeth promptly after eating. Most antioxidant components in pomegranate juice are lost at high temperatures, so the main appeal of this candy is the experience of “chewing juice” rather than a nutritional supplement.
