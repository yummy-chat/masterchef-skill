# Plum Overnight Oats Bowl
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Rolled Oats (Old Fashioned) About 1/2 cup
🌱 Chia Seeds About 1 tablespoon
🥛 Milk About 1/2 cup (plus extra to adjust consistency)
🍶 Yogurt About 1/4 cup
🍁 Maple Syrup To taste
🧂 Salt To taste
🍑 Plums 2 (pitted and chopped)
🍫 Cocoa Hazelnut Nut Butter 1-2 tablespoons

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥣 Airtight container
🥄 Spoon
🏷️ Measuring spoons
🔪 Knife
🪵 Cutting board

## Steps
1. In an airtight container, add rolled oats and chia seeds in sequence. Pour in milk at a roughly 1:1 ratio, then add yogurt at a roughly 1:0.5 ratio of oats to yogurt. Add maple syrup and salt to taste, and stir thoroughly to combine.
   **Key Point**: All ingredients must be evenly mixed; the liquid-to-solid ratio can be adjusted to personal preference, but avoid making it too thin.
2. Cover the container and place it in the refrigerator to rest for at least 4-6 hours.
   **Key Point**: Sufficient time allows the oats to fully absorb liquid and soften, resulting in a creamy texture.
3. The next day, remove the overnight oats from the refrigerator. Stir with a spoon and check the consistency. If too thick, add milk little by little to adjust to the desired consistency.
   **Key Point**: When adjusting consistency, add milk gradually to avoid making it too thin.
4. Wash the plums, remove the pits, cut into small pieces, and arrange them on top of the oats.
5. Use a spoon to scoop an appropriate amount of cocoa hazelnut nut butter and spread it on the inside of the bowl's walls.
6. When serving, stir the nut butter into the oats as desired, and enjoy together with the plum pieces.

## Health Tips
This overnight oats dish is rich in dietary fiber and high-quality protein, making it suitable as a breakfast option for those needing to control blood sugar or lose weight. However, maple syrup and nut butter should be used in moderation to avoid excess calories. Plums are rich in vitamin C and, when paired with oats, can enhance antioxidant effects. However, those with a weak spleen or cold stomach should avoid eating it on an empty stomach.
