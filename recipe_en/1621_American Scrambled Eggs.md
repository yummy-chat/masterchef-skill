# American Scrambled Eggs
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍳 **Ingredients**  
- 🥚 Eggs, 3 pieces  
- 🥛 Whole milk/cream, 10g  
- 🧈 Butter, 5g  
- 🧂 Salt, 1g  

## Cookware and Equipment  
- 🍳 Non-stick frying pan (preferably smaller diameter)  
- 🥣 Large bowl  
- 🥄 Mixing tool  

## Preparation Steps
1. Crack the eggs into a large bowl, add salt, and whisk thoroughly until frothy. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
   **Key point**: Resting allows the egg mixture to develop more even bubbles, resulting in fluffier scrambled eggs.
2. Cut the butter into small pieces and place them in the pan. Heat over low heat. Once the butter is slightly warm, pour in the egg mixture and stir continuously.
   **Note**: Use low heat throughout to prevent the eggs from overcooking and becoming tough.
3. Once the butter has completely melted, quickly flip the egg mixture and break it into fine, fluffy pieces. Turn off the heat just before the eggs are fully set.
4. Add the milk and stir for 15 seconds to keep the scrambled eggs moist and creamy. Transfer to a plate.
   **Key point**: Adding milk after turning off the heat locks in moisture, resulting in a smoother, more tender texture.

## Health Tips  
Eggs provide high-quality protein, while butter and milk add a rich flavor, making this dish ideal for an energy-boosting breakfast. However, due to its cholesterol content, individuals with high blood lipids should consider using low-fat milk and reducing the number of egg yolks. Slow cooking over low heat is key, and adding milk after turning off the heat helps lock in a moist texture.
