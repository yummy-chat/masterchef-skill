# Poached Egg
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 1 egg

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔥 Gas stove
🍳 Non-stick pan or frying pan
🍲 Lid
🥣 Small bowl
🥄 Wooden spatula or spatula

## Cooking Steps
1. Pour enough water into the pot, bring to a boil over high heat, then immediately turn off the heat.
   **Note**: Ensure enough water; turning off the heat is key to prevent the egg from dispersing.
2. Crack the egg into a small bowl, bring the bowl close to the water surface, and gently slide the egg into the pot.
   **Note**: The action must be gentle to keep the egg shape intact.
3. Cover with the lid and let it sit for 2 minutes using residual heat to allow the egg white to initially set.
4. Turn the heat back on to medium-low, and gently push the egg with a spatula to prevent sticking to the bottom.
   **Note**: Do not use high heat to avoid violent boiling that could break up the egg and create excessive foam.
5. Continue cooking over medium-low heat for about 5 and a half minutes.
   **Note**: Timing starts from when the heat is turned back on.
6. Carefully remove the poached egg with a slotted spoon. It can be eaten directly or placed in cool boiled water for easier peeling.

## Health Tips
Eggs provide high-quality protein, suitable for weight loss diets or as an energy boost for breakfast. For those with high cholesterol, it is recommended to consume no more than one per day. Pay attention to gentle heat during cooking to avoid breaking up the egg white.
