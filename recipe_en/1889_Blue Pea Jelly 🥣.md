# Blue Pea Jelly 🥣
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥣 Pea starch, to taste
🌿 Butterfly pea flowers, to taste (for coloring the water)
💧 Water, to taste
🧄 Garlic, to taste (mashed into a paste)
🌿 Cilantro, to taste
🧴 Vinegar, to taste
🧴 Light soy sauce, to taste
🌶️ Chili flakes, to taste
🧴 Garlic water, to taste (mashed garlic mixed with water)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Pot
🥣 Bowl
🔪 Cutting board
🥄 Spatula or masher (for scraping texture)
🧴 Small bottle (for making garlic water)
🥢 Chopsticks or spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Add water to a pot, put in butterfly pea flowers, and bring to a boil to extract a deep purple liquid. Reserve some for making the jelly and set the rest aside.
2. Mash the garlic into a fine paste, place it in a small bottle, add an appropriate amount of water, and shake well to make garlic water.
3. Take some of the butterfly pea flower liquid, slowly pour in the pea starch, and stir until there are no dry lumps, forming a smooth batter.
   **Key Point**: The starch must be fully dissolved to avoid clumps.
4. Pour the prepared starch batter into a pot, heat over medium heat, and stir continuously until the liquid becomes thick and semi-transparent.
   **Key Point**: Stir constantly during heating to prevent sticking and clumping; stop when the mixture changes from thin to thick.
5. Pour the cooked thick liquid into a bowl, let it sit, and cool until fully set.
6. Flip the set jelly onto a cutting board, use a spatula to scrape wavy patterns on the surface, then cut into strips.
7. Place the cut jelly strips into a bowl, drizzle with garlic water, vinegar, and light soy sauce. Sprinkle with chili flakes and cilantro, then mix well.
   **Note**: Adjust seasoning amounts to personal taste.

## Health Advice
Pea jelly is naturally colored with butterfly pea flowers. It is rich in carbohydrates and low in protein, making it ideal for summer refreshment. However, those with high blood sugar should pair it with vegetables.
