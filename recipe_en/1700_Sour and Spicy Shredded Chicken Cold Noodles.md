# Sour and Spicy Shredded Chicken Cold Noodles
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients
🐔 Chicken breast 200g (small fillet) or 300g (large fillet)
🍜 Fresh noodles 500g
🥜 Peanuts a handful
🧅 Scallion 1 and a half stalks (divided for chopped scallion and cooking meat)
🧄 Garlic 4 cloves
🥒 Cucumber appropriate amount
🌿 Cilantro appropriate amount
🫚 Ginger 2 slices

## Seasonings
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorns a dozen (for cooking meat) + 1 pinch (for oil)
🌶️ Chili flakes 2 tablespoons
⚪️ White sesame seeds (toasted) 1 tablespoon
🧂 Light soy sauce 2 tablespoons
🧂 Dark soy sauce 1/3 tablespoon
🧂 Fragrant vinegar or black vinegar 2 tablespoons
🧂 Oyster sauce 1 tablespoon
🧂 Sesame oil 1/2 tablespoon
🧂 Sugar 1/2 tablespoon
🧂 MSG 1/4 teaspoon
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount (for frying peanuts, frying Sichuan peppercorn oil)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Snow pot or soup pot
♨️ Air fryer
🥄 Strainer/slotted spoon
🥣 Stainless steel bowl/large bowl
🔥 Gas stove
🥢 Chopsticks

## Instructions
1. **Prepare chicken breast**: Add cold water to a pot, put in ginger slices, scallion sections, and a dozen Sichuan peppercorns, bring to a boil. After water boils, add chicken breast, cover immediately and turn to the lowest heat, cook small fillet for 3 minutes, large fillet for 4 minutes; turn off heat, do not remove lid, let rest for 10 minutes. Remove and soak in ice water, cool completely, then shred by hand.
**Key point**: Use cold water with aromatics to remove gamey flavor, slow cook on low heat and finish with residual heat, ensuring the chicken shreds are tender and not dry.
2. **Make peanuts**: Place peanuts in air fryer, spray a layer of cooking oil and shake to coat, roast at 180℃ for 9 minutes, set aside.
3. **Prepare vegetables**: Cut cucumber into thin shreds, cut cilantro into segments.
4. **Prepare soul sauce**: In a bowl, put minced garlic, chopped scallion, chili flakes, and toasted white sesame seeds. Heat a small amount of oil in a pot, add a pinch of Sichuan peppercorns and fry over low heat until fragrant. Once peppercorns change color, immediately remove them, then pour the hot oil into the bowl to release the aroma. Then add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, vinegar, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and MSG in order, stir to combine.
**Key point**: The hot oil must "release and saturate the aroma"; after frying the Sichuan peppercorns, be sure to remove them promptly to avoid bitterness from overcooking.
5. **Cook noodles**: Bring another pot of water to a boil, add fresh noodles and cook until done. Remove and immediately rinse thoroughly under cold water until completely cooled, drain well.
**Key point**: Rinsing must be "thoroughly cooling", washing away surface gelatinized starch to ensure the noodles are smooth and chewy.
6. **Combine and mix**: Put drained noodles in a large bowl, top with shredded chicken, cucumber shreds, cilantro segments, and peanuts. Pour in the prepared sauce, mix thoroughly by hand or with chopsticks.

## Health Advice
This cold noodle dish offers a high-protein, low-carb combination, suitable for cooling and stimulating appetite in summer. Chicken breast is low in fat and easy to digest, suitable for weight loss or fitness enthusiasts. It is recommended to reduce the amount of peanuts to lower additional calories; thoroughly drain the noodles after rinsing to slow down blood sugar spikes.
