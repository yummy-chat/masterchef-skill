# Sweet and Sour Fried Eggs
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 6 eggs  
🧂 5 tbsp water  
🍅 4 tbsp tomato ketchup  
🍬 3 tbsp sugar  
🍶 2 tbsp white vinegar  
🫒 1 tbsp sesame oil  
🧂 A pinch of salt  
🍯 A little dark soy sauce  
💧 Some starch water (for thickening)  
🫒 Some cooking oil (for frying eggs and oil-splashing sauce)  
⚪ Some white sesame seeds (for garnish)  

## Cookware and Equipment
🥄 Fine mesh strainer  
🍳 Non-stick pan or frying pan  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Wooden spoon or soup spoon  
🍲 Wok  

## Steps
1. Crack the eggs one by one into a bowl. Strain through a fine mesh strainer to remove excess runny egg white, ensuring the egg liquid is clean and round.  
2. Prepare the sweet and sour sauce: In a bowl, add 5 tbsp water, 4 tbsp tomato ketchup, 3 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp white vinegar, 1 tbsp sesame oil, and a pinch of salt. Stir until well combined.  
   **Key point**: Follow the 54321 ratio strictly for a balanced flavor.  
3. Heat the pan, add a generous amount of cold oil, and reduce to medium-low heat. Pour in the strained egg liquid. Fry until the bottom is set and the edges are slightly golden and crispy. Flip and continue frying the other side until golden, with the edges crispy and fragrant.  
   **Key point**: Ensure the edges are slightly scorched for a crispy texture.  
4. In another pan, add cold oil and the sweet and sour sauce. Add a little dark soy sauce for color. Bring to a boil over heat. Once the sauce is boiling, slowly drizzle in the starch water while stirring continuously until the sauce thickens. This makes the thickener.  
5. Prepare the oil-splashed thickener: Clean and dry the wok, then heat it. Add an appropriate amount of oil, heat until about 70-80% hot, then pour in the freshly thickened sweet and sour sauce. Stir rapidly over high heat. When you hear a sizzling sound, the sauce will become glossy and shiny.  
   **Key point**: This is a crucial step to release aroma and make the sauce smooth.  
6. Arrange the fried eggs neatly on a plate. Immediately pour the hot sweet and sour sauce over them, and finally sprinkle with white sesame seeds for garnish.  

## Health Tips
This dish replaces meat with eggs, offering a low-cost, high-protein option. However, the sweet and sour sauce contains a high amount of sugar, so it is recommended to pair it with vegetables for a balanced diet. Suitable for those needing a quick energy boost. For diabetics, reduce the sugar amount appropriately.
