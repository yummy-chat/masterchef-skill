# Soup-Filled Baozi (Steamed Buns with Soup)
Cuisine: Henan Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Ground beef 500g
🧈 Beef tendon soup gel 500g (prepared in advance by boiling, cooling, and refrigerating until set, then finely chopped)
🧅 Large green onion, white part 200g (softened with salt)
🌾 All-purpose flour 400g
💧 Hot water at 70℃ 250g
🥚 Egg white 1
🧂 Salt 6g (for filling)
🧂 Salt 4g (for soup)
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns 1g (for soup) +1g (for seasoning water)
🌿 Fennel seeds 1g (for soup) +1g (for seasoning water)
⭐ Star anise 1 (for soup) +1 (for seasoning water)
🍂 Bay leaves 3
🫚 Ginger 1 piece
🧅 Green onion, white part 1 stalk (for soup)
🌶️ White pepper powder less than 1g
🌶️ Sichuan pepper powder 1g

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Bowl (for mixing filling)
🥄 Strainer (for filtering seasoning water)
🔪 Kitchen knife (for chopping soup gel)
🪓 Rolling pin
🧺 Bamboo steamer
🪵 Chopping board
🥢 Small bowl (for dipping sauce)

## Preparation Steps
### Making Beef Tendon Soup
1. Place 500g beef tendon, 1 stalk green onion (white part), 1 piece ginger, 3 bay leaves, 1g Sichuan peppercorns, 1g fennel seeds, 1 star anise, and 4g salt in a pot. Add enough water and boil to make beef tendon soup.
2. Let the soup cool, then refrigerate until fully set. Remove and chop finely; set aside.
**Key point**: Must be refrigerated until fully set before chopping and mixing into the filling to create the soup-filled effect.

### Preparing the Beef Filling
1. Make seasoning water: Boil water with 1g Sichuan peppercorns, 1g fennel seeds, and 1 star anise. Strain and set aside 125g of the strained seasoning water.
**Key point**: The seasoning water must be strained to remove spice residues, avoiding texture issues.
2. Add 125g seasoning water to 500g ground beef. Stir continuously in one direction until the meat becomes sticky and forms strings.
**Key point**: Must stir in one direction until it reaches a stringy, sticky consistency to ensure the final product is tender and juicy.
3. Add 200g green onion (white part) softened with salt and continue to mix evenly.
4. Add 1 egg white, 1g Sichuan pepper powder, and less than 1g white pepper powder. Mix in the chopped beef tendon soup gel, stir evenly, then refrigerate until ready to use.
**Key point**: Must refrigerate after adding the beef tendon soup gel to prevent it from melting prematurely during wrapping.

### Making the Dough Wrappers
1. Combine 400g all-purpose flour with 250g hot water at 70℃ and knead into a dough. Let it rest until it reaches the right consistency.
**Key point**: Resting the dough increases its elasticity, making it easier to roll out and wrap.

### Rolling and Wrapping
1. Divide the rested dough into small pieces. Press each piece flat and use a rolling pin to roll into a round wrapper that is thicker in the center and thinner at the edges.
**Key point**: The thicker center and thinner edges support the filling and prevent the pleated area from being too thick, affecting the texture.
2. Place an appropriate amount of chilled beef filling in the center of the wrapper. Using your thumb and index finger, pinch pleats evenly in one direction, naturally sealing the top.
**Key point**: Pinch pleats evenly in one direction to ensure neat pleats and a natural seal, preventing filling from leaking during steaming.

### Steaming
1. Place the wrapped buns on a steamer lined with parchment paper. Steam over boiling water over medium-low heat for 10 minutes. Turn off the heat and remove.
**Key point**: Must steam over boiling water to prevent the buns from collapsing; use medium-low heat to avoid the buns breaking and losing the soup.

### Serving
1. Prepare a dipping sauce with soy sauce, vinegar, and water. Pick up the steamed buns and dip before eating.
**Key point**: The dipping sauce ratio can be adjusted to personal taste; it effectively enhances the fresh and savory flavor.

## Health Advice
These beef soup-filled baozi are high in protein and rich in collagen, making them suitable for those with high physical energy expenditure or needing calcium supplementation. However, they are high in calories and sodium, so it is recommended to pair them with light vegetables for a balanced meal. Enjoy immediately while hot; otherwise, the soup gel will solidify and the texture will significantly deteriorate.
