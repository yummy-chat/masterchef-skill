# Cold Tossed Vermicelli
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍜 Vermicelli: appropriate amount (dried, needs to be soaked in advance)
🥒 Cucumber: appropriate amount (shredded)
🥕 Carrot: appropriate amount (shredded)
🍄 Wood ear mushroom: appropriate amount (shredded)
🥜 Peanuts: appropriate amount (cooked peanuts)
🌿 Cilantro: appropriate amount (cut into segments)
🧄 Minced garlic: appropriate amount (more can be added)
🧂 Light soy sauce: 2 tablespoons
🧂 Salt: 2 teaspoons
🧂 MSG: 2 teaspoons
🍬 White sugar: 1 tablespoon
🍶 White vinegar: 2 tablespoons
🌶️ Chili powder: appropriate amount
🫒 Hot oil: appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Large bowl (for soaking vermicelli and cold tossing)
🫖 Kettle (for boiling water)
🥢 Chopsticks (for stirring)

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the dried vermicelli in a bowl and pour in boiling water to soak for about 10 minutes, until the vermicelli is completely soft.
   **Note**: Use boiling water and control the soaking time to about 10 minutes.
2. Remove the soaked vermicelli and rinse or soak in cold water until cool.
   **Note**: Rinsing with cold water makes the vermicelli more chewy and prevents it from clumping.
3. Shred the cucumber, carrot, and wood ear mushroom into thin strips. Prepare the cilantro segments, cooked peanuts, and minced garlic.
4. Place the drained vermicelli in a large bowl, and layer the cucumber shreds, carrot shreds, wood ear mushroom shreds, peanuts, and cilantro segments on top in order.
5. Drizzle light soy sauce over the ingredients, then sprinkle salt, MSG, white sugar, and white vinegar. Finally, add the minced garlic and chili powder.
   **Note**: Minced garlic can be added generously. Adjust the seasoning ratio to balance sour, spicy, sweet, and salty flavors.
6. Heat oil in a separate pot until it smokes, then pour the hot oil over the minced garlic and chili powder.
   **Note**: Use high-temperature hot oil to release the aroma of the seasonings.
7. Use chopsticks to thoroughly mix all ingredients and seasonings, then plate and serve.

## Health Advice
This cold tossed vermicelli is low in fat and high in fiber, making it a refreshing choice during weight loss. However, vermicelli has a relatively high carbohydrate content, so portion control is recommended. Peanuts provide high-quality fats, but consumption should be moderate.
