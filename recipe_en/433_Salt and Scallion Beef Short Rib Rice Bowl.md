# Salt and Scallion Beef Short Rib Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef short ribs – as needed  
🧅 Onion – about half (finely diced)  
🧅 Scallions – as needed (white and green parts, chopped)  
🥚 Pasteurized egg – 1 (yolk only)  
🍚 Cooked rice – 1 bowl  
🧂 Black pepper – as needed (freshly ground)  
🧂 Salt – as needed  
🫒 Sesame oil – 1 tablespoon  
🍋 Lemon juice – a splash  
🐟 Bonito flakes powder – 1 tablespoon  
🍶 Sweet soy sauce – a splash (for drizzling at the end)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board  
🧂 Pepper mill  
🥣 Bowl  
🍳 Frying pan  
✂️ Kitchen scissors  
🥄 Spoon

## Steps
1. **Make the salt and scallion sauce**: Mix the diced onion and chopped scallions. Add freshly ground black pepper, salt, sesame oil, lemon juice, and bonito flakes powder. Stir until well combined.
2. **Prepare the beef short ribs**: Remove the surface sinew. Score a crosshatch pattern on the meat side to help it absorb flavor and cook evenly.
3. **Marinate**: Place the beef short ribs in a bowl. Sprinkle with black pepper, salt, and a generous amount of the salt and scallion sauce. Mix well and marinate for 40 minutes.
4. **Sear**: Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the marinated beef short ribs and sear both sides until browned. Browning on the surface is a normal caramelization reaction.  
   **Note**: If you are concerned about doneness, use scissors to cut the meat into smaller pieces directly in the pan, then stir-fry briefly until your desired level of doneness.
5. **Assemble**: Fill a bowl with cooked rice. Top with the seared beef short ribs. Place the pasteurized egg yolk in the center. Spoon a generous amount of fresh salt and scallion sauce over the meat. Finally, drizzle with a little sweet soy sauce.

## Health Tips
Beef short ribs are rich in iron and protein. Paired with egg yolk, they provide B vitamins, making this dish suitable for muscle building and recovery from fatigue. The lemon juice in the salt and scallion sauce helps cut through the richness, but be mindful of sodium intake.
