## Steps
1.  **Prepare the orange peel**: Cut the orange peel into fine strips. Add a little salt and mix well. Marinate for about 20 minutes. Rinse off the salt with water, squeeze dry. Add a little white sugar, mix well, and continue marinating for another 20 minutes to remove the bitterness.
2.  **Marinate the beef**: Add a little salt and baking soda to the shredded beef and mix well. Then add ground pepper, light soy sauce, and a little dark soy sauce and mix well. Gradually add the green onion and ginger water in small batches, mixing until the water is fully absorbed each time to make the beef texture smooth. Add a small handful of cornstarch and mix well to coat. Finally, drizzle a layer of sesame oil to seal in moisture. Refrigerate and marinate for 20 minutes.
3.  **Prepare the aromatics and sauce**: Mix together the dried chili pepper sections, pickled erjingtiao pepper sections, and wild mountain pepper sections. In a bowl, combine three tablespoons of light soy sauce, half a tablespoon of huangjiu, a little mature vinegar, ground pepper, a little dark soy sauce, chicken bouillon powder, and the reserved orange juice. Stir to combine and set aside.
4.  **Flash-fry the beef**: Heat the wok over high heat and add enough oil. When the oil is hot, add the marinated beef. Quickly separate the shreds with chopsticks and stir-fry until the beef is completely变色. Use a slotted spoon to remove the beef and drain the oil.
5.  **Burst the aromatics**: Leave some base oil in the wok. Keep the heat high. Add the minced garlic, the three types of chili peppers, and the julienned orange peel. Stir-fry until fragrant.
6.  **Combine and stir-fry**: Return the flash-fried beef to the wok. Quickly stir-fry everything together until well combined.
7.  **Add cilantro and the sauce**: Add a generous amount of cilantro. Stir-fry over high heat until the cilantro just begins to wilt. Quickly pour the prepared sauce along the edge of the wok. Stir-fry rapidly a few times to evenly coat the ingredients with the sauce. Turn off the heat immediately.
8.  **Assemble the rice bowl**: Serve the stir-fried cilantro beef over hot steamed rice. Mix well and enjoy.
    **Key Points**: Marinating the orange peel to remove bitterness is the finishing touch for this dish's unique aroma. The tenderness of the beef relies on baking soda and gradually incorporating green onion and ginger water. Use a large amount of cilantro and do not stir-fry it for long to maintain its fresh, crisp, and aromatic texture.
