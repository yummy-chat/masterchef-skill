# Microwave Cold Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥜 Raw peanuts, to taste  
🍜 Alkaline noodles, to taste (dry noodles can be used directly)  
🥒 Cucumber, about 1 piece (shredded)  
🥕 Carrot, about 1/2 piece (shredded)  
🧄 Garlic, 5-6 cloves (some minced, some crushed)  
🌶️ Small chili peppers, to taste (sliced into rings)  
🌿 Cilantro, a little (optional, for final garnish)  
🍳 Cooking oil, to taste (for mixing peanuts and drizzling on seasoning)  
🌶️ Chili flakes, to taste  
⚪ White sesame seeds, to taste  
🧂 Light soy sauce, to taste  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, a small amount (for color)  
🧴 Sesame oil, a small amount  
🧂 Salt, a pinch  
🥤 Sprite, to taste (optional, for sweet and spicy flavor)  
🍅 Ketchup, to taste (optional, for sweet and spicy flavor)  
🍋 Lemon, to taste (optional, squeeze juice for Thai-style sour and spicy flavor)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
♨️ Microwave oven  
🥣 High-temperature resistant ceramic or glass bowl (metal utensils strictly prohibited)  
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
🛍️ Ziplock bag (for crushing garlic, optional)  

## Instructions  
1. **Fry the peanuts**: Place raw peanuts in a heat-resistant bowl, add a small spoonful of cooking oil, and mix well. Microwave on high heat for about 5 minutes, stopping halfway to stir once. Once the peanuts are darker in color and crispy, immediately sprinkle with a pinch of salt while hot. Set aside to cool.  
2. **Cook the noodles**: Place dry alkaline noodles in a large bowl, pour in boiling water to cover the noodles, and cover with a lid. Microwave on high heat for 3 minutes. Check with chopsticks to ensure no hard core remains, then remove and immediately soak in cold water for a moment. Drain well.  
3. **Prepare the seasoned oil**: In another bowl, add small chili peppers, minced garlic, chili flakes, and white sesame seeds. Drizzle in enough cooking oil to moisten the dry ingredients. Microwave on high heat for about 3 minutes. When you hear a crackling sound or see the sesame seeds change color and the oil boil, immediately remove. Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and sesame oil, then stir to combine into a sauce.  
4. **Mix the noodles**: Place the drained noodles in a large bowl. Top with shredded cucumber, shredded carrot, and the fried peanuts. Pour the sauce over everything, put on gloves, and toss thoroughly to combine.  
   - For a sweet and spicy flavor, add Sprite and ketchup while mixing.  
   - For a Thai-style sour and spicy flavor, squeeze in lemon juice.  
5. Plate, garnish with cilantro, and enjoy.  

## Health Tips  
This cold noodle dish uses the microwave for all heating steps, making it perfect for summer without needing to use the stove. It is ideal for those who dislike heat and kitchen fumes. Peanuts and sesame seeds provide quality fats and vitamin E, but Sprite and ketchup increase sugar intake, so consume in moderation.
