# Homemade Beef Patty Burger
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Yellow beef approx. 24 yuan (cut into small pieces, with fat)
🥓 Beef fat small amount (adds oil, prevents the patty from being dry)
🍔 Hamburger buns as needed
🧀 Cheese slices as needed
🍅 Tomato as needed (sliced)
🧂 Black pepper salt as needed (add before mincing)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Meat grinder
📏 Blue plastic measuring cup (used as a shaping mold)
🍱 Glass storage container
🍳 Frying pan
♨️ Microwave
📄 Baking paper

## Steps
1. Cut the yellow beef into small pieces and mix with an appropriate amount of beef fat, ensuring a balanced fat-to-lean ratio to avoid affecting the texture.
2. Place the meat pieces into the meat grinder and sprinkle with black pepper salt for seasoning.
3. Start the meat grinder and grind the meat into a uniform, fine mince.
4. Line the bottom of the storage container with baking paper. Use the blue measuring cup as a mold, fill it with an appropriate amount of mince, and press it into a patty of uniform thickness. Place baking paper between each layer of patties to prevent sticking.  
   **Key point**: The mold ensures each patty is uniform in thickness and size, allowing for even heating during frying.
5. Cover the storage container and freeze overnight in the refrigerator to allow the patties to fully set.
6. Remove the frozen patties and peel off the baking paper for later use.
7. Spray a small amount of oil into the frying pan, add the frozen patties, cover with a lid, and cook over medium-low heat until fully cooked through.  
   **Note**: Frozen patties must be cooked with the lid on to ensure the center is fully cooked; avoid overcooking, otherwise the meat will become dry.
8. Heat the hamburger buns in the microwave for 1 minute until warm.
9. Layer the bottom bun with tomato slices, the cooked beef patty, and a cheese slice, then top with the other bun.  
   **Key point**: Place the cheese slice while hot, using the residual heat of the patty to naturally melt it, enhancing the texture.

## Health Tips
This handmade beef patty burger is rich in high-quality protein and iron, but the beef fat increases the calorie content. It is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. Suitable for replenishing energy after exercise, but those with weak digestion should control the portion size.
