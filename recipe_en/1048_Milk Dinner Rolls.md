# Milk Dinner Rolls
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥛 250ml milk (warmed to hand temperature)  
🧂 5g high-sugar tolerant yeast  
🍞 Approximately 400g bread flour (can be adjusted as needed)  
🍬 Approximately 35g white sugar  
🧂 A pinch of salt  
🥚 1 egg  
🥛 Approximately 30g condensed milk (optional)  
🧈 Approximately 20g butter (melted, can be replaced with flavorless vegetable oil)  
🍞 Some bread flour (for surface decoration)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Large bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
♨️ Oven  
🥄 Small saucepan  
🫙 Plastic wrap  
🫓 Silicone kneading mat  
🔪 Dough scraper  
🍳 Baking tray  
🫗 Sieve  

## Preparation Steps
1. Heat the milk to hand temperature, add the yeast, stir until dissolved, then let it sit to activate.
2. Place the bread flour, white sugar, and salt into a large bowl, and mix evenly with chopsticks.
3. Add the egg and condensed milk, then pour in the yeast milk. Stir into a flaky texture, then knead into a dough by hand.
   - **Note**: It is normal for the initial dough to be very sticky; do not over-knead.
4. Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest and relax for 30 minutes.
5. Add the melted butter and continue kneading and pressing until the butter is fully absorbed, and the dough surface becomes smooth and non-sticky.
6. Roll the dough into a ball, place it in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it ferment in a warm place until it doubles in volume.
7. Remove the dough, gently press to release gas, and use a dough scraper to divide it evenly into 16 portions.
8. Take one small dough portion, use folding and pinching techniques to seal it into a round ball shape, place it seam-side down on the baking tray; repeat for all portions, leaving appropriate space between them.
9. Place in a warm spot for the second fermentation. Allow the dough to expand to 1.5 to 2 times its original volume, feeling light to the touch.
10. Sift a thin layer of bread flour over the surface of the fermented dough.
    - **Alternative**: You can also brush with whole egg liquid for a golden surface.
11. Preheat the oven to 180°C top and bottom heat, place the baking tray in the middle rack, and bake for about 20 minutes until the surface turns golden brown.
    - **Key point**: Adjust time and temperature according to your own oven.

## Health Suggestions
This milk dinner roll is rich in high-quality protein and calcium, making it suitable for breakfast or as a snack. However, it contains relatively high amounts of sugar and butter. Those managing their weight are advised to reduce the amount of condensed milk. The bread has a soft texture that is easy to digest, making it suitable for the elderly and children, but be sure to pair it with vegetables and fruits to balance nutrition.
