# Cheese and Sweet Potato Baked Rice Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Four Seasons Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍠 1 sweet potato (baked and mashed for the filling)  
🌾 200g glutinous rice flour  
🥛 180g milk (for the batter)  
🥚 1 egg  
🧀 About 2 slices of cheese  
🌽 40g corn oil  
🍬 25g sugar  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer  
🥣 Mixing bowl + whisk  
🥄 Small bowl (for mashing)  
🔪 Knife  
📄 Parchment paper  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Wash the sweet potato and place it on the air fryer rack, adding a small amount of water to the bottom of the rack. Set to 200°C and bake for 35 minutes, until the skin is wrinkled and a chopstick can easily pierce through.  
   **Key point**: Adding water prevents the skin from burning and makes it easier to remove and mash the flesh later.  
2. Remove the baked sweet potato, peel off the skin, place the flesh in a small bowl, and press it into a smooth mash with your hands.  
   **Key point**: The sweet potato must be fully baked to achieve a smooth, lump-free mash.  
3. Prepare the rice cake batter: In a mixing bowl, sequentially add 200g glutinous rice flour, 180g milk, 40g corn oil, 25g sugar, and 1 egg. Stir with a whisk until the batter is smooth, lump-free, and in a flowing state.  
   **Consistency check**: When lifting the whisk, the batter should drip down slowly and continuously.  
4. Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper. First, pour a small amount of batter to cover the bottom. Evenly spread the sweet potato mash over the batter, place the cheese slices on top, then pour the remaining batter to completely cover the filling.  
   **Key point**: Adding the batter in layers ensures the filling is in the middle and prevents it from overflowing during baking.  
5. Place the basket in the air fryer, set to 160°C, and bake for 45 minutes.  
   **Consistency check**: The surface of the rice cake should be golden brown, springy to the touch, with no flowing batter.  
   **Key point**: Low-temperature slow baking ensures the inside is fully cooked while preventing the surface from burning.  
6. Remove the baked rice cake, cut into small pieces with a knife while hot. Cutting while hot results in clean edges and prevents sticking to the knife.  

## Health Advice  
This cheese and sweet potato baked rice cake is rich in carbohydrates and calcium, making it suitable for post-workout energy replenishment or an afternoon tea snack. However, diabetic patients should control their portion size. It is recommended to pair it with unsweetened tea.
