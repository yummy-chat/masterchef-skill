# Ham and Egg Stir-fried Cucumber
Cuisine: Home-style  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: 🍃🥗 Suitable for all seasons  

---

### 🥬 Ingredients
- 🥒 1 cucumber (about 200g)
- 🥚 2 eggs
- 🍖 1 ham sausage (about 40g)
- 🌶️ 1 red chili pepper (optional)

### 🧂 Seasonings
- 🧂 2g salt
- 🍶 3ml light soy sauce
- 🛢️ 10ml cooking oil

### 🔪 Utensils
- 🍳 Wok or frying pan
- 🥣 Bowl
- 🥢 Chopsticks
- 🔪 Chef's knife
- 🥄 Cutting board

---

### 📝 Steps
1. Wash the cucumber, cut into half-moon slices, and set aside.
2. Cut the ham into half-moon slices and set aside.
3. Finely chop the red chili pepper (optional) and set aside.
4. Crack the eggs into a bowl, beat until well-blended to form an egg mixture.
5. Heat the wok, pour in 5ml of cooking oil, reduce to low heat, add the egg mixture, stir with chopsticks to scramble until the eggs are set and slightly golden, in a semi-cooked state, then remove and set aside.
6. **Without washing the wok**, pour in the remaining 5ml of cooking oil, add the cucumber slices, and stir-fry over high heat for 1 minute.
7. Return the semi-cooked eggs to the wok, season with 2g of salt and 3ml of light soy sauce, immediately add the ham slices and chopped chili pepper (if using), and quickly stir-fry until evenly combined.
   **Note**: The ham already contains salt, so it must be added after seasoning to prevent the ham from absorbing extra salt.
8. Turn off the heat, transfer to a plate.

---

## Health Advice
This dish is rich in high-quality protein and vitamins, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or seeking light meals. Cucumber is best stir-fried and served immediately to prevent excess water from affecting the texture; eggs are most tender when semi-cooked, so be careful with the heat and avoid overcooking.
