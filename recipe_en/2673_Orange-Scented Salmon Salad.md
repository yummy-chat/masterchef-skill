# Orange-Scented Salmon Salad
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn (September-October)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐟 Salmon fillet 1 piece  
🥬 Leafy green lettuce to taste  
🥑 Avocado to taste  
🍊 Gannan navel orange 1 (for grating zest; flesh diced)  
🥜 Nuts to taste  
🫒 Olive oil to taste (for marinating salmon + making dressing)  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🌶️ Black pepper to taste  
🍊 Orange zest to taste (grated from navel orange peel)  
🍎 Apple cider vinegar to taste  
🥫 Dijon mustard to taste  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Frying pan  
🔪 Chef’s knife + cutting board  
🧽 Grater  
🧺 Salad spinner or colander  
🥛 Measuring cup  
🥄 Spoon  

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the leafy green lettuce and drain well using a colander or salad spinner. Grate the zest from the Gannan navel orange, then dice the orange flesh and set aside.  
   **Note**: When grating the zest, keep away from the stove to prevent the volatile orange aromatic oils from igniting. Gannan navel oranges are seedless, so they can be diced and used directly.  
2. Pat the salmon fillet dry with paper towels. Evenly coat both sides with olive oil, season with salt and black pepper, and rub with orange zest. Let it marinate for a short while.  
   **Key point**: After marinating, gently scrape off some of the orange zest to prevent it from burning during frying.  
3. Heat a frying pan over medium heat. Place the marinated salmon fillet in the pan and fry until both sides are fragrant, the surface changes color and develops brown patches. Remove and set aside.  
4. Prepare the salad dressing: In a measuring cup, mix olive oil, apple cider vinegar, and Dijon mustard to taste, and stir until well combined.  
5. Line the bottom of a plate with the drained lettuce. Add scooped avocado flesh, drizzle with the prepared dressing, and gently toss to combine.  
6. Sprinkle with the diced orange pieces, then add the nuts. Finally, place the pan-fried salmon fillet on top and serve.

## Health Advice
Salmon is rich in omega-3 fatty acids. Combined with avocado and nuts, this is a high-quality fat combination that supports heart and brain health, making it suitable for weight loss periods and those with high mental demands. The freshness of orange zest and apple cider vinegar balances the oils. However, be careful not to overdo the dressing to avoid excess calories.
