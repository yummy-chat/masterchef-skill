# Homemade Hot Pot
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Large shrimp, as needed
🦆 Duck blood curd, 2 boxes
🌿 Cilantro, one bunch
🐷 Pork kidney, 1 pair
🐚 Abalone, 6 pieces
🐂 Beef tenderloin, as needed
🐮 Tripe, 500g
🍄 Straw mushrooms, as needed
🥒 Asparagus lettuce, as needed
🥬 Lettuce, as needed
🍈 Winter melon, as needed
🧄 Minced garlic
🌱 White sesame seeds
🌶️ Dried chili pepper segments
🥜 Erba sauce (sesame paste: peanut butter = 2:8)
🥜 Peanut oil, a small amount (for thinning the sauce)
💧 Water, a small amount (for thinning the sauce)
🌿 Chinese chive flowers
🥫 Fermented bean curd liquid
🧂 Salt (for dipping sauce and shrimp paste seasoning)
🍬 White sugar
🧂 MSG
🧅 Scallions, chopped
🧂 White pepper powder, scallion-ginger water, cornstarch (for shrimp paste)
🍺 Beer, as needed (to soak pork kidney for removing odor)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍲 Dual-flavor hot pot
🔥 Induction cooker
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥣 Stainless steel bowls, plastic bowls (for soaking and serving)
🥄 Spoon (for removing abalone, pouring oil, stirring)
🧺 Colander (for washing straw mushrooms)

## Instructions
1. **Prepare vegetables**: Peel the asparagus lettuce and cut into diamond-shaped slices. Wash the lettuce and winter melon, and cut into appropriate chunks.
2. **Prepare duck blood curd**: Rinse the duck blood curd and directly pour it into a bowl, keeping it in chunks.
3. **Prepare beef tenderloin**: Slice the beef thinly against the grain. No marinating is needed.
4. **Prepare tripe**: Clean the tripe thoroughly, tear it into large pieces by hand, and arrange on a plate.
5. **Prepare pork kidney**: Cut the pork kidney in half and thoroughly remove the white central part. On the kidney pieces, first make deep cuts without cutting through, then cut completely through on the second cut to create a "centipede cut". Soak the cut kidney slices in beer, then rinse repeatedly until the water runs clear to remove the odor. Set aside.
6. **Make shrimp paste**: Peel the large shrimp and devein them. Crush them with the flat side of the knife, then coarsely chop to retain some texture. Add salt, white pepper powder, scallion-ginger water, and cornstarch. Stir in one direction until the shrimp mixture becomes very sticky, then repeatedly slap it for dozens of times to make the shrimp paste firmer.
7. **Prepare abalone**: Use a spoon to scoop out the abalone meat along the shell wall. Clean thoroughly and slice thinly with a sharp knife.
8. **Prepare straw mushrooms**: Wash the straw mushrooms. They are used to enhance the flavor of the broth.
9. **Prepare dipping sauce**: In a bowl, combine Erba sauce, a little peanut oil, and water. Stir in one direction until smooth and creamy. Add Chinese chive flowers, fermented bean curd liquid, salt, white sugar, and MSG. Mix well. In another bowl, place minced garlic, white sesame seeds, and dried chili pepper segments. Pour in hot oil to release the aroma, then pour in the sesame paste mixture. Finally, sprinkle with chopped scallions and cilantro and stir to combine.
10. **Prepare the broth**: Use a dual-flavor hot pot. In the clear broth pot, add straw mushrooms, scallion segments, and other ingredients to enhance the umami flavor.
11. **Cook**: Place the tripe, pork kidney, beef slices, shrimp paste, duck blood curd, abalone slices, and vegetables into the boiling pot in order of how quickly they cook. Cook until done and enjoy.

## Health Advice
Although hot pot is delicious, it is recommended to prioritize cooking vegetables and lean meat, and avoid excessive consumption of high-fat tripe and dipping sauce. This is suitable for those needing to control calorie intake. Duck blood curd and shrimp paste are rich in protein, but the pork kidney, after being soaked in beer to remove odor, must be thoroughly cooked to prevent the risk of parasites.
