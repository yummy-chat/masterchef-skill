# Braised Fish Maw and Chicken Soup with Pumpkin Purée
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 Fish maw (swim bladder) approx. 3 jin (after processing yields approx. 1 jin of white maw)
🍗 Chicken soup, as needed
🎃 Pumpkin purée, as needed
🫘 Red dates, as needed
🍒 Goji berries, as needed
🧂 Table salt, as needed
🍶 White vinegar, as needed
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed
🌾 Flour, as needed
🌿 Peppercorns, as needed
🫚 Ginger slices, as needed
🧅 Scallion segments, as needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
📦 Plastic storage container
🥣 Clear glass bowl
🕸️ Metal drying rack
🔥 Oven (with dehydrating mode)
🍲 Steamer
🫕 Clay pot

## Preparation Steps
1. **Process the fish maw**: Clean the fish maw thoroughly, use a toothpick to prick each one to release air, remove the inner membrane, and keep only the outer white gelatinous layer.
   **Note**: The inner membrane must be removed completely, otherwise the texture will be affected.

2. **Wash and deodorize**: Place the white maw in clean water, add ginger slices, scallion segments, and salt, and scrub twice. Then add white vinegar, cooking wine, and flour, and knead for 5 minutes to remove any off-odors and fish oil. Finally, rinse thoroughly with clean water 2–3 times until the water runs clear and the fish maw appears white.
   **Note**: Rinse thoroughly to ensure no residual impurities remain.

3. **Make dried fish maw**: Spread the cleaned white maw flat on a metal drying rack and air-dry in a ventilated area until completely dry.
   **Alternative method**: If the weather is unfavorable, cut the white maw open and spread it flat on a baking rack, then dry in the oven at 53°C with the dehydrating mode for 12 hours to make dried fish maw.

4. **Rehydrate the dried fish maw**: Place an appropriate amount of dried fish maw in a steamer, add ginger slices, and steam for 20 minutes until softened. Remove and soak in clean water overnight until the volume significantly increases and the color turns pale white.
   **Key point**: The steaming step is to soften the tissue, facilitating subsequent water absorption and rehydration.

5. **Braise into soup**: Add chicken soup and the rehydrated fish maw to a clay pot, then add pumpkin purée, red dates, peppercorns, and salt to taste. Stir well and simmer for 20 minutes until the soup becomes thick and golden, and the fish maw has a crisp and bouncy texture.
   **Note**: Add the pumpkin purée towards the end, as its main function is to color and flavor the soup, and it should not be cooked for too long.

6. **Finish and serve**: Add the goji berries just before turning off the heat.
   **Key point**: Add the goji berries at the end to preserve their nutrients and color.

## Health Advice
This soup is rich in collagen and various trace elements, suitable for those with dry skin or who need nourishment. However, fish maw has a relatively high cholesterol content, so people with high blood lipids should consume it in moderation. Pumpkin purée is not only used for coloring but also provides vitamin A, making the nutrition more balanced.
