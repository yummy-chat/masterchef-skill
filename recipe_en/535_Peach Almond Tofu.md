# Peach Almond Tofu  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍑 White chocolate (as needed, for peach shell)  
🥛 Almonds 50g (soak for 2 hours in advance)  
💧 Water 100g (for soaking and blending almonds)  
🥛 Milk approx. 200ml  
🥛 Almond drink 250ml  
🥛 Whipping cream (as needed)  
🌰 Almond powder 10g  
🍬 White sugar (as needed)  
🍮 Gelatin powder 10g (dissolve in a small amount of water)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Small milk pot  
🍑 Silicone peach mold  
🌀 Blender  
🧽 Cheesecloth or fine strainer  
🥄 Spoon  
🥣 Bowls, cups  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Steps  
1. **Make the chocolate peach shell**: Melt white chocolate over a water bath. Pour into the silicone peach mold, rotate to coat the inner walls evenly, then pour out excess chocolate. Freeze for about 20 minutes to set. Remove and repeat the coating once more, then freeze again until fully hardened.  
   **Key point**: Apply two coats to ensure the shell is complete and won’t break.  

2. **Make the almond paste**: Place the soaked almonds and water into a blender, blend thoroughly into a fine paste.  

3. **Strain the almond paste**: Press through cheesecloth or a fine strainer to obtain pure almond milk. The residue can be used for other purposes.  
   **Key point**: Straining ensures a smooth, melt-in-the-mouth texture.  

4. **Cook the almond milk**: In a pot, add milk, almond drink, whipping cream, almond powder, and the strained almond milk. Pour in the pre-dissolved gelatin solution. Keep stirring over low heat for about 3 minutes, until all ingredients are well combined and gelatin is fully dissolved. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.  
   **Key point**: Use low heat and stir continuously to prevent the sugary liquid from scorching; allow the cooked liquid to cool before pouring into the shells, or it will melt the chocolate.  

5. **Assemble and set**: Remove the hardened chocolate peach shells from the freezer and carefully unmold. Fill the shells with the cooled almond milk mixture. Refrigerate until the filling is fully set.  

## Health Tips  
This dessert is rich in protein and calcium, making it a delicate treat. However, it is high in sugar and fat, so portion control is advised. Gelatin powder provides collagen, which benefits skin elasticity. Those with sensitive stomachs should enjoy in moderation.
