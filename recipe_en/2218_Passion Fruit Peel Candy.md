# Passion Fruit Peel Candy
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥭 20 passion fruits (choose purple-skinned passion fruits)
🍬 200 grams rock sugar

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🔪 Peeler
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥄 Spoon
🥣 Glass bowl
🍲 Stainless steel pot (cooking pot)
🍳 Wok (iron/non-stick)
🥄 Slotted spoon
🔥 Induction cooker
🍴 Wooden spatula

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the passion fruits thoroughly, then use a peeler to remove the thin outer skin.
   **Note**: Only peel off the very thin outer layer; do not peel too deeply.
2. Cut a small opening in each passion fruit and use a spoon to scoop out the pulp, keeping the empty peels.
3. Cut the peels in half and remove the inner white membrane.
   **Note**: The inner membrane must be removed, otherwise it will affect the final texture.
4. Cut the cleaned peels into thin strips.
5. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add the peel strips, and blanch for about 2 minutes, until the peels become soft.
   **Note**: Blanching removes the astringent taste from the peels.
6. Remove the peels and rinse them in cold water, washing thoroughly several times. Then firmly squeeze out all the water from the peels.
   **Note**: Squeezing out the water helps with flavor absorption and drying during the subsequent cooking process.
7. Put the squeezed peels into a wok, add 200 grams of rock sugar, and stir constantly over medium heat until the rock sugar is completely melted.
8. Once the rock sugar has melted, reduce the heat to medium-low and continue slowly stir-frying for about 50 minutes.
   **Doneness check**: The mixture will go from having a lot of moisture to drying out, and finally the peel's color will turn white.
   **Note**: If the pan starts to scorch during this process, remove the peels, wash the pan, and then return the peels to continue stir-frying until the moisture is gone and the color turns white, then remove from the pan.

## Health Advice
Passion fruit peel candy has a relatively high sugar content, so it is recommended to eat only 2-3 strips per serving to avoid excessive sugar intake. It is suitable as a small snack for satisfying cravings, but it is not recommended during weight loss.
