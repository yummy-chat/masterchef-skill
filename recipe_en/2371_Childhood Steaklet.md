# Childhood Steaklet
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Lean beef 200g (ground into a fine mince)
🥚 Egg 1 (egg white only)
🍞 Breadcrumbs 30g (for coating the steaklet, adding a crispy texture)

**Ingredients for Marinating the Beef Mince**
🧂 Salt to taste
🫑 Black pepper to taste
🦪 Oyster sauce to taste
🍶 Cooking wine to taste

**Compound Basting Sauce**
🌶️ Chili powder to taste
🔥 BBQ seasoning to taste
🫘 Toasted sesame seeds to taste
🌿 Cumin powder/cumin seeds to taste
🧄 Minced garlic to taste
🍯 Honey to taste
🫗 Light soy sauce to taste
🌰 Sesame oil to taste
🫙 Chili oil to taste
💧 Water a small amount (to adjust consistency)
🛢️ Hot oil one tablespoon (for pouring over dry ingredients to release aroma)

## Cookware & Equipment
⚙️ Meat grinder
🪣 Stainless steel bowl (for mixing and marinating the meat)
🧤 Disposable gloves (for mixing the meat)
🫙 Plastic bag/sealable bag (to assist with breading)
🔲 Rolling pin (to flatten the steaklet)
📝 Parchment paper/baking tray
♨️ Electric oven
🥢 Chopsticks or tongs
🥄 Silicone brush
🪥 Skewers (for skewering the steaklet)
🍽️ Deep dish (for preparing the sauce)

## Cooking Steps
1.  Cut 200g of lean beef into pieces, place them in a meat grinder, and grind into a fine mince. Transfer to a stainless steel bowl.
2.  Add salt, black pepper, oyster sauce, cooking wine, and egg white to the mince. Wearing gloves, use your hands to mix and repeatedly slap the meat against the bowl until it becomes sticky and firm.
    **Key Point**: Thoroughly slapping the meat is crucial for a firm and bouncy texture.
3.  Divide the seasoned meat into equal portions and roll into balls. Place one meatball into a sealable bag with breadcrumbs. Use a rolling pin to gently flatten it into a patty. Turn and press repeatedly to ensure the breadcrumbs coat the patty evenly and firmly.
4.  Preheat the oven. Line a baking tray with parchment paper. Arrange the breaded beef patties flat on the tray. Bake at 200°C for 15–18 minutes.
    **Doneness Check**: Bake until both sides are golden and crispy, and the patty is fully cooked through.
5.  While the patties are baking, prepare the sauce: In a deep dish, combine chili powder, BBQ seasoning, toasted sesame seeds, cumin powder, cumin seeds, and minced garlic. Heat one tablespoon of cooking oil and pour it evenly over the dry ingredients to release their aroma.
6.  Next, add honey, light soy sauce, sesame oil, chili oil, and a small amount of water to the dish. Stir thoroughly to combine into a thick compound sauce.
7.  Remove the baked beef patties from the oven. Insert two bamboo skewers horizontally and parallel through each patty to secure its shape. Use a silicone brush to dip into the sauce and generously coat both sides of the steaklet.
    **Key Point**: Apply the sauce generously and thoroughly to ensure rich flavor.
8.  Serve immediately while hot. The exterior should be crispy, while the inside remains tender and firm, with a rich, complex BBQ flavor.

## Health Advice
Lean beef is high in protein and low in fat, making it suitable for muscle building and weight loss. Breadcrumbs provide crispiness but are higher in calories; it is recommended to pair with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. Limit the amount of sweet sauce to control sugar intake. For those with high blood pressure, reduce the amount of oyster sauce and light soy sauce.
