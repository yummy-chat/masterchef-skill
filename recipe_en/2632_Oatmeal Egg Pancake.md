# Oatmeal Egg Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 Egg  
🌾 Oatmeal  
🥛 Milk  
🥬 Spinach (optional)  
🧈 Butter  
🧂 Salt  
🌶️ Pepper  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan  

## Cooking Steps
1. Mix milk and oatmeal together, stirring until thick and sticky.
2. Beat the eggs until the egg mixture has a uniform color.
3. Pour the beaten eggs into the oatmeal-milk mixture, and continue stirring until it becomes thick and even.
4. Heat a non-stick pan, add butter, and let it melt, spreading evenly over the bottom of the pan.
5. Pour the mixed batter into the pan and spread it out into a pancake shape.
6. Cook over low heat for 2–3 minutes; if adding vegetables, sprinkle chopped spinach in at this point.
7. Flip the pancake and cook over low heat for another 2 minutes.
8. Once both sides are golden brown, remove from the pan. It can be served with leftover milk as a breakfast.

## Health Tips
This oatmeal egg pancake is rich in high-quality protein and dietary fiber, making it suitable for weight loss periods or as breakfast. Pairing it with vegetables can increase vitamin intake. Be mindful of the amount of butter used to avoid excessive calories.
