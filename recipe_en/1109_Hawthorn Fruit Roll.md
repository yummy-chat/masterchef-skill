# Hawthorn Fruit Roll  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn (September–October)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍏 Hawthorn berries (pitted, as needed)  
🍎 Apples (peeled and cut into chunks, as needed)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥘 Stainless steel steamer rack  
🌀 Blender  
🕸️ Sieve  
🫓 Baking tray  
📜 Parchment paper  
🔲 Spatula  
🔥 Oven  

## Steps  
1. Line a baking tray with parchment paper, ensuring it completely covers the bottom.  
   **Note**: The parchment paper must fully cover the bottom of the tray.  

2. Pit the hawthorn berries, peel and cut the apples into moderate-sized chunks for steaming.  
   **Note**: Clean the fruit thoroughly, and keep the apple chunks at a uniform size.  

3. Bring water to a boil in a wok, place the steamer rack inside, and spread the hawthorn berries and apple chunks evenly on the rack. Cover and steam over high heat until the fruit can be easily pierced with chopsticks.  
   **Note**: Ensure there is enough water to prevent burning dry.  

4. Remove the steamed fruit, let it cool slightly, then transfer to a blender and blend into a smooth puree, ensuring no lumps remain.  
   **Note**: Make sure to blend until no large particles are present.  

5. Strain the fruit puree through a sieve, pressing it with a spoon to pass through into the parchment-lined baking tray.  
   **Note**: This step is crucial for a smooth texture—be patient during the process.  

6. Use a spatula to spread the puree evenly into a uniform rectangle on the tray.  
   **Note**: The thickness must be consistent; otherwise, thin parts may burn and thick parts may not dry properly.  

7. Place the tray in a preheated oven and bake at a low temperature until the puree is dry, the edges lift easily, and it is no longer sticky to the touch. During baking, adjust the tray’s orientation as needed for even heating and monitor closely to prevent burning.  
   **Note**: Keep a close watch while baking to avoid burning.  

8. Remove the tray, let it cool slightly, then gently peel off the entire fruit roll. It can be rolled up or cut into shapes for serving.  
   **Note**: Peel gently to avoid tearing.  

## Health Tips  
Hawthorn berries and apples offer a naturally sweet and sour flavor, rich in pectin and vitamin C. They make an excellent additive-free healthy snack, especially suitable for stimulating appetite and aiding digestion in autumn. Note that while homemade fruit rolls contain less sugar, their acidity may stimulate stomach acid; those with excess stomach acid should avoid eating on an empty stomach.
