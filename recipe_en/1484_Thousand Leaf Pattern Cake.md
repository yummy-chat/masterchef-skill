# Thousand Leaf Pattern Cake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season dish  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥚 20 eggs (separate egg whites and yolks)  
🥛 266g milk  
🌽 233g corn oil  
🌾 366g cake flour  
🍫 Cocoa powder, as needed (for making the pattern)  
🍬 300g white sugar (add to egg whites in three batches)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🫙 Stainless steel bowl (large, grease-free and water-free)  
🥄 Hand whisk  
🔌 Electric hand mixer  
🔪 Spatula  
🔥 Oven  
📜 Baking tray  
📄 Parchment paper  
🍶 Piping bag  
🪥 Toothpick  
🧪 Measuring cup/graduated bottle  

## Steps  
1. Prepare two large grease-free and water-free bowls. Thoroughly separate the egg whites from the egg yolks.  
2. Add milk and corn oil to the bowl with the egg yolks. Whisk until combined. Sift in the cake flour in batches, stirring until the batter is smooth and lump-free, with clear, non-dissipating lines when drizzled.  
3. Add the white sugar to the egg whites in three batches. Use an electric hand mixer to beat on high speed until stiff peaks form—when lifted, the whisk should have firm, pointed peaks, and the meringue should not flow when the bowl is inverted.  
4. Take about one-third of the meringue and add it to the egg yolk mixture. Fold gently with a spatula until combined. Then pour back into the remaining meringue and fold gently and quickly until fully incorporated, being careful not to deflate the mixture.  
5. Line the baking tray with parchment paper. Pour the cake batter into the tray and spread it evenly with a scraper.  
6. Take a small amount of the remaining batter, mix in the cocoa powder to make a dark color, and transfer to a piping bag. Cut a small tip and pipe parallel horizontal lines evenly over the surface of the light-colored batter.  
7. Use a toothpick to draw vertical lines perpendicular to the horizontal lines, alternating up and down motions, to create the classic thousand leaf pattern.  
8. Preheat the oven to 170℃. Place the baking tray in the middle rack and bake for about 22 minutes, until the cake surface is golden brown and springs back when pressed.  
9. Remove from the oven, unmold, let cool, then cut into pieces and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This cake uses 20 eggs, with the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks for excellent fluffiness. Ideal for sharing at gatherings. For those on a weight loss plan, cut into small portions and pair with black coffee to balance the flavor.
