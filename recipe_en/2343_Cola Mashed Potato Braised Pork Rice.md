# Cola Mashed Potato Braised Pork Rice  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Pork belly, appropriate amount (cut into small pieces)  
🧅 Onion, about half (shredded)  
🥔 Potatoes, appropriate amount (cut into chunks)  
🍚 Rice, appropriate amount (as base)  
🥬 Greens, appropriate amount (for pairing)  
🧂 Light soy sauce, 3 tablespoons  
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon  
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon  
⭐ Star anise, 2 pieces  
🥤 Cola, 1 can  
💧 Hot water, half a bowl  
🌿 White sesame seeds, appropriate amount (for garnish)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan/wok  
🥘 Lid  
🥄 Spatula  
🥣 Bowl/measuring cup  
🥤 Can (of cola)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Cut the pork belly into small pieces, shred the onion, and cut the potatoes into chunks. Set aside.  
2. Place the pork belly pieces directly into a non-stick pan without oil, and fry until the surface is slightly charred and fat is rendered.  
   **Key point**: Use the pork belly's own fat for frying.  
3. Add the shredded onion and stir-fry until soft and translucent.  
4. Pour in the sauce mixture made with light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, and star anise. Stir-fry quickly and evenly to coat the ingredients.  
5. Pour in the entire can of cola and mix well.  
6. Add half a bowl of hot water, enough to roughly submerge the ingredients. Cover with the lid, reduce the heat to medium-low, and simmer for 30 minutes.  
7. Remove the lid, add the potato chunks, cover again, and continue to simmer for 15 minutes until the potatoes are fully cooked.  
8. Remove the lid and turn the heat to high to reduce the sauce, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients.  
   **Note**: Stir continuously to prevent burning.  
9. Serve the rice in a bowl, top with the braised pork and mashed potatoes in cola sauce, and pair with blanched greens.  
10. Sprinkle with white sesame seeds as garnish.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is highly creative, but the pork belly is rich in fat, and the cola contains a significant amount of sugar. It is recommended to pair it with plenty of greens and control the amount of braising sauce used. Suitable for occasional indulgence; those with high blood pressure, blood sugar, or blood lipids should consume with caution.
