# Cola Instant Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍜 Instant noodles 2 packets (including seasoning packets)
🥚 Eggs 3
🍤 Shrimp 4
🌿 Cilantro to taste (cut into sections)
🌶️ Bird's eye chili to taste (sliced into rings)
🍶 White vinegar a few drops
🧂 Salt a pinch
🥤 Cola 1 can

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🥄 Spatula
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🔥 Gas stove

## Cooking Steps
1.  Crack the 3 eggs into a bowl, keeping the yolks intact. Add a few drops of white vinegar to remove the fishy smell, and place the shrimp inside for later use.
   **Key point**: White vinegar neutralizes the eggy smell; the shrimp does not require any pre-treatment.

2.  Cut the cilantro into sections and slice the bird's eye chili into rings for later use.

3.  Place the frying pan on the stove over heat, pour in a little cooking oil. When the oil is hot, sprinkle in a pinch of salt, then pour in the egg mixture with the shrimp.
   **Key point**: Fry over the lowest heat throughout to prevent burning. Once the bottom of the egg mixture is set, gently shake the pan to release the omelet from the bottom. Fry both sides until golden and fully cooked.

4.  Use the spatula to cut the cooked omelet into small pieces directly in the pan.

5.  Pour the entire can of cola into the pan. After it comes to a boil, add the two instant noodle cakes and sprinkle in one packet of the instant noodle seasoning.

6.  Cover the pan with a lid, turn the heat to the lowest setting, and simmer for about 3 minutes.
   **Key point**: Be sure to turn the heat to the lowest setting to prevent the broth from reducing too quickly.

7.  Remove the lid, sprinkle with the cilantro sections and bird's eye chili rings, and serve immediately.

## Health Recommendations
Although cooking noodles in cola seems like an unconventional pairing, the eggs and shrimp provide high-quality protein, and the bird's eye chili promotes metabolism, making it suitable for occasional indulgence. Note that cola has a high sugar content; individuals with diabetes or those on a weight loss regimen should reduce the portion by half as appropriate.
