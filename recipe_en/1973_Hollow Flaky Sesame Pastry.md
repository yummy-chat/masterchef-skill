# Hollow Flaky Sesame Pastry  
Cuisine: Henan Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 All-purpose flour 500g (for dough)  
💧 Warm water 300g (about 40°C)  
🧂 Salt 3g  
🫒 Cooked soybean oil 30g (for kneading)  
🌾 All-purpose flour appropriate amount (for oil pastry)  
🫒 Cooked soybean oil appropriate amount (for oil pastry)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel mixing bowl  
🔪 Cutting board  
🪄 Rolling pin  
♨️ Oven (with adjustable upper and lower heat)  
📋 Baking tray or wire rack  
🥢 Food tongs  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Mix 500g flour, 3g salt, and 30g cooked soybean oil in a bowl. Gradually add 300g warm water while stirring. Knead into a slightly soft smooth dough, achieving a clean bowl, clean hands, and smooth dough.  
**Key Point**: Warm water speeds up proofing and saves time; a softer dough is easier to work with.  

2. In a separate bowl, mix an appropriate amount of flour with cooked soybean oil to form a spreadable oil paste. Adjust until smooth; no precise ratio is needed.  
3. Let the kneaded dough rest briefly to relax the gluten, making it easier to roll out later.  
4. Take a portion of the rested dough, roll it into a long strip, press it flat, and roll it out into a large thin rectangular sheet.  
**Key Point**: Roll the sheet as thin as possible for better layers.  

5. Evenly spread a layer of oil paste over the dough sheet. Hold both ends and gently stretch to make the sheet thinner and longer.  
**Key Point**: Stretching increases layers, adding richness when rolling.  

6. Starting from one end, stretch and roll tightly into a long roll. Lightly even it out and let it rest briefly again.  
7. Use a "hollowing" technique to pinch off small dough pieces from the rolled log, avoiding direct flattening that would damage the layers.  
8. Check the pinched ends: If the ends are open, roll them out, fold the surrounding dough toward the center, and seal tightly. Ensure all openings are completely closed to prevent air leakage during baking.  
**Key Point**: Complete sealing is key to achieving a hollow interior.  

9. Flatten the sealed dough pieces with your palm, then gently roll them into regular rectangular shapes.  
10. Brush the baking tray with oil and place the shaped dough on it. Preheat the oven to 230°C top heat and 200°C bottom heat. Bake for about 3 minutes.  
11. Once the bottom is colored, flip and continue baking until both sides are golden brown and the pastry puffs up. Remove immediately.  
**Heat Judgment**: The pastry should fully expand and turn golden before removing.  

12. The finished pastry is crispy and flaky on the outside, hollow inside, and perfect for filling with meat or vegetables.  

## Health Advice  
This hollow flaky pastry is a high-carb and high-fat combination, suitable for occasional consumption. For those on a weight loss or blood sugar control plan, it is recommended to eat half a piece per serving. The rich pastry layers pair well with vegetables and protein (e.g., sliced beef and lettuce) for a balanced meal.
