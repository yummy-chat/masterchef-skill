# Braised Beef Short Ribs
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍖 Beef short ribs 1 kg (preferably machine-cut)
🧅 Scallions appropriate amount (cut into sections)
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced)
🫘 Sesame seeds appropriate amount (for garnish)
🌿 Chopped scallions appropriate amount (for garnish)
🫒 Oil 1 tablespoon
🍬 Sugar 1 tablespoon
⭐ Star anise appropriate amount
🌳 Cinnamon stick appropriate amount
🌸 Cloves appropriate amount
🍃 Bay leaves appropriate amount
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorns 1 tablespoon
🌶️ Dried chili peppers 1 tablespoon
🍅 Tomato paste 1 teaspoon
🧂 Light soy sauce 2 tablespoons
🧂 Dark soy sauce 1 teaspoon
🧂 Salt 1/8 teaspoon (adjust to taste)

## Cookware and Equipment
♨️ Pressure cooker
🥘 Stockpot
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥄 Wooden spoon or spatula
🥢 Chopsticks

## Instructions
1. **Soak to remove blood**: Place beef short ribs in a large bowl, add warm water and soak for about 30 minutes. Discard the bloody water and rinse clean.
   - Key point: Warm water helps draw out blood more effectively, reducing gamey flavor.
2. **Blanch**: Place short ribs in a pot with cold water and bring to a boil over high heat. Remove the short ribs from the center of the boiling liquid and drain well.
   - Key point: Removing from the boiling center prevents picking up scum from the pot edges, saving an extra rinsing step.
3. **Caramelize sugar**: Pour 1 tablespoon of oil into the pressure cooker. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar to the cold oil and turn the heat to high. Once the sugar begins to change color, reduce to the lowest heat and stir continuously until the sugar turns a deep amber color and small bubbles appear.
   - Visual cue: The sugar syrup changes from light yellow to deep amber.
4. **Sear for color**: Add scallion sections and ginger slices, then immediately add the blanched short ribs. Stir-fry quickly to coat each piece evenly with the caramelized sugar.
5. **Add spices**: Add star anise, cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves, Sichuan peppercorns, and dried chili peppers. Stir-fry until fragrant.
6. **Add tomato paste**: Add 1 teaspoon of tomato paste and continue stir-frying until the red oil is released.
7. **Add liquid and season**: Pour in hot water until it reaches about halfway up the short ribs. Use chopsticks to adjust the position of the ribs so most of the meat is submerged. Add 2 tablespoons of light soy sauce and 1 teaspoon of dark soy sauce. Stir to combine, taste, and add a little salt if needed.
   - Key point: Must use hot water to prevent the meat from tightening and toughening.
8. **Pressure cook**: Cover the pressure cooker and bring to high heat until steam escapes. Reduce to low heat and cook under pressure for 15 minutes.
   - Note: Do not overcook, or the meat will completely separate from the bone, affecting the final presentation.
9. **Reduce sauce**: Turn off the heat, release the pressure, and open the lid. Remove the spices. Turn the heat to high and reduce the sauce, gently stirring the short ribs with a wooden spoon to prevent sticking, until the sauce thickens and clings to the meat.
   - Visual cue: The sauce changes from thin to thick, coating the short ribs like a glaze. Stir gently as the meat is very tender at this point.
10. **Plate and garnish**: Transfer the braised short ribs to a serving plate. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped scallions.

## Health Notes
Beef short ribs are rich in iron, which is beneficial for blood replenishment. However, after pressure cooking, the fat content is relatively high, so it is recommended to serve with light vegetables. Pay attention to the heat during sauce reduction to prevent burning.
