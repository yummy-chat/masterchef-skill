# Tuna and Corn Sandwich
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍞 Whole wheat toast, 2 slices  
🥚 Hard-boiled egg, 1  
🐟 Canned tuna, about half a can (drained)  
🌽 Corn kernels, 2-3 tablespoons (drained)  
🥗 Mayonnaise, to taste  
🧂 Black pepper, to taste (freshly ground)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🔨 Potato masher  
🧂 Black pepper grinder  
🔪 Butter knife/spatula (for spreading filling)  
🔪 Bread knife (serrated)  
🪓 Wooden cutting board  
🍽️ Plate  

## Instructions  
1. Place the hard-boiled egg in the bowl and mash it into fine crumbs using a potato masher.  
2. Add the drained tuna and corn kernels, squeeze in mayonnaise, and mix thoroughly until well combined.  
   - **Note**: The amount of mayonnaise can be adjusted to personal taste, directly affecting the filling's moisture and flavor.  
3. Using a black pepper grinder, grind a small amount of fresh black pepper into the filling and mix again.  
4. Take one slice of whole wheat toast and spread the prepared filling evenly over the surface.  
5. Top with the other slice of toast and gently press down to ensure they stick together tightly.  
6. Using a serrated bread knife, cut the sandwich diagonally into triangles, then arrange on a plate for serving.  
   - **Key point**: Use a serrated knife with a back-and-forth sawing motion to prevent the filling from oozing out and to keep the cut edges neat.  

## Health Tips  
Whole wheat toast provides dietary fiber, while tuna and eggs offer high-quality protein. The corn kernels add vitamins, making this a nutritionally balanced meal. Ideal for busy office workers or students. Note that mayonnaise is relatively high in calories; you can halve the amount or substitute it with yogurt.
