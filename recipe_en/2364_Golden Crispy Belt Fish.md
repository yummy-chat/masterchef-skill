# Golden Crispy Belt Fish  
Cuisine: Zhejiang Cuisine  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🐟 Small-eye belt fish (Zhoushan belt fish) –适量  
🧂 Salt –适量  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
✂️ Scissors  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🧽 Steel wool or abrasive scrubbing pad  
🧻 Paper towels  
🥢 Tongs  

## Steps  
1. Choose fresh small-eye belt fish, ensuring the eyes are bright and not cloudy.  
2. Use scissors to cut diagonally from under the fin, twist, and pull out the内脏 completely.  
3. Use an abrasive pad or steel wool to thoroughly scrape off the silver scales on the fish's surface until the white-pink skin is exposed.  
4. Tear off the dorsal fin by hand; make two cuts at the base of the ventral fin with a knife, then break off the fin bones inside.  
5. Use scissors to cut open the belly, completely remove the black membrane and blood near the spine, and rinse clean with water.  
6. Pat the belt fish dry and cut into uniform pieces about 5–6 cm long; thinner tail pieces can be set aside for other uses.  
7. Evenly sprinkle a thin layer of salt on both sides of the fish pieces, and let marinate for about 30 minutes.  
8. After marinating, water will seep out from the fish. Use paper towels to thoroughly press and dry each piece on the surface.  
9. In a non-stick pan, add enough oil to cover about one-third of the fish's thickness. Heat over medium heat until the oil temperature reaches about 180°C (350°F).  
10. Place the belt fish pieces skin-side down into the pan, keeping space between them. Fry over medium heat until fine, uniform bubbles appear around the edges.  
11. Fry over medium heat for about 3 minutes total, approximately 1–1.5 minutes per side. Flip when the surface darkens and shows slight golden-brown color, being careful not to break the pieces.  
12. Turn to medium-high heat and continue frying for 1–2 minutes until both sides are evenly golden and the outer shell is noticeably crispy.  
    **Key point**: With less oil, the temperature rises quickly. You can directly increase the heat in the pan for a second fry without removing the fish.  
    (Fry for another one to two minutes over medium-high heat until both sides are golden and the outside is crispy.  
    **Key point**: With less oil, the temperature rises quickly—just turn up the heat in the pan for the second fry without taking it out.)  
13. Use tongs to remove the belt fish and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.  

## Health Tips  
Belt fish is rich in high-quality protein and DHA, making it suitable for students, mental workers, and elderly people needing calcium supplementation. After frying, the calorie content is higher, so it is recommended to pair with vegetables for a balanced diet. Use salt sparingly when marinating; those with hypertension can consume in moderation.
