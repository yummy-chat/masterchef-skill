# Sprite Sesame Dip
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🫙 Sesame paste 3 parts  
🥤 Sprite 3 parts  
🧂 Light soy sauce 2 parts  

## Utensils & Equipment  
🥣 Bowl  
🥄 Spoon  

## Instructions  
1. Take a bowl, and add 3 parts sesame paste, 3 parts Sprite, and 2 parts light soy sauce in order.  
2. Stir continuously in one direction with a spoon until the paste becomes smooth, silky, and forms long, continuous strands.  
   **Key Point**: The golden ratio is 3:3:2. The Sprite thins the sesame paste while adding a refreshing sweetness with a pleasant aftertaste. Stir patiently in one direction to achieve a smooth, well-blended texture.

## Health Advice  
Sprite Sesame Dip is primarily made with sesame paste, which is relatively high in calories. It is recommended to use in moderation. Suitable for pairing with hot pot or cold dishes, but should not be left out for long, as the carbonation may dissipate and affect the flavor.
