# Shredded Cabbage
Cuisine: Hunan Cuisine
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Suggestion: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥬 1 head of cabbage
🥩 200g pork belly
🌶️ 2 red chili peppers
🧄 2 cloves of garlic
🫚 2 slices of ginger
🧅 0.5 stalk of garlic sprouts
🫒 60ml cooking oil
🍶 5ml cooking wine
🥢 5ml light soy sauce
🍇 5ml balsamic vinegar
🧂 5g salt
💛 2g chicken bouillon

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥣 Bowl
🔪 Knife, cutting board

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the cabbage in half lengthwise and remove the hard white core.
2. Tear the cabbage into pieces by hand, place in a bowl, add 2 grams of salt, wash with water, then drain and set aside.
3. Slice the ginger, slice the garlic, cut the red chili peppers into rings, and cut the garlic sprouts into sections. Set aside.
4. Slice the pork belly into thin pieces, rinse with water, and drain.
5. Add 30 ml of cooking oil to the wok and heat over high heat. Add the cabbage and stir-fry quickly for 1 minute. Add 3 grams of salt and continue stir-frying for about 2 minutes until the cabbage is 70% cooked. Remove and set aside.
6. Add another 30 ml of cooking oil to the wok. Add the sliced pork belly and stir-fry over high heat for 1 minute until the meat changes color.
7. Add the sliced ginger, sliced garlic, red chili pepper rings, and garlic sprout sections. Stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
8. Return the cabbage to the wok. Add 5 ml of balsamic vinegar, 5 ml of light soy sauce, 2 grams of chicken bouillon, and 5 ml of cooking wine. Stir-fry over high heat for 2 minutes until all ingredients are well mixed. Transfer to a plate and serve.

## Health Advice
This shredded cabbage dish is rich in dietary fiber and vitamin C. The pork belly provides high-quality protein but is also high in fat. It is recommended to pair it with coarse grain rice. For a better texture and less greasiness, slice the pork belly thinly and render the fat. Individuals with high blood lipids can reduce the amount of meat or substitute it with lean meat.
