# Genshin Impact Three-Color Rice Dumplings
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Glutinous rice flour 100g  
💧 Hot water 75g (for kneading dough)  
🍓 Strawberry powder approx. 1–2g (for pink dough)  
🍵 Matcha powder approx. 1–2g (for green dough)  
🥛 Whipping cream to taste  
🍬 Sugar to taste (20g can be used for dough, plus for whipping cream seasoning)  
🧂 Sea salt to taste (for whipping cream seasoning)  
🍯 Honey to taste (for whipping cream seasoning)  
🍪 Crushed biscuits a small amount (for decoration)  
🍓 Strawberry powder a small amount (for sprinkling on top)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍲 Cooking pot  
🥄 Strainer  
🥚 Whisk  
🍢 Bamboo skewers  
🍽️ Plate or paper box  

## Preparation Steps
1. Knead the dough: Place the glutinous rice flour in a bowl, gradually add hot water while stirring with chopsticks until flaky, then knead into a smooth, non-sticky dough.
   **Note**: The water amount must be precise; the dough is ready when it no longer sticks to your hands. If you prefer sweetness, add about 20g of sugar at this point.
2. Color the dough: Place strawberry powder and matcha powder in separate small cups, dissolve each with a small amount of hot water, then mix each with the corresponding portion of glutinous rice flour and knead until evenly combined to obtain pink and green dough.
   **Key**: Dissolving the powders in hot water first prevents uneven color distribution in the dough.
3. Shape the balls: Divide each colored dough into equal small portions, roll each into small balls of about 20–30 grams, keeping their sizes as uniform as possible.
4. Cook the balls: Bring a pot of water to a boil, add all the balls, and keep boiling until all float to the surface. Immediately remove and immerse in cold water to cool.
   **Criterion**: When all balls float, they are fully cooked; the cold water bath gives a chewy texture and prevents sticking.
5. Make the cream topping: In a bowl, combine whipping cream, sugar, sea salt, and honey. Whisk until thick with visible lines but still pourable.
   **Note**: The consistency of the cream topping directly affects its drizzling effect; do not over-whip until it becomes too thick.
6. Assemble and decorate: Thread the balls onto bamboo skewers in the order of pink, white, and green. Drizzle with the cream topping, and finally sprinkle a small amount of strawberry powder and crushed biscuits for decoration.
   **Key**: The color order must match the original game design for high authenticity.

## Health Tips
Glutinous rice products are relatively easy to digest but are high in calories. It is recommended to consume 1–2 skewers per serving, ideally after exercise for energy replenishment. Diabetic patients should reduce sugar intake, and low-fat whipping cream can be used to lower calorie content.
