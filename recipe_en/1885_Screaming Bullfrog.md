# Screaming Bullfrog
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty: ★★★★
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Bullfrog meat pieces 800g
🥒 Pickled ginger 20-30g
🌶️ Pickled chili peppers 5-10g
🌶️ Wild mountain pepper 10g
🌶️ Green and red chili peppers 20g
🧄 Garlic 30-50g
🌿 Chopped green onion 5g
🧂 Broad bean paste 20-30g
🧂 Salt 15g
🧂 White pepper powder 10g
🍺 Beer 400-500ml
🍶 Cooking wine 10ml
🫒 Sichuan pepper oil 5-10ml
🌽 Cornstarch 30g
🐖 Lard 20ml
🫒 Cooking oil 200ml

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥘 Strainer
🥣 Container (for marinating)
🔪 Knife
🪵 Cutting board

## Cooking Steps
1. **Clean and remove odor**: Wash the bullfrog meat and drain it. Add over 10g of salt and over 50ml of beer, and knead with hands for 5 minutes to remove the gamey smell. Then rinse with clean water until no blood or impurities flow out. Drain and place in a container for later use.
> ⚠️ When draining, you can press with a strainer for assistance.

2. **Marinate the meat**: Add 5g salt, 30g cornstarch, 10ml cooking wine, and 5g white pepper powder. Mix evenly with your hands and marinate for 5-10 minutes.
> ⚠️ When mixing cornstarch, ensure it evenly covers each piece of meat.

3. **Prepare the aromatics**: Slice or shred the pickled ginger, pickled chili peppers, and wild mountain pepper. Cut the green and red chili peppers into rings. Peel the garlic and set aside.
4. **Quick fry in oil**: Heat the wok, add 200ml cooking oil, and heat to 60% oil temperature. Add the marinated bullfrog meat and quickly deep-fry for 10 seconds, then immediately remove.
> ⚠️ Do not exceed the time too much, or the meat will become tough and dry.

5. **Prepare the base oil**: Remove the bullfrog meat and drain the oil. Pour out the hot oil from the wok into a bowl, leaving 30ml of base oil. Add 20ml of lard. If lard is unavailable, keep a total of 50ml cooking oil in the wok.
6. **Fry the aromatics**: When the oil reaches 60% heat, add pickled ginger, pickled chili peppers, wild mountain pepper, and garlic. Fry until fragrant, then add 20g broad bean paste. Stir-fry over medium heat until red oil appears, controlling the time within 30 seconds. Pour in 400ml of beer.
7. **Simmer to infuse flavor**: Add the fried bullfrog meat and gently stir with a spatula. Add 5g white pepper powder and 5ml Sichuan pepper oil. Simmer over medium heat for 3 minutes.
8. **Reduce sauce and finish**: Increase the heat to high and reduce the sauce for half a minute. Add the green and red chili pepper rings, cook for another 10 seconds, then prepare to remove from the wok.
9. **Plate and garnish**: Transfer to a bowl and sprinkle with chopped green onion. Serve immediately.
### 💡 Key Tips
- It is recommended to choose small live bullfrogs for more tender meat.
- If you cannot handle spicy food, reduce pickled chili peppers by half to 2.5g for a mildly spicy taste.
- Wild mountain pepper can be replaced with dried red chili peppers.
- Cornstarch can be replaced with dry starch.
- Lard and Sichuan pepper oil are optional ingredients.
- When reducing the sauce, watch carefully to avoid burning.

## Health Advice
This dish features bullfrog meat, which is high in protein and low in fat. Paired with pickled chili peppers and beer, it promotes digestion and is suitable for those building muscle or craving a satisfying meal. However, its sodium content is relatively high, so people with high blood pressure should consume less. It is recommended to pair it with rice to balance the spiciness.
