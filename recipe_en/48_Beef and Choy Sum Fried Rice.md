# Beef and Choy Sum Fried Rice
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Rice 1 bowl
🥚 Eggs 2
🥩 Beef about 150g (minced)
🥬 Choy sum 1 bunch (about 150g, stem and leaves finely chopped)
🧄 Garlic 1 clove (minced)
🧂 Salt to taste
🦪 Oyster sauce to taste
🫒 Cooking oil as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick wok
🥄 Spatula
🔪 Cutting board and chef's knife
🥣 Bowl (for mixing rice)

## Steps
1. Crack the eggs directly into the white rice and stir thoroughly until each grain of rice is evenly coated with the egg mixture.
2. Mince the beef. Wash the choy sum and finely chop both stems and leaves. Mince the garlic.
3. Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add the minced beef and quickly break it apart, stir-frying until it changes color and is halfway cooked.
4. Add the egg-coated rice to the wok and stir-fry continuously until the rice is loose and each grain is distinct, with the egg fully set and coating the rice.
5. Add the chopped choy sum and continue stir-frying until it is just cooked.
6. Season with salt and oyster sauce to taste. Stir-fry until well combined, then transfer to a plate.
**Key point**: The fried rice must be stir-fried until loose and each grain is distinct; maintain medium-high heat throughout for a quick stir-fry.

## Health Tips
This fried rice uses an egg-coating method to increase protein content. Combined with beef and choy sum, it offers balanced nutrition and strong satiety, making it suitable for office workers needing an energy boost. Be mindful of the amount of oyster sauce and salt used to avoid excessive sodium intake.
