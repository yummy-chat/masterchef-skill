# Spicy Tossed Cucumber
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 Cucumber 1 (straight, seeds removed, cut into segments)  
🧂 Salt 3 tbsp (for pickling)  
🍬 Sugar 3 tbsp (for pickling)  
🥜 Peanuts, crushed, as needed  
🌿 Cilantro, as needed  
🧄 Garlic, minced, as needed (crush garlic and mince)  
🌶️ Small chili peppers 2 (sliced into rings)  
🥄 Light soy sauce 1 tbsp  
🍶 Vinegar 2 tbsp  
🍬 Sugar 1/2 tbsp (for sauce)  
🧂 Chicken powder 1 tbsp  
🦪 Oyster sauce 1 tsp  
🛢️ Sesame oil 2 tbsp  
🌫️ White pepper powder, a pinch  
🌶️ Dried chili peppers, as needed (for chili oil)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪚 Cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥣 Small stainless steel bowl  
🍳 Iron wok  
🥄 Wok spatula  
🥄 Strainer  
🍽️ Plate  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🧤 Disposable gloves

## Steps
1. Cut off the ends of the cucumber, split it lengthwise, use a knife to remove the central seeds, then cut into even segments.
   **Key Point**: Removing the seeds ensures the finished dish has a crunchy texture.
2. Place the cucumber segments in a bowl, add 3 tablespoons of salt and 3 tablespoons of sugar, mix well, and let sit for 15 minutes to draw out water and remove raw flavor.
3. Rinse the pickled cucumber segments with clean water to wash off the salt and sugar, then gently squeeze out the water with your hands. The cucumbers should now be soft but still firm.
4. Prepare the sauce: In a small stainless steel bowl, add minced garlic and sliced small chili peppers. Add 1 tablespoon light soy sauce, 2 tablespoons vinegar, 1/2 tablespoon sugar, 1 tablespoon chicken powder, 1 teaspoon oyster sauce, 2 tablespoons sesame oil, and a pinch of white pepper powder. Stir until well combined and set aside.
5. Make the chili oil: Pour an appropriate amount of oil into a wok and heat until it is very hot. Quickly add the dried chili pepper segments.
   **Key Point**: The oil temperature must be high, and the action must be fast to prevent the peppers from burning.
6. Immediately pour a spoonful of the hot chili oil over the prepared sauce. The sauce will bubble up, releasing a strong aroma of garlic and chili.
7. Pour all of the oil-infused sauce into the large bowl containing the cucumbers. Wearing disposable gloves, mix thoroughly so that every piece of cucumber is evenly coated with the sauce.
8. Plate the cucumbers, drizzle the remaining sauce from the bowl on top, and sprinkle with crushed peanuts and cilantro.

## Health Tips
Removing the seeds makes the cucumber crunchy and low-calorie, making it suitable for relieving greasiness during weight loss. However, the sesame oil and oyster sauce in the sauce are relatively high in calories, so consumption should be moderated.
