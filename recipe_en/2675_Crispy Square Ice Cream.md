# Crispy Square Ice Cream
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥛 Whipping cream 150g (chilled for over 3 hours in advance)  
🍬 White sugar 30g  
🥛 Whole milk 75g  
🍫 White chocolate 50g (for the crispy shell)  
🥥 Coconut oil 15g (for the crispy shell)  
🎨 Food coloring, as needed (optional for coloring)  
🍵 Matcha powder, as needed (optional for coloring)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Hand whisk  
👝 Piping bag  
🧊 Silicone ice cube tray  
🥃 Glass cup  
🩰 Toothpick  
🍽️ Plate (pre-chilled in the freezer)  
🫗 Bowl of hot water (for double boiler heating)  

## Instructions  
1. Place the whipping cream and white sugar together in a glass bowl. Whisk with a hand whisk until the mixture is smooth, thick, and shows visible lines.  
   **Key point**: The whipping cream must be chilled for over 3 hours in advance to whip easily; whip until it is smooth, thick, and shows lines—no need to whip until fully stiff.  
2. Add the whole milk to the whipped cream and stir until smooth and thick, forming a paste-like consistency.  
3. Transfer the ice cream mixture into a piping bag.  
4. Use the piping bag to pipe the ice cream mixture into the silicone ice cube tray. Fill each cavity completely, then gently tap the tray to level the surface and release air bubbles.  
5. Place the tray in the freezer and freeze for **at least 2 hours**, until completely firm.  
6. Prepare the crispy shell: Place the white chocolate pieces and coconut oil in a glass cup. Position the cup in a bowl of hot water to heat using a double boiler method, stirring continuously until fully melted and smooth with no lumps.  
   **Optional coloring**: After melting, add a small amount of food coloring or matcha powder and stir to combine.  
7. Remove the frozen ice cream cubes from the tray, unmold them, and place them on the pre-chilled plate.  
8. Pierce one ice cream cube with a toothpick, dip it into the chocolate mixture, and coat it evenly. Remove and let it sit for a moment until the shell sets.  

## Health Advice  
This ice cream uses whipping cream and milk as its base, with a moderate sugar content, making it suitable for a summer treat. However, be mindful of portion sizes to avoid excess calories. It should be consumed with caution by children and individuals with diabetes. Pairing it with fruit is recommended to supplement the vitamins often lacking in frozen desserts.
