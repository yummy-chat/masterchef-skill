# Fried Chicken
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥛 Milk 350ml  
🍋 Lemon 1 (juiced)  
🧅 Onion ½ (sliced into rings)  
🧄 Garlic 1 head (minced)  
🫚 Ginger 1 small slice (minced)  
🍗 Chicken about 1-1.5kg (drumettes or boneless thighs recommended)  
🥚 Eggs 2  
🌾 Flour 300g  
🌽 Cornstarch 80g  
🥄 Baking powder 1 tsp  
🧂 Salt 15g (for marinade) + 1 tsp (for coating)  
🧂 Black pepper 5g (for marinade) + 1 tsp (for coating)  
🌶️ Chili powder 5g (for marinade) + 1 tbsp (for coating)  
🌶️ Erjingtiao chili flakes 3 tbsp (for marinade)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🫙 Measuring cup  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🔪 Knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥄 Spoon or whisk  
🥘 Metal baking tray  
♨️ Oil pot  
🌡️ Thermometer (optional)  
🗑️ Draining rack  
🔗 Tongs  
📦 Plastic wrap  

## Steps  
1. Mix 350ml milk with lemon juice and let sit for 5 minutes until the milk curdles into buttermilk marinade.  
2. Add onion rings, minced garlic, minced ginger, 15g salt, 5g black pepper, 5g chili powder, and 3 tbsp Erjingtiao chili flakes to the curdled milk. Stir well.  
3. Crack in 2 eggs and mix again into a uniform marinade.  
4. Score the chicken pieces a few times for better flavor absorption, then coat them thoroughly in the marinade. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 8 hours, preferably overnight.  
5. Place 300g flour, 80g cornstarch, 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tsp baking powder into a baking tray. Stir well to make the dry coating mix.  
6. Remove the marinated chicken and coat evenly with the dry mix. You can apply just one layer, or for a thicker, flaky crust, shake off excess powder, dip back into the marinade, and coat again.  
7. Heat oil to 160°C. Fry the coated chicken for 4 minutes, turning occasionally for even cooking, until cooked through and the crust is set. Remove.  
8. Raise oil temperature to 180°C. Return the chicken for a second fry of 30 seconds to expel excess oil and make the crust extra crispy. Do not overcrowd the pot to avoid a sudden drop in oil temperature.  
9. Once golden and crispy, remove immediately and place on a draining rack to drain excess oil.  

## Health Advice  
It is recommended to limit consumption to no more than once a week, and pair with a fresh vegetable salad to balance the oil. This dish is high in protein but also high in calories. Moderate consumption after exercise can replenish energy, but those with poor digestion should eat with caution.
