# Dry Pot Chicken Wings and Prawns

Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken wings (moderate amount, scored)  
🦐 Prawns (moderate amount, deveined)  
🥔 Potatoes (moderate amount, cut into strips or chunks)  
🪷 Lotus root (moderate amount, sliced)  
🥦 Broccoli (moderate amount, cut into small florets)  
🌽 Corn (moderate amount, cut into sections)  
🥬 Celery (moderate amount, cut into sections)  
🧅 Onion (moderate amount, cut into chunks)  
🧄 Garlic (moderate amount, whole cloves)  
🧅 Scallions (moderate amount, sprinkle after cooking)  
🫘 Sesame seeds (moderate amount, sprinkle after cooking)  
🧂 Crispy coating powder (moderate amount)  
🫒 Cooking oil (moderate amount, spray)  
🍯 BBQ sauce (moderate amount)  
🌶️ Dry pot base (moderate amount)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
♨️ Air fryer  
🫗 Oil sprayer  
🥄 Spatula / spoon  

## Steps  
1. Score the chicken wings along the bone, butterfly the prawns and remove the vein; slice lotus root and potatoes into slices or strips, break broccoli into small florets, cut corn into sections, and cut celery and onion into chunks.  
2. Place the prepared chicken wings and prawns in a bowl, add dry pot base, mix well, and let marinate for a short while.  
3. Put the marinated chicken wings, prawns, and all vegetables into the air fryer basket.  
4. Evenly sprinkle crispy coating powder, and toss to lightly coat the ingredients.  
5. Spray a uniform layer of cooking oil over the ingredients using an oil sprayer.  
6. Set the air fryer to 180°C and bake for 15 minutes.  
   **Note**: At around 7–8 minutes, pull out the basket, flip the ingredients with a spatula, spray a little more oil, and continue baking for the remaining time until the meat is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.  
7. Once done, pull out the basket, add BBQ sauce, and mix thoroughly with a spatula.  
8. Push the basket back in, and bake at high temperature for another 3–5 minutes to thicken the sauce and create a caramelized surface on the ingredients.  
9. After removing from the air fryer, sprinkle with scallions and sesame seeds.  

## Health Advice  
This dry pot chicken wings and prawns dish uses an air fryer instead of deep frying, reducing oil intake while preserving protein and vegetable fiber, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts or those managing calorie intake. Be mindful to control the amount of BBQ sauce and dry pot base to avoid excessive sodium consumption.
