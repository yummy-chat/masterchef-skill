# Premium Monkey Head Mushroom 🍄  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍄 Dried monkey head mushroom 1 lb (washed, stems trimmed)  
🍗 Chicken bone broth as needed (for steaming to enhance aroma)  
🧂 Washing soda 5.3 oz (ratio for 1 lb mushrooms)  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🍾 Sichuan pepper wine to taste  
🧅 Scallions to taste  
🧄 Ginger to taste  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🥘 Boiling pot  
🍲 Steamer  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥣 Bowl (for soaking and rinsing)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Rinse the dried monkey head mushroom in clean water, add enough boiling water, and soak for 24 hours until fully softened.  
   **Key point**: Must use boiling water; insufficient soaking time will affect the final texture.  
2. Squeeze the soaked mushrooms dry, and trim off any tough base pieces.  
   **Key point**: Squeeze out moisture and remove the stems to ensure thorough alkali treatment.  
3. Add water to a pot, place the mushrooms and washing soda in it, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 40 minutes.  
   **Texture check**: The mushroom is ready when a chopstick can easily pierce through; if not, continue cooking.  
4. Immediately rinse the cooked mushrooms under running water, then soak for 12 hours, changing the water frequently until no alkali taste remains.  
   **Key point**: Rinse thoroughly to remove any alkaline residue and avoid a bitter aftertaste.  
5. Place the mushrooms in a steaming bowl, top with scallions and ginger, sprinkle with salt, drizzle with Sichuan pepper wine, and pour in the chicken bone broth. Steam over high heat for 3 hours.  
   **Key point**: Steaming infuses flavor and enhances the rich taste; aim for a tender, tofu-like texture.  

## Health Tips  
Although monkey head mushroom is vegetarian, after being prepared with chicken broth and using the traditional alkali method, its nutritional density rivals that of meat dishes. It is suitable for those who need to supplement protein but prefer less oil. Note that the washing soda must be completely rinsed away, otherwise, the bitterness will affect the taste.
