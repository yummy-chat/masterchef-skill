# Milk and Mung Bean Paste
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫘 Mung beans 150g (soaked for about 2 hours in advance)
🍚 Glutinous rice 10g
🥛 Milk 200g (or coconut milk)
💧 Water 1000g
🍬 Rock sugar 50g (adjust to taste)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥤 Blender (high-speed blender)
🫗 Glass measuring cup or pitcher
🫙 Glass jar (for refrigerated storage)

## Instructions
1. Add the soaked mung beans, glutinous rice, rock sugar, milk, and water into the blender.
2. Secure the lid tightly, select the "soy milk" mode, and start the machine to automatically blend and heat until boiling.
3. Once the program ends, pour the hot milk and mung bean paste into a glass measuring cup while still hot.
4. Allow it to cool naturally to room temperature, then transfer to the refrigerator for chilling.
   **Note**: It must be cooled naturally first before refrigerating to avoid affecting the texture or damaging the refrigerator.
5. Serve chilled directly after refrigeration. It can also be poured into popsicle molds to freeze into mung bean popsicles, or frozen directly to make mung bean slush.

## Health Tips
The combination of mung beans and milk provides high-quality protein and dietary fiber, making it suitable for cooling down and cutting greasiness in summer. The amount of rock sugar can be halved to control sugar intake; diabetic patients should consume with caution.
