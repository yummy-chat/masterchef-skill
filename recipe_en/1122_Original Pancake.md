# Original Pancake
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Cake flour 160g  
🧂 Baking powder 7g  
🍬 Sugar 30g  
🥚 Eggs 2  
🥛 Milk 120g (can be increased as needed to adjust batter consistency)  
🧈 Butter 15g (melted in advance)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥄 Whisk  
🍳 Frying pan  
🪵 Wooden spatula

## Preparation Steps
1. Place cake flour, baking powder, and sugar into a glass bowl. Mix thoroughly with a whisk until evenly combined.
2. Add eggs, milk, and melted butter. Stir until the batter is smooth, lump-free, and has good flowability.  
   **Note**: Do not overmix; adjust the amount of milk as needed based on the batter's consistency. A batter with good flowability results in moister pancakes.
3. Preheat a frying pan over medium-low heat. Ladle an appropriate amount of batter into the pan, allowing it to naturally spread into a round shape.  
   **Doneness check**: Flip the pancake when numerous small bubbles appear on the surface and the edges have set.
4. Gently flip the pancake with a wooden spatula. Continue cooking until the other side turns golden brown.  
   **Note**: Maintain medium-low heat throughout the cooking process to prevent burning.
5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until all batter is used.

## Health Suggestions
This pancake uses baking powder instead of yeast, making it quick to prepare and ideal for breakfast or afternoon tea. Milk and butter provide calcium and a small amount of fat, but the sugar and refined flour content is relatively high. It is recommended to serve with fruit or yogurt to increase dietary fiber and protein intake.
