# Beer Chicken
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken thighs 5 (chopped into pieces)  
🫚 Ginger to taste (sliced)  
🧅 Scallions to taste (cut into sections)  
🌶️ Dried chili peppers to taste  
🌶️ Dried Sichuan peppercorns to taste  
🍺 Beer 1 bottle (about 500ml)  
🧂 Light soy sauce to taste  
🧂 Ground white pepper to taste  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🧂 Oyster sauce to taste  
🧂 Pork rib sauce 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Sesame oil 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Dark soy sauce a little  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for marinating)  
🍲 Clay pot (with lid)  
🥄 Soup spoon  
♨️ Gas stove  

## Cooking Steps
1. Wash the chicken thighs, place them on a cutting board, and chop into evenly sized pieces, ensuring they are easy to flavor and cook through.
2. Put the chicken pieces into a stainless steel bowl, add light soy sauce, ground white pepper, salt, oyster sauce, and pork rib sauce. Mix thoroughly with your hands, and marinate for 10 minutes.
   **Note**: Make sure each piece of chicken is evenly coated with the seasonings for consistent flavor.
3. Layer the bottom of the clay pot with ginger slices and scallion sections, then sprinkle in the dried chili peppers and dried Sichuan peppercorns.
   **Key point**: The ginger and scallion base adds aroma and prevents the chicken from directly contacting the pot bottom and burning.
4. Arrange the marinated chicken pieces flat on top of the scallions and ginger, and drizzle with sesame oil.
5. Slowly pour the beer along the edge of the pot until the liquid surface completely covers the chicken, ensuring it is fully submerged in the broth during simmering.
6. Place the clay pot on the stove over heat. Bring the broth to a boil, then cover with the lid, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
7. Remove the lid, drizzle in a little dark soy sauce, and gently mix with a soup spoon to evenly color the chicken.
   **Note**: Dark soy sauce is used only for coloring. Do not add it too early, or the final dish may turn too dark or taste bitter.
8. Cover again and continue to simmer over the residual heat or low heat until the sauce thickens slightly, the chicken becomes tender, and the color turns a glossy reddish-brown. Turn off the heat and serve.

## Health Advice  
Chicken is rich in high-quality protein, and pairing it with ginger and scallions helps warm the body and dispel cold, making it suitable for most people. Most of the alcohol in the beer evaporates during cooking, but those with high uric acid should consume in moderation. Pork rib sauce contains sugar, so those managing blood sugar levels can reduce the amount as needed.
