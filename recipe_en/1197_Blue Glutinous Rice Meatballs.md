# Blue Glutinous Rice Meatballs
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦋 Butterfly pea flowers, as needed (soaked in water to create blue liquid, used for soaking the glutinous rice)  
🍚 Glutinous rice, as needed (pre-soaked in butterfly pea flower water until blue, then drained)  
🥩 Ground pork, as needed  
🧅 Scallion, as needed (finely chopped)  
🫚 Ginger, as needed (finely chopped)  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🍶 Cooking wine, as needed  
🥢 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🍬 White sugar, a pinch (optional)  
💧 Water, a small amount (added while mixing the meat filling)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍽️ Plate  
♨️ Steamer  

## Preparation Steps
1. Soak butterfly pea flowers in water until the liquid turns blue. Strain out the flower residue, then place the glutinous rice into the blue liquid. Soak thoroughly until evenly colored, then remove and drain. Set aside.  
   **Note**: The soaking time affects the depth of the color; adjust according to preference.  
2. Add the chopped scallions, ginger, salt, cooking wine, light soy sauce, a pinch of sugar, and a small amount of water to the ground pork. Using chopsticks, stir continuously in one direction until the filling becomes sticky and elastic.  
   **Key point**: Stirring until the meat is sticky and elastic is crucial for the meatballs' tender and bouncy texture. Be sure to stir continuously in the same direction.  
3. Lightly moisten your palms with water to prevent sticking. Take an appropriate amount of meat filling and roll it into evenly sized meatballs.  
4. Gently roll the meatballs in the blue glutinous rice so that the surface is evenly coated. Press lightly to shape and ensure the rice adheres firmly.  
5. Arrange the coated meatballs on a plate, leaving some space between them. Place the plate into the steamer.  
6. Turn the heat to high and bring the water in the steamer to a boil, creating steam. Cover and steam continuously until the glutinous rice becomes translucent and glossy, and the meatballs are fully cooked through. Remove from the steamer.  
   **Doneness check**: The rice grains should be transparent and soft, and the meatballs should have no pink juice seeping out from the inside.  

## Health Advice  
The glutinous rice coating becomes soft and easy to digest after steaming, making this dish suitable for the elderly and children. The meat filling must be stirred thoroughly until sticky and elastic to ensure a tender and bouncy texture. Butterfly pea flower provides natural coloring that is safe and harmless, but it should be used with caution by pregnant women.
