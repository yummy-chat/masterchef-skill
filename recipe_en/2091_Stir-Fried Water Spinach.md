# Stir-Fried Water Spinach
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥬 Water spinach, as needed (choose stems that snap easily and are water-rich, wash, trim, and cut into sections)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (minced into garlic paste)
🧂 Salt, as needed (used in two stages: for blanching sugar-salt water and for the sauce)
🍬 White sugar, a pinch (for blanching sugar-salt water to help lock in chlorophyll)
🧂 Chicken bouillon, as needed (for the sauce)
🥄 Light soy sauce, as needed (for the sauce)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥣 White bowl (for mixing sugar-salt water)
🥃 Glass bowl (for mixing the final sauce)
🥄 Slotted spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare two liquid mixtures in advance**: In a small half bowl of clean water, add salt and a pinch of white sugar, stir to dissolve into a sugar-salt water. In another bowl, combine salt, chicken bouillon, and light soy sauce, mix into a sauce and set aside.
2. **Blanch with oil and water together**: Heat a small amount of oil in a wok. Hold the water spinach in one hand and the sugar-salt water in the other, pour both into the wok simultaneously, and quickly blanch.
   **Key point**: Use the instant temperature difference and sugar-salt mixture to lock in chlorophyll and prevent the water spinach from turning yellow. Immediately remove with a slotted spoon after blanching, and drain thoroughly.
3. **Fragrant garlic**: Clean the wok, add a little oil again, then add the minced garlic. Sauté over medium-high heat until fragrant.
   **Key point**: The garlic must be sautéed first to fully release its aroma.
4. **Return to wok and stir-fry quickly**: Return the drained water spinach to the wok, quickly pour the prepared sauce along the edge of the wok, and stir-fry over high heat until evenly coated.
   **Note**: Maintain high heat throughout. The stir-frying and sauce pouring should be done quickly. As soon as the seasonings are evenly incorporated, remove from heat immediately to preserve a crisp texture and vibrant green color.
5. **Plate immediately**: Transfer the stir-fried water spinach to a serving dish right away to prevent residual heat from wilting the leaves and dulling the color. The finished dish should be bright green, crisp, refreshing, and without excess liquid.

## Health Tips
Water spinach is rich in dietary fiber and vitamins, making it ideal for cleansing the intestines and cutting through greasiness in summer. Adding sugar and salt during blanching helps preserve the color. Quick stir-frying over high heat is recommended to maintain a tender-crisp texture. Those with a weak or cold spleen and stomach should consume it in moderation.
