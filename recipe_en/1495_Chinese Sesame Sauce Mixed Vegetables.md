# Chinese Sesame Sauce Mixed Vegetables  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 Spinach - to taste (trimmed, roots removed, blanched)  
🥒 Celtuce (Wosun) - half (peeled and sliced, served raw)  
🥒 Cucumber - to taste (shredded)  
🥕 Carrot - to taste (peeled and shredded)  
🌿 Dragon Beard Vegetable - to taste (soaked and rinsed with drinking water)  
🍜 Wide Noodles (Crystal Cellophane Noodles) - to taste (soaked and blanched for 2 minutes)  
🍠 Taro Starch Noodles (white plastic track type) - to taste (soaked and rinsed with drinking water)  
🥥 Lotus Root Slices (large white lotus root slices) - to taste (soaked until soft)  

🧄 Garlic - to taste (mashed into paste)  
🥜 Sesame Paste - 2 tablespoons  
🥜 Peanut Butter - 1 tablespoon  
💧 Water (or sesame oil) - about 9 tablespoons (added in batches)  
🍶 Light Soy Sauce - 3 tablespoons  
🍶 Vinegar - 2 tablespoons  
🌶 Sichuan Pepper Oil - 1 tablespoon  
🌶 Chili Oil (Làzi Yóu) - 2 tablespoons  
🧂 Chicken Bouillon - 1 pinch  
🍬 White Sugar - 2 pinches  
🧂 Salt - to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🫗 Large Stainless Steel Bowl  
🥒 Grater (Shredder)  
🔪 Peeler  
🧄 Garlic Masher (small mortar and pestle)  
🍲 Pot (for blanching)  
🥄 Spoon (for measuring seasonings)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Prepare the vegetables: Trim and wash the spinach, removing roots. Peel the celtuce and slice thinly. Shred the cucumber and carrot separately. Soak the dragon beard vegetable, taro starch noodles, and lotus root slices in water beforehand, then rinse with cold boiled water. Blanch the wide noodles in boiling water for 2 minutes and drain. Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 1 minute and drain.  
2. Mash the garlic: Place garlic cloves in the garlic masher, add a pinch of salt, and mash until it becomes a thick paste.  
3. Prepare the sesame sauce: In a bowl, combine sesame paste and peanut butter. Add 3 tablespoons of water first, stir vigorously in one direction until it expands and turns white. Gradually add the remaining 5–6 tablespoons of water, stirring until it reaches a pourable yogurt-like consistency.  
4. Mix everything together: Place the prepared spinach, celtuce slices, cucumber shreds, carrot shreds, dragon beard vegetable, wide noodles, taro starch noodles, and lotus root slices into a large bowl. Add the garlic paste, sesame sauce, light soy sauce, vinegar, Sichuan pepper oil, chili oil, chicken bouillon, white sugar, and salt. Mix thoroughly until every ingredient is evenly coated with the sauce.  
5. Serve and enjoy: Transfer to a plate and serve immediately. It pairs especially well with steamed buns (mantou).  

## Health Tips  
This is a colorful, high-fiber cold dish. Blanching the spinach removes oxalic acid, improving calcium absorption. Sesame paste and peanut butter provide healthy fats, making it suitable for weight loss diets or those needing extra dietary fiber. Note that taro starch noodles and lotus root slices are relatively high in calories, so those trying to lose weight should control their portions.
