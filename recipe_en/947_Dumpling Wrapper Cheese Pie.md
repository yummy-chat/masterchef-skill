# Dumpling Wrapper Cheese Pie
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥟 Dumpling wrappers, as needed  
🍠 Purple sweet potato, as needed (peeled and sliced)  
🎃 Pumpkin, as needed (peeled and sliced)  
🧀 Mozzarella cheese shreds, as needed  
🥛 Milk, a small amount  
🍬 White sugar, as needed  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🔪 Pressure cooker  
🥣 Bowls  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Spoon  
🫓 Rolling pin

## Preparation Steps
1. Peel and slice the purple sweet potato and pumpkin, then steam them in a pressure cooker until fully cooked.
2. Remove and place them into separate bowls. While still hot, add an appropriate amount of white sugar and a little milk, then mash into a fine puree.  
   **Key point**: The finer the puree, the better the texture; adjust the sugar amount according to sweetness and taste.
3. Add a sufficient amount of mozzarella cheese shreds to the puree, mix well, divide into several equal portions, and roll into ball-shaped fillings.
4. Take one dumpling wrapper, use a rolling pin to roll it slightly thinner, place one filling ball in the center, wrap and seal it like a dumpling, then gently press it flat into a round disc with your palm.  
   **Note**: Apply even pressure when flattening to avoid squeezing out the filling.
5. Brush a thin layer of oil on the non-stick pan, place the pie discs in the pan, and cook over medium-low heat. Once one side turns golden, flip and cook until both sides are golden and the pie puffs up.  
   **Note**: Do not use too high heat to prevent the outside from burning while the inside remains undercooked.

## Health Advice
This dumpling wrapper cheese pie is rich in carbohydrates and high-quality protein. Purple sweet potato and pumpkin provide dietary fiber and vitamins, making it suitable as a breakfast or snack. Pay attention to controlling the intake of sugar and oil; individuals with blood sugar sensitivity can reduce the white sugar. The calcium in mozzarella cheese is beneficial for bones, but it is not low in calories, so it should not be consumed in excess at one time.
