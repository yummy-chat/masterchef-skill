# Cola Fried Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 Rice: to taste  
🥚 Eggs: 3  
🌭 Ham sausage: to taste  
🌿 Scallions: to taste  
🧂 Light soy sauce: to taste  
🧂 Dark soy sauce: to taste  
🧂 Oyster sauce: to taste  
🌶️ Doubanjiang (chili bean paste): to taste  
🥤 Cola: to taste  
🧂 Black pepper: to taste  
🫒 Oil: to taste  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Spatula  
✂️ Scissors  

## Steps  
1. Heat the wok over high heat, add oil, crack in two eggs, and fry until both sides are fully set. Turn off the heat and remove.  
   **Key point**: Avoid high heat when frying the eggs to prevent burning.  

2. Cut the fried eggs into small pieces and return them to the wok.  
3. Turn the heat back on, add cola, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, doubanjiang, and oyster sauce. Stir to combine.  
   **Note**: If using sugar-free cola, add 5 ml more dark soy sauce. If not using doubanjiang, also add 5 ml more dark soy sauce.  

4. Heat until the liquid in the wok reduces to about one-third, then add the rice and ham sausage. Stir-fry evenly.  
   **Key point**: If you prefer a drier texture, extend the stir-frying time as needed.  

5. Reduce to low heat, make a well in the center of the rice, crack in one egg, cover the wok with a lid, and simmer for 2 minutes.  

6. Remove the lid, stir-fry until the newly added egg is fully cooked. Sprinkle with scallions and black pepper, then transfer to a plate.  
   **Simplified alternative**: You can crack all three eggs into the wok and scramble them in step 1, skipping the step of making a well and adding an egg.  

## Health Advice  
Cola fried rice offers a sweet and savory flavor, with the caramel taste of cola balancing the greasiness of the fried rice. However, be mindful of the amount used to avoid excessive sweetness. Suitable for those looking to try something new. Those with high blood sugar or on a weight loss diet should consume with caution, as the combination of cola and carbohydrates results in higher calories.
