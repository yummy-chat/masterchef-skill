# Peach Slice Cake
Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Rice 1 portion
🍚 Glutinous rice 2 portions
🥜 Walnuts appropriate amount (blanch in water to remove skin and astringency)
🖤 Black sesame seeds appropriate amount
🍬 White granulated sugar 190g
🍯 Honey 20g
💧 Water 35g (for making syrup)
🫒 Oil with mild flavor 25g (such as corn oil or sunflower oil)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel basin
🥄 Measuring cup (or cut a plastic bottle)
🥢 Chopsticks
🍳 Wok (oil-free)
🌀 Grinder/blender
🧴 Coarse/fine sieve
📦 Square stainless steel lunch box (mold)
♨️ Steamer + pot
🧻 Damp cheesecloth
🔪 Dough cutter/kitchen knife
🪵 Cutting board

## Preparation Steps
1.  Make syrup: Pour white granulated sugar, honey, water, and oil into a basin, stir well into a uniform syrup, and let sit for more than 3 hours.
2.  Make cooked rice flour: Mix rice and glutinous rice in a 1:2 ratio, wash and drain. Use an oil-free wok to stir-fry over medium-low heat until the grains turn slightly yellow and crispy.
    **Note**: The grains must be fried to a crispy texture that can be eaten directly.

3.  Grind the cooked rice into fine powder using a grinder, then sift through a sieve to ensure a fine texture.
4.  Blanch the walnuts once in water, rinse thoroughly to remove astringency, and drain for later use.
5.  Mix rice flour and syrup: Pour approximately 280g of cooked rice flour into a basin, add the rested syrup, and rub and mix by hand.
    **Key point**: The mixture should reach the consistency of "moist kinetic sand" — it can be pressed into a ball but easily crumbles when lightly pinched. If too dry, adjust by adding a small amount of syrup or oil.

6.  Prepare walnut filling: Take an appropriate amount of walnut kernels, coat them lightly in the remaining syrup bowl, then sprinkle with a little rice flour and mix well.
7.  Line the bottom of the mold with parchment paper. Spread a layer of the mixed rice flour and press firmly with a flat tool.
    **Note**: It must be pressed very firmly, otherwise it will easily crumble when slicing.

8.  Add a layer of walnut filling in the middle, sprinkle with black sesame seeds, then add the remaining rice flour and press firmly again.
9.  First steaming: Place the mold in a steamer, cover the surface with a damp cheesecloth. Steam over medium-high heat after the water boils for about 5 minutes to allow the sugar inside to slightly melt and set.
10. Second steaming after unmolding: Remove the set rice cake from the mold and place it on a steaming cloth. Steam over medium-low heat for another 5 minutes to thoroughly cook.
    **Note**: It is best to use a bamboo steamer lid to prevent dripping water from falling onto the cake.

11. Follow-up treatment: While still hot, sift a layer of cooked rice flour onto the surface of the rice cake to keep it moist. Let it cool completely, then slice with a knife dusted with cooked flour.

## Health Suggestions
This peach slice cake is relatively high in carbohydrates and can raise blood sugar quickly. It is recommended to consume 1–2 slices per serving, paired with plain tea. The nuts and black sesame seeds provide quality fats, making it suitable for an afternoon energy boost. However, diabetic patients should strictly control their intake.
