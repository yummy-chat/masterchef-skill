# Cold Mixed Lettuce Shreds
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥬 1 lettuce (peeled)  
🥕 1 small piece of carrot (shredded for color)  
🧄 Appropriate amount of garlic cloves (mashed into garlic paste)  
🧂 Appropriate amount of salt (for pickling and seasoning)  
🍬 A pinch of sugar  
🧂 A pinch of chicken bouillon  
🍶 Appropriate amount of white vinegar  
🍾 Appropriate amount of light soy sauce  
🫒 A dash of sesame oil  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel basin  
🫙 Glass bowl  
🧺 Strainer  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Cut the lettuce into uniform thin shreds, and shred the carrot finely for later use.  
   **Key point**: The lettuce shreds should be as thin as possible for a crisper texture and better flavor absorption.  
2. Place the lettuce and carrot shreds in a basin, mix with salt, and let sit for 5 minutes until water releases.  
   **Key point**: Pickling removes the raw taste of lettuce, resulting in a cleaner flavor.  
3. Discard the released water, add cold boiled water to soak for about 2 minutes, then thoroughly drain using a strainer.  
   **Key point**: Soaking allows the dehydrated lettuce to reabsorb water, restoring a crisp texture.  
4. Prepare the garlic water dressing: In a bowl, add garlic paste, then sequentially add salt, sugar, chicken bouillon, white vinegar, light soy sauce, and sesame oil. Stir until the sugar and salt are fully dissolved.  
5. Pour the dressing over the drained lettuce shreds, mix thoroughly with chopsticks, and plate for serving.  

## Health Tips  
This cold mixed lettuce shreds dish is crisp and low in calories, rich in dietary fiber and vitamins, making it suitable for those on a weight loss diet or needing a light and refreshing meal. Soaking in cold boiled water after pickling restores a crunchy texture and reduces saltiness.
