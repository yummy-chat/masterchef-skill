# Old Beijing Stir-Fried Three Dices
Cuisine: Beijing Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potato (peeled, cut into 1 cm cubes)
🥕 Carrot (peeled, cut into cubes similar in size to the potato)
🥒 Cucumber (seeded and cored, then cut into small cubes)
🧄 Scallion, Ginger, Garlic (minced, used as the "Three Aromatics")
🫘 Soybean Paste (20 grams)
🥫 Soy Sauce (20 ml)
🍶 Sichuan Pepper Wine (10 ml, can substitute with cooking wine)
🍬 Sugar (5 grams, to neutralize bitterness, adjust to taste)
💧 Water (50 ml)
🫒 Sesame Oil (适量, for mixing with cucumber cubes)
🥜 Peanut Oil (适量, for stir-frying)
⭐ Star Anise (1 piece, for frying to enhance aroma)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🔪 Chef's Knife
🪓 Cutting Board
🥄 Spoon
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks

## Cooking Steps
1.  Immediately after cutting the potato into cubes, soak them in clean water to wash away surface starch and prevent oxidation.
2.  Cut the carrot into cubes and set aside directly on a plate.
3.  Split the cucumber lengthwise, remove the seeds and core, then cut into small cubes; mix the cucumber cubes thoroughly with an appropriate amount of sesame oil to lock in moisture and enhance aroma.
4.  Prepare the sauce mixture: In a bowl, combine soybean paste, soy sauce, Sichuan pepper wine, and sugar. Stir until well mixed, then add water and stir again to combine. Set aside.
   **Key Point**: Prepare the sauce mixture in advance to avoid scrambling during cooking and ensure even seasoning.
5.  Heat oil in the wok, add the star anise, and fry over low heat until it changes color and releases aroma. Then, add the drained potato cubes and stir-fry over medium heat until the surface is golden brown.
   **Key Point**: The potato cubes must be stir-fried until the surface is golden brown. This helps them set and easily absorb the sauce.
6.  Add the minced scallion, ginger, and garlic, and stir-fry until fragrant. Then, add the carrot cubes and stir-fry over medium heat for about 1 minute.
   **Key Point**: Carrots contain fat-soluble vitamins and need to be stir-fried with oil for better nutrient absorption.
7.  Pour in the prepared sauce mixture, stir everything together, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the wok, and simmer for about 5 minutes to allow the potato and carrot to fully absorb the flavors and cook through.
8.  Uncover the wok. The sauce should be noticeably reduced by now. Add the prepared cucumber cubes, turn the heat to high, and stir-fry quickly for about 1 minute, until the cucumber is just cooked but still crisp. Serve immediately.
   **Key Point**: The cucumber must be added last and stir-fried quickly over high heat to retain its crisp texture and prevent it from releasing water and becoming soft.

## Health Advice
This stir-fry combines three different vegetable cubes, offering a rich source of vegetable fiber and vitamins. Potato provides carbohydrates, and carrots need to be cooked in oil to release their nutrients. It is suitable for those who need to control oil intake but still crave a flavorful dish. Note that soybean paste is high in salt, so use less soy sauce accordingly.
