# Boiled Assorted Medley 🥟

Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧊 1 box frozen assorted ingredients (beef balls, fish balls, dumplings, wontons, sliced sausage, fried tofu puffs, etc., no need to thaw)
🥄 2 tablespoons starch
💬 Water, as needed
🫒 A small amount of cooking oil

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🫗 Glass lid

## Steps
1. Take the frozen assorted ingredients out of the freezer and pour them directly into the non-stick frying pan. No need to thaw.
2. Evenly sprinkle in 2 tablespoons of starch. Add enough water to reach just the bottom depth of the ingredients. Stir gently to create a starchy water mixture.
3. Drizzle a small amount of cooking oil over the surface of the starchy water.
4. Cover with the lid and cook over medium heat until all the water in the pan has completely evaporated.
   **Key Indicator**: When a golden, crispy edge appears around the pan, a thin, crispy crust has formed on the bottom.
5. Turn off the heat and bring the entire pan to the table to serve.

## Health Tips
This dish is made in one pot and offers a relatively balanced nutrition. The frozen medley provides protein and a small amount of carbohydrates, but the starchy water adds extra calories. People with high blood pressure should use less sliced sausage.
