# Shredded Potatoes with Meat Sauce
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Ground pork belly 100g (with some fat)
🥔 Potatoes 600g (about the size of two palms)
🌶️ Thin-skinned peppers 50g (about three, remove seeds if you prefer less heat)
🧅 Scallion 30g (about a palm-length piece)
🧂 MSG 3g (one teaspoon)
🌶️ Chili flakes 10g (one tablespoon, for aroma)
✨ Thirteen-spice 0.5g (a pinch, optional)
🫒 Cooking oil as needed (normal amount for stir-frying)
🍶 Cooking wine 5g (half a tablespoon)
🥢 Light soy sauce 10g (one tablespoon)
🫘 Fermented soybean paste 60g (two full tablespoons)

## Utensils and Equipment
🔪 Peeler
🧇 Grater
🪚 Cutting board, knife
🍳 Wok, spatula
🥣 Metal bowl
♨️ Steamer, steaming rack
🥄 Spoon

## Instructions
1. **Prepare the meat**: Choose ground pork belly with some fat; lean meat alone will be tough and dry, so some fat should be retained.
2. **Handle the potatoes**: Peel the potatoes, then grate them into thin shreds using a grater. Loosen and spread them directly in a metal bowl. **Do not rinse with water**.
3. **Prepare the aromatics**: Remove the stems and seeds from the thin-skinned peppers and cut them into small dice. Finely chop the scallion into scallion slices.
4. **Layer the seasonings**: Spread the diced peppers and scallion slices evenly over the shredded potatoes. Sprinkle the MSG, chili flakes, and thirteen-spice on top.
5. **Cook the meat sauce**:
   - Add the cooking oil to a wok and heat over high heat until slightly warm. Reduce the heat to **medium-low**, add the ground meat, and quickly break it apart while stir-frying.
   - Stir-fry until the meat changes color, turns golden, and shrinks slightly. Sizzle the cooking wine and stir-fry to remove any gamey smell.
   - Turn the heat to **the lowest setting**, add the light soy sauce and fermented soybean paste, and stir-fry until the sauce is fragrant and the meat and paste are well combined.
     **Note**: Be sure to use the lowest heat to prevent the sauce from burning.
6. **Combine**: Turn off the heat and pour the cooked meat sauce evenly over the seasoned shredded potatoes.
7. **Steam**:
   - Fill the steamer with water, place the steaming rack inside, and set the metal bowl on the rack.
   - Turn the heat to high. Once the water boils and steam appears, start timing. **Steam over high heat for 17 minutes**.
     **Key point**: Start timing from when the steam appears to ensure accurate cooking time.
8. **Remove and mix**: Immediately turn off the heat and take out the bowl. Use a spoon to thoroughly mix the shredded potatoes, meat sauce, and seasonings. Serve and enjoy.

## Health Tips
This dish relies on ground pork belly and fermented soybean paste for flavor. The potatoes are not rinsed to retain starch, giving a soft, sticky texture that is filling and suitable for those needing energy replenishment. Note that the shredded potatoes should not be washed; be sure to mix the seasonings well before steaming to prevent dryness. Some chili seeds can be left for added aroma, but remove them all if you prefer less heat.
