# Beef Clear Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Beef shank approx. 710g (cut into large pieces)
🥩 Beef tenderloin approx. 200g (cut into thick slices)
🌱 Mung bean sprouts 1 small bowl
🌿 Chopped celery to taste
🫚 Ginger slices 3-4 slices
🧅 Scallion segments 2-3 sections
🌿 Cinnamon stick 1 small piece
⭐ Star anise 1 piece
🧂 Cumin a pinch
🧄 Fried garlic crisp 1 tablespoon
🍯 Galangal powder 1 tablespoon

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥘 Stainless steel soup pot
🥄 Strainer
🥢 Tongs
🔪 Cutting board
🥣 Soup ladle
🍚 Bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare the beef**: Soak the beef shank and beef tenderloin in cold water in advance to remove blood.
2. **Start with cold water**: Place the soaked beef together with ginger slices, scallion segments, cinnamon stick, star anise, and cumin into the stainless steel soup pot, and add enough cold water.
   **Key point**: Adding the spices along with the beef into cold water allows for fuller flavor release.
3. **Cook the broth**: Bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, then reduce to low heat and simmer for about 1 hour, until the beef can be easily pierced through with tongs.
4. **Braise for flavor**: After turning off the heat, cover the pot tightly. You may place a damp towel or a heavy object on the lid to seal it. Let it sit and braise for 1-2 hours to make the beef even more tender.
5. **Chill and slice**: Remove the braised beef, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap, and refrigerate until set. Once fully cooled, slice into thin pieces.
   **Note**: Slicing after chilling results in firmer meat that is less likely to fall apart.
6. **Prepare the bowl base**: In a bowl, place chopped celery, fried garlic crisp, and galangal powder.
7. **Blanch and arrange toppings**: Use the boiling hot beef broth to quickly blanch the mung bean sprouts, then transfer them into the bowl. Next, add the sliced beef.
8. **Pour the broth to finish**: Pour the piping hot beef broth over the ingredients, instantly releasing the aroma from the bowl base, and serve.

## Health Advice
Beef clear soup is rich in high-quality protein and iron, making it suitable for those who need to replenish energy or are in recovery. However, the soup has a high purine content, so gout patients should consume it in moderation. The beef, through cold soaking and slow cooking, becomes tender and easy to digest; the texture is even better when sliced after chilling.
