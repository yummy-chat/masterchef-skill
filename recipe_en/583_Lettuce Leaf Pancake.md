# Lettuce Leaf Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: 🌱 Spring (January-April)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥬 Lettuce leaves
🥚 Eggs
🌾 Starch
🫒 Cooking oil
🥄 Light soy sauce
🧂 Chicken bouillon

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Knife
🥣 Bowl
🍳 Pan
🥄 Spatula

## Steps
1. Wash and finely chop the lettuce leaves, place in a bowl, add eggs, starch, light soy sauce, and chicken bouillon, and mix thoroughly into a batter.
   **Key point**: Blanching lettuce leaves directly for cold dishes can easily result in bitterness; frying avoids this issue while retaining the fresh aroma.

2. Heat a flat-bottomed pan, pour in an appropriate amount of cooking oil, pour the prepared batter into the pan, and use a spatula to evenly spread it into a round pancake shape, frying over medium-low heat.
   **Note**: Use a small amount of oil, and spread the batter to an even thickness so that it heats uniformly.

3. Fry the first side for about 120 seconds until the bottom is golden and set, carefully flip with a spatula, continue frying for about 60 seconds until the other side is golden, then remove from the pan and plate.
   **Key point**: Flip gently to keep the pancake shape intact; adjust frying time based on heat and pancake thickness, ensuring both sides are golden and cooked through.

## Health Advice
This lettuce leaf pancake turns often-discarded leaves into the star ingredient, rich in vitamins and dietary fiber. Paired with eggs for high-quality protein, it is ideal for cleansing the intestines and reducing fat in spring. Be mindful to use less oil and control salt when frying to avoid excess calories.
