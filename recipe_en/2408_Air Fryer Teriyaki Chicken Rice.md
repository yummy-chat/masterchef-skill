# Air Fryer Teriyaki Chicken Rice  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥬 Ingredients  
- Chicken 900g  
- Ritz crackers (savory cookies can be substituted) 16 pieces (48g)  
- Eggs 2  

🧂 Seasonings  
- Soy sauce 100-125ml  
- Sugar 60-65g  
- White vinegar 30-35ml  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Kitchen Tools  
- Air fryer  
- Foil tray  
- Mixing bowl  

## Cooking Instructions  
1. Mix soy sauce, sugar, and white vinegar together, stirring until the sugar is completely dissolved, to make the teriyaki sauce. Set aside.  
2. In another bowl, combine the chicken, eggs, half of the teriyaki sauce, and crushed Ritz crackers. Mix thoroughly until well combined.  
3. Line the bottom of the air fryer with a foil tray, place the meat mixture into it, and evenly drizzle the remaining teriyaki sauce over the top.  
4. Set the air fryer temperature to 350°F and bake for 40 minutes.  
5. Once the surface is golden and crispy, remove it from the air fryer. Cut into pieces and plate. Serve with rice.  

## Health Advice  
This teriyaki chicken rice dish is high in sugar, so it's recommended to pair it with plenty of vegetables to help balance blood sugar levels. The chicken is rich in protein, but the Ritz crackers add hidden carbs and sodium, so portion control is important during weight loss. You can reduce the sugar in the sauce or use a sugar substitute.
