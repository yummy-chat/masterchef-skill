# Sichuan Dan Dan Noodles
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients
🥩 Ground pork
🥬 Pickled mustard greens (rinse to remove salt before cooking)
🍜 Thin noodles
🥜 Crushed peanuts
🧅 Chopped green onions

## Seasonings
🧂 Soy sauce (used once for stir-frying meat topping and once for the bowl sauce)
🍶 Vinegar
🌶️ Sichuan pepper powder
🍬 White sugar
🧂 Chicken bouillon
🥜 Sesame paste (about 2 tablespoons, most important)
🐷 Lard
🌶️ Chili oil
🚿 Hot water (enough to make the noodle soup base)
🫒 Cooking oil (for stir-frying meat topping and pickled mustard greens)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🥘 Large pot (for boiling noodles)
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Soup spoon
🪵 Wooden spatula
🥢 Chopsticks

## Instructions
1. Rinse the pickled mustard greens with clean water to remove excess salt, then drain.
2. Prepare the bowl sauce: In the bowl, add the stir-fried pickled mustard greens, soy sauce, vinegar, Sichuan pepper powder, white sugar, chicken bouillon, sesame paste, lard, and chili oil.
   - Key point: Sesame paste is considered the most important seasoning.
3. Stir-fry the meat topping: Add oil to the frying pan. When the oil is at 30% heat, add the ground pork. Stir-fry until it changes color, then add soy sauce. Continue to stir-fry until the meat topping is fully aromatic.
   - Doneness check: The meat topping emits a fragrant aroma.
4. Stir-fry the pickled mustard greens: Add the rinsed pickled mustard greens to the pan used for the meat topping. Stir-fry until the moisture is evaporated.
   - Doneness check: The texture is better when the pickled mustard greens are dry.
5. Prepare the noodle soup: Pour an appropriate amount of hot water into the bowl with the sauce and stir well.
6. Cook the noodles: Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add the noodles. Cook until a gently squeezed noodle shows no hard core in the middle, then immediately turn off the heat and remove the noodles.
   - Key point: Avoid overcooking.
7. Assemble: Place the cooked noodles into the bowl with the sauce. In order, sprinkle on the crushed peanuts, stir-fried meat topping, and chopped green onions.

## Health Suggestions
Sesame paste is the soul of Dan Dan Noodles but is high in calories. It is recommended to have this as a main meal rather than with rice. Suitable for office workers who enjoy spicy food and meat, but be mindful of the amounts of ground pork and lard used to avoid excessive fat intake.
