# Qiqihar Barbecue
Cuisine: Northeastern Chinese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef (beef loin or shoulder blade) appropriate amount  
🧅 Purple onion appropriate amount  
🌿 Cilantro appropriate amount  
🧂 Chicken bouillon 1 spoon  
🧂 Salt a pinch  
🍬 White sugar 1 spoon  
🌶️ White pepper powder a pinch  
🌿 Sichuan peppercorn powder 2 spoons  
🌱 Cumin powder 2 spoons  
🌶️ Chili powder 2 spoons  
⚪️ Sesame seeds appropriate amount  
🥣 Light soy sauce 1 spoon  
🦪 Oyster sauce a pinch  
🫒 Sesame oil appropriate amount  
🫒 Soybean oil about 1 bowl  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Large glass bowl  
🍳 Metal baking pan  
🔥 Portable gas stove  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🔗 Food tongs  
🧤 Disposable gloves  

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the beef against the grain into thin slices, slice the onion into strips, and cut the cilantro into sections.
2. Place the beef slices, onion strips, and cilantro sections into a large bowl. Add chicken bouillon, salt, white sugar, white pepper powder, Sichuan peppercorn powder, cumin powder, chili powder, sesame seeds, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Mix thoroughly by hand.
   **Note**: **Do not add cornstarch or eggs**, otherwise the meat will easily burn during grilling.
3. After mixing well, pour in about 1 bowl of soybean oil and mix again to allow the oil to seal in the moisture.
4. Heat the baking pan, spread the marinated meat slices flat on the pan, grill until one side is golden and fragrant, then flip and continue grilling until fully cooked.
   **Note**: Adjust grilling time according to meat slice thickness and heat; use tongs to assist with flipping.
5. The grilled meat can be eaten directly, or wrapped in lettuce leaves with garlic slices.

## Health Tips  
High-heat quick grilling locks in the tenderness of the beef, while the sweetness of onion and cilantro cuts the richness, making it ideal for gatherings with friends and family. Be mindful of the amount of soybean oil used to avoid excess calories; those with diabetes or high blood pressure should reduce salt and sugar.
