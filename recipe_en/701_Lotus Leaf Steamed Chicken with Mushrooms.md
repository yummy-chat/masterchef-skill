# Lotus Leaf Steamed Chicken with Mushrooms
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Half a chicken (cut into pieces about 3 cm square)  
🍄 5-6 shiitake (or dried shiitake) mushrooms (sliced)  
🫚 A few slices of ginger  
🧅 Some scallions (chopped, for final garnish)  
🍬 Sugar, to taste  
🦪 Oyster sauce, to taste  
🐔 Chicken bouillon (Lee Kum Kee concentrated chicken bouillon), about half a tablespoon  
🌾 Cornstarch, to taste  
🍶 Rice wine, to taste  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🧻 Kitchen paper towels  
🥣 Large glass bowl  
🥄 Spoon  
🍃 Bamboo steamer  
🍳 Wok (used as the base pot for steaming)  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the chicken into pieces about 3 cm square. Pat dry the surface moisture with kitchen paper towels and place in a large bowl.
**Note**: Drying the moisture helps the chicken absorb the marinade better, resulting in a smoother texture after steaming.

2. Add the sliced mushrooms, ginger slices, sugar, oyster sauce, and chicken bouillon. Mix and toss thoroughly until well combined.
**Key**: This recipe does not use salt; all the savory flavor comes from the chicken bouillon.

3. Add cornstarch and mix again until the cornstarch evenly coats the chicken, sealing in the moisture.
4. Bring water to a boil in the steamer. Just before steaming, drizzle rice wine over the marinated chicken and quickly mix well.
**Key**: The rice wine must be added only right before steaming; do not let it sit for more than 5 minutes after adding, otherwise it will affect the texture of the chicken.

5. Line the bottom of the steamer with a lotus leaf. Spread the mixed chicken and mushrooms evenly over the lotus leaf, avoiding thick piles. Optional: Cover the chicken with another small lotus leaf to concentrate the aroma.
6. Once the steamer water is boiling, place the steamer inside, cover, and steam over medium heat for 8 minutes.
7. Steam until the chicken is fully cooked through and appears glossy on the surface. Remove and garnish with chopped scallions before serving.

## Health Advice  
This lotus leaf steamed chicken with mushrooms is low in salt and rich in juice, making it suitable for people managing blood pressure. The steaming method is low in oil and healthy, while the chicken is tender and easy to digest.
