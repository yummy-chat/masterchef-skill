# Huangjihuang Three-Sauce Braised Pot
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken wings (drumettes) 10 pieces  
🦐 Fresh shrimp 15 pieces  
🥔 Potatoes适当 amount (cut into rolling knife chunks)  
🥕 Carrots appropriate amount (cut into rolling knife chunks)  
🧅 Onion half (sliced)  
🫑 Green bell pepper 1 (cut into pieces)  
🌶️ Red bell pepper 1 (cut into pieces)  
🥬 Celery appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🍠 Yellow sweet potato appropriate amount (cut into rolling knife chunks)  
🫚 Ginger a few slices  
🧄 Garlic a few cloves  
🧈 Butter 1 piece  
🌿 Cilantro appropriate amount  

**Sauce Seasoning**  
🧂 Sweet bean paste 170g  
🍅 Ketchup 120g  
🧂 Soy sauce 200g  
🦪 Oyster sauce 50g  
🧂 Salt 2 tablespoons  
🧂 Chicken bouillon 1 tablespoon  
🧂 MSG 1 tablespoon  
🍶 Cooking wine 100ml  
🍬 Rock sugar 20g  
🍯 Honey a little  
🧂 Fragrant vinegar 20g  
🧂 White pepper powder 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Spicy and numbing seasoning 2 tablespoons  
🥔 Potato starch 100g (mix with 70ml water to dissolve)  
💧 Water 1 bowl (for cooking the sauce) + 70ml (for mixing starch)  
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount (to seal the sauce)  

**Marinating and Vegetable Seasoning**  
🌶️ Chili sauce 1 tablespoon  
🧂 White sesame seeds a little  
🧂 Chili powder appropriate amount (for mixing vegetables)  
🧂 Chicken bouillon a little (for mixing vegetables)  

**Sprinkle Toppings**  
🧂 White pepper powder appropriate amount  
🧂 Cumin powder appropriate amount  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🥣 Glass bowls (multiple)  
🥄 Wooden spoon or long-handled spoon  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Spoon  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🫗 Pot lid  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Prepare the secret sauce: Place sweet bean paste, ketchup, soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, chicken bouillon, MSG, cooking wine, rock sugar, honey, fragrant vinegar, white pepper powder, and spicy and numbing seasoning into a bowl and stir evenly. Pour the mixed sauce into a pot, add one bowl of water, stir, and boil over high heat for 5-6 minutes. Meanwhile, mix the potato starch with 70ml of water until smooth and stringy, then strain it through a slotted spoon.
2. When the sauce is boiling, quickly pour the starch water in a thin stream while stirring rapidly with a spoon. Turn off the heat and continue stirring for about 1 minute until the sauce thickens and becomes stringy. Pour a layer of cooking oil on the surface after removing from the pot to prevent a skin from forming.  
   **Key point**: Potato starch cannot be substituted; stir quickly when thickening to prevent lumps.
3. Marinate the main ingredients: Make two slits on each chicken wing, place them and the fresh shrimp in a bowl. Add 1 tablespoon of chili sauce and a little white sesame seeds, mix well, and let marinate briefly.
4. Prepare the vegetables: Slice the ginger and garlic, cut the celery into sections, cut the green and red bell peppers into pieces, slice the onion, and cut the yellow sweet potato, potatoes, and carrots into rolling knife chunks. Place all vegetables in a large bowl, add chili powder and a little chicken bouillon, and mix well.  
   **Note**: Cut the ingredients into pieces of similar size to ensure even cooking.
5. Layer the pot: Heat the non-stick frying pan and melt the butter. Spread the mixed vegetables evenly on the bottom of the pan, then layer the marinated chicken wings and fresh shrimp on top. Sprinkle with white pepper powder and cumin powder.  
   **Key point**: Butter enhances flavor; vegetables at the bottom prevent the meat from sticking.
6. First braise: Cover the pot and braise over high heat for 5 minutes to quickly generate steam inside.
7. Add sauce and second braise: Remove the lid, take 2-3 tablespoons of the secret sauce into a bowl, quickly stir until smooth and stringy, then evenly drizzle it over the ingredients. Cover and braise over medium heat for 3 minutes.  
   **Key point**: The sauce needs to be stirred again to restore its consistency; spread it thinly and evenly.
8. Finish: After braising, remove the lid, garnish with cilantro, and serve.

## Health Advice  
This dish is rich in protein and carbohydrates. It is recommended to pair it with light mixed vegetables or clear soup to balance nutrition. The sauce contains high sugar and sodium, so people with high blood pressure or those managing sugar intake should consume in moderation.
