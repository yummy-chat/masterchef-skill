# Electric Rice Cooker Roast Chicken
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Whole chicken 1 (about 750g, 1.5 jin)  
🧅 Onion, appropriate amount (shredded)  
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic, appropriate amount (whole cloves)  
🧅 Scallion, appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🍊 Xinhui tangerine peel, a small amount (pre-soaked until soft and shredded)  
🤍 White sesame seeds, appropriate amount  

🧂 Salt 2g  
🍬 Sugar 5g  
🦪 Oyster sauce 20g  
🌶️ Ground pepper, appropriate amount  
🥄 Light soy sauce 20ml  
🥄 Dark soy sauce 10ml  
🍶 Hua Diao wine, half a bottle cap  
🍯 Honey, about one layer (brushed on after cooking)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok (for boiling water to blanch the chicken skin)  
🍚 Electric rice cooker  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🖌️ Brush  

## Preparation Steps
1. **Blanch the Chicken Skin**: Fill a wok with water and bring to a boil. Hold the chicken by the legs and immerse the whole chicken in the boiling water. Blanch quickly until the skin tightens and turns pale yellow. Immediately remove.
   **Note**: The water must be fully boiling before adding the chicken. Do not blanch for too long to avoid toughening the meat.
2. **Prepare the Marinade**: In a glass bowl, add salt, sugar, oyster sauce, ground pepper, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and Hua Diao wine. Stir until fully combined.
3. **Marinate the Chicken**: Evenly pour the prepared marinade over the blanched chicken. Use your hands to rub and massage thoroughly, ensuring the inside and outside of the chicken are fully coated with the sauce. Marinate for **1 hour** to allow the flavors to penetrate.
4. **Line the Pot Bottom**: Evenly spread the shredded onion, sliced ginger, whole garlic cloves, and scallion sections on the bottom of the electric rice cooker inner pot.
   **Key Point**: Lining with vegetables prevents the chicken from sticking and burning on the bottom while adding aroma.
5. **Load the Pot**: Place the marinated chicken into the electric rice cooker, ensuring **the breast side faces down and the back side faces up**. Pour any remaining marinade from the bowl over the chicken, and scatter the pre-soaked tangerine peel shreds beside the chicken.
   **Key Point**: Placing the breast side down allows the thicker breast meat to cook through more easily.
6. **Cook in the Rice Cooker**: Close the lid, press the "Cook" button. Once the program switches to the "Keep Warm" light, let it sit for a short while to ensure the chicken is fully cooked.
7. **Garnish and Serve**: Open the lid, remove the roast chicken, and place it on a serving plate. Use a brush to apply a thin layer of honey over the chicken skin, then sprinkle with white sesame seeds before serving.
   **Key Point**: Brushing with honey adds sheen and a sweet aroma; sesame seeds enhance texture and visual appeal.

## Health Advice
This dish uses a braising method instead of deep frying, effectively reducing calorie content and making it suitable for consumption during weight loss periods. Cooking with the breast side down helps prevent the meat from becoming too dry, and pairing it with tangerine peel aids in cutting greasiness. Be mindful of portion control when eating.
