# Fortune Bag Zongzi  
Cuisine Type: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Glutinous rice, as needed  
🥚 Salted egg yolks, multiple (separated from about 6 eggs)  
🥩 Meat filling, as needed (seasoned in advance according to personal preference)  
🌿 Zongzi leaves, as needed  
🥚 Eggs, about 6 (yolks only, used to make salted egg yolks)  
🧂 Seasonings for the meat filling (the exact types are not shown in the video; usually soy sauce, dark soy sauce, salt, sugar, etc.)

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
✂️ Scissors  
🍚 Rice cooker  
🥄 Metal steaming rack  
🥣 Bowl  
🔪 Knife

## Preparation Steps  
### Preparation Stage  
1. Use scissors to trim off the hard stem at the base of each zongzi leaf, making the leaves pliable and preventing tearing during wrapping.  
2. Crack the eggs onto a metal steaming rack to strain out the egg whites, keeping the yolks intact to create salted egg yolks.  
3. Season the meat filling in advance and set aside.

### Wrapping & Cooking  
1. Lay 3–4 prepared zongzi leaves in a crisscross pattern inside a bowl to form a pouch shape.  
   **Key**: The leaves must overlap in a crisscross pattern to ensure a sturdy structure.  
2. Place a layer of glutinous rice in the center of the leaves, add the salted egg yolks and seasoned meat filling, then cover with another layer of glutinous rice to fully encase the filling.  
   **Key**: Position the filling in the center, and use enough glutinous rice to completely wrap it.  
3. Gather the zongzi leaves from the edges of the bowl upward, pinch them tightly at the top to form a fortune bag shape, then tie securely with cotton string just below the gathered opening.  
   **Key**: Pinch the opening tightly and tie firmly to prevent unravelling during cooking.  
4. Place the wrapped fortune bag zongzi into the inner pot of the rice cooker, add enough water, cover with the lid, and select the “Standard Cook” or similar program until the glutinous rice is soft and fully cooked.  
   **Note**: Ensure enough water is added to prevent burning; the exact cooking time depends on the size of the zongzi and the rice cooker model.

## Health Advice  
Fortune Bag Zongzi is high in carbohydrates and protein, making it suitable for replenishing energy in people with high physical exertion. However, the salted egg yolks and meat filling are relatively high in fat, so it is recommended to pair with light tea to cut the greasiness. Glutinous rice digests slowly, so the elderly and children should control their intake to avoid increasing digestive burden.
