# Maocai (Sichuan-Style Poached Vegetables)
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐂 Beef, about 250g (sliced)
🐄 Tripe, appropriate amount (washed thoroughly)
🐄 Layered tripe, appropriate amount (washed thoroughly)
🌭 Small sausages, appropriate amount (cut once in the middle)
🌭 Ham sausage, appropriate amount (sliced into large pieces)
🌭 Crispy sausages, appropriate amount (cross-cut on the top)
🫘 Konjac, appropriate amount (cut into strips)
🥬 Lettuce stem, appropriate amount (sliced)
🍈 Winter melon, appropriate amount (sliced)
🥔 Potato, appropriate amount (sliced)
🪷 Lotus root, appropriate amount (sliced)
🍄 Enoki mushrooms, appropriate amount
🌱 Bean sprouts, appropriate amount
🫘 Tofu skin, appropriate amount
🧅 Scallion, 1 (cut into sections)
🫚 Ginger, 1 piece (sliced)
🧄 Garlic, appropriate amount (some minced, some sliced)
🌶️ Xiaomi chili, appropriate amount (cut into rings)
🌿 Cilantro, appropriate amount (for garnish)
🫒 Rapeseed oil, generous amount
🫘 Hot pot base paste, two pieces
🧂 Sichuan peppercorns, appropriate amount
🌟 Star anise, appropriate amount
**Dipping Sauce**
🌶️ Cooked chili oil, half a bowl
🥜 Sesame paste, 1 tablespoon
🧂 Light soy sauce, a small amount
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1 cap
🍶 Aged vinegar, a small amount
🫒 Sesame oil, appropriate amount
🌿 Sichuan pepper oil, appropriate amount
🥣 Maocai original broth, 2-3 tablespoons

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Large iron wok
🥄 Soup ladle
🔪 Cutting board and knife
🥢 Strainer
🥣 Large bowl/basin (for serving)
🍵 Small bowl (for dipping sauce)

## Instructions
**Prepare Ingredients**
1. Clean beef, tripe, and layered tripe thoroughly. Rinse tripe and layered tripe repeatedly.
2. Slice the beef. Cut small sausages once in the middle. Slice ham sausage into large pieces. Cross-cut the tops of crispy sausages.
3. Cut konjac into strips. Slice lettuce stem, winter melon, potato, and lotus root into thin slices. Cut scallion into sections. Slice ginger. Mince some garlic and slice the rest.
**Stir-fry the Base Paste**
4. Pour a generous amount of rapeseed oil into the large iron wok and heat until 70% hot. Add scallion sections, ginger slices, garlic slices, Sichuan peppercorns, and star anise. Stir-fry until fragrant.
5. Reduce the heat to low, add two pieces of hot pot base paste, and stir slowly until fully melted and a rich aroma is released.
**Note**: Turn the heat to low when adding the hot pot paste, or it will burn easily.
6. Add enough water, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and simmer for a few minutes to intensify the broth.
7. Use a strainer to remove and discard all solids from the broth, leaving only the clear, red, glossy soup base.
**Blanch the Ingredients**
8. Add ingredients to the pot in order of how long they take to cook:
   - First, add lotus root and potato slices (harder vegetables). Cook for about 3 minutes, then remove and spread them at the bottom of a large bowl.
- Next, add konjac, enoki mushrooms, winter melon, lettuce stem, tofu skin, and bean sprouts. Cook until done, then remove.
- Then add all types of sausages. Cook until done, then remove.
- Finally, add tripe, layered tripe, and beef slices. Blanch tripe for about 10 seconds. Remove beef as soon as it changes color. Layer these on top.
**Key Point**: Ingredients like tripe and beef become tough easily, so cook them quickly.  "Take in and out seven times up and down" as the saying goes.

**Prepare the Dipping Sauce**
9. In a small bowl, add cooked chili oil, sesame paste, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, aged vinegar, Xiaomi chili rings, minced garlic, sesame oil, and Sichuan pepper oil.
10. Add 2-3 tablespoons of the original blanching broth and stir well to release the aroma of the seasonings.
**Plate and Garnish**
11. Drizzle the prepared dipping sauce evenly over the blanched ingredients.
12. Garnish with chopped scallions and cilantro. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice
Maocai is rich in ingredients and has a spicy, savory flavor. However, it is high in oil and sodium. It is recommended to serve with staple foods and light vegetables to balance nutrition. Those with high blood pressure or cholesterol should consume in moderation. Blanch tripe and beef for about 10 seconds for the best texture.
