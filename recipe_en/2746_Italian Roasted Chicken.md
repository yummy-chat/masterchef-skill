# Italian Roasted Chicken  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for all seasons  

🧄 Ingredients  
- 🍗 Chicken thighs (1–2 per person)  
- 🧄 Garlic (1 clove per person)  
- 🌿 Parsley (1 stalk per person)  
- 🍋 Lemon  

🧂 Seasonings  
- 🧂 Salt (2g per person)  
- 🌑 Black pepper (0.5g per person)  
- 🫒 Olive oil (5ml per person)  
- 🍋 Lemon juice (5ml per person)  

🍳 Utensils  
- Oven  

📝 Steps  
1. Rinse the chicken thighs and pat them dry with paper towels.  
   **Key point**: Drying the surface helps with marination and results in crispier skin during roasting.  
2. Evenly coat the chicken thighs with salt, black pepper, olive oil, and minced garlic. Marinate for at least 15 minutes to allow full flavor absorption.  
3. Preheat the oven to 180°C.  
4. Place the marinated chicken thighs on the middle rack of the oven and roast for 30–40 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked (no pink in the center, juices run clear).  
   **Note**: Adjust roasting time based on the size and thickness of the chicken thighs; ensure thorough cooking to avoid the risk of salmonella.  
5. While roasting, finely chop the parsley, cut the lemon, and squeeze out the juice for later use.  
6. Once the chicken is roasted, remove it from the oven and immediately drizzle evenly with lemon juice.  
7. Sprinkle with chopped parsley, plate, and serve hot.  

## Health Advice  
This Italian roasted chicken is low in fat and high in protein, making it an excellent choice for muscle building and fat loss. Pair it with vegetables for a balanced meal. Lemon juice aids digestion and boosts immunity, while black pepper should be used in moderation.
