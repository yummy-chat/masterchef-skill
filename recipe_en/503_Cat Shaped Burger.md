# Cat Shaped Burger
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍔 Cat-shaped hamburger buns 6 pieces (pre-made, thawed)
🍗 Char siu chicken thigh fillets 2 pieces (pre-marinated, frozen)
🍅 Tomato 1 piece (sliced)
🥬 Lettuce appropriate amount (washed and drained)
🧀 Cheese slices 2 slices
🍫 Chocolate sauce a small amount (for drawing facial expressions)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
♨️ Air fryer
🍳 Cast iron skillet (for heating burger buns, prone to burning)
🔪 Knife, cutting board
🥄 Squeeze bottle (for chocolate sauce)
🔗 Baking tongs/food tongs

## Cooking Steps
1. **Roast Chicken Thighs**: Place frozen chicken thighs directly into the air fryer without thawing, set temperature to 200℃, bake for 14 minutes until fully cooked, remove and cut into bite-sized pieces.
2. **Heat Burger Buns**: Split thawed burger buns horizontally, heat cut sides in a cast iron skillet over low heat until lightly golden and fully warmed through.
   **Note**: Cast iron has strong heat retention; slightly higher heat can easily burn the buns. Keep heat low throughout and monitor closely. If not confident, reheat in a low-temperature oven instead.
3. **Assemble Burgers**: Take the bottom bun, layer in this order: lettuce → tomato slices → chicken pieces → cheese slice → another layer of lettuce → tomato slices, then cover with the cat-shaped top bun.
4. **Decorate**: Using a squeeze bottle with chocolate sauce, pipe cat eyes, nose, and whiskers on top of the burger, then serve.

## Health Tips
This burger uses an air fryer to cook the chicken thighs, reducing frying-related calories. Tomatoes and lettuce provide dietary fiber and vitamins, making it suitable as an occasional creative meal. Be mindful of cheese and chocolate sauce portions to avoid excess calories.
