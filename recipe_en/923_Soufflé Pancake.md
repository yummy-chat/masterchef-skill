# Soufflé Pancake
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Suggestion: All seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥚 Eggs
🥛 Milk
🌾 Cake flour
🍬 White sugar

## Kitchenware & Equipment
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Hand whisk
🔌 Electric mixer
🥄 Silicone spatula
🧁 Piping bag
✂️ Scissors
🍳 Electric griddle
🥘 Lid
🍽 Plate
🔍 Sieve

## Steps
1. **Separate egg whites and yolks**: Place the egg whites and yolks into two clean, grease-free, and water-free bowls.  
   **Key point**: The bowl for egg whites must be completely free of water and grease, otherwise it will affect the whipping results.
2. **Make the egg yolk batter**: Add 30g milk to the egg yolks and mix well with a hand whisk. Sift in 35g cake flour and stir in a "Z" shape until no dry flour remains and the batter is smooth and silky.  
   **Note**: Avoid stirring in circles to prevent the batter from developing gluten.
3. **Whip the egg whites**: Add 25g white sugar to the egg whites and beat at high speed with an electric mixer for about 3 minutes, until stiff peaks form when the beater is lifted.
4. **Combine the batters**: Add half of the whipped meringue to the egg yolk batter and quickly fold it in with a silicone spatula until combined. Then pour the mixed batter back into the remaining meringue and continue folding until the color is even and no large lumps of egg white remain.  
   **Key point**: Use a folding motion throughout; do not stir in circles to prevent the meringue from deflating.
5. **Fill the piping bag**: Transfer the batter into a piping bag, twist the top tightly, and snip a small opening at the tip.
6. **Pan-fry**: Preheat the electric griddle over medium-low heat and brush a thin layer of cooking oil to prevent sticking. Pipe the batter vertically into the pan, stacking it slightly high to make the finished product fuller. Cover with a lid and let it cook for about 10 minutes, until the bottom is set and golden brown.  
   **Heat**: Maintain medium-low heat throughout.
7. **Flip**: Carefully flip the pancakes with a spatula, cover again, and cook for another 3 minutes until both sides are golden brown, the pancake springs back when pressed, and the inside is fully cooked.
8. **Plate and serve**: Remove from the pan, dust with powdered sugar or milk powder, or drizzle with yogurt or honey before serving.

## Health Tips
This soufflé pancake features an airy meringue base, with calories mainly from the egg yolks and sugar, making it suitable as an occasional light dessert. It is recommended to serve with unsweetened yogurt or fruit to reduce extra sugar intake. For those with lactose intolerance, replace milk with oat milk.
