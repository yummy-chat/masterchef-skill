# Italian Vegetable Frittata
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍄 Mushrooms: about 100g (sliced thinly)  
🍅 Cherry tomatoes: about 10 (halved, reserve 2-3 whole for garnish)  
🥬 Spinach: 1 small bunch (trim roots, can be blanched)  
🥓 Bacon: about 3-4 slices (cut into thin strips)  
🧄 Garlic: 2-3 cloves (crushed and minced)  
🧅 Onion: 1/4 (finely minced)  
🥚 Eggs: 6  
🧂 Salt: to taste  
🧂 Black pepper: to taste (sprinkle on serving)  
🌶️ Chili oil: to taste (drizzle on serving)  
🧀 Parmesan cheese: generous amount (freshly grated on finished dish)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🥢 Spatula  
🔪 Chinese chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🍴 Fork  
🥄 Slotted spoon (optional)  
🪩 Aluminum foil  

## Steps  
1. **Prepare ingredients:** Remove mushroom stems and slice thinly; remove cherry tomato stems and halve, reserving 2-3 whole for garnish; trim spinach roots, blanch in boiling water for 20 seconds if desired, then drain; cut bacon into thin strips; crush and mince garlic; finely mince onion.  
2. **Prepare egg mixture:** In a bowl, crack 6 eggs, add salt, and whisk thoroughly with a fork until the egg mixture is even.  
3. **Sauté base:** Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat, add a base oil, then add minced onion and garlic, slowly sautéing until the onion is translucent.  
4. **Cook mushrooms:** Add sliced mushrooms, turn heat to high, stir-fry, sprinkle a little salt, and cook until mushroom surfaces are dry, steam reduces, and they are slightly shriveled.  
5. **Add cherry tomatoes:** Add cherry tomatoes, continue stir-frying until evenly heated.  
6. **Add bacon:** Add bacon strips, stir-fry briefly.  
7. **Add spinach:** Add spinach, use a spatula to toss and stir at the bottom of the pan until it quickly wilts.  
8. **Add oil to coat bottom:** Just before adding the egg mixture, increase heat, add a little more oil, and quickly swirl the pan to evenly coat the bottom.  
9. **Pour and stir egg mixture:** Pour the egg mixture with one hand while using a spatula with the other; immediately push the egg mixture from the edges toward the center, lifting the set egg layer with the spatula tip to allow raw egg to flow to the bottom and set.  
10. **Set and garnish:** When most of the egg mixture is set and the surface is slightly moist, use a spatula to smooth the surface, then place the reserved whole cherry tomatoes on top for garnish. Reduce heat to low and cook for 3-5 minutes.  
11. **Steam with foil:** Cover the pan with aluminum foil (shiny side down), remove from heat, and let rest for 10 minutes to finish cooking with residual heat.  
12. **Crisp the bottom:** Return the pan to the stove, heat over medium heat for about 1 minute to crisp the bottom.  
13. **Plate and season:** Slide the frittata onto a plate, drizzle with chili oil, sprinkle with black pepper, and add a generous amount of freshly grated Parmesan cheese.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is rich in protein and vegetable fiber, making it suitable for fitness or weight loss diets. Note that bacon and cheese are high in sodium; those with high blood pressure should consume in moderation. It is recommended to serve with whole wheat bread to balance carbohydrate intake.
