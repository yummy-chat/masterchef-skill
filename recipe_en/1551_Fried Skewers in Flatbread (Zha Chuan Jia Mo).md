# Fried Skewers in Flatbread (Zha Chuan Jia Mo)
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🧅 Onion, appropriate amount (finely chopped)  
🧄 Minced garlic, appropriate amount  
🧅 Scallion white part, appropriate amount  
🍢 Skewer ingredients such as sausage, broccoli, crab sticks, etc., appropriate amount (choose according to preference and thread onto bamboo skewers)  
🥖 Flatbread, appropriate amount  

🫒 Rapeseed oil, appropriate amount (for frying and making the sauce)  
🌿 Cumin seeds, appropriate amount  
🍅 Ketchup, appropriate amount  
🍯 Sweet bean sauce, appropriate amount  
🦪 Oyster sauce, appropriate amount  
🍬 White sugar, appropriate amount  
💧 Hot water, appropriate amount  
🌶️ Ground Sichuan pepper, appropriate amount  
🌶️ Chili powder, appropriate amount  
🤍 Sesame seeds, appropriate amount  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Deep pot/fryer  
🥄 Spatula  
🥣 Bowl  
✂️ Scissors  

## Steps  
1. Finely chop the onion; prepare the minced garlic and scallion white part.  
2. Pour rapeseed oil into a deep pot and heat over medium heat until warm. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and scallion white part. Stir-fry until the edges turn slightly golden and the aroma is released.  
3. Add the cumin seeds and stir-fry until fragrant.  
4. Add ketchup, sweet bean sauce, oyster sauce, and white sugar. Stir-fry until well combined and aromatic.  
5. Pour in hot water, stir to adjust the consistency, and bring to a boil.  
6. Add ground Sichuan pepper and chili powder. Reduce to medium-low heat and continue simmering until the sauce thickens and turns a glossy, reddish paste.  
7. Before removing from heat, sprinkle in sesame seeds and mix well to make the secret skewer sauce.  
8. In a separate pot, heat oil to 180°C (350°F). Add the skewered ingredients and fry until cooked through and the surface turns golden brown.  
9. Evenly brush or dip the fried skewers into the prepared sauce.  
10. Heat or toast the flatbread, then use scissors to cut it open. Stuff the cut flatbread with the sauce-coated skewers.  

## Health Advice  
This fried skewers in flatbread dish is high in calories. It is recommended to pair it with a refreshing vegetable salad or cold cucumber to balance the oil intake. Suitable for occasional consumption. Individuals with high blood pressure should be mindful of the sodium content in sweet bean sauce and oyster sauce used in the sauce and may reduce the amount accordingly.
