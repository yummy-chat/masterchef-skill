# Chocolate Lava Cake
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍫 Dark chocolate 267g (70% cocoa content)
🧈 Unsalted butter 208g
🍬 White sugar 92g
🥚 Eggs 6 (about 233g)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl (heat-resistant)
🥄 Spatula
🍳 Pan or saucepan (for double boiling)
🥚 Whisk
🌡 Food thermometer
🫗 Strainer
🍞 Loaf pan (e.g., SN2070 type) or glass lunch box
📜 Baking paper
🔥 Oven

## Steps
1. Place the dark chocolate pieces and unsalted butter chunks together in a heat-resistant bowl, and heat over a water bath.
   **Key point**: When about one-third of the chocolate has melted, add the butter and stir until smooth; there is no need to wait for both to fully melt, as residual heat from the mixture will finish the process.
2. While the chocolate-butter mixture is not yet fully melted, add the white sugar and stir until evenly combined.
   **State check**: At this point, the overall temperature of the chocolate mixture should be about 50℃.
3. In a separate bowl, beat the whole eggs and heat over a water bath while stirring with a whisk.
   **Key point**: The purpose of heating is to raise the egg temperature to approximately 50℃, similar to the chocolate mixture; do not whip, and try to avoid creating too many air bubbles.
   **State check**: The egg liquid should reach about 50℃.
4. Strain the warmed egg liquid through a strainer to remove egg strings or foam, resulting in a finer texture.
5. Pour the strained warm egg liquid into the warm chocolate-sugar mixture.
   **Key point**: Stir slowly and continuously in a circular motion from the center with a whisk.
   **State check**: The mixture will not emulsify at first; after continuous stirring, the chocolate and eggs will fully combine to form a smooth, glossy, silk-like batter.
6. Line the loaf pan with baking paper and pour the mixed batter into the pan.
   **Key point**: Fill the pan to about 70–80% full.
7. Place the batter-filled pan into a preheated oven and bake at 180℃ for about 18–20 minutes.
   **State check**: After baking, measure the center temperature of the cake with a thermometer; it should reach 62–63℃ before removing from the oven, at which point the center will be molten.
   **Key point**: Do not bake for too long, otherwise the molten center will solidify.
8. After removing the cake from the oven, allow it to cool slightly, then unmold and slice for serving.
   **Key point**: For a denser texture similar to raw chocolate, refrigerate the cake before serving.

## Health Advice
This chocolate lava cake is very high in calorie density, making it suitable for small portions when you need a lot of energy (e.g., before an exam or a long hike). It is recommended to pair it with unsweetened black coffee or tea to balance the sweetness. People with diabetes or those controlling sugar intake should approach with caution.
