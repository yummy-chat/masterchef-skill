# Steamed Noodles with Braised Pork and Vegetables
Cuisine: Henan Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍖 350g pork belly  
🍖 2 medium-sized celery stalks  
🍖 500g fresh noodles (must be the thinnest kind)  
🧂 10cm scallion  
🧂 5 cloves garlic  
🧂 20g ginger slices  
🧂 2 green peppers (optional)  
🧂 3 dried red chili peppers (optional)  
🧂 20 Sichuan peppercorns (optional)  
🧂 Cooking oil (peanut oil is best)  
🧂 10g salt  
🧂 5g five-spice powder  
🧂 15ml light soy sauce  
🧂 10ml dark soy sauce  
🧂 Cooking wine (amount not specified, use as needed)  

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok  
🍳 Steamer (with steamer rack)  
🍳 Chopsticks  
🍳 Spatula  

## Cooking Steps
1. Remove the skin from the pork belly and cut into thin slices, 2 cm × 6 cm × 0.5 cm; remove the leaves and the bottom 2 cm from the celery, split each stalk in half lengthwise, then cut into 2 cm sections; peel and mince the garlic, slice the scallion into 0.2 cm thick rounds, and cut the ginger into fine shreds.
2. Heat the wok until smoking, pour in 3ml of cooking oil to coat the wok, then discard the oil. Add fresh oil, then add the pork slices, scallion, ginger, garlic, and dried red chili peppers. Stir-fry evenly for 1 minute. Add cooking wine, light soy sauce, and dark soy sauce, and stir-fry for another 1 minute. Add 500ml of hot water, cover, and simmer for 3 minutes.
3. Add the celery and green peppers to the wok, season with salt and five-spice powder, cover, and continue to simmer for 3 minutes, then turn off the heat.
4. Add 1000ml of water to the steamer and bring to a boil. Spread the fresh noodles evenly on the steamer rack and steam for 15 minutes. Once cooked, remove the noodles, use chopsticks and your hands to separate them, and let them cool naturally on a cutting board.
5. Place the cooled noodles into the wok with the braised meat and vegetables. Using chopsticks in one hand and a spatula in the other, turn the vegetables over the noodles and mix until the noodles are evenly colored.
6. Spread the mixed noodles on the steamer rack again, steam for 10 minutes, then turn off the heat and serve.

## Health Tips
This steamed noodle dish combines meat and vegetables, offering ample protein and dietary fiber. It is suitable for those with high physical exertion or needing a filling meal. However, since pork belly is high in fat, it is advisable to control portion sizes or pair it with a light soup.
