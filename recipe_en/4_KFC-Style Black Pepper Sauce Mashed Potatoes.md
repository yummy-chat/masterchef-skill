# KFC-Style Black Pepper Sauce Mashed Potatoes
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes 500g (about 2 medium-large ones)
🥛 Milk or heavy cream 50-100g (adjust for moisture)
🧈 Butter about 5g (for making the sauce)
🦪 Oyster sauce half tablespoon (about 5-7g)
🌽 Starch (cornstarch or potato starch) half tablespoon (about 5g)
🧂 Salt a pinch
⚫ Black pepper add to taste
💧 Water 3 tablespoons (about 30g)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
♨️ Rice cooker or steamer
🔪 Knife, cutting board
🥣 Bowls, spoon
🍳 Pan
🥄 Potato masher (or rice paddle)

## Cooking Steps
1. Peel the potatoes and cut into slices about 1 cm thick. Place in a rice cooker or steamer and steam for about 15 minutes until a chopstick can easily pierce through.
2. Remove the steamed potatoes and mash them with a potato masher. Add the milk or heavy cream in small amounts, stirring constantly, until the desired smoothness and thickness is achieved.
   **Note**: Add less milk for a firmer texture, or more for a softer, looser texture. For an ultra-smooth result, use a blender.
3. In a small bowl, mix the oyster sauce, starch, black pepper, a pinch of salt, and water. Stir well to form the black pepper sauce, and set aside.
4. In a pan, melt the butter over medium-low heat. Pour in the prepared sauce and stir continuously until the sauce comes to a boil with large bubbles, the color darkens, and the texture thickens. Remove from heat immediately.
   **Key point**: Do not stop stirring to prevent sticking or clumping.
5. Pile the mashed potatoes onto a plate and pour the prepared black pepper sauce over the top. Serve immediately.

## Health Tips
Replacing heavy cream with milk in mashed potatoes can reduce fat by half, making it suitable for weight loss. Black pepper and oyster sauce enhance flavor; use less salt for a healthier option.
