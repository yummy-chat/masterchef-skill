# Purple Rice Wrapped Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Glutinous rice 200g
🍚 Purple rice 200g
🥬 Lettuce 1 leaf per serving
🥩 Pork floss (meat floss) to taste
🥒 Pickled mustard (zhacai) to taste
🍅 Ketchup to taste
🥛 Mayonnaise to taste

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Large glass bowl
♨️ Rice cooker
🥄 Silicone spatula
🥄 Metal spoon
🫙 Plastic wrap

## Steps
1.  Mix glutinous rice and purple rice in a 1:1 ratio, add enough fresh water, soak for more than 3 hours, then drain the water.
2.  Place the soaked rice into the inner pot of the rice cooker, add the same amount of water as for daily rice cooking, and start the cooking program until done.
3.  Remove the cooked purple rice, and quickly stir and toss it with a silicone spatula to cool it down until it is no longer hot to the touch.
4.  Spread a layer of plastic wrap on the cutting board, place an appropriate amount of purple rice on it, and flatten it with the spatula.
5.  Layer on pork floss, lettuce leaves, then squeeze on ketchup and mayonnaise, and evenly sprinkle pickled mustard over the top.
6.  Lift the four corners of the plastic wrap, gather the rice and fillings towards the center, and squeeze tightly into a firm round or oval ball. Remove the plastic wrap and serve.
   **Note**: When wrapping, be sure to squeeze tightly to ensure the rice ball is firm and does not fall apart.

## Health Advice
Purple rice wrapped rice is rich in dietary fiber and complex carbohydrates, making it suitable as an energy replenishment after exercise. However, mayonnaise and pork floss have a high amount of hidden calories, so portion control is important during weight loss periods.
