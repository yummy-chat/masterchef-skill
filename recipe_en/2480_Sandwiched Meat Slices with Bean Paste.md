# Sandwiched Meat Slices with Bean Paste
Cuisine: Sichuan Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🐖 Pork belly with skin, appropriate amount (for making sandwiched meat slices)  
🍚 Glutinous rice, appropriate amount (soaked in advance, with distinct grains and soft texture)  
🫘 Bean paste, appropriate amount (filling for the sandwiched meat)  
🍬 White sugar, appropriate amount (for caramelizing)  
🍬 Brown sugar, appropriate amount (for making brown sugar sauce with lard)  
🐖 Lard, appropriate amount  
🫒 Vegetable oil, a small amount (for caramelizing)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🫕 Large iron wok  
♨️ Steamer cabinet  
🪣 Stainless steel large basin  
🪝 Long iron hook  
📦 Tray  
🥣 Earthenware bowl  
🥄 Wok spatula/spatula  

## Preparation Steps  
1. **Caramelize sugar**: Place the wok over heat, add a small amount of vegetable oil and white sugar. Stir continuously over low heat until the syrup turns a deep jujube-red color.  
**Key point**: Heat control is crucial. If the color is too light, the sweetness won’t suppress the meaty odor; if too dark and bitter, it will ruin the entire batch of meat.  

2. **Soak glutinous rice**: Place the glutinous rice in a large basin. In summer, use cold water; in winter, use hot water. Soak for at least 3 hours until the grains are plump, distinct, and soft.  
3. **Prepare pork belly**: Wash the pork belly and boil it in a pot of water with some of the caramelized sugar added until it is just cooked through. Remove, then brush another layer of caramelized sugar onto the skin. Immediately plunge it into a hot oil wok and fry until the skin turns golden red and blistered.  
**Key point**: The fried meat must be immediately soaked in the previous meat-cooking broth to rehydrate and soften, forming a tiger-skin wrinkled surface—this is a crucial step for achieving the unique texture and absorbing the sauce.  

4. **Cut the sandwiched meat slices**: Cut the prepared pork belly into “continuous slices,” ensuring even thickness so that each slice can evenly wrap the bean paste.  
5. **Prepare brown sugar sauce**: Melt the brown sugar together with lard, mixing evenly to form a brown sugar and lard sauce.  
6. **Mix glutinous rice**: Pour the soaked and drained glutinous rice into the brown sugar and lard sauce, mixing thoroughly.  
7. **Assemble in bowls**: Place one slice of meat at the bottom of an earthenware bowl, fill with an appropriate amount of bean paste, then cover with another slice of meat. Spread the mixed glutinous rice evenly over the meat slices and press down firmly.  
8. **Steam**: Place in the steamer cabinet and steam for about 40 minutes.  
9. **Invert**: After steaming, remove the bowl, cover it with a plate, and quickly invert it so that the meat slices are on top and the glutinous rice is on the bottom. Serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
This dish is high in sugar and fat. It is recommended to pair it with refreshing vegetables or light tea. It is suitable for those with high physical exertion or those needing to gain weight. Those with high blood pressure, high blood sugar, or high cholesterol should only taste it in small amounts.
