# Lychee Chicken Soup
Cuisine: Cantonese  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 Fresh chicken, half (do not use frozen chicken)  
🍈 Lychees, 10 pieces (peeled and pitted, reserve the lychee shells)  
🫘 Red dates, to taste  
🌰 Lotus seeds, to taste  
🌿 Codonopsis root, to taste  
🫚 Ginger, a few slices  
🧂 Salt, to taste (season before serving)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍲 Clay pot  
🥄 Soup ladle (for skimming foam and serving soup)  
🥄 Slotted spoon (for removing chicken)  
🥣 Glass bowl (for holding lychee flesh, dried ingredients, etc.)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Prepare lychees**: Peel the lychees, keeping the shells intact. Use chopsticks to poke out the pits and remove the lychee flesh. Set aside the shells and flesh separately.  
**Key point**: Lychee shells are a source of flavor in the soup; do not discard them.  
2. **Prepare chicken**: Wash the fresh chicken and chop it into small pieces.  
3. **Blanch**: Place the chicken pieces in cold water and bring to a boil over high heat. Skim off the large amount of white foam that rises to the surface. Blanch for about 3 minutes, then remove and rinse clean with water.  
**Key point**: Thoroughly skimming the foam makes the soup clearer and sweeter.  
4. **Add to pot**: Place the rinsed chicken pieces in the clay pot. Add codonopsis root, ginger slices, red dates, and lotus seeds. Finally, add the reserved **lychee shells**.  
5. **Add water and simmer**: Pour enough water into the clay pot at once. Bring to a boil over high heat, then immediately reduce to the **lowest heat**, cover, and simmer slowly for 60 minutes.  
6. **Swap ingredients**: Remove the lid. Use chopsticks to take out all the lychee shells (now darkened in color) and discard them. Add the prepared **lychee flesh** and continue to simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.  
**Key point**: Do not add too much lychee flesh at once; it is recommended to use 10 lychees for half a chicken to avoid making the soup overly sweet.  
7. **Season**: Add salt to taste, stir well, and serve.

## Health Advice
This lychee chicken soup uses the fruit shells to enhance flavor and the fruit flesh to add sweetness, making it especially suitable for a nourishing summer meal. Lychees are warm in nature, and cooking them with chicken helps neutralize dryness and heat. However, people with diabetes should control the amount of lychees used. It is recommended to pair the soup with greens to balance the sweetness.
