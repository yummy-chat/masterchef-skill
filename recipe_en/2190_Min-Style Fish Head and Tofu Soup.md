# Min-Style Fish Head and Tofu Soup
Cuisine: Fujian Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 1 bighead carp head (from a 4-5 lb whole fish, make a few cuts on thick parts)  
🧅 300g firm tofu (broken into pieces by hand)  
🫚 1 piece ginger (sliced)  
🧅 Scallions, to taste (cut into sections)  
🫘 A handful of goji berries  
🫒 Vegetable oil, as needed (for frying fish head)  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🌿 White pepper, to taste  

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Soup ladle  
🫗 Large soup bowl  
🔪 Heavy chef's knife  
🪵 Round thick wooden cutting board  

## Cooking Steps
1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok to 60% hot. Add the fish head and fry over medium heat until both sides are golden brown and set.  
**Key point**: Fry the fish head thoroughly to release the fish oil aroma, which is essential for achieving a milky white soup later.

2. Add scallion sections and ginger slices, then pour in enough boiling water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and cook for about 8 minutes until the soup turns milky white.  
**Note**: Must use boiling water; cold water will cause the protein to coagulate suddenly, preventing the soup from emulsifying and turning white.

3. Break the firm tofu into irregular chunks by hand and gently add to the soup. Continue to simmer over medium heat for about 5 minutes, allowing the tofu to absorb the broth and flavor.  
**Key point**: Hand-broken pieces have rougher surfaces than knife-cut ones, making them easier to absorb the soup and resulting in a more tender texture.

4. Add goji berries, salt, and white pepper, then stir to combine. Transfer to a large soup bowl and serve.  
**Note**: Goji berries should not be cooked for too long; adding them at the end preserves their color and sweetness.

## Health Tips
Fish head tofu soup is rich in high-quality protein and calcium, making it especially suitable for students needing brain nourishment and people working long hours. The isoflavones in tofu and the DHA in the fish head work synergistically to help enhance memory. Consume in moderation to avoid excessive purine intake.
