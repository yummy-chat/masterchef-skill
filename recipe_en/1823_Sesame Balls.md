# Sesame Balls
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫘 Black beans 150g (rinsed clean)
🌾 Black rice 150g (rinsed clean)
🌰 Black sesame seeds 300g (rinsed clean)
🍯 Honey 180ml (used as a binder and seasoning)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok (dry-frying)
🔪 High-speed blender (or food processor)
🥣 Large bowl
🧤 Disposable gloves

## Instructions
**Preparing the Powders**
1. Pour the black beans into a clean wok. Stir-fry over medium heat continuously until the skins crack, the raw bean smell disappears, and a fragrance similar to soybeans emerges. Remove from the wok and let cool.
2. Pour the black rice into a clean wok. Stir-fry over medium heat until it becomes crispy to the bite. Remove from the wok and let cool.
3. Pour the black sesame seeds into a clean wok. Stir-fry over medium heat until they feel dry and release a rich sesame aroma. Remove from the wok and let cool.
4. Place the cooled black beans and black rice separately into the blender. Grind into fine powders, then set each aside in separate bowls.
5. Place the cooled black sesame seeds into the blender. Grind them into a fine, paste-like consistency.
   **Note**: When grinding the sesame seeds, do not over-process, or they will release oil and turn into sesame paste. Under-processing will result in a rough, dry texture. Grind in short bursts, checking the consistency frequently. Use a spatula to scrape down the sesame seeds from the sides of the blender and continue grinding until they become a sticky, glossy, and cohesive paste. Then, transfer to a large bowl.

**Forming the Balls**
1. Add the black bean powder and black rice powder to the large bowl containing the black sesame paste. Mix initially to combine.
2. Add the honey. Wearing disposable gloves, use your hands to knead the mixture until evenly combined.
3. Check the moisture of the mixture: Take a small amount and try to form a ball. It should hold together without crumbling. If the mixture is too dry, add a bit more honey as needed.
4. Take an appropriate amount of the mixture, squeeze it firmly in your hand to compress it, ensuring it will not fall apart.
5. Place the compressed mixture in your palm and gently roll it into a round ball. During rolling, oil will seep to the surface, making the ball gradually become glossy and shiny.
6. Repeat this process to form all the mixture into evenly sized balls. Arrange them on a plate and serve.

## Health Advice
These sesame balls are rich in high-quality protein and unsaturated fatty acids from black beans, black rice, and black sesame seeds. They are suitable for those needing to nourish the brain, protect hair, and enhance physical strength. Note that honey has a high sugar content; individuals with diabetes or those managing sugar intake should consume in small amounts.
