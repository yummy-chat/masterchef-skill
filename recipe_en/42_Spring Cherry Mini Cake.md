# Spring Cherry Mini Cake  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Spring (January–April)

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🧀 Cream cheese 200g (divided into two portions, 100g each)  
🍪 Digestive biscuits 2 pieces  
🥛 Milk about 100g  
🍥 Gelatin sheets 2 sheets (about 10g, pre-soaked in cold water until softened)  
🍒 Cherries appropriate amount (halved and pitted)  
🥜 Pistachio paste appropriate amount  
🍒 Cherry jam appropriate amount  
🍬 White sugar appropriate amount (for seasoning the pistachio cheese mixture)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
📦 Rectangular plastic food container (mold)  
🥣 Stainless steel bowls 2  
🥣 Small bowl 1  
🫖 Hot water basin (for double boiler)  
🥄 Hand whisk or spatula  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
❄️ Refrigerator

## Instructions  
1. Divide 200g cream cheese evenly into two portions of 100g each, placing each in a separate bowl. Mix one portion with an appropriate amount of cherry jam, and the other with pistachio paste and white sugar to taste.  
2. Using a whisk or spatula, beat each cheese mixture until smooth and lump-free.  
   **Note**: If the cheese has lumps, place the bowl over hot water and stir until smooth.  
3. Remove the softened gelatin sheets and place them in a small bowl with about 100g milk. Set the small bowl in a hot water basin and heat over a double boiler, stirring continuously, until the gelatin is completely dissolved.  
4. Roughly divide the gelatin-milk liquid into two even portions, pour each into one of the two flavored cheese mixtures, and stir quickly until well combined.  
5. First, pour the pink cherry cheese mixture into the rectangular mold. Place in the freezer for about 15 minutes, until the surface is slightly set and no longer sticky to the touch.  
6. Remove the mold from the freezer and arrange the halved cherries, cut side down, evenly over the first layer.  
7. Next, pour the green pistachio cheese mixture over the cherries, covering them. Gently press the two digestive biscuits into the surface of the green mixture.  
8. Freeze for about 2 hours, until the cake is fully set.  
9. Remove from the mold by inverting it. Decorate as desired with whipped cream, cherries, and biscuit pieces, then slice and serve.

## Health Tips  
This cake contains cream cheese and nut paste, providing protein and healthy fats. Cherries are rich in antioxidants, making it suitable for spring gatherings. However, note that gelatin and cream cheese are relatively high in calories; it is recommended to pair with a light tea to balance the flavor.
