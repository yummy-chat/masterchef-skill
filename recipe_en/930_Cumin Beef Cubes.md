# Cumin Beef Cubes
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef short ribs – appropriate amount (cut into cubes)  
🥝 Kiwi – 1 piece (peeled, for tenderizing the beef)  
🧅 Onion – appropriate amount (sliced)  
🥕 Carrot – appropriate amount (sliced)  
🧅 Scallion – appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🥬 Celery – appropriate amount (cut into sections)  
🍺 Beer – appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt – a pinch  
🧂 Chicken powder – a small amount (first use) + 1 tablespoon (second use)  
🧂 White pepper powder – a small amount (first use) + appropriate amount (second use)  
🥄 Light soy sauce – 1 tablespoon  
🦪 Oyster sauce – 1 tablespoon  
🫒 Sesame oil – 1 tablespoon  
🥄 Cornstarch – 2 tablespoons  
🌶️ Chili powder – 2 tablespoons (for marinating) + appropriate amount (for sprinkling after cooking)  
🌿 Cumin powder – 2 tablespoons (for marinating) + appropriate amount (for sprinkling after cooking)  
🫒 Cooking oil – 1 tablespoon (for marinating) + 1 teaspoon (before roasting)  
🌿 Cumin seeds – appropriate amount (optional, add before roasting)  
⚪ White sesame seeds – appropriate amount (for sprinkling after cooking)  
🌱 Chopped scallions – appropriate amount (for sprinkling after cooking)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, knife  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🧺 Small sieve or strainer  
♨️ Air fryer  
🍽️ Plate

## Instructions
1. **Prepare Ingredients**: Cut the beef short ribs into even cubes; slice the onion, carrot, scallion, and celery into strips or slices; peel the kiwi and set aside.  
2. **First Marination**:  
   - Place the onion, carrot, scallion, and celery into a bowl, add the peeled kiwi, and use your hands to squeeze and mash until the vegetables soften and release juice and the kiwi turns into a paste.  
   - Add the beef cubes, along with a small amount of chicken powder, a pinch of salt, and a small amount of white pepper powder. Pour in an appropriate amount of beer, and mix thoroughly again with your hands.  
   - Cover with plastic wrap and let it marinate for about 15 minutes, until the beef absorbs the liquid and swells slightly in volume.  
   **Key Point**: The protease in kiwi acts as a natural meat tenderizer, while the beer removes gamey flavors, enhances aroma, and softens the meat. Ensure thorough mixing for full penetration of the marinade.  
3. **Second Marination**:  
   - Use a sieve to strain out all the vegetable residue, keeping only the beef cubes.  
   - Add, in order, 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon of chicken powder, an appropriate amount of white pepper powder, 1 tablespoon of sesame oil, 2 tablespoons of cornstarch, 2 tablespoons of chili powder, and 2 tablespoons of cumin powder. Mix well with your hands until the beef cubes are evenly coated with the seasoning mixture and slightly sticky.  
4. **Pre-Roasting Preparation**:  
   - Add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and mix well to seal in the juices and prevent sticking.  
   - Spread the beef cubes evenly in the air fryer basket.  
5. **Roasting**:  
   - First round: Roast at 180°C for 10 minutes, then remove and turn or shake the beef cubes to ensure even cooking.  
   - Second round: Increase the temperature to 200°C and roast for another 5 minutes to dry the surface and create a crispy, fragrant outer layer with sizzling oil.  
6. **Plating and Serving**:  
   - While hot, sprinkle the beef cubes with white sesame seeds, chili powder, and cumin powder, and finally garnish with chopped scallions.  
   **Key Point**: The dry seasonings are instantly aromatic when they meet the hot oil and beef. Mix well and serve immediately for a crispy exterior and tender interior with a rich cumin flavor.

## Health Tips
Using kiwi and beer to tenderize the beef reduces reliance on traditional commercial tenderizing powders, preserving more of the protein. This dish is suitable for muscle-building individuals, but be mindful of the high sodium and oil content from the seasonings. Pairing it with a light vegetable salad is recommended to balance the calories.
