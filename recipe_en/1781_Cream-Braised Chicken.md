# Cream-Braised Chicken
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍗 Chicken thighs 3 pieces (skin-on, boneless)  
🥔 Small potatoes about 6 (peeled and halved)  
🧅 Red onion about half (finely minced)  
🧄 Garlic 4 cloves (crushed and minced)  
🍝 Spaghetti 1 serving (pre-cooked for about 11 minutes)  
🧂 Salt to taste (a little for marinating + 1 teaspoon for braising)  
🌿 Black pepper to taste (for marinating)  
🧈 Butter 1 large piece  
🌶️ Chili powder to taste  
🍂 Bay leaves 3 pieces  
🥛 Heavy cream 150 ml (about half a can)  
💧 Water as needed (just enough to barely cover ingredients)  
🌱 Rosemary 1 whole sprig (for braising) + a few fresh leaves (for garnish)  
🧀 Shredded cheese generous amount (sprinkled on serving)

## Kitchen tools & Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🍳 Frying pan (deep sauté pan)  
🔗 Tongs  
🥛 Measuring cup  
🥄 Strainer/colander  
🍽️ Plate

## Instructions
1. Peel the small potatoes and halve them; finely mince the red onion; crush and mince the garlic; set aside.
2. Season both sides of the chicken thighs with salt and black pepper, massage, and let marinate for 15 minutes. Before cooking, pat dry the surface with paper towels to prevent oil splatter.
3. Heat the frying pan over high heat, add a little oil, place the chicken thighs skin-side down in the pan, and cook over medium-high heat until the skin is golden and crispy. Remove and set aside, leaving the pan with the rendered oil.
4. Reduce to low heat. Without cleaning the pan, add the potato pieces, minced onion, and minced garlic, stir-fry until fragrant. Add a large piece of butter, chili powder, and bay leaves, continue stirring for about 1 minute to release the aroma.
5. Return the seared chicken thighs to the pan, pour in the heavy cream and enough water to barely cover the ingredients, add the whole rosemary sprig, and season with 1 teaspoon of salt. Cover with a lid and braise for about 25 minutes, until the sauce thickens and turns a caramel brown.
6. Add the pre-cooked and drained spaghetti to the pan, and toss well with the chicken, potatoes, and sauce.
7. Plate: first serve the noodles, then arrange the chicken and potatoes on top, sprinkle generously with shredded cheese, and garnish with fresh rosemary leaves.

## Health Advice
This cream-braised chicken is rich in protein and carbohydrates, suitable for those needing an energy boost. However, the cream and cheese are high in fat, making this dish not low in calories. It is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition.
