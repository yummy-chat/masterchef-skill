# Ants Climbing a Tree 🐜🌳
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧄 Ingredients  
- Sweet potato vermicelli  
- Ground pork (or ground beef)  

🧂 Seasonings  
- Pixian chili bean paste  
- Light soy sauce  
- Dark soy sauce  
- Cooking oil  
- Minced garlic  
- Minced ginger  
- Scallions (optional)  

🍳 Kitchenware  
- Wok  
- Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps
1. Soak sweet potato vermicelli in cold water for 20 minutes until softened, then drain and set aside.
2. Finely mince garlic and ginger separately and set aside.
3. Heat the wok over high heat, add 10ml of cooking oil, then stir-fry minced garlic and minced ginger until fragrant.
4. Add ground pork and stir-fry until the meat turns white and a slight layer of oil appears on the surface.
5. Add Pixian chili bean paste and continue stir-frying until red oil is released.
6. Pour in light soy sauce and dark soy sauce, and stir-fry evenly.
7. Add 300ml of water and bring to a boil over high heat.
8. Add the soaked and drained vermicelli, gently stirring with chopsticks to prevent sticking.
9. Reduce to medium-low heat and cook for about 5 minutes, until the vermicelli has fully absorbed the broth and the liquid is slightly reduced.
10. Sprinkle with chopped scallions to taste, then turn off the heat and plate.
⚠️ Key Points
- Do not overcook the vermicelli, as it may become brittle and lose its texture; control the amount of liquid to avoid drying out.
- Pixian chili bean paste is high in salt, so adjust the amount of light soy sauce as needed.
- Adding 0.5g of white pepper powder can enhance the aroma and flavor.

## Health Advice
Ants Climbing a Tree uses sweet potato vermicelli as a partial substitute for staple grains, increasing dietary fiber intake. However, Pixian chili bean paste is high in salt, so individuals with high blood pressure should control the amount of condiments used.
