# Tomato Instant Noodles  
**Cuisine:** Common Home-style  
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐  
**Seasonal Recommendation:** Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍜 Instant noodles – 1 pack  
🍅 Tomato – to taste (cut into small pieces)  
🥬 Baby bok choy – a few stalks (washed)  
🥚 Egg – 1 (for a soft-centered fried egg)  
🧅 Scallions – to taste (chopped)  
🫒 Cooking oil – a small amount (for sautéing the base)  
🫘 Black bean chili sauce (Lao Gan Ma) – about 1 tablespoon  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Wok/frying pan  
🥄 Soup ladle  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍳 Frying pan (for frying the egg)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Cut the tomato into small pieces, and chop the scallions.  
2. Heat the wok over the fire, add a small amount of cooking oil and heat it up. Add the chopped scallions and sauté until fragrant.  
   **Key:** Proceed to the next step immediately upon smelling the scallion aroma; the aromatizing process must be thorough.  
3. Add the tomato pieces and stir-fry over high heat quickly until soft and juicy.  
4. Add one tablespoon of black bean chili sauce and stir-fry evenly to release the fermented bean aroma.  
5. Add enough hot water to the wok and bring to a boil over high heat.  
6. Once the water is boiling, first add the vegetable seasoning packet that comes with the instant noodles and let it simmer to release its aroma.  
7. Add the noodle cake and the seasoning powder packet. Maintain high heat and cook for about 2 minutes.  
   **Note:** Keep the heat high throughout to ensure the noodle texture.  
8. When the noodles are nearly done, add the baby bok choy and blanch until it turns bright green and is just cooked.  
9. Meanwhile, in a separate frying pan, fry a soft-centered egg: the egg white should be set while the yolk remains runny.  
10. Ladle the cooked noodles and soup into a bowl, top with the soft-centered egg, and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This noodle dish is relatively high in salt and fat content. It is recommended to consume less of the broth and add more vegetables. People with high blood pressure should be mindful of their intake.
