# Steamed Cold Fish (Liangban Jiyu)
Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 Crucian carp (similar size)
🌶️ Green Xiaomila peppers
🌶️ Red Xiaomila peppers
🧄 Ginger
🧄 Garlic
🧅 Scallion
🌿 One bunch of Chinese chives
🍾 Cooking wine
🧂 Salt
🧂 Chicken bouillon
🍬 White sugar
🍾 Light soy sauce
🍾 Vinegar (a little)
🍾 Oyster sauce
🍾 Sichuan pepper oil
🧂 White pepper powder
🐷 Lard
🫒 Cooking oil
💧 Cold boiled water

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍲 Large cooking pot
🥣 Bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Spoon
🔥 Stove

## Preparation Steps
1.  Scale the crucian carp, remove gills and internal organs, rinse clean, and score the thick parts of the fish body with a knife. Place in a bowl with scallion sections, ginger slices, and cooking wine, rub evenly, and marinate to remove fishy odor.
2.  Finely slice the green and red Xiaomila peppers into rings, mince the ginger and garlic, chop the scallion into small pieces, and mince the Chinese chives.
3.  Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, combine the pepper rings, minced ginger and garlic, chopped scallion, and minced Chinese chives. Add salt, chicken bouillon, white sugar, light soy sauce, a little vinegar, oyster sauce, and Sichuan pepper oil. Finally, add cold boiled water and stir well.
4.  Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add scallion sections, ginger slices, white pepper powder, cooking wine, and a little lard. When the water is just about to boil (barely simmering), gently place the crucian carp in one by one.
    **Key point**: Maintain a heat that is "about to boil but not quite boiling" throughout. Cook for about 10 minutes to ensure the fish remains intact and tender.
5.  Remove the cooked crucian carp and arrange on a plate. Pour most of the prepared sauce over the fish, then sprinkle another layer of minced Chinese chives on top.
6.  Heat the cooking oil in a wok until smoking. Evenly pour the hot oil over the minced Chinese chives and sauce to release the aroma. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice
Crucian carp is rich in high-quality protein and calcium, which aids muscle repair and bone health. This cold preparation method uses little oil and is low in fat, making it suitable for weight loss periods or those needing a light diet. It is recommended to serve with coarse grain staples and to avoid eating excessively spicy food on an empty stomach to prevent digestive irritation.
