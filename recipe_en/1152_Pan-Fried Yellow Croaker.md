# Pan-Fried Yellow Croaker
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 1 yellow croaker (about 600g, scaled, gills and innards removed, back cut open into a fan shape)
🧅 Scallions, a little (for marinade)
🧅 Onion, a little (sliced)
🍋 Lemon, a little (squeeze before serving)
🍶 Rice wine, 30g
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns, 3g
🧂 Salt, 20g
💧 Water, about 2 jin (for preparing the marinade)
🥜 Peanut oil, as needed (for frying the fish)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🪵 Round thick wooden cutting board
🥣 Stainless steel basin (for cleaning and marinating the fish)
🔪 Knife
🧻 Kitchen paper towels (for drying the fish)
🍳 Frying pan
🫗 Pot lid (for assisting with flipping)
🍽️ Plate (for serving)

## Steps
1. **Prepare the yellow croaker**: Remove the scales. Use the tip of the knife to remove the gills in reverse. Cut open from the back, sever the head bone, and flatten the fish body into a fan shape. Remove the innards and roe. Rinse the black membrane inside the belly repeatedly with water until the water runs clear.
2. **Prepare the marinade**: In a stainless steel basin, combine the rice wine, Sichuan peppercorns, scallions, and sliced onion. Rub them vigorously to extract the juice. Add the salt and 2 jin of water, stirring until the salt is completely dissolved.
3. **Wet marinate**: Fully submerge the prepared fish in the marinade and let it sit for at least 1 hour. After removing, use kitchen paper towels to dry both sides of the fish thoroughly, ensuring the surface is completely dry.
4. **Heat the pan and oil**: Heat the frying pan over medium heat. Pour in an appropriate amount of peanut oil and swirl the pan to coat the bottom evenly.
5. **Fry the inner side**: Place the dried fish **flesh side down** into the pan. Cook over medium-low heat for about 5 minutes, until the surface of the fish is lightly golden, set, and crispy.
   - **Key**: The flesh side must be fried first. Do not start with the skin side, or the skin will lose its crispiness if exposed to heat for too long.
6. **Flip the fish**: Invert the pot lid over the fish. Press down firmly on the lid with one hand and quickly flip the pan so the fish lands on the lid. Then slide the fish back into the pan with the skin side down.
7. **Fry the skin side**: Continue cooking over medium-low heat until the skin is golden brown, slightly charred, and crispy to the touch.
8. **Remove and plate**: Use the same flipping method to turn the fish so the flesh side is facing up, then transfer it to a plate.
9. **Finish and enhance flavor**: Squeeze a little fresh lemon juice over the fish just before serving. Enjoy.

## Health Advice
This pan-fried yellow croaker is rich in high-quality protein and Omega-3 fatty acids, making it suitable for those needing brain and heart health support. However, the marinade contains a relatively high amount of salt, so individuals with high blood pressure should moderate their intake. Be sure to thoroughly dry the fish before frying to avoid oil splatter.
