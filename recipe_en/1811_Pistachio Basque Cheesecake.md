# Pistachio Basque Cheesecake
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🧀 Cream cheese 320g (must be at room temperature)  
🥚 Whole eggs 2 (room temperature)  
🥚 Egg yolk 1 (room temperature)  
🌽 Cornstarch 13g  
💚 Pistachio paste 75g  
🥛 Heavy cream 200g (room temperature)  
🍬 Granulated sugar 60g  
🍓 Fresh raspberries a few (for decoration)  
🍦 Light purple cream a small amount (for decoration, can be made by whipping berry jam with heavy cream)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Blender/food processor  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  
🥣 Hand whisk  
🍰 6-inch round cake pan  
📄 Parchment paper  
🔥 Oven  
🔪 Cake knife  
🎂 Piping bag  
🧊 Refrigerator (for overnight chilling)  

## Instructions  
1. Place the room-temperature cream cheese, granulated sugar, whole eggs, egg yolk, cornstarch, and pistachio paste into the blender. Blend until the mixture is smooth and free of lumps.  
   **Note**: All ingredients must be brought to room temperature in advance to prevent oil and water separation, which would affect the texture.  
2. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl. Gradually add the room-temperature heavy cream, and gently mix with a hand whisk until a uniformly green batter forms.  
   **Key point**: Stir by hand only; do not whip, to ensure the cake has a dense and firm texture.  
3. Line the 6-inch cake pan with parchment paper, pour the batter directly into the pan, and gently tap the pan several times to release large air bubbles.  
4. Place into a preheated oven and bake at 230°C for about 25 minutes, until the surface turns deep brown. Remove from the oven.  
5. Allow to cool naturally to room temperature, then transfer to the refrigerator to chill overnight.  
6. After chilling, remove from the pan, cut into slices, and plate. Use a piping bag to pipe light purple cream around the edge of the plate, and garnish with fresh raspberries.  

## Health Suggestions  
Pistachio paste is rich in quality unsaturated fatty acids, but cream cheese and heavy cream are high in calories. It is recommended to cut into small portions and enjoy, pairing with the sweet and sour raspberries to balance the flavor. Suitable for those who love rich cheesecake flavor; pay attention to controlling total sugar intake.
