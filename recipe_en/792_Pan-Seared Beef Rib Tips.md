# Pan-Seared Beef Rib Tips
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef rib tips (with connective tissue, cut into evenly sized cubes)
🧂 Salt to taste
🧂 Black pepper to taste (freshly ground)
🫒 Vegetable oil small amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, knife
🍳 Frying pan
🥢 Tongs

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the beef rib tips into evenly sized small cubes.
2. Add salt, freshly ground black pepper, and a small amount of vegetable oil to the beef cubes. Mix well with your hands to coat the surface of the beef cubes evenly with a thin layer of oil.
   **Note**: Use only a small amount of oil, just a thin layer, to avoid excessive greasiness when searing.
3. Preheat the frying pan thoroughly, then place the beef cubes in the pan. Sear over medium heat, following the technique used for pan-searing steak.
   **Key Point**: Be patient and sear until all six sides of the beef cubes are caramelized, the exterior is crispy, and the interior remains tender, pink, and juicy.
4. Use tongs to remove the beef cubes from the pan and plate them. Serve immediately. The finished dish is crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, with the connective tissue providing a rich texture.

## Health Advice
Beef rib tips are rich in high-quality protein and iron, making them suitable for those needing to boost blood circulation or build muscle. Pay attention to controlling the amount of salt and oil used to avoid excessive intake of sodium and saturated fat.
