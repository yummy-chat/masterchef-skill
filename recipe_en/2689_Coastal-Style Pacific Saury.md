# Coastal-Style Pacific Saury
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐟 Pacific saury (about 7 fish, each about 150g, cleaned)  
🧅 Purple onion (适量, minced)  
🌿 Cilantro (适量, minced)  
🧄 Garlic (适量, minced)  
🟢 Scallion (适量, minced)  
🧂 Salt (a pinch, sprinkle on pan bottom before frying)  
🫒 Cooking oil (适量)  
🍶 Rice wine (适量)  
🥄 Soy sauce (适量)  
🍬 Sugar (1 teaspoon, about 5g)  
💧 Water (50g)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Small bowl/stainless steel bowl  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🔗 Spatula  
🥄 Soup spoon  
🍽️ Plate

## Steps
1. Cut open the belly of each Pacific saury, remove the internal organs, and use the knife tip to remove the gills. Rinse the black membrane inside the belly thoroughly, and wash with clean water.  
   **Note**: The black membrane must be completely removed to avoid fishy smell; the scales of Pacific saury are very fine and can be left on.
2. Mince the onion, cilantro, scallion, and garlic separately. Set aside.
3. In a bowl, mix rice wine, soy sauce, sugar, and water. Stir well to make the sauce.
4. Heat the pan, add cooking oil. When the oil is hot, evenly sprinkle a thin layer of salt on the bottom of the pan. Reduce to low heat, place the Pacific saury in the pan. Drizzle a little oil on the fish skin, then turn to high heat to fry.  
   **Note**: Sprinkling salt helps prevent sticking; lightly press the fish with a spatula and tilt the pan for even heating. Minimize flipping, and fry until both sides are set and golden brown.
5. Remove the fried fish from the pan. Pour out the excess oil without washing the pan. Directly add the minced onion, cilantro, scallion, and garlic into the pan, and stir-fry until fragrant.
6. Pour in the prepared sauce while stirring. Bring to a boil, then return the fried fish to the pan. Cook over medium-high heat for 1–2 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Continuously spoon the sauce over the fish during cooking to ensure even flavor absorption.
7. Once flavored, turn off the heat. Remove from the pan and plate.

## Health Tips
Pacific saury is rich in omega-3 fatty acids and high-quality protein, making it suitable for those with eye strain or needing cardiovascular care. When frying fish, minimize flipping, sprinkle salt to prevent sticking, and thoroughly remove the black membrane to avoid fishy taste.
