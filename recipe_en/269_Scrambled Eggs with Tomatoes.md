# Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Difficulty level: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍅 Ingredients:  
- Tomatoes  
- Eggs  

🧂 Seasonings:  
- Cooking oil  
- Salt  
- Sugar (optional)  
- Scallions (optional)  

🍳 Kitchenware:  
- Pan  
- Spatula  
- Bowl  
- Plate  

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash the tomatoes thoroughly.
2. If peeling is desired, blanch the tomatoes in boiling water until the skin cracks slightly, then quickly immerse them in cold water, peel off the skin, remove the stem, and cut into pieces no larger than 4 cm on each side. Set aside.
3. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt, and beat until well blended to form the egg mixture; a small amount of vinegar can be added to enhance fluffiness and reduce any eggy odor.
4. Place the pan over heat, pour in cooking oil, and heat it up.
5. When the oil reaches about 60% heat (moderately hot), pour in the egg mixture, quickly stir-fry until set and slightly golden, then remove and set aside.
6. Without cleaning the pan, add the tomato pieces directly and use the spatula to gently press and stir-fry for about 20 seconds until they soften and release juice.
7. Return the previously cooked eggs to the pan, stir-fry evenly with the tomatoes; ketchup and a small amount of water can be added for more sauce according to taste, or pair with cooked meat.
8. Add the remaining salt and sugar, sprinkle with scallions, and quickly stir-fry until well combined.
9. Turn off the heat, transfer to a plate, and serve.

## Health Tips
Tomatoes are rich in lycopene and vitamin C, while eggs provide high-quality protein. Their combination offers complementary nutrition, suitable for the whole family. It is recommended to use less oil and sugar, especially for those with high blood sugar, who may omit the sugar. Avoid overheating during cooking to preserve the nutrients in the tomatoes.
