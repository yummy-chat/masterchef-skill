# Tomato Tofu Soup
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍅 Tomato × 1
🥚 Egg × 1
🫘 Tofu × 100g
🫚 Ginger × 1 slice
🧅 Scallion × 0.5 stalk
🧂 Cooking oil × 5ml
🧂 Salt × 2g
🧂 Chicken bouillon × 2g
🧂 Starch × 5g

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥣 Bowl

## Cooking Steps
1. Dice the tomato into small pieces, crack the egg into a bowl and beat until smooth, cut the tofu into small cubes and set aside.
2. Heat cooking oil in a wok until hot, add the ginger slice, then after 5 seconds, add the diced tomato and stir-fry for 30 seconds.
3. Pour boiling water into the wok. Once the soup comes to a boil, after 60 seconds, slowly pour in the beaten egg and tofu cubes.
4. When the soup boils again, drizzle in the starch slurry and gently stir in the same direction for 2 rotations.
5. Add chicken bouillon, salt, and chopped scallion, then after 30 seconds, remove from heat and serve.

## Health Tips
This soup is low in fat and high in protein, rich in lycopene and calcium, making it suitable for weight loss periods or for those needing eye care. Tofu and eggs provide high-quality plant and animal protein, but note that thickening with starch may slightly raise the glycemic index; individuals with diabetes can reduce the amount as needed.
