# Beef Bone Soup Noodles
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥩 Beef back bones 1 box (about 6-8 pieces)  
🧅 Purple onion about half (sliced)  
🥚 Eggs 6  
🍜 Homemade wide noodles as needed  
🥬 Leafy greens (such as Shanghai bok choy) a few stalks  
🌶️ Dried chili peppers 3-4  
🍅 Tomato paste 1 small packet  
🧂 Light soy sauce 2 parts (for marinade)  
🧂 Dark soy sauce to taste  
🦪 Oyster sauce to taste  
🍬 Rock sugar to taste  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🌿 Black pepper to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil a little  
💧 Water 8 parts (for marinade)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Stand mixer with pasta roller  
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board  
🍳 Small saucepan  
🫙 Glass airtight jar  
🍲 Cast iron pot (or deep stew pot)  
🌀 Immersion blender  
🥄 Slotted spoon/strainer  
🔗 Tongs  
📄 Parchment paper  

## Steps
1. Make the noodles: Roll the dough through a pasta roller into thin sheets, fold and cut into wide noodles, dust with dry flour to prevent sticking, set aside.
2. Make soft-boiled marinated eggs:
   - Combine water, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, and rock sugar in a pot. Bring to a boil until the rock sugar is completely dissolved, then remove from heat and let cool.
   - Place eggs in cold water, bring to a boil, and cook for exactly 6 minutes 30 seconds after boiling. Remove and immediately cool under cold running water, then peel the shells.
   - Place the peeled eggs into the cooled marinade. Use a piece of parchment paper to press tightly into the jar opening so the eggs are fully submerged in the marinade. Refrigerate to soak.
3. Place beef back bones in cold water and blanch. Skim off any foam, then remove. Slice the purple onion.
4. Spray a little cooking oil into the pot, add the sliced onion and stir-fry until softened. Add the tomato paste and stir-fry evenly.
5. Add the beef back bones, pour in enough hot water to fully cover the ingredients. Add the dried chili peppers and a few star anise. Bring to a rolling boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer slowly until the beef is tender and falls off the bone.
6. Remove the dried chili peppers. Take a small amount of broth, add salt and black pepper, and use an immersion blender to blend into a fine chili puree. Pour it back into the soup and stir well, making the broth richer and smoother.
7. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and cook the wide noodles and greens until done. Remove and drain.
8. Place the noodles in a bowl, ladle in the beef soup, arrange large pieces of beef back bones and greens on top, add a soft-boiled egg cut in half, and sprinkle with black sesame seeds for garnish.

## Health Advice  
Beef bone soup noodles are rich in collagen and high-quality protein. Paired with soft-boiled eggs and seasonal vegetables, they offer balanced nutrition, suitable for those with high physical exertion. Note that the marinated eggs are high in sodium; individuals with high blood pressure are advised to reduce soup intake.
