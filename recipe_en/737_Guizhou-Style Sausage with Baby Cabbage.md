# Guizhou-Style Sausage with Baby Cabbage
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥬 Baby cabbage 300g
🌭 Guizhou-style sausage 200g

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Pot

## Cooking Steps
1. Pour 750ml of water into the pot and bring to a boil over high heat.
**Key point**: The water level should cover the ingredients; adjust the amount according to the size of the pot.

2. Add the Guizhou-style sausage and keep boiling over high heat for 13 minutes.
3. Add the baby cabbage and continue boiling for 2 minutes.
4. Turn off the heat, and use chopsticks or a slotted spoon to remove the sausage and baby cabbage.
5. Cut the baby cabbage into sections and slice the sausage, then arrange on a plate and serve.
**Key point**: If no knife is available, they can be eaten directly without cutting.

## Health Advice
Sausage is high in salt and fat; pairing it with light baby cabbage can balance the flavor. It is suitable for occasional indulgence, but people with high blood pressure should control their portion. The water used to boil the sausage contains a lot of salt and fat, so it is recommended to discard it directly.
