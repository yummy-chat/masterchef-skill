# Chinese Style Savory Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧂 Salt 3g
🍬 Sugar 5g
🌾 Cornstarch 10g
🥫 Soy Sauce 5g
🍗 Flavor Seasoning (such as chicken powder, cumin, pepper salt) 3g
🌾 All-purpose flour 200g
🥩 Ground meat 50g
🧄 Garlic 2 cloves
🧅 Scallion 1/4 (leafy part)
🥚 Egg 1 (optional)
🥕 Carrot (optional)
🛢️ Oil 30ml

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥘 Wok (can be replaced by a non-stick pan)

## Steps
1. Thaw the ground meat, then add half of the salt, sugar, soy sauce, flavor seasoning, and all the cornstarch. Mix well and marinate for 30 minutes.
2. Place the flour, egg, remaining salt, sugar, soy sauce, and flavor seasoning in a bowl. Add about 100ml of water and stir into a thick, pourable batter.
3. Mince the garlic.
4. Cut the scallion into sections.
5. Finely dice the carrot.
6. Heat the wok and pour in enough oil.
7. Once the oil is hot, add the minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.
8. Add the marinated ground meat and stir-fry until it changes color and is cooked through.
9. Add the diced carrot and continue stir-frying until the oil turns golden.
10. Turn off the heat and let it cool for 2 minutes.
11. Pour the cooked meat mixture into the raw batter and stir to combine.
12. Reheat the non-stick pan and brush a thin layer of oil on the bottom.
13. Reduce the heat to low, pour the batter into the pan, and swirl to spread it evenly to a moderate thickness.
14. While the top of the pancake is still uncooked, sprinkle the scallion sections over it.
15. Keep the heat low and cook until the bottom is set and firm.
16. Flip and continue cooking over low heat until the other side is set.
17. Cook each side for an additional 20 seconds.
18. Turn off the heat, remove, and plate.

## Health Advice
This savory pancake combines ground meat with carrot and minced garlic for a balanced nutrition. However, it uses a fair amount of oil for frying, so it is recommended to control the oil amount or serve with a vegetable salad. Suitable for those needing an energy boost; people with high blood lipids should consume in moderation.
