# Red Kidney Bean Rice Bowl  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All-season meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🧅 Dried red kidney beans  
🧅 Basmati rice or regular white rice  
🧅 Onion  
🧅 Tomato  
🧅 Ginger  
🧅 Garlic  
🧅 Green chili pepper  
🧅 Cilantro  
🧅 Bay leaves  
🧂 Cooking oil or ghee  
🧂 Turmeric powder  
🧂 Red chili powder  
🧂 Coriander powder  
🧂 Cumin powder  
🧂 Garam masala  
🧂 Salt  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Pressure cooker or regular soup pot  
🍳 Wok or skillet  
🍳 Rice cooker  

## Instructions  
1. Soak dried red kidney beans in water for at least 8 hours, then drain and set aside.  
2. In a pressure cooker, add the kidney beans and 600ml of water. Cover and heat over high heat until steam pressure builds, then reduce to medium heat and cook for 15–20 minutes until the beans are easily mashable.  
3. If using a regular pot, add the kidney beans and enough water to cover, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and cook for 60–90 minutes until fully soft. Reserve the cooking liquid.  
4. In a wok, heat 30ml of oil over medium heat. Add 2 bay leaves and fry until fragrant.  
5. Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.  
6. Add the grated ginger and garlic, and sauté for 1 minute.  
7. Add the chopped tomato, and cook until fully soft and the oil begins to separate.  
8. Add 3g turmeric powder, 5g red chili powder, 5g coriander powder, 3g cumin powder, and 6g salt. Sauté for 2 minutes to blend the flavors.  
9. Pour in the cooked kidney beans along with their liquid, add the green chili pepper, and stir well. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 15–20 minutes.  
10. Use the back of a spatula to mash some of the beans to thicken the sauce. Stir in 3g garam masala, turn off the heat, and sprinkle with chopped cilantro.  
11. Rinse the rice, place it in a rice cooker with 400ml of water, and cook until done.  
12. Serve the rice in bowls, top with the kidney bean curry, and optionally accompany with onion salad and lemon wedges.  

## Health Tips  
Red kidney beans are rich in dietary fiber and plant protein. Paired with whole-grain rice, this dish helps stabilize blood sugar, making it suitable for vegetarians and those managing blood sugar levels. Note that beans must be thoroughly cooked to avoid potential food poisoning.
