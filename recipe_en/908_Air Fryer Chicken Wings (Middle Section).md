# Air Fryer Chicken Wings (Middle Section)
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍗 Chicken wings (middle section, pre-marinated, preferably in Orleans-style seasoning)
🌿 Dried basil (optional)
🧂 Yunnan single-mountain dipping powder (optional, as a substitute for BBQ seasoning)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔥 Air fryer
📦 Aluminum foil baking tray
🧻 Plastic wrap

## Cooking Steps
1. Remove the chicken wings from the refrigerator, place them skin-side down into an aluminum foil baking tray, sprinkle with dried basil, cover with plastic wrap, and thaw at room temperature for 20 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Ensure sufficient thawing time so the inside of the chicken wings is completely softened before cooking, otherwise they may become burnt on the outside while remaining raw on the inside.

2. Remove the plastic wrap, place the baking tray into the air fryer, set the temperature to 200°C, and cook for 10 minutes.
   **Key Point**: It is recommended to use an air fryer with precise temperature control to avoid burning due to temperature fluctuations.

3. Take out the baking tray, flip the chicken wings, sprinkle with dried basil again, and continue cooking at 200°C for 7 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Dried basil is mainly used for aroma and decoration, and can be omitted; the dipping powder can be served separately at mealtime to enhance flavor.

## Health Tips
Chicken wings themselves are not low in fat, but the air fryer can render out some of the oil, making them relatively healthier. Note that the marinated chicken wings are high in sodium, so it is recommended to serve with a vegetable salad or dip in vinegar. Suitable for busy office workers and those seeking quick, low-oil cooking.
