# Steamed Shredded Potato and Egg Cake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥔 Potato (cut into even thin shreds)  
🥚 3 eggs  
🥔 One tablespoon of potato starch (enough to coat the shredded potatoes)  
🧄 Appropriate amount of minced garlic  
🧅 Appropriate amount of chopped scallions  
🌶️ Appropriate amount of millet peppers  
🧂 Appropriate amount of salt  
🫘 One tablespoon of sesame seeds  
🌶️ One tablespoon of chili flakes  
🛢️ Appropriate amount of hot oil (for pouring over toppings)  
🧂 Appropriate amount of MSG  
🦪 Appropriate amount of oyster sauce  
🥄 Half a tablespoon of light soy sauce  
🍶 Half a tablespoon of rice vinegar  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Strainer  
🍽️ Deep dish plate  
♨️ Steamer  
🔥 Gas stove  
🥣 Small bowl  
🍳 Small pot (wok ladle)  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Instructions
1.  Cut the potato into even thin shreds, rinse several times with clean water, drain, then place in a bowl. Add one tablespoon of potato starch and mix thoroughly, ensuring each shred is evenly coated with starch.
   **Note**: It is recommended to add a little salt to the potato shreds before mixing with starch to create a base flavor, which improves the taste.
2.  Spread the evenly coated potato shreds into a deep dish plate, press flat, and make three small indentations. Crack one egg into each indentation.
3.  Place the plate into the steamer basket, invert another plate on top as a cover, and close the lid tightly.
4.  Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and steam for 10 minutes.
   **Key point**: Covering with a plate prevents dripping water, keeping the cake dry and firm; do not steam for too long to maintain the elasticity of the potato shreds.
5.  While steaming, prepare the sauce: take a small bowl, add minced garlic, chopped scallions, millet peppers, sesame seeds, and chili flakes. Pour in hot oil to release the aroma.
6.  Then add salt, MSG, oyster sauce, light soy sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir well to combine into a sauce.
7.  Remove the steamed potato cake, let it cool naturally, then invert onto a cutting board. Cut into bite-sized pieces and arrange on a serving plate.
8.  Evenly drizzle the prepared sauce over the potato cake pieces. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice  
This steamed shredded potato and egg cake is steamed instead of fried, making it low in fat and filling, suitable for weight loss periods or those with poor digestion. Potatoes are rich in potassium, and eggs provide high-quality protein. However, it is recommended to use the oil, salt, and sauces in the sauce in moderation to avoid excessive sodium intake.
