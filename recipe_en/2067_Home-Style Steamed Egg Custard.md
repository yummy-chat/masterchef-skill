# Home-Style Steamed Egg Custard

Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥚 3 eggs  
💧 Boiling water (as needed, to mix with the egg mixture)  
🧂 A pinch of salt  
🫙 Soy sauce (or steamed fish soy sauce), to taste  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🫗 Strainer  
🍚 Rice cooker  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Crack the eggs into a glass bowl and whisk thoroughly with chopsticks until the egg mixture is even.  
2. Pour boiling water into the egg mixture, add a pinch of salt, stir well, and skim off any foam from the surface using a fine strainer.  
   **Key point**: Using boiling water to mix with the egg mixture makes the steamed egg custard more tender and smooth; skimming off the foam ensures a smooth and glossy surface.  
3. Place the bowl with the egg mixture into the rice cooker, cover, and steam until the egg mixture is fully set.  
   **Note**: The specific steaming time should be adjusted based on the heat of the rice cooker to avoid overcooking, which can make the texture tough.  
4. Remove the steamed egg custard, make a few cuts on the surface, and drizzle with soy sauce before serving.  

## Health Advice  
This steamed egg custard is rich in protein and low in fat, making it suitable for the elderly and children to supplement nutrition. Using boiling water to mix the egg mixture gives the custard a silky, pudding-like texture, but be mindful of the salt amount—those with high blood pressure should reduce salt intake.
