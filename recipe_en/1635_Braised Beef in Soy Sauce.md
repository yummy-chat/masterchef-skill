# Braised Beef in Soy Sauce
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef shank 3 jin (cut into large pieces)  
🫚 Ginger slices appropriate amount (used for soaking, blanching, and braising)  
🧅 Scallions appropriate amount (for blanching)  
🍶 Cooking wine appropriate amount (for blanching to remove gamey flavor)  
🌿 10 types of spices: star anise, Sichuan peppercorns, cinnamon, bay leaves, fennel seeds, angelica root, black cardamom, lemongrass, dried chili peppers, dried hawthorn slices (each in appropriate amount)  
🍬 Rock sugar appropriate amount  
🧂 Soy sauce (light/ dark soy sauce) about one bowl  
🥄 Sweet bean paste appropriate amount  
🧂 Table salt appropriate amount  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife  
🥣 Stainless steel basin (for soaking beef)  
🍳 Wok (for blanching)  
🍲 Braising pot (with lid, for braising)  
🥄 Soup ladle (for skimming foam and stirring)  

## Steps  
1. **Soak to remove blood**: Cut the beef shank into large pieces, place them in a basin, add ginger slices, and pour in cold water until the beef is fully submerged. Soak for 1-2 hours until the blood has fully seeped out, then remove and rinse clean.  
   **Key**: Must soak in cold water for a long time; this is the core step for removing gamey flavor and excess blood.  

2. **Blanch to set shape**: Place the soaked beef in a pot with cold water, add ginger slices, scallion segments, and cooking wine. Bring to a boil over high heat, promptly skim off the foam, and continue boiling for about 10 minutes. Remove the beef and transfer it to a braising pot, reserving the original broth for later use.  
   **Key**: The original broth from cooking the meat is the soul of the braising liquid; do not discard it.  

3. **Slow braise over low heat**: Add the spices, rock sugar, soy sauce, ginger slices, sweet bean paste, and salt to the braising pot. Then pour in the reserved original broth, ensuring the liquid fully covers the beef. Bring to a boil over high heat, then turn to low heat, cover, and simmer for 2 hours, until a chopstick can easily pierce through the meat without it falling apart.  
   **Key**: Do not remove the beef immediately after turning off the heat; let it soak in the braising liquid overnight so the color and savory flavor fully penetrate, resulting in better texture.  

4. **Slice and enjoy**: Remove the braised beef shank, slice it, and serve directly. It can also be paired with noodles or used in cold dishes.  

## Health Advice  
Braised beef in soy sauce is a high-protein, low-fat quality choice, suitable for muscle building and weight loss. However, it is high in sodium, so those with high blood pressure should consume it in moderation.
