# Salt and Pepper Enoki Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients  
🍄 Enoki mushrooms, to taste (torn into small clusters)  
🌽 Cornstarch, about 1 tablespoon  
🌾 Flour, about 1 tablespoon  
🫑 Green and red bell peppers, to taste (finely diced)  
🧅 Onion, to taste (finely diced)  

## Seasonings  
🧂 Fine salt, 10g (for soaking)  
🧂 Salt, 3g (added to coating mix)  
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste (drizzled into batter)  
🧂 Salt and pepper seasoning, to taste (sprinkled after cooking)  
🌶 BBQ seasoning, to taste (sprinkled after cooking)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🪣 Large bowl/stainless steel bowl  
🥄 Slotted spoon/strainer  
🍳 Wok/frying pan  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍽️ Plate  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Instructions
1. **Prepare the main ingredient**: Tear the whole cluster of enoki mushrooms into small clusters for better flavor and thorough frying.
2. **Wash and soak**: Add 10g of fine salt to clean water, soak the enoki mushrooms for 10 minutes, then remove and drain thoroughly.
3. **Make the dry coating**: In a bowl, combine about 1 tablespoon of cornstarch, 1 tablespoon of flour, and 3g of salt. Mix well with your hands.
4. **Coat with dry mix**: Place the drained enoki mushrooms into the dry coating mix, evenly cover them with a thin layer, shake off excess powder, and arrange on a plate for later use.
5. **Prepare the batter**: Gradually add water to the remaining dry coating mix, stirring until it reaches a yogurt-like consistency. Then add the finely diced green and red bell peppers and onion, drizzle in a little cooking oil, and stir until evenly combined.
6. **First fry**: Heat oil to 170°C (340°F). Add the coated enoki mushrooms and fry over medium-low heat until they set, float, and the mushroom strands bloom like flowers.
7. **Add the batter**: Once the enoki mushrooms have set and opened up, evenly drizzle the vegetable batter over the oil in the wok using your hand, allowing the batter to adhere to the mushroom strands for added color and aroma.
8. **Fry until cooked**: Maintain medium-low heat, gently stir with chopsticks, and fry until a light golden brown and the batter is fully cooked.
9. **Remove and drain oil**: Use a slotted spoon to remove the mushrooms and drain excess oil.
10. **Second fry for crispiness**: Raise the oil temperature to 180°C (355°F). Return the enoki mushrooms to the oil and fry for about 20–30 seconds, until a distinct golden brown and the surface is crispy.
11. **Season and serve**: After the second fry, quickly remove and drain oil. Plate, then evenly sprinkle with salt and pepper seasoning and BBQ seasoning.

## Health Advice  
This dish is high in fiber and low in fat, but the frying process significantly increases its calorie content. It is recommended to pair it with a light, refreshing salad to balance the oil. Thoroughly draining the mushrooms and the second fry are key to achieving crispiness. Best enjoyed hot for optimal flavor, as it tends to soften upon cooling.
