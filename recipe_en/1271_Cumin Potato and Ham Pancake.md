# Cumin Potato and Ham Pancake
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Year Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potato
🌭 Ham Sausage
🫓 Hand-Pulled Dough Wrap
🥚 Egg
🫘 Black Sesame
🫒 Cooking Oil
🧂 Salt
🧄 Minced Garlic
🧅 White Scallion
🌿 Cumin Seeds
🍾 Light Soy Sauce
🌶️ Chili Powder
💧 Water
🫗 Sesame Oil
🌿 Ground Cumin
🥡 Cornstarch Slurry
🧅 Green Scallion

## Cookware and Tools
🍳 Wok
🥄 Wooden or Wok Spatula
🫗 Brush
🔥 Oven
📜 Aluminum Foil
🥘 Baking Tray
🔪 Cutting Board
🫥 Plastic Wrap

## Instructions
1. Peel the potato and cut into uniform small cubes; dice the ham sausage and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a wok, add the potato cubes with a pinch of salt, and stir-fry until the edges are slightly golden and tenderized.
   **Key Point**: Potatoes must be stir-fried first to ensure they are fully cooked.
3. Add the diced ham sausage and continue stir-frying, then add minced garlic, white scallion, and cumin seeds until fragrant.
   **Note**: The cumin flavor needs to be fully released during stir-frying.
4. Drizzle light soy sauce along the edge of the wok and stir-fry quickly; add chili powder and stir-fry until red oil and spicy aroma emerge.
5. Pour in a small amount of water, add a few drops of sesame oil, sprinkle with ground cumin, and stir-fry evenly.
   **Key Point**: Adding water helps the seasonings meld and prepares for the subsequent thickening.
6. Drizzle in a small amount of cornstarch slurry, stir-fry quickly until the sauce thickens and evenly coats the ingredients, then turn off the heat.
   **Note**: The filling should be thick but not watery, making it easy to wrap.
7. Add the green scallion, stir briefly, and remove from the wok.
8. Take one sheet of hand-pulled dough wrap, keep the middle plastic divider in place, and place an appropriate amount of filling in the center of the wrap. Use the plastic divider to fold the wrap from all sides toward the middle, pinch the seams tightly to seal, forming a square or round patty.
   **Key Point**: The dough wrap is soft; using the plastic divider prevents sticking and aids shaping; ensure seams are tightly sealed to prevent filling leakage during baking.
9. Place the sealed patties seam-side down on a baking tray lined with aluminum foil, brush the surface evenly with egg yolk, and sprinkle with black sesame seeds.
10. Preheat the oven to 200°C, place the baking tray on the middle rack, and bake for about 15 minutes until the surface is golden and crispy.
    **Note**: Baking time and temperature should be adjusted according to your oven conditions.

## Health Advice
Potatoes provide carbohydrates, the hand-pulled dough wrap is high in fat, ham sausage has high sodium content, and overall calorie content is relatively high. It is recommended to pair with unsweetened tea or a vegetable salad to balance nutrition and consume in moderation.
