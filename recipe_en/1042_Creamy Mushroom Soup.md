# Creamy Mushroom Soup
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🧈 Ingredients
- White mushrooms: 200 g
- Onion: 50 g
- Butter: 15 g
- Flour: 10 g
- Milk: 200 ml
- Heavy cream: 30 ml
- Water: 100 ml

🧂 Seasonings
- Salt: 2 g
- Black pepper, ground: 1 g

🍳 Kitchenware
- Frying pan
- Food processor (optional)

## Instructions
1. Slice the white mushrooms thinly and finely dice the onion. Set aside.
2. Place a frying pan over low heat, add the butter and heat until melted. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent.
3. Add the sliced mushrooms and stir-fry until they release liquid and soften. Sprinkle in the flour and stir quickly and evenly, ensuring the ingredients are coated with the flour.
4. Pour in the milk and water, stirring continuously. Keep over low heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent sticking to the bottom.
5. Reduce to low heat and continue cooking for about 10 minutes, until the soup thickens.
6. Drizzle in the heavy cream and continue heating for 1 minute. Season with salt and ground black pepper, stir well, then remove from heat.
7. For a smoother texture, blend the soup with a food processor until velvety smooth.

## Notes
- Reserve a small amount of mushroom slices and pan-fry them until browned in advance to use as a garnish when serving, enhancing aroma and texture.
- If substituting water with chicken broth, the flavor will be richer. It is recommended to keep the total liquid volume within 300 ml.
- For a thicker consistency, you may increase the amount of flour and heavy cream.
- It is best to consume immediately after preparation; not recommended for refrigeration or freezing.

## Health Advice
This creamy mushroom soup is rich in protein and calcium, making it suitable for children or individuals who need an energy boost. However, butter and heavy cream are high in calories; during weight loss, it is recommended to reduce the amounts or substitute with low-fat milk.
