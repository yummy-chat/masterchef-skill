# Green Pepper Fish Fillets
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 Black fish fillets, appropriate amount (about 1 pound of fish, sliced, pre-marinated)
🌶️ Twist peppers (green peppers), appropriate amount (sliced into rings)
🧄 Minced garlic, appropriate amount
🌶️ Dried chili pepper, 1 piece (cut into sections)
🧅 Scallions, appropriate amount (cut into sections)
🍲 Black fermented soybeans, 1 tablespoon
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount (used in batches)
🍶 Cooking wine, appropriate amount
🌿 White pepper powder, appropriate amount
🌾 Cornstarch, appropriate amount
🫗 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount
🦪 Oyster sauce, appropriate amount
🫒 Cooking oil, appropriate amount

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Frying pan
🥄 Spatula
🫗 Strainer basket
🔪 Cutting board and chef's knife
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Seasoning spoon
🫖 Kettle (for blanching)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Marinate the Fish Fillets**: Drain the fish fillets of excess water. Add salt, cooking wine, white pepper powder, and cornstarch. Use your hands to thoroughly mix until the seasonings evenly coat the fish fillets. Let sit to marinate for about 10 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Mixing must be even. The cornstarch helps lock in moisture, making the fish fillets more tender and smooth.

2. **Blanch the Fish Fillets**: Bring water in a pot to a boil. Add the marinated fish fillets and blanch until they change color, then immediately remove and drain.
   **Note**: Do not blanch for too long. Stop as soon as the fillets change color to prevent them from becoming tough.

3. **Fry the Base Aromatics**: Pour cooking oil into a frying pan and heat over high heat. Add the minced garlic and dried chili pepper sections, stir-frying quickly until fragrant.
   **Key Point**: Do not let the dried chili peppers burn. Remove from heat as soon as the aroma is released.

4. **Stir-fry Green Peppers and Black Fermented Soybeans**: Add the green pepper rings and stir-fry over high heat until just cooked. Then add the black fermented soybeans and stir-fry until a rich, savory aroma is released.
   **Key Point**: The black fermented soybeans must be stir-fried first to fully release their salty and savory flavor.

5. **Combine and Stir-fry the Fish Fillets**: Add the blanched fish fillets. Season with salt, light soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Stir-fry quickly over high heat, ensuring the seasonings coat all ingredients evenly.
   **Note**: Use high heat throughout and stir-fry quickly. Do not cook the fish fillets for too long to maintain their tender texture.

6. **Plate and Serve**: Sprinkle in the scallion sections, stir-fry a few times quickly, then turn off the heat and serve.

## Health Advice
Green pepper fish fillets are high in protein and low in fat. The combination of black fermented soybeans and green peppers can help stimulate appetite and aid digestion, making this dish suitable for people on a weight-loss diet or those with weaker teeth. It is recommended to reduce the amount of salt used, as the fermented soybeans and oyster sauce already provide saltiness. Note that after blanching, the fish fillets should be stir-fried quickly over high heat to keep them tender and smooth.
