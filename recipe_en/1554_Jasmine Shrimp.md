# Jasmine Shrimp
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

🍶 Yellow wine (Shaoxing wine) 适量 (for removing fishiness, added during coating)  
🌽 Cornstarch a small amount (about a pinch for coating, a little for sauce)  
🍄 Songxianxian (or chicken bouillon) a small amount (for enhancing freshness)  
🌶️ Ground white pepper a small amount  
🍬 Granulated sugar a pinch  
🫒 Cooking oil 适量 (for oiling and as base oil)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🥣 Glass bowl (for marinating and mixing sauce)  
🥄 Metal spoon (for adding seasonings and stirring)  
🍳 Wok  
🥢 Slotted spoon  

## Steps  
1. Add salt, yellow wine, and a small amount of cornstarch to the shrimp. Mix well with your hands until the shrimp feel springy and flavorful.  
2. In an empty bowl, combine salt, songxianxian, white pepper, a pinch of sugar, an appropriate amount of jasmine tea liquid, and a small amount of cornstarch. Stir until well blended to make the sauce. Set aside.  
   **Key point**: Prepare the sauce in advance for quick stir-frying over high heat.  
3. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the coated shrimp and blanch until they just begin to curl. Immediately remove with a slotted spoon.  
4. In another pan, heat oil to 50% hot (about 150°C or 300°F). Add the blanched shrimp and quickly oil-blast them, then immediately remove.  
   **Note**: The purpose of oil-blasting is to enhance the shrimp's texture; work quickly.  
5. Leave a small amount of oil in the pan. Pour in the prepared sauce and heat until it thickens. Add the oil-blasted shrimp and jasmine flower buds. Stir-fry rapidly over high heat until evenly coated, then immediately remove from the pan.  
   **Key point**: Quick stir-frying over high heat preserves the shrimp's tenderness and the jasmine's fragrance.  

## Health Tips  
Shrimp are high in protein and low in fat, while jasmine has a calming and refreshing effect, making this dish suitable for all seasons. Ensure the shrimp are coated well and stir-fried quickly over high heat to avoid a tough texture.
