# Braised Beef and Dried Tofu Sticks in Clay Pot
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn and Winter meals  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef slices, to taste (pre-sliced thin, rinsed and drained)  
🫘 Fried dried tofu sticks, 2 pieces (pre-soaked until soft)  
🧅 Ginger, 1 small piece (sliced)  
🧅 Scallions, to taste (chopped)  
🧄 Garlic, to taste (minced)  
🥜 Shacha sauce, about 2 tablespoons (divided for marinating and sauce)  
🦪 Oyster sauce, to taste  
🧂 Soy sauce, to taste  
🌿 White pepper powder, to taste  
🥔 Cornstarch, 1 tablespoon  
💧 Water, a small amount (for sauce)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍲 Clay pot  
🥢 Chopsticks or gloves for marinating  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl or basin (for marinating and mixing sauce)  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🥄 Spoon  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Rinse the beef slices and drain off excess water. Add 1 tablespoon of shacha sauce, oyster sauce to taste, soy sauce to taste, white pepper powder to taste, and 1 tablespoon of cornstarch. Use your hands to mix thoroughly, ensuring each slice is evenly coated. Marinate for at least 15 minutes.  
   **Note**: Ensure every slice of beef is evenly coated with the marinade. Cornstarch helps retain moisture, making the texture more tender.  
2. In a small bowl, combine 1 tablespoon of shacha sauce, oyster sauce to taste, and a small amount of water. Stir well to prepare the braising sauce for later use.  
3. Tear the softened dried tofu sticks into bite-sized pieces and spread them evenly on the bottom of the clay pot.  
4. Lay the marinated beef slices flat on top of the dried tofu sticks. Sprinkle with ginger slices, minced garlic, and chopped scallions. Finally, pour the prepared sauce evenly over the top.  
5. Cover the clay pot with its lid. Braise over medium-low heat until the sauce comes to a boil and the beef is fully cooked through and has changed color. The dried tofu sticks should have absorbed the sauce and become soft.  
   **Key**: Clay pots have strong heat retention, so use medium-low heat to avoid scorching the bottom or drying out the sauce too quickly.  
6. Turn off the heat and serve directly in the clay pot at the table. The dried tofu sticks will be tender and flavorful, while the beef is smooth and silky.  

## Health Tips  
The combination of dried tofu sticks and beef provides calcium and iron, making it ideal for warming the body in autumn and winter. However, shacha sauce has a high sodium content, so people with high blood pressure should consume the broth sparingly. Adding cornstarch to the beef during marinating helps lock in moisture, but the marinating time should not exceed 30 minutes, or the meat may become tough.
