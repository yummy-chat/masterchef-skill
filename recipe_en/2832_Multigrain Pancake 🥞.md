# Multigrain Pancake 🥞  
Cuisine: Shandong Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 1000g bread flour  
🌽 150g corn flour  
🟢 75g mung bean flour  
🫘 150g soybean flour  
🥚 1 egg per serving  
🥬 Lettuce, as needed  
🥔 A handful of shredded potatoes (pre-cooked)  
🥓 1 piece of crispy fried cracker  
🍗 Meat floss, as needed  
🍄 Spicy enoki mushrooms, as needed  
🌭 1 sausage  
🍢 Sichuan-style chicken tenders, as needed  
🧅 Chopped scallions, as needed  
🌿 Cilantro, as needed  
🧂 10g salt  
🧂 2g thirteen-spice powder  
🧂 2g baking soda  
🧂 3g edible alkali  
💧 1400g water (for batter)  

🧄 200g garlic chili sauce  
🍯 50g sweet bean sauce  
🦪 25g oyster sauce  
🧉 40g chu hou paste  
🦐 40g seafood sauce  
🍲 80g chicken bouillon  
🧂 30g light soy sauce  
🧂 6g MSG  
🍬 100g white sugar  
🧂 5g Nande seasoning  
💧 130g water (for sauce)  
🫒 60g cooking oil  
🧅 10g each of minced scallion, ginger, and garlic  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for batter)  
🍵 Glass bowl (for sauce)  
🍳 Wok (for simmering sauce)  
🔥 Special pancake griddle  
🪥 Homemade oil applicator (spice shaker + new cloth)  
🪚 Bamboo dragonfly / spreader  
🔪 Metal spatula  
🖌️ Brush  
🔪 Knife  
⚖️ Kitchen scale  

## Instructions  
1. **Prepare the secret sauce**: In a bowl, combine garlic chili sauce, sweet bean sauce, oyster sauce, chu hou paste, seafood sauce, chicken bouillon, light soy sauce, MSG, white sugar, Nande seasoning, and 130g water. Stir thoroughly until uniform. Set aside.  
2. **Cook the sauce**: Heat a wok, add 60g cooking oil, and sauté minced scallion, ginger, and garlic until fragrant. Pour in the mixed sauce, bring to a boil over high heat until bubbles form, then immediately remove from heat and transfer to a bowl.  
   **Note**: Use sufficient heat and stop as soon as it boils—avoid prolonged cooking.  
3. **Prepare the multigrain batter**: Mix bread flour, corn flour, mung bean flour, soybean flour, baking soda, edible alkali, salt, and thirteen-spice powder. Add 1400g water and stir into a smooth, lump-free batter. Let rest for 1 hour. The rested batter should be thick and form a ribbon when poured.  
4. **Make the oil applicator**: Roll a clean cloth tightly and stuff it into a spice shaker, leaving the cloth slightly above the rim. This is used to apply an ultra-thin layer of oil to the griddle.  
5. **Preheat and oil the griddle**: Fully preheat the pancake griddle, then quickly wipe a thin layer of oil using the applicator.  
   **Key**: Use minimal oil to prevent the batter from sliding and not spreading properly.  
6. **Spread the pancake**: Pour a ladle of batter onto the center of the griddle. Immediately use the bamboo dragonfly to spread it into a thin, even circle with a rotating motion.  
7. **Add and spread the egg**: While the pancake surface is still slightly uncooked, crack an egg on top. Use the bamboo dragonfly to quickly break the yolk and spread it evenly across the entire pancake.  
8. **Add toppings and fold**: Just before the egg sets, sprinkle chopped scallions, cilantro, and meat floss. Use a spatula to lift the edges and fold the pancake in half into a semicircle.  
   **Key**: Use the egg's stickiness to secure the toppings.  
9. **Brush sauce and add fillings**: Evenly brush a layer of the cooked sauce onto the folded pancake. Layer lettuce, shredded potatoes, crispy fried cracker, spicy enoki mushrooms, sausage, and Sichuan-style chicken tenders in sequence.  
10. **Roll and cut**: Firmly roll the pancake from the filled side into a tight log. Use a spatula and knife to cut it in half. Serve with the cut sides facing up on a plate.  

## Health Tips  
Multigrain pancakes combine various flours, eggs, meat, vegetables, and homemade sauce, offering comprehensive nutrition but relatively high calories. They are ideal for a breakfast energy boost. Be mindful of the amount of crispy cracker and sauce consumed. Adding lettuce and shredded potatoes increases dietary fiber. It is recommended to reduce the amount of sausage and chicken tenders.
