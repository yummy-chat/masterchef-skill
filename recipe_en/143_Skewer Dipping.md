# Skewer Dipping

Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Season: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
**Basic Batter**
🌾 Low-gluten flour 4g (or all-purpose flour 20g)
🌽 Cornstarch 4g (or 20g)
💧 Water 120ml (or 160ml)

**General**
🍢 Various skewer ingredients (vegetables, meatballs, sliced meat, etc.) as needed

**Sweet and Spicy Sauce**
🫒 Vegetable oil 10g
🌶️ Chili dipping powder 5g
🌶️ Coarse chili flakes 5g
🧂 Matsutake mushroom seasoning 1g
🧂 Salt 2g
🍬 White sugar 5g
🫘 Sweet bean paste 15g
💧 Water 250ml
🌾 Low-gluten flour 4g (for thickening)
🌽 Cornstarch 4g (for thickening)
🫘 Sesame seeds 5g

**Garlic and Spicy Sauce**
🫒 Vegetable oil 10g
🧄 Minced garlic 20g (half raw, half cooked)
🌿 Sichuan pepper powder 1.5g
🌿 Five-spice powder 2g
🌶️ Chili dipping powder 5g
🧂 Light soy sauce 5g
🦪 Oyster sauce 5g
🍬 White sugar 3g
🧂 Salt 3g
💧 Water 250ml
🌾 Low-gluten flour 4g (for thickening)
🌽 Cornstarch 4g (for thickening)
🫘 Sesame seeds 5g

**Cumin Sauce**
🫒 Sesame oil 10g
🌿 Cumin powder 3g
🌿 Five-spice powder 1.5g
🌿 Sichuan pepper powder 1.5g
🌿 White pepper powder 1.5g
🧂 Salt 3g
🧂 Light soy sauce 8g
💧 Water 250ml
🌾 Low-gluten flour 4g (for thickening)
🌽 Cornstarch 4g (for thickening)

**Sesame Paste Sauce**
🫘 Sesame paste 65g
🥜 Peanut butter 15g
🧂 Soy sauce 15g
🦪 Oyster sauce 5g
🍬 White sugar 5g
🧂 Salt 2g
💧 Warm water 160ml
🌾 Low-gluten flour 1g (for thickening)
🌽 Cornstarch 1g (for thickening)
🫘 Sesame seeds 3g

**Sweet and Sour Sauce**
🍇 Black vinegar 125g
💧 Water 125ml
🌶️ Fresh chili peppers 15g (chopped)
🍬 White sugar 15g
🧂 Salt 1g
🫘 Toasted sesame seeds 3g

**Skewer Cooking Seasoning**
🫒 Vegetable oil as needed
🧂 Salt as needed

## Cookware and Tools
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥘 Stockpot
🥄 Whisk
🥄 Silicone spatula
🥣 Mixing bowl or glass bowl
🥛 Measuring cup or measuring spoon

## Steps
1. **Prepare the basic batter**: Mix low-gluten flour, cornstarch, and water in a ratio of approximately 1:1:3. Stir until smooth with no lumps. Set aside.
2. **Assemble skewers**: Thread vegetables, meatballs, sliced meat, and other ingredients onto skewers in bite-sized portions. Set aside.
3. **Make the sweet and spicy sauce**:
   - Heat 10g of vegetable oil in a pot over low heat. Add chili dipping powder and coarse chili flakes, and stir-fry until fragrant.
   - Add sweet bean paste and stir well.
   - Pour in 250ml of water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Season with matsutake mushroom seasoning, salt, and white sugar.
   - Slowly pour in the prepared batter while stirring continuously. Cook until the sauce thickens.
   - Sprinkle in sesame seeds before removing from heat.
4. **Make the garlic and spicy sauce**:
   - Heat 10g of vegetable oil in a pot over low heat. Stir-fry 3/4 of the minced garlic until golden. Add Sichuan pepper powder, five-spice powder, and chili dipping powder, and stir-fry until fragrant.
   - Pour in 250ml of water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Season with light soy sauce, oyster sauce, white sugar, and salt.
   - Slowly pour in the thickening batter while stirring continuously until the sauce thickens.
   - Remove from heat, mix in the remaining 1/4 raw minced garlic, and sprinkle in sesame seeds.
5. **Make the cumin sauce**:
   - Heat 10g of sesame oil in a pot over low heat. Stir-fry cumin powder, five-spice powder, Sichuan pepper powder, and white pepper powder until fragrant.
   - Pour in 250ml of water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Season with salt and light soy sauce.
   - Slowly pour in the thickening batter and stir until the sauce thickens.
6. **Make the sesame paste sauce**:
   - Add sesame paste and peanut butter to a pot. Heat over extremely low heat and stir continuously to prevent scorching.
   - Gradually add warm water in batches to thin the sesame paste.
   - Bring to a boil, then season with soy sauce, oyster sauce, white sugar, and salt.
   - Finally, add a very small amount of thickening batter, stir a few times, and sprinkle in sesame seeds before removing from heat.
   **Note**: Sesame paste scorches easily; keep the heat low and stir constantly throughout. Use less thickening batter than for other sauces.
7. **Make the sweet and sour sauce**:
   - Combine black vinegar, water, chopped fresh chili peppers, white sugar, and salt in a pot. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat.
   - Sprinkle in toasted sesame seeds. No thickening needed. Can be served hot or cold.
8. **Cook the skewers**:
   - Bring water to a boil in a stockpot. Add an appropriate amount of vegetable oil and salt.
   - Place the assembled skewers into the boiling water. Cook until done according to the characteristics of each ingredient. Remove and drain.
9. **Enjoy**: Dip the cooked skewers directly into the prepared sauces. Dip and eat as you go.

## Health Advice
This skewer dipping dish has rich sauces but a high sodium content. It is recommended to pair with plain vegetable skewers to balance intake. People with high blood pressure should reduce dipping into the sweet and spicy sauce and the garlic and spicy sauce. For weight control, prefer the sweet and sour sauce or the sesame paste sauce, and use less of the sauces thickened with batter.
