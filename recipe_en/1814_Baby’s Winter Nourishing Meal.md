# Baby’s Winter Nourishing Meal
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Winter (November–December)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍚 Rice, about one bowl (cooked)  
🐟 Salmon, 2 thin slices (raw)  
🥕 Cherry radishes, 3 pieces (whole)  
🍄 Button mushrooms, 3–4 pieces (sliced or cubed)  
🍖 Lamb chop, 1 small piece (bone-in)  
🥬 Celtuce (stem lettuce), about 1/4 root (peeled and shaved into thin slices)  
🥬 Lettuce, 2–3 leaves (torn into pieces by hand)  
🦐 Dried shrimp,适量  
🥚 Egg, 1 (raw)  
🫗 Dark seasoning sauce (e.g., soy sauce),适量 (spray over after steaming)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Ceramic bowl with handle  
🔪 Peeler, small knife  
🍲 Steamer (electric steamer)  
🥄 Spoon

## Steps
1. Place the cooked rice into the ceramic bowl.
2. Lay the raw salmon slices flat on top of the rice.
3. Add the bone-in lamb chop, whole cherry radishes, sliced button mushrooms, shaved celtuce slices, and torn lettuce leaves in order.
4. Sprinkle in the dried shrimp, then crack the raw egg on top.  
   **Note**: Distribute the ingredients as evenly as possible in the bowl to ensure uniform heating.
5. Place the filled ceramic bowl into the steamer and steam over medium-high heat for about 15 minutes.  
   **Doneness check**: The lamb chop is cooked through, the egg is set, and all ingredients are soft.
6. Carefully remove the bowl after steaming, and immediately spray the dark seasoning sauce evenly over the food surface.
7. Use a spoon to thoroughly stir all the ingredients in the bowl, mixing the rice, vegetables, meat, and seasoning sauce evenly, then serve to the baby.

## Health Tips
Salmon, lamb chop, and egg provide high-quality protein, iron, and zinc, suitable for boosting your baby’s immunity in winter. Cherry radishes and celtuce are rich in vitamins and become soft and easy to digest after steaming. Dried shrimp are high in sodium; soak them in water beforehand to reduce salt content before serving to your baby.
