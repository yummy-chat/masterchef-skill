# Eight-Shrimp Broth Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🦐 Large shrimp 8 (separate shells from meat, butterfly the shrimp meat)  
🍜 Instant noodle cake 1 piece  
🧅 Scallion to taste (finely shredded, mixed with sesame oil)  
🧂 Salt a pinch (for marinating shrimp meat)  
🌶️ Pepper to taste (for marinating shrimp meat)  
🍶 Cooking wine to taste (for marinating shrimp meat)  
🫒 Cooking oil to taste (for extracting shrimp oil and brushing the pot)  
🍲 Chicken broth seasoning packet 1 pack (Baixiang Old Hen Chicken Noodle seasoning packet)  
🫗 Sesame oil to taste (for mixing shredded scallions and finishing)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🫕 Small clay pot  
🔪 Knife  
🥣 Metal bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🥄 Strainer  
🥄 Spoon  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Freeze the shrimp for easier shelling, separate the shells from the meat, and butterfly the shrimp meat from the back. Add a pinch of salt, pepper, and cooking wine, gently toss until the shrimp meat slightly becomes sticky, then set aside to marinate.  
2. Finely shred the scallion and mix well with sesame oil, set aside.  
3. Pour a little oil into the small clay pot to coat, add the shrimp shells, and slowly render over medium-low heat until the shells release a distinct aroma and orange-red shrimp oil emerges in the pot.  
4. Add hot water, turn to high heat, and boil for 5 minutes.  
5. Turn to low heat, skim off the foam with a strainer, remove the shrimp shell residue, and clarify the broth.  
6. Add the chicken broth seasoning packet, stir well to fully combine the broth with the shrimp soup.  
7. Add the instant noodle cake and the marinated shrimp, cover with the lid, and cook over low heat for 3-5 minutes until the noodles reach the desired tenderness and the shrimp are fully cooked and turned red.  
8. Remove the lid, add the sesame oil-coated shredded scallion, and serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
This shrimp broth noodles dish combines shrimp oil and chicken broth, rich in protein and calcium, perfect for satisfying cravings without being greasy. When rendering shrimp oil, use low heat to fully extract the essence, while also paying attention to controlling the calories from the noodle cake.
