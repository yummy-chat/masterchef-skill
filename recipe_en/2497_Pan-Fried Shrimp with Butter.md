# Pan-Fried Shrimp with Butter
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🦐 Shrimp about one box (pre-thawed homemade peeled shrimp)  
🧈 Butter one small piece (about 10-15 grams)  
🧂 Black pepper to taste (ground)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick skillet  
🥄 Spatula or tongs  
🌰 Black pepper grinder  
🍽️ Serving plate  

## Steps  
1. Remove the shrimp from the freezer in advance and allow them to fully thaw at room temperature or in the refrigerator.  
2. Preheat the skillet thoroughly, then add the thawed shrimp. Cook over medium heat, flipping them occasionally.  
3. Once both sides of the shrimp are golden and they are almost fully cooked, add the butter.  
   **Key point**: Turn off the heat immediately after adding the butter. Let it melt and evenly coat the shrimp using the residual heat of the pan. Avoid continuous heating to prevent the butter from burning and turning bitter.  
4. Grind an appropriate amount of black pepper over the shrimp using a pepper grinder, gently toss to combine, then transfer to a serving plate.  

## Health Tips  
This dish is high in protein and low in fat. Shrimp is a high-quality protein source, making it suitable for those aiming to lose fat or build muscle. Be mindful of the butter amount to avoid excessive calories.
