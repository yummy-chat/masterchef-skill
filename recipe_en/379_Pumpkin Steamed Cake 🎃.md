# Pumpkin Steamed Cake 🎃
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: All Year Round

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🎃 500g old pumpkin (peeled and thinly sliced)
🥚 1 egg
🌾 400g all-purpose flour
🍬 1 tablespoon sugar
🍞 5g yeast
🫘 About 12 red dates (for surface decoration)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Steamer
🥣 Glass bowl (for mixing and proofing)
🥢 Chopsticks
🍰 Spatula (for smoothing batter)
📦 Square mold
🖌️ Oil brush
🧤 Heat-resistant tongs
🔪 Knife and cutting board
🔖 Plastic wrap

## Steps
1. Peel the old pumpkin, cut into thin slices, place in a steamer, and steam over high heat for 15 minutes until soft and tender.
2. Let the steamed pumpkin slices cool, then add sugar and mash into a puree with a spoon.
3. Add the egg and yeast, and stir thoroughly until well combined.
4. Sift in the all-purpose flour, and stir with chopsticks in one direction until the batter is thick and no longer runny.
5. Cover with plastic wrap and proof in a warm place until doubled in volume, with a honeycomb interior.
   **Note**: Proofing time depends on room temperature; rely on volume increase and internal structure.
6. Stir the batter with chopsticks to fully release the gas.
7. Brush oil on the inside and sides of the square mold, pour in the batter, and smooth the surface with a spatula.
8. Let it rest for a second proofing until the batter visibly expands.
9. Press the red dates onto the surface of the proofed batter as decoration.
10. Bring water in the steamer to a boil, place the mold inside, and steam over high heat for 25 minutes.
    **Key Point**: Only place the mold in after the water is boiling; after turning off the heat, let it sit for 3 minutes before opening the lid to prevent shrinking.

## Health Tips
Pumpkin steamed cake is rich in dietary fiber and vitamin A, making it suitable for those needing healthy blood sugar control or improved digestive function. Because it uses pumpkin puree and a small amount of sugar, it is lighter than typical desserts, but be mindful of portion size to avoid bloating.
