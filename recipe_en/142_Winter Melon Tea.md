# Winter Melon Tea
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: ☀️ Summer (April–August)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥒 Winter melon 1000g  
🧂 Rock sugar 300g  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🥣 Large pot  
🧴 Plastic wrap  
🕸️ Strainer  

## Steps  
1. Peel and deseed the winter melon, then cut into chunks no larger than 4 cm per side.  
2. Mix the winter melon chunks with rock sugar evenly, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the juices to seep out.  
3. Pour the marinated winter melon along with its juices into a pot. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium-low heat and simmer for 1–2 hours, stirring frequently to prevent sticking and burning.  
4. After simmering, strain the liquid through a strainer, discard the winter melon pulp, and keep only the clear liquid.  
5. Allow the liquid to cool naturally, then pour it into a clean container, seal, and refrigerate for storage.  
6. The result is concentrated winter melon tea. When drinking, dilute with cold or hot water according to personal preference; it is suitable both hot and cold.  

## Health Advice  
Winter melon tea is a natural summer refreshment, rich in vitamin C and potassium. It helps promote urination, reduce swelling, cool the body, and quench thirst, making it ideal for those who sweat heavily in hot weather. However, it contains a high amount of rock sugar, so individuals with diabetes or those monitoring sugar intake should consume it in small amounts.
