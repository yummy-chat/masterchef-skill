# Shrimp Paste-Stuffed Fried Dough Sticks with Citrus Yogurt Dressing  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥖 Fried dough sticks (youtiao) 2 pieces (cut into 3-4 cm sections)  
🍤 Shrimp 适量 (smash and mince into shrimp paste)  
🥓 Pork fat 约15 g (finely chop and add to shrimp paste)  
🥚 Egg white 1  
🧅 Scallions 约1 stalk's worth (finely chopped)  
🥗 Sweet salad dressing 约3 tbsp  
🥛 Yogurt 约1 small cup  
🍋 Lime ½ (juice extracted)  
🫘 White and black sesame seeds 约1 tsp  
🌱 Microgreens or sprouts for garnish 适量  
🧂 Salt 适量  
🌿 White pepper powder 适量  
🌾 Cornstarch 1 tbsp (for shrimp paste) + 适量 (for coating dough sticks)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Spoon  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
🕳️ Slotted spoon  
🥘 Wire rack for draining oil  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Lightly smash the shrimp with the flat side of the knife, then mince into a fine paste. Place in a bowl. Add the minced pork fat, egg white, salt, white pepper powder, and 1 tbsp cornstarch. Repeatedly slap the mixture against the bowl until the shrimp paste becomes firm, sticky, and elastic. Finally, mix in the finely chopped scallions.  
2. Dust the cut ends of the dough stick sections with a thin layer of cornstarch. Stuff the shrimp paste into the cut openings, filling them completely and smoothing the surface.  
   **Key Point**: Dusting with cornstarch helps the shrimp paste adhere better to the dough sticks under high heat.  
3. Pour oil into the wok and heat to 350°F (180°C). Place the stuffed dough sticks shrimp-paste side down, and fry over medium heat until the shrimp paste is set and fully cooked, and the dough sticks turn golden brown and crispy and float to the surface. Remove and drain on a wire rack.  
4. Prepare the citrus yogurt dressing: Mix the sweet salad dressing, yogurt, and lime juice until well combined. Adjust the sweet-sour balance to taste.  
5. Leave a small amount of oil in the wok. Pour in the dressing and heat over low heat until it begins to bubble slightly. Add the fried stuffed dough sticks and toss quickly to coat each piece evenly with the sauce. Immediately turn off the heat and sprinkle in the white and black sesame seeds, mixing well.  
   **Note**: Toss quickly to prevent the dough sticks from becoming soft.  
6. Plate the dish, drizzle with extra dressing if desired, garnish with microgreens, and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This dish features bouncy shrimp paste and crispy dough sticks, but the combination of salad dressing and deep-frying results in high calories. Be sure to control portion sizes and pair with light vegetables for a balanced meal. When frying, placing the shrimp-paste side down first helps seal in the juices and prevents the dough sticks from absorbing excess oil and turning soft.
