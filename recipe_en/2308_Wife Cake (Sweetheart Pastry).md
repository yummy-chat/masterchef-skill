# Wife Cake (Sweetheart Pastry)
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥒 Winter melon 3 jin (seeded, peeled, and grated into fine shreds)
🌾 All-purpose flour 250 grams (for water-oil dough)
🌾 Cake flour 200 grams (for oil dough)
🍬 White sugar 160 grams (30 grams for water-oil dough, 130 grams for filling)
🫒 Cooking oil 195 grams (80 grams for water-oil dough, 90 grams for oil dough, 25 grams for stir-frying filling)
💧 Water 100 grams
🥚 Egg适量 (for brushing the surface)
🌰 White sesame seeds适量 (sprinkled on top for aroma)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🧀 Grater (for grating winter melon)
🍲 Large pot (for stir-frying winter melon filling)
🥣 Large bowl (for kneading dough)
🥖 Rolling pin (for laminating and rolling out dough)
📋 Scraper (for dividing dough portions)
🔪 Knife (for making slits)
🔥 Oven (for baking)
🍳 Baking tray (for holding pastry bases)

## Cooking Steps
1. Prepare the winter melon: Remove seeds and peel from the winter melon, then grate it into fine shreds using a grater; for difficult-to-grate parts, you can insert a chopstick to assist.
2. Cook the filling: Place the winter melon shreds into a large pot, add 130 grams of white sugar and 25 grams of cooking oil. Bring to a boil over high heat, stirring constantly to prevent sticking. When the liquid reduces and the filling thickens and bubbles, reduce to low heat. Continue stirring for about 40 minutes until the moisture is fully evaporated. Remove and let cool for later use.
3. Make the water-oil dough: Combine 250 grams of all-purpose flour, 30 grams of white sugar, 100 grams of water, and 80 grams of cooking oil. Knead into a slightly soft dough, cover, and let rest for 30 minutes.
4. Make the oil dough: Combine 200 grams of cake flour and 90 grams of cooking oil, mix and knead into a uniform dough. Cover and set aside.
5. Divide the dough: Divide the water-oil dough into 20 portions, roll into balls, and cover to prevent drying. Divide the oil dough into 20 portions and roll into balls.
6. Wrap the oil dough: Take one water-oil dough ball, flatten it, wrap an oil dough ball inside, and seal the opening tightly using your thumb and forefinger.
7. Laminate the dough: Roll the wrapped dough piece into a long strip, then roll it up. Roll it out into a long strip again, and roll it up once more. No resting time is needed; proceed directly to increase layers.
8. Roll out and wrap the filling: Flatten the rolled dough piece, roll it into a thin round wrapper, and place the cooled winter melon filling in the center. Gather the edges and seal the opening like wrapping a bun, then gently flatten.
9. Pre-bake preparation: Brush a layer of egg wash on the surface of each pastry base. Use a knife to cut 3 slits on the top, and sprinkle with white sesame seeds.
10. Bake: Preheat the oven for 10 minutes. Place the baking tray on the middle rack and bake at 180°C (356°F) with top and bottom heat for about 35 minutes, until the surface is golden brown and crispy. Remove and serve.

## Health Advice
Wife Cake features a winter melon filling encased in a flaky pastry, offering a sweet and fragrant taste. Due to the use of significant sugar and oil in the preparation, it is recommended to enjoy this pastry in moderation as a snack and to be mindful of calorie intake.
