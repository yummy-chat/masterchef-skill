# Crispy Meat Pancake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍚 All-purpose flour 2 jin (for dough)  
🧂 Baking soda 2 grams  
🧂 Salt 10 grams (for dough)  
💧 Warm water 560 grams  
🍚 All-purpose flour 100 grams (for oil pastry)  
🥩 Ground pork belly 1 jin  
🌿 Minced ginger 15 grams  
🧅 Chopped scallions (appropriate amount, sprinkled on the meat filling during assembly)  
🧂 Salt 20 grams (for oil pastry)  
🌶️ Ground pepper 6 grams  
🌱 Five-spice powder 6 grams  
🫒 Rapeseed oil 200 grams (for oil pastry)  
🧂 Salt 8 grams (for seasoning the filling)  
🍶 Baijiu (white liquor), as needed  
🥢 Premium light soy sauce 20 grams  
💧 Sichuan peppercorn water 60 grams (soak Sichuan peppercorns in boiling water in advance)  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🔪 Cutting board  
🥖 Rolling pin  
🥄 Spoon  
♨️ Electric griddle  
🔪 Chef's knife  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Make the dough: Combine 2 jin all-purpose flour, 2 grams baking soda, and 10 grams salt evenly. Gradually add 560 grams of warm water, stirring into flocculent pieces, then knead into a smooth dough until the dough surface is smooth, hands are clean, and the bowl is clean.
   **Note**: The dough must be thoroughly kneaded to increase elasticity, making it easier to roll thin later.
2. Rest the dough: Roll the dough into a long strip, brush the surface with a layer of oil. Divide into pieces of about 120 grams each, and roll each piece into a small long strip. Arrange them neatly, brush again with oil, cover tightly with plastic wrap, and let rest for 1 hour.
   **Note**: Brushing with oil and sealing prevents the dough pieces from drying out and sticking together. Adequate resting makes the dough softer and easier to handle.
3. Make the oil pastry: Mix 100 grams flour with 20 grams salt, 6 grams ground pepper, and 6 grams five-spice powder. Heat 200 grams rapeseed oil until it smokes, then let it cool to about 200°C. Pour the hot oil into the flour mixture, stirring continuously to form a thin oil paste.
   **Note**: The oil temperature must be moderate; too low won't release the aroma, too high will burn the flour.
4. Prepare the meat filling: Add 15 grams minced ginger, 8 grams salt, an appropriate amount of baijiu, and 20 grams premium light soy sauce to 1 jin of ground pork belly. Mix well by hand. Gradually add 60 grams of Sichuan peppercorn water, stirring vigorously in one direction until the filling becomes firm and elastic.
   **Note**: Stirring consistently in one direction is crucial for the filling to fully absorb the liquid, resulting in a tender and juicy texture.
5. Assemble the pancake: Take a rested dough piece, roll it out longer and flatten it, then roll it into a thin, elongated sheet. Spread a thin layer of oil pastry evenly over the surface. Place the meat filling on top and sprinkle with chopped scallions. Starting from one end, stretch the dough sheet backward while rolling it up, trying to pull the dough as thin as possible. When you reach the end, apply oil pastry to any uncoated area, continuing to stretch and roll. Once rolled, stand it upright, tuck the seam underneath, and shape it into a round pancake blank.
   **Note**: Stretching while rolling is key to creating rich layers; the dough sheet should be as thin as possible.
6. Fry: Preheat the electric griddle's upper and lower plates to 180°C, and add an appropriate amount of oil. Flatten and round the pancake blank by hand, then place it into the griddle. Cover and cook for 8 minutes on the first side. Flip, cover again, and cook for another 5 minutes until both sides are golden brown. Remove from the griddle.
   **Note**: Control the temperature and timing carefully to ensure the inside is fully cooked and the outside is crispy.

## Health Advice  
This crispy meat pancake features a crunchy exterior and juicy filling. It is rich in protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable as an energy-boosting meal. Pair it with a light vegetable salad to balance the oil intake. Due to its relatively high salt and fat content, individuals with high blood pressure or those needing to limit fat intake should consume it in moderation.
