# Homemade Bone Broth & Vegetable Soup Concentrate
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🦴 Pork leg bone (with meat)
🧅 Ginger (sliced)
🥬 Lotus root (cut into large chunks)
🥔 Chinese yam (peeled and cut into chunks)
🌰 Chestnuts (shelled)
🍄 Monkey head mushroom (dried, pre-soaked)
🫘 Lotus seeds (dried, pre-soaked)
🌸 Lily bulbs (dried, pre-soaked)
🍶 Cooking wine (for blanching)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan (for blanching)
♨️ Pressure cooker (for simmering)
🥣 Blender/Immersion blender (for pureeing)
🎨 Piping bag (for filling molds)
🧊 Silicone molds (for shaping)
❄️ Refrigerator (for freezing)

## Cooking Steps
1. Place the pork leg bones in a pot with cold water, add ginger slices and cooking wine, blanch to remove any gamey smell, then remove and rinse under running water.
   **Key Point**: Blanching removes blood and impurities; rinsing ensures a clear broth base.
2. Pre-soak the dried monkey head mushrooms, lotus seeds, and lily bulbs in water until fully softened.
3. Cut the lotus root into large chunks, peel and cut the Chinese yam into chunks, and shell the chestnuts. Set aside.
4. Place the blanched pork leg bones, lotus root chunks, yam chunks, chestnuts, soaked monkey head mushrooms, lotus seeds, and lily bulbs all into the inner pot of the pressure cooker.
5. Close the lid, select the "Soup" or "Beans/Tendon" mode, and cook until the ingredients are tender and soft, to the point where a chopstick can easily pierce the lotus root and yam.
6. Remove some of the cooked vegetables and an appropriate amount of broth, and pour them into the blender cup.
7. Blend into a smooth and fine puree with no obvious chunks.
   **Key Point**: If the puree is too thick, add a little broth to adjust the consistency.
8. Transfer the puree into a piping bag and evenly squeeze it into clean silicone mold cavities, filling each to about 80% full.
9. Cover the mold with its lid and place it in the refrigerator to freeze until completely solid and firm.
10. Once frozen solid, remove from the molds and store sealed in the freezer. To use, take one cube and add it to boiling soup; it will melt and provide a rich broth.

## Health Tips
This soup is nutritionally balanced. Lotus root and Chinese yam nourish the spleen and stomach, while chestnuts and lotus seeds calm the mind. It is suitable for the whole family. Note that the pork leg bone broth base is relatively high in purines, so those with gout should consume it in moderation. When making the puree, control the amount of broth added to avoid it being too thin.
