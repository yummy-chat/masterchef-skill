# Sesame Sauce Shrimp Paste and Wide Rice Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍠 Wide rice noodles, appropriate amount (pre-soaked until soft or directly cooked)  
🦐 Shrimp paste, about 2-3 pieces  
🧅 Scallions, chopped, appropriate amount  
🧄 Garlic, minced, appropriate amount  
🌶️ Bird's eye chili, appropriate amount  
🥜 Sesame paste, appropriate amount  
🥄 Light soy sauce, 3 tablespoons  
🥄 Dark soy sauce, 1 tablespoon  
🥄 Black vinegar, 2 tablespoons  
🍬 White sugar, 1 tablespoon  
🌶️ Chili flakes, appropriate amount  
⚪ White sesame seeds, appropriate amount  
🫒 Cooking oil, appropriate amount  
💧 Water, appropriate amount (to thin the sesame paste)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Small bowl (for mixing sesame paste and sauce)  
🍲 Stockpot  
🥢 Slotted spoon  
🥄 Spoon  
🥣 Large bowl (for mixing)  

## Preparation Steps
1.  Place the sesame paste in a small bowl. Add a small amount of water in batches, stirring continuously in one direction until it forms a smooth, yogurt-like paste. Set aside.
2.  In another bowl, combine the chopped scallions, minced garlic, bird's eye chili, chili flakes, and white sesame seeds. Pour in hot oil heated until smoking to release the aroma.
3.  Once the oil has cooled slightly, add the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, black vinegar, and white sugar. Stir well, then pour in the prepared sesame paste mixture. Mix thoroughly to form the final sauce.
4.  Cook the wide rice noodles in boiling water until soft and translucent. Remove and rinse with cold water or allow to cool to prevent sticking.
5.  Cook the shrimp paste in boiling water until all pieces float to the surface and slightly expand. Remove and drain.
6.  Place the wide rice noodles and shrimp paste together in a large bowl. Pour in the prepared sesame sauce mixture and toss thoroughly until the sauce evenly coats the ingredients. Serve immediately.

## Health Advice  
This dish provides high-quality protein from the shrimp paste, while the wide rice noodles offer a strong sense of satiety. However, the calorie content from the sesame paste and oil is not low, so it is recommended to pair it with a portion of stir-fried vegetables to balance nutrition. This dish is suitable for those who crave bold flavors but do not want to completely abandon health; the chili and vinegar also help stimulate the appetite and cut through grease.
