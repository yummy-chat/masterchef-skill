# Braised Beef Ribs with Tomato
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥩 Beef ribs 2 jin (with tendon, fat, and lean meat, cut into large chunks)
🍅 Tomatoes 800g (400g diced, 400g cut into chunks)
🥔 Potatoes, as needed (cut into chunks)
🍬 Rock sugar 30g
🍶 Huadiao wine, one bottle cap
🧅 Scallions, as needed (finely chopped)
🧄 Ginger, as needed (finely chopped)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (finely chopped)
🧂 Light soy sauce 30mL
🧂 Salt 5g

## Cookware & Equipment
🥘 Wok
🔥 Stove
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥄 Soup spoon
🔪 Cutting board and knife

## Cooking Steps
1. Place beef ribs in cold water and blanch for about 5 minutes to remove gamey smell. Remove and let cool, then cut into large chunks.
   **Key point**: Let cool before cutting to maintain shape; cut into large chunks to retain the texture of fat, lean, and tendon.
2. Peel the tomatoes. Dice half and cut the other half into chunks. Cut the potatoes into chunks and set aside.
3. Heat the wok over high heat, add an appropriate amount of oil. Once hot, add beef ribs and stir-fry over high heat until the surface turns a caramelized brown.
   **Key point**: The caramelized brown surface is when the beef is at its most aromatic.
4. Add rock sugar and stir-fry with the beef until coated with caramel color.
5. Splash in one bottle cap of Huadiao wine along the edge of the wok to enhance the meat's aroma.
6. Add finely chopped scallions, ginger, and garlic, and stir-fry until fragrant.
   **Key point**: Finely mincing the aromatics makes them easier to mix with rice.
7. Drizzle in light soy sauce and stir-fry until the fermented bean aroma is released.
8. Add the diced tomatoes and stir-fry until they break down into a sauce and release their flavor.
9. Add enough hot water to submerge the ingredients. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to low heat. Simmer for an hour and a half.
   **Key point**: Must use hot water to keep the meat tender and not tough.
10. After the time is up, uncover, add the potato chunks and remaining tomato chunks, and bring to a boil over high heat.
11. Season with salt, cover again, and simmer for about 10 minutes until the potatoes are soft.
    **Key point**: Add salt at the end to keep the meat tender; remove from heat once the potatoes are soft.

## Health Advice
The combination of tomatoes and beef ribs offers a sweet and sour flavor that appetizes, along with iron and vitamin C. It is recommended to pair with whole wheat bread or mixed grain rice to increase dietary fiber intake. Gout patients should consume in moderation.
