# Fried Rice Congee
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 1 bowl of rice (rinsed thoroughly and completely dried)
💧 5 bowls of water (5:1 ratio to fried rice)
🧂 Salt, to taste
🥬 Vegetables (such as chopped greens, optional)
🥩 Lean ground meat (optional)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the wok over heat, ensuring it is dry and free of oil and water. Add the dried rice and stir continuously to heat evenly. Fry until the rice turns golden brown and releases a toasted aroma.
2. Remove the fried rice from the wok and let it cool slightly.
3. Return the fried rice to the wok, add water in a 1:5 ratio. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium-low heat and simmer slowly until the broth thickens and the rice grains become soft and puffed.
4. Season with an appropriate amount of salt to taste.
5. Add chopped vegetables or lean ground meat to supplement protein and vitamins.
6. Ladle into bowls and serve.

## Health Advice
Fried rice congee helps strengthen the spleen and nourish the stomach, making it suitable for those with weak digestion or poor appetite. When frying the rice, maintain low heat throughout to avoid burning, which would affect the flavor.
