# Cumin Lamb Chops  
Cuisine: Xinjiang Cuisine  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Suggestion: Winter (November-December)  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍖 Fresh lamb chops, as needed  
🥕 White radish, a small amount (cut into chunks, used for stewing to remove gamey flavor and enhance aroma)  
🧅 Purple onion, as needed (large chunks for stewing, a small amount finely chopped for final seasoning)  
🫚 Ginger, as needed (used in stewing to remove odor)  
🧂 Salt, a small amount (used separately during stewing and pan-frying)  
⚪ White peppercorns, a small amount (added whole during stewing)  
🌶️ Dried chili segments, a small amount  
🫗 Zanthoxylum oil, a small amount  
🌶️ Chili powder, a small amount  
🌿 Cumin powder, as needed  
🫒 Vegetable oil, a small amount (for brushing the pan)  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Wok (or soup pot, for stewing lamb chops)  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🍳 Frying pan  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🔥 Gas stove  

## Instructions  
1. Wash the fresh lamb chops, soak them in cold water for about 1 hour to fully draw out residual blood, then remove and drain.  
2. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add ginger slices, soaked lamb chops, white radish chunks, purple onion chunks, and white peppercorns. Reduce to medium-low heat and simmer for 45 minutes until the lamb is tender.  
**Key Point**: Add ingredients after the water reaches a boil to quickly seal the surface, locking in internal juices.  
3. Remove the cooked lamb chops and drain the liquid. Set aside the white radish and broth for other uses.  
4. Heat a frying pan over high heat, brush with a thin layer of oil, add the lamb chops, and fry both sides evenly until golden brown and the exterior is crispy.  
**Key Point**: Fry quickly over high heat to form a crisp crust while keeping the inside tender.  
5. Sprinkle dried chili segments and chopped onion into the pan, drizzle with zanthoxylum oil, add a small amount of salt, chili powder, and cumin powder. Stir-fry quickly until evenly coated, making sure each lamb chop is covered with seasoning.  
6. Turn off the heat, plate, and serve immediately.  

## Health Advice  
Lamb chops are rich in high-quality protein and iron. Stewing with white radish reduces greasiness, making them suitable for winter nourishment, but those with high blood lipids should control their intake. Pan-fry over high heat to seal in juices, and season with less salt and more cumin for a healthier option.
