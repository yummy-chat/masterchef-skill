# Stir-fried Shredded Potatoes with Millet and Pork Cracklings
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥔 Potatoes, as needed (peeled and cut into thin shreds, soaked in water to remove starch)
🐖 Pork leaf fat, as needed (cut into small pieces for cracklings)
🌾 Millet, as needed (cooked into a thick millet porridge in advance)
🧂 Light soy sauce, as needed
🧂 Dark soy sauce, as needed
🧂 Salt, as needed (for blanching shredded potatoes)
🌶️ Dried chili peppers, as needed
🫒 Dried Sichuan peppercorns, as needed

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥘 Cooking pot
🥄 Strainer or slotted spoon
🥄 Soup ladle
🍽️ Bowls, plates

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the pork leaf fat into uniform small pieces. Bring water to a boil in a pot, blanch the fat pieces, then remove and drain. Add a small amount of base oil to the pan, add the fat pieces, and stir-fry continuously until the lard is fully rendered and the cracklings turn golden brown. Remove the cracklings and set aside.
   **Note**: Blanching removes impurities and odor; stir constantly during rendering to prevent scorching.
2. Cook the millet with water in a pot until thick and porridge-like. Prepare before stir-frying.
3. Wash, peel, and finely shred the potatoes, then soak in water to remove excess starch. Bring water to a boil in a pot with a little salt. After boiling, add the shredded potatoes and blanch briefly over high heat until the shreds droop naturally when picked up with chopsticks. Immediately remove and rinse under cold water, then drain.
   **Key point**: The ideal blanching level is when the shreds droop naturally; rinsing with cold water maintains their crispness.
4. Add a small amount of lard to the pan. Fry the dried Sichuan peppercorns over low heat until deep red, then remove and discard. Add the pork cracklings and dried chili pepper segments, stir-frying over medium heat until fragrant.
   **Note**: The peppercorns are used only for their aroma; this infused oil forms the flavor base of the entire dish.
5. Add the drained shredded potatoes and stir-fry quickly over high heat. Add light soy sauce and dark soy sauce for seasoning and color, continuing to stir-fry rapidly.
6. Add an appropriate amount of the cooked millet porridge to the pan. Stir well to coat the shredded potatoes with a light, sticky grain aroma, then remove from heat and plate.

## Health Suggestions
This dish uses pork cracklings and shredded potatoes, supplemented with millet porridge for a slightly sticky texture. It provides carbohydrates, fat, and a small amount of protein, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost. Pair with a vegetable salad to balance the greasiness. Those with sensitive stomachs should limit crackling intake to avoid digestive discomfort.
