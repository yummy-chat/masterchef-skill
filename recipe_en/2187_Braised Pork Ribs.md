# Braised Pork Ribs
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork ribs, appropriate amount (baby back ribs)
🧅 Scallion, appropriate amount (cut into sections)
🫚 Ginger, appropriate amount (sliced)
🌿 Dried galangal, a small amount
🍶 Vinegar, two tablespoons
🍶 Cooking wine, four tablespoons
🫘 Soybean paste, two tablespoons
🦪 Oyster sauce, one tablespoon
🧴 Dark soy sauce, one tablespoon
🧴 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount (a swirl)
🍬 Rock sugar, seven or eight pieces
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount (add at the end to adjust flavor)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok (with lid)
🥣 Colander
🥄 Spatula
🥢 Small bowl (for pre-mixing vinegar and cooking wine)

## Cooking Steps
1. Soak the pork ribs in cold water to remove blood.
   **Note**: For best results, soak overnight in the refrigerator to help tenderize and enhance flavor.
2. Remove the ribs and drain thoroughly. Set aside.
3. In a small bowl, mix two tablespoons of vinegar with four tablespoons of cooking wine until well combined.
   **Key point**: This is tip number one—vinegar and cooking wine must be mixed beforehand.
4. Heat the wok over high heat, add a small amount of oil, then add scallion sections and ginger slices. Sauté over low heat until fragrant.
5. Add the drained ribs and pan-fry until the surface is lightly browned and golden.
6. Pour the pre-mixed vinegar and cooking wine mixture along the edge of the wok. Stir-fry quickly until the vinegar smell has completely evaporated and there is no pungent aroma.
   **Key point**: Tip number one—ensure the sour smell is fully cooked off.
7. Reduce heat to low. Add soybean paste, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, and rock sugar.
   **Note**: Do not add salt or other ingredients at this stage.
8. Stir-fry slowly over low heat until the sauce evenly coats the ribs and the rock sugar has completely dissolved.
9. Pour in boiling water, adding enough to completely submerge the ribs in one go.
   **Key point**: Water must be added all at once; avoid adding more during cooking.
10. Add a small amount of dried galangal slices.
11. Cover with the lid, reduce heat to the lowest setting, and braise for 40 minutes.
12. After the time is up, turn the heat to high. Taste and add salt as needed to adjust the flavor.
13. Stir-fry over high heat to reduce the sauce until it thickens and evenly clings to the surface of the ribs. Remove from heat and serve.

## Health Tips
This braised pork rib dish is rich in protein and calcium, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost. Be mindful of the amount of salt and rock sugar used to avoid excessive sodium and sugar intake. It is recommended to pair with light vegetables for balanced nutrition. Those with weaker digestive function should consume in moderation.
