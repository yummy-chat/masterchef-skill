# Mashed Potato Salad
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥔 Potato: about 2 (medium size, peeled and cut into chunks)
🥕 Carrot: about 1 (peeled and cut into strips)
🥚 Egg: 1
🍖 Ham: about 1 stick (diced)
🥭 Mango: 1 (flesh removed and diced)
🥒 Cucumber: about half (peeled and diced)
🥬 Lettuce: to taste (washed and torn into small pieces)
🍅 Cherry tomatoes: about 5 (washed and halved)
🧂 Black pepper: to taste (freshly ground)
🥄 Mayonnaise: to taste
🥗 Salad dressing: to taste (optional flavored dressing)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
♨️ Egg steamer (or steamer pot)
🍳 Frying pan + spatula
🔪 Cutting board + knife
🥒 Peeler
🥣 Potato masher
🧺 Colander
🥣 Large bowl

## Steps
1. Thoroughly wash all vegetables and fruits; peel the potatoes and carrots, cut the potatoes into large chunks, and cut the carrots into long strips.
2. Place the potato chunks, carrot strips, and whole egg in the shell into the egg steamer, and steam until completely tender, easily pierced through with a chopstick.
3. Heat a frying pan over medium-low heat, add no oil or only a little oil, pour in the diced ham, and fry until the surface is slightly charred and the edges are golden brown. Remove and set aside.
4. Peel the mango, remove the flesh, and dice; peel the cucumber and dice; tear the lettuce into small pieces; halve the cherry tomatoes. Prepare all ingredients.
5. Place the steamed potatoes and peeled hard-boiled egg into a large bowl, and use a potato masher to mash into a smooth paste.
6. Add freshly ground black pepper and mayonnaise to the bowl, and mix thoroughly to season.
7. Add the fried ham cubes, carrot strips, cucumber cubes, and lettuce pieces. Gently fold to combine, avoiding overmixing to prevent excess water from being released.
8. Spoon the salad into a bowl, and top with cherry tomato halves arranged in your desired shape.
9. Optionally, drizzle with a little salad dressing to enhance the flavor, according to personal preference.

## Health Advice
Mashed potato salad is rich in carbohydrates, protein, and vitamins, making it suitable as an energy replenishment after workouts or as a light lunch. Note that mayonnaise is high in calories; you can reduce the amount or substitute with yogurt. The refreshing flavor of mango and cucumber can help balance any greasiness.
