# Tomato Rice
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍚 Rice 2 cups  
🍤 Large shrimp as needed  
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms as needed (sliced)  
🥕 Carrot as needed (diced)  
🌽 Corn kernels as needed  
🥓 Chinese sausage as needed (sliced)  
🍅 Tomato 1 piece  
🧅 Scallion as needed (chopped)  
🧂 Light soy sauce as needed  
🧂 Dark soy sauce as needed  
🦪 Oyster sauce as needed  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🍚 Rice cooker  
🥄 Measuring cup  
🍴 Rice paddle  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Steps  
1. Rinse the rice thoroughly and place it in the inner pot of the rice cooker.  
2. Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Stir evenly with chopsticks so the rice grains are uniformly coated with the sauce.  
3. Layer the large shrimp, sliced shiitake mushrooms, diced carrot, corn kernels, and sliced Chinese sausage in order on top.  
4. Place the whole tomato in the center of the ingredients.  
5. Pour in approximately 2 cups of water.  
6. Place the inner pot into the rice cooker, cover with the lid, and start the standard rice cooking program.  
7. Once the cooking program is complete, open the lid and sprinkle with chopped scallion.  
8. Use a rice paddle to mash the whole tomato, then mix thoroughly with the rice and all other ingredients until well combined.  

## Health Tips  
This rice dish combines meat, vegetables, and staple food in one pot, offering simple preparation. Chinese sausage, light soy sauce, and oyster sauce are high in salt, so pay attention to total sodium intake. Shrimp and shiitake mushrooms provide high-quality protein and dietary fiber, making this suitable for those seeking balanced nutrition and efficiency.
