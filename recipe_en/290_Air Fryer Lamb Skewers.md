# Air Fryer Lamb Skewers  
Cuisine: Xinjiang Cuisine  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🐑 Lamb (适量, cut into 1-2 cm cubes, with a mix of fat and lean meat)  
🧅 Purple onion (适量, sliced)  
🫑 Bell peppers (green, red, yellow) (适量, cut into small pieces)  
🥔 Potato (about 1, cut into thick slices)  

🧂 Light soy sauce (适量)

🧂 Salt (适量)  
🧂 Chicken bouillon (适量)  
🍬 White sugar (适量)  
🫒 Cooking oil (适量, added last during marinating)  
🍯 BBQ sauce (适量, for brushing after cooking)  
🌶️ Chili powder (适量, for sprinkling)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🍢 Bamboo skewers  
♨️ Air fryer  
🍽️ Baking tray (or sheet pan)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Cut the lamb into 1-2 cm cubes. Slice the purple onion, cut bell peppers into small pieces, and cut the potato into thick slices. Set aside.  
2. Place lamb cubes and onion slices in a stainless steel bowl. Add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, chicken bouillon, and white sugar. Mix well with your hands. Then drizzle in cooking oil and mix again. Marinate for 30 minutes.  
**Note**: Adding oil last helps seal in the meat juices.  
3. Thread the skewers, alternating pieces of fat, lean meat, and bell peppers. Mixing fat and lean meat enhances tenderness.  
4. Arrange potato thick slices evenly at the bottom of the air fryer.  
5. Lay the skewers flat on top of the potato slices, leaving gaps between each skewer for even heat distribution.  
6. Set the air fryer to 180°C and cook for 20 minutes, until the skewers are golden brown and sizzling with rendered fat.  
7. Remove the skewers. Brush with BBQ sauce to taste and sprinkle with chili powder or other dry seasonings. Serve immediately.  
**Key Point**: The potato slices soaked with meat juices are exceptionally flavorful and can be plated together.  

## Health Tips  
Choose lamb with a good mix of fat and lean meat. Adding oil last during marinating locks in juices. Placing potato slices at the bottom absorbs the meat juices. Suitable for those who are fitness-conscious or managing sugar intake, in moderation.
