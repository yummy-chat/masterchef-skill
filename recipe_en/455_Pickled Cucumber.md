# Pickled Cucumber
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 3 cucumbers (about 500g, remove core and cut into strips)
🌶️ 2 red chili peppers (about 30g, remove seeds and cut into strips)
🧄 3 cloves garlic (sliced)
🧂 3g + 3g salt (used in two separate portions)
🌸 3g Sichuan peppercorns
🌶️ 3g dried chili peppers
🧅 Some scallions (shredded)
🫚 Some ginger (shredded)
💧 500g water
🍬 10g sugar
🧂 2g chicken bouillon
🍶 80g white vinegar (add after turning off heat)

## Tools and Equipment
🔪 Kitchen knife
🪵 Cutting board
🥢 Chopsticks
🫙 Glass jar with airtight seal
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥄 Slotted spoon or spatula

## Steps
1. Wash cucumbers, cut into sections then slice into strips, removing the central core.
   **Key point**: Must remove core to avoid excess moisture that affects crisp texture.
2. Cut red chili peppers lengthwise, remove seeds and slice into small strips; slice the garlic.
3. Place cucumber strips, chili pepper strips, and garlic slices in a bowl. Add 3g salt, mix well, and let sit for 5 minutes.
4. Squeeze out the released liquid and transfer to a clean, dry, and oil-free glass jar with an airtight seal.
5. Heat oil in the pan over low heat, add Sichuan peppercorns and dried chili pepper pieces, and stir-fry until fragrant.
6. Add shredded scallions and ginger, continue stir-frying until fragrant.
7. Pour in 500g water and bring to a boil over high heat.
8. Once boiling, add the remaining 3g salt, sugar, and chicken bouillon, stirring until fully dissolved.
9. Turn off the heat, then immediately add 80g white vinegar and stir to combine.
   **Key point**: Must add white vinegar after turning off heat to prevent acetic acid from evaporating, which would reduce sourness.
10. Allow the liquid to cool completely, then pour it into the glass jar containing the cucumbers, ensuring the liquid covers the ingredients.
11. Seal the jar and place it in the refrigerator or a cool area to pickle overnight before serving.

## Health Advice
This pickled cucumber is refreshing and appetizing, ideal for cooling down in summer. However, it has a high salt content, so people with high blood pressure should consume it in moderation. The flavor is best after pickling overnight, so please be patient.
