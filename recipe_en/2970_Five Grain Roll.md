# Five Grain Roll
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 Millet appropriate amount (for making the skin base)  
🌾 Flour appropriate amount (to mix with millet porridge to form dough)  
🍚 Black sticky rice appropriate amount (approximately equal to the amount of red beans)  
🫘 Red beans appropriate amount (approximately equal to the amount of black sticky rice)  
🫒 Red dates appropriate amount (chopped)  
🥜 Walnuts appropriate amount (chopped)  
🥛 Milk (or similar white liquid) appropriate amount (added when mixing the filling to increase moisture and flavor)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍲 Cooking pot  
🍚 Rice cooker  
🍳 Wok  
🍞 Cutting board  
🥖 Rolling pin  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🧑🍳 Steamer  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps
1. Cook the black sticky rice and red beans together until soft and sticky, add an appropriate amount of milk, and stir evenly to form a moist filling.  
   **Key point**: The filling should maintain an appropriate level of moisture for easy spreading and rolling later.

2. Chop the red dates and walnuts into moderately sized pieces and set aside.

3. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the millet and cook until soft. Gradually add the flour in batches, stirring constantly until it forms a flaky texture, then knead into a smooth and soft dough.  
   **Key point**: Flour should be added in batches based on the amount of millet porridge; the dough should be soft and not sticky to the hands.

4. Dust the work surface with dry flour and roll the dough into a large, thin oval-shaped skin.  
   **Key point**: Dry flour prevents sticking; try to roll the skin as thin as possible.

5. Spread the black sticky rice filling evenly over the skin, sprinkle with the chopped red dates and walnuts, and tightly roll it up from one end into a long strip.  
   **Key point**: Spread the filling evenly and roll it tightly.

6. Use a knife to cut the long strip into evenly sized segments, making clean and decisive cuts.

7. Line the steamer with a steaming cloth, place the segments on it leaving space between them, and steam for 15 minutes until the dough expands, the color brightens, and the interior is fully cooked.

## Health Advice
This five grain roll is rich in dietary fiber and complex carbohydrates, making it suitable for people who need to control blood sugar or increase satiety. However, be mindful of the natural sugars in milk and red dates; consume in moderation during a weight loss period.
