# Shanxi Lemonade 🍋
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍋 1 yellow lemon (for zesting, juicing, and slicing)
🍋 Proper amount of fragrant lemon (for slicing and marinating, strong aroma)
🧊 Proper amount of ice cubes (for muddling and filling the cup)
🧂 A pinch of salt (for scrubbing the peel and enhancing flavor when making syrup)
🍬 Proper amount of white sugar (for marinating fragrant lemon slices)
🍬 Proper amount of rock sugar (for making lemon syrup)
🥛 Proper amount of condensed milk (to add creamy flavor)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, kitchen knife
🥣 Wooden mortar and pestle (for muddling and extracting juice and aroma)
🕳️ Strainer
🍳 Frying pan, gas stove
🥄 Slotted spoon
🍶 Cocktail shaker (or sealable bottle)
🥤 Drinking cup

## Steps
1. Use salt to scrub the peel of the yellow lemon and fragrant lemon, then rinse clean. Shave off the yellow zest of the yellow lemon, peel and cut the flesh into pieces; slice the remaining yellow lemon and remove the seeds, and slice the fragrant lemon. Take some fragrant lemon slices and marinate them briefly with white sugar, set aside.
2. Place the peeled yellow lemon pieces into a wooden mortar, muddle to extract the juice, then strain through a fine mesh strainer to collect the lemon juice, set aside.
3. In a frying pan, add rock sugar, water, the shaved lemon zest, the strained lemon juice, and a little salt. Heat over medium-low heat, stirring continuously. Once the syrup bubbles vigorously and the liquid turns slightly yellow, immediately turn off the heat. Quickly use a slotted spoon to remove the lemon zest and other solids, obtaining the lemon syrup.
   **Note**: Stir continuously during boiling to prevent burning at the bottom; filter quickly after turning off the heat to avoid the residual heat making the syrup thicker.
4. Place 3 slices of sugar-marinated fragrant lemon, 3 slices of plain yellow lemon, and 2 ice cubes at the bottom of a cocktail shaker. Use a pestle to firmly muddle about 18 times, fully releasing the aroma and juice. Then add enough ice cubes to fill most of the shaker.
5. Pour about 30 ml of lemon syrup into the shaker, then add a suitable amount of condensed milk.
6. Pour in about 500 ml of drinkable water.
7. Tightly seal the cocktail shaker lid, then shake vigorously and forcefully to fully combine all ingredients and create foam.
8. Pour the shaken lemonade into a drinking cup and serve.

## Health Advice
This lemonade uses condensed milk to partially replace sugar, adding calcium and a creamy flavor, making it suitable for summer to cool down and replenish water and trace elements. However, the total sugar content from the syrup and condensed milk is relatively high. Those controlling sugar intake can reduce the amount of condensed milk or use sugar substitutes.
