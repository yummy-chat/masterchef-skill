# Butter Garlic Pepper Shrimp
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🦐 Large freshwater prawns (Rochester prawns) 1 lb (remove antennae, butterfly the back)
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (minced, more for better flavor)
🧅 Scallion 1 stalk (white part diced, green part cut into sections, use separately)
🧈 Butter about 30g (one large chunk)
🧂 Salt appropriate amount
🍬 Sugar a pinch (to enhance freshness)
🌶️ Black and white pepper appropriate amount (mix both types)
🍶 Rice wine or cooking wine appropriate amount (drizzle along the edge of the clay pot at the end)

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Wok
✂️ Kitchen scissors
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board
🫕 Clay pot (preheated in advance)

## Preparation Steps
1. Use kitchen scissors to remove the prawn antennae and butterfly the back along the ridge for better flavor absorption.
2. Finely mince the garlic; dice the white part of the scallion and cut the green part into sections, set aside separately.
3. Heat the wok over high heat until very hot, quickly stir-fry the prepared prawns until the shells are crispy and the inside is not fully cooked through, then set aside.
4. Add butter to the wok, immediately reduce to low heat, and sauté the minced garlic and diced scallion whites until fragrant.
5. Return the pre-fried prawns to the wok, increase to high heat, add salt, sugar, and the mixed black and white pepper, quickly stir-fry evenly to coat the prawns with the seasoning.
6. Transfer all ingredients from the wok into the preheated clay pot, cover with the lid, drizzle a circle of rice wine or cooking wine along the edge of the lid, and bake over high heat for 30 seconds to 1 minute to allow the wine aroma and heat to fully blend.
7. Remove the lid, sprinkle in the green scallion sections, toss evenly, and serve.

## Health Tips
Prawns are a high-protein, low-fat quality ingredient. Butterflying reduces cooking time and prevents the meat from becoming tough. The combination of butter and pepper enhances flavor but is relatively high in calories; it is recommended to control the amount of butter or substitute part with olive oil. The cooking method from quick stir-frying to clay pot baking preserves the freshness and aroma of the ingredients, suitable for a daily diet that balances flavor and nutrition.
