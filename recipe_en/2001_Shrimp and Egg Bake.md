# Shrimp and Egg Bake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Year Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 3 eggs
🦐 Shrimp, as needed
🌭 Half a sausage (sliced)
🫒 Olive oil, as needed
🍄 1 tbsp mushroom sauce
🧄 Garlic, minced, as needed
🌶️ Small chili peppers, as needed
🧅 Scallions, chopped, as needed
🧂 Salt, as needed
🌿 Black pepper, as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
♨️ Air fryer
🥣 Foil bowl
🔪 Knife, cutting board
🥄 Spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Evenly spray a layer of olive oil on the inner wall of the foil bowl to prevent sticking.
2. Crack the eggs into the bowl, add the shrimp, sausage slices, mushroom sauce, minced garlic, small chili peppers, chopped scallions, salt, and black pepper. Gently stir until evenly mixed.
   **Note**: Stir only until evenly mixed; no need to beat. Using a foil bowl eliminates the need to wash dishes.
3. Place the foil bowl into the air fryer, set to 180°C, and bake for 15 minutes.
   **Doneness Check**: The finished product should have a golden surface and be fully cooked through.
4. Remove and serve directly.

## Health Advice
Shrimp and eggs provide high-quality protein, suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or fitness enthusiasts. However, the sausage and small chili peppers result in slightly higher sodium content; those sensitive to blood pressure should consume in moderation.
