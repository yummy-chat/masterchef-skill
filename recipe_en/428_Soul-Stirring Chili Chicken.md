# Soul-Stirring Chili Chicken
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken thighs, 2 pieces (boneless, keep the meat surface flat)
🧂 Salt, to taste (for marinating, sprinkle a layer; for the sauce, one spoon)
🌿 Black pepper, to taste (sprinkle a layer on the meat surface)
🥄 Light soy sauce, two spoons
🍬 Sugar, one spoon
🫒 Sesame oil, two spoons
🍶 Rice vinegar, two spoons
🌶️ Chili flakes, one spoon
🥜 Sesame paste, one spoon
🤍 White sesame seeds, one spoon

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, kitchen shears (for deboning)
🪓 Cutting board
🧻 Paper towels
🍳 Non-stick pan (for oil-free water-searing)
📦 Aluminum foil
🥣 Bowl (for mixing the sauce)

## Steps
1. **Prepare the chicken thighs**: Gently pound the chicken thighs with the back of a knife or your fist to tenderize. Make a circular cut around the ankle. Using kitchen shears, cut along the bone and remove it, keeping the chicken intact and flat.
2. **Marinate**: Evenly sprinkle a layer of salt and a layer of black pepper on both sides of the deboned chicken. Massage and rub with your hands, then let rest for a moment to absorb the flavors.
3. **Make the sauce**: In a bowl, combine one spoon of salt, two spoons of light soy sauce, one spoon of sugar, two spoons of sesame oil, two spoons of rice vinegar, one spoon of chili flakes, one spoon of sesame paste, and one spoon of white sesame seeds. Stir thoroughly to create the "Soul-Stirring Chili Sauce."
4. **Sear the skin side**: Heat a non-stick pan over high heat. Do not add oil. Place the chicken skin-side down in the pan. Once the skin starts to render fat, cover with aluminum foil, place a weight on top, and reduce to medium heat. Sear for about 5 minutes until the skin is golden and crispy.
   - **Key point**: Utilize the chicken skin's own fat; the weight ensures even cooking.
5. **Sear the meat side**: Remove the foil, flip the chicken so the meat side is down. Cover again with foil and press with a weight. Sear over medium heat for about 5 minutes.
6. **Re-crisp the skin**: Remove the foil, flip the chicken back to skin-side down, and sear for another 2 minutes to restore crispness. Both sides should be golden and fully cooked.
7. **Slice and drizzle with sauce**: Remove the chicken and cut into large pieces. Arrange on a plate. While still hot, evenly pour the prepared sauce over the chicken. Serve immediately.

## Health Tips
The chicken thighs are skinless and seared without added oil, providing rich protein with controllable fat content. This is suitable for those focusing on fat loss and muscle gain. Note that the sauce contains sesame paste and sesame oil, which are not low in calories, so use it in moderation.
