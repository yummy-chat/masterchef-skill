# Shredded Radish Vegetarian Balls
Cuisine: Northeast Chinese
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 White radish, about 1 large (peeled)
🥕 Carrot, about 1 small (for color)
🧂 Salt, 3 grams
🧂 MSG, a pinch
🍬 Sugar, to taste
🌿 Thirteen Spices, to taste
🥔 Potato starch, 1 tablespoon
🌶️ Chili powder, to taste
🧄 Minced garlic, to taste
🌶️ Small chili peppers, to taste (sliced into rings)
⚪ White sesame seeds, to taste
🥢 Soy sauce, to taste
🍶 Fragrant vinegar, to taste
🌿 Cilantro, to taste (chopped)
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for heating and pouring over aromatics)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Peeler
🧀 Grater
🥣 Stainless steel bowls (multiple)
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Large bowl (for mixing filling)
🥘 Steamer (with steaming cloth)
🥄 Wooden spoon / metal spoon
🍽️ Plates (multiple)

## Instructions
1.  Prepare the dipping sauce: In a bowl, combine chili powder, minced garlic, small chili pepper rings, and white sesame seeds. Pour in hot, sizzling cooking oil to release the aroma. Then add soy sauce and fragrant vinegar, sprinkle with chopped cilantro, and stir to combine. Set aside.
**Key point**: The oil must be boiling hot to fully release the fragrance of the dry ingredients.
2.  Prepare the main ingredients: Peel the white radish and grate it into fine shreds using a grater. Grate the carrot into fine shreds in the same way. Mix the two together.
**Note**: The shreds should be fine and even.
3.  Blanch: Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil. Add the mixed radish shreds and blanch over high heat for about 30 seconds.
**Key point**: Blanching removes the pungent taste of the radish, preventing a "burning" sensation in the final product.
4.  Cool and squeeze dry: Immediately remove the radish shreds and transfer them to cold water until completely cool. Then, thoroughly squeeze out all the water with your hands.
**Check for doneness**: The water must be squeezed out completely; otherwise, the balls will not hold their shape.
5.  Season and mix filling: Place the squeezed-dry radish shreds in a large bowl. Add salt, MSG, sugar, Thirteen Spices, and potato starch one by one. Use your hands to mix and knead evenly, until the shreds are thinly coated with a starchy paste.
**Check for doneness**: The mixture should be loose but able to be formed into a ball. If it is too dry, add a small amount of water; if too wet, add more starch.
6.  Shape: Take an appropriate amount of filling and roll it tightly into a small ball in your palm. Try to make the balls uniform in size.
7.  Steam: Arrange the balls on a steaming tray lined with a steaming cloth. Cover with the lid. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and steam for 8 minutes.
**Check for doneness**: The steamed balls should appear translucent, crystal-clear, and springy to the touch.
8.  Remove and plate. Serve with the prepared dipping sauce.

## Health Advice
This Shredded Radish Vegetarian Balls dish is low in fat and high in fiber, rich in vitamin C and dietary fiber. It is suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or with poor digestion. Note: The radish shreds must be thoroughly squeezed dry, otherwise, the balls will easily fall apart after steaming. Be careful of hot oil splatters when pouring hot oil over the dry ingredients for the dipping sauce.
