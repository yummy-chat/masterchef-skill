# Crispy Ramen Egg
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Eggs (1 whole raw egg per crispy egg)
🍜 Fire Chicken Noodles (pre-cooked and mixed with sauce)
🧅 Onion (sliced or minced)
🥬 Kimchi (chopped)
🥫 Ketchup (appropriate amount, base for sweet and sour sauce)
🧂 Salt (a small amount)
🍬 Sugar (appropriate amount)
🍾 Vinegar (appropriate amount)
🧄 Minced garlic (a small amount)
🌾 Cornstarch (appropriate amount, pre-mixed with water to make a slurry)

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🍳 Wok (for deep-frying and making the sauce)
🥄 Large soup spoon (molding tool)
🥄 Slotted spoon (for removing crispy eggs)
🥄 Wooden spatula or mixing spoon (for stirring the sauce)

## Instructions
1. Mix flour and water in a 1:1 ratio until smooth to make a batter.
2. Cook the Fire Chicken Noodles, then mix with the sauce, shredded onion, and chopped kimchi to form the filling.
3. Mix cornstarch with an appropriate amount of water to make a slurry; set aside.
4. Heat oil in a wok to 60% hot, then fully submerge the large soup spoon in the hot oil to heat it thoroughly.
   **Key**: The spoon must be fully preheated to prevent the batter from sticking.
5. Pour an appropriate amount of batter into the hot spoon and swirl to evenly coat the bottom.
   **Key**: The batter must not flow into the oil, as this can cause splattering.
6. Layer the mixed kimchi and Fire Chicken Noodle filling evenly on top of the batter, pressing down lightly.
7. Crack a whole raw egg into the center of the filling, allowing the egg liquid to naturally cover the filling and fill the edges of the spoon.
8. Carefully lower the filled spoon into the hot oil. Once the bottom batter sets quickly, gently shake the spoon to release the egg patty into the oil.
9. Deep-fry all initially set crispy eggs together over medium-high heat until both sides are golden and crispy, then remove and drain oil.
   **Key**: Double-frying makes the outer crust crispier.
10. In a separate pan, heat a little oil, sauté minced garlic until fragrant, add ketchup and stir-fry until the oil turns red, then pour in an appropriate amount of water and bring to a boil.
11. Season with a small amount of salt, sugar, and vinegar, adjusting to a balanced sweet and sour taste.
12. Pour the cornstarch slurry into the boiling sauce, stirring quickly until the sauce thickens and becomes glossy.
13. Drizzle the prepared sweet and sour sauce evenly over the fried crispy ramen eggs.

## Health Advice
This dish is high in calories. It is recommended to serve with a salad and pair with moderate exercise. The combination of ramen and fried eggs is rich in carbohydrates and fats, suitable for occasional indulgence, but not for frequent consumption.
