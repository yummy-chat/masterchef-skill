# Whole Wheat Bagel Breakfast Sandwich  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥯 Whole wheat bagel dough (pre-fermented dough) as needed  
🌾 Oats, about 2 tablespoons  
⚪ White sesame seeds, about 2 tablespoons  
⚫ Black sesame seeds, about 2 tablespoons  
🎃 Pumpkin seeds, about 2 tablespoons  
🥬 Lettuce, as needed  
🍗 Fried chicken, 2-3 pieces (plain leftover fried chicken)  
🥝 Kiwi, 1  
🍍 Pineapple-flavored farmer cheese, 1 box (contains pineapple jam and farmer cheese)  
🍯 Honey water, as needed (honey mixed with water, for brushing the surface)  
🍅 Ketchup, as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔥 Oven  
🫕 Pot (cast iron or soup pot, for boiling bagels)  
🍞 Baking sheet and parchment paper  
🖌️ Silicone brush  
🥢 Slotted spoon or chopsticks  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🍽️ Plate and utensils  

## Instructions  
1.  Mix the oats, white sesame seeds, black sesame seeds, and pumpkin seeds in a bowl until evenly combined. Set aside as the surface grain topping.  
2.  Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. It is recommended to use a large pot to boil several bagels at once.  
3.  Once the water is boiling, add the bagel dough. Boil each side for 30-40 seconds, then remove and drain.  
   **Key indicator**: If the bagels sink to the bottom after being added to the water, they are under-proofed and will have a bread-like texture. If the surface wrinkles after boiling, they are over-proofed.  
4.  Place the boiled bagels on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the surface evenly with honey water, then immediately sprinkle the mixed grain topping over them.  
   **Note**: Brushing with honey water increases stickiness, helping the seeds adhere and aiding in browning.  
5.  Preheat the oven to the appropriate temperature in advance. Bake the bagels until the surface is golden and cooked through.  
6.  Using the residual oven heat or a low temperature, wrap the leftover fried chicken in aluminum foil and reheat.  
7.  Stir the pineapple jam into the farmer cheese until well combined, making a pineapple cheese sauce.  
8.  Slice the baked bagels horizontally. Layer with lettuce, the reheated fried chicken, and drizzle with ketchup.  
9.  Slice the kiwi. Arrange on a plate with the bagel sandwich and the pineapple cheese sauce. Serve.  

## Health Tips  
This sandwich uses whole wheat bagels paired with fried chicken, cleverly balancing carbohydrates and protein. However, the fried chicken is high in oil and calories due to the high-temperature cooking—pairing it with kiwi and lettuce is recommended to supplement vitamins and dietary fiber. Suitable for young people needing a quick energy boost. For weight loss, it is advised to remove the fried chicken skin.
