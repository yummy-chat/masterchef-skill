# Mango Coconut Jelly
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥥 1 coconut (the empty shell is used as a container; the coconut water and meat are used in other steps)  
🥭 Mango, as needed (cut into small cubes)  
🧊 Coconut milk jelly liquid, as needed (yellow liquid, usually made by mixing coconut milk with a setting agent like gelatin)

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Knife  
🥄 Spoon  
🍳 Pot (for heating to set) or ❄️ Refrigerator (for chilling to set)  
🥣 Container (for receiving and holding ingredients)

## Steps
1. Prepare the empty coconut shell and set it aside as a container.
2. Pour the prepared coconut milk jelly liquid into the coconut shell.
3. Add the diced mango.
4. Use either heating or refrigeration to solidify the liquid into a jelly form.  
   **Doneness Check**: The liquid should become a semi-transparent, brownish-yellow solidified state.
5. Remove the set coconut jelly and serve directly or with a simple garnish.

## Health Tips
This coconut jelly is rich in the healthy fats from coconut milk and vitamin C from mango, making it a suitable refreshing summer dessert. However, it is high in sugar and calories, so it is best consumed in moderation. People with weaker digestive systems should control their portion size.
