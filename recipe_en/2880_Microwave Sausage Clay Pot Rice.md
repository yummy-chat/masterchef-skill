# Microwave Sausage Clay Pot Rice
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
- 🍚 Rice 200 ml  
- 🌭 Chinese sausage 1 piece  
- 🥚 Egg 1 piece  
- 🥕 Carrot 1 piece  
- 🧅 Spring onion 1 stalk  
- 🥬 Green vegetables as needed  

## Seasonings  
- 🧂 Salt 5 g  
- 🛢️ Cooking oil 15 ml  
- 🍾 Light soy sauce 10 ml  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
- 📡 Microwave oven  
- 🥣 2 large bowls (microwave-safe recommended)  
- 🥄 1 small bowl  
- 🧤 Heat-resistant gloves or towel  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Wash the rice thoroughly, place it in a large bowl, add 400 ml of water, and cover with a lid.  
2. Put the large bowl in the microwave and heat on high for 6 minutes. Meanwhile, slice the Chinese sausage, wash the green vegetables, slice the carrot, and chop the spring onion into small pieces.  
3. Take another large bowl, add the green vegetables and carrot slices, then add 10 ml of cooking oil and 5 g of salt. Mix well.  
4. Pour 10 ml of light soy sauce and 5 ml of cooking oil into a small bowl, and set aside.  
5. After 6 minutes, use heat-resistant gloves to remove the rice bowl. The rice should be about 80% cooked.  
6. Evenly spread the sliced Chinese sausage over the surface of the rice, return it to the microwave, and continue heating on high for 2 minutes.  
7. Remove the bowl, then place the bowl with green vegetables and carrot in the microwave and heat on high for 4–5 minutes until cooked.  
8. Arrange the heated vegetables on top of the sausage rice, crack the egg in the center, and continue heating on high for 40–60 seconds according to preference.  
9. Remove the sausage rice bowl, place the small bowl with light soy sauce and oil in the microwave, and heat on high for 30 seconds.  
10. Take out the small bowl, drizzle the heated soy sauce and oil mixture over the rice, and sprinkle with chopped spring onion.  
11. Any leftover vegetables can be dipped in the remaining soy sauce and eaten.  

## Health Advice  
Chinese sausage provides high-quality protein and iron, paired with carrots and green vegetables to supplement dietary fiber, making it suitable for a fast-paced lifestyle. Pay attention to controlling the microwave time to prevent the sausage from becoming overly dry and hard due to excessive heating.
