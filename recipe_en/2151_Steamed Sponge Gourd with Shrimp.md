## Cooking Steps
1. Prepare black tiger shrimp: Remove head and shell, reserve the heads; butterfly the shrimp and devein, clean thoroughly.
2. Wash the shrimp: Add a small amount of salt and starch to the cleaned shrimp, rub well, then rinse clean and drain.  
   **Note**: This step is for cleaning only, not marinating, to ensure accurate final seasoning.
3. Marinate the shrimp: To the drained shrimp, add cooking wine, salt, and starch, mix well, and marinate for about 20 minutes, until no water remains at the bottom of the bowl.
4. Prepare the vegetables:  
   - Trim 2–3 cm from the bottom of the enoki mushrooms, cut into 3 cm segments, and separate any clumps.  
   - Peel and slice the ginger, then cut into shreds.  
   - Peel the tough skin of the sponge gourd, trim the ends, cut lengthwise into four sections, then slice into pieces about 1 cm thick.
5. Prepare the clams:  
   - Expel sand: Place clams in a bowl, add water to just cover, add salt until the water tastes as salty as seawater, and let sit for about 1 hour.  
   - Cook for broth: Put the sand-expelled clams into a small saucepan, add cooking wine and a small cup of water, cover, and bring to a boil over high heat. After steam appears, cook for about 3 minutes until the clams open, then remove the clam meat for later use and strain the liquid to make broth.
6. Stir-fry the shrimp heads: Add a little oil to the frying pan over medium-high heat, add the shrimp heads, and fry on both sides until slightly charred and colored. Press with chopsticks to release flavor, then remove and discard the heads.
7. Pan-fry the shrimp: Using the shrimp oil in the pan, add the marinated shrimp and fry until both sides turn red, curl, and become pink; transfer the shrimp to a wire rack to drain excess oil.  
   **Note**: Placing the fried shrimp on a wire rack prevents them from overcooking.
8. Sauté aromatics and sponge gourd: Add a little more oil to the pan, add the crushed garlic and ginger shreds, and stir-fry over medium heat until the oil bubbles and releases fragrance. Add the sponge gourd slices and stir-fry until the edges become translucent and soft.
9. Braise: Pour in the clam broth, add the enoki mushrooms, cover, and simmer for about 10–15 minutes, until the sponge gourd and enoki mushrooms are fully tender.
10. Thicken and season: Mix starch with water to make a slurry, pour into the pan to thicken, then add the pan-fried shrimp and clam meat. Season with salt, sesame oil, and white pepper powder, stir well, and serve.

## Health Tips
This dish is low in fat and high in protein. Sponge gourd helps cool the body. Shrimp provide calcium, making it suitable for those seeking a low-fat diet and maintaining fitness during summer. Ensure clams are thoroughly sand-expelled; otherwise, it may affect the texture.
