# Soft Egg Wrap Pancake  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 All-purpose flour 200g  
🧂 Salt 5g  
💧 Warm water 450g  
🥚 Egg 1  
🖤 Black sesame seeds, as needed  
🌿 Chopped scallions, as needed  
🥬 Greens, as needed (choose according to preference)  
🥔 Shredded potatoes, as needed (pre-cooked or blanched)  
🌭 Sausage, 1  
🍯 Sweet bean paste, as needed  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🥄 Spoon  
🔪 Spatula  
🖌️ Brush  
🥣 Bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Instructions  
1. In a bowl, combine flour and salt. Add warm water and stir until a smooth, lump-free batter forms. Let it rest for 30 minutes.  
2. Heat a non-stick pan over low heat, brush with a thin layer of oil. Pour in an appropriate amount of batter and quickly swirl the pan to spread it evenly into a thin pancake.  
3. Cook over low heat until the surface sets and changes color. Crack an egg on top, and gently spread it with chopsticks to cover the entire pancake.  
4. When the egg is half set, evenly sprinkle black sesame seeds and chopped scallions.  
5. Flip the pancake and continue cooking until the bottom is golden brown. Evenly brush a layer of sweet bean paste.  
6. Layer greens, shredded potatoes, and sausage on the pancake. Roll it up tightly starting from the filled side. Slice into pieces or serve whole.  

## Health Tips  
This soft egg wrap pancake is nutritionally balanced and suitable for breakfast or a light meal. Be mindful of the amount of sweet bean paste to avoid excessive sodium intake. Adding vegetables can boost dietary fiber. It’s recommended to pair with soy milk or light tea to refresh and complement the flavors.
