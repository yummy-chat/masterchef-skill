# Oil-Poached Cherry Tomato Fried Egg on Toast  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍅 Oil-poached cherry tomatoes, half a box (includes olive oil, garlic, rosemary, etc.)  
🥚 Eggs, 4  
🍞 Toast bread, about 2 slices (or baguette)  
🌭 Small sausages, about 4  
🧂 Coarsely ground black pepper, to taste  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🍳 Frying pan  
🔥 Oven  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🍽️ Serving plate  

## Instructions  
1. Pour the oil-poached cherry tomatoes along with their oil and aromatics into a cold frying pan. Heat over medium-low heat.  
   **Note**: No additional oil is needed, as the oil-poached cherry tomatoes already contain sufficient olive oil.  
2. Once the oil is heated, crack the eggs directly into the gaps between the cherry tomatoes in the pan.  
3. Cover the pan with a lid and reduce to medium-low heat, simmering for about 3–4 minutes, until the egg whites are fully set and the yolks reach the desired doneness.  
4. While frying the eggs, place the toast and small sausages in a preheated oven and bake until the bread surface is dry and crispy and the sausage skins are slightly charred.  
5. Remove the toasted bread from the oven and place the fried oil-poached cherry tomatoes and eggs on top of the toast.  
6. Sprinkle a suitable amount of coarsely ground black pepper over the finished dish.  

## Health Tips  
This dish uses the olive oil from the oil-poached cherry tomatoes to fry the eggs, saving oil while adding flavor. However, note that the cherry tomatoes and sausages are high in sodium; people with high blood pressure are advised to reduce the amount of sausages. Toasting the bread until crispy and pairing it with runny eggs creates an ideal combination of quality carbohydrates and protein, making it suitable for an energy-boosting breakfast.
