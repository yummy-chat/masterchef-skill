# Stir-Fried Crispy Cucumber Peel with Pork
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥒 Tender cucumbers 10 jin (the more tender, the better the texture)  
🥩 Pork belly, as needed  
🌶️ Fresh chili peppers, as needed (red chili, etc., sliced)  
🫚 Ginger, as needed (julienned)  
🧄 Garlic leaves or scallions, as needed (for aroma at the end of cooking)  
🧂 Salt, about 2 tablespoons (half for blanching, half for pickling, enough to cover each layer of cucumbers)  
🍾 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🌶️ Chili sauce, as needed  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍲 Large pot (for blanching and adding weight during pressing)  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥢 Disposable chopsticks  
🛍️ Muslin bag  
🪵 Wooden frame + wooden board (or tofu mold, for shaping and pressing)  
🧊 Plastic container (to catch liquid)  
🫙 Sealed glass jar  
🍳 Wok  

## Steps  
1. Wash 10 jin of fresh tender cucumbers thoroughly.  
2. Fill a pot with water, add about 1 tablespoon of salt. Once the water boils, add the cucumbers and blanch for 2-3 minutes until the skin softens, then remove.  
   **Note**: Do not cook until fully done, or the texture will become mushy.  
3. Remove the cucumbers and, while still hot, poke several small holes in each cucumber with chopsticks.  
4. Place the cucumbers in a muslin bag, put it in a plastic container lined with a wooden frame and board, and weigh it down with heavy objects. Press for at least 24 hours.  
   **Key point**: The drier the pressing, the longer the storage time and the crisper the texture.  
5. Take out the pressed shriveled cucumbers, rinse them with cooled boiled water or purified water, then squeeze out excess water by hand. If the weather is fine, they can be briefly sun-dried.  
6. Place the cucumbers in a clean, dry sealed glass jar. Layer cucumbers and salt alternately, pressing firmly with your hand. Finally, seal the jar tightly and pickle for 3-7 days until sour.  
   **Note**: If the jar lid is not airtight, cover the jar mouth with a food bag before tightening the lid.  
7. Take out the pickled crispy cucumber peel, wash it, and cut into small pieces for later use. Meanwhile, prepare ginger shreds, garlic slices, red chili pepper slices, pork belly slices, and garlic leaves.  
8. Heat a wok with oil over high heat. First, stir-fry the ginger shreds and garlic slices until fragrant, then add the pork belly slices and stir-fry until the fat renders.  
9. Add the crispy cucumber peel and red chili pepper slices, and stir-fry evenly.  
10. Season to taste with salt, light soy sauce, and chili sauce, then stir-fry again.  
11. Just before serving, sprinkle in the garlic leaves, toss a few times, and plate.  
    **Key point**: Garlic leaves or scallions should be added last to better retain their aroma.  

## Health Advice  
This stir-fried crispy cucumber peel with pork condenses the essence of 10 jin of cucumbers, offering rich dietary fiber and probiotics, ideal for stimulating the appetite and relieving greasiness in summer. Those with sensitive stomachs should control their intake of pickled vegetables; people with high blood pressure should reduce the amount of salt used.
