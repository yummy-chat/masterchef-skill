# Whole Wheat Toast
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients
🌾 Whole wheat flour 150g
🍞 Bread flour 150g
💧 Water 165g
🥚 Whole egg liquid 50g

## Health Tips
Whole wheat toast is rich in dietary fiber and B vitamins, suitable for those who need to control blood sugar or enhance satiety. Replacing butter with vegetable oil can reduce saturated fat intake, though total calorie control should still be observed.
