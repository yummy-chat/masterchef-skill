# Buttered King Oyster Mushrooms  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍄 King oyster mushrooms, to taste (sliced diagonally into thick pieces, optional crosshatch scoring on surface)  
🧄 Garlic, a large amount (minced)  
🧈 Butter, to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil, a small amount  
🧂 Salt, one spoon  
🧂 Black pepper, to taste  
🧂 Light soy sauce, a small amount  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Large frying pan  
♨️ Gas stove  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🔗 Cooking tongs  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Slice the king oyster mushrooms diagonally into thick pieces. Optionally score a crosshatch pattern on the surface to help absorb flavors.  
2. Mince the garlic into fine pieces and set aside.  
3. Heat the frying pan over the stove. Add butter and allow it to melt, then immediately add a small amount of cooking oil to prevent the butter from burning too quickly.  
4. Sprinkle one spoon of salt and an appropriate amount of black pepper into the oil.  
5. Place the king oyster mushroom slices in the pan. Adjust to high heat and fry until both sides are golden brown with slight char marks.  
   **Key point**: Don’t worry about the pan scorching; achieving a rich color is essential for releasing a deep aroma.  
6. Add all the minced garlic and stir-fry quickly until evenly combined.  
7. Drizzle in a small amount of light soy sauce, stir rapidly to coat each mushroom slice evenly with the savory sauce.  
8. Plate and serve hot. The garlic blends with the butter, offering a rich, thick, and savory texture.  

## Health Suggestions  
Pan-frying king oyster mushrooms with butter helps absorb fat-soluble vitamins, while garlic provides antibacterial components. This dish is suitable for those looking to enrich the texture of a vegetarian meal. Be mindful of controlling the amounts of butter and cooking oil to avoid excess calories. For individuals with high blood lipids, the proportion of butter can be appropriately reduced.
