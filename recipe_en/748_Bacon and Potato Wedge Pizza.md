# Bacon and Potato Wedge Pizza
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍞 Bread flour 300g  
💧 Water 200g  
🧈 Butter 10g (or 20g for richer flavor, no need to melt in advance)  
🍬 White sugar 20g  
🍄 Instant dry yeast 3g  
🧂 Salt 3g  
🥚 Hard-boiled egg 1 (for making the base sauce)  
🥗 Mayonnaise 100g (optional flavor)  
🧀 Mozzarella cheese, as needed (spread in two layers for stretching)  
🥓 Bacon, as needed (smoked flavor recommended)  
🍟 Frozen fries or potato wedges, as needed (or fresh potatoes cut into chunks)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🫗 Stainless steel mixing bowl  
🥄 Spatula  
🛡️ Plastic wrap or pot lid  
🥖 Rolling pin  
🍴 Fork  
🌀 Piping bag with nozzle  
🔥 Oven or air fryer  
🧻 Parchment paper  

## Steps  
1. **Mix dry and wet ingredients**: Place bread flour, water, white sugar, instant dry yeast, salt, and butter cubes into a bowl. There is no need to soften the butter in advance; mix directly until a rough dough forms with no dry flour.
2. **First proofing**: Stir the dough with a spatula until it comes together, then cover with plastic wrap or a pot lid and let it rest at room temperature for 15 minutes. No kneading is required.
3. **First fold**: The dough will be quite sticky at this point. Fold the edges toward the center once, cover, and let it rest again for another 15 minutes.
4. **Second fold and final rise**: Repeat the folding process. The dough will have developed more gluten and will no longer be sticky. Cover and let it continue rising until it doubles in size and has a honeycomb interior.
5. **Roll out the pizza base**: Dust a work surface with flour, take out the dough, and roll it into a circle with a thinner center and thicker edges. The crust should be thick and resemble the rim of a wheel, resulting in a bread-like texture after baking.
6. **Prick the base**: Use a fork to evenly prick holes all over the dough base to prevent it from puffing up too much during baking.
7. **Apply the base sauce**: Mash the hard-boiled egg and mix it with mayonnaise until smooth. Transfer to a piping bag and pipe the mixture over the dough base, then spread it evenly with a spoon into a thin layer. Avoid making it too salty. This sauce is the key to the flavor.
8. **Layer the toppings**: Spread a layer of mozzarella cheese, followed by bacon slices and fries or potato chunks, then cover with a thick layer of mozzarella cheese. Spreading the cheese in two layers enhances the stretchiness. Arrange the ingredients freely.
9. **Bake**: Preheat the oven in advance. Place the pizza on the middle rack and bake at 230°C (top heat) and 220°C (bottom heat) for 15 minutes. If using an air fryer, lower the temperature by 20°C and check frequently during baking to prevent burning.
10. **Decorate and serve**: After removing from the oven, you can drizzle another layer of mayonnaise on top. Cut and serve while hot.

## Health Tips  
Bacon and potato wedge pizza is a high-carb, high-protein combination, suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. However, mayonnaise and cheese are high in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad for balance. Using baked potato wedges instead of fried reduces fat, and pre-frying the bacon can help render out excess grease.
