# Cheese and Beef Stuffed Mushroom Tower
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Ground beef 300g (recommended with some fat, such as Wagyu)  
🍄 White mushrooms 8-10 (choose whole, nice-looking ones as containers)  
🧅 Onion a little (finely minced)  
🧄 Garlic 4 cloves (finely minced)  
🌿 Thyme a little (finely minced)  
🧀 Parmesan cheese to taste (grated for filling and topping)  
🍄 Mushroom stems to taste (trimmings, can be chopped for other use)  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🌶️ Freshly ground black pepper to taste  
🧈 Butter a small amount (melted and added to the filling)  
💧 Water a small amount  
🫒 Olive oil to taste (drizzled on top to prevent drying)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🔪 Small knife or spatula  
🍳 Cast iron pan or baking dish  
🖌️ Brush  
🧀 Grater  
♨️ Oven (preheated to 180°C)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Finely mince the onion, garlic, and thyme separately.  
2. Place the ground beef in a bowl, add the minced aromatics, and season with salt and black pepper. Pour in the melted butter and a little water, and stir in one direction with chopsticks until the meat mixture becomes sticky and firm.  
3. Wash the white mushrooms and remove the stems. Use the tip of the knife to hollow out and deepen the small cavity inside each mushroom to hold more filling. The removed mushroom stems can be chopped for other use.  
4. Fill the seasoned meat mixture into the mushroom cavities, pressing firmly and mounding it slightly above the rim, as the meat will shrink during baking.  
5. Lightly brush the bottom of the baking dish with oil. Arrange the stuffed mushrooms in the dish and drizzle a little olive oil over the tops to prevent them from drying out.  
6. Preheat the oven to 180°C and bake the mushrooms for 8 minutes.  
7. Remove the baking dish from the oven and thickly cover the top of each mushroom with grated Parmesan cheese.  
8. Return to the oven and continue baking at 180°C for 3 to 5 minutes, until the cheese is completely melted and lightly browned.  
9. After removing from the oven, sprinkle with a little cracked black pepper or grated cheese as desired. Serve hot.  
   **Key Points**: The meat mixture must be stirred until sticky for a good texture; the mushroom cavities should be hollowed out generously; fill the mixture mounded above the rim; drizzling olive oil before the first bake keeps them moist; the cheese layer should be thick enough to achieve a browned, stringy effect.  

## Health Tips  
The combination of beef and mushrooms is low in fat and high in protein, making it suitable for muscle building or controlling carbohydrate intake. It is recommended to pair with a green salad to balance the flavor. Note that Parmesan cheese is high in sodium, so those sensitive to blood pressure should consume it in moderation.
