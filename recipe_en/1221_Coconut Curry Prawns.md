# Coconut Curry Prawns
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🦐 Black Tiger Prawns, as needed (palm-sized, better to prepare more)  
🥥 Coconut Milk, 150 ml (can add more)  
🧅 White Onion, half (sliced into strips)  
🥜 Cashew Nuts, as needed (roasted, chopped for garnish)  
🌶️ Bird's Eye Chili, as needed (optional, for extra heat and garnish)  
🌿 Cilantro, as needed (for garnish)  
🧂 Yellow Curry Paste, 1 packet  
🍬 Sugar, as needed  
🐟 Fish Sauce, as needed (if available, enhances umami)  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🍳 Frying Pan  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🔪 Knife and Cutting Board  

## Instructions
1. Peel the black tiger prawns, leaving the tail segment intact. Slice open along the back, remove the digestive tract, rinse, and drain.  
   **Key Point**: Ensure the digestive tract is completely removed, otherwise it will affect the texture.

2. Slice the white onion into strips and chop the roasted cashew nuts. Set aside.

3. Heat oil in a frying pan. Add the prawn heads and fry over low heat until they turn red and the oil becomes a rich orange-red.  
   **Key Point**: Extracting the prawn oil thoroughly is the basis of the dish's flavor.

4. Remove and discard the prawn heads, leaving the oil in the pan. Add the onion strips and sauté over low heat until softened and fragrant.

5. Squeeze in the entire packet of yellow curry paste. Stir-fry together with the onion over low heat until the curry paste and oil are fully combined and aromatic.

6. Add the prawns and stir-fry until the surface changes color and they are half-cooked. Do not overcook.

7. Pour in the coconut milk and stir to combine. Add a little water if the sauce is too thick. After it comes to a boil, season with sugar and fish sauce.  
   **Key Point**: The curry paste already contains salt, so additional salt is usually not needed. Taste and adjust before serving.

8. Add the bird's eye chili and cook briefly. Keep enough sauce in the pan; do not reduce it completely, as it is intended for mixing with rice.

9. Plate the dish, sprinkle with chopped cashew nuts and cilantro, and finally drizzle a swirl of coconut milk for garnish.

## Health Advice
This coconut curry prawn dish is rich in high-quality protein and healthy fats, making it suitable for those needing an energy boost. Coconut milk is relatively high in calories, so watch your portion size. Pairing it with mixed grain rice can increase dietary fiber and balance nutrition.
