# Original Flavor Crispy Noodles and Lime Flavor Crispy Noodles
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍜 Instant fried noodles, about 1 packet  
🫒 Vegetable oil, as needed (to increase lubrication)  
🧂 Light soy sauce, as needed  
🧂 Aromatic powder (compound seasoning powder), as needed  
🧈 Butter, 1 small piece (for original flavor)  
🍋 Lime jam, as needed (for lime flavor)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Flat-bottomed pan (for boiling noodles)  
🥄 Strainer/slotted spoon  
🔥 Oven  
🥣 Baking tray and parchment paper  
🍶 Bowl (for mixing noodles)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Boil the instant noodles in a pot of boiling water until the noodle block just loosens. Remove immediately and rinse with plenty of cold water, then drain thoroughly.  
   **Note**: Thorough draining is key to achieving crispness later; residual moisture will cause baking failure.  

2. Add vegetable oil, light soy sauce, and aromatic powder to the drained noodles, and mix well to season evenly.  

3. Spread the seasoned noodles flat on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. You can place a small piece of butter on top to enhance the milky aroma.  

4. Preheat the oven, then place the baking tray on the middle rack. Bake with top and bottom heat until the noodle block is completely dry and crispy, with a golden color. Temperature reference: 160–180°C, bake for 10–15 minutes. Adjust based on actual conditions, ensuring it is fully baked without over-browning.  

5. Remove and let cool to obtain the original flavor crispy noodles.  

6. Take a portion of the baked original flavor crispy noodle block, break it into small pieces with your hands, drizzle with lime jam, and mix well. The amount of jam can be adjusted according to personal preference, so the broken noodles are evenly coated with a sweet and sour flavor.  

## Health Advice  
This is a high-calorie dish, so people on a diet should consume it with caution. The lime flavor can provide some vitamin C, but overall calorie intake should still be monitored.
