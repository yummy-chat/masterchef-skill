# Sea Salt Mashed Potatoes
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty Level: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥔 1 large potato  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🥛 Milk, to taste  
🌽 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch  
🦪 1 tablespoon oyster sauce  
💧 5 tablespoons water  
🧂 Black pepper, to taste

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
📡 Microwave  
🥣 Bowl  
🍽️ Plate  
🎞️ Plastic wrap  
🥄 Spoon

## Instructions
1. Wash the potato and wrap it completely with a damp paper towel to lock in moisture.  
2. Microwave on high heat for 6–8 minutes, until the potato is fully soft and cooked through.  
3. Remove the potato, peel off the skin, and mash it into a smooth puree with a spoon.  
4. Add salt and milk to the mashed potato, stirring until smooth.  
5. Take a bowl, line it with plastic wrap, fill with the seasoned mashed potato, and press flat with the back of a spoon.  
6. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, mix 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch, 1 tablespoon oyster sauce, 5 tablespoons water, and black pepper to taste, stirring into a thin paste.  
7. Place the sauce bowl in the microwave, heat for 10–15 seconds, then remove and stir. Repeat this process until the sauce thickens.  
   **Note**: Heat in short intervals and stir to prevent the cornstarch from clumping, resulting in a thick sauce consistency.  
8. Invert the bowl of mashed potato onto a plate, lift off the bowl and plastic wrap to unmold, then drizzle with the prepared sauce.

## Health Tips
These mashed potatoes are quickly made in the microwave and paired with an oyster sauce dressing, offering a low-fat, satisfying option suitable for those seeking a low-oil diet. Potatoes are rich in potassium and vitamin C; pay attention to the amount of salt and oyster sauce used to control sodium intake.
