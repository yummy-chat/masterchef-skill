# Stir-Fried Shredded Pancake
Cuisine: Common home-cooked dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥬 Half a cabbage (finely shredded)
🥕 Half a carrot (finely shredded)
🥚 3 eggs
🫓 About 300g (1 plate) shredded pancake
🧅 Scallion (shredded), as needed
🧄 Minced garlic (as needed, essential garlic)
🧂 A pinch of salt
🍶 A splash of Shaoxing wine
🧂 A pinch of Song Xian Xian (or MSG substitute)
🍾 Soy sauce, as needed
🍶 Vinegar, as needed
🫒 Sesame oil, as needed
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed

## Utensils and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪚 Cutting board
🥣 Glass bowl
🥢 Chopsticks
🍳 Frying pan (non-stick)
🥄 Silicone spatula (or chopsticks)
🫙 Lid

## Instructions
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and a splash of Shaoxing wine, and whisk thoroughly until well combined. Finely shred the cabbage and carrot, and shred the scallion. Set aside.
2. Heat the pan over heat, add cooking oil. Once the oil is hot, pour in the egg mixture. Stir-fry over medium-high heat until the eggs are just set and scrambled. Remove and set aside.
3. Add a little more oil to the pan. Add the shredded scallion and stir-fry over medium heat until fragrant.
4. Add the shredded cabbage and carrot. Turn the heat to high and stir-fry until the vegetables are slightly softened.
5. Season with salt and Song Xian Xian (or MSG substitute). Stir well to combine.
6. Evenly spread the shredded pancake over the vegetables. **Do not stir at this point.** Cover the pan with a lid, reduce the heat to low, and steam for about 1 minute to soften and allow the pancake to absorb the flavors.
7. Remove the lid. Add the cooked eggs back to the pan. Drizzle soy sauce, vinegar, and sesame oil along the edge of the pan. Finally, sprinkle the minced garlic on top.
8. Turn the heat to high. Quickly toss everything together using chopsticks or a silicone spatula until the shredded pancake, vegetables, and seasonings are well combined. Serve immediately.

## Health Tips
Stir-fried shredded pancake is rich in carbohydrates and vegetable fiber, making it suitable for those needing a quick energy boost. However, since the shredded pancake is pan-fried with oil, it is relatively high in calories. During weight loss, it is recommended to pair it with a clear soup. Garlic and vinegar aid digestion, but be mindful of the soy sauce amount to avoid excessive sodium intake.
