# 🍸 B52 Bomber
Cuisine: Others
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
- 🥃 Kahlúa coffee liqueur 10ml
- 🍶 Baileys Irish cream liqueur 10ml
- 🍾 Skyy original vodka 10ml

## Kitchenware & Equipment
- 🥄 Bar spoon
- 🥃 Shot glass
- 🔥 Lighter

## Steps
1. Pour Kahlúa coffee liqueur into the shot glass until it fills about 1/3.
2. Hold the back of the bar spoon flat against the inner wall of the glass, and slowly pour Baileys Irish cream liqueur over the back of the spoon until it fills about 1/3. Control the pouring speed to keep the layers distinct, avoiding mixing with the bottom coffee liqueur layer.
3. Slowly pour Skyy original vodka over the back of the spoon as the top layer.
4. Use a lighter to gently warm the rim of the glass.
5. Ignite the surface of the drink; a light blue flame should appear.
6. Insert a straw and drink quickly in one go, or drink directly from the glass.

## Health Advice
It is recommended to use a straw to consume the entire drink quickly in one gulp to reduce the burning sensation from the layered alcohol. Suitable for adults who can handle strong spirits. Do not attempt if you have a sensitive stomach.
