# Meat and Egg Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 Rice
🥚 Eggs
🥩 Ground meat
🧅 Scallion
🧂 Dark soy sauce
🧂 Light soy sauce
🍶 Vinegar
🍬 Sugar
🫒 Fried shallot oil (optional)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥘 Lid
🥣 Bowl

## Steps
1. Rinse the rice, cook with an appropriate amount of water until done, and set aside.
2. Heat the wok and add 30 ml of cooking oil.
3. Add the ground meat, reduce to medium heat, and fry until both sides are slightly browned and fragrant.
4. Crack the eggs directly into the wok, keeping the yolks intact, and immediately cover with the lid.
5. In a small bowl, mix 10 ml dark soy sauce, 25 ml light soy sauce, 20 ml vinegar, 15 g sugar, and 10 g fried shallot oil, stirring to combine into a sauce.
6. Open the lid, evenly pour the sauce over the contents of the wok, cover again, and continue simmering for 3 minutes, allowing the meat and eggs to fully absorb the sauce.
7. Turn off the heat, and pour the meat, eggs, and sauce from the wok over the rice.
8. Ensure the gas is turned off, then enjoy.

## Notes
- If you prefer a sweeter taste, increase the sugar by 10 g.
- After cooking, be sure to turn off the gas to ensure safety.

## Health Advice
This meat and egg rice bowl is rich in protein and carbohydrates, making it suitable for a quick energy boost after a busy meal. It is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition, and to control the amount of sauce used, reducing sugar and sodium for a healthier option.
