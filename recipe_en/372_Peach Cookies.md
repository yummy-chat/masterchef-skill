# Peach Cookies
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 All-purpose flour 250g (can substitute with 300g cake flour)
🌽 Corn starch 50g
🧂 Baking soda 3g
🧂 Aluminum-free baking powder 4g
🧈 Softened butter (or lard) 180g
🥚 Eggs 4 (about 200g, yolks only)
🌿 Black sesame seeds, as needed
🧂 Salt 2g
🍬 Powdered sugar 100g

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🔌 Electric hand mixer
⚖️ Kitchen scale
🧹 Sifter
🥄 Silicone spatula
🍳 Baking sheet
📄 Parchment paper
🖌️ Brush
🔥 Oven

## Steps
1. Mix all-purpose flour, corn starch, baking soda, baking powder, and salt evenly as the dry ingredients.
   **Note**: Mix thoroughly to ensure even distribution of leavening agents.
2. Separate the yolks from 4 eggs and beat them into a yolk mixture, set aside.
3. Beat the softened butter with an electric hand mixer until it turns pale, increases in volume, and becomes light and fluffy.
4. Gradually add the yolk mixture to the beaten butter, then add the powdered sugar. Fold with a silicone spatula until the color is uniform, avoid overmixing.
5. Sift the dry ingredients into the butter mixture and fold with a silicone spatula until no dry flour remains. Then gently knead into a dough by hand to avoid overworking and developing gluten.
6. Take about 25g of dough, roll into a ball, flatten into a disc shape, place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and press a small indent in the center with your thumb.
7. Evenly brush a layer of egg wash on the surface of the unbaked cookies, then sprinkle with black sesame seeds.
8. Preheat the oven to 180°C for 10 minutes. Place the baking sheet on the middle rack and bake at 180°C (top and bottom heat) for about 18 minutes. Monitor closely in the last few minutes to prevent burning.

## Health Advice
This is a classic Chinese pastry high in oil and sugar. It is recommended to control portion sizes, with 1-2 pieces per serving as an afternoon tea snack. Due to the high content of butter and powdered sugar, those with high blood pressure, high blood sugar, high cholesterol, or those on a weight loss diet should consume it sparingly.
