# Fresh Garlic Chili Sauce
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌶️ 2 jin small chili peppers (about 1000 grams, can substitute with line peppers to adjust spiciness)
🧄 1 jin garlic (about 500 grams, ratio with chili peppers about 1:2)
🧂 70 grams edible salt (if using only 1 jin of chili peppers, recommend 35-40 grams)
🍶 A small bottle cap of white liquor (for aroma)
🍬 Sugar or MSG (optional, to enhance flavor)
🫒 About 500-600 grams cooking oil (should be enough to cover the chili sauce, tea oil offers better flavor)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🧺 Bamboo basket (for holding and washing chili peppers)
🥣 Large glass bowl (for mixing ingredients)
🥅 Drying net/strainer (for drying chili peppers)
⚙️ Electric meat grinder (for chopping garlic and chili peppers)
🫗 Strainer (for filtering impurities when pouring oil)
🍳 Wok (for heating cooking oil)
🫙 Sealed glass jar (for storing finished product)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Handle the chili peppers**: Wash the chili peppers thoroughly, wear gloves throughout when removing the stems, spread them flat on a drying net and air dry in a ventilated area, ensuring no raw water residue remains on the surface.
2. **Handle the garlic**: Peel the garlic and place it in the meat grinder, pulse 4-5 times until it forms garlic paste with visible granules, pour into a large bowl and set aside. Be careful not to grind it into a puree.
3. **Chop the chili peppers**: Place the dried chili peppers in the meat grinder, similarly pulse into chili pepper pieces with granules.
4. **Mix and marinate**: Pour the chopped chili peppers into the bowl with the garlic paste, add edible salt and white liquor, add sugar or MSG as needed, mix thoroughly and let it sit to marinate.
5. **Make the seasoning oil**: Pour cooking oil into the wok, heat over medium heat until slightly warm, immediately turn off the heat and let it cool briefly. Note that the oil should not smoke, to avoid cooking the chili peppers and destroying their fresh, crisp texture.
6. **Pour oil and mix**: Slowly pour the cooled oil through a strainer into the chili and garlic mixture, stir with a spoon until fully combined, ensuring the chili and garlic are thoroughly soaked in the oil.
7. **Bottle and store**: Transfer into a clean, dry sealed jar, close tightly and refrigerate. For a milder spiciness and more harmonious flavor, let it marinate overnight before consuming.

## Health Advice
This fresh garlic chili sauce is rich in vitamin C and allicin, making it suitable as an appetizer to accompany meals. However, it is high in salt and oil, so people with high blood pressure should consume it in moderation. It is recommended to pair it with light vegetables and avoid eating on an empty stomach to prevent irritating the digestive system.
