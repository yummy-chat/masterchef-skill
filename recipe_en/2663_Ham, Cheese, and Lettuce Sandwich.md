# Ham, Cheese, and Lettuce Sandwich
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥚 1 egg  
🧀 About 2 slices of cheese  
🍞 About 3 slices of bread  
🍖 About 2 slices of ham  
🥬 About 1 leaf of lettuce  
🫙 Sauce (mayonnaise or mustard) to taste  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🥄 Wooden spatula  
🫗 Lid  
🫕 Plastic wrap  
✂️ Scissors  

## Instructions
1. Crack one egg and beat it into a uniform egg mixture.
2. Preheat a non-stick pan, pour in the egg mixture, and cook over medium-low heat to form an egg crepe. When the bottom is set and the surface is still runny, place two slices of cheese in the center.
3. Use a wooden spatula to fold the edges of the egg crepe toward the center, wrapping the cheese to form a square egg packet. Cover with a lid and cook gently for a moment, then remove and set aside.
4. On a piece of plastic wrap, layer in order: one slice of bread → two slices of ham → one slice of bread → one leaf of lettuce → squeeze an appropriate amount of sauce → place the egg packet → finally top with one slice of bread.
5. Wrap the entire sandwich tightly with plastic wrap and press firmly, then cut in half with scissors.

## Health Tips  
This sandwich is rich in protein and calcium, making it suitable for breakfast or post-workout energy replenishment. Mayonnaise is high in calories, so it is recommended to use low-fat dressing or mustard to control calorie intake.
