# Passion Fruit Hand-Torn Chicken
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken breast (skinless)
🟠 Passion fruit about 2 (take the pulp)
🧄 Minced garlic (plenty)
🧅 Small onion 1 (sliced)
🍄 Mushrooms appropriate amount (shiitake, enoki, crab mushrooms, etc.)
🌿 Mint leaves, Thai basil or basil appropriate amount
🫚 Ginger slices
🍶 Cooking wine (optional)
🧂 Salt
🧂 Light soy sauce
🧂 Vinegar
🧂 Sesame oil or Sichuan pepper oil (optional)
🧂 Fish sauce
🍯 Honey (or 🍬 white sugar)
🌶️ Chili peppers or bird's eye chilies (optional)
🧂 Pepper (optional)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Pot
🍴 Fork (2)
🔪 Knife
🥣 Large bowl
🥄 Strainer
🧂 Salt and pepper grinder
🧻 Kitchen paper

## Preparation Steps
1. **Preprocess chicken breast**: Use the fork tip to gently prick the surface of the skinless chicken breast several times to allow flavor to penetrate.
   **Key point**: The fork should only break through the surface skin, not too deep.

2. **Prepare the sauce**: In a bowl, combine plenty of minced garlic, passion fruit pulp, light soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil or Sichuan pepper oil, fish sauce, honey or white sugar, salt, pepper, and chili peppers. Stir well.
   **Key point**: The ratio of sour, salty, and sweet can be adjusted to personal taste. The sauce amount should be enough to coat all ingredients thoroughly.

3. **Prepare side ingredients**: Slice the small onion; blanch the mushrooms in boiling water until cooked, drain, and use kitchen paper to absorb excess moisture.
   **Key point**: Mushrooms must be thoroughly drained of water to prevent the finished dish from being too watery.

4. **Cook the chicken breast**: Place the chicken breast in cold water, add ginger slices, cooking wine, and salt. Bring to a boil over high heat, then immediately turn off the heat, cover the pot, and let it sit for 10 minutes to cook through using residual heat.
   **Key point**: Starting from cold water helps the flavor penetrate better; turn off the heat as soon as it boils to ensure the meat remains tender and not dry.

5. **Drain and hand-tear**: Remove the chicken breast and mushrooms, use kitchen paper to thoroughly absorb surface moisture. Use two forks to tear the cooked chicken breast into thin shreds.
   **Key point**: The finer you tear it, the easier it absorbs the flavor. You can also use chopsticks for assistance.

6. **Mix and combine**: In a large bowl, place the shredded chicken, mushrooms, herbs, and raw onion slices. Pour in a sufficient amount of the sauce and toss thoroughly to combine.
   **Key point**: Add the raw onion last to maintain its crunchy texture; ensure all ingredients are evenly coated with the sauce.

7. Plate and serve. It is tangy and appetizing, with a rich tropical flavor.

## Health Suggestion
Chicken breast is low in fat and high in protein. Paired with passion fruit and herbs, this is an excellent refreshing and weight-loss-friendly option for summer. Ensure mushrooms are thoroughly drained of water to avoid excess liquid affecting the texture of the finished dish.
