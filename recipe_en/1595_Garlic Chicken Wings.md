# Garlic Chicken Wings
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Half a chicken (about 1.7 jin, cut into approximately 30 pieces)
🧄 Garlic, to taste
🫚 Ginger, to taste
🧅 Scallions, about 2 yuan worth
🧂 Light soy sauce, to taste
🦪 Oyster sauce, to taste
🧂 Salt, to taste
🍬 Sugar, to taste
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment
🍳 Wok/frying pan
🔪 Cutting board, knife
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Metal spoon
📄 Kitchen paper

## Cooking Steps
1. Clean the chicken pieces thoroughly with water, drain well, then use kitchen paper to pat dry any remaining surface moisture.  
   **Key point**: The surface must be completely dry, otherwise oil will splatter easily when frying, and it will be difficult to achieve a golden, crispy crust.
2. Add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and sugar to the chicken pieces. Mix well and marinate until fully flavored.
3. Heat the wok, add oil, then place the marinated chicken pieces flat in the pan. Fry over medium-low heat.  
   **Note**: Do not flip frequently during frying. Wait until the bottom is set and chicken oil is rendered, then flip to fry the other side until both sides are golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.
4. Leave a small amount of oil in the pan, add ginger slices and scallion segments, and stir-fry until fragrant.
5. Return the fried chicken pieces to the pan and stir-fry quickly with the ginger and scallions until well combined.
6. Drizzle a little light soy sauce along the edge of the pan to enhance flavor, stir-fry evenly, then remove from the pan and serve.

## Health Tips
Garlic chicken wings are high in protein and low in fat, making them suitable for muscle building and weight loss. The frying process tends to absorb oil, so it is recommended to blot off excess oil with kitchen paper before eating. Reduce the amount of sugar and oyster sauce in the marinade to lower hidden sugar intake.
