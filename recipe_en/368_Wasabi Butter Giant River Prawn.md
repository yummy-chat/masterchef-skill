# Wasabi Butter Giant River Prawn
Cuisine: 🍽️ Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: 🌿 All-Season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🦐 Giant river prawn  
🌿 Cilantro  
🧄 Garlic  
🧈 Butter  
🌶️ Wasabi  
🍬 White sugar  
🫕 Light soy sauce  
🫕 Oyster sauce  
🧂 Salt  
🍶 Cooking wine/rum/beer  

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Wok/frying pan  
✂️ Scissors  
🥣 Small bowl  

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut off the sharp head and tail spikes, antennae, and legs of the giant river prawns. Cut along the back to butterfly them, remove the digestive tract, rinse clean, and drain.
   **Key point**: Butterfly to a moderate depth to allow flavor absorption while keeping the prawn shape intact.

2. Prepare the wasabi sauce in advance: Place light soy sauce, oyster sauce, wasabi, salt, and white sugar in a small bowl, stir well, and set aside.

3. Wash the cilantro and cut into sections. Mince the garlic into paste, then set aside.

4. Pour enough oil into the wok and heat over medium heat until it reaches 60% hot. Add the giant river prawns directly and fry until the shells are golden and crispy, then remove.
   **Note**: Avoid flipping the prawns frequently while frying; let one side set before flipping to ensure a crispy, fragrant shell.

5. Using the remaining oil in the wok, sauté the minced garlic over high heat until fragrant and before it changes color. Add the fried prawns and butter, then quickly stir-fry to coat the prawns evenly with butter.
   **Key point**: The minced garlic must be added after the prawns are removed from the wok to prevent it from turning black and bitter from prolonged cooking.

6. Pour in the prepared wasabi sauce, keep the heat high and bring to a boil while stir-frying. As the sauce reduces, drizzle in the alcohol.
   **Tip**: Adjust the amount of wasabi according to personal taste; if it is too pungent, extend the heating time to reduce the spiciness.

7. When the sauce has slightly thickened, add the cilantro sections and quickly stir-fry a few times, then remove from the wok and plate.

## Health Advice
Giant river prawns are rich in high-quality protein and zinc, suitable for adults needing nutritional supplementation. However, the addition of butter and sugar increases the calorie content, so it is recommended to pair with vegetables for a balanced meal.
