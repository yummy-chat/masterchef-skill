# Hollow Sesame Pancake
Cuisine: Shaanxi Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 All-purpose flour 500g
🍚 Dry yeast 4g
🍬 Granulated sugar 3g
🧂 Salt 2g
💧 Warm water 260g (approximately 30℃, add in batches)
🧈 Oil paste moderate amount (mixture of flour and oil, for filling)
🫘 White sesame seeds moderate amount (for surface decoration)
💧 Water a small amount (for adhering sesame seeds)
🫒 Vegetable oil moderate amount (for brushing the dough surface)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Large glass bowl (for kneading dough)
🔪 Cutting board (for kneading, dividing, filling, and rolling)
♨️ Commercial oven (with top and bottom heat, including a metal plate)
🍳 Baking sheet
🖌️ Pastry brush

## Steps
1. **Knead the dough**: Mix all-purpose flour, dry yeast, granulated sugar, and salt evenly. Gradually add warm water in batches, stirring until flocculent, then knead into a smooth dough.
**Note**: The water temperature should not be too high to avoid killing the yeast.

2. **Rest the dough**: Cover the dough and let it rest for about 10 minutes.
**Note**: This dough does not need to fully ferment; resting for 10 minutes is sufficient to maintain the pancake’s soft texture.

3. **Divide into portions**: Roll the rested dough into a long strip, divide into small pieces of about 30g each, and roll each into a smooth ball.

4. **Fill with oil paste**: Take one dough piece and use your thumb to create a well in the center. Spread a layer of oil paste at the bottom of the well, ensuring the edges remain oil-free. Use a gathering method, turning the dough while bringing the edges together, then pinch the opening tightly. Tear off any excess dough, and place the sealed side down.
**Key point**: The oil paste must not touch the edges, otherwise the seal may not close tightly; the closure must be secure to prevent air from escaping during baking.

5. **Adhere sesame seeds**: Dip the smooth side of the filled dough piece in a little water, then coat it thoroughly with white sesame seeds. Place the sesame-side up on a plate and gently press flat.

6. **Roll out**: Flip the dough piece and gently roll it out with a rolling pin; flip again and gently roll into a thin round pancake.
**Note**: Handle gently to avoid breaking and letting air escape; rolling on both sides ensures even thickness.

7. **Brush with oil**: Brush a thin layer of vegetable oil on the surface of the rolled-out pancake.

8. **Set by grilling**: Place the pancake on the preheated metal plate of the oven, grilling each side for about 10 seconds until the surface is set and colored.
**Note**: This step uses the commercial oven’s metal plate; for home ovens, replace with a non-stick pan over low heat until lightly browned.

9. **Bake**: Transfer the set pancake to a baking sheet and bake for about 3 minutes. Remove and flip, then bake for another 2 minutes, until the pancake is fully puffed and golden brown.
**Key point**: Monitor the heat throughout; flip quickly; remove as soon as puffed to avoid burning.

10. **Finish**: Remove the pancakes from the oven—each puffed up like a small ball, with a crispy shell and soft hollow interior. They should not collapse after cooling, making them ideal for filling with ingredients.

## Health Advice
This hollow sesame pancake is primarily made from flour, high in carbohydrates. It is recommended to pair it with protein and vegetables (e.g., braised beef, lettuce) for a balanced diet. Suitable for those needing quick energy replenishment, but diabetic patients should control their consumption.
