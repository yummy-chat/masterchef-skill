# Grill Shop Cucumber Salad
Cuisine: Common home cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 Cucumber 500g (preferably dry cucumber, or long dark green water cucumber)
🥜 Peanuts 50g
🌿 Cilantro about 30g (2 sprigs, cut into sections)
🧄 Garlic 20g (4 cloves, pressed into garlic paste)
🌱 Cilantro roots 2 (washed and thoroughly dried)
🧅 Scallion about 10g (use only the green part, cut into thick strips)
🧅 Onion about 10g (shredded)
🫚 Ginger about 5g (2 slices)
🌶️ Chili powder about 15g (1.5 level tablespoons)
🌶️ Dried chili segments about 4g (a small amount)
⚪️ White sesame seeds about 3g (1/3 tablespoon)
🫒 Cooking oil 60g
🌸 Sichuan peppercorns about 1.5g (a pinch)
🧂 MSG about 3g (1 teaspoon)
🍬 White sugar about 10g (1 level tablespoon)
🥄 Premium soy sauce (or light soy sauce) about 30g (3 tablespoons)
🍶 White vinegar (or aged vinegar/aromatic vinegar) about 30g (3 tablespoons)
🫗 Sesame oil about 5g (half a tablespoon)
🧂 Salt about 1.5g (half a level teaspoon)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Small pot (snow pot or milk pot)
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Large bowl
♨️ Air fryer
🔪 Knife and cutting board

## Preparation Steps
1. Place the chili powder, dried chili segments, and white sesame seeds into a heatproof bowl. Mix evenly.
2. Drizzle a little cooking oil over the peanuts and shake to coat evenly. Thoroughly dry the cilantro roots. Cut the scallion leaves into thick strips. Shred the onion. Slice the ginger.
3. Cut off the ends of the cucumber. Use the flat side of a knife to smash the cucumber, then cut diagonally into diamond-shaped pieces. Cut the cilantro into sections. Press the garlic into a paste and set aside.
4. Pour cooking oil into a small pot. Add the cilantro roots, scallion strips, onion shreds, and ginger slices. Heat over high heat until bubbles appear on the oil surface, then immediately reduce to low heat. Fry slowly until all aromatics turn golden brown. Remove and discard the solids.
   **Note**: The cilantro roots must be thoroughly dried to prevent splattering when added to the oil.
5. Keep the heat on low. Place the Sichuan peppercorns into a slotted spoon, submerge in the hot oil, and fry until the color darkens. Remove and discard the peppercorns.
   **Key Point**: Using a slotted spoon to fry the Sichuan peppercorns separately prevents them from mixing into the oil and affecting the texture.
6. Wait for the oil temperature to drop slightly, until the smoke reduces but a little is still visible. Pour the hot oil into the chili powder mixture, stirring quickly and evenly to create chili oil. Let it cool completely.
   **Key Point**: If the oil temperature is too high, the chili powder will burn. If too low, the aroma will not be released. Pouring at this stage results in crispy, non-burnt chili oil.
7. Once the chili oil has fully cooled, add MSG, white sugar, premium soy sauce, white vinegar, sesame oil, and salt. Mix well to create the sauce.
8. Place the peanuts into the air fryer and cook at 180°C for 6 minutes. Remove and let them cool completely.
   **Note**: The peanuts must be fully cooled to achieve a crispy texture.
9. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber pieces, cilantro sections, garlic paste, and cooled peanuts. Pour in the prepared sauce. Wearing gloves, gently mix everything together. Plate and serve.

## Health Advice
Cucumber has high water content and is very low in calories. Paired with peanuts, which add quality fat intake, this dish is suitable as an appetizer for those on a weight loss or blood sugar control diet. Note that the sauce is relatively high in sugar and oil; it is recommended to use less sauce when eating.
