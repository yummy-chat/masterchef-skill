## Cooking Steps
1.  Wash the beef short ribs and cut into large chunks, as the beef will shrink significantly during braising.
2.  Place the beef in a pot with cold water, bring to a boil over high heat, skim off the foam, blanch thoroughly, then remove.
3.  Rinse the beef with hot water to remove any remaining foam. Strain the original beef broth through a fine-mesh strainer to remove impurities, and reserve the clear broth.
4.  Cut a large piece of ginger into chunks and prepare the spices.
5.  Stir-fry the dried cabbage and oyster mushrooms in a pan, then spread them evenly in the bottom of the hot pot bowl. Prepare fresh cabbage leaves separately.
6.  Heat the wok and add rapeseed oil. First, rub the wok surface with the large ginger chunks to prevent the beef from sticking, fry until fragrant, then remove the ginger or leave it in the wok.
7.  Add the drained beef and stir-fry over high heat until the moisture evaporates, the fat from the beef tendons renders and melts, and the surface of the meat is slightly seared.
8.  Add 1 tablespoon of broad bean paste, the prepared dry spices, 1 tablespoon of yellow wine, and 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce. Reduce the heat to medium-low and stir-fry until the beef is evenly coated with sauce.
9.  Transfer the stir-fried beef to a pressure cooker and add the reserved beef broth.
10. Add a small amount of rock sugar to enhance flavor and some dark soy sauce for color. Cover the pressure cooker, bring it up to pressure, then reduce the heat to low and cook for 15 minutes.
11. Allow the pressure cooker to cool naturally or release the pressure. Open the lid, taste the broth, and adjust the seasoning with salt, MSG, and white pepper powder to your preference.
12. In the hot pot bowl with the base vegetables, place the raw cabbage leaves. Pour the braised beef and its broth over the top.
13. Neatly arrange the fried tofu puffs on the surface. Garnish with shredded red chili peppers, garlic leaves, and cilantro.
14. Place a lit alcohol burner under the hot pot bowl and eat while it simmers. After finishing the meat, you can use the broth to cook additional vegetables.

## Health Note
Beef is high in protein and low in fat, making it suitable for winter nourishment. However, beef short ribs have a relatively high fat content, so it is recommended to pair them with cabbage and oyster mushrooms to aid digestion. Using a pressure cooker shortens the braising time, which reduces the amount of purines leached into the broth.
