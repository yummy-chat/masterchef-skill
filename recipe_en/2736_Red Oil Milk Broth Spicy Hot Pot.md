# Red Oil Milk Broth Spicy Hot Pot  
Cuisine: Sichuan  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍄 Shiitake mushrooms 3–4 pieces (some scored in a cross pattern, trimmings reserved)  
🍅 Tomato 1 piece (peeled and chopped)  
🥬 Lettuce to taste (washed and torn into large pieces)  
🫘 Dried tofu skin to taste (cut into strips or slices)  
🍜 Vermicelli 1 small bundle (pre-soaked until soft)  
🌶️ Dried chili peppers to taste  
🟢 Green Sichuan peppercorns to taste  
🧱 Hot pot base about half a packet (used in two portions)  
🥛 Milk 1 carton  
🍬 White sugar a small pinch  
🔴 Goji berries a few (for garnish)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Frying pan  
🥄 Strainer or slotted spoon  
🔪 Knife and cutting board  
🥢 Chopsticks and spoon  

## Steps  
1. Heat an ample amount of cooking oil in a pan. Once hot, reduce the heat to low, add the dried chili peppers and green Sichuan peppercorns, and stir-fry until fragrant.  
**Note**: Stir-fry over low heat to prevent scorching.  
2. Add the mushroom trimmings and stir-fry until fragrant. Then add half of the hot pot base and cook over low heat until fully melted and the oil turns bright red.  
3. Add the peeled and chopped tomato, and stir-fry until it releases juice, softens, and turns into a paste.  
4. Pour in enough boiling water, bring to a boil over high heat, and cook for 3 minutes to develop a rich broth.  
5. Add a small pinch of white sugar to enhance flavor, then pour in the milk. Once the broth turns milky white, reduce the heat to low.  
6. Use a strainer or slotted spoon to remove all solid residues, ensuring a clear and smooth broth.  
**Key point**: Thoroughly removing the solids is essential for a drinkable broth.  
7. Bring the milk broth back to a boil. First, add the remaining hot pot base and cook for 2–3 minutes until melted. Then add the cross-scored shiitake mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes. Finally, add the tofu skin, vermicelli, and lettuce, and cook for about 30 seconds until just tender.  
**Note**: Add ingredients in order of cooking time; vermicelli can be added slightly before or at the same time as the vegetables.  
8. Ladle the cooked ingredients and broth into a bowl, and garnish with a few goji berries.  

## Health Advice  
This Red Oil Milk Broth Spicy Hot Pot uses milk to balance the spiciness, making it gentle on the stomach while retaining the bold Sichuan peppery flavors. It is ideal for those craving a spicy treat but concerned about getting overheated. Avoid overcooking the vermicelli to prevent it from becoming too soft and losing its texture.
