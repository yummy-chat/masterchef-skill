# Five-Spice Secret Steamed Pork with Rice Flour
Cuisine: Hubei Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork belly about 1 jin (slightly lean, slightly frozen then cut into thick slices)
🥔 Potatoes appropriate amount (peeled and cut into chunks for layering)
🧅 Scallions appropriate amount (some sliced for soaking in water, some chopped for garnish)
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced for soaking in water)
🍚 Homemade five-spice rice flour appropriate amount (enough to coat the meat slices, made by stir-frying and grinding glutinous rice, millet, fennel, star anise, cinnamon, and green Sichuan pepper into coarse granules)
🍶 Cooking wine one tablespoon
🥢 Light soy sauce two tablespoons
🦪 Oyster sauce one tablespoon
🍬 Sugar a small pinch

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board
🥣 Bowl
🍳 Wok (oil-free and water-free)
🥤 Blender (with grinding cup)
♨️ Steamer
🍽️ Steaming tray

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut scallions into segments, slice ginger, and brew in boiling water to make scallion-ginger water. Let cool and set aside.
2. Slightly freeze the pork belly, then cut into uniform thick slices.
3. To the meat slices, sequentially add two tablespoons of scallion-ginger water, one tablespoon of cooking wine, two tablespoons of light soy sauce, one tablespoon of oyster sauce, and a small pinch of sugar. Mix thoroughly by hand, then let marinate to absorb flavor.
4. Peel and cut potatoes into chunks, and spread evenly on the bottom of the steaming tray.
5. Make five-spice rice flour: In an oil-free and water-free wok, add glutinous rice, millet, fennel, star anise, cinnamon, and green Sichuan pepper. Stir-fry over low heat for about 2–4 minutes, until the glutinous rice turns slightly yellow and the aroma is rich. Ensure to keep the heat low and stir constantly to prevent burning.
6. Pour the stir-fried mixture into the blender's grinding cup and grind into coarse granular powder.
7. Pour the prepared five-spice rice flour into the marinated meat slices and mix by hand, ensuring each slice of meat is evenly coated with rice flour.
8. Lay the coated meat slices flat on top of the potatoes, trying not to stack too thickly.
9. Place the steaming tray into the steamer, cover with the lid, and steam over medium heat for 1 hour. Ensure there is enough water in the steamer during this time; if need to replenish, add boiling water.
10. After steaming, remove and sprinkle with fresh chopped scallions. Serve hot immediately.

## Health Suggestion
Steamed pork with rice flour is soft, sticky, and flavorful. Pork belly is rich in high-quality protein and fat, paired with potatoes as a base to increase dietary fiber. It is recommended to pair with stir-fried vegetables to balance nutrition. People with high blood lipids should control their portion size.
