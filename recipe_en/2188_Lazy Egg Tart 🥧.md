# Lazy Egg Tart 🥧  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

🛒 Ingredients  
🥚 Egg tart liquid 1 box  
🥟 Egg tart shells 1 box  

🧂 Seasonings  
🧀 Shredded cheese (optional, about 10g)  

🍳 Kitchenware  
🔥 Oven (preferably with top and bottom heat)  
🧤 Heat-resistant gloves  
🥘 Baking tray  

## Steps  
1. Preheat the oven to 200°C for 10 minutes.  
2. Arrange the egg tart shells evenly on the baking tray. Pour about 10 ml of egg tart liquid into each shell, filling to about 4/5 of the shell's height.  
3. Place the baking tray in the oven and bake at 190°C with top and bottom heat for 10–20 minutes. To speed up the formation of caramelized brown spots on the surface, increase the top heat to 200–210°C.  
4. Once the egg tart liquid surface shows caramelized brown spots, the shells are fully puffed and oil seeps out, remove from the oven.  

## Notes  
- Do not fill egg tart liquid beyond 4/5 of the shell's height to prevent overflow during baking.  
- Adjust baking time based on the development of caramelized brown spots.  
- Sprinkle about 10g of shredded cheese on the surface of the egg tart liquid to make cheese egg tarts.  
- Always wear heat-resistant gloves when removing the baking tray to prevent burns.  

## Health Tips  
Lazy egg tarts rely mainly on semi-finished products, but be mindful of portion control as the tart shells contain a lot of oil and sugar. Suitable for occasional indulgence or entertaining guests, not recommended as a daily breakfast staple.
