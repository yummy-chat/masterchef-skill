# American-Style Spicy Squid Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🦑 1 whole squid (cleaned, scored on the inside, and cut into pieces)
🥕 Carrot, to taste (sliced)
🌶️ Red chili pepper, to taste (sliced)
🫑 Green chili pepper, to taste (sliced)
🧅 Half a small white onion (julienned)
🧅 Scallion, to taste (cut into sections)
🍚 White rice, to taste (as the base for the rice bowl)
🫒 Cooking oil, to taste (for stir-frying)
🧂 1 tbsp Korean chili paste (gochujang)
🍬 White sugar, to taste
🧂 Soy sauce, to taste
🌶️ Chili powder, to taste
🧄 Minced garlic, optional
🫚 Minced ginger, optional
🌿 Sesame seeds, a small amount (for garnish on top)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔪 Knife
🪓 Cutting board
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Spoon
🥣 Bowl (for mixing the sauce)
🍲 Stone pot or deep bowl (for serving rice and topping)

## Cooking Steps
### Preparation Stage
1. Lay the cleaned squid flat, score the inside with a crosshatch pattern, then cut into bite-sized pieces.
   **Note**: Scoring the inside helps the squid curl when heated and allows it to absorb flavor more easily.
2. Slice the carrot, red chili pepper, and green chili pepper into pieces, and the scallion and white onion into sections or julienned strips.
   **Note**: Vegetables can be adjusted according to preference, such as adding broccoli.
3. In a bowl, mix together the Korean chili paste, white sugar, soy sauce, and chili powder until well combined. Minced garlic and ginger can be added to taste.
   **Note**: Prepare the sauce in advance to ensure even flavor distribution during stir-frying.

### Cooking Stage
1. Heat the wok over high heat, add oil, and first stir-fry the julienned onion and scallion sections until fragrant.
2. Then add the sliced carrot, chili pepper slices, and the scored squid pieces. Stir-fry over medium-high heat until the squid changes color and curls, and the vegetables are just cooked through.
   **Doneness check**: The squid should be curled and white, and the vegetables should be brightly colored.
3. Finally, pour in the prepared sauce and stir-fry quickly until everything is evenly coated with the sauce.
   **Note**: Add the sauce last and stir-fry quickly to avoid releasing too much liquid.
4. Place hot rice into a stone pot or deep bowl, spoon the stir-fried spicy squid and its juices over the rice, and sprinkle with a little sesame seeds as garnish. Serve immediately.

## Health Tips
This spicy squid rice bowl is rich in high-quality protein and dietary fiber. Squid is low in fat and high in protein, but the chili paste, sugar, and salt content should be moderate. It is suitable for those who are fitness-conscious and watching their calorie intake as an occasional treat. Be mindful of the amounts of chili powder and sauce used to avoid irritating the stomach. Pairing it with more vegetables makes for a more balanced meal.
