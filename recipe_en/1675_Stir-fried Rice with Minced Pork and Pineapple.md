# Stir-fried Rice with Minced Pork and Pineapple
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍖 Minced pork, to taste  
🍚 Cooked rice, about 1 large bowl  
🥚 Eggs, to taste (beaten in advance, can be seasoned with a pinch of salt)  
🍍 Pineapple, about half (cut into small cubes)  
🧈 Butter, 1 small piece  
🧂 Salt, a pinch  
🟡 Curry powder, 1 teaspoon  
🥄 Light soy sauce, a small amount  
🌿 Scallions, chopped, a small amount  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Wok (non-stick preferred)  
🥄 Wooden spatula  

## Instructions  
1. Mix the beaten eggs with the cooked rice until well combined, and add a pinch of salt for a base flavor.  
2. Heat the wok thoroughly, then pour in cooking oil. Add the minced pork and stir-fry until it separates and becomes fragrant.  
3. Add the rice mixed with egg to the wok. Reduce the heat to medium-low and stir-fry continuously until the rice is dry, the grains are distinct, and it becomes fluffy.  
   **Key point**: Stir-fry until each grain is dry and the rice bounces lightly in the wok, achieving a texture that is fluffy but not sticky.  
4. Add the curry powder and light soy sauce, and stir-fry quickly and evenly so that every grain of rice is coated with color and aroma.  
5. Add a small piece of butter. Once fully melted, add the pineapple cubes and continue stir-frying for about 30 seconds.  
6. Before removing from heat, sprinkle in the chopped scallions, stir well, and serve immediately.  
   **Note**: The butter enhances the overall aroma. The amounts of curry powder and light soy sauce can be adjusted to personal taste.  

## Health Tips
