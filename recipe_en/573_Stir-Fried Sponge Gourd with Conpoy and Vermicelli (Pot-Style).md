# Stir-Fried Sponge Gourd with Conpoy and Vermicelli (Pot-Style)
Cuisine: Cantonese
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥒 1 sponge gourd (peeled, leaving some green skin, cut evenly into rounds or strips)
🍜 Vermicelli,适量 (soaked in water until soft in advance)
🦪 About 1 handful of conpoy (dried scallops) (soaked in hot water until soft, then torn into shreds)
🧄 A large amount of garlic (made into golden and raw garlic, i.e., a mixture of cooked garlic paste and raw garlic paste)
🧂 Salt, to taste
🌾 A small amount of cornstarch
🫒 A small amount of cooking oil

## Cookware & Equipment
♨️ Steamer
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for mixing ingredients)
🍽️ Steaming plate
🥢 Food tongs
🔪 Knife and cutting board

## Instructions
1.  Soak conpoy in hot water until soft, drain, and tear into fine shreds by hand; soak vermicelli in cold or warm water until soft, drain, and set aside.
2.  Peel the sponge gourd, leaving a small amount of green skin for added texture, and cut evenly into rounds or strips according to shape.
3.  Place the cut sponge gourd and conpoy shreds in a stainless steel bowl. Add salt, a large amount of golden and raw garlic, a little cornstarch, and a small amount of cooking oil.
    **Key point**: Gently mix evenly by hand so that the ingredients are coated with a thin batter. Proceed to the next step immediately after mixing to prevent the sponge gourd from releasing water.
4.  Spread the soaked vermicelli on the bottom of a steaming plate, then evenly spread the mixed sponge gourd and conpoy on top of the vermicelli.
5.  Bring water to a boil in a steamer, place the steaming plate inside, cover with a lid, and steam over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes.
    **Key point**: Do not steam for too long to maintain the crispness of the sponge gourd and the soft elasticity of the vermicelli.
6.  Remove and serve. The dish is rich in garlic aroma, with sweet sponge gourd and vermicelli that have absorbed the savory juices.

## Health Tips
Sponge gourd is rich in water and dietary fiber, while conpoy provides high-quality protein and natural umami flavor, making it suitable for cooling and relieving heat in summer. The short steaming time helps retain nutrients, and the vermicelli, after absorbing the broth, is easier to digest. Note that the amount of golden and raw garlic should be moderate to avoid excessive spiciness that may irritate the stomach.
