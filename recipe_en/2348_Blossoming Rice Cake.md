# Blossoming Rice Cake
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍚 400g rice
🥛 360ml pure milk
🌾 80g all-purpose flour
🍄 6g yeast
🍬 60g sugar
🍇 Raisins (适量)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🫗 Large glass bowl
🔪 Blender
🥄 Whisk
🧁 Paper cup molds
♨️ Steamer
🕸️ Strainer

## Instructions
1. Rinse the rice thoroughly and prepare the milk, flour, yeast, sugar, and raisins.
2. Pour the rinsed rice and pure milk into the blender, blend into a smooth and grain-free rice paste, then transfer to a large glass bowl.
3. Add the all-purpose flour, yeast, and sugar to the rice paste, and stir until uniform and smooth. If there are still lumps, strain it through a strainer to ensure the paste is fine.
4. Cover the rice paste and let it ferment in a warm place until it doubles in volume. After fermentation, stir thoroughly with a whisk to release the trapped gas.
5. Divide the rice paste into paper cup molds, filling them to 90% full, and sprinkle raisins on top for decoration.
6. Bring water to a boil in the steamer, place the molds in once steam appears, and steam over high heat for 20 minutes. Turn off the heat, let it rest for 3 minutes before opening the lid to prevent the cake from shrinking. Remove and let cool before serving. The texture is soft and chewy.

## Health Tips
This blossom rice cake is made from fermented rice and milk, giving it a soft texture that is easy to digest, making it suitable for breakfast or afternoon tea. Diabetics should reduce the amount of sugar used.
