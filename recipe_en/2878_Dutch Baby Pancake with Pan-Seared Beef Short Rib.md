# Dutch Baby Pancake with Pan-Seared Beef Short Rib
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧈 Butter 10g
🥚 Eggs 2
🥛 Milk 120g
🌾 Cake flour 50g
🌿 Vanilla extract 2 drops (optional)
🥩 Beef short rib 1 piece
🍌 Banana 1
🫐 Raspberries as needed
🍇 Blueberries as needed
🥛 Yogurt as needed
🥬 Arugula as needed
🍅 Cherry tomatoes 4-5
🥗 Salad dressing as needed
🧂 Black pepper as needed
🍵 Tea bag 1 (green tea)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🔥 Cast iron skillet (small size)
🔥 Oven
🥣 Measuring cup/bowl
🫗 Flour sieve
⚙️ Electric grinder
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🥢 Tongs
🔪 Knife
🪓 Cutting board
🧤 Oven mitts
🔲 Wire rack

## Steps
1. Preheat the oven to 200°C. Place the cast iron skillet and add the butter.
   **Key Point**: The butter needs to be heated together with the skillet in the next step until melted and bubbling.
2. Make the batter: Mix the eggs, milk, cake flour, and vanilla extract until smooth. Strain through a flour sieve to remove lumps, ensuring a fine and silky batter.
   **Key Point**: Straining is essential for a tender pancake texture and cannot be skipped.
3. Place the cast iron skillet with the butter into the preheated oven and bake for about 5 minutes, until the butter is fully melted and slightly bubbling.
   **Key Point**: The skillet and butter must reach a sufficiently high temperature; this is the core condition for the batter to puff up quickly after being poured in.
4. Remove the hot skillet, quickly pour in the strained batter, and immediately return it to the oven. Bake for about 20 minutes until the batter has fully puffed up, the edges are tall and risen, and the surface is golden brown.
   **Key Point**: Do not open the oven door during baking, or the pancake will collapse.
5. While the pancake bakes, prepare the steak: Pat the beef short rib dry with paper towels. Heat a non-stick frying pan until very hot, then add the steak. Sprinkle with black pepper and sear both sides to your preferred doneness, then remove.
6. Transfer the seared steak to a wire rack and let it rest briefly to allow the juices to redistribute. Then place it in the oven to bake for about 5 more minutes.
7. Assemble the Dutch baby pancake: Place yogurt into the indentation in the center of the baked pancake. Top with sliced banana, and garnish with raspberries and blueberries.
8. Prepare the side salad: Arrange arugula on a plate, add the sliced steak pieces, decorate with cherry tomatoes, drizzle with salad dressing, and grind a little black pepper over the top.
9. Brew a cup of green tea to serve alongside.

## Health Tips
This dish is high in protein and low in carbohydrates, suitable for fitness and muscle-building individuals. The flour and fruit sauces in the pancake can easily lead to excess calories, so portion control is recommended. Beef short rib is rich in iron; be mindful of the cooking temperature to avoid overcooking, which can make the meat tough and dry.
