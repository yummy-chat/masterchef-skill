# Honey Crispy Butter Chicken  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍗 Chicken (boneless chicken thighs recommended) – appropriate amount  
🧅 Purple onion – appropriate amount (sliced into rings)  
🧄 Garlic – appropriate amount (minced)  
🍅 Cherry tomatoes – appropriate amount (for garnish)  
🌾 Flour – appropriate amount (for coating)  
🧂 Oyster sauce – appropriate amount  
🧂 Light soy sauce – appropriate amount  
🧂 Garlic powder – appropriate amount  
🍯 Honey – appropriate amount  
🌶️ Sichuan pepper powder – appropriate amount  
🧈 Butter – appropriate amount  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl  
🍳 Frying pan  
🔪 Cutting board / knife  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Cut the chicken into pieces and place them in a stainless steel bowl. Add onion rings, oyster sauce, light soy sauce, garlic powder, honey, and Sichuan pepper powder.  
   **Key point**: Wear gloves and mix thoroughly, repeatedly kneading the chicken to allow the seasonings to penetrate. Use the onion to remove any gamey smell. Marinate for a while until flavorful.  
2. Remove the marinated chicken and evenly coat with a thin layer of flour. Shake off any excess flour.  
3. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat, add the chicken, and fry slowly.  
   **Key point**: Fry until both sides are golden brown, forming a crispy outer shell, and the inside is fully cooked.  
4. In another pan, add a small piece of butter and melt over low heat. Add minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Then add honey and light soy sauce, stirring and cooking until the sauce bubbles and thickens.  
5. Pour the fried chicken into the sauce pan and quickly stir-fry, ensuring each piece is evenly coated with the buttery garlic honey sauce.  
6. Remove from heat and plate. Garnish with cherry tomatoes or other fresh vegetables. Serve immediately while hot.  

## Health Tips  
This dish combines honey and butter with chicken, offering a crispy, sweet, and rich flavor, but it is relatively high in calories. It is recommended to pair it with light vegetables (such as cucumber salad) to balance nutrition. It is suitable for times when physical energy is expended or when craving a treat. For those managing sugar intake, the amount of honey can be reduced appropriately.
