# Mustard Giant River Prawns
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Giant river prawns 250g
🧄 Garlic 1-2 cloves
🌶️ Small chili peppers 1-2 (omit if you don't eat spicy food, or substitute with red bell pepper)
🧈 Butter 20g
🫒 Cooking oil 80ml
💚 Green mustard 20g (for the sauce)
🍚 Cornstarch 10g (for the sauce)
🫗 Light soy sauce 30g (for the sauce)
🌫️ White pepper powder 5g (for the sauce)
🍬 White sugar 3g (for the sauce)
🍯 Oyster sauce 15g (for the sauce)
🧂 Salt 3g (for the sauce)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Knife
🍳 Wok
🥣 Small bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. Cut the giant river prawns open along the back, remove the digestive tract and sand sacs, rinse clean with water, drain thoroughly, and evenly coat with a layer of cornstarch.
2. Mince the garlic. In a small bowl, combine light soy sauce, oyster sauce, white sugar, white pepper powder, and salt. Squeeze in green mustard to taste, add water to dilute, then add cornstarch and stir until dissolved. Mix well to form the sauce.
3. Heat the wok over high heat, pour in cooking oil (enough to cover the bottom of the pan), add the drained giant river prawns, and gently pan-fry over low heat.
4. Once the prawn oil is released, add the minced garlic and small chili peppers, stir-frying until fragrant.
5. When the garlic aroma is strong, add the butter. Once the butter melts, quickly stir to combine evenly, then immediately pour in the prepared sauce.
6. Cover the wok with a lid, braise for 2 minutes until the sauce thickens. Reduce the sauce, then remove from heat and plate.

## Key Points
- After adding the butter to the pan, it must be melted and stirred quickly; do not heat for too long, or it will affect the flavor.
- The sauce must be prepared and mixed in advance; adding cornstarch helps thicken the sauce.

## Health Tips
Giant river prawns are rich in high-quality protein. Pairing them with green mustard can stimulate the appetite, making this dish suitable for healthy individuals who prefer bold flavors. Note that the total calorie content of butter and cooking oil is relatively high, so it is advisable to control the amount of oil used. Cutting the prawns open along the back makes them easier to absorb flavors, but the cooking time should not be too long to prevent the meat from becoming tough.
