# Quick Tofu Pudding
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥛 Ready-made Soy Milk 1 liter (bottled is fine)
🧊 Glucono Delta Lactone 3 grams
💧 Water 20 grams (for dissolving the glucono delta lactone)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🥣 Porcelain Bowl
♨️ Microwave Oven
🍚 Rice Cooker (with inner pot)
🥄 Spoon

## Steps
1. Pour 1 liter of ready-made soy milk into a porcelain bowl.
   **Note**: Must use a porcelain bowl; do not use an iron or glass bowl.
2. Place the porcelain bowl with soy milk in the microwave. Heat on high power for 2 minutes to bring the soy milk to a boiling hot state.
3. While heating, take out the rice cooker inner pot. Add 20 grams of water and 3 grams of glucono delta lactone. Stir with a spoon until the glucono delta lactone is completely dissolved.
4. Remove the soy milk from the microwave. Wait until a thin skin forms on the surface, then quickly pour the soy milk from a height into the rice cooker inner pot containing the glucono delta lactone water.
   **Key point**: Must pour from a height; **absolutely do not stir**.
5. Immediately cover the rice cooker lid, press the "keep warm" button, and let it sit for 30–35 minutes to allow the tofu pudding to naturally solidify and set.
6. Remove the solidified tofu pudding and serve. You may add sugar, fermented rice, or honey according to personal taste.

## Health Tips
This dish uses ready-made soy milk and glucono delta lactone, saving time and effort. It is suitable for breakfast to supplement high-quality plant protein. Note that the soy milk must be poured in boiling hot and not stirred to ensure proper solidification.
