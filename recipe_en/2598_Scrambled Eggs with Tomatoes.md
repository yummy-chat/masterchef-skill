# Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients
🍅 Tomato 2 pieces (finely dice one, cut the rest into wedges)
🥚 Eggs 4 pieces (prefer varieties with high yolk ratio and deep color)
💧 Water 4 tablespoons (add to egg mixture for fluffiness)
🌽 Cornstarch slurry 1 tablespoon (mix cornstarch with water, add to egg mixture for a tender texture)
🧅 Scallion appropriate amount (mince for sautéing)
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (mince for sautéing)

## Seasonings
🧂 Salt appropriate amount
🍬 Sugar appropriate amount (enhances flavor, balances acidity)
🫙 Light soy sauce appropriate amount (adds color and umami)
🍶 Rice vinegar a little (optional, add if eggs have a fishy smell to remove it)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪵 Cutting board
🍲 Boiling pot (for blanching tomatoes to remove skin)
🍳 Wok
🥄 Soup spoon
🥢 Chopsticks
🥣 Glass bowl (for beating eggs)
⤵️ Strainer (to drain oil from eggs)

## Instructions
1. Peel tomatoes: Score a cross on the top of each tomato, blanch in boiling water for a moment, remove, and peel off the skin.
2. Cut tomatoes: Finely dice one tomato and cut the rest into wedges, set aside separately. The fine dice is used to make the sauce, while the wedges retain the fruit texture.
3. Prepare egg mixture: Beat the eggs, add 4 tablespoons of water and 1 tablespoon of cornstarch slurry, and whisk thoroughly. If the eggs have a fishy smell, add a few drops of rice vinegar.
4. Scramble eggs: Heat oil in a wok over high heat, pour in the egg mixture. Let it set briefly at the bottom, then push from the edges toward the center with a spatula. When mostly set, break into large pieces and immediately remove from the wok. Use medium-high heat throughout, work quickly, and aim for the eggs to be just set with a golden color for the tenderest texture.
5. Cook tomato sauce: Leave a little oil in the wok, sauté the minced scallion and ginger until fragrant. Add the finely diced tomato and stir-fry over high heat until completely soft and a thick sauce forms.
6. Combine and season: Return the scrambled eggs to the wok, add the tomato wedges, season with salt, sugar, and light soy sauce. Stir-fry over medium heat for 1-2 minutes until the tomato wedges are slightly softened and the sauce thickens, evenly coating all ingredients. Add the tomato wedges later to retain their juicy texture.

## Health Tips
This dish of scrambled eggs with tomatoes is rich in lycopene and high-quality protein, suitable for all ages. For a healthier version, use less oil and control the sugar. Adding water and cornstarch slurry to the eggs makes them fluffier and more tender, but be mindful of seasoning. It is a classic home-style dish enjoyed by people of all ages.
