# Versatile Pork Filling
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork leg meat 2 jin (recommended fat-to-lean ratio 3:7 or 2:8)
🥚 Egg 1
🫚 Scallion appropriate amount (used twice: for making seasoned water and for slicing to drizzle with hot oil)
🫚 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced or smashed for seasoned water)
⭐ Star anise 2-3
🌿 Sichuan peppercorns a small handful
💧 Water about 100 ml (for making seasoned water)
🍶 Huadiao wine appropriate amount
🍶 Light soy sauce appropriate amount
🦪 Oyster sauce appropriate amount
🍶 Dark soy sauce appropriate amount
🌫 White pepper powder appropriate amount
🧂 Salt appropriate amount
🫒 Sesame oil appropriate amount
🫒 Cooking oil appropriate amount (for the final oil drizzle)

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife/Cutting board
🌀 Meat grinder
🍳 Saucepan
🥄 Strainer
🥣 Large bowl
🤲 Hands (for mixing)
🍳 Small pot
🥄 Spoon
🔥 Stove

## Preparation Steps
1. **Process the Pork**: Remove the skin and sinew from the pork leg meat, cut into large chunks, and set aside.
2. **Make Seasoned Water**: Add about 100 ml of water to a saucepan, add star anise and Sichuan peppercorns, simmer on low heat for about 3 minutes, turn off the heat and let steep until cooled. Then add scallion and ginger, squeeze them with your hands to release the juices, strain the seasoned water through a strainer, and let it cool completely.
   **Key Point**: The seasoned water must be completely cool to avoid cooking the meat filling.
3. **Grind the Meat**: Place the meat chunks into a meat grinder and pulse until you get a coarse paste with some texture. Transfer to a large bowl.
4. **Add Water and Mix**: Add the seasoned water to the meat filling in three additions, mixing vigorously in one direction each time until the water is fully absorbed, until the filling is moist and sticky.
5. **Liquid Seasoning**: Add Huadiao wine, light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and dark soy sauce in sequence, continuing to mix in the same direction until well combined.
6. **Dry Seasoning and Kneading**: Add white pepper powder and salt, and mix vigorously in one direction until the filling offers significant resistance and becomes very thick and springy.
   **Key Point**: Kneading the filling until it is springy is the key to a tender and juicy filling.
7. **Add Egg and Sesame Oil**: Crack in one whole egg, mix until incorporated, then drizzle in sesame oil and mix well.
8. **Drizzle Hot Oil for Aroma**: Spread the chopped scallion on top of the meat filling. Heat oil in a small pot until it reaches about 60% hot (around 180°C/350°F), then quickly pour it over the scallion to release the aroma.
9. **Refrigerate Until Use**: Refrigerate the prepared filling. Mix well again before using.

## Health Advice
This pork filling is rich in protein but also high in fat. It is recommended to pair it with vegetables such as cabbage or Chinese chives to cut the richness and add dietary fiber. Suitable for making dumplings, steamed buns, or meatballs. The recommended serving size is 100-150 grams per person.
