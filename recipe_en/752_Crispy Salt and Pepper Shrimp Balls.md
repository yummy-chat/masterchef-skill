# Crispy Salt and Pepper Shrimp Balls
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Cooking Steps
1. **Prepare the shrimp**: Shell the black tiger shrimp, reserving the tail segment. Butterfly the back, remove the vein, and rinse clean.
   **Key Point**: Butterflying removes the vein and allows the shrimp to curl up and become plump when heated.

2. **Marinate**: Add salt, freshly ground black pepper, and a small amount of cooking wine to the shrimp. Mix well by hand. Crack in the egg and mix thoroughly. Add two tablespoons of cornstarch, and mix again until the shrimp are evenly coated. Let sit for 10 minutes to marinate.
   **Key Point**: Season first, then batter to ensure the shrimp meat is tender and the outer shell becomes crispy after frying.

3. **Prepare aromatics**: Slice the green chili pepper, bird's eye chili into rings, and the garlic into slices.

4. **Fry the shrimp balls**: Pour enough oil into the pan and heat over medium-high heat. Add the marinated shrimp one by one, and fry over medium-high heat until the surface is golden and crispy. Once the shrimp are fully cooked, quickly remove with a strainer and drain the oil.
   **Key Point**: Shrimp cook quickly and should not be fried for too long to avoid a tough texture; the frying oil can be strained and reused.

5. **Sauté aromatics**: Leave a small amount of oil in the pan, reduce to low heat, and sauté the garlic slices and green and red chili rings until fragrant.

6. **Combine and season**: Return the fried shrimp balls to the pan. Quickly sprinkle with a generous amount of salt and plenty of black pepper, and stir-fry rapidly until each shrimp ball is evenly coated. Remove from heat immediately.
   **Key Point**: The stir-frying must be quick to ensure even seasoning and that the shrimp balls remain crispy and undamaged.

7. **Plate and serve**: Transfer to a plate. Squeeze a little lemon juice on top for extra flavor if desired, adjusting to personal taste.

## Health Advice
This crispy salt and pepper shrimp ball dish is rich in high-quality protein and a small amount of healthy fats, making it suitable for those looking to build muscle or control weight. However, since it is fried, it is relatively high in calories, so it is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance the meal. Be careful not to fry the shrimp for too long to maintain tenderness. The spiciness of black pepper or Sichuan peppercorn should be consumed in moderation by those with a sensitive stomach.
