# Creative Egg Noodle Soufflé  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All year round  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍜 Dried noodles, about 50g  
🥚 Egg, 1  
🥛 Yogurt, as needed  
🍒 Cherries, 2  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Small food processor  
🥣 Electric hand mixer  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🥄 Silicone spatula  
🍳 Small non-stick frying pan  
🍽️ Plate  

## Instructions  
1. Place the dried noodles in a small food processor and grind into a fine powder.  
   **Note**: Ensure the noodles are ground into a uniform powder.  
2. Separate the egg white and yolk into two clean, oil-free, and water-free bowls.  
   **Note**: The egg white bowl must be completely free of oil and water for successful whipping.  
3. Use an electric hand mixer on high speed to whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form, with upright small peaks when the beater is lifted.  
   **Checkpoint**: The egg whites transform from liquid to a white, fluffy, glossy solid foam.  
4. Add an appropriate amount of yogurt to the egg yolk and mix evenly with a spatula to form a uniform light yellow liquid.  
5. Add about 1/3 of the whipped egg whites to the egg yolk-yogurt mixture, and fold gently from the bottom up using a spatula until combined.  
   **Key point**: Use a folding motion; avoid stirring in circles to prevent deflating the egg whites.  
6. Pour the mixed egg yolk batter back into the remaining whipped egg whites, and continue folding with the spatula until evenly combined, forming a fluffy light yellow batter.  
   **Checkpoint**: The mixture should be uniform in color and fluffy in texture, with no visible lumps of egg white.  
7. Scoop the batter into the center of a preheated small non-stick pan, and cook over medium-low heat.  
   **Checkpoint**: Cook until the bottom is set and golden brown.  
8. Carefully flip the soufflé and continue cooking the other side until golden brown.  
   **Key point**: Flip gently to maintain its shape. Consider covering the pan to use steam to ensure the inside is cooked through.  
9. Remove the cooked soufflé to a plate, drizzle with yogurt on top, and garnish with cherries.  

## Health Tips  
This dish replaces flour with ground noodles, offering a low-carb, high-protein creative approach, suitable for those needing to control carbohydrate intake. However, soufflé is still essentially a high-protein dessert, so consume in moderation. Ensure the bowl for whipping egg whites is completely free of oil and water, or whipping may fail.
