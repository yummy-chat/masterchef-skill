# Universal Meat Filling
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐖 Ground pork 1 jin (fat-to-lean ratio: 30% fat, 70% lean)
🧅 Scallions, as needed (used for making seasoned water and for final mixing)
🪵 Ginger, as needed (used for making seasoned water)
🥚 Egg, 1
💧 Warm water, about 3 liang (corresponding to 1 jin of meat filling, for making scallion-ginger water)
🍶 Hua Tiao wine, 1 spoon
🧴 Light soy sauce, 2 spoons
🧴 Dark soy sauce, half a spoon
🧂 Salt, 1/4 spoon
🌶️ White pepper, two shakes
🫒 Seasoned oil, 1 spoon (oil pre-fried with spices)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Knife
🥣 Bowl
🥄 Spoon (soup spoon size, for measuring seasonings)
🥢 Chopsticks or hands (for mixing the meat filling)

## Preparation Steps
1. Lightly crush the scallion sections and ginger slices with the back of a knife. Place them in a bowl and add warm water to soak. Let it steep to make scallion-ginger seasoned water.
2. Strain the scallion-ginger water to remove the solids. Add the water to the ground meat in three batches. Stir vigorously in one direction continuously until the liquid is fully absorbed by the meat.
   **Key Point**: The recommended ratio of meat to water is about 1 jin of meat to 3 liang of water. The judgment standard is when the meat filling becomes lighter in color and smoother in texture.
3. Add Hua Tiao wine, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, salt, and white pepper in sequence. Then crack in one egg and mix quickly and evenly.
   **Note**: At this stage, the meat filling should be relatively soft and loose. If you are concerned that it is too soft to handle for wrapping, you can refrigerate it for half an hour.
4. Add one spoon of pre-fried seasoned oil and mix well.
   **Key Point**: The seasoned oil enhances aroma and helps lock in the moisture of the meat filling.
5. Just before you start wrapping, add the chopped scallions or other side ingredients and mix evenly.
   **Key Point**: Do not add side ingredients too early.

## Health Suggestion
This universal meat filling has a balanced fat-to-lean ratio. Mixing the filling with scallion-ginger water helps remove any gamey taste and locks in moisture. It is suitable for making dumplings, meatballs, etc., and is high in protein and easy to digest. Be careful to control the amount of salt; individuals with high blood pressure can reduce it by half. Chilling the filling in the refrigerator makes it easier to handle.
