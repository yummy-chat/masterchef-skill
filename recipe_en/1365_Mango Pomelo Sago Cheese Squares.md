# Mango Pomelo Sago Cheese Squares
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥭 1 mango (diced)
🍊 1 pomelo (peeled into segments)
🍚 Sago, as needed (cooked until transparent with no white core)
🧀 200g cream cheese
🌾 180g glutinous rice flour (water-milled glutinous rice flour)
🌽 45g cornstarch
🥛 250ml milk
🫒 20g oil (kneaded into the dough)
🍬 30g sugar (for the glutinous rice skin)
🍬 20g sugar (for the filling)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Pot
🕳️ Sieve
📡 Microwave (or steamer)
🫙 Plastic wrap
📦 Baking tray/square container
🔪 Knife
🪚 Cutting board
🪯 Rolling pin
🧤 Disposable gloves

## Preparation Steps
1. Dice the mango into cubes, peel the pomelo into segments, and cook the sago until transparent with no white core, then rinse with cold water and set aside.
**Key point**: The sago must be fully cooked; rinsing with cold water prevents sticking.

2. Add 20g sugar to the cream cheese and stir until smooth, then add half of the diced mango, all the pomelo segments, and the sago, mixing evenly.
**Note**: The cheese should be softened in advance and stirred until free of lumps.

3. Spread the filling onto a baking tray lined with plastic wrap, shape it into a long strip, and freeze for about 2 hours until firm.
**Key point**: Sufficient freezing time is needed; otherwise, the filling may deform during wrapping.

4. Make the glutinous rice skin: Mix the glutinous rice flour, cornstarch, and 30g sugar, then pour in the milk and stir into a smooth batter. Strain through a sieve, cover with plastic wrap.
**Note**: The batter must be strained to remove lumps, ensuring a fine texture.

5. Heat in the microwave on high for about 4 minutes until fully set.
**Key point**: Adjust heating time based on microwave power; the dough should be fully cooked.

6. Let the dough cool slightly until no longer hot to the touch, then add 20g oil and knead until smooth and elastic. Wrap in plastic wrap.
**Note**: Lightly oil your hands to prevent sticking while kneading.

7. Dust a cutting board with cooked starch to prevent sticking, roll the dough into a thin sheet, wrap it around the frozen filling strip, seal the edges, and trim off excess dough.
**Key point**: The dough sheet should be rolled evenly in thickness; expel as much air as possible when wrapping.

8. Wrap with plastic wrap and freeze for another 2 hours or more to set.
**Note**: Freezing before cutting yields cleaner, sharper pieces.

9. Remove and cut into pieces. The skin is soft and chewy, while the filling is cold and rich.

## Health Advice
This Mango Pomelo Sago Cheese Square is rich in vitamin C from mangoes, dietary fiber from pomelo, and creamy cheese. It is ideal for cooling and stimulating appetite in summer, but it has a high calorie content and should be consumed in moderation. Ensure the sago is fully cooked; those with diabetes or on a weight-loss diet should reduce the sugar content.
