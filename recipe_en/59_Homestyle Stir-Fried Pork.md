# Homestyle Stir-Fried Pork

Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Pork belly with skin, appropriate amount (sliced evenly in thickness)  
🫑 Spiral chili peppers, appropriate amount (seeded and cut into chunks or strips)  
🧄 Garlic, 4-5 cloves (minced or sliced)  
🌶️ Small chili peppers, 2-3 (cut into segments)  
🫘 Sweet bean paste, about 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount  
🍶 Yellow wine, appropriate amount  
🫗 Steamed fish soy sauce, a little  
🧂 MSG, a little  
🍬 Sugar, a little  
🫒 Vegetable oil, a little (for sautéing, optional)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🍳 Wok  
🥄 Wok spatula/ Spatula  
🪓 Chef knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🥣 Bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. Wash and seed the spiral chili peppers, then cut into chunks or strips. Slice or mince the garlic. Cut the small chili peppers into segments. Scrape the pork belly skin clean and slice into pieces of even thickness.
2. Heat the wok over high heat, without oil or with only a little oil. Add the spiral chili peppers and stir-fry over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes, until the peppers soften, the surface slightly wrinkles, and a fragrance is released. Remove and set aside.  
   **Key Point**: This step removes some moisture from the peppers and enhances their aroma; there is no need to fully cook them.
3. Leave a little base oil in the wok. Add the pork belly slices and cook over medium-low heat for about 5-7 minutes, until the edges of the meat slightly curl, the fat becomes translucent, the lean meat turns golden, and a clear layer of oil is rendered from the meat.  
   **Key Point**: Be sure to fully render the fat from the pork belly so the dish is rich but not greasy, and the skin becomes crispy. Excess oil can be removed if needed.
4. Add the sweet bean paste, light soy sauce, yellow wine, and steamed fish soy sauce to the pork belly one after another. Stir-fry evenly to release the aroma of the sauce.  
   **Key Point**: Add the sweet bean paste first and stir-fry it to release its fragrance before adding the other liquid seasonings.
5. Add the garlic slices and small chili pepper segments, continuing to stir-fry until fragrant.
6. Add the pre-stir-fried spiral chili peppers. Increase the heat to high and stir-fry quickly for about 30 seconds, allowing the peppers to fully absorb the meat juices and sauce flavors.  
   **Key Point**: Stir-fry over high heat quickly; do not cook for too long to maintain the crisp and tender texture of the peppers.
7. Finally, add a little MSG and sugar to enhance the freshness. Stir-fry evenly and then serve.

## Health Advice
In this dish, the pork belly renders fat through stir-frying, and the chili peppers lose some moisture. The combination of protein and vitamin C is balanced, but the fat content is still high. It is recommended to serve with cucumber salad or brown rice to balance calories and dietary fiber. This dish is suitable for those who enjoy meat and need to control sugar intake. For individuals with high blood pressure, the amount of soy sauce and sweet bean paste should be reduced.
