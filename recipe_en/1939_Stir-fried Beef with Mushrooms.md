# Stir-fried Beef with Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef, appropriate amount (pre-marinated)  
🍄 Mushrooms, appropriate amount  
🧅 Onion, appropriate amount (sliced)  
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount  
🫒 Oil, appropriate amount  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Pan  
🔪 Knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥄 Spatula  

## Instructions  
1. Place the pan over high heat, add oil, and quickly stir-fry the marinated beef until the color turns white, then remove and set aside.  
2. Add a little more oil to the pan, add the mushrooms and onion slices, and stir-fry until fragrant.  
3. Return the cooked beef to the pan, stir-fry together with the mushrooms and onions over medium heat until the ingredients are fully cooked and the flavors are well combined.  
4. Transfer to a plate and serve.  

## Health Tips  
Beef is high in protein and low in fat, mushrooms are rich in dietary fiber, and onions have antioxidant properties, making this dish suitable for those who are fitness-conscious or watching their fat intake. When marinating the beef, use less oil and salt, and avoid overheating the onions during stir-frying to prevent bitterness.
