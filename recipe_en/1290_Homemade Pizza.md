# Homemade Pizza
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All-Season Meal

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌾 All-purpose flour 900g
🧂 Salt 6g + to taste
🍬 Granulated sugar 50g + to taste
🍞 Yeast 10g
💧 Water 480ml
🫒 Cooking oil 60g + to taste
🧄 Garlic 6 cloves (minced)
🧅 Onions 4 (minced, reserve some for rings)
🥩 Ground beef 500g
🍅 Tomatoes 10 (peeled and chopped, reserve some for slicing)
🧀 Mozzarella cheese, as needed
🫑 Green bell pepper, as needed (sliced)
🌿 Bay leaves, as needed
🥫 Tomato paste, as needed
🧂 Black pepper, as needed
🥄 Soy sauce 30g

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl
🪓 Rolling pin
🔪 Knife
🍳 Wok or skillet
🥄 Wooden spatula
🍲 Steamer basket
📌 Bamboo skewer
🔥 Oven
🍕 Pizza cutter
📄 Parchment paper

## Steps
1. **Make the dough**: Combine flour, salt, sugar, and yeast. Gradually add water and mix until no dry flour remains. Add 60g oil and knead into a smooth dough.
   **Note**: Oil makes the texture softer; kneading on a work surface is easier.
2. **Proof**: Place dough back in the bowl, cover, and proof until doubled in size.
3. **Cook the pizza sauce**: Heat oil in a pan over low heat. Sauté garlic and minced onion until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Add peeled and chopped tomatoes, then black pepper, sugar, salt, soy sauce, bay leaves, and tomato paste. Stir well and simmer over low heat until thick.
   **Note**: Cover to prevent splatters, stir occasionally to prevent sticking; cook until thick.
4. **Deflate and divide**: Use a rolling pin to roll out the proofed dough to release air. Knead briefly and divide into 6 equal portions.
5. **Shape the crust**: Roll each portion into a large round disc. Use both hands to push up the edges, then place on parchment-lined steamer trays. Poke evenly with a bamboo skewer.
   **Note**: Push up edges to form the pizza rim; poking prevents bubbles during steaming.
6. **Steam the crust**: Place in a cold steamer, bring water to a boil, then steam for 12 minutes. Turn off heat and let rest for 3 minutes before removing to cool.
7. **Assemble the pizza**: On the crust, layer pizza sauce, a layer of mozzarella, bell pepper slices, onion rings, tomato slices, then another layer of mozzarella.
8. **Store**: Wrap assembled pizzas in sealed bags and freeze.
9. **Bake**: Take frozen pizza directly from the freezer, place in a preheated 200°C oven, and bake for 20 minutes.
   **Note**: No need to thaw; bake directly.

## Health Advice
This pizza crust is steamed before baking, reducing the oil used compared to traditional methods, making it a good way to control fat intake. Suitable for family gatherings, but note that ground beef and cheese are calorie-dense; portion control helps balance nutrition. Pair with a fresh salad to increase dietary fiber.
