# Braised Beef with Tomatoes and Potatoes
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Beef short ribs 1000g (choose pieces with more tendon, cut into slightly larger square chunks)  
🍅 Tomatoes 6 (choose ripe ones, peel and cut into small pieces or thin slices)  
🥔 Potatoes 2 (peel and cut into chunks)  
🥕 Carrot 1 (peel and cut into chunks)  
🧅 Onion 1 (cut into chunks)  
🌿 Cilantro a handful (sprinkle in before serving)  
🌶️ Dried chili segments to taste  
🥄 Light soy sauce two ladlefuls  
🥄 Dark soy sauce 1/3 ladle  
🥄 Oyster sauce 1/3 ladle  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🍬 Sugar to taste (adjust based on tomato acidity, recommended to balance into a sweet and sour flavor)  
🧂 MSG to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil to taste  

## Cookware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🍲 Soup pot  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🏺 Soup ladle  
🔪 Kitchen knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🪵 Wooden lid  

## Preparation Steps  
**Preparation Stage**  
1. Cut the beef short ribs into slightly larger square chunks.  
2. Place the beef in cold water, bring to a boil over high heat, then turn off the heat. Remove and rinse off any foam with warm water.  
   - Note: Be sure to start with cold water; if the beef is fresh enough, do not add any seasonings during blanching.  
3. Score a cross on the top of each tomato, blanch in boiling water until the skin curls up, peel off the skin, and cut into small pieces or thin slices for later use.  
   - Key point: Use a generous amount of tomatoes, as they are essential to the dish’s flavor.  
4. Peel and cut the potatoes, carrot, and onion into chunks, and set aside.  

**Cooking Stage**  
1. Heat the wok, add oil, then add scallion segments, ginger slices, two star anise, and dried chili segments. Sauté over high heat until fragrant.  
2. Add the blanched beef and stir-fry until the surface moisture of the beef is dry.  
3. Keep the heat high, add two ladlefuls of light soy sauce, 1/3 ladle of dark soy sauce, and 1/3 ladle of oyster sauce. Stir-fry evenly to coat the beef with the seasonings.  
4. Add the tomatoes and stir-fry until they soften and release juice, forming a rich broth base.  
5. Add enough hot water, bring to a boil over high heat, then cover with a lid and reduce to low heat. Braise for 1.5 to 2 hours.  
   - Note: Use hot water and ensure the amount is sufficient.  
6. Once the beef is tender, add the potato chunks, carrot chunks, and onion chunks. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and braise for about 30 minutes, until the potatoes can be easily pierced with chopsticks.  
7. Season with salt, sugar, and MSG to taste, stir well, and finally sprinkle in cilantro before serving.  

## Health Advice  
This dish is rich in protein and lycopene. The beef, when braised until tender, is also suitable for elders with weaker teeth. Note that potatoes and carrots should be added last to minimize vitamin loss.
