# Cold Tossed Cucumber
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 Cucumber
🧄 Garlic
🫗 Vinegar
🫗 Soy sauce
🧂 Salt
🫗 Sesame oil
🫗 Oyster sauce

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🥣 Bowl
🧊 Refrigerator (optional)

## Steps
1. Place the cucumber on a cutting board, smash it with the knife blade, then cut into segments about 3 cm long.
2. Place the cut cucumber segments into a bowl.
3. Crush the garlic and finely mince it.
4. Add vinegar, soy sauce, salt, oyster sauce, and minced garlic to the bowl in order, stir evenly, and let it marinate for 15 minutes.
5. Drizzle with sesame oil, mix well again, and serve.

## Health Tips
Cold tossed cucumber is a refreshing, low-calorie side dish with high water content, rich in vitamins and dietary fiber, suitable for weight loss or appetizing in summer. Be mindful to control the amount of salt and oyster sauce to avoid excessive sodium intake.
