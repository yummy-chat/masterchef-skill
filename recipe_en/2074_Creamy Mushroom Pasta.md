# Creamy Mushroom Pasta
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🥧 Tart shell 1 (for making the soup base)  
🍖 Ham to taste (diced)  
🍄 Button mushrooms to taste (sliced)  
🧅 Purple onion to taste (diced)  
🌽 Corn kernels to taste  
🍝 Dried pasta to taste (broken into pieces)  
🦐 Shrimp to taste  
🥛 Milk to taste (enough to submerge ingredients)  
🧈 Heavy cream a generous amount  
🧀 Cheese slice 1  
🧂 Black pepper sea salt to taste  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🍚 Electric rice cooker  
🥄 Spatula  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🔗 Tongs  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Place the tart shell into the inner pot of the electric rice cooker, add an appropriate amount of water, and stir with a spatula until the tart shell is completely melted, forming a thick soup base.
2. Add diced ham, sliced button mushrooms, diced purple onion, corn kernels, and the broken dried pasta in sequence.  
   **Note**: The dried pasta must be broken first to fit the cooking space of the rice cooker.
3. Continue adding milk, shrimp, and a generous amount of heavy cream, and finally top with one cheese slice.  
   **Note**: The liquid should roughly cover the ingredients; place the cheese slice last so it melts easily and forms a creamy surface layer.
4. Close the rice cooker lid and start the cooking program. After the program finishes, the soup should be thick and bubbling, and the pasta should be softened.  
   **Time & State**: Follow the regular rice cooking program of the rice cooker; after completion, the soup is thick and the pasta is tender yet chewy.
5. Open the lid, sprinkle in black pepper sea salt, and stir thoroughly with chopsticks to blend the seasonings.
6. Use tongs to lift the pasta into a plate, then pour the remaining creamy mushroom soup and toppings from the pot over the pasta.

## Health Advice  
This dish is rich in calcium and high-quality protein, suitable for those looking to build muscle or supplement calcium, but it is high in calories. It is recommended to pair with a portion of leafy green vegetables to balance nutrition. If lactose intolerant, consider using lactose-free milk instead.
