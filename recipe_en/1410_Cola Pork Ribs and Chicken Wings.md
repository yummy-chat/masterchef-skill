# Cola Pork Ribs and Chicken Wings
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐔 Chicken wings (equal quantity to ribs)
🐖 Pork ribs (equal quantity to chicken wings)
🫚 Ginger slices, to taste
🧅 Scallion segments, to taste
⭐ Star anise, to taste
🌿 Cinnamon stick, to taste
🌶️ Dried chili peppers, to taste
🥤 Cola, one bottle (enough to submerge the ingredients)
🧂 Light soy sauce, half a tablespoon
🧂 Salt, one teaspoon
🌿 Chopped scallions, to taste (for garnish)
🌶️ Chopped red chili, to taste (for garnish)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🍳 Wok or frying pan
🥄 Spatula or spoon
🍲 Lid
🍽️ Plate
🥣 Small bowl

## Preparation Steps
1. Score the chicken wings and cut into pieces, cut the pork ribs into segments; slice the ginger, cut the scallions into segments, and set aside.
   **Key Point**: Cut the ingredients into uniform sizes to ensure even heat distribution.

2. Place the wok on the heat and heat it up, then pour in an appropriate amount of cooking oil and wait for the oil temperature to rise.
   **Key Point**: The wok should be clean and preheated thoroughly; a proper oil temperature effectively prevents sticking.

3. First, add the ginger slices and fry until fragrant, then add the pork ribs and chicken wings and stir-fry them directly.
   **Key Point**: No need to blanch; stir-frying raw maximizes the tenderness of the meat, preventing it from becoming tough and dry.

4. Add the scallion segments, star anise, cinnamon stick, and dried chili peppers, and stir-fry everything together to fully release the aroma of the spices.

5. Continue stir-frying and searing until the surface of the ingredients turns golden brown, and a noticeable amount of oil separates at the bottom of the wok.
   **Key Point**: Render out excess fat for a cleaner, less greasy final dish.

6. Pour in one bottle of cola, ensuring it completely submerges the ingredients.
   **Key Point**: Use enough cola to guarantee even absorption of flavor during braising.

7. Add half a tablespoon of light soy sauce and one teaspoon of salt, and gently stir to combine.
   **Note**: The cola contains caramel for natural coloring, so no additional sugar is needed.

8. Cover the wok with a lid, reduce the heat to medium, and braise for 15 minutes.
   **Key Point**: Braise slowly over medium heat to allow the ingredients to fully absorb the sauce and develop flavor.

9. Remove the lid, transfer the finished dish to a plate, and garnish with chopped scallions and chopped red chili.
   **Key Point**: Sprinkle the garnishes immediately after plating to enhance the dish's appearance and flavor.

## Health Advice
Cola pork ribs and chicken wings are prepared using a stir-frying technique to preserve the tenderness of the meat. The sugar in the cola provides natural coloring, but the overall sugar content is relatively high. It is recommended to pair this dish with stir-fried vegetables to balance nutrition.
