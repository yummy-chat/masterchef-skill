# Skipjack Tuna and Seaweed Corn Rice  
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
- 🍚 Northeast rice  
- 🌽 Instant corn kernels 80g  
- 🍘 Skipjack tuna and seaweed flakes 20g  

## Seasonings  
(No additional seasonings required)  

## Kitchenware  
- 🍚 Rice cooker (recommended: Mijia IH rice cooker, ❌pressure cooker not recommended)  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Wash the Northeast rice thoroughly, place it in the rice cooker, and add water until it covers the rice by about 3mm.  
2. Start the cooking program and wait for the rice to finish cooking.  
3. Take out the hot rice, add the corn kernels, and mix well.  
4. Sprinkle with skipjack tuna and seaweed flakes, lightly mix together, and serve.  

## Health Tips  
This rice dish is primarily carbohydrate-based. It is recommended to pair it with a protein source (such as a fried egg or chicken breast) and a small plate of leafy greens to ensure balanced nutrition. The skipjack tuna and seaweed flakes provide natural umami and saltiness, so no additional seasoning is needed, making it suitable for those on a low-sodium diet.
