# Fish Soup Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🐟 2 crucian carp
🥚 3 eggs
🥬 Some green vegetables
🍅 1 tomato (sliced)
🍝 1 serving of thin dried noodles
🫚 1 small piece of ginger (shredded)
🫒 Some cooking oil
🧂 Some salt
🌶️ Some white pepper powder

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick frying pan
🥄 Silicone spatula
🥢 Strainer
🧻 Cheesecloth
🍲 Clay pot or casserole
🍲 Soup bowl

## Steps
1. Clean the crucian carp thoroughly, removing the black membrane inside the belly, rinse, and pat dry the surface with a paper towel.
2. Add cooking oil to a non-stick frying pan, place the whole fish in, and fry over medium-low heat for about 2-3 minutes, until one side is golden and set.
   **Key point**: Sprinkle a little salt on the fish to help with flavor and prevent sticking.
3. Carefully flip the fish and fry the other side until golden, keeping the fish shape intact.
4. Pour enough boiling water into the pan all at once, and add the shredded ginger.
   **Key point**: Must add boiling water and enough water at once; this is key to making the soup milky white.
5. Cover with a lid, bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
   **Key point**: Cooking with the lid on creates "wok air," making the broth thicker and whiter.
6. Strain the cooked fish soup through a strainer lined with cheesecloth into another container to obtain a smooth, boneless, and fishbone-free thick soup. The strained fish bones can be set aside for other uses.
7. In another pan, add a little cooking oil, crack in the eggs, and fry until both sides are golden for a sunny side up or fried egg.
8. Pour the strained fish soup into the pan with the fried egg and bring to a boil.
9. Once the soup is boiling, add the thin dried noodles, loosen them with chopsticks, and cook according to the package instructions, being careful not to overcook.
10. When the noodles are almost done, add the greens and tomato slices, and cook until the vegetables are tender.
11. Adjust salt to taste, sprinkle in white pepper powder, stir well, and turn off the heat.
12. Ladle the noodles, soup, fried egg, and vegetables into a bowl. Serve and enjoy while hot.
    **Extra suggestion**: The strained fish bones can be seasoned with a little seasoning and baked in an air fryer until crispy, making use of everything.

## Health Advice
Fish soup noodles are rich in high-quality protein and calcium, making them suitable for the elderly, children, and postpartum individuals who need calcium supplementation. The broth is already flavorful enough, so salt and pepper should be added in moderation; those with high blood pressure should use less salt.
