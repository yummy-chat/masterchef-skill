# Cantonese-Style Steamed Chicken
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Half a young hen (remove the tail, chop into thumb-sized pieces)
🌼 Dried daylily buds (golden needles) appropriate amount (soaked in advance, squeezed dry)
🍄 Black fungus appropriate amount (soaked in advance, squeezed dry)
🧅 Ginger appropriate amount (sliced)
🧅 Small onions 3-4 pieces (sliced)
🍈 Red dates 1-2 pieces (shredded)
🍒 Goji berries a pinch (soaked in water and set aside)
🧄 Scallion whites a pinch (cut into sections)

🧂 Salt appropriate amount
🐟 Fish sauce appropriate amount
🥡 Light soy sauce appropriate amount
🥡 Dark soy sauce a pinch
🌾 Cornstarch a pinch
🥜 Peanut oil appropriate amount

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, chef's knife
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🍳 Iron plate or copper plate (for good heat conduction)
♨️ Steamer / Wok
🥢 Chopsticks

## Preparation Steps
1. Remove the tail from the young hen, chop into thumb-sized pieces, ensuring even size for even seasoning and tender texture when steamed.
2. Prepare sliced ginger, sliced small onions, scallion white sections, shredded red dates, soaked and squeezed dried daylily buds and black fungus, and soaked goji berries.
3. Add salt, fish sauce, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and a pinch of cornstarch to the chicken pieces. Use your hands to thoroughly massage and coat the chicken evenly with the seasonings.
4. Add all the prepared vegetables to the chicken and mix well again.
5. Drizzle in peanut oil and mix once more to seal in the seasoning juices.
6. Transfer the mixed ingredients to the iron or copper plate, spreading them evenly to ensure uniform heat distribution.
7. Add sufficient water to the steamer, bring to a full boil over high heat. Place the plate in the steamer, cover, and steam vigorously over high heat for 7–8 minutes.
**Key Point**: At around 4 minutes of steaming, open the lid and use chopsticks to turn the chicken pieces, ensuring even cooking throughout.
8. After steaming, remove the plate. Carefully pour out the accumulated juices from the plate, then drizzle them back evenly over the chicken, allowing the ingredients to fully absorb the liquid. Ready to serve.

## Health Advice
This steamed chicken incorporates black fungus and dried daylily buds to increase dietary fiber, paired with red dates and goji berries for balanced nutrition and low fat content, suitable for those needing to control fat intake. Fish sauce has a relatively high salt content, so individuals with high blood pressure should reduce the amount used.
