# Steamed Shrimp Dumplings
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Ground pork, as needed
🍤 Large shrimp, as needed (heads and body shells removed, tails left intact)
🥟 Dumpling wrappers, as needed (store-bought)
🧅 Scallions, as needed (finely chopped)
🧂 Light soy sauce, 2 tbsp
🧂 Dark soy sauce, 1/2 tbsp
🦪 Oyster sauce, 1/2 tbsp
🧂 Salt, 1 tsp
🧂 Chicken bouillon, 1 tsp
🍛 Thirteen Spices, 1 tsp
🫒 Hot oil, as needed (drizzled over scallions at the end)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Bowl or basin (for mixing filling)
🥢 Chopsticks (for stirring filling)
✋ Hands (for handling shrimp and wrapping dumplings)
♨️ Steamer
🍽️ Plate

## Steps
1. Place the ground pork in a bowl. Add light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, chicken bouillon, thirteen spices, and scallions. Stir thoroughly until well combined.
2. Then drizzle with cooking oil heated to about 70% hot. Stir again to fully meld the filling and aroma.
   **Key point**: Drizzling hot oil at the end is a crucial step for enhancing aroma. The filling must be stirred until evenly textured and firm.
3. Remove the heads and body shells of the large shrimp, leaving the tails intact.
   **Note**: The tails must be kept; this is key to the dumplings' presentation and visual appeal.
4. Place a dumpling wrapper in your palm. Add an appropriate amount of meat filling, then place one prepared shrimp on top of the filling.
5. Using both hands, pinch the dumpling wrapper together from the sides toward the center, pleating it gradually until you reach the shrimp tail. Ensure the tail is exposed and seal the edges tightly.
   **Key point**: The wrapping method must prevent the dumplings from leaking filling while ensuring an appealing shape.
6. Arrange all wrapped dumplings in the steamer.
   **Note**: The dumplings should be placed in the steamer only after steam has started rising.
7. Once the water is boiling, place the steamer over it, cover, and steam for 15 minutes, until the dumpling wrappers appear translucent and the filling is fully cooked.

## Health Advice
These steamed shrimp dumplings are rich in high-quality protein and moderate fat, suitable for those needing an energy boost. However, note that the ground pork and hot oil increase the calorie content; those watching their weight should consume in moderation. Pairing with a vegetable salad is recommended to balance nutrition. Steaming is a healthier cooking method compared to frying.
