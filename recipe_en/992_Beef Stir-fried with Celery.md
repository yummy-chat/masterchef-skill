# Beef Stir-fried with Celery
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef 150g (shredded, cut against the grain)
🌿 Celery 300g (sliced diagonally into segments)
🌶️ Red chili pepper to taste (sliced diagonally into strips)
🧄 Garlic to taste (crushed)
🧅 Scallions and ginger as needed (for making scallion-ginger water to marinate the meat)
🧂 Salt a little (used twice: for marinating the meat and stir-frying the celery)
🥣 Scallion-ginger water to taste
🌾 Cornstarch to taste (for coating to lock in moisture)
🎨 Dark soy sauce a little (for color)
🫒 Cooking oil to taste (for greasing the pan and sealing the meat)
🍬 Sugar a little (to enhance freshness)
🥄 Light soy sauce to taste (for seasoning)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🔪 Knife and cutting board
🥣 Bowls or basins (for marinating the beef and holding ingredients)

## Preparation Steps
1. **Pre-treat the beef**
   Rinse the beef clean. First, slice it against the grain into large thin pieces, then shred it against the grain into thin strips. Cutting against the grain breaks the fibers to prevent a tough texture.

2. **Marinate the beef**
   Add a little salt and scallion-ginger water to the shredded beef. Quickly mix and knead until it becomes sticky.
   **Key point**: Salt and kneading are essential for drawing out moisture; otherwise, the starch coating may fall off later, affecting the meat’s texture.

3. **Coat and seal with oil**
   Add cornstarch and mix well. Then drizzle in a little dark soy sauce and mix evenly for color. Finally, add a little cooking oil and mix to seal in the moisture.

4. **Prepare the vegetables**
   Slice the celery diagonally into segments. Slice the red chili pepper diagonally into thin strips. Crush the garlic and set aside.

5. **Quick-fry the beef**
   Heat the wok, then add oil to coat it. Turn off the heat and let the oil temperature drop slightly. Turn the heat back on, keeping it at 30–40% of full heat. Add the shredded beef. Stir-fry over low heat until it changes color and is just cooked through. Remove and set aside.
   **Key point**: Heat the wok before adding oil to prevent sticking. Use low oil temperature for a tender result.

6. **Sauté the aromatics**
   Using the residual oil in the wok, add the crushed garlic and red chili pepper strips. Sauté until fragrant. Add the celery segments and stir-fry until just cooked through. Sprinkle with a little salt to season the celery.

7. **Combine and season**
   Return the cooked beef strips to the wok. Add a little sugar and an appropriate amount of light soy sauce. Turn the heat to high and stir-fry quickly until everything is evenly mixed. Transfer to a plate and serve.

## Health Tips
Beef with celery is low in fat and high in fiber, making it suitable for those focusing on fitness and blood sugar control. Always cut the beef against the grain, and keep the celery crunchy for the best texture.
