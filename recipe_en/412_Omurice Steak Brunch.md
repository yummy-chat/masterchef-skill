# Omurice Steak Brunch  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍄 White mushrooms 3 (diced)  
🍅 Tomato half (diced)  
🌿 Celery leaves to taste (chopped)  
🥩 Beef sausage 1 (diced)  
🥚 Eggs about 2-3 (beaten)  
🧀 Shredded cheese to taste  
🥩 Steak about 100g  
🍞 Bread slices 2  
🥬 Endive to taste  
🍓 Strawberries 2 (diced)  
🍰 Fruit tart 1 piece (prepared in advance)  
🧂 Salt to taste  
⚫ Black pepper to taste  
🫒 Cooking oil to taste  
🥛 Sour cream 1 tablespoon  
🥛 Vanilla milk to taste (for hot latte)  
🍵 Matcha powder to taste  
🧊 Ice ball 1  
🥛 Milk to taste (for cold matcha latte)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🪵 Cutting board  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🍳 Cast iron frying pan  
♨️ Air fryer  
🥛 Milk frother  
🥣 Measuring cup  
🔧 Grinder  
🥄 Spatula  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Dice the white mushrooms, tomato, and beef sausage; chop the celery leaves; wash the endive and dice the strawberries.  
2. Prepare the filling: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat, pour in cooking oil and heat. Sauté the mushroom dices and sausage dices until fragrant, then add the tomato dices and cook until soft. Season with salt.  
   **Note**: If you prefer the raw texture of tomatoes, you can skip sautéing them.  
3. Make the omelet: Pour a little cooking oil into a cast iron frying pan and heat. Pour in the beaten eggs, tilt the pan to spread the egg mixture evenly into a thin omelet, keeping the heat on medium-low. When the bottom is set and the surface is still slightly moist, place the sautéed filling on one side, sprinkle with celery leaves and shredded cheese. Use a spatula to fold the other side of the omelet over the filling, lightly pressing the edges to seal.  
4. Sear the steak: Heat a cast iron pan over high heat, add a little cooking oil, and place the steak in the pan. Reduce to medium-high heat and sear until both sides are browned, adjusting the internal doneness to your preference. Remove and wrap in aluminum foil to rest for a moment.  
5. Heat the bread: Place the bread slices in the air fryer until warm and crispy on the surface.  
6. Prepare the beverages:  
   - Hot latte: Pour the vanilla milk into the milk frother, froth and heat, then pour into a coffee cup.  
   - Cold matcha latte: Dissolve the matcha powder in a small amount of water in a cup, add the ice ball, then pour in the milk.  
7. Assemble and plate: Place the omelet, diced steak, heated bread slices, endive, and strawberries on a plate. Top the omelet with a tablespoon of sour cream, and grind black pepper over the steak to taste. Serve with the fruit tart, hot latte, and cold matcha latte.

## Health Advice  
This brunch is rich in protein, making it suitable for fitness enthusiasts or weekend enjoyment. Note that the combination of bread and fruit tart may result in a high carbohydrate content, which could lead to higher calorie intake. Pairing with green tea to cut through the richness is recommended. Enjoy in moderation.
