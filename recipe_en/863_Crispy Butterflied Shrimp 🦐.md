# Crispy Butterflied Shrimp 🦐

## Cooking Steps
1. Lay the shrimp flat on a cutting board. Slit open along the back, squeeze out the shrimp brain and remove the vein. Rinse thoroughly under clean water and drain well.
   **Note**: The shrimp brain will make the dish bitter, and the vein contains impurities. Both must be completely removed.

2. In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic,适量 water, and适量 salt. Stir well to make a garlic sauce.

3. Pour the garlic sauce into the bowl with the shrimp. Gently mix with chopsticks and marinate for 5 minutes.
   **Note**: Using chopsticks to stir prevents injury from shrimp spines; discard any excess marinade after marinating.

4. After discarding the marinade, add 2 egg yolks and mix well with chopsticks. Gradually add potato starch in batches, stirring continuously until each shrimp is evenly coated with the batter.

5. Thoroughly dry the wok, then pour in enough oil. Heat until it reaches 60% hot, then add the shrimp. Fry over medium heat for about 3 minutes until the shrimp are cooked through, then remove and drain.
   **Key Point**: The wok must be dried before adding oil to prevent splattering; add shrimp at 60% oil temperature, fry slowly over medium heat until done.

6. Increase the oil temperature, then return the shrimp for a second fry. As soon as the shell becomes crispy, immediately remove and drain. Plate and serve.
   **Key Point**: The second fry should be brief to ensure a crispy exterior and tender interior.

## Health Tips
This dish uses egg yolks and starch batter for frying, resulting in a crispy exterior and tender interior. It is rich in high-quality protein but high in fat, suitable for occasional consumption rather than as a daily light meal. Control oil usage and pair with vegetables to balance nutrition; those with high blood lipids should consume in moderation.
