# Five-Spice Edamame and Peanuts
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April–August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🫘 Fresh edamame in pods, as needed  
🥜 Raw peanuts in shells, as needed  
🫚 Ginger, a few slices  
🧂 Salt, as needed (a small amount for washing, heavier for cooking)  
🌶️ Dried chili peppers, as needed  
⭐ Star anise, as needed  
🌿 Cinnamon stick, as needed  
🍃 Bay leaves, as needed  
🌰 1 black cardamom  
🫒 Cooked oil, as needed (to keep edamame bright green)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🪣 Stainless steel basin  
🍲 Stainless steel stockpot  
🥄 Soup spoon  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🍽️ Stainless steel flat plate  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Place the edamame and peanuts separately in clean water, add a little salt, and scrub repeatedly until clean.  
   **Key point**: Washing with salt more effectively removes surface dirt.  
2. Use scissors to snip off both ends of the edamame, and pinch one end of each peanut to create a crack.  
   **Key point**: This is a crucial step for flavor absorption.  
3. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add star anise, cinnamon stick, bay leaves, black cardamom, dried chili peppers, and ginger slices. Reduce heat to medium and boil for 3–5 minutes to extract the spice base.  
4. Add the prepared peanuts in shells, keeping the liquid at a rolling boil.  
5. After the peanuts have boiled for 3 minutes, add the edamame.  
6. Add one spoonful of cooked oil to the pot and stir well.  
   **Key point**: Cooked oil helps keep the edamame bright green and prevents darkening.  
7. Continue boiling for another 3 minutes, then add enough salt.  
   **Note**: Do not add salt too early, as it may cause the edamame to discolor.  
8. Once the salt is well mixed and the liquid returns to a boil, turn off the heat. Avoid prolonged cooking to prevent a mushy texture.  
9. After turning off the heat, let the edamame and peanuts soak in the spiced broth to absorb flavor, **without covering the pot**.  
   **Note**: Covering the pot will cause the edamame to turn yellow and become mealy.  
10. Once fully infused, use a slotted spoon to remove and quickly spread them on a flat plate to cool, preventing residual heat from affecting color and texture.  

## Health Suggestions  
This five-spice edamame and peanut dish is a perfect summer side. Edamame is rich in plant protein and dietary fiber, while peanuts provide healthy fatty acids. However, watch your salt intake to avoid water retention. It works well as a snack or with drinks, but avoid excessive sodium consumption.
