# Simon’s Braised Beef Brisket in Chu Hou Sauce
Cuisine: Cantonese
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
**🥩 Main Ingredients**
- Beef brisket (preferably the cut with tendons, like "hang nam") 500-600g
- Ginger 30g
- Garlic half a head
- Bird’s eye chili 1

**🧂 Seasonings**
- Bay leaves 2
- Sichuan peppercorns 0.5g
- Star anise 2
- Dried chilies 3
- Cloves 3
- Licorice slices 2
- Fermented red bean curd (nam yu) 2 pieces
- Pixian chili bean paste (doubanjiang) 15g
- Rock sugar 10g
- Huadiao wine 10g
- Baijiu (white liquor) 20g
- Chu hou paste 50g
- Oyster sauce 20g
- Dark soy sauce 5g
- Light soy sauce 60g
- Char siu (barbecue pork) sauce 20g

## Kitchenware and Equipment
**🍳 Kitchen Tools**
- Braising pot
- Pressure cooker (highly recommended)

## Steps
1. Cut the ginger into 1.5 cm cubes, peel the garlic, slice the bird’s eye chili into 1 cm pieces, and place them together in bowl 1. Place bay leaves, Sichuan peppercorns, star anise, dried chilies, cloves, and licorice slices in bowl 2. Place the fermented red bean curd in bowl 3. Place the chili bean paste in bowl 4. Place the rock sugar in bowl 5. Mix the Huadiao wine and baijiu in bowl 6. Mix the chu hou paste, oyster sauce, char siu sauce, dark soy sauce, and light soy sauce in bowl 7.
2. Do not cut the beef brisket. Place it whole in cold water, bring to a boil over high heat, then remove. Rinse off any surface impurities, cut into 4 cm chunks, and drain well.
3. Heat the pot over high heat, add oil, then add the contents of bowl 1 and stir-fry until fragrant.
4. Reduce to medium-low heat, add bowl 2, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds.
5. Add bowl 3, crush it with a spatula and stir-fry.
6. Add bowl 4 and stir-fry for about 30 seconds.
7. Add bowl 5 and stir-fry until the sugar melts.
8. Add the beef brisket chunks and stir-fry until evenly colored.
9. Turn the heat to high, pour bowl 6 along the edge of the pot, and stir-fry quickly until the surface of the beef is slightly charred.
10. Pour in bowl 7, and stir-fry quickly until the sauce thickens slightly and starts to bubble.
11. Transfer everything to a braising pot or pressure cooker, and add enough water to cover the ingredients.
12. If using a regular pot, simmer on low heat for 3 hours. If using a pressure cooker, cook under pressure for 40 minutes once it reaches full pressure.
13. Open the lid, adjust seasoning with salt, light soy sauce, or sugar to taste. First, remove the beef brisket.
14. If adding radish or potato, peel and cut into 2 cm thick slices, add to the pot and cook for 10–15 minutes. Once done, remove and place alongside the beef brisket.
15. Strain the cooking liquid and pour it over the beef and vegetables in the bowl.

## Health Tips
This dish is rich in savory sauce and has tender, soft beef brisket, making it suitable for those needing protein and energy. However, it is high in sodium; those with high blood pressure are advised to reduce the sauce用量 by half. Using a pressure cooker significantly shortens cooking time while retaining the collagen in the beef. It is recommended to serve with plenty of vegetables for a balanced meal.
