# Bell Pepper and Beef Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

🟡 Yellow bell pepper, half (cut into small pieces)

🍄 White mushrooms, 6-8 (sliced)

## Instructions
1. Place the beef chunks in a stainless steel tray. Add salt, black pepper, olive oil, and a small amount of cornstarch. Mix well with your hands, then let it marinate.
   **Note**: Adding cornstarch makes the beef texture more tender. Ensure it is thoroughly mixed so that the seasoning evenly coats the meat.
2. Cut half a red bell pepper and half a yellow bell pepper into small pieces. Cut the purple onion into pieces and slice the white mushrooms. Set aside.
   **Key point**: Only half a bell pepper is needed; the onion serves as a flavoring vegetable and releases aroma when stir-fried, enhancing the overall flavor.
3. Heat the cast iron pan until fully hot. Pour in an适量 amount of olive oil. Add the marinated beef chunks and sear over medium-high heat for about 3-4 minutes, until the surface of the beef is fully browned and the edges are slightly charred.
   **Note**: Ensure the pan is fully preheated before adding the ingredients to prevent the beef from sticking.
4. Add the onion pieces and stir-fry for about 1 minute until softened and fragrant. Then add the bell pepper pieces and stir-fry for about 1 minute until just cooked through.
   **Key point**: Do not stir-fry the bell pepper for too long to retain a crisp texture.
5. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pan and stir-fry together with the other ingredients for about 2 minutes, until the mushrooms shrink and soften.
   **Note**: Simply stir-fry the mushrooms promptly after adding; it does not affect the final result.
6. Taste the dish before serving. Add a little salt if needed to adjust the seasoning, and stir-fry evenly until the flavor is balanced.
   **Key point**: Adding salt at the end prevents over-salting if added too early.
7. Serve white rice in a deep ceramic plate. Arrange the stir-fried bell pepper and beef around the rice. Optionally, sprinkle a little black sesame seeds for garnish.
8. Place the rice bowl on a wooden serving tray and serve.

## Health Suggestions
The combination of beef and bell pepper is a golden pairing of high-quality protein and vitamin C. The vitamin C in bell pepper also aids in iron absorption. This dish is suitable for those building muscle or managing blood sugar, but white rice can be replaced with brown rice to reduce carbohydrate load.
