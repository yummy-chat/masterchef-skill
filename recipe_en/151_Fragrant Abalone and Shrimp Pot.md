# Fragrant Abalone and Shrimp Pot
Cuisine: Common Home-Cooked Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦐 Headless shrimp 250g (shelled with tails on, deveined and butterflied)  
🐚 Abalone 1 piece (washed and sliced)  
🥔 Potato appropriate amount (peeled and thinly sliced)  
🥒 Lotus root appropriate amount (peeled and thinly sliced)  
🍄 King oyster mushroom appropriate amount (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic appropriate amount (diced)  
🌶️ Red oil chili bean paste 2 tbsp  
🫘 Green Sichuan peppercorns a small handful  
🍶 Light soy sauce a drizzle  
🍬 Sugar a pinch  
🧂 Chicken bouillon a pinch  
🌱 Celery stalks appropriate amount (cut into segments)  
🥜 Toasted sesame seeds appropriate amount  
🌽 Cornstarch appropriate amount (for coating shrimp)  
🫗 Cooking oil appropriate amount (for deep frying)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board  
🍳 Frying pan/wok  
🥄 Slotted spoon  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🍽️ Serving dish  

## Steps
1. Remove the heads and shells of the fresh shrimp, keeping the tails intact. Butterfly and devein them. Evenly coat the prepared shrimp with cornstarch and set aside.
2. Peel and thinly slice the lotus root and potato. Rinse off surface starch with water, then drain. Slice the king oyster mushroom, slice the abalone, and dice the garlic.
3. Add enough cooking oil to the pan. Heat over medium-high heat until bubbles form when a chopstick is inserted. First, fry the shrimp until the shell separates from the meat and the surface turns golden, then remove. Next, fry the lotus root and potato slices until the edges are slightly charred, then remove and drain the oil.
4. Leave a small amount of oil in the pan. Sauté the diced garlic over low heat until fragrant. Add two tablespoons of red oil chili bean paste and stir-fry over low heat until the red oil releases.
5. Turn to medium heat. Add the green Sichuan peppercorns. Add the fried lotus root and potato slices, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds. Then add the abalone slices and briefly stir-fry. Next, add the king oyster mushroom slices and stir-fry until softened. Finally, add the fried shrimp and stir-fry until evenly combined.
6. Drizzle an appropriate amount of light soy sauce along the edge of the pan. Add a pinch of sugar and chicken bouillon. Turn to high heat and quickly stir-fry for about 1 minute. 30 seconds before turning off the heat, sprinkle in the toasted sesame seeds and celery segments. Stir-fry until the celery is just cooked, then turn off the heat.
7. Transfer the stir-fried dish to a serving plate. Place one abalone in its shell on top as a garnish.

## Health Advice
Rich in high-quality protein and dietary fiber. Shrimp and abalone provide low-fat, high-protein content. However, deep-frying and chili bean paste increase the calorie and sodium levels. It is recommended to pair with light vegetables to balance the meal. Suitable for those needing protein supplementation. For individuals with high blood pressure, reduce the amount of base sauce used.
