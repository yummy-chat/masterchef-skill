# Scrambled Eggs
Cuisine: Common home-style dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥚 Eggs (preferably pasteurized eggs)
🥛 Milk
🧂 Cooking oil
🌶️ Black pepper (optional)
🧂 Salt (optional)

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan
🥄 Spatula

## Preparation Steps
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk and 5ml of cooking oil, and whisk thoroughly until well combined. Set aside.
2. Place a non-stick pan over high heat for about 30 seconds, add 5ml of cooking oil, and continue heating for another 30 seconds, then reduce to low heat.
3. Pour the whisked egg mixture into the pan, let it sit for 5 seconds, then use a spatula to slowly push the eggs from the edges toward the center.
4. Stir-fry until the eggs are mostly set, then turn off the heat and transfer to a plate.
5. Season with black pepper or salt to taste, if desired.

## Health Tips
Scrambled eggs are rich in high-quality protein and calcium, making them ideal for a quick energy boost at breakfast and easy to digest for children and the elderly. Be mindful of the amount of oil and salt used. While milk adds tenderness and smoothness, it slightly increases the calorie content.
