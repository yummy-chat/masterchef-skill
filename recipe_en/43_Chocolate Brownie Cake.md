# Chocolate Brownie Cake
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🌾 Cake flour: 5 bottle caps (using a red mineral water bottle cap as a measure)
🍫 Cocoa powder: 2 bottle caps
🥄 Baking powder: 1 small inner cap (the size of the bottle cap's inner lid)
🍬 Sugar: 1 bottle cap
🥛 Milk: 4 bottle caps
🍫 Chocolate: as needed (for a gooey center)
🥜 Crushed nuts: as needed (for surface decoration)

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
☕ Microwave-safe cup or bowl
🥄 Spoon
🧢 Red mineral water bottle cap (measuring tool)
🔲 Microwave oven

## Steps
1. Pour cake flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and sugar into the cup. Stir thoroughly with a spoon until evenly combined.  
   **Key Point**: Make sure the baking powder is fully mixed, otherwise the cake may rise unevenly.
2. Add milk and stir into a smooth, thick batter. The batter should be able to coat the spoon and flow slowly.
3. Press the chocolate piece into the center of the batter, and sprinkle crushed nuts on the surface.
4. Place the cup in the microwave and heat on high for 1 minute 40 seconds. Remove once the batter has set and expanded in volume.  
   **Note**: Microwave power varies; if not fully set, extend the heating time as needed.

## Health Advice
This mug brownie uses bottle caps for quick measuring and is convenient for occasional indulgence, but it is high in sugar and calories. It is recommended to pair it with unsweetened yogurt or fruit to balance blood sugar; do not overheat to avoid a dry texture.
