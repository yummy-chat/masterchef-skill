# Luosifen (Snail Noodles)
Cuisine: Guangxi Cuisine
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍜 Rice noodles
🦪 Snail meat
🥒 Pickled bamboo shoots
🥜 Peanuts
🍘 Fried tofu skin
🍄 Wood ear mushrooms
🧂 Soup base packet
🍶 Vinegar
🌶️ Chili oil

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Cooking pot
🔥 Stove
🥢 Chopsticks

## Cooking Steps
1. Pour 1 liter of water into the pot and bring to a boil over high heat.
2. Add the rice noodles and cook for 3 to 5 minutes, gently stirring with chopsticks to prevent sticking.
**Key point**: The cooking time for the noodles can be shortened according to personal preference for a chewier texture.
3. Add the soup base packet, adjusting the amount to taste.
4. Add the ingredients that require cooking to fully absorb the flavors.
5. Add the seasoning packet according to personal taste and stir well.
6. Remove the noodles and soup and transfer them into a bowl.
7. Add the remaining ingredients to avoid compromising the texture from prolonged soaking.
**Key point**: Ingredients are divided into those that need cooking and those that do not; add them at different times to maintain optimal texture.
8. Enjoy while hot.

## Health Advice
Luosifen is rich in carbohydrates, protein, and probiotics, making it suitable for those needing energy and gut health. It is recommended to control the amount of chili oil to protect the stomach, and pickled bamboo shoots contain nitrites, so occasional consumption is better.
