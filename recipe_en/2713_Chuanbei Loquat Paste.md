# Chuanbei Loquat Paste
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April to August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍑 Loquats 6 jin (about 60 pieces, choose ripe and fully ripe ones)  
✨ Chuanbei powder (Fritillaria cirrhosa powder) 6 grams  
🍬 Rock sugar 2 jin  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Peeler  
🔪 Small knife  
🍲 Large pot  
🥄 Wooden spoon  
🫙 Glass jar (must be free of water and oil, sterilized with boiling water)  

## Preparation Steps
1. Peel the loquats, use a small knife to remove the hard stem at the top, cut them in half, remove the pits, and clean off any hard parts at both ends.
   **Note**: Ensure all tough stems and pits are completely removed to avoid affecting the texture.
2. Pour all the prepared loquat flesh into a large pot, add 2 jin of rock sugar, and cook over low heat. Stir occasionally with a wooden spoon to prevent sticking.
3. As the temperature rises, the loquats will naturally release juice. Stir with the wooden spoon while gently crushing the flesh. Cook for about 20 minutes until the rock sugar is completely melted.
4. Continue cooking over low heat for about 60 minutes. The mixture will gradually thicken, and the loquat pieces will noticeably shrink. At this point, add 6 grams of Chuanbei powder, reduce to low heat, and stir to fully incorporate the powder.
5. Keep over low heat and stir constantly until the mixture becomes very thick. When lifted with a wooden spoon, it should form thick strands and not drip easily—this indicates it is done.
6. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature. Transfer into the prepared clean glass jar and seal for storage.
   **Note**: The glass jar must be free of water and oil and sterilized with boiling water to ensure hygienic storage.

## Health Advice
This homemade Chuanbei loquat paste helps moisten the lungs and relieve coughs. It is suitable for occasional consumption during seasonal changes or when experiencing throat discomfort. Due to its high rock sugar content, it should be used with caution by those with diabetes. It is recommended to take one spoonful and mix with warm water for drinking, rather than spreading it on bread like jam.
