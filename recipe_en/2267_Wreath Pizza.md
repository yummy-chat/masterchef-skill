# Wreath Pizza  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🫓 1 round pizza dough (store-bought flatbread or pizza base)  
🌽 Mixed vegetable medley (green peas, diced carrots, corn kernels) to taste  
🍖 Diced ham to taste  
🧀 Shredded cheese to taste  
🍅 Ketchup to taste  
🥫 BBQ sauce or other dark-colored sauce to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥘 Round baking pan or deep dish  
🥃 Transparent glass cup  
🥄 Wooden spoon  
🔥 Oven  

## Instructions  
1. Lay the round pizza dough flat in the baking pan. Place a transparent glass cup upside down in the exact center of the dough.  
   **Note**: The glass cup is the core mold for shaping the wreath. Ensure it is stable and does not shift.  
2. Spread ketchup and BBQ sauce on the dough outside the glass cup. You may apply them in separate sections—no need to cover the entire dough completely.  
3. Evenly sprinkle the mixed vegetable medley over the sauce. Then layer on the diced ham, and finally cover the top evenly with shredded cheese.  
4. Cut the edge of the dough into several sections. Fold each section toward the central glass cup, pressing the tip against the bottom of the cup or inserting it into the filling to secure.  
   **Key point**: Fold steadily to keep the wreath shape neat and prevent excessive filling from spilling out.  
5. Place the baking pan into the preheated oven. Bake until the cheese is fully melted and the edges of the crust turn golden brown.  
   **Note**: Adjust the specific temperature and time based on your oven and dough thickness. Monitor closely during baking to avoid burning.  
6. After baking, remove from the oven. As an optional decorative step, pour a yellow beverage into the central glass cup for a fun effect.  

## Health Tips  
This is a creative wreath pizza featuring vegetables and ham, providing protein and dietary fiber. For a healthier option, use a whole-wheat crust and reduce the amount of sauce. It is ideal for sharing at gatherings, but be mindful of the sodium content in cheese and ham.
