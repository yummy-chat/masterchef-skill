## Preparation Steps
1. Shred the zucchini and carrot, place them in a bowl, add salt, mix well, and let sit for 10 minutes.
   **Key point**: Salting helps draw out excess moisture from the vegetables, preventing the filling from becoming watery.
2. Squeeze the pickled zucchini and carrot shreds firmly to remove excess water.
3. Add the wood ear mushrooms, scrambled and chopped eggs, and dried shrimp to the squeezed vegetables. Then add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, and mix thoroughly.
4. Take one wonton wrapper, place an appropriate amount of filling in the center, fold the wrapper in half, and pinch the edges tightly to seal. Then bring the two bottom corners together and pinch them to form a round shape.
   **Key point**: Ensure the edges and the closure are sealed tightly to prevent the dumplings from coming apart during steaming.
5. Brush the steamer rack with oil to prevent sticking, and arrange the wrapped crystal dumplings evenly on it.
6. Bring water in a pot to a boil, place the steamer rack on top, cover, and steam over high heat for 10 minutes.

## Health Suggestions
These crystal dumplings feature a low-fat, high-fiber filling of zucchini and carrot, paired with dried shrimp and eggs for protein, making them suitable for those looking to control calorie intake. Be sure to squeeze the pickled vegetables dry; otherwise, the wonton wrappers may become moist and tear easily.
