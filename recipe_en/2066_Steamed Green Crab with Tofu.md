# Steamed Green Crab with Tofu
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Autumn

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🦀 Green crab 2
🧈 Soft tofu 1 block (cut into small pieces for plating)
🧂 Edible salt to taste
🫚 Ginger to taste (sliced)
🧅 Scallion to taste
🫒 Cooking oil to taste
🌶️ Red pepper, minced a little (for garnish)
🧅 Scallion, chopped a little

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Stainless steel basin
✂️ Kitchen scissors
🪵 Wooden cutting board
🔪 Chef's knife
🪥 Old toothbrush
🍲 Steamer
🥢 Chopsticks
🥄 Soup spoon
🍽️ White porcelain plate

## Steps
1. Select crab: Use scissors to bleed the crab at the mouth; distinguish male and female by the shape of the bottom of the shell, and judge fatness by pressing the shell.
   **Key point**: Bleeding in advance prevents the legs from falling off when cutting the crab.
2. Prepare crab: Pry open the shell, remove the sandbag inside the shell, trim the edges of the shell flat; cut off the gills, mouthparts, and tips of the crab legs; scrub the crab body and shell with an old toothbrush; cut the crab body diagonally in half into pieces.
   **Key point**: The sandbag and gills are inedible and must be removed; scrub the shell surface gently to avoid damaging the crab meat.
3. Prepare tofu: Cut the tofu into small pieces and arrange on a plate, sprinkle with a little salt on the surface.
   **Key point**: Salt adds a base flavor to the tofu.
4. Plate and remove odor: Place the crab pieces on the tofu, cover with the crab shell and claws, place ginger slices on the shell, and put scallion in the plate.
   **Key point**: Ginger and scallion are used to remove odor and enhance aroma.
5. Steam: Bring water in the steamer to a boil, place the plated crab and tofu in the steamer, and steam over high heat for 6 minutes.
   **Key point**: Boiling water ensures sufficient steam.
6. Post-steaming process: Remove, discard the ginger slices and scallion, and flip the crab shell face up.
7. Garnish and drizzle oil: Sprinkle with chopped scallion and minced red pepper; heat cooking oil to 60% hot, then drizzle evenly over the dish to release the aroma.

## Health Tips
The tofu base absorbs the crab juices, providing a synergistic supplement of protein and calcium, especially suitable for autumn nourishment. Be sure to remove the crab gills and sandbag completely; those with gout should consume in moderation.
