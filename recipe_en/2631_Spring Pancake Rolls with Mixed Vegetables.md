# Spring Pancake Rolls with Mixed Vegetables
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Spring (January-April)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🌾 Flour, about 300 g  
🍜 Dried vermicelli, one handful (softened in advance in boiling water)  
🥬 Chinese chives, about 300 g (trim off tough root ends, cut into 5-6 cm segments)  
🌱 Soybean sprouts, about 300 g  
🥚 Eggs, 3  
💧 Water, about 200 g (75 g boiling water + 125 g cold water, for kneading dough)  
🧂 Salt, about 2 g (for kneading dough) + appropriate amount (for stir-frying bean sprouts)  
🍶 Light soy sauce, appropriate amount (mix with softened vermicelli for color and flavor)  
🧂 Chicken bouillon, appropriate amount (half mixed into vermicelli, half added before removing from heat)  
🫒 Cooking oil, appropriate amount (for brushing dough pieces, making oil paste, and stir-frying)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🍳 Wok  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🔪 Cutting board and bench scraper  
🍽️ Plate  
🖌️ Silicone oil brush  
🫙 Plastic wrap  
🥄 Small bowl/dish (for mixing oil paste)  
🥢 Rolling pin  
♨️ Electric griddle  
🥄 Wooden spatula/cooking spoon  

## Preparation Steps  
1. **Soak Vermicelli**  
   - Place dried vermicelli in a bowl, pour in boiling water and soak for about 10 minutes until softened; drain.  
   - Add light soy sauce and a little chicken bouillon, mix well to color and flavor the vermicelli.  
2. **Knead Dough**  
   - Mix flour with about 2 g salt, first pour in about 75 g boiling water, stir into fluff with chopsticks.  
   - Then add about 125 g cold water, knead into a smooth dough, cover with plastic wrap and let rest for 10 minutes.  
3. **Prepare Vegetables**  
   - Wash Chinese chives, trim off tough root ends, cut into 5-6 cm long segments.  
   - Wash soybean sprouts and drain.  
4. **Make Oil Paste**  
   - Place about 15 g flour in a small bowl, add about 20 g cooking oil, stir into a yogurt-like paste and set aside.  
5. **Rest Dough Pieces**  
   - Roll dough into a long log, cut into 12 evenly sized pieces.  
   - Brush a layer of oil on a plate, place dough pieces on it, brush another layer of oil on top, cover with plastic wrap, and let rest at room temperature for at least 1 hour.  
6. **Form Pancake Bases**  
   - Take rested dough pieces, press flat with hand. Take 4 pieces as a group, evenly spread oil paste between each layer, stack neatly.  
   - Gently press down, then roll out with a rolling pin into a thin pancake about 20 cm in diameter.  
7. **Cook Spring Pancakes**  
   - Preheat electric griddle, place thin pancake on it, cover and cook for about 40 seconds.  
   - When the pancake puffs up, flip and cook for another about 20 seconds, until both sides show light golden brown spots and layers separate completely.  
   - While hot, gently separate the stack of 4 pancakes to get paper-thin spring pancakes.  
8. **Stir-fry Mixed Vegetables**  
   - Heat oil in wok, pour in beaten eggs, stir-fry quickly until set and broken into pieces, cook until golden brown with some brown bits, then remove.  
   - Leave a little oil in the wok, add bean sprouts and stir-fry until just tender, season with salt.  
   - First add the white part of chives and stir-fry briefly, then add chive leaves, stir-fry quickly until green.  
   - Add a little chicken bouillon, add soaked vermicelli and cooked eggs, stir-fry quickly until well combined, then immediately remove from heat.  
9. **Assemble and Enjoy**  
   - Roll the stir-fried mixed vegetables in the cooked spring pancakes and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This spring pancake roll with mixed vegetables is rich in dietary fiber and protein, making it an excellent choice for a spring detox and suitable for those pursuing a light diet. Stir-fry chives and bean sprouts over high heat quickly to avoid releasing too much water, which could affect the texture.
