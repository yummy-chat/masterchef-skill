# Home-Style Mixed Radish
Cuisine: Common home cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥕 500g white radish (washed, unpeeled)  
🧄 Ginger, to taste (sliced)  
🧄 Garlic, to taste (sliced)  
🌶️ Green and red chili peppers, to taste (chopped)  
🍋 3 slices lemon (sliced)  
🧂 10g salt (for first pickling to draw out water)  
🧂 20g light soy sauce  
🍬 15g granulated sugar  
🧂 30g vinegar  
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorn oil, to taste (needs to be preheated until hot)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🔪 Chef's knife  
🥢 Cutting board  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🧤 Disposable gloves  
🥄 Spoon (for pouring hot oil)  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Cut the white radish lengthwise in half, then slice crosswise into moon-shaped pieces about the thickness of a coin.  
   **Key point**: Do not peel; the radish skin maintains a crunchy texture; slices should be uniform in thickness.  
2. Place the radish slices in a bowl, add 10g salt, mix evenly, and let sit for 30 minutes to allow water to release.  
3. After pickling, use your hands to thoroughly squeeze out the water from the radish slices.  
   **Key point**: Be sure to squeeze dry for better flavor absorption; do not rinse with water to avoid diluting the base flavor.  
4. Add ginger slices, garlic slices, chopped green and red chili peppers, and lemon slices to the squeezed radish slices.  
5. Add 20g light soy sauce, 15g granulated sugar, and 30g vinegar.  
6. Place Sichuan peppercorns on top of the seasonings, then use a spoon to pour in hot Sichuan peppercorn oil until you hear a sizzling sound, releasing the aroma.  
   **Key point**: Pouring hot oil is the crucial step for enhancing aroma; the oil temperature must be high enough.  
7. Use chopsticks to thoroughly mix all ingredients and seasonings.  
8. Cover with a lid or plastic wrap, let it sit for another 30 minutes, then serve.  

## Health Suggestions  
This cold radish dish is low in calories and rich in dietary fiber, suitable for weight loss periods or as an appetizer before meals. Keeping the radish skin during pickling maximizes the crunchy texture, but those with poor kidney function should avoid excessive consumption. Be mindful of salt intake; squeeze out water thoroughly after pickling before seasoning for a healthier result.
