# Thick Crust Orange Bread Sandwich
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍊 Thick Crust Orange Bread 1 piece
🥚 Egg 1 piece
🧀 Cheese slice 1 slice
🥬 Lettuce 1~2 leaves
🍯 Yellow sauce appropriate amount

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Non-stick pan
🔥 Gas stove
✂️ Kitchen scissors
🥢 Tongs

## Cooking Steps
1. Use kitchen scissors to cut the thick crust orange bread horizontally from the middle, keeping one side connected to form an open-mouthed sandwich shape.
2. Place the pan on the heat without oil, add the bread, and dry-fry both sides until the surface is slightly crispy and char marks appear.
   **Key Point**: Dry-frying enhances the bread's crispy texture.
3. Crack the egg into an empty spot on the pan, frying it together with the bread until the egg white is set to make a sunny-side-up egg.
   **Tip**: Fry simultaneously to improve efficiency.
4. Take one piece of bread, place the cheese slice on it, then immediately put the fried egg on top, using the residual heat of the egg to slightly melt the cheese.
5. Place the lettuce on the egg and squeeze an appropriate amount of yellow sauce.
6. Cover with the other piece of bread, cut diagonally with kitchen scissors, and serve.

## Health Advice
Thick crust orange bread is rich in dietary fiber, while eggs and cheese provide high-quality protein and calcium, making it suitable as a balanced breakfast. Be mindful of the amount of yellow sauce used to avoid excessive sugar intake.
