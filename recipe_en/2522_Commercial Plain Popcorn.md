# Commercial Plain Popcorn
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🌽 Dried popcorn kernels (round) 110g
🧈 Popcorn专用 oil (containing palm oil and coconut oil) 100g
🍬 Popcorn sugar (special sugar powder, containing salt, lecithin, and flavoring) 80g

## Cookware and Equipment
♨️ Commercial popcorn machine (with heating and stirring functions)
💨 Hair dryer or electric fan
🥄 Stainless steel strainer or draining tray
🥄 Spoon
🌡️ Thermometer

## Preparation Steps
1. Pour the popcorn oil and popcorn sugar together into the pot of the popcorn machine.
   **Key point**: Oil and sugar must be added simultaneously.
2. Turn on the heating and stirring functions, heat the oil-sugar mixture until it is completely melted, and the temperature rises to about 180°C.
   **State judgment**: The oil-sugar mixture is in a uniform liquid state with fine small bubbles appearing on the surface.
   **Key point**: The oil temperature must reach 180°C; do not add the popcorn kernels earlier or later.
3. Pour in the dried popcorn kernels, continue stirring and heating, and observe the first kernel pop.
4. When a kernel has popped, immediately cover the pot with the lid, and continue heating at maximum heat.
   **State judgment**: Continue heating until the popping sounds inside the pot basically stop.
5. When the popping sounds basically stop, immediately turn off the heat and quickly pour all the popcorn into the stainless steel drain tray.
   **Key point**: Turn off the heat promptly to prevent burning at the bottom of the pot.
6. Immediately use a hair dryer or electric fan to quickly blow cool air over the freshly made popcorn.
   **Key point**: Rapid cooling is a crucial step to prevent sticking and maintain crispness; do not skip it.
7. After the popcorn has completely cooled, gently tap it with the back of a spoon to separate any clumps of popcorn, then it is ready to be boxed.
   **Additional notes**:
   - For chocolate flavor: After the oil and sugar have melted in step 2, add 70g of chopped compound chocolate, stir until completely melted into a dark syrup, then pour in the popcorn kernels. Follow the same steps thereafter; after removing from the pot, cool it more quickly with the fan, as chocolate tends to stick more easily.
   - For fruit flavor: Adjust the oil amount to 130g and replace the popcorn sugar with 90g of regular granulated sugar. After the popcorn is done and the lid is opened, immediately pour in 60g of strawberry-flavored drink powder, then turn off the heating switch while keeping the stirring function running for about 30 seconds, using the residual heat to evenly coat the powder. Note that the heat must be turned off first before stirring; otherwise, the powder is prone to burning.

## Health Advice
This plain popcorn is made using the method of cooking oil and sugar together, resulting in relatively high calories. It is recommended to consume only occasionally as a snack and not as a substitute for meals. Suitable for sharing at gatherings, but due to its high sugar and oil content, those with high blood pressure, high blood sugar, or high blood lipids should consume it in moderation.
