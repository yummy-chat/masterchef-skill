# Stuffed Mushrooms
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍄 8-10 mushrooms (stems removed, caps kept intact)  
🥩 Appropriate amount of pork (with a little fat, minced into ground meat)  
🧅 2 spring onions (chopped into scallions)  
🧂 Salt to taste  
🧂 Chicken bouillon to taste  
🧂 Light soy sauce to taste  
🌽 Cornstarch to taste  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
💧 Sink  
🔪 Chef's knife, cutting board  
🥣 Bowls, plates  
♨️ Steamer  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Clean the mushrooms thoroughly, remove the stems, and keep the caps intact for later use.  
   - **Key Point**: Removing the stems creates a groove to fill with stuffing.  
2. Place the minced meat in a bowl, add salt, chicken bouillon, light soy sauce, and cornstarch. Mix vigorously in one direction with your hand until the mixture becomes sticky and firm, then sprinkle in the scallions and mix well.  
   - **Key Point**: Stirring makes the meat filling firm and improves texture; adjust seasonings to personal taste.  
3. Using a spoon or your hand, take the prepared meat filling and stuff it into the groove of each mushroom cap. Gently press it down, arrange them meat-side up on a plate.  
   - **Key Point**: The filling should be filled to slightly above the cap level for a fuller shape after steaming.  
4. Bring water to a boil in the steamer, place the stuffed mushrooms inside, cover the lid, and steam over high heat for 20 minutes.  
   - **Key Point**: Maintain high heat throughout; after 20 minutes, the mushrooms and filling will be fully cooked and ready to serve.  

## Health Advice  
This dish of stuffed mushrooms offers a balanced combination of protein and dietary fiber, making it suitable for those on a weight-loss diet or needing nutritional supplementation. However, it is recommended to control the ratio of fat to lean meat in the filling to avoid excessive saturated fat intake. The steaming method is low in oil and healthy, suitable for people of all ages.
