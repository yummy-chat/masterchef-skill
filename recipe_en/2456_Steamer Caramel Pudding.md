# Steamer Caramel Pudding
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥛 Milk 2 cartons (500ml)
🥚 Eggs 4 (about 165g, or 3 large eggs, must be at room temperature)
🍬 White sugar 50g (for caramel)
🍬 White sugar 16g (about 2 bottle caps, to add to egg mixture)
🥛 Condensed milk as needed (optional, to enhance milk aroma)

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥣 Stainless steel bowl or large bowl
🥣 Small bowl or ramekin (pudding mold)
🫗 Strainer
♨️ Steamer
🥄 Spoon
🔪 Small knife
🎞️ Plastic wrap or aluminum foil

## Steps
1. Place 50g white sugar and 20g water in the wok, heat over low heat. Do not stir throughout the process. Once the water evaporates and the sugar caramelizes naturally to a golden-yellow color, immediately add 30g freshly boiled hot water, stirring to combine into a caramel liquid. Thinly coat the bottom of the container with the caramel liquid and set aside to cool.
2. Pour 2 cartons of milk into the same wok used for caramel, heat until the edges just begin to simmer, then turn off the heat. In another bowl, crack 4 room-temperature eggs, add 16g white sugar, and whisk thoroughly. Slowly pour the hot milk into the egg mixture in batches while stirring constantly to prevent the eggs from curdling from the heat.
3. Strain the egg-milk mixture through a strainer once to remove lumps and foam, then pour into the container with the caramel. The larger the container's surface area, the longer the subsequent steaming time will need to be.
4. Seal the container's opening with aluminum foil or plastic wrap, then poke a few small holes with a toothpick for ventilation. Place the container in the steamer, and once the water is boiling, reduce to low heat and steam for 25 minutes.
5. After steaming, remove and let cool by immersing in cold water. Once cooled, run a small knife along the inner edge of the container, then invert onto a plate to unmold.

## Health Tips
This caramel pudding is rich in protein and calcium, making it suitable as an occasional dessert. However, it is high in sugar, so it is recommended to reduce the sugar or use a sugar substitute for the caramel. Steaming over low heat ensures a delicate and smooth texture, making it suitable for the elderly and children.
