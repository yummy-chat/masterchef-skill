# Garlic Soy Sauce  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons  

🍖 **Ingredients**  
- 2 cloves garlic  
- 5 g white sesame seeds  

🧂 **Seasonings**  
- 15 ml peanut oil  
- 30 ml soy sauce  

🍳 **Utensils**  
- 1 dipping sauce bowl  
- Pot  

📝 **Instructions**  
1. Crush the garlic cloves and set aside.  
2. Take a dipping sauce bowl and pour in the soy sauce.  
3. Heat peanut oil in a pot until hot, add the crushed garlic, and fry for about half a minute.  
4. After half a minute, turn off the heat, pour the hot oil along with the garlic into the dipping sauce bowl, and stir evenly with chopsticks.  

## Health Advice  
Garlic soy sauce uses hot oil to release the garlic aroma, enhancing the flavor of the seasoning, but the oil temperature should be controlled to avoid overheating, which may burn the garlic and create bitterness. This dipping sauce is high in sodium, so individuals with high blood pressure or those on a salt-restricted diet should use it sparingly. It can add flavor to plain ingredients but should not be consumed as a standalone main dish.
