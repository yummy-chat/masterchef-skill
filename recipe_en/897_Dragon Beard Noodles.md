# Dragon Beard Noodles  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons Meal  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🌾 Flour – appropriate amount  
💧 Cold water – appropriate amount  
🎃 Pumpkin – appropriate amount (for carving the dragon boat container)  
🧂 Salt – appropriate amount  

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment  
🧺 Flour sifter  
🔪 Carving knives (various types)  
🥣 Large bowl/basin  
🍽️ Cutting board  
🍳 Oil pan  
🥢 Chopsticks  
📌 Fine needle  

## Steps  
1. Sift the flour to make it fine in texture.  
2. Make a well in the center of the flour, add an appropriate amount of salt, and gradually pour in cold water, mixing into snowflake-like dough flakes.  
   **Note**: Salt enhances gluten strength.  
3. Knead the dough flakes into a smooth dough, combining pushing and kneading motions, then cover with a damp cloth and let rest for 15 minutes. During the resting period, take it out and knead repeatedly to fully develop the gluten.  
4. Take the pumpkin and use carving knives to carve out a dragon boat-shaped container, including the dragon's head, body, and tail, and assemble it into shape.  
   **Note**: Carving requires patience and care; even a slight mistake can ruin the work.  
5. "Slip the dough" on the rested dough: Hold both ends of the dough with both hands, apply even force, shake up and down, cross and switch positions to align the gluten strands. During the process, repeatedly dip in water to relax the gluten, then knead and slip again until the dough strip is smooth and pliable.  
6. On a floured cutting board, "stretch the dough strip": Hold both ends of the dough strip with both hands, pull and stretch, fold in half, and dust with flour to prevent sticking. Repeat the folding and stretching at least 13 times. Each fold doubles the number of strands, resulting in 8,192 strands as fine as dragon beard.  
   **Key**: Ensure the strands do not stick together. Ordinary noodles reach their limit at 10 folds, but Dragon Beard Noodles require at least 13 folds.  
7. Place the stretched ultra-fine noodles into a low-temperature oil pan, gently stir with chopsticks to prevent sticking, fry until set, then remove and drain the oil.  
   **Note**: The noodles are so fine they dissolve in water, so they must be fried to set. Fry slowly at a low temperature to avoid burning.  
8. Carefully arrange the fried Dragon Beard Noodles into the pumpkin dragon boat container and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This Dragon Beard Noodle dish is extremely difficult to make and is suitable for cooking enthusiasts who want to test their patience and hand strength. After frying, it is relatively high in calories. It is recommended to pair it with light vegetables for balanced nutrition and to control the serving size.
