# Beijing-Style Iron Griddle Roasted Lamb  
Cuisine: Beijing Cuisine  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥩 Lamb: about 500g (remove sinew, slice into moderately thick pieces)  
🧅 Scallion: 1 stalk (white part, cut into rolling knife chunks)

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
🔪 Chef’s knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Large bowl  
♨️ Griddle (cast iron grill pan)  
🥢 Chopsticks  

## Preparation Steps  
1.  Remove sinew from lamb and cut into evenly thick slices.  
2.  In a large bowl, mix salt, sugar, black pepper, and soy sauce, then toss with lamb slices until evenly coated.  
3.  Gradually add ginger juice water and mushroom water, kneading each time until the liquid is fully absorbed by the lamb.  
4.  Add a little fermented shrimp oil and sesame oil, mix gently, then sprinkle a tiny amount of cornstarch and toss well.  
**Note**: Traditional recipe uses no cornstarch; here a minimal amount is added to reduce liquid release during cooking.  
5.  Cut scallion into small rolling knife chunks; wash cilantro thoroughly, keeping roots and leaves intact.  
6.  Heat the griddle fully until very hot, then brush a very thin layer of cooking oil on the surface.  
7.  Place half the scallion pieces in the center of the griddle, then pile all marinated lamb on top.  
**Key**: Use the steam generated by the heated scallions underneath combined with the high heat of the griddle to quickly seal in moisture and cook the lamb.  
8.  When the lamb is slightly browned and oil appears at the bottom of the pan without excess liquid, add the remaining raw scallion pieces and all cilantro.  
9.  Toss quickly for about 10 seconds until the lamb is dry and the scallions and cilantro are just cooked, then remove from heat.  
**Key**: Ensure no liquid accumulates at the bottom of the pan when serving, to maintain a dry, aromatic texture.  

## Health Advice  
This griddle-roasted lamb dish is high in protein and fat, making it suitable for cold seasons or after heavy physical exertion. Lamb is warming in nature and may cause internal heat; pair with cold cucumber salad or green tea, and limit portion size to 150 grams per serving.
