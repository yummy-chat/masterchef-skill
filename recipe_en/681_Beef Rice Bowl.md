# Beef Rice Bowl
Cuisine: Common home-style dish  
Cooking difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Instructions  
1. **Prepare the vegetables**: Finely slice the onion, mince the garlic, and chop the scallions.  
   - Finely slicing the onion helps it soften and release fragrance quickly when stir-fried.  
2. **Make the sauce**: In a bowl, combine 3 tbsp light soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 1 tbsp ketchup, 2 tbsp honey, 1 bottle cap of Huadiao wine, 3 g sugar, 2 g salt,适量 black pepper, 5 g cornstarch, and 30 ml water. Stir until no dry powder remains. If a darker color is preferred, add a small amount of dark soy sauce.  
   - This is the key sauce; prepare it in advance for later use.  
3. **Blanch the beef**: Bring water to a boil in a pot, add half a bottle cap of Huadiao wine, then add the frozen beef rolls. Blanch until the beef slices just change color and are cooked through, then quickly remove and drain excess water.  
**Key point**: Remove immediately upon color change to keep the meat tender.  
4. **Pan-fry**: Heat a small amount of cooking oil in a wok over medium heat. Add the blanched beef slices and stir-fry until each slice is lightly browned on the surface. Then push the beef to one side of the wok.  
5. **Sauté aromatics**: Using the remaining oil in the wok, sauté the sliced onion and minced garlic until fragrant. Then stir-fry together with the beef.  
6. **Add sauce and simmer**: Pour the prepared sauce into the wok, stir well to combine, then reduce the heat to low. Cover with a lid and simmer for about 2 minutes, allowing the beef and onion to fully absorb the sauce.  
7. **Serve**: Remove the lid, add a few drops of sesame oil, sprinkle with chopped scallions, and turn off the heat. Serve the beef and sauce over rice.  
   - Adding sesame oil and scallions at the end preserves their aroma best.

## Health Tips  
Beef paired with onion and sauce provides a combination of protein and vegetables, making it suitable for quick energy replenishment in a fast-paced lifestyle. Note that beef rolls are relatively high in fat; it is recommended to pair this dish with stir-fried seasonal vegetables for balanced nutrition. For those watching oil intake, reduce the amount of cooking oil used in stir-frying.
