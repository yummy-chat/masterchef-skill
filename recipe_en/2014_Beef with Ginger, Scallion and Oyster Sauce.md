# Beef with Ginger, Scallion and Oyster Sauce
Cuisine: Cantonese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Beef 500g (sliced against the grain into pieces approximately 6cm × 3cm × 0.3cm)
🫚 Ginger, to taste (sliced)
🌶️ Red bell pepper, to taste (sliced)
🧅 Scallion, to taste (cut into sections)
🍚 Baking soda, about 1.5g
🍾 Light soy sauce, about 4.5g
💧 Water, to taste (to mix with light soy sauce for marinating)
🫒 Cooking oil (peanut oil), about 4.5g (for sealing the marinade) + additional as needed (for stir-frying)
🦪 Oyster sauce, about 1 tablespoon
🍬 Sugar, to taste
🍶 Wine, to taste (sprinkled in during stir-frying)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪵 Cutting board
🥣 Glass bowl
🥄 Spoon
🍳 Wok
🔥 Gas stove

## Cooking Steps
1. Cut the beef against the grain into slices approximately 6cm long, 3cm wide, and 0.3cm thick.
**Key point**: Must cut against the grain to break down the fibers; this is the first step to tenderness.
2. Prepare the marinade: Place the beef slices in a bowl, add baking soda and mix well. Separately, combine light soy sauce with an appropriate amount of water, pour it over the beef, and mix thoroughly to allow the beef to absorb the liquid.
**Key point**: Baking soda tenderizes; the sequential mixing of soy sauce and water is the core of achieving smooth, tender beef.
3. Drizzle about 4.5g of peanut oil over the marinated beef, mix to seal the surface, and let it marinate for half an hour.
**Key point**: Sealing with oil prevents oxidation, allowing the beef to absorb moisture and swell during resting, locking in juices.
4. Heat the wok over high heat, add an appropriate amount of oil, and add the ginger slices. Stir-fry over medium heat until the edges of the ginger are slightly curled and fragrant.
**Key point**: The ginger must be fully fragrant to enhance the flavor of the subsequent beef.
5. Turn the heat to high, add the marinated beef slices, and stir-fry quickly. When the beef starts to change color, sprinkle a small amount of wine along the edge of the wok to enhance aroma.
**Key point**: Stir-fry quickly over high heat with plenty of oil, controlling the time to about 1.5 minutes, no more than 2 minutes, to maintain tenderness.
6. Add the red bell pepper slices and stir-fry, then add about 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce and a little sugar, and stir-fry until evenly combined.
**Key point**: Adjust the amount of oyster sauce according to the quantity of beef; sugar is used to enhance freshness.
7. Add the scallion sections, stir-fry quickly for a few seconds until the scallions soften slightly but remain vibrant green, then immediately turn off the heat.
**Key point**: Add scallions last and remove from heat quickly; the total cooking time for the beef is about 3 minutes to prevent it from becoming tough.
8. Remove from the wok and plate.

## Health Advice
This dish uses baking soda and precise heat control to achieve tender beef, while ginger, scallion, and oyster sauce enhance aroma and freshness. It is suitable for fitness muscle-building or as a quick dish with rice. Control the salt during marinating, as oyster sauce and light soy sauce already contain saltiness; people with high blood pressure can reduce the amount appropriately.
