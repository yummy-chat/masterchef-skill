# Italian Bolognese Pasta  
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All-season meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍝 Italian pasta  
🥩 Ground meat  
🧅 White onion  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🍳 Pasta pot  
🍳 Wok or frying pan  
🥄 Spatula or stirring tool  
🥘 Colander or strainer  

## Cooking Steps  
1. Fill a pot with plenty of water, bring to a rolling boil over high heat, then add the Italian pasta and cook for 6-12 minutes according to the package instructions until it reaches the desired firmness.  
2. While the pasta is cooking, dice the white onion into small cubes.  
3. In a separate wok or frying pan, add oil and heat over medium heat, then add the diced onion and stir-fry.  
4. Continuously stir to prevent the onion from burning, and cook until the onion becomes translucent.  
5. Add the ground meat, continue to stir and break it apart, until the meat is fully cooked and turns brown.  
6. Add the Bolognese sauce, and stir briefly to combine.  
7. Drain the cooked pasta using a colander, then either toss it into the meat sauce and mix well, or pour the meat sauce over the pasta before serving.  

## Health Tips  
Italian Bolognese pasta is rich in protein and carbohydrates, making it a good choice for quick energy replenishment after exercise. However, the sauce tends to be high in sodium, so it is recommended to pair it with a vegetable salad to balance nutrition. For those on a weight-loss plan, opt for whole-wheat pasta and reduce the amount of sauce used.
