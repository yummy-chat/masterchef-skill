# Shrimp and Pork Potstickers
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥩 Ground pork, to taste  
🥚 1 egg  
🍤 Shrimp, about 16-20 (shelled)  
🥟 Wonton or dumpling wrappers, as needed  
🧅 Scallions, to taste (chopped, for garnish)  
🧂 Salt, to taste  
🌶️ White pepper powder, a pinch  
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed (for greasing the pan)  
💧 Water and cornstarch slurry, as needed (cornstarch mixed with water)  
🖤 Black sesame seeds, a pinch

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
⚙️ Meat grinder (for preparing the ground pork)  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🍲 Pan lid

## Steps
1.  Place the ground pork in a bowl, crack in the egg, add salt and white pepper powder. Using chopsticks, stir vigorously in one direction until the mixture becomes sticky and clumps together.
2.  Take one dumpling wrapper and lay it flat in your palm. Place an appropriate amount of the meat filling in the center, then arrange two shrimp side by side on top. Fold the wrapper in half and pinch the center and both ends firmly, leaving the two ends of the shrimp slightly exposed.
    **Note**: The shrimp should remain whole and centered to achieve a "gold and jade" effect.
3.  Preheat the non-stick frying pan and brush a thin layer of oil. Arrange the prepared dumplings neatly in the pan. Cook over low heat until the bottoms are set and light golden brown.
4.  Pour in the prepared cornstarch slurry, enough to cover about 1/3 of the dumplings' height. Cover with the lid, turn the heat to medium-low, and steam-cook until the filling is cooked through and the liquid has evaporated, forming a thin, crispy bottom.
5.  When all the liquid in the pan has completely evaporated and you hear a sizzling sound, with the bottoms golden and crispy, sprinkle with chopped scallions and black sesame seeds. Turn off the heat and transfer to a serving plate.

## Health Tips
Shrimp and pork provide high-quality protein, paired with the crispy starch base, making this dish suitable for those needing an energy boost. It is recommended to control the serving size of potstickers to avoid excessive fat intake.
