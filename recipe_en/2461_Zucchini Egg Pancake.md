# Zucchini Egg Pancake  
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥒 1 zucchini (about 250g, shredded and squeezed dry)  
🥕 Carrot, to taste (optional, shredded or finely diced)  
🧅 Onion/shallot, a little (minced)  
🌾 50g flour  
🥚 1 egg  
🧀 Parmesan cheese, to taste (optional, grated)  
🧂 Salt, a pinch  
🌿 Black pepper, to taste  
🫒 Olive oil (or other vegetable oil), to taste  

**All-Purpose Yogurt Sauce (optional)**  
🥛 Plain yogurt, to taste  
🧄 Minced garlic, a little  
🧂 Salt and black pepper, a pinch  
🫒 Olive oil, a little  
🌿 Chopped scallions, a little  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
🥄 Grater  
🥣 Glass bowl  
🔪 Chef's knife and cutting board  
🍴 Fork/spoon  
🍳 Frying pan  
🪢 Spatula  
🧀 Grater (for cheese)  
🥄 Small wooden bowl and spoon  

## Preparation Steps  
1. Shred the zucchini into thin strips, sprinkle with a pinch of salt, mix well, and let sit for 10 minutes to release moisture.  
2. Mince the onion or shallot; shred or finely dice the carrot. In a pan, add a little oil and briefly sauté the shredded carrot to improve texture.  
3. Squeeze the salted zucchini strips dry by hand or with cheesecloth. This step is **key** to the final texture.  
4. Prepare the dipping sauce: In a bowl, combine plain yogurt, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, a little olive oil, and chopped scallions, and mix well to make the all-purpose yogurt sauce.  
5. Mix the batter: In a large bowl, combine the squeezed-dry zucchini, minced onion, and sautéed shredded carrot. Add 50g flour, 1 egg, salt, black pepper, and optional grated Parmesan cheese. Stir until a lump-free batter forms.  
6. Fry: Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat and add enough olive oil. Reduce to **medium-low heat**. Scoop the batter into the pan and gently flatten into round pancakes with a spatula.  
7. Fry until one side is golden brown, then carefully flip. Continue frying over medium-low heat patiently, flipping a few times, until both sides are **golden brown and crispy on the surface**.  
8. Remove from the pan and serve with the yogurt sauce or other condiments.  

## Health Tips  
This zucchini egg pancake replaces part of the flour with vegetables, offering lower calories and rich dietary fiber. It is suitable for those watching their weight or who love vegetables. Pairing it with yogurt sauce adds protein and flavor, but be mindful of the oil amount when frying to avoid excess absorption.
