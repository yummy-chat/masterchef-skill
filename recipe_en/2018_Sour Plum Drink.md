# Sour Plum Drink
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ★★★★
Seasonal Recommendation: ☀️ Summer (April–August)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🫒 Black Dates
🍒 Smoked Plums
🍂 Hawthorn Slices (raw)
🍬 Yellow Rock Sugar
🌿 Licorice
🍊 Dried Tangerine Peel
🌰 Red Cardamom
🌼 Dried Osmanthus Flowers
🍬 Yellow Rock Sugar

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🥣 Basin
🍲 Pot
🥄 Stirring Tool
🧊 Refrigerator

## Procedure
1. Rinse the black dates, smoked plums, hawthorn slices, licorice, dried tangerine peel, and red cardamom with clean water. Place them in 1.5 liters of water and soak at room temperature for at least two hours.
2. Heat over medium-high heat until boiling. Cover, reduce to low heat, and simmer gently for 40 minutes. This is the first decoction.
3. Place the yellow rock sugar in a basin. Strain the first decoction into the basin while hot, and stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
4. Return the strained herbs to the pot. Add 600 milliliters of water, bring to a boil over high heat, then cover and cook over medium heat for 20 minutes. This is the second decoction.
5. Combine the second decoction with the rock sugar mixture while hot and mix well. When the temperature drops to 60–70°C, add the dried osmanthus flowers. Cover and let it cool naturally, then refrigerate for at least three hours.
6. Strain out the osmanthus flowers before drinking, and sip slowly.

## Health Advice
Sour Plum Drink is made by simmering ingredients like smoked plums and hawthorn, which aid digestion and promote fluid production. Consumed in summer, it can invigorate the spleen, stimulate appetite, relieve heat, and cool the body, especially suitable for those with poor appetite or after meals to aid digestion. Note that the amount of yellow rock sugar can be adjusted to taste; people with diabetes or those controlling sugar intake should reduce the quantity.
