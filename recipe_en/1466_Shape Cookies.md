# Shape Cookies
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍪 Basic cookie dough, as needed (made from flour, butter, sugar, etc. Specific recipe and quantities not shown in the video)  

## Kitchenware & Equipment  
🖨️ 3D printer  
💻 Computer and 3D modeling software  
🍳 Baking sheet  

## Steps  
### Preparation Phase  
1. Choose a favorite design and obtain the corresponding 2D or 3D graphic file.  
2. Use computer modeling software to convert the design into a mold model suitable for 3D printing.  
3. Use the 3D printer to print the designed cookie mold.  
4. Prepare or make the cookie dough.  

### Production Phase  
1. Roll the cookie dough into a sheet of even thickness, then press out shapes using the custom 3D-printed mold.  
   **Key Point**: The pressing process is simple and suitable for beginners.  
2. Place the shaped raw cookie pieces in a preheated oven and bake.  
   **Note**: The video does not provide baking temperature or time; adjust according to the recipe for the cookie dough being used.  

## Health Suggestions  
When making cookies with 3D-printed molds, control the ratio of sugar and fat. You can use low-sugar recipes or reduce the amount of butter. This is suitable for parent-child interaction. Ensure the 3D-printed mold is made of food-grade material and avoid high-temperature deformation.
