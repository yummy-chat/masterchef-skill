# Crispy Banana
Cuisine Type: Common Home Cooking
Difficulty Level: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍌 Bananas: Several (peeled, cut into diagonal chunks)
🌾 Cornstarch: To taste
🥣 Batter: To taste (flour mixture, see previous video for recipe, pre-mixed)

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment
🔪 Chef's knife
🪓 Cutting board
🍽️ Plate
🥣 Bowl
🍳 Deep fryer
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥢 Chopsticks

## Instructions
1. Peel the bananas and cut them into evenly sized diagonal chunks to ensure even heating.
2. Place the cut banana chunks in a plate, sprinkle with an appropriate amount of cornstarch, and toss with your hands until each piece is evenly coated with a thin layer of powder.
3. Dip the coated banana chunks one by one into the pre-mixed batter, ensuring the batter evenly covers the surface.
   **Note**: The batter should have a moderate consistency; too thick or too thin a coating will affect the final crispiness.
4. Heat oil in a pot to the proper temperature. Add the batter-coated banana chunks one by one, frying until the surface is set and slightly golden. Remove and drain the oil.
   **Key**: The first fry is mainly to set the coating; the oil temperature needs to be high enough to quickly seal the outer layer.
5. Increase the oil temperature, then return the set banana chunks to the pot for a second fry until they turn an even golden color.
   **Judgment**: When the color turns golden and the pieces make a crisp sound when colliding, they are perfectly crispy.
6. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain the oil thoroughly. The finished product has a crispy outer shell, while the inside is soft and mushy like banana puree, with a rich aroma.

## Health Tips
Bananas are high in potassium and low in sodium, which helps stabilize blood pressure, but after frying, the calorie content is higher. It is recommended to pair with green tea to cut the grease. Diabetic patients should be mindful that under the crispy coating, there is natural fruit sugar and carbohydrates, so consumption should be controlled.
