# Shredded Chicken Cold Noodles with King Oyster Mushrooms
Cuisine: Sichuan
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍗 Chicken breast 1 piece
🍄 King oyster mushroom 1 piece
🫑 Green bell pepper appropriate amount (julienned)
🥕 Carrot appropriate amount (julienned)
🥜 Peanut butter 1 tablespoon
🫘 Sesame paste 1 tablespoon
🥜 Cashew nuts appropriate amount
🌶️ Chili flakes (coarse and fine) appropriate amount
🫒 Sesame oil appropriate amount
🫗 Hot oil appropriate amount
🥤 Sprite appropriate amount
🦪 Oyster sauce 1 tablespoon
🧂 Salt a pinch
🥡 Light soy sauce 1 tablespoon
🥡 Dark soy sauce or braised soy sauce 1 tablespoon
🧂 Chicken bouillon appropriate amount
🤍 White sesame seeds appropriate amount

## Kitchen Tools & Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife
🪓 Cutting board
🍳 Wok
🥣 Soup pot / Frying pan
🥄 Slotted spoon
🥣 Stainless steel bowl
🥢 Rolling pin
🫙 Glass bowl
🥄 Wooden spoon

## Preparation Steps
1. Slice the chicken breast in half horizontally, then lightly pound it with the back of a knife to loosen the fibers.
2. Place the chicken breast in cold water, add cooking wine and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, skim off the foam, cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes.
3. Remove the chicken breast and immediately immerse it in ice water or cold water to cool quickly. Lightly pound it with a rolling pin to loosen, then tear into fine shreds.
4. In a bowl, combine the coarse and fine chili flakes, then drizzle in sesame oil to moisten. Pour hot oil into the chili flakes in three batches, stirring well after each addition.
5. Continue by adding Sprite, oyster sauce, salt, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sesame paste, peanut butter, and chicken bouillon to the bowl. Stir in a clockwise direction until fully combined. Finally, sprinkle with white sesame seeds and cashew nuts.
**Note**: You may choose either sesame paste or peanut butter, or mix both; the Sprite adds a mild sweetness and thins the sauce.
6. Trim the root end of the king oyster mushroom, slice it thinly, then cut into fine shreds. Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the mushroom shreds, and cook for 1 minute after the water returns to a boil. Remove and immerse in cold water, drain, and transfer to a bowl.
**Note**: Cut the shreds as finely as possible to mimic the shape of noodles; do not overcook—keep them smooth and tender.
7. Pour the prepared sauce over the mushroom shreds, then arrange the bell pepper shreds, carrot shreds, and chicken shreds on top. Serve.

## Health Advice
This dish replaces traditional noodles with king oyster mushrooms, reducing calories and carbohydrate content, making it suitable for a weight-loss diet. The chicken breast, combined with nut-based sauces, provides high-quality protein and healthy fats. Note that the combination of Sprite and sesame paste may increase the calorie count, so moderate consumption is recommended.
