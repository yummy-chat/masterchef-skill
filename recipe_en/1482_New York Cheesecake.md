# New York Cheesecake

Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All-year-round  

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🍪 Digestive biscuits 80g (can be substituted with Oreo)  
🧈 Unsalted butter 20g (melted before use)  
🧀 Cream cheese 300g  
🥛 Plain yogurt 200g  
🍬 White sugar 45g  
🥚 Eggs 2 (added one at a time)  
🥛 Whipping cream 55g  
🍋 Lemon juice 10g  
🌿 Vanilla pod 1/4 (or vanilla extract)  
🌽 Corn starch 15g (must be sifted)  

## Cookware & Equipment
🫙 Sealed bag  
🥖 Rolling pin  
🥣 Large bowl  
🥄 Spatula  
🥚 Hand whisk  
🍰 6-inch springform cake pan  
📄 Parchment paper / baking paper  
🔥 Baking tray  
🪶 Aluminum foil  
📻 Microwave / stove (for melting butter)  
🔥 Oven  
🧱 Cooling rack  
🔪 Knife  

## Instructions  
1. Place the digestive biscuits in a sealed bag and crush them into fine crumbs using a rolling pin. Transfer to a bowl, add melted butter, and mix well with a spatula. Cut parchment paper to fit the bottom and sides of the cake pan, then line the pan. Pour the biscuit crumbs into the pan, press firmly with a flat tool, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.  

2. In a large bowl, add room-temperature softened cream cheese, yogurt, and white sugar. Beat with a hand whisk until smooth and free of lumps. Add eggs one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition until the batter is smooth. Stir in vanilla seeds or vanilla extract and lemon juice. Sift in the corn starch, mix until no dry flour remains, then pour in the whipping cream and stir until the batter is smooth and even.  

3. Remove the chilled crust from the refrigerator and pour in the cheese batter. **Note**: Wrap the outside of the springform pan tightly with aluminum foil to prevent water from seeping in. Fill the baking tray with hot water to about one-third of its height, then place the pan in the tray. Preheat the oven to 160°C and bake for 50–60 minutes. **Doneness check**: The surface should be golden brown, and the center should jiggle slightly when the pan is gently shaken.  

4. Once baked, remove the pan from the oven and let it cool completely on a cooling rack. Wrap with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.  

5. After thorough chilling, unmold the cake. Slice with a preheated knife and serve.  

## Health Advice  
This New York cheesecake is rich in protein and calcium, but also high in sugar and calories. It is recommended for occasional enjoyment. Pair with black coffee or plain tea to balance the sweetness. Keep each serving to about 80g or less to avoid blood sugar spikes.
