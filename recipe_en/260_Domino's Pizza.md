# Domino's Pizza  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All-season meal  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
### Dough Ingredients  
🍞 High-gluten flour 300g (one full cup plus four notches)  
🧂 Salt 3g (one capful, inner ring)  
💧 Water 210g (7 notches)  
🧈 Butter 10g (one piece)  
🍬 Sugar 20g (4 capfuls)  
🫙 Yeast 3g (two-thirds capful)  

### Meat Sauce Ingredients  
🥩 Ground meat 250-500g (pork or beef)  
🧅 Onion 1 (diced)  
🍅 Tomato 2 (peeled and chopped)  
🧄 Minced garlic, to taste  
🧀 Mozzarella cheese, to taste (must cover with a thick layer)  
🌭 Sausage, to taste (optional, sliced)  
🫑 Bell peppers, to taste (optional, cut into rings)  

### Meat Sauce Seasonings  
🍬 Sugar 1 tablespoon  
🧂 Black pepper or white pepper, to taste  
🦪 Oyster sauce 1 tablespoon  
🥫 Ketchup or Xinjiang tomato canned 3 tablespoons (Xinjiang tomato canned is the "soul," offering better flavor)  
🥛 Whole milk 2 tablespoons  
🧂 Song Xian Xian (or chicken bouillon), to taste  
🌿 Rosemary or pizza herb, to taste (optional, can be omitted)  

## Kitchen Utensils and Equipment  
🫖 Stainless steel bowl (for mixing dough and fermenting)  
🥢 Rolling pin (for rolling out the dough base)  
🔲 Cutting board (for handling dough, dust with dry flour to prevent sticking)  
🍳 Wok (for cooking meat sauce)  
🔥 Oven (for baking pizza)  
♨️ Air fryer (alternative to oven)  
🪨 Large iron pot with stone slab (backup alternative)  
📄 Baking paper (to line the baking tray to prevent sticking)  
🍴 Fork (for poking holes to prevent rising)  
🖌️ Brush (for spreading sauce, can substitute with a spatula)  

## Preparation Steps  
### Making the Meat Sauce  
1. Heat the pan, melt the butter, then add minced garlic and diced onion, stir-frying until fragrant.  
2. Sprinkle in a little salt, add the ground meat, and stir-fry until it changes color.  
3. Add sugar, diced tomatoes, black pepper, ketchup, and oyster sauce in order, continuing to stir-fry.  
4. Finally, pour in whole milk, Song Xian Xian, and herbs, then turn to medium-high heat and reduce the sauce until thick.  
   **Key point**: Xinjiang tomato canned can significantly enhance the flavor. The cooked meat sauce can be used directly on pizza or mixed with noodles.  

### Dough Preparation and Fermentation  
1. Mix all dough ingredients, stirring until no dry flour remains, then add butter and stir again until no dry flour remains.  
   **Key point**: No kneading required; just mix evenly until no dry flour remains.  
2. Cover with a lid or plastic wrap and let rest for 15 minutes.  
3. Fold the dough from all sides toward the center in one fold, cover, and let rest for another 15 minutes. Repeat this folding and resting process 2–3 times.  
   **State check**: The dough changes from sticky to smooth and elastic, capable of stretching into a longer membrane.  
   **Key point**: Use folding instead of kneading to strengthen the gluten network.  
4. Place the folded dough in a bowl, cover, and let ferment in a warm place.  
   **State check**: Once the volume has noticeably increased and the interior shows a dense honeycomb structure, it's ready.  
   **Key point**: Insufficient fermentation will result in a hard dough base.  

### Shaping and Assembly  
1. Dust the cutting board generously with dry flour, take out the fermented dough, and divide it evenly into two portions.  
2. Press, pat, or use a rolling pin to shape each portion into a round dough base with a thin center and thicker edges.  
   **Key point**: The method is flexible; the aim is to release some gas and set the shape.  
3. Transfer the dough base to a baking tray lined with baking paper.  
4. Use a fork to evenly poke holes in the center of the dough base.  
   **Key point**: Poking holes prevents the dough base from puffing up during baking.  
5. Spread a thick layer of meat sauce, then a thick layer of mozzarella cheese.  
6. Add sausage slices, bell pepper rings, etc., according to personal preference, and cover with another layer of cheese.  
   **Key point**: Generous toppings are a hallmark of the homemade version.  

### Baking  
- **Oven**: Preheat to 230°C top heat, 220°C bottom heat, bake for 15 minutes.  
- **Air fryer**: 220°C, bake for 18 minutes.  
- **Large iron pot alternative**: Place a stone slab in the pot, set the pizza on the slab, and cook over high heat.  
**Key point**: Home ovens need to be fully preheated to reach high temperatures.  

### Finishing  
Bake until the cheese is fully melted, the surface is golden, and the edges are puffed up, then remove and enjoy.  

## Health Advice  
This pizza base omits the kneading step, but holes must be poked in the base before baking to prevent puffing. Cheese is rich in calcium and protein but also high in calories, making it suitable for sharing at gatherings; portion control is advised. The canned tomatoes provide antioxidants like lycopene, which are key to the flavor.
