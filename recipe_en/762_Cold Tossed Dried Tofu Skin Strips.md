# Cold Tossed Dried Tofu Skin Strips
Cuisine: Northeast Chinese
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🫘 Dried tofu skin, as needed (cut into chopstick-thick strips)
🥕 Carrot, as needed (cut into thin strips)
🌿 Cilantro, as needed (cut into sections)
🥜 Fried peanuts, as needed
⭐ Star anise, 1 piece
🧅 Scallion, as needed (cut into sections)
🫚 Ginger, as needed (sliced)
🌶️ Dried chili peppers, as needed (cut into sections)
🧄 Garlic, as needed (part minced for stir-frying, part minced for final oil pouring)
🧴 Light soy sauce, half a tablespoon
🧂 Salt, as needed
🧂 MSG, as needed
🦪 Oyster sauce, as needed
🍂 Five-spice powder, as needed
🌶️ Chili flakes, 1 tablespoon
🫒 Cooking oil, as needed

## Cookware and Equipment
🍳 Wok
🥄 Slotted spoon
🔪 Chef's knife
🪓 Cutting board
🥣 Bowl/Plate

## Preparation Steps
1. Stack the dried tofu skin and cut into chopstick-thick strips; cut the carrot into thin strips, cut the cilantro into sections, and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a wok to about 50% hot, add the dried tofu skin strips and fry for about 3 minutes, until the surface forms small tiger-skin patterns and is set, then remove and drain oil.
**Note**: Keep the heat low throughout to prevent the spices from burning.
3. Leave a small amount of base oil in the wok, reduce to low heat, add star anise, scallion sections, ginger slices, and dried chili pepper sections, and slowly fry until fragrant.
**Note**: Keep the heat low throughout to prevent the spices from burning.
4. Add half a tablespoon of light soy sauce to release the sauce aroma, then add an appropriate amount of water.
5. Season the water with salt, MSG, oyster sauce, and five-spice powder, stir to combine and bring to a boil. Add the fried dried tofu skin strips, cover the wok, reduce to the lowest heat, and braise for 7-8 minutes, allowing them to fully absorb the sauce.
6. Remove the braised dried tofu skin strips and place in a bowl. Top with 1 tablespoon of chili flakes, then place the raw minced garlic on top of the chili flakes.
**Key point**: The minced garlic must be placed on top of the chili flakes to prevent the chili flakes from burning due to high oil temperature when pouring hot oil.
7. Ladle a spoonful of hot oil over the minced garlic and chili flakes to release the fragrance.
8. Add the carrot strips, cilantro sections, and fried peanuts. Use your hands to thoroughly toss and mix all ingredients with the seasoning, then plate and serve.

## Health Advice
Dried tofu skin is rich in plant protein, making it suitable as a high-protein side dish for fitness or weight-loss individuals. However, after frying and adding peanuts, the calorie content increases, so it is recommended to pair it with a light main dish. Carrots and cilantro provide vitamins and dietary fiber, but pay attention to controlling the amount of oil used.
