# Crispy Egg Bread
Cuisine: Common Home-Style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍞 400 grams high-gluten flour  
🥚 4-5 eggs (beaten into egg liquid)  
🍬 30 grams white sugar  
🫒 20 grams corn oil  
🌾 4 grams yeast  
⚪ White sesame seeds, as needed (for surface decoration)  
💧 Water, as needed (for adhering the sesame seeds)

## Kitchenware and Equipment
🥣 Glass bowl  
🫙 Plastic wrap  
🔪 Cutting board and knife  
🍳 Non-stick pan  
🥢 Chopsticks  
🧺 Strainer or wire rack (for draining oil)

## Preparation Steps
1. In a glass bowl, add the high-gluten flour, yeast, and white sugar. Pour in the egg liquid and corn oil. First, stir with chopsticks until it becomes flocculent, then knead into a smooth and soft dough.
2. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it ferment at room temperature for about 30 minutes, until it reaches a semi-fermented state.
3. Transfer the dough to a cutting board. Knead to release the air. Roll it into a long strip and divide it into 6-7 equal-sized pieces.
4. Knead each piece until the surface is smooth, and shape it into an oval. Brush a little water on the surface, roll it in white sesame seeds, and gently press to secure.
5. Let the shaped bread dough rest and proof for about 30 minutes, until it significantly increases in volume and feels lighter to the touch.
6. Pour enough oil into a pan and heat it to about 30-40% full (low heat). Gently place the bread dough into the oil. Keep the heat on medium-low and fry slowly.
   **Key Point**: The oil temperature should not be too high to avoid the exterior burning before the interior is cooked through.
7. Fry until the bottom side is set and a light golden brown, then flip. Continuously turn the pieces during frying to ensure even heating. Fry for about 10 minutes until both sides are golden brown. Remove and drain the oil.

## Health Advice
This crispy egg bread is rich in carbohydrates and protein, making it suitable for replenishing energy after exercise. However, since it is fried, it is relatively high in calories. It is recommended to pair it with vegetables or tea to balance the greasiness. Those with weak digestion or those monitoring their sugar intake should consume it in smaller portions.
