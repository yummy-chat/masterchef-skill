# Tomato Shrimp Pasta  
Cuisine: Common Home Cooking  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons  

## Ingredients & Seasonings  
🍝 Pasta, as needed  
🍤 Shrimp, as needed  
🧅 Onion, about half (sliced)  
🍅 Yellow/red cherry tomatoes, about 10 (halved)  
🥦 Broccoli, 1 small head (cut into small florets)  
🌱 Snow peas, about 10 pieces  
🧀 Cheese slice, 1 slice  

🧂 Pure tomato paste, about 15g  
🧂 Ketchup, as needed  
🧂 Salt, as needed  
🧂 Garlic black pepper sauce, about 1 tablespoon  
🧄 Minced garlic, about 2 cloves  
🫒 Olive oil, as needed  
💧 Water, as needed  

## Cookware & Equipment  
🍳 Non-stick frying pan  
🥣 Pot for boiling  
🥣 Bowl  
✂️ Scissors  
♨️ Microwave  
🥢 Tongs  
🥄 Spoon  

## Instructions  
1. **Prepare the sauce in advance**: In a bowl, mix pure tomato paste, ketchup, a pinch of salt, and water. Stir evenly and set aside.  
   **Note**: Pre-mixing the sauce prevents scrambling during cooking.  

2. **Prepare vegetables**: Slice the onion, halve the cherry tomatoes; cut the broccoli into small florets, remove strings from snow peas, place them in a bowl, and mix with a pinch of salt.  

3. **Cook the pasta**: Bring water to a boil in a pot, add the pasta, and stir to separate. When the water returns to a gentle boil, turn off the heat, cover, and let it sit for 10 minutes.  
   **Note**: The simmering method saves energy; no need to rinse the pasta with cold water after cooking.  

4. **Microwave vegetables**: Place the salted broccoli and snow peas in the microwave and heat on high for about 5 minutes until tender. Remove, mix in the garlic black pepper sauce, then microwave on high for another 1 minute.  

5. **Pan-fry the shrimp**: Pour olive oil into a frying pan, cook the shrimp until they change color and curl, then remove and set aside.  

6. **Sauté the base**: Leave a little oil in the pan, sauté minced garlic over low heat until fragrant, then add the onion slices and cook until softened. Next, add the cherry tomatoes and cook until they release some juice.  

7. **Add the sauce**: Pour in the prepared tomato sauce, bring to a boil, and stir evenly.  

8. **Combine the pasta**: Scoop the cooked pasta directly into the sauce pan, add the pan-fried shrimp, and quickly toss with tongs until each strand is evenly coated with the sauce.  

9. **Serve and melt cheese**: Plate the pasta, place a slice of cheese on top while it's still hot, allowing the residual heat to melt it naturally. Serve alongside the microwaved vegetables.  

## Health Tips  
This tomato shrimp pasta is paired with broccoli and snow peas for abundant vegetables, while shrimp provides high-quality protein. It suits those on a weight-loss journey or aiming for balanced nutrition. Note that the cheese slice is relatively high in calories and may be reduced or omitted as desired.
