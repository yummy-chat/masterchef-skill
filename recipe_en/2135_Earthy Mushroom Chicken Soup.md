# Earthy Mushroom Chicken Soup
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍗 Chicken Thighs, appropriate amount (about one pack)  
🍄 Earthy Mushrooms, half a jin (approx. 250g)  
🥩 Pork Belly, one strip  
🥕 Carrots, 2  
🍆 Eggplants, 2-3  
🥚 Eggs, 2  
🧅 Scallions, appropriate amount  
🐟 Small Fish, one  
🦀 Small Crabs, 30  
🧂 Soy Sauce, appropriate amount  
🧂 Salt, appropriate amount

## Utensils and Equipment
🔪 Chef's Knife, Cutting Board  
🚰 Sink  
🍳 Wok  
🫕 Stewing Pot (Clay Pot)  
🥄 Strainer  
🫗 Oil Pan  
🍽️ Plates, Bowls  
🥄 Soup Spoon

## Steps
1. **Prepare the main ingredients**: Wash the chicken thighs. Clean the earthy mushrooms and trim off the roots. Cut the pork belly into small pieces. Slice the carrots and eggplants. Clean the small crabs and small fish. Beat the eggs, and chop the scallions into small pieces.  
   **Key point**: Wash the mushrooms thoroughly; place the cut ingredients separately by type.
2. **Blanch the chicken thighs**: Put the chicken thighs in cold water and bring to a boil. Skim off any foam, then remove the chicken.
3. **Simmer the soup**: Place the blanched chicken thighs and cleaned earthy mushrooms into the stewing pot. Add enough water and simmer over low heat.  
   **Key point**: Keep the liquid at a gentle simmer without a full boil. Cook until the soup is rich in color and the chicken is tender.
4. **Prepare the side dishes**: Blanch the carrot slices in boiling water, then remove. Heat a wok with a little oil, stir-fry the pork belly until it renders fat and turns slightly yellow. Add the carrot and eggplant slices, stir-fry. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble. Finally, add soy sauce to taste and stir evenly.  
   **Key point**: Render the fat from the pork belly fully before adding the vegetables; stir-fry over medium-high heat throughout.
5. **Deep-fry**: Pour oil into a pan and heat to medium-high. Deep-fry the small crabs and small fish until golden and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.  
   **Key point**: Control the oil temperature to prevent burning.
6. **Serve**: Plate the soup, stir-fried dishes, and deep-fried items separately. Sprinkle the fried items with chopped scallions as garnish.  
   **Key point**: Serve in separate dishes to maintain individual temperatures and textures.

## Health Advice
This dish is rich in protein and mushroom fiber. However, the small crabs and small fish are deep-fried, which increases calorie content. It is recommended to consume them in moderation, making this ideal for sharing during gatherings. The chicken soup itself is light; it is advisable to drink more soup and eat fewer fried items to balance nutrition. Note that pork belly and eggplant absorb a significant amount of oil, so it is best not to eat this dish daily.
