# Cucumber and Century Egg Salad
Cuisine: Sichuan  
Difficulty: ⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: All seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🥒 1 cucumber (about 300g, partially peeled, crushed with a knife, then cut into chunks)  
🥚 5 yellow-skinned century eggs (washed with shells on, peeled in water, then cut into wedges)  
🫚 1 small piece ginger (minced)  
🧄 2 cloves garlic (minced)  
🌶️ 2 small red chili peppers (minced)  
🌿 Cilantro to taste (cut into sections)  
🥜 Fried peanuts to taste (optional)  
🧂 About 3g salt  
🧂 MSG to taste  
🍬 About 5g sugar  
🥄 About 4g light soy sauce  
🍶 About 3g rice vinegar  
🌶️ Spicy chili oil to taste  

## Utensils and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife  
🪓 Cutting board  
🥣 Stainless steel bowl (for washing, mixing dressing, and tossing salad)

## Instructions
1. Wash the cucumber and century eggs (with shells on) in clean water.  
**Key point**: Century eggs must be washed with shells on to prevent dirt from contaminating the inside when peeled.
2. Partially peel the cucumber by leaving some strips of skin, crush it with the back of a knife, then cut into small chunks.  
**Key point**: Peeling reduces bitterness; crushing helps the cucumber absorb flavor better.
3. Tap the century eggs to crack the shells, then peel them in water. Cut each egg into four even wedges.  
**Key point**: Peeling in water keeps the eggs intact and less likely to break; wet the knife beforehand to prevent sticking.
4. Mince the ginger, garlic, and small red chili peppers. Cut the cilantro into sections.
5. In an empty bowl, add salt, MSG, sugar, light soy sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir until the granules are dissolved.  
**Key point**: Always mix liquid and powdered seasonings first before adding oily seasonings.
6. Add spicy chili oil and stir again.
7. Add the minced ginger, minced garlic, minced chili peppers, and fried peanuts. Stir to combine.
8. Add the century egg wedges and cucumber chunks to the bowl, then add the cilantro sections last. Toss thoroughly until all ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing. Plate and serve.

## Health Notes
Cucumber and century egg salad is refreshing and appetizing, but century eggs are high in sodium. Those with high blood pressure should consume in moderation. Crushing the cucumber helps it absorb the dressing better. For a lower-calorie version during weight loss, reduce the amount of chili oil—it remains tangy, spicy, and low in calories.
