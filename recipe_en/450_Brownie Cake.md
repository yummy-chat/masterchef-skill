# Brownie Cake
Cuisine: Other
Difficulty: ⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for all seasons

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🧈 Butter 120g
🍫 Dark chocolate 130g
🥚 Eggs 3
🌾 Cake flour 100g
🍫 Cocoa powder 10g
🥜 Walnuts 80g
🍬 White sugar 150g

## Kitchenware & Equipment
🍳 Two pots (one large, one small, for double boiler)
🥣 Whisk
🥣 Glass bowl
🍴 Spatula
🔥 Oven
📦 Square baking pan (mold)
📄 Parchment paper
🔪 Long knife

## Instructions
1.  Fill a pot with water and bring to a boil. Place a smaller pot or container over the water. Add 120g butter and melt it using the double boiler method, stirring until completely melted.
2.  Remove from heat. Add 130g chocolate chunks to the melted butter. Stir using residual heat until completely melted, forming a smooth and silky chocolate mixture. Set aside.
3.  In a clean bowl, crack 3 eggs and add 150g white sugar. Whisk until the color is uniform. No need to whip to soft peaks.
4.  Sift 100g cake flour and 10g cocoa powder into the egg mixture. Stir using a zigzag or cutting motion to avoid overworking the gluten (do not stir in circles). Mix until no dry flour remains, forming a smooth and thick batter.
5.  Pour the slightly cooled chocolate-butter mixture into the batter. Fold together evenly with a spatula.
6.  Add 80g walnuts and continue folding until evenly distributed. The final batter should be smooth, silky, and thick.
7.  Pour the batter into a square baking pan lined with parchment paper. Smooth the surface with a spatula. If using no parchment paper, evenly brush the inside of the pan with a thin layer of cooking oil to prevent sticking.
8.  Preheat the oven to 180°C for 10 minutes. Place the pan on the middle rack and bake at 180°C (top and bottom heat) for 18 minutes. During the last 3 minutes, keep an eye on it. The cake is done when the surface is dry and no longer sticky to the touch.
9.  Once baked, remove from the oven. Let cool slightly. Use the parchment paper to lift the cake out of the pan. Place on a cutting board and cut into desired shapes.

## Health Advice
Brownies are rich in antioxidant dark chocolate and are suitable for a quick energy boost after exercise. However, they are high in sugar and fat, so it is recommended to cut into small pieces and enjoy them with unsweetened yogurt to balance the flavor.
