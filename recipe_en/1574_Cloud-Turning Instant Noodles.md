# Cloud-Turning Instant Noodles
Cuisine: Common Home-style Dish
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Suitable for All Seasons

## Ingredients and Seasonings
🍅 2 tomatoes (score a cross on top, blanch in boiling water, peel, and dice finely)
🥚 2 eggs (beat with a pinch of salt into egg wash)
🧅 Some onion (finely minced)
🌭 2 starch sausages (coat the surface with a layer of starch)
🍜 2 packs of instant noodles (noodle cakes)
🌊 Some water starch (relatively thick)
🧂 A little salt (used separately for the egg wash and broth base)
🌶️ 2 tablespoons of chili bean paste with red oil
🍅 Some ketchup (for color adjustment)
🥬 Some kimchi
🧴 Some dark soy sauce (for color adjustment)
🍬 A bit more sugar (to enhance freshness and balance acidity)
🧂 A little chicken bouillon

## Cookware and Equipment
🔪 Chef’s knife, cutting board
🥣 Bowls, small basin
🍳 Pan (non-stick pan)
🥄 Spatula, chopsticks
🥄 Soup ladle
🍲 Stockpot
🔥 Stove

## Preparation Steps
### Preparation Phase
1. Score a cross on the top of the tomatoes, place them in a bowl, pour boiling water over them for 30 seconds, remove, peel, and finely dice.
2. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt into egg wash; finely mince the onion; evenly coat the surface of the starch sausages with a layer of starch.

### Frying the Starch Sausages
1. Heat the pan, pour in a generous amount of oil, add the starch-coated sausages, and fry over medium heat.
2. Turn frequently, fry until the surface is golden brown and crispy, then remove and set aside.

### Preparing the Broth Base
1. Leave a small amount of oil in the pan, add the minced onion and minced garlic, and stir-fry until fragrant.
2. Add two tablespoons of chili bean paste with red oil, and stir-fry until the red oil releases.
3. Add the diced tomatoes, stir-fry over medium heat until soft and mushy, forming a thick sauce.
4. Add ketchup for color adjustment, then add the kimchi and stir-fry evenly.
5. Pour in enough boiling water, season with salt, dark soy sauce, a bit more sugar, and chicken bouillon, then bring to a boil over high heat and cook for 3 minutes.
   **Note**: The amount of sugar can be slightly increased to balance the acidity of the tomatoes and kimchi.

### Thickening and Making Egg Flowers
1. Reduce the heat to low or turn off the heat, slowly drizzle in the thick water starch while stirring until the broth becomes thick and creamy.
2. **Turn off the heat**, drizzle the egg wash into the pot in a thin stream, gently stir with a spoon to break it up, and let the residual heat cook it into silky egg flowers.
   **Key**: The heat must be turned off before adding the egg wash to ensure the egg flowers are tender and smooth.

### Cooking the Noodles and Assembly
1. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil, add the two instant noodle cakes, and cook until done.
2. Remove the noodles and place them in a bowl, then pour the thick tomato and egg flower broth over them.
3. Slice the fried starch sausages and arrange them on top of the noodles.

## Health Advice
The thick tomato and egg flower broth, paired with crispy fried starch sausages, provides lycopene and protein, but the dish is high in calories and sodium. It is suitable for occasional consumption, and individuals with high blood pressure should be mindful of salt intake.
