# Strawberry Milk Ice Cream
Cuisine: Other
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Seasonal Recommendation: Spring (January to April)

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🥛 Milk 350g
🧀 Mascarpone Cheese 125g
🍬 Granulated Sugar 90g
🍬 Dextrose Powder 42.5g
🍓 Strawberries 250g
🧪 Stabilizer 2.5g

## Cookware & Equipment
🍳 Small Saucepan
⚖️ Digital Scale
🥄 Hand Whisk
🔪 Immersion Blender
🫗 Strainer
🍨 Ice Cream Maker
📦 Rectangular Silicone Mold
❄️ Refrigerator

## Instructions
1. Mix the granulated sugar, dextrose powder, and stabilizer together until evenly combined. Set aside.
2. Pour the milk into a small saucepan. Heat over low heat until lukewarm, then add the mixed sugar powder and stabilizer. Stir until completely dissolved.
3. Add the mascarpone cheese and blend with an immersion blender until smooth and uniform.
4. After the liquid has cooled slightly, add the strawberries and blend again with the immersion blender until smooth.
5. Strain the strawberry milk mixture through a strainer to remove strawberry seeds and other impurities.
6. Pour the strained liquid into the ice cream maker. Freeze and churn according to the machine's instructions.
7. Once churning is complete, transfer the mixture into a rectangular silicone mold and freeze in the refrigerator to set for at least overnight.
8. Remove from the mold and enjoy. The texture is creamy and smooth, with a harmonious blend of milk and strawberry flavors.

## Health Advice
This strawberry milk ice cream is rich in calcium and vitamin C, making it suitable for cooling off in summer. However, it is high in sugar, so it is recommended to control the serving size. The mascarpone cheese enhances the smooth texture, but its calorie content should also be noted.
