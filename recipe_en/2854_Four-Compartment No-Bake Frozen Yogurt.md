# Four-Compartment No-Bake Frozen Yogurt
Cuisine: Other  
Cooking Difficulty: ⭐⭐  
Seasonal Recommendation: Summer (April-August)  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🥛 Yogurt, about 2 cups  
🥛 Whole milk, about 500ml  
🍚 Fermented rice wine (jiuniang), about 1 tablespoon  
🫐 Blueberry jam, about 1 cup  
🍓 Strawberry jam, about 1 cup  
🍫 Chocolate sauce, as needed  
🍓 Strawberries, about 4 pieces (sliced for decoration)  
🫐 Blueberries, about 9 pieces (for decoration)  
🌸 Osmanthus sauce, as needed (for decoration)  
🍫 Cocoa powder, as needed (for decoration)  

## Kitchen Tools and Equipment  
✂️ Scissors  
🔥 Microwave  
🥢 Stirring rod  
🥣 Paper cups  
🧊 Refrigerator  
🔪 Knife  
🥄 Spoon  
📦 Plastic wrap  

## Instructions  
1. Take a discarded cookie tin, use scissors to cut it into a four-compartment mold shape, and line the inside with plastic wrap, ensuring all inner surfaces are covered for easy unmolding later.  
2. Pour the blueberry jam and strawberry jam into separate paper cups, then heat them in the microwave until softened and in a pourable liquid state.  
3. Take a portion of the whole milk, add chocolate sauce, and stir until well combined to make chocolate milk; take another portion of whole milk and mix it to create a light purple milk.  
4. Into the four compartments of the mold, pour the heated blueberry jam, strawberry jam, plain yogurt, and fermented rice wine respectively. Then, pour the prepared purple milk and chocolate milk into the remaining compartments. Do not fill each compartment too full, leaving room for decoration.  
5. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator to chill or freeze until fully set; the liquid should feel solid to the touch.  
6. Once removed, decorate each compartment accordingly: cover the strawberry jam compartment with sliced strawberries, the blueberry jam compartment with blueberries, drizzle the fermented rice wine compartment with osmanthus sauce, and sprinkle the chocolate compartment with cocoa powder.  
7. Use a knife to cut around the edges of the tin, lift the plastic wrap to unmold the entire frozen block, then either slice it or scoop it out for serving.  

## Health Suggestions  
This frozen yogurt is layered with fruit jams and fermented rice wine, offering a sweet and tangy, low-fat option suitable for summer. Those with lactose intolerance can substitute lactose-free milk. Be mindful of the amount of chocolate sauce and osmanthus sauce used to keep the calorie content in check.
