# Blueberry Lemon Boule  
Cuisine: Other  
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐  
Season: All Seasons  

## Ingredients and Seasonings  
🍞 High-gluten flour (as needed)  
🫐 Frozen blueberries (60g)  
🍋 Lemon (about half)  
🧂 Salt (a pinch)  
🍬 Sugar (a pinch)  
🫙 Polish starter (as needed)  

## Kitchenware and Equipment  
♨️ Toshiba water oven  
⚖️ Digital scale  
🥣 Stand mixer  
🧺 Proofing basket  
🔪 Scoring knife  
📜 Parchment paper  
🫙 Plastic wrap  

## Steps  
1. The night before, prepare the Polish starter boule dough: Mix flour, Polish starter, salt, and sugar. Use the liquid from thawed blueberries to replace part of the water in the dough. Knead with a stand mixer until the gluten is fully developed.  
   **Key point**: Thaw the blueberries in advance and strain out the pulp. Use the juice to replace an equal amount of water.  
2. Spread the rested dough on a surface. Top with the thawed blueberry pulp and lemon zest. Fold the dough to enclose the fillings.  
3. Place the dough in a container. Perform a coil fold every 30 minutes, for a total of 3 times. Then let it proof at room temperature until doubled in size—the dough should be airy and feel light.  
   **Note**: Wet your fingers with water to prevent sticking during coil folds. Handle gently to avoid deflating.  
4. Turn out the proofed dough and shape it. Place it in a floured proofing basket. Freeze for about 30 minutes to firm up.  
5. Remove the dough and invert it onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Dust the surface evenly with flour. Use a scoring knife to create patterns.  
6. Place the baking sheet in the water oven. Select the combination cooking mode, preheat to 230°C, and bake for 30 minutes.  
   **Key point**: Utilize the water oven's steam and baking functions to achieve a crispy crust without needing a Dutch oven.  
7. Once baked, remove the boule and let it cool completely on a wire rack before slicing and serving. Reheat using the crispy heating mode if desired.  

## Health Tips  
This blueberry lemon boule is rich in dietary fiber and antioxidants, making it suitable for those pursuing a healthy diet. The sugar content is well controlled—no additional sugar is needed. Using juice from frozen blueberries instead of water not only adds natural color but also enhances the flavor.
