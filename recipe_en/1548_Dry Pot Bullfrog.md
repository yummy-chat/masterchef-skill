# Dry Pot Bullfrog

Cuisine: Sichuan
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐
Season: Suitable year-round

## Ingredients & Seasonings
🐸 Bullfrogs several (cleaned and cut into evenly sized pieces)
🧅 Onion 1 (cut into chunks)
🌶️ Erjingtiao green chili peppers several (sliced into rings)
🌶️ Dried chili peppers to taste (cut into sections)
🌸 Dried Sichuan peppercorns to taste
🫚 Ginger to taste (some smashed for marinating, some diced for stir-frying)
🧄 Garlic to taste (sliced)
🧅 Scallions/Spring onions to taste (scallion segments for marinating, chopped scallions for garnish)
🌿 Cilantro to taste (cut into segments)

🧂 Salt to taste
🧂 White pepper powder to taste
🍶 High-proof liquor to taste
🧂 Chicken bouillon to taste
🫗 Soy sauce to taste
🫒 Sesame oil to taste
🌶️ Sichuan peppercorn oil to taste
🧂 Cumin powder to taste
🤍 White sesame seeds to taste
🥔 Sweet potato starch to taste
🫘 Pixian broad bean paste about 1 tablespoon
🌶️ Secret spicy sauce about 1 tablespoon

## Tools & Equipment
🔪 Cutting board, Chef's knife
🥣 Large stainless steel mixing bowl
🍳 Wok
🥄 Strainer / Spatula
🥢 Wok spatula, Chopsticks
🫗 Oil container / Ladle

## Instructions
1. **Prepare Bullfrog**: Kill, skin, gut, and wash the bullfrogs. Cut into evenly sized pieces.
2. **Prepare Aromatics**: Smash and mince garlic, dice ginger, cut scallions into segments and chop some for garnish, cut onion into chunks, slice green chilies into rings, cut dried chilies into sections, cut cilantro into segments.
3. **Marinate Bullfrog**: Place bullfrog pieces in a bowl. Add salt, white pepper, smashed ginger, scallion segments, and high-proof liquor. Mix well and marinate for 20 minutes.
**Key Point**: Ensure sufficient marinating time for removing gamey taste and infusing flavor.

4. **Remove Marinade**: After marinating, pick out and discard the ginger pieces and scallion segments.
5. **Coat with Starch**: Add sweet potato starch. Mix until each piece of bullfrog is evenly coated with a thin layer.
**Key Point**: Do not use too much starch; a thin coating is sufficient.

6. **Deep-fry Bullfrog**: Pour enough oil into the wok and heat to around 350°F (180°C). Carefully add bullfrog pieces in batches, without stirring initially. Once the surface is set, gently separate them with a slotted spoon. Fry until golden brown and the meat is firm. Remove and drain the oil.
**Note**: Ensure the oil is hot enough to prevent the starch coating from falling off. Let the pieces set before stirring to avoid the coating from breaking.

7. **Stir-fry Base**: Leave a small amount of oil in the wok. Reduce to low heat. Add scallion segments, diced ginger, and minced garlic. Stir-fry until fragrant.
8. **Stir-fry Dried Spices**: Add dried Sichuan peppercorns and dried chili sections. Keep the heat low and stir-fry until fragrant. Be careful not to burn them.
9. **Stir-fry Paste**: Clear a space in the center of the wok. Add Pixian broad bean paste and secret spicy sauce. Stir-fry slowly over low heat until the oil turns red and the paste is aromatic.
10. **Combine with Bullfrog**: Add the fried bullfrog pieces. Turn the heat to high and stir-fry quickly, ensuring each piece is evenly coated with the base mixture.
11. **Add Vegetables**: Add the onion chunks and green chili pepper rings. Stir-fry over high heat for about 30 seconds to 1 minute until the vegetables are just cooked but still crisp.
**Key Point**: Do not overcook the vegetables; keep them crunchy.

12. **Final Seasoning**: Season with salt, chicken bouillon, soy sauce, sesame oil, Sichuan peppercorn oil, cumin powder, and white sesame seeds. Stir-fry quickly to combine.
13. **Serve**: Turn off the heat. Transfer to a large serving plate. Garnish with cilantro segments and chopped scallions. Serve immediately.

## Health Note
Bullfrog is high in protein and low in fat, suitable for muscle building or weight loss diets. However, deep-frying and the spicy sauce add significant calories, so it is recommended to pair this dish with light vegetables for balance. Those with weak digestion should adjust the spiciness to their tolerance and may reduce the amount of dried chili peppers.
