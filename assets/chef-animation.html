Di San Xian (地三鲜)
Northeast Chinese · Difficulty ⭐⭐⭐ · Season: All year
Ingredients
🍆 Chinese eggplant 350g (cut into chunks)
🥔 Potato 250g (peeled, cut into thin chunks)
🌶️ Green pepper 100g (cut into chunks)
🧄 Garlic 40g (about 8 cloves, minced)
🧂 Soy sauce 30g (about 3 tbsp)
🦪 Oyster sauce 20g (about 2 tbsp)
🍯 Dark soy sauce 3g (for color)
🍬 Sugar 6g
🧂 MSG 3g (1 tsp)
🌾 Corn starch 5g (for sauce)
💧 Water 100g
🫒 Cooking oil (enough to submerge ingredients)
Preparation
1Lightly dampen eggplant, dust with corn starch, shake to coat evenly
Coating reduces oil absorption
2Cut potato into thin chunks, pepper into chunks
3Mince 8 cloves of garlic
4Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy, sugar, MSG, corn starch and water in a small bowl
Cooking
1Heat oil until hot, fry potato chunks for ~4 min until golden and cooked through
Turn to medium heat when large bubbles appear
2Remove potato, raise heat. Fry eggplant ~3 min until golden on all sides
Don't stir immediately after adding eggplant
3Quickly flash-fry pepper in residual oil, remove immediately
4Leave a little oil, sauté 1/3 garlic with residual heat, pour in sauce, bring to bubble
5Add potato, eggplant and pepper, toss quickly to coat
6Turn off heat, add remaining 2/3 raw garlic, toss gently and serve
Raw garlic at the end is the soul of this dish
Health Tips
Eggplant absorbs significant oil. Serve with whole grain rice or light soup to balance. Rich in vitamin C and potassium. Control frying time to minimize acrylamide.
